How To Make Wavy Hair Thicker Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Sep 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have wavy hair that lacks volume and thickness, you may be wondering how to enhance your natural texture. Luckily, there are several methods you can try to make your wavy hair look fuller and more voluminous. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make wavy hair thicker.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Wavy Hair Thicker
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Thick and Wavy Hair

How to Make Wavy Hair Thicker

If you want to make your wavy hair look thicker, there are several things you can do:

  • Use volumizing hair products
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down
  • Add layers to your haircut
  • Try hair extensions
  • Eat a healthy diet

We'll go into more detail on each of these methods below.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Use Volumizing Hair Products

One of the easiest ways to add volume and thickness to your wavy hair is to use volumizing hair products. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are specifically designed to add volume to fine hair. These products contain ingredients that coat the hair shaft and plump up the strands, making your hair look fuller and thicker.

To use volumizing hair products, follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo.
  2. Apply a volumizing conditioner to the ends of your hair, avoiding the roots.
  3. Towel-dry your hair gently.
  4. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and throughout your hair.
  5. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.

2. Blow-Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Another way to add volume to your wavy hair is to blow-dry it upside down. This method helps to lift the roots and create more volume at the crown of your head. To blow-dry your hair upside down, follow these steps:

  1. Flip your hair over so that your head is upside down.
  2. Start blow-drying your hair at the roots, using a medium heat setting.
  3. Use your fingers to lift and separate the hair as you blow-dry.
  4. Once your hair is about 80% dry, flip your head back up and finish blow-drying your hair as usual.

3. Add Layers to Your Haircut

Adding layers to your haircut can also help to make your wavy hair look thicker. Layers create movement and texture in the hair, which can make it look fuller and more voluminous. Talk to your hairstylist about adding layers to your haircut to give your wavy hair a thicker, more voluminous appearance.

4. Try Hair Extensions

If you want an instant fix for thin, fine hair, consider trying hair extensions. Hair extensions can add length, volume, and thickness to your hair, giving you the look of full, voluminous wavy hair without having to wait for your natural hair to grow out. There are several types of hair extensions available, including clip-ins, tape-ins, and sew-ins.

5. Eat a Healthy Diet

Your hair is a reflection of your overall health, so if you want to keep your wavy hair looking thick and healthy, it's important to eat a balanced diet. Make sure you're getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet, as these nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth. Foods that are good for your hair include lean protein, leafy greens, nuts, and whole grains.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you make your wavy hair look thicker:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look limp and flat.
  • Avoid using heavy, greasy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I make my wavy hair thicker without using heat?

A: Yes, you can make your wavy hair thicker without using heat. Try using volumizing hair products, adding layers to your haircut, or trying hair extensions.

Q: Will using hair extensions damage my natural hair?

A: Hair extensions can cause damage to your natural hair if they are not applied or maintained properly. Make sure you follow the instructions carefully and choose a reputable stylist to apply your extensions.

Hair Care for Thick and Wavy Hair

If you have thick, wavy hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for thick and wavy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair over time.
  • Avoid using heavy, greasy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and make it look limp and flat.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your wavy hair look thicker and more voluminous. Whether you prefer to use volumizing hair products, blow-dry your hair upside down, or try hair extensions, there are plenty of ways to enhance your natural texture and achieve the look of full, thick wavy hair.
