Best Men s Cut For Thick Straight Hair

Written by Frank Nov 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a man with thick, straight hair, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a cut that will flatter your face shape and work with your hair's texture without requiring too much maintenance. In this guide, we'll explore the best men's cuts for thick straight hair, as well as tips for styling and care.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

The best men's cut for thick straight hair is one that is short and layered. This type of cut will help to reduce the weight of your hair, making it easier to manage and style. It will also add texture and movement to your hair, which is important for maintaining a modern and stylish look.

When choosing a cut, it's important to consider your face shape. A cut that works well for one man may not work well for another. For example, a round face may benefit from a cut that adds height at the crown, while a square face may look best with a cut that is shorter on the sides.

How to

The best way to get the perfect cut for your thick straight hair is to visit a professional stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape and recommend a cut that is tailored to your specific needs. However, if you prefer to cut your own hair or have a friend do it for you, here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  • Comb your hair straight back from your forehead.
  • Using scissors or clippers, cut your hair to the desired length. For a short, layered look, cut the hair on the sides and back shorter than the hair on top.
  • Blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  • Use thinning shears to remove excess bulk from your hair, if necessary.
  • Style your hair using a comb or brush and a styling product of your choice.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide for getting the perfect men's cut for thick straight hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair. This will help to keep your hair healthy and manageable.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet.
  3. Comb your hair straight back from your forehead.
  4. Using a pair of scissors, cut the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length. For a short, layered look, cut the hair on the sides and back shorter than the hair on top.
  5. Blend the hair on the sides and back into the hair on top using scissors or clippers.
  6. Use thinning shears to remove excess bulk from your hair, if necessary.
  7. Style your hair using a comb or brush and a styling product of your choice. For a natural look, use a matte finish product. For a more polished look, use a high-shine product.
  8. Finish off your look by using a hairspray or hair wax to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your men's cut for thick straight hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your style.

FAQ

What is the best men's cut for thick straight hair?

The best men's cut for thick straight hair is one that is short and layered. This type of cut will help to reduce the weight of your hair, making it easier to manage and style. It will also add texture and movement to your hair, which is important for maintaining a modern and stylish look.

How do I style my men's cut for thick straight hair?

To style your men's cut for thick straight hair, use a comb or brush and a styling product of your choice. For a natural look, use a matte finish product. For a more polished look, use a high-shine product. Finish off your look by using a hairspray or hair wax to hold your style in place.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important for maintaining a healthy and stylish look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and reduce frizz.
  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain your style.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can achieve the perfect men's cut for thick straight hair. Remember to consult with a professional stylist for the best results, and take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and stylish look.
