How To Know If Hair Colour Suits You A Comprehensive Guide

Are you thinking of changing your hair colour but not sure if it suits you? Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will look good on you. In this article, we'll show you how to know if hair colour suits you and give you tips on how to find the perfect shade.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know If Hair Colour Suits You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Finding the Perfect Hair Colour
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Know If Hair Colour Suits You

There are a few things to consider when choosing a hair colour that suits you:

  • Your skin tone
  • Your eye colour
  • Your natural hair colour
  • Your personal style and preferences

It's important to keep in mind that not all hair colours will suit everyone. Just because your favorite celebrity has a certain hair colour doesn't mean it will look good on you.

One way to determine if a hair colour suits you is to look at your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, you'll look best with warm hair colours like honey blondes, golden browns, and coppery reds. If you have cool undertones, you'll look best with cool hair colours like ash blondes, cool browns, and burgundy reds.

Another way to determine if a hair colour suits you is to look at your eye colour. If you have light eyes, you'll look best with lighter hair colours. If you have dark eyes, you'll look best with darker hair colours.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to know if hair colour suits you:

  1. Determine your skin tone
  2. Determine your eye colour
  3. Consider your natural hair colour
  4. Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone and eye colour
  5. Consider your personal style and preferences
  6. Consult with a professional hairstylist
  7. Try a temporary hair colour product
  8. Take a selfie and ask for opinions from friends and family

Remember, it's important to choose a hair colour that makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Hair Colour

Here are some tips for finding the perfect hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you have to maintain your hair colour
  • Choose a hair colour that complements your wardrobe
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades
  • Take inspiration from your favorite celebrities
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights for a more natural look

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair colour:

Q: Will a drastic hair colour change suit me?

A: It depends on your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style. It's best to consult with a professional hairstylist before making a drastic hair colour change.

Q: How can I tell if a hair colour will suit me without trying it?

A: Look at your skin tone and eye colour to determine what hair colours will complement them. You can also try a temporary hair colour product to see how it looks on you.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Here are some hair care tips for coloured hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for coloured hair
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can keep your coloured hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Choosing a hair colour that suits you can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style, you can find the perfect hair colour that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades and consult with a professional hairstylist for their expert opinion.
