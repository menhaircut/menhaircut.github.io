Volume Haircut Long Hair Adding Bounce And Life To Your Locks

Written by Eveline Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair is a prized possession for many women, but sometimes it can fall flat and lackluster. That's where a volume haircut comes in. This type of cut adds body, bounce, and life to your long locks, making them look more voluminous and healthy. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about volume haircut long hair, including how to get it, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Volume Haircut Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining Volume Haircut Long Hair

How to Get a Volume Haircut Long Hair

A volume haircut long hair is a cut that removes some of the weight from your hair, making it look lighter and more voluminous. This type of cut is best for those with thick, heavy hair that tends to fall flat. To get a volume haircut, you'll need to visit a professional stylist who can help you determine the best cut for your hair type and face shape.

During your consultation, your stylist will likely recommend layers, which can add volume and movement to your hair. They may also suggest thinning out your ends or adding face-framing layers to help give your hair more dimension.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you're interested in getting a volume haircut long hair, here are some general steps you can expect:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best cut for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair to ensure it's clean and free of any styling products.
  3. Sit down in the stylist's chair and discuss the desired length and layers.
  4. Let the stylist work their magic, cutting your hair to create the desired volume and shape.
  5. Leave the salon feeling lighter and more voluminous than ever before!

Tips for Maintaining Volume Haircut Long Hair

Once you've got your volume haircut, you'll want to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining volume haircut long hair:

  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, to add even more body to your hair.
  • Avoid weighing down your hair with heavy styling products or oils.
  • Aim to wash your hair every other day, as over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it appear flat.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to help lift the roots and add volume.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair and make it appear limp.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get a volume haircut long hair?

A: This largely depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule of thumb, you should aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and voluminous.

Q: Will a volume haircut long hair work for thin hair?

A: While a volume haircut is best for those with thick, heavy hair, it can still work for those with thin hair. Your stylist may suggest adding layers or using volumizing products to help give your hair more body.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail or updo with a volume haircut long hair?

A: Absolutely! A volume haircut long hair can still be styled in a variety of ways, including ponytails, updos, and braids. Talk to your stylist about the best styling options for your hair type and desired look.

Hair Care for Volume Haircut Long Hair

Maintaining healthy, voluminous hair starts with good hair care. Here are some tips for keeping your volume haircut long hair looking its best:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage and make your hair appear flat.
  • Invest in a quality hairbrush with natural bristles to help distribute your scalp's natural oils throughout your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair and make it appear limp.
  • Aim to eat a healthy, balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and health.

By following these tips and getting regular trims, your volume haircut long hair will look healthy, bouncy, and full of life!
