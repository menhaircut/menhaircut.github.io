How To Cut Your Head With Clippers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to save some money and cut your own hair, using clippers can be a great option. Not only is it a cost-effective solution, but with the right technique, you can achieve professional-looking results from the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of cutting your head with clippers.

How to Cut Your Head with Clippers

Before we get started, it's important to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a set of clippers, a mirror, hair clips or ties, a comb, and a pair of scissors. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Clipper Guard Size

The first step is to choose the right clipper guard size. This will determine how short your hair will be. If you want a more natural look, start with a longer guard size and work your way down until you reach your desired length.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair using hair clips or ties. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. This will make it easier to cut your hair evenly and avoid any missed spots.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Using your clippers, start at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up the side of your head. Use a comb to guide the clippers and ensure a straight cut. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Work on the Back

Once you've finished the sides, move on to the back of your head. Again, start at the bottom and work your way up. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

Step 5: Finish with the Top

Finally, move on to the top of your head. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Use the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut. If you want a longer look on top, use scissors to trim any excess hair.

Tips for Cutting Your Head with Clippers

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting your hair too short.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure you haven't missed any spots.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing can lead to uneven cuts.
  • Use scissors to trim any excess hair for a more natural look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your head with clippers:

Can I use clippers on wet hair?

It's best to use clippers on dry hair. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly and can cause the clippers to pull or snag.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want it to be. Generally, cutting your hair every 4-6 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Can I cut my own hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. Just make sure to follow the steps outlined in this guide and take your time to achieve the best results.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Cutting your head with clippers can be a cost-effective and convenient way to maintain your hair. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve professional-looking results from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
