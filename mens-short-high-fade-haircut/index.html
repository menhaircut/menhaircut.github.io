Mens Short High Fade Haircut The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on the mens short high fade haircut! This trendy hairstyle has taken the world by storm and is a popular choice among men of all ages. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Mens Short High Fade Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Short High Fade Haircut
  • FAQs

Description

The mens short high fade haircut is a popular hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back, with longer hair on top. This style is achieved by gradually fading the hair from short to longer as it reaches the top of the head. The high fade refers to the length of the hair on the sides and back, which is typically cut shorter than the hair on top. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

How to Get a Mens Short High Fade Haircut

If you’re interested in getting a mens short high fade haircut, the first step is to find a skilled barber who can help you achieve the look you want. It’s important to communicate your expectations clearly with your barber and bring in photos of the style you want to ensure that you’re both on the same page.

Once you’ve found a barber you trust, it’s time to decide on the specifics of your haircut. This includes choosing the length of the hair on top, the length of the fade, and any other details you want to incorporate, such as a part or texture.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here’s a step-by-step guide on getting a mens short high fade haircut:

  1. Start by shampooing and conditioning your hair to remove any product buildup and ensure that your hair is clean and easy to work with.
  2. Consult with your barber to determine the specifics of your haircut, including the length of the hair on top and the length of the fade.
  3. Your barber will start by cutting the hair on the sides and back to the desired length, gradually fading it shorter as it approaches the top of the head.
  4. The hair on top will be cut to the desired length and styled according to your preferences.
  5. If desired, your barber can incorporate additional details, such as a part or texture, to complete the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Mens Short High Fade Haircut

Maintaining a mens short high fade haircut takes some effort, but the results are well worth it. Here are some tips for keeping your haircut looking its best:

  • Visit your barber regularly for touch-ups to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • To keep your hair looking sleek and styled, use a hair product such as pomade or wax.
  • To maintain the fade, use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back as needed.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

FAQs

How long does a mens short high fade haircut last?

A mens short high fade haircut typically lasts 2-3 weeks before it starts to grow out and lose its shape. To maintain the look, it’s recommended to visit your barber for touch-ups every 4-6 weeks.

Can a mens short high fade haircut work for all hair types?

Yes, a mens short high fade haircut can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair.

Hair Care for Mens Short High Fade Haircut

To keep your mens short high fade haircut looking its best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and prevent damage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to maintain a stylish and sophisticated mens short high fade haircut that turns heads wherever you go.
