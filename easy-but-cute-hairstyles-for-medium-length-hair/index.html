Easy But Cute Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Oliver Sep 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles for your medium length hair? It's time to switch things up and try out some new styles that are not only cute but also easy to do. In this article, we will show you some easy but cute hairstyles that you can do in no time.

Description

Medium length hair can be a bit tricky to style, but it doesn't have to be. With a little bit of creativity, you can create some amazing hairstyles that will make you stand out from the crowd. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a special event.

How to

Before we dive into the hairstyles, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a brush, hair ties, bobby pins, and some hair accessories. Now let's get started.

1. Messy Bun

This is the perfect hairstyle for those lazy days when you don't want to spend too much time on your hair. Simply gather your hair into a high ponytail, twist it around, and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out some strands to create a messy look, and you're good to go.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

This is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. Simply gather the top half of your hair and tie it with a hair tie. You can leave some strands loose to frame your face. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a special event.

3. Braids

Braids are a great way to add some texture to your hair. You can do a simple braid, a fishtail braid, or a Dutch braid. The possibilities are endless. You can also add some accessories like flowers or beads to make your braids stand out.

4. Low Ponytail

A low ponytail is a simple and elegant hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Simply gather your hair at the nape of your neck and tie it with a hair tie. You can also wrap a strand of hair around the hair tie to hide it.

Step by Step

1. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair around.
  3. Secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some strands to create a messy look.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

  1. Gather the top half of your hair.
  2. Tie it with a hair tie.
  3. Leave some strands loose to frame your face.

3. Braids

  1. Choose the type of braid you want to do.
  2. Start braiding your hair.
  3. Add some accessories if desired.

4. Low Ponytail

  1. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck.
  2. Tie it with a hair tie.
  3. Wrap a strand of hair around the hair tie to hide it.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle:

  • Use a brush to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Use hair accessories to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy but cute hairstyles for medium length hair:

1. How long should my hair be for these hairstyles?

Your hair should be at least shoulder length for these hairstyles. If your hair is shorter than that, you can still try some of these styles, but you may need to modify them to suit your hair length.

2. Can I do these hairstyles on curly hair?

Yes, you can do these hairstyles on curly hair. In fact, some of these styles look even better on curly hair.

3. How long does it take to do these hairstyles?

These hairstyles should take no more than 10-15 minutes to do. They are perfect for those mornings when you're short on time.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, which can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these easy but cute hairstyles for medium length hair, you'll never have a bad hair day again. Try them out and see which ones work best for you.
