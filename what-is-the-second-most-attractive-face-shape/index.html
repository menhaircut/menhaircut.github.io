What Is The Second Most Attractive Face Shape

Written by Petter Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're curious about what is the second most attractive face shape, you're in the right place! In this blog post, we will explore the answer to this question and provide some tips on how to enhance your natural beauty.

Description

Everyone has a unique face shape, and some are considered more attractive than others. While beauty is subjective, research has shown that there are certain face shapes that are more commonly viewed as attractive. According to experts, the second most attractive face shape is the oval face shape.

An oval face shape is characterized by a gently curved forehead, a slightly narrow jawline, and high cheekbones. This shape is often considered the "ideal" face shape because it is well-balanced and versatile. People with oval faces can pull off a variety of hairstyles and makeup looks with ease.

How to Identify Your Face Shape

If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are a few simple steps you can take to identify it. Start by pulling your hair back and looking at your face in the mirror. Take note of the widest parts of your face, such as your cheekbones, forehead, and jawline.

If your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your forehead and jawline are slightly narrower, you likely have an oval face shape. If your forehead is the widest part of your face and your jawline is narrow, you may have a heart-shaped face. If your jawline is the widest part of your face, you may have a square face shape. If your face is roughly equal in width and length, you may have a round face shape.

Step by Step Tips to Enhance Your Natural Beauty

Regardless of your face shape, there are a few tips you can follow to enhance your natural beauty.

  1. Take care of your skin: A healthy, glowing complexion is always in style. Be sure to cleanse, moisturize, and protect your skin from the sun.
  2. Experiment with makeup: Makeup can be a fun way to play up your best features. Try different looks and techniques to find what works best for you.
  3. Choose hairstyles that flatter your face shape: Different face shapes look best with different hairstyles. Experiment with different cuts and styles to find what flatters your features.
  4. Dress for your body type: Wearing clothes that fit well and flatter your figure can help you feel more confident and attractive.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shapes

If you have an oval face shape, there are a few hair care tips you can follow to enhance your natural beauty.

  1. Experiment with bangs: Bangs can help balance out an oval face shape and draw attention to your eyes.
  2. Try different partings: Changing up your parting can give your hair more volume and add dimension to your face.
  3. Avoid overly long hairstyles: While oval faces can pull off a variety of hairstyles, overly long hair can overwhelm your features. Consider a medium-length cut for a flattering look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about face shapes and beauty:

What is the most attractive face shape?

While beauty is subjective, many experts consider the oval face shape to be the most attractive.

What is the rarest face shape?

The rarest face shape is the diamond face shape, which is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin.

Can you change your face shape?

While you can't change your bone structure, certain hairstyles and makeup techniques can create the illusion of a different face shape.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the second most attractive face shape is the oval face shape. If you have an oval face shape, consider experimenting with different hairstyles and makeup looks to enhance your natural beauty. Remember, beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, so embrace what makes you unique!
