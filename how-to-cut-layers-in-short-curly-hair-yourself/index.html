How To Cut Layers In Short Curly Hair Yourself

Written by Aprilia Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon every time you need a haircut? Or maybe you want to save some money? Whatever the reason, cutting layers in short curly hair yourself is not as hard as it may seem. In this article, we will show you how to cut layers in short curly hair yourself in a few easy steps. Let's get started!

Step by Step Guide

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Once you have the tools, you can follow these steps:

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow-dry it. Make sure it is completely dry before you start cutting.
  2. Divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.
  3. Start with the front section. Take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold it between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Repeat this process for the entire front section.
  4. Move to the back sections. Take a small section of hair from the bottom and comb it straight down. Hold it between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Repeat this process for the entire back section.
  5. Release the top sections and blend them with the layers you just cut. Take a small section of hair from the top and comb it straight down. Hold it between your fingers and cut it at an angle. Repeat this process for the entire top section.
  6. Once you have finished cutting, use a mirror to check the back of your head. Make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Layers in Short Curly Hair

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, but with these tips, you can make the process easier:

  • Start with small sections of hair. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once you've cut it.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it is wet. Wet hair is longer than dry hair, so you may cut more than you intended.
  • Make sure your scissors are clean. Dirty scissors can transfer bacteria to your hair and scalp.
  • Take your time. Rushing can lead to mistakes.

FAQs

Can I cut layers in my hair if it is curly?

Yes, you can cut layers in curly hair. In fact, cutting layers can help define your curls and add volume.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to get rid of split ends. As a general rule, you should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, you should use hair cutting scissors. Regular scissors can damage your hair and make it harder to cut.

Hair Care after Cutting Layers in Short Curly Hair

After you have cut your hair, it is important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it brittle.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet. Use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Now that you know how to cut layers in short curly hair yourself, you can save time and money by doing it at home. Remember to take your time and follow the steps carefully to get the best results.
