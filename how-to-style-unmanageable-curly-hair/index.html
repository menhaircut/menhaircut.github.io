How To Style Unmanageable Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Unmanageable curly hair can be frustrating to deal with, especially if you don't know how to style it properly. But fear not, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful and manageable curly hairstyle. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to style unmanageable curly hair, as well as some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Style Unmanageable Curly Hair

The key to styling unmanageable curly hair is to use the right products and techniques to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, it's important to start with clean hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize and define your curls. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.

3. Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

Apply a curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your damp hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz. Use a quarter-sized amount of product and distribute it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

4. Use a Diffuser Attachment

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and use it to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Use a low heat and low speed setting to avoid damaging your hair.

5. Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair gently. This will help enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

6. Finish with a Hair Oil

Finish your hairstyle by applying a hair oil or serum to your ends to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Styling Unmanageable Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style unmanageable curly hair:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it more prone to frizz. Try to wash your hair no more than two or three times a week.

2. Use a Microfiber Towel

When drying your hair, use a microfiber towel to avoid damaging your hair and causing frizz. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel and instead gently scrunch your hair to remove excess water.

3. Sleep with a Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help reduce frizz and prevent breakage. Satin is less likely to cause friction on your hair than cotton, which can lead to frizz and tangles.

4. Try Different Products

Experiment with different curl-enhancing products to find the ones that work best for your hair. Don't be afraid to try different brands and formulas until you find the perfect one for your curls.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircut for curly hair is one that allows your curls to bounce and move freely. A layered haircut with long layers can help enhance your curls and add volume to your hair.

How often should you trim curly hair?

You should trim your curly hair every 3-4 months to prevent split ends and breakage.

Can you straighten curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten curly hair using a flat iron or a blow dryer with a straightening attachment. However, it's important to use heat protectant products to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Advice for Unmanageable Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for unmanageable curly hair:

1. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

2. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to help moisturize and nourish your hair. This will help reduce frizz and keep your curls healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and cause frizz, especially if you have unmanageable curly hair. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair product.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and manageable curly hairstyle. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
