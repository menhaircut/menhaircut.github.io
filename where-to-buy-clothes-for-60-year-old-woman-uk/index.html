If you're a 60-year-old woman looking for stylish and comfortable clothing, you're in luck! There are plenty of options available for you to choose from in the UK. In this article, we'll explore some of the best places to buy clothes for women over 60, as well as provide you with some tips on how to choose the perfect outfit.

How to Choose Clothes for Women Over 60

When it comes to choosing clothes for women over 60, comfort and style are key. You want to find pieces that are comfortable enough to wear all day, but also fashionable enough to make you feel confident and stylish.

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing clothes:

Tip #1: Choose Comfortable Fabrics

When looking for clothes, opt for fabrics that are soft and comfortable, such as cotton, linen, and silk. These fabrics are breathable and will keep you cool and comfortable all day long.

Tip #2: Look for Classic Styles

Classic styles never go out of fashion, so look for pieces that are timeless and elegant. A well-tailored blazer, a simple shift dress, or a pair of straight-leg trousers are all great options for women over 60.

Tip #3: Consider Your Body Shape

When choosing clothes, consider your body shape and choose pieces that flatter your figure. If you're pear-shaped, for example, choose clothes that highlight your waist and skim over your hips.

Tip #4: Accessorize

Accessories can make all the difference when it comes to completing an outfit. A statement necklace or a pair of stylish shoes can elevate a simple outfit and make you feel confident and stylish.

Where to Buy Clothes for Women Over 60

Now that you have some tips on how to choose clothes, let's look at some of the best places to buy clothes for women over 60 in the UK.

1. Marks & Spencer

Marks & Spencer is a popular department store that offers a wide range of clothing for women over 60. They have a great selection of classic styles that are both comfortable and fashionable.

2. John Lewis

John Lewis is another great department store that offers a wide range of clothing for women over 60. They have a great selection of brands that cater to mature women, such as Phase Eight and Hobbs.

3. JD Williams

JD Williams is an online retailer that specializes in clothing for mature women. They offer a wide range of sizes and styles, so you're sure to find something that suits you.

4. Bonmarche

Bonmarche is a popular high-street retailer that offers affordable clothing for women over 60. They have a great selection of classic styles that are both comfortable and stylish.

5. Chums

Chums is another online retailer that specializes in clothing for mature women. They offer a wide range of sizes and styles, so you're sure to find something that suits you.

Hair Care Tips for Women Over 60

When it comes to looking and feeling your best, hair care is just as important as clothing. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

Tip #1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

As you age, your hair can become more fragile, so it's important to use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Tip #2: Condition Regularly

Conditioner can help to add moisture to your hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny. Try to condition your hair at least once a week.

Tip #3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to use them sparingly. When you do use them, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Tip #4: Embrace Your Natural Texture

As you age, your hair may become thinner or lose its natural color. Embrace your natural texture and work with what you have. A short, cropped hairstyle can be both stylish and easy to maintain.
