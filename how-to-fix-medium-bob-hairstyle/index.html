How To Fix Medium Bob Hairstyle

Written by Robby Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to style your medium bob hairstyle? Fear not, we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you on how to fix your medium bob hairstyle effortlessly. From the tools you need to the step by step process, we have it all covered. So, let's get started!

Description

A medium bob hairstyle is a classic and timeless look that suits almost all face shapes. It is a versatile haircut that can be styled in many ways. However, maintaining this hairstyle can be a bit challenging, especially if you are not familiar with the right techniques. In this article, we will show you how to fix your medium bob hairstyle to make it look chic and effortless.

How to

Here is a step by step guide on how to fix your medium bob hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step in fixing your medium bob hairstyle is to wash and condition your hair. Use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to nourish your hair and make it more manageable. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before styling your hair, apply a heat protectant to prevent damage from heat styling tools. Heat protectant spray or cream is readily available in the market, and you can choose one based on your hair type and texture. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections and blow-dry it using a round brush. Start from the roots and work your way to the ends to create volume and bounce. Use the brush to curl the ends inward for a more polished look. Repeat this process until you have dried all the sections of your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it in many ways. You can create loose waves using a curling iron or straighten it using a flat iron. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture to your hair. Experiment with different styles to find the perfect one that suits your face shape and personality.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you fix your medium bob hairstyle:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in good quality heat styling tools such as a curling iron and flat iron.
  • Use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture to your hair.
  • Visit a professional hairstylist regularly to maintain your hairstyle and trim split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about fixing medium bob hairstyle:

Can I fix my medium bob hairstyle at home?

Yes, you can easily fix your medium bob hairstyle at home. All you need is the right tools and techniques.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from drying out.

What should I do if my hair is too frizzy?

If your hair is too frizzy, use a smoothing serum or oil to tame it. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Hair Care

Maintaining a medium bob hairstyle requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip off its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair styling products as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

With these tips and techniques, you can fix your medium bob hairstyle and make it look chic and effortless. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Happy styling!
