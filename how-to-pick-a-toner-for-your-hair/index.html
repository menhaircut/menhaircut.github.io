How To Pick A Toner For Your Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to enhance the color of your hair or neutralize unwanted tones, a toner can do wonders. However, with so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to pick the right one for your hair. In this guide, we'll help you navigate through the process of choosing a toner for your hair, from understanding the basics to picking the right shade.

What is a Toner?

A toner is a hair product that helps to neutralize unwanted tones and enhance the color of your hair. It's typically used after bleaching or coloring your hair to achieve a more desirable shade. Toners come in different forms, including demi-permanent hair color, color-depositing conditioners, and hair masks.

How to Pick a Toner for Your Hair

1. Determine Your Hair Color

The first step to picking the right toner is to determine your hair color. This will help you understand which tone you need to neutralize or enhance. If you have naturally blonde hair, you might need a toner that neutralizes yellow or orange tones. If you have brown hair, you might want to enhance warm or cool tones, depending on your preference.

2. Understand Your Hair Type

It's important to choose a toner that suits your hair type. If you have dry or damaged hair, you might want to opt for a toner that's gentle and nourishing. If you have oily hair, you might need a toner that's lightweight and doesn't weigh down your hair.

3. Decide on the Form

Toners come in different forms, including demi-permanent hair color, color-depositing conditioners, and hair masks. Demi-permanent hair color is the most long-lasting and can last up to 6-8 weeks. Color-depositing conditioners and hair masks are temporary and can be used as often as needed.

4. Pick the Right Shade

The most important step in picking a toner is to choose the right shade. Toners come in different shades, from warm to cool tones. If you're looking to neutralize yellow or orange tones, you might need a toner with a purple or blue base. If you're looking to enhance warm tones, you might want a toner with a red or gold base.

Tips for Using a Toner

Here are some tips to help you use a toner effectively:

1. Follow the Instructions

Always follow the instructions on the packaging when using a toner. This will ensure that you get the best results and avoid damaging your hair.

2. Test the Toner

Before applying the toner all over your hair, do a patch test to see how it reacts with your hair. This will help you avoid any allergic reactions or unexpected results.

3. Use a Neutralizing Shampoo

After using a toner, it's important to use a neutralizing shampoo to restore the pH balance of your hair. This will help to prevent any unwanted color changes and keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

1. Can I use a toner on natural hair?

Yes, you can use a toner on natural hair. However, it's important to choose a toner that's suitable for your hair type and color.

2. How often should I use a toner?

The frequency of toner use depends on the type of toner you're using. Demi-permanent hair color can be used every 6-8 weeks, while color-depositing conditioners and hair masks can be used as often as needed.

3. Can a toner damage my hair?

When used correctly, toners shouldn't damage your hair. However, if you leave the toner on for too long or use it too frequently, it can cause dryness and breakage.

Hair Care Tips

Aside from using a toner, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and vibrant:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage and breakage.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning your hair at least once a week can help to keep it moisturized and nourished.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair and cause color fading. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun.

With these tips, you're now equipped to pick the right toner for your hair and keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Remember to always follow the instructions and take good care of your hair to achieve the best results.
