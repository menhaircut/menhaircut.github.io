How To Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Medium length hair is versatile and can be styled in various ways to suit different occasions. Whether you are going to work, attending a party, or just running errands, there's a hairstyle for every mood and need. In this ultimate guide, we will show you how to hairstyles for medium length hair that are easy to do and will make you look fabulous.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Medium Length Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Medium Length Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Medium Length Hair

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, it's essential to understand how to style medium length hair. Here are some general tips:

  • Start with clean and dry hair. If your hair is wet, blow dry it first.
  • Use heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage when using hot tools.
  • Invest in quality hair products such as a good hairspray, mousse, or styling cream.
  • Make sure your hair is detangled before styling.
  • Don't rush through the styling process. Take your time and be patient.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics let's get into the different hairstyles for medium length hair.

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the base to form a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out some pieces of hair around your face to create a messy look.
  5. Spray with hairspray to hold in place.

2. Beach Waves

Beach waves are perfect for a casual day out or a date night.

  1. Section your hair into two parts.
  2. Take a curling iron and curl each section away from your face.
  3. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold in place.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is a chic and effortless look.

  1. Take the top half of your hair and pull it back.
  2. Secure it with a hair tie or clip.
  3. Tease the crown of your head for extra volume.
  4. Spray with hairspray to hold in place.

Tips for Medium Length Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your medium length hair:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scrunchies.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles. You might discover a new favorite.
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQ

1. What are some hairstyles for medium length hair?

There are many hairstyles for medium length hair, including messy buns, beach waves, and half-up, half-down styles.

2. How do I style my hair for a formal event?

For a formal event, consider an updo such as a chignon or a sleek ponytail.

3. How do I prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

Use a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz. Avoid using hot tools on high heat, and try not to touch your hair too much throughout the day.

Hair Care Tips

Lastly, here are some general hair care tips to keep your medium length hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hot tools on high heat as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.

Now that you know how to hairstyles for medium length hair, it's time to experiment and have fun with your hair. Remember to take care of your hair and use quality hair products to keep it healthy and shiny.
