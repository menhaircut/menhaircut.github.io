What To Do When No Hairstyle Suits You

Are you struggling to find a hairstyle that suits you? It can be frustrating when you try different hairstyles and nothing seems to work. But don't worry, there are solutions to this problem. In this article, we will discuss what to do when no hairstyle suits you and how to find the perfect hairstyle. Let's get started!

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in finding a hairstyle that flatters your features. The most common face shapes are round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, look at your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead. You can also use a measuring tape to measure your face's length and width.

If your jawline is rounded, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a round face shape. If your forehead and jawline are roughly the same width, and your cheekbones are not prominent, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a heart-shaped face. If your face is longer than it is wide, and your forehead and jawline are roughly the same width, you have an oval face shape. If your cheekbones are wider than your forehead and jawline, you have a diamond face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Find Your Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find the perfect hairstyle. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Research and collect hairstyles that flatter your face shape. You can use Google or Pinterest to search for hairstyles for your face shape.
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist and show them the pictures you've collected. They can recommend which hairstyle would work best for you.
  3. Try on different hairstyles using virtual makeover apps. These apps allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles virtually.
  4. Experiment with wigs or hair extensions. Wigs or hair extensions can allow you to try on different hairstyles without committing to a permanent change.

Tips to Consider When Choosing a Hairstyle

Here are some tips to consider when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle. Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Consider your hair texture. Choose a hairstyle that works with your hair texture. If you have curly hair, you may want a hairstyle that accentuates your curls.
  • Consider your hair length. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your hair length. If you have short hair, you may want a hairstyle that adds volume.
  • Consider your face shape. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. A hairstyle that looks good on a heart-shaped face may not look good on a square-shaped face.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hairstyle:

Q: What if I don't like any of the hairstyles that flatter my face shape?

A: If you don't like any of the hairstyles that flatter your face shape, consider trying a different hair color or hair accessories.

Q: Can I still wear a hairstyle that doesn't flatter my face shape?

A: Yes, you can wear any hairstyle you want. However, a hairstyle that flatters your face shape will enhance your features and make you look more attractive.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after every wash.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools frequently.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Remember, finding the perfect hairstyle takes time and patience. Don't be afraid to try new things, and always consult with a professional hairstylist before making any permanent changes to your hair.
