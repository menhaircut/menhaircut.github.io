Best Hair Color Ideas For 50 Year Old Brunettes

Written by Oliver Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a 50-year-old brunette looking to switch up your hair color, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best hair color ideas for women over 50. Whether you're looking to go bold or subtle, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

As we age, our hair naturally loses its pigment, which can result in dull, lackluster locks. However, adding a pop of color can instantly brighten up your complexion and give you a more youthful appearance. Choosing the right hair color can be daunting, especially if you're not sure where to start. That's why we've put together this guide to help you find the perfect shade for your skin tone and personal style.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When it comes to choosing the right hair color, there are a few things to consider. First and foremost, you want to find a shade that complements your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, opt for rich, warm shades like caramel or honey. If you have cool undertones, try icy blondes or ashy browns. Another factor to consider is your personal style. Are you someone who likes to stand out, or do you prefer a more natural look? Do you want something low-maintenance or are you willing to put in the effort to maintain your color?

Step by Step Guide

1. Determine your skin tone: Before choosing a hair color, it's important to determine your skin tone. Hold a white piece of paper up to your face in natural light. If your skin appears yellow or golden, you have warm undertones. If your skin appears pink or rosy, you have cool undertones.

2. Choose a shade: Based on your skin tone, choose a shade that complements it. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm shades like caramel or honey. If you have cool undertones, try ashy browns or icy blondes.

3. Consult with a stylist: If you're unsure which shade to choose, consult with a stylist. They can help you find the perfect color for your skin tone and personal style.

4. Prepare your hair: Before coloring your hair, make sure it's in good condition. Use a deep conditioning treatment to nourish and hydrate your locks.

5. Apply the color: Follow the instructions on your hair color kit to apply the color evenly. If you're unsure how to apply the color, consult with a stylist.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

1. Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner: Regular shampoo and conditioner can strip your hair of its color. Use products specifically designed for colored hair to maintain its vibrancy.

2. Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can fade your hair color. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your locks from the sun.

3. Touch up your roots: As your hair grows, your roots will start to show. Touch them up every 4-6 weeks to keep your color looking fresh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair at home. However, if you're unsure how to apply the color or which shade to choose, it's best to consult with a stylist.

Q: Will coloring my hair damage it?

A: Coloring your hair can cause damage if not done correctly. However, using a deep conditioning treatment before and after coloring can help minimize damage.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It's recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks to keep your color looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Heat can damage your hair and cause it to break.

3. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

4. Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.

5. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and ideas, you're well on your way to finding the perfect hair color for your 50-year-old self. Whether you choose a bold new shade or a subtle change, embrace your new look with confidence and style.
