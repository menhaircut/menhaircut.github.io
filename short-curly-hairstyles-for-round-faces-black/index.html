Short Curly Hairstyles For Round Faces Black

Written by Oliver Jul 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a proud owner of a beautiful round face and curly hair? Then you must know how challenging it can be to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Luckily, there are plenty of short curly hairstyles for round faces black that can help you look stunning and confident. In this article, we will provide you with useful tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice to help you rock your curly hair with pride.

Description

Round faces have a soft and feminine appearance, and curly hair adds a playful and romantic touch. However, finding the right hairstyle that balances the roundness of your face and enhances your natural curls can be tricky. That's why we've compiled a list of the best short curly hairstyles for round faces black to help you achieve a chic and trendy look.

How to

Before we dive into the different hairstyles, here are some general tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a style that adds height to your crown and elongates your face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that emphasize the roundness of your face.
  • Experiment with different lengths and layers to find the perfect balance.
  • Use styling products that enhance your curls and add volume.

Now, let's take a look at some of the best short curly hairstyles for round faces black:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a short and sassy look that highlights your facial features and enhances your natural curls. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave some length on top. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and create volume on the crown.

2. Curly Bob

The curly bob is another popular hairstyle that looks great on round faces. It's a medium-length cut that frames your face and adds volume to your curls. To achieve this look, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair into a chin-length bob with layers. Use a curl-defining product to create bouncy and defined curls.

3. Afro Puff

The afro puff is a fun and playful hairstyle that adds height to your crown and elongates your face. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail on the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band. Use a pick to fluff out your curls and create a voluminous afro puff.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the perfect short curly hairstyle for your round face:

1. Pixie Cut

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a curl-enhancing product.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and create volume.
  4. Using scissors, cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave some length on top.
  5. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and create volume on the crown.

2. Curly Bob

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a curl-defining product.
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and create volume.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair into a chin-length bob with layers.
  5. Use a curl-defining product to create bouncy and defined curls.

3. Afro Puff

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product and use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  3. Gather your hair into a high ponytail on the crown of your head and secure it with an elastic band.
  4. Use a pick to fluff out your curls and create a voluminous afro puff.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you rock your short curly hairstyle:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and damage to your curls while you sleep.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type and curl pattern.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short curly hairstyles for round faces black:

1. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face and curly hair?

Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face and curly hair. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your features.

2. How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.

3. Can I use heat styling tools on my curly hair?

Yes, you can use heat styling tools on your curly hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting to prevent damage. It's also important to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and beautiful curly hair, it's important to follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you take care of your curls:

  • Wash your hair with lukewarm water, as hot water can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and a moisturizing conditioner to keep your curls soft and hydrated.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to restore moisture and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and minimize damage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and damage to your curls while you sleep.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock your short curly hairstyle with confidence and style. Experiment with different styles and products to find the ones that work best for you, and don't be afraid to embrace your natural curls and round face. You are beautiful just the way you are!
