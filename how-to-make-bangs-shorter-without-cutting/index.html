How To Make Bangs Shorter Without Cutting A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 30, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your long bangs getting in your eyes but don't want to cut them off completely? Well, you're in luck! There are several ways to make your bangs shorter without cutting them. In this article, we'll go over some easy and effective methods to help you achieve the shorter bangs you desire.

How to Make Bangs Shorter Without Cutting

If you're looking for ways to make your bangs shorter without cutting them, you've come to the right place. Here are some ways to shorten your bangs:

1. Twist and Pin Method

One way to shorten your bangs is to twist and pin them back. Here's how:

  1. Take a small section of your bangs and twist it tightly.
  2. Pin the twisted section back with a bobby pin.
  3. Repeat the process until your desired length is achieved.

This method is perfect for those who want to keep their bangs out of their face but still want them to look stylish.

2. Trim the Bottom Layer

If you don't want to cut your bangs off completely, try trimming the bottom layer. Here's how:

  1. Comb your bangs straight down.
  2. Take a small section of the bottom layer and trim it with scissors.
  3. Repeat the process until your desired length is achieved.

Remember to trim slowly and carefully to avoid cutting too much off.

3. Use Hair Wax

Hair wax can be used to help shorten your bangs. Here's how:

  1. Take a small amount of hair wax and rub it between your fingers.
  2. Apply the wax to your bangs and style them as desired.
  3. Repeat the process until your desired length is achieved.

Hair wax can help hold your bangs in place and make them easier to style.

Tips for Making Bangs Shorter Without Cutting

When trying to make your bangs shorter without cutting them, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Start small and work your way up to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Be patient and take your time to get the desired length.

FAQ

Can I make my bangs shorter without cutting them?

Yes, there are several ways to make your bangs shorter without cutting them. Some methods include twisting and pinning them back, trimming the bottom layer, and using hair wax.

Will these methods damage my hair?

No, these methods should not damage your hair if done correctly. However, it's important to be gentle and not pull or tug on your hair too much.

Hair Care

When trying to make your bangs shorter without cutting them, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

With these easy and effective methods, you can make your bangs shorter without cutting them. Whether you prefer the twist and pin method or using hair wax, there's a solution for everyone. Remember to be patient and take your time to get the desired length. Happy styling!
