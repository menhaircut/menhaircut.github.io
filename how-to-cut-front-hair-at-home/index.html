How To Cut Front Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 04, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of constantly going to the salon to get your front hair trimmed? With a little practice and patience, you can learn how to cut your front hair at home. Not only will this save you time and money, but it’ll also give you the freedom to experiment with different styles without having to worry about the cost.

How to Cut Front Hair at Home:

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural fall of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair down the middle. This will help you determine how much hair you want to cut.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Forward

Comb your hair forward so that it covers your forehead. This will be the section of hair that you’ll be cutting.

Step 4: Cut Your Hair

Using your scissors, make small snips along the bottom of your hair. Make sure to cut vertically and not horizontally. This will help create a natural, feathery look.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After every few snips, check your progress in the mirror. You don’t want to cut too much hair at once, so take your time and make sure you’re happy with the length.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

Once you’ve cut all the hair in the front, use your scissors to blend the hair with the rest of your hair.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as you normally would. You can use a flat iron or curling iron to create different looks.

Tips for Cutting Front Hair at Home:

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting your front hair at home:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up. You can always cut more hair, but you can’t put it back once it’s gone.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Always cut vertically, not horizontally. This will help create a natural, feathery look.
  • Take your time and don’t rush. It’s better to take longer and get it right than to rush and make a mistake.

FAQ:

1. Can I cut my front hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your front hair at home with the right tools and a little bit of practice.

2. How do I know how much hair to cut?

Use a comb to part your hair down the middle and comb your hair forward. This will help you determine how much hair you want to cut.

3. How often should I cut my front hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to trim it. As a general rule of thumb, you should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care:

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and a little bit of practice, you’ll be able to cut your front hair at home and achieve the perfect look every time.
