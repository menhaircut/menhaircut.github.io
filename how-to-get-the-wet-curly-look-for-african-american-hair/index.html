How To Get The Wet Curly Look For African American Hair

Written by Petter Oct 22, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to achieve the wet curly look for your African American hair? Look no further, we have got you covered. This look is perfect for those who want to add some texture to their hair without using heat or chemicals. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to get the wet curly look for African American hair. Let's dive in!

Description

The wet curly look is a popular hairstyle that involves creating defined curls with wet hair. This style is perfect for those who want to add some texture to their hair without using heat or chemicals. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be achieved with minimal effort.

How to

To achieve the wet curly look, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your hair and make it easier to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently pat it dry with a towel.

Step 2: Apply a curl enhancing product

Apply a curl enhancing product evenly throughout your hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that each section is evenly coated with product.

Step 4: Scrunch your hair

Take each section of hair and scrunch it upwards towards your scalp. This will help to create defined curls. Continue to scrunch each section until your entire head is covered.

Step 5: Allow your hair to air dry

Allow your hair to air dry completely. Avoid using a blow dryer as this can cause frizz and damage to your hair. Once your hair is dry, you can use a diffuser attachment on a low heat setting to add some volume to your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect wet curly look:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. This will help to reduce frizz and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying. This can cause frizz and disrupt the curl pattern.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the wet curly look:

Can I achieve this look with relaxed hair?

Yes, you can achieve the wet curly look with relaxed hair. Just be sure to use a curl enhancing product and scrunch your hair to create defined curls.

How often should I wash my hair to maintain this look?

It's best to wash your hair once a week to maintain this look. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, be sure to follow a regular hair care routine. This should include washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, deep conditioning once a week, and using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Overall, the wet curly look is a great way to add some texture to your African American hair. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect wet curly look for your hair.
