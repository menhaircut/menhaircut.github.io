Best Face Shape For Rectangle Glasses

Written by Eveline Jun 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect glasses to frame your face? Rectangle glasses might be just what you need! But, before you head to the optical store, it's important to know which face shape looks best with rectangle glasses. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the best face shape for rectangle glasses.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose Rectangle Glasses for Your Face Shape
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Rectangle Glasses
  4. Tips for Wearing Rectangle Glasses
  5. FAQ About Rectangle Glasses and Face Shapes
  6. Hair Care Tips for Wearing Rectangle Glasses

Description

Rectangle glasses are a popular choice for people who want a classic, professional look. They have a rectangular frame that's wider than it is tall, with angular edges that create a sharp, defined look. These glasses work well for people with certain face shapes, but not everyone can pull them off.

How to Choose Rectangle Glasses for Your Face Shape

The best face shape for rectangle glasses is a square or round face. Rectangle glasses help to balance out the roundness of these face shapes by adding definition and structure. They also work well for people with oval faces, as long as the glasses aren't too large.

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to avoid rectangle glasses, as they can make your chin look even narrower. Similarly, if you have a long, narrow face, rectangle glasses can make your face look even longer.

Rectangle Glasses for Square Faces

For people with square faces, rectangle glasses are a great choice because they soften the angular lines of the face. Look for glasses with rounded edges or a curved frame to balance out the squareness of the face.

Rectangle Glasses for Round Faces

Round faces can benefit from the structure that rectangle glasses provide. Look for glasses with angular edges to add definition to the face. Avoid glasses with curved frames or rounded edges, as they can make the face look even rounder.

Rectangle Glasses for Oval Faces

Oval faces are lucky in that they can wear almost any type of glasses. However, when it comes to rectangle glasses, it's important to choose a frame that's not too large. Large rectangle glasses can overwhelm an oval face, so opt for a smaller frame instead.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Rectangle Glasses

Choosing rectangle glasses is easy when you follow these simple steps:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Decide on the size of the glasses you want
  3. Try on different styles of rectangle glasses
  4. Choose a frame that complements your face shape and personal style
  5. Make sure the glasses fit properly

By following these steps, you'll be able to find the perfect pair of rectangle glasses for your face shape.

Tips for Wearing Rectangle Glasses

Here are a few tips to help you rock your rectangle glasses:

  • Choose a frame color that complements your skin tone and hair color
  • Match your glasses to your outfit style
  • Consider adding anti-glare coating to your lenses to reduce eye strain
  • Keep your glasses clean to maintain their clarity

With these tips, you'll be able to wear your rectangle glasses with confidence.

FAQ About Rectangle Glasses and Face Shapes

Can people with heart-shaped faces wear rectangle glasses?

No, heart-shaped faces are not the best match for rectangle glasses. The angular edges of the glasses can make the chin look even narrower, which is not flattering.

Are there different sizes of rectangle glasses?

Yes, rectangle glasses come in different sizes to fit different face shapes. It's important to choose a size that's proportional to your face.

Can rectangle glasses be worn with any outfit?

Yes, rectangle glasses can be worn with any outfit. However, it's important to consider the style of the glasses when choosing an outfit.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Rectangle Glasses

Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind when wearing rectangle glasses:

  • Consider getting a shorter haircut to show off your glasses
  • Style your hair away from your face to avoid tangling with your glasses
  • Use hair products that won't leave residue on your glasses

With these hair care tips, you'll be able to show off your rectangle glasses with confidence.

In conclusion, rectangle glasses are a great choice for people with square, round, or oval faces. By following these tips, you'll be able to choose the perfect pair of rectangle glasses for your face shape and wear them with confidence.
