Best Long Hairstyles For Long Faces

Written by Petter Jul 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Long faces are characterized by a narrow forehead, prominent chin, and high cheekbones. If you have this face shape, you may find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that complements your features. Fortunately, there are many long hairstyles that can work well for long faces. In this article, we'll explore some of the best long hairstyles for long faces, including how to style them, tips for maintaining them, and answers to frequently asked questions.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hairstyles for Long Faces

  • Hair Care for Long Faces

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Long Face

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face, the goal is to create balance and proportion. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Look for hairstyles that add width to your face, such as layers, waves, or curls.
  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer, such as straight, one-length cuts.
  • Consider getting bangs to shorten the appearance of your forehead.
  • Avoid center parts, which can elongate your face even more.
  • Experiment with different lengths and textures to find what works best for you.

Now that you have an idea of what to look for, let's explore some of the best long hairstyles for long faces.

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for long faces because they add volume and movement to the hair. The layers can be cut in a variety of lengths, from subtle to dramatic, depending on your preference. To style, simply blow dry your hair with a round brush or use a curling iron to add waves or curls.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a popular choice for long faces because they help to shorten the appearance of the forehead. They can be cut in a variety of lengths and thicknesses, depending on your preference. To style, blow dry your bangs with a round brush, sweeping them to one side. You can also use a flat iron to straighten them or a curling iron to add a slight wave.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great choice for long faces because they add volume and texture to the hair. To achieve this look, apply a texturizing spray to damp hair and scrunch it with your hands. You can also use a curling iron to add loose waves. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

4. Braids

Braids are a versatile hairstyle that work well for long faces. They can be worn in a variety of styles, from a simple side braid to a more intricate fishtail braid. To achieve this look, start with clean, dry hair and apply a small amount of styling cream to help hold the braid in place. You can also add accessories such as beads or ribbons to add a pop of color.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hairstyles for Long Faces

Now that you have some ideas for long hairstyles that work well for long faces, let's walk through a step-by-step guide to styling them.

1. Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  4. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add waves or curls. You can also use a straightening iron for a sleeker look.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Blow dry your bangs with a round brush, sweeping them to one side.
  4. You can also use a flat iron to straighten them or a curling iron to add a slight wave.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Beachy Waves

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends.
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create waves.
  4. You can also use a curling iron to add loose waves.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

4. Braids

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling cream to your hair to help hold the braid in place.
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  4. Create the braid of your choice, such as a side braid or fishtail braid.
  5. You can also add accessories such as beads or ribbons to add a pop of color.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hairstyles for Long Faces

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the life of your hairstyle between washes.

FAQs about Long Hairstyles for Long Faces

1. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a long face?

While straight hair can elongate your face, it can still work if you add volume and movement with layers, waves, or curls. You can also consider getting bangs to help shorten the appearance of your forehead.

2. Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer, such as straight, one-length cuts or center parts. Instead, look for hairstyles that add width and volume to your face, such as layers, waves, or curls.

3. How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care for Long Faces

Long faces require regular maintenance to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips and choosing the right long hairstyle for your long face, you can create a look that's both flattering and stylish.
