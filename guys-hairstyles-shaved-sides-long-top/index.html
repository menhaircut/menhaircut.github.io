Guys Hairstyles Shaved Sides Long Top: A Relaxed Guide | Hair Care Tips

If you're looking for a versatile and trendy hairstyle, then the shaved sides long top hairstyle might be just what you need. This hairstyle has been one of the most popular looks for men in recent years, and it's not hard to see why. With its clean and edgy appearance, it's perfect for guys who want to make a statement with their hair. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips for maintaining it, and more.

Description

The shaved sides long top hairstyle is characterized by a short or bald fade on the sides and longer hair on the top. The hair on top can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or even in a man bun. The hairstyle is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face while still having a stylish look. It's also a great option for guys who want to experiment with different hair textures and lengths.

How To Achieve The Shaved Sides Long Top Look

The first step in achieving the shaved sides long top look is to find a skilled barber who can give you a clean fade. The fade should start at the temples and gradually blend into the longer hair on top. You can choose how short or long you want the top of your hair to be, but it's best to leave at least 3 inches of hair to work with.

Step By Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the shaved sides long top look:

  1. Visit a skilled barber and show them a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  2. Ask for a short or bald fade on the sides, leaving at least 3 inches of hair on top.
  3. Once you've got your haircut, wash and dry your hair.
  4. Apply a styling product such as pomade, wax, or gel to your hair.
  5. Style your hair according to your preference. You can slick it back, create a messy look, or even tie it up in a man bun.

Tips For Maintaining Your Shaved Sides Long Top Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your shaved sides long top haircut:

  • Visit your barber for a touch-up every 2-3 weeks to keep your fade looking fresh.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair each day to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Consider investing in a good hair dryer to help you style your hair more easily.

FAQs About Shaved Sides Long Top Haircuts

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaved sides long top haircuts:

Q: Is the shaved sides long top hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How long does it take to style the shaved sides long top hairstyle?

A: This depends on your hair length and texture, but on average it can take anywhere from 5-15 minutes to style your hair.

Q: Can I still wear hats with a shaved sides long top haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear hats with this hairstyle. Just make sure to adjust the hat to fit comfortably over your hair.

Hair Care Tips For Shaved Sides Long Top Haircuts

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining a healthy and stylish shaved sides long top haircut:

  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons or curling irons on your hair as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the shaved sides long top hairstyle, it's time to visit your barber and try it out for yourself. With its versatility and edgy look, it's a hairstyle that's sure to turn heads.
