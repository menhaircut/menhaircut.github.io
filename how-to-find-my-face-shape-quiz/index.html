How To Find My Face Shape Quiz A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of not knowing what haircut or hairstyle suits you best? One of the keys to finding the perfect haircut or hairstyle that suits you is knowing your face shape. But, how do you find out your face shape? The answer is simple: take a face shape quiz. In this article, we will guide you on how to find my face shape quiz and give you some tips on hair care based on your face shape.

How to Find My Face Shape Quiz

There are several online face shape quizzes available that you can take. These quizzes are designed to help you determine your face shape based on your facial features. Most quizzes are free and easy to use. However, it is important to choose a reliable quiz to ensure accurate results. Here are some of the most popular face shape quizzes:

  • SheKnows Face Shape Quiz
  • Oprah Magazine Face Shape Quiz
  • Healthline Face Shape Quiz

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to take a face shape quiz:

  1. Choose a reliable face shape quiz.
  2. Follow the instructions provided by the quiz and answer the questions truthfully.
  3. Submit your answers and wait for the results.
  4. Once you receive your results, take note of your face shape.

It is important to note that the results of the face shape quiz may not be 100% accurate. It is always best to consult with a professional hairstylist for expert advice.

Tips Based on Your Face Shape

Knowing your face shape can help you choose the right haircut or hairstyle that suits you best. Here are some tips based on your face shape:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the ideal face shape. You can pretty much pull off any haircut or hairstyle. However, if you want to accentuate your face shape, go for a layered haircut or a side-swept fringe.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape has full cheeks and a rounded chin. To make your face appear longer, go for a long layered haircut or a side-swept fringe. Avoid blunt cuts and center partings.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and a square forehead. To soften your face, go for a layered haircut with soft waves or curls. Avoid straight and blunt cuts.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wide forehead and a narrow chin. To balance your face, go for a layered haircut with side-swept bangs or a chin-length bob. Avoid short haircuts and blunt cuts.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has wide cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. To accentuate your cheekbones, go for a layered haircut with soft waves or curls. Avoid short haircuts and blunt cuts.

FAQs

What is a face shape quiz?

A face shape quiz is an online quiz that helps you determine your face shape based on your facial features.

How accurate are face shape quizzes?

The accuracy of face shape quizzes may vary. It is always best to consult with a professional hairstylist for expert advice.

Can my face shape change?

Yes, your face shape can change over time due to aging, weight gain or loss, and other factors.

Hair Care Based on Your Face Shape

Aside from choosing the right haircut or hairstyle, it is also important to take care of your hair based on your face shape. Here are some hair care tips based on your face shape:

Oval Face Shape

Since an oval face shape can pretty much pull off any hairstyle, focus on keeping your hair healthy and shiny. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Round Face Shape

For a round face shape, avoid adding too much volume on the sides of your head. Instead, focus on adding volume on the top of your head to elongate your face. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heavy styling products.

Square Face Shape

For a square face shape, focus on adding softness to your hair. Use a smoothing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heavy styling products. Consider getting a hair treatment to maintain your hair's softness and shine.

Heart Face Shape

For a heart face shape, focus on adding volume to the lower half of your face. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider getting a layered haircut with side-swept bangs to balance your face.

Diamond Face Shape

For a diamond face shape, focus on maintaining the natural shape of your face. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heavy styling products. Consider getting a hair treatment to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, taking a face shape quiz can help you determine your face shape and choose the right haircut or hairstyle that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair based on your face shape to maintain its health and shine. If in doubt, always consult with a professional hairstylist for expert advice.
