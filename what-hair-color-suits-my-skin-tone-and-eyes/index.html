What Hair Color Suits My Skin Tone And Eyes

Written by Frank Jul 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering a hair color change, it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and eye color. Not all hair colors suit everyone, so it's essential to take into consideration these factors when choosing a new hair color. In this blog post, we'll provide you with tips and tricks to help you determine which hair color suits your skin tone and eyes.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Analyzing Your Eye Color
  • Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining which hair color suits your skin tone is to figure out what your skin tone actually is. There are three main types of skin tones:

  • Warm
  • Cool
  • Neutral

If you have a warm skin tone, your skin will have a yellow, peachy, or golden undertone. If you have a cool skin tone, your skin will have a pink, red, or bluish undertone. If you have a neutral skin tone, your skin will have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

A quick way to determine your skin tone is to look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear greenish, you have warm undertones. If they appear bluish, you have cool undertones. If they appear a mix of green and blue, you have neutral undertones.

Analyzing Your Eye Color

Another factor to consider when choosing a hair color is your eye color. Certain hair colors can make your eyes pop, while others can make them appear dull. Here's a quick guide to which hair colors suit different eye colors:

  • Blue eyes: Blonde, red, and brown shades work well with blue eyes.
  • Brown eyes: Almost any hair color can complement brown eyes, but warm shades like caramel and honey can enhance their warmth.
  • Green eyes: Red and copper shades can make green eyes pop, while blonde and brown shades can bring out their golden flecks.
  • Hazel eyes: Brown shades can make hazel eyes appear more brown, while blonde shades can bring out their green and gold tones.

Choosing the Right Hair Color

Now that you know your skin tone and eye color, it's time to choose the right hair color for you. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • If you have warm undertones, go for warm shades like golden blonde, honey brown, or copper red.
  • If you have cool undertones, go for cool shades like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or burgundy.
  • If you have neutral undertones, you can pull off both warm and cool shades.

However, it's important to keep in mind that these are general guidelines. Your hair colorist can provide you with more specific recommendations based on your individual features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Color

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take steps to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent your new hair color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause your new hair color to fade faster.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your hair color looking fresh.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

Yes, but it's important to choose a dye that is gentle and specifically formulated for sensitive skin. Always do a patch test before dyeing your hair to ensure that you don't have an allergic reaction.

Can I dye my hair if I have gray hair?

Yes, but you may need to choose a stronger dye or leave the dye on for longer to ensure that it fully covers the gray hair. Your hair colorist can provide you with more specific recommendations.

Do I need to use a toner after dyeing my hair?

It depends on the hair color you've chosen and your desired outcome. Toners can help neutralize any unwanted tones in your hair and give it a more even color. Your hair colorist can advise you on whether a toner is necessary for your hair color.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair after dyeing it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color, you can achieve a gorgeous new look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
