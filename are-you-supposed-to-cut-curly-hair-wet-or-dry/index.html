Are You Supposed To Cut Curly Hair Wet Or Dry

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 · 3 min read

As someone with curly hair, you know that finding the right haircut and styling techniques can be a challenge. One of the questions that always comes up is whether you should cut curly hair wet or dry. In this article, we’ll explore the pros and cons of both methods and help you decide which one is right for you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Before we dive into the details, let’s define what we mean by cutting curly hair “wet” or “dry.” Wet cutting is when your hair is washed and dampened, while dry cutting is when your hair is completely dry. Both methods have their advantages and disadvantages, and the choice often comes down to personal preference and the type of curls you have.

How To

If you’re heading to the salon, make sure to communicate your preference to your stylist. Most stylists are comfortable with both techniques, but it’s always best to let them know what you prefer.

If you’re cutting your hair at home, you’ll need different tools depending on whether you’re cutting wet or dry. For wet cutting, you’ll need a spray bottle to dampen your hair, while dry cutting requires a good pair of scissors and a mirror.

Step By Step

Wet Cutting

  1. Wash your hair and let it air dry until it’s damp.
  2. Section your hair and use a spray bottle to dampen each section.
  3. Comb through each section and cut as desired.

Dry Cutting

  1. Wash and style your hair as usual.
  2. Section your hair and use a mirror to see the back.
  3. Trim small sections as desired, being careful not to cut too much at once.

Tips

  • Wet cutting is usually best for thicker, coarser curls, while dry cutting is ideal for finer, looser curls.
  • Always use sharp scissors when cutting your hair to avoid split ends.
  • If you’re unsure which method to use, experiment with both and see which one gives you the best results.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my curly hair myself?

A: Yes, but it’s best to start with small trims and work your way up to more drastic changes. It’s also important to use the right tools and techniques to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Should I cut my hair wet or dry if I want a drastic change?

A: It’s usually best to cut your hair dry if you want a drastic change, as you’ll be able to see the results immediately and make adjustments as needed.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you cut your hair wet or dry, it’s important to take good care of your curls to keep them healthy and looking their best. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, avoid heat styling whenever possible, and invest in a good deep conditioning treatment to keep your curls looking their best.

So, are you supposed to cut curly hair wet or dry? Ultimately, the choice is up to you. Experiment with both methods and see which one gives you the best results. With a little trial and error, you’ll find the perfect technique for your curls.
