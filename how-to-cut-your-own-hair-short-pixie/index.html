How To Cut Your Own Hair Short Pixie A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're thinking about cutting your own hair into a short pixie cut, you're not alone. Many women are opting for this low-maintenance, edgy look that shows off their features and personality. But before you grab your scissors, there are a few things you should know. In this article, we'll walk you through how to cut your own hair into a short pixie cut, including tips, tricks, and frequently asked questions.

Description

A short pixie cut is a hairstyle that features short, choppy layers and a tapered neckline. It's a bold and daring look that's perfect for women who want to show off their features and stand out from the crowd. It's also a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling or upkeep.

How to Cut Your Own Hair Short Pixie: Step-by-Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting your own hair into a short pixie cut is to gather your tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section off your hair into manageable sections. Start at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use hair clips to keep each section in place.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Using your scissors, start cutting your hair in small sections. Start at the back and work your way up to the crown of your head. Use your comb to guide your scissors and create a tapered, choppy look.

Step 4: Trim the Sides

Once you've cut the back of your hair, move to the sides. Take small sections and use your scissors to create a choppy, layered look. Be sure to cut both sides evenly.

Step 5: Finish with the Top

Finally, move to the top of your head. Use your comb to section off the hair and cut it to the desired length. Be sure to create texture and layers for a messy, edgy look.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair Short Pixie

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair into a short pixie cut:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Take your time and go slowly
  • Use sharp scissors for a clean cut
  • Don't be afraid to create texture and layers
  • Trim small sections at a time

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair into a short pixie cut?

Yes, you can cut your own hair into a short pixie cut. However, it's important to have the right tools and take your time to avoid any mistakes.

How do I create the choppy, layered look of a short pixie cut?

To create the choppy, layered look of a short pixie cut, use your scissors to cut small sections of hair at a time. Create texture and layers by cutting at different angles and lengths.

How often should I trim my short pixie cut?

You should trim your short pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Cut

To keep your short pixie cut looking its best, be sure to shampoo and condition your hair regularly. Use a light, leave-in conditioner to add moisture and shine. Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Cutting your own hair into a short pixie cut can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a bold and edgy look that shows off your personality and style. Follow these steps, tips, and FAQs to get started on your own short pixie cut today!
