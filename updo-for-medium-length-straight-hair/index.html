Updo For Medium Length Straight Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length straight hair, you may think that your styling options are limited. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can create a stunning updo that is perfect for any occasion. From weddings to proms to formal events, an updo is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. In this article, we'll show you how to create an updo for medium length straight hair, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Create an Updo for Medium Length Straight Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's talk about the tools and products you'll need to create the perfect updo for medium length straight hair. Here's what you'll need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastics
  • Hair spray
  • Texturizing spray or powder
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron or flat iron (optional)

Now that you have your tools and products, it's time to start creating your updo.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create an updo for medium length straight hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply heat protectant spray if you plan on using a curling or flat iron.
  2. Create a deep side part and section off the top half of your hair.
  3. Tease the roots of the bottom section to add volume and texture.
  4. Gather the bottom section of your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck and secure with a hair elastic.
  5. Release the top section of your hair and use a curling or flat iron to create loose waves or curls.
  6. Apply texturizing spray or powder to the roots of your hair to add volume and grip.
  7. Gather the top section of your hair into a loose bun or chignon and secure with bobby pins.
  8. Take small sections of hair from the bun and wrap them around the base to conceal the bobby pins.
  9. Finish with a light mist of hair spray to hold the style in place.

And voila! You now have a beautiful updo for medium length straight hair that is perfect for any occasion.

Tips for Creating the Perfect Updo

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating an updo for medium length straight hair:

  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair to add volume and texture.
  • Use texturizing spray or powder to add grip and hold to your hair.
  • The messier the bun, the better! Don't worry about making it perfect.
  • Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair to make them less noticeable.
  • Finish with a light mist of hair spray to hold the style in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about updos for medium length straight hair:

Can I create an updo without teasing my hair?

Yes! If you don't want to tease your hair, you can use a texturizing spray or powder to add volume and grip.

How do I prevent my updo from falling out?

Use bobby pins that match the color of your hair and finish with a light mist of hair spray to hold the style in place.

Can I create an updo with straight hair?

Yes! You can use a curling or flat iron to add waves or curls to your straight hair before creating your updo.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Straight Hair

Here are some tips to keep your medium length straight hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Don't wash your hair every day to prevent stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create a stunning updo for your medium length straight hair in no time. Whether you're attending a wedding or a formal event, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
