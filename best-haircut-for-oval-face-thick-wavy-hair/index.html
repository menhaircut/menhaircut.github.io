Best Haircut For Oval Face Thick Wavy Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have an oval face with thick and wavy hair, you are blessed with a versatile hair type that can be styled in many ways. However, choosing the right haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture can be a daunting task. In this guide, we will help you find the best haircut for oval face thick wavy hair that suits your personality and lifestyle. We will cover everything from the description, how-to, step-by-step guide, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to ensure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips

Description

Oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile face shape as it can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, if you have thick and wavy hair, the best haircut for oval face would be one that accentuates your features and controls your hair volume. The ideal haircut for oval face thick wavy hair should balance your facial proportions, highlight your cheekbones, and add definition to your jawline. Some of the best haircuts for oval face with thick and wavy hair are layered cuts, long bobs, shoulder-length cuts, and pixie cuts. These haircuts provide movement, texture, and volume to your hair, making it more manageable and stylish.

How to

The first step in getting the best haircut for oval face thick wavy hair is to find a skilled hairstylist who understands your hair type and face shape. You can consult with your hairstylist and share your preferences, lifestyle, and hair care routine. Based on your inputs, your hairstylist can suggest the best haircut for you. You can also do some research on your own by browsing online for haircut ideas and inspiration.

Things to consider before choosing a haircut

Before choosing a haircut, you should consider the following factors:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture and density
  • Your lifestyle and personality
  • Your hair care routine and maintenance

Step by step

Here are the steps to get the best haircut for oval face thick wavy hair:

  1. Choose a skilled hairstylist who understands your hair type and face shape.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist and share your preferences, lifestyle, and hair care routine.
  3. Browse online for haircut ideas and inspiration.
  4. Select the haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  5. Show the haircut picture to your hairstylist and discuss the details.
  6. Get the haircut done by your hairstylist.
  7. Style your hair using the right products and tools.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your best haircut for oval face thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip away the natural oils and make your hair dry and frizzy.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or serum to tame your hair frizz and add shine.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair and minimize breakage.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your natural hair texture and volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for oval face thick wavy hair:

Q: What is the best haircut for oval face with thick and wavy hair?

A: The best haircut for oval face with thick and wavy hair is a layered cut, long bob, shoulder-length cut, or pixie cut. These haircuts provide movement, texture, and volume to your hair, making it more manageable and stylish.

Q: Can I get bangs with oval face and thick wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can get bangs with oval face and thick wavy hair. However, you should choose the bangs that suit your face shape and hair texture. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are ideal for oval face with thick wavy hair as they frame your face and add softness to your features.

Q: How often should I get a haircut for oval face thick wavy hair?

A: You should get a haircut for oval face thick wavy hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hair shape and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of your haircuts may vary depending on your hair growth rate and personal preference.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips for oval face thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair and minimize breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid heat styling your hair too often as it can damage your hair cuticle and make your hair dry and frizzy.
  • Protect your hair from sun damage by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.

With these tips and advice, you can find the best haircut for oval face thick wavy hair that suits your personality and lifestyle. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and choose the haircut that accentuates your features and enhances your natural hair texture. With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock your thick wavy hair and feel confident and beautiful.
