How To Change Hairstyle Permanently Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jun 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Changing your hairstyle can be a great way to refresh your look and boost your confidence. However, if you're looking for a more permanent change, you may be wondering how to make it happen. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks for changing your hairstyle permanently, including how to choose the right style, what to expect during the process, and how to take care of your new 'do once it's complete.

Table of Contents

  1. Choose the Right Style
  2. Find an Experienced Stylist
  3. Prepare Your Hair
  4. Understand the Process
  5. Take Care of Your New 'Do
  6. FAQ

Choose the Right Style

The first step in changing your hairstyle permanently is to choose the right style for you. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when making your decision. For example, if you have a round face, a long, layered cut can help elongate your features. If you have thick, curly hair, a short, choppy cut may be easier to maintain.

Take some time to browse magazines, websites, and social media for inspiration. Save some pictures of styles you like and bring them to your stylist for reference.

Find an Experienced Stylist

When it comes to changing your hairstyle permanently, it's important to find an experienced stylist who can guide you through the process. Look for someone with a strong portfolio of work and positive reviews from previous clients.

You may also want to schedule a consultation with your stylist before committing to the change. This will give you a chance to discuss your goals, ask questions, and make sure you're on the same page.

Prepare Your Hair

Before making any permanent changes to your hair, it's important to prepare it for the process. This may involve cutting off any damaged ends, deep conditioning your hair, or avoiding heat styling for a period of time.

Your stylist may also recommend a specific haircare routine to follow leading up to the change. This may include using certain products or avoiding others to ensure your hair is in the best possible condition.

Understand the Process

Depending on the type of permanent change you're making to your hair, the process may differ. For example, if you're getting a perm or a relaxer, the process may involve chemicals and heat to alter the structure of your hair.

It's important to understand the process and what to expect before beginning. Your stylist should walk you through each step and answer any questions you may have.

Take Care of Your New 'Do

Once your new hairstyle is complete, it's important to take care of it properly to ensure it lasts as long as possible. This may involve using specific products, avoiding certain activities or hairstyles, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape.

Your stylist may also recommend a specific aftercare routine to follow. Be sure to follow their advice to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQ

How long will my new hairstyle last?

The length of time your new hairstyle will last depends on the type of change you've made and how well you take care of it. Some styles may last several weeks or even months, while others may require more frequent maintenance.

Will changing my hairstyle permanently damage my hair?

Any permanent change to your hair has the potential to cause damage. However, an experienced stylist should take steps to minimize this risk and help you maintain the health of your hair throughout the process.

What should I do if I'm not happy with my new hairstyle?

If you're not happy with your new hairstyle, talk to your stylist. They may be able to make adjustments or offer suggestions for how to make the style work better for you. However, keep in mind that some changes may be difficult to undo completely.

Changing your hairstyle permanently can be a big decision, but with the right preparation, guidance, and care, it can also be a positive one. Follow these tips and tricks to achieve the look you've been dreaming of.
