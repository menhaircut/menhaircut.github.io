How To Know If I Will Suit Short Hair

Written by Robby Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering chopping off your locks but aren't sure if short hair will suit you? It's a common concern, but there are ways to figure out if a short hairstyle is right for you. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you decide if you should take the plunge and go for a shorter look.

Table of Contents

  • Determine Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Texture
  • Think about Your Lifestyle
  • Short Haircut Ideas
  • Hair Care for Short Hair
  • FAQ

Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in deciding if short hair is right for you is to determine your face shape. Certain face shapes are better suited to certain hairstyles, so it's important to know yours before making a decision. The four main face shapes are:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart

Each face shape has specific characteristics that can be emphasized or minimized with the right haircut. If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are plenty of online guides and quizzes that can help you figure it out.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. Short hair can look great on any hair type, but some styles may be more work than others depending on your texture. If you have fine, straight hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can look chic and sophisticated. If you have thick, curly hair, a layered bob or shaggy cut can help control volume and add definition to your curls.

Think about Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also an important consideration when deciding on a short haircut. If you're someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, a short cut can be a great way to show off your skills. However, if you're someone who prefers low-maintenance hairstyles, a short cut may not be the best choice. Short hair requires more frequent trims to maintain its shape, and you may need to spend more time styling it to get the look you want.

Short Haircut Ideas

Now that you have a better idea of what to consider when deciding if short hair is right for you, it's time to start thinking about specific haircut ideas. Here are a few popular styles to consider:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Shag
  • Crop
  • Bowl cut

Each of these styles can be customized to suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. It's important to work with a stylist who can help you choose a cut that will flatter your features and fit your needs.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Once you've decided on a short haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Invest in a high-quality styling product
  • Get regular trims to maintain your shape
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week

Caring for short hair is often easier than caring for longer hair, but it still requires some effort to keep it looking healthy and stylish.

FAQ

Can anyone pull off a short haircut?

Short hair can look great on anyone, but it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A short haircut can actually make your face look slimmer and more defined if you choose the right style for your face shape.

How often do I need to get my short hair cut?

Short hair needs to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

What kind of styling product should I use for short hair?

The best styling product for short hair depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Talk to your stylist for recommendations.

Can I still wear a ponytail with short hair?

Depending on the length of your hair, you may be able to wear a small ponytail or half-up style. Talk to your stylist about options for styling your short hair.

Hopefully these tips have helped you decide if short hair is right for you. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
