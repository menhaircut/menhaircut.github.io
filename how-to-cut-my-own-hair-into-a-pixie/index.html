How To Cut Your Own Hair Into A Pixie A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair into a pixie, but don't want to go to a salon, you can do it yourself at home! With a few tools and some patience, you can achieve a stylish and trendy pixie cut that will have you feeling confident and fabulous. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair into a pixie:

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • Clips to section your hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. It's important to have clean hair before you start cutting. Blow dry your hair straight, so you can see the length and shape of your hair more clearly.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Clip each section to keep them separated.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section of your hair. Use your comb to create a straight line across the nape of your neck. Then, hold your hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Repeat this process, working your way up to the crown of your head. Remember to cut small sections of hair at a time, and to check your work in the mirror frequently.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the back, move on to the sides of your hair. Use your comb to create a guide for the length you want. Then, pull your hair outwards and cut it straight across. Do this on both sides of your head, making sure to keep the length even.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top section of your hair. Use your comb to create a guide for the length you want. Then, hold your hair between your fingers and cut it straight across. Make sure to cut small sections of hair at a time, and to check your work in the mirror frequently.

Step 6: Clean up the Edges

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use your scissors to clean up any uneven edges or stray hairs. This will give your pixie cut a polished and professional look.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair into a Pixie

  • Start with longer hair, as it's easier to cut and style.
  • Cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer when it dries.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Use a mirror to check your work frequently.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, start with a small trim and work your way up to a pixie cut.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair into a pixie if I've never cut my hair before?

While it's possible to cut your own hair into a pixie, it's recommended that you have some experience cutting hair before attempting this style. If you're new to cutting hair, start with a simple trim and work your way up to a pixie cut.

Do I need to use hair cutting scissors?

Yes, it's important to use hair cutting scissors, as they are sharp and precise. Regular scissors can damage your hair and create uneven edges.

Can I still style my hair after cutting it into a pixie?

Yes, there are many ways to style a pixie cut. You can use styling products like gel, mousse, or hairspray to create different looks, or you can simply brush your hair back for a sleek and polished style.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair into a Pixie

After cutting your hair into a pixie, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons or flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

With these tips and a little bit of practice, you can cut your own hair into a pixie and rock a bold and stylish new look!
