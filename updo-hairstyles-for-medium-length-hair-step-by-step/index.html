Updo Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair Step By Step

Written by Robby Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for an easy and stylish way to wear your medium length hair? Look no further than updo hairstyles! Updos are perfect for any occasion, from weddings to a day at the office. In this blog post, we will provide step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect updo for medium length hair.

Table of Content

  1. How to
  2. Step by step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

Before we get into the specific updos, let's go over some general tips for achieving the perfect updo:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a texturizing spray to give your hair some grip
  • Invest in some good quality hair pins
  • Don't be afraid to use hair extensions for extra length and volume

Step by step

The Messy Bun

The messy bun is the perfect updo for when you're in a rush or don't want to spend too much time on your hair.

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape
  3. Secure the bun with hair pins
  4. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face for a more relaxed look

The Braided Updo

The braided updo is a great option for a more formal occasion.

  1. Start by creating a deep side part
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and begin braiding it
  3. Continue braiding, adding more hair as you go, until you reach the nape of your neck
  4. Repeat on the other side
  5. Take the two braids and twist them together, securing with hair pins

The Half-Up Half-Down Updo

The half-up half-down updo is a great option if you want to show off your length while still having an elegant updo.

  1. Start by sectioning off the top half of your hair
  2. Tie the top section into a ponytail
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape
  4. Secure the bun with hair pins

Tips

  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different updo styles to find what works best for you
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and tame flyaways
  • Accessorize with a headband or hair clip to add some extra flair

FAQ

How long does my hair need to be for an updo?

Medium length hair is perfect for updos! As long as your hair is long enough to be pulled back into a ponytail, you can achieve an updo.

Do I need to use hair extensions for an updo?

No, hair extensions are not necessary for an updo. However, if you want to add extra length or volume, extensions can be a great option.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for achieving the perfect updo. Make sure to use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on your hair. Regular trims can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these step-by-step instructions and tips, you can achieve the perfect updo for your medium length hair. Whether you're headed to a wedding or just want to change up your look, updos are a great option!
