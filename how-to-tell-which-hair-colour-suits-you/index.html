How To Tell Which Hair Colour Suits You

Written by Frank Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task. With so many shades and hues to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one will suit you best. In this article, we'll walk you through how to tell which hair colour suits you, step by step. We'll also provide you with tips on how to maintain your hair after colouring it. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in determining which hair colour suits you is to consider your skin tone. There are two main categories of skin tones: warm and cool. Warm skin tones have yellow, peach, or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink, red, or blue undertones. To determine your skin tone, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones, and if they appear blue, you have cool undertones.

Once you've determined your skin tone, consider your natural hair colour. If you have warm undertones, shades like honey blonde, copper, and auburn will look great on you. If you have cool undertones, shades like platinum blonde, ash brown, and jet black will complement your skin tone.

Another factor to consider is your eye colour. If you have warm skin tones and brown or hazel eyes, shades like golden blonde and chestnut brown will enhance your eye colour. If you have cool skin tones and blue or green eyes, shades like ash blonde and deep brown will make your eyes pop.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to determining which hair colour suits you:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on your wrist.
  2. Consider your natural hair colour.
  3. Take into account your eye colour.
  4. Choose a shade that complements your skin tone, natural hair colour, and eye colour.
  5. Consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing a hair colour:

  • Start with a subtle change if you're unsure.
  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance the colour will require.
  • Take into account the season - lighter shades are great for summer, while darker shades are perfect for winter.
  • Experiment with temporary hair colour before committing to a permanent change.
  • Consider the colour of your clothing and how it will look with your new hair colour.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair colour:

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have sensitive skin?

A: Yes, but it's important to use a gentle dye formulated for sensitive skin.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It depends on the rate of your hair growth, but typically every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I've recently had a perm or relaxer?

A: It's best to wait at least two weeks after a chemical treatment before dyeing your hair.

Hair Care

After you've coloured your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its vibrancy and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can fade the colour.
  • Wash your hair in cool water to prevent colour bleeding.
  • Consider a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know how to tell which hair colour suits you, you can confidently choose a shade that will enhance your natural beauty. Remember to take into account your skin tone, natural hair colour, and eye colour, and don't be afraid to consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure. Happy colouring!
