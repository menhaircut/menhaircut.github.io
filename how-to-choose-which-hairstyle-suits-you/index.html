How To Choose Which Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Daniel Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially when you have so many options to choose from. With so many factors to consider, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. However, with the right guidance, you can easily choose the perfect hairstyle that suits your personality and facial features. In this article, we will guide you step by step on how to choose which hairstyle suits you.

  • How to Choose Which Hairstyle Suits You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle
  • FAQs

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to choosing the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you determine which hairstyles will look best on you. There are six main face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back into a ponytail and stand in front of a mirror. Use a washable marker to outline the shape of your face on the mirror. Once you have determined your face shape, you can move on to the next step.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. Different hairstyles work better with different hair types. For example, if you have thin hair, you may want to consider a short, layered haircut to add volume. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to consider a longer haircut to help manage your hair.

Step 3: Choose a Style That Suits Your Personality

Your hairstyle should reflect your personality. If you are a bold and adventurous person, you may want to consider a more edgy hairstyle. On the other hand, if you are more conservative, you may want to stick with a classic hairstyle. Choose a hairstyle that you feel comfortable with and that reflects your personality.

Step 4: Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, you may want to consider a low-maintenance hairstyle. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on your hair, you may want to consider a more intricate hairstyle.

Step 5: Consult with Your Hair Stylist

Your hair stylist is your best resource when it comes to choosing the perfect hairstyle. They can help you determine your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle to help you choose the perfect hairstyle. Don't be afraid to ask for their opinion and guidance.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Consider the length of the hairstyle
  • Think about the color of your hair
  • Consider your skin tone
  • Think about the shape of your forehead
  • Consider your hair texture
  • Look for inspiration in magazines and online

FAQs

What hairstyle suits a round face?

A round face looks best with hairstyles that add length and definition to the face. Some good options are long layers, side-swept bangs, and a layered bob.

What hairstyle suits a square face?

A square face looks best with hairstyles that soften the angles of the face. Some good options are soft waves, a side-swept bang, and long layers.

What hairstyle suits an oval face?

An oval face is considered to be the most versatile face shape. Most hairstyles look great on an oval face, but some good options are a long bob, a pixie cut, and side-swept bangs.

Hair Care Tips

Once you have chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

Choosing the perfect hairstyle can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these tips and guidelines, you can easily choose the perfect hairstyle that suits your personality and facial features.
