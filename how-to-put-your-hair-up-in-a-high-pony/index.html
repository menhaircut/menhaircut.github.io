How To Put Your Hair Up In A High Pony A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling in your face? Or do you simply want a sleek and stylish look for a night out? Putting your hair up in a high pony is the perfect solution! Follow this step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect high ponytail.

How to

To create a high ponytail, you will need a brush, hair ties, and a hair clip. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any knots or tangles. This will make it easier to create a smooth and sleek ponytail.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using your hands, gather all of your hair at the crown of your head. Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place.

Step 3: Secure Your Ponytail

Take a hair clip and secure it to the base of your ponytail. This will help to keep your ponytail in place and prevent it from sagging throughout the day.

Step 4: Tighten Your Ponytail

Use your hands to tighten the hair tie around your ponytail. This will create a sleek and polished look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your high ponytail by spraying it with hairspray. This will help to keep any flyaways in place and ensure that your ponytail stays sleek and polished all day long.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • If you have thick hair, use two hair ties to secure your ponytail in place.
  • If you have fine hair, tease the crown of your head before creating your ponytail to add volume.
  • Use a small amount of hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about putting your hair up in a high ponytail:

1. How high should I put my ponytail?

Your ponytail should be placed at the crown of your head for a classic high ponytail look.

2. Can I put my hair up in a high ponytail if I have short hair?

Yes! If you have short hair, you can still achieve a high ponytail by using hair extensions or a hairpiece.

3. Can I wear a high ponytail to a formal event?

Yes! A high ponytail can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. To make it more formal, add a few curls to your ponytail or accessorize it with a hair clip.

Hair Care

Putting your hair up in a high ponytail can put stress on your hair and scalp. To keep your hair healthy, make sure to:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can rock a high ponytail with confidence!
