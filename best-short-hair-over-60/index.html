Best Short Hair Over 60 A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Aprilia Sep 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're over 60, you may be wondering what the best short hairstyles are for you. Short hair can be a great choice for older women, as it's low-maintenance and can help you look younger and more vibrant. In this guide, we'll explore the best short haircuts for women over 60, as well as offer tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more brittle, making it harder to maintain longer styles. Short haircuts can help to flatter your face shape, enhance your features, and give you a more youthful appearance. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, there is a short hairstyle that will work for you.

How To

To find the best short haircut for you, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. A good stylist can help you choose a cut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

The best short hairstyles for women over 60 will depend on your face shape. If you have a round face, a layered bob or a short pixie cut can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. If you have a square face, a short bob with soft layers can help to soften your features. If you have an oval face, you can wear almost any short haircut.

Think About Your Hair Texture

The best short hairstyles for women over 60 will also depend on your hair texture. If you have thin or fine hair, a short bob with layers can help to add volume and texture. If you have thick or curly hair, a short pixie cut can help to tame your locks and give you a more polished look.

Consider Your Personal Style

The best short hairstyles for women over 60 will also depend on your personal style. If you prefer a classic look, a sleek bob or a short crop may be the perfect choice. If you prefer a more edgy look, a spiky pixie cut or a choppy bob may be more your style.

Step by Step

If you're ready to take the plunge and try a short haircut, here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Find Inspiration

Before you go to the salon, do some research and find some inspiration for your new haircut. Look online for pictures of short haircuts that you like, and bring them with you to the salon.

Step 2: Consult with Your Stylist

When you get to the salon, consult with your stylist about the best short haircut for you. Show them the pictures you've found and discuss your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Step 3: Get the Cut

Once you've decided on a haircut, sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic. They'll cut and style your hair to create a flattering and easy-to-maintain look.

Step 4: Maintain Your Hair

Short haircuts are low-maintenance, but they still require some upkeep. Visit the salon every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your haircut looking fresh. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing and maintaining a short haircut:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape, hair texture, and personal style
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Visit the salon every 4-6 weeks for a trim
  • Experiment with different styling products to create different looks
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for women over 60?

The best short hairstyles for women over 60 will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Some popular options include the pixie cut, the bob, and the crop.

How can I maintain my short haircut?

Short haircuts are low-maintenance, but they still require some upkeep. Visit the salon every 4-6 weeks for a trim, and use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

What styling products should I use for my short haircut?

The styling products you use will depend on your hair texture and personal style. Some popular options include mousse, gel, and pomade.

Hair Care

Whether you have long hair or short hair, it's important to take care of your locks to keep them healthy and strong. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach and hair dyes, too often

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and strong, no matter what your age.
