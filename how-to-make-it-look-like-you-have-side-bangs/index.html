How To Make It Look Like You Have Side Bangs

Written by Bardi Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of getting side bangs but not quite ready to take the plunge? Or maybe you have had side bangs before and miss the look but don't want to commit to cutting them again. Whatever the reason, we have a solution for you! Here's our guide on how to make it look like you have side bangs without actually cutting them.

Description

Side bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add dimension and frame your face. However, cutting bangs can be a big commitment, and it may not be for everyone. Luckily, there are ways to achieve the look of side bangs without actually cutting them. This is a great option for those who want to switch up their look without the commitment of a haircut.

How to

Step 1: Section Your Hair
Begin by sectioning your hair in a diagonal line from the top of one ear to the top of the other ear. Use a hair tie or clip to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 2: Create a Twist
Take the sectioned hair and twist it away from your face, creating a small bun. Pin the bun in place with bobby pins.

Step 3: Adjust the Length
Use a comb to adjust the length of your "bangs." You can make them longer or shorter depending on your preference. Once you have the desired length, use hairspray to hold the twist in place.

Step 4: Style Your Hair
Now that you have your faux side bangs, you can style the rest of your hair as usual. You can leave it down or put it up in a ponytail or bun.

Step by Step

Step 1: Section your hair in a diagonal line from the top of one ear to the top of the other ear.

Step 2: Twist the sectioned hair away from your face, creating a small bun. Pin the bun in place with bobby pins.

Step 3: Use a comb to adjust the length of your "bangs." Once you have the desired length, use hairspray to hold the twist in place.

Step 4: Style the rest of your hair as usual.

Tips

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Experiment with the length of your "bangs" to find the perfect look for you.
  • Use hairspray to keep the twist in place and prevent flyaways.
  • You can also use a hairband instead of bobby pins to hold the twist in place.

FAQ

Can I use this method on any hair type?

Yes, this method works on any hair type, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

How long will the faux side bangs last?

The faux side bangs will last all day as long as you use hairspray to hold them in place.

Can I wear my hair up with the faux side bangs?

Yes, you can wear your hair up in a ponytail or bun with the faux side bangs. Just make sure to adjust the length of the twist accordingly.

Hair Care

Make sure to take care of your hair by using a good shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools. Regular trims will also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make it look like you have side bangs, you can switch up your look without the commitment of a haircut. Have fun experimenting with different lengths and styles!
