How To Get Freeform Dreads Short Hair

Written by Luffy Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Freeform dreads are a popular hairstyle among people who want to achieve a unique and natural look. If you have short hair and want to get freeform dreads, there are several ways to achieve this style. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to get freeform dreads on short hair.

Description

Freeform dreads are a type of dreadlocks that are formed naturally without any specific pattern or technique. This style is achieved by allowing hair to tangle and knot together over time. Freeform dreads can be achieved on any hair type, including short hair.

How to

Getting freeform dreads on short hair requires patience and dedication. Here are the steps on how to get freeform dreads on short hair:

Step 1: Stop Using Shampoo

The first step to getting freeform dreads on short hair is to stop using shampoo. Shampoo strips your hair of its natural oils and makes it harder for your hair to tangle and knot together. Instead, use a natural hair cleanser like apple cider vinegar or baking soda to clean your hair.

Step 2: Avoid Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your hair can prevent it from tangling and knotting together, which is essential for freeform dreads. Avoid brushing your hair and use your fingers to separate any knots that form.

Step 3: Encourage Knotting

To encourage knotting, you can rub your hair vigorously with a towel or wear a hat that will help your hair tangle and knot together. You can also sleep with your hair covered to encourage knotting.

Step 4: Separate Your Dreads

As your hair begins to knot and tangle, separate any large knots into smaller sections. This will help to create uniform dreadlocks and prevent them from growing together.

Step 5: Maintenance

Once you have achieved freeform dreads on short hair, maintenance is essential to keep them looking their best. Use a natural oil like coconut or jojoba oil to moisturize your hair and prevent dryness. You can also use a crochet hook to tighten any loose hairs or knots.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when getting freeform dreads on short hair:

  • Be patient - it can take several months to achieve freeform dreads on short hair
  • Avoid using hair products that contain chemicals or alcohol
  • Cover your hair at night to prevent it from becoming frizzy
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

FAQ

Can I get freeform dreads on relaxed hair?

Yes, you can get freeform dreads on relaxed hair. However, it may take longer to achieve the desired results due to the hair being chemically treated.

How often should I wash my freeform dreads?

You should wash your freeform dreads as needed, using a natural hair cleanser like apple cider vinegar or baking soda.

How do I prevent my freeform dreads from unraveling?

To prevent your freeform dreads from unraveling, use a natural oil like coconut or jojoba oil to moisturize your hair and prevent dryness. You can also use a crochet hook to tighten any loose hairs or knots.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential when getting freeform dreads on short hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hair products that contain chemicals or alcohol
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a natural oil like coconut or jojoba oil
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using heat on your hair

Getting freeform dreads on short hair requires patience and dedication, but the results are worth it. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the unique and natural look of freeform dreads.
