Description

Faux locs are a trendy hairstyle that mimics the look of traditional locs without the commitment. They can be done with synthetic hair, but many people wonder if they can do faux locs with human hair. The answer is yes, you can. Using human hair for faux locs gives a more natural look and feel, and it can be styled in various ways. However, it's essential to use the right type of human hair and follow the proper techniques to achieve the best results.

How to Do Faux Locs with Human Hair

The process of doing faux locs with human hair is similar to using synthetic hair. The main difference is that you'll need to prepare the human hair before installing it. Here are the steps to follow:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Choose the right type of human hair - You need to select human hair that can withstand the twisting and wrapping required for faux locs. The best options are human hair extensions that are at least 18 inches long and have a kinky or curly texture.

  2. Wash and condition the hair - Before installing the hair, wash and condition it to remove any dirt and debris. Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep the hair soft and hydrated.

  3. Dry the hair - After washing, dry the hair thoroughly using a towel or blow dryer. Don't install wet hair, as it can lead to mildew and odor.

  4. Divide the hair - Divide the hair into small sections using a rat-tail comb. The sections should be around the same size as the locs you want to create.

  5. Twist the hair - Take each section of hair and twist it tightly from the root to the ends. You can use a gel or pomade to help keep the twists in place.

  6. Wrap the twists - Once you've twisted each section of hair, wrap it tightly around itself to create a faux loc. You can use a crochet hook or latch hook to pull the hair through the loc and secure it in place.

  7. Repeat - Continue twisting and wrapping the hair until you've created the desired number of locs.

  8. Style the locs - Once you've installed the locs, you can style them in various ways, such as updos, ponytails, and braids. Use a lightweight oil or cream to keep the locs moisturized and prevent frizz.

Tips for Faux Locs with Human Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing faux locs with human hair:

  • Choose high-quality human hair extensions that match your natural hair texture and color.

  • Wash and condition the hair before installing it to remove any chemicals or residue.

  • Use a lightweight gel or pomade to help keep the twists in place while wrapping the hair.

  • Wrap the hair tightly to create a secure faux loc that won't unravel.

  • Use a crochet hook or latch hook to help with the installation process.

  • Be gentle when styling the locs to avoid pulling or damaging the hair.

  • Keep the locs moisturized with a lightweight oil or cream to prevent dryness and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I do faux locs with short hair?

A: It's possible to do faux locs with short hair, but it may require using extensions to achieve the desired length and thickness.

Q: How long do faux locs with human hair last?

A: Faux locs with human hair can last up to 8 weeks with proper care and maintenance.

Q: Can I swim with faux locs with human hair?

A: It's not recommended to swim with faux locs with human hair, as exposure to chlorine and saltwater can damage the hair.

Hair Care for Faux Locs with Human Hair

To keep your faux locs with human hair looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your scalp and locs every 7-10 days using a sulfate-free shampoo.

  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that can weigh down the hair and cause buildup.

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

  • Cover your hair with a silk or satin scarf at night to protect it from friction and breakage.

  • Don't pull or tug on the locs, as this can cause damage to the hair follicles.

Overall, doing faux locs with human hair is a great way to achieve a natural-looking hairstyle that's versatile and easy to maintain. With the right techniques and products, you can create beautiful locs that will last for weeks.
