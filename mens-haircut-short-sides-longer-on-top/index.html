Mens Haircut Short Sides Longer On Top The Perfect Hairstyle For The Modern Man

Written by Luffy Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, mens haircut short sides longer on top is the perfect hairstyle for you. This hairstyle has been popular for many years and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, tips to maintain it, and answer some frequently asked questions.

Description

Mens haircut short sides longer on top is a versatile hairstyle that involves cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head short while leaving the hair on top longer. This style can be worn in various ways, such as a slicked-back look, a messy fringe, or a textured crop. It works well with many face shapes and hair types, making it a popular choice for men of all ages.

How to Achieve the Look

The first step in achieving the mens haircut short sides longer on top is to decide on the length and style you want for the top of your head. Once you've decided on the length, ask your barber to cut the sides and back of your head short using clippers or scissors. The length of the sides and back can vary depending on your preference, but a good rule of thumb is to keep it around a #2 or #3 guard length.

After cutting the sides and back, your barber will then trim the top of your hair to the desired length and style. This could be a classic side-parted look, a messy fringe, or a textured crop. Once the top is trimmed, your barber will blend the top and sides together to create a seamless look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the mens haircut short sides longer on top:

  1. Decide on the length and style you want for the top of your head.
  2. Ask your barber to cut the sides and back of your head short using clippers or scissors.
  3. Trim the top of your hair to the desired length and style.
  4. Blend the top and sides together to create a seamless look.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Mens haircut short sides longer on top is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal upkeep. Here are some tips to help you maintain the look:

  • Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep the sides and back short.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to keep the top of your hair in place and add texture.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q. What face shape is best for mens haircut short sides longer on top?

A. Mens haircut short sides longer on top works well with most face shapes, but it particularly suits men with oval or square faces.

Q. Can I style mens haircut short sides longer on top in different ways?

A. Yes, you can style mens haircut short sides longer on top in various ways, such as a slicked-back look, a messy fringe, or a textured crop.

Q. Will mens haircut short sides longer on top suit my hair type?

A. Mens haircut short sides longer on top works well with most hair types, but it particularly suits men with thick or wavy hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, it's essential to take good care of it. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage the hair and scalp.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or straightening, as it can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking great, whether you have mens haircut short sides longer on top or any other hairstyle.
