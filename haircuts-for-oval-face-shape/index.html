Haircuts For Oval Face Shape The Ultimate Guide

If you have an oval face shape, you are blessed with a versatile facial structure that can look great with a wide range of haircuts. However, choosing the right hairstyle can still be a daunting task. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide to the best haircuts for oval face shapes, including how to determine your face shape, step-by-step haircutting instructions, tips for styling and maintenance, and answers to frequently asked questions.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the perfect haircut for your face shape is to determine what shape your face is. To determine your face shape, follow these simple steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back off your face.
  2. Take a close look at the shape of your face.
  3. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Compare these measurements to the following face shape categories:
  • Oval: forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, with gently rounded hairline and chin
  • Round: forehead and jawline are the same width, with full cheeks and rounded chin
  • Square: forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are similar in width, with a square or angular jawline
  • Heart: forehead is wider than the jawline, with a pointed chin
  • Diamond: cheekbones are the widest part of the face, with a narrow forehead and jawline
  • Triangle: jawline is wider than the forehead, with a pointed chin

If you have an oval face shape, congratulations! You can rock a variety of haircuts and styles. Keep reading to find out which haircuts work best for you.

Best Haircuts for Oval Face Shape

As mentioned earlier, oval face shapes are versatile and can wear many different haircuts. However, there are a few styles that are particularly flattering for this face shape:

Long Layers

Long layers are a great choice for oval faces because they add movement and volume to the hair without overwhelming the face. You can wear your hair straight or wavy with this cut, and it will look great either way.

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob that falls just above the chin is another great option for oval faces. This cut emphasizes the jawline and cheekbones while framing the face in a flattering way.

Side-Swept Bangs

If you want to add some bangs to your haircut, go for side-swept bangs that hit just below the brow line. This style draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones while softening the forehead.

Shoulder-Length Waves

If you prefer longer hair, try shoulder-length waves. This cut adds volume and texture to the hair while framing the face in a flattering way. You can wear your hair in loose waves or more defined curls.

Step-by-Step Haircutting Instructions

If you want to cut your hair at home, follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and separate it into two sections.
  3. Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and cut it to the desired length.
  4. Continue cutting small sections of hair, working your way toward the front of your head.
  5. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  6. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.
  7. Trim any bangs or layers as desired.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling and Maintenance

Once you have your perfect haircut, it's important to know how to style and maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools to prevent damage.
  • If you have long hair, consider using a hair mask once a week to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Aim for every 2-3 days instead.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are tailored to your hair type and needs.
  • When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume and movement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can oval faces wear short hair?

Absolutely! Oval faces can wear short hair just as well as long hair. In fact, a short pixie cut can be a great choice for oval faces.

What hairstyles should oval faces avoid?

Oval faces can wear many different hairstyles, but there are a few to avoid. For example, avoid hairstyles that are too long and flat, as these can make the face appear longer. Also, avoid blunt bangs that hit at the eyebrow level, as these can make the face appear shorter.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's a good idea to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Oval Face Shape

Finally, here are some general hair care tips that are especially beneficial for oval faces:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent damage and tangling while you sleep.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of nutrients that promote healthy hair, such as protein, iron, and vitamin E.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With this guide, you should now be equipped to find the perfect haircut for your oval face shape. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
