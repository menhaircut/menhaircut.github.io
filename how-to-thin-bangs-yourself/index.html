How To Thin Bangs Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of bulky, heavy bangs that get in your eyes and make you feel hot and uncomfortable? Thinning out your bangs can be a great solution, but it can also be expensive to go to a salon every time you need a trim. Luckily, with a few simple tools and techniques, you can easily thin out your bangs at home. Here's how to do it:

How to Thin Bangs Yourself

What You'll Need:

Before you get started, make sure you have these tools on hand:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips or bobby pins

Step-by-Step Guide:

Follow these steps to thin out your bangs:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair, making sure your bangs are completely dry.
  2. Use your comb to separate your bangs from the rest of your hair.
  3. Clip back the rest of your hair to keep it out of the way.
  4. Comb your bangs straight down over your forehead.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the middle of your bangs.
  6. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and point your scissors vertically towards your bangs.
  7. Cut into the section of hair, making small snips along the length of the hair.
  8. Repeat this process with small sections of hair along your bangs, making sure to alternate the sections to avoid creating a visible line.
  9. Check your progress frequently by combing your bangs and looking at them straight on in the mirror.
  10. Stop when you're happy with the amount of thinning you've done.

Tips for Thinning Your Bangs:

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind as you thin out your bangs:

  • Start with a small amount of thinning and work your way up. You can always take more hair off later, but you can't put it back on.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp to avoid frayed ends.
  • Always point your scissors vertically towards your bangs to avoid creating choppy layers.
  • Take breaks and check your progress frequently to avoid over-thinning your bangs.

FAQs:

Here are a few frequently asked questions about thinning bangs:

Can I thin my bangs with thinning shears?

Yes, you can use thinning shears to thin out your bangs. However, if you're not experienced with using these tools, it's best to stick with regular scissors to avoid accidentally cutting too much hair.

How often should I thin out my bangs?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how thick your bangs are. Generally, it's a good idea to thin out your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and light.

Hair Care for Thinned-Out Bangs:

Once you've thinned out your bangs, it's important to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools on your bangs too often, as this can damage the hair.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your thinned-out bangs.

Thinning out your bangs at home can be a great way to save money and achieve the look you want. With the right tools and techniques, you can thin out your bangs safely and easily. Follow these tips and you'll be on your way to lighter, more comfortable bangs in no time!
