Why Is My Hair Falling Out On The Sides

Written by Eveline Aug 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are noticing hair loss on the sides of your head, you may be wondering why this is happening and what you can do to stop it. Hair loss can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, age, hormones, and medical conditions. In this article, we will explore the common causes of hair loss on the sides of the head and provide tips on how to prevent and treat it.

What Causes Hair Loss on the Sides of the Head?

Hair loss on the sides of the head is often caused by a condition called androgenetic alopecia, commonly known as male or female pattern baldness. This condition is hereditary and is characterized by hair thinning and loss on the top and sides of the head. Hormonal changes, such as those that occur during menopause, can also contribute to hair loss on the sides of the head.

Other potential causes of hair loss on the sides of the head include:

  • Stress and anxiety
  • Poor nutrition
  • Chemical damage from relaxers and hair dyes
  • Medical conditions such as thyroid disorders and autoimmune diseases
  • Traction alopecia from tight hairstyles

How to Prevent Hair Loss on the Sides of the Head

Preventing hair loss on the sides of the head requires a combination of lifestyle changes and hair care practices. Here are some tips to help prevent hair loss:

Eat a Balanced Diet

A diet rich in vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of protein, iron, zinc, and vitamins A, C, and D in your diet.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Tight hairstyles, such as braids and weaves, can cause traction alopecia, a condition that leads to hair loss. Avoid hairstyles that pull on the hair and scalp.

Reduce Stress

Stress can contribute to hair loss. Practice relaxation techniques, such as meditation and yoga, to reduce stress levels.

Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Harsh chemicals in hair care products can damage the hair and contribute to hair loss. Use gentle shampoos and conditioners that are free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.

How to Treat Hair Loss on the Sides of the Head

If you are already experiencing hair loss on the sides of the head, there are several treatments available to help slow down or stop the process. Here are some options:

Medications

Medications such as minoxidil (Rogaine) and finasteride (Propecia) can help slow down hair loss and promote hair growth.

Hair Transplant

A hair transplant involves taking hair from another part of the scalp and transplanting it to the balding area. This is a surgical procedure and should be performed by a qualified professional.

Scalp Micropigmentation

Scalp micropigmentation is a non-surgical procedure that involves tattooing tiny dots on the scalp to create the appearance of hair follicles. This can be a good option for those who are not candidates for hair transplant surgery.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Regardless of whether you are experiencing hair loss or not, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt and oil buildup.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage the hair and lead to breakage. Use them sparingly or not at all.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and breakage.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Exposure to the sun can damage the hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can stress cause hair loss on the sides of the head?

Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss on the sides of the head. When you are stressed, your body produces hormones that can lead to hair loss.

Can hair loss on the sides of the head be reversed?

Depending on the cause of hair loss, it may be possible to reverse it. Talk to your doctor or a hair loss specialist to discuss treatment options.

Are there any natural remedies for hair loss on the sides of the head?

Some natural remedies that may help prevent hair loss include taking supplements like biotin and using essential oils like rosemary and lavender on the scalp. However, it is important to talk to your doctor before trying any natural remedies.

Conclusion

Hair loss on the sides of the head can be a frustrating and distressing experience. However, there are steps you can take to prevent and treat hair loss, including eating a balanced diet, avoiding tight hairstyles, reducing stress, using gentle hair care products, and considering medical treatments like medications and hair transplants. By taking care of your hair and addressing any underlying medical conditions, you can keep your hair healthy and strong for years to come.
