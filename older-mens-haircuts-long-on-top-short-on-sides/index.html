Older Men s Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides A Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're an older man looking to switch up your hairstyle, the long on top, short on sides look could be the perfect fit for you. This classic haircut is trendy yet timeless, and it's versatile enough to suit any personal style. Whether you're looking for a polished, professional look or a more laid-back vibe, this haircut could be the perfect option.

Description

The long on top, short on sides haircut is exactly what it sounds like. The hair on top of the head is left longer, while the sides are trimmed shorter. This creates a sharp, clean look that's perfect for men who want to look polished and put together. This haircut can be styled in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and tousled.

How to

If you're interested in getting a long on top, short on sides haircut, the first step is to find a good barber. Look for a barber who has experience with this type of haircut and can give you the look you want. Once you've found a barber you trust, it's time to discuss your ideal length and style.

Typically, the hair on the top of the head should be at least 2-3 inches long, while the sides should be buzzed short. The exact length and style will depend on your personal preferences and face shape. Your barber will be able to guide you in choosing the perfect length and style for your hair.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a long on top, short on sides haircut:

  1. Find a good barber who has experience with this type of haircut
  2. Discuss your ideal length and style with your barber
  3. Sit back and relax while your barber trims your hair
  4. Style your hair at home using your preferred products

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a long on top, short on sides haircut:

  • Choose a length and style that flatters your face shape
  • Invest in quality hair care products to keep your hair looking and feeling its best
  • Schedule regular appointments with your barber to maintain your haircut
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you

FAQ

How often should I get this haircut?

It's recommended to get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

What products should I use to style my hair?

The products you use will depend on your hair type and preferred style. Some popular options include pomade, wax, and styling cream.

Can this haircut work for men with thinning hair?

Yes, this haircut can work for men with thinning hair. Your barber can help you choose a length and style that flatters your hair type.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking and feeling its best, it's important to invest in quality hair care products. Look for a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type. You may also want to consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

In addition to using the right products, it's important to avoid over-washing your hair and using hot tools too frequently. These habits can damage your hair and make it harder to style. Instead, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days and let it air dry whenever possible.

By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can rock a long on top, short on sides haircut with confidence and style.
