How To Cut Men s Hair Messy Look The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a hairstyle that's effortlessly cool and low-maintenance? The messy look is the perfect option for you. It's a versatile style that works for various hair types and face shapes. Plus, it's easy to achieve with the right tools and techniques. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's hair messy look step-by-step. We'll also provide tips and tricks to help you perfect this style. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Cut Men's Hair Messy Look
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

The messy look is a popular hairstyle among men. It's a relaxed and effortless style that's perfect for those who don't want to spend too much time styling their hair. The messy look can be achieved with various hair lengths and textures. It's a versatile style that works for different occasions, from casual hangouts to formal events.

How to Cut Men's Hair Messy Look

The messy look is all about texture and volume. To achieve this style, you need to cut your hair in a way that creates movement and dimension. Here are some key steps to follow:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo to create texture and body.

Step 2: Towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 3: Use a pair of scissors to cut off any split ends or damaged hair.

Step 4: Divide your hair into sections and start cutting it in a random pattern. Use the scissors to create different lengths and angles. Don't worry about making it look perfect; the key is to create texture and movement.

Step 5: Use a razor or thinning shears to remove bulk and add texture. This will help create the messy look.

Step 6: Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it with some hair wax or pomade. Use your fingers to create a tousled and messy look.

Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Use a pair of sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

Tip 2: Don't cut your hair too short; it should have enough length to create movement.

Tip 3: Use a razor or thinning shears to add texture and remove bulk.

Tip 4: Use a hair wax or pomade to style your hair. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh your hair down.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the messy look with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve the messy look with short hair. Just make sure to leave enough length to create movement and texture.

Q: How often should I cut my hair to maintain the messy look?

A: It depends on your hair growth rate and personal preference. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and texture.

Hair Care Tips

Tip 1: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create texture and body.

Tip 2: Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair and strip it of its natural oils.

Tip 3: Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Tip 4: Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Cutting men's hair messy look is a simple and low-maintenance style that works for various occasions. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect messy look. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding damaging hot tools. With the right care and maintenance, your hair will look effortlessly cool and stylish.
