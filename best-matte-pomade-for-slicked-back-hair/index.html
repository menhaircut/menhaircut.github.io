Best Matte Pomade For Slicked Back Hair

Welcome to our blog post about the best matte pomade for slicked back hair. If you're looking for a product that will help you achieve a classic, stylish look, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing the top matte pomades on the market that will help you achieve a slicked back hairstyle that lasts all day.

Description

Matte pomades are a great choice for those who want a natural-looking finish without the shine that comes with traditional pomades. Slicked back hair is a timeless style that can be worn by anyone, and it's also easy to achieve with the right pomade. The best matte pomades for slicked back hair will provide a strong hold that lasts all day, while also being easy to wash out at the end of the day.

How to

To achieve a slicked back hairstyle using matte pomade, you'll need to follow a few simple steps.

Step 1: Choose the right pomade

Choosing the right pomade is essential for achieving a slicked back hairstyle that lasts all day. Look for a pomade that provides a strong hold and a matte finish, like the ones we'll be discussing in this article.

Step 2: Apply the pomade

Take a small amount of the pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply it to your hair, starting at the crown and working your way down to the tips. Be sure to distribute the pomade evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Style your hair

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair back and away from your face. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or flyaways as you go.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a slicked back hairstyle with matte pomade:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Scoop out a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the crown and working your way down to the tips. Be sure to distribute it evenly.
  4. Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair back and away from your face.
  5. Smooth out any bumps or flyaways as you go.
  6. If necessary, add more pomade to areas that need extra hold.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to keep your style in place all day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using matte pomade for slicked back hair:

  • Start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to achieve your desired style.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different pomades to find the one that works best for you.
  • Wash your hair thoroughly at the end of the day to avoid buildup.

FAQ

Q: Can I use matte pomade on wet hair?

A: It's best to use matte pomade on dry hair. Wet hair can dilute the hold of the pomade, making it less effective.

Q: Will matte pomade make my hair look greasy?

A: No, matte pomade is designed to provide a natural-looking finish without any shine or greasiness.

Q: Can I restyle my hair throughout the day?

A: Yes, you can restyle your hair throughout the day by adding more pomade and using a comb or your fingers to reshape your style.

Hair Care

Using a good shampoo and conditioner is important to keep your hair healthy and looking its best. Be sure to wash your hair thoroughly at the end of the day to remove any pomade buildup. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Now that you know all about the best matte pomades for slicked back hair, it's time to try them out for yourself. With the right product and a little bit of practice, you'll be able to achieve a classic, stylish look that lasts all day.
