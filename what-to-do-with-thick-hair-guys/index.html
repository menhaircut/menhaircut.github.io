What To Do With Thick Hair Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jun 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it gives you volume and body, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you’re a guy with thick hair, don’t worry, we’ve got you covered. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks on what to do with thick hair guys.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Thick Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Hair
  • Tips for Managing Thick Hair
  • FAQs about Thick Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

How to Style Thick Hair

When it comes to styling thick hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that works with your hair type. Second, you’ll need to use the right products to achieve your desired look. Here are some popular hairstyles for guys with thick hair:

  • Short and textured
  • Buzz cut
  • Crew cut
  • Pompadour
  • Quiff

The key to styling thick hair is to add texture and movement. You can achieve this by using a texturizing spray or pomade. These products will give your hair a messy, undone look that’s popular right now.

Products to Use:

Here are some of the best products to use on thick hair:

  • Texturizing spray
  • Pomade
  • Gel
  • Mousse
  • Wax

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Thick Hair

Now that you know what products to use, here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your thick hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a texturizing spray or pomade to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  5. Style your hair using your fingers, creating a messy, undone look.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Remember, the key to styling thick hair is to add texture and movement. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the style that works best for you.

Tips for Managing Thick Hair

Managing thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, it’s possible. Here are some tips for managing your thick hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Don’t wash your hair every day, as this can dry out your scalp and make your hair frizzy.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush to detangle your hair without causing damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips, you can keep your thick hair healthy and manageable.

FAQs about Thick Hair

Q: How often should I wash my thick hair?

A: You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.

Q: What’s the best haircut for guys with thick hair?

A: Short and textured haircuts work well for guys with thick hair.

Q: Can I use a hairdryer on my thick hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Should I use a leave-in conditioner on my thick hair?

A: Yes, a leave-in conditioner can help to moisturize and detangle your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

In addition to using the right products and techniques, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for guys with thick hair:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular exercise to promote circulation and healthy hair growth.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your thick hair looking healthy and vibrant.

Conclusion

Thick hair can be a challenge to manage and style, but with the right techniques and products, it’s possible. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find the look that works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair by following a healthy diet and exercise routine, and using high-quality hair care products.
