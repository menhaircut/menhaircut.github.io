Face Framing Bangs For Wavy Hair How To Get The Look

Written by Luffy Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have wavy hair, you might be curious about how to style it with face framing bangs. Not only can bangs add a stylish touch to your hair, but they can also help frame your face and accentuate your features. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve the perfect face framing bangs for wavy hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect Face Framing Bangs
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair with Bangs

Description

Face framing bangs are a great way to add some dimension and texture to your hair. They can help to soften your features and give your hair a more youthful look. With wavy hair, the key is to find a length and shape that works well with your natural texture.

When it comes to styling face framing bangs on wavy hair, you have a few options. You can wear them straight or wavy, depending on your preference. Either way, it's important to use the right products and tools to help enhance your natural texture and keep your bangs looking fresh and stylish.

How to Achieve the Look

Before you start styling your face framing bangs, it's important to determine the right length and shape for your face. You can consult with your hairstylist to help determine the best style for you.

Once you have the right length and shape, you can start styling your bangs. You can use a flat iron to straighten them or a curling iron to add some waves. It's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Another option is to air dry your bangs and use a curl enhancing cream or mousse to help enhance your natural texture. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve face framing bangs for wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Determine the desired length and shape for your bangs.
  3. Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs, depending on your preference.
  4. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  6. Apply a curl enhancing cream or mousse to your bangs to help enhance your natural texture.
  7. Use a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Perfect Face Framing Bangs

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect face framing bangs for wavy hair:

  • Choose the right length and shape for your face.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling your bangs.
  • Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs, depending on your preference.
  • Air dry your bangs and use a curl enhancing cream or mousse to enhance your natural texture.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural waves.
  • Use a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear face framing bangs with any face shape?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right length and shape for your face. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best style for you.

Q: Can I style my bangs without heat tools?

A: Yes, you can air dry your bangs and use a curl enhancing cream or mousse to enhance your natural texture.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the desired length and shape.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair with Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your wavy hair with bangs looking healthy and stylish:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet to prevent damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect face framing bangs for your wavy hair. Experiment with different styles and products to find the look that works best for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
