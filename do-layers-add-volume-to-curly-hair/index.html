Do Layers Add Volume To Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair is stunning, but that doesn't mean it's not challenging to style. One of the biggest problems people with curly hair face is the lack of volume. But, have you ever wondered if layers can help solve this problem? In this article, we will answer the question, "do layers add volume to curly hair?" and provide you with some tips on how to achieve voluminous curly hair.

What are Layers?

Layers are a popular haircutting technique that involves cutting the hair at different lengths. The top layer is usually shorter, and the layers underneath gradually get longer. This creates the illusion of movement and texture in the hair, making it look less flat and more voluminous.

Do Layers Add Volume to Curly Hair?

Yes, layers can add volume to curly hair. By cutting the hair at different lengths, layers create space between the hair strands, giving the hair more body and bounce. Curly hair tends to be weighed down by its own weight, which is why layers are an excellent solution for those looking to add volume to their hair.

It's important to note that layers should be cut in a way that complements your curl pattern. The wrong layering technique can result in frizz and an unflattering shape. So, it's best to consult with a hairstylist who specializes in cutting curly hair.

How to Achieve Voluminous Curly Hair with Layers

Here's how you can achieve voluminous curly hair with layers:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, damp hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a styling product that enhances your curls. This can be a mousse, gel, or cream.
  3. Flip your hair upside down and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help create volume at the roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define your curls even further. Start from the middle of your hair shaft and wrap your hair around the barrel, leaving the ends out.
  5. Finish off your look with a hairspray or a lightweight oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Voluminous Curly Hair

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when your hair is wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Don't overuse hair products. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Q. How many layers should I have in my curly hair?

A. The number of layers you need depends on your hair length and curl pattern. It's best to consult with a hairstylist who specializes in cutting curly hair to determine the right number of layers for you.

Q. Can layers make my curly hair look shorter?

A. Yes, layers can make your curly hair look shorter. This is because the top layer is usually shorter than the layers underneath. However, this can also make your hair look more voluminous and bouncy.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often. Heat can damage your curls and cause frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Final Thoughts

Layers can add volume to curly hair, but it's important to get them cut by a hairstylist who specializes in curly hair. With the right layering technique and proper hair care, you can achieve voluminous, bouncy curls that will turn heads.
