Mens Haircuts 2021 Long On Top

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that is trendy and fashionable? Look no further than mens haircuts 2021 long on top! This style has been popular for years and shows no signs of slowing down. In this article, we will cover how to achieve this style, tips for maintenance and care, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

What is the Mens Haircut 2021 Long on Top?

The mens haircut 2021 long on top is a style where the hair on the top of the head is left longer than the sides and back. This style is often paired with an undercut or fade on the sides to create a sharp contrast. The length on top can vary from a few inches to shoulder-length, depending on personal preference.

How to Achieve the Mens Haircut 2021 Long on Top

The first step in achieving this style is to find a skilled barber or stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Bring in some inspiration photos to show them what you are looking for. The next step is to decide on the length you want on top and the type of fade or undercut you prefer on the sides. Your stylist can help you make these decisions.

Once you have your cut, styling is easy. Simply blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go and use a pomade or wax to hold it in place. You can also experiment with different partings to create a unique look.

Tips for Maintenance and Care

Maintaining the mens haircut 2021 long on top requires regular trims to keep the length in check. Depending on your hair type and how quickly it grows, you may need a trim every 4-6 weeks. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

When styling, use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying to prevent damage to your hair. Also, avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Instead, use a small amount and build up as needed.

FAQ

What face shape is best for the mens haircut 2021 long on top?

This style can work with most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on those with oval or square faces. If you have a round face, consider adding some volume to the top to create the illusion of length.

Can I wear this style if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can add texture and volume to this style. Just make sure to find a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair.

Do I need to use a specific type of product to style this haircut?

No, you can use any type of pomade or wax to style this haircut. Experiment with different products to find one that works best for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the mens haircut 2021 long on top. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, opt for air drying or using a cool setting on your blow dryer.

Regular trims are also important to keep the length in check and prevent split ends. If you notice any damage or breakage, consider getting a trim to remove the damaged ends.

Overall, the mens haircut 2021 long on top is a stylish and versatile look that can work for any man. With proper maintenance and care, you can rock this style with confidence and ease.
