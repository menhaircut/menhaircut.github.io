Hair Styles For Round Faces 2021 Tips How To And Faq

Written by Eveline Nov 22, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for the perfect hair style for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will go over the best hair styles for round faces in 2021. Read on for tips, how-to’s, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How-to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have a soft and curvy shape with a width and height that are almost equal. The best hair styles for round faces are those that create the illusion of length and add angles to the face. In 2021, there are a variety of hair styles that flatter round faces, including long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts. The key is to find a style that accentuates your best features and balances out your face shape.

How-to

The first step in finding the perfect hair style for your round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width and height of your face and comparing the two. If they are almost equal, you have a round face. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hair styles that flatter your features.

Step-by-Step

Here are the steps to finding the perfect hair style for your round face:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research hair styles that flatter round faces.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for you.
  4. Maintain your hair with regular trims and proper care.

Tips

Here are some tips for finding the perfect hair style for your round face:

  • Choose styles that add height and length to your face.
  • Avoid styles that add width to your face.
  • Consider adding layers or bangs to create angles and dimension.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Don’t be afraid to consult with a hairstylist for professional advice.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair styles for round faces:

  • Q: What is the best hair length for round faces?
    • A: Long hair is typically the most flattering for round faces, but shorter styles can work too as long as they add height and length to the face.
  • Q: Are bangs good for round faces?
    • A: Yes, side-swept bangs can create angles and balance out a round face.
  • Q: What should I avoid in a hair style for my round face?
    • A: Avoid styles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or fullness at the sides.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining any hair style, especially for those with round faces. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your hair style.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water for healthy hair from the inside out.

Now that you have all the information you need, it’s time to find the perfect hair style for your round face. Remember to choose a style that accentuates your best features and balances out your face shape. Don’t be afraid to experiment and consult with a hairstylist for professional advice. With the right hair style and proper care, you can look and feel your best in 2021 and beyond.
