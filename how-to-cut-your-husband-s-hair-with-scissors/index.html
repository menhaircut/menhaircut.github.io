How To Cut Your Husband s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money on haircuts for your husband, or you just want to try your hand at cutting hair, you might be wondering how to cut your husband's hair with scissors. While it can seem intimidating at first, with the right tools and some basic knowledge, you can give your husband a great haircut that he'll love. Read on for a step-by-step guide.

How to

To start, you'll need some basic tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Cape or towel
  • Spray bottle (optional)

Once you have your tools ready, you can get started:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure your husband's hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut, and can also cause the hair to shrink up as it dries, which could result in a shorter haircut than you intended.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Use the comb to section your husband's hair into manageable sections. Start at the back of the head and work your way forward, clipping up each section as you go.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Once you have your sections in place, you can start cutting. Use the comb to hold the hair in place, and use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Be sure to work slowly and carefully, making small cuts to avoid taking off too much at once.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

As you work your way around your husband's head, you may notice that the hair looks uneven or choppy. To blend the hair, use the scissors to make small snips in the hair, working in a back-and-forth motion to create a more natural look.

Step 5: Trim the Sides and Back

If you're using clippers, you can use them to trim the sides and back of your husband's head. Use a guard to control the length of the hair, and work in a smooth, even motion to avoid creating any choppy lines.

Step 6: Clean Up the Neckline

Once you're finished cutting, use the scissors to clean up the neckline. This will give the haircut a more polished look.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Once you're done cutting, you can style your husband's hair as desired. Use a styling product to create a polished look, or leave it natural for a more relaxed style.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Work slowly and carefully, making small cuts to avoid taking off too much at once.
  • Use a comb to hold the hair in place as you cut.
  • Blend the hair as you go to create a more natural look.
  • Use clippers to trim the sides and back, if desired.
  • Clean up the neckline with scissors for a polished look.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about cutting hair:

Can I Cut My Husband's Hair with Scissors?

Yes, you can cut your husband's hair with scissors. With the right tools and some basic knowledge, you can give him a great haircut at home.

Do I Need to Use Clippers?

No, you don't need to use clippers. While they can be helpful for trimming the sides and back, you can achieve a great haircut with just scissors.

What if I Mess Up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always make adjustments as you go, and you can always take your husband to a professional stylist to fix any errors.

Hair Care

After you've finished cutting your husband's hair, be sure to take care of it properly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair soft and healthy, and avoid using heated styling tools too often, as these can damage the hair. With the right care, your husband's hair will look great until his next haircut.

Now that you know how to cut your husband's hair with scissors, you can give him a great haircut at home. With the right tools and some basic knowledge, you can save money on haircuts while still achieving a great look.
