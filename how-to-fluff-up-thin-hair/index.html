How To Fluff Up Thin Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thin hair, you know how hard it can be to create volume and body in your hair. But don't worry, there are many ways to fluff up thin hair and make it look fuller and thicker. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to achieve that voluminous look you've always wanted.

How to Fluff Up Thin Hair

There are many ways to fluff up thin hair, such as using volumizing products, blow drying your hair, and choosing the right haircut. Here are some tips on how to fluff up thin hair:

Use Volumizing Products

One of the easiest ways to add volume to thin hair is to use volumizing products. Look for products such as volumizing mousse, root lift sprays, and volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Apply the products to your hair while it's damp, then blow dry your hair upside down to create volume.

Choose the Right Haircut

The right haircut can make a huge difference in how your hair looks. If you have thin hair, choose a haircut that adds volume and body to your hair. Consider getting layers, as they can add texture and movement to your hair. Avoid getting a blunt cut, as it can make your hair look flat and lifeless.

Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can add volume and body to your hair. To do this, blow dry your hair upside down using a round brush. This will create lift at the roots and add volume to your hair.

Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair can also add volume and body. To do this, take a small section of your hair and backcomb it using a teasing comb. Then, gently smooth the top layer of your hair over the teased section to create a voluminous look.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to fluff up thin hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add volume to your hair and create a fuller look.

Step 2: Apply Volumizing Products

Apply volumizing products, such as mousse or root lift spray, to your hair while it's still damp. This will help create volume and body in your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow dry your hair upside down using a round brush. This will create lift at the roots and add volume to your hair.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Take small sections of your hair and backcomb them using a teasing comb. Then, gently smooth the top layer of your hair over the teased section to create a voluminous look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a light hairspray to hold your hair in place and add shine.

Tips for Fluffing Up Thin Hair

Here are some additional tips for fluffing up thin hair:

Use a Diffuser

Using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer can help create volume and body in your hair.

Avoid Heavy Products

Avoid using heavy products, such as oils or serums, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.

Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look limp and lifeless. Try washing your hair every other day or every few days.

FAQs

How can I make my thin hair look thicker?

You can make your thin hair look thicker by using volumizing products, blow drying your hair, choosing the right haircut, and teasing your hair.

What is the best haircut for thin hair?

The best haircut for thin hair is one that adds volume and body, such as a layered haircut.

Can I use hairspray on thin hair?

Yes, you can use hairspray on thin hair. Just be sure to use a light hairspray that won't weigh down your hair.

Hair Care for Thin Hair

Here are some tips for caring for thin hair:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look even thinner.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fluff up thin hair and create a voluminous, full-bodied look. Remember to use volumizing products, choose the right haircut, blow dry your hair, and tease your hair to add volume and body. With the right hair care routine, you can have healthy, beautiful hair.
