How To Cut Your Hair With Scissors Only A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this year 2023, cutting your own hair has become a necessity for many people. With salons closed and social distancing measures in place, DIY haircuts have become the norm. But what if you don't have clippers or a fancy cutting kit? Don't worry, you can still cut your hair with just a pair of scissors. In this article, we'll show you how to cut your hair with scissors only in a relaxed English language that's easy to follow.

How to Cut Your Hair with Scissors Only

If you're ready to take matters into your own hands, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair ties or clips to keep your hair in place. If you have long hair, you may also want to consider getting a handheld mirror so you can see the back of your head.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to see where you need to cut and ensure that your hair is evenly trimmed.

Step 3: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Using your comb, divide your hair into sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward. Use hair ties or clips to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start with the bottom section of your hair and work your way up. Take small sections of hair and trim the ends with your scissors. Use the comb to guide your scissors and make sure you're cutting straight.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you've finished cutting each section, take a step back and check your progress. Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them.

Step 6: Finish with the Bangs

If you have bangs, save them for last. Use your comb to separate your bangs from the rest of your hair and trim them to your desired length.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Scissors Only

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up. It's easier to cut more hair off than to try and fix a mistake.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your hair can be tiring, so don't be afraid to take a break and come back to it later.
  • Don't try to cut too much off at once. It's better to take it slow and cut a little bit at a time.

FAQs

Can I cut my hair with kitchen scissors?

It's not recommended to use kitchen scissors to cut your hair. They may not be sharp enough and could result in uneven cuts.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to fix it. Alternatively, you can try to fix it yourself by trimming the uneven spots or wearing your hair in a different style.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair with Scissors Only

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking fresh.

Cutting your hair with scissors only may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it's definitely doable. Just remember to take it slow, be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
