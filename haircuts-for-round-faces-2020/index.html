Haircuts For Round Faces 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of getting the same old haircut that doesn't flatter your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the latest haircuts for round faces 2020. From short to long hair, we have compiled a list of hairstyles that will make your face look slimmer and more defined. So, let's get started!

Description

Having a round face can be challenging when it comes to finding the right haircut that suits you. The goal is to create an illusion of a more elongated face rather than accentuating the roundness. The haircuts we have listed below will help you achieve just that.

How To: Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the haircuts, it's important to determine your face shape. To do so, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Once you have traced your face, step back and compare it with the following face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond

If your traced outline resembles a circle, then you have a round face shape.

Step by Step: Haircuts for Round Faces 2020

Now that you have determined your face shape let's move on to the haircuts.

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that is perfect for round faces. It creates an illusion of a slimmer face by adding height and volume to the crown of your head. You can add bangs to give your face more definition or leave it swept to the side.

2. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's a perfect haircut for round faces as it adds volume to the crown of your head and frames your face. You can add layers to the sides to give your face more definition or keep it straight.

3. Long Layers

If you prefer long hair, then long layers are the way to go. It adds volume to the crown of your head and elongates your face. You can add bangs to give your face more definition or keep it swept to the side.

4. Side Bangs

Side bangs are perfect for round faces as they add angles to your face. It creates an illusion of a slimmer face and gives your hair more movement.

Tips

When getting a haircut for a round face, keep these tips in mind:

  • Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Layered haircuts are your best friend.
  • Adding height and volume to the crown of your head will elongate your face.
  • Side-swept bangs are a great way to add angles to your face.

FAQ

1. Can I get a bob with a round face?

Yes, you can. A layered bob is a perfect haircut for round faces as it adds volume to the crown of your head and frames your face.

2. Should I avoid bangs with a round face?

Avoid blunt bangs as they can make your face look rounder. However, side-swept bangs are a great way to add angles to your face.

3. Can I get long hair with a round face?

Yes, you can. Long layers are perfect for round faces as it adds volume to the crown of your head and elongates your face.

Hair Care

After getting a new haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis as it can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

There you have it, our comprehensive guide to haircuts for round faces 2020. Remember to pick a haircut that suits your personality and lifestyle.
