Description

As we age, our faces start to lose their youthful contours, and we need to choose hairstyles that can help to rejuvenate our looks. For women with square faces, the key is to soften the angles and create more curves. The best hairstyle for square face female over 50 is one that enhances your features and complements your personality while taking into account your hair texture and thickness.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Square Face Female Over 50

The first step in choosing the best hairstyle for square face female over 50 is to determine your face shape. If you have a square face, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. To soften your features, you should go for hairstyles that create more curves and add volume to the crown of your head. Consider hairstyles that are shorter in length and have layers around the face. Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs, which can make your face appear more angular.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture and thickness. Fine, thin hair can benefit from shorter hairstyles that add volume and movement, while thick, coarse hair can handle longer styles with layers and texture.

Hairstyle Ideas for Square Face Female Over 50:

  • Chin-length bob with layers
  • Short pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Layered shoulder-length cut with waves
  • Medium-length shag with choppy layers
  • Long layered cut with face-framing layers

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair

To style your hair, start by washing and conditioning it with products that are appropriate for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant before blow-drying it. Use a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head and add curls or waves around the face. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips on How to Maintain Your Hairstyle

To maintain your hairstyle, you should schedule regular appointments with your hairstylist to trim your hair and keep it in shape. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type, and avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid blunt cuts, straight bangs, and hairstyles that are too long and flat. These can all make your face appear more angular and less feminine.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a square face?

A middle part can work well for some square faces, but it can also emphasize the angularity of your face. Consider adding side-swept bangs or layers around the face to soften your features.

Do I need to use heat tools to style my hair?

No, you don't have to use heat tools to style your hair. You can air-dry your hair and use styling products to add volume and texture. If you do use heat tools, be sure to use a heat protectant and avoid using them too often.

Hair Care Tips for Square Face Female Over 50

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it in shape.

By following these hair care tips and choosing the best hairstyle for your square face, you can look and feel your best at any age.
