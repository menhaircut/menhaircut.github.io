How To Layer Thick Straight Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your thick straight hair falling flat and lacking volume? Layering your hair may be the solution you need. Layering helps to add depth and texture to your hair, making it look more voluminous and lively. In this post, we will guide you on how to layer thick straight hair, step by step. We will also provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect layered look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Layer Thick Straight Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Layering Thick Straight Hair
  • FAQs about Layering Thick Straight Hair
  • Hair Care for Layered Thick Straight Hair

How to Layer Thick Straight Hair

The first step in layering thick straight hair is to determine the length and style you want to achieve. Do you want to add volume to your hair, or do you want to create a more defined style? Once you have decided on the look you want to achieve, you can start layering your hair.

Layering thick straight hair involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create texture and volume. It is important to note that layering can cause your hair to appear thinner if not done correctly. Therefore, it is essential to go to a professional hairstylist who is experienced in layering thick straight hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to layer thick straight hair:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before going to the salon. This will make it easier for the stylist to work with your hair.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist about the length and style you want to achieve.
  3. The hairstylist will then section your hair and cut it at different lengths to create layers.
  4. The hairstylist will also add texture to your hair by using thinning shears or razors.
  5. Once the hairstylist has finished layering your hair, they will style it to give you the desired look.

Tips for Layering Thick Straight Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when layering thick straight hair:

  • Choose a professional hairstylist who has experience in layering thick straight hair.
  • Bring a picture of the style you want to achieve to show your hairstylist.
  • Be open to suggestions from your hairstylist about the best style for your face shape and hair type.
  • Use hair products that add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.

FAQs about Layering Thick Straight Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about layering thick straight hair:

1. How often should I get my hair layered?

You should get your hair layered every 6-8 weeks to maintain the style and volume.

2. Can I layer my hair at home?

It is not recommended to layer your hair at home, especially if you have thick straight hair. Layering requires precision and expertise to avoid damaging your hair.

3. Will layering my hair make it look thinner?

If layering is done correctly, it should add volume and texture to your hair. However, if too much hair is removed, it can make your hair look thinner.

Hair Care for Layered Thick Straight Hair

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your layered thick straight hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, especially when it is wet.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the layers and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.

In conclusion, layering thick straight hair is a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. However, it is important to go to a professional hairstylist who is experienced in layering thick straight hair to avoid damaging your hair. Follow our step by step guide, tips, and hair care advice to maintain your layered look.
