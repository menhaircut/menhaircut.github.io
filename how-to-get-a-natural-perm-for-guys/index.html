How To Get A Natural Perm For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering getting a natural perm, there are a few things you should know. A perm is a chemical process that changes the structure of your hair, making it curly or wavy. While perms are traditionally associated with women, men can also get perms to add volume and texture to their hair. In this article, we'll show you how to get a natural perm for guys, including tips for hair care and maintenance.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Natural Perm for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance
  • FAQ

How to Get a Natural Perm for Guys

Before you get started, it's important to choose a hairstylist who has experience with perms. A good hairstylist will be able to assess your hair type and recommend the right perm solution and technique for you. Here are the steps to follow:

1. Consultation

During your consultation, your hairstylist will ask about your hair history, including any previous chemical treatments, and assess your hair type and texture. Based on this information, they will recommend a perm solution and technique that will work best for you.

2. Shampoo and Condition

Before the perm, your hair will need to be clean and free of any styling products. Your hairstylist will shampoo and condition your hair to prepare it for the perm solution.

3. Rollers

Next, your hairstylist will wrap your hair around rollers to create the desired curl pattern. The size of the rollers will depend on the type of curl you want to achieve.

4. Perm Solution

Once the rollers are in place, your hairstylist will apply the perm solution to your hair. The solution will break down the protein bonds in your hair, allowing it to take on the shape of the rollers.

5. Processing

Your hairstylist will monitor the perm solution to ensure it doesn't over-process your hair. The processing time will vary depending on your hair type and the strength of the perm solution.

6. Neutralizer

After the processing time is up, your hairstylist will apply a neutralizer to your hair to stop the chemical process. This will help to lock in the curl pattern.

7. Rinse and Style

Finally, your hairstylist will rinse out the perm solution and style your hair to your desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're looking to get a natural perm for guys, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a hairstylist who has experience with perms.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the right perm solution and technique for you.
  3. Clean your hair thoroughly before the perm.
  4. Wrap your hair around rollers to create the desired curl pattern.
  5. Apply the perm solution to your hair.
  6. Monitor the processing time to ensure it doesn't over-process your hair.
  7. Apply a neutralizer to your hair to stop the chemical process.
  8. Rinse out the perm solution.
  9. Style your hair to your desired look.

Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance

After getting a natural perm, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the curl pattern and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after the perm to allow the curl pattern to set.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid heat styling tools that can damage your hair, such as flat irons and blow dryers.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

Can I get a natural perm if I have straight hair?

Yes, you can get a natural perm even if you have straight hair. The perm solution will break down the protein bonds in your hair, allowing it to take on the shape of the rollers.

How long does a natural perm last?

A natural perm can last up to six months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of it.

Can I color my hair after getting a natural perm?

It's best to wait at least two weeks before coloring your hair after getting a perm. The chemical process of coloring your hair can be harsh on your strands, and you want to give your hair time to recover from the perm before adding more chemicals.

Getting a natural perm for guys can be a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. By following these steps and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy your new style for months to come.
