Mens Hairstyles Long On Top Short On Sides And Back The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're in search of a trendy and versatile hairstyle, look no further than the mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back. This style has gained popularity in recent years and shows no signs of slowing down. With a variety of ways to wear it, this haircut is perfect for those who want to keep things fresh and stylish.

Description

The mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back is a modern take on the classic short back and sides cut. This style features longer hair on the top of the head, which can be styled in a variety of ways, while the sides and back are kept short. This provides a sharp contrast between the two lengths, creating a bold and edgy look.

How to

To achieve the mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist. They will be able to cut your hair to the desired length and style the top accordingly. When it comes to styling, you have a few options:

1. The Quiff

The quiff is a timeless hairstyle that works perfectly with the mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back. To style a quiff, apply some pomade or wax to the top of your hair and comb it upwards and backwards. Use a blow dryer to add volume and hold the style in place.

2. The Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that can be adapted to suit the mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back. Simply part your hair on one side and comb the longer section to the opposite side. Use a bit of pomade or wax to keep the style in place.

3. The Messy Look

If you prefer a more relaxed and casual style, try the messy look. Apply some texturizing spray or sea salt spray to the top of your hair and tousle it with your fingers. This will create a natural, tousled look that is perfect for everyday wear.

Step by Step

To get the perfect mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back, follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose your length

Decide how long you want the top of your hair to be. This will depend on your personal preferences and face shape. If you're not sure, ask your hairstylist for advice.

Step 2: Get the cut

Visit a professional hairstylist and ask for a mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back cut. Make sure they understand the style you want and have experience cutting this type of haircut.

Step 3: Style your hair

Choose your preferred styling method and apply the appropriate products. Experiment with different styles until you find one that suits you.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back:

- Keep your hair clean and well-groomed to maintain the style. - Use high-quality hair products to ensure your hair stays in place. - Experiment with different styling methods to find the perfect look for you. - Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair trimmed and in good condition.

FAQ

Q: How long should the top of my hair be?

A: This will depend on your personal preferences and face shape. Ask your hairstylist for advice.

Q: How often should I visit my hairstylist?

A: It's recommended to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair trimmed and in good condition.

Q: Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

A: Absolutely! Experiment with different styles until you find one that suits the occasion.

Hair Care

To keep your mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. - Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. - Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.

With the mens hairstyles long on top short on sides and back, you can achieve a trendy and versatile look that is perfect for any occasion. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best and experiment with different styles to find the perfect one for you.
