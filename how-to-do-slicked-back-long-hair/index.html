How to Slick Back Long Hair

Slicked back hair is a classic style that has been around for decades. It is a versatile look that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions. The key to achieving the perfect slicked back style is to start with clean, dry hair. This will help to ensure that your hair stays in place throughout the day.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect slicked back long hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt and oil from your hair, leaving it clean and fresh.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp, but not soaking wet. You may want to use a blow dryer to speed up the process.
  3. Apply a generous amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips. Use a comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  4. Using a comb, slick your hair back starting at the hairline and working your way back towards the crown of your head. Ensure that your hair is smooth and free of any bumps or knots.
  5. If you have any stray hairs, use a small amount of gel or pomade to smooth them down.
  6. Once your hair is slicked back, use a hair dryer on a low setting to set the style in place. Use your hands to help shape your hair as you dry it.
  7. Finish off the look by applying a small amount of hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Slicking Back Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back long hair:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade that is specifically designed for slicked back hairstyles.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, avoid using too much product as it can weigh your hair down
  • If you have thick or curly hair, you may need to use more product to achieve the desired look.
  • Use a comb with fine teeth to ensure that your hair is smooth and free of any bumps or knots.
  • For a more relaxed look, try using a matte finish product instead of a shiny one.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicked back long hair:

Q: Can I slick back long hair if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a slicked back hairstyle even if you have a receding hairline. However, you may need to use more product to help keep your hair in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have slicked back long hair?

A: It is recommended that you wash your hair every two to three days if you have slicked back long hair. This will help to prevent product buildup and keep your hair looking its best.

Q: Can I slick back long hair if it is curly?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a slicked back look if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use more product to help control your curls.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your slicked back long hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent product buildup.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked back long hair in no time. Whether you're getting ready for a special occasion or just want to switch up your everyday style, this classic look is sure to turn heads. So go ahead and give it a try!
