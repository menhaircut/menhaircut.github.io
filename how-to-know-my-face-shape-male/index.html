How To Know My Face Shape Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Sep 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Knowing your face shape is essential if you want to choose the right hairstyle, beard, or glasses. However, many men struggle to determine their face shape, as there are several factors to consider. In this article, we'll guide you through the process step by step, providing useful tips and FAQs along the way.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Measure Your Face
  • Step 2: Identify Your Facial Features
  • Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle or Beard
  • FAQs About Face Shapes for Men
  • Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Step 1: Measure Your Face

The first step to determine your face shape is to measure your face. You'll need a tape measure or a ruler and a mirror. Stand in front of the mirror and measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure the widest part of your forehead.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upward. Then double that number.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Write down these measurements and keep them handy for the next step.

Step 2: Identify Your Facial Features

Now that you have your measurements, it's time to identify your facial features. Look for the following characteristics:

  • Forehead: Is it wider than your cheekbones or narrower?
  • Cheekbones: Are they the widest part of your face or narrower?
  • Jawline: Is it rounded or squared?
  • Face length: Is it short, medium, or long compared to the width of your face?

Take note of these features and move on to the next step.

Step 3: Determine Your Face Shape

Now that you have your measurements and facial features, you can determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes for men:

  • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the jawline, and the face length is longer than the width.
  • Round: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jawline is rounded.
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all equally wide, and the jawline is squared.
  • Rectangle: Face length is the longest measurement, and the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width.
  • Diamond: Cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the forehead and jawline are narrower.
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones, and the jawline tapers to a narrow chin.

Compare your measurements and facial features to these descriptions and determine your face shape. If you're still unsure, ask a friend or a stylist for their opinion.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle or Beard

Once you know your face shape, you can choose the right hairstyle or beard to complement it. Here are some tips:

  • Oval: Any hairstyle or beard works well with this versatile face shape.
  • Round: Choose hairstyles with height on top and short sides to elongate the face. Avoid round or full beards.
  • Square: Choose hairstyles with texture and movement to soften the jawline. Go for a short, trimmed beard.
  • Rectangle: Choose hairstyles with volume on the sides to balance the length of the face. Keep the beard short and neat.
  • Diamond: Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top and keep the sides short. A thin beard can help balance the face.
  • Heart: Choose hairstyles with volume on top and a narrow, pointed beard or goatee to balance the face.

Remember that these are just guidelines, and you should choose a hairstyle or beard that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

FAQs About Face Shapes for Men

Q: Can face shape change over time?

A: No, your face shape is determined by your bone structure and will not change over time.

Q: Can different hairstyles or beards change my face shape?

A: Hairstyles and beards can create the illusion of a different face shape, but they won't change your bone structure.

Q: Can I have a combination of face shapes?

A: Yes, it's possible to have a combination of face shapes, such as a square forehead and a heart-shaped chin.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Regardless of your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Choose a hairstyle that works with your natural hair texture.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and tidy.

By following these tips, you'll not only look great but also keep your hair and scalp healthy.

Conclusion

Determining your face shape is an important step in choosing the right hairstyle, beard, or glasses. By following the steps and tips in this article, you can confidently identify your face shape and choose a style that works for you. Remember that these are just guidelines, and you should always choose a style that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
