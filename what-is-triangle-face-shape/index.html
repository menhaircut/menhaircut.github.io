What Is Triangle Face Shape

Written by Eveline Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're reading this article, chances are you're curious about what a triangle face shape is. A triangle face shape is just one of the many face shapes that people can have. Understanding your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories to enhance your natural features.

What is a Triangle Face Shape?

A triangle face shape is characterized by a wide forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrow chin. This face shape is also known as a pear face shape. If you have a triangle face shape, your forehead may be wider than your jawline, and your chin may be pointed or narrow. This face shape is more commonly seen in women than men.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Determining your face shape is easy. All you need is a mirror and a few minutes of your time. First, pull your hair back and examine your face. Look at the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your chin is pointed or narrow, you likely have a triangle face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Determine Your Face Shape:

  1. Pull your hair back and away from your face.
  2. Examine your face in the mirror.
  3. Measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Tips for Triangle Face Shapes

If you have a triangle face shape, there are a few tips you can use to enhance your natural features:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a layered bob.
  • Avoid hairstyles that emphasize your jawline, such as a blunt bob or a middle part.
  • Wear earrings that draw attention to your cheekbones, such as hoops or dangle earrings.
  • Use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of a more balanced face shape.
  • Choose sunglasses with a strong brow line to balance out your forehead.

FAQ About Triangle Face Shape

1. Can I still wear a middle part if I have a triangle face shape?

Yes, you can still wear a middle part if you have a triangle face shape. Just be aware that it may emphasize your jawline, so it's not the most flattering option for this face shape.

2. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a triangle face shape?

If you have a triangle face shape, you should avoid hairstyles that emphasize your jawline, such as a blunt bob or a flat-ironed hairstyle. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a textured pixie cut or a layered bob.

3. How can I contour my face to balance out my triangle face shape?

You can use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of a more balanced face shape. Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to shade the areas under your cheekbones and jawline, and a lighter shade to highlight your forehead, temples, and chin.

Hair Care for Triangle Face Shapes

If you have a triangle face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to enhance your natural features. Use shampoos and conditioners that are formulated for your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo to add body and fullness to your hair. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls defined and frizz-free.

In conclusion, a triangle face shape is just one of the many face shapes that people can have. Understanding your face shape can help you choose the right hairstyles, makeup, and accessories to enhance your natural features. Use the tips and tricks in this article to make the most of your triangle face shape.
