How To Flat Iron Thick Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to flat iron thick curly hair! If you have naturally curly hair that is thick and difficult to manage, you know how challenging it can be to straighten your hair. But fear not, with the right techniques and tools, you can achieve sleek and straight locks that will make you feel confident and beautiful. In this article, we will discuss step-by-step instructions on how to flat iron thick curly hair, tips and tricks to make the process easier, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips to maintain healthy locks. Let's get started!

How to Flat Iron Thick Curly Hair

Before we start, let's talk about the tools you will need to flat iron your hair. You will need a flat iron, heat protectant spray, a comb or brush, and hair clips to section your hair. It's important to choose a high-quality flat iron with adjustable temperature settings to avoid damaging your hair.

The first step is to wash and condition your hair as usual. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the heat of the flat iron. Next, blow dry your hair using a round brush to straighten your hair as much as possible. Make sure your hair is completely dry before using the flat iron.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these step-by-step instructions to flat iron thick curly hair:

  1. Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips.
  2. Starting with the bottom section, take a small section of hair (about 1 inch) and run the flat iron down the length of the hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  3. Repeat step 2 with each section of hair, working your way towards the top of your head.
  4. Once you have finished straightening all sections of your hair, run the flat iron over your hair one more time to ensure all sections are straight.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to make the process of flat ironing thick curly hair easier:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the heat of the flat iron.
  • Section your hair into manageable sections to make the process easier.
  • Use a comb or brush to detangle your hair before flat ironing.
  • Choose a high-quality flat iron with adjustable temperature settings to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't flat iron your hair too often to prevent damage and breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about flat ironing thick curly hair:

  • How often should I flat iron my hair? It's best to limit flat ironing to once a week to prevent damage and breakage.
  • What temperature should I set my flat iron to? It depends on your hair type and texture. For thick curly hair, start with a lower temperature setting and gradually increase the heat until you achieve the desired straightness.
  • Can I flat iron my hair if it's wet? No, it's important to make sure your hair is completely dry before flat ironing to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to maintain healthy locks:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

There you have it, our complete guide on how to flat iron thick curly hair. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve sleek and straight locks that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain healthy locks. Happy styling!
