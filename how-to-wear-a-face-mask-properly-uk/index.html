How To Wear A Face Mask Properly In The Uk

Written by Daniel Jul 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Wearing a face mask has become a part of our daily routine due to the COVID-19 pandemic. However, it's important to wear it properly to ensure maximum protection. In this article, we'll discuss how to wear a face mask properly in the UK and answer some frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Face Mask Properly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Face Mask
  • FAQs
  • How to Care for Your Hair While Wearing a Face Mask

How to Wear a Face Mask Properly

The first step in wearing a face mask properly is selecting the right type of mask. The UK government recommends using a three-layered face mask, which can filter out small particles and droplets. It's important to ensure that the mask fits well and covers both your nose and mouth.

Here are some other tips for wearing a face mask properly:

  • Wash your hands before putting on the mask
  • Ensure that the mask fits snugly against your face
  • Do not touch the mask while wearing it
  • Wash your hands before and after removing the mask

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear a face mask properly:

  1. Wash your hands with soap and water or use hand sanitizer before touching the mask.
  2. Hold the mask by the ear loops or ties and place it over your nose and mouth.
  3. Ensure that the mask fits snugly against your face and covers your nose and mouth.
  4. Loop the ear loops or ties around your ears or head, respectively.
  5. Adjust the mask to ensure that there are no gaps between the mask and your face.
  6. Do not touch the mask while wearing it.
  7. When removing the mask, take it off from the ear loops or ties.
  8. Wash your hands with soap and water or use hand sanitizer after removing the mask.
  9. Wash the mask with soap and water after each use.

Tips for Wearing a Face Mask

Here are some additional tips for wearing a face mask:

  • Do not wear the mask under your nose or chin
  • Do not reuse disposable masks
  • Do not touch the mask while wearing it
  • Wash the mask with soap and water after each use
  • Carry a spare mask with you in case the first one gets dirty or wet

FAQs

Q: What type of mask should I wear?

A: The UK government recommends using a three-layered face mask, which can filter out small particles and droplets.

Q: How often should I wash my mask?

A: You should wash your mask with soap and water after each use.

Q: Can I wear a face shield instead of a mask?

A: A face shield cannot replace a face mask, as it does not provide the same level of protection. However, it can be worn in addition to a mask for extra protection.

How to Care for Your Hair While Wearing a Face Mask

Wearing a face mask can cause friction on your hair, leading to breakage and damage. Here are some tips for caring for your hair while wearing a mask:

  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as ponytails or braids, as they can cause further friction.
  • Use a silk or satin hair scarf to protect your hair from friction.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any sweat or dirt that may have accumulated under the mask.

By following these tips, you can wear a face mask properly and protect both yourself and others from the spread of COVID-19.
