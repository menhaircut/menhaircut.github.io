Mens Long Straight Haircuts A Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to grow your hair long and straight, you're in luck. Long, straight haircuts for men are becoming more popular than ever. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to decide on a style that suits you. This guide will walk you through everything you need to know about mens long straight haircuts, from how to get the look to tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

Mens long straight haircuts typically involve growing your hair out to shoulder-length or longer, and keeping it straight and sleek. This style can be achieved with a variety of hair types, from fine to thick, and works great for both curly and straight hair. Whether you want a classic look or something more modern, there's a long straight haircut out there for you.

How to Get the Look

The first step to getting a long straight haircut is to grow your hair out. This can take some time, so be patient. Once your hair is long enough, you can visit a stylist to get a trim and shape that suits your face and style. Some popular long straight haircuts for men include:

Layered Cut

A layered cut involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create texture and volume. This style works great for thick hair and can be styled with a side part or slicked back.

Straight and Sleek

If you're looking for a classic and clean look, straight and sleek is the way to go. This style involves keeping the hair all one length and using a straightening iron to keep it straight and smooth.

The Man Bun

The man bun is a popular long straight haircut that involves pulling the hair back into a bun on the top of the head. This style works great for both casual and formal occasions.

Step by Step

To achieve a long straight haircut, follow these steps:

  1. Grow out your hair to shoulder-length or longer.
  2. Visit a stylist to get a trim and shape that suits your face and style.
  3. Use a straightening iron to keep your hair straight and sleek.
  4. Apply hair products like pomade or hair spray to keep your hair in place.
  5. Visit your stylist regularly for trims and maintenance.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy and stylish long straight hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Apply a heat protectant spray before using a straightening iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Try different hair products like pomade or hair spray to find what works best for your hair type and style.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and maintenance, as long hair can be prone to split ends and breakage.

FAQ

Can I get a long straight haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still get a long straight haircut if you have curly hair. However, it may take more time and effort to straighten your hair with a straightening iron or other hair tools.

What hair products should I use for a long straight haircut?

You can use a variety of hair products for a long straight haircut, depending on your hair type and style. Pomade, hair spray, and hair oil are all good options to keep your hair in place and maintain a sleek look.

How often should I visit my stylist for trims?

It's recommended to visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for trims and maintenance, as long hair can be prone to split ends and breakage.

Hair Care

Long straight hair requires regular maintenance to stay healthy and stylish. Here are some tips for caring for your long straight hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip the hair of natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as it can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when wet, and a boar bristle brush to style when dry.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a stylish and healthy mens long straight haircut that suits your face and style. Whether you opt for a layered cut or a sleek and straight style, the possibilities are endless.
