What Number Haircut Should I Get

Written by Aprilia Sep 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you wondering what number haircut you should get? It can be confusing, especially if you're not familiar with different clipper guard sizes. In this blog post, we'll break it down for you and provide tips on how to choose the best haircut for your style and face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Haircut Number
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care Tips

Description

The number haircut refers to the length of hair that is left after trimming with clippers. The guard number determines the length of the hair. The higher the number, the shorter the hair will be, and vice versa. It's essential to choose the right haircut number to achieve the desired style and look.

How to Choose the Right Haircut Number

There are several factors to consider when choosing the right haircut number:

  • Your face shape
  • Your hair texture
  • Your desired style

It's important to keep in mind that different hair clipper sizes will produce different results on different hair textures and face shapes. For example, a #1 clipper guard might look great on someone with a round face, but not so much on someone with an angular face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right haircut number:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research different hairstyles that complement your face shape
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness
  4. Consult with your hairstylist or barber
  5. Trial and error - don't be afraid to try different haircut numbers

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right haircut number:

  • Bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your hairstylist or barber
  • Communicate clearly with your stylist or barber about your desired look
  • Consider maintenance - shorter haircuts require more frequent trips to the salon or barber
  • Experiment with different hair product to achieve your desired style

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircut numbers:

What is the shortest haircut number?

The shortest haircut number is 0, which leaves about 1/16 of an inch of hair.

What is the longest haircut number?

The longest haircut number is 8, which leaves about 1 inch of hair.

What haircut number is a buzz cut?

A buzz cut is typically a #1 or #2 haircut.

Hair Care Tips

After getting the right haircut number, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use hair products that are appropriate for your hair type

Remember, choosing the right haircut number is all about understanding your face shape, hair texture, and desired style. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things to find the perfect haircut for you.
