How To Spike Short Hair African American A Step By Step Guide

If you're an African American with short hair, you may be wondering how to style it in a way that's trendy and modern. Spiking short hair is a great option that can give you a bold and edgy look. In this guide, we'll show you how to spike short hair African American style, step by step.

  • How to Spike Short Hair African American
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Spike Short Hair African American

Before we dive into the step-by-step process, let's first discuss what you'll need to spike your short hair. Here are some essential tools:

  • Hair gel or pomade
  • A comb
  • A hair dryer
  • Hairspray

Now that you have your tools, let's get started!

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is damp, it will be more difficult to spike and may not hold its shape.

Step 2: Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your fingers and rub it into your hair, starting at the roots and working your way up to the tips.

Step 3: Use a comb to create the desired shape of your spikes. You can create spikes that are uniform in size or vary in length for a more textured look.

Step 4: Use a hair dryer on low heat to dry your hair while shaping your spikes with your fingers. This will help set the gel and create a strong hold.

Step 5: Apply a light mist of hairspray over your spikes to help them maintain their shape throughout the day.

Tips for Spiking Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect spiked look:

  • Use a strong-hold hair gel or pomade to ensure that your spikes stay in place.
  • Experiment with different spike lengths and shapes to find the look that best suits you.
  • If your hair is too short to spike, consider using hair extensions or a hairpiece to add length.
  • Don't be afraid to use a little hairspray to keep your spikes in place throughout the day.

FAQs

Q: Can I spike my hair if it's relaxed?

A: Yes, you can still spike your hair if it's relaxed. Just be sure to use a hair gel or pomade that's appropriate for your hair type.

Q: How long will my spikes last?

A: The length of time your spikes will last depends on the strength of the hair gel or pomade you use and your hair type. Generally, you can expect your spikes to last all day.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can lead to dryness and damage.

Now that you know how to spike short hair African American style, it's time to give it a try! With a little practice and the right products, you can achieve a bold and edgy look that's sure to turn heads.
