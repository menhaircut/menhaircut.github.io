Best Men s Shampoo For Thick Hair Uk

Written by Christine Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how difficult it can be to find the right shampoo. Many shampoos can leave your hair feeling heavy and greasy, making it difficult to style. However, there are many shampoos on the market specifically designed for thick hair that can help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we will discuss the best men's shampoo for thick hair in the UK.

What to Look for in a Shampoo for Thick Hair

Before we dive into the best shampoos for thick hair, it's important to understand what to look for when choosing a shampoo. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Ingredients

Look for shampoos that contain natural ingredients like tea tree oil, peppermint, and eucalyptus. These ingredients can help to stimulate the scalp and promote hair growth.

2. Moisturizing Properties

Thick hair can often be dry and prone to breakage. Look for shampoos that contain moisturizing properties like shea butter and coconut oil to help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

3. Strength

Thick hair can be strong and resistant to damage, but it can also be difficult to manage. Look for shampoos that are designed to strengthen and protect your hair.

Top Men's Shampoos for Thick Hair in the UK

Here are five of the best men's shampoos for thick hair in the UK:

1. American Crew Daily Shampoo

American Crew is a well-known brand in the men's grooming industry, and their daily shampoo is perfect for thick hair. This shampoo contains natural ingredients like rosemary and thyme to help stimulate hair growth, while also strengthening and moisturizing your hair.

2. Redken Brews Thickening Shampoo

Redken Brews Thickening Shampoo is designed specifically for men with fine or thinning hair. However, it can also be used on thick hair to add volume and thickness. This shampoo contains protein and glycerin to help strengthen and moisturize your hair.

3. Kiehl's Damage Repairing & Rehydrating Shampoo

Kiehl's Damage Repairing & Rehydrating Shampoo is perfect for men with thick, dry hair. This shampoo contains argan oil to help moisturize and strengthen your hair, while also repairing damage and reducing frizz.

4. Baxter of California Daily Fortifying Shampoo

Baxter of California Daily Fortifying Shampoo is designed to strengthen and protect your hair from damage. This shampoo contains coconut extract to help moisturize and nourish your hair, while also adding volume and thickness.

5. Jack Black True Volume Thickening Shampoo

Jack Black True Volume Thickening Shampoo is perfect for men with thinning hair. This shampoo contains natural ingredients like basil leaf and white lupine to help stimulate hair growth, while also strengthening and thickening your hair.

How to Use Shampoo for Thick Hair

Using shampoo for thick hair is simple. Here are a few tips:

1. Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair thoroughly with warm water.

2. Apply Shampoo

Apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp and work it through your hair.

3. Massage Your Scalp

Massage your scalp with your fingertips for a few minutes to help stimulate hair growth.

4. Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove all of the shampoo.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining thick hair:

1. Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to breakage. Try to wash your hair every other day.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help to detangle your hair without causing damage.

3. Use a Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a hair dryer or straightener, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I use any shampoo for thick hair?

No, not all shampoos are created equal. Look for shampoos specifically designed for thick hair.

2. How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.

3. Can I use conditioner with my shampoo?

Yes, using conditioner after shampooing can help to moisturize and detangle your hair.

4. Can I use a shampoo for thinning hair on my thick hair?

Yes, some shampoos for thinning hair can also be used on thick hair to add volume and thickness.

Conclusion

Thick hair can be difficult to manage, but with the right shampoo, it can be easier to style and maintain. Look for shampoos that contain natural ingredients, moisturizing properties, and are designed to strengthen and protect your hair. Follow our tips for maintaining thick hair, and you'll be well on your way to achieving the look you want.
