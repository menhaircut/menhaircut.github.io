Mens High Fade Short Haircut A Trendy Look For Men In 2023

Written by Frank Oct 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that will make you stand out in a crowd, then you should try the mens high fade short haircut. This is a popular hairstyle that has been trending for the past few years and is still going strong in 2023. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, hair care tips, and more.

Description

The mens high fade short haircut is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that features a short length on the sides and back of the head, while the hair on top is left longer. The hair on top is usually styled in a messy or textured way, giving it a relaxed and casual look. The high fade refers to the gradual tapering of the hair from the top to the sides and back, creating a clean and sharp look.

How to Get Mens High Fade Short Haircut

The mens high fade short haircut can be achieved by going to a professional hairstylist or barber. They will be able to use clippers to create the high fade on the sides and back of your head, while leaving the hair on top longer. It's important to bring a picture of the hairstyle you want to your hairstylist or barber to ensure that they understand the look you're going for.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide on how to get the mens high fade short haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to ensure that it's clean and easy to work with.
  2. Go to a professional hairstylist or barber and show them a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  3. Ask for a high fade on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top longer.
  4. Once the haircut is complete, style the hair on top using a pomade or wax to create a messy and textured look.

Tips for Maintaining Mens High Fade Short Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens high fade short haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist or barber regularly to keep the high fade looking sharp and clean.
  • Use a quality pomade or wax to style the hair on top and keep it looking textured and messy.
  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens high fade short haircut:

How long does the mens high fade short haircut last?

The mens high fade short haircut typically lasts for 4-6 weeks, depending on how fast your hair grows.

What face shapes work best with the mens high fade short haircut?

The mens high fade short haircut works well with most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on those with oval or square faces.

Can I style the hair on top in different ways?

Yes, you can style the hair on top in different ways, such as slicking it back, combing it to the side, or leaving it messy and textured.

Hair Care Tips for Mens High Fade Short Haircut

Here are some hair care tips for the mens high fade short haircut:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Overall, the mens high fade short haircut is a trendy and stylish hairstyle that can give you a relaxed and casual look. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve and maintain this popular hairstyle with ease.
