Low Maintenance Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Women With Glasses

Written by Eveline Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and more fragile, and this is especially true for women. If you wear glasses, finding a low maintenance hairstyle can be even more challenging. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll show you some of the best low maintenance hairstyles for 60 year old women with glasses. Let's get started!

Description

Low maintenance hairstyles are perfect for women who don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair. These hairstyles require minimal styling and can be easily maintained with regular trims and occasional color touch-ups. For 60 year old women who wear glasses, it's important to choose a hairstyle that frames your face and complements your glasses. A good hairstyle can help you look younger and more stylish.

How to

The first thing you need to do is choose a hairstyle that works for your face shape and hair type. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to go for a layered bob that adds volume to the crown of your head. If you have thin hair, a short pixie cut with layers can give you the appearance of thicker hair. Once you've chosen your hairstyle, you can follow these steps to maintain it:

Step 1: Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly is important to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. If you have color-treated hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.

Step 2: Use a hair mask once a week

A hair mask can help nourish and strengthen your hair. Apply a hair mask once a week and leave it on for at least 20 minutes before rinsing it off. You can use a store-bought hair mask or make your own using natural ingredients like coconut oil, honey, and avocado.

Step 3: Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims are important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Depending on your hairstyle, you may need to trim your hair every 4-8 weeks. If you have a short hairstyle, you'll need more frequent trims to maintain the shape.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your low maintenance hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • If you have curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls
  • If you have straight hair, use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz or flyaways
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands and clips, to add some variety to your hairstyle

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about low maintenance hairstyles for 60 year old women with glasses:

What are the best low maintenance hairstyles for 60 year old women?

The best low maintenance hairstyles for 60 year old women include short pixie cuts, layered bobs, and shoulder-length hairstyles with layers. These hairstyles require minimal styling and can be easily maintained with regular trims.

What kind of glasses look best with a low maintenance hairstyle?

The best glasses for a low maintenance hairstyle are ones that complement your face shape and hair color. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to choose glasses with angular frames to balance out your face shape. If you have blonde hair, you may want to choose glasses with warm-toned frames to enhance your hair color.

Hair Care

Remember, taking care of your hair is important to keep it healthy and strong. Follow the tips we've outlined in this article and visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your low maintenance hairstyle. With the right care and attention, you can look and feel your best at any age!
