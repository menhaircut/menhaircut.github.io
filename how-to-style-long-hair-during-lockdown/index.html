How To Style Long Hair During Lockdown Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Aug 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

With the ongoing pandemic, many people are still stuck at home, and with that comes the challenge of keeping your long hair looking fabulous. Whether you are working from home, attending virtual meetings, or simply lounging around the house, you can still keep your locks looking stylish and healthy. In this blog post, we will be sharing some tips and tricks on how to style long hair during lockdown.

How to

The first step in styling long hair during lockdown is to keep it healthy. This means washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a good quality shampoo and conditioner. You may also consider using hair masks or treatments to keep your hair moisturized and nourished. Once your hair is healthy, you can start experimenting with different hairstyles.

Step by Step

1. Braids: Braids are a great way to add some variety to your hair. You can try different types of braids such as French braids, fishtail braids, or Dutch braids. You can also create a braided crown or a braided bun.

2. Ponytail: A simple ponytail can also look stylish. You can opt for a sleek high ponytail or a messy low ponytail. You can also add some texture by teasing the hair at the crown or adding some waves to the ponytail.

3. Buns: Buns are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. You can try different types of buns such as a messy bun, a sleek bun, or a braided bun. You can also add some accessories such as a headband or a scrunchie to add some flair to your bun.

4. Half-up, half-down: A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great option for those who want to keep their hair down but also want to avoid having it in their face. You can create a half-up, half-down hairstyle by pulling back the top section of your hair and securing it with a clip or a hair tie.

Tips

1. Experiment with different hairstyles. Don't be afraid to try something new.

2. Use hair accessories such as headbands, scrunchies, or hair clips to add some flair to your hairstyle.

3. Don't forget to use heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools such as a curling iron or a straightener.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair during lockdown?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you sweat or get oily. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every other day or every three days.

Q: How can I keep my hair healthy during lockdown?

A: Regularly washing and conditioning your hair, using hair masks or treatments, and avoiding heat styling tools can help keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Aside from styling your hair, it's also important to take care of your hair during lockdown. Here are some hair care tips:

1. Avoid using hot tools such as hair dryers or straighteners too often. These can damage your hair.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to avoid damaging your hair when brushing.

3. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.

4. Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or a scarf.

In conclusion, styling long hair during lockdown can be a fun and creative way to pass the time. With the right tools and techniques, you can keep your locks looking stylish and healthy. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
