Short Haircuts For Oval Face Girl The Ultimate Guide

Are you an oval face girl looking to switch up your hairstyle? Look no further! We've compiled a list of the best short haircuts for oval face girls, along with tips on how to style and care for your new 'do.

Description

Oval faces are considered to be one of the easiest face shapes to style. This is because of their balanced proportions, making them a great canvas for a variety of hairstyles. Short haircuts are particularly flattering for oval face girls as they highlight the face's symmetry and create a sleek and modern look.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Oval Face

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. For oval face girls, asymmetrical bobs, pixie cuts, and blunt bobs are all great options. Asymmetrical bobs create a playful look while pixie cuts exude confidence and edge. Blunt bobs create a classic and timeless look.

It's also important to consider your hair texture when choosing a short haircut. If you have fine hair, opt for a layered bob or pixie cut to add volume. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or asymmetrical bob will work best to create a sleek and polished look.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob is a great short haircut for oval face girls looking for a playful and edgy look. This haircut is characterized by having one side longer than the other, creating an asymmetrical effect. This style works best for oval face girls with wavy or straight hair.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a daring and bold short haircut that exudes confidence and edge. This style works well for oval face girls with fine hair, as it creates volume and texture. For a softer look, ask your stylist to add layers or choppy ends.

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is a classic and timeless short haircut that works well for oval face girls with thick hair. This style is characterized by having a straight cut that falls just above the shoulders. For a modern twist, ask your stylist to add layers or choppy ends.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Oval Face Girls

Asymmetrical Bob

  1. Start by applying a volumizing mousse to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry hair using a round brush, focusing on lifting the roots for added volume.
  3. Once hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any kinks or waves.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to the longer side of the bob to create a messy, undone look.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place all day.

Pixie Cut

  1. Start by applying a volumizing mousse to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry hair using a round brush, focusing on lifting the roots for added volume.
  3. Once hair is dry, use a texturizing spray to add texture and movement to your hair.
  4. Use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define your layers or choppy ends.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place all day.

Blunt Bob

  1. Start by applying a straightening serum to damp hair.
  2. Blow dry hair using a round brush, focusing on creating a straight and sleek look.
  3. Once hair is dry, use a flat iron to straighten any kinks or waves.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to add shine and prevent frizz.
  5. Finish with a medium hold hairspray to keep your style in place all day.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to maintain your short haircut.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add variety to your style.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider trying out different hair colors, such as highlights or balayage, to add dimension to your short haircut.

FAQ

Can oval face girls pull off a pixie cut?

Yes! Oval face girls have the perfect face shape for a pixie cut. This short haircut highlights the face's symmetry and creates a sleek and modern look.

Will a blunt bob work for oval face girls with fine hair?

A blunt bob can work for oval face girls with fine hair, but it's important to add layers to create volume and texture. Without layers, the haircut can appear flat and lifeless.

How often should I visit my stylist to maintain my short haircut?

Depending on how fast your hair grows, it's recommended to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for trims to maintain your short haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-washing and drying out your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and short haircut ideas, you'll be ready to switch up your style and embrace your oval face! Remember to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and hair colors.
