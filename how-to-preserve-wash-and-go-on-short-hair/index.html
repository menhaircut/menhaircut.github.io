How To Preserve Wash And Go On Short Hair

Written by Christine Jul 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Wash and go hairstyles are a popular choice for those who want to achieve a low-maintenance look. However, preserving this style on short hair can be a challenge. In this article, we will provide tips and tricks on how to preserve your wash and go on short hair.

Description

A wash and go is a hairstyle that involves washing your hair, applying a styling product, and allowing it to air dry. This style works well for those with naturally curly or wavy hair. However, it can be challenging to maintain this style on short hair.

How to Preserve Wash and Go on Short Hair

Preserving your wash and go on short hair requires a few extra steps. Here are some tips to help you maintain your style:

Step 1: Use the right products

Using the right products is essential for preserving your wash and go on short hair. Look for products that are designed for curly or wavy hair, such as curl-enhancing gels or creams. These products will help define your curls and keep them in place.

Step 2: Apply products correctly

When applying products, make sure to distribute them evenly throughout your hair. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.

Step 3: Dry your hair properly

Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. Avoid using a regular hair dryer, as it can cause frizz and damage your curls.

Step 4: Sleep with a satin cap or pillowcase

Sleeping with a satin cap or pillowcase can help preserve your wash and go. Cotton pillowcases can cause friction, which can lead to frizz and breakage.

Step 5: Refresh your curls

Refresh your curls in the morning by spritzing them with water and applying a small amount of product. This will help revive your curls and keep them looking fresh.

Tips to Preserve Wash and Go on Short Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you preserve your wash and go on short hair:

  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Q: How long does a wash and go last?

A: A wash and go can last anywhere from a few days to a week, depending on your hair type and how well you maintain it.

Q: Can I wash my hair every day with a wash and go?

A: It's not recommended to wash your hair every day, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils. Try washing your hair every 2-3 days and refreshing your curls with water and product in between washes.

Hair Care

Preserving your wash and go on short hair requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by wearing a hat or scarf

Conclusion

Preserving your wash and go on short hair requires proper hair care and the right products. Follow these tips and tricks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.
