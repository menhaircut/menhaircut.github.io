Step-by-Step Instructions for Popular Little Boy Haircuts

While itâs always best to leave hair cutting to the professionals, these step-by-step instructions can help you achieve some popular little boy haircuts at home:

Crew Cut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers to buzz the sides and back of the head, starting with a longer guard and working your way down to a shorter one.
  3. For the top of the head, use scissors to cut the hair short and tapered, leaving it slightly longer in the front.
  4. Blend the top and sides with scissors or clippers.
  5. Style with a bit of hair gel if desired.

Side Part

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers to cut the sides and back short, leaving the top longer.
  3. Create a part on one side of the head.
  4. Use scissors to cut the top of the hair to the desired length.
  5. Blend the top and sides with scissors or clippers.
  6. Style by combing the hair to the side with a bit of hair gel.

Undercut

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use clippers to shave the sides and back very short, leaving the top longer.
  3. Cut the top of the hair to the desired length with scissors.
  4. Style with a bit of hair gel or pomade.
