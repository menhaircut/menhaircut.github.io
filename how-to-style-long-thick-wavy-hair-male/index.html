How To Style Long Thick Wavy Hair Male

Written by Oliver Aug 22, 2023 · 5 min read

Long thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. It looks great when it’s styled properly, but it can be difficult to manage. Luckily, there are many ways to style long thick wavy hair male that will leave you looking sharp and stylish. In this blog post, we’ll go through step by step instructions on how to style long thick wavy hair male, offer some tips and answer some common questions.

How to Style Long Thick Wavy Hair Male

The first step to styling long thick wavy hair male is to find the right haircut. A layered haircut will help to reduce the bulk and weight of your hair, making it easier to manage. Once you have the right haircut, you can start styling your hair.

One popular way to style long thick wavy hair male is to use a hair product such as a pomade, wax or gel. These products will help to hold your hair in place and give it a more defined look. To use a hair product, start by applying a small amount to your hands and rubbing it into your hair. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working towards the ends.

Another way to style long thick wavy hair male is to use a hair dryer. Start by towel drying your hair and then use a hair dryer to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go. You can use a round brush to help smooth out your hair and give it more volume.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to style long thick wavy hair male:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb it through with a wide-tooth comb.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair product to your hands and rub it into your hair.
  4. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working towards the ends.
  5. Use a hair dryer to blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go.
  6. Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair and give it more volume.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use a styling product such as a pomade or wax to define your hair.
  8. Finish off your style with a hairspray to help hold your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Long Thick Wavy Hair Male

Here are some tips for styling long thick wavy hair male:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair to help keep your hair healthy and manageable.
  • Get a layered haircut to help reduce the bulk and weight of your hair.
  • Use a hair product such as a pomade, wax or gel to help hold your hair in place.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go to help smooth out your hair and give it more volume.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to help give it more volume.
  • Use a styling product such as a pomade or wax to help define your hair and give it a more polished look.
  • Finish off your style with a hairspray to help hold your hair in place.

FAQs about Styling Long Thick Wavy Hair Male

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long thick wavy hair male:

Q: What kind of haircut is best for long thick wavy hair male?

A: A layered haircut is best for long thick wavy hair male as it will help to reduce the bulk and weight of your hair.

Q: What kind of hair product should I use to style my long thick wavy hair?

A: You can use a hair product such as a pomade, wax or gel to help hold your hair in place and give it a more defined look.

Q: How should I blow dry my long thick wavy hair?

A: Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go to help smooth out your hair and give it more volume. Use a round brush to help give your hair more volume.

Hair Care for Long Thick Wavy Hair Male

Here are some tips for taking care of long thick wavy hair male:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for wavy hair to help keep your hair healthy and manageable.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.

By following these tips and using the right styling products, you can easily style long thick wavy hair male and keep it looking great. With a little bit of effort, you can have a stylish and manageable hairstyle that will turn heads.
