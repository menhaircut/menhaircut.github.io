What Short Hairstyles Look Good On A Round Face

Written by Eveline Aug 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of always having long hair? Do you have a round face and don't know what short hairstyle will look good on you? In this article, we will give you some tips and ideas on what short hairstyles look good on a round face.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face is characterized by having full cheeks and a rounded chin. Short hairstyles can be a great way to accentuate your features and make your face look slimmer. However, not all short hairstyles may be suitable for a round face. It's important to choose a style that will complement your face shape and enhance your natural beauty.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, consider the following:

  • Face shape: Round faces look best with hairstyles that add length and angles to the face.
  • Hair texture: Fine hair can benefit from layers and volume, while thick hair may need to be thinned out.
  • Personal style: Choose a style that reflects your personality and makes you feel confident.

Step by Step

Here are some short hairstyles that look good on a round face:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is perfect for round faces. It is a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling. The cut features short layers that add texture and volume to the hair. To style, simply apply some texturizing spray and tousle with your fingers.

2. Bob with Side-Swept Bangs

A bob with side-swept bangs is a great way to add length and angles to a round face. The style features a short bob with layers that frame the face. The side-swept bangs add asymmetry to the style, making it more flattering for a round face.

3. Shaggy Layers

Shaggy layers can add volume and texture to fine hair. The style features layers that start at the crown and gradually get longer towards the ends. The layers are cut in a choppy, textured style that adds volume and movement to the hair.

Tips

  • Avoid blunt cuts that can make the face appear fuller.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to the hair.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume to the hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.

FAQ

What short hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt cuts or heavy layers around the chin area.

Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

No, a pixie cut can actually make a round face look slimmer by adding height and volume to the top of the head.

Hair Care

When caring for short hair, it's important to keep it healthy and hydrated. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable. Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage the hair. Instead, use a texturizing spray or mousse to style your hair.

In conclusion, there are many short hairstyles that look good on a round face. It's important to choose a style that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a short hairstyle. With the right cut and styling, you can rock a short hairstyle with confidence.
