How To Cut Men s Hair Beginner A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of spending money on haircuts or want to try your hand at cutting men's hair, then you've come to the right place. Cutting men's hair can seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's hair beginner, including tips, FAQ, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparation
  • How to Cut Men's Hair Beginner: Step-by-Step
  • Tips for a Great Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting men's hair, you'll need to gather a few tools:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting Cape or Towel
  • Clip or Hair Tie
  • Spray Bottle with Water
  • Barber's Mirror

Once you have all of these tools, you're ready to begin.

Preparation

Before you start cutting hair, it's important to prepare both the person you'll be cutting and the area you'll be working in. Here are a few steps to follow:

  • Have the person shower and towel-dry their hair.
  • Put on the haircutting cape or drape a towel over their shoulders.
  • Have them sit in a comfortable chair in a well-lit area.
  • Make sure your clippers and scissors are clean and oiled.
  • Brush any tangles out of their hair.

Once you've completed these steps, you're ready to start cutting.

How to Cut Men's Hair Beginner: Step-by-Step

Follow these steps to cut men's hair beginner:

  1. Start with the clippers. Choose the guard size you want for the length of hair you'll be cutting. Begin at the nape of the neck and work your way up, using the clippers to trim the hair to the desired length.
  2. Move to the sides. Using the same guard size, trim the hair on the sides of the head, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length.
  3. Blend the sides and top. Switch to scissors and comb the hair on top of the head straight up. Use the scissors to trim the hair to the desired length, blending it into the hair on the sides.
  4. Cut the bangs. If your client wants bangs, use scissors to cut them to the desired length. Comb them down and trim them straight across.
  5. Trim the neckline. Use the clippers to trim the hair on the back of the neck to the desired length.
  6. Check for any missed spots. Use the comb and scissors to trim any stray hairs or missed spots.
  7. Style the hair. Use a styling product to style the hair and finish the look.

Tips for a Great Haircut

Here are a few tips to keep in mind while cutting men's hair beginner:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and trim it to the desired length.
  • Take your time and go slowly to avoid mistakes.
  • Always cut less rather than more. You can always go back and trim more later.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from all angles.

FAQs

Here are a few frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair beginner:

  • How long should I leave the hair on top of the head?
  • What should I do if I accidentally cut too much hair?
  • Can I cut my own hair?
  • What's the best way to clean my clippers and scissors?

Hair Care Advice

After you've finished cutting men's hair beginner, it's important to take care of the hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Shampoo and condition the hair regularly.
  • Use a styling product to keep the hair in place.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat.
  • Avoid using too much heat from styling tools, as this can damage the hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily cut men's hair beginner from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the steps carefully to achieve a great-looking cut.
