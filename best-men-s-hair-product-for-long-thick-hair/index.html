Best Men s Hair Product For Long Thick Hair

Written by Christine Nov 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long thick hair, finding the right hair product to keep it looking great can be a challenge. With so many products on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your hair type. In this article, we will look at the best men's hair product for long thick hair, how to use it, and some tips for hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use
  • Step by Step
  • Tips for Hair Care
  • FAQs

Description

The best men's hair product for long thick hair is a pomade. Pomades are perfect for men with long thick hair because they provide a strong hold that lasts all day. They are also very versatile and can be used to create a variety of hairstyles.

When choosing a pomade, it is important to look for one that is water-based. This will make it easier to wash out of your hair at the end of the day. It is also a good idea to look for a pomade that is free of harsh chemicals and artificial fragrances.

How to Use

Using a pomade is easy. Simply take a small amount of the product and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, apply it to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Use your fingers to style your hair as desired.

It is important to use the right amount of pomade. Using too much can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down. Using too little may not provide enough hold. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to using pomade for long thick hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  4. Use your fingers to style your hair as desired.
  5. If needed, add more pomade until you achieve the desired hold.

Tips for Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your long thick hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and free of buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hair dryers and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep it looking neat.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause breakage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best men's hair product for long thick hair:

What is the difference between a pomade and a wax?

Pomades are water-based and provide a shiny, slick look. Waxes are oil-based and provide a matte finish. Pomades are easier to wash out, while waxes are more difficult to remove from your hair.

Can I use pomade on wet hair?

No, it is best to use pomade on dry hair. Wet hair will dilute the product and make it less effective.

How often should I use pomade?

It is best to use pomade sparingly, as overuse can make your hair look greasy and weigh it down. Use it only when you need to style your hair.

Can pomade cause hair loss?

No, pomade does not cause hair loss. However, using too much pomade or not washing it out properly can cause buildup and scalp irritation, which may lead to hair loss.

In conclusion, the best men's hair product for long thick hair is a water-based pomade. It provides a strong hold and is easy to wash out of your hair. Use it sparingly and follow our tips for hair care to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
