What Does An Undercut Haircut Look Like A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, the undercut is a great option. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit different hair types and face shapes. So, what does an undercut haircut look like? In this article, we'll give you a complete guide on this popular hairstyle. We'll cover everything from its description, how-to, step-by-step, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care.

Description

An undercut haircut is a style where the hair on the sides and back of the head is shaved or cut very short, while the hair on the top remains long. This creates a contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back. The length of the hair on top can vary depending on the person's preference, but it's usually longer than the sides and back. This style is popular among men and women of all ages and can be customized to suit different styles and personalities.

How To

If you're thinking of getting an undercut haircut, here's how to achieve the look:

Step 1: Choose your style

Before getting an undercut, decide on the style you want. You can choose a classic undercut where the hair on the top is slicked back, a disconnected undercut where there's a clear distinction between the top and sides, or a textured undercut where the hair on top is styled messy or spiky.

Step 2: Decide on the length

The length of the hair on top and the sides will depend on your preference. If you want a more dramatic look, go for a shorter cut on the sides. If you want a more subtle look, keep the sides slightly longer.

Step 3: Visit a professional

It's recommended to visit a professional hairstylist to get an undercut haircut. They can give you advice on the best style and length for your hair type and face shape. They can also ensure that the cut is done correctly and evenly.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once you've got your undercut, you can style your hair on top using hair gel, pomade, or wax. You can slick it back, create a side part, or style it messy or spiky.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting an undercut haircut:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type
  • Visit a professional hairstylist to ensure a clean and even cut
  • Maintain the length of the hair on top to avoid an uneven look
  • Use the right hair products to style your hair

FAQ

Is an undercut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, an undercut can be adapted to suit different hair types, whether it's straight, wavy, or curly. However, it's important to choose a style that suits your hair type and face shape.

Do I need to shave the sides of my head for an undercut?

No, you don't necessarily have to shave the sides of your head for an undercut. You can opt for a shorter cut on the sides instead.

How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain my undercut?

This will depend on how fast your hair grows. Generally, you'll need to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length of the hair on top and keep the sides and back short.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to take care of your hair after getting an undercut:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use conditioner to keep the hair on top soft and shiny
  • Avoid using too much hair product as it can weigh down your hair
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the length and shape of your undercut

Overall, an undercut is a stylish and trendy haircut that can be adapted to suit different styles and personalities. By following our guide, you can achieve the perfect look that's right for you.
