How To Make A Big Forehead Look Good Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a big forehead, you're not alone. Many people feel self-conscious about their forehead size, but there are ways to make it look good. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make a big forehead look good.

Description

Having a big forehead can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you feel about it. Some people embrace their forehead and even style their hair to show it off. Others prefer to hide it with bangs or hairstyles that cover their forehead. Whatever your preference, there are ways to make a big forehead look good.

How to Make a Big Forehead Look Good

1. Experiment with Bangs

Bangs are a great way to make a big forehead look smaller. You can choose from blunt, side-swept, or wispy bangs, depending on your face shape and hair type. Blunt bangs are ideal for those with straight hair, while side-swept bangs work well for wavy or curly hair. Wispy bangs are a good option for those who want a softer, more natural look.

2. Try a Middle Part

A middle part can help balance out a big forehead. It creates the illusion of a narrower forehead and draws attention to the center of your face. To achieve this look, use a comb to create a straight line down the center of your head. You can also try a deep side part, which will help cover one side of your forehead.

3. Go for a Shorter Hairstyle

A shorter hairstyle can make a big forehead look smaller. A bob, pixie, or lob (long bob) can help balance out your face and draw attention away from your forehead. If you're not ready to go super short, try a shoulder-length cut with layers.

4. Embrace Your Curls

If you have curly hair, embrace it! Curls can help soften the look of a big forehead and add volume to your hair. Try a curly updo or wear your hair in loose, natural curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding a hairstyle that flatters your big forehead is to determine your face shape. Common face shapes include oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your features.

Step 2: Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Try different hairstyles to see what works best for you. Take inspiration from celebrities or ask your hairstylist for advice. Don't be afraid to try something new!

Step 3: Use Hair Accessories

Hair accessories can help draw attention away from your forehead. Try wearing a headband, scarf, or hair clip to add interest to your hairstyle.

Step 4: Practice Good Hair Care

Healthy hair is key to any great hairstyle. Make sure you're using the right shampoo and conditioner for your hair type and use heat protectant when styling with hot tools. Regular haircuts and trims will also keep your hair looking its best.

Tips

- Don't be afraid to try new things!

- Use hair accessories to add interest to your hairstyle.

- Practice good hair care for healthy, beautiful hair.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for a big forehead?

The best hairstyles for a big forehead include bangs, middle parts, shorter hairstyles, and curly hairstyles.

Can I still wear my hair up if I have a big forehead?

Yes! Wearing your hair up can be a great way to show off your forehead and draw attention to your face.

What hair accessories should I use to make my forehead look smaller?

Headbands, scarves, and hair clips can all help draw attention away from your forehead.

Hair Care

Healthy hair is key to any great hairstyle. Make sure you're using the right shampoo and conditioner for your hair type and use heat protectant when styling with hot tools. Regular haircuts and trims will also keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know how to make a big forehead look good, go ahead and experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember, the most important thing is to embrace your unique features and rock them with pride!
