How To Make Guys Long Hair Look Good A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair is a popular style among both men and women, but it can be challenging to maintain and style. If you're a guy with long hair, you might be wondering how to make it look good without resorting to a simple ponytail or messy bun. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to make your long hair look great, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

How to Make Guys Long Hair Look Good

The key to making guys long hair look good is to find a style that works for you and your hair type. Here are some popular options:

1. The Man Bun

The man bun is a classic style for guys with long hair. To achieve this look, gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head, then twist the hair around itself to create a bun. Secure with a hair tie or bobby pins. This style is great for a more casual or relaxed look.

2. The Half-Up, Half-Down Look

If you don't want to commit to a full-on bun, try the half-up, half-down look. To achieve this style, gather the top half of your hair (from your temples up) and secure it with a hair tie at the back of your head. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose. This look is great for a more polished appearance.

3. The Braided Look

Braids are a great option for guys with long hair. You can try a single braid down the back or multiple braids throughout your hair. This style is great for a more bohemian or festival-inspired look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have some style options, let's go through a step-by-step guide on how to make your long hair look good:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to making your long hair look good is to wash it regularly. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your locks clean and healthy. Avoid overwashing, as this can strip your hair of natural oils.

Step 2: Apply Product

Once your hair is clean and dry, apply a styling product to help hold your style in place. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel, depending on your preference and hair type.

Step 3: Choose Your Style

Decide on the style you want to achieve, whether it's a man bun, half-up, half-down look, or braids.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Gently comb or brush your hair into the desired style, using your fingers to shape it as needed. Use a hair tie or bobby pins to secure the style in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To help hold your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray or texturizing spray.

Tips for Styling Long Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling long hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When brushing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid pulling or damaging your locks.

2. Experiment with Different Products

Try out different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.

3. Don't Overdo It with Heat

Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, as this can lead to damage and breakage. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

FAQs

1. How Often Should I Wash My Long Hair?

You should aim to wash your long hair every 2-3 days, depending on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets.

2. Can I Still Style My Long Hair if I Have Thinning Hair?

Absolutely! You can still style your long hair even if you have thinning hair. Consider using volumizing products, such as a thickening shampoo or mousse, to help create the illusion of fuller hair.

3. How Do I Prevent Split Ends?

To prevent split ends, avoid using excessive heat on your hair, and get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care Advice for Long Hair

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your long locks healthy and shiny:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are gentle and nourishing, to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Once a week, use a deep conditioning hair mask to help nourish and hydrate your locks.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

4. Get Regular Trims

Get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the step-by-step guide, you can make your guys long hair look good and keep it healthy and shiny. Whether you prefer a man bun, braids, or the half-up, half-down look, there's a style that will work for you.
