Would I Suit Side Bangs A Guide To Choosing The Perfect Fringe

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're considering getting side bangs, you may be wondering if this popular hair trend will suit you. Side bangs can add a touch of glamour, frame your face, and give your hair a fresh new look. However, not everyone can pull off this style.

Description

Side bangs are a type of fringe that falls to one side of your face. They can be short or long, depending on your preference. Side bangs are a versatile style that can be worn with different hair lengths and textures. They can also be styled in different ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly.

How to

If you're wondering "would I suit side bangs?", the best way to find out is to try them out. Here's how:

  1. Consult with your hair stylist. They can advise you on which type of side bangs will suit your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose the right length. Short side bangs will suit round faces, while longer side bangs will suit oblong or oval faces.
  3. Consider your hair texture. If you have curly hair, you may want to opt for longer, layered side bangs that blend in with the rest of your hair.
  4. Experiment with different styles. You can wear your side bangs straight, swept to the side, or pinned back.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting side bangs:

  1. Decide on the length and style of your side bangs.
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into side bangs.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle, starting from the outside and working your way in.
  4. Check the length and adjust as necessary.
  5. Style your side bangs as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting side bangs:

  • Consider your lifestyle. Side bangs require regular trimming and styling, so if you don't have the time or inclination to maintain them, they may not be the right choice for you.
  • Use a good quality hair serum to keep your side bangs looking sleek and shiny.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and lengths until you find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about side bangs:

Q: Will side bangs suit my face shape?

A: Side bangs can suit different face shapes, but it's important to choose the right length and style for your face. Consult with your hair stylist to find out what will suit you best.

Q: How do I style my side bangs?

A: You can style your side bangs straight, swept to the side, or pinned back. Use a good quality hair serum to keep them looking sleek and shiny.

Q: How often do I need to trim my side bangs?

A: Side bangs require regular trimming to keep them looking neat and tidy. How often you need to trim them depends on the length and style, but you should aim to trim them every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your side bangs:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to give your hair a deep conditioning treatment.
  • Trim your side bangs regularly to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to choose the perfect side bangs and how to take care of them, you can confidently rock this trendy hairstyle. So go ahead and book that appointment with your hair stylist – your new look awaits!
