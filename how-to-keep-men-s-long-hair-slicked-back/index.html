How To Keep Men s Long Hair Slicked Back A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair has become increasingly popular among men in recent years. And with that trend comes the desire to style it in different ways. One of the most popular and classic looks is the slicked-back hairstyle. However, keeping long hair slicked back can be a challenge. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to keep men's long hair slicked back, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • How to Keep Men's Long Hair Slicked Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Keep Men's Long Hair Slicked Back

Slicking back long hair requires a bit of effort, but with the right tools and products, it can be achieved easily. Here are the basic steps:

Step One: Shampoo and Condition

The first step in slicking back long hair is to make sure it's clean and conditioned. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for long hair. Avoid using too much conditioner, as it can weigh down your hair and make it harder to style. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it.

Step Two: Apply a Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your damp hair. A pomade or gel works well for slicked-back hairstyles. Rub a small amount of product between your palms and apply it evenly to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. Make sure you use enough product to hold your hair in place, but not so much that it looks greasy or weighed down.

Step Three: Brush Back Your Hair

Once you've applied the styling product, use a brush to slick back your hair. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, brushing your hair in the direction you want it to go. Use a comb to help smooth out any bumps or flyaways.

Step Four: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer can help set your hairstyle and give it a sleek look. Use a round brush to help shape your hair as you blow-dry it. Make sure you use a low heat setting to avoid damaging your hair. Once your hair is dry, you can apply a bit more product if needed.

Tips for Slicking Back Long Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back hairstyle:

  • Use a high-quality styling product designed for your hair type. A pomade or gel works well for most men's hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting to avoid damaging it.
  • Use a round brush to help shape your hair as you blow-dry it.
  • If you have curly hair, use a straightening iron before applying styling product.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • If your hair is thin or fine, use a volumizing product to help give your hair more body and thickness.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping men's long hair slicked back:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. However, if you have particularly oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Q: Can I use a hair wax instead of a pomade or gel?

A: Yes, a hair wax can also work well for slicked-back hairstyles. Just make sure you choose a wax that's designed for your hair type.

Q: How do I keep my hair from falling out of place?

A: Use a strong-hold styling product and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day. You can also use hairspray to help keep your hair in place.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for long hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straightening irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your long hair slicked back and looking great. Remember to choose the right products for your hair type and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
