How To Do Half Up Half Down Space Buns With Short Hair

Written by Luffy Oct 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles and want to try something new? Look no further than half up half down space buns! This style is a fun and unique way to spice up your hair routine, and it's perfect for those with shorter locks. In this article, we'll go over how to achieve this trendy look step by step, as well as provide tips and answer some common questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to
  2. Step by Step
  3. Tips
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to

First, let's go over the basics of this hairstyle. Half up half down space buns involve creating two buns on the top of your head while leaving the rest of your hair down. To achieve this look with short hair, you'll need to use some tricks to add volume and length to your locks.

What You'll Need

  • Brush or comb
  • Hair elastics
  • Hairpins
  • Hairspray
  • Texturizing spray (optional)

Step by Step

Follow these steps to achieve half up half down space buns with short hair:

  1. Start by brushing or combing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. If your hair is fine or lacks volume, spray some texturizing spray on your roots to add texture.
  3. Take a section of hair at the top of your head and divide it into two equal parts.
  4. Tie each section into a small ponytail using hair elastics.
  5. Twist each ponytail around the elastic until it forms a bun, and secure it with hairpins.
  6. Use hairspray to set the buns in place and tame any flyaways.
  7. If desired, you can pull some strands of hair out around your face to frame it.

And voila! You now have a trendy and stylish half up half down space buns with short hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect half up half down space buns with short hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to make them less noticeable.
  • If your hair is too short to create two separate buns, try twisting both sections together into one bun instead.
  • Add some hair accessories such as a hair clip or headband to add some extra flair to your look.
  • If your hair is particularly stubborn or refuses to stay in place, use some hairspray or hair gel to keep it in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about half up half down space buns with short hair:

Can I achieve this look with curly hair?

Yes! Curly hair can add some extra volume and texture to this hairstyle, making it even more fun and unique.

How do I make my buns stay in place?

Using hairpins and hairspray can help to secure your buns in place. If your hair is particularly stubborn or slippery, you can try using some hair gel to help it stay put.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to take good care of it. This includes regularly washing and conditioning your hair, using heat protectant when styling, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. Additionally, using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to nourish and strengthen your locks.

Now that you know how to do half up half down space buns with short hair, it's time to try it out for yourself! With a little practice and some patience, you'll be rocking this trendy hairstyle in no time.
