How To Cut Short Textured Bob For A Trendy Look

Written by Christine Sep 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and want to try something new? A short textured bob might be perfect for you! This chic and trendy hairstyle is perfect for anyone looking to switch up their look. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut short textured bob in a relaxed English language.

How to Cut Short Textured Bob

To achieve the perfect short textured bob, you'll need to follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Preparation

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, clips, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 2: Divide the Hair

Divide the hair into four sections: top, left, right, and back. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Start with the back section. Use the comb to create a vertical part in the center of the head. Hold the hair between two fingers and cut at an angle. Repeat this process for the rest of the back section.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Move onto the side sections. Comb the hair downward and cut at an angle towards the back. Repeat this process on the other side.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move onto the top section. Comb the hair upward and cut at an angle towards the back. This will create layers and texture.

Step 6: Finishing Touches

Once you've finished cutting, use the comb to check for any unevenness. Make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Short Textured Bob

To achieve the perfect short textured bob, keep these tips in mind:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use the point-cutting technique to add texture and soften the edges.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid mistakes.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a short textured bob:

Is a short textured bob suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a short textured bob can work for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

How often should I trim a short textured bob?

We recommend trimming your short textured bob every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care for Short Textured Bob

To keep your short textured bob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Visit a professional stylist for regular trims and maintenance.

Conclusion

A short textured bob is a trendy and chic hairstyle that can be achieved with the right tools and techniques. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve the perfect short textured bob for your hair type. Remember to take care of your hair with proper hair care and maintenance to keep it looking its best.
