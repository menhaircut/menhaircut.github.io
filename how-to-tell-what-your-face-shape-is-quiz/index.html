If you've ever wondered what your face shape is, then you're in luck! Here are some tips and tricks to help you figure out what your face shape is, and how to style your hair accordingly.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. To determine your face shape, you can take a quiz or measure your face. A quiz can help you narrow down your face shape, but measuring your face is the most accurate method.

Measuring Your Face

To measure your face, you'll need a tape measure and a mirror. Here are the steps:

  1. Measure your forehead from the widest point.
  2. Measure your cheekbones from the widest point.
  3. Measure your jawline from the widest point.
  4. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.

After measuring your face, you can compare your measurements to the following guidelines:

  • Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement. The forehead and jawline are also rounded.
  • Oval: The face length is greater than the width of the cheekbones, and the forehead is wider than the jawline.
  • Square: All measurements are fairly similar. The jawline is sharp and angled.
  • Heart: The forehead is wider than the cheekbones, and the chin is pointed.
  • Diamond: The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the chin is pointed.
  • Triangle: The jawline is wider than the forehead, and the chin is pointed.

Step by Step Guide

If you prefer a quiz to determine your face shape, here's a step by step guide:

  1. Take a photo of your face straight on, with your hair pulled back.
  2. Print out the photo.
  3. Draw a line from the top of your forehead to the tip of your chin.
  4. Draw a line from the widest point of your cheekbones to the other widest point.
  5. Draw a line from the bottom of your nose to the tip of your chin.
  6. Compare your drawing to the face shape descriptions to determine your face shape.

Tips for Styling Your Hair According to Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use these tips to style your hair:

  • Round: Long, layered hair can help elongate the face. Avoid adding volume to the sides.
  • Oval: Most hairstyles look good on an oval face shape. Play around with different lengths and styles.
  • Square: Soften the angles of the face with wavy or curly hair. Avoid straight, blunt cuts.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs can balance out a pointed chin. Avoid volume at the top of the head.
  • Diamond: Short hairstyles can accentuate the cheekbones. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs.
  • Triangle: Bob hairstyles can balance out a wider jawline. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides.

FAQs

What if my face shape is a combination of two shapes?

It's possible to have a combination of two face shapes. In this case, you can use the tips for both shapes to find a hairstyle that works for you.

Can I change my face shape with makeup?

You can use makeup to contour your face and create the illusion of a different shape. However, your actual face shape cannot be changed with makeup.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Regardless of your face shape, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on the hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.

Now that you know how to determine your face shape, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features. Whether you have a round, square, or heart-shaped face, there's a hairstyle out there that's perfect for you!
