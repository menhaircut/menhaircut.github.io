The Best Shampoo For Thick Wavy Hair In The Uk A Complete Guide

Written by William Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know that finding the right shampoo can be a daunting task. With so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the one that suits your hair type the best. In this article, we have curated a list of the best shampoos for thick wavy hair available in the UK, along with some tips and tricks to help you maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

Why is it important to use the right shampoo for thick wavy hair?

Thick wavy hair requires special care as it tends to be more prone to frizz, dryness, and tangling. Using the right shampoo can help you keep your hair moisturized, nourished, and manageable. A good shampoo can also help you maintain the natural texture and shine of your hair while protecting it from damage caused by heat styling, pollution, and other environmental factors.

How to choose the right shampoo for thick wavy hair?

When choosing a shampoo for thick wavy hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for shampoos that are specifically formulated for thick, wavy hair. These shampoos usually contain ingredients that help to nourish and hydrate your hair, such as coconut oil, shea butter, and argan oil.
  • Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz.
  • Consider your hair texture and concerns. If you have dry hair, look for shampoos that are moisturizing and hydrating. If you have oily hair, look for shampoos that are lightweight and clarifying.
  • Read reviews and recommendations from other people with similar hair types to get an idea of what works best.

1. Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl & Shine Shampoo

This shampoo is specially formulated to moisturize and define curls while reducing frizz and flyaways. It contains coconut oil, hibiscus flower extract, and silk protein to nourish and strengthen your hair.

2. OGX Argan Oil of Morocco Shampoo

This shampoo is infused with argan oil, which is known for its moisturizing and nourishing properties. It helps to tame frizz, add shine, and protect your hair from heat damage.

3. L'Oreal Paris Elvive Dream Lengths Restoring Shampoo

This shampoo is designed to help you achieve longer, stronger, and healthier hair. It contains a blend of castor oil, vitamins B3 and B5, and amino acids to nourish and repair your hair.

4. Aveda Be Curly Shampoo

This shampoo is perfect for those with curly or wavy hair. It contains wheat protein and organic aloe to enhance curl definition and reduce frizz.

5. TRESemme Botanique Nourish & Replenish Shampoo

This shampoo is formulated with a blend of coconut milk and aloe vera to nourish and hydrate your hair. It helps to tame frizz and leave your hair looking and feeling soft and smooth.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy and Beautiful Thick Wavy Hair

Using the right shampoo is just one part of maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some additional tips:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and choose a lower heat setting.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best shampoo for thick wavy hair?

The best shampoo for thick wavy hair depends on your hair type and concerns. Look for shampoos that are specifically formulated for thick, wavy hair and contain nourishing and hydrating ingredients.

How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?

It is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz.

Can I use conditioner after shampooing my thick wavy hair?

Yes, using a conditioner after shampooing can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, making it more manageable and less prone to frizz.

Conclusion

Choosing the right shampoo for thick wavy hair is crucial for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. By following our tips and using one of the top 5 shampoos we recommended, you can keep your hair moisturized, nourished, and manageable. Remember to also take care of your hair by avoiding heat styling tools, using a wide-tooth comb, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
