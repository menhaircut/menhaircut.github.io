Hairstyles To Hide Thin Hair On Top

Written by Bardi Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly worrying about your thin hair on top? Don't fret, there are many hairstyles that can help you conceal your hair loss and give you the confidence you deserve. In this article, we will discuss some of the best hairstyles to hide thin hair on top, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

Thinning hair on top is a common problem that affects both men and women. While there are many causes of hair loss, including genetics, stress, and hormonal changes, there are several hairstyles that can help to disguise thinning hair. These hairstyles work by adding volume and texture to the hair, making it appear fuller and thicker.

How to

When it comes to hiding thin hair on top, there are several hairstyles that can be considered. The key is to choose a style that works with your hair type and face shape. Some popular options include:

1. Short Haircuts

A short haircut can be a great option for those with thin hair on top. A pixie cut or a bob can add volume to the hair, making it appear fuller and thicker. Be sure to ask for layers to add texture and movement to the hair.

2. Layered Haircuts

A layered haircut can also be a great option for those with thin hair on top. Layers can add volume and texture to the hair, making it appear fuller and thicker. Be sure to ask for long layers that start at the cheekbones or collarbone.

3. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can be a great option for those with thin hair on top. The bangs can help to frame the face and add volume to the hair. Be sure to ask for long, side swept bangs that start at the crown of the head.

4. Updos

An updo can be a great option for those with thin hair on top. A messy bun or a braided updo can add volume and texture to the hair, making it appear fuller and thicker.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair to hide thin hair on top:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume and texture to the hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Lift the hair at the roots as you blow dry to add volume.

3. Style Your Hair

Choose one of the hairstyles mentioned above and style your hair accordingly. Be sure to add volume and texture to the hair to make it appear fuller and thicker.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you hide thin hair on top:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down the hair.
  • Consider using a hair topper or wig if you are experiencing significant hair loss.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage the hair and make it appear thinner.

FAQ

Q: Can thin hair on top be reversed?

A: While there is no cure for thinning hair, there are several treatments that can help to slow down or stop hair loss, including medications and hair transplant surgery.

Q: How can I make my hair grow thicker?

A: Maintaining a healthy diet and lifestyle can help to promote hair growth and thickness. Additionally, using a hair growth shampoo and taking supplements such as biotin and collagen can also help to promote hair growth.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for thin hair on top, it's important to be gentle and avoid damaging the hair. Here are some tips on how to care for your thinning hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for thinning hair.
  • Avoid brushing or combing the hair when wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or clips, as they can pull on the hair and cause breakage.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals such as bleach or hair dye, as they can damage the hair.

In conclusion, there are many hairstyles that can help to conceal thin hair on top. Whether you opt for a short haircut, layered haircut, side swept bangs, or an updo, the key is to add volume and texture to the hair to make it appear fuller and thicker. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve a beautiful and confident hairstyle that makes you feel your best.
