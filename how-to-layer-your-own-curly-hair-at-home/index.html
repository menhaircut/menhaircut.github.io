How To Layer Your Own Curly Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Layering your curly hair can add volume, shape, and bounce to your locks. While getting a professional haircut is always an option, you can also layer your own curly hair at home. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of layering your curly hair, along with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect layered look.

How to Layer Your Own Curly Hair at Home

Layering curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it at home. Here’s how:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start layering your curly hair, you’ll need to gather some tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Hair ties

Make sure your scissors are sharp and clean. You don’t want to cut your hair with dull or dirty scissors.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a curly hair-friendly shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Once you’ve washed and conditioned your hair, gently towel dry it or use a diffuser to dry it.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair ties or clips to secure each section.

Step 5: Start Layering

Start with the back sections of your hair. Take a small section of your hair and twist it tightly. Hold the twisted section of hair between your fingers and cut the ends with your scissors.

Repeat this process with the rest of the back sections, making sure to cut each section to the same length.

Once you’ve finished the back sections, move on to the front sections.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you’ve finished layering your hair, remove the hair ties or clips and shake your hair to blend the layers. You can also use your fingers to gently blend the layers.

Tips for Layering Your Curly Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you layer your curly hair at home:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Twist each section tightly before cutting to ensure a more even cut.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while layering.
  • Don’t cut your hair when it’s wet. Curly hair tends to shrink when it dries, so you may end up cutting more than you intended.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about layering curly hair at home:

Can I layer my curly hair if it’s short?

Yes, you can layer your curly hair no matter the length. However, the shorter your hair, the more difficult it may be to layer it yourself.

How often should I layer my curly hair?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you want to change your look. Generally, it’s recommended to layer your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry.

Layering your curly hair at home can be a fun and rewarding experience. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the tips and techniques outlined in this article. With a little practice and patience, you’ll be able to achieve the perfect layered look for your curly hair.
