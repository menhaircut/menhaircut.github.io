What is DHT?

DHT, or dihydrotestosterone, is a hormone that is produced in the body when testosterone is converted by the enzyme 5-alpha-reductase. DHT is responsible for the development of male sex characteristics, but it can also bind to hair follicles and cause them to shrink, leading to hair loss. DHT hair loss is most common in men, but women can also experience it.

How does DHT hair loss shampoo work?

DHT hair loss shampoos work by blocking the production of DHT or by removing it from the scalp. These shampoos typically contain ingredients like ketoconazole, saw palmetto, and pyrithione zinc, which have been shown to block DHT production or remove it from the scalp. By using a DHT hair loss shampoo, you can help prevent DHT from binding to your hair follicles and causing hair loss.

How to use DHT hair loss shampoo

Using DHT hair loss shampoo is simple. Wet your hair and scalp, then apply the shampoo and massage it into your scalp for a few minutes. Rinse thoroughly and repeat if necessary. Can DHT hair loss shampoo regrow hair?

DHT hair loss shampoo can help prevent further hair loss, but it may not be able to regrow hair that has already been lost.
