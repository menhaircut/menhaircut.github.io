Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips that suit your face shape:

Oval Face Shape

Women with oval face shapes can rock any type of hairstyle. However, if you want to accentuate your face shape, go for a layered haircut that frames your face.

Round Face Shape

Women with round face shapes should go for hairstyles that add height and length to their face. Long layered haircuts, side-swept bangs, and pixie cuts with volume on top are some great options.

Square Face Shape

Women with square face shapes should go for hairstyles that soften their angular features. Soft waves, layered haircuts, and side-swept bangs are some great options.

Heart Face Shape

Women with heart face shapes should go for hairstyles that balance their forehead and chin. Side-swept bangs, long layers, and textured bobs are some great options.

Diamond Face Shape

Women with diamond face shapes should go for hairstyles that balance their cheekbones and jawline. Side-swept bangs, long layers, and chin-length bobs are some great options.
