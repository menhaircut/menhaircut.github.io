What Hair Length Is Best For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Luffy Nov 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Heart shaped faces have a wider forehead and a pointed chin, which makes it essential to choose the right hairstyle to balance out the facial features. The right hair length can accentuate the cheekbones and create symmetry. In this post, we will guide you on what hair length is best for heart shaped face.

Description

The heart-shaped face is wider at the forehead and temples and narrows down to a pointed chin. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jawline is usually sharp. The aim of choosing the right hair length is to create balance and symmetry in the face shape.

How to Choose the Right Hair Length for Heart Shaped Face?

Choosing the right hair length for a heart-shaped face depends on the individual's facial features, lifestyle, and personal preference. Here are some tips to consider when choosing the right hair length:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Identify the shape of your face.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hair length for your face shape.
  3. Consider your lifestyle and maintenance level when choosing a hair length.
  4. Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find the one that suits you the best.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Length for Heart Shaped Face

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair length for heart-shaped face:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the lower half of the face.
  • Consider hairstyles that create width at the jawline.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head.
  • Consider layers that start at the chin level to add dimension to the face.
  • Consider a side parting to create balance in the face shape.

FAQ

What are the best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces?

The best hairstyles for heart-shaped faces are those that add volume to the lower half of the face and create width at the jawline. Some popular hairstyles include long layers, side-swept bangs, and shoulder-length hair.

Can heart-shaped faces pull off short hair?

Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off short hair, but it is essential to choose the right length and style. Short hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head should be avoided, and instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of the face.

What should heart-shaped faces avoid?

Heart-shaped faces should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the crown of the head as it can make the forehead appear wider. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short as it can highlight the pointed chin.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Face

Regardless of the hair length, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

The right hair length for heart-shaped faces can create balance and symmetry in the facial features. Consider your lifestyle, maintenance level, and facial features when choosing the right hair length. Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find the one that suits you the best. Take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance.
