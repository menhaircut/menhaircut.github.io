How To Curl Your Hair With Foam Curlers

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to curl your hair? Foam curlers are a great alternative for achieving bouncy curls without damaging your hair. In this article, we will discuss how to curl your hair with foam curlers, step by step. We will also include tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve perfect curls every time.

How to

Before starting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Here are the steps to curl your hair with foam curlers:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into small sections. The smaller the sections, the tighter the curls. Use hair clips to keep the sections in place.

Step 2: Prepare Your Foam Curlers

Take your foam curlers and separate them into two equal halves. Make sure the sticky sides are facing each other. Spray some water on the foam curlers to make them slightly damp.

Step 3: Wrap Your Hair Around the Curlers

Take one section of your hair and wrap it around the foam curler. Start from the ends and roll the curler towards your scalp. Once you reach your scalp, secure the curler by bending the ends together. Repeat this process for all your hair sections.

Step 4: Wait for Your Hair to Dry

Leave the foam curlers in your hair until your hair is completely dry. You can use a hair dryer to speed up the process, but make sure to use a low heat setting. Alternatively, you can sleep with the foam curlers in your hair and remove them in the morning.

Step 5: Remove the Foam Curlers

Once your hair is dry, carefully remove the foam curlers by gently pulling them out. Avoid pulling too hard as this can cause your curls to fall out.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use smaller foam curlers for tighter curls and larger foam curlers for looser curls.
  • Spray some hairspray on each section before wrapping it around the foam curler to help the curls hold better.
  • For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions.
  • If you have thin hair, use fewer foam curlers to avoid weighing down your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about foam curlers:

Q: Can I use foam curlers on wet hair?

A: It is best to use foam curlers on dry hair as wet hair can cause the curls to fall out.

Q: How long should I leave the foam curlers in my hair?

A: It depends on how thick your hair is and how tight you want your curls to be. Generally, leaving the foam curlers in your hair for 2-3 hours should be enough.

Q: How can I make my curls last longer?

A: Use hairspray to set your curls and avoid touching your hair too much. You can also sleep with a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz.

Hair Care

Using foam curlers is a great way to curl your hair without damaging it with heat. However, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as bleach or hair dye.

Now that you know how to curl your hair with foam curlers, you can achieve beautiful curls without damaging your hair. Follow these tips and hair care advice to keep your hair healthy and strong.
