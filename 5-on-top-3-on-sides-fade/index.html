5 On Top 3 On Sides Fade A Guide To This Classic Men s Haircut

If you're looking for a classic men's haircut that never goes out of style, the 5 on top 3 on sides fade is a great option. This haircut is versatile and looks good on almost everyone. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the 5 on top 3 on sides fade, including how to get it, tips for styling it, and how to take care of your hair afterwards.

Description

The 5 on top 3 on sides fade is a classic men's haircut that features short sides and back that gradually fade into longer hair on top. The "5 on top" refers to the length of hair on the crown of the head, which is usually around 1/2 inch to 1 inch long. The "3 on sides" refers to the length of hair on the sides and back of the head, which is usually around 1/4 inch to 3/8 inch long. The fade is the gradual transition between these two lengths of hair.

How to Get a 5 on Top 3 on Sides Fade

If you're interested in getting a 5 on top 3 on sides fade, the first step is to find a good barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. You can ask for recommendations from friends or family members, or search online for barbers in your area who specialize in men's haircuts.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you've found a barber, here's a step-by-step guide to getting a 5 on top 3 on sides fade:

  1. Start by washing your hair to remove any dirt or product buildup.
  2. Sit in the barber chair and explain to your barber that you want a 5 on top 3 on sides fade.
  3. Your barber will start by cutting the sides and back of your hair to the desired length (usually around 1/4 inch to 3/8 inch).
  4. Next, your barber will gradually blend the hair on the sides and back into the longer hair on top.
  5. Finally, your barber will trim the hair on top to the desired length (usually around 1/2 inch to 1 inch) and style it as desired.

Tips for Styling a 5 on Top 3 on Sides Fade

Once you've got your 5 on top 3 on sides fade, here are some tips for styling it:

  • Use a high-quality styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to hold your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your fade.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 5 on top 3 on sides fade:

  • Is the 5 on top 3 on sides fade a good choice for all hair types?
  • Can you style a 5 on top 3 on sides fade with curly hair?
  • How often should you get a 5 on top 3 on sides fade?
  • What type of styling product should you use for a 5 on top 3 on sides fade?

Hair Care Tips for a 5 on Top 3 on Sides Fade

To keep your 5 on top 3 on sides fade looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as from a hair dryer or straightener.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

Now that you know everything there is to know about the 5 on top 3 on sides fade, it's time to book your appointment with a skilled barber or stylist and try out this classic men's haircut for yourself.
