Medium Length Hairstyles For Thick Curly Hair Over 50

Written by William Aug 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 50 with thick curly hair? Do you struggle to find the perfect hairstyle that suits you? Look no further because we have the solution for you. In this article, we will discuss the best medium length hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50. Let's get started!

Description

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and loses its natural shine. However, with the right hairstyle, you can still look beautiful and confident. Medium length hairstyles are perfect for women over 50 as they are easy to maintain and style. If you have thick curly hair, then you have a lot of options to choose from. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect medium length hairstyle for thick curly hair over 50 is to consider your face shape. If you have a round face, then opt for layered hairstyles that frame your face. If you have a square face, then go for a side-swept fringe to soften your features. If you have an oval face, then you can experiment with different styles.

Next, consider your hair texture. If you have thick curly hair, then you can go for a layered bob or a shoulder-length cut with long layers. This will add volume and definition to your curls. If you want to keep your hair long, then go for a layered cut that will add movement to your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to tame frizz and define your curls.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated and nourished.

Step 3: Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Step 4: Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.

Step 5: Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define your curls and add volume.

Tips

- Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools on your hair.

- Avoid using a brush on your curly hair as it can cause frizz and damage.

- Try different hair accessories such as headbands, clips, and scrunchies to add some variety to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have thick curly hair over 50?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair long. Just make sure to get regular trims and opt for a layered cut to add movement and definition to your hair.

Q: What products should I use to style my thick curly hair?

A: Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair such as leave-in conditioners, curl enhancing creams, and styling gels.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.

- Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid frizz and breakage.

- Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day as it can cause damage and breakage.

There you have it, the best medium length hairstyles for thick curly hair over 50. Remember to consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a hairstyle. With these tips, you can rock any hairstyle with confidence.
