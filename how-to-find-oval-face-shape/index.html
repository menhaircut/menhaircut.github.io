How To Find Your Oval Face Shape A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you unsure about your face shape? Figuring out your face shape is important to determine the right hairstyle, makeup, and accessories that flatter your features. One of the most common face shapes is oval, which is characterized by balanced proportions and softly rounded edges. In this article, we’ll show you how to find your oval face shape and give you some tips on how to enhance your natural beauty.

Table of Contents

  • Description of Oval Face Shape
  • How to Find Your Oval Face Shape
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Oval Face Shape
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

Description of Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with a gently curved jawline and a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the face tapers down to a narrow chin. This face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off most hairstyles and makeup looks. It’s also the most common face shape among celebrities, such as Beyoncé, Rihanna, and Angelina Jolie.

How to Find Your Oval Face Shape

There are a few ways to determine your face shape, but the easiest method is to measure your face with a tape measure or ruler. Here’s how:

Step by Step Guide

  • Measure the width of your forehead at the widest point, which is usually just above the eyebrows.
  • Measure the width of your cheekbones at the widest point, which is usually across the apples of your cheeks.
  • Measure the width of your jawline at the widest point, which is usually at the base of your jawbone.
  • Measure the length of your face from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.
  • Compare your measurements to determine your face shape. If your cheekbone measurement is the widest and your face tapers down to a narrow chin, you have an oval face shape.

Another way to determine your face shape is to take a selfie and trace the outline of your face with a marker or lipstick. Step back and look at the shape you’ve traced. If it’s longer than it is wide, with softly rounded edges, you have an oval face shape.

Tips for Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you’re in luck! This face shape is the most versatile and can pull off most hairstyles and makeup looks. Here are some tips to enhance your natural beauty:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a pixie cut, a bob, or long layers.
  • Try different bang styles, such as side-swept or blunt.
  • Highlight your cheekbones with a blush or bronzer.
  • Play up your eyes with bold eyeshadow or eyeliner.
  • Wear statement earrings to draw attention to your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

What other face shapes are there?

Other common face shapes include round, square, heart, and diamond.

Can face shape change over time?

While your bone structure won’t change, your face shape can appear different depending on factors such as weight gain or loss, aging, and hairstyle.

Do I need to follow beauty rules based on my face shape?

No, these tips are just guidelines to help you enhance your natural features. Feel free to experiment and have fun with your look!

Hair Care for Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you can pull off most hairstyles. However, it’s important to choose a style that flatters your features and complements your personality. Here are some hair care tips for oval face shape:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head, such as a layered cut or a side part.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or too voluminous, as they can make your face appear longer.
  • If you have curly hair, embrace your natural texture with a layered cut that frames your face.
  • If you have straight hair, add some texture with a choppy cut or beachy waves.
  • Don’t be afraid to try different hair colors, such as highlights or ombre, to add dimension to your hair.

Now that you know how to find your oval face shape, you can confidently choose hairstyles, makeup, and accessories that enhance your natural beauty. Remember, these tips are just guidelines – feel free to experiment and have fun with your look!
