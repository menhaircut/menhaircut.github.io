Short Hair For Heart Shaped Face 2021 Perfect Styles For You

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, then you’ll know how challenging it can be to choose the right hairstyle. You want to make sure that your hair looks great, but you also want to make sure that it flatters your face shape. In this blog post, we’ll be talking about short hair for heart shaped face 2021. We’ll be discussing the best styles, tips, and hair care advice to help you look your best.

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a pointed chin. The goal of a good hairstyle for a heart-shaped face is to balance out the features and create a more symmetrical look. Short hair is a great option for heart-shaped faces because it draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones while de-emphasizing the chin.

How To

The first step to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your heart-shaped face is to determine your face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features. Here are some tips for choosing the right short hairstyle for your heart-shaped face:

Step 1: Identify Your Face Shape

The easiest way to identify your face shape is to take a picture of yourself with your hair pulled back. Look at the shape of your face and compare it to the different face shapes (oval, round, square, heart, and diamond). If your face is widest at the temples and tapers down to a pointed chin, then you have a heart-shaped face.

Step 2: Choose Your Length

Short hair is a great option for heart-shaped faces because it can help to balance out the features. However, you still need to choose the right length for your face shape. A chin-length bob or a pixie cut can work well for heart-shaped faces. Avoid anything too short or too long, as this can make your face look unbalanced.

Step 3: Consider Your Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to frame your face and draw attention to your eyes. However, you need to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. For heart-shaped faces, side-swept bangs can be a great option. They help to balance out the width of your forehead and draw attention to your eyes.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for your heart-shaped face:

  • Choose a style with volume at the crown to create balance
  • Avoid styles that are too flat or sleek
  • Avoid styles with heavy layers around the chin area
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a middle part if you have a heart-shaped face. However, it’s usually better to wear your hair with a side part, as this can help to balance out the width of your forehead.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a bob if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: Yes, a bob can be a great option for heart-shaped faces. Just make sure that the length is right for your face shape and that you choose a style with volume at the crown.

Hair Care

Once you’ve chosen your new short hairstyle, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently
  • Apply a heat protectant before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

Short hair for heart shaped face 2021 can be a great way to change up your look and create a more balanced appearance. Just make sure to choose the right length, style, and bangs for your face shape, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
