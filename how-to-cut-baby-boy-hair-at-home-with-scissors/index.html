How To Cut Baby Boy Hair At Home With Scissors

Written by Aprilia Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of taking your baby boy to the salon for haircuts? Not to mention the cost and the hassle of keeping your little one still. With a little bit of practice, you can learn how to cut your baby boy's hair at home. In this article, we'll show you how to cut baby boy hair at home with scissors.

Description

Cutting your baby boy's hair at home can be a bit intimidating. But with the right tools and techniques, you can do it like a pro. In this article, we'll walk you through the process step by step. We'll also give you some tips and tricks to make the process easier and more efficient.

How to Cut Baby Boy Hair at Home with Scissors

Before you start cutting your baby boy's hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. You may also want to have a towel or two handy to catch any stray hairs.

Step 1: Wet Your Baby's Hair

Spray your baby's hair with water to make it easier to cut. This will help the hair lie flat and make it easier to see what you're doing.

Step 2: Comb Your Baby's Hair

Comb your baby's hair to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair at the back of the head. Using your fingers as a guide, cut the hair to the desired length. You can use the comb to hold the hair in place while you cut.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Once you've finished cutting the back, move on to the sides. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it to the same length as the back. Make sure you cut the hair evenly on both sides.

Step 5: Trim the Bangs

If your baby has bangs, trim them last. Use the comb to hold the hair in place and cut it to the desired length. Make sure the bangs are even on both sides.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the process easier and more efficient:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use sharp scissors to make clean cuts.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Take breaks if your baby becomes fussy or restless.

FAQ

How often should I cut my baby boy's hair?

It's recommended to cut your baby's hair every 3-4 months, but this can vary depending on how quickly their hair grows.

What do I do if I make a mistake while cutting my baby's hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your baby's hair, don't panic. It will grow back! You can always take your baby to a professional to fix any mistakes.

Hair Care

After cutting your baby's hair, make sure to wash it with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using any styling products on your baby's hair, as they can be harsh on their delicate skin.

Cutting your baby boy's hair at home with scissors can be a fun and rewarding experience. With a little bit of practice, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. Just remember to take your time and be patient, and your little one will have a fresh new haircut in no time!
