What Length Hair Is Best For A Round Face

Written by Oliver Jun 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but not getting the desired look? Do you have a round face and unsure of what length hair will suit you the best? If yes, then this article is perfect for you. In this article, we will discuss what length hair is best for a round face, how to style it, and tips to maintain it.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A round face has soft curves, and the length of the face is almost equal to the width. The best length hair for a round face is one that adds length to your face and creates an illusion of an oval shape. Medium to long hair length is perfect for a round face as it elongates the face and adds definition to the cheekbones.

How to

The first step in determining the best length hair for a round face is to determine your face shape correctly. You can do this by measuring the width and length of your face. If the measurements are almost equal, then you have a round face. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that suit your face shape.

Step by step

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle or side. A side part will help create a diagonal line, which will elongate your face.
  2. Avoid blunt cuts as they tend to make your face appear rounder. Opt for layers as they add volume and movement to your hair.
  3. Consider getting a side-swept fringe as it creates an asymmetrical look and adds length to your face.
  4. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bob or a pixie cut.
  5. Opt for hairstyles that are longer at the front and shorter at the back, such as a long bob.

Tips

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and create a more defined look.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back as they tend to make your face appear rounder.
  • Accessorize with statement earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from your face shape.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bob or a pixie cut.

What hairstyles should I opt for if I have a round face?

Opt for hairstyles that are longer at the front and shorter at the back, such as a long bob.

Hair Care

It is essential to take care of your hair to maintain the length and style. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair. Avoid using hot tools regularly and use a heat protectant before styling your hair. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the style.

In conclusion, the best length hair for a round face is medium to long as it adds length to your face and creates an illusion of an oval shape. Consider getting layers and a side-swept fringe to add volume and movement to your hair. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bob or a pixie cut. Take care of your hair to maintain the length and style.
