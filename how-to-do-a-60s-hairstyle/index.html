How To Do A 60S Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 25, 2023 · 3 min read

The 60s hairstyle is a classic look that never goes out of style. It's a great way to add a vintage touch to your look, and it's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to do a 60s hairstyle in a few easy steps. Whether you're going to a party or just want to switch up your look for the day, this tutorial will help you achieve the perfect 60s hairstyle.

Description

The 60s hairstyle is a retro look that was popularized in the 1960s. It's characterized by voluminous hair, often styled into bouffants, beehives, or flipped-out ends. This look was popularized by iconic figures such as Brigitte Bardot, Audrey Hepburn, and Jackie Kennedy.

How to

To achieve the perfect 60s hairstyle, you'll need a few tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb
  • Rollers or hot rollers
  • Hairspray
  • Teasing brush
  • Bobby pins

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these easy steps to achieve the perfect 60s hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Comb through your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Use rollers or hot rollers to add volume to your hair. Roll your hair away from your face and towards the back of your head. Use bobby pins to secure the rollers in place.
  3. Spray your hair with hairspray to set the curls.
  4. Remove the rollers and use a teasing brush to tease the hair at the crown of your head. This will add volume and height to your hair.
  5. Gently smooth the top layer of your hair with a comb or brush.
  6. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and twist it backwards. Pin it in place with bobby pins.
  7. Repeat this process with a few more sections of hair, twisting them backwards and pinning them in place.
  8. Spray your hair with hairspray to set the style.
  9. Use a comb or brush to smooth any flyaways or frizz.
  10. Finish with a final spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect 60s hairstyle:

  • Use hot rollers for a longer-lasting curl.
  • Tease your hair gently to avoid damaging it.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them in the hairstyle.
  • Finish with a shine spray for added gloss and shine.

FAQ

How long does a 60s hairstyle take to create?

A 60s hairstyle can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

What hair length is best for a 60s hairstyle?

A 60s hairstyle can be achieved on any hair length, but longer hair will give you more styling options.

What type of hair works best for a 60s hairstyle?

A 60s hairstyle can work on any hair type, but it's best suited for hair with some natural volume.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and strong, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 60s hairstyle in no time. Whether you're going for a retro look or just want to switch up your hairstyle, this tutorial is a great place to start. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
