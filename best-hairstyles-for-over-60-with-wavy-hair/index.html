Best Hairstyles For Over 60 With Wavy Hair

Written by William Nov 15, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're over 60 and have wavy hair, you might be looking for a hairstyle that's easy to maintain and makes you feel confident. Luckily, there are many hairstyles that can suit your hair type and age. In this post, we'll discuss the best hairstyles for over 60 with wavy hair, including how to style them and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Wavy hair can be a great asset, especially if you know how to style it properly. But as you get older, your hair may become thinner and drier, making it harder to find a hairstyle that works for you. The best hairstyles for over 60 with wavy hair should be easy to maintain, enhance your natural texture, and complement your face shape.

How to

The key to finding the best hairstyle for over 60 with wavy hair is to consider your face shape and personal style. Some popular hairstyles for wavy hair include:

Layered Bob

A layered bob is a classic hairstyle that can suit any face shape. This hairstyle works particularly well for people with wavy hair because it enhances the natural texture of your hair. To style a layered bob, apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair and blow-dry your hair using a round brush. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Long Layers

If you prefer longer hair, long layers can be a great option. This hairstyle can add volume and movement to your hair, making it look more youthful. To style long layers, apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair and air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition to your waves.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can be a great way to frame your face and add dimension to your hair. This hairstyle works particularly well for people with wavy hair because it can enhance the natural texture of your hair. To style side-swept bangs, blow-dry your hair using a round brush and sweep your bangs to one side. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Step by step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for styling a layered bob:

  1. Apply a volumizing mousse to damp hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush.
  3. Apply a texturizing spray to add definition to your waves.
  4. Finish with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your hair and keeping it healthy:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Wash your hair every other day or every three days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or hair dye, as this can weaken your hair.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for wavy hair?

The best haircut for wavy hair depends on your face shape and personal style. Some popular haircuts for wavy hair include layered bob, long layers, and side-swept bangs.

How do I style my wavy hair?

To style wavy hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair and air-dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer. Finish with a texturizing spray to add definition to your waves.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important, especially as you get older. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips, you can maintain healthy, beautiful hair that looks great at any age. Remember to choose a hairstyle that works for your face shape and personal style, and don't be afraid to experiment with different looks!
