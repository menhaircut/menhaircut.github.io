How To Get Semi Curly Hair For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to switch up your style? Semi curly hair is a great option for guys who want to add some texture and volume to their hair. In this guide, we’ll show you how to get semi curly hair for guys in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Semi Curly Hair for Guys
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Semi Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Semi Curly Hair

How to Get Semi Curly Hair for Guys

Semi curly hair is achieved by styling your hair in a way that creates loose curls or waves. Here are a few ways you can get semi curly hair:

  • Using a curling iron
  • Using hair rollers
  • Getting a perm
  • Using hair products to create curls

Each method has its own pros and cons, so it’s important to choose the one that works best for your hair type and lifestyle.

Step by Step Guide

If you’re looking to get semi curly hair for guys, follow these simple steps:

1. Shampoo and condition your hair

Clean hair is easier to style and will hold the curls better. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type and rinse thoroughly.

2. Towel dry your hair

Don’t use a hair dryer as it can damage your hair and cause frizz. Instead, gently towel dry your hair until it’s damp.

3. Apply a heat protectant

If you’re using a curling iron or hair rollers, it’s important to apply a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair. Use a spray or cream and distribute evenly throughout your hair.

4. Use a curling iron or hair rollers

If you’re using a curling iron, set it to a low heat setting and wrap small sections of your hair around the barrel. Hold for a few seconds and release. If you’re using hair rollers, roll small sections of your hair onto the rollers and secure with clips. Leave in for at least 20 minutes or until your hair is completely dry.

5. Apply a styling product

Once your hair is dry, apply a styling product to enhance the curls and provide hold. Use a mousse or curl cream and distribute evenly throughout your hair.

6. Finish with hairspray

To keep your semi curly hair in place, finish with a light hold hairspray. Spray evenly throughout your hair and allow it to dry.

Tips for Maintaining Semi Curly Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining semi curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow drying your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry as it can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb when your hair is wet to prevent breakage
  • Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause the curls to become loose
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

Frequently Asked Questions

Can guys get perms?

Yes, guys can get perms. However, it’s important to consult with a professional stylist to determine if a perm is suitable for your hair type and desired style.

How long does semi curly hair last?

Semi curly hair can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on the method used to achieve the curls and the products used to maintain them.

Can I still use hair products with semi curly hair?

Yes, you can still use hair products with semi curly hair. In fact, using the right products can enhance the curls and provide hold.

Hair Care for Semi Curly Hair

Here are a few tips for caring for semi curly hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as it can damage your hair
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your curls
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve and maintain semi curly hair for guys. Experiment with different methods and products to find what works best for you.
