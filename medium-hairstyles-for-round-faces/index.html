Medium Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Christine Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles that don't flatter your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best medium hairstyles for round faces that will enhance your best features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

Description

Round faces have a rounded chin and full cheeks, making it important to choose hairstyles that add length and volume to the face. Medium hairstyles are a great option for round faces as they create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face.

How to

The key to finding the perfect medium hairstyle for your round face is to choose one that creates volume on top and adds length to the face. Here are some tips on how to achieve this:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

Medium hairstyles that fall just below the shoulders or at the collarbone are ideal for round faces as they create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long as they can make the face look rounder.

Step 2: Add Layers

Layered medium hairstyles are perfect for round faces as they add volume on top and create the illusion of a longer face. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that start at the cheekbones and gradually get longer towards the ends.

Step 3: Create Volume

Adding volume to the crown of the head is a great way to elongate the face. Use a volumizing mousse or spray and blow-dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a medium hairstyle for your round face:

- Soft curls or waves add volume and texture to the hair, creating a more elongated face shape. - Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces as they create a diagonal line that adds length to the face. - Avoid blunt cuts and straight hairstyles as they can make the face look rounder. - Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and make your face appear slimmer.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium hairstyles for round faces:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

The best haircut for a round face is one that adds length and volume to the face. Medium hairstyles that fall just below the shoulders or at the collarbone are ideal for round faces.

Do bangs look good on round faces?

Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces as they create a diagonal line that adds length to the face. However, avoid blunt bangs as they can make the face look rounder.

Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

Straight hairstyles can make the face look rounder, so it's best to avoid them if you have a round face. Instead, opt for hairstyles with soft curls or waves that add volume and texture to the hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. - Avoid using hot styling tools too often as they can damage the hair. - Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools. - Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and free of split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right medium hairstyle, you can enhance your best features and feel confident and beautiful with your round face.
