Short Layered Cut For Thick Wavy Hair A Modern And Chic Look

Written by Bardi Jun 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know how hard it can be to manage. It can be challenging to find the right haircut that flatters your face and complements your hair texture. Luckily, a short layered cut for thick wavy hair can be the perfect solution for those who want to maintain a chic and modern look while keeping their hair manageable. In this article, we’ll discuss everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Description

A short layered cut for thick wavy hair is a hairstyle that combines short layers with a wavy texture to create a chic and modern look. This style is perfect for those who want to create volume and texture in their hair while keeping it manageable. The layers in this cut help to create movement and volume, making the hair look fuller and thicker.

How to Achieve a Short Layered Cut for Thick Wavy Hair

The first step to achieving a short layered cut for thick wavy hair is to find a stylist who understands your hair type and knows how to work with it. Once you find the right stylist, you can start discussing the specifics of your haircut. Here’s a step-by-step guide to achieving this chic hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your hair healthy and moisturized, which is essential for creating a smooth and defined wavy texture.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair, removing excess water. Avoid rubbing your hair harshly with the towel, as this can cause frizz and damage to your hair.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair. This will help to moisturize your hair and protect it from heat styling tools.

Step 4: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Using a diffuser attachment, blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting to create volume and texture. Use your fingertips to scrunch your hair while blow-drying to create a defined wavy texture.

Step 5: Cut Your Hair

Ask your stylist to cut your hair into short layers, starting from the crown of your head. The layers should be cut at an angle to create a textured look. Your stylist may also add long side-swept bangs to frame your face.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, you can style it using a curling iron or a straightening iron to create defined waves or sleek and straight hair. Finish off your look with a hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short layered cut for thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a high heat setting, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to create volume and texture.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to protect it from heat styling tools.
  • Consider adding highlights or balayage to your hair to create dimension and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about a short layered cut for thick wavy hair:

1. Will a short layered cut work for all face shapes?

A short layered cut can work for most face shapes, but it's important to consult with your stylist to find the right cut that flatters your features.

2. Is a short layered cut suitable for all hair types?

A short layered cut can work for most hair types, but it's essential to find a stylist who has experience working with thick and wavy hair.

3. How often should I get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Maintaining a short layered cut for thick wavy hair requires the right hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools on a high heat setting, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair to protect it from heat styling tools.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

A short layered cut for thick wavy hair can be a great way to add volume, texture, and movement to your hair. Follow these tips and techniques to achieve this trendy hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
