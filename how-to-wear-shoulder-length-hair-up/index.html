How to Wear Shoulder Length Hair Up - Step by Step Guide

Shoulder length hair is the perfect length for easy hairdos. It's not too long for a casual look, and it's not too short for a fancy updo. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to wear shoulder length hair up in a variety of styles. From simple ponytails to elegant chignons, we've got you covered.

How to Wear Shoulder Length Hair Up

Wearing shoulder length hair up is easy, and there are many styles to choose from. You can wear your hair up in a simple ponytail for a casual look, or you can create an elegant updo for a special occasion. Here are some popular styles:

1. Ponytail

A ponytail is one of the easiest and most popular ways to wear shoulder length hair up. Simply gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with an elastic band. You can wear your ponytail high or low, and you can also add accessories like a scarf or a headband for a pop of color.

2. Half Up Half Down

A half up half down hairstyle is a great way to show off your shoulder length hair while keeping it out of your face. To create this style, gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a clip or a hair tie. The bottom half of your hair can be left loose or curled for a more formal look.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a relaxed and effortless way to wear shoulder length hair up. To create this style, gather your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy, undone look.

4. Chignon

A chignon is an elegant and sophisticated way to wear shoulder length hair up. To create this style, gather your hair at the nape of your neck and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and add a hair accessory like a flower or a sparkly clip for a touch of glamour.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for creating a simple ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head.
  3. Secure your hair with an elastic band.
  4. Adjust your ponytail to your desired height.
  5. Add accessories like a scarf or a headband if desired.

Here's a step-by-step guide for creating a messy bun:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head.
  3. Twist your hair into a bun.
  4. Secure your bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few strands to create a messy, undone look.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips for styling shoulder length hair:

  • Add texture with a sea salt spray or a texturizing powder.
  • Use bobby pins to secure your hair in place.
  • Invest in hair accessories like clips, headbands, and scarves.
  • Experiment with different partings to change up your look.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and smoothness.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my shoulder length hair up for a formal event?

A: Yes, there are many elegant updos that work well for formal events, like a chignon or a French twist.

Q: What should I do if my hair is too thick for a ponytail?

A: You can try using a hair tie that is designed for thick hair, or you can create a half up half down hairstyle instead.

Q: Can I wear a messy bun to work?

A: It depends on your workplace's dress code. A messy bun can be a casual and relaxed hairstyle, so it may not be appropriate for a more formal setting.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips for shoulder length hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent over-washing and dryness.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and techniques, you can wear your shoulder length hair up in a variety of styles. Whether you're going to work or a special event, there's a hairstyle for every occasion. So go ahead and experiment with different updos, and have fun with your hair!
