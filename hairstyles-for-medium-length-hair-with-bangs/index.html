Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair With Bangs A Guide For 2023

If you're looking for a versatile and chic hairstyle, medium length hair with bangs is a great option. Not only does it suit any face shape, but it also allows for a variety of styling options. In this blog post, we'll explore different hairstyles for medium length hair with bangs, how to achieve them, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Style Medium Length Hair with Bangs

To style your medium length hair with bangs, start by washing and blow-drying your hair. Once your hair is dry, you can use a straightener to create sleek and straight bangs or use a curling iron to add waves to the ends of your hair. You can also use a flat iron to create a wavy texture.

For a more relaxed look, you can create loose waves using a curling iron and then tousle your hair with your fingers. To add volume to your hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray on your roots and blow-dry your hair upside down. You can also use a dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hairstyles for Medium Length Hair with Bangs

The Bob

The bob is a classic hairstyle that looks great on medium length hair with bangs. To achieve this look, ask your hairdresser for a blunt, chin-length bob with bangs that fall just above your eyebrows. You can wear this hairstyle straight and sleek or add some texture by using a curling iron to create loose waves.

Layers

Adding layers to your medium length hair with bangs can add movement and dimension to your hair. Ask your hairdresser to add layers that frame your face and start at chin-length. You can wear this hairstyle straight or add waves to the ends of your hair for a more relaxed look.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great option for medium length hair with bangs. To achieve this look, start by applying a texturizing spray to your damp hair. Then, use a curling iron to create loose waves. Once you've curled your hair, use your fingers to tousle your hair and create a relaxed, beachy look.

Braids

Braids are a great way to add some variety to your medium length hair with bangs. You can create a simple braid or a more intricate braid, like a fishtail braid or a Dutch braid. To create a braid, start by parting your hair and then sectioning it off. Then, braid your hair and secure the end with a hair tie.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Length Hair with Bangs

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs about Medium Length Hair with Bangs

What face shapes suit medium length hair with bangs?

Medium length hair with bangs suits any face shape, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How do I choose the right bangs for my face shape?

If you have a round face, opt for longer bangs that hit below the eyebrows. If you have a square face, go for side-swept bangs that soften your features. If you have a heart-shaped face, choose bangs that are wispy and textured.

Can I still wear a ponytail with medium length hair with bangs?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail with medium length hair with bangs. You can either leave your bangs out or pin them back with bobby pins.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair with Bangs

To keep your medium length hair with bangs looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type and avoid using too much heat on your hair. You can also use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Overall, medium length hair with bangs is a versatile and chic hairstyle that can be styled in a variety of ways. Whether you opt for a bob, layers, beachy waves, or braids, make sure to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
