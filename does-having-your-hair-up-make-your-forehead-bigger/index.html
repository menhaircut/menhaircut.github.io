Does Having Your Hair Up Make Your Forehead Bigger

Written by Aprilia Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, many people prefer to wear their hair up in a ponytail, bun, or other updos. But there has been a common belief that having your hair up can make your forehead look bigger. Is this really true? Let's find out.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

It's a common misconception that having your hair up can make your forehead look bigger. However, this is not entirely true. The size of your forehead is determined by your genetics and bone structure, and your hairstyle cannot make it bigger or smaller.

What happens when you tie your hair up is that your hairline becomes more visible, which can create an illusion of a bigger forehead. But this does not mean that your forehead has actually increased in size.

How to

If you're worried about your forehead looking bigger with your hair up, there are a few things you can do to create a more flattering hairstyle:

1. Try a side part

A side part can help to balance out your forehead and make it look less prominent. You can also add some volume to the crown of your head to create the illusion of a shorter forehead.

2. Opt for a messy bun

A messy bun can create a soft, romantic look that detracts from the size of your forehead. Try pulling a few strands of hair loose around your face to create a more relaxed, natural look.

3. Avoid slicked-back styles

Slicked-back hairstyles can emphasize your hairline and make your forehead look bigger. Instead, try a loose, textured style that adds volume to your hair and creates a more balanced look.

Step by step

Here's how to create a flattering hairstyle that doesn't emphasize your forehead:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and blow-dry your hair to create a clean, smooth base for your hairstyle.

2. Create a side part

Use a comb to create a side part that flatters your face shape.

3. Tie your hair up

Gather your hair into a loose bun or ponytail at the nape of your neck. Avoid pulling your hair back too tightly, as this can emphasize your hairline and make your forehead look bigger.

4. Add volume

Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair. You can also tease the crown of your head to create the illusion of a shorter forehead.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you create a flattering hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that flatters your face shape.
  • Use accessories like headbands or scarves to create a more interesting hairstyle.
  • Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair back too tightly, as this can cause hair breakage and damage to your scalp.
  • Invest in high-quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ

Does having a big forehead make you less attractive?

No, having a big forehead does not make you less attractive. Beauty comes in all shapes and sizes, and there is no one-size-fits-all definition of attractiveness.

Can you make your forehead smaller with surgery?

Yes, there are surgical procedures that can reduce the size of your forehead, such as hairline lowering surgery or forehead reduction surgery. However, these procedures are invasive and should only be considered after careful consultation with a qualified surgeon.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and strong is essential for creating a flattering hairstyle that doesn't emphasize your forehead. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Condition your hair every time you wash it to keep it moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from sun, wind, and pollution by wearing a hat or using a protective styling product.

In conclusion, having your hair up does not make your forehead bigger. It's all about finding a flattering hairstyle that works for your face shape and hair type. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can create a beautiful, confident look that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
