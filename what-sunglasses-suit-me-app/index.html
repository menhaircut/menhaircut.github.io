What Sunglasses Suit Me App The Ultimate Guide To Finding Your Perfect Shades

Written by Frank Nov 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're anything like me, you know how difficult it can be to find the perfect pair of sunglasses. With so many styles and shapes to choose from, it's hard to know where to even begin. Thankfully, there's a solution: the "What Sunglasses Suit Me" app. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about using this app to find your perfect shades.

Description

The "What Sunglasses Suit Me" app is a tool that helps you find the perfect pair of sunglasses based on your face shape, skin tone, and personal style. It uses an advanced algorithm to analyze your facial features and recommend styles that will complement your unique look.

How to Use the What Sunglasses Suit Me App

Step 1: Download the App

The first step in using the "What Sunglasses Suit Me" app is to download it onto your mobile device. The app is available for both iOS and Android and can be found in the App Store or Google Play Store.

Step 2: Take a Selfie

Once you've downloaded the app, take a selfie of your face. Make sure you're facing the camera straight on and that your entire face is visible. The app will use this photo to analyze your facial features and recommend sunglasses that will suit you.

Step 3: Answer a Few Questions

Next, the app will ask you a few questions about your personal style and preferences. This will help it narrow down the recommendations to styles that fit your taste.

Step 4: Get Your Recommendations

After you've answered the questions, the app will generate a list of recommendations for sunglasses that will suit your face shape, skin tone, and personal style. You can browse through the options and choose the ones that you like best.

Tips for Using the What Sunglasses Suit Me App

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using the "What Sunglasses Suit Me" app:

  • Make sure your selfie is well-lit and shows your entire face.
  • Answer the questions as honestly as possible to get the most accurate recommendations.
  • Consider trying on a few different styles in person to see which ones you like best.

FAQ

Is the "What Sunglasses Suit Me" app free?

Yes, the app is completely free to download and use.

Can I trust the recommendations from the app?

While the app's recommendations are based on an advanced algorithm, they should be taken as suggestions rather than strict rules. It's always a good idea to try on a few different styles in person to see which ones you like best.

What if I don't like any of the recommended styles?

If you don't like any of the recommended styles, you can always try retaking the selfie or answering the questions differently to see if you get different results.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Sunglasses

Wearing sunglasses can be a great way to protect your eyes from the sun's harmful UV rays, but it can also cause some hair-related issues. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid wearing sunglasses on top of your head, as this can cause your hair to become tangled and damaged.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to protect your hair from the sun and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Consider wearing a hat or using a hair tie to keep your hair out of your face while wearing sunglasses.

Overall, the "What Sunglasses Suit Me" app is a great tool for anyone who's looking to find the perfect pair of shades. With its advanced algorithm and personalized recommendations, you're sure to find a style that suits your unique look and personal style.
