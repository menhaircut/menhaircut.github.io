What Faces Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Aprilia Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair has been a trend for several years now, and it's not going anywhere soon. However, not everyone can pull off a short hairdo. This guide will help you determine what faces look good with short hair and how to style your hair to achieve the perfect look.

Description

Short hair is versatile but requires a certain face shape to pull it off. You can have different types of short haircuts, from a pixie cut to a bob, but not everyone can wear them. Face shape plays a crucial role in determining whether a short hairdo will look good on you or not.

How to Determine if Short Hair is Right for You

Before going for a short hairdo, you need to determine your face shape. Here are the different face shapes and what short haircuts suit them:

  • Oval: Oval faces look great with almost all types of short haircuts, from pixie cuts to bobs. You can experiment with different styles to find the perfect one for you.
  • Round: Round faces look good with short haircuts that add height to the crown. A pixie cut with volume on top will suit round faces well.
  • Square: Square faces look good with soft, layered short haircuts. A bob with layers will soften the angles of your face.
  • Heart: Heart-shaped faces look good with short haircuts that add width to the forehead. A pixie cut with side-swept bangs will suit this face shape.
  • Diamond: Diamond-shaped faces look good with short haircuts that add volume to the top and sides. A textured bob or a pixie cut with choppy layers will suit this face shape.
  • Long: Long faces look good with short haircuts that add width to the face. A layered bob or a pixie cut with volume on the sides will suit this face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

  1. Wash your hair and towel-dry it.
  2. Apply a styling product of your choice, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  4. If you have a pixie cut, use a texturizing product to add texture and definition to your hair.
  5. If you have a bob, use a flat iron to straighten your hair or create loose waves.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining short hair:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair in shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • If you have color-treated hair, use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your color.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and clips, to add some variety to your style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair:

  • Can anyone pull off short hair? Short hair can look good on anyone, but it's important to choose a style that suits your face shape.
  • How often should I get my hair trimmed? It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Can I still style my short hair? Yes, short hair is versatile and can be styled in different ways using styling products and tools.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires minimal maintenance, but it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many styling products to prevent buildup.
  • Use a heat protectant before using heat-styling tools to prevent damage.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a short hairdo that suits your face shape and style. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its health and beauty.
