How To Style Mid Length Thick Hair

Written by Christine Jul 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have mid length thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. It can take forever to dry, and once it does, it can be difficult to make it look presentable. But don't worry, with the right techniques and products, you can style your mid length thick hair into a beautiful and manageable look. In this article, we will show you step by step how to style your mid length thick hair.

Description

Mid length hair is considered to be between the chin and shoulders. If you have thick hair, it means that each strand of hair is thicker than normal hair. Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. It can be difficult to handle, but it also means that you have a lot of hair to work with. With the right techniques, you can style your mid length thick hair into a beautiful and manageable look.

How to Style Mid Length Thick Hair

Styling mid length thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can create a look that you love. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step to styling your mid length thick hair is to wash it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for thick hair. This will help to make your hair more manageable and easier to style.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Once you have washed your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner. This will help to detangle your hair and make it easier to comb through. It will also add moisture to your hair, which is important for styling thick hair.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Start by blow drying the roots of your hair, then move to the ends. Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair as you blow dry it.

Step 4: Section your hair

Section your hair into three parts: the top section, the middle section, and the bottom section. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 5: Curl your hair

Use a curling iron to curl each section of your hair. Start with the bottom section and work your way up. Use a medium-sized curling iron to create loose curls.

Step 6: Apply hairspray

Once you have curled your hair, apply a hairspray to help hold the curls in place. Use a light hold hairspray to avoid making your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your mid length thick hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or other heat styling tools.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Try different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my mid length thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you may be able to wash it every other day or even less frequently.

Q: Can I use a straightener instead of a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener to create curls in your hair. However, it may take longer to curl your hair with a straightener than with a curling iron.

Hair Care

To keep your mid length thick hair healthy and beautiful, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for thick hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your mid length thick hair into a beautiful and manageable look. Remember to take care of your hair, and it will reward you with healthy and beautiful locks.
