How Long Is A Number 3 Haircut In Mm

Written by Frank Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who loves to experiment with their hair or just wants to get a fresh haircut, you might have come across the term "number 3 haircut" quite often. But what does it actually mean? How long is a number 3 haircut in mm? In this blog post, we'll answer all your questions related to this popular haircut.

Description

A number 3 haircut refers to the length of the guard used on the clipper while cutting the hair. The guard is measured in millimeters, and a number 3 guard is approximately 9mm long. This means that the hair will be cut to a length of 9mm all around the head, giving it a uniform and clean look.

How to

If you want to get a number 3 haircut, you can either do it yourself with a clipper or go to a professional hairstylist. Here's how to do it yourself:

  1. Choose the right clipper guard: Make sure you have a number 3 guard for your clipper.
  2. Start with clean, dry hair: Make sure your hair is free from any product, and it's completely dry.
  3. Start cutting: Turn on the clipper and start cutting your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Make sure to keep the clipper level and move it in a smooth motion.
  4. Trim the edges: Use scissors to trim any hair that the clipper missed, especially around the ears and the back of the neck.
  5. Clean up: Once you're done cutting your hair, clean up any hair clippings that might have fallen on the floor or your clothes.

Step by Step

If you're still not sure how to get a number 3 haircut, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner to get rid of any product buildup, and towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Choose the right guard

Attach the number 3 guard to your clipper. Make sure it's securely attached and won't come off while you're cutting your hair.

Step 3: Start cutting

Start cutting your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Move the clipper in a smooth motion, keeping it level all around your head.

Step 4: Trim the edges

Once you're done cutting your hair, use scissors to trim any hair that the clipper missed, especially around the ears and the back of the neck.

Step 5: Clean up

Clean up any hair clippings that might have fallen on the floor or your clothes. Rinse your hair with water to get rid of any loose hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while getting a number 3 haircut:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting it.
  • Use a mirror to see the back of your head while cutting.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and make sure you're cutting your hair evenly.
  • If you're not comfortable cutting your hair yourself, go to a professional hairstylist.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about number 3 haircuts:

What other guard lengths are available?

The guard lengths range from 0 to 8, with 0 being the shortest and 8 being the longest.

Can I use a number 3 guard on all hair types?

Yes, a number 3 guard can be used on all hair types, but the length of the hair might vary depending on the thickness and texture of the hair.

How often do I need to get a number 3 haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. On average, you might need to get a number 3 haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After getting a number 3 haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know how long a number 3 haircut is in mm, you can decide if it's the right haircut for you. Whether you're doing it yourself or going to a professional hairstylist, make sure to follow the tips and take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
