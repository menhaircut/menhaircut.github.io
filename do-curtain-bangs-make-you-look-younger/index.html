Do Curtain Bangs Make You Look Younger

Description

One of the most popular hair trends of the moment is curtain bangs. These bangs are named after the way they frame the face, much like curtains on a window. Many people wonder if curtain bangs make you look younger, and the answer is yes!

Curtain bangs can help to soften the face and create a more youthful appearance. They also draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, which can make you look more awake and vibrant.

If you're considering getting curtain bangs, read on for a step-by-step guide on how to get them and tips for maintaining them.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

The first step to getting curtain bangs is to find a stylist who has experience cutting them. Not all stylists are familiar with this technique, so do your research before booking an appointment.

When you go to your appointment, bring pictures of the type of curtain bangs you want. This will help your stylist understand your vision and create the perfect look for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curtain bangs:

  1. Wash and dry your hair as you normally would.
  2. Separate the front section of your hair that you want to be your curtain bangs.
  3. Hold the section of hair between your index and middle fingers, and point-cut the ends to create a soft, feathery look.
  4. Use a comb to create a center part in your bangs.
  5. Take small sections of hair on either side of the part and twist them away from your face.
  6. Using scissors, point-cut the ends of the twists to create a soft, blended look.
  7. Repeat steps 5 and 6 until you've created the desired amount of curtain bangs.
  8. Style your curtain bangs with a round brush and blow dryer, or let them air dry for a more natural look.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for maintaining your curtain bangs:

  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from getting greasy between washes.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.
  • Use a small amount of hair oil to keep your bangs looking shiny and healthy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look flat.

FAQs About Curtain Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

1. Who can get curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs can be flattering on almost anyone. However, if you have a very short forehead or a cowlick in your bangs, curtain bangs may not be the best option for you.

2. How long do curtain bangs take to grow out?

It usually takes about 4-6 months for curtain bangs to grow out enough to blend in with the rest of your hair.

3. Can I wear my curtain bangs in different styles?

Yes! Curtain bangs can be swept to the side, pinned back, or worn straight down.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs require a little extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your bangs soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your bangs, as this can damage the hair and make it look dry and frizzy.
  • If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them look greasy and flat.

Now that you know all about curtain bangs, you can decide if they're the right look for you. Remember, they can help you look younger and more vibrant, but they do require some extra care and maintenance.
