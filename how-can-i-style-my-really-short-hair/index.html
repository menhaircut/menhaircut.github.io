How Can I Style My Really Short Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair is trendy, practical, and low maintenance. However, styling it can be a challenge, especially if you want to change your look frequently. Whether you have a pixie cut, a bob, or a buzz cut, there are plenty of ways to style your short hair and make a statement. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to style your really short hair and look fabulous.

How to Style Your Really Short Hair

Styling short hair can be quick and easy if you know what you're doing. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Start with clean and dry hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Blot your hair dry with a towel, and let it air dry or use a blow dryer on low heat.

2. Apply a styling product

To add texture and hold to your hair, apply a styling product such as a mousse, gel, or wax. Choose a product that suits your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Apply a small amount of the product to your fingertips and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair

Depending on the style you want, use a comb or your fingers to shape your hair. For a sleek and polished look, use a comb to create a side part or slick your hair back. For a messy and tousled look, use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create texture.

4. Finish with a hairspray or pomade

To set your style and add shine, finish with a hairspray or pomade. Choose a product that suits your hair type and the level of hold you want. Spray the hairspray from a distance, or apply the pomade to your fingertips and run it through your hair.

Tips for Styling Your Really Short Hair

Here are some tips to make styling your short hair easier and more effective:

1. Experiment with different styles

Short hair is versatile and can be styled in many ways. Experiment with different styles to find what suits you best. You can try a side part, a tousled look, a slicked-back style, or a textured crop, among others.

2. Use the right tools

To style your hair effectively, use the right tools such as a comb, a brush, a blow dryer, or a flat iron. Choose tools that suit your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

3. Consider your face shape

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape and features. Some styles may flatter your face shape more than others. For example, a pixie cut can accentuate your cheekbones and jawline, while a bob can soften your features.

4. Don't be afraid to accessorize

Accessories such as headbands, clips, and scarves can add interest and style to your short hair. Experiment with different accessories to create a unique and personalized look.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it twice a week. If you exercise frequently or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash it more often.

Q: Can I dye my short hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your short hair. However, it's important to choose a dye that suits your hair type and the color you want to achieve. Consult a professional stylist or do a patch test before dyeing your hair at home.

Q: How can I protect my short hair from damage?

A: To protect your short hair from damage, avoid using heat styling tools too often, use a heat protectant spray, avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Hair Care for Really Short Hair

Short hair is low maintenance, but it still requires some care to look its best. Here are some tips to keep your short hair healthy and shiny:

1. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are gentle on your hair and scalp. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Trim your hair regularly

To maintain your short hairstyle, trim your hair every four to six weeks. This will help prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the sun.

4. Eat a healthy diet

A healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can promote hair growth and strength. Eat foods such as salmon, nuts, spinach, and eggs to nourish your hair from the inside out.

Styling really short hair can be fun and exciting. With these tips and tricks, you can create a variety of looks that suit your personality and style. Remember to take care of your hair and protect it from damage, and you'll have a fabulous short hairstyle that turns heads.
