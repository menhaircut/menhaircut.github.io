How To Make Your Hair Slicked Back Naturally A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a sleek and polished hairstyle, then you might want to try the slicked-back look. Not only is it a classic style, but it's also easy to achieve and perfect for any occasion. The best part? You can achieve this look naturally without using any harsh chemicals or products. In this post, we'll show you how to make your hair slicked back naturally.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Your Hair Slicked Back Naturally

The first step to achieving a slicked-back look is to have the right hair type. This style works best on thick, straight hair. If you have curly or wavy hair, you can still achieve this look, but it might require more effort.

Next, you'll need to gather a few key products. To make your hair slicked back naturally, you'll need:

  • A comb or brush
  • A natural hair gel or pomade
  • A hair tie or clip (optional)

With these products on hand, you're ready to start styling your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make your hair slicked back naturally:

  1. Wash your hair with a natural shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any excess oil or buildup from your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair until it's damp. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to style.
  3. Apply a small amount of natural hair gel or pomade to your palms and rub them together to warm the product.
  4. Using your fingers or a comb, apply the product to your hair, starting from the back and working your way forward. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.
  5. Use a comb or brush to slick your hair back, starting from your forehead and working your way back. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  6. If you have long hair, you can tie it back into a low ponytail or bun. This will help keep your hair in place and prevent it from falling forward.
  7. Finish the look by applying a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your fingertips and running them over your hair to smooth out any remaining frizz or flyaways.

Tips for Slicked-Back Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Start with clean, damp hair. This will help the hair gel or pomade adhere to your hair better.
  • Use a natural hair gel or pomade that won't damage your hair or cause buildup.
  • Don't apply too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a fine-toothed comb to create a sleek, polished look.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, you may need to use a hair straightener to achieve a sleek look.

FAQs

Can I achieve a slicked-back look with curly or wavy hair?

Yes, you can still achieve a slicked-back look with curly or wavy hair, but it may require more effort. You may need to use a hair straightener to achieve a sleek look, and you may need to use more hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

How often should I wash my hair if I want to achieve a slicked-back look?

It's best to wash your hair every other day if you want to achieve a slicked-back look. This will help remove any excess oil or buildup from your hair, while still allowing your hair to retain its natural oils.

Hair Care for Slicked-Back Hair

If you want to keep your hair looking sleek and polished, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips for hair care for slicked-back hair:

  • Use a natural shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals or alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid tying your hair back too tightly, as this can cause hair loss and breakage.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

By following these tips and using natural hair products, you can achieve a slicked-back look that is both stylish and healthy for your hair.
