How To Thin Your Own Hair With Thinning Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Thinning out your hair can be a great way to reduce volume, remove weight, and add texture. However, it can be difficult to achieve the desired results without the right tools and technique. If you're looking to thin your own hair with thinning scissors, this guide will show you how to do it safely and effectively.

Description

Thinning scissors are a special type of scissors that have teeth or notches on one or both blades. These teeth create gaps in the hair as you cut, which helps to remove some of the bulk and thickness. Thinning scissors can be used on any hair type, but they are particularly useful for people with thick, heavy, or curly hair.

Thinning your hair with scissors can be a bit tricky, especially if you've never done it before. However, with a little practice and patience, you can achieve great results at home.

How to Thin Your Own Hair with Thinning Scissors

Here are the steps you need to follow to thin your own hair with thinning scissors:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start thinning, make sure your hair is clean and dry. If your hair is wet or damp, it will be more difficult to see exactly how much you're cutting, and you may end up taking off too much.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use clips or ties to section your hair into several parts. This will make it easier to work on one section at a time, and will prevent you from accidentally thinning too much hair at once.

Step 3: Hold the scissors correctly

Hold the thinning scissors with the teeth or notches pointing upwards. This will help you to cut the hair evenly and prevent any accidental snipping.

Step 4: Take a small section of hair

Take a small section of hair from the top of your head, using the comb to hold it in place. Make sure you only take a small section, as you can always take more later if needed.

Step 5: Cut the hair

Hold the scissors at a 45-degree angle, and gently close them along the length of the hair. Don't close the scissors completely, as this will create a blunt cut. Instead, aim to cut about half of the hair in each snip.

Step 6: Check your progress

After you've cut the first section, let it fall back into place and check your progress. If you're satisfied with the amount of thinning, move on to the next section. If you want to thin it more, repeat the process on the same section until you achieve your desired result.

Step 7: Repeat on all sections

Work your way through all the sections of your hair, taking small sections and thinning them as desired.

Tips for Thinning Your Hair with Thinning Scissors

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when thinning your hair with thinning scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors for the best results
  • Be patient and take your time
  • Don't thin too much hair at once
  • Work in good lighting and use a mirror to check your progress
  • Comb your hair regularly to check for any uneven areas

FAQ

How often should I thin my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, you should aim to thin your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Can I use regular scissors to thin my hair?

Regular scissors can be used to thin hair, but they are not as effective as thinning scissors. If you want to achieve the best results, it's best to invest in a pair of good quality thinning scissors.

Is it possible to thin hair too much?

Yes, it is possible to thin your hair too much, which can result in thin, wispy ends. To avoid this, make sure you only take small sections of hair and check your progress regularly.

Hair Care

Thinning your hair can be a great way to change up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair afterwards. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

Thinning your own hair with thinning scissors can be a great way to achieve a new look without having to visit the salon. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve great results at home and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
