Mens Hairstyles Short Sides Medium Top

Written by Daniel Nov 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish yet practical hairstyle, then the mens hairstyles short sides medium top is a great option. This look has been popular for a while now, and it's not hard to see why. The short sides and medium top give you the best of both worlds - a clean, polished look on the sides, and plenty of volume and texture on top. In this post, we'll go over everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to maintain it.

Description

The mens hairstyles short sides medium top is a versatile look that can be customized to suit your individual style. Essentially, it involves cutting the hair short on the sides and leaving some length on top. The length on top can vary, but typically it's around 2-3 inches. This allows you to style the hair in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured.

How to Get the Look

If you're interested in trying out the mens hairstyles short sides medium top, here's how to get the look:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how short you want the sides to be. Generally, a fade or undercut works well for this style. The length on top should be around 2-3 inches, but this can vary depending on your preference.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Using clippers, cut the hair on the sides to your desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to create a fade. Alternatively, you can use scissors to create an undercut.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Cut the hair on top to your desired length, leaving it longer than the sides. Blend the top into the sides using scissors or clippers.

Step 4: Style

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as pomade or wax, and style it as desired. You can slick it back, create a side part, or leave it messy and textured.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve and maintain the mens hairstyles short sides medium top:

  • Use a good quality styling product to help hold your style in place throughout the day.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the mens hairstyles short sides medium top:

Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this style can work for all hair types, although the styling techniques may vary depending on your hair type and texture.

Can I wear this hairstyle to work?

Yes, this style is versatile enough to be worn in a variety of settings, including the workplace.

How often do I need to get a trim?

You should get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair when sporting the mens hairstyles short sides medium top:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Overall, the mens hairstyles short sides medium top is a stylish and practical hairstyle that can be customized to suit your individual style. With the right cut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, you can rock this look with confidence.
