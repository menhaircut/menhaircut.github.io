Hair Length For Heart Shaped Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you probably already know that finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. The good news is that there are plenty of hair length options that will flatter your face shape. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about choosing the right hair length for heart shaped face, including how to determine your face shape, how to style your hair, and tips for maintaining your look.

Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right hair length for your heart-shaped face is to determine your face shape. Start by pulling your hair back and looking in the mirror. Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and cheekbones with a narrow chin. If you're still not sure, try tracing the outline of your face on a mirror with lipstick or eyeliner. This will help you see the shape of your face more clearly.

Short Hair

If you have a heart-shaped face and want to go for a short hair length, your best bet is a pixie cut or a bob. These styles will help balance out the width of your forehead and cheekbones, while adding volume to your hair. Keep in mind that shorter styles require more upkeep, so you'll need to be prepared to visit the salon more frequently for trims.

Medium Hair

If you prefer a medium hair length, consider a lob (long bob) or shoulder-length cut. These styles will help soften the angles of your face while still providing a flattering frame. You can also add layers to your hair to create movement and volume.

Long Hair

Long hair can be a great option for heart-shaped faces, but it's important to keep in mind that too much length can weigh down your face and make it appear longer. Stick to a length that falls between your collarbone and chest, and consider adding layers or face-framing highlights to create dimension.

Styling Tips

When styling your hair, consider adding volume at the crown to balance out your forehead. You can also try side-swept bangs to draw attention away from your forehead and towards your eyes. Avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear longer and narrower.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of the length of your hair, it's important to take good care of it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage, and avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis. If you do need to use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What is the best hair length for heart shaped face?

The best hair length for heart shaped face depends on your personal preference and style. Short haircuts like pixie or bob cuts are great for heart shaped faces. Medium length hair is also a great option, and long hair can work as well if it's kept at the right length.

How do I style my hair if I have a heart shaped face?

When styling your hair for heart shaped face, consider adding volume at the crown to balance out your forehead. You can also try side-swept bangs to draw attention away from your forehead and towards your eyes. Avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear longer and narrower.

What hair care tips should I follow for heart shaped face?

Regardless of the length of your hair, it's important to take good care of it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage, and avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis. If you do need to use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Regular trims are also important to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing the right hair length for heart shaped face can be a challenge, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to find a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.
