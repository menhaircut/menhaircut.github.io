How To Make A Guy s Hair Naturally Curly

Written by Robby Jul 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a guy with straight hair, you might be wondering how you can achieve those natural-looking curls that seem to be so popular. Fortunately, there are a few tricks you can use to add some texture and wave to your hair without resorting to chemical treatments or heat styling tools. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps of how to make a guy's hair naturally curly, including tips on how to care for your curls and keep them looking great.

Description

Having naturally curly hair is something that many people envy, but if you're a guy with straight hair, you might feel like you're missing out. The good news is that you don't have to be born with curly hair to achieve those bouncy, voluminous curls that are so popular these days. With a little bit of effort and some careful styling, you can transform your straight hair into a head full of bouncy, natural-looking curls.

How to Make a Guy's Hair Naturally Curly

Step 1: Start with Clean, Damp Hair

The first step in creating natural-looking curls is to start with clean, damp hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then towel dry it until it's damp but not dripping wet. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

Step 2: Apply Some Curl-Enhancing Products

Once your hair is damp, it's time to apply some curl-enhancing products. Look for products that are specifically designed to add texture and wave to your hair, such as mousse, curl cream, or sea salt spray. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and tips.

Step 3: Scrunch Your Hair

Next, it's time to scrunch your hair. This technique involves gently squeezing your hair in your hands, which helps to create natural-looking curls. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, gently scrunching your hair as you go. Avoid using too much pressure, as this can cause your hair to frizz.

Step 4: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Once you've scrunched your hair, it's time to let it air dry. Avoid using a hair dryer, as this can cause your hair to become frizzy and damage the curls you've created. Instead, let your hair dry naturally, either by sitting in front of a fan or by simply letting it air dry on its own.

Step 5: Define Your Curls

Once your hair is dry, you can define your curls by gently running your fingers through your hair. This will help to separate the curls and add volume to your hair. You can also use a curling iron or a curling wand to define your curls even further, but be careful not to use too much heat or you could damage your hair.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best possible results when trying to make your guy's hair naturally curly:

  • Choose the right products: Look for products that are specifically designed to add texture and wave to your hair, such as mousse or curl cream.
  • Avoid using heat: Heat can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style, so try to avoid using a hair dryer or other heat styling tools.
  • Be gentle: When scrunching your hair, be gentle and avoid using too much pressure, as this can cause your hair to frizz.
  • Let your hair air dry: Air drying is the best way to create natural-looking curls, so avoid using a hair dryer if possible.
  • Experiment with different techniques: Everyone's hair is different, so don't be afraid to experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can I make my hair naturally curly without using any products?

While it's possible to create curls without using any products, it can be more difficult to achieve natural-looking curls without the help of some curl-enhancing products. Products like mousse or curl cream can help to add texture and wave to your hair, making it easier to create natural-looking curls.

Can I use a hair dryer to create natural-looking curls?

While you can use a hair dryer to create curls, it's generally best to avoid using heat styling tools if you're trying to create natural-looking curls. Heat can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style, so it's best to let your hair air dry whenever possible.

Hair Care

To keep your newly curly hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips to help you maintain healthy, beautiful curls:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as brushes can cause your curls to become frizzy.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause your curls to lose their shape.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create natural-looking curls that will have everyone wondering how you managed to achieve such beautiful, bouncy hair.
