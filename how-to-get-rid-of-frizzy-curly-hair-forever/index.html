How To Get Rid Of Frizzy Curly Hair Forever

Written by Bardi Sep 20, 2023 · 5 min read

Frizzy hair can be a nightmare for those with curly hair. It often feels like no matter what you do, your hair is always going to be frizzy. But fear not, there are ways to get rid of frizzy curly hair forever. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks to help you achieve smooth, manageable curls.

Description

Frizz is a common issue for those with curly hair. It occurs when the hair’s cuticle layer is raised, allowing moisture to enter and swell the hair shaft. This causes the hair to become frizzy, dry, and difficult to manage. If you’re tired of dealing with frizzy hair, read on to learn how to get rid of it for good.

How to Get Rid of Frizzy Curly Hair Forever

Getting rid of frizzy curly hair may seem impossible, but it’s not. Here are some tips to help you achieve smooth, manageable curls:

1. Use the Right Products

The first step in getting rid of frizzy curly hair is to use the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil. These ingredients can help to moisturize and tame frizz.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage curly hair and make it more prone to frizz. To avoid this, try to air dry your hair as much as possible and avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons.

3. Use a Microfiber Towel

Regular towels can be rough on curly hair and cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel to gently blot your hair dry. This will help to reduce frizz and breakage.

4. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can contribute to frizz. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

5. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce friction and prevent frizz. Silk is also gentle on hair, so you’re less likely to wake up with tangles and knots.

Step by Step Guide to Get Rid of Frizzy Curly Hair Forever

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you get rid of frizzy curly hair:

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter and coconut oil. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to moisturize and detangle your hair, reducing the risk of frizz.

Step 3: Use a Curl Enhancer

Apply a curl enhancer to your hair, focusing on the mid-lengths and ends. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Dry Your Hair

Gently blot your hair with a microfiber towel to remove excess water. Then, either air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. Avoid touching your hair while it’s drying to prevent frizz.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Oil

Once your hair is dry, apply a hair oil to your ends to moisturize and add shine. Avoid applying too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Tips for Maintaining Frizz-Free Curls

Here are some tips to help you maintain frizz-free curls:

1. Don’t Over-Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Aim to wash your hair no more than twice a week.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help to detangle your hair without causing breakage or frizz. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

3. Avoid Touching Your Hair

Touching your hair too much can cause frizz. Try to avoid playing with your hair or running your fingers through it throughout the day.

4. Use a Satin Sleep Cap

If you don’t want to invest in a silk pillowcase, try using a satin sleep cap instead. This will help to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my curly hair?

A: It’s best to avoid using a regular towel to dry curly hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: What should I do if I wake up with frizzy hair?

A: If you wake up with frizzy hair, try wetting your hands and running them over your hair to smooth it down. You can also apply a small amount of hair oil to your ends to moisturize and tame frizz.

Hair Care for Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for frizzy curly hair:

1. Moisturize Regularly

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it’s important to moisturize it regularly. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage curly hair and make it more prone to frizz. If you’re going to be spending time outdoors, wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray on your hair.

3. Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Harsh chemicals like sulfates and alcohol can strip curly hair of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Look for products that are free of these ingredients and are gentle on your hair.

4. Embrace Your Curls

Finally, don’t be afraid to embrace your curls. Curly hair is beautiful, and with the right care and maintenance, you can achieve smooth, frizz-free curls.
