How To Make Shoulder Length Hair Look Thicker

Written by Eveline Sep 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having thin and lifeless hair? Do you want to add volume and thickness to your shoulder length hair? If you answered yes, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll show you how to make shoulder length hair look thicker with easy tips and tricks. So, let's get started!

Description

Shoulder length hair is a popular choice for many women because it's versatile and easy to maintain. However, if your hair is thin or fine, it can be challenging to style and make it look voluminous. Fortunately, there are many ways to add thickness and volume to your hair without spending a fortune on expensive products or treatments.

How to Make Shoulder Length Hair Look Thicker

Follow these simple steps to add volume and thickness to your shoulder length hair:

Step 1: Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products contain ingredients that add volume and lift to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller. Look for products that are specifically designed for fine or thin hair.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a simple and effective way to add volume to your hair. Start by flipping your hair over, so it's hanging down towards the floor. Then, use a blow dryer to dry your hair while lifting it at the roots. This will create volume and lift, making your hair look thicker and fuller.

Step 3: Use Hair Extensions

If you want an instant solution for thin or fine hair, try using hair extensions. Hair extensions can add length and volume to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. Choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a natural look.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an old school trick for adding volume and thickness to your hair. To tease your hair, start by sectioning it off and backcombing the roots with a teasing brush. This will create volume and lift, making your hair look thicker and fuller.

Step 5: Use Hair Products

There are many hair products available that can add volume and thickness to your hair. Look for products like volumizing mousse, root lift spray, and texturizing spray. These products can help create volume and lift, making your hair look thicker and fuller.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your shoulder length hair look thicker:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Try a new hairstyle or haircut that adds volume and movement to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy and strong.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making shoulder length hair look thicker:

Q: Can I make my hair look thicker without using hair products?

A: Yes, you can make your hair look thicker by blow drying it upside down or teasing it. However, using hair products can help create more volume and lift.

Q: Can I use hair extensions if my hair is thin?

A: Yes, hair extensions can be a great solution for thin or fine hair. Just make sure to choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a natural look.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Remember, taking care of your hair is essential to keeping it healthy and strong. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy and strong.

That's it! Follow these tips, and you'll have thicker and fuller shoulder length hair in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and choose the right products for your hair type. With a little effort and the right techniques, you can have the hair of your dreams!
