How To Make A Too Short Dress Longer Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jul 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have a favorite dress that is just a bit too short for your liking? Don't worry! There are several ways to make a too short dress longer without spending a lot of money. In this article, we'll show you how to add length to your dress using simple techniques.

Description

Short dresses can be great for showing off your legs and making a statement. However, they can also be a hassle to wear, especially if they're too short for your comfort. Whether you have a dress that's too short because of shrinkage or because you grew taller, there are several ways to make it longer. In this article, we'll cover some of the most popular methods to make a too short dress longer, including adding fabric, wearing leggings, or pairing it with boots.

How to Make a Too Short Dress Longer

Method 1: Add Fabric

If your dress is too short by just a few inches, consider adding fabric to the hemline. This can be done by sewing on a strip of matching fabric to the bottom of the dress. To do this, follow these steps:

  1. Measure the length you want to add to the dress.
  2. Cut a strip of fabric that's the same length as the measurement you took in step 1.
  3. Pin the fabric to the hemline of the dress, making sure the right sides are facing each other.
  4. Sew the fabric to the hemline using a straight stitch.
  5. Trim any excess fabric and turn the dress right side out.

This method can work well for dresses that have a simple hemline or are made of a lightweight fabric like cotton or polyester.

Method 2: Wear Leggings

If your dress is too short to wear on its own, consider pairing it with leggings. Leggings can add coverage to your legs and make the dress look longer. Choose leggings that are a similar color to the dress or go for a bold contrast.

When wearing leggings with a dress, choose a pair that is made of a thick material like cotton or spandex. This will help prevent them from looking too sheer or see-through.

Method 3: Pair with Boots

Another way to make a too short dress longer is to pair it with boots. Choose a pair of boots that are knee-high or over-the-knee to add coverage to your legs. This will create the illusion that the dress is longer than it really is.

When choosing boots to wear with a dress, consider the style and color of the dress. For example, if you have a floral dress, choose a pair of brown or black boots to match.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to make a too short dress longer:

  1. Measure the length you want to add to the dress.
  2. Cut a strip of fabric that's the same length as the measurement you took in step 1.
  3. Pin the fabric to the hemline of the dress, making sure the right sides are facing each other.
  4. Sew the fabric to the hemline using a straight stitch.
  5. Trim any excess fabric and turn the dress right side out.

Tips

When making a too short dress longer, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a fabric that matches the dress in color and texture.
  • Be careful not to make the dress too long, as this can look awkward.
  • Always use a sewing machine or needle and thread to attach the fabric to the hemline.
  • Consider taking the dress to a tailor for a professional alteration.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a too short dress longer:

1. Can I use fabric glue to add length to my dress?

While fabric glue can be used to hem pants or skirts, it's not recommended for adding length to a dress. This is because the fabric glue may not hold up well over time.

2. Can I use iron-on hem tape to add length to my dress?

Iron-on hem tape can be used to temporarily hem a dress, but it's not recommended for adding length. This is because the hem tape may not hold up well over time and the dress may end up looking uneven.

3. Can I add length to a dress that has a lace hemline?

Yes, you can add length to a dress that has a lace hemline. Simply sew a strip of matching lace to the bottom of the dress using a straight stitch.

Hair Care

When wearing a too short dress, your hair can play a big role in your overall look. Consider styling your hair in a way that complements the dress. For example, if you have a strapless dress, wear your hair in an updo to show off your shoulders. If you have a dress with a high neckline, wear your hair in a sleek ponytail to keep the focus on the dress.

Remember, making a too short dress longer doesn't have to be difficult or expensive. By following these tips and tricks, you can add length to your favorite dress and feel confident and comfortable wearing it.
