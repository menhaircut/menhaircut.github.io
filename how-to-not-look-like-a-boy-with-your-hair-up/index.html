How To Not Look Like A Boy With Your Hair Up

Written by Daniel Oct 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of always looking like a boy every time you put your hair up? Worry no more! With these simple tips and tricks, you can elevate your hair game and look fabulous even with your hair up.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in not looking like a boy with your hair up is to choose the right hairstyle. There are several hairstyles you can try, such as a messy bun, a sleek ponytail, or a braided updo. Once you've chosen your hairstyle, the next step is to accessorize. Headbands, hair clips, and scrunchies can add some femininity to your look.

Another way to avoid looking like a boy with your hair up is to create volume. You can do this by teasing your hair at the crown before putting it up. This will give your hair some height and make it look more feminine. Additionally, you can use a volumizing spray or mousse to add some extra oomph to your hair.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating a feminine hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Tease your hair at the crown to create volume
  3. Gather your hair into a ponytail, leaving some hair out at the front to frame your face
  4. Tie your hair with an elastic band
  5. If you're going for a messy bun, twist your ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins
  6. If you're going for a sleek ponytail, brush your ponytail to smooth out any bumps or kinks
  7. Add a headband or hair clip to accessorize

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose accessories that match your outfit
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles
  • Consider getting a haircut that flatters your face shape
  • Use a hairspray to help your hairstyle stay in place

FAQ

Q: What if I have short hair?

A: You can still create a feminine hairstyle with short hair. Try a pixie cut with a side-swept fringe, or add some volume with a texturizing spray.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people wash their hair every day, while others wash it once or twice a week. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining a healthy, shiny, and feminine look. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can create feminine hairstyles that will make you look and feel fabulous.
