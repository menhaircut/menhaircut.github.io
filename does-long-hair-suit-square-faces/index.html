Does Long Hair Suit Square Faces

Description

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can be a daunting task, especially if you have a square face. Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and square chin. People with square faces often wonder if long hair is a good option for them. In this blog post, we will explore whether long hair suits square faces and provide some tips and tricks for styling long hair with a square face.

How to

Before we dive into whether long hair suits square faces, let's first understand how to determine your face shape. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face. If your jawline is wider than your forehead and your face is roughly the same length and width, you likely have a square face.

Step by step

If you have determined that you have a square face, don't worry! Long hair can definitely suit square faces if styled correctly. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Choose a length that falls past your shoulders. Longer hair will help soften the angles of your face and create a more feminine look.
  2. Add layers to your hair to create movement and volume. Layers can help soften your strong jawline and add dimension to your hair.
  3. Avoid blunt cuts or straight hair. These can emphasize the angles of your face and make it appear more square.
  4. Consider a side part. A side part can help balance out your face and create a more flattering look.
  5. Add waves or curls to your hair. Adding some texture to your hair can help soften the angles of your face and create a more feminine look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling long hair with a square face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too voluminous on top. These can make your face appear even more square.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hair. Bangs can help balance out your face and create a more flattering look.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back. These can emphasize the angles of your face and make it appear more square.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles until you find one that you love. Everyone's face shape is unique, so what works for someone else may not work for you.

FAQ

Q: Can short hair suit square faces?

A: Yes, short hair can definitely suit square faces. However, it's important to choose a style that is tailored to your face shape. Consider a bob or pixie cut with layers and texture to soften your features.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail! Just make sure to add some volume or texture to your hair to soften your features.

Q: Should I avoid side bangs if I have a square face?

A: No, side bangs can actually be very flattering on square faces! Just make sure to choose a style that is tailored to your face shape.

Hair Care

When styling long hair with a square face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools on your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips in mind, you can confidently rock long hair with a square face. Remember, the key is to choose a style that is tailored to your face shape and experiment until you find what works best for you.
