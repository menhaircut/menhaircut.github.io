Description

Thick wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. It can look great when styled properly, but it can also be difficult to manage. Fortunately, there are plenty of men's hairstyles that work well with thick wavy hair. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best options for men who want to show off their natural texture.

How to Style

When it comes to styling thick wavy hair, the key is to embrace the texture. Don't try to fight it with too much product or too much heat. Instead, work with your hair's natural shape and use products that enhance its texture.

One of the best ways to style thick wavy hair is with a messy, textured look. To create this style, use a lightweight styling product like a sea salt spray or a texture spray. Apply the product to damp hair and then use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair. Let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while maintaining its natural texture.

Another popular option for men with thick wavy hair is the undercut. This style features short sides and a longer top that can be styled in a variety of ways. To create an undercut, have your barber or stylist fade the sides of your hair and leave the top longer. You can then style the top with a pomade or a wax for a sleek, polished look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling thick wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a lightweight styling product like a sea salt spray or a texture spray.
  3. Use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair, working the product into your hair.
  4. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while maintaining its natural texture.
  5. If you want a more polished look, use a pomade or a wax to style your hair.
  6. Use a comb or your fingers to create the shape you want.
  7. Finish with a hairspray or a finishing spray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining

Here are some tips for maintaining your thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy.
  • If you have longer hair, consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyles for thick wavy hair:

  • Q: Are there any hairstyles that don't work well with thick wavy hair?
  • A: While most hairstyles can be adapted to work with thick wavy hair, some styles like slicked-back hair or flat-top haircuts may be more difficult to achieve.
  • Q: How often should I wash my thick wavy hair?
  • A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most men with thick wavy hair can get away with washing their hair every other day.
  • Q: Can I still get a fade if I have thick wavy hair?
  • A: Yes, a fade can work well with thick wavy hair. Just make sure your barber or stylist knows how to work with your hair type.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair, as this can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

With these tips and styles, you can rock your thick wavy hair with confidence!
