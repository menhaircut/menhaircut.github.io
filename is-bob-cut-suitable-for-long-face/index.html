Is Bob Cut Suitable For Long Face

Written by Oliver Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you on the lookout for a new hairstyle but worried about how it might look on your long face shape? One of the most popular hairstyles that never goes out of style is the Bob cut. But is it suitable for those who have a long face shape? Let's find out!

Description

A Bob cut is a short to medium length hairstyle in which the hair is typically cut straight around the head, usually at jaw-level. It is a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can suit different face shapes, hair textures, and ages. However, when it comes to long face shapes, some variations of the Bob cut can flatter the face while others may accentuate its length.

How to

To determine if a Bob cut is suitable for your long face shape, you need to consider the length, angle, and layers of the cut. Here are some tips to help you achieve a Bob cut that flatters your face shape:

Step 1: Consider the Length

The length of your Bob cut should ideally fall between your chin and collarbone. This length helps to balance out the proportions of your face and create the illusion of width. Avoid going too short or too long, as this can make your face appear even longer.

Step 2: Choose the Right Angle

The angle of your Bob cut can also affect how it flatters your long face shape. A Bob cut with a slight angle towards the front can help to create the illusion of a shorter face. On the other hand, a Bob cut with a straight edge or an angle towards the back can make your face look longer.

Step 3: Add Layers

Adding layers to your Bob cut can help to soften your face shape and add volume to your hair. However, be careful not to add too many layers or make them too short, as this can create a choppy and unflattering look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when considering a Bob cut for your long face shape:

  • Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs to help shorten your forehead and balance out your face shape.
  • Avoid blunt cuts or severe angles that can elongate your face shape.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a wavy or textured Bob, to add dimension to your hair and face shape.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with long face shapes to help you choose the best Bob cut for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about Bob cuts and long face shapes:

Q: Can a Bob cut make my face look even longer?

A: Yes, a Bob cut with a straight edge or an angle towards the back can make your face look longer. It's important to choose a Bob cut with a slight angle towards the front to help create the illusion of a shorter face.

Q: Should I add layers to my Bob cut if I have a long face shape?

A: Yes, adding layers to your Bob cut can help to soften your face shape and add volume to your hair. However, be careful not to add too many layers or make them too short, as this can create a choppy and unflattering look.

Q: Can I wear a Bob cut if I have a high forehead?

A: Yes, you can wear a Bob cut if you have a high forehead. Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs to help shorten your forehead and balance out your face shape.

Hair Care

To maintain your Bob cut and keep it looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools or harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair nourished and healthy.

In conclusion, a Bob cut can be suitable for a long face shape if you choose the right length, angle, and layers. By following these tips and consulting with a hairstylist, you can achieve a Bob cut that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style.
