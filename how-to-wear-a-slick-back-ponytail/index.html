How To Wear A Slick Back Ponytail A Guide To Rocking This Classic Hairstyle

Written by William Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to look polished and put-together without spending hours on your hair? Look no further than the slick back ponytail, a classic hairstyle that's easy to achieve and perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to wear a slick back ponytail, step by step. We'll also share tips on how to make it work for your hair type and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

How to Wear a Slick Back Ponytail: Step by Step

Before we get started, here's a list of what you'll need:

  • Comb or brush
  • Hair elastic
  • Hair gel or pomade

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

It's best to start with freshly washed and dried hair for this hairstyle. If your hair is naturally curly or wavy, you may want to straighten it first to make it easier to slick back.

Step 2: Apply hair gel or pomade

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hands and run it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to distribute the product evenly.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Using a comb or brush, comb your hair straight back from your forehead. You may want to use a fine-toothed comb to create a sleeker look.

Step 4: Secure your hair in a ponytail

Once you've combed your hair back, gather it into a ponytail at the back of your head. Use a hair elastic to secure it in place.

Step 5: Smooth out any bumps

If you notice any bumps or flyaways, use a bit more hair gel or pomade to smooth them down. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Step 6: Finish with hairspray

To keep your slick back ponytail in place all day, finish with a spritz of hairspray.

Tips for Wearing a Slick Back Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your slick back ponytail:

  • Experiment with different hair gels or pomades to find one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you have a widow's peak or other hairline feature you don't want to emphasize, try leaving a few strands of hair loose at the front.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, you may want to add some volume to your ponytail by teasing it slightly at the crown.
  • A slick back ponytail works well with both casual and formal outfits, so feel free to wear it wherever you like!

Slick Back Ponytail FAQ

Can I wear a slick back ponytail with short hair?

Yes! While it may be easier to achieve this hairstyle with longer hair, you can still rock a slick back ponytail with shorter hair. Just make sure to use plenty of hair gel or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Is a slick back ponytail a good hairstyle for working out?

Definitely! A slick back ponytail is a great hairstyle for working out because it keeps your hair out of your face and stays in place during rigorous activity.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy with a slick back ponytail?

Use a small amount of hair gel or pomade and apply it evenly throughout your hair. Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy. You can also try using a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add texture to your hair.

Hair Care for a Slick Back Ponytail

Wearing a slick back ponytail regularly can put strain on your hairline and cause breakage. To keep your hair healthy, make sure to:

  • Take breaks from wearing a tight ponytail or updo.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips in mind, you can wear a slick back ponytail with confidence and style. Whether you're headed to work, a night out, or the gym, this versatile hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
