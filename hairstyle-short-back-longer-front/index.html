Hairstyle Short Back Longer Front A Trendy Look In 2023

Written by Luffy Sep 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both stylish and easy to maintain, the short back longer front haircut might be for you! This trendy look has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. In this article, we'll go over the basics of this hairstyle and provide you with some tips on how to achieve it. Let's get started!

What is a Short Back Longer Front Hairstyle?

A short back longer front hairstyle is a haircut that features shorter hair on the back of the head and longer hair on the front. This style is sometimes called a "long bob" or a "lob" and is popular among women who want a versatile and low-maintenance look. The length of the front hair can vary, but it typically falls anywhere between chin-length and shoulder-length.

How to Achieve a Short Back Longer Front Hairstyle

If you're interested in getting a short back longer front haircut, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving this trendy look:

Step 1: Choose the Right Length

The first step in getting a short back longer front hairstyle is to decide how long you want your hair to be. The length of the front hair will depend on your personal preference and face shape. If you have a round face, you may want to opt for longer front hair to help elongate your features. If you have a heart-shaped face, you may want to go for a shorter front to balance out your forehead.

Step 2: Find a Skilled Stylist

Once you've decided on the length of your hair, it's important to find a skilled stylist who can create the look you want. Look for a stylist who has experience cutting short back longer front hairstyles and who can provide you with recommendations based on your face shape and hair texture.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair will also play a role in the final look of your short back longer front hairstyle. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for layers to help reduce volume. If you have thin hair, you may want to go for a blunt cut to create the illusion of more volume.

Step 4: Maintain Your Haircut

Once you've achieved your short back longer front hairstyle, it's important to maintain your haircut to keep it looking its best. Schedule regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. You may also want to invest in some quality hair care products to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

Tips for Styling a Short Back Longer Front Haircut

Now that you know how to achieve a short back longer front hairstyle, here are some tips for styling it:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the front of your hair
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and movement
  • Use a straightener or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair
  • Accessorize with a headband or hair clips to add some extra style to your look

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short back longer front hairstyles:

Will a short back longer front hairstyle work for all face shapes?

Yes, a short back longer front hairstyle can work for all face shapes. It's important to work with a skilled stylist to find the right length and shape for your specific face shape.

Can I still wear my hair up with a short back longer front hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short back longer front hairstyle. Experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.

How often should I get my hair trimmed with a short back longer front hairstyle?

You should aim to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Short Back Longer Front Hairstyles

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short back longer front hairstyle looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools
  • Invest in a quality hairbrush to prevent tangles and breakage

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short back longer front hairstyle with confidence. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut or a trendy new style, this look is sure to turn heads. So why not give it a try?
