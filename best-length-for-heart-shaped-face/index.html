Best Length For Heart Shaped Face

Written by Oliver Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know that finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. Your face shape is characterized by a broad forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The key to finding the best length for heart shaped face is to balance these features and create a flattering look. In this article, we’ll explore the different options for heart shaped faces and give you some tips on how to choose the perfect length for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Length for Heart Shaped Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Heart Shaped Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Heart Shaped Face

Description

The best length for heart shaped face depends on your individual features and personal style. Generally, heart shaped faces look best with hair that falls below the chin and around the shoulders. This can help to balance out the width of the forehead and create a more proportional look. However, some heart shaped faces can pull off shorter styles, like a pixie cut or a bob, if they are styled correctly.

How to Choose the Best Length for Heart Shaped Face

When choosing the best length for your heart shaped face, consider the following factors:

  • Face Shape: Heart shaped faces look best with hair that balances out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin.
  • Hair Texture: Your hair texture will also impact the best length for your face shape. Fine hair might look better with shorter cuts, while thick hair can handle longer styles.
  • Personal Style: Ultimately, the best length for heart shaped face is the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when choosing the best length for heart shaped face:

  1. Start by identifying your face shape. If you’re not sure, take a selfie and look at the shape of your face.
  2. Consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you might want to opt for a shorter cut. If you have thick hair, you can go longer.
  3. Look for inspiration. Browse magazines or online galleries for hairstyles that appeal to you and might work with your face shape.
  4. Talk to your stylist. Once you’ve identified a few styles you like, consult with your stylist to see what will work best for your hair type and face shape.
  5. Try it out. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles until you find the perfect one for you.

Tips for Heart Shaped Face

Here are some additional tips for finding the best length for heart shaped face:

  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and volume.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your forehead look even wider.
  • Try a side part or center part to balance out your features.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best length for heart shaped face:

Can heart shaped faces pull off short hair?

Yes, some heart shaped faces can pull off shorter styles like a pixie cut or a bob. However, it’s important to choose a style that balances out your features and creates a proportional look.

What is the most flattering hairstyle for heart shaped face?

The most flattering hairstyle for heart shaped face is one that falls below the chin and around the shoulders. This length helps to balance out the width of the forehead and the narrowness of the chin.

Can heart shaped faces wear bangs?

Yes, heart shaped faces can wear bangs, but you should avoid blunt bangs that can make your forehead look even wider. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that add texture and movement to your hair.

Hair Care for Heart Shaped Face

Once you’ve found the best length for your heart shaped face, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care for heart shaped face:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

By following these tips, you can find the best length for heart shaped face and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.
