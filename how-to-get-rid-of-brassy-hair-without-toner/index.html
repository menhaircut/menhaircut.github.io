How To Get Rid Of Brassy Hair Without Toner

Written by Christine Aug 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read
How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair Without Toner | Hair Care Tips

If you have blonde or light-colored hair, you may be familiar with the dreaded brassiness that can occur over time. This unwanted warm, orange or yellow tones can make hair look dull and unflattering. While toner is a popular solution, not everyone wants to use it. The good news is that there are other ways to get rid of brassy hair without toner. In this article, we'll explore how to do so, as well as some tips and hair care advice to keep your locks looking their best.

How to Get Rid of Brassy Hair Without Toner

There are several ways to get rid of brassy hair without toner. Here are some of the most effective methods:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a popular solution for toning down brassiness. These shampoos contain purple pigments that neutralize the warm tones in your hair. To use, simply wet your hair and apply the shampoo, leaving it on for a few minutes before rinsing out. Use once or twice a week, depending on your hair's needs.

2. Try a Blue Shampoo

Similar to purple shampoos, blue shampoos contain blue pigments that can help neutralize brassiness in blonde hair. While not as popular as purple shampoos, they can be just as effective.

3. Use a Toning Conditioner

Toning conditioners work similarly to toning shampoos but are gentler on hair. They contain pigments that can help neutralize brassiness while also conditioning and moisturizing your hair. Use after shampooing and leave in for a few minutes before rinsing out.

4. Apply a Hair Mask

Hair masks are a great way to nourish and strengthen your hair while also toning down brassiness. Look for masks that contain ingredients like argan oil, keratin, or violet pigments. Apply to damp hair and leave on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing out.

5. Use Lemon Juice

Lemon juice is a natural remedy for brassy hair. The acid in lemon juice can help remove buildup and brighten hair. Simply mix equal parts lemon juice and water and apply to damp hair. Leave on for a few minutes before rinsing out.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know the different methods to get rid of brassy hair without toner, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

Step 1: Choose your method

Decide which method you want to use to get rid of brassiness. You can try one or a combination of methods for best results.

Step 2: Wash hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove buildup and prepare your hair for the treatment.

Step 3: Apply treatment

Apply your chosen treatment to your hair, following the instructions on the product. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Leave on

Leave the treatment on for the recommended time. This can vary depending on the product you're using, so make sure to read the instructions carefully.

Step 5: Rinse out

Rinse out the treatment with warm water, making sure to remove all product residue.

Step 6: Condition hair

Apply a conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy. Leave on for a few minutes before rinsing out.

Step 7: Dry and style hair

Dry and style your hair as usual, and enjoy your newly toned locks!

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Now that you've successfully toned down your brassiness, here are some tips to help maintain your hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and damage. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Minimize heat styling

Heat styling can cause damage and dryness to hair, which can exacerbate brassiness. Try to minimize heat styling as much as possible, and use a heat protectant when you do.

3. Protect hair from the sun

UV rays from the sun can also cause damage to hair and make brassiness more noticeable. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

4. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make hair look dull and lifeless. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I use toner on relaxed hair?

Yes, toner can be used on relaxed hair, but it's important to make sure your hair is healthy and strong enough to handle it. Consult with a stylist to determine if toner is right for you.

2. How often should I use purple shampoo?

This can vary depending on your hair's needs. Start by using it once a week and adjust as needed. If you notice your hair becoming too ashy or dull, cut back on use.

3. Can I use lemon juice on colored hair?

It's generally not recommended to use lemon juice on colored hair, as it can cause fading and damage. Stick to using it on natural hair.

Hair Care is Key

Getting rid of brassy hair without toner is possible with the right methods and hair care routine. By using the tips and advice in this article, you can keep your hair looking healthy, vibrant, and free of brassiness. Try out different methods and find what works best for your hair type and needs.
