When Did Big Hair Go Out Of Style

Written by Christine Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Big hair was a popular trend in the 1980s and early 1990s. This hairstyle was characterized by voluminous curls, waves, and lots of hairspray. However, this trend eventually faded away, and many people wonder when big hair went out of style. In this blog post, we will explore the history of big hair and when it became less popular.

Description

Big hair was a style that was popularized by celebrities and musicians in the 1980s. This hairstyle was often achieved with the use of large rollers, curling irons, and lots of hairspray. The goal was to create a voluminous look that was both glamorous and eye-catching.

However, as the 1990s approached, big hair started to lose its popularity. Many people began to opt for more natural and simpler hairstyles. The grunge movement, which was characterized by messy, unkempt hair, also played a role in the decline of big hair.

How to Achieve Big Hair

If you are interested in achieving big hair, there are several steps you can take. First, you will need to invest in some quality hair products, including volumizing shampoo, conditioner, and hairspray. You may also want to consider using hot rollers, a curling iron, or a round brush to create volume and lift.

Once you have the right products and tools, you can start by washing and conditioning your hair. Then, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots and blow-dry your hair upside down to create lift. Next, use a curling iron or hot rollers to create curls or waves. Finally, finish off the look with a generous amount of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve big hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using volumizing products.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down to create lift.
  4. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create curls or waves.
  5. Tease your hair at the crown to create more volume.
  6. Finish off the look with a generous amount of hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Big Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining big hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of natural oils and make it harder to style.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can flatten the volume.
  • When sleeping, use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to style.

FAQs

What other hairstyles were popular in the 1990s?

In the 1990s, other popular hairstyles included the Rachel cut, the bob, and the pixie cut.

What products should I use to achieve big hair?

You should use volumizing shampoo, conditioner, mousse or spray, and hairspray.

Is big hair making a comeback?

While big hair has not yet made a full comeback, some elements of the style have been seen on the runway and in fashion magazines.

Hair Care

Regardless of the hairstyle you choose, it is important to take good care of your hair. This includes washing it regularly, using quality hair products, and avoiding excessive heat and styling. By following these tips, you can achieve healthy, beautiful hair that looks great in any style.


In conclusion, big hair was a popular trend in the 1980s and early 1990s. However, as the 1990s approached, big hair started to lose its popularity. Many people began to opt for more natural and simpler hairstyles. While big hair has not yet made a full comeback, some elements of the style have been seen on the runway and in fashion magazines.
