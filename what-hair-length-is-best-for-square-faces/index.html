What Hair Length Is Best For Square Faces

Written by Robby Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face, you may be wondering what hair length is best for you. Square faces have a strong, angular jawline and a forehead that is almost as wide as the cheekbones. The right haircut can soften these features and bring balance to your face. In this article, we'll explore what hair length is best for square faces, and provide some tips on how to style your hair to flatter your face shape.

Description

Square faces have a very strong, angular jawline and a forehead that is almost as wide as the cheekbones. The right haircut can soften these features and bring balance to your face. The key to finding the right hair length for a square face is to bring attention away from the jawline and toward the top of the head. Haircuts that are longer on top and shorter on the sides can help to create the illusion of a more oval face shape.

How to Choose the Right Hair Length

The best hair length for square faces is one that brings attention away from the jawline and towards the top of the head. Haircuts that are longer on top and shorter on the sides can help to create the illusion of a more oval face shape. This can be achieved with a variety of hairstyles, including:

  • Long layers
  • Shoulder-length hair
  • Medium-length hair with layers
  • Short bobs

It's important to avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make the jawline appear even more prominent. Bangs can also be a good option for square faces, as they can help to soften the forehead and bring attention to the eyes.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've found the right hair length for your square face, it's important to style it in a way that flatters your features. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair
  3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume
  4. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the right hair length and style for your square face, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray

Frequently Asked Questions

Can square faces pull off short hair?

While short haircuts can work for some square faces, it's important to avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make the jawline appear even more prominent. Instead, opt for a longer pixie cut or a short bob that is longer on top and shorter on the sides.

Are bangs good for square faces?

Yes, bangs can be a good option for square faces, as they can help to soften the forehead and bring attention to the eyes. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs for your face shape. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs can be a good option for square faces.

Can square faces pull off long hair?

Yes, square faces can pull off long hair, but it's important to add layers to create movement and soften the jawline. A classic long-layered haircut can be a good option for square faces.

Hair Care for Square Faces

When it comes to hair care for square faces, the most important thing is to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep it moisturized. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

By following these tips, you can find the right hair length and style for your square face, and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
