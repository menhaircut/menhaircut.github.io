How To Cut Long Hair Straight At Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair is a beautiful asset to have, but it can be difficult to maintain. Trimming your hair is an essential part of caring for it and keeping it healthy. Cutting long hair straight at the back can be a challenging task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. In this blog post, we will guide you through a step-by-step process of how to cut long hair straight at the back.

How to Cut Long Hair Straight at Back:

Before you start cutting your hair, it's essential to have the right tools. Here are the tools you'll need:

  • Sharp hairdressing scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Mirror

Now that you have your tools let's get started with the process of cutting long hair straight at the back.

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the tools you need. You can purchase hairdressing scissors and a comb from any beauty supply store. If you don't have clips, you can use hair ties to keep your hair in place while cutting.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

To cut your hair straight at the back, you'll need to section your hair into two sections. Part your hair down the middle, from the crown of your head to the nape of your neck. Use clips to secure each section of hair in place.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Start by cutting the hair in the back section. Take a small section of hair at the bottom of the back section, and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut straight across using hairdressing scissors. Continue doing this until you have cut all the hair in the back section.

Once you have finished cutting the back section, release the front section, and comb it straight down. Cut the hair in the front section the same way you cut the hair in the back section. Make sure both sections are the same length.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair Straight at Back:

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before cutting.
  • Use sharp hairdressing scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Start by cutting small sections of hair, then work your way up to larger sections.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Check the length of your hair regularly by using a mirror to view the back of your head.

FAQs:

Q: How often should I cut my long hair?

A: It's recommended to cut long hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I cut my hair straight at back by myself?

A: Yes, you can. However, it's recommended to have someone help you to ensure you cut your hair evenly.

Hair Care After Cutting Long Hair:

After cutting your hair, it's essential to take care of it to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as a straightener or curling iron too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

That's it! Follow these steps to cut your long hair straight at the back and keep it healthy. Remember to take your time and be patient. With practice, you'll become an expert at cutting your hair straight.
