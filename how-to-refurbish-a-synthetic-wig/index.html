How To Refurbish A Synthetic Wig Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you own a synthetic wig, you know how convenient it is to have a quick and easy solution for a bad hair day. However, synthetic wigs can become tangled, frizzy, and dull over time. Fortunately, there are several ways to refurbish your synthetic wig and bring it back to life. In this article, we'll discuss how to refurbish a synthetic wig step by step, answer some frequently asked questions, and provide some hair care tips to keep your wig looking fresh.

How to Refurbish a Synthetic Wig

Step 1: Detangle Your Wig

The first step in refurbishing your synthetic wig is to detangle it. Use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to gently comb through the wig, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots. Be patient and gentle, and avoid pulling or tugging at the hair.

Step 2: Wash Your Wig

After detangling, it's time to wash your wig. Fill a sink or basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swirl the wig in the water for about two minutes, then rinse it thoroughly with cool water. Avoid rubbing or twisting the hair, as this can cause damage.

Step 3: Condition Your Wig

Next, apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair and gently work it through with your fingers. Let the conditioner sit for three to five minutes, then rinse it out with cool water. This will help to soften the hair and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Dry Your Wig

After washing and conditioning, gently squeeze the excess water out of your wig. Do not rub or wring the hair, as this can cause damage. Lay the wig flat on a towel and let it air dry. Avoid using a hair dryer or other heat source, as this can damage the hair fibers.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Once your wig is dry, it's time to style it. Use a wig brush or your fingers to gently style the hair, starting at the ends and working your way up. You can also use a small amount of wig spray or mousse to help hold the style.

Tips for Refurbishing Your Synthetic Wig

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results when refurbishing your synthetic wig:

  • Use cool water when washing and rinsing your wig, as hot water can damage the hair fibers.
  • Avoid using regular shampoo or conditioner on your wig, as these products can be too harsh and cause damage.
  • Do not brush your wig when it is wet, as this can cause tangles and damage.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or in a wig box to help maintain its shape.

FAQs About Refurbishing a Synthetic Wig

Q: Can I use regular shampoo on my synthetic wig?

A: No, regular shampoo can be too harsh for synthetic wigs and can cause damage. Use a wig shampoo specifically designed for synthetic hair.

Q: How often should I wash my synthetic wig?

A: It depends on how often you wear your wig and how much product you use. Generally, you should wash your wig every six to eight wears.

Q: Can I use a hair dryer to dry my wig?

A: No, heat can damage synthetic hair fibers. Let your wig air dry on a towel or wig stand.

Hair Care Tips for Synthetic Wigs

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your synthetic wig looking fresh:

  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat sources such as ovens, grills, and hair dryers.
  • Store your wig in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can cause frizz and damage.
  • Use a wig brush or comb specifically designed for synthetic hair to avoid damaging the fibers.

By following these steps and tips, you can refurbish your synthetic wig and keep it looking fresh and stylish for longer. With a little care and maintenance, your wig can be a convenient and fashionable solution for any bad hair day.
