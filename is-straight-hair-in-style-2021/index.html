Is Straight Hair In Style 2021

Written by Robby Nov 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're wondering whether straight hair is in style for 2021, the answer is yes! Straight hair has always been a classic look that never goes out of style. However, the way in which it is styled can vary depending on current trends.

Description

Straight hair is a popular hairstyle that can be achieved using various methods, including using a straightening iron, chemical relaxers or natural methods such as air-drying or blow-drying. It is a low-maintenance style that can be worn in a variety of ways, including sleek and straight, or with a slight wave or curl.

How to Achieve Straight Hair

There are several ways to achieve straight hair. One of the most popular methods is to use a straightening iron. To do this, first, wash and condition your hair. Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from the heat. Then, section your hair into small sections and run the straightening iron through each section until your entire head is straightened.

Another way to achieve straight hair is through chemical relaxers. However, this method can be damaging to your hair if not done correctly. It is recommended to have a professional stylist perform this process.

Lastly, you can achieve straight hair through natural methods such as air-drying or blow-drying. To air-dry, simply let your hair dry naturally after washing it. To blow-dry, use a round brush and blow-dryer to straighten your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt or oil from your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant to your hair to protect it from damage. This is especially important if you plan to use a straightening iron.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections to make it easier to straighten. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 4: Straighten Your Hair

Run the straightening iron through each section of your hair until your entire head is straightened. Be sure to move the iron slowly and smoothly to avoid damaging your hair.

Tips for Styling Straight Hair

Here are some tips for styling straight hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair
  • Use a shine serum to add shine to your hair
  • Try adding subtle waves or curls to your hair for a more textured look
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve straight hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can achieve straight hair without using heat. Air-drying or blow-drying your hair using a round brush can help straighten your hair naturally.

Q: Will straightening my hair damage it?

A: Straightening your hair can cause damage if not done correctly. Be sure to use a heat protectant and move the straightening iron slowly and smoothly through your hair. It is also recommended to limit the use of heat styling tools to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Straight Hair

To keep your straight hair looking its best, it is important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for straight hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Conclusion

Straight hair is definitely in style for 2021, and it's a classic look that never goes out of fashion. Whether you choose to straighten your hair using heat or natural methods, be sure to take good care of it to keep it looking its best.
