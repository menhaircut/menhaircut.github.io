What s My Face Shape App Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of guessing your face shape? Do you want to know what hairstyle suits you best? Look no further, as the "what's my face shape app" can help you determine your face shape in just a few clicks. In this article, we will go through how to use the app, step by step guide, tips, and hair care for your particular face shape. Let's dive in!

Description

The "what's my face shape app" is an app that helps you determine your face shape. It uses a photo of your face to analyze your facial features and gives you your face shape. This app is available for both iOS and Android users.

How to Use the App

Step 1: Download the App

The first step is to download the app on your smartphone. You can find it on the App Store or Google Play Store.

Step 2: Take a Photo

The second step is to take a photo of your face. Make sure that your face is centered and there are no obstructions in the photo.

Step 3: Upload the Photo

The third step is to upload the photo on the app. The app will then analyze your facial features and give you your face shape.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Take a Photo

Make sure that you take a photo of your face in good lighting. Avoid taking selfies with filters or any other alterations, as it may affect the accuracy of the analysis.

Step 2: Upload the Photo

Once you have taken a photo, upload it on the app. The app will ask you to align some dots on your face to ensure that the analysis is accurate.

Step 3: Get Your Face Shape

After uploading the photo, the app will analyze your facial features and give you your face shape. The app will also give you suggestions on what hairstyles suit your face shape best.

Tips

Here are some tips to get the most accurate analysis:

  • Take a photo in good lighting
  • Avoid using filters or any other alterations
  • Make sure your face is centered in the photo
  • Align the dots on your face as accurately as possible

FAQ

Is the "what's my face shape app" accurate?

Yes, the app is accurate as it uses facial recognition technology to analyze your facial features.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes, you can use the app on multiple devices as long as you have the same account.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, the app is free to use. However, there may be some in-app purchases for additional features.

Hair Care for Different Face Shapes

Here are some hair care tips for different face shapes:

Oval Face

For an oval face, almost any hairstyle works. You can try a layered haircut or a side part to accentuate your cheekbones.

Round Face

For a round face, a high ponytail or an updo can elongate your face shape. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs.

Square Face

For a square face, soft waves or layers can soften your jawline. Avoid straight bangs and blunt cuts.

Heart Face

For a heart face, a bob or a pixie cut can complement your face shape. Avoid heavy bangs and long hair.

Diamond Face

For a diamond face, a side-swept bang or a layered haircut can balance your face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add volume on top.

There you have it, your ultimate guide to the "what's my face shape app". With this app, you can now determine your face shape and choose the best hairstyle for you. Remember, your face shape is unique, so embrace it!
