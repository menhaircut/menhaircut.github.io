What Hairstyle Looks Best On Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing tips and tricks on what hairstyle looks best on round face. From short to long hairstyles, we'll cover it all. So, let's get started!

Description

A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of a hairstyle for a round face is to elongate and slim down the face shape. Choosing the right hairstyle can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face, which is considered the most flattering face shape.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

1. Length

The length of your hair can make a big difference in how it complements your face shape. Long hair can elongate a round face, while short hair can make it appear wider. If you prefer shorter hairstyles, consider a pixie cut or a bob that falls just below the chin.

2. Layers

Layers can add volume and texture to your hair, but be careful not to add too much volume around your cheeks. Opt for long, face-framing layers that start below the chin.

3. Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to complement a round face, but make sure to choose the right style. Side-swept bangs that hit just below the cheekbone can create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Avoid blunt bangs that fall straight across the forehead.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

Follow these steps to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

Before you can choose the right hairstyle for your face shape, you need to determine what your face shape is. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back from your face. Use a washable marker to draw the outline of your face on the mirror. Step back and take a look at the shape you've drawn. Is it round, square, oval, or heart-shaped?

2. Research Hairstyles

Once you've determined your face shape, start researching hairstyles that complement it. Look for hairstyles on celebrities or models with a similar face shape to yours. Save pictures of hairstyles you like to show your hairstylist.

3. Consult with a Hairstylist

Book a consultation with a hairstylist who specializes in your hair type and texture. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like and discuss which ones would work best for your face shape. Your hairstylist can also recommend haircuts or styles that will complement your features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it's important to maintain it. Here are a few tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, use volumizing products to add body. If you have curly hair, use products that enhance your curls.

2. Schedule Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help maintain the shape and style of your hair. Schedule appointments with your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause split ends. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools, and limit your use to once or twice a week.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume around your cheeks, such as blunt bangs or short, choppy layers. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can make your face appear wider.

Can I wear my hair in a center part if I have a round face?

A center part can work for some people with a round face, but it can also make your face appear wider. Consider a side part or a deep side part instead.

What are some hairstyles that look best on a round face?

Long, face-framing layers, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles that add height at the crown can all complement a round face. Consider a long bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers.

Hair Care for Round Face

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are a few tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to remove dirt and oil. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Limit your use of heat styling tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, to once or twice a week. Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face. Remember to consult with a hairstylist who can recommend the best haircut or style for your features. With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you'll be able to look and feel your best!
