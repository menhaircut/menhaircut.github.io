Long Haircuts For Round Faces 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Jun 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you someone with a round face and long hair? Do you find it challenging to choose the right haircut that flatters your face shape? Look no further! In this blog post, we will discuss the latest long haircuts for round faces 2021.

Description

Round faces have a circular shape with a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. If you have a round face, you need a haircut that adds length and definition to your face. Long haircuts are perfect for round faces as they create an illusion of length and draw attention away from your face's roundness.

How To

Before getting a haircut, it is essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Look for inspiration from magazines or social media platforms and choose a haircut that suits your preferences.

When you visit a stylist, communicate your preferences and ask for their recommendations. They can suggest a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and face-framing layers are some popular long haircuts for round faces.

Step by Step

Step 1: Choose a long haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Communicate your preferences and ask for recommendations from a stylist.

Step 3: Get your hair cut and styled by a professional stylist.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a long haircut for round faces:

  • Opt for long layers and face-framing layers to add definition to your face.
  • Side-swept bangs can create an illusion of length and slim down your face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts and one-length haircuts as they can make your face look wider.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories and styles to add variety to your long haircut.

FAQ

1. What are the best long haircuts for round faces?

The best long haircuts for round faces are long layers, face-framing layers, and side-swept bangs.

2. Can I wear my long hair straight if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your long hair straight if you have a round face. However, it is recommended to add layers and texture to your hair to create an illusion of length and slim down your face.

3. How can I style my long haircut for a special occasion?

You can style your long haircut in different ways for a special occasion. You can try a half-up, half-down hairstyle, a sleek ponytail, or loose waves. Adding hair accessories like hair clips or headbands can also elevate your hairstyle.

Hair Care

Long haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote hair growth.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, long haircuts for round faces can be flattering and stylish. Experiment with different styles, and don't be afraid to try something new!
