Short Haircuts For Ladies With Oval Face

Written by Petter Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face, you are in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, if you are looking for a short haircut that will flatter your features, we have got you covered. In this article, we will be discussing the best short haircuts for ladies with oval face.

Description

An oval face shape is characterized by balanced proportions and a slightly rounded jawline. Your forehead and cheekbones are usually the widest parts of your face, while your chin is slightly narrower. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle.

How-to

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for an oval face, it is essential to consider your hair texture and personal style. Short haircuts can be edgy, chic, or classic, depending on how you style them.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose a short haircut that complements your personal style and lifestyle.
  3. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.
  4. Use styling products that enhance your hair texture and add volume or shine.
  5. Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for your oval face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or structured, as they can make your face look longer.
  • Choose a haircut that adds volume to the top of your head to balance out your face shape.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for you.
  • Consider adding layers to your haircut to add movement and texture.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for ladies with oval face:

Q. Can I wear bangs with an oval face?

A. Yes, you can wear bangs with an oval face. However, it is essential to choose a bang style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Q. Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

A. No, a short haircut can actually balance out an oval face shape by adding volume to the top of the head.

Q. Can I wear a pixie cut with an oval face?

A. Yes, a pixie cut can look fantastic on an oval face. However, it is essential to choose a style that complements your hair texture and personal style.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but it is still essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Keep your hair hydrated by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Overall, short haircuts can be a great choice for ladies with oval face, as they can flatter your features and enhance your personal style. Just remember to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
