Best Brush To Slick Back Hair Your Ultimate Guide

Written by William Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the best brush to slick back your hair? If so, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll explore the top brushes for slicking back hair, as well as provide tips and tricks for achieving the perfect look. Whether you have short or long hair, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description: What is Slicked Back Hair?

Slicked back hair is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It involves using a hair product to smooth the hair back from the forehead and temples, creating a sleek and polished look. This style is perfect for formal occasions or for anyone who wants to look sharp and put-together. The key to achieving the perfect slicked back look is to use the right brush.

How to Slick Back Hair: Step by Step Guide

Before we dive into the best brushes for slicked back hair, let's go over the basic steps for achieving this style:

  1. Wash and dry your hair. Slicked back hair looks best on clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a hair product. Use a pomade, gel, or wax to help hold your hair in place.
  3. Brush your hair back. Use a brush to smooth your hair back from your forehead and temples.
  4. Style as desired. You can leave your hair slicked back or add a part for a more polished look.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair

Now that you know the basic steps for slicking back your hair, here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Start with a small amount of product. You can always add more if needed.
  • Use a comb to create a part if desired.
  • Experiment with different brushes to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't forget to smooth the sides and back of your hair for a polished look.
  • Use hairspray to help hold your style in place.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What type of brush is best for slicking back hair?

The best brush for slicking back hair is a boar bristle brush. Boar bristle brushes are gentle on the hair and scalp, and they help distribute natural oils throughout the hair, which can help prevent breakage and split ends.

Can I slick back long hair?

Yes, you can slick back long hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve the look without the right brush and products. We recommend using a pomade or wax to help hold your hair in place, and a paddle brush to smooth your hair back.

How do I prevent my hair from looking greasy when slicked back?

Use a small amount of product and avoid applying it directly to your roots. You can also try using dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil.

Hair Care: How to Care for Slicked Back Hair

Now that you know how to achieve the perfect slicked back look, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for slicked back hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent product buildup.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Slicked back hair is a classic hairstyle that can be worn by anyone. With the right brush and products, you can achieve the perfect look in just a few easy steps. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you. We hope this guide has been helpful in your quest for the best brush to slick back your hair!
