Low Fade Haircut Long Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Oct 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to try a new haircut that is stylish and low-maintenance? Look no further than the low fade haircut long top. This trendy haircut combines a low fade on the sides with longer hair on top, creating a versatile and stylish look that can be dressed up or down. In this guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know about the low fade haircut long top, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Description

The low fade haircut long top is a modern twist on the classic short back and sides haircut. The sides of the hair are trimmed short and tapered down to the skin, while the hair on top is left longer and styled to suit your preferences. This haircut is ideal for those who want a stylish and low-maintenance look that is easy to style and maintain.

How to Get a Low Fade Haircut Long Top

To get a low fade haircut long top, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Decide how long you want the hair on top to be. This will depend on your personal preference and the style you want to achieve.

Step 2: Trim the Sides

Ask your barber to trim the sides of your hair with clippers, starting with a longer guard and gradually working down to a shorter guard at the bottom. This will create a tapered effect.

Step 3: Blend the Hair

Use scissors to blend the hair on top with the sides, creating a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair on top using your preferred product, such as pomade, wax, or gel. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Styling a Low Fade Haircut Long Top

Here are some tips for styling a low fade haircut long top:

- Use a small amount of product to avoid a greasy look. - Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. - Use a comb to create a neat and tidy look. - Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to go for added volume and hold. - Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

FAQ

1. Is the low fade haircut long top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the low fade haircut long top is suitable for all hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

2. How often do I need to get a trim?

We recommend getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

3. Can I style my hair differently every day?

Yes, the low fade haircut long top is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

Hair Care

To keep your low fade haircut long top looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons, as these can damage your hair. - Use a heat protectant spray if you do need to use hot tools. - Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage. - Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance low fade haircut long top. Experiment with different styles and find the one that suits you best.
