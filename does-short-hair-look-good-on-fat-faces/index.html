Does Short Hair Look Good On Fat Faces

Written by Luffy Sep 21, 2023 · 3 min read

Short hair has always been a popular hairstyle choice for women. However, when it comes to fat faces, some women may feel unsure about whether or not short hair will suit them. In this blog post, we will answer the question, does short hair look good on fat faces?

Description

Before we dive into the answer, let’s define what we mean by “fat face.” A fat face is a face with fuller cheeks and a rounder jawline. Some women may feel self-conscious about their face shape, but it’s important to remember that beauty comes in all shapes and sizes.

How to

So, does short hair look good on fat faces? The answer is yes! Short hair can actually be a great option for women with fat faces. Short haircuts can help to balance out the roundness of the face, making it appear slimmer and more angular. Additionally, short hair can draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones, creating a more defined look.

Step by Step

If you’re considering a short haircut and have a fat face, here are some steps you can take to ensure that it looks great:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist. They can help you choose a haircut that will flatter your face shape.
  2. Consider a haircut with layers or angles. These can help to create a more angular look and add dimension.
  3. Opt for shorter hairstyles that fall above the chin. These can help to elongate the neck and balance out the roundness of the face.
  4. Choose a hairstyle with side-swept bangs. These can help to create a more defined look and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling short hair on a fat face:

  • Avoid hairstyles with heavy volume at the sides, as this can make the face appear wider.
  • Experiment with different hair textures, such as waves or curls, to add dimension to your hair.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension in your hair.
  • Accessorize with earrings or necklaces to draw attention away from the face.

FAQ

Q: Can women with fat faces wear short pixie cuts?

A: Yes! Pixie cuts can be a great option for women with fat faces. However, it’s important to choose a pixie cut that is tailored to your face shape. Consider opting for a pixie cut with longer layers or side-swept bangs to create a more defined look.

Q: Will a bob haircut look good on a fat face?

A: Absolutely! Bobs can be a great option for women with fat faces. Consider opting for a longer bob that falls just above the shoulders to create a more elongated look.

Hair Care

Regardless of your face shape or haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

In conclusion, short hair can look great on fat faces. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create a look that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
