How To Cut A Man s Hair Using Clippers And Scissors

Written by William Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting your own hair or someone else's hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're not a professional hairdresser. However, with the right tools and guidance, it's possible to give a good haircut using clippers and scissors. In this article, we'll walk you through the process step by step so you can achieve a clean and professional-looking cut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Man's Hair Using Clippers and Scissors
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for a Successful Haircut
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After the Haircut

How to Cut a Man's Hair Using Clippers and Scissors

The first thing you'll need to do is gather your tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, hair clippers, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It's also a good idea to have a mirror and a cape or towel to protect clothing.

Before you begin, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can make it difficult to cut and can cause the hair to stick together, making it harder to cut evenly.

Step 1: Start with the Clippers

Begin the haircut with the clippers by choosing the right guard size. The guard size will depend on how short you want the hair. Start with a higher guard size and work your way down until you reach the desired length.

Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown of the head. Use long, smooth strokes with the clippers, following the natural curve of the head.

Step 2: Use Scissors for the Top

Once you've finished the sides and back with the clippers, it's time to move on to the top of the head. Use the scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length.

Start at the front and work your way towards the back, cutting small sections of hair at a time. It's important to use a comb to keep the hair straight and to ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Blend the Sides and Top

The next step is to blend the sides and top of the hair together. To do this, use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle, creating a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Use the comb to lift the hair up and cut at a slight angle, blending the hair together. It's important to take your time and to make small cuts at a time to avoid cutting too much hair.

Step 4: Trim the Sideburns and Neckline

Finally, use the clippers and scissors to trim the sideburns and neckline. Use the clippers to create a clean line around the ears and the back of the neck. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs and to blend the hairline into the neck.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting a man's hair using clippers and scissors:

  1. Gather your tools
  2. Clean and dry the hair
  3. Start with the clippers
  4. Use scissors for the top
  5. Blend the sides and top
  6. Trim the sideburns and neckline

Tips for a Successful Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a man's hair:

  • Start with a higher guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair
  • Use a comb to keep the hair straight and to ensure an even cut
  • Take your time and make small cuts at a time
  • Use the mirror to check your progress
  • Don't cut wet hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a man's hair:

Can I cut my own hair?

Yes, you can cut your own hair using clippers and scissors. However, it can be difficult to cut the back of your head, so you may need someone to help you.

What kind of scissors should I use?

You should use hairdressing scissors, which are sharper and more precise than regular scissors.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want it. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style.

Hair Care After the Haircut

After the haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair healthy and shiny

With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you can confidently cut a man's hair using clippers and scissors. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll have a great-looking haircut in no time!
