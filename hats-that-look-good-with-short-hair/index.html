Hats That Look Good With Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling like you can't wear hats because of your short hair? Fear not, because we have compiled a list of hats that look good with short hair. From beanies to fedoras, we've got you covered. Keep reading for tips, tricks, and hair care advice to make sure you're rocking your short hair and hat look with confidence.

How To Choose A Hat That Complements Short Hair

When it comes to choosing a hat for short hair, the key is to find one that complements your facial features and hair texture. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider the shape of your face. If you have a round face, opt for hats with sharp angles, such as fedoras. If you have a more angular face, try a beanie or a beret.
  • Think about your hair texture. If you have curly hair, opt for a hat with a looser fit, such as a floppy sunhat. If you have straight hair, try a structured cap or a newsboy hat.
  • Choose a hat that fits well. A hat that is too tight or too loose can throw off the balance of your outfit.

Top Hats That Look Good with Short Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a hat, here are some of our top picks:

Beanies

A classic beanie is a great option for short hair. It's versatile and can be worn with a variety of outfits. Try a slouchy beanie for a more relaxed look, or a fitted beanie for a more polished look.

Fedoras

Fedoras are a great option for those with a more angular face shape. They add a touch of sophistication to any outfit and come in a variety of materials, such as wool or straw.

Berets

Berets are a French-inspired hat that can add a touch of elegance to any outfit. They work well with short hair because they don't add much volume to the top of the head.

Baseball Caps

A baseball cap is a great option for those looking for a more casual look. They come in a variety of colors and materials, and can be worn with a variety of outfits.

Hair Care Tips for Wearing Hats

Wearing a hat can sometimes lead to flat or static hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great while wearing a hat:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and add texture to your hair.

FAQ About Hats That Look Good with Short Hair

Can I wear a hat if I have short hair?

Absolutely! There are plenty of hats that look great with short hair.

What kind of hat should I wear if I have curly hair?

Try a hat with a looser fit, such as a floppy sunhat, to avoid flattening your curls.

What kind of hat should I wear if I have straight hair?

Try a structured cap or a newsboy hat to add some volume to your hair.

How can I keep my hair looking good while wearing a hat?

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, blow dry your hair upside down, and use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.

In Conclusion

Whether you're wearing a beanie or a fedora, there are plenty of hats that look great with short hair. Keep in mind your facial features and hair texture when choosing a hat, and follow our hair care tips to keep your hair looking great. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking your short hair and hat look with confidence.
