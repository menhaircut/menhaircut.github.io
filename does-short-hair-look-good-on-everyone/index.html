Does Short Hair Look Good On Everyone

Written by Petter Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you contemplating the idea of chopping off your long locks and going for a shorter hairstyle? Maybe you're wondering if short hair will look good on you. The truth is, while short hair can look great on many people, it's not necessarily a one-size-fits-all solution. In this article, we'll explore the question, "Does short hair look good on everyone?" and provide tips on how to determine if it's the right choice for you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine if Short Hair Will Look Good on You
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Short Hair
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair

  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Determine if Short Hair Will Look Good on You

The first thing to consider when deciding if short hair will look good on you is your face shape. While short hair can be flattering on many face shapes, some styles may be more flattering on certain shapes than others. For example, a pixie cut can be great for heart-shaped faces, while a bob may be better for oval faces.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have thin, fine hair, a short cut may make your hair look even thinner. On the other hand, if you have thick, curly hair, a short cut may be a great way to show off your natural texture.

Lastly, consider your personal style and comfort level. Short hair can be a dramatic change, and it takes some time to get used to. Make sure you're comfortable with the idea of a shorter cut before taking the plunge.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Short Hair

If you've decided that short hair is for you, here's a step-by-step guide to getting the look:

  1. Find inspiration: Look for pictures of short hairstyles that you like and bring them to your hairdresser.
  2. Consult with your hairdresser: Your hairdresser can help you determine the best short style for your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Make the cut: Once you and your hairdresser have decided on a style, it's time to make the cut.
  4. Style your new look: Your hairdresser can show you how to style your new short hair, or you can experiment with different styling products and techniques at home.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some tips for styling short hair:

  • Invest in good styling products: Short hair requires different styling products than long hair. Look for products specifically designed for short hairstyles, such as pomade or texturizing spray.
  • Experiment with different styles: Short hair can be surprisingly versatile. Try different styles, such as a messy pixie or a slicked-back bob, to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize: Short hair is a great canvas for accessories. Try adding a headband or hair clips to add some interest to your look.

FAQs about Short Hair

Q: Will short hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the style you choose. A bob that hits at the chin can actually make your face look slimmer, while a super-short pixie may make your face appear rounder.

Q: Will short hair be easier to maintain?

A: Short hair can be easier to maintain than long hair, but it still requires regular trims and styling.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short cut?

A: Yes! While it may take some creativity, you can still wear your hair up with a short cut. Try using bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

  • Get regular trims: Short hair can start to look unkempt if it's not trimmed regularly. Plan on getting a trim every 4-6 weeks.
  • Protect your hair from heat: Short hair is more susceptible to heat damage than long hair, so be sure to use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Keep your hair moisturized: Short hair can become dry and brittle, so use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner and consider using a weekly deep conditioning treatment.

So, does short hair look good on everyone? The answer is no, but it can look great on many people. Ultimately, the decision to go short is a personal one that should be based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. With the right cut and styling, short hair can be a chic and stylish look that's easy to maintain.
