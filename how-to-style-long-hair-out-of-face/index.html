How To Style Long Hair Out Of Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Dec 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair can be beautiful, but it can also be a hassle when it constantly falls in your face. Whether you’re working out, running errands, or just trying to keep your hair out of your eyes, there are plenty of ways to style long hair out of your face. In this guide, we’ll go over some popular hairstyles and techniques to keep your hair out of your face while still looking stylish.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Long Hair Out of Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Long Hair Out of Face
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Long Hair

How to Style Long Hair Out of Face

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, it’s important to note that the key to keeping your hair out of your face is to use the right products and techniques. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use bobby pins and hair ties that match your hair color to keep them discreet.
  • Apply a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.
  • Use a headband or scarf to keep hair off of your forehead.
  • Consider trimming your bangs or layers if they are constantly falling in your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Braids

Braids are a classic way to keep your hair out of your face. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle.
  3. Take a section of hair from one side of your head and begin a French braid at the crown of your head.
  4. Braid down the back of your head, incorporating more hair as you go.
  5. Secure the end of the braid with a hair tie.
  6. Repeat on the other side of your head.

2. Top Knot

A top knot is a quick and easy way to keep your hair off your face. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with a hair tie.
  4. Use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces.

3. Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is perfect for keeping hair out of your face while still showing off your long locks. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Tease the ponytail for added volume.
  4. Leave the bottom half of your hair down.

Tips for Styling Long Hair Out of Face

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your long hair out of your face:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or clips.
  • Use a straightening iron or curling wand to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Try a messy bun for a more relaxed look.
  • Use dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Generally, it’s recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I still wear my hair down if I want to keep it out of my face?

A: Yes! You can use bobby pins or a headband to keep your hair off of your forehead while still wearing it down.

Hair Care for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and techniques, you can keep your long hair out of your face while still looking fabulous. Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you!
