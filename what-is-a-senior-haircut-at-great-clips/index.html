What Is A Senior Haircut At Great Clips

Written by Daniel Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a senior citizen looking for a great haircut at an affordable price? Great Clips is here to help! At Great Clips, we offer a senior haircut that is designed specifically for our senior customers. In this blog post, we will discuss what a senior haircut at Great Clips is, how to get one, step by step instructions, tips, FAQ, and hair care tips.

Description

A senior haircut at Great Clips is a haircut designed for customers 65 years and older. This haircut is priced lower than our regular adult haircut and includes a shampoo, haircut, and a style. Our stylists are trained to provide the best haircut experience for our senior customers, taking into consideration any special needs or concerns they may have.

How To Get A Senior Haircut

To get a senior haircut at Great Clips, simply walk into one of our salons and request a senior haircut. Our stylists will be happy to assist you and provide you with a great haircut experience. You can also make an appointment online or through our mobile app to ensure that you get the time and date that works best for you.

Step By Step Instructions

If you are unsure of what to expect during a senior haircut at Great Clips, here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Check in at the front desk and let the receptionist know that you are here for a senior haircut.
  2. Wait for your turn or check-in online to save time.
  3. When your stylist is ready, they will call your name and escort you to the cutting chair.
  4. The stylist will ask you what type of haircut you want and if you have any special requests or concerns.
  5. The stylist will then shampoo your hair to make sure it is clean and ready for the haircut.
  6. Next, the stylist will cut your hair based on your preferences and requests.
  7. Finally, the stylist will style your hair to your liking.
  8. Check out at the front desk and pay for your haircut. Don't forget to leave a tip if you were satisfied with your experience.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your senior haircut at Great Clips:

  • Bring a photo or a magazine with a hairstyle that you like to show to the stylist.
  • Be sure to communicate any special needs or concerns you may have, such as mobility issues or hearing difficulties.
  • Ask the stylist for hair care tips and product recommendations.
  • Consider signing up for our Great Haircut Club to receive discounts and special offers on future haircuts.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about senior haircuts at Great Clips:

Q: Is a senior haircut at Great Clips only for customers 65 and older?

A: Yes, a senior haircut at Great Clips is designed specifically for customers 65 and older.

Q: How much does a senior haircut cost at Great Clips?

A: The cost of a senior haircut at Great Clips varies by location, but it is typically priced lower than our regular adult haircut.

Q: Can I request a specific stylist for my senior haircut?

A: You can request a specific stylist, but we cannot guarantee that they will be available at the time of your appointment.

Q: Can I use coupons or discounts for a senior haircut at Great Clips?

A: Yes, you can use coupons or discounts for a senior haircut at Great Clips if they are applicable to your location.

Hair Care

After your senior haircut at Great Clips, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style and health of your hair. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as a hair dryer or straightener, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the style of your haircut.
  • Use hair products, such as hairspray or gel, to keep your hair in place and add volume or texture.
