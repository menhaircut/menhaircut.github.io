How To Cut Side Bangs Korean Style A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 01, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Are you looking to change up your hairstyle and try something new? Look no further than Korean-style side bangs! This trendy look has been popular in Korea for years and is now gaining popularity worldwide. Not only is it a stylish choice, but it also frames your face beautifully. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut side bangs Korean style in a relaxed English language, so you can achieve this chic look from the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut Side Bangs Korean Style

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your own side bangs, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It's also a good idea to have a mirror nearby so you can check your progress as you go.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning off the hair you want to cut into bangs. Use your comb to make a straight line from the crown of your head to the outer corner of your eyebrow. Then, use the comb to separate this section from the rest of your hair and clip it up so it's out of the way.

Step 3: Wet Your Bangs

Using your spray bottle, mist your bangs with water. This will help keep them in place and make them easier to cut.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Starting from the outer corner of your eyebrow, use your scissors to cut your bangs in a diagonal line towards the center of your forehead. Make sure to cut a little bit at a time and constantly check your progress in the mirror. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's been cut!

Step 5: Blend Your Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs to the desired length, it's time to blend them in with the rest of your hair. Use your comb to blend your bangs with the rest of your hair, gently pulling the hair towards the front to create a seamless look.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Now that your bangs are cut and blended, it's time to style them. Use a round brush and a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your bangs. You can also use a straightening iron to create a sleek and straight look.

Tips for Cutting Side Bangs Korean Style

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind as you cut your own side bangs:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's easier to cut more hair than to put hair back once it's been cut.
  • Make sure your scissors are sharp. Dull scissors can lead to uneven cuts and damaged hair.
  • Take breaks and step back from the mirror to check your progress. It's easy to get caught up in the moment and make mistakes.
  • Don't be afraid to seek professional help if you're unsure about cutting your own hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting side bangs Korean style:

How long should my bangs be?

This is a personal preference, but generally, Korean-style side bangs start at the outer corner of your eyebrow and go down to your nose or mouth.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic! It's just hair and it will grow back. You can always try to fix it or seek professional help if needed.

Can I cut my bangs dry?

It's best to cut your bangs when they are wet, as this will make them easier to cut and style.

Hair Care After Cutting Side Bangs Korean Style

Now that you've cut your side bangs Korean style, it's important to take care of them to keep them looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh.
  • Consider using hair oil or serum to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Now that you know how to cut side bangs Korean style, you can rock this trendy look with confidence! Just remember to take your time, start with small cuts, and have fun experimenting with different styles.
