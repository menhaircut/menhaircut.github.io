Best Hair Cut For Curly Hair And Round Face A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Frank Jul 28, 2023 · 3 min read

In this guide, we’ll help you find the best hair cut for curly hair and round face shapes. If you have curly hair and a round face, you know how challenging it can be to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. But fear not, we’ve got you covered! We’ll walk you through how to select the best cut, offer styling tips, and share hair care advice to keep your locks looking fabulous.

Description

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. It’s beautiful, voluminous, and full of personality. However, it can be challenging to manage, especially if you have a round face shape. The key is to find a haircut that balances your face shape while enhancing your natural curls.

How to

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the best hair cut for curly hair and round face shapes is to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three are roughly the same width, you have a round face shape.

Step 2: Look for Haircuts that Add Length

The best haircuts for round faces are those that add length to your face. Layered cuts, long bobs, and asymmetrical styles are all great options. When it comes to curly hair, avoid blunt cuts as they can add too much volume to your face.

Step 3: Consider Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers can help accentuate your best features while softening your face shape. Ask your stylist for layers that start at your cheekbones and work their way down. This will help elongate your face while adding movement and texture to your curls.

Tips

  • Opt for a side part rather than a center part to create the illusion of length.
  • Avoid heavy bangs as they can make your face appear shorter.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls and add volume.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your curls.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your curls looking healthy and bouncy.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for curly hair and round face?

The best haircut for curly hair and round face shapes is one that adds length to your face. Layered cuts, long bobs, and asymmetrical styles are all great options.

Should I avoid blunt cuts if I have curly hair and a round face?

Yes, blunt cuts can add too much volume to your face, making it appear rounder. Opt for layered cuts instead.

How can I enhance my curls?

Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls and add volume. Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your curls.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and bouncy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. Apply a leave-in conditioner to damp hair to help detangle and define your curls. Use a microfiber towel or a cotton t-shirt to gently dry your hair to avoid frizz. Finally, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls while they’re wet.

Now that you have all the tips and tricks, it’s time to find your perfect haircut! Remember, the key is to add length to your face while enhancing your natural curls. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the one that flatters you the most.
