Will Bangs Suit My Face Shape

Written by Frank Sep 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but wondering if they will suit your face shape? Bangs can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some freshness to your look. However, if you choose the wrong bangs for your face shape, it can end up looking unflattering. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about whether bangs will suit your face shape or not.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining whether bangs will suit your face shape is to figure out what your face shape is. This can be done by looking in the mirror and tracing the outline of your face with a washable marker. You can also take a photo of your face and trace it on a piece of paper. Once you have your face shape, you can move on to choosing the right type of bangs for you.

Types of Bangs

There are several types of bangs to choose from, including:

  • Straight Bangs
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Curly Bangs
  • Blunt Bangs
  • Wispy Bangs

The type of bangs you choose will depend on your face shape and your personal preference.

Bangs for Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck because almost any type of bangs will suit you. Straight bangs, side-swept bangs, and blunt bangs are all great options for oval face shapes.

Bangs for Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to avoid bangs that are too short or too straight. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer in the front and shorter on the sides. This will help to elongate your face and give the illusion of a more oval shape.

Bangs for Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to avoid blunt bangs that are cut straight across. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side than the other. This will help to soften the angles of your face and make it look more oval.

Bangs for Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to avoid blunt bangs and opt for side-swept bangs instead. The bangs should be longer on one side than the other and should hit just above your eyebrows. This will help to balance out your forehead and chin.

Bangs for Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you want to avoid blunt bangs and opt for side-swept bangs instead. The bangs should be longer on one side than the other and should hit just above your eyebrows. This will help to balance out your forehead and chin.

Bangs for Triangular Face Shape

If you have a triangular face shape, you want to avoid blunt bangs and opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides. This will help to balance out your forehead and chin and make your face look more oval.

Tips for Choosing Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing bangs:

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you're someone who likes to workout or play sports, you may want to opt for shorter bangs that won't get in the way.
  • Consider your hair texture: If you have curly hair, you may want to avoid blunt bangs and opt for side-swept bangs instead.
  • Consider your hairline: If you have a high hairline, you may want to avoid blunt bangs that will make your forehead look even larger.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about bangs and face shapes:

  • Can bangs make my face look slimmer?
  • Yes, side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face and make it look slimmer.
  • Can bangs make my forehead look smaller?
  • Yes, blunt bangs can help to make your forehead look smaller.
  • Can bangs work with curly hair?
  • Yes, side-swept bangs can work well with curly hair.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to keep your bangs looking great:

  • Wash your bangs every day to keep them looking fresh.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to give them some shape.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them look greasy.

In conclusion, whether or not bangs will suit your face shape depends on your individual face shape and the type of bangs you choose. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can choose the perfect bangs for your face shape and keep them looking great with proper hair care.
