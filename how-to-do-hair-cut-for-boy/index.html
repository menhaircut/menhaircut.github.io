How To Do Hair Cut For Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to save money on haircuts or simply want to learn how to give your son a fresh new look, then learning how to do a hair cut for a boy is a great skill to have. With the right tools and techniques, you can easily achieve a great haircut that your son will love. In this article, we'll go over the step-by-step process of how to do a hair cut for a boy, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a Hair Cut for Boy
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Do a Hair Cut for Boy

Before we begin, it's important to make sure that you have the right tools for the job. You'll need a pair of hair cutting scissors, a set of clippers with different guard sizes, a comb, and some hair clips to keep the hair out of the way. Once you have your tools ready, you can start by following these steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing and drying your son's hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Section the hair and clip it out of the way. Start at the bottom and work your way up.
  3. Start with the clippers and choose the guard size based on the length you want. Begin at the nape of the neck and work your way up.
  4. Blend the sides and back with scissors. Use the clippers to create a guideline, then blend with scissors to remove any harsh lines.
  5. Trim the top with scissors. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Use the scissors to create texture and remove any bulk.
  6. Create the fringe or bangs. Use the scissors to create a curved line, then blend it with the rest of the hair.
  7. Check for any uneven areas and trim as needed.
  8. Use the clippers to clean up the neckline and around the ears.
  9. Style the hair as desired using hair gel, pomade, or other styling products.
  10. Clean up the area and put away your tools.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips to make the process of cutting your son's hair easier:

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use the comb as a guide to create even length.
  • Use the point-cutting technique to create texture and remove bulk.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and check for any uneven areas.
  • Watch tutorials online to learn different techniques and styles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a boy's hair:

  • How often should I cut my son's hair? It depends on how fast his hair grows and the style you want. Generally, a haircut every 4-6 weeks is recommended.
  • What if my son moves or fidgets during the haircut? Try distracting him with a toy or a movie. You can also have someone else hold him still.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don't panic. You can always trim more hair or take your son to a professional for a fix.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it's important to take care of your son's hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Brush or comb the hair regularly to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Avoid using too much styling products that can weigh down the hair or cause buildup.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the style.

By following these tips and techniques, you can easily learn how to do a hair cut for a boy and give your son a fresh new look. Good luck!
