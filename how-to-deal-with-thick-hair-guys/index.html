How To Deal With Thick Hair Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Dealing with thick hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While having a full head of hair can be a confidence booster, styling and maintaining it can be quite challenging. In this article, we will provide you with tips and tricks on how to deal with thick hair guys. Read on to learn more.

Table of Content

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Deal with Thick Hair Guys
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling tips, let's talk about hair care. It is important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Using the right shampoo and conditioner can make a huge difference in the health of your hair. Look for products that are designed for thick hair and contain natural ingredients such as keratin, argan oil, and coconut oil.

2. Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every two days.

3. Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools such as a blow dryer or straightener, it is important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

How to Deal with Thick Hair Guys

Now that we've covered hair care, let's talk about how to deal with thick hair guys. Here are some tips:

1. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help manage thick hair. Ask your stylist to thin out your hair and add layers to make it more manageable.

2. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make styling thick hair much easier. Look for products that are designed for thick hair, such as a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for your hair type. Some styles that work well for thick hair include the messy quiff, the slick back, and the messy fringe.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style thick hair:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for thick hair.

2. Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a volumizing mousse or a texturizing spray.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.

4. Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your fingers or a comb to achieve your desired look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for dealing with thick hair:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Using a wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Don't Overuse Product

Using too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy. Use a small amount and add more as needed.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Don't try to fight your natural texture. Embrace it and work with it to achieve your desired look.

FAQ

1. Can I dye my thick hair?

Yes, you can dye your thick hair. However, it is important to use a dye that is designed for thick hair and to follow the instructions carefully to prevent damage.

2. How often should I wash my thick hair?

You should aim to wash your thick hair every other day or every two days to prevent it from becoming dry and brittle.

3. What is the best way to style thick hair?

The best way to style thick hair is to experiment with different styles and find what works best for your hair type. Some styles that work well for thick hair include the messy quiff, the slick back, and the messy fringe.

Dealing with thick hair can be a challenge, but with the right hair care and styling tips, you can make it work for you. Remember to take care of your hair and embrace your natural texture to achieve your desired look.
