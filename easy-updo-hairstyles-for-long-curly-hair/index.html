Easy Updo Hairstyles For Long Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Oct 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post about easy updo hairstyles for long curly hair! If you have long curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find a hairstyle that looks great and stays put. Fortunately, there are plenty of updo options that are perfect for curly hair. In this post, we’ll share some tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions for creating easy updos that will keep your curls looking fabulous.

How to Create Easy Updo Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair

Creating an updo for long curly hair can seem daunting, but it's actually quite simple. The key is to start with clean, dry hair and use the right products to keep your curls in place. Here are the steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair and scrunch your curls to encourage definition.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to prevent frizz.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling cream or gel to your hair to keep your curls in place.
  5. Create your updo using one of the techniques outlined below.
  6. Spray your hair with a lightweight hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide to Easy Updo Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair

There are several easy updo hairstyles that work well for long curly hair. Here are step-by-step instructions for three of our favorites:

The Curly Top Knot

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Fluff out the bun to create volume.

The Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail and twist it into a bun.
  2. Secure the bun with bobby pins, leaving some pieces of hair loose for a messy look.
  3. Pull out some curls around your face to frame it.

The Braided Updo

  1. Part your hair down the middle and create two braids, one on each side.
  2. Wrap each braid around the back of your head and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Tuck the ends of the braids under and secure them with bobby pins.

Tips for Creating Easy Updo Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you create easy updo hairstyles for long curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before styling to keep it moisturized.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to keep your updo looking natural.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find the one that works best for your hair type and face shape.

FAQs About Easy Updo Hairstyles for Long Curly Hair

Q: How do I keep my updo from falling out?

A: To keep your updo in place, use bobby pins and hairspray. Bobby pins should be placed in a crisscross pattern to secure the hair. Spritz your hair with a lightweight hairspray to keep it in place.

Q: Can I create an updo with second-day hair?

A: Yes, you can create an updo with second-day hair. In fact, second-day hair can be easier to work with because it has more texture. Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair and add volume before styling.

Hair Care Tips for Long Curly Hair

Long curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangles.

With these tips and tricks, you can create easy updo hairstyles for long curly hair that look great and stay put. Whether you’re headed to work, a special occasion, or just want to switch up your look, these updos are sure to impress.
