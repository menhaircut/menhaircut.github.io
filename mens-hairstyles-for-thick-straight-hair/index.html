Mens Hairstyles For Thick Straight Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Dec 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick straight hair, you may be wondering what the best hairstyles are for you. Luckily, there are plenty of options that can work with your hair type. In this guide, we’ll go over some of the top mens hairstyles for thick straight hair, as well as tips for maintaining and caring for your hair.

How to Style Mens Hairstyles for Thick Straight Hair

When it comes to styling mens hairstyles for thick straight hair, the key is to find a cut that works for you and your hair type. Some popular options include:

  • Short and textured: A short, textured cut can help to break up the thickness of your hair and create some movement.
  • Undercut: An undercut involves shaving the sides and back of your head, while leaving the hair on top longer. This can create a cool, edgy look.
  • Slicked back: If you have longer hair, slicking it back can create a sleek and sophisticated look.
  • Side part: A classic side part can work well with thick straight hair, creating a clean and polished look.

Ultimately, the best hairstyle for you will depend on your personal style and preferences.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mens Hairstyles for Thick Straight Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling mens hairstyles for thick straight hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as a pomade or gel, evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape.
  4. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold your style in place.

Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you and your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Hairstyles for Thick Straight Hair

Maintaining mens hairstyles for thick straight hair doesn’t have to be difficult. Here are some tips to help:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair serum to tame frizz and add shine.

With the right care and maintenance, your mens hairstyle for thick straight hair can look great for weeks on end.

FAQs About Mens Hairstyles for Thick Straight Hair

What is the best haircut for thick straight hair?

The best haircut for thick straight hair depends on your personal style and preferences. Some popular options include short and textured, undercut, slicked back, and side part.

How do I style my hair if I have thick straight hair?

To style mens hairstyles for thick straight hair, start with clean, towel-dried hair and apply a styling product evenly throughout. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into your desired shape, and finish with a hairspray or other finishing product.

How do I maintain mens hairstyles for thick straight hair?

To maintain mens hairstyles for thick straight hair, get regular haircuts, use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid over-washing your hair, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair serum.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Straight Hair

Here are some general hair care tips for thick straight hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause damage.

By following these tips, you can keep your thick straight hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Final Thoughts

Whether you prefer a short and textured cut or a slicked-back style, there are plenty of mens hairstyles for thick straight hair to choose from. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this guide, you can create a look that’s both stylish and easy to maintain.
