Do Curtain Bangs Work On Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Christine Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick wavy hair and want to try out a new hairstyle, you may be wondering if curtain bangs are a good choice. Curtain bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add dimension and style to any hair type. In this article, we will explore whether curtain bangs work on thick wavy hair, as well as how to achieve this look and take care of your hair.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of bangs that are long and parted in the middle, framing the face on both sides. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years because it can flatter any face shape and hair type. Curtain bangs work particularly well on thick wavy hair because they can add movement and texture to the hair, while still framing the face in a flattering way.

How to Achieve Curtain Bangs on Thick Wavy Hair

If you have thick wavy hair and want to try out curtain bangs, here are the steps to achieve this look:

Step 1: Consult with a Hairstylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it is important to consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine whether curtain bangs will work with your face shape and hair type. They can also give you tips on how to style and maintain your new hairstyle.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

The length of your curtain bangs will depend on your personal preference and face shape. If you have a round face, longer curtain bangs can help elongate your face. If you have a heart-shaped face, shorter curtain bangs can balance out the width of your forehead.

Step 3: Part Your Hair in the Middle

The key to achieving curtain bangs is to part your hair in the middle. This will create the signature "curtain" effect. Use a comb to create a straight part down the middle of your hair.

Step 4: Trim Your Bangs

Using sharp scissors, trim your bangs to the desired length. It is important to cut your bangs while they are dry, as wet hair can shrink when it dries. Start by cutting a little bit at a time, and gradually work your way up to the desired length.

Step 5: Style Your Curtain Bangs

To style your curtain bangs, you can either blow dry them straight or let them air dry for a more natural look. You can also use a curling iron to add waves or curls to your bangs. Finish off your look with a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs on Thick Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your curtain bangs on thick wavy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and frizz-free.

FAQ

Do curtain bangs work on all face shapes?

Yes, curtain bangs can work on all face shapes. The key is to choose the right length and style for your face shape.

How often should I trim my curtain bangs?

It is recommended to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with curtain bangs. Simply pull your hair back into a ponytail and leave your bangs out.

Hair Care for Thick Wavy Hair

If you have thick wavy hair, it is important to take care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of thick wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh your hair down.

In conclusion, curtain bangs can work on thick wavy hair and can add a stylish and modern edge to your hairstyle. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a look that is both flattering and easy to maintain.
