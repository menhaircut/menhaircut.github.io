Does Long Hair Make Your Face Look Shorter

Written by William Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long hair has always been a symbol of beauty and femininity. From the time immemorial, women have been growing their hair as a symbol of their grace and elegance. However, there is a lingering question among many women about whether long hair makes their face look shorter. In this blog post, we will explore the answer to this question and provide you with some tips on how to style your long hair to suit your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The length of your hair can affect the perception of your face shape. Long hair can make your face look shorter, while short hair can make your face look longer. This is because long hair can weigh down your face and make it appear fuller. Additionally, long hair can obscure your facial features and make your face appear less defined.

However, the effect of long hair on your face shape depends on several factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and hairstyle. In the following sections, we will discuss how to make long hair work for your face shape.

How to

If you have a round face shape, long hair can make your face look shorter and wider. To counteract this effect, consider adding layers to your hair to create more movement and volume around your face. Additionally, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or a one-length cut.

If you have a square face shape, long hair can soften your jawline and make your face appear less angular. Consider adding face-framing layers to your hair and avoiding hairstyles that are too severe, such as a center part or a blunt bob.

If you have an oval face shape, long hair can complement your features and make your face appear more balanced. Consider adding layers to create movement and volume, or wearing your hair in loose waves or curls.

Step by step

Here are some steps to take if you want to make long hair work for your face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape
  2. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for you
  4. Consider adding layers or face-framing pieces to create movement and volume
  5. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face or are too severe

Tips

Here are some additional tips to make long hair work for your face shape:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that best suits you
  • Add highlights or lowlights to create dimension in your hair
  • Consider getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to prevent breakage and damage to your hair

FAQ

Does long hair make your face look thinner?

Long hair can create the illusion of a thinner face, especially if it is styled in loose waves or curls. Additionally, adding layers can create movement and volume around your face, which can make it look slimmer.

Does short hair make your face look longer?

Short hair can make your face look longer, especially if it is styled in a pixie cut or a bob. Additionally, short hair can highlight your facial features and create the illusion of a more defined face shape.

Hair Care

Keeping your long hair healthy and strong is key to making it work for your face shape. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair no more than 2-3 times per week
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on high heat settings
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized

By following these tips, you can ensure that your long hair looks its best and complements your face shape. Remember, the key to making long hair work for you is to experiment with different styles and find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
