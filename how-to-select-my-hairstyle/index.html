How To Select My Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Selecting a hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. With so many options to choose from, it's easy to feel overwhelmed. But fear not! In this article, we'll guide you through the process of selecting the perfect hairstyle for you. From determining your face shape to considering your lifestyle, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Content

Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in selecting a hairstyle is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will help you determine which hairstyles will look best on you. The main face shapes are oval, round, square, heart, and diamond. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner pencil. Then, compare your drawing to the images of the different face shapes online to see which one matches yours.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when selecting a hairstyle. Hair texture can be fine, medium, or thick, and it can also be straight, wavy, curly, or kinky. Different hairstyles will look better on different hair textures. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that require a lot of volume, as they may make your hair look flat. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that are too heavy, as they may make your hair look bulky.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when selecting a hairstyle. If you lead an active lifestyle and don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, you may want to choose a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you work in a professional environment, you may want to choose a more polished hairstyle that looks neat and tidy.

Try Different Styles

Once you've determined your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, it's time to start trying out different hairstyles. You can do this by looking at pictures online, trying on wigs, or asking your hairstylist for advice. Remember that not all hairstyles will look good on you, so don't be afraid to experiment until you find the perfect one.

Ask for Opinions

When selecting a hairstyle, it's always a good idea to ask for opinions from friends, family, and your hairstylist. They may be able to offer insights that you haven't considered and help you make a decision that you'll be happy with.

Maintain Your Hair

Once you've selected your hairstyle, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. This may involve regular haircuts, styling, and using hair products that are appropriate for your hair type. Your hairstylist can offer advice on how to maintain your hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyles for a round face are those that create the illusion of length and definition. This may include hairstyles with layers, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles that are longer in the front than in the back.

What is the best hairstyle for fine hair?

The best hairstyles for fine hair are those that add volume and texture. This may include hairstyles with layers, curls, and waves.

What is the best hairstyle for thick hair?

The best hairstyles for thick hair are those that are lightweight and easy to manage. This may include hairstyles with layers, a bob, or a pixie cut.

How often should I get a haircut?

How often you should get a haircut depends on your hair type and the style you have. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and trimmed.

Now that you know how to select your hairstyle, it's time to start experimenting and find the perfect one for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, and don't be afraid to try different styles until you find the perfect one. Good luck!
