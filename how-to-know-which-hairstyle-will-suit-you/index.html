How To Know Which Hairstyle Will Suit You

Written by Eveline Oct 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Or maybe you're just not sure which hairstyle will suit you best. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll give you tips and tricks on how to know which hairstyle will suit you best.

Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. Different hairstyles can accentuate or downplay certain features, so it's important to know what works best for you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Round face: go for hairstyles with height at the crown and length below the chin. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs.
  • Square face: soften your features with wavy or curly hair. Avoid straight bangs and one-length cuts.
  • Heart-shaped face: add width to the chin area with chin-length or longer styles. Avoid heavy layers around the crown.
  • Oval face: lucky you, almost any hairstyle will suit you!

Remember, these are just guidelines, so don't be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a big role in which hairstyle will suit you best. For example, if you have naturally curly hair, a short pixie cut might not be the best choice. Here are some tips:

  • Fine hair: go for short, layered cuts to add volume. Avoid heavy, blunt cuts.
  • Thick hair: try longer styles with layers to thin out your hair. Avoid one-length cuts.
  • Curly hair: go for longer styles with layers to add movement. Avoid short, blunt cuts.
  • Straight hair: lucky you, almost any hairstyle will suit you!

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in which hairstyle will suit you best. If you're always on the go and don't have time to style your hair, a low-maintenance cut might be best. On the other hand, if you love experimenting with different styles, a more versatile cut might be the way to go.

Personality

Your personality can also influence which hairstyle will suit you best. If you're outgoing and love attention, a bold, edgy cut might be perfect for you. If you're more reserved, a classic, timeless cut might be more your style.

Hair Length

When it comes to hair length, there are so many options to choose from. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Short hair: perfect for low-maintenance, edgy looks.
  • Medium-length hair: versatile and easy to style.
  • Long hair: great for romantic, feminine looks.

Hair Color

Your hair color can also play a role in which hairstyle will suit you best. For example, if you have a bold hair color like pink or purple, a simple, classic cut might be best to balance out the look. On the other hand, if you have a more natural hair color, you can experiment with bolder cuts and styles.

Budget

Your budget can also play a role in which hairstyle will suit you best. If you can't afford to go to the salon every few weeks, a low-maintenance cut might be best. On the other hand, if you're willing to invest in your hair, a more high-maintenance cut might be worth it.

Try Before You Commit

Before you commit to a new hairstyle, it's always a good idea to try it out first. You can use virtual hairstyling apps or even try on wigs to see how a certain style will look on you.

Hair Care

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are suited to your hair type.
  • Don't over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: How do I know if a certain hairstyle will suit me?

A: Consider your face shape, hair texture, personality, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle. It's also a good idea to try it out first using virtual hairstyling apps or wigs.

Q: Can I pull off a bold, edgy cut?

A: If you have the personality to match, then absolutely! Just make sure to choose a cut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing a new hairstyle can be daunting, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect look for you. Remember, at the end of the day, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your own skin (and hair!).
