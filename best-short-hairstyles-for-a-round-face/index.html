Best Short Hairstyles For A Round Face

Written by Bardi Nov 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect short hairstyle for your round face? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best short hairstyles for a round face shape. From pixie cuts to bob hairstyles, these hairstyles will flatter your face shape and enhance your beauty.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded jawline. When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, it is important to select a style that will elongate your face shape and provide balance. Short hairstyles with volume on top and length on the sides will give the illusion of a longer face shape.

How To

The first step to finding the perfect short hairstyle for your round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are similar in size, you have a round face shape.

Once you have determined your face shape, it is time to select a short hairstyle that will flatter your features. Pixie cuts, bob hairstyles, and asymmetrical cuts are all great options for a round face shape.

Step By Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Research short hairstyles that flatter a round face shape, such as pixie cuts, bob hairstyles, and asymmetrical cuts.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist to determine which short hairstyle will work best with your hair texture and face shape.

Tips

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this will make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding layers to your short hairstyle to create movement and texture.
  • Use styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, to add volume to the top of your hair.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for a round face shape?

Pixie cuts, bob hairstyles, and asymmetrical cuts are all great options for a round face shape.

How can I add volume to my short hairstyle?

Use styling products, such as mousse or hairspray, to add volume to the top of your hair.

Hair Care

To maintain your short hairstyle, it is important to visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from drying out. Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep it moisturized.

In conclusion, finding the perfect short hairstyle for a round face shape is all about balance and proportion. By selecting a style that elongates your face shape and adds volume to the top of your hair, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your new look.
