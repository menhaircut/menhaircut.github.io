Best Hair Brush For Blow Drying Thick Hair Uk A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick hair, you know that blow drying can be a real hassle. But with the right hair brush, you can easily achieve a smooth and voluminous look. In this post, we'll take a look at the best hair brushes for blow drying thick hair in the UK. From round brushes to paddle brushes, we'll cover everything you need to know to get the perfect blowout at home.

Description

Thick hair can be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to blow drying. You need a brush that can handle the density and texture of your hair without causing any damage. The best hair brushes for blow drying thick hair in the UK are designed to do just that. They are made with high-quality materials and are specifically designed to work with thick hair.

How to Choose the Best Hair Brush for Blow Drying Thick Hair UK

When choosing a hair brush for blow drying thick hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the size and shape of the brush. Round brushes are great for adding volume, while paddle brushes are better for smoothing and detangling. You'll also want to look for a brush with bristles that are strong enough to work through thick hair without pulling or causing damage.

Step by Step Guide to Blow Drying Thick Hair with the Best Hair Brush

Now that you've chosen the perfect hair brush for blow drying your thick hair, it's time to put it into action. Follow these simple steps for a flawless blowout:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume and body to your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove any excess moisture. You want your hair to be damp, but not dripping wet.
  3. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the blow dryer.
  4. Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to work through your hair with the brush and blow dryer.
  5. Starting with the bottom sections, use your hair brush to pull the hair straight and blow dry it until it is completely dry.
  6. Move on to the middle sections, using the same technique to blow dry your hair until it is completely dry.
  7. Finish with the top sections, using your brush to add volume at the roots and blow drying your hair until it is completely dry.
  8. Once your hair is dry, use a cool shot of air from the blow dryer to set the style.
  9. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Using the Best Hair Brush for Blow Drying Thick Hair UK

Here are a few additional tips to help you get the most out of your hair brush:

  • Use a brush with natural boar bristles to add shine and prevent frizz.
  • Start with a lower heat setting on your blow dryer to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair clip to hold the section of hair you are working on in place.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction of the brush to create a smooth and polished look.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

What is the best hair brush for blow drying thick hair?

The best hair brush for blow drying thick hair is a round brush with natural boar bristles. This will help to add volume and shine to your hair while preventing frizz.

What size brush should I use for blow drying thick hair?

The size of the brush you use will depend on the length of your hair. For shorter hair, use a smaller brush. For longer hair, use a larger brush.

How do I prevent damage when blow drying my thick hair?

To prevent damage when blow drying your thick hair, use a heat protectant spray and start with a lower heat setting on your blow dryer. You should also use a brush with bristles that are strong enough to work through your hair without pulling or causing damage.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

In addition to using the best hair brush for blow drying thick hair, it's important to take care of your hair on a daily basis. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair. You should also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

With the right hair brush and a few simple tips, you can easily achieve a smooth and voluminous blowout at home. So go ahead and give it a try!
