How To Fade The Top Of Your Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle or add some edge to your look, a fade is a great option. Fading the top of your hair can create a stylish and modern look that's perfect for any occasion. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to fade the top of your hair. From the tools you'll need to the step-by-step process, we've got you covered.

What You'll Need

Before you get started, you'll need to gather a few tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers
  • Guard attachments
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Mirror

How to Fade the Top of Your Hair

Step 1: Decide on Your Desired Look

The first step is to decide on the look you're going for. Do you want a high fade or a low fade? A skin fade or a tapered fade? Look up some inspiration photos and decide on your preferred style.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Remove any tangles or knots with a comb. If you have longer hair, clip or tie the top section out of the way so you can focus on the sides and back.

Step 3: Start with the Guard Attachment

Attach a guard to your clippers and start by trimming the sides and back of your hair. Use an upward motion to create a gradual fade. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to create a smooth transition.

Step 4: Blend the Top and Sides

Once you've achieved your desired length on the sides and back, it's time to blend the top section. Use scissors to trim the top section of your hair to the desired length. Then, use the clippers without a guard to blend the top and sides together. Use a comb to guide the clippers and create a seamless blend.

Step 5: Refine the Fade

Now that you've blended the top and sides, it's time to refine the fade. Use a shorter guard attachment to create a more gradual fade around the edges. Use the clippers to create a clean line around the ears and neckline.

Step 6: Check Your Work

Once you've finished fading your hair, check your work in the mirror. Make any necessary touch-ups to ensure a clean and even fade.

Tips for Fading the Top of Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect fade:

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and work your way down to a shorter one to create a smooth transition.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and create a seamless blend.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Check your work in the mirror frequently to ensure an even fade.

FAQ

Can I Fade the Top of My Hair at Home?

Yes, you can fade the top of your hair at home. However, it's important to have the right tools and to take your time to ensure a clean and even fade.

How Often Should I Fade the Top of My Hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your look. On average, you may need to touch up your fade every 2-4 weeks.

Hair Care for Faded Hair

Now that you've achieved the perfect fade, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to fade the top of your hair like a pro. Whether you're looking for a low fade or a high fade, with a little patience and practice, you'll be able to achieve the perfect look.
