Short Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by William Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you may find it challenging to choose a hairstyle that complements your facial features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll go over some of the best short hairstyles that are perfect for heart-shaped faces. Whether you want a bob, pixie or shag, we've got you covered!

Description

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a broad forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of choosing a short hairstyle for a heart-shaped face is to balance out your facial features by creating volume around the jawline and cheekbones while softening the forehead.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Heart Shaped Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for a heart-shaped face, consider the following:

Face Shape

Consider your face shape when choosing a short hairstyle. A hairstyle that flatters a heart-shaped face may not look as flattering on a round or square-shaped face.

Hair Texture

The texture of your hair is also an important factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle. Fine hair may not hold a voluminous look, while thick hair can create too much volume, making your face look wider.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also a crucial factor to consider when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires little styling.

Step by Step Guide to Short Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces

Now that you know what to look for, let's dive into some of the best short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces.

1. Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is a great option for heart-shaped faces. The bangs help to balance out the forehead, while the length of the bob accentuates the jawline.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a daring and trendy hairstyle that looks great on heart-shaped faces. The short length creates volume around the cheekbones, while the tapered back and sides create balance.

3. Shag Cut

A shag cut is a versatile hairstyle that looks great on all face shapes, especially heart-shaped faces. The layers create volume around the cheekbones, while the length on top softens the forehead.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces

Now that you have your perfect short hairstyle, it's essential to maintain it. Here are some tips:

Regular Trims

Regular trims help to maintain the shape of your short hairstyle. Plan to visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks.

Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in quality hair products that are designed for your hair type. These products help to maintain the health of your hair and keep your short hairstyle looking fresh.

FAQs

What short hairstyles are best for heart-shaped faces?

Bob with bangs, pixie cut, and shag cut are the best short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces.

Can heart-shaped faces pull off a short hairstyle?

Yes! Heart-shaped faces are perfect for short hairstyles because they can create volume around the cheekbones while softening the forehead.

Hair Care for Short Hairstyles for Heart Shaped Faces

Short hairstyles require minimal maintenance, but it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

Wash and Condition Regularly

Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and shiny. Choose products that are designed for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a protective hair product that contains SPF.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for heart-shaped faces can be challenging to choose, but with the right style, you'll look amazing. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a short hairstyle. Don't forget to maintain your short hairstyle with regular trims and quality hair products.
