How To Make Thin Curly Hair Look Fuller Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Jun 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of your thin curly hair looking lifeless and flat? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with thin curly hair struggle to achieve that full and voluminous look. But with a few simple tips and tricks, you can transform your hair and achieve a fuller, more voluminous look. In this article, we'll share some of our favorite tips and tricks for making thin curly hair look fuller.

  • How to Make Thin Curly Hair Look Fuller
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Thin Curly Hair Look Fuller

There are several ways to make thin curly hair look fuller:

  1. Use volumizing products
  2. Blow-dry your hair upside down
  3. Add layers to your hair
  4. Try a diffuser
  5. Use hot rollers
  6. Tease your hair
  7. Try hair extensions

These tips may sound simple, but they can make a big difference in the overall look and feel of your hair. Let's dive into each tip in more detail.

1. Use volumizing products

Volumizing products, such as mousses, sprays, and gels, can help add volume and fullness to your hair. Look for products specifically designed for thin hair, as these will be lightweight and won't weigh your hair down. Apply the product to your roots and work it through your hair, focusing on the areas where you want more volume.

2. Blow-dry your hair upside down

Blow-drying your hair upside down can help add volume and lift to your roots. To do this, flip your head upside down and blow-dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a round brush to help create volume and shape.

3. Add layers to your hair

Adding layers to your hair can help create movement and volume. Ask your hairstylist to add layers that frame your face and add volume to the crown of your head. This will help create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair.

4. Try a diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool for adding volume and definition to curly hair. Attach the diffuser to your blow-dryer and use it to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go. This will help enhance your natural curls and add volume.

5. Use hot rollers

Hot rollers are a great way to add volume and fullness to your hair. Roll your hair in sections, starting at the crown of your head and working your way down. Leave the rollers in for at least 20 minutes, then remove them and gently brush out your hair. This will create soft, bouncy curls with lots of volume.

6. Tease your hair

Teasing your hair is a great way to add volume and texture. To tease your hair, take small sections of hair and backcomb them at the roots using a teasing comb. Be gentle, as too much teasing can damage your hair.

7. Try hair extensions

If you're looking for a more dramatic way to add volume to your hair, consider trying hair extensions. There are many different types of extensions available, from clip-ins to sew-ins. Talk to your hairstylist about which type of extensions would work best for your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for making thin curly hair look fuller:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a volumizing product to your roots and work it through your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down, using a round brush to create volume and shape.
  4. Add layers to your hair to create movement and volume.
  5. Use a diffuser to enhance your natural curls and add volume.
  6. Try hot rollers to create soft, bouncy curls with lots of volume.
  7. Tease your hair to add volume and texture.
  8. Consider trying hair extensions for a more dramatic look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for making thin curly hair look fuller:

  • Avoid using heavy products, as these can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can break up your curls and make your hair look thinner.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as these can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ

Can I make my thin curly hair look fuller without using heat tools?

Yes, you can achieve a fuller look without using heat tools. Try using volumizing products, adding layers to your hair, and teasing your hair to add volume and texture.

Will using hot rollers damage my hair?

Using hot rollers occasionally is unlikely to cause significant damage to your hair. However, using them too often or leaving them in for too long can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thin curly hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look thin and lifeless.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a brush can break up your curls and make your hair look thinner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can dry out your hair and make it look thin and brittle.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it look thinner.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help nourish and hydrate your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can transform your thin curly hair and achieve a fuller, more voluminous look. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair, and don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice. With a little patience and care, you can have the beautiful, full hair you've always wanted.
