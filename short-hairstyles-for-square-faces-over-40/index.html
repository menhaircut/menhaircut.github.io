Short Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 40

Written by Daniel Oct 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new short hairstyle to flatter your square face shape? As we age, our features change and our hair may become thinner or more brittle. But that doesn't mean we have to sacrifice style! With the right cut and styling techniques, you can rock a chic and modern look that accentuates your best features. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for square faces over 40, and share tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Square Faces over 40

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for a Square Face

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a square face, the key is to create balance and soften the angles of the jawline. Avoid styles that emphasize the squareness of the face, such as blunt bobs or straight-across bangs. Instead, opt for layers, curls, and texture to add movement and dimension.

A pixie cut can be a great choice for square faces over 40, as it draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones while minimizing the jawline. A layered bob or lob (long bob) can also be flattering, especially if the layers are angled or asymmetrical to soften the jawline. For those who prefer longer hair, a shoulder-length cut with layers and waves can create a soft and feminine look.

When choosing a color, consider warm, natural tones that complement your skin tone and bring out your best features. Highlights or lowlights can also add depth and dimension to short hair, while balayage or ombré techniques can create a trendy, modern look.

Short Hairstyles for Square Faces over 40:

  • Pixie cut with side-swept bangs
  • Layered bob with angled or asymmetrical layers
  • Lob (long bob) with waves or curls
  • Textured, choppy bob
  • Shaggy pixie with layers and texture
  • Cropped bob with choppy layers and side-swept bangs

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Square Faces over 40

Now that you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your square face, it's time to learn how to style it like a pro. Follow these steps for a chic and modern look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant and styling product, such as mousse or volumizing spray, to add texture and hold.
  2. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use the brush to create volume and lift at the crown.
  3. If you have a pixie cut, use a styling wax or pomade to create texture and define the layers. For a bob or lob, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.
  4. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to set the style and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy, Gorgeous Short Hair

Short hair can be low-maintenance and easy to style, but it still requires proper care to stay healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping natural oils from the scalp.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and always use a heat protectant when you do.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Invest in high-quality styling products that are designed for your hair type and texture.

FAQs

What is the best short hairstyle for a square face over 40?

The best short hairstyle for a square face over 40 is one that creates balance and softens the angles of the jawline. A pixie cut, layered bob, or lob with waves are all great options.

What kind of highlights should I get for short hair?

For short hair, consider natural-looking highlights that complement your skin tone and add dimension. Balayage or ombré techniques can create a trendy, modern look.

Hair Care Tips for Square Faces over 40

In addition to regular trims and using high-quality products, there are other steps you can take to keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which promote hair growth and strength.
  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as perms or relaxers, which can damage hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve circulation and stimulate hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and flattering short hairstyle that accentuates your best features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
