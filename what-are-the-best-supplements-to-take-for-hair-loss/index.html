Saw Palmetto

Saw Palmetto is a natural supplement that has been shown to block the production of DHT, a hormone that can cause hair loss. DHT is produced when testosterone is converted by an enzyme called 5-alpha reductase. By blocking this enzyme, Saw Palmetto can help reduce hair loss and promote hair growth.

Recommended daily dosage: 160-320mg

Biotin

Biotin is a B-vitamin that is essential for the growth and maintenance of healthy hair. It helps to strengthen hair strands and prevent breakage, which can lead to hair loss. Biotin also improves the health of skin and nails.

Recommended daily dosage: 5,000mcg

Vitamin D

Vitamin D is important for many functions in the body, including hair growth. Studies have shown that people with hair loss often have low levels of vitamin D. It is also important for the absorption of calcium, which is essential for healthy hair follicles.

Recommended daily dosage: 800-1,000 IU

Iron

Iron is a mineral that is important for the growth and development of healthy hair. Low levels of iron can cause hair loss, especially in women. Iron is also important for the production of hemoglobin, which carries oxygen to the hair follicles.

Recommended daily dosage: 18mg (women), 8mg (men)

Zinc

Zinc is a mineral that is important for the growth and repair of tissues, including hair. It also helps to regulate the production of oil in the scalp, which can affect hair growth. Low levels of zinc can lead to hair loss and a dry, flaky scalp.

Recommended daily dosage: 8-11mg

Probiotics

Probiotics are beneficial bacteria that help to maintain a healthy gut. They also play a role in the absorption of nutrients, including those that are important for hair growth. Studies have shown that a healthy gut microbiome can promote healthy hair growth.

Recommended daily dosage: varies by strain

Collagen

Collagen is a protein that is important for the strength and elasticity of skin, hair, and nails. As we age, our bodies produce less collagen, which can lead to thinning hair and wrinkles. Taking a collagen supplement can help to promote healthy hair growth and improve skin elasticity.

Recommended daily dosage: varies by product

Omega-3

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential fats that are important for many functions in the body, including hair growth. They help to reduce inflammation, which can damage hair follicles and lead to hair loss. Omega-3s also improve the health of the scalp and hair shaft.

Recommended daily dosage: 250-500mg

Ashwagandha

Ashwagandha is an adaptogenic herb that has been used for centuries in Ayurvedic medicine. It helps to reduce stress and anxiety, which can contribute to hair loss. It also improves the health of the scalp and hair follicles.

Recommended daily dosage: 500-1,000mg
