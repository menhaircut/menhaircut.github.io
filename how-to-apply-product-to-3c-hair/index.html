Description

3c hair is curly hair that is prone to dryness and frizz. It requires specific products to keep it moisturized and defined. In this article, we will show you how to apply product to 3c hair and provide tips and tricks to keep your curls looking beautiful.

How to Apply Product to 3c Hair

Before applying product to your 3c hair, it is important to know your hair type and what products work best for it. 3c hair requires products that are moisturizing, lightweight and provide hold. Some popular products for 3c hair include leave-in conditioners, curl creams, gels and oils.

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start with freshly washed and conditioned hair.
  2. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to each section of your hair.
  4. Apply a curl cream or defining product to each section.
  5. Use a Denman brush or wide-tooth comb to distribute the products evenly throughout your hair.
  6. Apply a gel to each section to provide hold.
  7. Scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.
  8. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  9. Once your hair is dry, use an oil to seal in moisture and add shine.
  10. Fluff your hair to add volume and shape to your curls.

Tips and Tricks

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Apply products to soaking wet hair for maximum absorption.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair once it is dry to prevent frizz.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a silk or satin bonnet or pillowcase.

FAQ

Q: How often should I apply products to my 3c hair?

A: It is recommended to apply products to your 3c hair every 2-3 days to keep it moisturized and defined.

Q: Can I use the same products on my 3c hair that I use on my straightened hair?

A: No, 3c hair requires specific products that are moisturizing, lightweight and provide hold. Using products meant for straightened hair can cause breakage and damage to your curls.

Hair Care

Aside from applying products, it is important to take care of your 3c hair to keep it healthy and strong. This includes:

  • Washing your hair regularly with sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Deep conditioning your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Trimming your ends every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.
  • Protecting your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
