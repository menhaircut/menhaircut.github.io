Mens Hairstyles Back Of Head How To Achieve The Perfect Look

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd, then you should consider mens hairstyles back of head. This style is becoming increasingly popular among men of all ages, and it's easy to see why. Not only does it look great, but it's also very versatile, so you can wear it to work, to the gym, or to a night out with friends.

Description

Mens hairstyles back of head are characterized by longer hair at the back of the head, which is usually styled in a variety of ways. Some men choose to keep the hair straight and sleek, while others prefer a more tousled, messy look. The key to this style is to keep the hair longer at the back and shorter on the sides and top.

How to Achieve the Look

If you're interested in trying out mens hairstyles back of head, then you'll need to follow a few simple steps:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair Out

The first step is to grow your hair out to the desired length at the back of your head. This will typically take several months, so be patient and resist the urge to trim it too soon.

Step 2: Trim the Sides and Top

Once your hair has grown out, you'll need to trim the sides and top to create the desired shape. You can do this yourself with a pair of clippers, or you can visit a barber or stylist for a professional cut.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once your hair has been cut, you can style it in a variety of ways. Some popular options include slicking it back with gel, tousling it with your fingers, or using a hair dryer to create volume.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Hairstyles Back of Head

If you want to keep your mens hairstyles back of head looking great, then there are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Regular trims are essential to keep the sides and top looking neat and tidy.
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider investing in a good quality hairbrush to help distribute natural oils and prevent tangles.

FAQ

What face shape is best for mens hairstyles back of head?

Mens hairstyles back of head can work well with a variety of face shapes, but they tend to look particularly good on men with oval or square-shaped faces.

How often should I trim my hair to maintain this style?

You should aim to have your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep the sides and top looking sharp and tidy.

Can I still wear this style if I have thinning hair?

Absolutely! Mens hairstyles back of head can work well with thinning hair, as the longer length at the back can help to create the illusion of thickness and volume.

Hair Care for Mens Hairstyles Back of Head

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. This should include:

  • Washing your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools
  • Limiting exposure to the sun and wind
  • Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect mens hairstyles back of head and keep it looking great for years to come.
