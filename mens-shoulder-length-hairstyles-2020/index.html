Mens Shoulder Length Hairstyles 2020 A Complete Guide

If you're looking for a stylish, versatile, and low-maintenance hairstyle, then a shoulder-length cut could be just what you need. In this guide, we'll take a look at the best mens shoulder length hairstyles 2020 has to offer, as well as some tips for caring for your new 'do.

How to Get a Shoulder Length Hairstyle

Getting a shoulder length hairstyle is easy, but it's important to choose a cut that works for your hair type and face shape. If you have straight hair, you may want to consider a blunt cut that falls just above your shoulders. If you have curly hair, a layered cut can help to define your curls and prevent your hair from looking too heavy.

To get the perfect shoulder length cut, it's best to visit a professional stylist. They can help you choose the right cut for your hair type and face shape, and ensure that the length is even and flattering. You can also bring in pictures of hairstyles you like to help give your stylist an idea of what you're looking for.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Styling shoulder length hair is easy, and there are plenty of options to choose from. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve some of the most popular shoulder length hairstyles:

1. Messy Waves

  1. Start with damp hair
  2. Apply a styling mousse or sea salt spray
  3. Scrunch your hair with your hands to create waves
  4. Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray or pomade

2. Slicked Back

  1. Start with wet hair
  2. Apply a styling gel or pomade
  3. Comb your hair back with a fine-tooth comb
  4. Blow dry your hair on a low heat setting
  5. Finish with a hairspray or pomade

3. Low Ponytail

  1. Start with dry hair
  2. Brush your hair back and gather it into a low ponytail
  3. Secure with a hair tie
  4. Wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it
  5. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your new shoulder length hairstyle looking its best, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as this will minimize breakage
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth

Frequently Asked Questions

What face shapes work best with shoulder length hair?

Shoulder length hair can work well for a variety of face shapes, including oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid a blunt cut and opt for a layered cut instead.

What hair types work best for shoulder length hair?

Shoulder length hair can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly. However, it's important to choose a cut that works with your hair type, as this will make styling and maintenance easier.

How often should I trim my shoulder length hair?

It's generally recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

To keep your shoulder length hair looking its best, here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as this can cause damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers
  • Avoid tying your hair back too tightly, as this can cause breakage
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to enjoy your new shoulder length hairstyle with ease. Whether you opt for messy waves or a slicked back look, you're sure to turn heads with your stylish new 'do.
