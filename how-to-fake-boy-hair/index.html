How To Fake Boy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 21, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

If you're a girl who wants to experiment with a more masculine look, faking boy hair is a great way to do it. With the right techniques and products, you can easily create the illusion of short, messy, and edgy hair that's typically associated with boys. In this article, we'll show you how to fake boy hair and offer some tips and tricks to help you maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Fake Boy Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Fake Boy Hair

The first step to faking boy hair is to find the right style that suits your face shape and personality. There are many different styles to choose from, including the messy bedhead, the buzz cut, the undercut, and the quiff. Once you've found the style you like, you'll need to gather the right products and tools to create the look.

Some of the products you'll need include hair wax, pomade, hair spray, and styling gel. You'll also need a blow dryer, a comb, and a pair of scissors to help you trim and shape your hair. It's important to invest in high-quality products that won't damage your hair or cause irritation to your scalp.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to fake boy hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt, oil, or product build-up from your hair and scalp.
  2. Blow dry your hair until it's completely dry. Use your fingers to create some volume and texture in your hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your hands and rub them together. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.
  4. Use a comb to create some definition in your hair. You can create a messy bedhead look by running your fingers through your hair and scrunching it up. Or you can use a comb to create a more slicked-back style.
  5. If you want to create an undercut or buzz cut, use a pair of scissors to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Be careful not to cut too much off at once, as you can always go back and trim more later.
  6. Use a bit of hair spray or styling gel to hold your hair in place. This will help your hair stay in place throughout the day.
  7. Finish off your look by adding some accessories, such as a beanie, a baseball cap, or a bandana.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you fake boy hair:

  • Experiment with different styles and products until you find the perfect look for you.
  • Invest in high-quality products that won't damage your hair or cause irritation to your scalp.
  • Don't forget to trim your hair regularly to maintain your style.
  • Add some accessories, such as a beanie or a bandana, to complete your look.
  • Use a small amount of product at a time to avoid weighing your hair down or creating a greasy look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about faking boy hair:

Can I fake boy hair with long hair?

Yes, you can fake boy hair with long hair. You'll need to use the right products and techniques to create the illusion of short, messy hair. Consider creating a messy bun or using hair ties to create a faux undercut.

How do I maintain my fake boy hair?

To maintain your fake boy hair, you'll need to trim your hair regularly and use high-quality products that won't damage your hair or cause irritation to your scalp. You should also avoid using too much product at once, as this can weigh your hair down or create a greasy look.

Can I wash my hair after faking boy hair?

Yes, you can wash your hair after faking boy hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to remove any product build-up or dirt from your hair and scalp.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and the elements by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, faking boy hair is a fun and easy way to experiment with a new look. With the right products and techniques, you can easily create the illusion of short, messy, and edgy hair. Just remember to invest in high-quality products, trim your hair regularly, and use accessories to complete your look. And don't forget to take care of your hair by following a healthy hair care routine.
