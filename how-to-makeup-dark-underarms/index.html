How To Makeup Dark Underarms Tips And Tricks

Written by William Dec 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're someone who's always conscious about dark underarms, then this blog post is for you. Today, we're going to talk about how to makeup dark underarms using simple tips and tricks. Dark underarms can be caused due to various reasons such as shaving, waxing, using deodorants, or even genetics. However, with the right makeup techniques, you can easily cover them up and flaunt your favorite sleeveless dresses.

How to Makeup Dark Underarms?

Before we dive into the makeup tips, let's understand the causes of dark underarms. As mentioned earlier, dark underarms can be caused due to various reasons such as:

  • Shaving
  • Waxing
  • Using deodorants
  • Genetics
  • Dead skin cells

Now, let's move on to the makeup tips:

Step 1: Exfoliate

The first step before applying any makeup is to exfoliate your underarms. Exfoliation helps to remove dead skin cells and reveal brighter skin. You can use a gentle exfoliating scrub and massage it in circular motions for a few minutes. Rinse it off with water and pat dry.

Step 2: Moisturize

After exfoliating, it's important to moisturize your underarms. Moisturizing helps to keep your skin hydrated and prevents further darkening. You can use a lightweight moisturizer or even coconut oil.

Step 3: Apply Primer

Once you've moisturized your underarms, it's time to apply primer. Primer helps to create a smooth base for your makeup and also helps it to last longer. You can use a silicone-based primer for best results.

Step 4: Apply Concealer

The next step is to apply concealer to your underarms. Choose a concealer that matches your skin tone and apply it evenly on your underarms. You can use a makeup sponge or even your fingers to blend it in.

Step 5: Set with Powder

After applying concealer, it's important to set it with powder. Setting it with powder helps to prevent it from creasing and also helps it to last longer. You can use a translucent powder or even a powder that matches your skin tone.

Tips for Makeup Dark Underarms

Here are some tips to keep in mind while makeup dark underarms:

  • Choose a concealer that matches your skin tone
  • Blend the concealer evenly
  • Set your concealer with powder
  • Avoid using deodorants that contain alcohol
  • Exfoliate regularly to prevent further darkening

FAQ

Q. Can I use foundation instead of concealer?

A. Yes, you can use foundation instead of concealer. However, make sure to choose a foundation that matches your skin tone and blend it evenly.

Q. How often should I exfoliate my underarms?

A. You can exfoliate your underarms once or twice a week. However, make sure not to over-exfoliate as it can cause skin irritation.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your underarm hair. Shaving and waxing can cause darkening of the skin. Instead, try using hair removal creams or even laser hair removal. This will not only prevent darkening but also make your underarms look smoother and brighter.

So, those were some simple tips and tricks on how to makeup dark underarms. With the right makeup techniques and a proper skincare routine, you can easily cover them up and flaunt your favorite sleeveless dresses.
