How To Wear Short Thin Hair Up

Written by Robby Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short thin hair can be challenging to style, especially when you want to wear it up. However, with a few simple tricks, you can create beautiful updos that will elevate your look. In this article, we will show you how to wear short thin hair up in a relaxed and effortless way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Short Thin Hair Up
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing Short Thin Hair Up
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Thin Hair

How to Wear Short Thin Hair Up

First, it's essential to understand that wearing short thin hair up requires some preparation. You need to make sure your hair is clean, dry, and free of tangles. You can start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some texture and body.

Next, you need to gather your hair tools, including a brush, a comb, bobby pins, and hair ties. You may also want to use some styling products, such as a volumizing mousse or hairspray.

Step 1: Create a High Ponytail

The first step in wearing short thin hair up is to create a high ponytail. Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or tangles and gather your hair at the crown of your head. Secure it with a hair tie, making sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place.

Step 2: Tease Your Ponytail

Next, use a comb to tease your ponytail gently. This will add some volume and texture to your hair, making it easier to style. Make sure to tease the hair at the base of the ponytail, as this is where you want the most volume.

Step 3: Twist and Pin

Now it's time to create the updo. Take your ponytail and twist it into a tight bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place, making sure to hide the ends of your hair. You can also use a hairnet to keep your bun secure.

Step 4: Finishing Touches

Finally, use a comb to smooth out any flyaways, and spray your updo with hairspray to keep it in place. You can also add some accessories, such as a headband or a clip, to enhance your look.

Tips for Wearing Short Thin Hair Up

Here are some additional tips to help you wear short thin hair up:

  • Use hair extensions or a hairpiece to add volume and length to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or twists, to create more texture and interest.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear short thin hair up if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear short thin hair up regardless of your face shape. However, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face look rounder.

Q: How do I prevent my updo from falling apart?

A: To prevent your updo from falling apart, make sure you use enough bobby pins to secure it in place. You can also use a hairspray or a hairnet to keep your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips for Short Thin Hair

To keep your short thin hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry or oily.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

By following these tips and tricks, you can wear short thin hair up in a relaxed and effortless way. Whether you're headed to the office or a night out, these updos will add some sophistication and style to your look.
