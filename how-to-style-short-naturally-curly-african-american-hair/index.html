How To Style Short Naturally Curly African American Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking for a change? Styling short naturally curly African American hair can be a challenge, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a look that is both stylish and easy to maintain. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and answers to frequently asked questions to help you achieve the perfect style for your short, naturally curly hair.

Description

African American hair is naturally curly and can be challenging to style, especially when it's short. Short hair is often considered low maintenance, but that's not always the case with curly hair. It can be difficult to tame and style curly hair, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a look that's both stylish and easy to maintain. This article will provide you with everything you need to know to style your short, naturally curly African American hair.

How to Style Short Naturally Curly African American Hair

Styling short, naturally curly African American hair requires a few simple steps. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

The first step in styling your short, naturally curly African American hair is to start with clean hair. Use a gentle sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to cleanse your hair and scalp. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that can weigh down your curls.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help moisturize and define your curls. Work the product through your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly from root to tip. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Use a Curl Enhancing Cream

Next, use a curl enhancing cream to help define and hold your curls. Apply the product to your hair, scrunching it upwards to create definition and volume. This will help to enhance your natural curls and give them a more defined shape.

Step 4: Diffuse Your Hair

Once you've applied the curl enhancing cream, use a diffuser to dry your hair. This will help to set your curls and prevent frizz. Simply attach the diffuser to your blow dryer and use it on a low heat setting, scrunching your curls as you go.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Oil

Finally, finish your style with a hair oil to add shine and moisture to your curls. Apply a small amount of oil to your hands and work it through your hair, focusing on the ends. This will help to keep your curls hydrated and prevent frizz throughout the day.

Tips for Styling Short Naturally Curly African American Hair

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect style for your short, naturally curly African American hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before washing it.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls.
  • Try different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my short, naturally curly African American hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair once or twice a week, as over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I straighten my short, naturally curly African American hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to use heat protectant products and avoid applying too much heat to your hair.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my short, naturally curly African American hair?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner, curl enhancing cream, and hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and prevent frizz. Also, avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Hair Care for Short Naturally Curly African American Hair

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are a few tips to help you care for your short, naturally curly African American hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect style for your short, naturally curly African American hair. Experiment with different products and styles to find what works best for you, and don't be afraid to embrace your natural curls!
