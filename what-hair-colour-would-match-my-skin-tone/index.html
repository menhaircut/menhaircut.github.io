What Hair Colour Would Match My Skin Tone

Written by Daniel Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing a hair colour that complements your skin tone can be a daunting task. With so many shades and hues available, it can be overwhelming to determine which one would suit you best. In this article, we will guide you through the process of identifying your skin tone and choosing the perfect hair colour to match it.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Colour
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Description

When it comes to choosing a hair colour that complements your skin tone, there are a few things to consider. Your skin tone can be warm, cool, or neutral, and it is essential to determine which category you fall into before selecting a hair colour. Warm skin tones tend to have yellow or golden undertones, while cool skin tones have pink or blue undertones. Neutral skin tones have a mix of both warm and cool undertones.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

There are several ways to determine your skin tone. One of the easiest is to look at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If it's difficult to tell, you likely have a neutral skin tone. Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the jewellery you wear. If you look best in silver jewellery, you likely have a cool skin tone. If you look best in gold jewellery, you likely have a warm skin tone.

Undertones

It's crucial to understand the undertones of your skin tone when choosing a hair colour. Warm skin tones tend to look best in hair colours with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, caramel brown, or auburn. Cool skin tones tend to look best in hair colours with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum, or black. Neutral skin tones can pull off both warm and cool hair colours.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Now that you have determined your skin tone and undertones, it's time to choose the perfect hair colour. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

  1. Identify your skin tone as warm, cool, or neutral.
  2. Determine the undertones of your skin tone.
  3. Choose a hair colour with undertones that complement your skin tone.
  4. Consider your natural hair colour and how it will affect the outcome of your new hair colour.
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist for advice and recommendations.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you have chosen the perfect hair colour, it's essential to maintain it properly to keep it looking vibrant and healthy. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair colour:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the colour.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or hair product containing SPF.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage the colour and cause fading.
  • Consider getting touch-ups every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair colour looking fresh.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair colours work best for warm skin tones?

Warm skin tones tend to look best in hair colours with warm undertones, such as golden blonde, caramel brown, or auburn.

What hair colours work best for cool skin tones?

Cool skin tones tend to look best in hair colours with cool undertones, such as ash blonde, platinum, or black.

Can I dye my hair a colour that doesn't match my skin tone?

Yes, you can dye your hair any colour you like. However, choosing a hair colour that complements your skin tone can help enhance your natural beauty and make you look your best.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Coloured hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Consider getting regular touch-ups.

Choosing a hair colour that complements your skin tone is a great way to enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful. By following the steps outlined in this article and using our tips for maintaining your colour, you can achieve a stunning and vibrant new look.
