How To Do 60S Hair With Headband A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a classic and chic hairstyle, then the 60s hair with headband is the perfect choice for you. This iconic hairstyle is famous for its voluminous and bouncy curls that are paired with a headband to create a retro look. In this article, we'll show you how to recreate this hairstyle in just a few simple steps.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Do 60s Hair with Headband

The first step to achieving the 60s hair with headband look is to choose the right headband. Look for a headband that is thick and made of a soft material, like velvet. It should also be wide enough to cover your entire forehead.

Next, you'll need to create the voluminous curls that are the hallmark of this hairstyle. You can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create these curls. If you're using a curling iron, wrap small sections of hair around the iron and hold for a few seconds before releasing. If you're using hot rollers, leave them in for 10-15 minutes before removing.

Once you've created the curls, it's time to add the headband. Place the headband over your hairline and pull it back so that it sits snugly against your head. You can position the headband higher up on your head or closer to your forehead, depending on the look you want to achieve.

Finally, use a comb or your fingers to gently tease the hair at the crown of your head to create some extra volume. You can also use hairspray to help hold the curls in place and keep the headband from slipping.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Choose the right headband

2. Create voluminous curls using a curling iron or hot rollers

3. Place the headband over your hairline and pull it back

4. Tease the hair at the crown of your head to create extra volume

5. Use hairspray to hold the curls in place and prevent the headband from slipping

Tips for Perfect 60s Hair with Headband

- Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help create more volume in your hair

- Don't be afraid to tease your hair to create extra volume

- Use bobby pins to secure the headband in place if it keeps slipping

- Experiment with different headband styles and colors to find the perfect look for you

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the 60s hair with headband look with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still create the look with shorter hair. Instead of using hot rollers or a curling iron, use a small barrel curling iron to create tight curls.

Q: Can I wear a headband with bangs?

A: Yes, you can wear a headband with bangs. Just position the headband slightly further back on your head so that it sits above your bangs.

Hair Care Tips for 60s Hair with Headband

- Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or hot rollers

- Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair crunchy and difficult to style

- Wash your hair every few days instead of every day to help preserve the curls

- Use a wide-toothed comb to gently detangle your hair before styling

With these simple steps, you can achieve the classic and chic 60s hair with headband look in no time. Experiment with different headbands and curling techniques to find the perfect style for you.
