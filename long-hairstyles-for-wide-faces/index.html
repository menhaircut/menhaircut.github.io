Long Hairstyles For Wide Faces Tips Ideas And Styles

Written by Oliver Jul 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying to find the perfect long hairstyle for your wide face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with tips, ideas and styles that will flatter your wide face.

Description

Having a wide face can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that complements your features. However, there are plenty of long hairstyles that can help elongate your face and make it look slimmer.

How To

The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a wide face is to create an illusion of length. This can be achieved by choosing hairstyles that add height or volume to the top of the head.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones and jawline. If they are all roughly the same width, you have a wide face.

Step 2: Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head. This can be achieved with a voluminous updo or a hairstyle with layers that add volume to the crown of your head.

Step 3: Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight. These styles can make your face look wider.

Step 4: Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle. Bangs can help create the illusion of a narrower face by framing your forehead.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a long hairstyle for a wide face:

  • Choose hairstyles with layers. Layers can help elongate your face by adding movement and texture.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short. Short hairstyles can make your face look wider.
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair. Highlights can help draw attention away from your wide face.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a wide face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too flat. These styles can make your face look wider.

What hairstyles can I wear if I have a wide face?

Choose hairstyles that add height or volume to the top of your head. This can be achieved with a voluminous updo, a hairstyle with layers or bangs.

How can I create the illusion of a narrower face?

Choose hairstyles that elongate your face by adding height or volume to the top of your head. You can also consider adding bangs or highlights to draw attention away from your wide face.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips for maintaining long, healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent breakage and split ends.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often. These tools can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips, ideas and styles, you can find the perfect long hairstyle for your wide face. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
