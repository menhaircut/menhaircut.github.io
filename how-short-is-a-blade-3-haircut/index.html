How Short Is A Blade 3 Haircut

Written by Frank Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, short hairstyle, a blade 3 haircut may be the perfect choice for you. But how short is a blade 3 haircut, exactly? In this article, we'll take a closer look at blade 3 haircuts, including how to get one, how to care for it, and more.

Description

A blade 3 haircut is a type of buzz cut that uses a #3 guard on the clippers. This means that the hair is cut to a length of 3/8 of an inch, or about 9.5mm. The hair is cut evenly all over the head, creating a low-maintenance, clean-cut look.

How to

If you're interested in getting a blade 3 haircut, the first step is to find a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. When you're at the salon, ask for a blade 3 haircut and be specific about the length you want. If you're not sure what length is right for you, ask your stylist for recommendations based on your hair type and face shape.

During the cut, your stylist will use clippers with a #3 guard to trim your hair to the desired length. They may also use scissors to blend the hair around the edges for a more natural look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a blade 3 haircut:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Before your appointment, wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup or excess oil.

Step 2: Discuss the length with your stylist

Talk to your stylist about the length you want and ask for their recommendations.

Step 3: Get the cut

Your stylist will use clippers with a #3 guard to cut your hair to the desired length. They may also use scissors to blend the hair around the edges.

Step 4: Style your hair

Once your hair is cut, you can style it as desired using a styling product like pomade or wax.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining a blade 3 haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Consider using a styling product like pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, which can damage your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a blade 3 haircut work for all hair types?

A: A blade 3 haircut can work for most hair types, but it may not be the best choice for very curly or kinky hair.

Q: How often should I get a blade 3 haircut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how neat and tidy you want to keep your cut. Generally, you'll want to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Q: Can I style my blade 3 haircut in different ways?

A: While a blade 3 haircut is relatively short, you can still style it in different ways using a styling product like pomade or wax. Talk to your stylist about different styling options that will work with your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care

Caring for a blade 3 haircut is relatively simple. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Consider using a styling product like pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, which can damage your hair.

In conclusion, a blade 3 haircut is a short, low-maintenance style that can work for most hair types. To get the best results, be specific about the length you want and work with an experienced stylist who can help you achieve your desired look. With proper care and maintenance, your blade 3 haircut can look great for weeks to come.
