How To Sponge My Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to style your natural hair, sponge styling might be just what you need. Sponge styling is a popular technique that uses a special sponge to create defined curls and coils. It's a great option for those who want to add some texture and volume to their natural hair without using heat or chemicals. In this article, we'll show you how to sponge your natural hair in a few simple steps.

Description

Sponge styling is a technique that involves using a sponge with small holes to create defined curls and coils in natural hair. The sponge is used to twist and turn the hair, creating a textured look that's perfect for those who want to add some volume and definition to their natural hair. Sponge styling is a great alternative to using heat or chemicals, and it's a popular technique among those with curly and kinky hair.

How to Sponge My Natural Hair

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before you start sponge styling your hair, make sure it's clean and moisturized. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help keep your hair moisturized.

Step 2: Get the right sponge

There are different types of sponges available for sponge styling, but the most popular one is the twist sponge. Make sure you get a twist sponge with small holes that will create defined curls and coils. You can find twist sponges at your local beauty supply store or online.

Step 3: Section your hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to sponge style your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Use the sponge

Take your twist sponge and place it on a small section of your hair. Start twisting the sponge in a circular motion, moving it in the direction you want your curls or coils to go. Repeat this process on each section of your hair until you've covered your entire head.

Step 5: Define your curls

Once you've sponge styled your hair, you can define your curls or coils even further by using a curl defining cream or gel. Apply a small amount of product to your hair and use your fingers to define your curls or coils.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when sponge styling your natural hair:

- Always start with clean, moisturized hair to get the best results. - Use a small amount of product to avoid weighing down your hair. - Twist the sponge in the direction you want your curls or coils to go. - Don't use too much pressure when sponge styling your hair to avoid damaging it.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about sponge styling natural hair:

Can sponge styling damage my hair?

No, sponge styling does not damage hair. However, it's important to use a gentle touch when sponge styling to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

How long does sponge styling last?

The length of time that sponge styling lasts depends on your hair type and how well you maintain your hair. On average, sponge styling can last up to a week.

Can I sponge style wet hair?

Yes, you can sponge style wet or damp hair. In fact, some people find that sponge styling works better on wet hair.

Hair Care

To keep your sponge styled hair looking its best, it's important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized. - Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible. - Protect your hair with a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase when you sleep.

Sponge styling is a great way to add texture, volume, and definition to your natural hair. By following these simple steps and tips, you can achieve a beautiful, sponge styled look that's perfect for any occasion.
