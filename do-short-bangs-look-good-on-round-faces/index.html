Do Short Bangs Look Good On Round Faces

Written by Oliver Sep 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short bangs are a popular trend that can give you a fresh and youthful look. However, if you have a round face, you might be wondering if short bangs are a good idea. In this article, we'll explore whether short bangs look good on round faces and provide you with some tips and advice to help you decide if this style is right for you.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How To
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short bangs can be a great way to add some edge and personality to your hairstyle. However, when it comes to round faces, the key is finding the right length and shape to flatter your features. Short bangs can be tricky for round faces because they can emphasize the width of your face and make it look even rounder. However, if done right, short bangs can actually balance out your features and make your face appear slimmer.

How To

If you're considering short bangs for your round face, here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Choose the Right Length

The length of your bangs is crucial when it comes to flattery. For round faces, it's best to avoid bangs that are too short or too long. Instead, opt for bangs that hit just above your eyebrows, as this length will help to create the illusion of length and balance out your face.

2. Consider the Shape

The shape of your bangs is also important. If you have a round face, it's best to avoid bangs that are blunt or straight across. Instead, choose bangs that are slightly angled or layered, as these shapes will help to create the illusion of length and slimness.

3. Consult with Your Stylist

If you're unsure about what length or shape of bangs will best suit your face, it's always a good idea to consult with your stylist. They can help you choose the right style for your face shape and even show you different options using a virtual hairstyle tool.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you rock short bangs with a round face:

1. Add Some Texture

Adding some texture to your bangs can help to create the illusion of length and slimness. Try using a texturizing spray or wax to create a messy, tousled look.

2. Frame Your Face

Adding some layers around your face can also help to balance out your features. Ask your stylist to add some face-framing layers to your haircut to create a more flattering look.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and shapes of bangs to find the one that works best for you. You might be surprised at how different styles can completely change the look of your face.

FAQ

Q: Are short bangs a good idea for round faces?

A: Short bangs can be a good idea for round faces, as long as they are the right length and shape. Bangs that hit just above the eyebrows and are slightly angled or layered can help to create the illusion of length and slimness.

Q: How do I know if short bangs will look good on me?

A: The best way to know if short bangs will look good on you is to consult with your stylist. They can help you choose the right length and shape of bangs that will flatter your face shape.

Q: Can I wear short bangs if I have a double chin?

A: Short bangs can be tricky if you have a double chin, as they can emphasize the area. If you have a double chin, it's best to avoid short bangs and opt for longer, face-framing layers instead.

Hair Care

Short bangs require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your short bangs:

1. Trim Them Regularly

Short bangs grow quickly, so it's important to trim them regularly to keep them at the right length. You can either trim them yourself using scissors or visit your stylist for a professional trim every few weeks.

2. Use a Texturizing Product

Using a texturizing product can help to add some volume and texture to your bangs, making them easier to style and more flattering.

3. Keep Them Clean

Short bangs can get greasy and dirty quickly, so it's important to keep them clean. Wash your bangs every other day with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep them looking fresh and clean.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make your bangs look frizzy and dry. Avoid using heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners on your bangs, and instead let them air dry and style them with your fingers.

In conclusion, short bangs can look good on round faces as long as they are the right length and shape. Consult with your stylist and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. With the right care and maintenance, short bangs can be a fun and trendy addition to your hairstyle.
