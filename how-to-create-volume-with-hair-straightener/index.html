How To Create Volume With Hair Straightener

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have flat, lifeless hair that lacks volume? Are you tired of trying out different hair products that don't seem to work? Well, fear not! In this article, we'll show you how to create volume with a hair straightener. Read on to learn how to achieve voluminous hair that lasts all day long.

Description

Creating volume with a hair straightener is a great way to add bounce and body to your hair. It's a simple technique that involves using a straightener to lift your hair at the roots, creating a voluminous look. This technique is perfect for those who have naturally flat or thin hair, as it can instantly transform your hair from drab to fab!

How to

Creating volume with a hair straightener is easy, and it only takes a few steps. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual. Once you've towel-dried your hair, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. This will help your hair hold its shape and add extra volume.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, section your hair into manageable pieces. Use hair clips to keep each section in place while you work on the rest of your hair.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Straightener

Turn on your hair straightener and let it heat up. Make sure it's set to the right temperature for your hair type. For fine or thin hair, use a lower temperature, while thicker hair can handle higher temperatures.

Step 4: Start Straightening

Take a section of hair and place the straightener at the roots. Clamp the straightener down and hold it in place for a few seconds. Then, slowly glide the straightener down the length of your hair, creating a smooth, straight look.

Step 5: Lift Your Hair

Once you've straightened your hair, use the straightener to lift your hair at the roots. Clamp down on a section of hair near the roots, then twist the straightener away from your head. Hold it in place for a few seconds, then release. This will create a lifted, voluminous look.

Step 6: Finish Your Style

Repeat steps 4 and 5 on the rest of your hair, section by section. Once you've finished, flip your hair over and give it a good shake. This will help to loosen up your hair and add even more volume. Finally, apply a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

To get the best results when creating volume with a hair straightener, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage to your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Try backcombing your hair at the roots for an even more voluminous look.
  • Experiment with different styles, like loose waves or curls, to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

How long will my voluminous style last?

Your voluminous style should last all day long, especially if you use a light hairspray to hold it in place. However, if your hair tends to fall flat easily, you may need to touch up your style throughout the day.

Can I create volume with a hair straightener on short hair?

Absolutely! Creating volume with a hair straightener works on all hair lengths, including short hair. Just make sure to use a smaller straightener and section your hair carefully.

Hair Care

Creating volume with a hair straightener is a great way to add body and bounce to your hair. However, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and avoid using them every day. Additionally, make sure to deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and strong.

Now that you know how to create volume with a hair straightener, it's time to give it a try! Follow the steps outlined in this article, and you'll have voluminous hair in no time.
