How To Get Rid Of A Square Face Shape

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you have a square face shape and want to soften your features? You're not alone! Many people with square faces feel self-conscious about the boxy shape of their face. But don't worry, there are ways to minimize the squareness of your face and enhance your natural beauty. In this article, we'll share with you some tips and tricks on how to get rid of a square face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Rid of a Square Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Square Face Shape

How to Get Rid of a Square Face Shape

Before we get into the specifics, it's important to understand what a square face shape looks like. A square face has a strong jawline, wide forehead, and sharp angles on the cheeks. If you have a square face, you may feel that your features are too harsh or masculine.

The good news is that there are ways to soften the squareness of your face and create a more feminine, oval shape. Here are some tips:

1. Contour your face

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create the illusion of depth and shadow on your face. By contouring your cheeks, jawline, and forehead, you can make your square face appear more oval. Use a bronzer or contouring powder that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone, and apply it in the areas you want to slim down. Blend well for a natural look.

2. Highlight your cheekbones

Highlighting your cheekbones can help to balance out the sharp angles of your face. Apply a highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones, blending it upwards towards your temples. This will draw attention to the high points of your face and create a more lifted, contoured look.

3. Soften your jawline

If you have a strong jawline, you may want to soften the edges for a more feminine look. One way to do this is by using a lighter shade of contouring powder or bronzer along the jawline. This will create the illusion of a softer, less angular jawline. You can also try wearing your hair in a way that covers the sides of your face, such as a side-swept bang or long layers.

4. Wear the right hairstyle

Your hairstyle can also make a big difference in the appearance of your face shape. If you have a square face, avoid straight bangs and blunt cuts that accentuate the sharp angles. Instead, opt for soft, layered styles that frame your face and create a more oval shape. Side-swept bangs, long layers, and waves are all great options.

5. Use the right accessories

Accessories can help to draw attention away from the squareness of your face and create a more balanced look. Try wearing statement earrings or a bold necklace to draw attention to your upper body. Scarves and hats can also be a great way to add some dimension to your outfit and soften your features.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting rid of a square face shape:

  1. Contour your cheeks, jawline, and forehead using a bronzer or contouring powder that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone.
  2. Apply a highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones, blending it upwards towards your temples.
  3. Use a lighter shade of contouring powder or bronzer along the jawline to soften the edges.
  4. Wear soft, layered hairstyles that frame your face and create a more oval shape.
  5. Avoid straight bangs and blunt cuts that accentuate the sharp angles of your face.
  6. Wear statement earrings or a bold necklace to draw attention to your upper body.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for getting rid of a square face shape:

  • Experiment with different makeup techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Try wearing glasses with curved frames to soften the angles of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider getting a haircut that adds volume to the crown of your head, which can create the illusion of a longer, more oval face shape.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and movement.

FAQs

Q: Can I change the shape of my face permanently?

A: No, you cannot change the shape of your face permanently. However, there are ways to enhance your natural features and create the illusion of a different face shape.

Q: Are there any skincare products that can help with a square face shape?

A: While there are no skincare products that can change the shape of your face, using a moisturizer can help to keep your skin looking healthy and radiant. This can help to draw attention away from the squareness of your face.

Q: Will losing weight help to get rid of a square face shape?

A: Losing weight may help to reduce the appearance of a square face shape, as it can slim down the cheeks and jawline. However, this is not a guaranteed solution, as genetics also play a role in the shape of your face.

Hair Care for Square Face Shape

In addition to the tips we've already shared, here are some hair care tips specifically for square face shapes:

  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Opt for side parts or deep side parts to create the illusion of a longer, more oval face shape.
  • Try adding volume to the crown of your head with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to create volume and movement.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to create a more textured, voluminous look.

By following these tips and tricks, you can minimize the squareness of your face and enhance your natural beauty. Remember, confidence is key, so embrace your unique features and rock your square face with pride!
