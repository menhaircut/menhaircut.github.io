How To Cut Woman Hair With Scissors

Written by Oliver Jun 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of paying high prices for haircuts at the salon? Want to learn how to cut your own hair or your friend's hair? Cutting hair with scissors may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. In this article, we will go over step-by-step instructions on how to cut woman hair with scissors, including tips and FAQs.

How to

Step 1: Gather the Right Tools

Before you start cutting, you need to make sure you have the right tools. You will need:

  • Sharp haircutting scissors (do not use regular household scissors)
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water
  • Trimming shears (optional)

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Using your comb, part the hair down the middle and then from ear to ear. Clip the top section of hair away from the bottom section.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting with the bottom section of hair, take a small section and comb it straight down. Hold the section of hair with your fingers and cut it to the desired length. Repeat with small sections of hair, working your way around the head.

Once you have finished the bottom section, unclip the top section of hair and repeat the process.

Step 4: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, comb them straight down and cut them to the desired length. Make sure to cut them at an angle to create a natural look.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

After you have finished cutting, check your hair for evenness. Make sure both sides are the same length and that there are no uneven areas.

Tips

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Use your fingers to hold the hair instead of the comb.
  • Only cut a little bit of hair at a time to avoid cutting too much off.
  • Angle the scissors while cutting to create a natural look.
  • Use trimming shears to add texture and layers.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

It is recommended to cut hair when it is dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it actually is, which can lead to uneven cuts.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

What if I make a mistake while cutting?

If you make a mistake, do not panic. You can always go to a salon to fix it or try to fix it yourself by trimming a little bit more off.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting, make sure to take care of your hair. Use a deep conditioner to keep it healthy and moisturized, and avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.

Cutting your own hair can be a great way to save money and experiment with new styles. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve salon-worthy results at home.
