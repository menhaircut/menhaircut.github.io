How To Layer Short Hair Cut A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of your short haircut looking flat and lifeless? Adding layers to your hair can give it volume, texture, and movement. In this guide, we'll walk you through the process of layering short hair cut.

  1. How to Layer Short Hair Cut
  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Layer Short Hair Cut

Layering short hair cut involves cutting the hair at different lengths to create volume, texture, and movement. It's important to choose the right technique and tools to achieve the desired look.

Techniques for Layering Short Hair Cut

There are several techniques for layering short hair cut:

  • Razor Cutting: This technique involves using a razor to cut the hair at a steep angle. It creates soft, wispy layers and is ideal for fine hair.
  • Point Cutting: This technique involves using scissors to cut the hair at an angle. It creates chunky, textured layers and is ideal for thick hair.
  • Slide Cutting: This technique involves using scissors to cut the hair while sliding the blades down the hair shaft. It creates seamless layers and is ideal for all hair types.

Tools for Layering Short Hair Cut

The tools you'll need for layering short hair cut are:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Razor (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add lift and body to your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Clip each section out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Top Layers

Start with the top section. Take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Cut it at an angle using scissors or a razor. Repeat this process for the entire top section.

Step 4: Cut the Side Layers

Move on to the side sections. Take a small section of hair and hold it out from your head. Cut it at an angle using scissors or a razor. Repeat this process for both side sections.

Step 5: Cut the Back Layers

Finally, move on to the back section. Take a small section of hair and hold it out from your head. Cut it at an angle using scissors or a razor. Repeat this process for the entire back section.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Blend the layers by combing your hair and cutting any uneven pieces. Use a razor or scissors to soften any harsh lines.

Tips for Layering Short Hair Cut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect layered short haircut:

  • Start with small sections of hair to avoid cutting too much at once.
  • Use a razor for a softer, more textured look.
  • Use scissors for a more defined, chunky look.
  • Be patient and take your time. Layering short hair cut can be tricky, but with practice, you'll get the hang of it.

FAQs

Can I layer short hair cut at home?

Yes, you can layer short hair cut at home. Just make sure you have the right tools and technique.

What face shapes are best for layered short hair cut?

Layered short hair cut looks best on oval, heart, and square face shapes.

Hair Care for Layered Short Hair Cut

Here are some tips to keep your layered short hair cut looking its best:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add lift and body to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the layers.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and texture of your layered short hair cut.
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect layered short hair cut. Experiment with different techniques and tools to find the look that works best for you.
