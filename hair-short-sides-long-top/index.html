Hair Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a trendy and versatile haircut, then the hair short sides long top is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been popularized by celebrities and fashion icons, and it's easy to see why. It's a classic look that can be adapted to suit any face shape and hair texture. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about the hair short sides long top, from how to style it to hair care tips.

Description

The hair short sides long top is a staple hairstyle for men that involves keeping the hair on the sides and back of the head short, while leaving the hair on top long. The length of the hair on top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on personal preference. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

How to Style Hair Short Sides Long Top

The hair short sides long top is a versatile style that can be styled in many ways. Here are some popular ways to style this look:

  • Slicked back: Apply pomade or gel to damp hair and comb the hair back using a comb. This look is perfect for formal occasions or a night out.
  • Messy: Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or wax to dry hair and use your fingers to style the hair in a messy, tousled look. This look is perfect for a casual day out.
  • Side part: Apply pomade or gel to damp hair and use a comb to create a side part. This look is perfect for a classic, sophisticated look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the hair short sides long top:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product, like pomade or gel, to your hair.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Hair Short Sides Long Top

To keep your hair short sides long top looking fresh and stylish, follow these tips:

  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the length of the hair on the sides and back.
  • Use a high-quality styling product that suits your hair type.
  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, like blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage the hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the hair short sides long top:

Is the hair short sides long top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the hair short sides long top can be adapted to suit any face shape. Your barber can help you choose a style that complements your face shape.

What hair types work best with the hair short sides long top?

The hair short sides long top works best with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, it may be more difficult to style this look.

What styling products should I use?

The best styling products for the hair short sides long top are pomade, gel, and wax. Choose a product that suits your hair type and desired look.

Hair Care for Short Sides Long Top

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your hair short sides long top looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, like blow dryers and straighteners, as this can damage the hair.
  • Use a high-quality styling product that suits your hair type.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the length of the hair on the sides and back.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a stylish and versatile hair short sides long top that suits your personal style. Whether you prefer a slicked back look or a messy, tousled look, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
