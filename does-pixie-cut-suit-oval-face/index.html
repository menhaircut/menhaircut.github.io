Does Pixie Cut Suit Oval Face

Are you planning to get a pixie cut but wondering if it will suit your oval face? Well, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about whether a pixie cut suits an oval face or not. So, let's get started!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How-to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is usually cut around the ears and the back of the head. It is a bold and edgy haircut that has become increasingly popular in recent years. However, not everyone can pull off a pixie cut, especially if you have an oval face. An oval face is considered to be the most balanced and symmetrical face shape, with the forehead being slightly wider than the chin. So, the question arises, does a pixie cut suit an oval face?

How-to

The answer is yes, a pixie cut can definitely suit an oval face. In fact, an oval face is considered to be the most versatile face shape, and you can experiment with a variety of hairstyles. However, there are a few things to keep in mind before getting a pixie cut for an oval face.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow to ensure that a pixie cut suits your oval face:

  1. Consider your hair texture and thickness. The thickness and texture of your hair can make a huge difference in how a pixie cut looks on you. If you have thin hair, a pixie cut may not be the best option for you. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a pixie cut can look amazing.
  2. Choose the right length. A pixie cut can vary in length, and it is essential to choose the right length that suits your face shape. For an oval face, a shorter pixie cut that frames the face can work well.
  3. Consider your facial features. A pixie cut can highlight your facial features, so it is essential to consider them before getting the haircut. If you have a prominent nose or jawline, a pixie cut can accentuate them.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist. It is always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist before getting a pixie cut. They can recommend the best haircut for your face shape and give you tips on how to style it.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider adding bangs. Bangs can complement an oval face and add some texture to a pixie cut.
  • Experiment with different styles. A pixie cut is versatile, and you can experiment with different styles and textures to find what works best for you.
  • Maintain your haircut. A pixie cut requires regular maintenance, and it is essential to keep it trimmed to prevent it from looking unkempt.

FAQ

Can a pixie cut make an oval face look longer?

No, a pixie cut can actually make an oval face look shorter. This is because the short length of the haircut draws attention to the face and can make it appear wider.

What face shape is best suited for a pixie cut?

A pixie cut can suit a variety of face shapes, including round, heart, and square faces. However, it is important to choose the right length and style for your face shape.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your pixie cut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a styling product to keep your pixie cut in place and add some texture.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

So, there you have it. A pixie cut can definitely suit an oval face, as long as you keep in mind the length, texture, and facial features. With the right haircut and care, a pixie cut can be a stylish and edgy choice for an oval face.
