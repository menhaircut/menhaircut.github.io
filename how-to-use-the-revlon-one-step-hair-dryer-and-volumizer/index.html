How To Use The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer And Volumizer

Written by William Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to get salon-worthy hair at home, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is an excellent tool to add to your collection. This hair dryer and volumizer combo is perfect for all hair types and is designed to add volume and shine to your hair without causing damage. In this article, we'll show you how to use the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer for the best results.

Description

The Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is a hair tool that combines the power of a hair dryer with the volume of a styling brush. The brush is designed with tufted bristles that help detangle hair while the hot air blows through it. The oval shape of the brush is perfect for creating volume at the roots and curling the ends of your hair.

How to Use the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer

Step 1: Towel Dry Your Hair

Before you start using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, make sure your hair is towel-dried. It's best to use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair, as these materials are gentle on your hair and won't cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before you start using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer. This will help protect your hair from heat damage and keep it looking healthy.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Start with the Bottom Layers

Start with the bottom layers of your hair, working your way up to the top. Take a section of hair and place the brush at the roots. Slowly move the brush down the length of your hair while turning it inward to create a curl. Hold the brush in place for a few seconds to set the curl, then release.

Step 5: Move to the Top Layers

Once you've finished with the bottom layers, move on to the top layers of your hair. Use the same technique as before, starting at the roots and working your way down. For more volume, hold the brush at the roots for a few seconds to create a lift.

Step 6: Finish with a Blast of Cold Air

Once you've finished styling your hair, give it a blast of cold air to set the style. This will help your hair hold its shape and keep it looking shiny and healthy.

Tips for Using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer:

  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to work with.
  • Start with the bottom layers of your hair and work your way up.
  • For more volume, hold the brush at the roots for a few seconds to create a lift.
  • Finish with a blast of cold air to set the style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer:

Is the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is suitable for all hair types, including thick, curly, and fine hair.

How long does it take to dry and style your hair with the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer?

The time it takes to dry and style your hair with the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer depends on the length and thickness of your hair. On average, it takes about 15-20 minutes to style your hair.

Hair Care

Using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer is a great way to style your hair without causing damage, but it's still important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

By following these tips and using the Revlon One Step Hair Dryer and Volumizer, you can achieve beautiful, healthy-looking hair at home.
