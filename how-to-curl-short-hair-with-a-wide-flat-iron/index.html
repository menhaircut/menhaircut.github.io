How To Curl Short Hair With A Wide Flat Iron

Written by Bardi Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that you can't achieve those beautiful, bouncy curls that you see on celebrities and models. But with the right technique and tools, you can create stunning curls with a wide flat iron. In this article, we'll show you how to curl short hair with a wide flat iron in just a few easy steps.

  • How to choose the right flat iron

How to Prepare Your Hair for Curling

Before you start curling your hair, it's important to prepare it properly. Begin by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup that may be weighing down your hair.

Once your hair is clean, use a heat protectant spray to shield your hair from damage. Apply the spray evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

To give your hair some texture and hold, apply a small amount of mousse or styling cream. This will also help your curls to last longer.

How to Choose the Right Flat Iron

When it comes to curling short hair with a flat iron, you need to choose the right tool for the job. Look for a wide flat iron with a temperature control feature. The wider plates will help you to create bigger curls, while the temperature control will ensure that you don't burn your hair.

It's also important to choose a flat iron with ceramic plates, as these will distribute heat evenly and minimize damage to your hair.

How to Curl Short Hair with a Wide Flat Iron

Now that your hair is prepped and you have the right flat iron, it's time to start curling. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Divide your hair into sections, starting from the bottom.
  2. Take a small section of hair and clamp it between the plates of your flat iron.
  3. Twist the flat iron away from your face, making sure to twist it all the way to the ends of your hair.
  4. Hold the flat iron in place for a few seconds, then release.
  5. Repeat steps 2-4 until all of your hair is curled.

When you're finished, use your fingers to gently tousle your curls and give them some volume.

Tips for Achieving Perfect Curls

If you want your curls to look their best, follow these tips:

  • Use a smaller section of hair for tighter curls, or a larger section for looser curls.
  • Hold the flat iron vertically instead of horizontally for more natural-looking curls.
  • Don't hold the flat iron in place for too long, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a hairspray or texturizing spray to set your curls in place.

FAQ

Q: Can I curl short hair with a regular-sized flat iron?

A: Yes, you can, but a wide flat iron will give you bigger, more voluminous curls that are perfect for short hair.

Q: Can I curl my hair without using a heat protectant spray?

A: We highly recommend using a heat protectant spray to minimize damage to your hair.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: This depends on your hair type and how well you maintain your curls. On average, you can expect your curls to last for a day or two.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

To keep your curls looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make your curls frizzy.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing damage.

With these tips and techniques, you can create beautiful curls with a wide flat iron, no matter how short your hair is. Give it a try and see how easy it is to achieve the look you want!
