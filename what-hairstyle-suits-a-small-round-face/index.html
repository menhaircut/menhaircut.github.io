What Hairstyle Suits a Small Round Face - Tips and Tricks

A small round face can be challenging to style, but with the right haircut and hairstyle, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks on what hairstyle suits a small round face and how to style your hair to flatter your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A small round face has soft features, rounded chin, and cheeks, with the width and length in proportion to each other. The aim of styling hair for a small round face is to elongate the face shape and create an illusion of a more oval face. The ideal hairstyle for a small round face is one that adds height and volume to the crown area and avoids adding width on the sides.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a small round face, consider the following factors:

  • The length of your hair
  • Your hair texture
  • Your face shape
  • Your lifestyle and personality

Short haircuts work well for small round faces as they help to create an illusion of length. A pixie cut with choppy layers or a bob with angled ends can accentuate your cheekbones and elongate your face. Medium and long hairstyles with layers and volume on top also work well for small round faces. Curls and waves can add volume and texture to your hair and make your face look more oval.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when styling your hair for a small round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Use a volumizing mousse or spray on the roots
  3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, focusing on the crown area
  4. Create volume by lifting the roots with a comb or your fingers
  5. If you have long hair, consider adding layers and texture to the ends
  6. Avoid adding volume on the sides and keep your hair close to your head
  7. Use a hairspray or styling cream to hold your hair in place

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a small round face:

  • Avoid blunt bangs or heavy layers that can make your face look wider
  • Consider a side part or asymmetrical haircut to create more dimension
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what suits you best
  • Use hair accessories like headbands or clips to add interest to your style

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a small round face?

The best haircut for a small round face is one that adds height and volume to the crown area and avoids adding width on the sides. Short haircuts like a pixie cut or a bob with angled ends work well, as do medium and long hairstyles with layers and volume on top.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a small round face?

Avoid blunt bangs or heavy layers that can make your face look wider. Also, avoid hairstyles that add volume on the sides, like a bouffant or a wide-brimmed hat.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools. Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking fresh.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for a small round face can be a game-changer. With the right haircut, styling, and hair care, you can accentuate your best features and look your best. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personality when choosing a hairstyle, and don't be afraid to experiment.
