How To Fix Baby Hairs On Frontal A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who loves to wear wigs or hair extensions, you must know how frustrating it can be to deal with baby hairs on your frontal. These tiny, wispy hairs can ruin your hairline and make your wig or extension look unnatural. But don't worry, fixing baby hairs on the frontal is not rocket science. In this article, we'll guide you through some easy steps to fix those baby hairs and achieve a flawless hairline.

Description

Baby hairs are the short, fine hairs that grow around the hairline. They are usually soft and wispy, making them difficult to manage. Baby hairs on the frontal can be more noticeable when you wear wigs or extensions, especially if your hairline is not well-defined.

How to Fix Baby Hairs on Frontal: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Comb Your Hairline

Before you start fixing your baby hairs, make sure your hairline is smooth and free of tangles. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently comb your hairline from the forehead to the nape of your neck.

Step 2: Apply Edge Control

Edge control is a styling product that helps to tame baby hairs and keep them in place. Using a small brush, apply a small amount of edge control along your hairline. Make sure to apply it evenly and avoid applying too much product.

Step 3: Use a Toothbrush

Using a soft-bristled toothbrush, gently brush your baby hairs in the direction you want them to go. This will help to smooth them out and keep them in place. Be gentle and avoid applying too much pressure, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hairline.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've fixed your baby hairs, style your hair as desired. You can wear a wig or extension, or style your natural hair. Whatever style you choose, make sure to keep your hairline smooth and well-defined.

Tips for Fixing Baby Hairs on Frontal

Here are some additional tips that can help you fix your baby hairs on the frontal:

  • Use a small amount of edge control to avoid a sticky or greasy look.
  • Don't apply too much pressure when brushing your baby hairs.
  • For a more natural look, leave some baby hairs out instead of taming them all.
  • Use a toothbrush with soft bristles to avoid damage to your hairline.
  • Try different edge control products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use gel instead of edge control?

A: Yes, you can use gel to fix your baby hairs on the frontal. However, edge control is specifically designed for this purpose and tends to work better than gel.

Q: How often should I fix my baby hairs?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you wear wigs or extensions. You may need to fix your baby hairs every day or every few days.

Q: Can I use a hairdryer to fix my baby hairs?

A: No, using a hairdryer can cause damage to your hairline and make your baby hairs frizzy. It's best to use a toothbrush or a small brush to fix your baby hairs.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips that can help you maintain a healthy hairline:

  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent friction and breakage.
  • Don't overuse styling products, as they can cause buildup and damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can fix your baby hairs on the frontal and achieve a flawless hairline. Remember to be gentle and patient when styling your hair, and always prioritize hair health over looks. Happy styling!
