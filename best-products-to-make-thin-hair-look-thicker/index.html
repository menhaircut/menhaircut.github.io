Best Products To Make Thin Hair Look Thicker

Written by Petter Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thin hair, you may often feel like you're stuck with a flat and lifeless look. However, with the right products and techniques, you can make your hair look fuller and thicker. In this article, we'll share some of the best products to make thin hair look thicker, including shampoos, conditioners, hair treatments, and styling products.

How to Make Thin Hair Look Thicker

Before we dive into the best products for thicker-looking hair, let's first discuss some general techniques for making your hair look fuller:

  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair more body.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Try teasing or backcombing your hair to create more volume.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to add dimension to your hair.

Best Products for Thicker-Looking Hair

Now that you know some general techniques for making your hair look thicker, let's take a look at some of the best products for achieving that fuller look:

Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

A volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add body and thickness to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and panthenol, which can strengthen hair and boost volume.

Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo can be a game-changer for thin hair. It can help absorb excess oil and add texture to your hair, which can make it look thicker. Apply dry shampoo to the roots of your hair and brush it through for best results.

Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are tiny pieces of keratin that can be sprinkled onto your hair to make it look thicker. They come in a variety of colors to match your hair, and can be applied to dry hair with a special applicator.

Hair Thickening Spray

Hair thickening spray can help add volume and texture to your hair without weighing it down. It can also provide some hold and help your hairstyle last longer. Apply the spray to damp hair and blow-dry for best results.

Hair Mousse

Hair mousse is a lightweight styling product that can help add volume and hold to your hair. Apply it to damp hair and blow-dry for best results. Look for a mousse that is specifically designed for thin hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Thicker-Looking Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving thicker-looking hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  3. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.
  4. Apply hair fibers to any areas where your hair is particularly thin.
  5. Apply a hair thickening spray to your hair, focusing on the roots.
  6. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, focusing on the roots.
  7. When your hair is dry, apply a small amount of hair mousse to add volume and hold.
  8. Tease or backcomb your hair at the crown for even more volume.
  9. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Thicker-Looking Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your thicker-looking hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental stressors.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs about Thinning Hair

What causes thinning hair?

Thinning hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, stress, and certain medical conditions.

Can hair grow back after thinning?

In some cases, hair can grow back after thinning. However, this depends on the cause of the thinning and the individual's overall health.

What are some natural remedies for thinning hair?

Some natural remedies for thinning hair include using essential oils, massaging the scalp, and taking supplements like biotin and vitamin D.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair

If you have thinning hair, it's important to take good care of it to prevent further damage. Here are some hair care tips for thinning hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as brushing can cause breakage.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back into tight hairstyles, as this can cause traction alopecia.
  • If you're experiencing hair loss, talk to your doctor or a dermatologist to determine the underlying cause and develop a treatment plan.

By following these tips and using the right products, you can make your thin hair look fuller and thicker. Experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you.
