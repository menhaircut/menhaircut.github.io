Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and make it easier to style your hair.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two or three parts. This will make it easier to style your hair.

Step 5: Create a Ponytail

Create a ponytail with one section of your hair. Use a hair tie to secure it.

Step 6: Create a Bun

Twist the ponytail around the base to create a bun. Use bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

Step 7: Repeat Steps 5 and 6

Repeat steps 5 and 6 with the other sections of your hair. Create as many buns as you want.

Step 8: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your updo hairstyle for long hair African American with a light mist of hairspray. This will keep your hair in place and prevent frizz.
