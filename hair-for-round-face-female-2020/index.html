Hair For Round Face Female 2020 How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by Bardi Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering what the best hairstyle is for you. In 2020, there were many trendy hairstyles that complemented round faces, but what are the most popular ones in 2023? In this blog post, we'll guide you through the best hair for round face female 2020 and beyond. From how to choose the perfect style to hair care tips, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

How to Choose the Perfect Hair for Round Face Female 2020?

The key to choosing the perfect hair for a round face is to create the illusion of length. This means that you should opt for hairstyles that elongate your face rather than add volume to it. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Look for hairstyles with layers

Layers can help add texture and movement to your hair while making your face appear slimmer. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face look rounder.

2. Consider side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer. Avoid straight bangs that end at your eyebrows, as they can make your face look wider.

3. Opt for hairstyles with volume on top

Hairstyles with volume on top can help elongate your face and balance out a round chin. Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or slicked back, as they can make your face look wider.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Hair for Round Face Female 2020

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here's a step by step guide to styling your hair for a round face:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help create the illusion of length and add texture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots and work it through your hair using your fingers. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create movement. Focus on lifting the hair at the roots to create the illusion of length.

Step 4: Style your hair

Style your hair according to the hairstyle you've chosen. If you've opted for layers, use a curling iron to create loose waves. If you've opted for side-swept bangs, blow dry them using a round brush to create volume and movement.

Tips for Maintaining Hair for Round Face Female 2020

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your round face, here are some tips for maintaining it:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. This will help your hair look fuller and healthier.

2. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help create the illusion of length and add texture to your hair.

3. Avoid using heat styling tools too often

Using heat styling tools too often can damage your hair and make it look flat. Try to air dry your hair as often as possible and use heat styling tools sparingly.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Straight hair can look great on a round face as long as it's styled in a way that elongates the face. Consider adding layers or volume on top to create the illusion of length.

2. Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as tight curls or blunt bobs that end at your chin. These can make your face look wider and rounder.

3. Can I wear short hair if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Short hair can look great on a round face as long as it's styled in a way that elongates the face. Consider adding layers or volume on top to create the illusion of length.

Hair for Round Face Female 2020: Final Thoughts

Choosing the perfect hair for a round face doesn't have to be difficult. By following our tips and tricks, you can find a hairstyle that complements your face shape and makes you feel confident. Remember to keep your hair healthy by using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner and trimming it regularly. With the right hair care routine and styling techniques, you can rock any hairstyle you choose!
