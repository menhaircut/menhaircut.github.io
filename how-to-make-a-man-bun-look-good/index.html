How To Make A Man Bun Look Good

Written by Robby Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your hair falling on your face during the hot summer months? Or maybe you just want to switch up your hairstyle? Whatever your reason, the man bun is a great option for men of all ages. But the question is, how do you make a man bun look good? In this article, we'll go through step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice on how to make a man bun look good.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make a Man Bun
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Making a Man Bun Look Good

How to Make a Man Bun

A man bun is a simple hairstyle that requires only a few steps:

  1. Brush your hair upwards towards the crown of your head.
  2. Twist your hair into a tight coil.
  3. Wrap your twisted hair around the base of your ponytail.
  4. Secure the bun with an elastic band.

That's it! You now have a man bun. But of course, we want to make sure it looks good, so let's dive into the step-by-step instructions.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Follow these step-by-step instructions for a perfectly styled man bun:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If you have long hair, brush it upwards towards the crown of your head. If you have shorter hair, use a comb to create a small ponytail at the back of your head.
  2. Twist your hair into a tight coil. Start at the base of your ponytail and twist your hair tightly until you reach the ends.
  3. Wrap your twisted hair around the base of your ponytail. Make sure the coil is tight and even.
  4. Secure the bun with an elastic band. Use a hair tie or elastic band to hold the bun in place. Make sure it's tight enough to hold the bun securely, but not so tight that it hurts.
  5. Adjust the bun as necessary. Gently tug on the sides of the bun to make it wider and more even. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  6. Finish with hairspray. Use a light mist of hairspray to hold your man bun in place.

Congratulations, you now have a perfectly styled man bun!

Tips for Making a Man Bun Look Good

Here are some tips for making your man bun look good:

  • Experiment with different placement. You can wear your man bun high on your head, low at the nape of your neck, or off to one side.
  • Try different styles. You can braid your hair before twisting it into a bun, or add a small braid around the base of the bun for a cool accent.
  • Accessorize. Add a headband, bandana, or even a hat to your man bun for a unique look.
  • Keep it clean. Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup and keep your man bun looking fresh.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about man buns:

Can anyone wear a man bun?

Yes, anyone can wear a man bun! However, it's best suited for men with longer hair.

How long does my hair need to be for a man bun?

Your hair should be at least 6 inches long to create a man bun.

Can I wear a man bun to work?

It depends on your workplace's dress code. Some companies may allow it, while others may not. Check with your HR department before wearing a man bun to work.

Hair Care for Man Buns

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy while rocking a man bun:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Use a good conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make your man bun look limp.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight when creating your man bun, as it can cause breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make a man bun look good in no time. So go ahead, try it out, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles!
