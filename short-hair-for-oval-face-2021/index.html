Short Hair For Oval Face 2021 Tips And Ideas

Written by Aprilia Jul 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of long hair? Want to try something new and refreshing? Well, short hair is the way to go! Short hair is not only trendy but also easy to manage. However, not all short hairstyles complement every face shape. If you have an oval face, you are in luck because you can pull off almost any short hairstyle. In this article, we will give you some tips and ideas for short hair for oval face in 2021.

Description: What is an Oval Face?

An oval face is characterized by balanced proportions, high cheekbones, and a narrow forehead. The jawline is slightly rounded, and the face is longer than it is wide. Oval faces are versatile and can pull off most hairstyles, including short hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Hair for Oval Face 2021

Choosing the right short hair for oval face 2021 can be overwhelming, especially if you are not familiar with the different types of short haircuts. Here are some things to consider when choosing the right short hair for your oval face:

Consider your Hair Texture

Your hair texture plays a significant role in how your short hair will look. If you have fine hair, consider a short bob or pixie cut to add volume to your hair. If you have thick hair, consider a layered haircut to reduce the bulkiness.

Consider your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle should also influence the type of short hair you choose. If you have an active lifestyle, a low-maintenance hairstyle like a pixie cut or a bob would be perfect. If you work in a formal setting, consider a sleek bob or lob.

Consider your Personality

Your personality should also reflect in your hairstyle. If you are bold and daring, consider a spiky pixie cut or a platinum blonde bob. If you are more conservative, consider a classic bob or a sleek pixie cut.

Step-by-Step Guide to Short Hair for Oval Face 2021

Now that you know what to consider when choosing the right short hair for oval face 2021, here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect short hair:

Step 1: Consult with your Hairstylist

The first step to achieving the perfect short hair is to consult with your hairstylist. They will help you choose the right style that complements your face shape, hair texture, and personality.

Step 2: Wash and Condition your Hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure to wash and condition it. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair and give it a clean finish.

Step 3: Section your Hair

Your hairstylist will section your hair and use hair clips to hold them in place. This will ensure that they cut your hair evenly and precisely.

Step 4: Cut your Hair

Using a pair of sharp scissors, your hairstylist will cut your hair according to the style you have chosen. They will start at the back and work their way to the front.

Step 5: Style your Hair

Your hairstylist will then style your hair using a blow dryer, flat iron, or curling iron. They may also use hair products to add texture and volume to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Oval Face 2021

Now that you have achieved the perfect short hair for oval face 2021, here are some tips for maintaining it:

Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type and style. This will help maintain the shape and texture of your short hair.

Trim your Hair Regularly

Short haircuts tend to lose their shape quickly. To maintain the shape and style of your short hair, make sure to trim it regularly.

Protect your Hair from Heat Damage

Using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using these tools.

FAQ about Short Hair for Oval Face 2021

Can I pull off a pixie cut with an oval face?

Yes, you can pull off a pixie cut with an oval face. Pixie cuts are perfect for oval faces because they highlight your best features.

What is the best short haircut for thick hair?

The best short haircut for thick hair is a layered haircut. This will reduce the bulkiness of your hair and make it easier to manage.

How often should I wash my short hair?

You should wash your short hair every two to three days. Washing it too often can strip your hair of its natural oils and leave it dry and brittle.

Hair Care for Short Hair for Oval Face 2021

Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Short hair tends to dry out quickly. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails. They can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

Protect your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, short hair for oval face 2021 is a great way to refresh your look. Make sure to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personality when choosing the right short hair for your oval face. Consult with your hairstylist, use the right hair products, and maintain your hair regularly to keep it looking its best.
