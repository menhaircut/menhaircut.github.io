Best Short Haircuts For Round Faces Male

Written by Bardi Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a male with a round face, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that complements your face shape and makes you look your best. Luckily, there are plenty of great short haircuts for round faces male that can help you achieve the perfect look. In this article, we'll go over some of the best options for men with round faces and give you some tips for maintaining your new style.

Description

A round face typically has soft, rounded features with a wider forehead and fuller cheeks. The goal when choosing a short haircut is to create more angles and definition to balance out the roundness of the face. This can be achieved through a variety of styles, from classic to modern cuts.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Round Faces Male

When choosing a short haircut, there are several factors to consider:

  • Face shape
  • Hair type and texture
  • Lifestyle and personal style

For round faces, it's best to choose a style that adds height and volume on top while keeping the sides short. This creates the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape. Avoid styles that are too short or flat on top, as they can make the face appear even rounder.

Step by Step Guide

Here are some steps to follow when choosing the best short haircut for round faces male:

  1. Identify your face shape
  2. Research different short hairstyles for round faces male
  3. Consult with a hairstylist or barber
  4. Choose a style that complements your face shape and personal style
  5. Maintain your new haircut with regular trims and styling

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Once you've chosen the perfect short haircut for round faces male, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep the style looking fresh
  • Use quality hair products to keep hair healthy and styled
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you
  • Consider using a hair dryer or styling gel for added volume on top
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet to promote healthy hair growth

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best short haircut for round faces male?

The best short haircut for round faces male is one that adds height and volume on top while keeping the sides short. This creates the illusion of a longer, more angular face shape. Examples include the classic crew cut, textured quiff, and messy fringe.

Can I still wear a beard with a short haircut for round faces male?

A beard can complement a short haircut for round faces male, but it's important to choose a style that doesn't add too much width to the face. A shorter, more trimmed beard is usually the best option.

How often should I get a trim for my short haircut?

It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the style looking fresh and well-maintained.

Hair Care Tips

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, stylish hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers or flat irons too often, as they can damage hair
  • Protect hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions with a hat or scarf
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals for optimal hair growth and health
  • Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate hair

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short haircut for round faces male and maintain it with ease. Remember to choose a style that complements your face shape and personal style, and don't be afraid to experiment with different cuts and styling techniques.
