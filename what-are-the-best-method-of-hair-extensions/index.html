What Are The Best Methods Of Hair Extensions

Written by Frank Nov 18, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of waiting for your hair to grow out? Do you want to add more volume and length to your hair? Hair extensions might be the solution for you. But with so many types of hair extensions available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to choose. In this article, we’ll break down the best methods of hair extensions to help you make an informed decision.

Description

Hair extensions are artificial hair that is attached to your natural hair to add length, volume, and thickness. They come in different types, lengths, colors, and textures to match your natural hair. Hair extensions are popular among women who want to switch up their look or enhance their natural hair. However, not all hair extensions are created equal, and some methods may damage your hair if not done correctly.

How to Choose the Right Hair Extension Method

Choosing the right hair extension method depends on several factors, including your hair type, lifestyle, and budget. Here are some things to consider when choosing the best method of hair extensions:

Hair Type

The texture and thickness of your hair can affect the type of hair extension method you can use. For example, if you have fine hair, you might want to consider tape-in or clip-in extensions because they are lightweight and won’t weigh down your hair. If you have coarse or curly hair, sew-in or fusion extensions might be a better option because they can blend well with your natural hair.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also affect the type of hair extension method you choose. If you’re active and like to swim or work out, clip-in extensions might be the best option because you can easily remove them before your activities. On the other hand, if you’re looking for a more permanent solution, sew-in or fusion extensions might be a better choice.

Budget

Hair extensions can be expensive, and the cost varies depending on the type of hair extension and the amount of hair you need. Clip-in extensions are the most affordable option, while fusion extensions can be the most expensive. Consider your budget and how much you’re willing to spend before choosing a hair extension method.

Step by Step Guide to the Best Hair Extension Methods

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hair extension method, let’s dive into the best methods and how they work.

Clip-In Hair Extensions

Clip-in hair extensions are the most popular and affordable hair extension method. They are easy to install and remove, making them perfect for special occasions or daily wear. Here’s how to install clip-in hair extensions:

  1. Brush your hair and clip it up.
  2. Open the clips on the hair extensions and place them on the roots of your hair.
  3. Secure the clips by snapping them shut.
  4. Release your hair and blend it with the extensions.

Tip: Use a teasing brush to add more volume and blend your natural hair with the extensions.

Tape-In Hair Extensions

Tape-in hair extensions are another popular method that is easy to install and maintain. Tape-in extensions are attached to your natural hair using a special tape that is safe and non-damaging. Here’s how to install tape-in hair extensions:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before installation.
  2. Section your hair and apply the tape-in extensions to the roots of your hair.
  3. Press the tape to secure the extensions to your natural hair.
  4. Repeat the process until all the extensions are installed.

Tip: Avoid washing your hair for 48 hours after installation to allow the tape to set.

Sew-In Hair Extensions

Sew-in hair extensions are a more permanent solution that can last up to three months with proper maintenance. Sew-in extensions are attached to your natural hair using a needle and thread. Here’s how to install sew-in hair extensions:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before installation.
  2. Braid your natural hair in cornrows.
  3. Sew the extensions to the cornrows using a needle and thread.
  4. Cut and style the extensions to blend with your natural hair.

Tip: Use a hair net to protect your natural hair while sewing in the extensions.

Fusion Hair Extensions

Fusion hair extensions are the most expensive and time-consuming method of hair extensions. Fusion extensions are attached to your natural hair using a keratin glue that is heated and bonded to your hair. Here’s how to install fusion hair extensions:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before installation.
  2. Separate your hair into small sections.
  3. Apply the keratin glue to the extensions and attach them to your natural hair.
  4. Use a hot tool to melt the glue and bond the extensions to your hair.

Tip: Avoid touching the bonds for 24 hours after installation to allow the glue to set.

Tips for Maintaining Hair Extensions

Proper maintenance is essential for keeping your hair extensions looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair extensions:

  • Use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the extensions.
  • Avoid using heat tools on the extensions to prevent damage.
  • Brush your extensions gently to avoid tangling and pulling out your natural hair.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair to prevent matting.
  • Visit a professional stylist for maintenance and removal.

FAQs

How long do hair extensions last?

The lifespan of hair extensions depends on the type of extension and how well they are maintained. Clip-in extensions can last up to a year, while sew-in and fusion extensions can last up to three months.

Can I color my hair extensions?

Most hair extensions can be colored, but it’s best to consult with a professional stylist before doing so. Improper coloring can damage the extensions and your natural hair.

Can I swim with hair extensions?

Yes, you can swim with hair extensions, but it’s best to wear a swim cap to protect the extensions from chlorine and saltwater.

Final Thoughts

Hair extensions can be a game-changer for adding length, volume, and thickness to your hair. However, choosing the right method and maintaining them properly is crucial for achieving the best results. Consider your hair type, lifestyle, and budget before choosing a hair extension method, and always consult with a professional stylist for installation and maintenance.
