How Long To See Results From Microneedling For Hair Loss

Written by Robby Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss and looking for a solution? Microneedling for hair loss is a popular treatment that involves using a micro-needling device to create tiny punctures in the scalp, stimulating hair growth. But how long does it take to see results from microneedling for hair loss? In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about microneedling for hair loss, including how long it takes to see results, how to prepare for the treatment, and more.

Table of Content

  1. How to Microneedle for Hair Loss
  2. Timeline for Results
  3. Tips for Maximizing Results
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After Microneedling

How to Microneedle for Hair Loss

The first step in microneedling for hair loss is to consult with a professional. They can assess your hair loss and determine if microneedling is a suitable treatment for you. If it is, they will explain the process and what to expect.

On the day of the treatment, the scalp will be cleaned and numbed with a topical anesthetic. The micro-needling device will then be used to create tiny punctures in the scalp, stimulating hair growth. The process typically takes 30-60 minutes, depending on the size of the treatment area.

After the treatment, the scalp may be red and tender for a few days. It's important to avoid touching or scratching the scalp during this time to prevent infection.

Timeline for Results

The timeline for results from microneedling for hair loss varies depending on the individual. In general, it can take several weeks to several months to see noticeable results.

Some people may see results as early as 2-4 weeks after the treatment, while others may not see results for several months. It's important to be patient and consistent with the treatment to see the best results.

Tips for Maximizing Results

Here are some tips for maximizing the results of microneedling for hair loss:

  • Be consistent with the treatment. Microneedling for hair loss typically requires several sessions spaced a few weeks apart to see the best results.
  • Follow the aftercare instructions provided by your professional to ensure proper healing and minimize the risk of infection.
  • Consider using hair growth products, such as minoxidil, in conjunction with microneedling for hair loss to maximize results.

FAQs

Is microneedling for hair loss painful?

The scalp is numbed with a topical anesthetic before the treatment, so most people do not experience significant pain during the procedure. However, some people may experience mild discomfort or a tingling sensation.

Is microneedling for hair loss safe?

When performed by a qualified professional, microneedling for hair loss is generally considered safe. However, there is a risk of infection or other complications if proper aftercare instructions are not followed.

How many sessions of microneedling for hair loss are required?

The number of sessions required varies depending on the individual and the severity of hair loss. Most people require several sessions spaced a few weeks apart to see the best results.

Hair Care After Microneedling

After microneedling for hair loss, it's important to take good care of the scalp to ensure proper healing and maximize results. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using harsh hair products, such as those containing sulfates or alcohol, for at least a week after the treatment.
  • Avoid exposing the scalp to direct sunlight for at least a week after the treatment.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing the hair to avoid pulling on the scalp.
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, such as braids or ponytails, for at least a week after the treatment.

Overall, microneedling for hair loss can be an effective treatment for stimulating hair growth. While it may take several weeks to several months to see noticeable results, being consistent with the treatment and following proper aftercare instructions can help maximize the results.
