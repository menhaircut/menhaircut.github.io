Top 5 Hairstyles For Square Face Male

Written by William Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that complements your features. Square faces have a strong jawline and forehead, and the width of the face is almost equal to the length. However, with the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and look your best. In this article, we will discuss the top 5 hairstyles for square face male.

Table of Content

  1. How to Determine if You Have a Square Face
  2. Top 5 Hairstyles for Square Face Male
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

How to Determine if You Have a Square Face

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's determine if you have a square face. You have a square face if:

  • Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are almost equal in width.
  • You have a strong jawline.
  • Your face is angular and has sharp edges.
  • Your face length is almost equal to the width.

If you have these features, then you have a square face.

Top 5 Hairstyles for Square Face Male

Now that we have determined that you have a square face, here are the top 5 hairstyles that will suit you:

1. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic and low maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for square face males. It emphasizes your jawline and makes your face look more angular. You can try different lengths, but we recommend going for a shorter length to complement your features.

2. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that suits square face males. The textured layers soften the edges of your face, and the hair on top adds volume and height to your face. It's a perfect balance of sharp and soft features.

3. Side Part

The side part is a timeless and classic hairstyle that suits almost all face shapes, including square faces. It adds volume to the top of your head and makes your face look more elongated. It's perfect for formal occasions, but you can also rock it on a casual day.

4. Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a bold and edgy hairstyle that suits square face males. It emphasizes your jawline and adds height to your face. You can style it in different ways, but we recommend keeping the sides short and the top long for a more defined look.

5. Slicked Back

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic and chic hairstyle that suits square face males. It emphasizes your forehead and adds length to your face. You can use a hair gel or pomade to achieve the slicked-back look. It's perfect for formal occasions or a night out with friends.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have chosen the perfect hairstyle, here are some tips to maintain it:

  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain the shape.
  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the hairstyle you have chosen.
  • Wash your hair regularly to maintain its health.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly for a trim or a new hairstyle.

FAQs

1. Can I try different hairstyles if I have a square face?

Yes, you can try different hairstyles that suit your features. However, we recommend going for hairstyles that complement your face shape.

2. Can I grow out my hair if I have a square face?

Yes, you can grow out your hair if you have a square face. However, we recommend keeping the hair on top longer to balance the width of your face.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair regularly, depending on your hair type and the hairstyle you have chosen. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often.

Hair Care Routine for Square Face Male

Here's a simple hair care routine that you can follow to maintain healthy and shiny hair:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo that suits your hair type.
  • Massage your scalp gently to stimulate blood flow.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all the shampoo.
  • Apply a conditioner to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with cold water to seal the cuticles and add shine.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine and prevent frizz.

With these tips and hairstyles, you can enhance your features and look your best. Remember to choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle. Experiment with different styles, and don't be afraid to try something new.
