Long Straight Hair For Heart Shaped Faces A Guide

Written by Frank Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone with a heart-shaped face and long straight hair? If so, you may have noticed that finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, we’ve got you covered! In this guide, we’ll walk you through how to style and care for your long straight hair to perfectly complement your heart-shaped face. From tips to step-by-step instructions, we have everything you need to know. So, let’s get started!

Description

A heart-shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and a narrow, pointed chin. Long straight hair can help balance out these features, but it’s important to find the right cut and style. The goal is to create volume around the jawline and chin area while keeping the hair sleek and straight.

How To

Step 1: Find the Right Cut

The first step in styling long straight hair for a heart-shaped face is to find the right cut. A layered cut can help create volume around the chin area, while blunt cuts can make the forehead look wider. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape.

Step 2: Blow-Dry with a Round Brush

After washing your hair, blow-dry it with a round brush to add volume and bounce. Focus on the roots and the ends, using the brush to pull your hair away from your face.

Step 3: Straighten Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a straightener to make it sleek and straight. Start at the roots and pull the straightener down to the ends. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damage to your hair.

Step 4: Add Volume

To add volume around the chin area, use a volumizing product like mousse or a texturizing spray. Apply it to the roots and use your fingers to work it through your hair.

Tips

Tip 1: Avoid Bangs

Avoid getting bangs if you have a heart-shaped face. Bangs can make your forehead look wider and take away from the balance of your face.

Tip 2: Use Hair Accessories

Use hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add interest and volume to your hairstyle. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Tip 3: Don’t Overdo It

Avoid over-styling your hair. Too much product or heat can damage your hair and make it look unhealthy. Stick to simple, sleek styles that enhance your natural beauty.

FAQ

What is the best length for long straight hair on a heart-shaped face?

The best length for long straight hair on a heart-shaped face is just above the shoulders or longer. This length helps balance out the wider forehead and narrow chin.

What products should I use to style my long straight hair?

Use a volumizing product like mousse or a texturizing spray to add volume to your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before using a straightener to avoid damage to your hair.

Can I still wear my hair up?

Absolutely! Experiment with different updo styles to find what works best for you. Try a sleek ponytail or a messy bun to change up your look.

Hair Care

Tip 1: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Look for a mask that is specifically formulated for your hair type.

Tip 2: Limit Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis. Too much heat can damage your hair and make it look unhealthy. Try air-drying your hair or using heat-free styling techniques.

Tip 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help prevent breakage and minimize damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to style and care for your long straight hair to perfectly complement your heart-shaped face. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape and hair type. Happy styling!
