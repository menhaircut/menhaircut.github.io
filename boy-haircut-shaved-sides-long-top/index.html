Boy Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top A Modern And Edgy Style

Written by Frank Oct 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and go for something modern and edgy, the boy haircut shaved sides long top might just be the style for you. This hairstyle is characterized by a shaved or closely cropped haircut on the sides and back, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways. In this blog post, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting this trendy haircut, including how to style it, tips for maintenance, and more.

How to Get a Boy Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

The first step in getting a boy haircut shaved sides long top is to find a skilled hairstylist who is experienced in this style. Once you've found a stylist you trust, you can work with them to determine the right length and shape for your hair. Generally, the sides and back of the hair are shaved or cut very short, while the hair on top is left longer and can be styled in a variety of ways.

During the haircut, your stylist will use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair to the desired length. They may also use scissors to trim the hair on top, depending on the look you're going for. Once the cut is complete, you can work with your stylist to style your hair in a way that complements your face shape and personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling a Boy Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

Once you've got your boy haircut shaved sides long top, it's time to start styling. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Apply product

To style a boy haircut shaved sides long top, you'll need to start by applying product to your hair. Depending on your hair type and the look you're going for, you can use a variety of products, including pomade, wax, gel, or mousse. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the longer hair on top.

Step 2: Use a comb or brush

Once you've applied product, use a comb or brush to create the desired shape and texture in your hair. You can comb your hair straight back for a classic look, or create a side part for a more modern style. Experiment with different parting and combing techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Step 3: Finish with hairspray

To help your style hold throughout the day, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips for Maintaining a Boy Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

While a boy haircut shaved sides long top can be a stylish and edgy look, it does require some maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your haircut:

Tip 1: Get regular trims

Since the sides and back of your hair are shaved or cut very short in a boy haircut shaved sides long top, it's important to get regular trims to maintain the length and shape of your hair. Depending on the rate of your hair growth, you may need to get a trim every 3-4 weeks.

Tip 2: Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Since the hair on top of your head is longer, it's important to keep it hydrated and healthy. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.

Tip 3: Experiment with different styling products

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and the look you're going for. Pomade, wax, gel, and mousse can all create different textures and hold levels, so try a few out to see which one you like best.

FAQs

Q: How long should the hair on top be in a boy haircut shaved sides long top?

A: The length of the hair on top can vary depending on personal preference, but it's generally between 2-4 inches.

Q: Is a boy haircut shaved sides long top suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a boy haircut shaved sides long top can be adapted to suit all face shapes. Your stylist can work with you to determine the right length and shape for your hair to complement your face shape.

Q: How do I know if a boy haircut shaved sides long top is right for me?

A: If you're looking for a modern and edgy hairstyle that's easy to maintain, a boy haircut shaved sides long top might be a good fit for you. Talk to your hairstylist about whether this style would work well with your hair type and face shape.

Hair Care Tips for a Boy Haircut Shaved Sides Long Top

While a boy haircut shaved sides long top can be a low-maintenance style, it's still important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your haircut:

Tip 1: Wash your hair regularly

To prevent buildup of product and oil, it's important to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo. Focus on the hair on top of your head, and be sure to rinse thoroughly to avoid any residue.

Tip 2: Use a heat protectant

If you use heat styling tools, like a blow dryer or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause damage to your hair, so be sure to protect it by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen spray.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your boy haircut shaved sides long top with confidence and style.
