How To Style Permed Hair In The Morning Male

Written by Frank Sep 26, 2023 · 3 min read

Permed hair is a popular hairstyle for men who want to add some texture and volume to their hair. However, styling permed hair in the morning can be a challenge. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to style permed hair in the morning male.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Style Permed Hair in the Morning Male
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips and Tricks
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Tips for Permed Hair

Description

Permed hair is a hairstyle that involves using chemicals to create curls or waves in the hair. This can be a great option for men who want to add some texture and volume to their hair. However, styling permed hair in the morning can be a challenge. The curls can easily become frizzy or lose their shape overnight, making it difficult to achieve a consistent look.

How to Style Permed Hair in the Morning Male

The key to styling permed hair in the morning is to have a consistent routine. This will help you achieve a consistent look every day. Here are some tips on how to style permed hair in the morning male:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Start by wetting your hair with warm water. This will help to reactivate the curls.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. This could be a curl-defining cream or a light gel.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently comb through your hair. This will help to distribute the product evenly and define the curls.
  4. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to maintain the shape of the curls and prevent frizz.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.
  6. Style your hair as desired using your fingers or a comb.
  7. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the natural oils from your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Use a conditioner specifically designed for permed hair to help maintain the shape of the curls.
  • Avoid using a brush on your hair, as this can cause the curls to become frizzy.
  • Consider using a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does a perm last?
A: A perm can last for several months, depending on the individual’s hair type and how well it’s cared for. Q: Can I still style my permed hair if I’ve slept on it?
A: Yes, you can still style your permed hair if you’ve slept on it. Simply wet your hair with warm water and follow the same styling routine.

Hair Care Tips for Permed Hair

  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage the hair and cause the curls to lose their shape.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos or styling products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out the hair and cause it to become brittle.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, styling permed hair in the morning male can be a challenge, but with the right routine and products, it’s definitely achievable. Follow our tips and tricks and you’ll be able to achieve a consistent and stylish look every day.
