How To Figure Out Your Face Shape In 4 Steps

Written by Eveline Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle or want to try out a new pair of glasses, it's important to know your face shape. Your face shape plays a significant role in determining what hairstyles and glasses will look best on you. In this article, we'll go through a step-by-step guide on how to figure out your face shape in 4 easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Measure Your Forehead
  • Step 2: Measure Your Cheekbones
  • Step 3: Measure Your Jawline
  • Step 4: Determine Your Face Shape

  • Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Step 1: Measure Your Forehead

The first step in determining your face shape is to measure your forehead. Using a measuring tape, measure across your forehead at the widest point. Write down this measurement.

Step 2: Measure Your Cheekbones

Next, measure your cheekbones. Place the measuring tape at the point where your cheekbones are the widest, typically just below the outer corner of your eyes. Measure across your cheeks and write down this measurement.

Step 3: Measure Your Jawline

The third step is to measure your jawline. Place the measuring tape at the tip of your chin and measure to the point below your ear where your jaw angles upwards. Multiply this measurement by two and write down the result.

Step 4: Determine Your Face Shape

Now that you have your measurements, it's time to determine your face shape. Use the table below to match up your measurements with your face shape:

Tips for Determining Your Face Shape

If you're having trouble determining your face shape, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Make sure your hair is pulled back away from your face so you can get an accurate measurement.
  • Use a flexible measuring tape to get precise measurements.
  • If your measurements are close to two different face shapes, take a look at pictures of people with those face shapes and see which one looks more similar to yours.

FAQs

What if my measurements don't match up exactly with one face shape?

It's common for your measurements to be close to two different face shapes. In this case, take a look at pictures of people with those face shapes and see which one looks more similar to yours.

Do face shapes change over time?

No, your face shape stays the same throughout your life. However, weight gain or loss can change the appearance of your face shape.

Can I have a combination of face shapes?

Yes, it's possible to have a combination of face shapes. In this case, use the face shape that is the most dominant in your measurements.

Hair Care Tips for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to determine what hairstyles will look best on you. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle will look good on an oval face shape.
  • Round: Hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as a high ponytail or top knot, will elongate a round face shape.
  • Square: Soft, layered hairstyles that frame the face will soften a square face shape.
  • Heart: Side-swept bangs and hairstyles that add volume around the chin area will balance out a heart-shaped face.
  • Diamond: Short hairstyles that highlight the cheekbones and eyes will complement a diamond face shape.
  • Rectangle: Long hairstyles with layers will soften a rectangle face shape.

By following these tips, you'll be able to determine your face shape and find hairstyles and glasses that are sure to flatter your features.
