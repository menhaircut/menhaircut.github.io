How To Dry And Style Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's voluminous and full of life; on the other hand, it can be unruly and difficult to manage. If you're looking for tips on how to dry and style your curly hair, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to properly dry your hair to the best styling products for curly hair. So, grab your hairbrush and let's get started!

How to Dry Curly Hair

Before we get into styling, it's important to properly dry your curly hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Microfiber Towel

Microfiber towels absorb water quickly and are gentle on curly hair, which can be fragile. Avoid using regular towels, as they can cause frizz and breakage.

2. Don't Rub Your Hair

Instead of rubbing your hair with the towel, gently scrunch it to remove excess water. This will help your curls maintain their shape.

3. Air Dry or Use a Diffuser

If you have time, air drying your hair is the best option. However, if you're in a rush, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair on a low heat setting. This will help maintain your curls' shape.

How to Style Curly Hair

Now that your hair is dry, it's time to style it. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so using a leave-in conditioner will help keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz. Apply it to your hair while it's still damp.

2. Use a Curl Enhancing Cream

Curl enhancing creams help define your curls and provide hold. Apply it to your hair while it's still damp and scrunch your hair to encourage curl formation.

3. Don't Touch Your Hair While It's Drying

Touching your hair while it's drying can cause frizz and disrupt your curls' shape. Once you've applied your styling products, leave your hair alone until it's completely dry.

4. Use a Diffuser Attachment

If you're using a hair dryer, use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair on a low heat setting. This will help maintain your curls' shape and prevent frizz.

Tips for Curly Hair Care

Here are some additional tips for taking care of your curly hair:

1. Use a Satin Pillowcase

Satin pillowcases are gentle on curly hair and help prevent breakage and frizz. Plus, they're luxurious!

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can be damaging to curly hair, so try to avoid it as much as possible. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims will help prevent split ends, which can cause frizz and disrupt your curls' shape.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with curly hair can go a week without washing, while others need to wash every two to three days.

Q: What's the best way to detangle curly hair?

A: Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to gently detangle your hair while it's wet and coated in conditioner.

Q: Can I brush my curly hair?

A: It's best to avoid brushing curly hair, as it can cause frizz and disrupt your curls' shape. If you must brush, use a wide-tooth comb while your hair is wet and coated in conditioner.
