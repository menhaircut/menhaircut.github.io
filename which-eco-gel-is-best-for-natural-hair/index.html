Description

Eco gel has become a popular styling product for natural hair. It is a styling gel made with natural ingredients that helps to define curls, reduce frizz, and hold hair in place. There are various types of eco gels available in the market, but not all of them are suitable for natural hair types. In this post, we will discuss which eco gel is best for natural hair and how to use it for optimal results.

How to Use Eco Gel on Natural Hair

Before applying eco gel, it is important to have clean and moisturized hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and condition it with a moisturizing conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized. You can also add a few drops of oil to seal in the moisture.

Step 3: Apply Eco Gel

Apply a small amount of eco gel to your hair, section by section, starting from the back. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute the gel evenly. Avoid using too much gel, as it can cause flaking and build-up.

Step 4: Style Hair

Use a diffuser or air dry your hair. Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use eco gel on natural hair:

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and condition it with a moisturizing conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and gently towel dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized. You can also add a few drops of oil to seal in the moisture.

Step 3: Divide Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to apply the eco gel. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Apply Eco Gel

Apply a small amount of eco gel to your hair, section by section, starting from the back. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to distribute the gel evenly.

Step 5: Define Curls

Use your fingers or a curl defining brush to define your curls. Shape them as desired.

Step 6: Dry Hair

Use a diffuser or air dry your hair. Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired.

Tips for Using Eco Gel on Natural Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using eco gel on natural hair:

  • Use a small amount of eco gel to avoid flaking and build-up.
  • Apply eco gel to damp hair for better results.
  • Use a diffuser to dry your hair instead of a blow dryer to avoid frizz.
  • Experiment with different eco gels to find the one that works best for your hair type.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can eco gel be used on all hair types?

A: Yes, eco gel can be used on all hair types, but it is particularly beneficial for natural hair types.

Q: Can eco gel cause flaking?

A: Yes, using too much eco gel can cause flaking and build-up. Use a small amount of eco gel to avoid this.

Q: Can eco gel be used on wet hair?

A: Yes, eco gel can be used on wet hair for better results.

Hair Care with Eco Gel

Eco gel is a great styling product for natural hair, but it is important to also take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair at night with a satin or silk scarf or bonnet.

In conclusion, eco gel is a great styling product for natural hair. It helps to define curls, reduce frizz, and hold hair in place. When using eco gel, it is important to have clean and moisturized hair, and to apply it in small amounts to avoid flaking and build-up. Try different types of eco gels to find the one that works best for your hair type. With proper hair care, you can achieve healthy and beautiful natural hair.
