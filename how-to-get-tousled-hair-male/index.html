How To Get Tousled Hair Male

Written by Frank Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that gives you a cool, effortless vibe? Tousled hair might be the perfect look for you. This hairstyle is all about creating a messy, textured look that appears like you just rolled out of bed. Tousled hair is popular amongst men of all ages, and it's easy to achieve with the right products and techniques. In this article, we'll show you how to get tousled hair male and provide tips for maintaining this look.

How to Get Tousled Hair Male: Step by Step

If you're ready to embrace the tousled hair look, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

The first step to achieving tousled hair is to start with clean, dry hair. Shampoo your hair and towel-dry it until it's damp. Avoid using a hairdryer as it can make your hair too straight and smooth. Let your hair air dry, or use a towel to scrunch it gently.

Step 2: Apply a texturizing product

To create texture and hold in your hair, apply a texturizing product like sea salt spray or hair wax. These products will give your hair a gritty, matte finish that's perfect for tousled hair. Use a small amount of product at first and apply it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Style your hair

Now it's time to style your hair. Use your fingers to scrunch and tousle your hair until you achieve the desired look. You can also use a comb or brush to create a more polished tousled look. Don't worry about making your hair look perfect - tousled hair is meant to be a little messy.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

To set your tousled hair in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your hair maintain its shape throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Tousled Hair

Now that you know how to get tousled hair male, here are some tips for maintaining this look:

Tip 1: Don't over-wash your hair

Tousled hair looks best when it's a little dirty, so don't over-wash your hair. If you wash your hair every day, try washing it every other day instead. This will help your hair maintain its natural oils, which will give it more texture.

Tip 2: Use a dry shampoo

If your hair starts to look too oily or greasy, use a dry shampoo to refresh it. Dry shampoo will absorb excess oil and give your hair more texture.

Tip 3: Don't overuse styling products

While styling products are essential for creating tousled hair, don't overuse them. Too much product can make your hair look greasy or weighed down. Use a small amount of product and build up gradually until you achieve the desired look.

Tip 4: Get regular haircuts

To maintain the tousled hair look, get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and trimmed. A good haircut can enhance your natural texture and make it easier to style your hair.

FAQs About Tousled Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about tousled hair:

Q: Can I achieve tousled hair if I have short hair?

A: Yes, tousled hair can work for any length of hair. If you have short hair, use a texturizing product to create a messy, textured look.

Q: How long does it take to style tousled hair?

A: Tousled hair is a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require much time to style. With the right products and techniques, you can achieve this look in just a few minutes.

Q: Is tousled hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, tousled hair can work for any face shape. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be adapted to suit your unique features.

Hair Care for Tousled Hair

To keep your tousled hair looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it harder to achieve tousled hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Tip 2: Use a hair mask once a week

To keep your hair nourished and hydrated, use a hair mask once a week. This will help prevent breakage and keep your hair looking healthy.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from heat

If you use heat styling tools like a flat iron or curling iron, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair. Too much heat can make your hair look frizzy and dry.

Now that you know how to get tousled hair male, it's time to try this cool, low-maintenance hairstyle for yourself. Follow these tips, and you'll have tousled hair that looks effortlessly stylish and cool.
