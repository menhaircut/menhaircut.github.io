How Do I Know If My Hair Colour Suits Me

Written by Aprilia Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing a hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you want to try something new. The last thing you want is to end up with a shade that doesn't suit you. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll guide you on how to know if a hair colour suits you, from choosing the right shade to maintaining it properly.

How to Choose the Right Shade

Before you start picking out colours, it's essential to consider your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. The perfect shade for you is one that will complement your features and make you feel confident.

Skin Tone and Hair Colour

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining which hair colour will work best for you. If you have a warm skin tone, then warm hair colours like golden blondes or rich browns will suit you. If you have a cool skin tone, then cool hair colours like ashy blondes or deep reds will complement you.

Undertones in the skin can also help determine the best hair colour. If you have a warm undertone, then shades with red, gold or yellow undertones will look best. For cool undertones, go for colours with blue or violet undertones.

Eye Colour and Hair Colour

Your eye colour can also influence which hair colour will suit you best. If you have blue or green eyes, then blonde shades like honey or caramel will make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, then warmer colours like mahogany or chestnut will complement you.

Face Shape and Hair Colour

Lastly, your face shape can also affect which hair colour will look best on you. If you have a round face, then darker shades like chocolate brown can help elongate your face. If you have an angular face, then brighter shades like platinum blonde can soften your features.

Tips in Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the right hair colour:

  • Consider the maintenance that comes with the hair colour you choose. Some shades require more upkeep than others.
  • Ask your stylist for their opinion on what shade would suit you best.
  • Try on wigs or use virtual hair colour apps to see how the shade looks on you before committing to it.
  • Take into account your lifestyle and how the hair colour will fit in with your daily routine.

FAQ on Hair Colour Suits Me

How long does it take to find the perfect hair colour?

It can take some time to find the perfect hair colour, but don't rush the process. Take the time to consider all of the factors we've mentioned to ensure that you end up with a shade that suits you and your lifestyle.

What if I want to try a bold hair colour?

If you want to try a bold hair colour, go for it! Just remember that it may require more maintenance and upkeep than a more natural shade. Make sure to also consider your skin tone and eye colour to ensure that the shade complements you.

Can I change my hair colour if I don't like it?

Yes, you can always change your hair colour if you don't like it. However, keep in mind that constant colouring can damage your hair. It's best to wait at least six weeks between colouring to allow your hair to recover.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you've found the perfect hair colour, it's important to take care of it to ensure that it lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair colour:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair colour.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often as they can damage your hair and fade your colour.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends, which can make your hair look dull and faded.

Remember, finding the perfect hair colour takes time and effort, but it's worth it when you end up with a shade that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Follow these tips and care for your hair properly to maintain your new hair colour and keep it looking vibrant and healthy.
