Best Short Beard Styles For Oval Face

Written by Oliver Oct 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect beard style for your oval face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the best short beard styles for oval face shapes. From the classic stubble to the trendy goatee, we've got you covered. So, let's dive in and find the perfect beard style for you!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oval face shape is one of the most versatile when it comes to beard styles. With a balanced and proportional face, you can rock almost any beard style you want. However, if you want to accentuate your features and make your beard stand out, then you need to choose a beard style that complements your face shape.

In this article, we will be discussing the best short beard styles for oval face shapes. These styles are easy to maintain, look great, and are perfect for any occasion. Whether you're going for a casual look or a more formal one, these beard styles will have you looking your best.

How to

Before we dive into the specific beard styles, let's talk about how to choose the right beard style for your face shape. The first thing you need to do is determine your face shape. To do this, measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If all three measurements are roughly equal, then you have an oval face shape.

Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for beard styles that complement your features. For oval face shapes, short beard styles work best. These styles help to accentuate your jawline and create a more defined look.

Step by Step

Now that you know how to choose the right beard style for your oval face shape, let's take a look at some of the best short beard styles out there.

The Classic Stubble

The classic stubble is a great option for oval face shapes. This style is easy to maintain and looks great with almost any outfit. To achieve the classic stubble, simply let your beard grow out for a few days until you have a short, even layer of hair. Then, trim it down to your desired length using a beard trimmer.

The Goatee

The goatee is a timeless beard style that looks great on oval face shapes. To achieve the goatee, shave your cheeks and neck, leaving only a small patch of hair on your chin. You can either keep the hair short or let it grow out a bit for a more rugged look.

The Chin Strap

The chin strap is a popular short beard style that works well on oval face shapes. To achieve the chin strap, shave your cheeks and neck, leaving a thin strip of hair along your jawline. You can either keep the hair short or let it grow out a bit for a more dramatic look.

Tips

Now that you know some of the best short beard styles for oval face shapes, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a good-quality beard trimmer to keep your beard looking neat and tidy.
  • Invest in a beard oil or balm to keep your beard soft and moisturized.
  • Regularly wash your beard to prevent any buildup of dirt or bacteria.
  • Experiment with different beard styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short beard styles for oval face shapes:

What is the best short beard style for oval face shapes?

The best short beard style for oval face shapes depends on your personal preference. Some popular options include the classic stubble, the goatee, and the chin strap.

How do I maintain a short beard?

To maintain a short beard, use a good-quality beard trimmer to keep it neat and tidy. You should also regularly wash your beard and invest in a beard oil or balm to keep it soft and moisturized.

Can I still look professional with a short beard?

Yes! Short beard styles can look professional as long as they are well-groomed and maintained. Just make sure to keep your beard neat and tidy, and you'll look great no matter what.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to remember that your beard is just one part of your overall appearance. To look your best, you need to take care of your hair as well. Make sure to use a good-quality shampoo and conditioner, and visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect short beard style for your oval face shape. Whether you choose the classic stubble or the trendy goatee, you'll look great and feel confident wherever you go.
