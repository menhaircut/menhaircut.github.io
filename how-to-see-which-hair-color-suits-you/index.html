How To See Which Hair Color Suits You

Written by Bardi Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure which one will suit you best. In this article, we'll provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to determine which hair color will flatter your skin tone and enhance your overall appearance.

Table of Contents

  1. Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone
  2. Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color
  3. Step 3: Choose a Hair Color
  4. Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  5. FAQs About Choosing a Hair Color
  6. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining which hair color suits you is to determine your skin tone. There are three main skin tones: warm, cool, and neutral. Here's how to figure out which one you have:

  • If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow, peachy, or golden undertone.
  • If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or blue undertone.
  • If you have neutral undertones, your skin will have a mix of warm and cool undertones.

To determine your skin tone, you can try looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If your veins appear blue, you likely have cool undertones. If you can't tell whether your veins are blue or green, you likely have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Next, consider your eye color. Your eye color can also play a role in determining which hair color will suit you best. Here are some general guidelines:

  • If you have blue or green eyes, warm hair colors like honey blonde, golden brown, or copper will likely suit you best.
  • If you have brown eyes, you can typically pull off a wider range of hair colors, including warm and cool shades.
  • If you have hazel eyes, warm hair colors will complement the green tones in your eyes, while cool hair colors will complement the brown tones.

Step 3: Choose a Hair Color

Now that you've determined your skin tone and eye color, it's time to choose a hair color. Here are some guidelines:

  • If you have warm undertones, warm hair colors like golden blonde, caramel brown, or auburn will likely suit you best.
  • If you have cool undertones, cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or cool brown shades will likely suit you best.
  • If you have neutral undertones, you can typically pull off a wider range of hair colors, including warm and cool shades.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're low-maintenance and don't want to spend a lot of time and money on touch-ups, choose a hair color that's close to your natural shade.
  • Consider the season. Warmer hair colors like copper and auburn may be more flattering in the fall and winter, while cooler shades like platinum blonde may be more flattering in the spring and summer.
  • Consider your personality. If you're bold and adventurous, consider a bright or unconventional hair color like pink or blue. If you're more conservative, stick with natural-looking shades.

FAQs About Choosing a Hair Color

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hair color:

  • What if I have multiple skin tones on my face?

    • You can use a color wheel to determine which hair color will complement the dominant tone on your face. For example, if you have warm undertones on your forehead and cool undertones on your cheeks, choose a hair color that falls somewhere in the middle of the warm-cool spectrum.

  • What if I don't want to commit to a permanent hair color?

    • You can try a semi-permanent or demi-permanent hair color that will gradually fade over time.

  • What if I want to go from dark hair to light hair?

    • It's best to go to a professional stylist for this type of color change to avoid damaging your hair. It may take several appointments to achieve the desired shade.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've chosen a hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to help preserve your hair color.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, which can cause your hair color to fade more quickly.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective hair product.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and guidelines, you can choose a hair color that will flatter your skin tone and enhance your natural beauty. Happy coloring!
