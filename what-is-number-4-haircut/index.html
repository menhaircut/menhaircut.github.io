What Is Number 4 Haircut Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re considering a new haircut, chances are you’ve come across the term “number 4 haircut” or heard your barber mention it. But, what exactly is a number 4 haircut? In this article, we’ll break down everything you need to know about this popular haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents:

1. Description

2. How to Get a Number 4 Haircut

3. Step-by-Step Guide

4. Tips for Styling Number 4 Haircut

5. FAQ

6. Hair Care for Number 4 Haircut

1. Description

A number 4 haircut is a popular clipper size that leaves hair 1/2 inch long. It’s a versatile haircut that works well for both men and women, and it’s great for those who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that’s easy to style. This haircut is also known as a “medium length haircut” and falls between a number 3 and a number 5 clipper size.

2. How to Get a Number 4 Haircut

Getting a number 4 haircut is easy, and it can be done at any barber shop or hair salon. Simply ask your barber or stylist for a number 4 haircut, and they’ll know exactly what to do. It’s important to communicate clearly with your barber or stylist about the length you want, as clipper sizes can vary slightly depending on the brand of clippers they use.

3. Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a number 4 haircut:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Sit in the barber’s chair and explain that you want a number 4 haircut.
  3. Your barber will start by using a number 4 clipper guard to trim the sides and back of your hair.
  4. Once the sides and back are trimmed, your barber will use scissors to trim the top of your hair to the desired length.
  5. Your barber will then blend the sides and top of your hair to create a seamless transition.
  6. Finally, your barber will style your hair using a comb or brush and hair product if desired.

4. Tips for Styling Number 4 Haircut

Here are some styling tips for your number 4 haircut:

  • Use a small amount of hair product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • If you have thin hair, use a volumizing product to add body and fullness.
  • For a messy, tousled look, use your fingers to style your hair instead of a comb or brush.
  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

5. FAQ

What is the difference between a number 3 and a number 4 haircut?

A number 3 haircut leaves hair 3/8 inch long, while a number 4 haircut leaves hair 1/2 inch long. The difference between the two is subtle but noticeable, and it’s a matter of personal preference which one you choose.

Can women get a number 4 haircut?

Yes, women can get a number 4 haircut. It’s a versatile haircut that works well for both men and women, and it’s a great option if you want a low-maintenance hairstyle that’s easy to style.

Is a number 4 haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a number 4 haircut is suitable for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair. However, the length of the haircut may look slightly different depending on your hair type and texture.

6. Hair Care for Number 4 Haircut

To keep your number 4 haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hairdryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails, as they can pull on your hair and cause breakage.
  • Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant.

In conclusion, a number 4 haircut is a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that works well for both men and women. It’s easy to style and maintain, and it’s a great option if you want a medium length haircut that’s not too short and not too long. With the right styling and hair care, your number 4 haircut can look great for weeks to come.
