Best Short Haircut For Square Face Female

Written by Luffy Sep 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right haircut can make all the difference when it comes to highlighting your best features. If you have a square face shape, you’ll want to choose a hairstyle that will soften your angles and highlight your eyes and cheekbones. In this article, we’ll explore the best short haircuts for square face females.

Description

A square face shape is defined by a strong jawline and a forehead that is almost the same width as the jawline. This face shape can often appear masculine, so it’s important to choose a haircut that will soften the angles and create a more feminine look. Short haircuts are a great option for square face females because they can help to highlight your best features while minimizing the squareness of your face.

How to

When choosing a short haircut for square face females, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you’ll want to choose a haircut that is layered and textured, as this will help to soften your angles. You’ll also want to choose a style that frames your face and highlights your eyes and cheekbones.

Another important factor to consider is the length of your hair. A short bob or pixie cut can be a great option for square face females, as they help to create a rounder and softer shape. However, you’ll want to avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as these can accentuate the squareness of your face.

Step by Step

1. Start by determining your face shape. If you have a square face shape, you’ll want to choose a haircut that will soften your angles and highlight your eyes and cheekbones. 2. Consider the length of your hair. Short bobs and pixie cuts are great options for square face females, as they help to create a rounder and softer shape. 3. Choose a haircut that is layered and textured, as this will help to soften your angles and create a more feminine look. 4. Look for hairstyles that frame your face and highlight your best features. 5. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as these can accentuate the squareness of your face.

Tips

1. Consider adding bangs to your haircut, as they can help to soften your forehead and highlight your eyes. 2. Experiment with different hair textures, such as waves or curls, as these can help to add volume and create a softer look. 3. Don’t be afraid to ask your hairstylist for recommendations on the best short haircut for your face shape. 4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair, as this can help to add dimension and create a more flattering look.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for square face females?

The best short haircut for square face females is one that is layered and textured, with a length that is not too short or too long. Short bobs and pixie cuts are great options, as they help to create a rounder and softer shape.

What should I avoid when choosing a short haircut for a square face?

You should avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as these can accentuate the squareness of your face. You should also avoid haircuts that are too angular or blunt, as these can create a harsh look.

Can I still wear my hair in a bob if I have a square face shape?

Yes, you can still wear your hair in a bob if you have a square face shape. Just make sure that the length is not too short, and that the haircut is layered and textured.

Hair Care

Once you’ve chosen the best short haircut for your square face shape, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type. 2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. 3. Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends. 4. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and keep it looking shiny.

With the right short haircut and a few simple hair care tips, you can create a look that highlights your best features and flatters your square face shape.
