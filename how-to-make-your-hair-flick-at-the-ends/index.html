How to Make Your Hair Flick at the Ends

Flicking the ends of your hair involves creating a subtle curve or flip at the bottom. It adds volume and movement to your hair and can make you look more put together. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Clean hair is essential when it comes to styling, so make sure to wash and condition your hair before attempting to flick the ends. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Make sure to towel dry your hair gently. Do not rub your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage. This step is crucial as flicking the ends involves using heat styling tools.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth finish. Make sure to concentrate on the ends and brush them upwards to create a subtle curve.

Step 5: Use a Straightener

Take a straightener and clamp it on the ends of your hair. Twist the straightener towards the outside, away from your face, and pull it downwards to create a flick at the ends. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you make your hair flick at the ends:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply heat protectant.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush.
  5. Take a straightener and clamp it on the ends of your hair.
  6. Twist the straightener towards the outside.
  7. Pull the straightener downwards to create a flick at the ends.
  8. Repeat on all sections of your hair.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when flicking the ends of your hair:

  • Make sure to use a straightener with ceramic plates as they distribute heat evenly.
  • Do not apply too much pressure on the straightener as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant with an SPF if you're going to be out in the sun.
  • Use a hairspray to hold the flicks in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to flick the ends of your hair?

Flicking the ends of your hair can take anywhere from 10-20 minutes depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

Can you flick the ends of curly hair?

Yes, you can flick the ends of curly hair. However, you'll need to straighten your hair first before flicking the ends.

How often should you flick the ends of your hair?

You can flick the ends of your hair as often as you like. However, make sure to take breaks in between to avoid heat damage.

Hair Care Tips

Flicking the ends of your hair involves using heat styling tools, which can cause damage if not done properly. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal parts as they can cause breakage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.

There you have it! A step-by-step guide on how to make your hair flick at the ends. It's a simple yet effective way to add some style to your hair. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid heat damage by following our hair care tips. Happy styling!
