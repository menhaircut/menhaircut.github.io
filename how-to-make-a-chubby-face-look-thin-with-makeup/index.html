How To Make A Chubby Face Look Thin With Makeup

Written by Bardi Oct 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your chubby face? Do you want to look slimmer and more defined? Well, you're in luck! With a few makeup tricks, you can make your face look thinner and more contoured. In this blog post, we will teach you how to make a chubby face look thin with makeup.

Description

A chubby face can be a result of genetics or weight gain. While some people love their plump cheeks, others want a more defined and sculpted look. Makeup can help create an illusion of a slimmer face without going under the knife. With the right techniques, you can create shadows and highlights on your face, making it look more contoured and angular.

How to

To make your face look thinner with makeup, you need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep your Face

Start by washing your face and applying a moisturizer. Let it absorb for a few minutes before applying any makeup.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Apply a foundation that matches your skin tone. Make sure to blend it well, especially around your jawline and hairline to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 3: Contour

Contouring is the key to creating a slimmer face. Use a contour powder or cream that is two shades darker than your skin tone. Apply it on the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline. Blend it well with a brush or a sponge to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Highlight

Highlighting is the opposite of contouring. It creates the illusion of light on your face, making it look more defined. Use a highlighter that is one or two shades lighter than your skin tone. Apply it on the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and nose bridge. Blend it well with a brush or a sponge to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Blush

Add some color to your cheeks with a blush. Apply it on the apples of your cheeks and blend it well with a brush or a sponge.

Step 6: Set

Set your makeup with a translucent powder. This will help your makeup last longer and prevent any shine on your face.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make your face look thinner with makeup:

  • Use a matte foundation to avoid any shine on your face.
  • Blend your makeup well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use a small brush for contouring to have more control.
  • Avoid using shimmery highlighters as they can emphasize your chubby cheeks.

FAQ

Can makeup really make my face look thinner?

Yes, makeup can create an illusion of a slimmer face. By using contouring and highlighting techniques, you can create shadows and highlights on your face, making it look more contoured and angular.

Do I need to use expensive makeup products?

No, you don't need to use expensive makeup products. There are many affordable options available in the market. The key is to find the right shade that matches your skin tone and to blend your makeup well.

Can I still wear glasses with this makeup look?

Yes, you can still wear glasses with this makeup look. Just make sure to blend your makeup well around your nose bridge to avoid any harsh lines.

Hair Care

Your hair can also play a role in making your face look thinner. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Get a haircut that frames your face well.
  • Avoid heavy bangs as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

By following these makeup and hair care tips, you can make your face look thinner and more defined. Remember, confidence is the key to feeling beautiful, no matter your face shape or size.
