How To Shave Men s Hair Short Back And Sides

Written by Petter Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short back and sides is a classic men's haircut that never goes out of style. This haircut is perfect for men who want a clean and neat look without sacrificing style. If you want to try this haircut, you can either go to a barber or do it yourself at home. In this article, we will guide you on how to shave men's hair short back and sides in relaxed English language.

Description

The short back and sides haircut is a style where the hair on the back and sides of the head is cut short while the hair on top is left longer. This haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways depending on your preference. It is also easy to maintain, making it a popular choice among men.

How to

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, comb, and mirror. Here are the steps on how to shave men's hair short back and sides:

Step 1: Choose the right guard length

The first step is to choose the right guard length for your clippers. The guard length will determine how short your hair will be. For a classic short back and sides, use a number 3 or 4 guard length. If you want it shorter, go for a number 2 or 1 guard length.

Step 2: Start with the sides

Start by trimming the hair on the sides of your head. Use the clippers and go against the direction of hair growth. Start at the bottom of your hairline and work your way up. Use the comb to guide the clippers and make sure you get an even cut. Repeat on the other side.

Step 3: Trim the back

Next, trim the hair on the back of your head. Use the same technique as you did on the sides. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Make sure the hair is even and symmetrical.

Step 4: Blend the sides and back

Now it's time to blend the sides and back. Use the clippers to shave the hair on the back of your head towards the sides. This will create a seamless transition between the two areas. Use the comb to guide the clippers and make sure you get an even cut.

Step 5: Trim the top

Finally, it's time to trim the hair on top of your head. Use the scissors and comb to cut the hair to your desired length. You can either keep it long or go for a shorter cut.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when shaving men's hair short back and sides:

  • Use sharp clippers and scissors to avoid uneven cuts.
  • Start with a longer guard length and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure the hair is even.
  • Take your time and go slow to avoid mistakes.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaving men's hair short back and sides:

Can I do it myself?

Yes, you can do it yourself as long as you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully. If you're not confident, it's better to go to a barber.

How often should I trim it?

It depends on how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should trim it every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go shorter or wait for the hair to grow back. If it's a major mistake, it's better to go to a barber to fix it.

Hair Care

To maintain your short back and sides haircut, you need to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized. You can also use hair wax or pomade to style it. Avoid using too much product as it can make your hair look greasy.

Now that you know how to shave men's hair short back and sides, you can try it at home or go to a barber. This classic haircut is perfect for any occasion and is easy to maintain. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow the steps carefully.
