Can Hrt Patches Cause Hair Loss

Written by Daniel Jun 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Hormone replacement therapy (HRT) is a common treatment for women who are experiencing symptoms of menopause. It can help alleviate hot flashes, night sweats, mood swings, and other symptoms. One form of HRT is patches, which are applied to the skin and release hormones into the bloodstream. However, many women are concerned about the potential side effects of HRT patches, including hair loss. In this article, we'll explore whether HRT patches can cause hair loss, and what you can do to prevent it.

What is HRT?

HRT is a treatment that replaces the hormones that decrease during menopause. It can be administered in a variety of ways, including pills, patches, creams, and injections. The hormones used in HRT are estrogen and progesterone, which are naturally produced in a woman's body. HRT can help alleviate the symptoms of menopause, such as hot flashes, night sweats, and vaginal dryness.

How Do HRT Patches Work?

HRT patches are designed to release hormones into the bloodstream through the skin. The patches are usually worn on the abdomen, buttocks, or thigh. The hormones in the patch are absorbed through the skin and into the bloodstream, where they can help alleviate the symptoms of menopause. HRT patches are typically changed once or twice a week, depending on the brand and dosage.

Can HRT Patches Cause Hair Loss?

Unfortunately, HRT patches can cause hair loss in some women. This is because the hormones in the patches can disrupt the natural hair growth cycle. Estrogen is known to promote hair growth, while progesterone can cause hair loss. When the hormones in the body are imbalanced, it can lead to hair loss.

Factors That Affect Hair Loss

There are several factors that can affect whether HRT patches cause hair loss, including:

  • The dosage of hormones in the patch
  • The length of time the patch is worn
  • The woman's age and overall health
  • Genetics

How to Prevent Hair Loss While Using HRT Patches

If you're concerned about hair loss while using HRT patches, there are several things you can do to prevent it:

  • Take a lower dosage of hormones
  • Change the patch more frequently
  • Use a different form of HRT, such as pills or creams
  • Supplement with vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth, such as biotin, vitamin D, and iron

Hair Care Tips While Using HRT Patches

There are also several hair care tips you can follow to prevent hair loss while using HRT patches:

  • Avoid harsh chemical treatments, such as coloring or perming
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on the hair, such as braids or ponytails
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons

FAQ About HRT Patches and Hair Loss

Can HRT pills cause hair loss?

Yes, HRT pills can also cause hair loss in some women. The same factors that affect hair loss with HRT patches also apply to HRT pills.

Is hair loss from HRT patches permanent?

No, hair loss from HRT patches is usually temporary. Once the hormones in the body are balanced, hair growth should return to normal.

Can HRT patches cause other side effects?

Yes, HRT patches can cause other side effects, such as headaches, breast tenderness, and vaginal bleeding. Talk to your doctor if you experience any side effects while using HRT patches.

Conclusion

While HRT patches can help alleviate the symptoms of menopause, they can also cause hair loss in some women. By taking a lower dosage of hormones, changing the patch more frequently, and following hair care tips, you can prevent hair loss while using HRT patches. As always, talk to your doctor if you have any concerns about HRT or hair loss.
