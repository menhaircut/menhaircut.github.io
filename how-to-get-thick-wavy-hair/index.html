How To Get Thick Wavy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've always wanted thick, wavy hair but have struggled to achieve it, you're not alone. Many people have thin or straight hair that just won't hold a wave, no matter what they do. Fortunately, there are several things you can do to increase your chances of getting the wavy locks you've always wanted. Here are some tips and tricks to help you get started:

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Thick Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair

How to Get Thick Wavy Hair

The first step to getting thick wavy hair is to make sure your hair is healthy. This means eating a balanced diet, drinking plenty of water, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools. Once your hair is healthy, you can start working on creating waves.

One way to create waves is to use a curling iron. Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that matches the size of the waves you want. If you want loose waves, choose a larger barrel. If you want tighter waves, choose a smaller barrel. To use the curling iron, section your hair and wrap each section around the barrel, holding it for a few seconds before releasing.

Another way to create waves is to use a hair straightener. This technique is called "the twist method." To use this method, take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger. Then, clamp the straightener down on the twisted hair and slide it down to the end. Release the hair and you should have a loose wave.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to get thick wavy hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to minimize damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Section your hair and use a curling iron or hair straightener to create waves.
  4. Once you've curled all of your hair, run your fingers through it to loosen the waves and create a more natural look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Maintaining Thick Wavy Hair

Once you've achieved thick wavy hair, you'll want to maintain it. Here are some tips to help you do that:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip the natural oils from your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs

Q: Can I get thick wavy hair if I have naturally thin hair?

A: Yes, you can. Follow the tips in this article to create the appearance of thicker, wavier hair.

Q: Can I get thick wavy hair without using heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can. Try using a salt spray or texturizing spray to create waves without heat styling.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have thick wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with thick wavy hair can go several days without washing, while others prefer to wash their hair more frequently.

Hair Care Products for Thick Wavy Hair

If you want to maintain thick wavy hair, it's important to use the right hair care products. Here are some products that are great for thick wavy hair:

  • A sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • A deep conditioning treatment
  • A texturizing spray or salt spray
  • A heat protectant spray
  • A hairspray for hold

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain thick wavy hair. Remember to take care of your hair, avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, and use the right hair care products to keep your locks looking their best.
