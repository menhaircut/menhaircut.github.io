Mens Short Haircut Style Names And Descriptions

Written by Christine Nov 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a man looking for a short haircut, you're in the right place! In this article, we'll go through the most popular mens short haircut style names and descriptions. From the classic buzz cut to the trendy textured crop, there's a short haircut for every man. Let's get started!

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic short haircut that is achieved by using clippers to cut the hair to the same length all over the head. It's a low-maintenance haircut that is perfect for men who want a clean and simple look. The buzz cut can be worn with a beard or without, and it's suitable for all face shapes.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is a timeless short haircut that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top. The hair is usually styled to stand up slightly, giving it a textured look. The crew cut is a versatile haircut that can be worn with or without a beard, and it's suitable for men with oval, square, or round face shapes.

Ivy League

The Ivy League haircut is a classic short haircut that features longer hair on top, with shorter hair on the sides and back of the head. The hair on top is usually styled to the side, giving it a preppy look. The Ivy League is a good haircut for men with square or oval face shapes, and it can be worn with or without a beard.

Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern short haircut that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top. The hair on top is usually styled forward or to the side, giving it a messy, textured look. The textured crop is a good haircut for men with round or oval face shapes, and it can be worn with or without a beard.

Fade Haircut

The fade haircut is a popular short haircut that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, which gradually fades into longer hair on top. The hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, such as a slicked-back look or a textured crop. The fade haircut is suitable for men with all face shapes, and it can be worn with or without a beard.

Pompadour

The pompadour is a classic short haircut that features longer hair on top, with shorter hair on the sides and back of the head. The hair on top is usually styled back and up, giving it a voluminous look. The pompadour is a good haircut for men with oval or diamond face shapes, and it can be worn with or without a beard.

Quiff

The quiff is a modern short haircut that features shorter hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top. The hair on top is usually styled forward and up, giving it a messy, textured look. The quiff is a good haircut for men with oval or square face shapes, and it can be worn with or without a beard.

Slicked Back

The slicked-back haircut is a classic short haircut that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that is styled back and away from the face. The slicked-back look is achieved by using a pomade or gel to hold the hair in place. The slicked-back haircut is suitable for men with oval or diamond face shapes, and it can be worn with or without a beard.

Side Part

The side part is a classic short haircut that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that is styled to one side. The side part is achieved by using a comb to create a part in the hair, and it can be worn with or without a beard. The side part is a good haircut for men with oval or diamond face shapes.

Spiky Hair

Spiky hair is a modern short haircut that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that is styled into spikes. The spikes are achieved by using a pomade or gel to hold the hair in place. Spiky hair is a good haircut for men with oval or square face shapes, and it can be worn with or without a beard.

FAQs

What is the best short haircut for men?

The best short haircut for men depends on their face shape and personal style. Some popular short haircuts include the buzz cut, crew cut, textured crop, and fade haircut.

Can short haircuts be styled?

Yes, short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, spiky, or textured. It's important to use the right products and tools to achieve the desired look.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut. However, this can vary depending on the individual's hair growth and personal preferences.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your short haircut looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a styling product that suits your hair type and the desired look.
  • Visit a professional stylist regularly to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you know the most popular mens short haircut style names and descriptions, you can choose the one that suits you best. Remember to follow a good hair care routine to keep your haircut looking its best. Happy styling!
