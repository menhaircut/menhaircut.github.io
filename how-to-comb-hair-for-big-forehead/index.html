How To Comb Hair For Big Forehead Tips Tricks And Techniques

Written by Aprilia Aug 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have a big forehead and often struggle with how to style your hair to make it look flattering? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with a prominent forehead find it challenging to pick the right hairstyle that complements their facial features. However, with the right techniques and tricks, you can make your big forehead look less noticeable and enhance your overall appearance.

Description

In this blog post, we'll guide you on how to comb your hair for a big forehead, offering tips and techniques to make your hair look stylish and flattering. We'll cover everything from choosing the right haircut to styling techniques that work best for you. Whether you have long hair, short hair, or medium-length hair, we have something for everyone.

How to Comb Hair for Big Forehead: Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in styling hair for a big forehead is choosing the right haircut. The right haircut can make a significant difference in how your hair looks on your face. If you have a large forehead, you should consider hairstyles that add volume to the sides and back of your head while keeping the hair length balanced.

For instance, a layered haircut with side bangs can help frame your face and cover your forehead. On the other hand, a pixie cut with a long fringe can add volume to the top of your head and create an illusion of a smaller forehead.

Step 2: Use the Right Hair Products

The right hair products can help you achieve the desired hairstyle and make it last longer. For instance, if you want to add volume to your hair, you can use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow-drying your hair. On the other hand, if you want to tame frizz and flyaways, you can use a smoothing serum or cream.

Step 3: Part Your Hair Correctly

The way you part your hair can also make a significant difference in how your hair looks on your face. If you have a big forehead, you should avoid a center part and opt for a side part instead. A side part can help create an illusion of a smaller forehead and add volume to the hair on the other side.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen the right haircut, used the right hair products, and parted your hair correctly, it's time to style your hair. You can use various styling techniques to add volume, texture, and definition to your hair.

For instance, you can blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a bouncy effect. You can also use a flat iron or curling wand to create waves or curls, depending on your preference. Similarly, you can use hair accessories like headbands, clips, and bobby pins to add a touch of elegance and style to your hair.

Tips for Combing Hair for a Big Forehead

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when combing your hair for a big forehead:

  • Always use a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush to comb your hair to avoid breakage and damage.
  • Never comb your hair when it's wet as it can cause more breakage and damage.
  • Use a good-quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz and dryness.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage and breakage. Use heat protectant sprays before using hot tools like flat irons and curling wands.
  • Don't overdo it with hair products as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy and flat.

FAQs

What kind of haircut is best for a big forehead?

A layered haircut with side bangs or a pixie cut with a long fringe is best for a big forehead as it can create an illusion of a smaller forehead and add volume to the sides and back of your head.

What kind of hair products should I use for a big forehead?

You should use hair products that help you achieve the desired hairstyle and make it last longer. For instance, you can use a volumizing mousse or spray if you want to add volume to your hair. Similarly, you can use a smoothing serum or cream if you want to tame frizz and flyaways.

How should I part my hair for a big forehead?

You should avoid a center part and opt for a side part instead if you have a big forehead. A side part can create an illusion of a smaller forehead and add volume to the hair on the other side.

Hair Care for Big Forehead

Lastly, taking care of your hair is crucial to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Use a good-quality conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling wands too often as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to provide essential nutrients to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

In conclusion, combing hair for a big forehead can be tricky, but with the right techniques and tricks, you can make your hair look stylish and flattering. Choose the right haircut, use the right hair products, part your hair correctly, and style it using various techniques to add volume, texture, and definition. Keep in mind the tips and FAQs mentioned above and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
