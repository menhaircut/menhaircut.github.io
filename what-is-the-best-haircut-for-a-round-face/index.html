What Is The Best Haircut For A Round Face

Written by William Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut that will flatter your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss what is the best haircut for a round face and provide you with tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

A round face shape is characterized by a wider forehead, fuller cheeks, and a rounded chin. The best haircut for a round face is one that adds length and creates angles, which will help to slim and elongate your face. A good haircut will also highlight your best features and draw attention away from any areas that you may not be as confident about.

How To

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it is important to consider the length and shape of the cut. Here are some tips to help you achieve the best haircut for your round face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for adding length and creating angles in a round face. Ask your stylist to cut long layers around your face and to keep the length below your chin to elongate your face.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a great way to draw attention away from a fuller forehead and create angles in a round face. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle and to sweep them to one side.

3. Bob With Angled Layers

A bob with angled layers is perfect for creating angles in a round face. Ask your stylist to cut your bob with longer layers in the front and shorter layers in the back to add length and create angles.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for a round face, especially if you have a petite frame. Ask your stylist to keep the hair longer on top and to add layers around your face to create angles.

Step By Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the best haircut for your round face:

1. Research

Research different haircuts that flatter a round face shape and find inspiration pictures to show your stylist.

2. Consultation

Schedule a consultation with your stylist to discuss your desired haircut and to get their professional opinion on what will work best for your face shape.

3. Cut

Once you have decided on a haircut, sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic.

4. Styling

After your haircut, ask your stylist for styling tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look at home.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best haircut for your round face:

1. Avoid Blunt Cuts

Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbone, as they can make your face appear wider.

2. Add Volume

Add volume to the top of your hair to create the illusion of length and slimness.

3. Embrace Texture

Embrace texture in your haircut to add interest and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

What Is The Most Flattering Hairstyle For A Round Face?

The most flattering hairstyle for a round face is one that adds length and creates angles, such as long layers, side swept bangs, and a bob with angled layers.

What Haircuts Should Round Faces Avoid?

Round faces should avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbone, as well as hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face.

Can I Still Have Short Hair With A Round Face?

Yes, you can still have short hair with a round face. A pixie cut with longer layers on top and shorter layers around the face can be a great option for a round face.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

1. Use A Good Shampoo And Conditioner

Use a good shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Use A Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know what is the best haircut for a round face, it's time to book your next appointment with your stylist and try out a new look!
