How To Shorten A Dress Without Hemming A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Shortening a dress can be a tricky task, especially if you don't want to hem it. However, with a bit of creativity and some simple techniques, you can shorten your dress without hemming it. This article will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to shorten a dress without hemming, as well as some tips and FAQs to help you along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Shorten a Dress Without Hemming: Step by Step
  • Tips for Shortening a Dress Without Hemming

How to Shorten a Dress Without Hemming: Step by Step

Before you begin, make sure you have the following items:

  • Scissors
  • Measuring tape
  • Sewing pins
  • Fabric glue

Now, let's get started:

Step 1: Determine the Desired Length

Try on the dress and determine how much shorter you want it to be. Measure the distance from the bottom of the dress to the desired length.

Step 2: Mark the Dress

Using sewing pins, mark the dress at the desired length. Be sure to mark all around the dress to ensure an even cut.

Step 3: Cut the Dress

Using scissors, carefully cut the dress at the marked line. Make sure the cut is straight and even.

Step 4: Apply Fabric Glue

Apply fabric glue to the bottom of the dress. Fold over the fabric and press firmly to secure the hem. Allow the glue to dry completely before wearing the dress.

Tips for Shortening a Dress Without Hemming

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when shortening a dress without hemming:

  • Use fabric glue that is specifically designed for hemming. This will ensure that the hem stays in place and doesn't come undone.
  • Iron the dress before you begin. This will make it easier to mark and cut the dress.
  • Test the fabric glue on a small, inconspicuous area of the dress before using it on the hem.
  • If you're not sure about the length you want, start by cutting the dress a little longer than you think you want it. You can always cut more off later, but you can't add fabric back.

FAQs About Shortening a Dress Without Hemming

Can I use fabric glue to shorten any type of dress?

Fabric glue can be used on most types of fabric, but it's always a good idea to test it on a small, inconspicuous area of the dress before using it on the hem.

Can I wash a dress that has been shortened with fabric glue?

Yes, you can wash a dress that has been shortened with fabric glue. However, be sure to follow the washing instructions on the glue packaging.

Will the hem stay in place if I use fabric glue?

Yes, if you use a fabric glue that is specifically designed for hemming, the hem should stay in place. However, it's always a good idea to test the glue on a small area of the dress before using it on the hem.

Hair Care Tips for Short Dress Wearers

Short dresses can be a lot of fun to wear, but they can also have an impact on your hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips for short dress wearers:

  • Consider wearing your hair up or in a ponytail to keep it out of your face while you're wearing a short dress.
  • Use a hair tie that won't leave a dent in your hair, such as a spiral hair tie.
  • Keep a travel-sized bottle of hairspray in your bag to touch up your hair throughout the day.
  • Consider using dry shampoo to freshen up your hair if you don't have time to wash it.

Shortening a dress without hemming might seem like a daunting task, but it's actually quite simple. With a bit of fabric glue and some basic tools, you can have a shorter dress in no time. Just be sure to follow the tips and FAQs provided in this article to get the best results. And don't forget to take care of your hair while you're wearing your new, shorter dress!
