How To Cut A Pixie Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Pixie haircuts are a popular choice for women who want a low-maintenance, chic hairstyle. However, cutting a pixie haircut can be intimidating for those who have never done it before. In this guide, we’ll show you how to cut a pixie haircut step-by-step, along with some tips and FAQs to help you achieve the perfect cut.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut a Pixie Haircut
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting a Pixie Haircut
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Pixie Haircuts

How to Cut a Pixie Haircut

Cutting a pixie haircut involves trimming the hair short all around the head, leaving a slightly longer length on top. The sides and back are usually cut short, while the top is left longer to create a textured, layered look. You can use clippers or scissors to achieve this look, depending on your preference and skill level.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a pixie haircut:

  1. Wash and dry the hair thoroughly.
  2. Section off the hair into four parts: top, left side, right side, and back.
  3. Start with the back section and use clippers to trim the hair short, starting at the nape of the neck and working your way up. Use a comb to guide the clippers and create an even, tapered look.
  4. Move on to the sides and use the clippers to trim the hair short, blending it with the back section.
  5. Use scissors to trim the top section of hair to the desired length. Start by cutting the hair in the center, then work your way outwards, using the previously cut hair as a guide.
  6. Use scissors to create layers and texture in the top section of hair. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut into it at an angle to create uneven lengths.
  7. Blend the top section of hair with the sides and back using scissors or clippers.
  8. Trim the hair around the ears and neckline as needed.
  9. Style the hair using a texturizing product to create volume and definition.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect pixie haircut:

  • Start with clean, dry hair to make it easier to see the natural texture and movement of the hair.
  • Use a comb to guide the clippers and create an even, tapered look.
  • Be cautious when cutting the hair around the ears and neckline, as these areas can be tricky to get right.
  • Use texturizing scissors to create layers and texture in the top section of hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect pixie cut for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a pixie haircut:

Q: How short should I cut my hair for a pixie cut?

A: The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference and face shape. Generally, the hair is cut to about 1-2 inches in length all around the head.

Q: Can I cut a pixie haircut at home?

A: Yes, you can cut a pixie haircut at home, but it may take some practice and skill to get it right. Consider consulting with a professional stylist before attempting a DIY haircut.

Q: How often do I need to trim my pixie haircut?

A: Pixie haircuts require regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and style.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Haircuts

Here are some tips for caring for your pixie haircut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good texturizing product to add volume and definition to your hair.
  • Consider getting regular scalp massages to promote healthy hair growth and circulation.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to cut a pixie haircut like a pro. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun experimenting with different styles and lengths.
