How To Big Chop 4C Hair Tips And Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with your 4C hair every day? Do you want to start fresh with a new look? If yes, then big chopping might be the solution you need. However, the thought of cutting all your hair off can be scary. In this guide, we will break down what big chopping is, how to do it, and how to take care of your hair after the chop.

Table of Contents

  • What is Big Chop?
  • How to Big Chop 4C Hair
  • Tips for Big Chop 4C Hair
  • Big Chop 4C Hair FAQ
  • Hair Care after Big Chop

What is Big Chop?

Big chop is a hair cutting technique that involves cutting off all of your hair at once, leaving you with a short natural hair. It's a great way to transition from relaxed or chemically treated hair to natural hair. Big chop is also a popular choice for those who want to start their natural hair journey from scratch.

How to Big Chop 4C Hair

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools including a pair of sharp scissors, a wide-tooth comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle Your Hair

Wash your hair and detangle it using a wide-tooth comb. This will make it easier to cut your hair without causing any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four or more parts using hair clips. This will make it easier for you to work on each section of your hair.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Start cutting your hair with a pair of sharp scissors. Cut as close to your scalp as possible but be careful not to cut yourself. Continue cutting until all your hair is cut off.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

After cutting your hair, you can style it the way you want. You can do a wash and go, twist-out, or any other style that you like.

Tips for Big Chop 4C Hair

Here are some tips to help you make the most out of your big chop:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a satin or silk pillowcase or bonnet.
  • Be patient, your hair will grow back. In the meantime, experiment with different styles and have fun with your new look.

Big Chop 4C Hair FAQ

Q: Will my hair grow back after the big chop?

A: Yes, your hair will grow back after the big chop. However, it may take some time to grow back to its original length.

Q: How often should I cut my hair after the big chop?

A: It's up to you how often you want to cut your hair. Some people prefer to cut their hair every few months, while others prefer to let it grow out.

Q: Can I color my hair after the big chop?

A: Yes, you can color your hair after the big chop. However, be sure to use hair dye that is suitable for your hair type and texture.

Hair Care after Big Chop

After the big chop, you need to take care of your hair to promote healthy growth. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping with a satin or silk pillowcase or bonnet.

Big chopping 4C hair can be a scary but exciting experience. Follow these tips and steps to make the most out of your new look. Remember to take care of your hair to promote healthy growth, and have fun experimenting with different styles.
