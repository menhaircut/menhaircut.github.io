What is Hair Mousse?

Hair mousse is a styling product that is used to add volume, texture, and hold to hair. It is typically applied to damp hair and then blow-dried to create the desired style. Mousse is a popular product for people with thick curly hair because it can help define curls and reduce frizz.

How to Use Hair Mousse

Step 3: Apply Mousse

Dispense a small amount of mousse into your hand and distribute it evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots and mid-lengths.

Use your hands to scrunch your hair, working the mousse into your curls. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 5: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting. Be sure to scrunch your hair as you dry it to enhance your curls.
