Do Side Bangs Slim Your Face Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Description

Side bangs have been a popular hairstyle for many years, and there's a good reason for that. Not only do they add a bit of flair to any hairdo, but they can also help to slim down the appearance of your face. If you've been considering side bangs but aren't sure if they'll work for you, keep reading to learn more about how side bangs can help you achieve a slimmer-looking face.

How to Get Side Bangs

If you're interested in getting side bangs, the first step is to find a hairstylist who specializes in cutting bangs. They will be able to recommend the best type of bangs for your face shape and hair type. Once you've found a stylist, you'll want to bring in pictures of side bangs that you like to help guide the stylist in creating the perfect look for you.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help your stylist to see the natural texture and movement of your hair.

2. Use a comb to separate the section of hair that will become your bangs. This section should be about 1-2 inches wide.

3. Use your fingers to pinch the hair where you want the cut to fall. This will help your stylist to see where the hair should be cut.

4. Hold the hair taut and cut it at an angle, leaving the longest pieces near the center of your forehead and gradually getting shorter towards the sides.

5. Style your new side bangs using a blow dryer and a round brush. Use the brush to create volume and movement, and the blow dryer to set the style in place.

Tips for Styling Side Bangs

1. Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying your bangs.

2. Use a small amount of hair serum or oil to help tame any frizz or flyaways.

3. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleek and polished look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do side bangs work for all face shapes?

A: Side bangs can work for any face shape, but the length and shape of the bangs may need to be adjusted depending on your individual features.

Q: How often do I need to trim my side bangs?

A: It's recommended to trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care Tips for Side Bangs

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and bangs healthy and hydrated.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause split ends.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid brushing your bangs too vigorously as this can cause breakage.

So, do side bangs slim your face? The answer is yes! Side bangs can help to create a more angular and defined look to your face, helping to create the appearance of a slimmer face. If you're considering side bangs, be sure to find a stylist who can help guide you in choosing the best style for you, and be sure to follow our hair care and styling tips to keep your side bangs looking their best.
