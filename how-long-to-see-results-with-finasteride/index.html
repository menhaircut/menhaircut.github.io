How to Use Finasteride

Finasteride is a prescription medication that is taken orally. It comes in a tablet form and is usually taken once a day with or without food. It is important to take finasteride exactly as prescribed by your doctor. Do not take more or less than the recommended dose.

Who Can Use Finasteride?

Finasteride is only approved for use in men. Women who are pregnant or may become pregnant should not handle finasteride tablets. If a woman comes into contact with a broken or crushed tablet, she should immediately wash the area with soap and water.

  5. Be consistent. Take finasteride at the same time each day.

Tips for Using Finasteride

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your finasteride treatment:

  • Be patient. It can take several months to see results with finasteride.
  • Be consistent. Take finasteride at the same time each day.

FAQs about Finasteride

How long does it take to see results with finasteride?

It can take several months to see results with finasteride. Some men may see results as early as three months, while others may not see results for up to a year.

The most common side effects of finasteride include decreased libido, erectile dysfunction, and decreased volume of ejaculate. These side effects are usually reversible and go away once finasteride treatment is stopped.
