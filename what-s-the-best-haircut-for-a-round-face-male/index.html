What s The Best Haircut For A Round Face Male

Written by Frank Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

For men with round faces, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. A good haircut can help to create a more defined jawline and elongate the face, while a bad one can make the face look even rounder. In this blog post, we'll be discussing some of the best haircuts for round-faced men, as well as some tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Best Haircuts for Round-Faced Men
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for round-faced men, it's important to determine whether or not you actually have a round face. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Take a close look at your face shape. Do you have a wide forehead and cheeks, with a rounded chin? If so, you likely have a round face.
  3. You can also use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they're roughly the same width, you probably have a round face.

Once you've determined that you have a round face, it's time to choose the right haircut.

Best Haircuts for Round-Faced Men

Here are some of the best haircuts for men with round faces:

1. Short Crop

A short, textured crop can help to make your face look longer and more defined. Ask your barber to use scissors to create texture, rather than clippers, which can make your hair look too uniform.

2. Slicked-Back Undercut

An undercut with slicked-back hair can help to elongate the face, especially if you keep the sides and back short. This style works best with straight or wavy hair.

3. Textured Quiff

A textured quiff can add height and definition to your hair, which can help to balance out a round face. Keep the sides and back short, and use a texturizing product to create volume and movement on top.

4. Side-Parted Pompadour

A side-parted pompadour can also help to elongate the face, especially if you keep the sides and back short. Use a pomade or wax to create volume and hold, and comb your hair to the side to create the part.

5. Messy Fringe

A messy fringe can help to create an asymmetrical look that draws attention away from the roundness of your face. Keep the sides and back short, and use a texturizing product to create movement and volume on top.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Here are some tips for styling your hair if you have a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look even rounder.
  • Use a texturizing product, like a pomade or wax, to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Consider getting a fade or undercut to keep the sides and back short, which can help to elongate the face.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for your face shape.

FAQs

1. Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round face. However, it's important to keep the length layered and textured, which can help to create a more defined jawline.

2. Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

Not necessarily. Bangs can work for round-faced men, as long as they're kept short and textured. Avoid heavy, blunt bangs, as they can make your face look even rounder.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows. Regular haircuts can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like blow dryers and straightening irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.
  • Eat a healthy diet, rich in vitamins and minerals, to promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut, you can create a stylish and flattering look for your round face.
