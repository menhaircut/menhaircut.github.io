Hairstyles For 60 Year Old Woman With Wavy Hair

Written by William Sep 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a 60 year old woman with wavy hair, you might be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. There are many options available to you, whether you want to keep your hair long and flowing or go for a shorter, more practical cut. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best hairstyles for women in their 60s with wavy hair.

Description

Wavy hair can be a blessing and a curse. On the one hand, it's naturally voluminous and full of body. On the other hand, it can be hard to manage and style. If you're a 60 year old woman with wavy hair, you'll want to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.

How to

When deciding on a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your face shape. A bob or pixie cut might look great on a woman with an oval face, but it might not be as flattering on someone with a square face. Your hair texture is also an important factor to consider. Wavy hair can be difficult to style, so you'll want to choose a cut that works with your natural texture.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a new hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture
  3. Look for inspiration online or in magazines
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to see what styles would work best for you
  5. Choose a style that you love and that makes you feel confident and beautiful

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your wavy hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural waves
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and make it frizzy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Consider using a sea salt spray to enhance your natural waves and add texture to your hair

FAQ

Q: What are some good hairstyles for 60 year old women with wavy hair?

A: Some good hairstyles for women in their 60s with wavy hair include long layers, a shoulder-length bob, or a pixie cut with textured layers.

Q: How can I style my wavy hair without using heat?

A: You can style your wavy hair without using heat by using a sea salt spray to enhance your natural waves, or by braiding your hair while it's wet and letting it air dry.

Hair Care

When caring for your wavy hair, it's important to use products that are designed for your hair type. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are formulated for wavy or curly hair, as these will help to enhance your natural waves and keep your hair moisturized. It's also a good idea to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, as this will help to keep your hair healthy and free from damage.

Overall, there are many great hairstyles for 60 year old women with wavy hair. Whether you choose to keep your hair long and flowing or go for a shorter, more practical cut, the key is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. By following these tips and caring for your hair properly, you can rock any hairstyle you choose!
