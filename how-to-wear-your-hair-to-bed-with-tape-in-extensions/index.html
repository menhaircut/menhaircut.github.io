How to Wear Your Hair to Bed with Tape In Extensions

If you have tape in hair extensions, it is important to properly care for them at night to prevent tangling, matting, and damage. Sleeping with tape in extensions can be tricky, but with the right techniques, you can wake up with beautiful, tangle-free hair.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to wear your hair to bed with tape in extensions:

  1. Brush your hair gently to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections, creating a part down the middle.
  3. Take each section and twist it away from your face, creating two loose buns at the nape of your neck.
  4. Secure the buns with bobby pins or hair ties.
  5. Wrap a silk or satin scarf around your head to protect your hair from friction and tangling.
  6. Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to further minimize friction.

Remember to be gentle when securing your buns, as pulling or tugging on your extensions can cause damage.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you protect your tape in extensions while you sleep:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush specifically designed for hair extensions to detangle your hair before bed.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause matting and tangling.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them moisturized and prevent dryness.
  • Avoid sleeping in tight braids or ponytails, as this can cause tension on your scalp and lead to hair loss.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing tape in extensions to bed:

Can I sleep with my hair down?

While it is possible to sleep with your hair down, it is not recommended for those with tape in extensions. Sleeping with your hair loose can cause tangling and matting, which can be difficult to remove without causing damage to your extensions.

What kind of pillowcase should I use?

It is best to use a silk or satin pillowcase, as these fabrics are gentle on your hair and minimize friction. Cotton pillowcases can cause tangling and breakage.

How often should I wash my hair extensions?

It is recommended to wash your tape in extensions every 4-6 weeks, depending on how often you wear them and how much product you use. Be sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and avoid applying conditioner to the roots of your hair to prevent slippage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the health and longevity of your tape in extensions. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Brush your hair gently and often to prevent tangling.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions, as this can cause damage and shorten their lifespan.
  • Avoid using oil-based products on your extensions, as these can cause slippage and weaken the adhesive.
  • Be gentle when washing and conditioning your extensions, and avoid rubbing or scrubbing them vigorously.
  • Do not sleep with wet hair, as this can cause matting and tangling.

By following these tips and techniques, you can protect your tape in extensions while you sleep and keep them looking beautiful and healthy for weeks to come.
