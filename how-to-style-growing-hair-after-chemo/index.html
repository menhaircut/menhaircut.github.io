How To Style Growing Hair After Chemo Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Nov 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to style your hair after undergoing chemotherapy? You’re not alone. Hair loss is a common side effect of chemotherapy, and many people find it difficult to style their hair as it grows back. In this article, we’ll share tips and tricks on how to style growing hair after chemo.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Growing Hair After Chemo
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Growing Hair After Chemo

Styling your hair after chemotherapy can be challenging, especially if you’re not used to styling short hair. Here are a few things you can do to make it easier:

  • Invest in the right hair care products
  • Experiment with different hairstyles
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands or scarves
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your hair

Invest in the Right Hair Care Products

When your hair starts to grow back, it may look and feel different from your pre-chemo hair. It may be more brittle, dry, or coarse. It’s essential to invest in hair care products that cater to your hair’s specific needs. Look for products that are gentle, sulfate-free, and moisturizing.

Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Experimenting with different hairstyles can be fun and exciting. If you’re not used to styling short hair, try watching tutorials on YouTube or ask your hairstylist for advice. You can try a pixie cut, a bob, or even a shaggy hairstyle. Don’t be afraid to try something new.

Accessorize Your Hair with Headbands or Scarves

If you’re not comfortable with your hair’s length, you can accessorize it with headbands or scarves. Headbands can add some color and style to your hair, and scarves can protect your hair from the sun and wind.

Get Regular Haircuts to Maintain the Shape of Your Hair

Getting regular haircuts is essential to maintain the shape of your hair. It can also help get rid of split ends and promote healthy hair growth. Your hairstylist can help you find a haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to style growing hair after chemo:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair gently, avoiding rough towel movements
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to protect your hair from heat damage
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a low-heat setting, starting at the roots and working your way down
  5. Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair
  6. Finish off with a hairspray or a hair serum to hold your style in place

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling growing hair after chemo:

  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons
  • Use hair accessories like hair clips or bobby pins to create different hairstyles
  • Consider using hair extensions if you’re not happy with the length of your hair

FAQ

Can I dye my hair after chemotherapy?

You should wait at least six months after your last chemotherapy treatment before dyeing your hair. It’s best to consult your doctor before making any changes to your hair care routine.

How long does it take for hair to grow back after chemotherapy?

Hair usually starts to grow back two to three weeks after chemotherapy treatment ends. It may take up to six months or longer for your hair to grow back fully.

Is it normal for my hair to look and feel different after chemotherapy?

Yes, it’s normal for your hair to look and feel different after chemotherapy. Your hair may be more brittle, dry, or coarse. It’s essential to use hair care products that cater to your hair’s specific needs.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips for growing hair after chemo:

  • Avoid heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids or ponytails
  • Wash your hair with sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently
  • Avoid using hair products with harsh chemicals

Styling growing hair after chemo can be challenging, but with the right hair care products and styling techniques, you can achieve beautiful hairstyles. Remember to be patient with your hair and enjoy the journey.
