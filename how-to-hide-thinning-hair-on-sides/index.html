How To Hide Thinning Hair On Sides Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Dealing with thinning hair on the sides can be a frustrating experience for both men and women. However, there are various ways you can hide it and feel more confident with your hair. In this article, we’ll discuss some tips and tricks on how to hide thinning hair on the sides.

Description

Thinning hair on the sides can be caused by several factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, stress, and poor hair care practices. It can be challenging to hide, especially if you have shorter hair. However, there are effective ways to make your hair look fuller and thicker.

How to Hide Thinning Hair on Sides

1. Get a Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is an excellent way to add volume to your hair and make it appear thicker. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair in layers and add some texture to the ends. This will create movement and give the illusion of fuller hair.

2. Use Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are an excellent way to conceal thinning hair on the sides. They come in small containers and are made of keratin, the same protein found in human hair. To use hair fibers, shake the container over your thinning areas and pat down gently. The fibers will cling to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

3. Try Hair Extensions

If you have longer hair, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and length to your hair. You can choose from clip-in extensions, tape-in extensions, or sew-in extensions. They are available in various colors and textures, so you can find one that matches your hair perfectly.

4. Use Hair Gel

Hair gel can be an excellent way to add volume to your hair and make it look thicker. Apply a small amount of gel to your hair and comb it through. This will create texture and hold your hair in place, making it look fuller.

Step by Step: How to Hide Thinning Hair on Sides

Step 1: Shampoo Your Hair

Start by shampooing your hair with a volumizing shampoo. This will add body and texture to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 2: Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush. This will create volume and make your hair look fuller.

Step 3: Apply Hair Fibers

If you’re using hair fibers, shake the container over your thinning areas and pat down gently. The fibers will cling to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

Step 4: Use Hair Gel

Apply a small amount of hair gel to your hair and comb it through. This will create texture and hold your hair in place, making it look fuller.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can try a layered haircut, a side part, or a messy look.

Tips for Hiding Thinning Hair on Sides

1. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly. This can cause hair breakage and make your hair look thinner.

2. Choose the Right Hair Products

Choose hair products that are designed to add volume and texture to your hair. Look for products that contain keratin, biotin, and other hair-strengthening ingredients.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Heat damage can cause hair breakage and make your hair look thinner. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or styling your hair with heat tools.

FAQ

Q: Can I use hair fibers on wet hair?

A: No, it’s best to use hair fibers on dry hair. Wet hair can cause the fibers to clump together and not spread evenly.

Q: Can I dye my hair if I’m using hair fibers?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you’re using hair fibers. Just make sure to apply the fibers after dyeing your hair.

Q: How long do hair extensions last?

A: Hair extensions can last from three months to a year, depending on the type of extensions and how well you take care of them.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Hair on Sides

1. Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling your hair can cause hair breakage and make your hair look thinner. Avoid using heat tools and tight hairstyles that pull your hair back tightly.

2. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can promote healthy hair growth. Eat foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals, such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.

3. Take Supplements

Supplements can help promote healthy hair growth. Look for supplements that contain biotin, vitamin D, and other hair-strengthening ingredients.

In conclusion, there are several ways to hide thinning hair on the sides, including getting a layered haircut, using hair fibers, trying hair extensions, and using hair gel. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can feel more confident and beautiful with your hair.
