How To Tell Your Face Shape App A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you curious about your face shape? Do you want to know which hairstyles or glasses will suit you best? Fortunately, there is an easy way to determine your face shape using a face shape app. In this article, we will guide you through the process of using a face shape app, from downloading to analyzing your results.

How to

Step 1: Download a Face Shape App

The first step is to download a face shape app from the App Store or Google Play Store. There are many free face shape apps available, so choose one that has good reviews and ratings.

Step 2: Take a Photo

Once you have downloaded the app, take a photo of yourself using the app’s camera function. Make sure that your face is well-lit and that your hair is pulled back so that your entire face is visible.

Step 3: Analyze Your Results

The app will analyze your photo and determine your face shape. Most face shape apps will provide you with a visual representation of your face shape, along with a description of the features that define your face shape.

Step 4: Use the Results

Now that you know your face shape, you can use this information to choose hairstyles, glasses, and makeup that will flatter your features. Many face shape apps also provide recommendations for hairstyles and glasses that will suit your face shape.

Tips

  • Make sure that you take a photo of your entire face, including your forehead, cheeks, and chin.
  • If you wear glasses, remove them before taking the photo.
  • If you have long hair, pull it back so that your entire face is visible.
  • Make sure that your face is well-lit and that there are no shadows on your face.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a face shape app on someone else?

A: Yes, you can use a face shape app on someone else, but make sure that the lighting and positioning of the camera are the same as when you took your own photo.

Q: Are face shape apps accurate?

A: Face shape apps use algorithms to analyze your facial features and determine your face shape. While they are not 100% accurate, they can provide a good indication of your face shape.

Q: Can I use a face shape app on a photo?

A: Yes, some face shape apps allow you to upload a photo from your camera roll or social media accounts.

Hair Care

Knowing your face shape can also help you choose the right haircut and hair care routine. For example, if you have a round face shape, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as layered cuts or a pixie cut.

Similarly, if you have a heart-shaped face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, as this can make your forehead look even larger. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the lower half of your face, such as long layers or a side-swept bang.

In addition to choosing the right haircut, it is important to use hair care products that are suited to your hair type and condition. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and body to your hair. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and defined.

By using a face shape app and choosing the right haircut and hair care routine, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and stylish every day.
