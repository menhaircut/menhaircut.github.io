Short Hairstyles For Oval Faces Over 50

Written by Frank Jul 24, 2023 · 3 min read

As we age, our hair may start to thin and lose its luster. But that doesn't mean we can't still rock a stylish haircut. Short hairstyles are a great option for women over 50, especially those with oval faces. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short hairstyles for oval faces over 50 and provide tips for caring for your locks.

Description

An oval face is considered the ideal shape, as it is symmetrical and balanced. Women with oval faces have a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width. Short hairstyles can accentuate the features of an oval face, highlighting the cheekbones and jawline.

How To

When choosing a short hairstyle, consider your hair texture and thickness. Fine hair may benefit from a layered cut, while thick hair can handle a blunt bob. A pixie cut is a popular choice for women over 50, as it is low maintenance and can be styled in various ways.

Consult with your stylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and hair type. Bring in pictures of styles you like and be open to their suggestions. They may recommend adding fringe or layers to frame your face.

Step by Step

1. Wash and condition your hair with products designed for your hair type.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as a mousse or gel.

3. Use a blow dryer and round brush to style your hair, focusing on the roots for volume.

4. Finish with a hairspray or texturizing spray.

Tips

1. Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

3. Schedule regular trims to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends.

4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights for dimension.

FAQ

Q: Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. A well-executed short hairstyle can actually make you look younger and more vibrant.

Q: Can I still style my hair in different ways with a short haircut?

A: Absolutely. Pixie cuts can be styled in various ways, including spiky, slicked back, or with added volume.

Hair Care

Caring for short hair is relatively simple. Wash and condition regularly, and use a styling product to enhance your natural texture. Avoid using hot tools every day, as this can cause damage and dryness. If you have color-treated hair, use a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for color protection.

With the right haircut and styling products, short hairstyles can be a great option for women over 50 with oval faces. Consult with your stylist to find the perfect cut for you, and don't forget to take care of your locks with regular trims and quality hair care products.
