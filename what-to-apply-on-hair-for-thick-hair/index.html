What To Apply On Hair For Thick Hair Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of dealing with thick hair that is unruly and difficult to manage? Do you find yourself struggling to find the right products that work for your hair type? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss what to apply on hair for thick hair and provide you with some tips and tricks to make styling your hair a breeze.

Description

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it can provide volume and texture to your hairstyles, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Finding the right products for your hair type is crucial to achieving your desired look.

When it comes to styling thick hair, you want to look for products that provide hold and control without weighing your hair down. You also want to look for products that provide moisture and nourishment to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

How to Apply Products on Thick Hair

Applying products to thick hair can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right techniques, you can achieve the perfect style. Here are some tips on how to apply products to thick hair:

  • Start with clean, damp hair
  • Section your hair to ensure even application
  • Use a quarter-sized amount of product and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly
  • Blow-dry your hair using a diffuser to enhance your natural curls or use a round brush to create a smooth, sleek look

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know how to apply products to your hair, let's break it down step by step:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start with clean, damp hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to nourish your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using hair clips or elastics. This will help ensure even product distribution and make styling easier.

Step 3: Apply product

Apply a quarter-sized amount of product to each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

Step 4: Style your hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls or use a round brush to create a sleek, smooth look. Style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your thick hair:

  • Use a leave-in conditioner to provide extra moisture and nourishment
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair without causing damage
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  • Try using a hair oil or serum to add shine and control frizz

FAQs

Q: What products should I use on my thick hair?

A: Look for products that provide hold and control without weighing your hair down. You also want to look for products that provide moisture and nourishment to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Q: How often should I wash my thick hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with thick hair can go longer between washes, while others may need to wash their hair more frequently. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days and adjust as needed.

Q: Can I use hair spray on my thick hair?

A: Yes, you can use hair spray on your thick hair to help hold your style in place. Look for a lightweight hair spray that won't weigh your hair down or make it look stiff.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

In addition to using the right products and styling techniques, taking care of your hair is essential to keeping it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your thick hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote hair growth and strength
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect style for your thick hair. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for you and don't be afraid to ask your stylist for advice. With a little patience and practice, you can have the beautiful, healthy hair you've always wanted!
