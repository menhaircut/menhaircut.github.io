How To Find Which Haircut Suits Me A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're like most people, you've probably found yourself wondering what haircut would look best on you at least once in your life. With so many different styles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to go for. However, finding the right haircut doesn't have to be a daunting task. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to find which haircut suits you best.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type
  • Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle
  • Step 4: Look for Inspiration
  • Tips for Choosing the Perfect Haircut
  • FAQs

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for you is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and oblong. To figure out which face shape you have, pull your hair back and take a look in the mirror. Pay attention to the shape of your jawline, cheekbones, and forehead.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head to elongate your face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

The next step in finding the perfect haircut for you is to consider your hair type. Do you have thick or thin hair? Is it curly or straight? Knowing your hair type will help you choose a hairstyle that will work with your natural texture.

For example, if you have thick, curly hair, you may want to consider a layered haircut to help manage the volume. On the other hand, if you have thin, straight hair, a blunt cut may be a better option to add the illusion of thickness.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a haircut, it's essential to think about your lifestyle. Are you someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, or do you prefer a low-maintenance look? Do you work in a professional environment that requires a more conservative style, or do you have the freedom to experiment with bolder cuts?

Consider your daily routine and choose a haircut that fits your lifestyle. For example, if you're always on the go and don't have much time to style your hair, a short, easy-to-maintain cut may be a good option for you.

Step 4: Look for Inspiration

Before heading to the salon, take some time to look for inspiration for your new haircut. Browse through magazines, social media, or even celebrity photos for ideas. Be sure to choose hairstyles that are similar to your hair type and face shape.

When you go to the salon, bring pictures of the hairstyles you like with you. This will help your stylist understand the look you're going for and make suggestions that will work best for you.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing the perfect haircut:

  • Consider your hair's natural growth pattern when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Don't be afraid to ask your stylist for their opinion on what will look best on you.
  • Be realistic about how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair each day.
  • Remember that hair grows back, so don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the perfect haircut:

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and the length of your hair. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

What should I do if I don't like my new haircut?

If you're not happy with your new haircut, talk to your stylist about what changes can be made. They may be able to make adjustments to the cut to better suit your preferences.

Can I pull off a drastic haircut change?

It depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. It's always a good idea to consult with your stylist before making a drastic change to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Finding the perfect haircut can take some time and experimentation, but with these tips, you'll be well on your way to discovering a style that suits you. Remember to take your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle into consideration, and don't be afraid to try something new!
