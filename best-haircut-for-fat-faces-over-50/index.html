Best Haircut For Fat Faces Over 50 A Guide For Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Oliver Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 50 with a round or chubby face? Finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. With the right style, you can accentuate your best features and boost your confidence. In this guide, we will discuss the best haircut for fat faces over 50, how to achieve it, and tips for maintaining it.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

The best haircut for fat faces over 50 is a style that elongates the face and creates the illusion of a slimmer profile. This can be achieved through layers, angled cuts, and volume at the crown. Avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt cuts, one-length styles, and heavy bangs.

Consult with your stylist to determine the best style for your face shape and hair type. Bring in photos of hairstyles you like and be open to their suggestions. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can transform your look and feel confident in your appearance.

How to

To achieve the best haircut for fat faces over 50, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best style for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose a layered or angled cut that elongates the face and creates volume at the crown.
  3. Avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt cuts, one-length styles, and heavy bangs.
  4. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to the hair.
  5. Style your hair with a round brush to create volume at the crown and smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  6. Finish with a lightweight hairspray or styling product to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Follow these steps to achieve the best haircut for fat faces over 50:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair down the center or to the side.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the crown and tease it with a comb to create volume.
  4. Smooth out the teased section with a round brush.
  5. Cut your hair in layers or angled cuts to elongate the face and create volume at the crown.
  6. Use a hair dryer and round brush to style your hair, focusing on the crown for added volume.
  7. Finish with a lightweight hairspray or styling product to hold the style in place.

Tips

Follow these tips to maintain the best haircut for fat faces over 50:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a fat face?

The best haircut for a fat face is a style that elongates the face and creates the illusion of a slimmer profile. This can be achieved through layers, angled cuts, and volume at the crown. Avoid styles that add width to the face, such as blunt cuts, one-length styles, and heavy bangs.

Can I have bangs with a fat face?

Yes, you can have bangs with a fat face, but it's important to choose the right style. Avoid heavy or blunt bangs that add width to the face. Instead, opt for side-swept or layered bangs that elongate the face and create a slimming effect.

What hair color is best for a fat face?

The best hair color for a fat face is one that adds dimension and depth to the hair. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create a natural-looking effect. Avoid solid or flat colors that can make the face appear wider.

Hair Care

Follow these hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heavy products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can flatter your face shape and feel confident in your appearance. Whether you prefer a short pixie cut or a long layered style, consult with your stylist to find the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
