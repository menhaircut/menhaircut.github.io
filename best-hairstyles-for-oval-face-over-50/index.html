How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face over 50

Before we dive into the best hairstyles, let's first understand what an oval face shape looks like. An oval face has a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow jawline. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off many hairstyles.

When choosing a hairstyle, consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style. A low-maintenance hairstyle that suits your lifestyle is ideal. A hairstyle that accentuates your best features, such as your cheekbones, is also recommended.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Face over 50

1. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that works well for women over 50 with oval faces. It accentuates the cheekbones and jawline and is easy to maintain. You can opt for a short bob or a longer one, depending on your preference.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and edgy hairstyle that looks great on women with oval faces. It highlights the cheekbones and adds volume to the hair. It's also an easy-to-style haircut that requires little maintenance.

3. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is a versatile hairstyle that works well for women with oval faces. It adds volume and texture to the hair and can be styled in different ways. You can opt for a long layered haircut or a shorter one, depending on your preference.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add some dimension to your hairstyle. They highlight the cheekbones and soften the face. If you have an oval face, opt for long side-swept bangs that blend well with the rest of your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieve the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen a hairstyle, it's time to achieve it. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Use a curling iron or a straightener to style your hair as desired.
  5. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining your Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

  • Visit your stylist regularly to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair.

FAQs about Best Hairstyles for Oval Face over 50

What is the most flattering hairstyle for oval face over 50?

A bob haircut, pixie cut, layered haircut, and side-swept bangs are some of the most flattering hairstyles for women with oval faces over 50.

Can I still have long hair if I have an oval face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have an oval face. Opt for a layered haircut to add volume and texture to your hair.

Do I need to use a lot of styling products to achieve the perfect hairstyle?

No, you don't need to use a lot of styling products to achieve the perfect hairstyle. Use a styling product that suits your hair type and style your hair as desired.

Hair Care Advice for Women over 50

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and drier. Here are some hair care tips for women over 50:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and health.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for oval face over 50, it's time to choose the one that suits you best. Remember to consider your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look your best at any age.
