How To Put Hair In Big Clip A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 03, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of struggling to put your hair up in a big clip? Look no further! In this article, we’ll be sharing tips and tricks on how to put hair in a big clip with ease. Whether you’re looking to create a quick updo or add some volume to your hair, a big clip can be a great accessory to have. Read on to learn more!

Description

A big clip is a hair accessory that can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, from simple updos to more elaborate looks. They come in different sizes and styles, so it’s important to choose the right one for your hair type and the look you want to achieve.

How to Put Hair in Big Clip

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to put hair in a big clip:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair into a ponytail or bun at the back of your head.
  3. Hold the big clip with both hands and open it by pressing down on the top part.
  4. Place the clip underneath your ponytail or bun, making sure that it covers the entire base.
  5. Release the top part of the clip and secure it onto your hair by pressing down.
  6. Adjust the clip as needed to make sure it’s secure and comfortable.

And that’s it! You now have a quick and easy updo using a big clip.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the process even easier:

  • Choose a big clip that matches your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair or to add extra hold.
  • Experiment with different styles and positions to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using a big clip:

What hairstyles can I create with a big clip?

You can create a variety of hairstyles with a big clip, including ponytails, buns, half-up hairstyles, and more.

Can I use a big clip on fine or thin hair?

Yes, but you may need to use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your hair in place before adding the clip.

How do I take out the clip?

To remove the clip, simply press down on the top part and slide it out of your hair.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair when using hair accessories like big clips. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using clips or other hair accessories that are too tight, as they can cause breakage and damage.
  • Be gentle when removing the clip to avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Use a detangling brush or comb to remove any knots or tangles before using a big clip.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily put your hair up in a big clip and create a variety of hairstyles. Whether you’re looking for a quick and easy updo or a way to add some volume to your hair, a big clip can be a great accessory to have on hand. Happy styling!
