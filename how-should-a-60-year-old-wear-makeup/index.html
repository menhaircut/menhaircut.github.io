How Should A 60 Year Old Wear Makeup

Written by Christine Sep 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our skin changes and so does our makeup routine. For those who are 60 years old, it's important to update your makeup routine to suit your changing skin needs. In this article, we will explore how should a 60-year-old wear makeup, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

The goal of makeup for a 60-year-old should be to enhance natural beauty, rather than covering up flaws. Start by prepping your skin with a moisturizer that suits your skin type. Next, use a primer to create a smooth base for makeup application.

When it comes to foundation, choose a formula that provides light to medium coverage to even out skin tone. Avoid heavy or cakey formulas that can settle into fine lines and wrinkles. A tinted moisturizer or BB cream can also work well for a natural look.

For the eyes, stick to neutral shades that complement your skin tone. Avoid overly shimmery or glittery shades, as they can emphasize fine lines and wrinkles. A swipe of mascara can help to define lashes and make eyes pop.

When it comes to lips, stick to natural shades that enhance your lip color. A lip balm or tinted lip gloss can also provide moisture and a hint of color.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide for applying makeup for a 60-year-old:

  1. Prep skin with a moisturizer
  2. Apply a primer
  3. Apply light to medium coverage foundation or tinted moisturizer/BB cream
  4. Use neutral eyeshadow shades
  5. Define lashes with mascara
  6. Apply natural lip color or lip balm/tinted lip gloss

Tips

Here are some additional tips for wearing makeup as a 60-year-old:

  • Avoid heavy powders that can settle into fine lines and wrinkles
  • Use a light hand when applying makeup to avoid a heavy or cakey look
  • Stick to neutral shades for a natural look
  • Experiment with different formulas to find what works best for your skin type
  • Consider using a makeup setting spray to keep makeup in place

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about makeup for 60-year-olds:

Q: Should I still wear eyeliner?

A: Yes, eyeliner can help to define eyes and make them pop. Stick to neutral shades and apply with a light hand to avoid a harsh look.

Q: Can I still wear bold lipstick?

A: Yes, but stick to natural shades that complement your skin tone. Bold shades can be overwhelming and draw attention away from other features.

Q: How do I cover age spots and dark circles?

A: Use a color-correcting concealer in a shade that matches your skin tone to cover age spots and dark circles. Apply a light layer of concealer and blend well.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and lifestyle. As we age, hair may become thinner and more fragile, so it's important to use gentle products and avoid heat styling as much as possible. Consider a short haircut or layers to add volume and texture to hair.

Now that you know how should a 60-year-old wear makeup, you can update your beauty routine to enhance your natural beauty and feel confident at any age.
