How To Style Long Hair In Summer Tips And Tricks

Written by William Sep 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Summer is a season of fun and relaxation, but it can also be a challenge for those with long hair. The heat, humidity, and sun can wreak havoc on your locks, leaving you with frizzy, dull, and lifeless hair. However, with the right styling techniques and products, you can keep your long hair looking fabulous all summer long.

  1. How to Style Long Hair in Summer
  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Long Hair in Summer

There are many ways to style long hair in summer, but the most important thing is to choose a style that is comfortable, easy to maintain, and suits your personal style. Here are some popular options:

1. Braids

Braids are a classic summer hairstyle that can keep your long hair neat and tidy. You can choose from a variety of styles, including French braids, fishtail braids, and crown braids. Braids are also great for protecting your hair from the sun and wind.

2. Ponytails

Ponytails are another simple and practical summer hairstyle. You can wear them high or low, slicked back or messy. For a more polished look, add a hair accessory like a scarf, a bow, or a clip.

3. Bun

A bun is a chic and elegant summer hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. You can wear it on top of your head or at the nape of your neck. A messy bun is a great option for a casual day out, while a sleek bun is perfect for a formal event.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style long hair in summer:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and let it air dry for a few minutes before styling.

2. Apply a heat protectant

If you plan to use heat tools like a curling iron or a straightener, apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage.

3. Choose your hairstyle

Decide on the hairstyle you want to wear. You can choose from braids, ponytails, buns, or any other style that suits your hair type and face shape.

4. Prep your hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Apply a styling product like mousse or gel to add texture and hold.

5. Create your hairstyle

Follow the steps for your chosen hairstyle. For example, if you want to create a braid, section your hair and start braiding from the top. Secure the end with a hair tie or a clip.

6. Finish with hairspray

Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place. Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips for Long Hair in Summer

Here are some tips for keeping your long hair healthy and beautiful in summer:

1. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or a scarf to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. You can also use a hair sunscreen or a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

2. Use a clarifying shampoo

Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup and sweat from your hair. This will help keep your hair clean and fresh.

3. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using heat tools like a blow dryer, a curling iron, or a straightener as much as possible. Let your hair air dry and use styling products to add texture and volume.

4. Drink plenty of water

Drink at least 8 glasses of water a day to keep your hair and scalp hydrated. This will prevent dryness and breakage.

FAQs

1. How often should I wash my hair in summer?

You should wash your hair as often as you need to, depending on your hair type and activity level. If you sweat a lot or swim in chlorinated water, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

2. Can I wear my hair down in summer?

Yes, you can wear your hair down in summer, but make sure to protect it from the sun and wind. Use a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

3. How can I prevent frizz in summer?

To prevent frizz, use a smoothing serum or a leave-in conditioner. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel, and let it air dry whenever possible.

Hair Care for Long Hair in Summer

Here are some hair care tips specifically for long hair in summer:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage. This will also help your hair grow faster and look healthier.

2. Deep condition your hair

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and repair your hair. This will help prevent dryness, breakage, and frizz.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This will prevent damage and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your long hair looking beautiful and healthy all summer long. Whether you prefer braids, ponytails, buns, or any other style, make sure to protect your hair from the sun, wind, and humidity. Happy styling!
