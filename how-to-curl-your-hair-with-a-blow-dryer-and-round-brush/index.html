How To Curl Your Hair With A Blow Dryer And Round Brush

Written by Eveline Nov 13, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking to add some volume and curls to your hair, using a blow dryer and round brush is a great option. Not only is it easy to do, but it also saves time compared to using a curling iron. In this article, we'll walk you through the steps on how to curl your hair with a blow dryer and round brush.

Description

The blow dryer and round brush method is a great way to add volume and curls to your hair without using a curling iron. It's a quick and easy way to style your hair while also maintaining its health. This method is perfect for those who are always on-the-go and don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair.

How to Curl Your Hair with a Blow Dryer and Round Brush

Step 1: Prepping Your Hair

The first step to any hair styling routine is to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some extra lift to your roots. Then, towel dry your hair until it's damp but not dripping wet. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to avoid any damage from the blow dryer.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips. Start with the bottom layers and work your way up. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Start Blow Drying

Take a round brush and start blow drying your hair section by section. Place the round brush under the section of hair and pull it down towards the ends while blow drying. Keep rolling the brush downwards as you blow dry to create a curl. Repeat this process until you've curled all the sections of your hair.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you're done curling your hair, finish off with some hairspray to hold the curls in place. You can also use a texturizing spray to add some extra volume to your hair.

Tips

  • Use a round brush that's appropriate for your hair length.
  • Blow dry your hair from the roots to the ends to add volume.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to avoid any damage to your hair.
  • Don't use too much hairspray as it can weigh down your curls.
  • Experiment with different round brush sizes to achieve different curl styles.

FAQ

Q: Can I use this method on all hair types?

A: Yes, this method works on all hair types. However, if you have very fine hair, you may need to use a smaller round brush to achieve the desired curl.

Q: Can I use this method on short hair?

A: Yes, you can use this method on short hair. Use a smaller round brush and focus on curling the ends of your hair.

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The curls should last for a few hours to a full day depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Hair Care

To maintain the health of your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before blow drying and avoid using excessive heat on your hair. It's also important to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid any damage to your hair. Additionally, consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your curls.
