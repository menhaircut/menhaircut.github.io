How To Straighten Hair With Blow Dryer And Comb

Written by Daniel Jun 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you want to achieve straight, sleek hair without using a flat iron, a blow dryer and comb may be your best bet. Not only is it less damaging to your hair, but it is also a quick and easy method. In this article, we will guide you on how to straighten hair with blow dryer and comb.

Table of Contents

  • How to Straighten Hair with Blow Dryer and Comb
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Make sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair from drying out.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Before you start blow-drying, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Using a wide-toothed comb, detangle your hair starting from the ends and working your way up to the roots. This will prevent breakage and make it easier to straighten your hair.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable parts using hair clips. This will make it easier to focus on one section at a time.

Step 5: Start Blow-Drying

Start blow-drying your hair using a round brush or a paddle brush. Hold the blow dryer about 6 inches away from your hair and point it downwards. Use the brush to guide your hair straight as you blow-dry.

Step 6: Repeat

Repeat the process on each section of your hair until it is all straightened out.

Tips

  • Use a high-quality blow dryer with multiple heat settings to avoid heat damage.
  • Blow-dry your hair in the direction you want it to lay.
  • Take breaks in between sections to avoid overheating your hair.
  • Use a cool shot button on your blow dryer to set your style in place.

FAQ

Can I straighten my hair with just a blow dryer?

Yes, you can use a blow dryer and a brush to straighten your hair without using a flat iron.

Is it damaging to straighten my hair with a blow dryer?

Blow-drying your hair can be damaging if you do not use a heat protectant and if you overheat your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair after straightening it with a blow dryer and comb. Use a moisturizing hair mask once a week and avoid heat styling as much as possible. Also, be sure to get regular trims to prevent split ends.

Now you know how to straighten hair with blow dryer and comb. With these tips and tricks, you can achieve sleek, straight hair without damaging your locks. Give it a try and see the results for yourself!
