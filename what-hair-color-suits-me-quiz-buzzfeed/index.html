What Hair Color Suits Me Quiz Buzzfeed Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color and want to switch things up? Or maybe you're curious to see if a different shade would suit you better. Either way, taking a hair color quiz can be a fun and helpful way to determine which hue will flatter you the most. In this article, we'll be focusing on the popular "what hair color suits me quiz buzzfeed" and giving you all the information you need to take it and get the best results.

Description

The "what hair color suits me quiz buzzfeed" is a quiz created by the popular online media company Buzzfeed. It's a series of multiple-choice questions that ask about your skin tone, eye color, natural hair color, and personal style. Based on your answers, the quiz will suggest a hair color that would look great on you.

How to Take the Quiz

To take the quiz, simply go to Buzzfeed's website and search for "what hair color suits me quiz buzzfeed." Click on the quiz, and it will take you through a series of questions. Make sure to answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability. The more accurate your answers are, the more likely you are to get a hair color suggestion that will suit you.

Step by Step Guide

1. Go to Buzzfeed's website and search for "what hair color suits me quiz buzzfeed."

2. Click on the quiz and begin answering the questions.

3. Make sure to answer each question honestly and to the best of your ability.

4. Once you've answered all the questions, the quiz will suggest a hair color that would look great on you.

Tips for Taking the Quiz

1. Take the quiz in natural light to get the most accurate results.

2. Don't overthink your answers – go with your gut instinct.

3. Be honest about your natural hair color and any treatments or dyes you've used in the past.

4. Consider the maintenance required for the hair color suggested by the quiz.

FAQ

Q: Is the "what hair color suits me quiz buzzfeed" accurate?

A: The quiz is not 100% accurate, but it can give you a good idea of which hair colors would suit you based on your answers.

Q: Can I take the quiz more than once?

A: Yes, you can take the quiz as many times as you like to see if you get a different result. However, keep in mind that the quiz is just a suggestion and you should ultimately choose a hair color that you feel confident and comfortable in.

Q: What if I don't like the hair color suggested by the quiz?

A: If you don't like the hair color suggested by the quiz, don't feel obligated to choose it. The quiz is just a guide, and ultimately you should choose a hair color that makes you feel happy and confident.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.

- Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and cause your color to fade.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

- Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant hair product.

Conclusion

The "what hair color suits me quiz buzzfeed" can be a fun and helpful way to determine which hair color would look best on you. However, it's important to remember that the quiz is just a suggestion and you should ultimately choose a hair color that makes you feel confident and comfortable. And once you've chosen your new color, make sure to take care of it properly to keep it looking great!
