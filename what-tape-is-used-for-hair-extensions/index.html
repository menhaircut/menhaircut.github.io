What Tape Is Used For Hair Extensions A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 04, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you considering getting hair extensions but have no idea what kind of tape is used? Look no further! In this article, we’ll be discussing the different types of tape used for hair extensions, how to apply and remove them, and tips on hair care.

Description

There are two main types of tape used for hair extensions: double-sided tape and single-sided tape. Double-sided tape is usually preferred for its strong hold, while single-sided tape is great for easy application and removal. Both types of tape are made with medical-grade adhesive to ensure they won’t damage your natural hair or scalp.

How to Apply Tape-In Hair Extensions

Before applying tape-in hair extensions, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. Then, section your hair horizontally, starting from the nape of your neck. Take a small section of hair at the bottom of the section and sandwich it between two tape-in wefts. Press firmly to secure the wefts in place. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair have tape-in wefts.

How to Remove Tape-In Hair Extensions

To remove tape-in hair extensions, first, saturate the wefts with a bonding remover solution. This will break down the adhesive and make it easier to remove the wefts without damaging your natural hair. Gently peel off the wefts from your hair, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Once all the wefts are removed, wash your hair thoroughly to remove any remaining adhesive.

Tips for Hair Care

After getting tape-in hair extensions, it’s important to take good care of them to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your hair and extensions.
  • Brush your hair gently and avoid tugging on the wefts.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the wefts as much as possible.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose braid or ponytail to prevent tangling.
  • Avoid swimming or exposing your hair to chlorinated water.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do tape-in hair extensions last?

With proper care, tape-in hair extensions can last up to 8 weeks. However, it’s important to get them removed and re-applied by a professional to prevent any damage to your natural hair.

Can I wash my hair with tape-in extensions?

Yes, you can wash your hair with tape-in extensions. Just make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner and avoid rubbing the wefts too aggressively.

Can I style my hair with tape-in extensions?

Yes, you can style your hair with tape-in extensions as long as you avoid using heat styling tools too often. Also, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Conclusion

Now you know what tape is used for hair extensions and how to apply and remove them. With proper care, tape-in hair extensions can give you the long, voluminous hair you’ve always wanted.
