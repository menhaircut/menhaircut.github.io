How Do I Make My Hair Super Curly Tips And Tricks In 2023

Written by Frank Sep 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to make your hair super curly, you've come to the right place! Whether you have naturally straight hair or relaxed locks, there are many ways to achieve beautiful, bouncy curls. In this article, we'll share tips and tricks on how to make your hair super curly, step by step. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions and provide hair care tips to ensure your curls stay healthy and vibrant. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Super Curly
  • Step by Step Guide to Get Super Curly Hair
  • Tips and Tricks for Super Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Super Curly Hair

How to Make Your Hair Super Curly

There are several methods you can use to make your hair super curly:

  • Using a curling iron or wand
  • Using flexi rods or perm rods
  • Using a hair straightener
  • Using a hot air brush
  • Using a hair dryer and diffuser

Each method has its own pros and cons and can give you different types of curls. Experiment with each one to find the one that works best for you.

Step by Step Guide to Get Super Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to get super curly hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start with clean, detangled hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to add moisture to your hair and prevent breakage.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to damp hair. This will help define your curls and make them more bouncy. Use a gel, mousse, or curl cream depending on your hair type.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that each section is evenly curled.

Step 4: Use Your Chosen Method

Use your chosen method to curl each section of hair. Follow the instructions for your specific method and be careful not to burn your hair.

Step 5: Allow Your Hair to Cool

After curling each section, allow your hair to cool completely before touching it. This will help set the curls and prevent them from falling out.

Step 6: Shake Out Your Curls

Once your hair has cooled, shake out your curls to give them more volume. Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to separate the curls and create a more natural look.

Tips and Tricks for Super Curly Hair

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the most out of your super curly hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz while you sleep
  • Wrap your hair in a silk scarf at night to maintain your curls
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz

Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my super curly hair last?

This depends on your hair type and the method you used to achieve your curls. Generally, super curly hair can last anywhere from a few days to a week or more.

How can I prevent my super curly hair from frizzing?

Use a frizz control serum or hair oil to prevent frizz. You can also use a diffuser when blow drying your hair to reduce frizz.

Can I use heat styling tools on relaxed hair?

Yes, but be careful not to use too much heat or too often, as this can damage your hair.

Hair Care for Super Curly Hair

To keep your super curly hair healthy and vibrant, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair and add moisture
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair super curly and keep it healthy and vibrant. Experiment with different methods and products to find the ones that work best for you. With a little practice, you'll have beautiful, bouncy curls that turn heads wherever you go!
