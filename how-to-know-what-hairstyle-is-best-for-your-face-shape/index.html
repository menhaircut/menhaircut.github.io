How To Know What Hairstyle Is Best For Your Face Shape

Written by Luffy Aug 24, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that just don't seem to fit your face? Well, the problem may not be the hairstyle, but rather the shape of your face. Knowing your face shape can help you choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your overall look. In this article, we'll guide you on how to know what hairstyle is best for your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. There are six basic face shapes:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is longer than it is wide, with a gently rounded jawline and forehead. This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off most hairstyles, from short pixie cuts to long beach waves.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape is characterized by a circular shape, with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape is to elongate the face and add height. Layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long waves are great options for this face shape.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape has a strong jawline and forehead, with a square or rectangular shape. Softening the angles of a square face can be achieved through the use of layers and texture. Bobs, textured pixie cuts, and long layers are great choices for this face shape.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape has a wide forehead and cheekbones, with a narrow chin. When choosing a hairstyle for this face shape, the goal is to balance out the forehead and narrow chin. Side-swept bangs, layered cuts, and chin-length bobs are great options.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape has a narrow forehead and jawline, with wide cheekbones. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to soften the angles and add width at the forehead and chin. Long layers, textured bobs, and side-swept bangs are great options.

Pear Face Shape

A pear face shape has a narrow forehead and widens at the jawline. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to balance out the jawline and add volume at the crown. Side-swept bangs, layered cuts, and shoulder-length styles are great options.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing a Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Consider your lifestyle. Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.
  2. Choose a length. Determine whether you want a short, medium, or long hairstyle.
  3. Consider your hair texture. Choose a hairstyle that works with your hair's natural texture.
  4. Choose a style that flatters your face shape. Refer to the face shape guide above to choose a style that enhances your features.
  5. Consult a stylist. A professional stylist can help you choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and lifestyle.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider your hair's thickness. A voluminous hairstyle may not work for thin hair, while a sleek style may not work for thick hair.
  • Consider your age. As we age, our hair texture and face shape change, so it's important to choose a hairstyle that flatters our current features.
  • Experiment with different styles. If you're unsure about a particular hairstyle, try it out with a wig or hair extensions before committing to a cut.
  • Take care of your hair. No matter what hairstyle you choose, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining its health and beauty.

FAQs

What is the most versatile face shape?

The oval face shape is considered the most versatile, as it can pull off most hairstyles.

What is the goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape?

The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape is to elongate the face and add height.

What is the goal when choosing a hairstyle for a square face shape?

The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a square face shape is to soften the angles of the face.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush or comb.

Choosing the perfect hairstyle for your face shape can make a huge difference in your appearance. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
