How To Make Your Hair Smell Good After Straightening It

Written by William Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will discuss how to make your hair smell good after straightening it. We all love the feeling of having straight hair, but sometimes the smell can be a bit unpleasant. With these simple steps, you can have great looking and great smelling hair.

Description

After straightening your hair, it is important to make sure that it smells good. You don't want to walk around with hair that smells like burnt hair or chemicals. In this article, we will provide you with some tips and tricks to make your hair smell good after straightening it.

How to

There are several ways to make your hair smell good after straightening it. Here are some of the most effective ways:

1. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

After straightening your hair, use a leave-in conditioner that has a pleasant scent. This will help to keep your hair smelling fresh and clean throughout the day. You can also use a hair perfume or fragrance mist to add an extra layer of scent.

2. Apply Essential Oils

Essential oils are great for adding a pleasant scent to your hair. Mix a few drops of essential oil with a carrier oil such as coconut oil or jojoba oil and apply it to your hair. You can also add a few drops of essential oil to your leave-in conditioner or hair perfume.

3. Use Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is great for absorbing excess oil and sweat from your hair. It also has a pleasant scent that can help to freshen up your hair. Apply dry shampoo to your roots and massage it into your scalp. Then brush out the excess powder.

4. Wash Your Hair with a Scented Shampoo

If your hair has an unpleasant smell after straightening it, consider washing it with a scented shampoo. Look for a shampoo that has a scent that you like and that is gentle on your hair. After washing your hair, apply a conditioner with a pleasant scent.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to make your hair smell good after straightening it:

Step 1: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

After straightening your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner that has a pleasant scent. This will help to keep your hair smelling fresh throughout the day.

Step 2: Apply Essential Oils

Mix a few drops of essential oil with a carrier oil and apply it to your hair. You can also add a few drops of essential oil to your leave-in conditioner or hair perfume.

Step 3: Use Dry Shampoo

Apply dry shampoo to your roots and massage it into your scalp. Then brush out the excess powder.

Step 4: Wash Your Hair with a Scented Shampoo

If your hair has an unpleasant smell after straightening it, wash it with a scented shampoo and apply a conditioner with a pleasant scent.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help keep your hair smelling good:

1. Avoid Smoking

If you smoke, your hair will absorb the smell of smoke. Avoid smoking or being around smokers to keep your hair smelling fresh.

2. Use a Hair Perfume

A hair perfume is specifically designed to add a pleasant scent to your hair. Spray it on your hair after straightening it for an extra layer of scent.

3. Avoid Overusing Hair Products

Using too many hair products can cause buildup and an unpleasant smell. Use only the necessary amount of hair products and avoid overusing them.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, it is recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I use regular perfume on my hair?

A: No, regular perfume is not designed for use on hair. It can cause damage and dryness. Use a hair perfume or fragrance mist instead.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for healthy and great smelling hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Look for a shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp. Avoid shampoos that contain harsh chemicals.

2. Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your hair after washing it helps to keep it healthy and moisturized.

3. Avoid Overusing Heat Styling Tools

Using heat styling tools too often can cause damage to your hair. Avoid using them every day and use a heat protectant spray before using them.

By following these tips and tricks, you can have great looking and great smelling hair after straightening it. Remember to take care of your hair and avoid using harsh chemicals and overusing hair products.
