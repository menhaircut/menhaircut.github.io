How To Add More Volume To Flat Hair In 2023

Written by William Aug 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to add more volume to your hair but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this article, we'll cover some tips and tricks on how to add more volume to flat hair in 2023. Whether you have short hair or long hair, curly hair or straight hair, these tips will work for you.

Table of Contents

  • How To Add More Volume to Flat Hair
  • Step By Step: Adding Volume to Flat Hair
  • Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Hair
  • FAQs About Adding Volume to Flat Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Hair

How To Add More Volume to Flat Hair

There are many ways to add more volume to flat hair, including using volumizing products, blow drying your hair upside down, teasing your hair, and using hot rollers. Let's take a closer look at each of these methods.

Volumizing Products

Volumizing products, such as mousse, root lifter, and volumizing spray, can help add volume to your hair. Apply these products to your roots and work your way down to the ends. Be sure not to apply too much product, as this can weigh your hair down.

Blow Drying Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down can help add volume to the roots. Flip your hair over and blow dry it while using a round brush to lift the hair at the roots. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help add volume to your curls.

Teasing Your Hair

Teasing your hair can help add volume and texture. Use a teasing comb or brush to lightly backcomb your hair at the roots. Be sure not to tease too aggressively, as this can damage your hair.

Using Hot Rollers

Hot rollers can help add volume and curl to your hair. Roll your hair in sections and let the rollers cool completely before removing them. Gently brush out your curls with a wide-tooth comb for a soft, voluminous look.

Step By Step: Adding Volume to Flat Hair

Now that you know the different methods for adding volume to flat hair, let's break it down into a step-by-step process.

Step 1: Start With Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help add volume to your hair. Be sure to rinse your hair thoroughly to avoid any product buildup.

Step 2: Apply Volumizing Products

Apply a small amount of volumizing product to your roots and work your way down to the ends. Be sure not to apply too much product, as this can weigh your hair down.

Step 3: Blow Dry Upside Down

Flip your hair over and blow dry it while using a round brush to lift the hair at the roots. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help add volume to your curls.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Use a teasing comb or brush to lightly backcomb your hair at the roots. Be sure not to tease too aggressively, as this can damage your hair.

Step 5: Use Hot Rollers

Roll your hair in sections and let the rollers cool completely before removing them. Gently brush out your curls with a wide-tooth comb for a soft, voluminous look.

Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Hair

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to flat hair:

  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo to help add texture and volume to your hair between washes.
  • Avoid using heavy conditioners, as these can weigh your hair down.
  • Consider getting a layered haircut to help add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your hair and cause it to become flat.

FAQs About Adding Volume to Flat Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about adding volume to flat hair:

Q: Can using too much product weigh my hair down?

A: Yes, using too much product can weigh your hair down and make it look flat.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people can go several days between washes, while others may need to wash their hair daily.

Q: Can backcombing my hair damage it?

A: Yes, backcombing your hair can cause damage if done too aggressively. Be sure to use a teasing comb or brush and be gentle when teasing your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Adding Volume to Flat Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for adding volume to flat hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and make your hair look flat.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to help keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

By following these tips and tricks, you can add more volume to your flat hair and achieve the voluminous look you've always wanted. Experiment with different methods and find the ones that work best for your hair type and lifestyle. With a little patience and practice, you can have beautiful, voluminous hair in no time!
