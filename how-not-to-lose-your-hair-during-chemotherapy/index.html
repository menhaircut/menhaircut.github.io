Step-by-Step Guide to Prevent Hair Loss

Step 2: Avoid Heat

Heat can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage, which can exacerbate hair loss during chemotherapy. Avoid using hot styling tools like curling irons and straighteners, and let your hair air dry instead of using a blow dryer.

Step 3: Be Gentle

During chemotherapy, your hair will be more fragile than usual. Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on it. Use a soft brush or wide-tooth comb, and avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails or braids.

Step 4: Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help support hair growth and minimize hair loss during chemotherapy. Eat plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables, and drink lots of water to stay hydrated.

While you may not be able to prevent hair loss entirely, there are some things you can do to maintain healthy hair during chemotherapy:
