What Is A Concave Layered Haircut

Written by Robby Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both fashionable and functional, a concave layered haircut might be right up your alley. This style is characterized by its unique layering technique that creates a graduated effect, with the hair being shortest at the crown and gradually lengthening towards the front. The end result is a chic and modern look that's perfect for any occasion.

Description

The concave layered haircut is a versatile style that works well with a variety of hair types and lengths. It involves cutting the hair at an angle, with the layers being shorter at the back and longer at the front. This creates a rounded, concave shape that frames the face and adds volume and movement to the hair. The layers can be subtle or more pronounced, depending on your preference, and the style can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How To

If you're interested in trying out a concave layered haircut, the first step is to book an appointment with a professional stylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape, and recommend the best technique and length for your hair. During the appointment, the stylist will cut the hair at an angle, with the layers being shorter at the back and longer at the front. They may also add subtle layers throughout the hair to create additional volume and movement.

Step by Step

1. Book an appointment with a professional stylist

2. Discuss your hair type and face shape with the stylist

3. Decide on the best technique and length for your hair

4. Allow the stylist to cut your hair at an angle, with the layers being shorter at the back and longer at the front

5. Add subtle layers throughout the hair to create additional volume and movement

Tips

1. Make sure to use high-quality hair products to maintain your concave layered haircut

2. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to enhance the dimension of the layers

3. Use a round brush when blow-drying to create additional volume and shape

4. Book regular appointments with your stylist to maintain the shape and length of your haircut

FAQ

Q: Will a concave layered haircut work with my hair type?
A: Yes, a concave layered haircut can work with a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair.

Q: How often should I get my concave layered haircut trimmed?
A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the layers.

Q: Can I style my concave layered haircut in different ways?
A: Yes, a concave layered haircut is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, including straight, wavy, or curled.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining the health and appearance of your concave layered haircut. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

1. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for your hair type

2. Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow-drying or using hot styling tools, as this can damage the hair

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and strengthen the hair

4. Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a protective spray

Overall, a concave layered haircut is a stylish and versatile option for anyone looking to switch up their hairstyle. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a dramatic transformation, this technique can be customized to suit your individual needs and preferences. With the right care and maintenance, your concave layered haircut can be a timeless and classic style that will never go out of fashion.
