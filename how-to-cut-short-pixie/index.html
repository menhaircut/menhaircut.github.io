How To Cut Short Pixie A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that will make a statement, a short pixie cut might be the perfect choice for you. This edgy and stylish cut is perfect for women of all ages and hair types, and it's easy to maintain with a little bit of regular trimming. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of cutting a short pixie cut, including tips and tricks to make the process as easy and stress-free as possible.

How to Cut Short Pixie: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, you'll need to gather the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and some hair clips to keep your hair out of the way as you work.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair before you start cutting. Wash and dry your hair as you normally would, and comb it out so that it's free of tangles.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to section your hair into manageable sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, clipping each section out of the way as you go.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, cutting each section to your desired length. Be sure to cut in small increments, as it's easier to take more off than to put it back on.

Step 5: Blend Your Layers

Once you've cut all of your hair to the same length, it's time to blend your layers. Use your scissors to carefully trim any longer pieces, blending them into the shorter layers.

Step 6: Cut Your Bangs

If you want bangs, now is the time to cut them. Use your comb to section your bangs, and cut them to your desired length.

Step 7: Finish Up

Once you've cut your hair to your desired length and shape, it's time to finish up. Use your scissors to carefully trim any stray hairs, and make sure that everything is even and balanced.

Tips for Cutting a Short Pixie Cut

Cutting a short pixie cut can be a little bit intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. Here are some tips to help you get the best possible results:

  • Start with small sections and work your way up.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Take your time and don't rush - it's better to take it slow and get it right.
  • Use your fingers to lift your hair up as you cut, to ensure that you're cutting at the right angle.

FAQs About Cutting a Short Pixie Cut

How often should I trim my short pixie cut?

It's a good idea to trim your short pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh and stylish.

Can I style my short pixie cut in different ways?

Yes! While a short pixie cut may seem limiting, there are actually many different ways to style it. Try using different hair products, or experiment with different styling techniques to find a look that works for you.

Hair Care for a Short Pixie Cut

While a short pixie cut is low-maintenance, it's still important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut a short pixie cut with confidence and ease. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or just a subtle update, a short pixie cut is a great choice for anyone who wants to make a statement with their hair.
