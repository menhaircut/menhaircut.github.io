Good Hairstyles For Round Face Shape A Guide For Flattering Your Features

Written by Oliver Oct 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling like your hair just doesn't suit your face shape? If you have a round face shape, you may feel like your hairstyle options are limited. But fear not! With the right cut and styling, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your hair. In this guide, we'll go over some of the best hairstyles for round face shapes and give you tips on how to care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Good Hairstyles for Round Face Shape
  • Tips for Round Face Hairstyles
  • Hair Care for Round Face Shape
  • FAQ

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into hairstyles, let's make sure you know your face shape. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you've drawn. Is it round, oval, square, heart-shaped, or diamond-shaped?

If you're still unsure, there are plenty of online face shape quizzes and templates you can use to help you determine your shape.

Good Hairstyles for Round Face Shape

So, you have a round face shape. What hairstyles will flatter your features? Here are some options to consider:

Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate your face and draw attention away from the roundness. Ask for layers that start at your chin or below, and avoid layers that start above your cheekbones.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help break up the roundness of your face and create angles. Ask for bangs that start at your eyebrow and taper down to your cheekbone.

Messy Bob

A messy bob can add texture and movement to your hair, which can help balance out the roundness of your face. Ask for a bob that hits just below your chin and has choppy layers.

High Ponytail

A high ponytail can create the illusion of a longer face. To make it work for a round face shape, pull the ponytail back tightly and add volume at the crown of your head.

Side Part

A side part can also create the illusion of a longer face. Part your hair to the side and let the longer side fall over your cheekbone.

Tips for Round Face Hairstyles

When choosing a hairstyle, keep these tips in mind:

  • Avoid blunt bangs that hit above your eyebrows, as they can make your face look rounder.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Consider adding layers or angles to your hair to create the illusion of length and angles.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, as they can make your face look wider.

Hair Care for Round Face Shape

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face shape?

Avoid blunt bangs, center parts, and hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head.

What hairstyles are good for a round face shape?

Long layers, side-swept bangs, messy bobs, high ponytails, and side parts are all good options.

How can I care for my hair if I have a round face shape?

Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid using hot tools too frequently, get regular trims, and consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil.

With these tips and ideas, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your round face shape and makes you feel confident. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
