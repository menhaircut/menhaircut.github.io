What Haircut Looks Good With A Round Face

Written by Frank Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles, but nothing seems to work with your round face? You are not alone. Many people struggle to find the perfect haircut that complements their face shape. In this blog post, we will discuss what haircut looks good with a round face, how to achieve it, and tips to maintain it.

Description

A round face has soft, curved lines with a width and length that are almost equal. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face, and the jawline is rounded. The goal of a haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. This can be achieved by adding height and volume on top, keeping the sides close to the head, and adding angles to the cut.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a round face, consider the length, layers, and angles. Here are some hairstyles that look good with a round face:

Long Layers

Long layers create movement and add volume to the hair, which makes the face appear longer. Avoid blunt cuts or one-length styles, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face. Instead, opt for layers that start at the chin or below and are blended to create a seamless look.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help elongate the face and draw attention away from the cheeks. Avoid straight, blunt bangs as they can make the face appear wider. Instead, go for a side-swept fringe that starts from the side of the forehead and blends into the rest of the hair.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut with textured layers can add height to the hair, creating the illusion of a longer face. The layers should be cut at an angle to create movement and volume. Avoid a blunt cut as it can make the face appear wider.

Bob

A bob cut that is longer in the front and shorter in the back can add angles to the face and create the illusion of length. Avoid a chin-length bob as it can accentuate the roundness of the face. Instead, opt for a longer bob that ends at the collarbone or below.

Step by Step

To achieve the perfect haircut for a round face, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who specializes in cutting hair for round faces.
  2. Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Ask for layers, angles, and volume to create the illusion of a longer face.
  4. Maintain the haircut by getting regular trims and using products that add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain a haircut for a round face:

  • Use volumizing products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to add height and movement to the hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as they can accentuate the roundness of the face.
  • Experiment with different parts and angles to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of the haircut and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What haircut makes a round face look thinner?

A haircut that adds height and volume on top and angles to the sides can create the illusion of a longer, thinner face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and textured pixie cuts are all good options.

Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

No, short haircuts can look good on a round face as long as they are cut in a way that adds height and angles. A textured pixie cut or a bob that is longer in the front and shorter in the back can be flattering on a round face.

Hair Care

To maintain a healthy, shiny, and voluminous haircut for a round face, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage the cuticles and make the hair look flat.
  • Use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and hold to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the haircut.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect haircut that flatters your round face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
