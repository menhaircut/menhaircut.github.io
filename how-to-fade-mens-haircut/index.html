How To Fade Men s Haircut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to get a fresh and stylish look, a faded haircut is the way to go. Fading is a technique where the hair gradually gets shorter from the top to the bottom. It's a popular choice among men because it suits different hair types and lengths, and it's easy to maintain. In this article, we'll show you how to fade men's haircut in simple steps so you can do it at home or guide your hairstylist to achieve your desired look.

How to Fade Men's Haircut

Before we start, here are some things you'll need:

  • hair clippers
  • guard attachments
  • comb
  • hair scissors
  • mirror
  • hair products (optional)

Step 1: Choose the Right Guard Attachment

The guard attachment determines the length of the hair. The lower the number, the shorter the hair. For a fade haircut, you'll need different guard attachments for each level of fading. Start with the longest guard attachment and work your way down to the shortest. It's essential to choose the right guard attachment because it will affect the final outcome of your haircut.

Step 2: Cut the Top

Start by cutting the top of your hair with scissors. Determine the length you want, and cut it evenly. Use a comb to separate the hair and create a clean line. Don't worry about the sides and the back yet.

Step 3: Start Fading

Attach the longest guard attachment to your clippers, and start cutting the hair on the sides and the back of your head. Start from the bottom and work your way up, moving the clippers in an upward motion. Repeat until you've cut all the hair with the longest guard attachment.

Step 4: Change the Guard Attachment

Attach the next shorter guard attachment and repeat the process, but this time, start from a higher point than the previous one. Make sure to blend the two lengths together to create a seamless transition. Repeat with the next shorter guard attachment until you've achieved the desired fade.

Step 5: Perfect the Fade

Use scissors or clippers without the guard attachment to trim any stray hair and perfect the fade. Check in the mirror to ensure that the fade is even and smooth.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Now that you've achieved the perfect fade, it's time to style your hair. You can use hair products like pomade, wax, or gel to create different styles. Experiment and find what works best for you.

Tips for Fading Men's Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect fade:

  • Take your time and be patient. Fading requires precision and attention to detail.
  • Start with a longer guard attachment than you think you need. You can always go shorter, but you can't go longer once you've cut your hair.
  • Don't rush. Fading takes time, so make sure you have enough time to do it properly.
  • Practice makes perfect. If you're not satisfied with your first attempt, don't worry. Keep practicing and refining your technique.

FAQs About Fading Men's Haircut

What is a fade haircut?

A fade haircut is a technique where the hair gradually gets shorter from the top to the bottom. It's a popular choice among men because it suits different hair types and lengths, and it's easy to maintain.

What are the different types of fade haircuts?

There are different types of fade haircuts, including high fade, low fade, mid fade, skin fade, and taper fade. Each type has a different level of fading and can create different styles.

Can I fade my own hair?

Yes, you can fade your own hair. However, it requires precision and attention to detail. Make sure you have the right tools and take your time to do it properly.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use hair products sparingly and avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect fade haircut and keep your hair looking great. Remember to take your time, be patient, and practice to refine your technique. Whether you're doing it at home or with the help of a hairstylist, a fade haircut is a stylish and versatile choice for men.
