Best Clothes To Wear In 60 Degree Weather

Written by Luffy Oct 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on the best clothes to wear in 60 degree weather. As the seasons change, it can be hard to know what to wear to stay comfortable and stylish. In this article, we'll give you tips on what to wear in 60 degree weather, how to put together an outfit, and answer some frequently asked questions. Let's get started!

Description

60 degree weather is a comfortable temperature, but it can still be chilly depending on the time of day and the amount of wind. It's important to wear the right clothes to stay warm and comfortable, but also not to overdo it and become too hot. In this guide, we'll show you how to dress for 60 degree weather for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a formal event.

How to Dress for 60 Degree Weather

When dressing for 60 degree weather, it's important to layer your clothes to stay warm and comfortable. Here are some tips on how to put together an outfit:

Step 1: Start with a Base Layer

Start with a base layer that's made of a lightweight material such as cotton or silk. This will help to regulate your body temperature and keep you comfortable. You can wear a long-sleeve shirt or a sweater as your base layer.

Step 2: Add a Mid-Layer

Add a mid-layer such as a cardigan, vest, or light jacket. This layer should be easy to take off if you get too warm. A denim jacket or a bomber jacket are great options for a casual look, while a blazer is perfect for a more formal occasion.

Step 3: Finish with Outerwear

Finish off your outfit with outerwear such as a coat, parka, or rain jacket. This layer should be weather-appropriate and easy to remove if you get too warm. A trench coat or a puffer jacket are great options for 60 degree weather.

Tips for Dressing for 60 Degree Weather

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when dressing for 60 degree weather:

  • Wear comfortable shoes such as sneakers or loafers.
  • Accessorize with a scarf or a hat to keep you warm.
  • Opt for light-colored clothing to reflect the sun's rays and keep you cool.
  • Avoid heavy fabrics such as wool or cashmere.

FAQ

What Should I Wear for a Casual Day Out?

For a casual day out, you can wear a pair of jeans, a t-shirt, and a denim jacket. Finish off your look with a pair of sneakers and a baseball cap.

What Should I Wear for a Formal Event?

For a formal event, you can wear a pair of dress pants, a button-up shirt, and a blazer. Finish off your look with a pair of dress shoes and a pocket square.

Can I Wear Shorts in 60 Degree Weather?

You can wear shorts in 60 degree weather, but it's important to layer your outfit. Wear a long-sleeve shirt or a sweater with your shorts to stay warm.

Hair Care

When dressing for 60 degree weather, it's important to protect your hair from the elements. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wear a hat or a scarf to protect your hair from the wind.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Opt for a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

Now that you know what to wear in 60 degree weather, you can stay comfortable and stylish no matter the occasion. Remember to layer your clothes, wear comfortable shoes, and accessorize with a scarf or a hat. Happy dressing!
