What Is The Best Hair Style For Round Faces

Written by Eveline Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying different hair styles but nothing seems to suit your round face? Don't worry, we have got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hair style for round faces that will enhance your facial features and make you look stunning. We will also provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and FAQs to help you achieve your desired look. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have soft curves and a circular shape, with a width and length that are almost equal. The best hair style for round faces is one that creates the illusion of length and slims down the face. It is recommended to avoid blunt cuts, middle parts, and heavy bangs that make the face appear rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and height to the crown, and elongate the face.

How to

The best hair style for round faces is a layered haircut with face-framing layers that start at the cheekbones or chin. This style creates vertical lines that elongate the face and adds volume to the crown. You can also add texture or curls to create more height and dimension.

Step by step

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.
  2. Ask for a layered haircut with face-framing layers that start at the cheekbones or chin.
  3. Add texture or curls to create more height and dimension.
  4. Style your hair with a side part or asymmetrical bangs to elongate the face.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best hair style for round faces:

  • Choose a haircut that creates vertical lines to elongate the face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, middle parts, and heavy bangs that make the face appear rounder.
  • Add texture or curls to create more height and dimension.
  • Style your hair with a side part or asymmetrical bangs to elongate the face.
  • Use volumizing products to add volume to the crown.

FAQ

What is the best length for round faces?

The best length for round faces is a medium to long length that falls below the chin. This creates vertical lines that elongate the face and adds volume to the crown.

Should I get bangs for my round face?

It is recommended to avoid heavy bangs that make the face appear rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept or asymmetrical bangs that elongate the face.

What is the best hair color for round faces?

The best hair color for round faces is one that creates dimension and depth. Highlights, balayage, and ombre are great options to add texture and dimension to the hair.

Hair Care

To maintain the best hair style for round faces, it is important to take care of your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the crown. Avoid using heavy styling products that weigh down the hair. Use a round brush when blow drying to add volume and create vertical lines. Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the best hair style for round faces that enhances your facial features and makes you look stunning. So go ahead, try out a new hairstyle and show off your beautiful face!
