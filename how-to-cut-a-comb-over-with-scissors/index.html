How To Cut A Comb Over With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to achieve a stylish and modern look with your hair? A comb over might be just what you need! The comb over is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades and has recently made a comeback in the world of men's grooming. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting a comb over with scissors.

How to Cut a Comb Over with Scissors

Cutting a comb over with scissors might seem intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, it can be quite simple. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a comb, and hair clippers (optional).

Step 2: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start cutting. This will make it easier to see the natural texture and shape of your hair.

Step 3: Create a Side Part

Create a side part on the side of your head where you want the comb over to be. Use the comb to create a clean, straight line.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

Using the hair clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of your hair to your desired length. The length will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your head.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Using the scissors, cut the hair on the top of your head to your desired length. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, cutting small sections at a time.

Step 6: Blend the Sides and Top

Using the scissors, blend the sides and top of your hair together. Make sure there are no blunt lines or uneven sections, as this will create a natural and seamless look.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Using a comb, style your hair into a comb over. Start at the side part and comb the hair over to the other side. Use a small amount of hair product, such as pomade or wax, to hold the hair in place.

Tips for Cutting a Comb Over with Scissors

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect comb over:

  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time to avoid mistakes.
  • Use the comb to guide your scissors and create a clean, straight cut.
  • Keep your scissors sharp to avoid pulling or damaging your hair.
  • Use hair clippers to create a fade or taper on the sides and back.

FAQ

Can I cut a comb over with clippers?

Yes, you can use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair for a comb over. However, using scissors to blend the sides and top together will create a more natural and seamless look.

What length should I cut my hair for a comb over?

The length of your hair will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your head. Generally, the sides and back of your hair should be shorter than the hair on the top of your head.

Do I need to use hair product to style a comb over?

Using hair product, such as pomade or wax, can help hold the hair in place and create a more polished look. However, it is not necessary if you prefer a more natural and relaxed look.

Hair Care for a Comb Over

To keep your comb over looking its best, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can damage the hair and cause split ends.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and style.
  • Use hair products sparingly to avoid buildup and damage to your hair.

Cutting a comb over with scissors can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and technique, you can achieve a stylish and modern look that is perfect for any occasion.
