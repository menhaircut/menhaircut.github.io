Good Hairstyles For Long Faces Female Flatter Your Features With These Haircuts

Written by Eveline Sep 21, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a long face shape, you may feel that your features are elongated and need some balance. Fortunately, there are many good hairstyles for long faces female that can help you flatter your shape and highlight your best features. From short bobs to long layers, there are plenty of options to choose from depending on your hair type, personal style, and face shape. In this article, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for long faces female and provide tips on how to maintain and style them.

Description

A long face shape is characterized by a high forehead, long chin, and slender jawline. The goal of a good hairstyle for this face shape is to create width and volume around the cheeks and temples, which can help balance out the elongated features. You can achieve this by adding layers, bangs, or texture to your hair. The key is to avoid styles that make your face look even longer, such as straight, flat hair that falls below your shoulders.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for long faces female, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for shorter styles that add volume and lift. If you have thick hair, you can go for longer styles that show off your natural texture. Here are some good hairstyles for long faces female that you can try:

Short Bob

A short bob is a classic haircut that looks great on women with long faces. This style features blunt or layered ends that fall just above the chin, creating width around the face. You can add bangs or side-swept fringe to soften your features and draw attention to your eyes. This style works best on straight or wavy hair.

Shoulder-Length Lob

A shoulder-length lob is a versatile haircut that can suit different face shapes, including long faces. This style features long layers that start at the collarbone and frame the face, creating movement and volume. You can add texture or waves to your hair to enhance the effect. This style works best on medium to thick hair.

Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut is a daring yet feminine haircut that can flatter long faces. This style features short layers that add volume and texture to your hair, creating a playful and edgy look. You can style your hair with a side part or messy fringe to add dimension. This style works best on fine hair.

Step by Step

Once you've chosen a hairstyle for long faces female, it's important to know how to maintain and style it. Here are some tips on how to do that:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help you achieve the look you want and keep your hair healthy. For example, if you have fine hair, you may want to use volumizing mousse or dry shampoo to add texture and lift. If you have thick hair, you may want to use a smoothing serum or oil to tame frizz and add shine.

2. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can help you create volume and movement, especially if you have straight or fine hair. To do this, use a round brush to lift your roots and dry your hair in sections. You can finish with a blast of cool air to set your style and add shine.

3. Experiment with Accessories

Add some flair to your hairstyle by experimenting with accessories such as headbands, hair clips, or scarves. These can help you add color, texture, or interest to your hair and make your style stand out.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for long faces female:

1. Avoid Center Parts

Center parts can make your face look longer and more narrow, which is not ideal for long faces. Instead, opt for side parts or asymmetrical styles that create width and balance.

2. Consider Bangs

Bangs can help you draw attention to your eyes and forehead, which can balance out your long face. However, you should choose the type of bangs that suit your face shape and hair type. For example, blunt bangs work best on straight hair, while side-swept bangs work best on wavy or curly hair.

3. Consult a Hairstylist

Consulting a hairstylist can help you choose the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. They can also provide tips on how to maintain and style your hair at home.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about good hairstyles for long faces female:

1. What is the best haircut for a long face?

The best haircut for a long face is one that adds width and volume around the cheeks and temples. Some good options include short bobs, shoulder-length lobs, and layered pixie cuts.

2. Can women with long faces have long hair?

Yes, women with long faces can have long hair, but they should avoid styles that make their face look even longer, such as straight, flat hair that falls below their shoulders. Instead, they should opt for styles that add volume and movement, such as layers or waves.

3. How do I style my hair if I have a long face?

If you have a long face, you can style your hair by adding volume, texture, or movement using hair products, blow-drying, or accessories. You can also experiment with different hairstyles and cuts to find the one that flatters your face shape and personal style.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important regardless of your face shape. Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that suits your hair type and scalp. Avoid using harsh chemicals or sulfates that can damage your hair and strip it of its natural oils.

2. Condition Regularly

Use a conditioner that nourishes your hair and helps detangle it. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair

Avoid using heat styling tools too often or at high temperatures that can damage your hair. You can also protect your hair from the sun, wind, or chlorine using a hat, scarf, or hair serum.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape, you can enhance your features and feel confident in your appearance. Remember to consult a hairstylist if you're unsure about which haircut to choose or how to style your hair.
