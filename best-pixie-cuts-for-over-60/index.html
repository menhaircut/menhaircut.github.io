Best Pixie Cuts For Over 60 A Guide For Effortless Style

Written by William Jul 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a refreshing new hairstyle, a pixie cut might be just what you need. Pixie cuts are low-maintenance, easy to style, and can make you look years younger. But with so many variations to choose from, it can be challenging to find the right one for you. In this post, we'll discuss the best pixie cuts for over 60, how to style them, and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Description

Pixie cuts are short hairstyles that typically feature cropped sides and back with longer hair on top. They can range from super short and spiky to longer and layered. Pixie cuts are popular among women of all ages but are especially popular among older women due to their ease of maintenance and youthful appearance.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Cut

Choosing the right pixie cut can be challenging, especially if you're not used to short hair. Here are some things to consider when choosing a pixie cut:

Face Shape

The first thing you should consider when choosing a pixie cut is your face shape. Different styles of pixie cuts look better on different face shapes. For example, a pixie cut with longer, side-swept bangs can balance out a round face, while a shorter, spikier cut can complement a square face.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in which pixie cut will work best for you. If you have thin hair, a textured pixie cut with lots of layers can add volume and dimension. If you have thick hair, a shorter, more structured cut can help tame your hair and make it easier to style.

Lifestyle

Finally, consider your lifestyle when choosing a pixie cut. If you're someone who likes to keep things simple, a low-maintenance pixie cut that requires minimal styling might be best for you. If you're someone who likes to experiment with different styles, a longer, more versatile pixie cut might be a better fit.

Step by Step: How to Style Pixie Cuts for Over 60

Styling a pixie cut is relatively straightforward, but there are a few things to keep in mind to ensure that your hair looks its best. Here's how to style a pixie cut:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a towel to blot your hair dry, but don't rub it, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as a mousse or styling cream. This will help create texture and hold your style in place.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer and a round brush to style your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots, which will help create volume.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to define your style. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create texture, or use a comb or brush to smooth it down.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Maintaining healthy hair is essential, no matter what your age. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

Moisturize Your Hair

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often, as this can damage your hair. If you do use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your hair and skin hydrated.

FAQs

Q: Will a pixie cut make me look older?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually make you look younger, as it can create a more youthful, playful appearance.

Q: Can I still style my hair if I have a pixie cut?

A: Yes, there are many ways to style a pixie cut, from adding texture to slicking it back.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a pixie cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with pixie cuts can get away with washing their hair every few days, while others may need to wash it daily.

Hair Care: Conclusion

Choosing a pixie cut can be a great way to refresh your look and embrace your natural beauty. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect pixie cut for you. And by following these styling and maintenance tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant for years to come.
