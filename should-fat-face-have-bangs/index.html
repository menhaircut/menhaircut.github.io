Should Fat Faces Have Bangs

Description

Are you wondering whether or not you should get bangs for your fat face? The answer is not as simple as yes or no. It ultimately depends on the shape of your face, the type of hair you have, and your personal style preferences. However, there are a few general guidelines you can follow to determine if bangs are right for you.

How to Determine if Bangs Are Right for You

The first step is to identify your face shape. If you have a round or oval face, bangs can help balance out the proportions of your face and create a more angular look. However, if you have a square or heart-shaped face, bangs may not be the best choice as they can accentuate the wider parts of your face.

Next, consider the thickness and texture of your hair. If you have thin hair, bangs can help create the illusion of more volume and fullness. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, bangs can add more weight to your hair and make it harder to manage.

Finally, think about your personal style preferences. Bangs can be a great way to change up your look and add some personality to your hairstyle. However, if you prefer a more low-maintenance hairstyle, bangs may not be the best choice.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Cut Bangs for a Fat Face

If you have decided that bangs are right for you, here is a step-by-step guide on how to cut them:

  1. Start with dry hair and section off the hair you want to cut into bangs.
  2. Use sharp hair cutting scissors to trim the hair to your desired length.
  3. Remember to cut a little bit at a time to avoid cutting too much off.
  4. Once you have your desired length, use the scissors to cut into the hair at an angle to create a softer, more natural look.
  5. Blend the bangs into the rest of your hair by using thinning shears to remove any bulky sections.

Tips for Styling Bangs for a Fat Face

Here are a few tips for styling your bangs:

  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs in the direction you want them to go.
  • If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.
  • Use a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your bangs.

FAQs about Bangs for a Fat Face

Q: Will bangs make my face look slimmer?

A: Bangs can help balance out the proportions of a round or oval face, creating a more angular look. However, they may not necessarily make your face look slimmer.

Q: Can I wear side-swept bangs with a fat face?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great option for a fat face as they can help create a more elongated look.

Q: Should I cut my bangs myself or go to a professional?

A: It is always best to go to a professional hairstylist to ensure that your bangs are cut evenly and to your desired length.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Bangs for a Fat Face

Here are a few tips for maintaining your bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at your desired length.
  • Use dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh and oil-free.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from getting greasy.

In conclusion, whether or not you should get bangs for your fat face ultimately depends on a variety of factors. By following these guidelines and tips, you can determine if bangs are right for you and how to style and maintain them.
