How To Straighten Wavy Hair Naturally At Home

Written by Robby Jul 14, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have wavy hair and you want to straighten it without using any harsh chemicals, you’re in the right place. In this article, we’ll show you how to straighten wavy hair naturally at home using simple ingredients that you can find in your kitchen. We’ll also give you some tips on how to take care of your hair after straightening it. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Straighten Wavy Hair Naturally at Home
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Straightening Wavy Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Straightening

How to Straighten Wavy Hair Naturally at Home

Straightening wavy hair naturally at home requires a few ingredients that you probably already have in your kitchen. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Coconut Milk
  • Lemon Juice
  • Cornstarch
  • Olive Oil
  • Shower Cap

Why These Ingredients?

Coconut milk contains proteins that help straighten and strengthen your hair. Lemon juice has acidic properties that help smooth out frizz and add shine to your hair. Cornstarch helps absorb excess oil and gives your hair a silky texture. Olive oil is a natural conditioner that helps keep your hair healthy.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to straighten wavy hair naturally at home:

  1. Mix 1 cup of coconut milk with 2 tablespoons of lemon juice and 2 tablespoons of cornstarch in a bowl.
  2. Heat the mixture on low heat for 5-10 minutes until it thickens.
  3. Add 2 tablespoons of olive oil to the mixture and mix well.
  4. Let the mixture cool down to room temperature.
  5. Apply the mixture to your hair, starting from the roots to the tips.
  6. Wear a shower cap and leave the mixture on your hair for 2-3 hours.
  7. Rinse your hair with water and shampoo as usual.
  8. Repeat this process once a week for best results.

Tips for Straightening Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when straightening wavy hair naturally at home:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying the mixture.
  • Wear an old t-shirt or a towel to avoid getting the mixture on your clothes.
  • Use a shower cap to keep the mixture from dripping onto your face or neck.
  • Wrap your hair in a towel or a cotton t-shirt to help absorb excess moisture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools after straightening your hair naturally to prevent damage.

FAQ

Can I Straighten Wavy Hair Naturally at Home?

Yes, you can straighten wavy hair naturally at home using simple ingredients that you can find in your kitchen.

Is it Safe to Straighten Wavy Hair Naturally?

Yes, it is safe to straighten wavy hair naturally at home as long as you use natural ingredients and avoid using harsh chemicals.

How Long Does it Take to Straighten Wavy Hair Naturally?

It takes about 2-3 hours to straighten wavy hair naturally at home using the coconut milk and lemon juice mixture.

How Often Should I Straighten Wavy Hair Naturally?

You should straighten wavy hair naturally once a week for best results.

Hair Care After Straightening

To keep your hair healthy and shiny after straightening it naturally at home, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools and let your hair air dry whenever possible.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair serum to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these simple steps, you can straighten wavy hair naturally at home without using any harsh chemicals. Remember to take care of your hair after straightening it to keep it healthy and shiny. Happy straightening!
