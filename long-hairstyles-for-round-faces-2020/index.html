Long Hairstyles For Round Faces 2020 Tips And Tricks

If you have a round face, you might have heard that long hairstyles are not for you. But don't worry, that myth is outdated. There are plenty of long hairstyles that flatter round faces and make you look fantastic. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks to choose the perfect long hairstyle for your round face shape in 2020.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Style Long Hair for a Round Face

How to Choose the Perfect Long Hairstyle for a Round Face

When choosing a long hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to elongate the face and create the illusion of more angles. Here are some tips to achieve this:

  • Go for layers: Layers add movement and dimension to your hair, which can make your face appear longer. Long layers that start at the chin or collarbone are a great option.
  • Avoid blunt cuts: Blunt cuts can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for soft, feathered ends that create a more tapered look.
  • Side-swept bangs: Side-swept bangs can add more angles to your face and make it appear longer. Avoid straight or blunt-cut bangs.
  • Keep it straight: Straight hair can elongate your face, while curly or wavy hair can make it appear rounder. If you have naturally curly hair, consider straightening it.
  • Consider highlights: Highlights can add dimension and depth to your hair, which can make your face appear longer.

Long Hairstyles for Round Faces in 2020

Here are some long hairstyles that are perfect for round faces in 2020:

  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Long bob with angled layers
  • Long hair with face-framing layers
  • Half-up half-down hairstyle with loose waves
  • Low ponytail with face-framing tendrils

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Long Hair for a Round Face

Now that you have chosen your perfect long hairstyle, here's how to style it:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that suit your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant if you plan to use heat styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and movement. Use a nozzle attachment to direct the airflow down the hair shaft.
  4. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a straightening iron to straighten your hair. You can also use a curling iron to create loose waves.
  5. Add some hairspray or texturizing spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hairstyles for Round Faces

Here are some tips to keep your long hairstyle looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner before swimming.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

FAQ about Long Hairstyles for Round Faces

1. Can I wear my hair in a center part if I have a round face?

A center part can make your face appear rounder, so it's best to avoid it. Instead, opt for a side part or a deep side part to create more angles.

2. Can I wear my hair in a high ponytail?

A high ponytail can make your face appear rounder, so it's best to avoid it. Instead, opt for a low ponytail or a half-up half-down hairstyle.

3. Can I wear my hair in a bun?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a bun as long as you create some volume at the crown of your head to elongate your face. Avoid tight buns that hug your head.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair and Round Faces

Long hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair no more than three times a week to prevent dryness.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as it can damage the hair cuticle.
  • Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using elastics with metal parts, as they can damage your hair. Opt for fabric or silicone elastics instead.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a long hairstyle with confidence, no matter your face shape. Remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel comfortable and confident, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
