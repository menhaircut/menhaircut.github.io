Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 50 With Glasses A Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Description

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round face and complements your glasses? As we age, our facial features tend to soften, and a round face shape becomes more apparent. However, that doesn't mean we can't rock a stylish and flattering haircut that embraces our unique features. In this article, we'll share some of the best hairstyles for round faces over 50 with glasses, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

How To

Before we dive into specific hairstyles, let's talk about some general guidelines to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round face with glasses: - Opt for a layered haircut that adds volume and movement to your hair. - Avoid blunt cuts that emphasize the roundness of your face. - Consider adding side-swept bangs, which can help elongate your face and draw attention away from the roundness. - Experiment with different parting styles to see which one flatters your face shape the most. - Don't be afraid to try different lengths, from short pixie cuts to longer bobs.

Step by Step

1. Start by consulting with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape and glasses. 2. If you have thick hair, consider getting a layered haircut to add movement and texture. 3. For thin hair, try a short pixie cut to add volume and shape. 4. If you have bangs, consider cutting them in a side-swept style to elongate your face. 5. Play around with different parting styles to see which one flatters your face the most. 6. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair. 7. Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair. 8. Use a round brush when blow-drying your hair to add volume and shape. 9. Finish off your hairstyle with a hairspray or styling cream to hold the style in place.

Tips

- Consider getting glasses with a thinner frame or no frame at all to avoid overpowering your hairstyle. - Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful. - Invest in quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and shiny. - If you have gray hair, embrace it! Gray hair can be beautiful and chic, and it adds a unique touch to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my glasses with a short haircut?
A: Absolutely! Short haircuts can look great with glasses, especially if you opt for a side-swept bang or a layered style. Q: What if I have curly hair?
A: Curly hair can be a bit trickier to style, but a layered haircut can help add shape and volume to your curls. Consider getting a haircut that's slightly longer in the back and shorter in the front to avoid adding too much volume to the sides of your face. Q: How often should I get a haircut?
A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, getting a haircut every 6-8 weeks can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips: - Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils. - Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair. - Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to minimize damage. - Get regular trims to prevent split ends from traveling up your hair shaft. - Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

Conclusion

Finding the perfect hairstyle for a round face with glasses can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right haircut and styling techniques, it's definitely achievable. Remember to consult with your hairstylist, experiment with different styles, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking a stylish and flattering haircut in no time!
