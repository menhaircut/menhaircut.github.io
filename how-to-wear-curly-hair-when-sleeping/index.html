How To Wear Curly Hair When Sleeping A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever woken up with a frizzy mess of curls after a night's sleep? It can be frustrating to spend a lot of time styling your hair only to have it ruined while you sleep. Fortunately, there are ways to wear curly hair when sleeping that will help maintain your curls and prevent frizz. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to wear curly hair when sleeping.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Wear Curly Hair When Sleeping
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Wearing Curly Hair When Sleeping

How to Wear Curly Hair When Sleeping

There are several ways to wear curly hair when sleeping, but the key is to protect your curls and prevent them from becoming tangled or frizzy. Here are some options:

  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase
  • Wear a satin bonnet or scarf
  • Pineapple your hair
  • Braid your hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Let's take a closer look at each of these options and how to wear curly hair when sleeping:

Sleep on a Satin Pillowcase

A satin pillowcase can help reduce friction between your hair and the pillow, which can prevent tangles and frizz. Here's how to use it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Sleep on a satin pillowcase.

That's it! This option is simple and easy.

Wear a Satin Bonnet or Scarf

A satin bonnet or scarf can also help protect your curls while you sleep. Here's how to use it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Put on a satin bonnet or scarf, making sure your curls are tucked inside.
  3. Adjust the bonnet or scarf as needed to ensure it stays in place throughout the night.

This option may take a little more effort, but it can be worth it if you're looking for extra protection for your curls.

Pineapple Your Hair

Pineappling your hair involves pulling your curls up into a loose ponytail at the top of your head. Here's how to do it:

  1. Flip your head forward and gather your curls at the top of your head.
  2. Tie your hair with a scrunchie or hair tie, making sure it's loose enough to prevent any kinks or dents.

This option is great for those with longer hair, as it keeps curls from getting flattened while you sleep.

Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair can also help prevent tangles and frizz. Here's how to do it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Create one or more loose braids, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.
  3. Secure the ends of the braids with a hair tie or scrunchie.

This option can create waves or loose curls, depending on the type of braid you use.

Tips for Wearing Curly Hair When Sleeping

Here are some additional tips to help you wear curly hair when sleeping:

  • Avoid using cotton pillowcases, as they can cause friction and lead to tangles and frizz.
  • Avoid using hair ties or scrunchies with metal parts, as they can snag and damage your curls.
  • If you're using a satin bonnet or scarf, make sure it's not too tight, as this can cause headaches or discomfort.
  • If you're braiding your hair, try to keep the braids loose to prevent any kinks or dents.

FAQs

How often should I wear a satin bonnet or scarf?

You can wear a satin bonnet or scarf every night if you'd like. It's a great way to protect your curls while you sleep.

What type of satin should I use?

Look for satin that's smooth and doesn't have any rough or textured areas. This will help reduce friction and prevent tangles and frizz.

Can I use a silk pillowcase instead of satin?

Silk can also be a good option for protecting your curls while you sleep. However, satin is typically more affordable and easier to care for.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy, bouncy curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, rather than a brush.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips, you can keep your curls looking their best, even while you sleep!
