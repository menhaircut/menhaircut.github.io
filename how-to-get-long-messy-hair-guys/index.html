How To Get Long Messy Hair For Guys In 2023

Written by Bardi Oct 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Long messy hair is a popular hairstyle for guys in 2023. It’s a style that’s easy to maintain and gives off a relaxed, carefree vibe. If you’re looking to achieve this look, then you’ve come to the right place. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get long messy hair for guys.

How to

Step 1: Grow out your hair

The first step to getting long messy hair is to grow out your hair. This can take some time, so be patient. It’s recommended to avoid getting haircuts for at least a few months to let your hair grow out.

Step 2: Get regular trims

While you’re growing out your hair, it’s important to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy. Split ends can cause your hair to look frizzy and unkempt, so trim them every few months.

Step 3: Use the right products

To achieve the messy look, you’ll need to use the right products. A sea salt spray can help give your hair texture and volume. Apply it to damp hair and scrunch it up with your hands.

Step 4: Style your hair

To style your hair, use your hands to tousle it around. You can also use a hair dryer on a low setting to help enhance the messy look. Remember, the goal is to look effortless and carefree.

Tips

  • Try different hair products to find what works best for you
  • Get inspiration from celebrities or social media influencers
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a side part or a messy bun
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice or tips

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz and breakage.

Can I still get long messy hair if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Long messy hair works well with all hair types, including curly hair. Just make sure to use products that enhance your natural curls.

Do I need to use a lot of product to achieve the messy look?

No, you don’t need to use a lot of product. A little bit goes a long way. Start with a small amount and add more if needed.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy and looks its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as straighteners or curling irons
  • Eat a healthy diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you’ll be able to achieve long messy hair that looks great and is healthy.

Conclusion

Long messy hair is a great hairstyle for guys in 2023. By following these steps and tips, you’ll be able to achieve the look you want. Remember to be patient, experiment with different styles, and take care of your hair to ensure it stays healthy.
