How To Make Your Hair Slicked Back In A Ponytail

Written by Luffy Oct 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are looking for a way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking chic, a slicked-back ponytail is the way to go. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion, from a day at the office to a night out with friends. In this article, we will show you how to make your hair slicked back in a ponytail, step-by-step.

Description

A slicked-back ponytail is a hairstyle where the hair is pulled back tightly into a ponytail and smoothed down to create a sleek look. This hairstyle is perfect for those with medium to long hair and is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking stylish.

How to

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair slicked back in a ponytail:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

It is important to start with clean, dry hair to ensure that your hair stays in place. If your hair is oily or wet, it will be more difficult to achieve a slicked-back look.

Step 2: Apply a styling gel or wax

To achieve a slicked-back look, you will need to apply a styling gel or wax to your hair. This will help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from frizzing up.

Step 3: Brush your hair back

Using a brush, brush your hair back, starting at the crown of your head. Make sure to brush your hair back tightly to ensure a sleek look.

Step 4: Secure your hair into a ponytail

Once your hair is brushed back, secure it into a ponytail using a hair tie. Make sure to pull your hair back tightly to ensure a sleek look.

Step 5: Smooth down any flyaways

Using a small amount of styling gel or wax, smooth down any flyaways or frizz that may be sticking out from your ponytail.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back ponytail:

  • Use a strong-hold styling gel or wax to ensure that your hair stays in place.
  • Brush your hair back tightly to ensure a sleek look.
  • Use a hair tie that matches the color of your hair to create a seamless look.

FAQ

How long will a slicked-back ponytail last?

A slicked-back ponytail should last all day if done correctly. However, if you have particularly thick or curly hair, you may need to touch up your hair throughout the day to ensure that it stays in place.

Can I wear a slicked-back ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, a slicked-back ponytail is a great hairstyle for a formal event. It is sleek and sophisticated and will keep your hair out of your face all night.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking sleek and shiny, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair slicked back in a ponytail, you can rock this chic and stylish hairstyle anytime, anywhere.
