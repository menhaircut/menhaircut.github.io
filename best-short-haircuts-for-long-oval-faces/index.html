Best Short Haircuts For Long Oval Faces

Are you looking for the best short haircuts for long oval faces? Look no further! This guide will provide you with a variety of styles to choose from that will flatter your face shape and leave you feeling confident and stylish.

Description

Long oval faces are characterized by a lengthened shape with a slightly wider forehead, narrow jawline, and high cheekbones. When choosing a short haircut for this face shape, it's important to find a style that balances out the proportions of the face and enhances its natural beauty.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Long Oval Faces

Choosing the right short haircut for your long oval face can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

  • Consider your hair type: Fine hair, thick hair, and curly hair all have different needs when it comes to short haircuts.
  • Find a style that adds volume to the sides of your face: This will help to balance out the length of your face.
  • Avoid styles that are too long or too short: Haircuts that fall below the chin or above the ears can make a long face appear even longer.
  • Consider bangs: Bangs can help to shorten the appearance of a long face and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Choose a style that complements your personal style and lifestyle: Your short haircut should be easy to maintain and make you feel confident.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving the Perfect Short Haircut

Now that you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your long oval face, it's time to achieve the look. Follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and comb out any tangles.
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume.
  5. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Maintaining your short haircut is essential to keeping it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims: Short haircuts require frequent trims to maintain their shape.
  • Use a dry shampoo: Washing your hair every day can strip it of its natural oils. Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your hair looking clean and fresh.
  • Invest in quality styling tools: Using high-quality tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes! There are plenty of ways to style short hair, including ponytails, braids, and updos.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look wider?

A: No, a well-chosen short haircut can actually help to balance out the proportions of your face.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require specific care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage and breakage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Now that you know how to choose the best short haircut for long oval faces, you can confidently rock a stylish new look. With the right care and maintenance, your short haircut will look great every day.
