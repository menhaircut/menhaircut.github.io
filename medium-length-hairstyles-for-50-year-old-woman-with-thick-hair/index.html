Medium Length Hairstyles For 50 Year Old Woman With Thick Hair

Written by Christine Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are a 50 year old woman with thick hair, you may be looking for a hairstyle that is both stylish and manageable. Medium length hairstyles are a great option for women with thick hair, allowing you to showcase your beautiful locks while still keeping them under control. In this article, we will explore some of the best medium length hairstyles for 50 year old woman with thick hair, as well as provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Medium length hairstyles are a great option for women who want to keep their hair at a manageable length without sacrificing style. For women with thick hair, medium length hairstyles can help to reduce bulk and create a more streamlined look. These hairstyles can also be easily styled and are versatile enough to work for both casual and formal occasions.

How to

If you are interested in trying out a medium length hairstyle, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a style. Some styles may work better for round faces, while others may be more flattering for square or heart-shaped faces. Similarly, some styles may work better for fine hair, while others may be better suited for thick hair.

Once you have chosen a style, you will need to work with your stylist to achieve the desired look. This may involve cutting layers into your hair, adding highlights or lowlights, or creating a more textured look. Your stylist can also provide tips on how to style your hair at home, including which products to use and how to use them.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a medium length hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Use a comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  3. Section your hair into manageable sections.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture to your hair.
  5. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair.
  6. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair, focusing on the roots.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use a round brush to add volume and shape.
  8. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product to hold your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for caring for your medium length hairstyle:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hairstyles for 50 year old woman with thick hair:

What are the best medium length hairstyles for thick hair?

Some of the best medium length hairstyles for thick hair include layered cuts, blunt cuts, and shaggy styles. These styles can help to reduce bulk and create a more manageable look.

How do I style my medium length hair?

You can style your medium length hair in a variety of ways, including wearing it straight, adding curls, or creating a messy, textured look. Your stylist can provide tips on how to achieve the desired look.

What products should I use on my medium length hair?

The products you use on your medium length hair will depend on your hair type and the style you are trying to achieve. Some products to consider include volumizing mousse, hairspray, and heat protectant spray.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair is important if you want to maintain a healthy, beautiful look. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your hair.

Overall, medium length hairstyles are a great option for 50 year old woman with thick hair. Whether you prefer a sleek, straight look or a more textured, messy style, there are plenty of options to choose from. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can achieve a beautiful, manageable hairstyle that makes you feel confident and stylish.
