Short Hairstyles For Fat Faces And Double Chins Black Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and hides your double chin? Look no further than these short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins black hair. In this article, we will guide you through the best hairstyles, tips, and hair care for a stunning look.

Description

Having a round or chubby face with a double chin can be a challenge to finding the perfect hairstyle. However, with the right hairstyle, you can enhance your beauty and confidence. Short hairstyles are a great option as they can add volume to your hair, elongate your face, and draw attention away from your double chin.

How to

Before you head to the hair salon, it is crucial to identify your face shape and hair type. You can do this by looking at yourself in the mirror and using a measuring tape to measure the length and width of your face. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a suitable hairstyle that flatters your features.

There are various short hairstyles that you can choose from, including pixie cuts, bobs, and shags. Pixie cuts are perfect for a bold and edgy look, while bobs are great for adding volume and framing your face. Shags are ideal for a messy and effortless style that can hide your double chin.

Step by step

Here are the steps to achieve a beautiful short hairstyle:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a suitable shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.
  3. Use a straightener or curling iron to style your hair according to your preferred short hairstyle.
  4. Apply hair serum or pomade to add shine and hold.
  5. Finish off with hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to maintain your short hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your hairstyle fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Choose hair products that are suitable for your hair type and hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to add variety to your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins black hair:

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin?

The best short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin is a bob or a shag. These hairstyles can elongate your face and draw attention away from your double chin.

Can I still have bangs with a short hairstyle?

Yes, you can still have bangs with a short hairstyle. However, it is essential to choose a suitable bang style that flatters your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, it is crucial to use products that are suitable for your hair type and hairstyle. For short hairstyles, it is recommended to use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair. Additionally, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can nourish your hair and prevent damage.

Overall, short hairstyles for fat faces and double chins black hair can be stunning and flattering when done correctly. By following these tips and hair care routine, you can achieve a beautiful and confident look.
