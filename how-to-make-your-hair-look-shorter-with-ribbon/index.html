How To Make Your Hair Look Shorter With Ribbon

Written by Oliver Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who struggles with long hair, you may be looking for ways to change it up. One way to do this is by using ribbon to make your hair look shorter. It's a simple and easy way to achieve a new look without having to cut your hair. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to make your hair look shorter with ribbon.

Description

If you're tired of your long hair and want to try something new, using ribbon can be a great alternative to cutting your hair. Ribbon is a versatile accessory that can be used in many ways, including making your hair appear shorter. By following these steps, you can achieve a new look without having to commit to a haircut.

How to

Here's how to make your hair look shorter with ribbon:

Step 1: Choose the Right Ribbon

The first step is to choose the right ribbon. You'll want to choose a ribbon that is sturdy enough to hold your hair in place but not too thick that it creates too much bulk. You can choose any color or pattern that you like, but it's best to choose a color that complements your hair color.

Step 2: Brush Your Hair

Once you have your ribbon, brush your hair to remove any tangles or knots. It's important to start with clean, dry hair to ensure that the ribbon stays in place.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair in a Ponytail

Next, tie your hair in a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Make sure it's not too tight, as you don't want to create any tension on your scalp.

Step 4: Wrap the Ribbon Around the Ponytail

Take your ribbon and wrap it around the base of your ponytail. Tie it in a knot or bow, depending on the length of your ribbon. You can also leave the ends of the ribbon loose for a more relaxed look.

Step 5: Tuck in Loose Ends

If there are any loose ends, tuck them in under the ribbon. This will help keep the ribbon in place and prevent it from slipping out throughout the day.

Step 6: Fluff Your Hair

Finally, fluff your hair to create the illusion of shorter hair. You can use your fingers or a comb to tease your hair and give it more volume.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when using ribbon to make your hair look shorter:

  • Choose a ribbon that complements your hair color.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before starting.
  • Don't tie your ponytail too tight, as this can cause tension on your scalp.
  • Fluff your hair after tying the ribbon to create the illusion of shorter hair.

FAQ

Can I use any type of ribbon?

Yes, you can use any type of ribbon that you like. However, it's best to choose a ribbon that is sturdy enough to hold your hair in place but not too thick that it creates too much bulk.

Will this damage my hair?

No, this method will not damage your hair. In fact, it's a great alternative to cutting your hair if you're looking for a new look.

How long will the ribbon stay in my hair?

The ribbon should stay in your hair for the entire day. However, if you notice it slipping out, you can always retie it to ensure that it stays in place.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair even when using ribbon to make it look shorter. This includes regular washing, conditioning, and avoiding heat styling tools. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle wet hair and avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes that can damage your hair.

Using ribbon to make your hair look shorter is a simple and easy way to achieve a new look without having to commit to a haircut. By following these steps and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy a new style and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
