Best Haircut For Round Face With Glasses

Written by William Nov 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for round face with glasses. If you have a round face and wear glasses, then choosing the right haircut can be challenging. But don't worry, we've got you covered. Read on to find out how to choose the best haircut for your round face with glasses.

Description

A round face has soft features and a circular shape. People with round faces often have a wider forehead, a prominent cheekbone, and a round chin. If you wear glasses, you need to choose a haircut that complements your face shape and doesn't clash with your frames. The right haircut can make your face look more balanced and symmetrical.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Round Face with Glasses

When choosing a haircut for your round face with glasses, there are a few things to consider:

1. Face Shape

The first thing to consider is your face shape. A round face requires a haircut that adds height and length to the face. The right haircut can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

2. Glasses Frames

The second thing to consider is your glasses frames. Your haircut should complement your frames and not clash with them. If you have bold frames, then a simple and understated haircut would be ideal. If you have thin frames, then a more dramatic haircut would work well.

3. Hair Texture

The third thing to consider is your hair texture. Your haircut should work with your hair texture and not against it. If you have curly hair, then a layered haircut would be perfect. If you have straight hair, then a blunt cut would work well.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Best Haircut for Round Face with Glasses

Follow these steps to choose the best haircut for your round face with glasses:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly the same, then you have a round face.

2. Research Haircuts

Research haircuts that work well for round faces. Look for haircuts that add height and length to the face. You can find inspiration on social media platforms like Instagram and Pinterest.

3. Consider Your Glasses Frames

Consider your glasses frames when choosing a haircut. Look for haircuts that complement your frames and don't clash with them. If you have bold frames, then a simple and understated haircut would be ideal. If you have thin frames, then a more dramatic haircut would work well.

4. Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist before getting a haircut. They can recommend the best haircut for your face shape and glasses frames. They can also suggest styling products that will work well with your hair texture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Use hair products that are suitable for your hair texture. If you have curly hair, then use products that enhance your curls. If you have straight hair, then use products that add volume and texture.

2. Schedule Regular Haircuts

Schedule regular haircuts to maintain your haircut's shape and length. This will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. Heat can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. Use heat protectant sprays before using heat styling tools.

FAQs

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face with glasses?

A: The best haircut for a round face with glasses is a layered haircut that adds height and length to the face. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too blunt.

Q: Can I wear bangs with glasses?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with glasses. However, make sure they complement your face shape and glasses frames. Side-swept bangs are a good option for round faces.

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: You should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and length.

Hair Care

Here are some general hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Avoid direct sunlight on your hair. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth. Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

In conclusion, choosing the best haircut for round face with glasses can be challenging, but with the right research and consultation with your hairstylist, you can find the perfect haircut that complements your face shape and glasses frames. Remember to maintain your haircut with regular haircuts and the right hair care routine.
