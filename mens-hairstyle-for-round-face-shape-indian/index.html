Mens Hairstyle For Round Face Shape Indian Tips And Ideas

Written by Christine Jun 15, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face shape, you may have been struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. Fortunately, there are plenty of mens hairstyles for round face shape Indian that can enhance your best qualities and make you look your best. In this article, we’ll provide you with tips, how-tos, and ideas to help you choose the best hairstyle for your round face shape.

Description: What is a Round Face Shape?

A round face shape is characterized by a circular face with full cheeks and a rounded chin. This face shape typically lacks prominent angles and has a soft, youthful appearance. Men with round face shapes often have trouble finding the right hairstyle as they need to create the illusion of length and angles to balance their features.

How to Determine Your Face Shape?

Before we dive into mens hairstyles for round face shape Indian, it’s important to determine your face shape. To do so, follow these simple steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Take a lipstick or a washable marker and trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape you drew. Determine which of the following categories it falls into: oval, square, rectangle, round, heart, or diamond.

Hairstyles for Round Face Shape Indian

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to explore mens hairstyles for round face shape Indian. Here are some ideas to get you started:

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles are a great option for men with round face shapes as they create the illusion of length and angles. Consider a buzz cut or a crew cut that is shorter on the sides and longer on top. This will draw attention to the top of your head and create a more elongated appearance.

Medium Hairstyles

Medium hairstyles can also work well for round face shapes as long as they incorporate some volume and texture. Consider a messy quiff or a textured crop that has layers and movement. This will add height to your face and create the illusion of angles.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles can be a bit tricky for round face shapes as they can make your face appear even rounder. However, if you want to rock long locks, consider a style that is layered and textured. This will create depth and dimension and draw attention away from the roundness of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hairstyle

Once you’ve chosen the best mens hairstyle for round face shape Indian, it’s important to know how to style it properly. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a styling product such as gel, pomade, or mousse.
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair in the desired direction.
  4. Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting to set the style.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or a matte wax to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

To keep your mens hairstyle for round face shape Indian looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as this can damage it.
  • Invest in good quality hair products that suit your hair type and style.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too short on top, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat or slicked back as this can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Can I pull off a side part with a round face shape?

Yes, you can. However, make sure to create some volume on top to balance out the roundness of your face.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair or a less active lifestyle, you may be able to wash your hair every other day.

Conclusion

Choosing the right mens hairstyle for round face shape Indian can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and ideas, you can find a style that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. Remember to determine your face shape, choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of length and angles, and maintain your hair with regular trims and good quality hair products.
