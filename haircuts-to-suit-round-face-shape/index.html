Best Haircuts To Suit Round Face Shape

Written by Eveline Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we all know, the shape of our face plays a crucial role in deciding which haircut would look good on us. One of the most common face shapes is the round face shape, and if you have it, you might be wondering which haircut would suit you the best. Don't worry; we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the best haircuts to suit round face shape. Let's dive in!

  • How to

Description

Before we dive into the haircuts, let's first understand what a round face shape is. A round face shape has a width and length that are almost equal, with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The aim is to create an illusion of length to make the face appear less round. The lengthening effect can be created by adding volume to the crown, keeping the sides shorter, and adding angles to the jawline.

How to choose a haircut for a round face shape?

Choosing a haircut for a round face shape can be challenging, but if you follow these simple tips, it can be a breeze.

1. Determine your face shape

Before you choose a haircut, it's essential to know your face shape. You can do this by measuring the widest part of your face and comparing it to the length of your face. A round face shape has a similar width and length.

2. Look for lengthening haircuts

The aim is to create an illusion of length. Look for haircuts that add volume to the crown, keep the sides shorter, and add angles to the jawline.

3. Avoid haircuts that add width to the face

Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of the face as this will make the face appear wider.

Step by step guide to the best haircuts for round face shape

1. Long Layered Haircut

A long layered haircut is perfect for a round face shape as it creates an illusion of length. The layers add volume to the crown and keep the sides shorter, creating angles to the jawline.

2. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut is a classic and timeless haircut that suits almost every face shape. For a round face shape, choose a bob that is longer in the front and shorter at the back. This creates an illusion of length and angles to the jawline.

3. Pixie Haircut

A pixie haircut is a short and edgy haircut that suits a round face shape. Choose a pixie that is longer in the front and shorter at the back, creating an illusion of length.

4. Side Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for a round face shape as they create angles to the jawline. Avoid blunt bangs as they add width to the face.

Tips to maintain your haircut

1. Regular trims

Regular trims are essential to maintain the shape of your haircut. Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut in shape.

2. Use the right products

Use the right products that suit your hair type and maintain the texture of your haircut.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools.

FAQs

Q. Can I wear a short haircut with a round face shape?

A. Yes, you can wear a short haircut with a round face shape. Choose a short haircut that is longer in the front and shorter at the back, creating an illusion of length.

Q. Should I avoid bangs with a round face shape?

A. No, you don't have to avoid bangs with a round face shape. Side-swept bangs are perfect for a round face shape as they create angles to the jawline.

Hair Care for Round Face Shape

1. Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and maintain the texture of your haircut.

2. Hair Oil

Use hair oil to nourish your hair and scalp. Massage your scalp with hair oil once a week for healthy hair.

3. Protect from Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.

In conclusion, the best haircuts to suit round face shape are long layered haircut, bob haircut, pixie haircut, and side-swept bangs. Remember to maintain your haircut by regular trims, using the right products, and protecting your hair from heat damage and the sun.
