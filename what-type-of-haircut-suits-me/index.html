What Type Of Haircut Suits Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Style

Written by Frank Aug 20, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of getting the same haircut every time you visit the salon? Do you want to switch things up and try a new look? Finding the perfect haircut can be challenging, but it's not impossible. In this article, we'll discuss how to determine what type of haircut suits you and provide some tips for maintaining your new style.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right haircut for you is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, you can stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker or lipstick. Alternatively, you can take a picture of your face and use an app or website that can analyze your face shape.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck because almost any haircut will suit you. You can choose from short, long, or medium-length haircuts, and they will all look great on you. However, avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead or eyes, as they can make your face appear longer.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you should choose a haircut that adds height to your face. A layered haircut that falls below your chin will elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make your face look wider.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you should choose a haircut that softens your angular features. A medium-length haircut with layers will soften your jawline and give your face a more feminine look. Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face appear more angular.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart face shape, you should choose a haircut that balances your features. A chin-length bob or a long, layered haircut will complement your face shape. Avoid short haircuts, as they can make your face look top-heavy.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you should choose a haircut that softens your sharp features. A chin-length bob or a shaggy hairstyle with layers will complement your face shape. Avoid haircuts with too much volume, as they can make your face appear wider.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you should choose a haircut that adds width to your face. A chin-length bob or a shoulder-length haircut with layers will complement your face shape. Avoid haircuts that are too short, as they can make your face appear longer.

Step by Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Haircut

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find your perfect haircut. Follow these steps to find the right haircut for you:

  1. Research different hairstyles that suit your face shape
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to determine what haircut will suit you best
  3. Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairstylist
  4. Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on what will suit you best
  5. Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on your hair
  6. Choose a haircut that complements your personality and style

Remember, finding the perfect haircut takes time and experimentation. Don't be afraid to try different styles until you find the one that suits you best.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Haircut

Once you've found your perfect haircut, it's essential to take care of it to maintain its shape and style. Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Use the right hair products for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions
  • Incorporate hair masks and deep conditioning treatments into your hair care routine

By following these tips, you can ensure that your new haircut stays fresh and stylish for longer.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about finding the right haircut:

What if I can't decide on a hairstyle?

If you're having trouble deciding on a hairstyle, consult with a hairstylist. They can provide you with guidance and advice on what will suit you best.

How often should I visit my hairstylist?

You should visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for trims and touch-ups. This will help maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Can I change my hairstyle if I don't like it?

Yes! If you don't like your new hairstyle, you can always change it. Consult with your hairstylist and discuss what changes you want to make.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining the health and appearance of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to prevent tangles and keep your hair soft
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated

By following these tips, you can ensure that your hair stays healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut can be challenging, but by following these tips and consulting with a hairstylist, you can find the perfect style for you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style, and don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.
