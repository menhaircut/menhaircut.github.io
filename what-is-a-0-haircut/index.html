What Is A 0 Haircut

Written by Aprilia Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A 0 haircut is a very short buzz cut that is achieved by using a clipper without any guard attached. This haircut is also known as a bald fade or a skin fade. The 0 refers to the length of hair left on the scalp, which is essentially nothing. This haircut is popular among men of all ages and is a low-maintenance style that requires very little upkeep.

How to Get a 0 Haircut

Getting a 0 haircut is relatively easy, but it does require some skill and the proper tools. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a 0 haircut:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You will need a clipper, a mirror, and a comb to get started. Make sure your clipper is fully charged and has a fresh blade.

Step 2: Start with a Longer Guard

Before you start cutting your hair with no guard at all, begin with a longer guard. This will help you get a feel for the clipper and the length of your hair. Start with a #2 or #3 guard and work your way down to a #1.

Step 3: Remove the Guard

Once you have worked your way down to a #1 guard, it is time to remove the guard completely. This will give you a 0 haircut.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Start at the back of your head and work your way up to the crown. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it with the clipper. Move the clipper in an upward motion to create a fade effect.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you have cut the hair with the clipper, use a comb and scissors to blend the hair. This will create a smoother transition between the shorter hair and the longer hair.

Step 6: Clean Up

After you have finished cutting your hair, use a mirror to check for any stray hairs. Clean up around the ears and the neckline with a trimmer or a razor.

Tips for Maintaining a 0 Haircut

A 0 haircut is a low-maintenance style, but there are a few things you can do to keep it looking its best:

  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the length
  • Keep the scalp moisturized with a good-quality hair oil or moisturizer
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup
  • Use a sunscreen on your scalp to protect it from the sun

FAQ

Is a 0 haircut the same as a buzz cut?

Yes, a 0 haircut is essentially a buzz cut with no guard attached to the clipper.

Can anyone get a 0 haircut?

Most people can get a 0 haircut, but it may not be suitable for everyone. If you have a very irregularly shaped head or scalp, it may be best to stick with a slightly longer haircut.

How often should I get a 0 haircut?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Most people can get away with getting a 0 haircut once every 3-4 weeks.

Hair Care for a 0 Haircut

Even though a 0 haircut is low-maintenance, it is still important to take care of your hair and scalp. Here are a few tips to keep your 0 haircut looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a moisturizer or hair oil to keep the scalp hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers or straighteners
  • Protect your scalp from the sun with a hat or a sunscreen

Conclusion

A 0 haircut is a very short buzz cut that is achieved by using a clipper without any guard attached. It is a popular style among men of all ages and requires very little upkeep. By following the steps outlined above, you can achieve a perfect 0 haircut at home. Remember to take care of your hair and scalp to keep your 0 haircut looking great.
