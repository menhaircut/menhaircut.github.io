Haircuts for Round Fat Faces Over 50

Description

As we age, our faces tend to lose their definition and may become rounder and fuller. It can be a challenge to find the right haircut that flatters your face shape, especially if you have a round fat face. In this article, we will provide tips and ideas for haircuts that are perfect for women over 50 with round fat faces.

How to

Before we dive into specific haircuts, it's important to determine your face shape. Grab a measuring tape and measure the distance from your hairline to your chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If these measurements are roughly the same, then you have a round face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to find the perfect haircut. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Opt for haircuts that create height and lengthen your face.
  • Consider adding layers to add texture and movement to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with bangs, but avoid blunt bangs that cut across your forehead.

Step by Step

Now that you know what to keep in mind, let's explore some haircuts that are perfect for women over 50 with round fat faces:

1. The Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut can add height and elongate your face. Consider adding layers to add texture and movement to your hair. This low-maintenance haircut is perfect for women who want to embrace their natural hair texture and don't want to spend a lot of time styling their hair.

2. The Bob

A classic bob is a timeless haircut that looks great on women of all ages. For women with round fat faces, consider a longer bob that hits just below the chin. You can also add layers to add texture and movement to your hair.

3. The Shag

A shag haircut is a great option for women who want to add texture and movement to their hair. This haircut features layers of varying lengths that frame your face and add volume to the crown of your head.

Tips

When getting a haircut, make sure to communicate with your stylist about your face shape and the look you're trying to achieve. Your stylist can recommend haircuts that will flatter your face shape and suit your lifestyle.

Remember to also take care of your hair by using products that suit your hair type and by getting regular trims to maintain your haircut.

FAQ

What are some hairstyles to avoid for round fat faces over 50?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as blunt bobs or curls that start at chin-level. These hairstyles can make your face look wider and rounder.

Can I still wear bangs with a round fat face?

Absolutely! Bangs can be a great way to add interest to your haircut. Consider side-swept bangs or wispy bangs that frame your face and add texture to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to use products that suit your hair type. If you have thin hair, use volumizing products to add fullness to your hair. If you have thick hair, use smoothing products to tame frizz and add shine.

Also, make sure to get regular trims to maintain your haircut and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
