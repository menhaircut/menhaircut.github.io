Best Men s Haircuts For Thick Straight Hair

Written by Daniel Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse at the same time. While it's great to have a full head of hair, it can sometimes be difficult to manage. Finding the right haircut for thick straight hair can be challenging, as you want a style that will complement your natural texture without being too difficult to maintain. In this article, we'll explore the best men's haircuts for thick straight hair, including how to achieve them and how to care for your hair to keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick straight hair is perfect for creating a variety of hairstyles. Whether you prefer a short, medium, or long hairstyle, there are plenty of options to choose from. Men with thick straight hair can create a polished and sophisticated look, or a more relaxed and casual style. The key is to find a haircut that works with your natural texture and complements your face shape.

How To

The first step in finding the best haircut for your thick straight hair is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and accentuates your best assets. Once you know your face shape, you can start exploring different haircuts and styles that will work for you.

Face Shapes

  • Oval
  • Square
  • Round
  • Heart
  • Diamond

Some of the best haircuts for men with thick straight hair include the classic crew cut, the textured crop, the slicked-back undercut, and the side part. These styles are versatile and easy to maintain, making them perfect for busy men who don't have a lot of time to spend styling their hair.

Step by Step

If you're interested in trying out one of these haircuts, here's how to achieve them:

Classic Crew Cut

  1. Start with a medium-length haircut.
  2. Use clippers to trim the sides and back of your hair to a short length.
  3. Leave the hair on top longer, and use scissors to trim it to your desired length.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product to create texture and hold.

Textured Crop

  1. Ask your barber for a short, textured cut.
  2. Use a small amount of hair product to create texture and hold.
  3. Use your fingers to style your hair in a messy, textured look.

Slicked-Back Undercut

  1. Ask your barber for an undercut with short sides and a longer top.
  2. Apply a small amount of pomade or gel to your hair.
  3. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working your way back.

Side Part

  1. Ask your barber for a medium-length cut with a side part.
  2. Use a comb to create a deep side part.
  3. Apply a small amount of styling product to create hold and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for your thick straight hair:

  • Choose a style that complements your face shape.
  • Consider your hair's natural texture when choosing a style.
  • Choose a style that's easy to maintain and style.
  • Use quality hair products to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about haircuts for men with thick straight hair:

What's the best haircut for thick straight hair?

The best haircut for thick straight hair depends on your individual preferences and face shape. Some popular options include the crew cut, textured crop, slicked-back undercut, and side part.

What's the easiest haircut to maintain?

The easiest haircut to maintain is one that's short and requires minimal styling. The crew cut and buzz cut are both great options for men with thick straight hair who want a low-maintenance style.

How often should I get a haircut?

You should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair looking its best.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your thick straight hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water or harsh chemicals on your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a regular basis.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your thick straight hair, you can create a stylish and low-maintenance look that will make you feel confident and look your best.
