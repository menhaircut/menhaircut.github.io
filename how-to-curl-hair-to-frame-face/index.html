How To Curl Hair To Frame Face A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Curling your hair is a great way to add volume and definition to your locks. But did you know that you can also use curls to frame your face and enhance your features? In this post, we’ll show you how to curl your hair to frame your face, step by step. Whether you have long or short hair, straight or curly, these tips will help you achieve a look that’s both flattering and stylish.

How to Curl Hair to Frame Face

Before we get started, let’s take a look at some of the tools you’ll need:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray
  • Sectioning clips

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use sectioning clips to divide your hair into small sections. Start by sectioning your hair at the crown of your head and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron or wand. Hold the curl for a few seconds and release it. Repeat this process on each section of hair, working your way around your head.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use a brush to gently brush out your curls. This will help to create a more natural-looking wave.

Step 5: Set Your Style

Finish off your style with a spritz of hairspray to help hold your curls in place.

Tips for Curling Hair to Frame Your Face

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Choose the right curling iron or wand for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a smaller barrel. If you have thick hair, use a larger barrel.
  • Don’t curl all of your hair in the same direction. Alternate the direction of your curls to create a more natural-looking wave.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Don’t hold your curls for too long. This can lead to over-curling and damage to your hair.
  • Brush out your curls for a more natural look.

FAQ About Curling Hair to Frame Your Face

Can I curl my hair if it’s already curly?

Absolutely! You can still curl your hair even if it’s already curly. Just make sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

How long will my curls last?

It depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, your curls should last for a few hours to a full day.

Can I curl my hair without a curling iron or wand?

Yes! You can curl your hair using a straightening iron or even with braids. There are plenty of tutorials online to help you achieve the look you want.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your curls:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day. This can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and breakage.

Now that you know how to curl your hair to frame your face, you can experiment with different styles and find the look that works best for you. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and avoiding excessive heat. With a little practice, you’ll be able to create a look that’s both flattering and stylish!
