How To Cut Your Own Hair With Scissors And Comb

Written by Bardi Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on expensive haircuts? Or maybe you just don't have the time to go to the salon? Whatever the reason, cutting your own hair with scissors and a comb is a great way to save money and time. In this article, we'll go over the steps you need to take to cut your own hair at home.

How To

The first thing you'll need to do is gather your tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to wet your hair and make it easier to cut.

Once you have your tools, you'll want to start by wetting your hair. This will make it easier to cut and will help you see any uneven spots.

Next, use your comb to section your hair. Start by dividing your hair down the middle, from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Then, divide each side into two sections, one in front of your ear and one behind.

Starting with one section at a time, comb your hair straight down and hold it between your index and middle fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair at the length you desire. It's important to only cut a little bit at a time, so you don't accidentally cut too much.

Continue cutting your hair section by section until you've cut all of it. You can then use your comb to blend any uneven spots and make sure your hair is even all around.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's much easier to cut more off than to try to fix a mistake.
  • Use a mirror to see the back of your head.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. It's better to have longer hair than to have to wait for it to grow back.
  • Watch tutorials online to get a better idea of how to cut your hair.

FAQ

Is it easy to cut your own hair with scissors and a comb?

Cutting your own hair can be challenging, especially if you're not used to it. However, with practice, it can become easier.

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

While it's possible to cut your hair with regular scissors, it's not recommended. Hair cutting scissors are designed to make precise cuts and are much easier to use.

How often should I cut my hair?

How often you cut your hair depends on how fast it grows and your desired length. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

Hair Care

After you've cut your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. This includes washing it regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding heat styling as much as possible.

By following these tips, you can successfully cut your own hair with scissors and a comb. Remember to take your time and start with small cuts, and you'll have a great haircut in no time!
