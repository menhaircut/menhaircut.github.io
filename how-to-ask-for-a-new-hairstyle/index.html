How To Ask For A New Hairstyle In Relaxed English Language

Written by Petter Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and want to switch things up? Asking for a new hairstyle can be intimidating, especially if you're not familiar with the terminology or don't know how to communicate your desired look. In this blog post, we'll teach you how to ask for a new hairstyle in relaxed English language. From how to describe your desired cut to tips for maintaining your new look, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Ask for a New Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your New Look
  • FAQs

How to Ask for a New Hairstyle

When asking for a new hairstyle, it's important to be specific about what you want. Here are some tips for communicating your desired look:

  • Bring in pictures: If you have a specific cut or style in mind, bring in pictures to show your stylist. This will help them understand exactly what you're looking for.
  • Use descriptive words: If you don't have pictures, use descriptive words to explain your desired look. For example, you might say "I want a shoulder-length bob with layers" or "I want a pixie cut with side-swept bangs."
  • Be realistic: While it's important to communicate your desired look, it's also important to be realistic about what will work with your hair type and face shape. Trust your stylist's expertise and be open to their suggestions.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you ask for a new hairstyle:

  1. Research hairstyles: Look online or in magazines for inspiration. Save pictures of hairstyles you like to show your stylist.
  2. Choose a stylist: Find a stylist you feel comfortable with and who has experience with the type of cut or style you want.
  3. Schedule a consultation: Before your appointment, schedule a consultation with your stylist to discuss your desired look.
  4. Show pictures: Bring in pictures of hairstyles you like to show your stylist.
  5. Communicate your desired look: Use descriptive words to explain your desired cut or style. Be realistic about what will work with your hair type and face shape.
  6. Ask for advice: Ask your stylist for their professional opinion on what will work best for you. They may have suggestions you haven't considered.
  7. Discuss maintenance: Ask your stylist about any maintenance required for your new hairstyle. They can recommend products and styling techniques to help you maintain your look.
  8. Book your appointment: Once you and your stylist have agreed on a style, book your appointment to get your new look.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Use the right products: Use products recommended by your stylist to help maintain your new look.
  • Get regular trims: Keep your new cut looking fresh by getting regular trims every 6-8 weeks.
  • Experiment with styling: Try different styling techniques to change up your look. Your stylist can recommend products and techniques to help you achieve different styles.
  • Protect your hair: Use heat protectant products when styling with hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about asking for a new hairstyle:

How do I know if a hairstyle will look good on me?

While it's important to consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a hairstyle, ultimately it's up to you to decide what you feel comfortable with. If you're unsure, ask your stylist for their professional opinion.

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you're unhappy with your new hairstyle, communicate with your stylist. They may be able to make adjustments to better suit your needs.

How do I maintain my new hairstyle?

Ask your stylist for product and styling recommendations to help you maintain your new look. Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to keep your cut looking fresh.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving and maintaining your desired hairstyle. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly: Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use conditioner: Use conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid heat styling: Limit heat styling to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet: Eating a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy.

Now that you know how to ask for a new hairstyle, it's time to book your appointment and try something new. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist and be open to their suggestions. With the right communication and maintenance, you'll have a new hairstyle that you love.
