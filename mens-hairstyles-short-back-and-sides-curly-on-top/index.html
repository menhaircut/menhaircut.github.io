Men's Hairstyles: Short Back and Sides Curly on Top

Description:

Short back and sides curly on top is a popular hairstyle for men. This style features a short cut on the back and sides of the head, with longer curly hair on top. This style is versatile and can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair textures. It is perfect for men who want an easy-to-maintain but stylish look.

How to Achieve the Look:

To achieve the short back and sides curly on top look, you will need a good barber who can give you a short cut on the back and sides of your head. The length of the hair on top of your head will depend on your preference, but it should be long enough to create curls. The barber will use scissors or clippers to cut the hair on the back and sides, and may use a razor to create a fade.

Step by Step Guide:

1. Wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel dry your hair until it is damp.

3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or mousse.

4. Use your fingers to scrunch the hair on top of your head, creating curls.

5. Use a hair dryer to dry your hair, using a diffuser to enhance the curls.

6. Use a styling product to define your curls and hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining the Style:

- Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

- Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls soft and defined.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush which can cause frizz.

- Visit your barber regularly to keep the back and sides of your hair short and neat.

FAQs:

Q: How long does the style last?

A: The style can last for several days if you use a good quality styling product and avoid touching your hair too much.

Q: Can this style work for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can work for all hair types, but it is particularly suited to men with curly or wavy hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair:

Curly hair requires extra care to keep it healthy and defined. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.

- Avoid using hot water, which can strip your hair of natural oils and cause frizz.

- Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
