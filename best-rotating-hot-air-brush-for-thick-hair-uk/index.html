The Best Rotating Hot Air Brush For Thick Hair Uk Top Picks For 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 16, 2023 · 6 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. Using the right tools, such as a rotating hot air brush, can make all the difference in achieving a smooth, voluminous look. In this article, we will review the best rotating hot air brush for thick hair UK, providing you with all the information you need to choose the right one for your needs.

What is a Rotating Hot Air Brush?

A rotating hot air brush is a hair styling tool that combines the functionality of a brush and a hair dryer. It is designed to dry and style hair simultaneously, using hot air and rotating bristles to create a smooth, shiny finish. The rotating function of the brush allows for easy styling, as it helps to achieve a natural-looking curl or wave. Rotating hot air brushes are particularly useful for those with thick hair, as they can help to reduce styling time and minimize the risk of damage.

How to Choose the Best Rotating Hot Air Brush for Thick Hair UK?

There are several factors to consider when choosing the best rotating hot air brush for thick hair UK, including:

Barrel Size

The barrel size of the rotating hot air brush will determine the type of curl or wave that can be achieved. For those with thick hair, a larger barrel size is recommended, as it will help to create more volume and reduce styling time.

Bristle Type

The bristle type of the rotating hot air brush can vary. Some brushes have natural boar bristles, while others have synthetic bristles. It is important to consider the type of bristle, as it can affect the overall finish of the hair.

Heat Settings

Rotating hot air brushes typically have multiple heat and speed settings. For those with thick hair, it is recommended to use a higher heat setting to reduce styling time. However, it is important to use caution when using a high heat setting, as it can damage the hair.

Brand and Price

Rotating hot air brushes are available at a range of price points, depending on the brand and features. It is important to choose a reputable brand and consider the price in relation to the features of the brush.

How to Use a Rotating Hot Air Brush for Thick Hair UK?

Using a rotating hot air brush for thick hair UK is easy. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Prep Hair

Wash and towel dry hair. Apply a heat protectant product and comb through hair to remove tangles.

Step 2: Divide Hair

Divide hair into sections, using clips to keep them in place.

Step 3: Start Styling

Take a section of hair and place the rotating hot air brush at the root. Slowly glide the brush down the length of the hair, rotating as needed to create a curl or wave. Repeat with each section of hair.

Step 4: Finish with Product

Once hair is styled, apply a finishing product, such as hairspray or serum, to help hold the style and add shine.

Tips for Using a Rotating Hot Air Brush for Thick Hair UK

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your rotating hot air brush for thick hair UK:

  • Use a heat protectant product to minimize damage to hair.
  • Start with a lower heat setting and increase as needed to avoid damage.
  • Divide hair into smaller sections for more precise styling.
  • Use a larger barrel size for more volume.
  • Rotate the brush in the direction you want the hair to curl for best results.

FAQ

Can a Rotating Hot Air Brush be Used on Thick Hair?

Yes, a rotating hot air brush can be used on thick hair. It is recommended to use a brush with a larger barrel size and higher heat setting for best results.

Can a Rotating Hot Air Brush Replace a Hair Dryer?

Yes, a rotating hot air brush can replace a hair dryer. It combines the functionality of a brush and hair dryer, allowing for simultaneous styling and drying.

Can a Rotating Hot Air Brush Damage Hair?

Yes, a rotating hot air brush can damage hair if used improperly or at too high of a heat setting. It is important to use caution when using a rotating hot air brush and follow the manufacturer's instructions.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

In addition to using a rotating hot air brush, it is important to take care of thick hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to prevent tangles.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-washing hair, as it can strip natural oils.

With the right rotating hot air brush and proper hair care, those with thick hair can achieve a smooth, voluminous look. Consider the factors outlined in this article when choosing the best rotating hot air brush for thick hair UK, and follow the tips and steps for optimal results.
