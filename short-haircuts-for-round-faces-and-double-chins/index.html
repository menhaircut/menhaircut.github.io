Short Haircuts For Round Faces And Double Chins

Written by Petter Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face and a double chin, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are many short haircuts that can flatter your facial features and minimize the appearance of a double chin. In this article, we'll explore some of the best short haircuts for round faces and double chins, as well as tips for maintaining your new hairstyle.

Description

Round faces and double chins are common features that many people struggle with. A round face is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded jawline. A double chin occurs when excess fat forms under the chin, creating a second chin. While these features can make it difficult to find the perfect haircut, there are many short hairstyles that can enhance your facial features and minimize the appearance of a double chin.

How To

When choosing a short haircut for a round face and double chin, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture. Short haircuts that flatter round faces and double chins typically have layers and volume around the crown of the head, which elongates the face and draws attention away from the chin. Some popular short haircuts for round faces and double chins include:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for round faces and double chins. This cut features short, cropped hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer layers on top. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, which can help elongate the face and draw attention away from the chin.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short haircut for round faces and double chins. This cut features hair that is chin-length or shorter, with layers that are shorter in the back and longer in the front. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, which can help elongate the face and draw attention away from the chin.

Step by Step

If you're considering a short haircut for your round face and double chin, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consult with a stylist who has experience working with round faces and double chins. They can recommend the best haircut for your facial features and hair texture.
  2. Choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and minimizes the appearance of your double chin.
  3. Maintain your new haircut with regular trims and styling. Use products that add volume and texture to your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short haircut for your round face and double chin:

  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear wider and draw attention to your double chin.
  • Consider adding bangs to your haircut, as they can help balance out a round face.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as adding waves or curls, to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for round faces and double chins:

Q: Can I still have a short haircut if I have a double chin?

A: Yes, there are many short haircuts that can flatter your facial features and minimize the appearance of a double chin.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a round face?

A: No, bangs can actually help balance out a round face and enhance your facial features.

Q: How often should I get my haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your short haircut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut and prevent split ends.

With the right short haircut and hair care routine, you can enhance your facial features and minimize the appearance of a double chin. Consult with a stylist and experiment with different short haircuts to find the perfect one for you.
