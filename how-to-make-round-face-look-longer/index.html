How To Make Round Face Look Longer Tips Faq And Hair Care

If you have a round face, you might feel like your features are not as defined as you would like them to be. But don't worry! There are several ways to make your face look longer and more angular, without having to resort to surgery or extreme measures. In this article, we'll share some tips, tricks, and hair care advice to help you achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Round Face Look Longer
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks

How to Make Round Face Look Longer

First of all, it's important to understand what causes a round face. Generally, it's due to excess fat around the cheeks, jawline, and chin. To make your face look longer, you need to create the illusion of a more defined jawline and cheekbones. Here are some ways to achieve this:

1. Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using a darker shade of foundation or powder to create shadows and define your features. To make your face look longer, apply contouring products along your jawline, under your cheekbones, and on the sides of your forehead. Blend well to avoid harsh lines.

2. Hairstyles

Hairstyles can also play a big role in making your face look longer. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head, while keeping the sides sleek and close to your face. This will create the illusion of a more elongated face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides, as this will only make your face look rounder.

3. Accessories

Accessories can also help you achieve a longer face shape. Choose earrings and necklaces that are long and angular, rather than round or circular. This will draw the eye downwards, creating the illusion of a more elongated face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your round face look longer:

  1. Start with a clean, moisturized face.
  2. Apply a primer to help your makeup last longer.
  3. Apply foundation as usual.
  4. Apply a contouring product along your jawline, under your cheekbones, and on the sides of your forehead.
  5. Blend well with a makeup brush or sponge.
  6. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks, blending upwards.
  7. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head, while keeping the sides sleek and close to your face.
  8. Choose accessories that are long and angular.
  9. Smile and enjoy your new, longer-looking face!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you make your round face look longer:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head.
  • Use a matte bronzer or contouring powder, rather than a shimmery one, to create shadows and definition.
  • Choose earrings and necklaces that are long and angular.
  • Use a highlighter on the tops of your cheekbones to create a more defined look.
  • Experiment with different makeup techniques, such as strobing or highlighting, to see what works best for you.

FAQ

What is the best contouring product for a round face?

The best contouring product for a round face is a matte bronzer or contouring powder that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone. Avoid shimmery products, as these can make your face look rounder.

Can hair color make my face look longer?

Yes, choosing the right hair color can help make your face look longer. Generally, lighter shades tend to create the illusion of a longer face, while darker shades can make your face look rounder.

Do I need to contour my entire face?

No, you don't need to contour your entire face. Focus on the areas where you want to create more definition, such as your jawline and cheekbones.

Hair Care Advice

Finally, here are some hair care tips to help you maintain a longer-looking face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head.
  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the top of your head, while keeping the sides sleek and close to your face.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and volume to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look flat.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained.

By following these tips, tricks, and hair care advice, you can make your round face look longer and more defined, without having to resort to extreme measures. Remember to experiment with different techniques and products to find what works best for you, and have fun with your new look!
