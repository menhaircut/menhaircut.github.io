How To Care For Relaxed Hair After Braids

Description

Relaxed hair requires special care after braids to ensure that it remains healthy and strong. Braids can put a lot of stress on the hair, and if not properly cared for, can lead to breakage and damage. In this article, we will discuss how to care for relaxed hair after braids and provide tips on how to maintain healthy hair.

How to

The first step in caring for relaxed hair after braids is to remove the braids carefully. Use scissors to cut the extension hair, then use a detangling brush to gently remove any knots or tangles. Be sure to avoid pulling or tugging on the hair to prevent breakage.

Once the braids are removed, wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. Follow up with a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture to your hair. You can also use a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your hair.

After washing and conditioning, allow your hair to air dry or use a hooded dryer on a low setting. Avoid using heat tools such as flat irons or blow dryers, as they can cause damage to your hair.

Step by Step

1. Remove braids carefully with scissors
2. Detangle hair gently with a detangling brush
3. Wash hair with sulfate-free shampoo
4. Apply deep conditioning treatment
5. Use leave-in conditioner to detangle hair
6. Air dry or use hooded dryer on low setting
7. Avoid using heat tools

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your relaxed hair after braids:

  • Use a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage while sleeping
  • Avoid wearing tight hairstyles, as they can pull on your hair and cause damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and promote healthy growth
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and start from the ends and work your way up to avoid pulling on your hair
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products on your hair, as they can weigh it down and make it look greasy

FAQ

Q: Can I use a clarifying shampoo after removing braids?
A: Yes, a clarifying shampoo can help remove any buildup or residue from your hair. However, be sure to follow up with a deep conditioning treatment to restore moisture to your hair.

Q: How often should I wash my hair after removing braids?
A: It is recommended to wash your hair once a week to prevent buildup and maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care

Caring for relaxed hair after braids requires patience and dedication. By following these tips and maintaining a healthy hair care routine, you can keep your hair strong and beautiful.
