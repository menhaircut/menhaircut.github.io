How Much Does It Cost To Bleach A Pixie Cut

Are you thinking about bleaching your pixie cut? If so, you may be wondering how much it will cost. In this article, we will discuss the cost of bleaching a pixie cut, as well as some tips for maintaining your newly bleached hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Bleach a Pixie Cut
  • The Cost of Bleaching a Pixie Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining Bleached Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Bleached Hair

How to Bleach a Pixie Cut

Bleaching your hair at home can be a cost-effective option, but it can also be risky. If you are not experienced with hair color, it may be best to go to a professional salon. However, if you are confident in your abilities, here is a step-by-step guide to bleaching your pixie cut:

Step 1: Gather Supplies

You will need:

  • Bleach powder
  • Developer
  • Gloves
  • Brush
  • Bowl
  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Old towel or t-shirt

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Put on your old towel or t-shirt to protect your clothes. Section your hair and apply petroleum jelly around your hairline and ears to prevent irritation.

Step 3: Mix the Bleach

Follow the instructions on the bleach powder and developer to mix them together.

Step 4: Apply the Bleach

Using your brush, apply the bleach mixture to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips.

Step 5: Wait

Leave the bleach on your hair for the recommended time, checking it regularly to avoid over-processing.

Step 6: Rinse and Condition

Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water and apply conditioner. Rinse again and style your hair as desired.

The Cost of Bleaching a Pixie Cut

The cost of bleaching a pixie cut will vary depending on several factors, including the salon you go to, your location, and the experience level of the stylist.

On average, you can expect to pay anywhere from $50 to $200 for a professional bleach job. If you decide to do it yourself at home, the cost will depend on the products you purchase. You can find bleach powder and developer at most beauty supply stores for around $20 to $30.

Tips for Maintaining Bleached Hair

Bleached hair requires special care to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your newly bleached pixie cut:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping the color
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater
  • Protect your hair from the sun

FAQs

Is it safe to bleach my hair at home?

While it is possible to bleach your hair at home, it can be risky if you are not experienced with hair color. It is best to go to a professional salon if you are unsure.

How often will I need to touch up my roots?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how noticeable the roots are. On average, you may need to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for Bleached Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, bleached hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools
  • Avoid over-washing your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair

Now that you know the cost of bleaching a pixie cut, as well as some tips for maintaining your newly bleached hair, you can make an informed decision about whether to take the plunge. Remember to always take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
