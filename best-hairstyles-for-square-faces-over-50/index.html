Best Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 50 A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Sep 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a square face and you’re over 50, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this guide, we’ll show you some of the best hairstyles for square faces over 50 that will help you look youthful, stylish, and confident.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Square Faces over 50
  • Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Square Faces over 50

Description

Square faces are characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and angular features. The key to finding the best hairstyle for a square face is to soften these features and create a balance. Hairstyles that add volume and texture to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back close to the head, work well for square faces over 50.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Square Faces over 50

When choosing the best hairstyle for a square face over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height and volume to the top of the head.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can emphasize the squareness of the face.
  • Consider adding layers to create movement and soften the features.
  • Choose hairstyles that frame the face and draw attention away from the jawline.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Here’s a step by step guide to styling your hair for a square face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair, lifting the roots at the crown of your head to create height.
  3. Use a round brush to create movement and volume, focusing on the top and crown of your head.
  4. Use a styling product, like a mousse or volumizing spray, to add texture and hold.
  5. If you have bangs, sweep them to the side to create a soft, feminine look.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are a few tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Add highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools, like curling irons and straighteners, too often, as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for square faces over 50:

What are the best hairstyles for a square face over 50?

The best hairstyles for a square face over 50 are those that add volume and texture to the top of the head, while keeping the sides and back close to the head. Layers and bangs can also help soften the features.

Can I wear my hair short if I have a square face?

Yes, you can wear your hair short if you have a square face, but it’s important to choose a style that doesn’t emphasize the squareness of your face. Consider a layered bob or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

What hair color looks best on a square face over 50?

Warm, natural-looking hair colors, like honey blonde, chestnut brown, and auburn, look great on square faces over 50. Avoid cool, ashy tones, which can wash out the complexion.

Hair Care for Square Faces over 50

Here are a few tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water, which can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, which can damage the hair follicles and lead to hair loss.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for square faces over 50, it’s time to find the perfect style for you. Whether you choose a layered bob or a long, layered style with side-swept bangs, remember to keep the top of your head voluminous and the sides close to your head to create balance and soften your features. With the right hairstyle, you’ll look youthful, stylish, and confident!
