How To Style Loose Beachy Waves

Written by Christine Oct 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a relaxed and easy hairstyle that screams summer? Look no further than loose beachy waves! This style is perfect for the beach, a day out with friends, or even a casual date night. Keep reading for our step-by-step guide on how to achieve this effortless look.

Description

Loose beachy waves are a popular, relaxed hairstyle that is perfect for the summer months. This style mimics the natural waves that occur when you spend a day at the beach, giving you a laid-back and effortless look.

How to

To achieve loose beachy waves, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as normal. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to prevent damage from the heat styling tools.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections. The smaller the sections, the tighter the waves will be. If you want looser waves, divide your hair into larger sections. Clip each section out of the way so that you can work on one section at a time.

Step 3: Use a Curling Iron

Using a curling iron or wand, wrap each section around the barrel away from your face. Hold the curling iron for about 10-15 seconds and then release the curl. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair.

Step 4: Shake Out the Curls

Once you have curled all of your hair, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls. Shake your hair out to create a more natural look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish the look with a light hold hairspray to keep the waves in place. You can also use a sea salt spray to add texture and create a more beachy look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect loose beachy waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel for looser waves
  • Apply a texturizing spray to your hair before curling for added texture
  • For a more natural look, curl random sections of your hair instead of curling every section
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add shine to your waves
  • For a more tousled look, use your fingers to twist and separate the curls after curling

FAQ

How long will loose beachy waves last?

Loose beachy waves can last up to 2-3 days. To prolong the life of your waves, sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid and use dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.

Can I achieve loose beachy waves on short hair?

Yes! You can still achieve loose beachy waves on short hair by using a smaller curling iron and curling closer to the root of your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful locks. Here are some tips to keep your hair in great condition:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Now that you know how to achieve loose beachy waves, it's time to hit the beach and show off your new look!
