How To Tame Frizzy Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Frizzy curly hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve soft, defined curls that are free from frizz. In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps you need to follow to tame your frizzy curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tame Frizzy Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Taming Frizzy Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Frizzy Curly Hair

How to Tame Frizzy Curly Hair

Taming frizzy curly hair starts with understanding what causes frizz. Frizz is caused by a lack of moisture in the hair, which causes the hair cuticle to lift and allow moisture to enter. This leads to the hair swelling and becoming frizzy. To tame frizzy curly hair, you need to focus on adding moisture to your hair and sealing it in.

Products to Use

The right products can make a big difference in taming frizzy curly hair. Here are some products to consider:

  • Shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl cream or gel
  • Hair oil

Look for products that are designed to add moisture to curly hair and help define curls. Avoid products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair and make frizz worse.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo designed for curly hair. Be sure to massage the shampoo into your scalp and roots, and avoid scrubbing your hair too vigorously. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water, and follow up with a conditioner designed for curly hair. Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and work it up towards your roots. Let the conditioner sit for a few minutes before rinsing it out with cool water.

Step 2: Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to add moisture and help prevent frizz. Apply the leave-in conditioner to damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up towards your roots. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly through your hair.

Step 3: Curl Cream or Gel

Apply a curl cream or gel to your hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz. Apply the product to damp hair, starting from the ends and working your way up towards your roots. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair as you apply the product, to help define your curls.

Step 4: Hair Oil

Finish off your hair routine with a hair oil to help seal in moisture and add shine. Apply a few drops of hair oil to your palms and rub them together. Apply the oil to the ends of your hair, working your way up towards your roots. Be careful not to apply too much oil, as it can weigh down your hair.

Tips for Taming Frizzy Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for taming frizzy curly hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair, as regular towels can cause frizz
  • Avoid using heat tools, as they can dry out your hair and make frizz worse
  • Protect your hair from the elements, such as wind and humidity, by wearing a hat or scarf
  • Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent friction and reduce frizz

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my frizzy curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much oil it produces. Some people with curly hair can get away with washing their hair once a week, while others may need to wash their hair every other day. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Q: Can I brush my frizzy curly hair?

A: It’s best to avoid brushing curly hair, as it can disrupt your curls and cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it’s wet.

Hair Care for Frizzy Curly Hair

Proper hair care is essential for taming frizzy curly hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair with hot water, as it can dry out your hair and make frizz worse
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add moisture
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

By following these tips and using the right products, you can tame your frizzy curly hair and achieve soft, defined curls. Experiment to find what works best for your hair, and be patient as you work to find the right routine.
