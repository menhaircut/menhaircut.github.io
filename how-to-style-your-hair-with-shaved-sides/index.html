How To Style Your Hair With Shaved Sides A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, shaved sides might be the perfect option for you. This look is versatile and can be adapted to various hair lengths and textures. However, it can be challenging to figure out how to style your hair with shaved sides, especially if you're new to this trend. In this article, we'll dive into the step-by-step process of styling your hair with shaved sides, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

How to Style Your Hair with Shaved Sides

Before we dive into the styling process, it's essential to note that there are different ways to shave your sides. Some people opt for an undercut, while others go for a buzz cut or a fade. The styling process may vary depending on the type of shaved sides you have.

With that said, here are some basic steps to style your hair with shaved sides:

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, grime, or product buildup from your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp, but not soaking wet.
  3. Apply a hair styling product of your choice, such as mousse, gel, or pomade. Make sure to focus on the top section of your hair, as this is the part that you'll be styling.
  4. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while using a brush to style it. If you have curly hair, you can use a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.
  5. Once your hair is dry, use a hair straightener or curling iron to create your desired look.
  6. Finally, apply a finishing product, such as hairspray, to hold your style in place.

Voila! You now have a perfectly styled hairdo with shaved sides.

Tips for Styling Shaved Sides

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect look with shaved sides:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type and desired style.
  • Use a high-quality blow dryer and styling tools to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Consider adding some color or highlights to your hair to enhance your look.
  • Don't be afraid to try different styling techniques, such as braids, twists, or updos.
  • If you're not confident in styling your hair yourself, don't hesitate to seek help from a professional stylist.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling hair with shaved sides:

Q: Will shaving my sides damage my hair?

A: Shaving your sides shouldn't damage your hair, as long as you take proper care of it. However, it's essential to note that shaving can cause irritation or ingrown hairs, so make sure to use a sharp razor and shaving cream.

Q: How often do I need to shave my sides?

A: This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your shaved sides. Typically, you'll need to shave your sides every two to four weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with shaved sides?

A: Absolutely! You can still style your hair in various ways, even with shaved sides. Try experimenting with different updos or half-up, half-down styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care for Shaved Sides

Taking care of your hair with shaved sides is crucial to maintaining a healthy scalp and hair growth. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-styling or using too many hair products, as this can cause buildup and damage your hair.
  • Moisturize your scalp regularly with hair oil or a leave-in conditioner.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you're now ready to rock your shaved sides with confidence and style. Remember to experiment with different looks and techniques to find the perfect hairstyle for you.
