Are Bangs Good For Thinning Hair On Top

Written by Oliver Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning hair on top can be a frustrating and confidence-damaging experience, but there are ways to work with it. One option that many women consider is getting bangs. But are bangs good for thinning hair on top? In this article, we'll explore this question and provide tips on how to make bangs work for you.

Description

When it comes to thinning hair on top, some women may feel like they have limited hairstyle options. However, bangs can be a great way to add volume and dimension to your hair. Bangs can also help to frame your face and draw attention away from thinning areas on your scalp.

While bangs may not be the best option for everyone with thinning hair, they can work well for those who have a healthy amount of hair around their hairline. It's important to consider your hair type, face shape, and personal style when deciding whether to get bangs.

How to Make Bangs Work for Thinning Hair on Top

If you're considering getting bangs with thinning hair on top, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Choose the right type of bangs: Blunt or heavy bangs can add weight and volume to hair, which can help to conceal thinning areas. Side-swept bangs can also be a great option, as they draw attention away from the top of your head.
  • Consider the length: Shorter bangs can be more difficult to style and maintain, so longer bangs may be a better option for those with thinning hair.
  • Work with a stylist: A professional stylist can help you choose the right type of bangs for your hair type and face shape. They can also help you maintain your bangs and keep them looking great.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for getting bangs with thinning hair on top:

  1. Choose the right type of bangs for your hair type and face shape.
  2. Consult with a stylist to determine the best length and style for your bangs.
  3. Have your stylist cut your bangs, paying close attention to the amount of hair around your hairline.
  4. Style your bangs as desired, using volumizing products if necessary.

Tips for Maintaining Bangs with Thinning Hair on Top

Here are a few tips for keeping your bangs looking great with thinning hair on top:

  • Use volumizing products: Products like mousse, root lifters, and texturizing sprays can help to add volume and dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing: Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can make it look flat and lifeless. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.
  • Consider hair extensions: If you're struggling with thinning hair on top, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and length to your hair.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair on Top

If you're struggling with thinning hair on top, there are a few things you can do to care for your hair:

  • Avoid harsh chemicals: Chemical treatments like perms and relaxers can damage hair and make it more prone to breakage. Try to avoid these treatments if possible.
  • Eat a healthy diet: Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Use gentle products: Use gentle shampoos and conditioners that are designed for thinning hair. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

In conclusion, bangs can be a great option for those with thinning hair on top. However, it's important to choose the right type of bangs and work with a professional stylist to ensure that they look great and complement your personal style. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can enjoy beautiful, voluminous bangs that help to conceal thinning areas on your scalp.
