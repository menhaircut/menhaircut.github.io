Bridal Updo Tutorial Wedding Prom Hairstyles For Long Hair

Written by Luffy Nov 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have long hair, you are in luck when it comes to choosing a hairstyle for your wedding or prom. There are many beautiful updos that you can create with your long hair that will make you look stunning on your special day. In this tutorial, we will show you how to create a beautiful bridal updo that is perfect for weddings or proms.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Create a Bridal Updo
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfecting Your Updo
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Create a Bridal Updo:

Before you start creating your updo, you will need to gather a few tools and products:

  • Hairspray
  • Bobby pins
  • Comb
  • Clear elastic bands

Once you have everything you need, you can start creating your updo.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash your hair the night before and let it air dry. This will give your hair some texture and make it easier to work with. If you have oily hair, you can use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil.

Step 2: Create a Part

Decide where you want your part to be and create it with a comb. You can choose a center part, side part, or no part at all.

Step 3: Gather Your Hair

Gather all of your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with an elastic band.

Step 4: Create a Bun

Twist your ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins. You can create a messy bun or a sleek bun, depending on the look you are going for. Use hairspray to keep your bun in place.

Step 5: Add Accessories

You can add accessories to your updo, such as a flower crown or a hair clip. This will add some extra flair to your look.

Tips for Perfecting Your Updo:

  • Practice your updo a few times before the big day to make sure you are comfortable with the process.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color so they are not visible.
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray to keep your updo in place.
  • Choose accessories that complement your dress and the overall theme of your wedding or prom.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Can I create an updo if I have short hair?

Yes, you can create an updo with short hair, but it may be more challenging. You can use hair extensions or accessories to add length and volume to your hair.

How long does it take to create an updo?

It usually takes about 30 minutes to create an updo, but it may take longer if you are doing it for the first time.

What type of hair works best for an updo?

Long hair works best for updos, but you can also create an updo with medium-length hair.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can create a beautiful bridal updo that will make you look and feel your best on your special day.
