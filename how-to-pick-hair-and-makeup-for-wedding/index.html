How To Pick Hair And Makeup For Your Wedding

Written by Christine Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to your wedding day, you want to look and feel your best. One of the most important aspects of your bridal look is your hair and makeup. With so many styles and options to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we'll discuss how to pick hair and makeup for your wedding, step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Your hair and makeup are important parts of your overall bridal look. The right hair and makeup can enhance your natural beauty, complement your dress and accessories, and make you feel confident and beautiful on your big day. However, picking the right hair and makeup can be overwhelming, especially if you're not familiar with different styles and techniques.

How to

When it comes to picking hair and makeup for your wedding, there are a few things you should consider:

1. Your Wedding Theme and Style

Your hair and makeup should complement your wedding theme and style. For example, if you're having a beach wedding, you may want to opt for natural, beachy waves and minimal makeup. If you're having a more formal wedding, you may want a sleek updo and a more dramatic makeup look. Consider the overall vibe of your wedding when picking your hair and makeup.

2. Your Dress and Accessories

Your hair and makeup should also complement your dress and accessories. If you have a statement necklace or earrings, you may want to opt for an updo that showcases them. If your dress has intricate detailing, you may want to keep your hair and makeup simple so as not to compete with the dress.

3. Your Personal Style

Finally, your hair and makeup should reflect your personal style. If you typically wear your hair down and natural, you may want to opt for a similar look on your wedding day. If you love bold, dramatic makeup, you may want to incorporate that into your bridal look.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to picking your hair and makeup for your wedding:

1. Research

Start by doing some research. Look at bridal magazines, wedding blogs, and Pinterest for inspiration. Save any looks that catch your eye.

2. Consult with Your Hair and Makeup Artists

Once you have some ideas, schedule consultations with your hair and makeup artists. Bring along any pictures or inspiration you have, and discuss your wedding theme, dress, and personal style. Your artists can help you narrow down your options and make recommendations based on your preferences.

3. Schedule a Trial Run

After you've discussed your options with your artists, schedule a trial run. This will give you a chance to see how your hair and makeup will look on your big day. Bring along any accessories you plan to wear, so you can see how they will complement your look.

4. Make Adjustments

If you're not happy with your trial run, don't be afraid to make adjustments. Discuss any changes you want with your artists, and schedule another trial run if necessary.

5. Finalize Your Look

Once you're happy with your hair and makeup, finalize your look with your artists. Make sure you have a clear plan for the day of your wedding, including any touch-ups that may be necessary.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for picking your hair and makeup for your wedding:

  • Consider your skin tone when picking your makeup colors.
  • Don't be afraid to incorporate accessories into your hair, such as flowers or a veil.
  • Make sure your hair and makeup will hold up throughout the day and night.
  • Consider booking a touch-up session with your artists for later in the day.

FAQ

What if I don't wear makeup normally?

That's okay! Your artists can create a natural, minimal makeup look that will enhance your natural beauty without feeling overwhelming.

What if I change my mind after the trial run?

That's okay too! Just discuss any changes you want with your artists and schedule another trial run if necessary.

Hair Care

Leading up to your wedding day, it's important to take care of your hair. Make sure you're using quality hair care products, and consider getting a trim a few weeks before the wedding to keep your hair looking healthy. On the day of your wedding, make sure your hair is clean and dry before your artists arrive.

Picking your hair and makeup for your wedding can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your wedding theme and style, your dress and accessories, and your personal style, you can create a look that makes you feel confident and beautiful on your big day.
