What To Do With Long Curly Hair Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with long curly hair, you may be wondering what to do with it. Long curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's unique and eye-catching. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to care for your long curly hair, how to style it, and some tips and tricks to make it look its best.

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How to Style Long Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Hair Care

Before we get into styling your long curly hair, it's important to take care of it. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so it's important to keep it moisturized. Here are some tips for taking care of your long curly hair:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can also dry out your hair. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment to hydrate your curls.
  • Don't towel dry your hair. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out the excess water.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as it can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.

By taking care of your hair, you'll be able to achieve the best possible results when styling it.

How to Style Long Curly Hair

There are plenty of ways to style long curly hair. Here are a few of our favorites:

  • Leave it down and natural. This is the easiest and most low-maintenance option.
  • Pull it back into a ponytail or bun.
  • Try a half-up, half-down style. This is a great option if you want to keep your hair out of your face.
  • Experiment with different partings. You might be surprised at how much of a difference a side part or a center part can make.
  • Add some accessories, like a headband or a scarf.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're new to styling long curly hair, it can be helpful to have a step-by-step guide to follow. Here's how to style your long curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.
  4. Part your hair as desired.
  5. If you're going for a half-up, half-down style, gather the top section of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip.
  6. If you're leaving your hair down, scrunch it gently to encourage your curls.
  7. If you're pulling your hair back into a ponytail or bun, use a hair tie or scrunchie to secure it.
  8. Add any accessories you like.
  9. Finish with a hairspray or other styling product, if desired.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for styling your long curly hair:

  • Try diffusing your hair with a blow dryer to enhance your curls.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as it can disrupt your curls.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to visit a stylist who specializes in curly hair. They can give you personalized advice and help you achieve the look you want.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling long curly hair:

Q: Should I brush my curly hair?

A: No, brushing curly hair can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Some people with curly hair can go a week or more without washing, while others need to wash every two to three days. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten your curly hair if you want to. Just be sure to use a heat protectant and a high-quality straightener.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make the most of your long curly hair. Whether you're keeping it natural or experimenting with different styles, remember to take care of your hair and have fun with it!
