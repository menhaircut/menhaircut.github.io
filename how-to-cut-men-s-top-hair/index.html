How To Cut Men s Top Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Dec 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting men's hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're not a professional barber. However, with the right tools and techniques, anyone can learn how to cut men's top hair. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a great haircut that will leave your client looking sharp and stylish.

Description

Men's top hair refers to the hair that sits on top of the head, also known as the crown. It's the area that most people focus on when they want to change their hairstyle, so it's important to get it right. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut men's top hair using simple techniques that anyone can follow.

How to Cut Men's Top Hair

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you begin cutting, it's important to prepare the hair. Start by washing and conditioning the hair to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup. Then, towel dry the hair until it's damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Use a comb to section the hair into four parts: the crown, the sides, and the back. Clip the sections you're not working on out of the way.

Step 3: Choose the Right Length

Decide on the length you want to cut the hair before you start. Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair to the desired length. Start from the back and work your way forward, making sure to blend the hair as you go.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Use thinning shears to blend the hair and create a more natural look. Start at the crown and work your way down, taking small sections of hair at a time.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Once you've finished cutting, check for evenness by combing the hair and looking for any uneven spots. If you find any, trim them with scissors or clippers until the hair is even.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors or clippers to avoid pulling or tugging at the hair
  • Work in small sections to ensure evenness
  • Take breaks if you need to rest your hands or if your client needs a break
  • Use a mirror to check the haircut from all angles

FAQ

What tools do I need to cut men's top hair?

You'll need a good pair of scissors, clippers, a comb, thinning shears, and hair clips to section the hair.

How often should men get their top hair cut?

This depends on the individual, but most men get their hair cut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After the haircut, it's important to take care of the hair to keep it looking its best. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to cut men's top hair, you can confidently take on any client and give them a great haircut. Remember to take your time, work in small sections, and check for evenness to achieve the best results.
