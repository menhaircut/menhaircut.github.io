Best Curling Iron For Short Fine Hair 2019 Your Ultimate Guide

Welcome to our ultimate guide on the best curling iron for short fine hair 2019. If you have short and fine hair, you might be struggling to find the perfect curling iron that will give you the curls and volume you desire. With so many different curling irons on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the best one for your hair type. This guide will help you find the perfect curling iron for your short and fine hair.

How to Choose the Best Curling Iron for Short Fine Hair 2019

When choosing a curling iron for short fine hair, there are several factors that you need to consider. Here are some things to keep in mind:

Barrel Size

The barrel size is one of the most important factors to consider when choosing a curling iron for short fine hair. A smaller barrel size is ideal for short hair because it allows you to create tighter curls and more volume. A barrel size between 1/2 inch to 1 inch is perfect for short hair.

Heat Settings

Heat settings are also important when it comes to choosing a curling iron for short fine hair. Make sure to choose a curling iron that has adjustable heat settings so that you can control the temperature. This is important because fine hair is more prone to damage from high heat.

Material

The material of the curling iron is also important. Ceramic and tourmaline curling irons are ideal for short fine hair because they distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz. Titanium curling irons are also a good option because they heat up quickly and retain heat well.

Step by Step Guide to Curling Short Fine Hair

Now that you have chosen the best curling iron for your short fine hair, it's time to learn how to use it. Here is a step by step guide to curling short fine hair:

Step 1: Prep your Hair

Before you start curling your hair, make sure that it is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Section your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Curl your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the curl. Repeat this process until all sections of your hair are curled.

Step 4: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have curled all of your hair, finish with a light hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Short Fine Hair

Here are some tips to help you curl your short fine hair:

Tip 1: Use Heat Protectant

Always use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Tip 2: Use Small Sections

Use small sections of hair when curling your hair to ensure that all sections are curled evenly.

Tip 3: Hold the Curl

Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds before releasing the curl. This will ensure that the curl is set and will last longer.

FAQ about Curling Short Fine Hair

Q: Can I use a curling iron on short fine hair?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron on short fine hair. Just make sure to choose a curling iron with a smaller barrel size and adjustable heat settings.

Q: How do I prevent heat damage when curling my hair?

A: Always use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair and don't use high heat settings.

Hair Care for Short Fine Hair

Taking care of your hair is important to ensure that it stays healthy and looks great. Here are some hair care tips for short fine hair:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for fine hair to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash

Overwashing your hair can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and damaged. Try to wash your hair every other day to keep it healthy.

Tip 3: Use a Lightweight Conditioner

Use a lightweight conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.

Tip 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair too often to prevent heat damage.

We hope this ultimate guide on the best curling iron for short fine hair 2019 has been helpful. Remember to choose a curling iron with a smaller barrel size, adjustable heat settings, and the right material for your hair type. Happy curling!
