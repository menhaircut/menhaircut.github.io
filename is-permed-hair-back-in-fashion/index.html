Is Permed Hair Back In Fashion

Written by Robby Nov 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Permed hair was a popular trend in the 80s and 90s, but it eventually went out of fashion. However, in recent years, we have seen a resurgence of permed hairstyles. If you are wondering whether permed hair is back in fashion, this article is for you. We will discuss everything you need to know about this trend, including how to get a perm, tips for styling permed hair, and how to care for it.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the shape of your hair. It involves wrapping your hair around rods and applying a chemical solution. The solution breaks down the protein bonds in your hair, allowing it to take on a new shape. Once the chemical has been applied, your hair is rinsed and dried, leaving you with bouncy, curly hair.

How to Get a Perm

If you want to get a perm, the first step is to find a stylist who specializes in this treatment. They will help you choose the right size of rods and the type of perm solution that will work best for your hair type. Once you have decided on the specifics, your stylist will section your hair and wrap it around the rods. The perm solution will be applied, and you will sit under a dryer for about 30 minutes. After that, your hair will be rinsed and styled.

Step by Step Guide

1. Find a stylist who specializes in perms.

2. Discuss the specifics of the perm, such as rod size and solution type.

3. Section your hair and wrap it around the rods.

4. Apply the perm solution and sit under a dryer for 30 minutes.

5. Rinse your hair and style it.

Tips for Styling Permed Hair

If you have permed hair, there are a few tips you should keep in mind for styling it. First, use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz. Second, avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can break up the curls. Third, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

FAQs

1. Will a perm damage my hair?

A perm can be damaging to your hair if it is not done correctly. However, if you go to a skilled stylist and take care of your hair properly after the treatment, you should not experience any damage.

2. How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from 2 to 6 months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

3. Can I color my hair after getting a perm?

You should wait at least 2 weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This will give your hair time to recover from the chemical treatment.

Hair Care

If you have permed hair, it is important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and bouncy. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can break up the curls. Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz. And finally, get regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Permed hair is definitely back in fashion. If you are considering getting a perm, make sure you find a skilled stylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Take good care of your hair after the treatment, and you will be rewarded with bouncy, curly locks that will turn heads wherever you go.
