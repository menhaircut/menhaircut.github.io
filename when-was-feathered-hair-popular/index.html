When Was Feathered Hair Popular A Look Back At The Trend

Written by Petter Aug 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Feathered hair was a popular hairstyle trend in the 1970s and 1980s. If you're curious about this iconic hairstyle and when it was popular, keep reading to learn more.

What is Feathered Hair?

Feathered hair is a hairstyle where the hair is cut in layers and styled to curve outward. The layers are feathered to blend together, creating a soft, textured look. The hairstyle was popularized by celebrities like Farrah Fawcett and became a popular trend in the 1970s and 1980s.

When Was Feathered Hair Popular?

Feathered hair was most popular in the late 1970s and early 1980s. This was a time when disco was popular, and people were looking for hairstyles that were fun and playful. Feathered hair fit the bill perfectly, as it was a carefree and natural-looking style that complemented the disco fashion of the era.

How to Get Feathered Hair

If you want to try feathered hair for yourself, here's how:

Step 1: Cut Your Hair in Layers

To get feathered hair, you'll need to start by getting a layered haircut. The layers should be cut at an angle, so they blend together naturally.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

To style your hair, you'll need a round brush and a blow dryer. Start by blow-drying your hair, using the round brush to create volume and lift. Then, use a curling iron to curl the ends of your hair outward, creating the feathered effect.

Step 3: Finish with Hairspray

To keep your feathered hair in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your hair hold its shape and stay looking great all day.

Tips for Maintaining Feathered Hair

If you want to keep your feathered hair looking great, here are some tips to follow:

  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your layers
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and shiny

FAQ About Feathered Hair

Q: Who made feathered hair popular?

A: Feathered hair was popularized by celebrities like Farrah Fawcett and Jaclyn Smith.

Q: Is feathered hair still in style?

A: While feathered hair isn't as popular as it once was, it can still be a fun and stylish choice for those looking for a retro look.

Q: Can feathered hair work for all hair types?

A: Feathered hair can work for many hair types, but it's best suited for those with medium to long hair.

Hair Care for Feathered Hair

To keep your feathered hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Avoid using harsh hair products, like hair spray or gel, too often

In conclusion, feathered hair was a popular hairstyle trend in the late 1970s and early 1980s. While it's not as popular as it once was, it can still be a fun and stylish choice for those looking for a retro look. If you're interested in trying feathered hair for yourself, follow the steps outlined above and take good care of your hair to keep it looking great.
