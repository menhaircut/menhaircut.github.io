Pixie Styles For Over 60 A Guide To Short Haircuts

Written by Frank Jul 13, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle? Pixie styles can be a great option for those who want a low-maintenance, chic look. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about pixie styles for over 60, from how to choose the right cut to hair care tips.

How to Choose the Right Pixie Style

The first step in choosing a pixie style is to consider your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to opt for a longer pixie cut that adds height to the crown of your head. If you have a square face, a shorter cut with layers can soften the angles of your face.

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered pixie cut can add volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a choppy, textured cut can help thin out your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Pixie Cut

1. Research and find inspiration for your desired pixie style.

2. Schedule an appointment with a hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts.

3. Bring pictures of your desired cut to your appointment.

4. Discuss your hair texture and face shape with your stylist to determine the best cut for you.

5. Sit back, relax, and let your stylist work their magic!

Tips for Styling Your Pixie Cut

1. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair.

2. Use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to define your pixie cut and add texture.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories, such as headbands or barrettes, to switch up your look.

4. Visit your stylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of your pixie cut.

FAQs about Pixie Styles for Over 60

Q: Will a pixie cut make me look older?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually make you look younger and more modern.

Q: Can I still dye my hair with a pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can still dye your hair with a pixie cut. However, be sure to use hair dye specifically formulated for short haircuts.

Q: Will a pixie cut work with my glasses?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can work with glasses. Consider frames that complement the shape of your face and the length of your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Styles

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

3. Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.

4. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

With these tips, you can rock a pixie cut at any age. Remember to choose a cut that complements your face shape and hair texture, and experiment with different styling products and accessories to switch up your look. Happy styling!
