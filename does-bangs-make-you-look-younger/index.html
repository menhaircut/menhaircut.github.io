Does Bangs Make You Look Younger The Ultimate Guide To Youthful Bangs

Written by William Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to refresh your look and turn back the clock, bangs might be the answer you're looking for. Bangs are a versatile and easy way to change up your hairstyle, but do they really make you look younger? In this guide, we'll take a closer look at the age-defying power of bangs and share some tips on how to get the perfect youthful bangs for your face shape.

  2. How to Get Youthful Bangs
  3. Step-by-Step Guide

Description

Bangs have been a popular hairstyle for centuries, and for good reason. They can transform your appearance, frame your face, and create a youthful look. The key to youthful bangs is choosing the right style for your face shape and hair type. A well-executed set of bangs can make you look years younger, while a bad haircut can do the opposite.

How to Get Youthful Bangs

The first step to getting youthful bangs is to choose the right style for your face shape. If you have a round face, go for longer, side-swept bangs that will elongate your face. If you have a square face, opt for wispy, textured bangs that will soften your features. If you have a heart-shaped face, try a curtain bang that will balance out your forehead and chin.

Choose the Right Length

The length of your bangs is also important when it comes to creating a youthful look. Shorter bangs that hit above the eyebrows can make you look younger, but they can also be high maintenance. If you're not ready to commit to shorter bangs, try longer, face-framing bangs that will give you a youthful glow without the upkeep.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type can also play a role in the type of bangs that will work best for you. If you have fine hair, opt for wispy, textured bangs that will add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, try a blunt, straight-across fringe that will provide a sleek, modern look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Getting the perfect set of youthful bangs can be tricky, but with the right technique, it's possible to achieve salon-worthy results at home. Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your own bangs:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair
  2. Section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape
  3. Use a comb to comb the hair straight down
  4. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the outside and working inward
  5. Trim the bangs to your desired length
  6. Use a texturizing spray or scissors to add texture and movement

Tips for Maintaining Your Bangs

Once you've got your youthful bangs, it's important to maintain them to keep them looking fresh and youthful. Here are some tips for maintaining your bangs:

  • Trim your bangs every four to six weeks to keep them looking neat and tidy
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and movement
  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and prevent greasiness
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent them from becoming oily or frizzy

FAQs

Do bangs make you look younger?

Yes, bangs can make you look younger if you choose the right style for your face shape and hair type. Shorter bangs that hit above the eyebrows can create a youthful look, but longer, face-framing bangs can also provide a youthful glow without as much upkeep.

What type of bangs are best for a round face?

For a round face, longer, side-swept bangs that will elongate your face are best. Avoid blunt, straight-across fringe that will make your face look wider.

How do you style bangs?

Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and movement. Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and prevent greasiness. Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent them from becoming oily or frizzy.

Hair Care for Youthful Bangs

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is key to maintaining youthful bangs. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect youthful bangs that will make you look and feel younger. Whether you choose shorter, blunt bangs or longer, face-framing fringe, the key is to choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair type. So go ahead and embrace the power of youthful bangs!
