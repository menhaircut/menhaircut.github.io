Best Short Haircuts For Diamond Shaped Face A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Oliver Nov 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you one of many women with a diamond-shaped face wondering what the best short haircuts are for your unique features? Look no further! Description of Diamond-Shaped Face

A diamond-shaped face is characterized by a narrow forehead and jawline, with the widest point of the face being the cheekbones. This face shape is often considered the most unique and striking, as it can be quite angular and dramatic. The goal of choosing a short haircut for a diamond-shaped face is to create balance by softening the angles and elongating the face.

How to Determine if You Have a Diamond-Shaped Face

If you’re unsure whether you have a diamond-shaped face, there are a few key features to look for:

  • High cheekbones that are the widest part of the face
  • A narrow forehead and jawline
  • A pointed chin
  • Sometimes a widow’s peak hairline

If you have these features, you likely have a diamond-shaped face!

Best Short Haircuts for Diamond-Shaped Faces

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to choose a flattering short haircut! Here are some of the best options:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short haircut that can look great on diamond-shaped faces. By keeping the hair close to the head, a pixie cut can elongate the face and draw attention to the eyes. Consider adding some layers to soften the look and create movement.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is another versatile short haircut that can work well for diamond-shaped faces. Try a chin-length bob with layers around the face to add volume and balance out the cheekbones. Or, opt for a longer bob that falls just below the jawline.

Layered Crop

A layered crop is a fun and edgy short haircut that can complement a diamond-shaped face. Ask your stylist for choppy layers around the crown of the head and textured bangs to balance out the cheekbones.

Side-Swept Bangs

If you’re not ready to commit to a full haircut, consider adding side-swept bangs to your current style. This can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face and balance out the cheekbones.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Diamond-Shaped Faces

Once you’ve chosen a short haircut that flatters your diamond-shaped face, it’s important to know how to style it. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair
  3. Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, directing it away from your face
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or waves
  5. Apply a small amount of styling wax or pomade to your fingertips and use it to define the ends of your hair
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to set your style

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Short haircuts can require more maintenance than longer styles, so here are some tips to keep your haircut looking great:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your shape
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your style between washes

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Diamond-Shaped Faces

What if I don’t want to cut my hair short?

If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers around the face to balance out your cheekbones. Avoid adding volume to the top of your head, as this can make your face appear wider.

Can I still wear bangs with a diamond-shaped face?

Absolutely! Side-swept bangs can be a great way to balance out your cheekbones and create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

Hair Care Tips for Diamond-Shaped Faces

Lastly, here are some general hair care tips for diamond-shaped faces:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy
  • Consider using a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush to prevent breakage
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair

Now that you’ve learned about the best short haircuts for diamond-shaped faces, it’s time to book your next appointment with your stylist! Remember to choose a style that flatters your unique features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
