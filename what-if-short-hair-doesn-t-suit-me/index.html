What If Short Hair Doesn t Suit Me

Written by Bardi Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair has been a popular trend for a while now. Many people have opted for a short haircut, and it looks amazing on them. However, what if you have recently cut your hair short, and it doesn't suit you? Don't worry! In this article, we will discuss what to do when short hair doesn't suit you.

Description

Short hair is a bold and daring choice that can completely transform your look. However, not everyone can pull off the short hair look. Some people find that short hair doesn't suit their face shape, hair texture, or personal style. It can be frustrating to get a haircut that doesn't make you feel confident and beautiful. But the good news is that there are solutions to this problem.

How to

If you have recently cut your hair short and don't like the result, the first thing you should do is talk to your hair stylist. They can help you identify what went wrong and suggest ways to fix it. You might need to add some layers, change the length or texture of your hair, or try a different hair color to make it work for you.

If you're not satisfied with your hairstylist's suggestions, you can always seek a second opinion. Look for a reputable hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts. They can give you a fresh perspective and offer new ideas for your hair. You can also look for inspiration online and see how other people with similar face shapes and hair textures style their short hair.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can take if you feel that short hair doesn't suit you:

Step 1: Book a consultation with your hairstylist.

Explain your concerns and ask for their professional opinion.

Step 2: Consider a different style or length.

Discuss with your hairstylist what other options are available to you. They might suggest adding layers, changing the length or texture, or trying a different hair color.

Step 3: Seek a second opinion.

If you're not satisfied with your hairstylist's suggestions, look for another hairstylist who specializes in short haircuts.

Step 4: Look for inspiration online.

Check out social media platforms like Instagram, Pinterest, and TikTok for short hair inspiration. Look for people with similar face shapes and hair textures to yours and see how they style their hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make short hair work for you:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories like headbands, hair clips, and scarves to add some personality to your short hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for short hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Practice good hair care habits like washing your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling, and avoiding harsh chemicals.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what to do when short hair doesn't suit you:

Q: How do I know if short hair suits me?

A: Short hair can suit anyone, regardless of their face shape, hair texture, or personal style. However, it's important to find a haircut that flatters your features and complements your personality.

Q: Can I grow out my short hair?

A: Yes, you can grow out your short hair if you don't like the result. It might take some time, but with regular trims and proper hair care, you can achieve your desired length.

Q: Should I try a different hair color?

A: Changing your hair color can be a great way to freshen up your look and make your short haircut work for you. Consider consulting with your hairstylist to find the right hair color for your skin tone and face shape.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for short hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as it can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or cream before blow-drying or using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut's shape and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes that can damage your hair and scalp.

Remember that short hair is a bold and daring choice that can completely transform your look. However, it's important to find a haircut that flatters your features and complements your personality. If you're not satisfied with your short hair, don't hesitate to seek a professional's opinion or experiment with different styles and hair care products.
