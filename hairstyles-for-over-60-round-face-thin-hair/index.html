Hairstyles for Over 60 Round Face Thin Hair: Tips, Ideas and Inspiration

As you get older, you may find that your hair starts to thin out and lose volume. This can be especially challenging if you have a round face, as you want to find a hairstyle that will flatter your features and make you feel confident. If you're looking for inspiration, we've put together a list of hairstyles for over 60 round face thin hair. From short bobs to long layers, there are plenty of options to choose from.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Round Face and Thin Hair

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to keep your face shape in mind. If you have a round face, you'll want to look for styles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, as this will help elongate your face. You'll also want to avoid styles that are too short or too long, as these can make your face appear even rounder. Here are a few tips:

  • Choose a style that adds volume to the crown of your head
  • Avoid styles that are too short or too long
  • Consider adding layers to give your hair more movement and texture
  • Try a side-swept fringe to draw attention to your eyes
  • Consider adding highlights to give your hair more dimension

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen a hairstyle, it's important to know how to style it to get the best results. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess moisture
  3. Add a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots of your hair
  4. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume at the crown
  5. If you have a fringe, blow dry it to the side to create a side-swept look
  6. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and movement to your hair
  7. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

As you age, it's important to take extra care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Limit the use of heat styling tools to avoid damaging your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated
  • Consider taking a hair supplement to promote hair growth

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a round face with thin hair?

Short bobs, long layers, and side-swept fringes are all great options for a round face with thin hair.

What hair color is best for thin hair?

Adding highlights or lowlights can give thin hair more dimension and make it appear thicker.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Generally, it's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care Products for Thin Hair

If you have thin hair, it's important to use products that will add volume and texture without weighing your hair down. Here are a few of our favorite products:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Root lifting spray or mousse
  • Texturizing spray
  • Hairspray
  • Dry shampoo (for days when you don't want to wash your hair)

There you have it, our guide to hairstyles for over 60 round face thin hair. With the right style and a little bit of care, you can have healthy, voluminous hair that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
