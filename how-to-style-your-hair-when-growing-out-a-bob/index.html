How To Style Your Hair When Growing Out A Bob

Written by Petter Oct 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you growing out your bob and struggling with how to style your hair during the awkward in-between stages? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks on how to style your hair when growing out a bob. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Your Hair When Growing Out a Bob
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

How to Style Your Hair When Growing Out a Bob

When growing out a bob, there are a few things to keep in mind when it comes to styling:

  • Be patient – hair grows about half an inch per month, so it can take a while to grow out
  • Experiment with different styles – try out different hairdos to find what works best for you
  • Be gentle – avoid using heat tools and harsh chemicals that can damage your hair and slow down the growth process

Now that you know what to keep in mind, let's talk about some specific styles you can try.

1. Braids and Updos

One of the easiest ways to style your hair when growing out a bob is to braid it or put it up in an updo. Braids are a great way to keep your hair out of your face and add some texture to your hair. You can try a simple three-strand braid or experiment with more intricate braids like fishtail or Dutch braids. Updos like buns and top knots are also great options for growing out a bob. They're easy to do and look chic and polished.

2. Half-Up, Half-Down

If you don't want to put all your hair up, try a half-up, half-down style. This style is perfect for those in-between stages when your hair is too long to wear down but too short to put up. Simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a clip or hair tie.

3. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great style for any length of hair, including growing out a bob. To achieve this look, start by applying a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair. Then, use a curling iron or wand to curl sections of your hair. Once all your hair is curled, run your fingers through it to loosen the curls and create a beachy, tousled look.

Step-by-Step Guide

To help you style your hair when growing out a bob, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Choose a style that works best for you – braids, updos, half-up, half-down, beachy waves, etc.
  3. Apply any necessary products like texturizing spray, mousse, or hairspray
  4. Follow the steps for your chosen style
  5. Finish with a light spritz of hairspray to keep your style in place

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you style your hair when growing out a bob:

  • Invest in some hair accessories like clips, headbands, and scrunchies to help you style your hair
  • Try out different partings to create different looks
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends

FAQs

Q: How long does it take to grow out a bob?

A: Hair grows about half an inch per month, so it can take anywhere from six months to a year to grow out a bob.

Q: Can I use heat tools when growing out a bob?

A: While it's not recommended to use heat tools on a regular basis, you can still use them occasionally. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid using high heat settings.

Q: What are some good hair care products for growing out a bob?

A: Some good hair care products for growing out a bob include texturizing spray, mousse, hairspray, and hair oil.

Hair Care Tips and Products

When growing out a bob, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and promote growth. Here are some hair care tips and products to try:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's free of sulfates and parabens
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to deeply condition your hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Try a hair growth serum or oil to promote hair growth

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your hair with confidence while growing out your bob. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles until you find what works best for you. Happy styling!
