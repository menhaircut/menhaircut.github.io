How To Use Revlon Volumizer For Fuller Hair

Written by Petter Oct 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of flat, lifeless hair? Revlon Volumizer might be the solution you're looking for! This multi-purpose styling tool can help you achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home. In this article, we will go over how to use Revlon Volumizer step by step, tips for best results, hair care tips, and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Use Revlon Volumizer
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Best Results
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Use Revlon Volumizer

Revlon Volumizer is a two-in-one tool that combines a round brush and a hairdryer. It helps dry and style your hair at the same time, making it a quick and easy solution for a voluminous blowout. Before using the Volumizer, make sure your hair is clean and dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to use Revlon Volumizer:

  1. Divide your hair into sections using clips.
  2. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up.
  3. Take a section of your hair and place the Volumizer brush underneath it.
  4. Hold the brush in place and pull it slowly through your hair, towards the ends.
  5. Repeat until the section is dry and smooth.
  6. Move on to the next section and repeat.
  7. Finish with a blast of cool air to set the style.

Remember to be patient and work in small sections for best results. You can also experiment with different brush sizes and heat settings to achieve your desired look.

Tips for Best Results

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your Revlon Volumizer:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Start with the lowest heat setting and increase as needed.
  • For extra volume, use the Volumizer on damp hair and add a volumizing mousse.
  • Use the cool shot button to set your style and add shine.
  • Clean the brush regularly to prevent buildup and maintain performance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I use Revlon Volumizer on wet hair?

A: No, it is recommended to use Revlon Volumizer on clean and dry hair.

Q: Can Revlon Volumizer be used on all hair types?

A: Yes, Revlon Volumizer can be used on all hair types, but you may need to adjust the heat and speed settings to suit your hair's needs.

Q: How often should I clean my Revlon Volumizer?

A: It is recommended to clean your Revlon Volumizer after every use to maintain performance and prevent buildup.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to using Revlon Volumizer, here are some general hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and voluminous:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

With these tips and the Revlon Volumizer, you can achieve fuller, voluminous hair at home. Remember to take care of your hair and use the Volumizer safely to prevent damage. Happy styling!
