How To Choose The Right Red Hair Color For Your Skin Tone

Written by Eveline Jun 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you thinking of switching up your hair color and going for a bold red? Red hair can be a stunning and eye-catching choice, but it's important to pick a shade that complements your skin tone. With so many different shades of red to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. In this article, we'll walk you through how to choose the right red hair color for your skin tone, so you can feel confident and beautiful with your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Skin Tone
  • Best Red Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones
  • Best Red Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

Determining Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right red hair color is to determine your skin tone. Your skin tone is the natural color of your skin, and it is categorized as either warm, cool, or neutral. Here's how to determine your skin tone:

  • Warm skin tone: If you have warm undertones, your skin will have a yellow, peachy, or golden hue. Your veins will appear greenish, and you typically tan easily.
  • Cool skin tone: If you have cool undertones, your skin will have a pink or blue undertone. Your veins will appear blue, and you typically burn easily.
  • Neutral skin tone: If you have a neutral skin tone, your skin will have a balance of both warm and cool undertones. Your veins may appear blue-green.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can choose a red hair color that will complement and enhance your natural complexion.

Best Red Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm undertones, you want to choose a red hair color that has warm or golden tones. Here are some of the best red hair colors for warm skin tones:

  • Copper
  • Auburn
  • Ginger
  • Golden red

These shades will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural warmth. Avoid red hair colors that are too cool or have blue or purple undertones, as they can clash with your warm skin tone.

Best Red Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool undertones, you want to choose a red hair color that has cool or blue undertones. Here are some of the best red hair colors for cool skin tones:

  • Burgundy
  • Cherry red
  • Plum
  • Mahogany

These shades will complement your skin tone and enhance your natural coolness. Avoid red hair colors that are too warm or have yellow or orange undertones, as they can clash with your cool skin tone.

Best Red Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you're lucky! You can pull off almost any shade of red. Here are some of the best red hair colors for neutral skin tones:

  • Deep burgundy
  • Scarlet
  • Cinnamon
  • Ruby

Experiment with different shades of red to find the one that best complements your natural complexion.

Tips for Maintaining Your Red Hair Color

Once you've chosen your perfect shade of red, it's important to take care of your hair to keep your color looking vibrant and fresh. Here are some tips for maintaining your red hair color:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for color-treated hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to too much heat, such as blow drying or flat ironing, as this can also fade the color.
  • Consider using a color-depositing shampoo or conditioner to refresh your color between salon visits.

With proper care, your red hair color can look beautiful and vibrant for weeks to come.

FAQs

What if I have a lot of redness in my skin?

If you have a lot of redness in your skin, you may want to avoid red hair colors that are too warm or have orange undertones, as they can exacerbate the redness in your skin. Instead, opt for a cooler red hair color with blue undertones.

Can I go from blonde to red in one salon visit?

It's possible to go from blonde to red in one salon visit, but it depends on the condition of your hair and the shade of red you're going for. Going from blonde to a dark red may be easier, but going for a lighter or brighter red may require multiple salon visits to achieve the desired result.

Can I dye my hair red at home?

It is possible to dye your hair red at home, but it can be tricky to get the shade right and avoid damaging your hair. We recommend consulting with a professional stylist to get the best results.

Choosing the right red hair color for your skin tone can be a fun and exciting process. With these tips and guidelines, you can confidently choose a shade of red that complements your natural complexion and enhances your beauty.
