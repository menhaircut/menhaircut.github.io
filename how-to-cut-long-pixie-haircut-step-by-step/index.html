How To Cut Long Pixie Haircut Step By Step

Written by Frank Jun 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering getting a long pixie haircut? It’s a stylish and modern look that can be versatile and low-maintenance. But before you head to the salon, you may want to learn how to cut long pixie haircut step by step. In this post, we’ll go through the process of cutting a long pixie haircut and give you some tips and tricks along the way.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Long Pixie Haircut
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long pixie haircut is a shorter hair style that is longer than a traditional pixie cut. It typically features longer layers on the top and shorter layers on the sides and back. This haircut can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture, and can be worn with or without bangs. It’s a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style that is easy to style and looks great.

How to Cut Long Pixie Haircut

If you’re ready to take the plunge and cut your hair into a long pixie haircut, there are a few things you should know before you start. First, it’s important to have the right tools on hand. You’ll need a pair of sharp hairdressing scissors, a comb, and some hair clips. You may also want to have a spray bottle of water on hand to keep your hair damp while you cut.

Step by Step

Step 1: Divide Your Hair

Start by dividing your hair into sections. Use the comb to create a part down the middle of your head, then divide your hair into four sections: two on the top and two on the bottom. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Step 2: Cut the Back

Start with the back section of your hair. Use the comb to create a horizontal part at the nape of your neck, then use the scissors to cut the hair below the part. Continue to create horizontal parts and cut the hair in small sections until you reach the desired length. Be sure to cut each section evenly to maintain a uniform length.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the side sections of your hair. Use the comb to create a diagonal part from the top of your ear to the back of your head. Comb the hair forward, then use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Continue to create diagonal parts and cut the hair until you reach the desired length.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Finally, it’s time to cut the top section of your hair. Use the comb to create a horizontal part at the crown of your head, then comb the hair forward. Use the scissors to cut the hair in a straight line, working from the back of your head to the front. Be sure to cut the hair evenly to maintain a uniform length.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, you can style it as desired. You can use a blow dryer and a round brush to add volume, or use a flat iron to create a sleek and straight look. You can also add texture and definition with hair products like mousse, wax, or pomade.

Tips

  • Start with dry hair to get a more accurate length
  • Use a mirror to check the length and shape of your cut
  • Don't hesitate to ask for help from a friend or professional hair stylist
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you

FAQ

How often should I get my long pixie haircut trimmed?

It's recommended to get your long pixie haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your cut.

Can I add bangs to my long pixie haircut?

Yes, you can customize your long pixie haircut by adding bangs. Talk to your hair stylist about the best bangs for your face shape and hair texture.

Hair Care

Now that you’ve learned how to cut long pixie haircut step by step, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. You can also use hair masks or oils to nourish your hair and keep it shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently rock a long pixie haircut and enjoy a stylish and low-maintenance look.
