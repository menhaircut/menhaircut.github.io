Slicked Back Top Short Sides A Stylish Haircut For Men

Written by Aprilia Sep 23, 2023 · 3 min read

When it comes to men's hairstyles, the slicked back top short sides look has been gaining popularity in recent years. This look is characterized by a sleek and polished top that is slicked back, with short sides that are faded or tapered.

Description

The slicked back top short sides hairstyle is a versatile and stylish look that can be worn by men of all ages. It is a great option for those who want a clean and polished look that is also low maintenance.

The key to achieving this look is to keep the top of your hair longer and slick it back using a high-quality hair gel or pomade. The sides should be cut short and either faded or tapered to give a clean and sharp look.

How to Achieve the Slicked Back Top Short Sides Look

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the slicked back top short sides look:

Step 1: Visit a Professional Barber

The first step in achieving this look is to visit a professional barber who is experienced in cutting men's hair. Your barber will be able to assess your hair type and recommend the best cut and style for you.

Step 2: Grow Out the Top

The top of your hair should be longer than the sides in order to achieve the slicked back look. Let it grow out until it reaches the desired length.

Step 3: Choose the Right Hair Products

Invest in high-quality hair products like a strong-hold gel or pomade to slick back the top of your hair. Make sure to choose a product that is suited for your hair type.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Apply the hair product to the top of your hair and use a comb to slick it back. Use clippers or scissors to trim the sides to the desired length and style them in a fade or taper.

Tips for Maintaining the Slicked Back Top Short Sides Look

Here are some tips to help you maintain your slicked back top short sides look:

  • Use a high-quality hair product that is suited for your hair type
  • Regularly visit your barber for touch-ups
  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup of product
  • Use a comb to keep your hair looking sleek and polished

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the slicked back top short sides hairstyle:

Q: Is this look suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, the slicked back top short sides look can be achieved on all hair types.

Q: Is this a low maintenance hairstyle?

A: Yes, this is a relatively low maintenance hairstyle that requires regular touch-ups with a barber.

Q: How often should I wash my hair with this hairstyle?

A: It is recommended to wash your hair every other day to prevent buildup of product.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, including blow dryers and straighteners
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water

The slicked back top short sides hairstyle is a stylish and versatile look that can be worn by men of all ages. By following these tips and maintaining a healthy hair care routine, you can keep your hair looking its best.
