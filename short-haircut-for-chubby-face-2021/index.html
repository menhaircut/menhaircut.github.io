Short Haircut For Chubby Face 2021 Best Styles For A Flattering Look

Written by Eveline Sep 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a chubby face, finding a hairstyle that flatters your features can be a daunting task. However, with the right haircut, you can accentuate your best features and look your best self. In 2021, short haircuts are all the rage, and they are perfect for those with a chubby face. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for a chubby face, how to style them, and some tips and tricks to keep your hair looking great.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for a Chubby Face

Before we get into the best short haircuts, it's essential to understand what works for a chubby face. The ideal haircut should create an illusion of length and add volume to the top while slimming down the sides of the face.

The best short haircuts for a chubby face are:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut that is perfect for those with a chubby face. It creates an illusion of height and volume, making your face look slim and elongated. This cut works best on those with a petite frame.

The Bob Cut

A bob cut is another excellent short haircut for a chubby face. It is versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape. The key is to keep the length just below the chin and add layers to create volume on top.

The Shaggy Cut

The shaggy cut is a fun and playful short haircut that works well on a chubby face. It adds volume to the top and creates a slimming effect on the sides of the face. This cut is perfect for those who want to add a bit of edginess to their look.

How to Style Short Haircut for a Chubby Face

Styling short haircuts for a chubby face is relatively easy. You can create different looks by using styling products and tools. Here are some tips to get you started:

Use a Volumizing Mousse

A volumizing mousse is a must-have for those with a chubby face. It adds volume to the top and creates a slimming effect on the sides. Apply the mousse to damp hair and blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume on top.

Invest in a Good Flat Iron

A good flat iron can do wonders for your short haircut. Use it to create a sleek and polished look that slims down your face. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Add Some Texture

Adding texture to your short haircut can create a more stylish and edgy look. You can use a texturizing spray or salt spray to create a messy and tousled look that works well on a chubby face.

FAQ

Can I have bangs with a short haircut for a chubby face?

Yes, you can have bangs with a short haircut for a chubby face. However, make sure to keep them long and side-swept to create a slimming effect on the face.

What is the best way to maintain a short haircut for a chubby face?

Regular trims are essential to maintain a short haircut for a chubby face. Visit your hairstylist every four to six weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and polished. You can also use a dry shampoo to extend the life of your haircut between washes.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircut for a Chubby Face

Short haircuts require minimal maintenance, but they still need some care to keep them looking great. Here are some hair care tips for a short haircut for a chubby face:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is specifically formulated for your hair type. Avoid shampooing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Condition Your Hair

Condition your hair every time you wash it to keep it soft and manageable. Use a lightweight conditioner that won't weigh down your hair.

Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools every day, as this can damage your hair. If you must use heat, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Short haircuts can be flattering and stylish for those with a chubby face. Choose the right haircut that suits your face shape and style it using the tips and tricks we have discussed. With the right care, you can keep your short haircut looking great and feel confident in your own skin.
