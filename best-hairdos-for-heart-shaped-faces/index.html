Best Hairdos For Heart Shaped Faces Tips Ideas And Hair Care

Heart shaped faces are one of the most beautiful and feminine face shapes. They are characterized by a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have a heart shaped face, you have a lot of options when it comes to hairstyles. In this article, we'll explore the best hairdos for heart shaped faces, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hairdos for heart shaped faces, it's important to determine if you actually have a heart shaped face. Here's how you can do that:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face.
  2. Look at the shape of your face. A heart shaped face will have a wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin.
  3. Measure your face. Heart shaped faces are typically longer than they are wide.

If your face matches these characteristics, then you have a heart shaped face!

Tips for Choosing a Hairstyle for Heart Shaped Faces

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a heart shaped face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Balance is key. You want to balance out the width of your forehead with the narrowness of your chin.
  • Soften your jawline. Heart shaped faces tend to have a pointy chin, so you want to choose a hairstyle that softens that area.
  • Highlight your cheekbones. Heart shaped faces often have high cheekbones, so you want to choose a hairstyle that highlights this feature.

Best Hairdos for Heart Shaped Faces

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, let's explore some of the best hairdos for heart shaped faces:

1. Long layers

Long layers are great for heart shaped faces because they help to create balance. The layers add movement and volume around the chin area, which helps to balance out the width of the forehead.

2. Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great choice for heart shaped faces because they help to soften the forehead and balance out the chin area. The bangs should be swept to the side, rather than straight across the forehead, to create a flattering look.

3. Bob

A bob is a great option for heart shaped faces because it helps to balance out the forehead and chin. The length of the bob should hit at the jawline, which helps to soften the chin area.

4. Pixie

If you're feeling daring, a pixie cut can be a great option for heart shaped faces. The short length helps to highlight the cheekbones and balance out the chin area. However, make sure to consult with your stylist to find a pixie cut that flatters your face shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair and a heart shaped face, you can still rock the above hairdos. Just make sure to work with your natural curls and choose a cut that flatters your face shape.

Can I still wear my hair straight?

Of course! The above hairdos are just suggestions. If you prefer to wear your hair straight, just make sure to choose a cut that flatters your face shape.

What if I have a round face instead of a heart shaped face?

If you have a round face, check out our article on the best hairdos for round faces.

Hair Care Tips for Heart Shaped Faces

To keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

By following these tips and choosing a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, you'll be sure to rock a beautiful and confident look!
