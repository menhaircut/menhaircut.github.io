How To Talk To Your Barber About Your Haircut A Guide For Men

Written by Oliver Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of leaving the barber’s chair with a haircut you didn’t want? Do you struggle to communicate your desired style to your barber? Talking to your barber about your haircut can be intimidating, but it doesn’t have to be. In this guide, we’ll give you tips on how to effectively communicate with your barber to get the perfect haircut every time.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Talk to Your Barber
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Communicating with Your Barber
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Talk to Your Barber

Before you even step foot into the barbershop, it’s important to have a clear idea of what you want. Look through magazines or online for haircut inspiration. Once you have an idea of what you want, it’s time to talk to your barber.

When you first sit down in the chair, tell your barber what you’re looking for. Be specific and use descriptive words. If you want a “short back and sides with a textured top,” say that. If you’re not sure what you want, tell your barber what you don’t want. For example, “I don’t want it too short on the sides” or “I don’t want any clippers used on the top.”

It’s important to remember that your barber is a professional and wants to give you the best haircut possible. Don’t be afraid to ask questions or for their opinion. They may have suggestions or advice that you hadn’t considered.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you communicate with your barber:

  1. Have an idea of what you want before you go to the barbershop.
  2. Be specific and use descriptive words when telling your barber what you want.
  3. Don’t be afraid to ask questions or for your barber’s opinion.
  4. If you’re not sure what you want, tell your barber what you don’t want.
  5. During the haircut, communicate any concerns or changes you’d like to make.
  6. Ask your barber for styling tips and product recommendations.
  7. Thank your barber and tip them for their services.

Tips for Communicating with Your Barber

Here are some additional tips to help you communicate with your barber:

  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to show your barber.
  • Be honest and direct about what you want.
  • Don’t be afraid to speak up during the haircut if something doesn’t feel right.
  • Ask your barber for their opinion or suggestions.
  • Be open to trying new styles or techniques.

FAQs

How do I know if my barber understands what I want?

Repeat back to your barber what you’ve asked for to ensure they understand. If you’re still unsure, ask them to clarify or show you pictures of what they’re planning to do.

What if I’m not happy with my haircut?

Communicate your concerns with your barber. They may be able to make adjustments or offer a solution. If you’re still unhappy, try a different barber next time.

How much should I tip my barber?

It’s customary to tip your barber 15-20% of the cost of the haircut. If you’re particularly happy with the service, feel free to tip more.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you’ve got your perfect haircut, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that’s suited for your hair type.
  • Use styling products in moderation to avoid buildup and damage.
  • Visit your barber regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy.

Talking to your barber about your haircut doesn’t have to be a daunting task. By following these tips and guidelines, you can effectively communicate with your barber to get the perfect haircut every time.
