How To Slick Back Short Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your short natural hair getting in your face all the time? Slicking it back can be a great solution. Not only does it keep your hair out of your face, but it can also give you a chic and edgy look. In this article, we’ll show you how to slick back short natural hair in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Back Short Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back Short Natural Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slicked Back Short Natural Hair

How to Slick Back Short Natural Hair

Slicking back short natural hair may seem like a challenge, but it’s actually quite simple. All you need is a good styling product and a few minutes of your time.

What You’ll Need:

  • Styling product (gel, pomade, or wax)
  • Wide-tooth comb or brush

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with Clean and Moisturized Hair

Before slicking back your hair, make sure it’s clean and moisturized. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated. This will make it easier to style and prevent breakage.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair. Use gel, pomade, or wax depending on your preference. Start with a small amount and add more as needed. Too much product can make your hair look greasy or crunchy.

Step 3: Comb or Brush Your Hair Back

Using a wide-tooth comb or brush, comb or brush your hair back. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down to the nape of your neck. Make sure your hair is smooth and free of tangles.

Step 4: Smooth Out Any Flyaways

If you have any flyaways or frizz, use a small amount of styling product to smooth them out. Take a small amount of product and rub it between your palms. Then, gently smooth it over your hair to tame any flyaways.

Step 5: Set Your Style

Once you’ve slicked back your hair, use a light-hold hairspray to set your style. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your hair and spray lightly. This will keep your hair in place and prevent it from frizzing or falling out of place.

Tips for Slicking Back Short Natural Hair

Slicking back short natural hair can be tricky, but these tips can make the process easier:

  • Start with clean and moisturized hair to make styling easier.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to avoid weighing down your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Smooth out any flyaways with a small amount of styling product.
  • Use a light-hold hairspray to set your style and prevent frizz.

FAQs

Can I slick back short natural hair without using styling product?

It’s possible to slick back short natural hair without using styling product, but it may not hold as well. If you want a sleek and polished look, we recommend using a lightweight styling product like gel, pomade, or wax.

Can I slick back short natural hair if it’s curly?

Yes, you can slick back short natural hair if it’s curly. Use a styling product to help smooth out your curls and a wide-tooth comb or brush to avoid damaging your hair. You may need to use more product to hold your curls in place.

How often should I slick back my short natural hair?

It’s best to avoid slicking back your short natural hair every day. This can cause breakage and damage to your hair. Instead, try slicking it back once or twice a week and wear your hair down for the rest of the week.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Short Natural Hair

When you slick back your short natural hair, it’s important to take good care of it to prevent breakage and damage. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Avoid slicking back your hair too tightly to prevent tension and breakage.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons or curling irons to prevent heat damage.
  • Wear a satin or silk scarf or bonnet at night to protect your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily slick back your short natural hair for a chic and polished look. Whether you’re heading to work or a special event, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads.
