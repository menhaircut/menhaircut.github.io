Mens Short Hairstyles 2022 A Guide To The Latest Trends

Written by Eveline Aug 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new look for 2022, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll take a look at the latest trends in mens short hairstyles 2022. From buzz cuts to textured crops, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Description

Mens short hairstyles have been around for decades, but they continue to evolve with each passing year. In 2022, we're seeing a return to classic looks with a modern twist. Think textured crops, buzz cuts, and sleek side parts. These styles are low maintenance and easy to maintain, making them perfect for busy guys on the go.

How to Achieve Mens Short Hairstyles 2022

If you're ready to try out a new look, here's how to achieve the latest mens short hairstyles:

1. Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a timeless classic that never goes out of style. To achieve this look, you'll need to trim your hair with clippers on the shortest setting. The result is a low-maintenance, no-fuss style that looks great on just about everyone.

2. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a popular style that's perfect for guys with thick or wavy hair. To achieve this look, you'll need to keep your hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. Use a texturizing product to create a messy, tousled look.

3. Side Part

The side part is a classic look that's making a comeback in 2022. To achieve this style, you'll need to keep your hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. Use a comb to create a side part, and then use a pomade to keep your hair in place.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the latest mens short hairstyles:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and dry your hair before you start styling. This will ensure that your hair is clean and free of any product buildup.

2. Choose your style.

Decide which style you want to try out, whether it's a buzz cut, textured crop, or side part.

3. Trim your hair.

Use clippers to trim your hair to the desired length. Be sure to use the appropriate guard size for the look you're trying to achieve.

4. Style your hair.

Use a texturizing product or pomade to create your desired style. Follow the instructions on the product packaging for best results.

Tips for Maintaining Mens Short Hairstyles

Here are a few tips for maintaining mens short hairstyles:

1. Get regular trims.

To keep your hair looking neat and tidy, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

2. Use the right products.

Use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve.

3. Protect your hair from the elements.

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other environmental factors.

FAQs

1. What is the most popular mens short hairstyle for 2022?

The textured crop is one of the most popular mens short hairstyles for 2022.

2. Can I achieve these styles at home?

Yes, you can achieve these styles at home with the right tools and products.

3. How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

Hair Care Tips

Here are a few hair care tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

1. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type.

For example, if you have dry hair, look for a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Avoid using hot tools.

Blow-drying, straightening, and curling your hair can damage it over time. If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray.

3. Protect your hair from chlorine.

If you swim regularly, use a swim cap or apply a leave-in conditioner before entering the water to protect your hair from chlorine.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the latest mens short hairstyles with ease. Remember to choose a style that works for your hair type and face shape, and don't be afraid to experiment with different products and techniques. Happy styling!
