How To Look Good With Side Bangs Tips Tricks And Haircare

Written by Christine Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Side bangs, also known as side-swept bangs, are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of sophistication and flair to your look. Whether you have long hair, medium hair, or short hair, side bangs can be a great way to change up your style without having to make a drastic cut. In this article, we will discuss how to look good with side bangs and provide you with tips, tricks, and haircare advice to keep your side bangs looking fabulous all day long.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Side Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Side Bangs
  • Tips for Looking Good with Side Bangs

How to Get Side Bangs

If you're considering getting side bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure that side bangs will flatter your face shape. Generally, side bangs look best on oval, heart-shaped, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid side bangs as they can make your face appear wider.

Next, decide how long you want your side bangs to be. The length of your bangs will depend on your personal style and the length of your hair. For longer hair, you may want to go with longer side bangs that blend with the rest of your hair. For shorter hair, shorter side bangs may be more appropriate.

Finally, find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. Be clear about the length and style of the side bangs you want, and make sure that you're comfortable with the cut before the hairstylist begins.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Side Bangs

Once you have your side bangs cut, it's important to know how to style them. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling side bangs:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a round brush, blow-dry your bangs while pulling them to the side.
  3. If necessary, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or curls.
  4. Apply a small amount of hair serum or oil to your bangs to add shine and tame any flyaways.

For a more voluminous look, you can also tease your bangs slightly at the roots with a teasing comb.

Tips for Looking Good with Side Bangs

Here are some tips for looking good with side bangs:

  • Make sure to trim your bangs regularly to prevent them from becoming too long.
  • Experiment with different parting positions to find the one that flatters your face shape the most.
  • Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs from becoming greasy or oily throughout the day.
  • If your bangs become too long, you can clip them back with bobby pins or a hair clip.

FAQs About Side Bangs

Q: How often do I need to trim my side bangs?

A: It's recommended that you trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from becoming too long.

Q: Can I wear my side bangs straight down?

A: Yes, you can wear your side bangs straight down, but be aware that this style may require more maintenance to keep them from falling into your eyes.

Q: Can I wear side bangs with curly hair?

A: Yes, side bangs can look great with curly hair, but you may need to use a flat iron or blow dryer to smooth out your bangs and make them more manageable.

Haircare Tips for Side Bangs

Here are some haircare tips to keep your side bangs looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and keep your bangs clean.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or flat ironing your bangs to prevent damage.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much throughout the day to prevent them from becoming greasy or oily.
  • Use a high-quality hair serum or oil to add shine and keep your bangs looking smooth.

With these tips, tricks, and haircare advice, you can look great with side bangs and keep them looking fabulous all day long. Whether you're going for a casual look or a more formal style, side bangs can be a great way to add some personality to your hair and make a statement.
