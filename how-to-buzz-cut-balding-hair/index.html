How To Buzz Cut Balding Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Oct 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are experiencing hair loss, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that suits you. However, a buzz cut is a great option for those with balding hair. In this article, we will guide you on how to buzz cut balding hair.

How to Buzz Cut Balding Hair

Before you start, ensure that you have the right tools for the job. You will need a clipper, a guard, and a mirror. Once you have everything ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

It is essential to start with clean and dry hair. Use a mild shampoo to wash your hair and then dry it completely.

Step 2: Choose the right guard

The guard determines the length of your hair. If you want a shorter buzz cut, choose a smaller guard. If you want a longer buzz cut, choose a larger guard.

Step 3: Start cutting

Start cutting your hair from the sides and back, moving towards the top of your head. Use the clipper in an upward motion to achieve an even cut.

Step 4: Trim the top of your head

Use the same guard size that you used for the sides and back of your head to trim the top of your head.

Step 5: Clean up the neckline

Use the clipper without a guard to clean up the neckline. Start from the middle of the neck and move towards the ears.

Step 6: Check for uneven spots

Use a mirror to check for any uneven spots. If you find any, use the clipper to even them out.

Step 7: Clean up

Once you are done, take a shower to wash off any remaining hair. Use a towel to dry your head.

Tips for Buzz Cutting Balding Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when buzz cutting balding hair:

  • Use a comb to guide the clipper for a more even cut.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Ask for help if you are not comfortable doing it yourself.

FAQ about Buzz Cutting Balding Hair

How short should I buzz cut my balding hair?

The length of your buzz cut depends on your personal preference. However, a shorter cut can help conceal bald spots.

Can I buzz cut balding hair without a guard?

Yes, you can. However, it is recommended to use a guard to achieve an even cut.

Will buzz cutting my hair make it grow back thicker?

No, buzz cutting your hair will not make it grow back thicker.

Hair Care Tips for Balding Hair

If you have balding hair, it is essential to take care of your scalp. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo to avoid irritating your scalp.
  • Massage your scalp to promote blood flow.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals.
  • Protect your scalp from the sun by wearing a hat or using sunscreen.

In conclusion, buzz cutting balding hair can be a great way to embrace your hair loss. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect buzz cut. Remember to take care of your scalp to promote healthy hair growth.
