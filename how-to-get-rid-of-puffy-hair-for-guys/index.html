How To Get Rid Of Puffy Hair For Guys

Written by Luffy Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with puffy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to try and tame it. Whether it's due to humidity, genetics, or just the wrong haircut, puffy hair can make you look unkempt and unprofessional. Fortunately, there are steps you can take to get rid of puffy hair and achieve the sleek, stylish look you desire. In this article, we'll go over how to get rid of puffy hair for guys, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Description

Puffy hair is a common problem for many guys, but it can be especially frustrating for those who want to maintain a polished, professional look. Puffy hair can be caused by a variety of factors, including humidity, genetics, and the wrong haircut or styling products. Whatever the cause, puffy hair can be difficult to manage and often requires a careful approach to hair care and styling.

How to Get Rid of Puffy Hair for Guys

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in getting rid of puffy hair is to choose the right haircut. Generally, shorter haircuts work better for guys with puffy hair, as longer styles can weigh down the hair and make it look even more voluminous. Additionally, you may want to ask your barber or stylist to thin out your hair or use texturizing techniques to help reduce puffiness.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can also help reduce puffy hair. Look for products that are specifically designed to add volume or texture, as these can help keep your hair from looking flat or lifeless. Additionally, consider using a lightweight hair oil or serum to help control frizz and add shine.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can also help reduce puffiness. Start by towel-drying your hair, then use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair while using a brush to smooth it out. Be sure to keep the blow dryer about six inches away from your hair to avoid causing damage or making your hair look frizzy.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, you can style it to achieve the look you want. Consider using a pomade or wax to add definition and hold, or use a styling cream to add texture and volume. Work the product through your hair with your fingers or a comb, and be sure to distribute it evenly to avoid clumps or uneven texture.

Tips

When trying to get rid of puffy hair, there are a few tips you should keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo to help add body and texture.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products or products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and make it look even more puffy.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can genetics cause puffy hair?

A: Yes, genetics can play a role in puffy hair. If you have naturally thick or curly hair, you may be more prone to puffiness and frizz. However, by following the steps outlined in this article, you can still achieve a sleek, polished look.

Q: Can humidity make puffy hair worse?

A: Yes, humidity can definitely make puffy hair worse. To combat this, consider using a humidity-resistant hair product or wearing a hat or headband to help keep your hair in place.

Hair Care

When it comes to taking care of your hair, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often - aim for two to three times a week.
  • Use a conditioner to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons too often, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and steps, you can successfully get rid of puffy hair and achieve the sleek, stylish look you desire. Remember to be patient and experiment with different techniques and products until you find the right combination that works for you.
