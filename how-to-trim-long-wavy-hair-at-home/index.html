How To Trim Long Wavy Hair At Home

Written by Aprilia Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Trimming long wavy hair at home can seem daunting, but with a few tips and tricks, it can be done easily and effectively. In this blog post, we will go over how to trim long wavy hair at home in a relaxed language. We will cover everything from how to prepare your hair to the step-by-step process of trimming, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

  • How to Trim Long Wavy Hair at Home
  • Preparing your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Process

Preparing your Hair

Before trimming your long wavy hair, it is important to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup from your hair, making it easier to trim.

Once your hair is clean, dry it thoroughly with a towel. You can also use a blow dryer, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Next, brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots. If your hair is particularly knotty, you can use a detangling spray or leave-in conditioner to help.

Step-by-Step Process

Now that your hair is prepared, it's time to start trimming. Follow these steps carefully to ensure the best results:

Step 1: Section your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair ties or clips. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you don't miss any areas.

Step 2: Start with the Ends

Begin by trimming the ends of your hair. Hold your hair in a ponytail, and cut across the bottom in a straight line. Make sure to use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 3: Work your way Up

Once you have trimmed the ends, start working your way up the hair, section by section. Take small sections of hair and trim them to the desired length.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After trimming each section, check for evenness by holding the hair up and looking for any uneven spots. If you notice any, trim them until the hair is even.

Step 5: Trim the Bangs

If you have bangs, trim them last. Hold the bangs between your fingers and cut them to the desired length.

Tips for Trimming Long Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results when trimming your long wavy hair at home:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair
  • Trim small sections at a time to avoid cutting off too much hair
  • Check for evenness regularly
  • Don't trim your hair when it is wet, as it can lead to uneven results
  • Take your time and be patient

FAQ

How often should I trim my long wavy hair?

It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I trim my hair when it is wet?

No, it is not recommended to trim your hair when it is wet, as it can lead to uneven results. Wait until your hair is dry before trimming.

Can I trim my own bangs?

Yes, you can trim your own bangs. Follow the same steps as trimming the rest of your hair, but make sure to hold the bangs between your fingers and cut them to the desired length.

Hair Care after Trimming

After trimming your long wavy hair, it is important to take care of it properly to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips and the step-by-step process outlined in this blog post, you can easily and effectively trim your long wavy hair at home. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
