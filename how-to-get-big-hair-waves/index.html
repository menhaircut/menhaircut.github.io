How to Get Big Hair Waves: A Step-by-Step Guide

Are you tired of your flat, lifeless hair? Do you dream of having big, beautiful waves? Look no further because we’ve got you covered. In this article, we’ll show you how to get big hair waves in a few easy steps. Whether you have long or short hair, these tips will work for you.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Big Hair Waves
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Getting Big Hair Waves
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Get Big Hair Waves

To get big hair waves, you’ll need a few key tools and products. You’ll need a curling iron or wand, heat protectant spray, hair clips, and hairspray. Let’s get started!

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift at the roots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Clip each section with a hair clip to keep it out of the way.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a 1-inch section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron or wand. Hold the curl for 10-15 seconds and release. Continue curling each section until all of your hair is curled.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Curls

Once your curls have cooled down, brush them out with a paddle brush. This will create soft, voluminous waves.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your waves in place. You can also use a texturizing spray to add more volume and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

To make things easier, here’s a step-by-step guide to getting big hair waves:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section your hair
  3. Curl your hair
  4. Brush out your curls
  5. Finish with hairspray

Tips for Getting Big Hair Waves

Here are some tips to help you get the best results:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a larger barrel size for bigger waves
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair before blow drying
  • Curl your hair away from your face for a more flattering look
  • Don’t brush your curls too much or you’ll lose the shape
  • Use a light hold hairspray to avoid weighing down your hair

FAQs

Can I get big hair waves with short hair?

Yes, you can. Use a smaller curling iron or wand and focus on curling just the ends of your hair.

How long will my big hair waves last?

It depends on your hair type and the products you use. Generally, your waves should last anywhere from 1-3 days.

Can I use a straightener to create big hair waves?

Yes, you can. Simply twist your hair around the straightener and pull it through to create waves.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair

Now that you know how to get big hair waves, it’s time to try it out for yourself. With a little practice, you’ll be able to create beautiful, voluminous waves that will turn heads wherever you go!
