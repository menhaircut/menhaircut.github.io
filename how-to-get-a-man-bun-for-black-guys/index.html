How To Get A Man Bun For Black Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a black guy looking to rock a stylish man bun? Well, you're in luck because we've got you covered! In this article, we'll take you through a step-by-step guide on how to get a man bun for black guys. But before we dive into the 'how-to', let's first understand what a man bun is.

What is a Man Bun?

A man bun is a hairstyle where the hair is tied up in a bun on top of the head. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, with many celebrities and athletes sporting the look. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways, from a messy bun to a sleek and polished one.

How to Get a Man Bun for Black Guys

Getting a man bun for black guys is not very different from getting one for any other hair type. However, there are a few things you need to keep in mind, especially if you have coarse or curly hair. Here's the step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Grow Your Hair

The first step to getting a man bun is to grow your hair long enough to tie it up. Depending on your hair type and growth rate, this could take anywhere from 6 months to a year. However, you don't have to wait for your hair to reach a certain length to start styling it. You can start experimenting with different looks as your hair grows.

Step 2: Decide on the Type of Man Bun

There are many different types of man buns, each with its own unique style. You can choose from a messy bun, a low bun, a half-up bun, or a top knot. Decide on the type of man bun that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 3: Gather Your Supplies

To create a man bun, you'll need a few supplies, including a hair tie, a comb or brush, and some styling products. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to keep your hair in place.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair

Before you start tying your hair up, you need to comb it to remove any tangles or knots. This will make it easier to style your hair and create a neat and polished look.

Step 5: Tie Your Hair Up

Once your hair is combed, it's time to tie it up. To create a man bun, gather your hair at the crown of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with a hair tie, making sure it's tight enough to hold your hair in place.

Step 6: Style Your Bun

Once your bun is in place, you can style it to suit your personal taste. You can leave it messy for a relaxed look or use some styling products to create a sleek and polished look.

Tips for Getting a Man Bun for Black Guys

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect man bun:

  • Use a soft bristle brush to comb your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Apply some leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles as your hair grows to find the perfect look.

FAQs about Getting a Man Bun for Black Guys

Q: Can I get a man bun with short hair?

A: Unfortunately, it's not possible to create a man bun with short hair. You'll need to grow your hair long enough to tie it up.

Q: How long does it take to grow hair long enough for a man bun?

A: It can take anywhere from 6 months to a year to grow hair long enough for a man bun, depending on your hair type and growth rate.

Q: Do I need to use styling products to create a man bun?

A: While it's not necessary to use styling products, they can help keep your hair in place and create a polished look.

Hair Care for Black Guys with a Man Bun

Once you've got your man bun, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like relaxers and dyes, as they can weaken your hair.

With these tips and steps, you're now ready to rock a stylish man bun like a pro!
