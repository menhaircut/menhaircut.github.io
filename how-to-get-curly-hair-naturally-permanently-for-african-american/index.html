Description

Many African Americans have naturally curly hair, but some may want to enhance their curls or even permanently change their hair texture. Chemical treatments like relaxers and perms can be damaging to hair over time, so some people may want to try natural methods for getting curly hair. This article will provide tips on how to get curly hair naturally permanently for African Americans.

How to

There are several natural methods for getting curly hair permanently. One popular method is the use of perm rods, which are small cylindrical rollers that can be used to create curls in hair. Another method is the use of a straw set, which involves wrapping small sections of hair around drinking straws and securing them with bobby pins. Both of these methods can be done at home with the right tools and techniques.

Perm Rods

To use perm rods to get curly hair naturally permanently for African Americans, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are moisturizing and gentle.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections and wrap each section around a perm rod.
  4. Secure the rod with a hair clip or bobby pin.
  5. Let your hair air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  6. Remove the perm rods and style your curls as desired.

Straw Set

To use a straw set to get curly hair naturally permanently for African Americans, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are moisturizing and gentle.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections and wrap each section around a drinking straw.
  4. Secure the straw with a bobby pin.
  5. Let your hair air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  6. Remove the straws and style your curls as desired.

Step by step

The following is a step-by-step guide on how to get curly hair naturally permanently for African Americans:

  1. Choose a natural method for getting curly hair, such as perm rods or a straw set.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products that are moisturizing and gentle.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  4. Section your hair into small sections.
  5. Wrap each section around a perm rod or drinking straw, depending on the method you chose.
  6. Secure the rod or straw with a hair clip or bobby pin.
  7. Let your hair air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until completely dry.
  8. Remove the rods or straws and style your curls as desired.
  9. Repeat the process as needed to maintain your curly hair.
  10. Take care of your hair by using moisturizing products, avoiding heat styling, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get curly hair naturally permanently for African Americans:

  • Choose products that are formulated for curly hair and are free of sulfates and other harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase or wearing a satin bonnet.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet, as this will prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly hair naturally permanently for African Americans:

Can I get curly hair permanently without using chemicals?

Yes, you can use natural methods like perm rods and straw sets to get curly hair without using chemicals.

Will using perm rods or straw sets damage my hair?

When done properly, using perm rods or straw sets should not damage your hair. However, using them too often or leaving them in for too long can cause damage or breakage.

How often should I use perm rods or straw sets to maintain my curly hair?

This will depend on your hair type and how quickly your hair grows. As a general rule, you should aim to use perm rods or straw sets every 4-6 weeks to maintain your curly hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, curly hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Use products that are formulated for curly hair and are free of harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair at night by sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase or wearing a satin bonnet.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet, as this will prevent breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using natural methods like perm rods and straw sets, you can get curly hair naturally permanently for African Americans.
