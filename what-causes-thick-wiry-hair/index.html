What Causes Thick Wiry Hair

Written by Petter Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post where we will be discussing what causes thick wiry hair. Thick wiry hair can be a blessing or a curse depending on how you choose to style it. However, for those who don't appreciate the thickness and coarseness of their hair, it can be a source of frustration. In this article, we will be looking at what causes thick wiry hair and some tips on how to manage it.

Genetics

One of the main causes of thick wiry hair is genetics. If your parents or grandparents have thick hair, then it is likely that you inherited that trait. The thickness of your hair is determined by the size of your hair follicles. If your hair follicles are larger than average, then your hair is likely to be thicker and coarser.

Hormones

Hormones can also play a role in the thickness of your hair. Androgens, which are male hormones, can cause hair to grow thicker and coarser. Women also have androgens, but in smaller amounts. However, if there is an imbalance of hormones in the body, it can lead to hair becoming thicker and coarser than usual.

Health Conditions

Certain health conditions can also cause hair to become thick and wiry. For example, people with hypothyroidism may experience changes in their hair texture, including thickening and coarsening. Cushing's syndrome, which is caused by excess cortisol in the body, can also cause hair to become thicker and coarser.

Heat Damage

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling wands can cause damage to the hair, leading to it becoming thicker and coarser over time. This is because the high temperatures can cause the hair to lose moisture, making it dry and brittle. Using heat styling tools too often can also cause split ends and breakage.

Hair Products

The hair products you use can also contribute to the thickness and coarseness of your hair. Some hair products contain ingredients that can dry out the hair or cause build-up, which can lead to hair becoming thicker and coarser. Using the wrong products for your hair type can also contribute to this problem.

Tips for Managing Thick Wiry Hair

Managing thick wiry hair can be challenging, but it is possible. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep hair hydrated
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol or other drying ingredients
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair instead of a brush
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage

FAQs

Can thick wiry hair be straightened?

Yes, thick wiry hair can be straightened using heat styling tools or chemical relaxers. However, it is important to use these methods sparingly to avoid damaging the hair.

Can diet affect the texture of hair?

Yes, a balanced diet that includes vitamins and minerals can improve the health of hair. Eating foods that are rich in protein, iron, and biotin can promote healthy hair growth and improve hair texture.

Hair Care Routine

Having a good hair care routine is essential for managing thick wiry hair. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Wash hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  2. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep hair hydrated
  3. Avoid using heat styling tools
  4. Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol or other drying ingredients
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle hair
  6. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage

By following these tips and steps, you can manage your thick wiry hair and keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
