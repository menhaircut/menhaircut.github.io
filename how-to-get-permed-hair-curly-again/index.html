How To Get Permed Hair Curly Again Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you feeling frustrated with your permed hair that has lost its curls? Don't worry, you are not alone. Many people face this issue after getting their hair permed. The good news is that you can get your permed hair curly again with some simple tricks and tips. In this article, we will show you how to get permed hair curly again in the easiest way possible.

Description

When you get a perm, your hair is chemically treated to create curls. However, over time, the curls may loosen or fall out due to various factors such as heat styling, exposure to the sun, and daily wear and tear. If you want to restore your curls, you can try some of the following methods:

How to Get Permed Hair Curly Again: Step by Step

1. Use a Curl Enhancing Shampoo

Start by using a curl enhancing shampoo to help define and enhance your curls. Look for a shampoo that contains ingredients like glycerin or panthenol that can help to moisturize and soften your hair.

2. Apply a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Next, apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair. This will help to nourish and strengthen your hair, making it more resilient. Look for a treatment that contains ingredients like keratin or biotin that can help to rebuild your hair's structure.

3. Use a Diffuser Attachment with Your Hairdryer

If you want to dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment with your hairdryer. This will help to distribute the heat evenly and reduce frizz. Make sure to scrunch your hair with your hands while you dry it to help define your curls.

4. Apply a Curl Defining Cream

After your hair is dry, apply a curl defining cream to help define and hold your curls. Look for a cream that contains ingredients like argan oil or shea butter that can help to moisturize and nourish your hair.

5. Sleep with a Silk Scarf or Pillowcase

If you want to preserve your curls overnight, sleep with a silk scarf or pillowcase. This will help to reduce friction and prevent your curls from getting flattened or frizzy.

Tips for Getting Permed Hair Curly Again

Here are some additional tips that can help you get your permed hair curly again:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause your curls to fall out.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using products that contain alcohol or sulfates, as these can dry out your hair and make it frizzy.
  • Consider getting a touch-up perm to help restore your curls if they have fallen out completely.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to get permed hair curly again:

Q: How long does a perm last?

A: A perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on the type of perm and your hair's natural texture.

Q: Can I get a perm if my hair is damaged?

A: It is not recommended to get a perm if your hair is severely damaged or over-processed. Consult with a hairstylist to determine if your hair is healthy enough for a perm.

Q: Can I wash my hair after getting a perm?

A: It is recommended to wait at least 48 hours before washing your hair after getting a perm. This will help to ensure that the chemicals have set properly and your curls have formed.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your permed hair:

  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can cause your curls to loosen.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will help to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause it to stretch and break.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help keep it moisturized and nourished.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get your permed hair curly again and enjoy beautiful, bouncy curls that last.
