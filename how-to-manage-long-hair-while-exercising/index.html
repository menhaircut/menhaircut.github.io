How To Manage Long Hair While Exercising

Written by Robby Jul 21, 2023 · 3 min read

Long hair can be a beautiful feature on anyone, but it can also be a hassle when it comes to exercising. If you have long hair, you know how difficult it can be to keep it out of your face and off your neck while working out. In this article, we will discuss how to manage long hair while exercising.

Table of Contents

  • How to Manage Long Hair While Exercising
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Manage Long Hair While Exercising

The first step to managing long hair while exercising is choosing the right hairstyle. There are several hairstyles that work well for keeping long hair out of your face and off your neck:

  • Ponytail
  • Braid
  • Bun
  • Headband

It's important to choose a hairstyle that is comfortable for you and doesn't give you a headache while exercising. Once you have chosen your hairstyle, follow these steps:

Step by Step

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a ponytail, braid, or bun.
  3. Secure your hairstyle with an elastic band.
  4. If necessary, use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.
  5. Put on a headband to keep any flyaways out of your face.

With these simple steps, you can keep your long hair under control while exercising.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for managing long hair while exercising:

  • Avoid using metal hair accessories as they can snag your hair.
  • Use a hair tie that is strong and won't slip out of your hair while exercising.
  • If you have thick or heavy hair, consider using two hair ties to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Should I wash my hair before exercising?

A: It's not necessary to wash your hair before exercising, but it's important to keep it clean to avoid any buildup of sweat and dirt.

Q: Can I use hair products while exercising?

A: It's best to avoid using hair products while exercising as they can weigh down your hair and make it more difficult to manage.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair while exercising. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup of sweat and dirt.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as they can cause damage.
  • If you must use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray.

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair healthy and beautiful while exercising.

In conclusion, managing long hair while exercising doesn't have to be a hassle. By choosing the right hairstyle, following the steps outlined above, and taking care of your hair, you can keep your long hair under control while working out.
