How To Know If Curly Hair Suits You Male

Written by Christine Sep 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a great addition to any male’s look, but not everyone can pull it off. It’s important to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style before taking the plunge. In this article, we’ll explore how to know if curly hair suits you as a male, and provide tips on how to care for your curly locks.

Table of Contents

  • Face Shape
  • Hair Type
  • Personal Style
  • Hair Care
  • FAQ

Face Shape

The first thing to consider when deciding if curly hair suits you as a male is your face shape. Curly hair tends to look best on those with square or oval faces, as it can help to soften sharp angles and add volume to the hairline. If you have a round or heart-shaped face, curly hair may not be the best choice, as it can make your face appear even rounder.

Another factor to consider is your hairline. If you have a receding hairline, curly hair can help to disguise this and add fullness to your hair. However, if you have a high hairline, curly hair may not be the best choice, as it can make your forehead appear even larger.

Hair Type

The next thing to consider is your hair type. Curly hair tends to work best on those with thick, coarse hair, as it can help to define curls and add volume. If you have thin or fine hair, curly hair may not be the best choice, as it can make your hair appear even thinner.

It’s also important to consider the natural curl pattern of your hair. If you have tight, springy curls, curly hair may be a great choice for you. However, if you have loose, wavy curls, you may want to consider a different hairstyle, as curly hair may not be the most flattering.

Personal Style

Finally, it’s important to consider your personal style when deciding if curly hair suits you as a male. If you have a more casual, laid-back style, curly hair can add a fun, carefree vibe to your look. However, if you have a more formal, polished style, curly hair may not be the best choice, as it can appear too messy or unprofessional.

It’s also important to consider your overall grooming habits. If you’re someone who likes to keep your hair short and well-groomed, curly hair may not be the best choice, as it can be more high-maintenance and require more styling.

Hair Care

If you’ve decided that curly hair suits you as a male, it’s important to know how to care for your curls. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to help define and moisturize your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your curls and make them appear greasy.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your curls looking their best.

FAQ

Is curly hair hard to maintain?

Curly hair can be more high-maintenance than straight hair, as it requires more moisture and styling to keep it looking its best. However, with the right products and techniques, curly hair can be easy to maintain.

Can I still straighten my hair if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have curly hair. However, it’s important to use heat protectant products and not straighten your hair too often, as this can cause damage.

Can I dye my curly hair?

Yes, you can dye your curly hair. However, it’s important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and to use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your color.

Curly hair can be a great addition to any male’s look, as long as you consider your face shape, hair type, and personal style before making the decision. With the right care and maintenance, curly hair can be easy to manage and look great every day.
