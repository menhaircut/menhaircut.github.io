Medium Length Hairstyles With Long Layers And Bangs

Written by Aprilia Dec 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a style that's easy to maintain, versatile, and stylish, then medium length hairstyles with long layers and bangs might be just what you need. This look is perfect for those who want to add some dimension and depth to their hair without going too short or too long. In this article, we'll discuss how to achieve this style, tips for maintenance, FAQ, and hair care.

Description

Medium length hairstyles with long layers and bangs are perfect for those who want to add some texture and movement to their hair. The layers create volume and body, while the bangs frame the face and bring attention to the eyes. This style is perfect for any face shape, and it can be customized to suit your personal style and preferences.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve medium length hairstyles with long layers and bangs, you'll need to visit your stylist. They can help you achieve the perfect length, layers, and bangs for your face shape and hair type. Here's how to achieve this look:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel dry your hair, and then blow dry it using a round brush to add volume and body.

Step 2: Cut the Layers

Next, your stylist will cut long layers into your hair. The layers will be cut at an angle, starting from the chin and working their way down to the ends of your hair.

Step 3: Cut the Bangs

Your stylist will then cut the bangs to frame your face. They can be cut straight across or at an angle, depending on your preference. Make sure your stylist takes into account your face shape and hair type when cutting your bangs.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, you can style it using a curling iron or straightener to add some texture and movement to your hair. You can also add some hair spray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintenance

To keep your medium length hairstyles with long layers and bangs looking fresh and stylish, here are some tips for maintenance:

  • Visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim to keep your layers and bangs looking fresh.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Consider adding some highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with this style?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this style. You can create a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style to change up your look.

Q: Is this style suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style is suitable for all hair types. Your stylist can customize the layers and bangs to suit your hair type and texture.

Q: Will this style require a lot of maintenance?

A: No, this style is easy to maintain. All you need is a trim every 6-8 weeks and some basic hair care products.

Hair Care

To keep your medium length hairstyles with long layers and bangs looking healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and prevent breakage.

Overall, medium length hairstyles with long layers and bangs are a stylish and versatile option for those who want to add some texture and movement to their hair. With the right cut and maintenance, you can rock this look with confidence.
