Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Over 60S A Guide

Written by Petter Nov 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle? If so, you may want to consider a shoulder length style. Shoulder length hair is versatile, easy to style, and can be flattering on a variety of face shapes. In this guide, we will go over some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for over 60s, how to style them, and tips for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose a Shoulder Length Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Choose a Shoulder Length Hairstyle

When choosing a shoulder length hairstyle, there are a few things to consider:

Face Shape

First, consider your face shape. Some hairstyles are more flattering on certain face shapes than others. For example, a layered bob can be flattering on a square or heart-shaped face, while a long bob with side-swept bangs can be flattering on a round face.

Hair Texture

Next, consider your hair texture. If you have thin or fine hair, a blunt cut may be more flattering than layers. If you have curly or wavy hair, layers can help to enhance your natural texture.

Lifestyle

Finally, consider your lifestyle. Do you want a low-maintenance style that you can wash and go, or are you willing to spend more time styling your hair each day? A shoulder length style can be versatile, so you can choose a style that fits your lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Shoulder Length Hair

Styling shoulder length hair can be quick and easy. Here is a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. If you have fine or thin hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body. If you have curly or wavy hair, use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold your style. For a sleek look, use a smoothing serum. For volume, use a volumizing mousse.

3. Use a Round Brush to Blow Dry

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. For extra volume, flip your head upside down while blow drying.

4. Style as Desired

Once your hair is dry, style as desired. You can wear your hair straight, wavy, or curly. You can also add accessories, such as clips or headbands, for a finishing touch.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

2. Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, so be sure to wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when spending time outdoors.

4. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can help to keep your hair soft and healthy.

FAQs

What is the best shoulder length hairstyle for over 60s?

The best shoulder length hairstyle for over 60s will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Some popular options include a layered bob, a long bob with side-swept bangs, and a shoulder length shag.

Can I still wear my hair long over 60?

Yes, you can still wear your hair long over 60 if you want to. However, a shoulder length style can be more flattering and easier to maintain.

How often should I wash my hair?

How often you should wash your hair will depend on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often than if you have dry hair. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

In conclusion, a shoulder length hairstyle can be a great option for over 60s. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a style, and use the tips in this guide to maintain healthy hair.
