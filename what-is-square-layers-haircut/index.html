What Is Square Layers Haircut

Written by Robby Oct 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're in search of a new hairstyle that's both stylish and practical, you might want to consider the square layers haircut. This trendy haircut has been taking the beauty world by storm, with its unique combination of sharp angles and soft layers. In this article, we'll dive into what exactly a square layers haircut is, how to get it, and some tips for maintaining it.

Description

A square layers haircut is a hairstyle that features layers cut in a square shape. The layers are usually cut at a sharp angle, creating a square shape at the bottom of the hair. This haircut is perfect for individuals who want to add some dimension and movement to their hair while still keeping a sleek and polished look.

How to Get a Square Layers Haircut

If you're interested in getting a square layers haircut, it's best to visit a professional stylist who has experience with this specific style. Here are the basic steps that your stylist will follow:

Step 1: Consultation

Your stylist will start by discussing your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine if a square layers haircut is the right choice for you.

Step 2: Shampoo and Condition

Next, your hair will be washed and conditioned to prepare it for the cutting process.

Step 3: Cut the Layers

Your stylist will then start cutting your hair in a square shape, starting from the back and working their way to the front. They will use a combination of scissors and a razor to create the sharp angles and textured layers.

Step 4: Style

Finally, your stylist will style your hair to showcase the layers and give you a finished look. They may use a round brush and blow-dryer to create volume, or they may use a straightener to create a sleek and polished finish.

Tips for Maintaining a Square Layers Haircut

Once you have your new square layers haircut, it's important to keep it looking fresh and maintained. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

Tip 1: Regular Trims

To keep your haircut looking sharp and polished, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will prevent split ends and keep the layers looking fresh.

Tip 2: Use Heat Protection

If you use heat styling tools like a blow-dryer or straightener, it's important to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 3: Use the Right Products

To keep your layers looking defined and textured, it's important to use the right hair products. A texturizing spray or mousse can help enhance the layers and give your hair some extra volume.

FAQ

Q: Is a square layers haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: While a square layers haircut can work for most hair types, it's best suited for individuals with medium to thick hair. If you have very fine hair, this haircut may not work as well for you.

Q: Will a square layers haircut work for my face shape?

A: A square layers haircut can work for most face shapes, but it's best suited for individuals with oval or square face shapes. If you have a round face shape, you may want to consider a different haircut that will help elongate your face.

Hair Care

A square layers haircut is a great choice for anyone looking for a stylish and practical haircut. By following these tips and maintaining your haircut with regular trims and the right products, you can keep your layers looking fresh and defined. Talk to your stylist today to see if a square layers haircut is right for you!
