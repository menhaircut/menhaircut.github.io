Half Up Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair With Bangs A Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have medium length hair with bangs and are looking for a new hairstyle, then you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best half up hairstyles for medium length hair with bangs. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, these hairstyles are perfect for any occasion. So, without further ado, let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Half up hairstyles are a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your beautiful locks. They're also perfect for those who don't want to commit to a full updo but still want to look put together. These hairstyles are versatile, easy to create, and can be worn for any occasion.

When it comes to medium length hair with bangs, the possibilities are endless. From braids to twists, there are many different ways to create a half up hairstyle that will work for you. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.

How to:

Creating a half up hairstyle for medium length hair with bangs is easier than you might think. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle or to the side, depending on your preference.
  2. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back. Secure it with a clip or hair tie.
  3. Repeat step 2 on the other side of your head.
  4. If you want to add some volume, tease the hair at the crown of your head.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the back of your head and wrap it around the hair tie or clip to conceal it.
  6. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  7. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step:

Let's take a closer look at some of the best half up hairstyles for medium length hair with bangs:

1. Half Up Top Knot

The half up top knot is a trendy and effortless hairstyle that's perfect for a casual day out. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle or to the side, depending on your preference.
  2. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back. Secure it with a clip or hair tie.
  3. Twist the hair that you pulled back and wrap it into a small bun at the top of your head.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. If you have bangs, you can leave them down or pin them to the side.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Half Up Fishtail Braid

A fishtail braid is a chic and elegant hairstyle that's perfect for a formal event. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by parting your hair in the middle or to the side, depending on your preference.
  2. Take a section of hair from the top of your head and pull it back. Secure it with a clip or hair tie.
  3. Create a fishtail braid with the hair that you pulled back.
  4. Secure the braid with a small elastic band.
  5. If you have bangs, you can leave them down or pin them to the side.
  6. Finish with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips:

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect half up hairstyle for medium length hair with bangs:

  • Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume to your hair.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the one that works best for your face shape.
  • Don't be afraid to accessorize with hair clips, headbands, or flowers.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to conceal them better.
  • Practice makes perfect. Keep trying different styles until you find the one that you love.

FAQ:

Q: What type of bangs work best with a half up hairstyle?

A: Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs work best with a half up hairstyle. They can be left down or pinned to the side.

Q: Can I create a half up hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes, you can create a half up hairstyle with short hair. However, you may need to use more bobby pins to secure the style in place.

Q: How long does it take to create a half up hairstyle?

A: It can take anywhere from 5 to 15 minutes to create a half up hairstyle depending on the complexity of the style.

Hair Care:

It's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it looks its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can cause damage.
  • Try to air dry your hair whenever possible.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

Now that you know how to create some of the best half up hairstyles for medium length hair with bangs, it's time to try them out for yourself. Remember to have fun and experiment with different styles until you find the one that you love.
