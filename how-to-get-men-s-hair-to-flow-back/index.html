How To Get Men s Hair To Flow Back A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to get your hair to flow back? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men find it difficult to achieve this classic hairstyle. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the perfect flow back look. In this article, we'll show you how to get men's hair to flow back in easy steps, answering all your questions and giving you some tips to keep your hair looking great.

How to Get Men's Hair to Flow Back

The first step to achieving the perfect flow back look is to choose the right haircut. You want to make sure that your hair is cut in a way that allows it to flow back easily. The ideal length for this hairstyle is between 3 and 5 inches on top, with shorter sides and back.

Once you have the right haircut, it's time to start styling your hair. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair thoroughly. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to clean your hair and remove any build-up that may be preventing your hair from flowing back. Rinse your hair well and towel dry it gently.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. A pomade or wax is the best choice for this hairstyle. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair, from root to tip.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Once you have applied the styling product, use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a brush to direct your hair back, away from your face. This will help to create the flow back effect.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Finally, use your hands to style your hair into the desired shape. You can use a comb to create a parting, or simply sweep your hair back with your fingers. Use a little more product if necessary, to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Getting Men's Hair to Flow Back

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect flow back look:

  • Use a high-quality styling product for best results.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to flow.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create a natural-looking parting.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQs About Men's Hair Flowing Back

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting men's hair to flow back:

What kind of product should I use?

The best products for this hairstyle are pomades or waxes. These products provide a strong hold, without making your hair look greasy.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every other day, or as needed. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it dry and brittle.

Can I achieve this look with short hair?

Yes, you can. However, it may be more difficult to achieve the flow back effect with shorter hair. Talk to your stylist about the best haircut to achieve this look.

Hair Care for Men's Flowing Hair

To keep your hair looking great, it's important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect flow back look. Remember to choose the right haircut, use the right products, and take care of your hair to keep it looking great.
