How To Cut Men s Hair Short On Sides Long On Top

Written by Oliver Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and want to try something new? If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then you might want to consider cutting your hair short on the sides and long on top. This hairstyle, also known as the undercut, has been popular for years and is perfect for men who want to achieve a modern and edgy look. In this article, we will show you how to cut men's hair short on sides long on top in relaxed English language.

Description

The undercut hairstyle is characterized by short hair on the sides and longer hair on top. This style is versatile and can be customized according to your preferences. You can choose to have a fade, taper, or undercut on the sides, while the top can be styled in various ways, such as slicked back, messy, or textured. This hairstyle is suitable for all hair types, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

How to Cut Men's Hair Short on Sides Long on Top

Step by Step

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure that you have the right tools. You will need a pair of scissors, clippers, comb, and hair styling products. Here's how to cut men's hair short on sides long on top:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Section off the top part of your hair and clip it away. This will help you focus on the sides first.
  3. Take your clippers and choose the guard size you want for your desired length. Start from the bottom and work your way up, using upward strokes. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Blend the sides and top by using scissors. Start from the top and work your way down, using a comb to guide you. Be careful not to cut too much hair at once.
  5. Unclip the top section of your hair and decide on the length you want. Use scissors to cut the hair to your desired length, keeping in mind that you will still need to style it later.
  6. Style your hair using your preferred product. You can slick it back, add texture, or leave it messy. Use a comb or your fingers to achieve your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut hairstyle:

  • Choose the right length for your face shape and hair type. Your barber can help you determine this.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as you can always go back and trim more if needed.
  • Use hair styling products to help you create your desired look and hold the style in place.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair short on sides long on top:

What face shape is best for an undercut?

The undercut hairstyle is suitable for most face shapes, but it looks particularly good on square and oval faces.

Can I cut my own hair short on sides long on top?

You can cut your own hair, but it's recommended to visit a professional barber for the best results.

How often should I get my undercut trimmed?

You should visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair short on sides long on top, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.
  • Use hair styling products that contain natural ingredients to avoid damaging your hair.

Cutting men's hair short on sides long on top is a trendy and stylish haircut that's perfect for those who want to make a bold statement. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve the perfect undercut hairstyle and keep your hair healthy and shiny. Visit your barber regularly to maintain your haircut and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
