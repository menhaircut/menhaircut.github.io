How To Do A Messy Ponytail With Medium Hair

Written by Petter Oct 13, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have medium length hair, you know that it can be difficult to find hairstyles that work for you. One great option is the messy ponytail. Not only is it easy to do, but it also looks chic and effortless. In this article, we will guide you through the steps of creating a messy ponytail with medium hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Create a Messy Ponytail
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Messy Ponytail
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Medium Hair

How to Create a Messy Ponytail

A messy ponytail is a great option for those who want a relaxed and casual look. To achieve this style, you will need some hair ties, bobby pins, and a texturizing spray.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create a messy ponytail with medium hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any knots or tangles.
  2. Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Use your fingers to pull out some strands of hair around your face and at the back of your head to create a messy look.
  4. If you want to add more texture to your hair, spray some texturizing spray on your roots and lengths.
  5. You can also use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls for a more textured look.
  6. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to cover it. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin.
  7. Use your fingers to loosen the ponytail and create a messy look.
  8. Finish off with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for a Perfect Messy Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.
  • Don't worry about making the ponytail too neat – the messier, the better!
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.
  • Apply texturizing spray to your roots and lengths for added texture.
  • Don't be afraid to pull out some strands of hair around your face to frame it.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about messy ponytails:

Can I create a messy ponytail with short hair?

It may be difficult to create a messy ponytail with short hair, but you can try using hair extensions or hair clips to add more length and volume to your hair.

How do I make my messy ponytail last all day?

Use a light hairspray to hold the style in place, and secure any loose strands with bobby pins. You can also apply some dry shampoo to your roots to absorb any excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh.

Hair Care for Medium Hair

To keep your medium hair healthy and shiny, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Creating a messy ponytail with medium hair is easy and effortless. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect casual look. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
