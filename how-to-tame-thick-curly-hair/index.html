How To Tame Thick Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thick curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it's voluminous, bouncy, and full of life. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage and style. If you have thick curly hair, you know the struggle of frizz, tangles, and undefined curls. But fear not, we've got you covered. In this guide, we'll show you how to tame your thick curly hair and keep it looking fabulous.

How to Tame Thick Curly Hair

The key to taming thick curly hair is moisture and definition. You want to keep your hair hydrated and well-defined to avoid frizz and tangles. Here are some tips to help you tame your thick curly hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to frizz. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning is essential for curly hair. It helps to restore moisture and nourish your curls. Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.

3. Use a leave-in conditioner

Leave-in conditioners are great for curly hair because they provide extra moisture and help to define your curls. Apply a leave-in conditioner after washing your hair and leave it in to dry.

4. Don't brush your hair

Brushing curly hair can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

5. Use a diffuser

A diffuser is a great tool for drying curly hair. It helps to distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide to taming your thick curly hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo

Use a sulfate-free shampoo to cleanse your hair and scalp. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and work it down to the ends of your hair.

Step 2: Condition your hair with a deep conditioning treatment

Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair and leave it in for at least 10 minutes. Rinse it out with cool water.

Step 3: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends. Don't rinse it out.

Step 4: Detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers

Gently detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Step 5: Use a diffuser to dry your hair

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and use it on a low heat setting. Use circular motions to dry your hair, scrunching your curls as you go.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to help you tame your thick curly hair:

1. Sleep on a silk pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentle on your hair and reduce frizz. They also help to retain moisture and prevent breakage.

2. Use a satin hair cap

Wearing a satin hair cap to bed can help to protect your curls and reduce frizz. It also helps to retain moisture and prevent breakage.

3. Experiment with different products

Not all curly hair products are created equal. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have thick curly hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week.

Q: How can I prevent frizz?

A: Keep your hair hydrated and use a diffuser when drying your hair. Avoid brushing your hair and use a satin hair cap or silk pillowcase.

Hair Care for Thick Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for keeping your thick curly hair healthy and beautiful:

1. Trim your hair regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks.

2. Protect your hair from the sun

The sun can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair from the sun.

3. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the heat setting low.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to tame your thick curly hair and keep it looking fabulous. Remember to keep your hair hydrated, defined, and protected from damage. Happy styling!
