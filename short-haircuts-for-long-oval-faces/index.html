Short Haircuts For Long Oval Faces A Trendy And Stylish Look

Written by Aprilia Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long oval face, you may find it challenging to pick the right haircut that flatters your features. However, with the right guidance, you can achieve a trendy and stylish look with short haircuts. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for long oval faces, how to style them, and hair care tips to maintain your look.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Long Oval Faces

When choosing a short haircut for long oval faces, consider the following:

  • The length of your face: Short haircuts that fall above the chin or at ear-length work best for long oval faces as they create the illusion of width.
  • Your hair texture: The texture of your hair can affect the type of short haircut that suits you. For instance, if you have thick hair, a layered bob or pixie cut can work well for you.
  • Your style preference: Short haircuts come in different styles and cuts, from pixie cuts to bobs. Choose a style that matches your personality and lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide on Styling Short Haircuts for Long Oval Faces

Styling short haircuts for long oval faces is easy and hassle-free. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and texture to your hair.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement to your hair.
  4. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or create loose waves.
  5. Finish off your look by applying a texturizing spray to your hair to add definition and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Long Oval Faces

To maintain your short haircut for long oval faces, consider the following tips:

  • Book regular appointments with your hairstylist to trim your hair and maintain its shape.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

FAQs About Short Haircuts for Long Oval Faces

What short haircuts work best for long oval faces?

Short haircuts that fall above the chin or at ear-length work best for long oval faces as they create the illusion of width.

Can I style my short haircut differently for different occasions?

Yes, you can style your short haircut differently for different occasions. For instance, you can create loose waves for a casual look or straighten your hair for a formal look.

Can I color my short haircut?

Yes, you can color your short haircut. Consult with your hairstylist to choose the best color that complements your skin tone and style.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Long Oval Faces

To maintain your short haircut, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Book regular appointments with your hairstylist to trim your hair and maintain its shape.

In conclusion, short haircuts for long oval faces are easy to style and maintain with the right guidance. Choose a haircut that flatters your features, style it to suit your personality and lifestyle, and follow the hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
