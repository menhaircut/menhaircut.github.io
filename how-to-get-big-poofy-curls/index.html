How to Get Big Poofy Curls - Step by Step Guide

Do you want to know how to get big poofy curls that last all day? If you're tired of flat, lifeless hair, you're in the right place. In this step by step guide, we'll show you how to create voluminous, bouncy curls that are sure to turn heads.

How to Get Big Poofy Curls

Here's what you'll need:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Hairspray
  • Heat protectant
  • Clips or hair ties

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step to getting big poofy curls is to start with clean, dry hair. Be sure to apply a heat protectant before blow drying your hair. You can also use a diffuser attachment to add volume to your roots.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting with the bottom layer. Secure the rest of your hair with clips or hair ties so that it doesn't get in the way.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Starting with the bottom layer, curl small sections of your hair using a curling iron or wand. Be sure to curl away from your face for a more natural look. Once you've curled a section, clip it up and move on to the next one.

Step 4: Let Your Curls Cool

After curling each section, let your hair cool before releasing the clips. This will help your curls set and last longer.

Step 5: Brush Out Your Curls

Once you've curled all of your hair, use a brush to gently brush out your curls. This will create a more natural, voluminous look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finally, finish your look with a generous amount of hairspray. This will help your curls stay in place all day.

Tips for Getting Big Poofy Curls

Here are some additional tips for getting big poofy curls:

  • Use a larger barrel curling iron or wand for bigger curls
  • Apply a volumizing mousse or spray before blow drying your hair
  • Tease your roots for added volume
  • Use a texturizing spray for a more undone, beachy look

FAQs

Q: Can I get big poofy curls with naturally straight hair?

A: Yes, you can still get big poofy curls with naturally straight hair. Use a volumizing mousse or spray before blow drying your hair, and tease your roots for added volume.

Q: How long will my big poofy curls last?

A: With the right products and techniques, your big poofy curls can last all day.

Hair Care for Big Poofy Curls

To keep your big poofy curls looking their best, be sure to use a hydrating shampoo and conditioner. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant before using hot tools.

Now that you know how to get big poofy curls, it's time to try it out for yourself. With a little practice and the right products, you'll be rocking voluminous, bouncy curls in no time.
