How To Frame Bangs For Round Faces

Written by Oliver Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you might be wondering if bangs will work for you. The answer is yes! With the right styling, bangs can be a great way to frame your face and highlight your features. In this post, we'll go over how to frame bangs round face in a way that flatters your face shape.

Description:

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and a circular shape. If you have a round face, you want to create the illusion of length and angles. Bangs can help with this, as they draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones and can create the illusion of a longer face.

How to:

When it comes to framing bangs for round faces, there are a few things to consider:

1. Length:

The length of your bangs is important when it comes to framing your face. Generally, longer bangs that hit just above your eyebrows work well for round faces. This length helps to elongate your face and create the illusion of angles.

2. Shape:

The shape of your bangs is also important. Side-swept bangs are a great option for round faces, as they create a diagonal line that helps to lengthen the face. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

3. Texture:

The texture of your bangs can also make a difference. Soft, wispy bangs can be a great option for round faces, as they help to create movement and texture. Avoid heavy, straight-across bangs, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

Step-by-Step:

Here's how to frame bangs round face:

Step 1:

Start by sectioning off the front portion of your hair that you want to turn into bangs. Use a comb to create a triangle shape, with the point of the triangle at the top of your head and the base of the triangle at your hairline.

Step 2:

Use scissors to cut your bangs to the desired length. Remember to cut them slightly longer than you want, as they will shrink when they dry.

Step 3:

Use a razor or thinning shears to add texture to your bangs. This will help them to blend in with the rest of your hair and create movement.

Step 4:

Style your bangs using a round brush and a blow dryer. Use the brush to create a slight curve in your bangs, and blow dry them in the direction you want them to fall.

Tips:

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when framing bangs for round faces:

- Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and help your bangs stand out. - Don't be afraid to play around with different lengths and shapes to find what works best for you. - If you're not sure whether bangs will work for you, try a clip-in bang extension first to see how they look.

FAQ:

Q: Can blunt bangs work for round faces?

A: While blunt bangs can work for some round faces, they generally aren't the best option. Blunt bangs can make your face appear even rounder, so it's better to go for a side-swept or wispy bang instead.

Q: What if I have curly hair?

A: If you have curly hair, you can still rock bangs! Just make sure to cut them a bit longer than you would if you had straight hair, as curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.

Q: How often do I need to trim my bangs?

A: You'll need to trim your bangs about every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

Hair Care:

When it comes to caring for your bangs, there are a few things to keep in mind:

- Use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes. - Avoid using too much heat on your bangs, as this can cause damage and make them look frizzy. - Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking their best.

With these tips and tricks, you can frame bangs round face and create a look that flatters your features and highlights your beauty. Don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your hair!
