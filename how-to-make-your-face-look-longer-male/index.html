How To Make Your Face Look Longer Male Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jul 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you feel like your face is too round or short? Don't worry, there are ways to make your face look longer and more elongated. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make your face look longer male. From haircuts to facial hair, we've got you covered. Let's get started!

Table of Contents:

  • Haircut
  • Facial Hair
  • Contouring
  • Accessories
  • Skincare
  • FAQs

Haircut

Your haircut can make a big difference in how your face looks. To make your face look longer, consider getting a haircut that adds height to your head. A short haircut with volume on top, such as a quiff or a pompadour, can create the illusion of a longer face. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too flat, as they can make your face look rounder.

Another tip is to avoid haircuts with too much volume on the sides. This can make your face look wider and shorter. Instead, keep the sides short and the top longer. This will create a vertical line that elongates your face.

Facial Hair

If you're a fan of facial hair, you can use it to make your face look longer. A well-groomed beard or goatee can create the illusion of a longer jawline. This is because the hair draws the eye downwards, creating a vertical line that lengthens your face.

On the other hand, avoid having too much hair on your cheeks, as this can make your face look wider. Keep your facial hair well-trimmed and neat to avoid looking unkempt.

Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that can help you create the illusion of a longer face. By using a darker shade of foundation or powder on the sides of your face and a lighter shade on the center, you can create the illusion of depth and length.

When contouring, focus on the area below your cheekbones and along your jawline. Blend well to avoid harsh lines and keep the look natural.

Accessories

Accessories can also help you make your face look longer. For example, wearing a hat with a high crown can add height to your head and create the illusion of a longer face. Sunglasses with a vertical or rectangular shape can also elongate your face.

When choosing accessories, avoid those that are too wide or round, as they can make your face look wider.

Skincare

Good skincare can also help you make your face look longer. By keeping your skin healthy and hydrated, you can improve its texture and appearance. This can make your face look more angular and defined.

Make sure to cleanse your skin regularly and use a moisturizer that suits your skin type. Avoid using products that are too harsh or heavy, as they can clog your pores and cause breakouts.

FAQs

1. Can losing weight make my face look longer?

Losing weight can help you achieve a more defined and angular face. However, it's important to do it in a healthy way and avoid crash diets or extreme weight loss programs.

2. Can chewing gum help me make my face look longer?

Chewing gum can help you exercise your jaw muscles and improve your facial definition. However, don't rely on it as the only solution to make your face look longer.

3. Can changing my posture help me make my face look longer?

Improving your posture can help you look taller and more confident, which can make your face look longer. Stand up straight and avoid slouching to create a taller silhouette.

Conclusion

There are many ways to make your face look longer male, from haircuts to skincare. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find what works best for you. Remember to keep it natural and avoid going overboard with makeup or accessories. With a little effort, you can achieve a more elongated and defined face.
