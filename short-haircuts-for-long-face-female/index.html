Short Haircuts For Long Face Female The Ultimate Guide

If you have a long face, you may feel like your hair always falls flat and lacks volume. However, with the right haircut, you can give your locks a new lease of life. In this post, we will take a look at some of the best short haircuts for long face female, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair. Here is what we will cover:

Description

Short haircuts are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. They can also be a great way to balance out a long face. However, choosing the right short haircut for long face female can be a bit tricky. You want to choose a haircut that will enhance your features and flatter your face shape. In this guide, we will take a look at some of the best short haircuts for long face female that will help you achieve a chic and stylish look.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Long Face Female

When choosing a short haircut for long face female, you want to look for styles that will add width to your face. This means choosing haircuts that have volume at the sides or that have layers to add texture. Here are some of the best short haircuts for long face female:

  • Pixie Cut: This is a great option for long face females who want to make a statement. The short layers will add volume to the sides of your face, while the longer layers on top will give you a chic and stylish look.
  • Bob Cut: Bobs are a classic haircut that never go out of style. For long face females, a bob with layers can help balance out your face shape and add volume.
  • Layered Cut: A layered cut is a great way to add texture and depth to your hair. Long layers can help frame your face and add volume, while short layers can add movement and texture.
  • Asymmetrical Cut: This is a bold and edgy haircut that can work well for long face females. The asymmetrical cut will add volume and balance out your face shape.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Short Haircut

Now that you have chosen the right short haircut for long face female, it's time to learn how to style it. Here is a step by step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to your roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and texture.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls to your hair.
  5. Finish with a texturizing spray to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut for Long Face Female

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut for long face female:

  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut and prevent split ends.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for long face female:

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look longer?

A: No, a short haircut can actually help balance out your face shape and make your face appear shorter.

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type, but most people with short hair can wash their hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, there are plenty of ways to style short hair up, such as with bobby pins, hair clips, or a small hair elastic.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Long Face Female

Here are some hair care tips for short haircuts for long face female:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and heat by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Short haircuts for long face female can be a great way to add volume and texture to your hair. By choosing the right haircut and following these styling and hair care tips, you can achieve a chic and stylish look that flatters your face shape. So go ahead and give it a try!
