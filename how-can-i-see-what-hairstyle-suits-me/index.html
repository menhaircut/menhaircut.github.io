How Can I See What Hairstyle Suits Me

Written by Christine Sep 24, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle, you may be wondering how to find the one that will best suit your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Fortunately, there are several ways to determine which hairstyle will look great on you. In this article, we'll explore how you can see what hairstyle suits you and offer some tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

Choosing a hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit you. Your face shape, hair type, and personal style all play a role in determining which hairstyle will look best on you. Fortunately, there are several tools and techniques you can use to find the perfect hairstyle.

How to See What Hairstyle Suits You

Here are some tips for finding the perfect hairstyle:

1. Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a flattering hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are several face shapes, including round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that will complement your features.

2. Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have thin hair, for example, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look even thinner. If you have curly hair, on the other hand, you may want to choose a hairstyle that will enhance your curls.

3. Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you're always on the go, for example, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you're more of a fashionista, on the other hand, you may want a hairstyle that allows you to experiment with different looks.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect hairstyle:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a tape measure to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. Once you have these measurements, you can determine your face shape. If your forehead, cheeks, and jawline are about the same width, you have a round face shape. If your forehead and jawline are about the same width, but your cheeks are narrower, you have a square face shape. If your forehead is wider than your jawline and your cheeks are high and rounded, you have an oval face shape. If your forehead is wider than your jawline and your chin is pointed, you have a heart-shaped face.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

Think about your hair type and what styles work best for it. If you have thin hair, for example, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look even thinner. If you have curly hair, on the other hand, you may want to choose a hairstyle that will enhance your curls.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you're always on the go, for example, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you're more of a fashionista, on the other hand, you may want a hairstyle that allows you to experiment with different looks.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips for choosing the perfect hairstyle:

1. Bring Pictures

Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hair appointment. This will help your stylist understand what you're looking for and suggest styles that will work best for you.

2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a hairstyle. If you have fine hair, for example, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can make your hair look even thinner. If you have thick hair, on the other hand, you may want a hairstyle that will thin out your hair and reduce bulk.

3. Experiment with Accessories

Accessories can be a great way to change up your hairstyle without committing to a new cut. Try adding a headband or scarf to your hair to give it a new look.

FAQ

1. How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. On average, most people need a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

2. How can I make my hair grow faster?

Eating a healthy diet, getting enough sleep, and minimizing stress can all help promote hair growth. You can also try using hair growth products or taking supplements.

3. How can I prevent split ends?

To prevent split ends, avoid using hot tools on your hair, avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, and use a deep conditioner regularly.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that's free of sulfates and harsh chemicals. These can strip your hair of its natural oils and lead to dryness and damage.

2. Condition Regularly

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. You can also protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

4. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By using these tips and techniques, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape, hair type, and personal style. Remember to take care of your hair by using gentle products, conditioning regularly, and protecting it from damage.
