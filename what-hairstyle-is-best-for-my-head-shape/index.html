What Hairstyle Is Best For My Head Shape

Written by William Sep 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, it's important to consider your head shape. Not all hairstyles work for all head shapes, so it's important to find one that complements your unique features. In this blog post, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you determine what hairstyle is best for your head shape.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Determine Your Head Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Your Head Shape
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Your Head Shape

How to Determine Your Head Shape

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for your head shape is to determine what shape your head is. There are four common head shapes: round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. Here's how to determine which one you have:

Round Head Shape

If your face is widest at your cheeks and you have a rounded chin, you likely have a round head shape.

Square Head Shape

If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, you have a square head shape.

Oval Head Shape

If your face is longer than it is wide and your forehead is slightly wider than your jawline, you have an oval head shape.

Heart-Shaped Head Shape

If your forehead is wider than your jawline and you have a pointed chin, you have a heart-shaped head shape.

Best Hairstyles for Your Head Shape

Once you've determined your head shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that complement it. Here are some suggestions:

Round Head Shape

If you have a round head shape, you should look for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head. This will elongate your face and balance out your features. A layered haircut with volume on top is a good choice, as is a side-swept bang.

Square Head Shape

If you have a square head shape, you should look for hairstyles that soften your features. A layered cut with soft, wispy bangs can help to soften your jawline. You can also try a wavy or curly hairstyle to add some volume and movement.

Oval Head Shape

If you have an oval head shape, you're in luck! Most hairstyles will work well for you. However, if you want to accentuate your features, try a long, layered cut or a side-swept fringe.

Heart-Shaped Head Shape

If you have a heart-shaped head shape, you should look for hairstyles that balance out your features. A layered cut with volume at the bottom can help to balance out a wider forehead. You can also try a side-swept fringe or a bob that hits at the chin.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you're always on the go, you may want a low-maintenance style. If you have more time to devote to your hair, you can opt for a more intricate style.
  • Think about your hair texture. If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to avoid a blunt cut. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid a lot of layers.
  • Look for inspiration. Browse magazines, social media, and even celebrity hairstyles to get ideas for your new look.

FAQs

Can I still wear a certain hairstyle even if it doesn't match my head shape?

Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions. Ultimately, you should choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Can I change my head shape with a certain hairstyle?

No, you can't change your head shape with a hairstyle. However, you can use certain hairstyles to accentuate or balance out your features.

Hair Care for Your Head Shape

Regardless of your head shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and split-end free.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Now that you know what hairstyle is best for your head shape, you can confidently choose a new look that complements your unique features. Remember, the most important thing is to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
