Why Was Curly Hair Popular In The 80S

Written by Oliver Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to the ultimate guide on why curly hair was so popular in the 80s! Whether you lived through the 80s or not, you've likely seen pictures of the big, bold, and beautiful hairstyles that defined the decade. Curly hair was all the rage and many people wanted to achieve the look. In this article, we'll explore the reasons why curly hair was so popular in the 80s, how to achieve the look, and how to care for your curly locks.

Description

The 80s was a decade of big hair and bold fashion choices. Curly hair was seen as a symbol of individuality, rebellion, and self-expression. Many people embraced their natural curls or used various styling techniques to achieve the look. Curly hair was often styled in a voluminous, teased, and sculpted fashion to create a dramatic effect.

How to Achieve the 80s Curly Hair Look

If you want to achieve the 80s curly hair look, there are many techniques you can use. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to hydrate your curls and prepare them for styling.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product to your damp hair. This will help to define your curls and reduce frizz.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. This will help to maintain the shape of your curls and add volume.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Tease your hair with a teasing comb to create volume and texture. This was a popular technique in the 80s and helped to create the big, bold hairstyles of the decade.

Step 5: Use Hairspray

Finish off your look with a generous amount of hairspray. This will help to hold your curls in place and maintain their shape throughout the day.

Tips for Caring for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep it hydrated.

Tip 2: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and damage to your curls.

Tip 3: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair, as this can damage your curls and make them frizzy.

Tip 4: Use a Hair Mask

Use a hair mask once a week to deeply condition and nourish your curls.

FAQ

Q: Why was curly hair so popular in the 80s?

A: Curly hair was seen as a symbol of individuality, rebellion, and self-expression in the 80s. Many people wanted to embrace their natural curls or achieve the look using various styling techniques.

Q: How do I achieve the 80s curly hair look?

A: To achieve the 80s curly hair look, start by washing your hair, applying a curl enhancing product, blow drying your hair, teasing your hair, and using hairspray to hold the look in place.

Q: How do I care for my curly hair?

A: To care for your curly hair, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle, avoid heat styling, and use a hair mask once a week to deeply condition your curls.

Hair Care

Caring for curly hair can be a challenge, but by following the tips outlined in this article, you can keep your curls healthy and looking their best. Remember to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, a wide-tooth comb, and to avoid heat styling. With a little extra care and attention, you can rock the 80s curly hair look like a pro!
