Does Lighter Hair Make Your Face Look Fatter

Written by Christine Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Lightening your hair can be a great way to switch up your look and add some variety to your hairstyle. However, many people are concerned that lighter hair might make their face look fatter. In this article, we'll explore whether or not this is true and provide some tips on how to avoid a rounder-looking face.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Many people believe that lighter hair can make your face look fatter. The reason for this is that lighter hair tends to reflect more light, which can create the illusion of a wider face. Additionally, if you have a round face shape, lighter hair can emphasize the roundness of your face.

However, this doesn't mean that you should avoid lightening your hair altogether. There are ways to prevent your face from looking fatter and still enjoy a new hair color.

How To

The key to preventing your face from looking fatter with lighter hair is to choose the right hair color and hairstyle.

Choose the Right Hair Color

If you have a round face shape, it's best to avoid hair colors that are too light or too dark. Instead, opt for a hair color that's close to your natural hair color or a shade or two lighter. This will create a more natural look and prevent your face from looking wider.

If you're unsure what hair color would suit you best, consult with a professional hair stylist. They can help you choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and face shape.

Choose the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can also make a big difference in how your face looks with lighter hair. If you have a round face shape, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or a messy bun.

You can also try hairstyles that have layers or bangs. These can help to break up the roundness of your face and create a more angular look.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to prevent your face from looking fatter with lighter hair:

  1. Consult with a professional hair stylist to choose the right hair color for your skin tone and face shape.
  2. Opt for a hair color that's close to your natural hair color or a shade or two lighter.
  3. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.
  4. Try hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or a messy bun.
  5. Consider hairstyles with layers or bangs to create a more angular look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when lightening your hair:

  • Use hair products that are specifically designed for colored hair to prevent damage and fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade more quickly.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often, as these can damage your hair.

FAQ

Does lightening hair make it look thinner?

Yes, lightening your hair can make it look thinner. This is because lighter hair reflects more light, which can create the illusion of thinner hair.

How can I prevent my face from looking fatter with lighter hair?

Choose a hair color that's close to your natural hair color or a shade or two lighter. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face and try hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head. Consider hairstyles with layers or bangs to create a more angular look.

Hair Care

Lightening your hair can be a fun way to switch up your look, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and maintain your new color. Here are some tips for caring for your hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for colored hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause the color to fade more quickly.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too often, as these can damage your hair.

By following these tips, you can enjoy your new hair color without worrying about your face looking fatter.
