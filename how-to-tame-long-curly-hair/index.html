How To Tame Long Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long curly hair can be beautiful, but it can also be a challenge to manage. If you're tired of frizz, tangles, and flyaways, don't worry! With the right techniques and products, you can tame your long curly hair and achieve the look you want. In this post, we'll show you how to do just that.

How to Prepare Your Hair

Before you start styling your long curly hair, it's important to prepare it properly. This means brushing or combing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots, using a wide-toothed comb or a brush designed for curly hair.

Once your hair is detangled, you may want to apply a leave-in conditioner or a hair oil to help smooth your hair and add shine. Be sure to choose a product that is specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid using too much, as this can weigh down your curls.

How to Detangle Your Hair

Detangling your long curly hair can be a time-consuming process, but it's important to do it right. Start by sectioning your hair into manageable sections, and then use a wide-toothed comb or a brush designed for curly hair to gently work out any tangles or knots. Be patient, and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage and damage.

If you have particularly stubborn tangles, you may want to use a detangling spray or a leave-in conditioner to help loosen them up. Apply the product to the affected area, and then use your fingers or a comb to gently work out the tangle.

How to Wash Your Hair

Washing your long curly hair is an important part of your hair care routine, but it's important to do it right. Start by wetting your hair thoroughly with warm water, and then apply a shampoo that is designed for curly hair. Massage the shampoo into your scalp, and then work it through the rest of your hair, focusing on the roots.

Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water, and then apply a conditioner that is designed for curly hair. Massage the conditioner into your hair, focusing on the ends, and then rinse it out with cool water.

How to Condition Your Hair

Conditioning your long curly hair is an important step in keeping it healthy and manageable. After washing your hair, apply a conditioner that is designed for curly hair. Massage the conditioner into your hair, focusing on the ends, and then leave it in for a few minutes before rinsing it out with cool water.

If you have particularly dry or damaged hair, you may want to use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help restore moisture and vitality to your hair.

How to Style Your Hair

Styling your long curly hair can be a lot of fun, but it's important to use the right techniques and products to achieve the look you want. Start by applying a styling product that is designed for curly hair, such as a mousse, gel, or curl cream. Apply the product to damp hair, and then scrunch your hair with your hands to enhance your curls.

If you want to create more defined curls, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help to set your curls and reduce frizz.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

To keep your long curly hair looking its best, there are a few tips and tricks you can follow. First, avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause frizz. Instead, embrace your natural curls and use styling techniques that enhance your hair's texture and volume.

Second, be gentle when detangling your hair, and avoid pulling or tugging on your curls. Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush designed for curly hair, and be patient.

Finally, protect your hair from the elements by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside, especially on windy or rainy days. This will help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking great.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner on my curly hair?

A: It's best to use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair, as these products are formulated to keep your curls looking their best. Regular shampoos and conditioners can be too harsh and may strip your hair of its natural oils, causing frizz and dryness.

Q: How often should I wash my long curly hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people with curly hair find that washing their hair every 2-3 days works well. You may need to wash your hair more or less frequently depending on how oily or dry your hair is, and how much styling product you use.
