How To Make Your Hair Naturally Curly For Black Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a black guy with straight hair, you may be wondering how to make your hair naturally curly. While genetics play a role in the texture of your hair, there are some steps you can take to enhance your natural curl pattern. In this article, we'll walk you through the process of making your hair naturally curly, from how to prepare your hair to the best products to use.

Table of Contents

  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Creating Curls
  • Choosing the Right Products
  • Caring for Your Curls
  • FAQs

Prepping Your Hair

The first step in making your hair naturally curly is to prepare it for styling. Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup from styling products or oils. Then, apply a deep conditioner to your hair to add moisture and prepare it for styling. After rinsing out the conditioner, gently pat your hair dry with a towel to remove excess water.

Next, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl enhancer to your hair. This will help define your natural curl pattern and add moisture to your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Creating Curls

Once you've prepped your hair, it's time to create curls. There are several techniques you can use to create curls, including:

  • Finger Coiling: Take small sections of hair and twist them around your finger to create coils. This technique works well for short hair.
  • Bantu Knots: Divide your hair into small sections and twist each section before wrapping it into a knot. This technique works well for medium-length hair.
  • Flexi Rods: Wrap small sections of hair around flexi rods and leave them in overnight to create curls. This technique works well for longer hair.

Experiment with different techniques to find the one that works best for your hair length and texture.

Choosing the Right Products

Choosing the right products is key to making your hair naturally curly. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl enhancers, leave-in conditioners, and styling gels. Avoid products that contain alcohol or sulfates, as these can dry out your hair and make it more prone to breakage.

Some of the best products for making your hair naturally curly include:

  • Cantu Coconut Curling Cream: This cream defines curls and adds moisture to your hair.
  • Kinky-Curly Custard: This gel defines curls and adds shine to your hair.
  • Shea Moisture Coconut & Hibiscus Curl Enhancing Smoothie: This cream defines curls and adds moisture to your hair.

Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair.

Caring for Your Curls

Once you've made your hair naturally curly, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them healthy and defined. Here are some tips for caring for your curls:

  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your hair and make your curls frizzy.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can keep your curls looking healthy and defined.

FAQs

Can I make my hair naturally curly if I have relaxed hair?

Yes, you can make your hair naturally curly even if you have relaxed hair. However, it may take more effort to define your curl pattern, as relaxed hair is often straighter than natural hair.

How long does it take to make my hair naturally curly?

The time it takes to make your hair naturally curly depends on your hair type, texture, and length. It may take several weeks or months to see significant results.

Can I use a diffuser to dry my hair?

Yes, you can use a diffuser to dry your hair. However, be sure to use a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

What should I do if my curls are frizzy?

If your curls are frizzy, try using a curl-defining product or oil to smooth them out. You can also use a diffuser to dry your hair to help define your curls.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking healthy.

By following these steps and tips, you can make your hair naturally curly and keep your curls looking healthy and defined. With patience and persistence, you can achieve the curly hair you've always wanted.
