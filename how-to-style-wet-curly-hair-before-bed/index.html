How To Style Wet Curly Hair Before Bed

Written by Petter Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style it in the morning. One way to make things easier is to style your hair before bed. This way, your hair has the chance to dry overnight and you wake up with perfectly styled curls. In this article, we will show you how to style wet curly hair before bed, step by step.

  • How to Style Wet Curly Hair Before Bed
  • Step by Step

How to Style Wet Curly Hair Before Bed

Styling wet curly hair before bed is a great way to save time in the morning. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to keep your curls defined and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply your favorite styling products to your wet hair. This could be a curl cream, gel, mousse, or any combination of these products. Be sure to apply the products evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair. This will help to distribute the styling products evenly and prevent any tangles from forming overnight.

Step 4: Plop Your Hair

Plopping is a technique that involves wrapping your wet hair in a t-shirt or microfiber towel to help define your curls and reduce frizz. To plop your hair, follow these steps:

  1. Lay a t-shirt or microfiber towel on a flat surface.
  2. Flip your wet hair over and lay it onto the center of the t-shirt or towel.
  3. Wrap the sides of the t-shirt or towel around your head, twisting them at the nape of your neck.
  4. Tuck the ends of the t-shirt or towel into the twist at the nape of your neck.

Step 5: Go to Bed

Leave your hair wrapped in the t-shirt or towel and go to bed. The t-shirt or towel will help to absorb excess moisture and define your curls as you sleep.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style wet curly hair before bed:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

FAQ

Can I style my curly hair before bed if it's not wet?

Yes, you can. However, wet hair is easier to style and will dry overnight, leaving you with perfectly defined curls in the morning.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once a week. Experiment to find what works best for you.

What should I do if my curls look flat in the morning?

Try flipping your head over and scrunching your hair to help revive your curls. You can also use a curl refreshing spray or mist to help redefine your curls.

Hair Care

To keep your curly hair healthy and looking its best, be sure to:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips and techniques, you can style your wet curly hair before bed and wake up with beautiful, defined curls in the morning. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
