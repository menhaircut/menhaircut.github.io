What Size Is Number 4 Haircut A Complete Guide In 2023

Written by Luffy Oct 07, 2023 · 3 min read

Welcome to our ultimate guide on what size is number 4 haircut. Whether you’re a hairstylist or someone who wants to get a haircut, understanding the sizes of clipper guards is important. In this article, we’ll explain what a number 4 haircut is, how to achieve this style, tips, frequently asked questions, and hair care tips for maintaining your haircut.

What is a Number 4 Haircut?

A number 4 haircut is a clipper guard size that leaves the hair 1/2 inch or 12.7 mm long. It’s a popular choice for men who want a short, but not too short, haircut. It’s also a great choice for women who want a short bob or pixie cut.

How to Achieve a Number 4 Haircut

If you’re a hairstylist, you’ll need to use a #4 clipper guard to achieve this haircut. Start at the nape of the neck and work your way up to the crown, using upward strokes. Be sure to blend the sides and the top for a seamless look.

If you’re cutting your own hair, it’s best to start with longer hair and gradually work your way down to a number 4. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make sure it’s even. You can also use scissors to trim any longer hairs that are out of place.

Tips for a Perfect Number 4 Haircut

1. Use a good quality clipper to achieve a clean cut.

2. Start with the longest guard and work your way down to a number 4.

3. Blend the sides and top for a seamless look.

4. Use a mirror to check the back of your head.

5. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that are out of place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I achieve a number 4 haircut with scissors?

A: It’s possible, but it’s not recommended. Clippers are designed to give a clean cut, while scissors can leave uneven edges.

Q: How often should I get a number 4 haircut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. On average, you should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

1. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

2. Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

3. Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length.

4. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

5. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

That’s it for our guide on what size is number 4 haircut. We hope you found this article helpful! Remember, a number 4 haircut is a great choice if you want a short, but not too short, hairstyle that’s easy to maintain.
