How To Cut Pretty Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Bangs can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle without making a drastic change. Whether you have long hair, short hair, curly hair, or straight hair, bangs can work for just about anyone. But, cutting bangs can be intimidating, especially if you've never done it before. In this post, we'll show you how to cut pretty bangs in a relaxed way that won't stress you out.

How to Cut Pretty Bangs

Step 1: Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

Before you start cutting, it's important to choose the right bangs for your face shape. If you have a round face, go for longer, side-swept bangs. If you have a square face, choose soft, wispy bangs. And if you have a heart-shaped face, go for blunt, straight-across bangs.

Step 2: Gather Your Tools

You'll need a few tools to cut your own bangs, including a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. Make sure your scissors are sharp so you can get a clean cut.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Use the comb to section off the hair you want to cut into bangs. Make sure the section is straight and even.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Start with small snips and work your way up, making sure to keep the hair even.

Step 5: Trim Your Bangs

After you've cut your bangs, use the scissors to trim any uneven pieces. You can also use a razor to soften the edges and make the bangs look more natural.

Step 6: Style Your Bangs

Once your bangs are cut, style them to your liking. You can blow dry them straight, curl them under, or leave them natural.

Tips for Cutting Pretty Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your own bangs:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use hair clips to keep the rest of your hair out of the way.
  • Cut your bangs when your hair is dry, not wet. Wet hair can be deceiving and may result in uneven bangs.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short. You can always go back and cut more, but it's harder to fix bangs that are too short.

FAQ

Can I cut bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still cut bangs if you have curly hair. Just make sure to cut them a little longer than you want them to be, as curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries.

How often should I trim my bangs?

It's a good idea to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly. Use a heat protectant if you plan on using heat styling tools, and try to avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut pretty bangs in no time. Just remember to take your time and start with small snips. Happy cutting!
