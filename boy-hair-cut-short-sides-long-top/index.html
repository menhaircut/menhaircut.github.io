Boy Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top The Ultimate Guide In 2023

If you're looking for a stylish and versatile hairstyle for your little boy, the short sides long top cut is the way to go. This popular haircut features a short buzz on the sides and back, while the top is left longer for styling versatility. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about this trendy hairstyle, from how to get it to how to care for it.

Description

The boy hair cut short sides long top is a trendy hairstyle that has been gaining popularity in recent years. This hairstyle is characterized by short buzzed sides and back, while the top is left longer for styling versatility. This haircut is perfect for boys who want a stylish and low-maintenance look that's easy to manage.

How to Get the Boy Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top

To get the boy hair cut short sides long top, you'll need to visit a professional hairstylist or barber. They will use clippers to buzz the sides and back of your child's head, leaving the top longer for styling. You can show your hairstylist pictures of the hairstyle you want to achieve to ensure that they understand your desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting the Boy Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top

If you're feeling confident and want to try cutting your child's hair yourself, here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the boy hair cut short sides long top:

  1. Start by washing and combing your child's hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Use clippers to buzz the sides and back of your child's head, starting with a longer guard and gradually moving to a shorter one.
  3. Leave the top longer and use scissors to cut it to the desired length.
  4. Blend the top with the sides and back using scissors or clippers.
  5. Style the hair on top using a comb or your fingers to achieve the desired look.

Tips for Styling the Boy Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top

Here are some tips for styling the boy hair cut short sides long top:

  • Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to help hold the hair in place.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as a side part or slicked back look.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the shape and length of the cut.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the boy hair cut short sides long top:

What face shape is best suited for the boy hair cut short sides long top?

The boy hair cut short sides long top is a versatile hairstyle that can suit a variety of face shapes. However, it tends to look best on boys with oval, square, or diamond-shaped faces.

How often should I get my child's hair cut?

This will depend on how quickly your child's hair grows and how quickly the style loses its shape. As a general rule, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of the cut.

Hair Care for the Boy Hair Cut Short Sides Long Top

To keep your child's hair looking healthy and styled, it's important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your child's hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as hairdryers or straighteners, which can damage the hair.
  • Protect your child's hair from the sun by using a hat or sunscreen.

With these tips and tricks, your child's short sides long top cut will look stylish and easy to manage. Whether you visit a professional hairstylist or try cutting it yourself, this trendy hairstyle is sure to make your child look and feel great.
