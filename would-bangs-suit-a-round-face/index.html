Would Bangs Suit A Round Face

Welcome to our hair care blog where we will be discussing whether bangs would suit a round face shape. If you are someone with a round face shape, you may be wondering whether bangs would flatter your features. In this article, we will be covering everything you need to know about whether bangs would suit a round face shape.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Determine if Bangs Would Suit a Round Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs for a Round Face Shape
  4. Tips for Styling Bangs with a Round Face Shape
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Bangs with a Round Face Shape

Description

Round face shapes have soft features with a circular shape. If you have a round face shape, you may have fuller cheeks and a rounded jawline. Bangs can be a great way to add structure and dimension to a round face shape. However, not all types of bangs will suit a round face shape. It's important to consider your face shape and hair texture when deciding whether to get bangs.

How to Determine if Bangs Would Suit a Round Face

The best way to determine whether bangs would suit a round face shape is to consider your face shape and hair texture. If you have a round face shape, you want to avoid heavy, blunt bangs that will shorten your face. Instead, opt for side-swept or asymmetrical bangs that will elongate your face. If you have fine hair, consider wispy or choppy bangs to add volume and texture to your hair.

Face Shape Guide

If you are unsure whether you have a round face shape, here is a guide to help you determine your face shape:

  • Oval: Length of your face is greater than the width of your cheekbones
  • Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement
  • Square: Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline have similar measurements
  • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones and narrows at the jawline
  • Diamond: Face length is the greatest measurement with narrow forehead and jawline

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Bangs for a Round Face Shape

If you have decided to get bangs for your round face shape, here is a step-by-step guide to cutting bangs at home:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Section off the hair at the crown of your head and clip it away
  3. Take a small section of hair from the front of your head and comb it straight down
  4. Decide on the length of your bangs and make a small snip with your scissors
  5. Continue to cut small sections of hair until you have reached the desired length
  6. Repeat on the other side of your head, making sure to match the length of your bangs
  7. Blend the bangs into the rest of your hair by point-cutting with your scissors
  8. Style your bangs with a round brush and blow dryer
  9. Finish with hairspray to hold your bangs in place

Tips for Styling Bangs with a Round Face Shape

Here are some tips for styling bangs with a round face shape:

  • Use a round brush to blow dry your bangs to add volume and shape
  • Try pinning your bangs to the side for a different look
  • Avoid heavy styling products that will weigh down your bangs and make them appear greasy
  • Consider getting a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your bangs

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off blunt bangs with a round face?

A: Blunt bangs can be difficult to pull off with a round face shape as they can make your face appear shorter. Instead, opt for side-swept or asymmetrical bangs that will elongate your face.

Q: How do I know if I have a round face shape?

A: If your cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement, you likely have a round face shape. You can also refer to our face shape guide above for more information.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Bangs with a Round Face Shape

Maintaining your bangs with a round face shape requires some extra care. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your bangs:

  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from becoming oily
  • Use dry shampoo to extend the life of your bangs between washes
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your bangs every day to prevent damage

So, would bangs suit a round face? The answer is yes, as long as you choose the right style for your face shape and hair texture. Whether you opt for side-swept, asymmetrical, or wispy bangs, bangs can be a great way to add structure and dimension to a round face shape. Follow our tips and tricks for styling and maintaining your bangs to keep them looking their best.
