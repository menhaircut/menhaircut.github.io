Medium Haircuts For Wavy Hair 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy, unmanageable hair? Do you want to try out a new haircut that complements your natural waves? Look no further than medium haircuts for wavy hair 2021. In this comprehensive guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this trendy and versatile hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Medium haircuts for wavy hair 2021 are all about embracing your natural texture. This hairstyle typically features shoulder-length hair with layers that enhance the waves and create movement. It's a low-maintenance look that can be styled in many different ways, from beachy waves to sleek and straight.

How to

If you're interested in trying out a medium haircut for your wavy hair, the first step is to find a stylist who is familiar with this type of cut. They can help you determine the best length and layering technique for your hair texture and face shape.

When it comes to styling, the key is to use products that enhance your waves without weighing them down. Look for lightweight mousses, gels, and creams that define your curls and add shine. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without creating frizz.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving medium haircuts for wavy hair 2021:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a lightweight styling product to enhance your waves.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair.
  4. If desired, use a curling iron or wand to touch up any loose or frizzy strands.
  5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray or texturizing spray.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting medium haircuts for wavy hair 2021:

  • Communicate clearly with your stylist about your hair goals and preferences.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for wavy hair.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or serums that can weigh down your waves.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Q: What face shapes are best suited for medium haircuts for wavy hair 2021?

A: This hairstyle looks great on a variety of face shapes, but it's particularly flattering for those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight with a medium haircut for wavy hair?

A: Absolutely! While this hairstyle is all about embracing your natural waves, it can also be straightened for a sleek and polished look.

Q: How often should I trim my hair with a medium haircut for wavy hair?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful waves with medium haircuts for wavy hair 2021. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair and create frizz.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and nourished.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner before swimming.

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care routine, medium haircuts for wavy hair 2021 can be a game-changer for anyone looking to embrace their natural texture. So go ahead and book that salon appointment - your waves will thank you!
