How To Cut Short Guy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Cutting short guy hair can be a challenge, especially if you're not used to working with men's hair. But with the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish and modern look that your clients will love. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut short guy hair step by step, from choosing the right haircut to finishing touches. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced stylist, you'll find everything you need to know here.

How to Cut Short Guy Hair

Before you start cutting, it's important to choose the right haircut for your client's face shape and hair type. Some popular short haircuts for men include the buzz cut, crew cut, and textured crop. Once you've chosen the right style, it's time to gather your tools:

  • Hair clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Water spray bottle
  • Cape or towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut short guy hair:

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Spray the hair with water to make it damp, but not soaking wet. Comb the hair to remove any tangles and create an even base.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

Attach the right clipper guard for the length of hair you want to cut. Start with a longer guard and work your way down if necessary.

Step 3: Start at the Sides

Start cutting at the sides of the head, using the clipper to create a straight line from the bottom to the top. Move the clipper in an upward motion to create a gradient effect.

Step 4: Move to the Top

Move to the top of the head and use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut in a straight line.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use the clipper to blend the sides and top together, creating a seamless transition. Move the clipper in an upward motion, using a shorter guard if necessary.

Step 6: Define the Hairline

Use the clipper to define the hairline around the ears and neck, creating a clean and polished look.

Step 7: Finishing Touches

Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs and create a polished finish. Style the hair as desired using pomade or other hair products.

Tips for Cutting Short Guy Hair

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect short haircut:

  • Choose the right haircut for your client's face shape and hair type.
  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down if necessary.
  • Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length.
  • Blend the sides and top together for a seamless transition.
  • Define the hairline around the ears and neck for a clean and polished look.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about cutting short guy hair:

What is the best haircut for short hair?

The best haircut for short hair depends on your client's face shape and hair type. Some popular short haircuts for men include the buzz cut, crew cut, and textured crop.

What tools do I need to cut short guy hair?

You'll need hair clippers, scissors, a comb, water spray bottle, and a cape or towel.

How do I blend the sides and top of short hair?

Use the clipper to create a gradient effect, moving in an upward motion from the bottom to the top. Use a shorter guard if necessary.

Hair Care for Short Guy Hair

To keep short guy hair looking its best, it's important to use the right hair care products. Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for men's hair to keep it clean and healthy. Apply a styling product like pomade or wax to create your desired look. And don't forget to trim the hair regularly to maintain the style.

Cutting short guy hair can be a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish and modern look that your clients will love. Follow our step-by-step guide and use our tips for the perfect cut every time.
