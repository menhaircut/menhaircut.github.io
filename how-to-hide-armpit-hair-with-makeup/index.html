How To Hide Armpit Hair With Makeup A Comprehensive Guide

Armpit hair is a natural phenomenon in both men and women. However, many people prefer to keep their armpits hair-free. While shaving and waxing are the most common ways to remove armpit hair, sometimes we may forget to remove it or don't have enough time. In such situations, makeup can be your savior. How to Hide Armpit Hair with Makeup

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to hide armpit hair with makeup:

Step 1: Exfoliate Your Skin

Before applying makeup, it's important to exfoliate your skin. This will help to remove dead skin cells and make your skin smooth. Use a gentle exfoliating scrub or a loofah to exfoliate your underarm area.

Step 2: Moisturize Your Skin

After exfoliating, it's important to moisturize your skin. This helps to keep your skin hydrated and smooth, making it easier to apply makeup. Choose a lightweight moisturizer that won't clog your pores.

Step 3: Apply Concealer

Choose a concealer that matches your skin tone. Apply the concealer to your underarm area using a clean makeup brush. Blend the concealer well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 4: Apply Foundation

Once you have applied the concealer, it's time to apply foundation. Choose a foundation that matches your skin tone. Apply the foundation to your underarm area using a clean makeup brush. Blend the foundation well to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Set the Makeup

After applying the concealer and foundation, it's important to set the makeup. Use a translucent powder to set the makeup. This will help to keep your makeup in place for a longer time.

Tips for Hiding Armpit Hair with Makeup

Here are some tips that will help you to hide your armpit hair with makeup:

  • Choose a concealer and foundation that matches your skin tone.
  • Blend the concealer and foundation well to avoid any harsh lines.
  • Use a lightweight moisturizer before applying makeup.
  • Set the makeup with translucent powder.
  • Avoid wearing tight clothes that can rub against your underarms and remove the makeup.

FAQs

Can I use any type of makeup to hide my armpit hair?

No, it's important to use makeup that is specifically meant for the underarm area. Avoid using makeup that contains harsh chemicals or fragrances.

How long will the makeup last?

The makeup will last for a few hours. However, it's important to avoid activities that can cause sweating, as this can remove the makeup.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your armpit hair, even if you choose to hide it with makeup. Here are some tips for armpit hair care:

  • Wash your armpit hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or fragrances on your armpit hair.
  • Trim your armpit hair regularly to avoid it from becoming too long or unruly.
  • Avoid wearing tight clothes that can rub against your underarms and cause irritation.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily hide your armpit hair with makeup. However, it's important to remember that armpit hair is a natural phenomenon and there's nothing wrong with having it. The choice to remove or hide it is entirely yours.
