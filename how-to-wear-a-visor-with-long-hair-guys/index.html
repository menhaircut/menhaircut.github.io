How To Wear A Visor With Long Hair Guys

Written by Robby Nov 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with long hair, you might be wondering how to wear a visor without looking like a mess. Visors are a great accessory for outdoor activities, whether you're playing sports or just hanging out in the sun. But when you have long hair, it can be a bit tricky to find the right way to wear a visor that looks good and keeps your hair out of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to wear a visor with long hair:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles and knots.
  2. Pull your hair back into a ponytail, making sure it's secure and not too tight.
  3. Put on the visor, positioning it slightly back from your hairline.
  4. Adjust the visor to make sure it's comfortable and not too tight on your head.
  5. Tuck any loose strands of hair under the visor.
  6. Use bobby pins to secure any stray hairs that won't stay tucked under the visor.

That's it! You're now ready to enjoy your day outdoors without your hair getting in your face.

Tips for Wearing a Visor with Long Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you wear a visor with long hair:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to keep your ponytail looking neat and tidy.
  • Don't pull your hair back too tightly, as this can cause headaches and damage to your hair.
  • If you have bangs or shorter layers, consider using a headband to keep them in place.
  • Choose a visor that fits your head properly and isn't too tight, as this can cause discomfort and headaches.
  • If you're playing sports or doing other activities that require a lot of movement, use extra bobby pins to secure your hair in place.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a visor with long hair:

Can I wear a visor with long hair if I have a receding hairline?

Yes, you can still wear a visor with long hair even if you have a receding hairline. Just make sure to position the visor slightly back from your hairline so it doesn't accentuate the receding hairline.

What type of visor should I wear with long hair?

You can wear any type of visor with long hair, but it's best to choose one that fits your head properly and is comfortable to wear. Look for visors made from lightweight, breathable materials like cotton or polyester.

Do I need to use hairspray to keep my hair in place under a visor?

No, you don't need to use hairspray to keep your hair in place under a visor. Simply tuck any loose strands of hair under the visor and use bobby pins to secure any stray hairs.

Hair Care for Long Hair Guys

Finally, here are some tips for taking care of your long hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause damage and breakage.

By following these tips, you can keep your long hair looking healthy and stylish while wearing a visor.

Now that you know how to wear a visor with long hair guys, you can enjoy your outdoor activities without worrying about your hair getting in your face. Just remember to choose a visor that fits well, tuck your hair under the visor, and use bobby pins to secure any stray hairs. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at wearing a visor with long hair in no time!
