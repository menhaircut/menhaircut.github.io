Best Haircut For Long Face With Wavy Hair

Written by Christine Oct 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't complement your long face with wavy hair? Look no further because we have the perfect solution for you. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for long face with wavy hair, including a description, how-to, step-by-step, tips, FAQ, and hair care. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The best haircut for a long face with wavy hair is a layered cut. Layered cuts add volume and texture to your hair, creating the illusion of a wider face. Additionally, the layers help to control the waviness of your hair, making it more manageable and easier to style.

How To

The first step in getting a layered haircut for a long face with wavy hair is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of cut. It's essential to find someone who understands your hair type and can create a cut that complements your face shape.

Once you have found a stylist, discuss your hair type, face shape, and desired style with them. They may suggest different lengths or layering techniques to achieve the best look for you.

Step-by-Step

1. Wash and condition your hair with products that are suitable for wavy hair.

2. Towel dry your hair, removing as much water as possible.

3. Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or curl cream, to enhance your waves.

4. Using a round brush, blow-dry your hair while scrunching it to enhance your waves.

5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to define any loose or frizzy waves.

6. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

- To maintain your layered cut, visit your stylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim.

- To avoid frizz, use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer when drying your hair.

- Experiment with different styling products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Will a layered cut work for all face shapes?

A: No, a layered cut is best for long face shapes. If you have a different face shape, your stylist may suggest a different type of cut.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight with a layered cut?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair with a layered cut. However, it's essential to use heat protectant products to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your long face with wavy hair looking its best, it's essential to follow a regular hair care routine. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using products that are suitable for your hair type, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. Additionally, be sure to visit your stylist every few months for a trim to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

In conclusion, getting a layered cut is the best haircut for a long face with wavy hair. It adds volume, texture, and manageability to your hair, creating a flattering and stylish look. Follow our how-to and tips, and you'll be rocking your new hairdo in no time!
