Short Hair Cut For Round Face And Curly Hair How To Get The Perfect Look

Written by Robby Jun 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of dealing with long, unruly curls that always seem to get in your way? Or do you have a round face that makes it difficult to find a haircut that flatters your features? If so, a short hair cut for round face and curly hair might be just what you need. This style is not only practical and low maintenance, but it can also enhance your natural beauty and give you a more youthful appearance.

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step

Description

A short hair cut for round face and curly hair is a versatile and fashionable style that can be customized to suit your individual preferences. It typically involves cutting the hair to a length that hits just below the chin or slightly above the shoulders, with layers added to create volume and movement. This style works well for those with naturally curly or wavy hair, as the texture adds dimension and body to the hair.

In addition, a short hair cut for round face and curly hair can help to elongate the face and draw attention to your best features. By framing the face with shorter layers and keeping the hair off the shoulders, you can create a more defined jawline and highlight your cheekbones.

How to

If you're interested in getting a short hair cut for round face and curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture. A skilled hairstylist can help you select a cut that will flatter your features and enhance your natural curls.

Once you've chosen a style, you'll need to maintain your haircut to keep it looking fresh and stylish. This may involve regular trims every 6-8 weeks, as well as using styling products to enhance your curls and add volume. You may also want to invest in a diffuser attachment for your hair dryer, which can help to enhance your curls without causing frizz or damage.

Step by step

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a short hair cut for round face and curly hair, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to ensure that the style will work well for your hair type and lifestyle.
  3. Bring photos of the style you want to your appointment to help your hairstylist understand your vision.
  4. Be prepared to invest in high-quality styling products and tools to maintain your haircut at home.
  5. Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your short hair cut for round face and curly hair:

  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your hair dryer to enhance your curls without causing damage.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing cream to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will a short hair cut for round face and curly hair work for me if I have very thick hair?

A: Yes, a short hair cut for round face and curly hair can work well for those with thick hair, as the layers can help to create volume and movement.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut if I have a short hair cut for round face and curly hair?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Q: Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a short hair cut for round face and curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your hair if you have a short hair cut for round face and curly hair. However, it's important to use a heat protectant and avoid over-styling to prevent damage.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair cut for round face and curly hair looking its best, it's important to follow a consistent hair care routine. This may involve using sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair's natural oils, as well as using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

In addition, it's important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners. You may also want to consider sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

With the right care and styling, a short hair cut for round face and curly hair can be a flattering and stylish choice for those with curly or wavy hair. Consult with a skilled hairstylist to find the perfect cut for your face shape and hair texture, and enjoy the low-maintenance beauty of this versatile style.
