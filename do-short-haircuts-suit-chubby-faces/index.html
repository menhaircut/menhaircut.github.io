Do Short Haircuts Suit Chubby Faces

Written by Frank Sep 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a chubby face and are looking for a new hairstyle, you may be wondering if a short haircut is the right choice. Short haircuts are trendy and stylish, but do they suit chubby faces? In this article, we will explore this topic and provide you with tips on how to choose the perfect short haircut for your face shape.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Chubby Faces

How to Choose a Short Haircut for Chubby Faces

The key to choosing the right short haircut for a chubby face is to create balance. A short haircut can make your face appear slimmer and more youthful, but it can also accentuate the roundness of your face if not styled correctly. The following are tips on how to choose a short haircut that will flatter your face shape:

  • Opt for short haircuts that add height to the crown of your head, such as pixie cuts and spiky hairstyles. This will create an illusion of a longer and slimmer face.
  • Avoid short haircuts that are too blunt or straight across, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Add layers to your short haircut to create movement and texture, which can also help to slim down the appearance of your face.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs to your short haircut, as they can help to balance out the roundness of your face.

By following these tips, you can choose a short haircut that will complement your face shape and highlight your best features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Chubby Faces

Now that you have chosen the perfect short haircut for your chubby face, it is time to style it. The following is a step-by-step guide on how to style short haircuts for chubby faces:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots of your hair.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.
  4. If your short haircut has bangs, use a flat iron to straighten them and sweep them to the side.
  5. Apply a small amount of hair wax or pomade to your fingertips and scrunch your hair to create texture and movement.
  6. Finish off your look with a light hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

By following these simple steps, you can style your short haircut to flatter your chubby face and create a trendy and fashionable look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Chubby Faces

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. The following are tips for maintaining short haircuts for chubby faces:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to maintain your short haircut and keep it looking fresh.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis, as they can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.
  • Incorporate a hair serum or oil into your hair care routine to add shine and smoothness to your short haircut.

By following these tips, you can keep your short haircut looking its best and maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

FAQs

Q. Can I wear a bob haircut if I have a chubby face?

A. Yes, you can wear a bob haircut if you have a chubby face. However, it is important to choose a bob haircut that is longer in the front and shorter in the back, as this will create an elongated appearance to your face.

Q. Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a double chin?

A. No, you do not need to avoid short haircuts if you have a double chin. Instead, choose a short haircut that adds height to the crown of your head and creates an elongated appearance to your face.

Q. Can I wear a pixie cut if I have a round face?

A. Yes, you can wear a pixie cut if you have a round face. However, it is important to choose a pixie cut that adds height to the crown of your head and creates an elongated appearance to your face.

Hair Care Tips for Chubby Faces with Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require special care to keep them looking their best. The following are hair care tips for chubby faces with short haircuts:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Blot your hair dry with a towel instead of rubbing it vigorously, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Incorporate a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment into your hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your short haircut looking its best and maintain healthy and beautiful hair.

Conclusion

In conclusion, short haircuts can suit chubby faces if styled correctly. By choosing the right short haircut for your face shape, styling it correctly, and maintaining healthy hair care habits, you can create a trendy and fashionable look that highlights your best features. Use these tips to find the perfect short haircut for your chubby face and enjoy your new look!
