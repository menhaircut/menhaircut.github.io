Medium Long Hairstyles For Guys How To Rock This Trendy Look

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to add to your grooming routine? Medium long hairstyles for guys are a great option for those who want to rock a trendy, versatile look. In this article, we’ll give you tips and tricks on how to style your hair in medium long hairstyles for guys. From how to choose the right cut to hair care tips, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Cut
  • Styling Tips for Medium Long Hairstyles for Guys
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Medium Long Hairstyles for Guys

How to Choose the Right Cut

Before you can start styling your medium long hairstyle, you need to choose the right cut. This will depend on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some popular medium long hairstyles for guys:

1. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great option for those with thick hair. Layers will help to remove some of the bulk while still keeping the length. This cut works well for oval and square face shapes.

2. Side-Parted Cut

The side-parted cut is a classic look that never goes out of style. This cut works well for those with straight or wavy hair. It’s also a great option for those with round or heart-shaped faces.

3. Textured Cut

A textured cut is great for those who want a more relaxed, messy look. This cut works well for those with curly or wavy hair. It’s also a great option for those with angular face shapes.

Styling Tips for Medium Long Hairstyles for Guys

Now that you’ve chosen the right cut, it’s time to style your medium long hairstyle. Here are some tips to help you get the look you want:

1. Use the Right Products

The right hair products can make all the difference in styling your medium long hairstyle. Use a matte pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair. Use a salt spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help to add volume and shape to your medium long hairstyle. Use a round brush to create volume and shape as you blow dry your hair. Use a cool setting to set your hair in place.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles for your medium long hairstyle. Try a side part, slicked back look, or messy texture. Find the style that works best for you.

FAQs

1. How often should I trim my medium long hairstyle?

You should trim your medium long hairstyle every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

2. Can I dye my medium long hairstyle?

Yes, you can dye your medium long hairstyle. However, it’s important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and personal style.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Long Hairstyles for Guys

Keeping your hair healthy is key to maintaining your medium long hairstyle. Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your hair looking and feeling great:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed to moisturize your hair. This will help to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons on your hair. These tools can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun’s harmful rays.


In conclusion, medium long hairstyles for guys are a versatile and trendy option for those looking to switch up their grooming routine. By choosing the right cut, using the right products, and taking care of your hair, you can rock this look with confidence. So go ahead, try out one of these medium long hairstyles and see how you like it!
