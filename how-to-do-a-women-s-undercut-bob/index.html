How To Do A Women s Undercut Bob Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 08, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle and make a bold statement, a women's undercut bob could be just what you need. This edgy cut features a longer top and shorter sides and back, with the hair underneath shaved close to the scalp. In this step-by-step guide, we'll walk you through the process of getting a women's undercut bob and provide you with some tips and tricks for caring for your new style.

Description

The women's undercut bob is a popular hairstyle that combines a classic bob cut with an edgy undercut. This cut features a longer top section that can be styled in various ways, while the sides and back are buzzed or shaved close to the scalp. This creates a dramatic contrast between the longer top section and the shorter undercut, making it a bold and eye-catching style.

How to

Getting a women's undercut bob involves a few key steps:

Step 1: Decide on the length and style

Before you get your undercut bob, you'll need to decide on the length and style you want. The length of the top section can vary from chin-length to shoulder-length, while the undercut can be buzzed or shaved at different lengths. You'll also want to consider how you want to style the top section, whether you prefer a sleek and straight look or loose waves.

Step 2: Find a stylist

Next, you'll need to find a stylist who specializes in women's undercut bobs. Look for someone who has experience with this style and can provide you with the look you want. You may want to bring in some photos of the style you're going for to give your stylist a visual idea of what you want.

Step 3: Get the cut

Once you've found a stylist, it's time to get the cut. Your stylist will start by sectioning off the top section of your hair and clipping it up. Then, they'll buzz or shave the sides and back to your desired length. Finally, they'll release the top section and cut it to your desired length and style.

Step 4: Style and maintain

After you've gotten your undercut bob, it's important to properly style and maintain it to keep it looking its best. You may need to invest in some new hair products, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to help style the top section. You'll also need to schedule regular appointments with your stylist to keep the undercut looking fresh and tidy.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide to getting a women's undercut bob:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Before you head to the salon, wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier for your stylist to work with and give them a better idea of your natural texture and thickness.

Step 2: Decide on the length and style

Before your appointment, spend some time thinking about the length and style you want for your undercut bob. Consider the shape of your face, your personal style, and how much maintenance you're willing to do.

Step 3: Find a stylist

Research stylists in your area who specialize in women's undercut bobs. Look for someone who has experience with this style and can provide you with the look you want.

Step 4: Consult with your stylist

When you arrive at the salon, consult with your stylist about the look you want. Bring in some photos to give them a visual idea of what you're going for.

Step 5: Section your hair

Your stylist will start by sectioning off the top section of your hair and clipping it up. This will keep it out of the way while they work on the undercut.

Step 6: Buzz or shave the undercut

Your stylist will use clippers or a razor to buzz or shave the sides and back of your head to your desired length. This may take some time, so be patient.

Step 7: Cut the top section

Once the undercut is done, your stylist will release the top section of your hair and cut it to your desired length and style. This may involve some layering, texturizing, or thinning depending on the look you want.

Step 8: Style and maintain

After your haircut, your stylist will style your hair and show you how to maintain it at home. You may need to invest in some new hair products, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to help style the top section. You'll also need to schedule regular appointments with your stylist to keep the undercut looking fresh and tidy.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your women's undercut bob:

  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy
  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to the top section of your hair
  • Schedule regular appointments with your stylist to keep the undercut looking fresh and tidy
  • Experiment with different styles and accessories to make the look your own

FAQ

1. Is a women's undercut bob high maintenance?

While a women's undercut bob can be a bit high maintenance, it ultimately depends on the length and style you choose. If you opt for a shorter undercut and a simple style for the top section, it can be relatively easy to maintain with regular trims and styling.

2. Will an undercut bob suit me?

An undercut bob can suit a variety of face shapes and hair textures, but it's important to consult with a stylist to find the best look for you. They can help you choose a length and style that flatters your features and complements your personal style.

Hair Care

To care for your women's undercut bob, it's important to invest in high-quality hair products that nourish and protect your hair. Look for products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and texture, and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often. Regular trims and appointments with your stylist can also help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.
