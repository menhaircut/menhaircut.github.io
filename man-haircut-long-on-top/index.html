Man Haircut Long on Top: How to Get the Look in 2023

If you're a man looking for a stylish and trendy haircut, then the long on top haircut is perfect for you. This hairstyle is versatile, easy to style, and looks great on any face shape. In this article, we will discuss how to get the man haircut long on top look in 2023, along with some tips and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Look
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Long on Top Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Long on Top Haircut

Description

The man haircut long on top is a popular hairstyle that involves keeping the hair on top long while trimming the back and sides short. This haircut is perfect for men who want a stylish and trendy look that is easy to maintain.

The long on top haircut can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or curly. It's a versatile haircut that can be adapted to suit any occasion or event.

How to Get the Look

The man haircut long on top is easy to get, but it's important to find a skilled barber who can achieve the look you want. Start by bringing a picture of the hairstyle you want to your barber, and communicate clearly about the length you want on top and the style you prefer.

It's important to note that the long on top haircut requires regular maintenance to keep it looking fresh and stylish. You'll need to visit your barber every four to six weeks to keep the back and sides trimmed and the top length in check.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the man haircut long on top:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Visit a skilled barber and bring a picture of the hairstyle you want.
  3. Communicate clearly about the length you want on top and the style you prefer.
  4. The barber will trim the back and sides while leaving the top long.
  5. Style your hair according to your preference.

Tips for Long on Top Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you want to get the man haircut long on top:

  • Choose a length that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep the back and sides trimmed and the top length in check.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Is the man haircut long on top suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the long on top haircut is versatile and can be adapted to suit any face shape.

Q: Can the long on top haircut be styled in different ways?

A: Yes, the long on top haircut can be styled in various ways, including slicked back, messy, or curly.

Q: How often do I need to visit my barber for a long on top haircut?

A: You'll need to visit your barber every four to six weeks to keep the back and sides trimmed and the top length in check.

Hair Care for Long on Top Haircut

Here are some tips to keep your long on top haircut looking healthy and stylish:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Invest in a good quality styling product that works for your hair type.
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh and stylish.

Getting the man haircut long on top is easy, but it's important to find a skilled barber who can achieve the look you want. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can keep your long on top haircut looking stylish and trendy. So go ahead and try out this versatile and trendy haircut in 2023!
