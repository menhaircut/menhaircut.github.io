What Is The Second Rarest Eye Color And Hair Color Combination

Written by Frank Jun 25, 2023 · 3 min read

If you’re curious about the rarest eye color and hair color combination, you might be surprised to learn that it’s not what you think. While green eyes and red hair are often considered the rarest combination, they’re actually not the second rarest. So, what is the second rarest eye color and hair color combination?

The Answer: Blue Eyes and Red Hair

That’s right - blue eyes and red hair are the second rarest eye color and hair color combination. According to genetics experts, only about 1% of the world’s population has this unique combination.

Why is Blue Eyes and Red Hair So Rare?

Blue eyes are already a rare eye color, with only about 17% of the world’s population having them. When you add red hair into the mix, the odds of finding someone with both traits become even slimmer.

The rarity of blue eyes and red hair is due to genetics. Both traits are recessive, meaning they can only be expressed if an individual inherits two copies of the gene - one from each parent.

How to Determine Your Eye and Hair Color Combination

If you’re curious about your own eye and hair color combination, take a look in the mirror. Your eye color is easy to spot, but determining your hair color may require a closer look.

Start by determining your base hair color - is it blonde, brunette, red, or black? From there, look for any highlights or lowlights that might change the overall color.

Another option is to consult with a professional hairstylist or colorist. They can help you determine your exact hair color and suggest ways to enhance or change it if desired.

Tips for Caring for Blue Eyes and Red Hair

If you’re lucky enough to have blue eyes and red hair, you may want to take extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Protect your skin from the sun with sunscreen and protective clothing to prevent sunburn and skin damage.
  • Use gentle, sulfate-free shampoos and conditioners to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use products specifically designed for color-treated hair to keep your red hair vibrant.
  • Choose makeup colors that complement your blue eyes and red hair, such as warm neutrals, golds, and browns.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about blue eyes and red hair:

Q: Are blue eyes and red hair more common in certain parts of the world?
A: While blue eyes and red hair are rare overall, they are slightly more common in certain parts of the world, such as Scandinavia and Ireland. Q: Can you have blue eyes and red hair if neither of your parents have those traits?
A: It’s possible, but unlikely. Blue eyes and red hair are both recessive traits, so they can only be expressed if an individual inherits two copies of the gene - one from each parent. Q: Can you dye your hair red if you have blue eyes?
A: Absolutely! Anyone can dye their hair red, regardless of eye color. However, if you have blue eyes and red hair naturally, you may want to choose a shade of red that complements your eye color.

In Conclusion

While green eyes and red hair may be the combination that first comes to mind when you think of rare eye and hair colors, blue eyes and red hair are actually the second rarest combination. If you’re lucky enough to have this unique combination, take care of your eyes and hair and embrace your individuality!
