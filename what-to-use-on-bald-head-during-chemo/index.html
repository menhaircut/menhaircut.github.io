How to Care for Your Bald Head During Chemotherapy

Chemotherapy can cause hair loss, including on the scalp. This can lead to scalp irritation, dryness, and sensitivity. Here are some tips on how to care for your bald head during chemotherapy:

  • Wash your scalp with a gentle, fragrance-free shampoo
  • Avoid using hot water on your scalp
  • Pat your scalp dry gently with a soft towel
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as hair dyes or perms
