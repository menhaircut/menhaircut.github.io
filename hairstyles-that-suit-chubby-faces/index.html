Hairstyles That Suit Chubby Faces Tips Tricks And Hair Care Guide

Written by Frank Aug 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Having a chubby face can be both a blessing and a challenge. On one hand, it can give you a youthful and cute look. On the other hand, it can make you feel self-conscious about your appearance, especially when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. If you're struggling with finding the right haircut for your chubby face, don't worry. In this guide, we'll share some tips, tricks, and hairstyle ideas that will suit your face shape and make you feel confident and beautiful.

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Hairstyles That Suit Chubby Faces
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs About Hairstyles for Chubby Faces

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for your chubby face is to determine your face shape. There are several face shapes, including oval, round, square, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you drew.
  4. Compare your shape to the five face shapes listed above to determine your face shape.

Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will flatter your features.

Hairstyles That Suit Chubby Faces

When it comes to hairstyles for chubby faces, the key is to create balance and symmetry. You want to avoid styles that add width to your face, as this can make it look even rounder. Here are some hairstyles that will suit your chubby face:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate your face and create a slimming effect. Ask your stylist to add layers that start below your chin and angle downwards. This will frame your face and draw attention away from your cheeks.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create an illusion of a slimmer face. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs diagonally across your forehead and sweep them to one side. This will draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, instead of your cheeks.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a great option for chubby faces, especially if you have a short neck. Ask your stylist to add volume to the top of your head and keep the sides short. This will create a balanced look and highlight your best features.

4. Bob Cut

A bob cut can be a flattering option for chubby faces, as long as it's not too short. Ask your stylist to cut your hair to chin-length and add some layers. This will create movement and texture, and draw attention away from your cheeks.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've found the right hairstyle for your chubby face, you want to make sure you style it properly to enhance your features. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

1. Use Hair Products

Use hair products that will add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse, texturizing spray, or dry shampoo. This will create movement and lift, and make your hair look fuller and more voluminous.

2. Avoid Center Parts

Avoid center parts, as they can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for side parts or deep side parts, as they will create a more balanced look.

3. Add Highlights

Add highlights to your hair to create dimension and depth. This will draw attention away from your cheeks and make your face look slimmer.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Chubby Faces

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail, but make sure it's not too tight. A loose ponytail will create a more relaxed and effortless look, and won't accentuate your cheeks.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have a chubby face?

A: It's not recommended to wear your hair in a middle part if you have a chubby face, as it can make your face look wider. Instead, opt for side parts or deep side parts.

Hair Care Tips for Chubby Faces

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for chubby faces:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are sulfate-free and contain natural ingredients.

2. Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair, and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing.

3. Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray. This will protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.

By following these tips, tricks, and hairstyle ideas, you can find the perfect haircut for your chubby face and feel confident and beautiful.
