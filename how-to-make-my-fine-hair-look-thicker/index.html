How To Make My Fine Hair Look Thicker

Written by Bardi Jun 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have fine hair that always looks limp and lifeless? Do you want to know how to make your hair look thicker? You've come to the right place! In this article, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to make your fine hair look thicker and fuller.

Description

Having fine hair can be frustrating, especially when you want to achieve a voluminous hairstyle. Fine hair tends to fall flat and lacks body, making it difficult to style. But don't worry, there are ways to make your hair look thicker without having to spend a fortune on hair products or salon treatments.

How to Make My Fine Hair Look Thicker

1. Use the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Choosing the right shampoo and conditioner is crucial when it comes to making your hair look thicker. Look for products that are designed for fine hair and claim to add volume. Avoid using heavy or moisturizing shampoos and conditioners as they can weigh your hair down.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a simple trick that can add volume to your hair. Flip your hair over and blow dry it in that position. This will create lift at the roots, making your hair appear thicker and fuller.

3. Use a Root-Lifting Spray

A root-lifting spray is a great way to add volume to your hair. Apply the spray to your roots before blow drying your hair. This will give your hair an extra lift and create the illusion of thicker hair.

4. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an old-school trick that still works wonders in making your hair look thicker. Take a small section of hair and backcomb it gently with a comb. This will create volume at the roots and make your hair look fuller.

5. Use Hair Extensions

If you want an instant solution to thick hair, hair extensions are your best bet. You can choose from clip-in extensions, tape-in extensions or sew-in extensions. They come in different lengths and colors, so you can find the perfect match for your hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2: Apply a Root-Lifting Spray

Apply a root-lifting spray to your damp hair and comb it through.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your hair over and blow dry it in that position. Use a round brush to create volume at the roots.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Take small sections of hair and tease them gently with a comb.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis as they can damage your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use hair mousse to make my hair look thicker?

A: Yes, hair mousse is a great styling product that can add volume to your hair. Apply it to damp hair before blow drying for best results.

Q: Will using hair extensions damage my hair?

A: Hair extensions can cause damage to your hair if not installed or maintained properly. Make sure you seek professional help when installing extensions and follow the recommended maintenance routine.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your fine hair look thicker and fuller. Remember, it's all about creating volume at the roots and using the right styling products. With a little bit of effort, you can achieve the voluminous hairstyle you've always wanted!
