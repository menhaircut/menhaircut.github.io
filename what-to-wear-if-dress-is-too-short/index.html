What To Wear If Dress Is Too Short Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’ve ever found yourself in this situation, you know how frustrating it can be. You’ve found the perfect dress, but it’s just a little too short. Don’t worry, there are plenty of ways to make it work. In this article, we’ll discuss how to wear a too-short dress while still looking stylish and feeling comfortable.

Description

The first thing you need to do is assess the situation. Is the dress just a little too short, or is it borderline inappropriate? If it’s the latter, you may need to consider finding a different outfit. But if it’s just a little too short, there are a few things you can do to make it work.

How to

One of the easiest solutions is to wear leggings or tights underneath the dress. This will not only make the dress more appropriate in length, but it will also keep you warm if it’s a cooler day. If you’re worried about the look being too casual, opt for opaque tights in a neutral color like black or navy.

If you’d prefer not to wear tights, you can also try layering the dress over a pair of skinny jeans or pants. This will create a more casual look, but it can still be stylish if you pair it with the right shoes and accessories.

Another option is to wear a longer jacket or blazer over the dress. This will not only add some length to the outfit, but it will also create a more structured look. Just make sure that the jacket or blazer is tailored and not too bulky, otherwise it will overwhelm the dress.

Step by Step

If you’re still struggling to make the dress work, try these steps:

  1. Assess the situation and determine if the dress is appropriate in length.
  2. If the dress is too short, try wearing leggings or tights underneath.
  3. Alternatively, layer the dress over a pair of skinny jeans or pants.
  4. Wear a longer jacket or blazer over the dress for a more structured look.
  5. Accessorize with statement jewelry or a belt to draw attention away from the length of the dress.
  6. Choose the right shoes – a pair of heels can elongate your legs and make the dress appear longer.
  7. Consider altering the dress – a tailor can add length to the hemline or create a more modest neckline.
  8. Experiment with different styling options until you find a look that works for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid wearing the dress with flats or sneakers, as this can make the outfit look frumpy.
  • Don’t be afraid to add some color or pattern to the outfit – just make sure that it doesn’t clash with the dress.
  • If you’re wearing a shorter dress, make sure that your hair and makeup are on point to balance out the look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what to wear if a dress is too short:

Q: Can I wear a short dress to a formal event?

A: It depends on the dress code of the event. If the dress code is black tie or formal, it’s best to choose a longer dress.

Q: How can I make a short dress look more formal?

A: Add some statement jewelry, wear a pair of heels, and carry a structured clutch. You can also opt for a dress with a more modest neckline and hemline.

Hair Care

When wearing a shorter dress, it’s important to consider your hair and makeup. A sleek updo or polished blowout can add sophistication to the outfit, while messy or undone hair can make the look appear too casual. Choose a hairstyle that complements the dress and makes you feel confident.
