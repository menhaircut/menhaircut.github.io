Does Thyroid Hair Loss Grow Back Everything You Need To Know

Written by Luffy Jun 15, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss due to thyroid issues? It can be a frustrating and disheartening experience, but the good news is that in most cases, thyroid hair loss does grow back. In this article, we’ll explore everything you need to know about thyroid hair loss and how to promote hair growth.

What Causes Thyroid Hair Loss?

The thyroid gland is responsible for producing hormones that regulate the body’s metabolism. When the thyroid gland is not functioning properly, it can lead to hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism. Both of these conditions can cause hair loss.

Hypothyroidism is a condition where the thyroid gland is not producing enough hormones. This can lead to dry, brittle hair that breaks easily. Hyperthyroidism, on the other hand, causes an overproduction of hormones, which can lead to thinning hair.

Does Thyroid Hair Loss Grow Back?

Many people wonder if their hair will grow back once their thyroid issues are under control. The answer is yes, in most cases. Once the thyroid gland is functioning properly, hair growth should return to normal within six to twelve months.

How to Promote Hair Growth

While waiting for your hair to grow back, there are steps you can take to promote hair growth and improve the overall health of your hair.

1. Take Your Medication

If you have hypothyroidism or hyperthyroidism, it’s important to take your medication as prescribed. This will help regulate your hormone levels and promote hair growth.

2. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet is important for overall health, but it’s also important for hair growth. Make sure to eat plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables to provide your body with the nutrients it needs to grow healthy hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage hair and make it more prone to breakage. Avoid using flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers as much as possible.

4. Consider Supplements

There are several supplements that can promote hair growth, including biotin, iron, and vitamin D. Talk to your doctor before taking any supplements to make sure they won’t interfere with your medication.

FAQ

How long does it take for hair to grow back after thyroid treatment?

It can take six to twelve months for hair to grow back after thyroid treatment.

Can thyroid hair loss be reversed?

Yes, thyroid hair loss can be reversed once the thyroid gland is functioning properly.

What is the best shampoo for thyroid hair loss?

There is no specific shampoo that is best for thyroid hair loss, but using a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo can help promote hair health.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to promote hair growth:

1. Don’t Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb is gentler on hair than a brush and can help prevent breakage.

3. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help prevent split ends, which can lead to breakage and prevent hair growth.

4. Be Gentle When Brushing

When brushing your hair, be gentle and start at the ends, working your way up to the roots. This can help prevent breakage and promote hair growth.

Final Thoughts

Thyroid hair loss can be a frustrating experience, but with the right treatment and care, hair growth should return to normal within six to twelve months. Follow these tips to promote hair growth and improve the overall health of your hair.
