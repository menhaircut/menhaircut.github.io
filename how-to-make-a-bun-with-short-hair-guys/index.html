How To Make A Bun With Short Hair Guys

Written by Oliver Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a guy with short hair who wants to switch up your hairstyle? A bun may be the perfect option for you! Contrary to popular belief, you don't need long hair to achieve a stylish and trendy bun. In this article, we'll show you how to make a bun with short hair guys, step by step.

Description

A bun is a classic hairstyle that has been around for centuries. It's a versatile style that can be worn for both formal and casual occasions. A bun can also help keep your hair out of your face, which is especially useful during hot and humid weather. For guys with short hair, a bun can help add volume and texture to your hair.

How to Make a Bun with Short Hair Guys

Making a bun with short hair guys is easier than you might think. Follow these steps to achieve a stylish and trendy bun:

Step 1: Start with clean hair

Before you start making your bun, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to wash your hair, then towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply some hair product

To help your hair stay in place and add some texture, apply a small amount of hair product to your hair. Use a pomade or wax that has a medium hold to keep your hair in place without making it look greasy.

Step 3: Gather your hair

Gather all your hair at the back of your head, making sure to leave out any short strands of hair that won't fit into the bun.

Step 4: Twist your hair

Twist your hair in a clockwise motion until it starts to coil up. Keep twisting until your hair forms a bun shape.

Step 5: Secure your bun

Use a hair tie or elastic band to secure your bun. Make sure it's tight enough to keep your hair in place, but not so tight that it hurts.

Step 6: Adjust your bun

Adjust your bun by pulling on the edges to make it look more voluminous. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair that are sticking out.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the perfect bun:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Don't make your bun too tight, as this can cause headaches and hair breakage.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making a bun with short hair guys:

Can guys with short hair make a bun?

Yes, guys with short hair can make a bun. You don't need long hair to achieve a stylish and trendy bun.

What hair products should I use to make a bun with short hair guys?

Use a pomade or wax with a medium hold to help your hair stay in place and add some texture.

How tight should I make my bun?

Your bun should be tight enough to keep your hair in place, but not so tight that it hurts or causes hair breakage.

Hair Care

While making a bun with short hair guys can be a stylish and trendy option, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make a bun with short hair guys in no time. Remember to experiment with different hair products and techniques to find the style that works best for you. Happy styling!
