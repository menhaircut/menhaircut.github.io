How To Slick Your Hair Back Like A Greaser

Written by Eveline Jun 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fan of the classic 1950s greaser look? Do you want to know how to slick your hair back like a greaser? Look no further, because we have the ultimate guide for you.

How to Slick Your Hair Back Like a Greaser

Slicking your hair back like a greaser is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. The key to achieving this look is to use the right products and techniques. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Pomade or hair gel
  • Comb or brush
  • Hairspray (optional)

Once you have these items, you can start to slick your hair back like a greaser.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve the perfect greaser hairstyle:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it is slightly damp.
  2. Take a small amount of pomade or hair gel and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the pomade or hair gel to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly from root to tip.
  4. Using a comb or brush, slick your hair back starting from the front of your hairline.
  5. Continue to comb or brush your hair back, making sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps.
  6. Once you have slicked your hair back, you can use hairspray to hold it in place.
  7. Repeat these steps if necessary, depending on the length and thickness of your hair.

And there you have it – the perfect greaser hairstyle!

Tips for Slicking Your Hair Back Like a Greaser

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect greaser hairstyle:

  • Use a comb with fine teeth to get a tight, slick look.
  • Don’t use too much pomade or hair gel, or it will make your hair look greasy.
  • Experiment with different types of pomade or hair gel to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • If you want a more natural look, use a matte-finish pomade or hair gel instead of a shiny one.
  • Don’t forget to wash your hair thoroughly to avoid build-up of product.

FAQs

Q: Can I slick my hair back like a greaser if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can slick your hair back like a greaser even if you have curly hair. However, you may need to use more product and a stronger hold hairspray to keep your curls in place.

Q: Can I slick my hair back like a greaser if I have short hair?

A: Yes, you can slick your hair back like a greaser even if you have short hair. Just use a small amount of product and comb your hair back tightly.

Q: Will slicking my hair back like a greaser damage my hair?

A: No, slicking your hair back like a greaser will not damage your hair as long as you use the right products and wash your hair regularly.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly to avoid build-up of product.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to slick your hair back like a greaser, you can rock this classic look with confidence. Just remember to use the right products and techniques, and don’t be afraid to experiment until you find the perfect style for you.
