How To Fix Messed Up Barbie Hair

Written by Eveline Aug 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Barbie dolls are a favorite among children and collectors alike. However, their hair can easily become tangled and messy, making it difficult to play with or display the doll. In this article, we will show you step-by-step how to fix messed up Barbie hair and restore it to its former glory.

Description

Barbie hair is made of synthetic fibers and is prone to tangling and matting. This is especially true if the doll has been played with frequently or stored improperly. Fixing Barbie hair can seem daunting, but with a few simple steps, you can have it looking like new again.

How To

To fix messed up Barbie hair, you will need a few tools:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Small scissors
  • Fabric softener
  • Warm water

Follow these steps:

Step 1: Detangle the Hair

Using a wide-tooth comb, gently start at the ends of the hair and work your way up to the roots. Be careful not to pull or tug on the hair, as this can cause further damage.

Step 2: Cut Any Knots or Tangles

If the hair has any large knots or tangles that cannot be combed out, use small scissors to carefully cut them out. Be sure to cut as close to the knot as possible without damaging the rest of the hair.

Step 3: Soak the Hair

Mix warm water with a small amount of fabric softener in a bowl. Soak the doll's hair in the mixture for 15-20 minutes. This will help to soften the hair and make it easier to comb.

Step 4: Comb the Hair Again

After soaking, remove the doll's hair from the bowl and gently comb it again with a wide-tooth comb. Be sure to start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Step 5: Rinse and Dry the Hair

Rinse the doll's hair with warm water to remove any remaining fabric softener. Gently squeeze out any excess water and lay the doll on a towel to air dry. Do not use a hair dryer, as this can damage the synthetic fibers.

Tips

  • Use a detangling spray to help ease the comb through the hair.
  • Be patient and work slowly to avoid damaging the hair.
  • Store the doll's hair in a braid or ponytail to prevent tangling in the future.

FAQ

Q: Can I use regular shampoo to clean Barbie hair?

A: No, regular shampoo can damage the synthetic fibers in the hair. Use fabric softener instead.

Q: How often should I clean Barbie hair?

A: It depends on how often the doll is played with or displayed. If the hair starts to look dull or tangled, it's time for a cleaning.

Hair Care

To keep Barbie hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can damage the synthetic fibers.
  • Store the doll's hair in a braid or ponytail to prevent tangling.
  • Clean the hair regularly with fabric softener to keep it soft and shiny.

By following these steps and tips, you can easily fix messed up Barbie hair and keep it looking great for years to come.
