Easy Care Short Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Eveline Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, there are plenty of easy care short hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and make you feel confident and stylish. In this article, we'll share some tips and ideas for short hairstyles that are perfect for round faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Short hairstyles are ideal for round faces because they can help to elongate the face and create a more angular look. The key is to choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown of the head, while keeping the sides and back relatively short. This can help to balance out the proportions of the face and create a more flattering shape.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider your hair type, texture, and personal style. Some popular options for round faces include pixie cuts, bobs, and layered styles. You can also experiment with different lengths and angles to find the perfect look for you.

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short, cropped style that can be very flattering for round faces. The key is to keep the hair on top of the head longer, while tapering the sides and back. This can help to add height and create a more angular look. You can also add side-swept bangs to create a softer, more feminine style.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic short hairstyle that can work well for round faces. The key is to keep the length at or just below the chin, with layers that frame the face. This can help to add volume and create a more angular shape. You can also add bangs to create a more dramatic look.

3. Layered Style

A layered style can be a great option for round faces, as it can help to create movement and volume. The key is to keep the layers long and wispy, with shorter layers around the face. This can help to elongate the face and create a more flattering shape. You can also add side-swept bangs to create a more feminine look.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a short hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair, then towel-dry it gently.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the crown of your head to add volume.
  3. Use a straightening iron to smooth any frizz or flyaways.
  4. If you're creating a bob or layered style, use a razor to create wispy layers around the face.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims to keep your style looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different products, such as mousse or wax, to create different textures and finishes.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round faces:

What is the best short hairstyle for a round face?

The best short hairstyle for a round face is one that adds height and volume to the crown of the head, while keeping the sides and back relatively short. Some popular options include pixie cuts, bobs, and layered styles.

Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a round face?

Yes, you can wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a round face. Side-swept bangs can be particularly flattering, as they can help to create a more angular shape.

Will a short hairstyle make my face look even rounder?

No, a short hairstyle can actually help to elongate the face and create a more angular look. The key is to choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown of the head, while keeping the sides and back relatively short.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a straightening iron or curling wand.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect easy care short hairstyle for your round face. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths, angles, and textures.
