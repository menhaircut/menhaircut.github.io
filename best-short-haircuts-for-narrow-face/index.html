Best Short Haircuts For Narrow Face Top Picks In 2023

If you have a narrow face, you may be wondering what short haircut styles will flatter your features. The good news is that there are plenty of options to choose from that can enhance your natural beauty and create a balanced, proportional look.

Description

A narrow face has a lengthened, elongated shape with a high forehead, long chin, and narrow cheeks. The best short haircuts for narrow face styles will help to create a fuller, more balanced look by adding volume and width to the hair around the sides of the face.

With that said, let's dive into some of the best short haircut options for narrow faces in 2023.

How to Choose the Best Short Haircut for Your Narrow Face

When choosing a short haircut style for a narrow face, it's important to consider your face shape and personal preferences. Here are some tips to help you choose the best short haircut for your narrow face:

  • Opt for short haircuts that add volume and width to the sides of the face
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can elongate the face further
  • Consider adding layers or texture to your hair to create a fuller look
  • Choose hairstyles that complement your natural features and personal style

Step by Step Guide: Best Short Haircuts for Narrow Face

Here are some of the top short haircut options for narrow faces in 2023:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short haircut style that works well for narrow faces. This style is short on the sides and back with longer, textured layers on top to add volume and dimension to the hair. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways, depending on your personal preferences.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another great short haircut option for narrow faces. This style is typically chin-length with layers and texture to create volume and width around the face. It's a timeless haircut that can be customized to suit your individual features and style.

3. Layered Cut

A layered cut is a great way to add texture and volume to your hair, especially if you have a narrow face. This style involves cutting your hair into multiple layers of different lengths to create a fuller, more voluminous look.

4. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add width to your face and balance out a narrow forehead. This style involves cutting your bangs at an angle and sweeping them to one side of your face, creating a soft, flattering look.

Tips to Maintain Your Short Haircut for Narrow Face

Once you've chosen the best short haircut for your narrow face, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you care for your short haircut:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Use high-quality hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type and needs
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors that can cause damage

FAQs

What short haircut styles are best for narrow faces?

The best short haircut styles for narrow faces are those that add volume and width to the hair around the sides of the face. Some popular options include the pixie cut, bob cut, layered cut, and side-swept bangs.

How often should I visit my stylist to maintain my short haircut?

It's recommended to visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for trims and touch-ups to keep your short haircut looking its best.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Narrow Face

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep in mind when maintaining your short haircut for narrow face:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and nourished
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and scalp
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet
  • Avoid using hair ties or clips that are too tight, as they can break your hair

With the right short haircut style and proper hair care, you can enhance your natural beauty and create a confident, balanced look that flatters your narrow face. Try out some of these top short haircut options for narrow faces in 2023 and see which one works best for you!
