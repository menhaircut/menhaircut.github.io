Why Is My Cat Losing Hair Around Her Eyes

Written by Daniel Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As a cat owner, you may have noticed that your furry friend is losing hair around their eyes. This can be a cause for concern, as it may indicate an underlying health issue. In this blog post, we will explore the possible reasons why your cat is losing hair around their eyes and what you can do to help.

Table of Contents

  • Possible Reasons for Hair Loss Around Cat's Eyes
  • How to Help Your Cat with Hair Loss Around Their Eyes
  • Step by Step Guide for Hair Care
  • Tips for Preventing Hair Loss Around Cat's Eyes
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Possible Reasons for Hair Loss Around Cat's Eyes

There are several reasons why your cat may be losing hair around their eyes. Some of the most common reasons include:

Allergies:

Cats can be allergic to a variety of things, including food, pollen, and household cleaners. Allergies can cause your cat to scratch or rub their eyes, which can result in hair loss around the eyes.

Fleas:

Fleas are a common problem among cats. When fleas bite your cat, they can cause itching and discomfort. If your cat is constantly scratching or rubbing their eyes, they may lose hair around their eyes.

Mites:

Mites are tiny parasites that can live on your cat's skin. They can cause a variety of skin problems, including hair loss around the eyes.

Infections:

Cats can develop infections in and around their eyes. These infections can cause hair loss and other symptoms, such as redness and swelling.

Injury:

If your cat has injured their eye or the surrounding area, they may lose hair as a result. This could be due to scratching, rubbing, or other types of trauma.

How to Help Your Cat with Hair Loss Around Their Eyes

If you notice that your cat is losing hair around their eyes, the first thing you should do is take them to the vet. Your vet can determine the underlying cause of the hair loss and recommend a course of treatment.

In the meantime, there are a few things you can do to help your cat feel more comfortable:

Keep the Area Clean:

Clean the area around your cat's eyes regularly to remove any dirt or debris that may be causing irritation.

Apply a Warm Compress:

A warm compress can help to soothe your cat's eyes and reduce swelling. Simply soak a clean cloth in warm water and hold it against your cat's eyes for a few minutes at a time.

Avoid Harsh Chemicals:

Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as perfumes and cleaning products, around your cat's eyes. These can cause irritation and worsen the hair loss.

Step by Step Guide for Hair Care

If your cat is losing hair around their eyes due to an underlying health issue, it's important to follow your vet's recommendations for treatment. In addition, you can take steps to care for your cat's hair to help promote healthy growth:

1. Brush Your Cat:

Regular brushing can help to remove any loose hair and prevent matting. Use a soft brush and be gentle around your cat's eyes.

2. Provide a Healthy Diet:

A healthy diet can help to promote healthy hair growth. Make sure your cat is getting all the nutrients they need by feeding them a balanced diet.

3. Use a Gentle Shampoo:

If you need to bathe your cat, use a gentle shampoo that is designed for cats. Avoid getting shampoo in their eyes.

Tips for Preventing Hair Loss Around Cat's Eyes

Here are a few tips for preventing hair loss around your cat's eyes:

1. Keep Your Cat's Eyes Clean:

Clean your cat's eyes regularly to remove any dirt or debris that may be causing irritation.

2. Check for Fleas:

Check your cat for fleas regularly and use flea prevention products if necessary.

3. Avoid Overgrooming:

Overgrooming can cause hair loss. Make sure your cat is not excessively grooming themselves in the eye area.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is hair loss around a cat's eyes a serious issue?

A: Hair loss around a cat's eyes can be a sign of an underlying health issue. It's important to take your cat to the vet to determine the cause of the hair loss.

Q: Can I use human shampoo on my cat?

A: No, human shampoo is not suitable for cats. Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for cats.

Q: Can allergies cause hair loss around a cat's eyes?

A: Yes, allergies can cause a variety of skin problems, including hair loss around the eyes.

In conclusion, if you notice that your cat is losing hair around their eyes, it's important to take them to the vet to determine the underlying cause. In the meantime, you can care for your cat's hair and take steps to prevent further hair loss.
