Does A Blunt Bob Suit A Round Face

Written by Daniel Sep 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you considering getting a blunt bob haircut but not sure if it will suit your round face? A blunt bob is a trendy and chic hairstyle that can enhance your facial features and make you look younger. However, not all haircuts are suitable for every face shape. In this article, we'll discuss whether a blunt bob haircut is ideal for round faces and provide some tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Blunt Bob Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide to Style a Blunt Bob
  • Tips to Make Your Blunt Bob Look Great

Description

A blunt bob is a short and straight haircut that falls just above the shoulders. It's a popular hairstyle among celebrities and fashion icons because it's low-maintenance, versatile, and suits most face shapes. However, if you have a round face, you may wonder if a blunt bob is the right choice for you.

Round faces have full cheeks, a rounded chin, and a less defined jawline. The goal of a haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer and slimmer face. A blunt bob can achieve this by adding height at the crown and elongating the neck. However, it's crucial to get the right length and angle to flatter your face shape.

How to Get a Blunt Bob Haircut

If you're considering getting a blunt bob, here are some tips to ensure you get the right cut for your face shape:

1. Consult with Your Hairstylist

Before getting a blunt bob, consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and angle for your face shape. They can also recommend whether to add layers or texture to add dimension to your hair.

2. Choose the Right Length

The length of your blunt bob should be just above or below your jawline. Avoid getting a haircut that falls at your chin or above your ears as it can make your face appear wider.

3. Consider the Angle

A blunt bob with a slight angle or longer front layers can help elongate your face and create a more defined jawline. Avoid a straight cut as it can make your face look rounder.

Step by Step Guide to Style a Blunt Bob

Now that you have your blunt bob haircut, here's how to style it:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Use a towel to dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from heat styling tools.

2. Blow-dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair and add volume at the crown. Blow-dry your hair in sections and direct the airflow downwards to avoid frizz.

3. Straighten Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a straightening iron to create a sleek and polished look. Straighten your hair in small sections, starting from the roots to the ends.

4. Add Texture

If you want to add texture to your blunt bob, use a texturizing spray, or curl your hair with a curling iron. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair.

Tips to Make Your Blunt Bob Look Great

Here are some tips to keep your blunt bob looking great:

1. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can prevent frizz and tangles and keep your hair smooth and shiny.

2. Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and dull. Try to wash your hair every other day or use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and make it look frizzy and dry. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools and avoid using them every day.

FAQs

1. Can a blunt bob make a round face look slimmer?

Yes, a blunt bob can make a round face look slimmer by adding height at the crown and elongating the neck.

2. Is a blunt bob suitable for thick hair?

Yes, a blunt bob is suitable for thick hair as it can help remove some of the weight and volume.

3. How often should I trim my blunt bob?

You should trim your blunt bob every six to eight weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Blunt Bob Haircut

Here are some hair care tips to keep your blunt bob looking healthy and shiny:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

2. Avoid Using Hot Water

Using hot water to wash your hair can strip it of its natural oils and make it look dry and dull. Use lukewarm water instead.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it look brittle and frizzy. Use a hat or a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, a blunt bob can suit a round face if you get the right length and angle. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape and use the tips and hair care advice in this article to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
