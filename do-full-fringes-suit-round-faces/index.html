Do Full Fringes Suit Round Faces

Written by William Nov 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering getting a full fringe but unsure if it will complement your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss whether full fringes suit round faces, how to style them, and hair care tips to maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have soft features with a width and length that are almost equal. The goal of a full fringe is to create an illusion of a longer face and add dimension to the overall look. So, the question is, do full fringes suit round faces? The answer is yes! A full fringe can complement round faces if done correctly.

How to

The key to getting a full fringe that suits round faces is to consider the shape of the fringe. A full fringe that falls just above the eyebrows or below the cheekbones creates an angular effect, making the face appear longer. It is also important to choose a fringe style that complements your hair texture and density.

Step by Step

Here's how to get a full fringe that suits round faces:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best fringe style for your face shape.
  2. Choose a fringe length that falls just above the eyebrows or below the cheekbones.
  3. Decide on the thickness of the fringe. A thicker fringe adds dimension to the face, while a thinner fringe creates a softer look.
  4. Style your fringe with a round brush and a blow dryer to create a voluminous effect.
  5. Use a straightener to add a sleek finish to the fringe.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a full fringe:

  • Consider your hair texture and density when choosing a fringe style.
  • Ask your hairstylist to add layers to your hair to create more dimension.
  • Maintain your fringe by trimming it every 4-6 weeks.
  • Use a hair serum to control frizz and add shine to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about full fringes and round faces:

Q: Can a full fringe make a round face appear wider?

A: Yes, if the fringe is cut too short or falls at the wrong length, it can make the face appear wider.

Q: What fringe styles should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid fringe styles that are too short or blunt, as they can make the face appear wider. Also, avoid fringe styles that are too heavy, as they can overwhelm the face.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain the health and appearance of your hair, especially if you have a full fringe. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or using a protective hair spray.

In conclusion, a full fringe can complement round faces if done correctly. Consider the shape of the fringe, the thickness, and the length to create an illusion of a longer face. Follow the tips and hair care advice to maintain your new look and keep your hair healthy and shiny.
