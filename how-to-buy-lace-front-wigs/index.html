How To Buy Lace Front Wigs A Guide For Beginners

Written by Eveline Aug 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Lace front wigs have become increasingly popular over the years as they are a great way to switch up your hairstyle without damaging your natural hair. However, buying a lace front wig can be overwhelming for beginners. With so many options available, it can be difficult to know where to start. In this guide, we will walk you through the process of how to buy lace front wigs.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Buy Lace Front Wigs
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Buying Lace Front Wigs
  4. Frequently Asked Questions
  5. Hair Care Tips for Lace Front Wigs

How to Buy Lace Front Wigs

Before you start shopping for a lace front wig, it's important to know what you're looking for. Here are some of the things you should consider:

  • Length
  • Color
  • Texture
  • Density
  • Cap construction
  • Price range

Knowing what you want will make the process of buying a lace front wig much easier.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow when buying a lace front wig:

Step 1: Research

Start by doing some research on the different types of lace front wigs available. Look at different brands, styles, and prices to get an idea of what you want.

Step 2: Determine Your Budget

Decide how much you are willing to spend on a lace front wig. Keep in mind that the price will vary depending on the length, density, and quality of the wig.

Step 3: Choose a Style

Decide on the length, color, and texture of the wig. Consider the occasion or purpose for the wig. Do you want a wig for everyday wear or a special event?

Step 4: Choose a Cap Construction

There are different types of cap constructions available for lace front wigs. The most common are:

  • Full lace cap
  • Lace front cap
  • Monofilament cap
  • Hand-tied cap

Each cap construction has its own pros and cons, so do your research and choose the one that suits your needs.

Step 5: Measure Your Head

Measure your head to ensure you get the right size wig. Use a tape measure to measure the circumference of your head from the hairline to the nape of your neck, then from ear to ear across the forehead.

Step 6: Choose a Wig Retailer

Choose a reputable wig retailer that offers a wide selection of lace front wigs. Read reviews and check their return policy before making a purchase.

Step 7: Place Your Order

Once you have chosen your desired lace front wig, place your order and wait for it to arrive.

Tips for Buying Lace Front Wigs

Here are some tips to help you buy the perfect lace front wig:

  • Buy from a reputable retailer
  • Read reviews before making a purchase
  • Choose a wig that matches your natural hair color
  • Take your time when choosing a wig
  • Consider the occasion or purpose for the wig

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a lace front wig?

A lace front wig is a type of wig that has a sheer lace panel at the front of the wig which creates the illusion of a natural hairline.

How long do lace front wigs last?

Lace front wigs can last up to a year with proper care and maintenance.

Can I wear a lace front wig every day?

Yes, you can wear a lace front wig every day. Just make sure to take care of it properly to ensure it lasts.

Hair Care Tips for Lace Front Wigs

Here are some tips for taking care of your lace front wig:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or paddle brush to detangle your wig
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your wig
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape
  • Avoid sleeping in your wig

Buying a lace front wig can be overwhelming, but with this guide, we hope to make the process easier for beginners. Remember to do your research, choose a reputable retailer, and take your time when choosing a wig. With proper care and maintenance, your lace front wig will last for a long time.
