Hairstyles For Long Face Indian Girls Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Sep 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are an Indian girl with a long face shape, you might have found it difficult to choose the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. A long face shape often means that your face is longer than it is wide, with a prominent forehead and a narrow chin. However, with the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your look. In this article, we will cover the best hairstyles for long face Indian girls, and provide you with tips and tricks to maintain your hair's health.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Long Face Indian Girl

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it is important to understand what kind of hairstyle will suit your face shape. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hairstyle:

1. Avoid Center Parting

If you have a long face shape, avoid center parting as it will only elongate your face further. Instead, go for a side parting, which will create the illusion of a wider face.

2. Add Volume

Avoid hairstyles that are flat or sleek, as they will make your face look longer. Instead, add volume to your hair with curls or layers, which will give the appearance of a wider face.

3. Avoid Short Bangs

Short bangs will only highlight your forehead and make your face look longer. Instead, opt for longer side-swept bangs, which will soften your features and balance your face shape.

Hairstyles for Long Face Indian Girls

Now that you know what kind of hairstyle will suit your face shape, here are some hairstyles that are perfect for long face Indian girls:

1. Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for long face shapes as they add volume and movement to your hair. You can add layers to your hair, starting from the chin and working your way down. This will add volume to the bottom of your hair, creating the illusion of a wider face.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for long face shapes as they soften your features and balance your face shape. You can go for long side-swept bangs that blend into your hair or short side-swept bangs that end just above your eyebrows.

3. Bob with Layers

A bob with layers is a great hairstyle for long face Indian girls as it adds volume and movement to your hair while also framing your face. You can add layers to your bob, starting from the chin and working your way down. This will add volume to the bottom of your hair, creating the illusion of a wider face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you have chosen the perfect hairstyle for your long face, here are some steps to style your hair:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently towel-dry your hair.

2. Apply Hair Serum

Apply a small amount of hair serum to your hair to prevent frizz and add shine. Distribute the serum evenly throughout your hair.

3. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement to your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to lift your hair and create volume.

4. Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, style it according to the chosen hairstyle. Use a curling iron or straightener to create curls or straighten your hair. Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your hair in place.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Indian Girls

Here are some hair care tips to maintain your hair's health:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, make sure to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will prevent damage and breakage.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage. This will also help maintain the shape of your chosen hairstyle.

3. Use a Deep Conditioning Mask

Use a deep conditioning mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished. This will also add shine and softness to your hair.

FAQs

What kind of hairstyle should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid center parting, short bangs, and hairstyles that are flat or sleek. Instead, go for side parting, layers, and side-swept bangs.

Can I add highlights to my hair if I have a long face?

Yes, you can add highlights to your hair to add dimension and movement to your hair. However, make sure to consult with a professional stylist to choose the right shade and placement of highlights.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils. However, this may vary depending on your hair type and lifestyle.


In conclusion, choosing the perfect hairstyle for long face Indian girls can be challenging, but with the right tips and tricks, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your look. Remember to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and use hair care tips to maintain your hair's health. With these simple steps, you can achieve the perfect hairstyle and feel confident in your look.
