Does Mousse Make Hair Look Thicker

Written by William Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our blog post for 2023 about the age-old question, "does mousse make hair look thicker?" If you're looking for a quick answer, then the answer is YES! But, let's dive a little deeper and explore how mousse can make your hair look thicker, how to apply it, and some tips for getting the best results.

How Does Mousse Make Hair Look Thicker?

Mousse is a lightweight hair styling product that can add volume and texture to your hair. It works by coating your hair strands and creating a thicker appearance. Mousse contains ingredients such as polymers, which help to build up the hair and hold it in place, giving the illusion of thicker hair.

It's important to note that mousse won't actually make your hair thicker in terms of the number of hair strands on your head. But it can definitely make your hair look and feel fuller and more voluminous, which is why it's a popular product for people with fine or thin hair.

How to Apply Mousse for Thicker Hair

Applying mousse properly is key to achieving the best results. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Mousse works best on clean, damp hair. So, make sure to wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner before applying mousse.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. You want your hair to be damp, but not dripping wet.

Step 3: Shake the Mousse Can

Shake the mousse can well before using it. This will ensure that the product is mixed properly.

Step 4: Dispense the Mousse

Dispense a golf ball-sized amount of mousse into your hand. If you have longer hair, you may need to use a little more.

Step 5: Apply Mousse to Hair

Apply the mousse to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use your fingers or a comb to distribute the mousse evenly throughout your hair.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help distribute the heat evenly and prevent frizz.

Tips for Using Mousse for Thicker Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using mousse:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help prep your hair before applying mousse.
  • Don't use too much mousse. A little goes a long way, and using too much can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to create even more volume and lift.
  • Use a root-lifting spray in addition to mousse for even more volume.
  • Choose a mousse that's specifically designed for your hair type. For example, if you have curly hair, look for a mousse that's formulated for curly hair.
  • Don't touch your hair too much after applying mousse. This can cause the product to break down and make your hair look flat.

FAQ about Mousse for Thicker Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about using mousse for thicker hair:

Q: Can mousse make your hair greasy?

A: Yes, using too much mousse or not washing it out properly can make your hair look greasy. Make sure to use a small amount of product and wash it out thoroughly.

Q: Can mousse cause hair damage?

A: No, mousse is a lightweight product that shouldn't cause any damage to your hair. However, using too much heat when blow-drying your hair can cause damage over time.

Q: How long does mousse last in your hair?

A: Mousse can last for several hours in your hair, but it may start to break down after a few hours. If you want to refresh your hair, you can apply a little more mousse or use a texturizing spray.

Hair Care for Thicker Hair

Using mousse is just one way to make your hair look thicker. Here are some other tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling or tugging on it.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dye.

Now that you know the answer to the question, "does mousse make hair look thicker?" you can go ahead and try it out for yourself. Remember to use a small amount of product, apply it evenly, and style your hair with a blow dryer for the best results. Happy styling!
