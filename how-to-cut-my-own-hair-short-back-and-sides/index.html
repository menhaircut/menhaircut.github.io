How To Cut My Own Hair Short Back And Sides A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Nov 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Cutting your own hair can be intimidating, especially if you're looking to create a short back and sides look. However, with the right tools and approach, it can be a straightforward process. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.

How to Cut My Own Hair Short Back and Sides

Tools Needed

Before you start, you'll need to gather the necessary tools:

  • Scissors
  • Hair clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Mirror
  • Cape or towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve a short back and sides haircut:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair where you want the fade to start. Use a hair tie or clip to hold the top section of hair in place.
  3. Use the clippers to buzz the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to achieve the desired length. Use a mirror to check your progress.
  4. Blend the hair between the buzzed section and the top section using scissors. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut at an angle to create a gradual blend.
  5. Remove the hair tie or clip and comb the top section of hair straight up. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Start by cutting a little at a time and check your progress in the mirror.
  6. Blend the sides and top section of hair using scissors. Hold a small section of hair between your fingers and cut at an angle to create a gradual blend.
  7. Check your work in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments.
  8. Clean up any stray hairs using the clippers and scissors.
  9. Shower and shampoo to remove any loose hairs.
  10. Style as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short back and sides haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard when using the clippers and work your way down. It's easier to take more hair off than to add it back.
  • Use a comb to help guide the scissors and create a more even cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and take longer than expected.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're unsure about anything. It's better to be safe than sorry.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair:

Is it hard to cut your own hair?

It can be intimidating at first, but with practice and the right tools, it can be a straightforward process.

What tools do I need to cut my own hair?

You'll need scissors, hair clippers, a comb, hair ties or clips, a mirror, and a cape or towel.

How can I achieve a short back and sides haircut?

Part your hair where you want the fade to start, use clippers to buzz the sides and back of your head, blend the hair between the buzzed section and the top section using scissors, and cut the top section of hair to the desired length.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, be sure to shower and shampoo to remove any loose hairs. You may also want to apply a styling product to achieve your desired look.

Cutting your own hair can be a rewarding experience and save you time and money in the long run. With the right tools and approach, achieving a short back and sides haircut is easier than you might think. Follow these steps, tips, and FAQ to achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home.
