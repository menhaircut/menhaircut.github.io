How Do I Know If I Would Suit Short Hair

Written by Christine Oct 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering chopping off your locks and going for a short hairstyle, but not sure if it would suit you? It's a common dilemma that many people face when contemplating a new haircut. Short hair can be a bold and refreshing change and can enhance your facial features, but it's essential to determine whether it's the right fit for you. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know to find out if you would suit short hair.

  2. Step-by-Step Guide

How to Determine If You Would Suit Short Hair

Before deciding to cut your hair short, there are a few factors to consider to determine if it would suit you. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining if a short hairstyle will suit you. Short haircuts tend to look best on individuals with oval, heart, or square-shaped faces. Round faces may not be the best fit for short hair, as it can make the face appear wider.

Hair Texture

Another factor to consider is your hair texture. Short haircuts work well on individuals with straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you may need to spend more time styling it to achieve the desired look.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair. Short hair may require more maintenance and frequent trims to keep the style looking fresh.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to determine if you would suit short hair:

Step 1: Look at Pictures

Start by looking at pictures of short haircuts that you like. Consider the haircuts that complement your face shape and hair texture.

Step 2: Try Virtual Hairstyling

Many websites and apps allow you to try on different hairstyles virtually. Upload a photo of yourself and experiment with different short haircuts to see which one suits you best.

Step 3: Consult with a Hairstylist

Consult with a hairstylist to determine if a short hairstyle would suit you. They can recommend a cut that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Tips for Choosing a Short Hairstyle

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle:

Consider Your Face Shape

Choose a haircut that complements your face shape. For example, if you have an oval face, a pixie cut or a bob would suit you well.

Think About Your Hair Texture

Consider your hair texture when choosing a short hairstyle. If you have thin hair, a layered cut can add volume. If you have curly hair, a short bob or lob may be a better fit.

Try on Wigs

Wigs are a great way to experiment with different hairstyles without committing to a cut. Try on different wig styles to see which one suits you best.

FAQs

Will Short Hair Suit My Face Shape?

Short hair can look great on individuals with oval, heart, or square-shaped faces. Round faces may not be the best fit for short hair, as it can make the face look wider.

How Do I Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for My Hair Texture?

Consider your hair texture when choosing a short hairstyle. For thin hair, a layered cut can add volume. For curly hair, a short bob or lob may be a better fit.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep the style looking fresh. Here are a few hair care tips for short hair:

Trim Regularly

Short haircuts require frequent trims to keep the style looking sharp. Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim.

Use the Right Products

Use products specifically designed for short hair, such as texturizing sprays or pomades, to enhance the style.

Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools.

Now that you know how to determine if you would suit short hair, it's time to take the plunge and try out a new hairstyle. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a short haircut. Follow our hair care tips to keep your new style looking fresh and stylish.
