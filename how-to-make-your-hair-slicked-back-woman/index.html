How To Make Your Hair Slicked Back Woman A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a sleek and polished hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion, the slicked back look might just be what you're after. This classic style works well for both men and women, but in this article, we'll focus on how to make your hair slicked back woman. Whether you have long or short hair, this style can work for you. Here's everything you need to know to achieve this look:

Description

The slicked back hairstyle is all about creating a smooth, polished look that's both stylish and professional. To achieve this style, you'll need to use a product that provides hold and shine, such as pomade or gel. With a little bit of practice, you can master this look and create a hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.

How to

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

The first step to creating a slicked back hairstyle is to start with clean, damp hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and then towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp. Avoid using a blow dryer, as this can cause frizz and make it harder to achieve a sleek look.

Step 2: Apply product

Once your hair is damp, it's time to apply the product. Choose a product that provides hold and shine, such as pomade or gel. Take a small amount of product and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Then, starting at the roots, apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb your hair back

Next, use a comb to slick your hair back. Start at the hairline and work your way back, using the comb to create a smooth, polished look. Make sure to comb your hair in the direction that you want it to lay, as this will help create a more polished finish.

Step 4: Smooth out any bumps

If you notice any bumps or flyaways, use your fingers or a comb to smooth them out. You want your hair to look as sleek and polished as possible, so take your time and make sure everything is in place.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finally, finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help hold everything in place and keep your hair looking polished throughout the day. Be sure to choose a hairspray that provides a natural-looking hold, as you don't want your hair to look stiff or crunchy.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when creating a slicked back hairstyle:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. You don't want to use too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or weighed down.
  • Use a fine-toothed comb to create a sleek, polished look. Avoid using a brush, as this can create more volume and make your hair look less slicked back.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that works best for you. Everyone's hair is different, so what works for one person may not work for another.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about the slicked back hairstyle:

Is the slicked back hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the slicked back hairstyle can work for all hair types, including curly, straight, and wavy hair. However, you may need to experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair type.

How long does the slicked back hairstyle last?

The slicked back hairstyle can last for several hours, depending on the product you use and your hair type. However, you may need to touch up your hair throughout the day to keep it looking polished.

Hair Care

Here are a few tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to create a sleek and polished slicked back hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. Whether you're heading to the office or a night out on the town, this classic hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
