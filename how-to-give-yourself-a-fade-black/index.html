How To Give Yourself A Fade Black A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Oct 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and stylish haircut, a fade black is a great option. The fade black is a popular haircut that features a gradual transition from short hair to longer hair, creating a faded effect. While getting a fade black at a barbershop is a great experience, you can also give yourself a fade black at home. In this article, we'll guide you through the step-by-step process of giving yourself a fade black.

How To

Before we begin, make sure you have the necessary tools. You'll need clippers, guards, a comb, scissors, and a mirror.

Step 1: Decide on the Length

The first step in giving yourself a fade black is deciding on the length you want. You can choose to have a high fade or a low fade, depending on how much hair you want to keep.

Step 2: Start with the Longer Hair

Once you've decided on the length, start with the longer hair. Use a clipper guard to trim the hair to the desired length.

Step 3: Gradually Shorten the Hair

As you move down the head, gradually shorten the hair by using shorter clipper guards. This will create the faded effect.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've reached the desired length, use the clipper without a guard to blend the hair between the different lengths.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Use scissors to trim the top of the hair to the desired length.

Step 6: Clean Up the Edges

Use a trimmer to clean up the edges around the hairline and ears.

Step 7: Check for Balance

Using a mirror, check for any uneven areas and make any necessary adjustments.

Step 8: Style

Once you're happy with the haircut, style it as desired.

Tips

  • It's important to take your time and be patient when giving yourself a fade black.
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a comb to help guide the clippers and create a clean, even cut.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your hair, seek the help of a professional barber.

FAQ

How often should I give myself a fade black?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your haircut. Generally, you should give yourself a fade black every 2-3 weeks.

Can I use any type of clippers?

It's recommended to use clippers specifically designed for haircuts. These clippers come with different guards to help achieve the desired length.

How do I maintain my fade black?

To maintain your fade black, you should trim the hair every 2-3 weeks and clean up the edges regularly. It's also important to use quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and styled.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to use quality hair care products. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Regular trims and haircuts can also help keep your hair looking great.

Now that you know how to give yourself a fade black, you can enjoy a fresh and stylish haircut from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, be patient, and follow the steps carefully to achieve the best results. Happy styling!
