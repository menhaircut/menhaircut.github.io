How To Cut Short Hair With Long Layers A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your boring, one-length short hair? Long layers can add texture and movement to your locks, giving you a fresh and trendy look. But, how do you cut short hair with long layers? In this guide, we will show you how to achieve the perfect long layers on short hair.

Description

Short hair with long layers is a popular hairstyle among women of all ages. This haircut features shorter hair on the top and longer layers at the bottom, creating movement and volume. Long layers can be cut in different lengths and angles to suit your face shape and hair type.

How to Cut Short Hair with Long Layers

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need sharp scissors, a comb, hair clips, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and blow-dry it until it is completely dry. Make sure your hair is detangled and smooth.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips. Start with the top section and clip away the rest of your hair. Comb the top section and hold it between your fingers.

Step 3: Cut the Top Section

Cut the top section of your hair in a straight line, following your fingers' guide. The length of the top section will depend on your preference and face shape. Use the top section as a guide for the rest of your hair.

Step 4: Cut the Bottom Layers

Release the next section of your hair and comb it. Cut the bottom layers at an angle, starting from the back and moving towards the front. This will create long layers that blend with the top section.

Step 5: Repeat the Process

Repeat the process for the rest of your hair, section by section. Make sure you cut the layers at the same angle and length. You can also add more layers to create more volume and texture.

Tips for Cutting Short Hair with Long Layers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting short hair with long layers:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Check your hair's length and symmetry regularly.
  • Experiment with different angles and lengths to find the perfect style for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my own hair?

A: Yes, you can cut your own hair, but it's best to have someone help you with the back of your head.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on your hair's growth rate and style. If you want to maintain your short hair with long layers, you should get a trim every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I add color to my short hair with long layers?

A: Yes, you can add color to your hair to enhance the layers' texture and depth. Consult with a professional stylist to find the best color for your skin tone and hair type.

Hair Care for Short Hair with Long Layers

To keep your short hair with long layers healthy and shiny, you should follow a daily hair care routine. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using hot tools frequently, as they can damage your hair. Apply a hair serum or oil to the ends of your hair to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut short hair with long layers, you can rock this trendy and chic hairstyle. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun experimenting with different styles!
