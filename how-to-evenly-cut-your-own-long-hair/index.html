How To Evenly Cut Your Own Long Hair

Written by Frank Jun 14, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of spending a fortune on haircuts? Do you have long hair but can't seem to find the time to go to a salon? Well, you're in luck! With a few simple steps, you can learn how to evenly cut your own long hair. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting your hair at home, without sacrificing style or quality.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step in cutting your own long hair is to invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors. Using regular scissors or kitchen scissors can result in uneven cuts and damage to your hair. Hair cutting scissors can be found at most beauty supply stores or online.

Next, decide on the length you want to cut your hair. It's important to keep in mind that cutting too much hair can be difficult to fix and can lead to an uneven cut. It's best to start with small trims and work your way up to your desired length.

Finally, follow our step-by-step guide to achieve an evenly cut style:

Step by step

Step 1:

Wash and dry your hair. It's important to start with clean hair to ensure an even cut.

Step 2:

Brush your hair thoroughly to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3:

Separate your hair into sections. Start by dividing your hair down the middle and then part it in the back. Secure each section with hair ties or clips.

Step 4:

Begin cutting your hair in small sections. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use the length of the hair from the previously cut section as a guide for the next section. This will help to ensure an even cut.

Step 5:

Continue to cut your hair in sections until you have achieved your desired length. Remember to take your time and make small cuts.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect at-home haircut:

  • Use a comb to help guide your scissors in a straight line.
  • Keep your scissors at a slight angle to create a softer, more natural look.
  • Take breaks in between sections to check for evenness and adjust as needed.
  • Don't rush the process. Cutting your hair takes time and patience.

FAQ

Can I cut my hair straight across?

Yes, you can cut your hair straight across. However, it's important to take small sections and make sure each section is even before moving on to the next section.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain healthy hair growth and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, on a daily basis.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat tools.

Cutting your own long hair can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a great way to save money and achieve the perfect haircut. Follow our step-by-step guide and don't forget our tips to help you achieve a beautiful, even cut. Happy cutting!
