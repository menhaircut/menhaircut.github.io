How To Style Long Coarse Hair Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Long coarse hair can be challenging to style, especially for men. However, with the right products and techniques, you can achieve a polished and fashionable look that suits your personality and lifestyle. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of styling long coarse hair for men, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Long Coarse Hair Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Long Coarse Hair Male
  • FAQ about Long Coarse Hair Male

How to Style Long Coarse Hair Male

Styling long coarse hair for men requires patience, creativity, and the right products. Here are some basic steps to follow:

1. Determine Your Hair Type

Before you start styling your hair, you need to determine your hair type. Long coarse hair can be curly, wavy, or straight, and each type requires a different approach. If you're not sure about your hair type, consult a hairstylist or use an online hair type test.

2. Choose the Right Products

To style long coarse hair, you need products that provide hold, texture, and moisture. Some of the best products for long coarse hair include:

  • Hair wax
  • Hair pomade
  • Hair clay
  • Hair gel
  • Leave-in conditioner

3. Experiment with Different Hairstyles

Long coarse hair offers endless styling possibilities for men. Some popular hairstyles for long coarse hair include:

  • Man bun
  • Top knot
  • Undercut
  • Cornrows
  • Braids
  • Layered cut
  • Mohawk
  • Surfer hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling long coarse hair for men:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Before you style your hair, you need to wash and condition it to remove dirt, oil, and buildup. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it.

2. Apply a Styling Product

Choose a styling product that suits your desired hairstyle and hair type. Apply a small amount of the product to your palms and rub them together. Run your fingers through your hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the tips. Make sure the product is evenly distributed.

3. Comb or Brush Your Hair

Use a comb or brush to shape your hair according to your desired hairstyle. For a sleek and polished look, use a comb to create a defined parting. For a messy and textured look, use your fingers to scrunch your hair or a brush to create waves and curls.

4. Finish with Hairspray or Serum

To set your hairstyle and add shine, finish with a hairspray or serum. Spray a light mist of hairspray over your hair or apply a small amount of serum to your palms and run them through your hair. Don't overdo it, as too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips for Styling Long Coarse Hair Male

Here are some tips to help you style long coarse hair for men:

  • Experiment with different products and hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you're using a blow dryer or styling iron.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip away natural oils and make your hair dry and brittle.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

FAQ about Long Coarse Hair Male

Q: Can I use the same products for curly and straight long coarse hair?

A: No, you need to use different products for curly and straight long coarse hair. Curly hair requires products that provide moisture and definition, while straight hair requires products that provide hold and volume.

Q: How often should I wash my long coarse hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less often to avoid stripping away natural oils.

Q: Can I style my long coarse hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can style your long coarse hair without using heat. Try using a styling product that provides hold and texture, such as hair wax or pomade, and air-dry your hair naturally.

Hair Care for Long Coarse Hair Male

Here are some tips for taking care of your long coarse hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot water, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize and nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy look.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes that can damage your hair.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your long coarse hair like a pro and enjoy a fashionable and confident look. Remember to experiment, have fun, and embrace your unique hair texture and personality.
