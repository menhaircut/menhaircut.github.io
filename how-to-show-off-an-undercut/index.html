How To Show Off An Undercut A Relaxed Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new hairstyle that is both edgy and easy to maintain? Look no further than the undercut. This versatile cut has been popular for years and shows no signs of slowing down. In this guide, we’ll show you how to show off an undercut to its fullest potential.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get an Undercut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut
  • FAQs About Undercuts
  • Hair Care Tips for Undercuts

How to Get an Undercut

The first step to showing off your undercut is getting one in the first place. If you’re not familiar with the cut, an undercut involves shaving the hair underneath the top layer of hair, leaving a longer layer on top. You can get an undercut at a salon or do it yourself at home with clippers.

To get the perfect undercut, show your stylist a photo of the style you want. They can help you decide on the right length and style for your face shape and hair type. If you’re doing it yourself, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling

Once you’ve got your undercut, it’s time to style it to show it off. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your undercut:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair (wax or pomade work well).
  3. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  4. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Don’t be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Undercut

To keep your undercut looking fresh, follow these tips:

  • Get regular touch-ups to keep the shaved area looking neat.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent oil buildup.
  • Use a high-quality styling product to keep your hair in place.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs About Undercuts

Here are some common questions about undercuts:

What face shape is best for an undercut?

Undercuts work well for most face shapes, but they look particularly good on those with oval or heart-shaped faces.

How often should I get a touch-up?

It’s recommended to get a touch-up every 4-6 weeks to keep your undercut looking fresh.

Can I style my undercut in different ways?

Yes, an undercut can be styled in many different ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Undercuts

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.

By following these tips, you can keep your undercut looking sharp and stylish for years to come.

Final Thoughts

Now that you know how to show off an undercut, it’s time to try the look for yourself. Whether you’re going for a sleek and polished style or a messy and textured look, an undercut is a versatile hairstyle that can be tailored to your individual taste. Follow these tips and you’ll be rocking your undercut like a pro in no time.
