What Is A Guard Haircut

Written by Bardi Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on what is a guard haircut! A guard haircut is a popular hairstyle that involves using clippers with guard attachments to cut hair to a specific length. This type of haircut is often used by barbers and hairdressers to create a uniform and clean look for men's haircuts. In this guide, we'll be covering everything you need to know about guard haircuts, including how they work, the different types of guard attachments, and how to care for your hair after getting a guard haircut.

How To Get a Guard Haircut

Getting a guard haircut is a relatively straightforward process that can be done by a professional barber or hairdresser. Here are the steps involved in getting a guard haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Guard Attachment

The first step in getting a guard haircut is to choose the right guard attachment for your desired hair length. Guard attachments come in different sizes, typically ranging from 1/8 inch to 1 inch. The smaller the guard attachment, the shorter the hair will be cut. Make sure to communicate your desired length to your barber or hairdresser.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before starting the haircut, your hair will need to be washed and dried. This will help your barber or hairdresser to see the natural texture and movement of your hair, making it easier to cut evenly.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Your barber or hairdresser will start cutting your hair using the guard attachment you chose. They will start at the bottom of your head and work their way up, making sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides.

Step 4: Clean Up

After the main haircut is done, your barber or hairdresser will use scissors and clippers to clean up any stray hairs and make sure the haircut is even all around.

Tips for Maintaining a Guard Haircut

Now that you know how to get a guard haircut, it's important to know how to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your guard haircut looking fresh:

- Visit your barber or hairdresser regularly for touch-ups and trims. - Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. - Experiment with different styling products to find the right one for your hair type and desired look.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a guard haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a guard haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape of the haircut.

Q: Can I get a guard haircut at home?

A: Yes, you can get a guard haircut at home using clippers and guard attachments. However, it's important to be cautious and take your time to avoid any mistakes.

Q: Are guard haircuts only for men?

A: No, guard haircuts can be done on both men and women. They are often used for shorter hairstyles, but can be adapted for longer hair as well.

Hair Care After a Guard Haircut

After getting a guard haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some tips for hair care after a guard haircut:

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and avoid pulling or breaking it. - Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent dryness. - Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage. - Trim your hair regularly to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

That's it for our guide on what is a guard haircut! We hope you found this information helpful and feel confident in getting a guard haircut for yourself. Remember to communicate with your barber or hairdresser about your desired length and take care of your hair after the haircut to keep it looking its best.
