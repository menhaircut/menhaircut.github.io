How To Curl The Left Side Of Your Hair

Written by Frank Oct 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having limp, lifeless hair on the left side? Do you want to add some volume and curl to your locks? Look no further, because we have the solution for you! In this post, we will go over step-by-step instructions on how to curl the left side of your hair. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Curl the Left Side of Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Tips for Curling Your Hair
  • FAQs

Step-by-Step Instructions

Before we begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Curling iron or wand
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Sectioning clips
  • Hairspray

Now, let's get started on curling the left side of your hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair as you normally would. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to avoid any damage from the curling iron. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your sectioning clips, divide your hair into two sections - left and right. Clip the right section to the side to keep it out of the way while you work on the left side.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from the left side and wrap it around your curling iron or wand. Hold for a few seconds and then release. Continue curling small sections of hair until you have curled all of the left side.

Step 4: Repeat on the Right Side

Once you have finished curling the left side of your hair, unclip the right side and repeat the same process.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To set your curls in place, spray your hair with a light hold hairspray. Avoid using too much product as this can weigh your hair down.

Tips for Curling Your Hair

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect curls:

  • Use a curling wand for more natural-looking curls
  • Start with a lower heat setting and gradually increase as needed
  • Curl away from your face to open up your features
  • For tighter curls, use smaller sections of hair
  • For looser curls, use larger sections of hair
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling hair:

Q: How long should I hold the curling iron for?

A: This depends on your hair type and the thickness of your hair. As a general rule, hold the curling iron for 5-10 seconds.

Q: Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use hot rollers or even braid your hair overnight for a more natural-looking curl.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools every day
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and dyes
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water for strong, healthy hair

And there you have it - a simple and easy way to curl the left side of your hair. With a little practice and some patience, you'll be able to achieve the perfect curls in no time!
