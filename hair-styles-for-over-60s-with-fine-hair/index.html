Best Hair Styles For Over 60S With Fine Hair

Written by Aprilia Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair naturally becomes thinner and finer. However, this doesn't mean we can't have stylish hair! In fact, there are many hair styles that can work well for those over 60 with fine hair. Here are some tips, tricks, and styles to help you achieve a fabulous look.

How to Choose the Right Hair Style

When choosing a hair style, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. A good hair stylist can help you determine what will work best for you.

Face Shape

The shape of your face can greatly impact what hair style will look best on you. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to go for a longer hair style to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a softer, layered style can help soften the angles.

Hair Texture

Fine hair can be more difficult to style, as it can lack volume and body. However, there are many hair products and techniques that can help add volume and texture.

Lifestyle

Consider your daily routine when choosing a hair style. If you don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, a low-maintenance style may be best for you.

Best Hair Styles for Over 60s with Fine Hair

Here are some hair styles that work well for those over 60 with fine hair:

Short and Sassy

A short, layered bob can be a great option for those with fine hair. It adds volume and texture, while still being easy to maintain.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and stylish choice for those over 60 with fine hair. It's a low-maintenance style that can be dressed up or down.

Shoulder-Length

A shoulder-length cut with layers can be a great option for those who want a longer style. It adds volume and movement, while still being easy to style.

Soft and Wavy

Soft, loose waves can be a great option for those who want a more romantic look. It adds volume and texture, while still being easy to maintain.

Tips for Styling Fine Hair

Here are some tips for styling fine hair:

Use the Right Products

Use products that add volume and texture, such as volumizing mousse or dry shampoo. Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair.

Blow Dry Upside Down

Blow dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots.

Use Velcro Rollers

Use velcro rollers to add volume and body to your hair.

Embrace Your Natural Texture

Work with your natural texture, rather than against it. Embrace your fine hair and find styles that work well with it.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my fine hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your fine hair. However, be sure to use a gentle, ammonia-free formula and avoid over-processing.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. Those with fine hair may need to wash more frequently, as it can become oily more quickly.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is important, especially as we age. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

Condition Regularly

Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and soft.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use heat protectant sprays and avoid using hot tools too often.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With the right hair style and care, those over 60 with fine hair can look and feel fabulous. Don't be afraid to experiment and find what works best for you!
