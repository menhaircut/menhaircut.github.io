FAQ

What are the best products to use to thicken your hair?

Some of the best products to use to thicken your hair include volumizing shampoo and conditioner, thickening sprays and mousses, texturizing sprays, and hair masks.

Can I use a hair mask to thicken my hair?

Yes, using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and strengthen your hair, which can lead to thicker, healthier-looking locks.

How often should I wash my hair?

It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid over-washing, which can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look dry and lifeless.
