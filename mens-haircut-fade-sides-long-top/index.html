Men s Haircut Fade Sides Long Top A Relaxed Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on the popular men's haircut fade sides long top. This hairstyle has been a trend for years, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In this article, we will cover everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to style it, tips, and hair care. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The men's haircut fade sides long top is a versatile hairstyle that suits different face shapes and hair types. This haircut features short sides that gradually blend into longer hair on top, creating a contrast between the two lengths. The fade can be high, mid, or low, depending on your preference. The long hair on top can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, quiff, messy, or textured. This haircut is perfect for men who want a modern and edgy look that's easy to maintain.

How to

To achieve the men's haircut fade sides long top, you need to go to a professional hairstylist who can blend the sides and top seamlessly. You can show them a picture of the style you want or describe it in detail. The hairstylist will use clippers of different sizes to create the fade and scissors to trim the top. They may also use a razor or trimmer to create a sharp line around the hairline and sideburns.

Step by Step

If you want to style your men's haircut fade sides long top at home, here are the steps:

  1. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  2. Towel dry your hair until it's damp.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as wax, pomade, or gel, to your hair.
  4. Use a comb or brush to distribute the product evenly and style your hair on top as desired.
  5. Use your fingers to create texture or messiness if you prefer.
  6. Use a trimmer or razor to maintain the fade and keep the hairline and sideburns neat.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting or styling a men's haircut fade sides long top:

  • Choose a fade that suits your face shape and hairline.
  • Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't overuse styling products as they can weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain the fade and trim the top.
  • Consider getting a beard or mustache to complement your haircut and enhance your facial features.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut fade sides long top:

  • Is this haircut suitable for curly or thick hair?
    Yes, it is. However, you may need to use more product and spend more time styling your hair to achieve the desired look.
  • How often should I get this haircut?
    It depends on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain the fade. Generally, you should get it trimmed every 2-4 weeks.
  • Can I modify this haircut to suit my style?
    Yes, you can. You can add a part, change the length, or dye your hair to create a unique look.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut fade sides long top looking sharp and healthy, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and scalp condition.
  • Avoid heat styling tools, such as blow dryers or flat irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and prevent hair loss.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, or chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood circulation and promote hair growth.

We hope this guide has been helpful in understanding the men's haircut fade sides long top. Remember, this haircut is all about confidence and personal style, so don't be afraid to experiment with different looks and techniques. Have fun!
