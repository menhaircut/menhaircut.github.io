How To Blow Dry Curly Frizzy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 09, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have curly, frizzy hair, you know how difficult it can be to style. With the right techniques and products, however, you can achieve a sleek and smooth look. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps to blow dry your curly frizzy hair.

Description

Blow drying curly frizzy hair can be a daunting task. But with the right tools and techniques, it can be done quickly and easily. In this guide, we’ll show you how to blow dry your curly frizzy hair in a way that will leave it looking smooth and shiny.

How to Blow Dry Curly Frizzy Hair

Here are the steps to blow dry your curly frizzy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step is to wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. This will help to moisturize your hair and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair while it is still damp. Use a product that is designed to tame frizz and define curls, such as a curl cream or mousse.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections. This will help you to blow dry your hair evenly and prevent tangles.

Step 4: Start Blow Drying

Start blow drying your hair using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer. Use a low heat setting and gently scrunch your hair with your hands as you dry it.

Step 5: Finish Drying

Once your hair is mostly dry, switch to a cool setting on your blow dryer and continue to scrunch your hair until it is completely dry.

Step 6: Apply a Serum

Apply a serum to your hair to add shine and tame any remaining frizz. Use a pea-sized amount and apply it to the ends of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you blow dry your curly frizzy hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair. These materials are gentler on your hair than regular towels.
  • Don’t use a brush while blow drying your hair. This can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that it dries evenly.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying your hair to prevent damage.

FAQ

Q: Can I blow dry my hair without a diffuser attachment?

A: Yes, you can blow dry your hair without a diffuser attachment. However, using a diffuser will help to define your curls and prevent frizz.

Q: How often should I wash my curly frizzy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you style it. Generally, curly hair should be washed every 2-3 days to prevent it from drying out.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, shiny hair. Here are some tips for caring for your curly frizzy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it is dry, as this can cause frizz.

By following these tips and techniques, you can blow dry your curly frizzy hair with ease. Remember to be gentle with your hair and use the right products to achieve a sleek and smooth look.
