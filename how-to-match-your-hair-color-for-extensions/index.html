How To Match Your Hair Color For Extensions

Written by Daniel Nov 28, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to add some length or volume to your hair with extensions, but unsure of how to match the color? Fear not, we’ve got you covered. Matching your hair color for extensions is crucial to ensure a seamless and natural look. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

Table of Contents

  1. Determine Your Hair Color
  2. Choose the Right Extension Color
  3. Test the Color
  4. Blend the Extension
  5. Hair Care Tips
  6. FAQ

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Color

The first step in matching your hair color for extensions is to determine your natural hair color. This can be done by examining your hair under natural light. Look at the roots of your hair and note the color. Take note of any highlights or lowlights as well.

It’s important to note any variations in your hair color as this will help to ensure a perfect match for your extensions. If you’re unsure about your hair color, you can always consult with a stylist for help.

Step 2: Choose the Right Extension Color

Once you’ve determined your natural hair color, it’s time to choose the right extension color. Look for extensions that match your hair color as closely as possible. If you have highlights or lowlights, choose extensions that have similar shades.

It’s always better to go for a slightly lighter shade as it can be darkened with hair dye if needed. However, avoid going for a darker shade as it can be more difficult to lighten the extension to match your hair color.

Step 3: Test the Color

Before attaching the extension, it’s important to test the color. This can be done by holding the extension up to your hair in natural light. If the colors are a perfect match, you’re good to go.

If the colors are slightly off, you can try adjusting the extension color by using a hair dye. However, it’s best to seek the help of a professional stylist to ensure that the color is matched perfectly.

Step 4: Blend the Extension

Once you’ve determined that the extension color matches your hair color, it’s time to blend the extension with your natural hair. This can be done by teasing or backcombing the roots of your hair and the extension.

You can also use a curling iron or straightener to blend the extension with your hair. It’s important to be gentle when blending the extension to avoid damaging your hair or the extension.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you’ve matched your hair color for extensions, it’s important to take good care of them. Here are some tips to help you maintain your extensions:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out the extension
  • Brush your hair gently to avoid pulling on the extension
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage the extension
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep the extension hydrated

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my extensions to match my hair color?

A: Yes, you can dye your extensions to match your hair color. However, it’s best to seek the help of a professional stylist to ensure that the color is matched perfectly.

Q: How long can I keep my extensions in?

A: The duration of your extensions will depend on the type and quality. Clip-in extensions can be worn for a day, while sew-in or tape-in extensions can be worn for several weeks to a few months.

Q: Can I swim with my extensions?

A: Yes, you can swim with your extensions. However, it’s best to wear a swim cap to protect the extension from chlorine and saltwater.

Matching your hair color for extensions can be daunting, but with these simple steps, you can achieve a natural and seamless look. Just remember to take good care of your extensions to ensure they last as long as possible. Happy styling!
