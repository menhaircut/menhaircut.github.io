Short Sides Haircut The Ultimate Guide For Men

Written by Christine Sep 03, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your old hairstyle and looking for a change? One of the most popular hairstyles for men in recent years is the short sides haircut. This style features shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on top, creating a versatile and trendy look that can be customized to fit anyone's unique style. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the short sides haircut, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Description

The short sides haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that features shorter hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top. This style is versatile and can be customized to fit anyone's unique style. The short sides provide a clean and polished look, while the longer hair on top can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or textured.

How to Get a Short Sides Haircut

Getting a short sides haircut is easy and can be done at any barbershop or salon. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step is to decide how short you want your sides to be. You can choose to have a fade, which is a gradual transition from short to longer hair, or a classic short cut, which is a blunt cut that ends at your sideburns. Your barber can help you decide which length will look best for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Decide on the Style

Once you have decided on your length, it's time to choose your style. You can opt for a classic look, such as a side part or slicked back, or a more modern textured style. Your barber can help you choose a style that will suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 3: Maintain Your Haircut

Once you have your short sides haircut, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking sharp. This means regular visits to your barber to keep your hair trimmed and shaped. You can also use styling products to help keep your hair in place and add texture.

Tips for Styling a Short Sides Haircut

Styling a short sides haircut is easy and can be done in a variety of ways. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your haircut:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Using the right styling products can make all the difference when it comes to styling your short sides haircut. Opt for a pomade or wax to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tip 2: Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. You can try a side part, slicked back, or textured look to see what works best for your hair type and face shape.

Tip 3: Keep Your Hair Clean

Keeping your hair clean and healthy is important when it comes to styling a short sides haircut. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type to keep it looking its best.

FAQ

Q: Will a short sides haircut work for any face shape?

A: Yes, a short sides haircut can be customized to fit any face shape. Your barber can help you choose a style that will complement your features.

Q: How often should I get my short sides haircut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get your short sides haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Q: Can I still style my hair if I have a receding hairline?

A: Yes, a short sides haircut can be styled in a way that will minimize the appearance of a receding hairline. Your barber can help you choose a style that will work best for you.

Hair Care for Short Sides Haircut

Keeping your hair healthy and looking its best is important when it comes to styling a short sides haircut. Here are some tips for hair care:

Tip 1: Use a Gentle Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wearing a hat or using a product with SPF can protect your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

In conclusion, the short sides haircut is a versatile and trendy men's hairstyle that can be customized to fit anyone's unique style. By following the steps in this guide and using the tips for styling and hair care, you can achieve a sharp and polished look that will turn heads wherever you go.
