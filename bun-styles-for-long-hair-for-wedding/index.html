Bun Styles For Long Hair For Wedding

Written by Luffy Aug 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have long hair and you're getting married, you might be wondering what to do with your hair. A bun is a classic hairstyle that's perfect for a wedding. It's elegant, timeless, and it keeps your hair out of your face so you can focus on enjoying your special day. In this article, we'll show you some beautiful bun styles for long hair that are perfect for weddings.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways. It's perfect for long hair because it keeps your hair out of your face and it's easy to style. There are many different types of buns you can choose from for your wedding day, from classic ballerina buns to messy buns. The type of bun you choose will depend on the style of your dress and the overall look you're going for.

How to

To create a bun, you'll need to start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Then, gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band. Twist the ponytail around the base of the elastic band to create a bun shape. Secure the bun with bobby pins and hairspray to keep it in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a classic ballerina bun for your wedding:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. Twist the ponytail around the base of the elastic band to create a bun shape.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Spray the bun with hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when creating a bun for your wedding:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color so they blend in.
  • Consider adding accessories like flowers or a veil to your bun for a more bridal look.
  • Practice creating your bun before your wedding day to make sure you get the look you want.
  • Use hairspray to keep your bun in place all day.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about bun styles for long hair for weddings:

What type of bun is best for my wedding?

The type of bun that's best for your wedding will depend on the style of your dress and the overall look you're going for. A classic ballerina bun is a timeless option, but you could also consider a messy bun or a braided bun for a more relaxed look.

Should I add accessories to my bun?

Adding accessories like flowers or a veil to your bun can help to create a more bridal look. Just make sure that the accessories you choose complement your dress and the overall style of your wedding.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful for your wedding day, make sure to take good care of it in the months leading up to your wedding. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when styling your hair, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Creating a beautiful bun for your wedding is easy with the right technique and some practice. With these bun styles for long hair, you'll be able to find the perfect hairstyle for your special day.
