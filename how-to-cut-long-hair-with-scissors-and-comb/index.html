How To Cut Long Hair With Scissors And Comb

Are you looking for a way to save money and cut your long hair at home? Cutting your own hair may seem intimidating at first, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. In this article, we will guide you on how to cut long hair with scissors and comb in a relaxed manner.

How to Cut Long Hair with Scissors and Comb

Before starting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a sharp pair of scissors, a wide-toothed comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

First, wash and dry your hair. Comb it thoroughly to remove any tangles.

Next, divide your hair into sections. Use the comb to create a parting in the middle of your head, then divide your hair into two sections. Clip one section out of the way and work on the other section first.

Technique 1: Point Cutting

Point cutting is a technique where you cut the hair at an angle, creating a textured, layered effect.

Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut into the ends of the hair, pointing the scissors upwards. This will create a soft, feathery effect.

Work your way around the section, taking small sections of hair and point cutting.

Repeat this technique on the other section of hair.

Technique 2: Blunt Cutting

Blunt cutting is a technique where you cut the hair straight across, creating a more uniform effect.

Take a section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across with the scissors.

Work your way around the section, taking small sections of hair and blunt cutting.

Repeat this technique on the other section of hair.

Step by Step Guide

1. Wash and dry your hair. Comb it thoroughly to remove any tangles.

2. Divide your hair into sections using a wide-toothed comb.

3. Clip one section out of the way and work on the other section first.

4. Use point cutting or blunt cutting technique to trim your hair.

5. Repeat the same technique on the other section of hair.

6. Comb through your hair to ensure it is even.

Tips for Cutting Long Hair

- Start with small sections of hair, and trim a little at a time. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.

- Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.

- Take breaks if your arms get tired. Cutting your own hair can be tiring, so make sure you take breaks to rest your arms.

- Don't cut your hair when it's wet. Wet hair tends to shrink when it dries, which can result in a shorter haircut than you intended.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I cut my hair with regular household scissors?

A: It is not recommended to use regular household scissors to cut your hair. They may be dull and can result in a uneven cut.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Q: Can I cut my own hair if it's curly?

A: Yes, you can cut your own curly hair. However, it is important to be cautious and only cut a little at a time to avoid any unevenness.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Avoid using heat styling tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to cut long hair with scissors and comb, you can save money and cut your hair at home. Remember to take your time and be cautious, and you'll have a great new haircut in no time!
