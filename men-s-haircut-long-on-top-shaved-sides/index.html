Men s Haircut Long On Top Shaved Sides A Trending Style In 2023

Written by Luffy Sep 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that's both trendy and low-maintenance? Look no further than the men's haircut long on top shaved sides. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's no wonder why. It's versatile, easy to style, and looks great on a variety of face shapes and hair types.

Description

The men's haircut long on top shaved sides is exactly what it sounds like. The hair on the top of the head is left long, while the sides and back are shaved or buzzed short. This creates a contrast between the longer, fuller hair on top and the shorter, more streamlined sides. The length on top can vary, but it's typically long enough to be styled in a variety of ways.

How to

If you're interested in getting a men's haircut long on top shaved sides, here's what to expect:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step in getting any new haircut is a consultation with your stylist. They'll ask you about your desired length, style, and maintenance level, and make recommendations based on your hair type and face shape. Be sure to bring in pictures of styles you like to give your stylist a clear idea of what you're looking for.

Step 2: Shave the Sides

Once you and your stylist have agreed on a plan, they'll start by shaving the sides and back of your head. This can be done with clippers or a razor, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Next, they'll cut the hair on top of your head to your desired length. This can be done with scissors or a razor, depending on the style you're going for.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

To create a seamless transition between the longer hair on top and the shorter sides, your stylist will blend the hair using scissors or clippers. This will create a natural-looking, cohesive style.

Step 5: Style

Finally, your stylist will style your hair using products and tools like a blow dryer or flat iron. They'll show you how to recreate the look at home, so you can maintain your new style in between appointments.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a men's haircut long on top shaved sides:

  • Choose a length that suits your face shape and hair type.
  • Invest in quality styling products to maintain your look.
  • Be prepared for regular maintenance appointments to keep the sides short.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut long on top shaved sides:

Will this style work for my hair type?

Yes! This style works well on a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. Your stylist can help you choose the right length and products to make the most of your hair type.

How often do I need to get a haircut?

To maintain the short sides, you'll need to get a haircut every 2-4 weeks. However, the length on top can be left longer and grown out between appointments.

Can I still part my hair with this style?

Yes! You can part your hair to the side or down the middle with this style. Just be sure to use a styling product to keep it in place.

Hair Care

To keep your men's haircut long on top shaved sides looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Style your hair with a quality product that provides hold and texture.
  • Protect your hair from heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim the sides regularly to maintain the style.

A men's haircut long on top shaved sides is a great choice for anyone looking for a trendy, low-maintenance style. With the right length, products, and maintenance, you can rock this look with confidence.
