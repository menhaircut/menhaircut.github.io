Description

Oval-shaped faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape when it comes to hairstyles. They can pull off almost any hair style. However, there are still some styles that are more flattering to an oval face than others. In this comprehensive guide, we will discuss the best hair style for oval face woman.

How to Choose the Best Hair Style for Oval Face Woman

When choosing a hair style, it is important to take into consideration the shape of your face. For oval-shaped faces, the best hair styles are those that highlight the natural symmetry of the face. These hair styles include:

  • Long Layers
  • Side Swept Bangs
  • Bob Cut
  • Pixie Cut
  • Wavy Bob

These hair styles will enhance your natural beauty and complement your facial features. It is also important to consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a hair style.

Long Layers

Long layers are a great hair style for oval faces as they add volume and movement to the hair. They also frame the face and enhance the natural symmetry of the face. Long layers work well for both thick and thin hair textures.

Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs are a flattering hair style for oval faces as they draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. They also add depth and dimension to the hair. Side swept bangs work well for both thick and thin hair textures.

Bob Cut

A bob cut is a classic hair style that works well for oval faces. It frames the face and enhances the natural symmetry of the face. A bob cut works well for both thick and thin hair textures.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold hair style that works well for oval faces. It highlights the natural symmetry of the face and draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones. A pixie cut works best for thin hair textures.

Wavy Bob

A wavy bob is a trendy hair style that works well for oval faces. It adds volume and movement to the hair and frames the face. A wavy bob works well for both thick and thin hair textures.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair

Once you have chosen a hair style, it is important to know how to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair:

  1. Wash your hair with shampoo and conditioner
  2. Towel dry your hair to remove excess water
  3. Apply a styling product to your hair such as mousse or gel
  4. Blow dry your hair using a round brush
  5. If you have chosen a hair style with bangs, blow dry them separately using a small round brush
  6. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add volume or curl to your hair
  7. Finish with a hairspray or other finishing product to hold the style in place

Tips to Keep Your Hair Looking Great

Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down

FAQs About Best Hair Style for Oval Face Woman

What if I have a round face instead of an oval face?

If you have a round face, the best hair styles are those that elongate the face. These hair styles include long layers, side swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

What if I have curly hair?

If you have curly hair, the best hair styles are those that enhance your natural curls. These hair styles include long layers, curly bobs, and curly pixie cuts.

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, the best hair styles are those that add volume and movement to the hair. These hair styles include wavy bobs, layered cuts, and pixie cuts.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Style

Here are some hair care tips to maintain your new hair style:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid washing your hair too often as it can dry out your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as it can damage your hair

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking great and maintain your new hair style.

Choosing the best hair style for oval face woman can be a daunting task, but with this comprehensive guide, you can find the perfect hair style to enhance your natural beauty. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and thickness when choosing a hair style and to follow the proper steps to style and care for your hair. With the right hair style and proper care, you can have beautiful, healthy hair that complements your facial features and enhances your natural beauty.
