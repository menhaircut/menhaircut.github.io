How To Soften Grey Hair Before Coloring Tips And Tricks

Are you tired of your grey hair looking dull and lifeless? Do you want to know how to soften grey hair before coloring? Look no further! In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to achieve silky soft hair that is ready for coloring.

How to Soften Grey Hair Before Coloring

Grey hair tends to be coarser and drier than other types of hair, which can make it difficult to color. However, there are several things you can do to soften your hair and make it more receptive to color. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

One of the best ways to soften grey hair is to use a deep conditioning treatment. Look for a product that contains natural oils such as coconut oil or argan oil, which are known for their moisturizing properties. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

2. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Product build-up can leave your hair feeling heavy and lifeless. To remove build-up and restore your hair's natural shine, use a clarifying shampoo once a week. This will help to remove any residue left behind by styling products and prepare your hair for coloring.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it more difficult to color. Instead, opt for heat-free styling methods such as air-drying your hair or using rollers. You can also try using a leave-in conditioner to help protect your hair from heat damage.

4. Use a Pre-Color Treatment

Before coloring your hair, use a pre-color treatment to help soften and prepare your hair for color. These treatments are specifically designed to help color adhere to your hair and can also help to prevent damage.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some tips for softening your grey hair, here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve silky smooth hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product build-up. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply a deep conditioning treatment

Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair, focusing on the ends. Leave the treatment on for at least 30 minutes before rinsing it out.

Step 3: Use a pre-color treatment

Apply a pre-color treatment to your hair and leave it on for the recommended time. This will help to prepare your hair for color and prevent damage.

Step 4: Color your hair

Follow the instructions on your hair color product and apply it to your hair. Leave the color on for the recommended time before rinsing it out and conditioning your hair.

Tips for Softening Grey Hair

Here are some additional tips for softening your grey hair:

1. Use a leave-in conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to help keep your hair soft and moisturized. Apply the conditioner to your hair after washing and leave it in for added hydration.

2. Brush your hair regularly

Brushing your hair regularly can help to distribute the natural oils in your hair, which can help to soften it.

3. Use a silk pillowcase

Using a silk pillowcase can help to prevent tangles and breakage, which can make your hair feel rough and dry.

FAQs

Q: Can I soften my grey hair without using chemicals?

A: Yes, you can soften your grey hair using natural remedies such as coconut oil or argan oil.

Q: How often should I use a deep conditioning treatment?

A: You should use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.

Q: Can I color my hair if it is dry and brittle?

A: It is not recommended to color dry and brittle hair as it can cause further damage. Soften your hair first before attempting to color it.

Hair Care for Softened Grey Hair

After softening your grey hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its softness and shine. Here are some tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it feel dry and rough. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to help maintain your hair's softness.

2. Avoid hot showers

Hot water can also strip your hair of its natural oils. Use lukewarm water when washing your hair to help maintain its softness.

3. Use a hair mask

Use a hair mask once a week to help keep your hair soft and moisturized. Look for a product that contains natural oils such as coconut oil or argan oil.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve silky soft grey hair that is ready for coloring. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its softness and shine.
