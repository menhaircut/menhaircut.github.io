What Hairstyle Looks Good With A Round Face

Written by Oliver Jun 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face, it's essential to choose a hairstyle that flatters your features. A flattering hairstyle can help elongate your face and make it appear more angular. In this article, we'll discuss what hairstyle looks good with a round face and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks, a rounded jawline, and a wide forehead. People with round faces often have softer features, and their face is approximately the same width and length. It's important to choose a hairstyle that complements these features and helps to elongate the face.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider your hair texture, length, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

Consider Your Hair Texture

The texture of your hair can play a significant role in determining which hairstyles will look best on you. If you have fine hair, you may want to opt for a shorter cut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, a longer cut with layers can help to lighten the weight and add movement to your hair.

Choose a Length That Flatters Your Face Shape

The length of your hair can also impact how your face appears. If you have a round face, you may want to avoid cuts that fall at chin or shoulder-length as they can make your face appear wider. Instead, consider a longer cut that falls below the collarbone or a short pixie cut that adds height to your face.

Consider Your Personal Style

Your personal style is a crucial factor when choosing a hairstyle. Consider the type of clothing you typically wear and the overall look you want to achieve. If you prefer a more sophisticated look, a sleek bob or a classic updo may be perfect for you. If you prefer a more relaxed, bohemian vibe, consider a messy bun or loose waves.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle, let's take a look at some step-by-step instructions to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before you choose a hairstyle, it's important to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width and length of your face and comparing the two. If they are approximately the same, you likely have a round face.

Step 2: Decide on a Length

Once you know your face shape, decide on a length that flatters your features. As we mentioned earlier, longer cuts can help to elongate the face, while shorter cuts can add volume and texture.

Step 3: Consider Layers

If you have thick hair, consider adding layers to lighten the weight of your hair and add movement. Layers can also help to frame your face and draw attention to your best features.

Step 4: Choose a Style

Finally, choose a style that suits your personal style and the occasion. Whether you opt for a sleek updo or loose waves, make sure it complements your face shape and flatters your features.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your look:

Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your hair looking healthy and fresh, it's important to trim it regularly. Depending on your hair type and length, you may need to trim your hair every 4-8 weeks.

Use the Right Products

Using the right hair products can help to maintain the health and appearance of your hair. Choose products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid using too much product, which can weigh down your hair.

Protect Your Hair

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to protect it from heat and environmental damage. Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools, and wear a hat or scarf to protect your hair from the sun and wind.

FAQs

What is the Best Hairstyle for a Round Face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that elongates the face and draws attention to your best features. Consider a longer cut with layers, a short pixie cut, or a sleek bob.

Can I Pull Off Bangs if I Have a Round Face?

Yes, you can pull off bangs if you have a round face. However, it's essential to choose the right style of bangs that flatters your face shape. Consider a side-swept bang or long, wispy bangs that add height to your face.

Hair Care Tips for a Round Face

Now that you know what hairstyle looks good with a round face, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking its best:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to wash it regularly. Depending on your hair type and texture, you may need to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

Use a Conditioner

Using a conditioner can help to keep your hair looking soft and smooth. Apply a conditioner after shampooing, and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

Avoid Over-Styling Your Hair

Over-styling your hair can lead to damage and breakage. Avoid using hot tools every day, and give your hair a break by wearing it in a ponytail or bun.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape, you can achieve a look that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
