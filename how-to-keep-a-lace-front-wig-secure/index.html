How To Keep A Lace Front Wig Secure Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 12, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re new to wearing lace front wigs, you might be wondering how to keep them secure. Whether you’re wearing a wig for fashion or medical reasons, it’s important to know how to keep it in place throughout the day. Here are some tips and tricks to help you keep your lace front wig secure.

Table of Contents

  • How to Secure Your Lace Front Wig
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Lace Front Wigs

How to Secure Your Lace Front Wig

There are several methods you can use to secure your lace front wig. Here are some of the most popular:

  • Adhesive: Many people use wig glue or tape to secure their lace front wigs. This method involves applying adhesive to your scalp and then pressing the wig onto it. It can provide a very secure hold, but some people find it uncomfortable or irritating.
  • Clips: Another option is to use wig clips. These are small clips that you can sew onto the wig cap, and then clip onto your natural hair. This method is less invasive than adhesive, but may not provide as secure a hold.
  • Comb and Elastic Band: Some lace front wigs come with combs and an elastic band built in. You can use the combs to secure the wig to your natural hair, and then adjust the elastic band to fit snugly around your head.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re using adhesive to secure your lace front wig, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Clean your scalp: Before applying adhesive, make sure your scalp is clean and dry. You can use rubbing alcohol to remove any oil or residue.
  2. Apply adhesive: Apply a thin layer of wig glue or tape to your scalp, following the manufacturer’s instructions. Be careful not to apply too much, as this can make the wig look unnatural.
  3. Attach the wig: Carefully place the front of the wig onto the adhesive, and press down firmly. Smooth out any bubbles or wrinkles.
  4. Style and go: Once your wig is secure, you can style it as desired and go about your day!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to help you keep your lace front wig secure:

  • Use a wig cap: A wig cap can help keep your natural hair in place, and make it easier to attach the wig.
  • Adjust the straps: If your wig has adjustable straps, make sure they are snug but not too tight. You don’t want to create unwanted pressure points on your scalp.
  • Use heat protectant: If you’re using heat to style your wig, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Store your wig properly: When you’re not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand to help maintain its shape and prevent tangling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about keeping their lace front wigs secure:

  • How often do I need to reapply adhesive? It depends on the type of adhesive you’re using, but most people find they need to reapply every 2-3 days.
  • Can I swim or shower with my lace front wig on? It’s not recommended, as water can weaken the adhesive and cause the wig to come loose.
  • Can I wear my wig to bed? It’s not recommended, as this can cause tangling and damage to the wig.

Hair Care for Lace Front Wigs

Proper hair care is important to keep your lace front wig looking and feeling its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your wig regularly: Depending on how often you wear it, you may need to wash your wig every 2-4 weeks. Use a wig-specific shampoo and conditioner, and let the wig air dry.
  • Avoid high heat: While some wigs are heat-resistant, it’s best to avoid exposing them to high heat from styling tools or direct sunlight.
  • Brush gently: Use a wide-tooth comb or wig brush to gently detangle your wig, starting at the ends and working your way up.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your lace front wig secure and looking great for as long as possible.
