Short Hairstyle For Round Face 2021 A Guide To Perfect Haircut

Written by Robby Sep 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. Fortunately, there are plenty of short hairstyles that can flatter your face shape and make you look amazing. This guide will give you all the information you need to choose the perfect short hairstyle for your round face in 2021.

Description

A round face is typically defined by a circular shape with prominent cheeks and a rounded jawline. The key to finding the perfect short hairstyle for this face shape is to create the illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved with layers, bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Round Face

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness
  • Think about your personal style and preferences
  • Choose a cut that highlights your best features
  • Consult with a stylist who specializes in short hairstyles for round faces

Step-by-Step Guide for the Best Short Hairstyles for Round Faces

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can work well for round faces. To create a pixie cut, your stylist will cut your hair short on the sides and back while leaving some length on top. This creates a longer, more angular look that can help elongate your face.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is another great option for round faces. This hairstyle can be customized to fit your face shape by adding layers and angles. For a round face, a bob with layers and an asymmetrical cut can help create the illusion of length and angles.

3. Short Shag Cut

If you have thick, coarse hair, a short shag cut can be a great option. This hairstyle features layers and choppy, textured ends that can help create a more angular look. A shag cut can also help add volume and movement to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hairstyle for Round Face

Once you've found the perfect short hairstyle for your round face, it's important to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips for maintaining your short hair:

  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny
  • Consider using a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair

FAQs

What short hairstyles are best for round faces?

Short hairstyles that feature layers, asymmetrical cuts, and textured ends are typically the best options for round faces.

What should I avoid when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face look rounder. Avoid blunt cuts and straight bangs, as these can also make your face look more circular.

Can I still wear my hair up with a short hairstyle for a round face?

Absolutely! There are plenty of ways to style short hair, including updos and braids. Talk to your stylist about the best ways to style your short hair for your face shape.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hairstyles for Round Face

Short hair can be more prone to damage than longer hair, so it's important to take good care of your locks. Here are a few tips for keeping your short hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools

Now that you have all the information you need, it's time to choose the perfect short hairstyle for your round face in 2021. With the right cut and styling, you can look and feel amazing every day.
