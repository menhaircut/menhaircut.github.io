Spiky Hairstyle 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and edgy? Look no further than the spiky hairstyle! In this ultimate guide, we will show you how to achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle for 2021. From how to style your hair to tips and tricks for maintenance, we've got you covered.

Description

The spiky hairstyle has been around for decades, but it's still a popular choice for men and women of all ages. This hairstyle is characterized by short, spiked hair that stands up straight from the scalp. It's a bold and edgy look that can be customized to suit your personal style.

How To Achieve A Spiky Hairstyle

Getting the perfect spiky hairstyle for 2021 is easier than you might think. Here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Choose The Right Haircut

The first step in achieving a spiky hairstyle is to choose the right haircut. You'll want to ask your hairstylist for a cut that's short on the sides and longer on top. This will give you the length you need to create the spikes.

Step 2: Use The Right Products

Next, you'll need to use the right products to achieve the perfect spiky look. A strong-hold gel or pomade is essential to keep your hair in place. You may also want to use a hairdryer to help set the spikes in place.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Once you have the right haircut and products, it's time to style your hair. Start by applying the gel or pomade to your hair, focusing on the longer strands on top. Use your fingers to shape the spikes and create the look you want. Then, use a hairdryer on a cool setting to help set the spikes in place.

Tips For Maintaining Your Spiky Hairstyle

Now that you have the perfect spiky hairstyle, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

Tip 1: Use The Right Shampoo

When washing your hair, use a shampoo that's designed for your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

Tip 2: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To keep your spiky hairstyle looking sharp, it's important to trim your hair regularly. This will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Tip 3: Don't Overuse Products

While products are essential for creating the perfect spiky look, it's important not to overuse them. Too much product can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

Q: Is A Spiky Hairstyle Suitable For All Hair Types?

A: Yes, a spiky hairstyle can be customized to suit all hair types. However, if you have very fine or thin hair, you may want to avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy.

Q: How Long Will A Spiky Hairstyle Last?

A: A spiky hairstyle can last for several days with the right products and maintenance. However, you may need to touch up your hair every day or so to keep it looking its best.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a spiky hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use A Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help keep your hair healthy and prevent damage. Look for a mask that's designed for your hair type.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Using heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can damage your hair and make it harder to style. Try to avoid using these tools as much as possible.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair From The Sun

The sun can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. Protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF.

Now that you know how to achieve the perfect spiky hairstyle for 2021, it's time to give it a try! With the right haircut, products, and maintenance, you can rock this bold and edgy look with confidence.
