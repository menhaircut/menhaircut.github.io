How To Cut My Hair Pixie Style A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a dramatic change to your hairstyle, a pixie cut might be just what you need. This short and sassy look is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish haircut. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your hair pixie style in easy-to-follow steps.

How to Cut My Hair Pixie Style

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically features short layers at the top and sides of the head, with longer layers at the back. This style is perfect for those who want to make a statement and show off their facial features.

To cut your hair pixie style, you'll need a few tools, including:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clippers (optional)
  • Hair ties

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your hair pixie style:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Start with the back section and use clippers or scissors to cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Move on to the side sections and cut them to the same length as the back.
  5. Use the comb to pull the top section of your hair up and cut it to the desired length.
  6. Create short layers by cutting the hair at an angle, starting at the back and moving forward.
  7. Blend the layers by cutting them at a 90-degree angle.
  8. Trim the hair around your ears and the nape of your neck.
  9. Check for any unevenness and make adjustments as needed.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Pixie Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair pixie style:

  • Start with a longer length and work your way shorter to avoid any mistakes.
  • Use hair ties to keep sections of your hair separated and out of the way.
  • Be patient and take your time to avoid any accidents.
  • Consider going to a professional stylist if you're not confident in your skills.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair pixie style:

How short should I cut my hair for a pixie cut?

The length of your hair depends on your preference and face shape. Generally, a pixie cut is cut to about two to three inches in length.

Can I cut my own hair pixie style?

Yes, you can cut your own hair pixie style, but it's recommended that you go to a professional stylist if you're not confident in your skills.

What face shapes look best with a pixie cut?

A pixie cut looks best on oval, heart, and square-shaped faces.

Hair Care for Pixie Haircuts

Here are some tips to keep your pixie haircut looking its best:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Visit your stylist every four to six weeks to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a styling product to add texture and definition to your hair.

Cutting your hair pixie style is a bold and daring move, but with the right tools, patience, and technique, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look. Remember to take your time, be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it.
