How To Pick A Good Hairstyle For Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Sep 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing a hairstyle can be a daunting task for guys. With so many options available, it’s easy to get overwhelmed. However, selecting the right haircut can make a significant difference in your overall appearance. In this article, we’ll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to pick a good hairstyle for guys.

Table of Contents

  • How to Pick a Good Hairstyle for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips and Tricks

How to Pick a Good Hairstyle for Guys

The first step in picking a good hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are several types of face shapes, including round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics, and certain hairstyles can accentuate or detract from these features.

Once you’ve determined your face shape, consider your hair texture and thickness. Some hairstyles work better with straight hair, while others are better suited for curly or wavy hair. Additionally, thicker hair can support more volume and length, while thinner hair may require a shorter cut.

Finally, consider your personal style and lifestyle. Do you want a low-maintenance haircut that can be styled quickly each morning, or are you willing to spend more time styling your hair to achieve a specific look?

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to pick a good hairstyle:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair texture and thickness.
  3. Think about your personal style and lifestyle.
  4. Research different hairstyles that work well with your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist to get their opinion on which hairstyles would work best for you.
  6. Bring in pictures of hairstyles that you like to your hairstylist.
  7. Talk to your hairstylist about the level of maintenance required for each hairstyle.
  8. Ask your hairstylist for styling tips and product recommendations.
  9. Try out your new hairstyle and make adjustments as needed.
  10. Maintain your new hairstyle with regular haircuts and proper hair care.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when selecting a new hairstyle:

  • Consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle.
  • Bring in pictures of hairstyles that you like to show your hairstylist.
  • Be open to trying new things, but also consider the level of maintenance required for each hairstyle.
  • Ask your hairstylist for product recommendations to help you maintain your new hairstyle.
  • Don’t be afraid to make adjustments to your hairstyle if it’s not working for you.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add height and length to your face, such as a pompadour or slicked-back undercut.

What is the best hairstyle for a square face?

Hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head and keep the sides short can help soften your features. Consider a messy fringe or side-swept quiff.

What is the best hairstyle for a heart-shaped face?

Hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face and keep the top relatively flat can help balance out a heart-shaped face. Consider a layered cut with side-swept bangs.

Hair Care Tips and Tricks

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a good hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

Choosing a good hairstyle can be a fun and rewarding experience. By following these tips and consulting with a professional hairstylist, you can find a hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.
