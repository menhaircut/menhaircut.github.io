How To Gel Men s Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Jul 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Men with curly hair often struggle to find the right styling product that can tame their curls without leaving them looking greasy or flat. Gel is a popular choice for men with curly hair because it provides a strong hold and definition without weighing down the curls. In this article, we will discuss how to gel men's curly hair and share some tips and tricks to achieve the perfect look.

How to Gel Men's Curly Hair

Gel is a styling product that comes in different strengths and finishes. For curly hair, it is best to use a strong-hold gel that can keep the curls in place for hours. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo that can cleanse your scalp without stripping your hair of its natural oils. Then, apply a conditioner that can moisturize and detangle your curls. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water and pat it dry with a microfiber towel. Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel as it can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Gel to Your Hair

Squeeze a dime-sized amount of gel onto your palm and rub your hands together to distribute it evenly. Then, apply the gel to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the tips. Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to make sure that the gel is evenly distributed throughout your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to style your curls. You can scrunch your hair upwards to enhance the curl pattern or use a hair pick to create volume at the roots. Avoid touching your hair too much as it can cause frizz.

Tips for Gelling Men's Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you gel men's curly hair:

  • Use a strong-hold gel that can keep your curls in place for hours.
  • Avoid using too much gel as it can make your hair look greasy and crunchy.
  • Apply the gel to damp hair for better distribution.
  • Use a hair pick or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your curls before applying the gel.
  • Avoid using a regular towel to dry your hair as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair without disrupting the curl pattern.

FAQs

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often. If you sweat a lot or work out, you may need to wash your hair more frequently.

Can I use gel on wet hair?

Yes, you can apply gel to damp hair for better distribution. Avoid using too much gel as it can make your hair look greasy and crunchy.

How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

To prevent frizz in curly hair, you can use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, and avoid touching your hair too much.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for men with curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and moisturize your curls.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a regular towel as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair by gently squeezing the curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve a stylish and defined look for your curly hair using gel. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and style.
