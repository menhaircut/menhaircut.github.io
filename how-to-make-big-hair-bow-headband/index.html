How To Make A Big Hair Bow Headband Diy Tutorial

Written by Oliver Sep 30, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for a fun and stylish way to accessorize your hair? Look no further than the big hair bow headband! This trendy accessory is perfect for any occasion, from a casual day out to a fancy event. In this tutorial, we will show you step-by-step how to make your own big hair bow headband. Let's get started!

Description

A big hair bow headband is a fashionable hair accessory that consists of a large bow attached to a headband. It is versatile and can be worn by people of all ages and hair types. You can make a hair bow headband using a variety of materials, such as ribbon, fabric, or even leather. In this tutorial, we will be using ribbon.

How to Make a Big Hair Bow Headband

Step 1: Gather Materials

Before you begin, you will need to gather the necessary materials. You will need:

  • 3 yards of ribbon (1 ½ inches wide)
  • Scissors
  • Hot glue gun
  • Headband

Step 2: Cut the Ribbon

Cut your ribbon into three pieces: one 24-inch piece, one 18-inch piece, and one 6-inch piece.

Step 3: Create the Bow

Take the 24-inch piece of ribbon and fold it in half, creating a loop. Pinch the center of the loop and secure it with a small piece of ribbon or string. This will create the center of the bow.

Next, take the 18-inch piece of ribbon and fold it in half, creating another loop. Place this loop on top of the first loop, making sure the ends of the ribbon are even. Secure the center of the loops with the small ribbon or string.

Finally, take the 6-inch piece of ribbon and wrap it around the center of the bow, covering the small ribbon or string. Secure the ends of the ribbon with hot glue.

Step 4: Attach the Bow to the Headband

Using hot glue, attach the bow to the headband. Hold the bow in place until the glue dries.

Step 5: Finishing Touches

Trim any excess ribbon and make sure the bow is securely attached to the headband.

Tips

  • Experiment with different types of ribbon to create a unique look.
  • If you don't have a hot glue gun, you can use fabric glue or super glue.
  • Make sure the headband is the right size for your head.

FAQ

Q: Can I make a big hair bow headband using fabric instead of ribbon?

A: Yes, you can use fabric instead of ribbon to make a big hair bow headband. Just make sure the fabric is the right size and thickness.

Q: Can I make a big hair bow headband without a headband?

A: Yes, you can attach the bow to a clip or barrette instead of a headband.

Hair Care

When wearing a big hair bow headband, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a hair tie or clip to secure your hair before putting on the headband.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray or other hair products, as they can make the headband slip.
  • Take off the headband before going to bed to avoid damaging your hair.

Now that you know how to make a big hair bow headband, you can create your own unique accessory to wear with any outfit. Have fun and be creative!
