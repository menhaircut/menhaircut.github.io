Mens Hairstyles Short Sides Curly Top

Written by Christine Aug 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and trendy? If so, then you should consider the mens hairstyles short sides curly top. This hairstyle has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason. It's stylish, versatile, and easy to maintain.

Description

The mens hairstyles short sides curly top is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and a curly top. The length of the top can vary depending on personal preference. This hairstyle is perfect for men who have naturally curly hair or those who want to add some texture and volume to their hair.

How To

If you want to achieve this hairstyle, you will need to follow these steps:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and make them more manageable.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a curl defining cream or mousse to your hair. This will help to enhance your curls and make them more prominent.
  3. Using a hair dryer and a diffuser attachment, dry your hair on a low heat setting. This will help to prevent frizz and maintain the shape of your curls.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a trimmer to cut the hair on the sides of your head. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve your desired length.
  5. Style the top of your hair using your fingers or a wide-tooth comb. You can also use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add some definition and hold to your curls.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your mens hairstyles short sides curly top:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for curly hair to help define your curls and make them more manageable.
  • Use a curl defining cream or mousse to enhance your curls and make them more prominent.

FAQ

Is the mens hairstyles short sides curly top suitable for all hair types?

This hairstyle is best suited for men who have naturally curly hair or those who want to add some texture and volume to their hair. If you have straight or wavy hair, this hairstyle may not be the best option for you.

How often should I trim the hair on the sides of my head?

It's recommended that you trim the hair on the sides of your head every 2-3 weeks to keep the style looking fresh and clean.

What products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a curl defining cream or mousse to enhance your curls and a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add some definition and hold to your curls.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair like bleach or hair dye as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and maintaining a good hair care routine, you can help keep your mens hairstyles short sides curly top looking its best.
