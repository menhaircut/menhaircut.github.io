Which Hairstyle Suits Round Face Female A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles but still can’t find the perfect one for your round face? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we will discuss which hairstyle suits round face female and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you look your best. So, let’s get started!

Description

A round face has softer features and a rounded chin, so it’s important to find a hairstyle that can balance out the roundness. The right hairstyle can make your face look slimmer and enhance your best features. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right hairstyle. But with our guide, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your round face.

How to Choose the Perfect Hairstyle for Round Face Female

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for a round face, it’s essential to consider the length, texture, and style that best suits your face shape. Here’s how to choose the perfect hairstyle:

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If they are all similar in length, then you have a round face.

Step 2: Consider your hair texture. Different hairstyles work better for different hair textures. If you have thick, curly hair, then you may want to consider a shorter hairstyle. If you have thin, straight hair, then longer hairstyles may work better.

Step 3: Look for hairstyles that create angles. Angled hairstyles can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face. A layered bob or a side-swept bang can work well.

Step 4: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face. This includes styles with excess volume around your cheeks or temples. Instead, opt for styles that add volume to the top of your head.

Tips for Round Face Female

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Consider highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair and enhance your features.
  • Avoid short hairstyles that end at your chin or cheekbones. This can make your face look wider.
  • Opt for styles that have a side part. This can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Invest in a good quality flat iron or curling iron to help you create your desired hairstyle.
  • Choose a hairstyle that you feel comfortable with and that suits your personality and lifestyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round face female:

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

The best hairstyle for a round face is one that creates angles and adds volume to the top of your head. A layered bob or a side-swept bang can work well.

Can a round face pull off a pixie cut?

Yes, a round face can pull off a pixie cut. However, it’s important to choose a pixie cut that has volume on top and is tapered at the sides.

What are some hairstyles to avoid for a round face?

Hairstyles to avoid for a round face include short hairstyles that end at your chin or cheekbones, and styles with excess volume around your cheeks or temples.

Hair Care for Round Face Female

While choosing the right hairstyle is important, it’s also essential to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect hairstyle for your round face and look your best. Remember to choose a style that suits your personality and lifestyle, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
