How To Cut Bangs At Home For Short Hair

Written by Frank Oct 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have short hair and want to change up your look, cutting bangs at home can be a great way to do so. Not only is it convenient, but it can also save you money. In this article, we'll show you how to cut bangs at home for short hair in an easy, step-by-step process.

What You'll Need

Before you get started, make sure you have the following supplies:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Water spray bottle

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and then wetting the front section that you want to cut. This will make it easier to handle and cut evenly.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Next, use a comb to section off the hair that you want to cut. This will be the area where your bangs will be. Use clips to hold the rest of your hair out of the way.

Step 3: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length of your bangs. It's best to start with a longer length and then gradually cut more off if needed.

Step 4: Cut Your Bangs

Using sharp scissors, start cutting your bangs. Cut them straight across and take small sections at a time. It's best to cut less initially, so you don't cut too much off.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you've cut your bangs, check your work in the mirror. Make sure they're even and at the length you want. If they aren't, you can always go back and trim them some more.

Tips

  • It's always best to start with a longer length and gradually cut more off if needed.
  • Make sure you use sharp scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut your bangs when they're dry. Wetting them first will make them easier to handle and cut evenly.
  • Take your time and don't rush. It's better to cut less initially and then go back and trim more if needed.

FAQ

Can I cut bangs at home for short hair?

Yes, you can definitely cut bangs at home for short hair. Just make sure you have the right supplies and follow the steps carefully.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go back and trim more off or even out your bangs. It's better to take your time and be careful.

Hair Care

After you've cut your bangs, make sure you take care of them properly. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep them healthy and shiny. You can also use hair styling products to help them stay in place.

Cutting bangs at home for short hair can be a fun and easy way to change up your look. Just make sure you have the right supplies and follow the steps carefully. Take your time, and don't rush. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at cutting your own bangs in no time!
