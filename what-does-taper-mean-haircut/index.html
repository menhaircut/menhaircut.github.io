What Does Taper Mean Haircut

Written by Bardi Nov 15, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Content:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you’re new to the world of men’s hairstyles, you may be wondering what does taper mean haircut? The word “taper” refers to a style of haircut that gradually gets shorter as it goes down the head. This gives the hair a more natural look and can be a great option for men who want a low-maintenance style.

A taper haircut can be done with a variety of hair lengths and styles, making it a versatile choice for many men. Whether you have long hair or short hair, a taper can be customized to suit your needs.

How to

If you’re interested in getting a taper haircut, it’s important to find a barber or hairstylist who is experienced in this style. They will be able to give you a clean and even taper that looks great from all angles.

Step by step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting a taper haircut:

  1. Decide on the length and style of your hair. This will help your barber know how to taper your hair.
  2. Start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down as you go lower on your head.
  3. Blend the hair between the different lengths to create a smooth and even taper.
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that were missed by the clippers.
  5. Detail the haircut by trimming around the ears and neckline.

Tips

To ensure that your taper haircut looks great and lasts as long as possible, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your taper.
  • Use a styling product to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Consider using a hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect taper for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about taper haircuts:

What’s the difference between a taper and a fade?

While taper and fade haircuts are similar, a taper gradually gets shorter as it goes down the head, while a fade has a more abrupt transition between lengths.

Can I get a taper with long hair?

Yes, a taper can be done with any length of hair. Your barber or hairstylist can customize the taper to suit your needs.

How often should I get a taper haircut?

This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain your style. In general, it’s a good idea to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your taper looking its best.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best after a taper haircut, it’s important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, which can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that’s rich in vitamins and minerals to support healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know what does taper mean haircut, you can decide whether it’s the right choice for you. With its natural look and versatility, a taper can be a great option for many men. Just be sure to find a skilled barber or hairstylist who can give you the perfect taper for your needs.
