What Happens When You Brush Wavy Hair

Written by Bardi Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wavy hair requires a different type of hair care regimen compared to straight or curly hair. If you have wavy hair, you may be wondering what happens when you brush it. In this blog post, we'll discuss the effects of brushing wavy hair and provide some tips on how to properly care for it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Brush Wavy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Brushing Wavy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Wavy Hair Care Tips

Description

Brushing wavy hair can have both positive and negative effects. On the one hand, it can help distribute natural oils throughout the hair, making it softer and shinier. On the other hand, brushing wavy hair too vigorously can cause frizz and breakage, making it look dull and damaged. The key is to brush wavy hair gently and in the right way.

How to Brush Wavy Hair

Before we get into the step-by-step guide, it's important to note that wavy hair should not be brushed while it's dry. Doing so can cause breakage and frizz. Instead, it's best to brush wavy hair when it's wet or damp.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. After showering, gently towel dry your hair.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to your hair to make it easier to comb through.
  4. Using a wide-tooth comb, start at the ends of your hair and work your way up, gently detangling any knots or tangles.
  5. Once your hair is detangled, use a paddle brush or a boar bristle brush to gently brush your hair in sections, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  6. Be sure to brush gently and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  7. Once you've brushed your entire head, use your fingers to gently scrunch your hair to encourage your waves.
  8. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair gently.
  9. Finish with a light mist of hairspray or a hair oil to add shine and hold.

Tips for Brushing Wavy Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when brushing wavy hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush with widely spaced bristles to avoid causing damage to your hair.
  • Brush your hair gently and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.
  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or detangler to make it easier to comb through your hair.
  • Don't over-brush your hair as it can cause frizz and damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I brush my wavy hair every day?

It's generally not necessary to brush wavy hair every day. In fact, over-brushing can cause damage and frizz. Instead, aim to brush your hair once or twice a week, or whenever it feels tangled or knotted.

What type of brush should I use?

For wavy hair, it's best to use a wide-tooth comb or a paddle brush with widely spaced bristles. Avoid using brushes with metal bristles, as they can cause damage and breakage.

Wavy Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep your wavy hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can cause damage and frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal components, as they can cause breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause breakage and frizz.

By following these tips, you can keep your wavy hair looking healthy and beautiful.
