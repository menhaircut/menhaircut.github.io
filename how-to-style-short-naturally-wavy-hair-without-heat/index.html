How To Style Short Naturally Wavy Hair Without Heat

Written by Bardi Nov 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat on your hair every day? You’re not alone. Many people with naturally wavy hair feel like they have to use heat to get the style they want. However, using heat on your hair can cause damage and weaken your strands over time. So, what’s the solution? In this post, we’ll show you how to style short naturally wavy hair without heat. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

If you have short naturally wavy hair, you might think that your styling options are limited. However, there are many ways to style your hair without using heat. By using the right products and techniques, you can enhance your natural waves and create a beautiful, effortless look.

How To

The first step to styling your short naturally wavy hair without heat is to find the right products. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, such as curl creams, mousses, and gels. These products will help define your waves and keep them in place without the need for heat.

Once you have your products, it’s time to start styling. Here are some techniques you can use:

Step By Step

1. Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it harder to style. Once you’ve washed and conditioned your hair, gently towel dry it.

2. Apply your products

Apply your curl cream, mousse, or gel to your damp hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends. Use a wide-tooth comb to comb through your hair and remove any tangles.

3. Scrunch your hair

Using your hands, scrunch your hair from the bottom up. This will help enhance your natural waves and create definition. Be gentle and avoid pulling or tugging on your hair.

4. Air dry your hair

Allow your hair to air dry naturally. Avoid using a hair dryer or any other heat styling tools. If you’re in a rush, you can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to speed up the drying process. However, be sure to use the lowest heat setting and keep the dryer at least six inches away from your hair.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling short naturally wavy hair without heat:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage
  • Try different products to find what works best for your hair
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends

FAQ

Q: Can I still use heat on my hair occasionally?

A: Yes, you can still use heat on your hair occasionally. However, try to limit your use of heat styling tools to once a week or less. And always use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Q: How do I revive my waves on second-day hair?

A: If your waves are looking a bit flat on second-day hair, try using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo. Spray the product onto your roots and gently massage it into your scalp. This will help add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

Finally, remember to take good care of your hair. This means eating a healthy diet, drinking plenty of water, and using gentle products that won’t damage your strands. With the right care and styling techniques, you can achieve beautiful, healthy hair without the need for heat.

So there you have it – our guide to styling short naturally wavy hair without heat. Give these techniques a try and see what works best for you. With a little patience and practice, you’ll be able to create a gorgeous, effortless style that showcases your natural waves.
