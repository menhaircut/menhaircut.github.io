What Is A 360 Frontal Wig

Written by Eveline Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A 360 frontal wig is a type of wig that is designed to provide full coverage around the perimeter of the head. It is made with a lace closure that covers the entire hairline, from ear to ear, and a weave that covers the rest of the head. This creates a natural-looking hairline and allows the wig to be styled in a variety of ways.

Description

A 360 frontal wig is called so because it has a 360-degree lace closure that goes all the way around the head. This type of wig is made with a lace cap that has hair strands attached to it. The hair strands are attached to the lace using a knotting technique that creates a natural-looking hairline.

The hair is usually made of human hair or synthetic hair, and it can be styled just like natural hair. You can straighten it, curl it, or even dye it to match your natural hair color.

How to Wear a 360 Frontal Wig

Wearing a 360 frontal wig is easy and can be done in a few simple steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

The first step in wearing a 360 frontal wig is to prepare your hair. If you have long hair, you should braid it or put it in a low bun. If you have short hair, you can simply brush it back and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 2: Put on a Wig Cap

The next step is to put on a wig cap. A wig cap will help to keep your natural hair in place and create a smooth surface for the wig to sit on. Simply place the wig cap over your head and secure it with bobby pins.

Step 3: Put on the Wig

Now it's time to put on the wig. Place the wig on your head and adjust it until it feels comfortable. Use the adjustable straps at the back of the wig to tighten or loosen the fit as needed.

Step 4: Style the Wig

Once the wig is in place, you can style it as desired. You can part it in the middle or on the side, or even pull it back into a ponytail.

Tips for Wearing a 360 Frontal Wig

Here are a few tips to help you get the most out of your 360 frontal wig:

  • Be gentle when brushing or combing the hair to avoid pulling out the strands.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling the hair with hot tools to prevent damage.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand when not in use to help maintain its shape.

FAQs

Q: How long does a 360 frontal wig last?

A: The lifespan of a 360 frontal wig depends on how well it is cared for and how often it is worn. With proper care, a high-quality wig can last for several months to a year or more.

Q: Can I wear a 360 frontal wig if I have a small head?

A: Yes, most 360 frontal wigs come with adjustable straps that allow you to tighten or loosen the fit as needed. If you have a particularly small head, you may need to have the wig customized to fit properly.

Hair Care for 360 Frontal Wigs

Proper hair care is essential to keep your 360 frontal wig looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash the wig regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid exposing the wig to excessive heat or sunlight.
  • Store the wig on a wig stand when not in use.
  • Avoid sleeping in the wig to prevent tangling and matting.

With proper care, a 360 frontal wig can look natural and beautiful for months or even years to come.
