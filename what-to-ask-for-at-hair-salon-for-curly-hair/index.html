What To Ask For At Hair Salon For Curly Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a fabulous look. If you have curly hair, it's essential to find a hair salon that specializes in curly hair. In this article, we'll discuss what to ask for at a hair salon for curly hair, so you can get the perfect haircut and style.

Table of Contents

  • How to Ask for a Curly Haircut
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Ask for a Curly Haircut

When you go to the hair salon, it's essential to communicate clearly with your stylist. Here are some tips on how to ask for a curly haircut:

  • Bring pictures of haircuts that you like. This will help your stylist understand what you're looking for.
  • Ask your stylist if they have experience cutting curly hair.
  • Use curly hair terminology such as layers, long layers, and shape.
  • Ask your stylist to cut your hair dry. This will help your stylist see the natural curl pattern and prevent shrinkage.

Remember to be specific about what you want and don't want. If you're not happy with the haircut, speak up and let your stylist know.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on what to ask for at a hair salon for curly hair:

  1. Ask for a consultation before the haircut. During the consultation, discuss your hair type, length, and texture with your stylist.
  2. Show pictures of haircuts that you like and explain what you want from the haircut.
  3. Ask your stylist to cut your hair dry to see the natural curl pattern.
  4. Ask for layers to create movement and shape.
  5. Ask for long layers to avoid a triangular shape.
  6. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can create too much volume.
  7. Ask for a shape that complements your face shape.
  8. Ask for minimal thinning or texturizing to avoid frizz.
  9. Ask for advice on styling products and techniques.
  10. Ask your stylist to show you how to style your hair at home.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

Here are some tips for styling curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet.
  • Apply styling products to wet hair to create a defined curl pattern.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair when it's dry to prevent frizz.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid hair breakage.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best haircut for curly hair?

The best haircut for curly hair is a layered haircut. Layers create movement and shape, which complement the natural curl pattern.

Should I cut my curly hair when it's wet or dry?

You should cut your curly hair when it's dry. This will help your stylist see the natural curl pattern and prevent shrinkage.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

You should cut your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to wet hair to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know what to ask for at a hair salon for curly hair, you can confidently go to the salon and get the perfect haircut and style. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist and ask for advice on hair care and styling techniques. With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can rock your curly hair with confidence.
