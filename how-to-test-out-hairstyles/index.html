How To Test Out Hairstyles Tips Tricks And Faqs

Written by Oliver Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Trying out a new hairstyle is always exciting, but it can also be nerve-wracking. How do you know if it will suit you? Will it be easy to maintain? These are just some of the questions that might be going through your head. Fortunately, there are some tips and tricks you can use to test out hairstyles before committing to them. In this article, we'll cover some of the best ways to experiment with your locks, as well as answer some common FAQs and provide some hair care tips.

  • How to Test Out Hairstyles
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Test Out Hairstyles

There are several ways you can test out a new hairstyle, from virtual tools to temporary changes. Here are some of the best methods:

Use Virtual Makeover Tools

One of the easiest ways to test out hairstyles is to use virtual makeover tools. These tools allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles, colors, and even makeup. Some of the most popular virtual makeover tools include L'Oreal's Style My Hair and Taaz.

Experiment with Wigs

If you're not ready to commit to a new hairstyle, you can try on wigs instead. Wigs come in a variety of styles and colors, so you can see what looks best on you without making any permanent changes. You can find wigs at most beauty supply stores or online.

Try Clip-In Extensions

If you want to test out longer or thicker hair, clip-in extensions are a great option. They're easy to use and can be removed whenever you want. You can find clip-in extensions at most beauty supply stores or online.

Experiment with Temporary Hair Color

If you're thinking about changing your hair color, you can try out temporary hair color first. Temporary hair color washes out after a few shampoos, so you can see if you like the color without making a permanent change. You can find temporary hair color at most drugstores or online.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to testing out hairstyles:

  1. Decide on the hairstyle you want to try.
  2. Choose the method you want to use (virtual makeover tool, wig, clip-in extensions, or temporary hair color).
  3. If using a virtual makeover tool, upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles.
  4. If using a wig, try on different styles and colors until you find one you like.
  5. If using clip-in extensions, follow the instructions to attach them to your hair and style as desired.
  6. If using temporary hair color, follow the instructions to apply the color and see how it looks.
  7. Take photos of yourself from different angles to see how the hairstyle looks.
  8. Decide if you like the hairstyle and want to make it permanent.

Tips for Testing Out Hairstyles

Here are some additional tips for testing out hairstyles:

  • Try out different hairstyles in different lighting. What looks good in natural light might not look as good in artificial light.
  • Take photos from different angles to see how the hairstyle looks from all sides.
  • Ask for feedback from friends or family members.
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a new hairstyle.
  • Be open to trying something new. You might be surprised at what looks good on you!

FAQs

What if I don't like the hairstyle?

If you don't like the hairstyle, simply remove the wig or clip-in extensions or wash out the temporary hair color. If you're using a virtual makeover tool, you can try on a different hairstyle.

What if the hairstyle damages my hair?

If you're using clip-in extensions, be sure to follow the instructions carefully to avoid damaging your hair. If you're using temporary hair color, be sure to choose a product that is gentle on your hair.

How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

Consider your face shape, hair texture, and skin tone when choosing a new hairstyle. You can also use virtual makeover tools to try on different hairstyles and see what looks best on you.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you're testing out different hairstyles or sticking with your current look, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth and health.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to test out hairstyles with confidence. Remember to have fun and be open to trying something new!
