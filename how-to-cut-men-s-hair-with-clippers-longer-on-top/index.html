How To Cut Men s Hair With Clippers Longer On Top

Written by Petter Nov 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a stylish yet low-maintenance haircut for men? Cutting men's hair with clippers longer on top is a great option. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of achieving this trendy haircut.

Description

The longer-on-top haircut is a versatile style that can be customized to suit your preferences. It's characterized by shorter sides and back, while the top is left longer to create a textured look. This style is ideal for men who want a neat and tidy haircut without having to spend a lot of time styling it every day.

How to Cut Men's Hair with Clippers Longer on Top

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers with various guards, a comb, and a pair of scissors. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect longer-on-top haircut:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Once your hair is clean, dry it completely using a towel or a blow dryer.

Step 2: Determine the Length of the Hair

Decide how long you want the hair on top to be. Use the clippers' guard that corresponds to the desired length.

Step 3: Start Cutting the Top

Using the clipper, start cutting the hair on top of your head, working from front to back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Back

Switch to a smaller guard on the clippers and start cutting the hair on the sides and back of your head. Use the longer guard to blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top.

Step 5: Trim the Hairline

Use the scissors to trim the hairline around your ears and the back of your neck.

Step 6: Check for Uneven Spots

Check for any uneven spots and trim them with the scissors.

Step 7: Style Your Hair

Once you've finished cutting your hair, style it according to your preferences. Use a hair product to add texture and hold to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to make the haircut process easier:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head for any uneven spots.
  • Trim your hairline regularly to maintain the haircut's shape.

FAQ

How often should I get my hair cut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly the style loses its shape. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Can I cut my hair with clippers myself?

Yes, you can. However, it's important to have the right tools and follow the proper techniques to achieve a clean and even haircut.

Hair Care

Maintaining a longer-on-top haircut is easy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. Regular trims will also help keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to cut men's hair with clippers longer on top, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance haircut at home. Remember to use the right tools and follow the proper techniques to achieve the best results.
