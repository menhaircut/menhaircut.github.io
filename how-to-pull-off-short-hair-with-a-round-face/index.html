How to Pull Off Short Hair with a Round Face

If you have a round face, you may be hesitant to try short hair. But fear not – with the right cut and styling, you can rock a short hairstyle and look amazing. Here are some tips to help you pull off short hair with a round face:

Step by Step

1. Choose the right cut – opt for a cut that adds height and volume to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a bob with layers.

2. Avoid blunt cuts – blunt cuts can make your face look rounder, so choose a cut with layers and texture.

3. Consider your hair texture – if you have thin hair, a shorter cut can add volume and fullness. If you have thick hair, a shorter cut can help control the volume.

4. Style your hair with volume – use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height and fullness to your hair.

5. Consider your face shape – if you have a round face, avoid styles that are too short or too long. Opt for a mid-length cut that falls just below your chin.

Tips

- Experiment with different partings to find what works best for you.

- Use a round brush when blow-drying to add volume to your hair.

- Avoid heavy bangs – they can make your face look rounder.

- Add texture to your hair with a texturizing spray or wax.

- Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short cut?

A: Yes, you can. Experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a half-up, half-down style.

Q: Will a short cut make my face look even rounder?

A: Not necessarily. With the right cut and styling, you can actually make your face look slimmer and more angular.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, you should get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your short hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair – use a heat protectant spray when blow-drying or using hot tools.

- Get regular trims to maintain your style and prevent split ends.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

- Protect your hair from the sun – wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray.
