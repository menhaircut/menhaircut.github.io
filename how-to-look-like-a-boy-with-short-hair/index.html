How To Look Like A Boy With Short Hair

Written by Christine Nov 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you interested in changing up your style and looking like a boy with short hair? It can be a fun and exciting way to experiment with your appearance. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as tips and tricks for maintaining your hair. Let's get started!

Description

Looking like a boy with short hair involves cutting your hair short and styling it in a more masculine way. This can be achieved through the use of products, such as gel or wax, and by using styling tools, such as a comb or brush. Additionally, incorporating more masculine clothing and accessories can help complete the look.

How To

Step 1: Cut Your Hair Short

The first step in achieving this look is to cut your hair short. This can be done by going to a professional stylist or by doing it yourself at home. A popular style for boys is a short, textured cut, with the sides and back shorter than the top. You can also ask your stylist for a fade or undercut, which will give you a more edgy look.

Step 2: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it in a more masculine way. You can use products like gel or wax to create a textured, messy look. Apply the product to your hair and use your fingers to style it into place. You can also use a comb or brush to create a more structured look.

Step 3: Dress the Part

To complete the look, incorporate more masculine clothing and accessories into your wardrobe. This could include wearing more neutral colors, like black, grey, or navy, and incorporating items like button-up shirts, blazers, or loafers. Accessories, like watches or hats, can also help complete the look.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Invest in good quality styling products, like gel or wax, to help maintain your hair.
  • Accessorize with masculine items, like watches or hats, to complete the look.
  • Find inspiration from male celebrities or fashion icons.

FAQ

Can I achieve this look with long hair?

While it may be more difficult to achieve this look with long hair, it's still possible. Consider getting a short haircut and adding layers to create texture. You can also experiment with styling your hair in a more masculine way using products and tools.

Do I have to dress in masculine clothing to look like a boy with short hair?

No, you don't have to dress in masculine clothing to achieve this look. However, incorporating more masculine clothing and accessories can help complete the look and give you a more authentic appearance.

Hair Care

Maintaining short hair requires regular trims to keep the style looking fresh. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using heat tools, like a blow dryer or flat iron, which can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry and use products to style it.

Overall, looking like a boy with short hair is a fun and exciting way to experiment with your appearance. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve this look and feel confident in your new style.
