How To Shape Hair Round Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Oct 20, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking for ways to style your hair to flatter your round face shape? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss how to shape hair round face and provide helpful tips and tricks to achieve the best hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • What is a Round Face Shape?
  • How to Shape Hair Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Round Face Shape

What is a Round Face Shape?

A round face shape is characterized by having a wide forehead, prominent cheeks, and a rounded chin. The width and length of the face are approximately the same, creating a circular shape. If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length and slimness.

How to Shape Hair Round Face

The key to shaping hair for a round face is to add height and length to the hair. This can be achieved through several different hairstyles, including:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • High ponytails
  • A-line bobs
  • Asymmetrical cuts

It’s important to avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs, chin-length bobs, and blunt cuts. These styles can make the face appear wider and rounder.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to shaping hair for a round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Part your hair off-center to create the illusion of length
  3. Add layers or texture to the hair to create height and movement
  4. Create a side-swept bang to draw attention away from the width of the face
  5. Avoid blunt cuts and straight lines, as they can add width to the face

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to shape hair for a round face:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add height to the hair
  • Avoid center parts, as they can make the face appear wider
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you
  • Ask your hairstylist for recommendations on styles that flatter your face shape

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaping hair for a round face:

Q: Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair straight, but it’s important to add volume and height to the hair to create the illusion of length.

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face?

A: The best haircut for a round face is one that adds height and length to the hair, such as long layers or an asymmetrical cut.

Hair Care for Round Face Shape

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
  • Get regular trims to keep the ends of your hair healthy

With these tips and tricks, you can shape your hair to flatter your round face shape and feel confident in your hairstyle.
