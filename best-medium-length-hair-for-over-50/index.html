Best Medium Length Hair For Over 50

Written by Eveline Nov 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we age, our hair tends to get thinner and loses its luster. However, this doesn't mean that you can't still have beautiful hair. In fact, medium length hair is perfect for women over 50 as it is easy to maintain and style. In this article, we will discuss the best medium length hair for over 50 and how to achieve it.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Achieve the Best Medium Length Hair for Over 50
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Hair
  5. Frequently Asked Questions
  6. Hair Care Products to Consider

Description

Medium length hair is generally considered to be hair that falls between the chin and the shoulders. This length is perfect for women over 50 as it is easy to style and maintain. The best medium length hair for over 50 is one that is layered, has texture, and is easy to style.

How to Achieve the Best Medium Length Hair for Over 50

To achieve the best medium length hair for over 50, you need to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Layers are essential for creating volume and movement in medium length hair. A long, side-swept fringe can also help to frame your face and create a youthful look. If you have thinning hair, consider adding some texture with a choppy cut or beachy waves.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the best medium length hair for over 50:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, lifting at the roots to create volume.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to add some soft waves. Alternatively, you can use a straightener to create loose curls.
  4. Finish with a texturizing spray to add some texture and hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium length hair:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium length hair for women over 50:

  • What is the best hairstyle for women over 50 with thinning hair?
    The best hairstyle for women over 50 with thinning hair is one that is layered and has texture. A choppy cut or beachy waves can also add volume and movement to thinning hair.
  • Can women over 50 still have long hair?
    Yes, women over 50 can still have long hair. However, it is important to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained with regular trims and deep conditioning treatments.
  • Do I need to use special products for my medium length hair?
    It is recommended that you use products that are specifically designed for your hair type and texture. This will help to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care Products to Consider

Here are some hair care products to consider for maintaining your medium length hair:

  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Texturizing spray
  • Deep conditioning treatment
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hair serum

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve the best medium length hair for over 50. Remember to embrace your natural texture and personal style, and have fun experimenting with different looks.
