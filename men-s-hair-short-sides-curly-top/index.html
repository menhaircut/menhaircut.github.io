Men s Hair Short Sides Curly Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish hairstyle, the men's hair short sides curly top is definitely one to consider. This versatile hairstyle can work for any occasion, from casual outings to formal events. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about this hairstyle, from how to get it to how to maintain it.

Description

The men's hair short sides curly top is a hairstyle where the sides and back of the head are cut short, while the top is left longer and curly. This hairstyle is also known as the curly undercut, as it combines the classic undercut with curly hair on top. The result is a stylish and unique look that's perfect for any man who wants to stand out.

How to

To get the men's hair short sides curly top, you'll need to visit your hair stylist and ask for an undercut with the top left long and curly. The stylist will use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair short, leaving the top longer. You can then style the top using a curling iron or hair products to enhance the curls and create a messy, textured look.

Step by Step

1. Visit your hair stylist and ask for an undercut with the top left long and curly.

2. Use clippers to cut the sides and back of your hair short.

3. Style the top using a curling iron or hair products to enhance the curls and create a messy, textured look.

Tips

1. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Use a curling iron or hair products to enhance the curls and create a messy, textured look.

3. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

FAQ

1. Is the men's hair short sides curly top suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this hairstyle can work for any hair type, whether you have straight, wavy, or curly hair.

2. How often do I need to get my hair trimmed?

You should get your hair trimmed every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

3. What hair products should I use to style my hair?

You can use a curl-enhancing cream, pomade, or hairspray to style your hair and enhance the curls.

Hair Care

To keep your men's hair short sides curly top looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage the curls.

3. Consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the men's hair short sides curly top with confidence and style. So why not give it a try today?
