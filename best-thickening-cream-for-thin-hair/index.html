Best Thickening Cream For Thin Hair Boost Your Hair Volume

Written by Robby Jun 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having thin hair that lacks volume and life? Well, don’t worry because there is a solution to your problem. The best thickening cream for thin hair can help you achieve the hair volume you’ve always wanted. In this article, we’ll go through the best thickening creams for thin hair and how to use them.

What is Thickening Cream?

Thickening cream is a hair product that helps to add volume and thickness to your hair. It contains ingredients that coat each strand of hair, making it appear thicker and fuller. It’s a great solution for people with thin hair who want to achieve a voluminous and bouncy look.

How to Choose the Best Thickening Cream for Thin Hair

Choosing the best thickening cream for thin hair can be overwhelming, especially with so many options available in the market. Here are some factors to consider when choosing a thickening cream:

Ingredients

The ingredients in the thickening cream should be suitable for your hair type. For example, if you have dry hair, look for a product that contains moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter.

Texture

The texture of the cream should be lightweight and non-greasy. A heavy cream can weigh down your hair, making it appear flat and lifeless.

Price

Thickening creams are available at different price points. Choose a product that fits your budget and delivers the desired results.

How to Use Thickening Cream for Thin Hair

Using thickening cream is easy and straightforward. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use it:

Step 1:

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Step 2:

Towel dry your hair until it’s slightly damp.

Step 3:

Take a small amount of thickening cream and apply it evenly to your hair, starting from the roots to the ends.

Step 4:

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and bounce.

Tips for Using Thickening Cream

Here are some tips to help you use thickening cream for thin hair:

Use a small amount

A little goes a long way when it comes to thickening cream. Use a small amount to avoid weighing down your hair.

Don’t apply it to the scalp

Applying thickening cream to the scalp can make your hair look greasy and flat. Apply it from mid-length to the ends of your hair.

Use it with other volumizing products

Using thickening cream with other volumizing products like mousse or hairspray can enhance the results.

FAQ About Thickening Cream for Thin Hair

Q: Will thickening cream make my hair look greasy?

A: No, if you use the right amount and apply it correctly, it won’t make your hair look greasy.

Q: Can I use thickening cream every day?

A: Yes, you can use it every day. However, it’s essential to use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any product buildup.

Q: Can I use thickening cream on colored hair?

A: Yes, you can use it on colored hair. However, it’s essential to choose a product that’s safe for color-treated hair.

Hair Care for Thin Hair

Aside from using thickening cream, here are some hair care tips for thin hair:

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it appear even thinner. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and avoid high temperatures.

Don’t Over-Wash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it look dull and lifeless. Wash your hair every other day or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your hair look thinner.

Conclusion

Thickening cream is an excellent solution for thin hair. It can help add volume and thickness to your hair, making it appear fuller and bouncier. When choosing a thickening cream, consider the ingredients, texture, and price. Remember to use a small amount and apply it correctly. With the right hair care routine and the best thickening cream for thin hair, you can achieve the hair volume you’ve always wanted.
