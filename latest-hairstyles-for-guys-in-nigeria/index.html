Latest Hairstyles For Guys In Nigeria A Guide To The Trendiest Cuts In 2023

Written by Frank Aug 21, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you looking for a fresh new hairstyle to try out this year? Look no further than Nigeria, where men's hairstyles are constantly evolving and pushing the boundaries. From bold buzz cuts to modern twists on classic cuts, there's something for every guy to try. In this guide, we'll take a look at the latest hairstyles for guys in Nigeria, including how to achieve them and tips for styling and maintaining them.

Table of Contents

  • Fade Cut
  • Afro Twist
  • Textured Crop
  • Dreadlocks

Fade Cut

The fade cut has been a popular hairstyle for guys in Nigeria for several years now, and it shows no sign of slowing down. This cut involves gradually tapering the hair from long at the top to short at the sides and back. The result is a clean, sharp look that works well with any face shape or hair texture.

To achieve a fade cut, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in this technique. They'll use clippers and scissors to create a gradual transition between the different lengths of hair. You can choose to keep the top long and styled, or go for a shorter, more low-maintenance look.

When styling a fade cut, use a matte pomade or wax to add texture and definition to the hair. Avoid using too much product, as this can make the hair look greasy or weighed down.

Tip:

Make sure to get regular trims to maintain the shape of your fade cut. This will prevent the hair from growing out too much and losing its sharp, clean look.

Afro Twist

The afro twist is a classic Nigerian hairstyle that has been reinvented in recent years with modern twists. This style involves growing out the hair into an afro and then twisting small sections of hair into defined coils. The result is a unique, textured look that's perfect for guys who want to stand out.

To achieve an afro twist, you'll need to have at least 4-5 inches of hair length. Use a curl-defining cream or gel to define the twists and help them hold their shape. You can choose to keep the twists small and tight, or go for a looser, more casual look.

When styling an afro twist, avoid using heavy products that can weigh down the hair. Instead, opt for lightweight creams or oils that will add moisture and shine without making the hair look greasy or weighed down.

Tip:

To keep your afro twist looking fresh, use a satin or silk pillowcase when you sleep. This will prevent the hair from getting tangled or frizzy.

Textured Crop

The textured crop is a versatile, modern haircut that's great for guys who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look. This cut involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, and leaving it slightly longer on top. The top is then textured with scissors or a razor to create a messy, effortless look.

To achieve a textured crop, ask your barber to cut the hair to around 1-2 inches on the sides and back, and leave it around 2-3 inches on top. They'll then use scissors or a razor to add texture and movement to the hair. You can choose to style the hair with a messy, tousled look, or go for a more sleek, refined style.

When styling a textured crop, use a lightweight pomade or wax to add texture and hold to the hair. Avoid using heavy products, as this can make the hair look greasy or weighed down.

Tip:

Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from styling products. This will keep your hair looking and feeling fresh.

Dreadlocks

Dreadlocks are a bold, statement-making hairstyle that's been popular in Nigeria for decades. This style involves growing out the hair and then twisting it into long, rope-like strands. Dreadlocks can be styled in a variety of ways, from long and loose to short and tight.

To achieve dreadlocks, you'll need to have at least 6-8 inches of hair length. You can start the locks yourself by sectioning off the hair and twisting it with beeswax or a locking gel, or you can go to a stylist who specializes in dreadlocks. It's important to maintain your dreadlocks by washing them regularly and retwisting them every few weeks to keep them looking neat.

When styling dreadlocks, use a lightweight oil or serum to add shine and moisture to the hair. Avoid using heavy products, as this can make the locks look dull or greasy.

Tip:

Protect your dreadlocks from damage by wearing a silk or satin scarf when you sleep. This will prevent the hair from rubbing against rough cotton pillowcases, which can cause breakage and frizz.

Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a modern take on the classic mohawk, and it's a great option for guys who want a bold, edgy look without committing to a full mohawk. This style involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, and leaving a strip of longer hair down the center of the head. The longer hair is then styled upwards to create a "hawk" effect.

To achieve a faux hawk, ask your barber to cut the hair to around 1-2 inches on the sides and back, and leave around 3-4 inches on top. Use a strong-hold gel or wax to style the hair upwards in the center, and use your fingers to create texture and movement.

When styling a faux hawk, avoid using too much product, as this can make the hair look greasy or weighed down. Instead, use a small amount of product and work it through the hair evenly.

Tip:

Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle the hair before styling. This will prevent breakage and make the hair easier to work with.

Hair Care Tips for Guys

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons, as these can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best hairstyle for guys with thinning hair?

A good option for guys with thinning hair is a short, textured crop. This style adds volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and thicker. You can also try using a volumizing shampoo and styling the hair with a lightweight mousse or wax.

How do I choose the right hairstyle for my face shape?

The best way to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape is to consult with a stylist. They can recommend cuts and styles that will complement your features and enhance your natural beauty. However, as a general rule, guys with round faces should go for haircuts that add height and length, while guys with square faces should go for haircuts that soften the angles of the face.

Can I style my hair differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! Your hairstyle can help you express your personality and individuality, so feel free to experiment with different looks for different occasions. For example, you might go for a sleek, refined style for a formal event, and a messy, tousled look for a casual night out with friends.

With so many great hairstyles to choose from, there's no reason why you can't find a look that works for you. Whether you're going for a bold, edgy style or a classic, refined look, the latest hairstyles for guys in Nigeria offer plenty of inspiration and options. So go ahead and experiment – you might just find your new signature look!
