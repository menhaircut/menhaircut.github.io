How To Trim Hair With Thinning Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having thick, bulky hair that is difficult to manage? Using thinning scissors can help you achieve a sleek, layered look that is easy to style. In this article, we will guide you through the process of trimming your hair with thinning scissors.

Description

Thinning scissors are a special type of haircutting tool that are designed to remove bulk from the hair. They have two blades that are serrated, which allows them to cut some hairs while leaving others intact. This creates a textured, layered effect that is ideal for people with thick hair.

How to Trim Hair with Thinning Scissors

Before you start trimming your hair with thinning scissors, you should make sure that you have the right tools. You will need a pair of thinning scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Dry your hair using a towel or a hair dryer until it is completely dry.

Step 2: Comb your hair

Using a comb, divide your hair into sections. This will make it easier to trim your hair with thinning scissors.

Step 3: Start trimming

Hold the thinning scissors in your dominant hand and use your other hand to hold the section of hair that you want to trim. Starting at the top of the section, make small cuts with the thinning scissors, working your way down to the bottom of the section.

Step 4: Comb the section of hair

After you have made a few cuts with the thinning scissors, comb the section of hair to see how it looks. If you want to remove more bulk, make more cuts with the thinning scissors.

Step 5: Repeat

Repeat steps 3 and 4 with the remaining sections of hair.

Tips

  • Start with a small section of hair and work slowly so that you don't remove too much hair at once.
  • Use the thinning scissors sparingly, as they can easily create an uneven, choppy look if overused.
  • Make sure that you are cutting the hair at an angle, rather than straight across, to create a layered effect.
  • Be careful not to cut your fingers with the thinning scissors, as they are very sharp.

FAQ

Can I use thinning scissors on wet hair?

No, it is best to use thinning scissors on dry hair, as wet hair can be more difficult to cut and may result in an uneven look.

How often should I trim my hair with thinning scissors?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, it is recommended that you trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and manageable.

Can I use thinning scissors on curly hair?

Yes, thinning scissors can be used on curly hair to remove bulk and create a more defined, layered look. However, it is important to be careful not to cut too much hair, as this can result in an uneven, choppy look.

Hair Care

In order to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it is important to take good care of it. This includes washing your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner, using heat protectant sprays when styling with heat tools, and avoiding excessive heat and chemical treatments. Additionally, eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help to promote hair growth and strength.

By following these tips and using thinning scissors to trim your hair, you can achieve a sleek, layered look that is easy to manage and style.
