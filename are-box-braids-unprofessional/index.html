Are Box Braids Unprofessional Debunking The Myth In 2023

Written by Daniel Jul 05, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're a black woman, you've probably heard the phrase "box braids are unprofessional" at some point in your life. This myth has been perpetuated for years, but in 2023, it's time to debunk it once and for all.

What Are Box Braids?

Box braids are a protective hairstyle that involves sectioning the hair into small, square-shaped parts and braiding each section from the root to the tip. This style can be done with or without extensions, and it's popular among black women because it's low-maintenance and can last for several weeks.

Why Are Box Braids Considered Unprofessional?

Box braids have been deemed unprofessional because they're seen as "too ethnic" or "too black" for the workplace. This stereotype is rooted in racism and discrimination, and it's time to break free from it.

Are Box Braids Actually Unprofessional?

No, box braids are not unprofessional. In fact, they can be a great choice for the workplace because they're low-maintenance and can be styled in a variety of ways. As long as your hair is neat and tidy, there's no reason why box braids should be considered unprofessional.

How to Style Box Braids Professionally

Here are some tips for styling your box braids professionally:

1. Keep Them Neat

Make sure your braids are neat and tidy. If any braids are sticking out or frizzy, use a small amount of gel or edge control to smooth them down.

2. Experiment with Updos

Box braids can be styled in a variety of updos, from buns to braided crowns. Experiment with different styles to find one that works for you.

3. Accessorize Strategically

Add a headband or hair clip to your braids to give them a professional touch. Just make sure the accessories are subtle and not too flashy.

FAQ About Box Braids in the Workplace

Q: Can I Wear Box Braids to a Job Interview?

A: Yes, you can wear box braids to a job interview. Just make sure they're neat and tidy.

Q: Will Wearing Box Braids Hurt My Chances of Getting a Job?

A: It shouldn't. Your skills and qualifications are what matter most in a job interview, not your hairstyle.

Q: Can I Wear Box Braids to a Conservative Workplace?

A: Yes, you can wear box braids to a conservative workplace. Just make sure they're neat and tidy, and consider styling them in a more traditional updo.

How to Take Care of Your Box Braids

Here are some tips for taking care of your box braids:

1. Keep Your Scalp Clean

Wash your scalp regularly with a gentle shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.

2. Moisturize Your Braids

Apply a lightweight oil or leave-in conditioner to your braids to keep them moisturized.

3. Protect Your Braids at Night

Wrap your braids in a silk scarf or bonnet at night to prevent friction and keep your braids looking fresh.

Conclusion

In 2023, it's time to break free from the myth that box braids are unprofessional. As long as your hair is neat and tidy, there's no reason why you can't wear this protective style to the workplace. So go ahead, rock those box braids with confidence!
