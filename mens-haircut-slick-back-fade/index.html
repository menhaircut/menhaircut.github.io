Mens Haircut Slick Back Fade The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that is both stylish and versatile, the slick back fade is a great choice. This classic men's haircut has been around for decades and continues to be popular today. In this guide, we'll take a closer look at the slick back fade and provide you with a step-by-step tutorial on how to achieve this look.

Description

The slick back fade is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides that gradually fades into longer hair on the top. The hair on top is then slicked back, creating a clean and polished look. This hairstyle can be worn by men of all ages and is suitable for a variety of occasions, from a day at the office to a night out on the town.

How To

Step 1: Choose Your Length

The first step in achieving a slick back fade is to decide on the length of your hair. The length of the sides and the top will depend on your personal preference and the shape of your face. If you have a round face, you may want to keep the sides shorter to create a more angular look. If you have a long face, you may want to keep the sides longer to balance out your features.

Step 2: Cut The Sides

Once you have decided on the length of your hair, it's time to cut the sides. The sides should be cut short, gradually fading into the longer hair on top. This can be done using clippers or scissors, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Cut The Top

After the sides have been cut, it's time to cut the top. The length of the top will depend on your personal preference, but it should be long enough to slick back. It's important to cut the top evenly to ensure a polished look.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair has been cut, it's time to style it. Start by applying a small amount of pomade or gel to your hair. Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting at the front and working your way back. Be sure to smooth out any bumps or stray hairs for a polished look.

Tips

  • Use a high-quality pomade or gel to ensure your hair stays in place.
  • If you have thick hair, you may want to blow dry it before applying product.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different lengths and styles to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy.

Q: Can I achieve a slick back fade with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve a slick back fade with curly hair. However, it may require more product and styling to keep your hair in place.

Q: What face shape is best suited for a slick back fade?

A: The slick back fade is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn by men of all face shapes. However, it's best suited for those with a square or oval face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it's important to take care of it. This includes washing it regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools, and avoiding harsh chemicals and treatments.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a slick back fade that is both stylish and easy to maintain. Whether you're heading to the office or a night out on the town, this classic men's haircut is sure to turn heads.
