How To Give Myself A Haircut Black Male A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jun 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money and time at the barbershop? Learning how to give yourself a haircut as a black male can save you both. Plus, you can customize your hairstyle to your preferences. In this article, we’ll provide a step-by-step guide on how to give yourself a haircut as a black male, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Give Myself a Haircut Black Male

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, it’s important to gather the right tools. You’ll need clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want a spray bottle with water to dampen your hair and a towel to clean up any hair mess.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let’s get into the details of giving yourself a haircut as a black male:

  1. Start with a high guard clipper attachment to trim your hair to your desired length. Use a mirror to see the back of your head and make sure to go over your entire head to ensure an even cut.
  2. Next, switch to a lower guard clipper attachment and trim the sides and back of your head. You can create a fade by gradually moving to a lower guard attachment as you go towards the bottom of your head.
  3. Use scissors to trim the top of your head to your desired length. Use a comb to pull your hair up and trim it evenly.
  4. Trim your sideburns and the hair around your ears with the clippers or scissors.
  5. Use the clippers to create a clean line around your neck and sideburns. You can also use a razor to create a sharper line.
  6. Check for any missed spots and make sure your hair is even all around.
  7. Style your hair as desired with products like hair gel or wax.

Tips for Giving Yourself a Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind as you give yourself a haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard attachment and gradually work your way to a shorter one to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a comb to make sure your hair is tangle-free before cutting.
  • Don’t rush the process – take your time to ensure an even cut.
  • Be cautious when using scissors near your scalp to avoid accidentally cutting your skin.
  • Watch tutorial videos or ask a friend for help if you’re unsure about any steps.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about giving yourself a haircut:

Q: How often should I give myself a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair growth and desired hairstyle. Generally, every 2-4 weeks is recommended.

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

A: It’s best to use hair cutting scissors, as they are sharper and designed for cutting hair.

Q: Should I cut my hair wet or dry?

A: It’s easier to cut hair when it’s wet, as it’s more manageable and easier to comb through. However, make sure to dry your hair before cutting to avoid any uneven cuts.

Hair Care After a Haircut

After giving yourself a haircut, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that works for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or blow dryers too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use hair oils or leave-in conditioners to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain its shape.

With this step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice, you’re ready to give yourself a haircut as a black male. Practice makes perfect, so don’t be discouraged if your first attempt doesn’t turn out perfectly. With time and patience, you’ll be a pro at giving yourself a fresh haircut.
