Which Hair Colour Suits Medium Complexion

Written by Robby Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a medium complexion and are thinking about changing your hair colour, there are many options available. However, choosing the right hair colour can be overwhelming, especially if you are not sure which colours will suit your skin tone. In this article, we will guide you on which hair colour suits medium complexion and provide you with tips on how to maintain your coloured hair.

Description

Medium complexion is a skin tone that falls between fair and dark. It is characterized by warm undertones and a natural glow. When it comes to choosing the right hair colour, it is essential to consider your skin tone to ensure that your hair complements your complexion. The right hair colour can enhance your natural features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour for Medium Complexion

Choosing the right hair colour for medium complexion can be challenging, but there are some factors to consider to help you make the right decision. Firstly, consider the undertones of your skin, whether it is warm, cool, or neutral. Warm undertones go well with golden, caramel, and honey colours, while cool undertones complement ash, platinum, and cool brown shades. Neutral undertones can go well with any hair colour. Secondly, consider your eye colour, as it can help you determine the best hair colour to enhance your natural features. Finally, consider your lifestyle and personality, as it can influence your hair colour decisions.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the right hair colour for medium complexion:

  1. Consider your skin undertones.
  2. Identify your eye colour.
  3. Consider your lifestyle and personality.
  4. Research different hair colours that suit medium complexion.
  5. Consult with a professional hair stylist.
  6. Try a temporary hair colour before committing to a permanent one.

Tips on Maintaining Coloured Hair

Maintaining coloured hair is essential to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips on how to maintain your coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as it can strip the colour.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage coloured hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair colour for medium complexion:

  1. Can I dye my hair if I have a medium complexion?
    Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a medium complexion. However, it is essential to choose the right hair colour that complements your skin tone.
  2. What are the best hair colours for medium complexion?
    Some of the best hair colours for medium complexion include golden, caramel, honey, ash, platinum, and cool brown shades.
  3. How often should I touch up my coloured hair?
    It depends on the colour and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you should touch up your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the colour.

Hair Care for Coloured Hair

Coloured hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for coloured hair:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as it can strip the colour.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage coloured hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

Choosing the right hair colour for medium complexion can be challenging, but it is essential to consider your skin undertones, eye colour, lifestyle, and personality when making your decision. Consult with a professional hair stylist if you are unsure, and remember to maintain your coloured hair by using colour-safe hair products and avoiding heat styling tools.
