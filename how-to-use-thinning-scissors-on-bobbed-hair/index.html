How To Use Thinning Scissors On Bobbed Hair

Are you looking to add some texture to your bobbed haircut? Thinning scissors might be the solution you need. Thinning scissors are a great tool to use when you want to remove some bulk from your hair without sacrificing length. In this article, we will guide you through the process of using thinning scissors on bobbed hair.

Description

Thinning scissors are a type of scissors that have a serrated edge, which creates small gaps in between the blades. These gaps help to remove bulk from the hair without changing the overall length. Thinning scissors are commonly used on thick hair, but they can also be used on bobbed hair to add texture and movement.

How to Use Thinning Scissors on Bobbed Hair

Before using thinning scissors on your bobbed hair, itâs important to make sure your hair is dry and tangle-free. Using thinning scissors on wet or tangled hair can lead to uneven results.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use hair clips to separate the top section of your hair from the bottom section.

Step 2: Hold the Scissors Correctly

Hold the thinning scissors with your dominant hand. Make sure the serrated edge is facing down towards your hair.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Start trimming your hair by holding a small section of hair with your non-dominant hand. Use the thinning scissors to make small cuts along the length of the hair.

Step 4: Check Your Progress

Check your progress regularly by releasing the section of hair and looking at the overall effect. Repeat the process as necessary, and continue working through each section of your hair until you achieve your desired look.

Tips for Using Thinning Scissors on Bobbed Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when using thinning scissors on bobbed hair:

  • Work slowly and carefully to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use a comb to help guide the thinning scissors through your hair.
  • Donât use thinning scissors too close to your roots, as this can lead to uneven results.
  • Remember to trim both sides of your hair evenly to achieve a balanced look.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about using thinning scissors on bobbed hair:

Can I use thinning scissors on all types of bobbed hair?

Yes, thinning scissors can be used on all types of bobbed hair, including blunt bobs and layered bobs.

How often should I use thinning scissors on my bobbed hair?

It depends on your hair type and desired look. If you have thick hair, you may need to use thinning scissors every few months to maintain your desired texture. If you have fine hair, you may only need to use thinning scissors once or twice a year.

Hair Care after Using Thinning Scissors on Bobbed Hair

After using thinning scissors on your bobbed hair, itâs important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and texture. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get a trim every few months to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.

Using thinning scissors on your bobbed hair can add texture and movement to your hairstyle. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a salon-quality look from the comfort of your own home.
