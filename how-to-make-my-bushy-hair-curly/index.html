How To Make My Bushy Hair Curly A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Sep 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're someone with bushy hair, you might feel like your hair is difficult to style. But did you know that it's actually possible to make your bushy hair curly? In this article, we'll be sharing all the tips and tricks you need to know to achieve beautiful, curly locks.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Make My Bushy Hair Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Make My Bushy Hair Curly:

There are a variety of methods you can use to make your bushy hair curly. Here are some of the most popular:

1. Use a Curling Iron:

If you're looking for quick, easy curls, a curling iron is the way to go. Simply section off your hair and wrap each section around the curling iron, holding for a few seconds before releasing.

2. Twist and Pin Method:

This method involves twisting small sections of hair and pinning them to your head with bobby pins. Leave them in for a few hours or overnight, and when you take them out, you'll have beautiful, natural-looking curls.

3. Braid Method:

Braiding your hair is another way to achieve curls. You can either braid your hair into one large braid or multiple small braids, leaving them in for a few hours or overnight before taking them out.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Now that you know the different methods, here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your bushy hair curly:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's specifically formulated for curly hair to help enhance your curls.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product, like a curl cream or mousse, to your hair to help define your curls and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Depending on the method you choose, use a curling iron, twist and pin, or braid your hair to achieve curls.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Set

Once you've curled your hair, let it set for a few hours or overnight so the curls can fully form.

Step 6: Add Finishing Touches

Once your curls have set, add any finishing touches you like, such as hairspray or a shine serum.

Tips for Curly Hair:

Here are some additional tips for maintaining and styling curly hair:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair:

Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so avoid washing it too frequently. Stick to washing your hair once or twice a week to help retain moisture.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb:

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to avoid damaging your curls or causing frizz.

3. Avoid Heat Styling:

Heat styling tools, like straighteners and blow dryers, can damage your curls and cause frizz. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.

FAQs:

Q: Can I make my bushy hair curly without heat?

A: Yes, you can use methods like the twist and pin method or braiding to achieve curls without using heat.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: Use a curl enhancing product and avoid over-washing your hair to help prevent frizz. You can also use a silk or satin pillowcase to help reduce friction and prevent frizz while you sleep.

Hair Care for Curly Hair:

Here are some additional tips for caring for your curly hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioner:

Use a deep conditioner once a week to help nourish and moisturize your curls.

2. Avoid Brushing Your Hair:

Brushing your hair can cause frizz and damage your curls. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture:

Don't be afraid to embrace your natural texture and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to make your bushy hair curly in no time. Remember to take good care of your hair and embrace your natural texture for beautiful, healthy curls.
