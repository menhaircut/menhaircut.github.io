What Hairstyles Suit Big Noses A Guide For 2023

Written by Bardi Oct 13, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, there are many factors to consider. One of the most important is the shape of your face, and if you have a big nose, it can be a challenge to find a style that flatters your features. In this guide, we'll explore what hairstyles suit big noses, including tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice to help you feel confident and stylish.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

When it comes to big noses, the key is to balance out your features. This means choosing a hairstyle that draws attention away from your nose and towards your other features, such as your eyes or cheekbones. Generally, you'll want to avoid styles that are too short or too long, as these can accentuate your nose. Instead, opt for a medium length style that frames your face.

How To

The first step in choosing a hairstyle for a big nose is to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and balances out your proportions. If you have a big nose, you'll likely have a long or oval face shape, which means you'll want to choose a style that adds width to your face.

Choose the Right Length

When it comes to length, a medium style is generally the most flattering for big noses. This means choosing a style that falls between your chin and shoulders. This length will help to balance out your features and draw attention away from your nose.

Try Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to draw attention away from your nose and towards your eyes. Opt for a side-swept style that falls just below your eyebrows, as this will help to frame your face and create balance.

Consider Layers

Layers can also be a great way to add volume and movement to your hair, which can help to balance out your features. Opt for long layers that start at your chin and fall towards your shoulders.

Tips

  • Choose a style that adds width to your face
  • Avoid styles that are too short or too long
  • Try bangs to draw attention to your eyes
  • Consider layers to add volume and movement
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a big nose?

You should generally avoid styles that are too short or too long, as these can accentuate your nose. Instead, opt for a medium length style that frames your face.

Can I still wear my hair up if I have a big nose?

Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a style that adds width to your face, such as a messy bun or a side-swept updo.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair, it's important to choose products that add volume and body to your locks. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and consider adding a volumizing mousse or spray to your styling routine. Additionally, make sure to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing a flattering hairstyle, you can feel confident and stylish no matter the size of your nose. Remember, it's all about balance and drawing attention to your best features!
