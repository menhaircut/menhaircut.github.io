How To Style Your Own Short Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jun 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Short hair can be a lot of fun to style, but it can also be challenging to figure out how to make the most of your locks. Whether you're looking for a sleek and polished look or something more casual and messy, there are plenty of options for styling short hair. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about how to style your own short hair, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair for a Sleek Look
  • How to Style Short Hair for a Messy Look
  • How to Style Short Hair with Accessories
  • How to Care for Your Short Hair
  • FAQs About Styling Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style for You

Before you even start thinking about how to style your short hair, you need to choose the right style for your face shape and hair type. Some popular short hair styles include bobs, pixie cuts, and shags, but there are plenty of other options out there as well. Consider consulting with a stylist to help you choose a style that will flatter your features and suit your lifestyle.

How to Style Short Hair for a Sleek Look

If you're going for a polished and professional look, there are several ways to style your short hair. One popular option is to use a flat iron to straighten your hair, and then use a pomade or wax to add shine and hold. You can also try blow-drying your hair with a round brush to create volume and smoothness.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair.
  3. Section your hair and use a flat iron to straighten it.
  4. Apply a small amount of pomade or wax to your fingertips.
  5. Rub your fingers together to distribute the product.
  6. Smooth your hair down with your hands, using the product to add shine and hold.

How to Style Short Hair for a Messy Look

If you prefer a more laid-back and casual look, there are plenty of ways to style your short hair for a messy and tousled effect. One option is to use a texturizing spray or salt spray to add volume and texture, and then use your fingers to create a messy, bedhead look.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or salt spray to your hair.
  3. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create texture.
  4. Flip your head upside down and shake your hair out.
  5. Use your fingers to create additional volume and texture as desired.

How to Style Short Hair with Accessories

Short hair can be a lot of fun to accessorize, whether you're using hair clips, headbands, or other hair accessories. One popular option is to use a scarf or bandana to create a retro-inspired look. Simply tie the scarf around your head, leaving the ends loose, and tuck your hair in underneath.

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Choose a scarf or bandana that you like.
  2. Fold the scarf in half to create a triangle shape.
  3. Place the scarf at the back of your head, with the point facing down.
  4. Tie the two ends of the scarf together at the top of your head.
  5. Tuck your hair in underneath the scarf.

How to Care for Your Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to look its best. Be sure to trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your style looking fresh. You should also use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for short hair, and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair to prevent damage.

FAQs About Styling Short Hair

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but generally you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with short hair. Simply gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie or clip.

Q: What should I do if my short hair is too curly or frizzy?

A: Consider using a smoothing serum or hair oil to tame frizz and add shine. You can also try blow-drying your hair with a diffuser attachment to create soft, defined curls.

Q: How can I add volume to my short hair?

A: Use a volumizing mousse or spray on damp hair, and then blow-dry your hair with a round brush to create lift and volume.

Q: Can I still wear a hair part with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear a hair part with short hair. Simply use a comb or your fingers to create a part in your hair, and style as desired.

Conclusion

Short hair can be a lot of fun to style, whether you're going for a sleek and polished look or something more casual and messy. By choosing the right style for your face shape and hair type, using the right products and tools, and taking good care of your hair, you can create a look that is uniquely you.
