How To Make Long Face Look Wider Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long face, you may feel self-conscious about it. But did you know that there are simple ways to make your face look wider? In this article, we will discuss how to make long face look wider with some easy tips and tricks.

Description

A long face can be defined as a face that is longer than it is wide. People with long faces often have prominent foreheads, narrow cheeks, and a pointed chin. While long faces can be beautiful, some people feel that their long face makes them look unbalanced or unattractive. Fortunately, there are a few easy ways to make your long face look wider.

How to Make Long Face Look Wider: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Hairstyle

One of the easiest ways to make your long face look wider is to choose the right hairstyle. A layered haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face can help balance out your features. Avoid long, straight hairstyles that can make your face look even longer.

Step 2: Use Contouring Makeup

If you want to make your long face look wider, contouring makeup can be your best friend. By using darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to highlight certain areas, you can create the illusion of a wider face. Focus on the sides of your face, the area under your cheekbones, and your temples.

Step 3: Wear Statement Earrings

Statement earrings can draw attention to the sides of your face and make it look wider. Choose earrings that are bold and colorful, or ones that have a unique shape. Avoid small, dainty earrings that can get lost on your face.

Tips for Making Your Long Face Look Wider

Here are some additional tips for making your long face look wider:

  • Wear glasses with a wide frame
  • Choose clothing with horizontal stripes or patterns
  • Avoid wearing your hair in a center part
  • Use bronzer to add warmth to your face
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and makeup looks to find what works best for you

FAQ

Q: Are there any hairstyles that I should avoid if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you should avoid long, straight hairstyles that can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for layered haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face.

Q: Can contouring makeup really make my face look wider?

A: Yes, contouring makeup can help create the illusion of a wider face by using darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to highlight certain areas.

Q: What kind of earrings should I wear to make my face look wider?

A: Statement earrings that are bold and colorful or have a unique shape can draw attention to the sides of your face and make it look wider.

Hair Care for Long Faces

While there are many ways to make your long face look wider, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type, and avoid heat styling tools as much as possible. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your long face look wider and feel more confident in your appearance.
