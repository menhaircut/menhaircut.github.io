Does Middle Parting Suit Long Faces

Written by Frank Jul 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long face shape, you might be wondering whether a middle parting will suit you. After all, it's a hairstyle that's been trending for a while now, with many celebrities and influencers sporting the look. But before you make the switch, it's essential to consider whether it's the right choice for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A middle parting can elongate your face shape, which might not be the most flattering look for those with long faces. However, there are ways to make the middle parting work for you, such as adjusting the length of your hair and the placement of the parting.

How to

When it comes to deciding on whether a middle parting suits a long face, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, the length of your hair can make a big difference. If you have long hair, a middle parting might not be the best option as it can make your face look even longer. Instead, consider a side parting or a layered haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face.

Secondly, the placement of the parting is crucial. If you have a high forehead, a middle parting can work well to balance out your features. However, if you have a low hairline, a middle parting can make your face look longer. In this case, a side parting might be a better option.

Step by step

To create a middle parting, start by dividing your hair into two equal sections. Use a comb to create a straight line from your forehead to the back of your head. Make sure the parting is centered, and use clips to keep the two sections of hair separate.

Once you have your parting in place, you can style your hair as desired. Straightening your hair can help to create a sleek and polished look, while adding waves or curls can add volume and texture.

Tips

  • Experiment with different parting placements to find the most flattering look for your face shape.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add volume to the sides of your face.
  • Use heat protectant products when styling your hair to prevent damage.

FAQ

Q. Can a middle parting work for all face shapes?

A. While a middle parting can work for many face shapes, it's not always the most flattering look for everyone. It's essential to consider your individual features and experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

Q. Will a middle parting make my face look longer?

A. A middle parting can elongate your face shape, especially if you have long hair. However, there are ways to make the look work for you, such as adjusting the length of your hair and the placement of the parting.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you choose a middle parting or a different hairstyle, taking care of your hair is essential for maintaining healthy and shiny locks. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type, and consider using a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair nourished and hydrated.

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy. If you're considering a drastic hairstyle change, it's always a good idea to consult with a hairstylist who can advise you on the best options for your face shape and hair type.
