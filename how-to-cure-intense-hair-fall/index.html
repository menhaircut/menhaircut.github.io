How To Cure Intense Hair Fall Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Hair fall is a common problem that affects millions of people worldwide. While some hair fall is natural, it can be a cause for concern if it is excessive. Intense hair fall can affect your overall appearance, confidence, and self-esteem.

If you're experiencing intense hair fall, don't worry – you're not alone. There are several ways to cure hair fall and regrow your hair. In this article, we'll discuss how to cure intense hair fall and provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve healthy hair.

How to Cure Intense Hair Fall

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, it's important to understand the root cause of your hair fall. Here are some of the most common causes of hair fall:

  • Hormonal changes
  • Nutritional deficiencies
  • Stress
  • Genetics
  • Medical conditions
  • Environmental factors

If you're unsure about the cause of your hair fall, it's best to consult a dermatologist. They can help identify the root cause and suggest the best course of treatment.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cure Intense Hair Fall

Step 1: Follow a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and vitamin D. Some of the best foods for healthy hair include:

  • Spinach
  • Eggs
  • Nuts
  • Greek yogurt
  • Salmon

It's also important to stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.

Step 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, can damage your hair and lead to hair fall. Avoid using these tools as much as possible, or use them on a low heat setting.

Step 3: Practice Good Hair Care

Good hair care is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using harsh chemicals and products that can damage your hair.

Step 4: Try Home Remedies

There are several home remedies that can help cure hair fall, such as:

  • Aloe vera
  • Onion juice
  • Coconut oil
  • Green tea
  • Apple cider vinegar

These remedies can help promote healthy hair growth and reduce hair fall.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

In addition to the above steps, here are some tips to help prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles
  • Massage your scalp regularly
  • Use a wide-toothed comb
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and treatments
  • Protect your hair from the sun

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall. When you're stressed, your body releases hormones that can affect your hair growth cycle.

Q: Is it normal to lose hair every day?

A: Yes, it's normal to lose 50-100 hairs a day.

Q: Can hair fall be cured permanently?

A: It depends on the cause of your hair fall. In some cases, hair fall can be cured permanently with the right treatment.

Final Thoughts

Intense hair fall can be a cause for concern, but it's not something that can't be cured. By following a healthy diet, practicing good hair care, and trying home remedies, you can reduce hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. If you're still experiencing hair fall after trying these tips, it's best to consult a dermatologist.
