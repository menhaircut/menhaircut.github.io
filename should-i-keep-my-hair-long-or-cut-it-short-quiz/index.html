Should I Keep My Hair Long Or Cut It Short Quiz

Written by Daniel Jun 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hairstyle, you might be wondering if you should keep your hair long or cut it short. This can be a tough decision, but taking a quiz can help you figure out what style would suit you best. In this article, we'll provide a quiz to help you decide whether to keep your hair long or cut it short. We'll also provide tips for hair care and answer some frequently asked questions.

Quiz

Answer the following questions to find out whether you should keep your hair long or cut it short:

  1. What is your face shape?
  2. What is your hair texture?
  3. How much time do you want to spend styling your hair?
  4. What is your lifestyle like?
  5. What is your personal style?

Once you've answered these questions, tally up your answers and see which category you fall into:

  1. If you answered mostly A's, you should keep your hair long.
  2. If you answered mostly B's, you should cut your hair short.
  3. If you answered a mix of A's and B's, you could try a mid-length hairstyle.

How to Cut Your Hair Short

If you've decided to cut your hair short, it's important to do it correctly to avoid any mistakes. Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting your hair short:

  1. Start with dry, clean hair.
  2. Section your hair into 4 parts: top, left, right, and back.
  3. Begin cutting the back section, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up.
  4. Move on to the side sections, cutting at an angle to create a gradual taper.
  5. Cut the top section to your desired length, using the sides as a guide.
  6. Blend any harsh lines or uneven spots with scissors or clippers.
  7. Style as desired.

Tips for Hair Care

Whether you decide to keep your hair long or cut it short, taking care of your hair is important. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and nutrients.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking enough water.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about keeping your hair long or cutting it short:

Q: Will cutting my hair make it grow faster?

A: No, cutting your hair does not make it grow faster. Hair growth is determined by genetics and overall health.

Q: What should I do if I don't like my short haircut?

A: If you don't like your short haircut, try to style it in different ways or consider getting extensions until it grows out.

Q: Will cutting my hair short make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, cutting your hair short can make your face look slimmer, especially if you have a round face shape.

We hope this quiz and article helped you make a decision about whether to keep your hair long or cut it short. Remember to take care of your hair no matter what style you choose!
