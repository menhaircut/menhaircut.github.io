How Short Is A Number 3 Haircut In Mm

Written by Daniel Nov 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you wondering how short a number 3 haircut is in millimeters? Well, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll be discussing everything you need to know about a number 3 haircut, including its length in millimeters, how to achieve it, tips, and hair care.

Description

A number 3 haircut is a popular choice among men who want a short but not too short haircut. It’s also known as a “3 guard” or “3 clipper” haircut. The number 3 refers to the length of the guard on the clipper used to cut the hair. A number 3 guard is 3/8 inch or 9.5mm long.

How to Achieve a Number 3 Haircut

To achieve a number 3 haircut, you’ll need a set of clippers with a number 3 guard. If you’re doing it yourself, start by washing and drying your hair. Then, use the clippers to cut your hair, starting at the sides and working your way up to the top. Use the same guard length throughout the entire head, making sure to go over each section multiple times to ensure an even cut.

If you’re not confident in cutting your own hair, it’s best to go to a professional barber or hair stylist. They will be able to achieve the desired length and style more accurately.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving a number 3 haircut:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Attach the number 3 guard to your clippers.
  3. Start at the sides and work your way up to the top.
  4. Use the same guard length throughout the entire head.
  5. Go over each section multiple times to ensure an even cut.
  6. Blend the sides and the top using a higher guard length or scissors.
  7. Trim the edges and the back of the neck with a trimmer or scissors.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a number 3 haircut:

  • Communicate clearly with your barber or hair stylist to ensure they understand the length and style you want.
  • Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a number 3 haircut.
  • Maintain your haircut regularly to keep it looking fresh and neat.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find the right one for your hair type and style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about a number 3 haircut:

Is a number 3 haircut short?

Yes, a number 3 haircut is considered a short haircut.

How long is a number 3 haircut in mm?

A number 3 haircut is 9.5mm or 3/8 inch long.

What does a number 3 haircut look like?

A number 3 haircut leaves the hair short but not too short. It’s a popular choice among men who want a neat and tidy look.

Hair Care

To keep your number 3 haircut looking fresh and neat, it’s important to maintain good hair care habits. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner to keep it soft and smooth, and using a hair product to style it as desired. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it look dull and lifeless.

Now that you know how short a number 3 haircut is in mm, you can confidently ask for this popular hairstyle at your next barber or hair salon appointment. Remember to communicate clearly with your stylist to ensure you get the desired length and style.
