Which Hair Cut Is Best For Long Face A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles for your long face? Worry no more! We have compiled a complete guide to help you determine the best hair cut for your long face shape. Read on to learn more.

Description: Long Face Shape

Long faces have a length greater than their width, with a narrow forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. This face shape requires a hair cut that balances the length and creates an illusion of a wider face.

How to Determine the Best Hair Cut for Long Face?

The best hair cut for a long face depends on various factors, including hair texture, thickness, and personal style. However, there are some general guidelines to follow when choosing the best hair cut for a long face:

  • Choose haircuts that add width to your face
  • Avoid haircuts that make your face look longer
  • Opt for hairstyles that create volume at the sides
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek and straight
  • Choose haircuts that add layers to your hair

Step-by-Step Guide to Choose the Best Hair Cut for Long Face

Follow these steps to choose the best hair cut for your long face:

  1. Measure your face length and width using a measuring tape
  2. Determine your face shape
  3. Research and gather inspiration for haircuts that suit your face shape
  4. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your hair type and face shape
  5. Consider your lifestyle and maintenance requirements before finalizing your hair cut

Tips for Choosing the Best Hair Cut for Long Face

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing the best hair cut for your long face:

  • Avoid center partings as they make your face look longer
  • Opt for side-swept bangs to create an illusion of a wider forehead
  • Avoid blunt cuts as they accentuate the length of your face
  • Choose haircuts that end above or below your jawline to add width to your face
  • Consider adding highlights to your hair to create dimension and volume

FAQs

1. What is the best haircut for a long face with straight hair?

The best haircut for a long face with straight hair is a long bob or a shoulder-length cut with layers. This will add volume and create an illusion of width.

2. Can curly hair be worn by long face shape?

Yes, curly hair can be worn by long face shapes. Opt for layered haircuts that add volume and create a balance between length and width.

3. What is the best haircut for a long face with thin hair?

The best haircut for a long face with thin hair is a layered cut with a side-swept fringe. This will add volume and create an illusion of thickness.

Hair Care for Long Face

After choosing the best hair cut for your long face, it's essential to maintain healthy hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to prevent hair breakage

With these tips and guidelines, you can choose the best hair cut for your long face shape and maintain healthy hair. Remember to consult with your hairstylist for personalized advice and recommendations.
