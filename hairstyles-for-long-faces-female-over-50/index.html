Hairstyles For Long Faces Female Over 50

Written by Robby Jun 04, 2023 · 3 min read

If you are a woman over 50 with a long face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. You want to choose a style that flatters your face shape while also complementing your age. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for long faces female over 50. From short and sassy to long and elegant, we’ve got you covered.

Description

A long face is characterized by a lengthened shape that is longer than it is wide. Women over 50 with long faces tend to have high cheekbones and a narrow jawline. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to add width and volume to the sides of the face while minimizing the length.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face, consider the following:

  • Opt for styles that add width to the sides of the face
  • Avoid styles that are too long or straight
  • Choose styles that add volume to the crown of the head
  • Consider layers to add texture and movement

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the best hairstyles for long faces female over 50:

Short and Sassy Pixie Cut

  1. Start with a short pixie cut that is tapered at the back and sides.
  2. Add layers throughout the top of the head for volume and texture.
  3. Use a pomade or wax to create a messy, tousled look.

Medium Layered Bob

  1. Start with a chin-length bob that is layered throughout.
  2. Use a curling iron to add loose waves throughout the hair.
  3. Tease the crown of the head for added volume.

Long Layered Waves

  1. Start with long hair that is layered throughout.
  2. Use a curling iron to add loose waves throughout the hair.
  3. Tease the crown of the head for added volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a long face:

  • Choose styles that add volume to the crown of the head.
  • Avoid styles that are too long or straight.
  • Consider layers to add texture and movement.
  • Use styling products to add volume and texture.

FAQ

What hairstyles are best for long faces?

Hairstyles that add width and volume to the sides of the face are best for long faces. These can include short and sassy pixie cuts, medium layered bobs, and long layered waves.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight as they can elongate the face. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat as they can make the face appear even longer.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair and use a heat protectant spray when styling.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right hairstyle, you can look and feel your best at any age.

Now that you have some ideas for hairstyles for long faces female over 50, it’s time to book an appointment with your stylist and try out a new look!
