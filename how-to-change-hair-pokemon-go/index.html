How to Change Hair in Pokemon Go - Tips and Tricks

If you're tired of your character's hairstyle in Pokemon Go, don't worry! You can change it easily. In this article, we will guide you on how to change hair in Pokemon Go. We will also share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the desired look. Let's get started!

How to Change Hair in Pokemon Go

Changing hair in Pokemon Go is quite simple. All you need to do is follow these steps:

Step by Step Guide

  1. Open Pokemon Go app on your mobile device.
  2. Go to your character's profile page by tapping on the avatar icon at the bottom left corner of the screen.
  3. Tap on the edit icon (pencil icon) next to your character's name.
  4. Now, tap on the "Style" option.
  5. You will see various options for changing your character's appearance, including hairstyles.
  6. Select the hairstyle that you like and tap on "Okay".
  7. That's it! Your character's hairstyle will be changed.

Tips for Changing Hair in Pokemon Go

Here are some tips to help you change your character's hair in Pokemon Go:

  • Make sure you have a good internet connection while changing your character's hair. Slow internet may cause the process to take longer than usual.
  • Try different hairstyles to find the one that suits your character the most.
  • Don't forget to save your changes by tapping on "Okay".

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing hair in Pokemon Go:

  • Q: Can I change my character's hair for free?
    A: Yes, changing your character's hair in Pokemon Go is free of cost.
  • Q: Can I change my character's hair more than once?
    A: Yes, you can change your character's hair as many times as you want.
  • Q: Do I need to be at a particular level to change my character's hair?
    A: No, you can change your character's hair at any level.

Hair Care Tips

Changing your character's hair in Pokemon Go can be a fun experience, but it's important to take care of your own hair too. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as hair dryers and straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

We hope this article has been helpful in guiding you on how to change hair in Pokemon Go. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect one for your character. Happy gaming!
