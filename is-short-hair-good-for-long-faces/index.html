Is Short Hair Good For Long Faces

Written by William Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long face shape and looking for a hairstyle that can make it look better? Short hair could be the answer. But, the question is, is short hair good for long faces? Let's explore this topic and see if this is the right hairstyle for you.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Long faces are characterized by a high forehead, a long chin and a narrow face shape. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for long faces is to create width and balance in the face. Short hair is a great option because it can create the illusion of width and volume in the right areas of the face.

However, not all short hairstyles are suitable for long faces. It's important to consider the texture and thickness of your hair, as well as your personal style and preference. With the right short haircut, you can enhance your facial features and feel confident.

How to

If you have a long face shape and want to try a short haircut, here's what you need to do:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist: Find a professional hairstylist who can give you advice on the best short hairstyles for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Consider your lifestyle: Choose a short hairstyle that fits your lifestyle and daily routine. If you're always on the go, a low-maintenance hairstyle may be the best option.
  3. Experiment with different styles: Try different short hairstyles and see which one suits you best. You can also use online hair makeover tools to visualize how you would look with different hairstyles.
  4. Accessorize: Add accessories like headbands, hair clips or hats to enhance your short hairstyle and create a personalized look.

Step by step

Here are some steps to follow when getting a short haircut for a long face:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry naturally.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist on the best short haircut for your face shape.
  3. Section your hair and cut it gradually, leaving some length on top.
  4. Add layers to create volume and texture.
  5. Style your hair using a blow dryer, straightener or curling iron.
  6. Apply a styling product like mousse or hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a long face:

  • Choose a short hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  • Avoid short hairstyles that are too flat or straight, as they can elongate your face even more.
  • Consider adding bangs or layers to frame your face and create balance.
  • Experiment with different hair colors to enhance your facial features.

FAQ

Q: Will short hair make my face look wider?

A: Short hair can create the illusion of width in the right areas of the face, such as the sides and crown. It's important to choose a short hairstyle that adds volume and texture to these areas.

Q: Can I still style my short hair if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can still style your short hair in different ways, such as adding curls or waves, using hair accessories or creating a messy look. The key is to choose a short hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your features.

Hair Care

Short hair requires regular maintenance and care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.
  • Apply a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.

Now that you know more about short hair for long faces, it's time to take the plunge and try a new hairstyle. With the right short haircut and styling techniques, you can enhance your features and feel confident.
