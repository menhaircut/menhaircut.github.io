Does A Round Face Look Better With Long Or Short Hair

Written by Christine Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with a round face and confused about whether long or short hair would suit you better? You're not alone! Many people with a round face often wonder which hairstyle would flatter their features the most. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about whether a round face looks better with long or short hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of whether a round face looks better with long or short hair, let's first understand what a round face is. A round face has soft curves and a circular shape with the width and length almost equal. People with round faces often have fuller cheeks and a rounded chin, making their face appear wider than it is.

When it comes to hairstyles, people with round faces need to choose a hairstyle that can elongate their face and create an illusion of sharper features. Both long and short hair can do this, but it depends on the specific style and how it complements your face shape.

How to

The first step to figuring out whether long or short hair would look better on your round face is to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. If they're all roughly the same width, you have a round face.

Once you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose a hairstyle. Here are a few things to consider:

Face-framing Layers

Face-framing layers can work well on a round face as they create an illusion of length and add definition to your face. Opt for long layers if you have long hair or short layers if you have short hair.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help add angles to your face and make it appear slimmer. This is a great option for both long and short hair.

Chin-Length Bob

A chin-length bob can work wonders on a round face as it creates a vertical illusion and makes your face appear longer. This style is best for those with short hair.

Step by Step

Now that you know what to consider when choosing a hairstyle for your round face, here's a step-by-step guide to help you:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that complement round faces.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to determine what style would work best for you.
  4. Take into account your hair type and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle.
  5. Experiment with different styles and lengths until you find the one that makes you feel most confident.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for your round face:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to your face.
  • Avoid styles that add width to your face.
  • Play around with different partings to create angles on your face.
  • Consider adding volume to the crown of your head to elongate your face.

FAQ

Q: Can people with round faces pull off short hair?

A: Yes, people with round faces can definitely pull off short hair. It all depends on the specific style and how it complements your face shape.

Q: Can people with round faces have bangs?

A: Yes, people with round faces can have bangs. Side-swept bangs work best for round faces as they create angles and make your face appear slimmer.

Hair Care

No matter what hairstyle you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suited for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can tug on your hair and cause breakage.

In conclusion, whether a round face looks better with long or short hair depends on the specific style and how it complements your face shape. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
