Long Hairstyle For Square Face Male

Written by Christine Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square face, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your features. In this article, we will discuss long hairstyles that are perfect for square face males. We will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve these hairstyles, tips on how to maintain your hair, and answer frequently asked questions. So, let's dive in!

Table of Content

  • How to Achieve Long Hairstyle for Square Face Male
  • Tips on Maintaining Long Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Long Hairstyle

How to Achieve Long Hairstyle for Square Face Male

The first step in achieving a long hairstyle for square face male is to grow your hair out. If you already have long hair, you can skip to the next step.

Once your hair has grown out, you can begin trimming it to achieve the desired length. For square face males, we recommend keeping the length around the collarbone or longer. This length will help soften the angular features of your face.

Next, you can add layers to your hair to create movement and texture. Layers can also help frame your face and soften your features. Be sure to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best layering technique for your hair type and face shape.

Finally, you can add styling products to your hair to enhance the texture and hold of your hairstyle. We recommend using a texturizing spray or paste to create a messy, beachy look or a pomade for a sleek and polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Grow out your hair to the desired length.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best layering technique for your hair type and face shape.
  3. Add styling products to enhance the texture and hold of your hairstyle.

Tips on Maintaining Long Hair

Maintaining long hair requires a bit of effort, but it's worth it in the end. Here are some tips to help you keep your long hairstyle looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and smooth.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often. These can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best long hairstyles for square face males?

The best long hairstyles for square face males are those that add movement and texture to the hair. We recommend hairstyles with layers, such as the shag or the messy bob.

How often should I trim my hair?

We recommend trimming your hair every 8-12 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail?

Absolutely! Long hair can still be pulled back into a ponytail or bun. Just be sure to use a gentle hair tie and avoid pulling your hair too tightly.

Hair Care for Long Hairstyle

Long hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your long hairstyle:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair. Lukewarm water is best.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to hydrate and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products that can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a long hairstyle that complements your square face and reflects your personal style. Remember to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best hairstyle for you and to keep your hair healthy and vibrant with proper care.
