How to Cut Your Hair Medium Length: Step by Step Guide

If you're looking for a fresh new look, cutting your hair to a medium length is a great option. Here's a step by step guide on how to do it yourself at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Your Hair Medium Length
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Your Hair Medium Length
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Your Hair Medium Length

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to hold your hair in place.

Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry. Once it's dry, comb through it to get rid of any tangles.

Next, divide your hair into sections. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want your hair to be. A medium length haircut usually falls between the shoulders and the collarbone.

Start Cutting

Start by cutting the hair at the back of your head. Use your comb to hold the hair in place and cut it straight across.

Once you have cut the hair at the back, move on to the sides. Use your comb to hold the hair in place and cut it at an angle.

Finally, move on to the front of your head. Use your comb to hold the hair in place and cut it at an angle, following the shape of your face.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide to cutting your hair medium length:

  1. Wash your hair and comb it through to get rid of any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and use hair clips to hold each section in place.
  3. Decide on the length you want your hair to be.
  4. Start cutting the hair at the back of your head.
  5. Move on to the sides, cutting the hair at an angle.
  6. Finally, move on to the front of your head, cutting the hair at an angle to follow the shape of your face.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Medium Length

Here are some tips to help you get the perfect medium length haircut:

  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to start with a little and cut more if needed.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your hair can be tricky, but with practice, you'll get better.
  • Watch tutorial videos online to help you get the hang of it.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair medium length:

How often should I cut my hair?

You should aim to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair again or go to a professional hairdresser to fix it.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heated styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid tying your hair up too tightly, as this can cause breakage.

Now that you know how to cut your hair medium length, give it a try and see how it looks. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't forget to take good care of your hair afterwards.
