Short Haircuts For Thin Hair Fat Face Flattering Styles For 2023

Written by Daniel Dec 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face and thin hair, finding a flattering hairstyle can be a challenge. Lucky for you, we’ve researched and compiled the best short haircuts for thin hair fat face. Whether you want to add volume, length, or texture, we’ve got you covered with these trendy, low-maintenance styles that will flatter your face shape and make your hair look fuller.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Thin Hair Fat Face

Before we dive into the best short haircuts, it’s important to know what to look for when choosing a hairstyle for a round, chubby face and thin hair. Here are some tips:

1. Opt for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts are perfect for thin hair and round faces as they add volume and make your face appear slimmer. Chin-length bobs, pixie cuts, and layered cuts are great options that work well for this face shape.

2. Go for Layered Cuts

Layered haircuts are perfect for thin hair as they create the illusion of fullness and texture. Layers can be added to any short haircut to give your hair more volume and movement.

3. Add Bangs

Bangs are a great way to frame your face and take attention away from your round cheeks. Side-swept bangs or wispy bangs work well for this face shape and add texture to thin hair.

4. Avoid Straight Hair

Straight hair can make your face appear wider, so it’s best to avoid it. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and texture, like curly or wavy hair.

Step by Step: How to Get the Perfect Short Haircut for Thin Hair Fat Face

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, it’s time to get the perfect short haircut for thin hair fat face. Follow these steps:

1. Choose the Right Length

Decide on the length you want for your haircut. Chin-length bobs work well for round faces as they make your face appear slimmer. Pixie cuts are great for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy look.

2. Add Layers

Ask your stylist to add layers to your haircut to add volume and texture. Layers can be added to any length, from a bob to a pixie cut.

3. Get Bangs

If you want bangs, ask your stylist to cut them based on your face shape. Side-swept bangs or wispy bangs work well for round faces and thin hair.

4. Style Your Hair

Once you’ve got your haircut, style it according to your preference. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair. Use a curling iron or straightener to add waves or curls to your hair for a more textured look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut for Thin Hair Fat Face

Short haircuts are low maintenance, but they still require some care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

1. Use the Right Products

Use products designed for thin hair to add volume and texture to your hair. Avoid heavy products that can weigh your hair down.

2. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will keep your haircut looking fresh and prevent split ends. Schedule a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain your cut.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot tools. Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQ: Short Haircuts for Thin Hair Fat Face

Q: What is the best short haircut for a round face with thin hair?

A: Chin-length bobs, pixie cuts, and layered cuts are great options for a round face with thin hair.

Q: How can I add volume to my thin hair?

A: Use volumizing products, like mousse or spray, and get a layered haircut to add volume and texture to your hair.

Q: Will bangs work for a round face with thin hair?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs or wispy bangs work well for a round face with thin hair.

Conclusion

Short haircuts for thin hair fat face can be flattering and low maintenance. Choose the right length, add layers, and get bangs to create a voluminous, textured look that flatters your face shape. Follow our tips for maintaining your short haircut and you’ll have a trendy, easy-to-style look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
