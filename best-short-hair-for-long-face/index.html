Best Short Hair For Long Face Perfect Hairstyles For Your Long Face

Written by Robby Sep 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. You want a style that complements your face shape and enhances your features. Short haircuts are a great option for long faces as they can add width and volume to your face. In this article, we will discuss the best short hair for long face, how to style them, and hair care tips to maintain your hair's health.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long face has a length that is longer than its width, and the forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all similar in width. The best short hair for long face is a style that adds width and volume to the face. Short haircuts that add volume to the crown of the head or create the illusion of width through the sides are ideal for a long face.

How to

Before choosing a short hairstyle for a long face, it's important to determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring your face's length and width and comparing them. The width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline should be roughly the same. Once you have identified your face shape, you can choose a short hairstyle that flatters your features.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape

Step 2: Choose a short hairstyle that will add width and volume

Step 3: Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut for your face shape

Step 4: Style your hair according to the cut

Tips

  • Choose a short hairstyle that adds volume to the crown of the head or creates the illusion of width through the sides
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short
  • Consider adding layers to your hair to add texture and volume
  • Use styling products to enhance volume and texture

FAQ

Q: Can I still have bangs with a short hairstyle for a long face?

A: Yes, you can still have bangs with a short hairstyle for a long face. Opt for side-swept bangs that will add width to the face.

Q: Will a bob haircut work for a long face?

A: Yes, a bob haircut can work for a long face if it's styled with layers that add volume to the crown of the head.

Hair Care

To maintain the health of your short hair, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat. Use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools to avoid damage to your hair. Regular trims are also essential to maintain the shape and style of your short haircut.

In conclusion, the best short hair for long face is a style that adds width and volume to the face. Consider your face shape and consult with your hairstylist to choose a short hairstyle that flatters your features. Follow a good hair care routine to maintain the health of your short hair and keep it looking its best.
