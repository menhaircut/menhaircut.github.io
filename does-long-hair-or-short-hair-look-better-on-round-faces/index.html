Does Long Hair Or Short Hair Look Better On Round Faces

Written by Petter Sep 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Round faces have a unique shape that requires specific hairstyles that can enhance their features. Choosing the right hairstyle for a round face can be daunting, and one common question that arises is, "does long hair or short hair look better on round faces?" In this article, we will explore the answer to this question and provide tips on how to choose the perfect hairstyle for a round face.

Description

A round face has soft, curved lines, no sharp angles, and a width that is almost equal to its length. When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it is essential to create an illusion of length and angles to balance the face's shape. Long hair or short hair can both look good on a round face, but it depends on the individual's facial features and hair type.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, consider the following:

  • Face shape - Determine your face shape and choose a hairstyle that can enhance your features. Consider the length, width, and angles of your face.
  • Hair texture - Choose a hairstyle that can work with your hair type. Curly hair, straight hair, and wavy hair have different requirements.
  • Personal style - Choose a hairstyle that matches your personality and style. You don't want to feel uncomfortable or out of place in your new hairstyle.

Long Hair vs. Short Hair

Long hair can elongate a round face and create an illusion of length. It can also add volume and texture to the hair, creating a balanced look. However, long hair can also make the face appear wider and accentuate the roundness. On the other hand, short hair can create angles and height, making the face look longer and slimmer. It can also draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. However, short hair can also make the face look wider and emphasize the roundness.

Step-by-Step Guide to Get the Perfect Hairstyle for a Round Face

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Determine your face shape and facial features.
  2. Consider your hair texture and type.
  3. Research different hairstyles that can work for round faces.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to get their professional opinion.
  5. Choose a style that matches your personal style and complements your features.
  6. Experiment with different haircuts, lengths, and styles until you find the right one.
  7. Maintain your hairstyle with regular trims, conditioning treatments, and styling products.

Tips for Styling Hair for Round Faces

Here are some tips for styling hair for round faces:

  • Choose hairstyles that add height and angles to the face, such as layered cuts, side-swept bangs, and long bobs.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as blunt cuts, heavy bangs, and short layers.
  • Experiment with different parts, such as a side part or center part, to create the illusion of length.
  • Use styling products that add texture and volume, such as mousse, texturizing spray, and dry shampoo.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back, as they can emphasize the roundness of the face.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I have bangs with a round face?

Yes, you can have bangs with a round face, but it is important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs create angles and draw attention to the eyes, while blunt bangs can make the face look wider. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best type of bangs for your face shape.

Can I have long hair with a round face?

Yes, you can have long hair with a round face, but it is essential to add layers and texture to create an illusion of length. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face and opt for hairstyles that elongate the face.

Can I have short hair with a round face?

Yes, you can have short hair with a round face, but it is important to choose a style that adds height and angles to the face. Avoid hairstyles that are too short or blunt, as they can emphasize the roundness of the face.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and volume to the hair.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down the hair and flatten it.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools to prevent damage to the hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.

In conclusion, whether long hair or short hair looks better on a round face depends on the individual's facial features and hair type. It is essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. With the right haircut, styling, and hair care, you can enhance your features and create a balanced look.
