The Best Facial Hair For Long Face A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jun 15, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a long face, finding the right facial hair style can be challenging. While some styles can make your face appear even longer, others can help balance out your features and create a more proportionate look. In this guide, we’ll explore the best facial hair styles for long faces, along with tips on how to maintain them and keep your beard looking its best.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Best Facial Hair for Long Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Beard
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Beard
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care for Your Beard

Description

Facial hair can be a great way to add definition and balance to your face, especially if you have a long face. However, not all styles are created equal, and what works for one person may not work for another. When choosing a facial hair style, it’s important to consider your face shape, hair type, and personal preferences.

How to Choose the Best Facial Hair for Long Face

The key to choosing the best facial hair for a long face is to create balance and definition. Here are some general guidelines to follow:

  • Opt for shorter styles, as longer styles can make your face appear even longer.
  • Avoid styles with too much volume or width at the sides, as they can widen your face.
  • Consider styles that add definition to your jawline, such as a goatee or chin strap beard.
  • Try different styles and experiment until you find what works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Beard

Once you’ve chosen a facial hair style, it’s important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your face and beard with a gentle cleanser.
  2. Trim your beard to the desired length using a pair of scissors or clippers.
  3. Shape your beard using a razor or trimmer to create the desired style.
  4. Apply a beard oil or balm to keep your beard hydrated and prevent dryness and irritation.
  5. Brush your beard regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Beard

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your beard:

  • Trim your beard regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the desired length.
  • Avoid over-washing your beard, as this can strip it of natural oils and cause dryness.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to help distribute oils and keep your beard looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a beard conditioner to keep your beard soft and manageable.

FAQs

What is the best facial hair style for a long face?

The best facial hair style for a long face is one that creates balance and definition. Shorter styles that add definition to the jawline, such as a goatee or chin strap beard, tend to work well.

Can I grow a beard if I have a long face?

Absolutely! In fact, a well-groomed beard can help balance out your features and create a more proportionate look. Just be sure to choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type.

What should I do if I have patchy facial hair?

If you have patchy facial hair, be patient – it can take several months for your beard to fill in. In the meantime, you can try using a beard oil or balm to promote growth and keep your skin and beard hydrated.

Hair Care for Your Beard

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your beard looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your beard regularly with a gentle cleanser.
  • Apply a beard oil or balm to keep your beard hydrated and prevent dryness and irritation.
  • Trim your beard regularly to maintain the desired length and prevent split ends.
  • Use a boar bristle brush to help distribute oils and keep your beard looking neat and tidy.
  • Consider using a beard conditioner to keep your beard soft and manageable.

By following these tips and choosing the right facial hair style for your long face, you can create a look that complements your features and adds definition to your face. Remember to be patient, experiment with different styles, and most importantly, have fun with your beard!
