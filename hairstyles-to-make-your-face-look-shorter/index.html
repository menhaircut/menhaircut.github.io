Hairstyles To Make Your Face Look Shorter A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long face shape? Do you want to make your face look shorter? If you answered yes to these questions, then this article is for you. We have compiled a list of hairstyles that can help you achieve a shorter look. Whether you have long, medium or short hair, we have something for everyone. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Description

Having a long face shape can sometimes be challenging when it comes to styling your hair. You may feel limited in your options, but the truth is there are plenty of hairstyles that can help balance out your face shape and make it appear shorter. The key is to add volume to the sides of your face and avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head.

How to

The first step in achieving a shorter face shape is to choose the right hairstyle. You want to go for styles that add width to the sides of your face and avoid styles that add height to the top of your head. Here are some hairstyles that can help:

Bob

A classic bob is a great option for those with a long face shape. The length should be above your shoulders and the ends should be textured to add volume to the sides of your face. You can also add layers to the front to frame your face and make it appear shorter.

Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can help balance out a long face shape by adding width to the forehead. They should be cut straight across and sit just above your eyebrows. You can also add layers to the rest of your hair to avoid a heavy look.

Layered Lob

A layered lob is a longer version of the classic bob. The length should be just above your shoulders and the layers should start at your chin. This style will add volume to the sides of your face and make it appear shorter.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve a shorter face shape:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  2. Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head.
  3. Use a volumizing product on the roots of your hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume to the sides of your face.
  5. Use a straightening iron to add texture to the ends of your hair.
  6. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a shorter face shape:

  • Avoid center parts as they can elongate your face shape.
  • Add highlights or lowlights to your hair to create depth and dimension.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short as they can make your face appear longer.
  • Accessorize with earrings and necklaces to draw attention away from your face.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for a shorter face shape:

What is the best hairstyle for a long face shape?

A bob or lob with layers is a great option for those with a long face shape.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that add height to the top of your head such as a high ponytail or top knot.

Can I still wear my hair straight if I have a long face shape?

Yes, you can still wear your hair straight but make sure to add volume to the sides of your face with a round brush.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy look. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot styling tools.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to avoid split ends.

Now that you have a better idea of what hairstyles can help make your face look shorter, it's time to experiment with different styles. Remember to choose a style that makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy styling!
