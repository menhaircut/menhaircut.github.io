Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair over 60 Round Face - Tips and Ideas

If you are over 60 with a round face and very thin hair, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll provide you with tips and ideas on the best hairstyles for very thin hair over 60 round face.

  • Tips to Maintain Thin Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle?

When it comes to choosing the right hairstyle for very thin hair over 60 round face, there are a few things you need to keep in mind. Firstly, you want to avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or bulky as they can make your face look even rounder. Secondly, you want to choose a hairstyle that adds volume to your hair to make it look fuller. Thirdly, you want to choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Round Face

Round faces work best with hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, elongating the face. Some of the best hairstyles for round faces include:

  • Short pixie cut
  • Layered bob
  • Long layers with side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical bob

Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair

When it comes to hairstyles for very thin hair, you want to opt for styles that add volume and texture to your hair. Some of the best hairstyles for very thin hair include:

  • Short layered pixie cut
  • Chin-length bob with layers
  • Short shag cut
  • Textured lob

Step-by-Step Guide for Hairstyles for Very Thin Hair over 60 Round Face

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the perfect hairstyle for very thin hair over 60 round face:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair texture
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best cut for your hair
  3. Use volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair
  4. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add volume at the roots
  5. Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair while blow-drying
  6. Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair
  7. Use a hair serum or oil to add shine to your hair
  8. Style your hair with a curling iron or hot rollers to add volume and texture
  9. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place

Tips to Maintain Thin Hair

Here are some tips to maintain thin hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that weigh down your hair

FAQs

Q. Will a short hairstyle suit a round face?

A. Yes, a short hairstyle can suit a round face as long as it adds volume to the crown of the head.

Q. Can I use hair extensions to add volume to my hair?

A. Yes, hair extensions can be a great way to add volume and length to thin hair. However, it's important to choose high-quality extensions and to have them applied professionally to avoid damage to your hair.

Q. Can I color my thin hair?

A. Yes, you can color your thin hair, but it's important to choose a gentle, ammonia-free formula that won't damage your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for thin hair:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions with a hat or scarf

With these tips and ideas, you can find the perfect hairstyle for very thin hair over 60 round face. Remember to choose a style that flatters your features and adds volume to your hair, and to maintain your hair with gentle, nourishing products.
