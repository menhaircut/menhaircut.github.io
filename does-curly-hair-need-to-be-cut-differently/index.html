Does Curly Hair Need To Be Cut Differently

Written by Robby Sep 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair is unique in its own way. It requires different care and maintenance than straight hair. Many people wonder if curly hair needs to be cut differently. The answer is yes! Curly hair needs to be cut in a specific way to avoid frizz and maintain its shape. In this article, we will discuss how to cut curly hair and provide tips on how to care for it.

How to Cut Curly Hair

Cutting curly hair is an art form. It requires precision and technique to avoid ruining the curl pattern. Here are some steps to follow when cutting curly hair:

Step 1: Wash and Detangle

Before cutting curly hair, it is important to wash and detangle it. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils. Detangle the hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. Do not use a brush as this can cause breakage.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using clips. This will make it easier to cut the hair evenly. Start with the bottom sections and work your way up.

Step 3: Cut the Hair

When cutting curly hair, it is important to cut it when it is dry. This will allow you to see the curl pattern and avoid cutting too much off. Use sharp scissors and cut the hair in a downward motion. Do not cut the hair straight across as this can cause frizz.

Step 4: Shape the Hair

After cutting the hair, shape it using your fingers. Avoid using a brush as this can cause frizz. Use a diffuser to dry the hair and enhance the curl pattern.

Tips for Caring for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to avoid frizz and maintain its shape. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

Tip 1: Use a Diffuser

When drying curly hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help enhance the curl pattern and avoid frizz.

Tip 2: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help prevent frizz and keep your curls intact. Cotton pillowcases can absorb moisture from the hair, causing it to become dry and frizzy.

Tip 3: Use a Deep Conditioner

Curly hair can become dry and brittle. Using a deep conditioner once a week can help moisturize and nourish the hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut curly hair when it is wet?

A: No, it is best to cut curly hair when it is dry. This will allow you to see the curl pattern and avoid cutting too much off.

Q: How often should I cut my curly hair?

A: It is recommended to cut curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires specific care and maintenance to keep it healthy and beautiful. Use sulfate-free products and avoid using heat tools too often. Deep condition once a week and avoid brushing the hair. Follow these tips and your curls will be looking fabulous!
