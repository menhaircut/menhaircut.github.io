How To Do Messy Wavy Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old straight hair or tight curls? Want to try something new and trendy? Messy wavy hair is the perfect style for you. It’s effortless, chic, and low-maintenance. In this article, we’ll show you how to do messy wavy hair in relaxed English, step-by-step. We’ll also provide tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect messy waves.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do Messy Wavy Hair
  • Tips for Messy Wavy Hair
  • FAQs about Messy Wavy Hair
  • Hair Care Tips for Messy Wavy Hair

How to Do Messy Wavy Hair

Before we start, make sure you have the right tools and products. You’ll need a curling iron, hairbrush, hair spray, and texturizing spray. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Towel-dry your hair until it’s slightly damp. Apply a texturizing spray to add volume and texture. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Curl Your Hair

Take small sections of hair and curl them away from your face using a curling iron. Hold each curl for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process until you’ve curled all your hair.

Step 3: Brush Out the Curls

Once you’ve curled your hair, use a hairbrush to gently brush out the curls. This will give your hair a more natural, messy look.

Step 4: Add Texture and Volume

Apply a texturizing spray to your hair to add volume and texture. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and create more waves. You can also use a hair spray to set the waves in place.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Spray

Finish your messy wavy hair look with a light mist of hair spray. This will help your waves stay in place all day long.

Tips for Messy Wavy Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy wavy hair:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel to create loose waves.
  • Don’t curl your hair too tightly. Loose curls will give you a more natural, messy look.
  • Brush out your curls gently to avoid creating frizz.
  • Apply a texturizing spray to your roots to add volume.
  • Don’t overdo it with hair spray. A light mist will be enough to keep your waves in place.

FAQs about Messy Wavy Hair

Q: Can I still do messy wavy hair if I have naturally straight hair?

A: Yes, you can. Follow the steps above, but use a curling iron to create waves in your straight hair. You can also apply a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

Q: How long will my messy wavy hair last?

A: Your messy wavy hair can last for up to a day or two. Use a hair spray to help your waves stay in place for longer.

Q: Can I use a sea salt spray instead of a texturizing spray?

A: Yes, you can. Sea salt sprays can add texture and volume to your hair, but they can also dry it out. Make sure to use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

Hair Care Tips for Messy Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your messy wavy hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to do messy wavy hair, it’s time to try it out for yourself. Follow these steps, use the right tools and products, and experiment with different techniques until you find the perfect messy waves for you. Don’t forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
