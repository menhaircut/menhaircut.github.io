Women s Short Haircuts For Thin Hair 2019 How To Get The Perfect Look

Are you tired of your thin hair always falling flat and looking lifeless? If so, it might be time to consider a short haircut. Short haircuts can add volume and texture to thin hair, giving you a more youthful and vibrant look. In this article, we'll go over the best women's short haircuts for thin hair in 2019, along with tips on how to maintain and care for your new style.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Thin Hair

When it comes to choosing the right short haircut for thin hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, consider your face shape. Some haircuts are more flattering for certain face shapes than others. For example, a bob haircut looks great on oval faces, while a pixie cut is perfect for heart-shaped faces.

Next, think about your hair texture. If you have fine, thin hair, you'll want to avoid haircuts that require a lot of layering, as this can make your hair look even thinner. Instead, opt for haircuts that add volume and texture, such as a short bob or a layered pixie cut.

Finally, consider your lifestyle. Are you someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, or do you prefer a low-maintenance look? If you're short on time, a choppy bob or a messy pixie cut might be the perfect solution.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Short Haircut

Now that you know what to look for in a short haircut for thin hair, it's time to get the perfect cut. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose the right stylist. Look for someone who specializes in short haircuts and has experience working with thin hair.
  2. Bring pictures. Bring in pictures of the haircut you want. This will give your stylist a better idea of what you're looking for.
  3. Discuss your hair type. Let your stylist know that you have thin hair and ask for their recommendations on the best haircut for your hair type.
  4. Get the cut. Sit back, relax, and let your stylist work their magic.
  5. Style your new haircut. Once you're home, experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Now that you have the perfect short haircut for your thin hair, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims. Short haircuts require more frequent trims to maintain their shape and prevent split ends.
  • Use the right products. Look for products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse or texturizing spray.
  • Avoid heat styling. Heat can damage your hair and make it look even thinner. If you must use heat, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low.
  • Experiment with different styles. Short haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Have fun experimenting with different looks.

FAQs about Women's Short Haircuts for Thin Hair

Q: Will a short haircut make my thin hair look even thinner?

A: Not necessarily. The right short haircut can add volume and texture to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller.

Q: Can I still style my short haircut if I have thin hair?

A: Yes! Short haircuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Q: How often should I get a trim with a short haircut?

A: Short haircuts require more frequent trims than longer haircuts. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips for thin hair:

  • Avoid over-washing. Washing your hair too frequently can strip it of its natural oils, making it look even thinner.
  • Use a gentle shampoo. Look for a shampoo that is designed for thin or fine hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products. Heavy styling products can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Protect your hair from heat. Use a heat protectant spray if you must use heat styling tools.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect short haircut for your thin hair and keep it looking great for years to come.
