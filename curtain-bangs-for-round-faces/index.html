Curtain Bangs For Round Faces A Complete Guide In 2023

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and want to try something new? Consider curtain bangs! Curtain bangs have been a popular trend for a few years now, and they're not going anywhere anytime soon. One of the best things about curtain bangs is that they are suitable for all face shapes, including round faces. In this blog post, we'll explore everything you need to know about curtain bangs for round faces, including how to style them, tips, and hair care.

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted in the middle, framing the face on either side. They're called 'curtain' bangs because they resemble the drapes that hang on a window. Curtain bangs are versatile and can be styled in many ways, whether you want them to be wispy, blunt, or choppy. They're an excellent choice for round faces because they can help elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

How to Style Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

Styling curtain bangs for round faces is relatively easy. Here's how:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray before blow-drying your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, making sure to add volume to the roots.
  3. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs, pulling them to either side of your face to create a curtain effect.
  4. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your bangs in place.

Voila! You're ready to rock your curtain bangs.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

If you're cutting your curtain bangs at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with dry, detangled hair.
  2. Part your hair down the middle, creating two equal sections.
  3. Clip the rest of your hair away from your face.
  4. Using sharp scissors, cut a small section of hair at a time, starting from the center and working your way outwards.
  5. Stop cutting when your bangs reach the length you desire. Remember that you can always trim more later.
  6. Use a comb to make sure your bangs are even on both sides.
  7. Style your bangs using the instructions above.

Remember to take your time and be patient. If you're not confident cutting your own bangs, it's always best to go to a professional hairdresser.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

Here are some tips for keeping your curtain bangs looking their best:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them at the desired length.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them oily and greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo in between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse or texturizing spray, to create different looks.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

FAQ about Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs for round faces:

Q: Are curtain bangs suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, curtain bangs are suitable for all face shapes, including round faces.

Q: Can I style my curtain bangs differently?

A: Yes, you can style your curtain bangs in different ways, such as adding waves or curls.

Q: How do I know if curtain bangs will suit me?

A: The best way to know if curtain bangs will suit you is to consult with a professional hairdresser.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs for Round Faces

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and nourished:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair oil or serum to add extra moisture to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

With these tips, you'll be able to maintain healthy and nourished hair, as well as beautiful curtain bangs.

Conclusion

Curtain bangs for round faces are a versatile and trendy hairstyle that can help elongate your face and create a more balanced look. With the right styling, cutting, and hair care, you'll be able to rock your curtain bangs with confidence. Remember, if you're not confident cutting your own hair, it's always best to consult with a professional hairdresser.
