How To Make Short Boy Hair Fluffy

Written by Petter Sep 28, 2023 · 3 min read

Short hair can be a challenge to style, especially for boys who want to achieve a fluffy look. But don't worry, there are several ways to make short boy hair fluffy. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this style. We'll also provide some tips and answer some frequently asked questions to help you maintain your fluffy hair. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Short Boy Hair Fluffy
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to follow to make short boy hair fluffy:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume to your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a small amount of mousse to your hair while it's still damp. Use your fingers to work the mousse through your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to lift your hair and create volume.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using your fingers to lift and shape it. Make sure to blow dry your hair in the opposite direction of the way it naturally falls. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots.

Step 4: Use a Round Brush

Using a round brush, lift your hair at the roots while blow drying it. This will help to create even more volume and shape.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once your hair is dry, finish with a light mist of hairspray. This will help to hold the shape and volume of your hair throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Fluffy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your fluffy hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair
  • Blow dry your hair in the opposite direction of the way it naturally falls
  • Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying
  • Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the shape and volume of your hair

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use gel instead of mousse?

A: While gel can also add volume, it can be heavier and weigh down your hair. Mousse is a better option for creating a fluffy look.

Q: Can I achieve a fluffy look with short, curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still achieve a fluffy look with curly hair. Follow the same steps, but use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and add volume.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to:

  • Get regular haircuts to prevent split ends and breakage
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can clog your pores and lead to scalp irritation
  • Eat a balanced diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth

Follow these tips and steps to achieve a fluffy look for your short boy hair. With a little bit of effort, you'll be rocking a stylish and voluminous hairstyle in no time!
