How To Cut A Short Wavy Bob A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Cutting your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're looking to create a specific style. One popular style that has been trending in recent years is the short wavy bob. This chic and effortless look can be achieved with just a few simple steps. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to cut a short wavy bob at home.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut a Short Wavy Bob
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting a Short Wavy Bob
  • FAQ

How to Cut a Short Wavy Bob

Cutting a short wavy bob can be a simple process if you follow these basic steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back
  3. Take a small section of hair and cut it to your desired length, starting from the back and working your way forward
  4. Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to cut at an angle to create a layered effect
  5. Use thinning shears to remove excess bulk and add texture
  6. Blend any uneven areas
  7. Style your hair using a curling iron or wand to create loose waves
  8. Finish with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for added texture and hold

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Wet hair can be harder to cut accurately and can result in uneven layers. If you need to wash your hair, make sure to dry it thoroughly before starting.

Step 2: Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back

Using clips or hair ties, separate your hair into four sections: top, sides, and back. This will make it easier to cut each section evenly.

Step 3: Take a small section of hair and cut it to your desired length, starting from the back and working your way forward

Starting at the back of your head, take a small section of hair and cut it to your desired length. Use this section as a guide for the rest of your hair, working your way forward and cutting each section to the same length.

Step 4: Repeat this process for each section of hair, making sure to cut at an angle to create a layered effect

As you work your way through each section of hair, make sure to cut at an angle to create a layered effect. This will give your hair a natural, textured look.

Step 5: Use thinning shears to remove excess bulk and add texture

After you've cut all of your hair to the desired length, use thinning shears to remove any excess bulk and add texture. This will help your hair lay more naturally and create a more effortless look.

Step 6: Blend any uneven areas

If you notice any uneven areas or choppy sections, use your scissors to blend them together for a more seamless look.

Step 7: Style your hair using a curling iron or wand to create loose waves

Once your hair is cut and blended, it's time to style it. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves, taking care to curl away from your face for a more open, flattering look.

Step 8: Finish with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for added texture and hold

Finish off your look with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add texture and hold. This will help your waves stay in place and give your hair a more tousled, beachy vibe.

Tips for Cutting a Short Wavy Bob

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting a short wavy bob:

  • Start with small sections of hair to ensure accuracy
  • Cut at an angle to create a natural, layered look
  • Use thinning shears to remove excess bulk and add texture
  • Blend any uneven areas for a more seamless look
  • Style your hair with a curling iron or wand for loose waves
  • Finish with a texturizing spray or sea salt spray for added hold and texture

FAQ

Can I cut a short wavy bob at home?

Yes! Cutting a short wavy bob at home is a simple process that can be done with just a few basic tools and some patience.

What tools do I need to cut a short wavy bob?

You'll need a pair of scissors, thinning shears, clips or hair ties, and a curling iron or wand.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

Don't worry! If you make a mistake, you can always even it out or blend it in with the rest of your hair. Take your time and work in small sections to ensure accuracy.

Hair Care Tips for Short Wavy Bobs

Now that you've cut your short wavy bob, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Get regular trims to maintain your style

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to cut and care for your short wavy bob with ease. Remember to take your time and have fun with it!
