Massaging your scalp can increase blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Use your fingertips to massage your scalp for a few minutes each day.

Use Essential Oils

Essential oils like peppermint, lavender, and rosemary can stimulate hair growth. Mix a few drops of essential oils with a carrier oil like coconut or olive oil and massage the mixture onto your scalp. Leave it on for a few hours before washing it out.
