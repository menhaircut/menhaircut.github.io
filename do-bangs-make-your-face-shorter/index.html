Do Bangs Make Your Face Shorter

Written by Oliver Oct 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're thinking about getting bangs, you might be wondering whether they will make your face look shorter. The answer to this question is not straightforward, as it depends on a variety of factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and the type of bangs you choose. In this blog post, we'll explore whether bangs make your face look shorter and provide some tips on how to choose the right bangs for your face shape.

Understanding Face Shapes

The first step in determining whether bangs make your face look shorter is to understand your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Depending on your face shape, certain types of bangs may be more flattering than others.

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile, and almost any type of bangs will look great on you. Round faces can benefit from bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle, as this can help elongate the face. Square faces can benefit from bangs that are longer on the sides and slightly shorter in the middle, as this can soften the angular features of the face.

Heart-shaped faces can benefit from side-swept bangs that are longer on one side and shorter on the other, as this can help balance out the wider forehead. Diamond-shaped faces can benefit from bangs that are shorter in the middle and longer on the sides, as this can help soften the cheekbones. Finally, oblong faces can benefit from bangs that are shorter in the middle and longer on the sides, as this can help shorten the appearance of the face.

Types of Bangs

Now that you understand your face shape, it's time to explore the different types of bangs. There are many types of bangs to choose from, including blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and choppy bangs.

Blunt bangs are cut straight across and can work well on oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. Side-swept bangs are swept to one side and can work well on round and diamond-shaped faces. Curtain bangs are parted in the middle and can work well on oval and oblong faces. Finally, choppy bangs are cut in an uneven, textured style and can work well on almost any face shape.

How to Choose the Right Bangs for Your Face Shape

When choosing the right bangs for your face shape, it's important to consider your hair texture and lifestyle. If you have curly hair, for example, you may want to opt for side-swept or curtain bangs that can be easily styled with your natural texture. If you lead an active lifestyle, you may want to opt for shorter bangs that won't get in the way during exercise.

It's also important to consider the maintenance required for different types of bangs. Blunt bangs, for example, require regular trims to maintain their shape, while choppy bangs can be left to grow out for a more natural look.

Tips for Styling Bangs

Once you've chosen the right bangs for your face shape and hair texture, it's important to learn how to style them properly. Here are some tips for styling bangs:

  • Blow dry your bangs in the opposite direction to create volume.
  • Use a round brush to smooth out your bangs while blow drying.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to smooth out any frizz or flyaways.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs for a sleek look.
  • Pin your bangs back with bobby pins or a headband for a different look.

FAQ

Q: Will bangs make my face look shorter?
A: It depends on your face shape and the type of bangs you choose. Certain types of bangs can help elongate the face, while others can make it appear shorter.

Q: What type of bangs are best for round faces?
A: Side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides and shorter in the middle can help elongate round faces.

Q: Can I still wear bangs if I have curly hair?
A: Yes! Side-swept or curtain bangs can work well with curly hair.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your bangs to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips for bangs:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to keep them clean and oil-free.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as this can weigh them down.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can transfer oils from your hands to your hair.

In conclusion, whether bangs make your face look shorter depends on a variety of factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and the type of bangs you choose. By understanding your face shape and choosing the right type of bangs for your hair, you can create a flattering and stylish look that complements your features.
