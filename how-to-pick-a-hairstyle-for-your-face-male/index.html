How To Pick The Perfect Hairstyle For Your Face Male

Written by Aprilia Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit your face shape. With so many different styles to choose from, it's easy to get overwhelmed. But don't worry, we're here to help! In this article, we'll guide you through the process of picking the perfect hairstyle for your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Picking a Hairstyle
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are four main face shapes: oval, square, round, and heart-shaped. Here's how to figure out which one you have:

  • Oval: Your face is longer than it is wide, with a rounded jawline and forehead.
  • Square: Your face is roughly the same length and width, with a strong, angular jawline.
  • Round: Your face is as wide as it is long, with a soft, rounded jawline.
  • Heart-shaped: Your face is wider at the forehead and tapers down to a pointy chin.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features.

Step-by-Step Guide to Picking a Hairstyle

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to start looking for a hairstyle that will work for you. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Research hairstyles: Look online or in magazines for hairstyles that are suited to your face shape.
  2. Consider your hair type: Think about your hair's texture and thickness. Some hairstyles work better with thick, curly hair, while others are better suited for fine, straight hair.
  3. Think about your lifestyle: Are you someone who likes to spend a lot of time styling your hair, or do you prefer a low-maintenance look? Make sure the hairstyle you choose works with your lifestyle.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist: A professional hairstylist can help you choose a hairstyle that will work for your face shape and hair type.
  5. Try it out: Once you've found a hairstyle you like, try it out! You can always make adjustments if it's not quite right.

Remember, the most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

  • Don't be afraid to try something new: If you've had the same hairstyle for years, it might be time to mix things up!
  • Consider your face's proportions: If you have a larger forehead, you might want to consider a hairstyle with bangs to balance it out.
  • Think about your wardrobe: Your hairstyle should complement your personal style and the clothes you wear.
  • Be realistic: If you don't have time to style your hair every day, don't choose a high-maintenance style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hairstyle:

  • What hairstyle is best for a round face? A layered hairstyle with volume on top can help elongate a round face.
  • What hairstyle is best for a square face? A hairstyle with soft, wispy layers can help soften a square jawline.
  • What hairstyle is best for a heart-shaped face? A chin-length bob can help balance out a heart-shaped face.
  • What hairstyle is best for an oval face? Almost any hairstyle will work with an oval face shape!

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use the right products: Choose shampoos and conditioners that are suited to your hair type.
  • Avoid heat damage: Limit your use of hot tools like flat irons and blow dryers.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray to prevent sun damage.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. Remember, confidence is key – wear your new hairstyle with pride!
