Inspiration

Black celebrities have been embracing their natural hair, and short hair is no exception. Here are some of the celebrities who have been rocking their short natural hair:

Viola Davis

Viola Davis is known for her acting skills, but her short natural hair has become an inspiration to many. She has been wearing her hair short for a while now and has shown how versatile this style can be.

How to Achieve the Look

If you want to achieve the short natural hair look, there are a few ways to do it:

Big Chop

The big chop is when you cut off all of your relaxed or chemically treated hair. This will leave you with short natural hair. This is the quickest way to achieve the look, but it can also be the most intimidating.

Transitioning

Transitioning is when you let your natural hair grow out while keeping the relaxed or chemically treated hair. This process can take months to years, depending on how long you want your hair to be before cutting off the relaxed ends.

Hair Care Tips

Short natural hair requires a different type of care than longer hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

Moisturize

Natural hair can be dry, so it's important to keep it moisturized. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated.

Protective Styles

Protective styles like braids, twists, or wigs can help protect your hair from damage. They can also give you a break from styling your hair every day.

Trim Regularly

Even though your hair is short, it still needs to be trimmed regularly. This will help get rid of split ends and keep your hair healthy.

FAQs

Can I color my short natural hair?

Yes, you can color your short natural hair. However, it's important to use a hair dye that is safe for natural hair and to follow the instructions carefully.

Can I straighten my short natural hair?

Yes, you can straighten your short natural hair. However, it's important to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

How often should I wash my short natural hair?

You should wash your hair as often as it needs it. Some people may need to wash their hair every week, while others may only need to wash it every two weeks.

In conclusion, short natural hair is a style that many black celebrities have embraced. If you want to achieve this look, there are a few ways to do it. It's also important to take care of your hair by moisturizing, using protective styles, and trimming regularly. Follow these tips, and you'll have healthy and beautiful short natural hair.
