How To Cut Thick Medium Length Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting hair at home can be a daunting task, especially if you have thick medium length hair. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a salon-worthy cut in the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we will provide you with step by step instructions on how to cut thick medium length hair at home, as well as tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect cut.

How to Cut Thick Medium Length Hair at Home

Before we begin, it is important to gather the right tools. You will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • Hair clips
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Once you have your tools, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will help you see the natural texture and movement of your hair, making it easier to cut.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into four equal parts: two at the front and two at the back. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 3: Cut the Back

Starting with the back section, take a small section of hair and comb it straight. Hold it between your index and middle fingers and cut the ends with your scissors. Repeat this process, working your way up the section of hair until you have cut all of the back sections.

Step 4: Cut the Front

Once you have finished cutting the back, move on to the front sections. Again, take small sections of hair and comb them straight. Hold the section between your fingers and cut the ends with your scissors.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

Now that you have cut the back and front sections, it is time to blend the layers. Starting with the back, take a small section of hair and hold it straight up. Cut into the hair using your scissors, creating a soft, blended look. Repeat this process on all sections of hair until you have blended the layers.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting and blending your hair, check for evenness. Take small sections of hair and compare the length to ensure that it is even.

Step 7: Add Texture

If you want to add texture to your hair, use your scissors to point cut the ends. This will create a softer, textured look.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting and texturizing your hair, style it as desired. You can blow dry it straight or add curls using a curling iron.

Tips for Cutting Thick Medium Length Hair at Home

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect cut:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up. This will help you achieve an even cut.
  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It is better to cut less and make multiple passes than to cut too much and have to start over.
  • Check for evenness frequently to avoid uneven cuts.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my own hair if it is thick and medium length?

Yes, you can cut your own hair if it is thick and medium length. Just be sure to follow the steps outlined in this guide and use the right tools.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always go to a salon and have it fixed. Alternatively, you can try to fix it yourself by trimming the ends or blending the layers.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, be sure to follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut thick medium length hair at home, it's time to give it a try. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you will achieve a beautiful cut in no time!
