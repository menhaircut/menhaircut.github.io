Will Blow Drying Your Hair Make It Curly

Written by Bardi Jul 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are someone who loves to experiment with your hair, you might have wondered whether blow drying your hair will make it curly. The answer to that question is a bit tricky, and it depends on different factors such as your hair type and the way you use your blow dryer. In this article, we will explore everything you need to know about blow drying your hair and its impact on curls.

  • Hair Type
  • Blow Dryer

Hair Type

The texture and thickness of your hair play a crucial role in determining whether blow drying will make it curly. If you have naturally straight hair, using a blow dryer won't make it curly. However, if you have wavy or curly hair, blow drying can enhance your curls or make them more defined.

For those with wavy hair, blow drying your hair with a diffuser attachment can add more definition and volume to your natural waves. On the other hand, if you have curly hair, blow drying can help to reshape your curls and add more bounce to your hair.

Blow Dryer

The type of blow dryer you use can also impact whether it will make your hair curly or not. If you use a blow dryer that doesn't have a diffuser attachment, it will likely straighten your hair or create loose waves.

However, if you use a blow dryer with a diffuser attachment, it can help to enhance your curls or waves. A diffuser attachment spreads the heat across a larger section of your hair, reducing the damage caused by concentrated heat. It also helps to distribute the air evenly, which can create more defined curls.

Blow Drying Technique

The way you use your blow dryer can also impact the outcome of your hair. If you use a high heat setting and aim the blow dryer directly at your hair, it can damage your hair and make it frizzy.

Instead, you should use a low or medium heat setting and hold the blow dryer at least 6 inches away from your hair. You should also use a diffuser attachment and move the blow dryer in a circular motion around your head. This technique will help to enhance your curls and add more volume to your hair.

Hair Products

The hair products you use before and after blow drying can also impact the outcome of your hair. If you want to enhance your curls, you should use products that are designed for curly hair. Some of the best hair products for curly hair include curl enhancing creams, gels, and mousse.

You should also use a heat protectant spray before blow drying your hair to prevent damage to your hair. After blow drying, you can use a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Blow Drying Curly Hair

If you want to blow dry your curly hair, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a diffuser attachment
  • Use a low or medium heat setting
  • Hold the blow dryer at least 6 inches away from your hair
  • Move the blow dryer in a circular motion
  • Use a curl enhancing product
  • Use a heat protectant spray
  • Use a hair oil or serum to add shine
  • Avoid blow drying your hair every day to prevent damage

FAQs

Q. Will blow drying my hair make it curly?

A. It depends on your hair type and the way you use your blow dryer. If you have naturally straight hair, blow drying won't make it curly. However, if you have wavy or curly hair, blow drying can enhance your curls.

Q. Can I blow dry my hair without a diffuser attachment?

A. Yes, you can. However, it will likely straighten your hair or create loose waves. If you want to enhance your curls, it's best to use a diffuser attachment.

Q. Can blow drying damage my hair?

A. Yes, blow drying can damage your hair if you use a high heat setting and aim the blow dryer directly at your hair. To prevent damage, use a low or medium heat setting and hold the blow dryer at least 6 inches away from your hair. You should also use a heat protectant spray.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, it's important to take care of it to prevent damage and maintain your curls. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz

So, will blow drying your hair make it curly? The answer is yes and no. It all depends on your hair type, the blow dryer you use, and the way you use it. However, with the right technique and products, you can enhance your curls and create a beautiful hairstyle.
