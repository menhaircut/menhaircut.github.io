How To Work Out What Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Aprilia Aug 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly trying out different hairstyles only to find that they just don't suit you? It can be frustrating to spend hours in front of the mirror, only to end up feeling disappointed with the end result. However, there are ways to figure out what hairstyle will suit you best. In this article, we will go through some tips and steps to help you work out what hairstyle suits you.

  • How To Work Out What Hairstyle Suits You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Finding the Perfect Hairstyle
  • FAQs

How to Work Out What Hairstyle Suits You

The first step in figuring out what hairstyle suits you is to consider your face shape. Everyone's face is different, and certain hairstyles will suit certain face shapes better than others. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face. Instead, opt for a style that will elongate your face.

Another important factor to consider is your hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that require a lot of volume, as they can make your hair look flat and lifeless. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, you may want to avoid hairstyles that add too much bulk to your hair.

When choosing a hairstyle, it's also important to consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule and don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of upkeep.

Step by Step Guide

1. Determine your face shape: The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. There are several different face shapes, including round, oval, square, heart, and diamond. Once you know your face shape, you can start looking for hairstyles that will complement your features.

2. Consider your hair type: The next step is to consider your hair type. Do you have fine hair, thick hair, or something in between? Knowing your hair type will help you choose a hairstyle that will work well with your hair's natural texture and thickness.

3. Think about your lifestyle: Finally, think about your lifestyle. Are you someone who likes to spend a lot of time on your hair, or do you prefer a low-maintenance style? Consider your daily routine and choose a hairstyle that will work well with your lifestyle.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

1. Look for inspiration: One of the best ways to find the perfect hairstyle is to look for inspiration. Browse through magazines, social media, and websites for ideas that you like. Save photos of hairstyles that catch your eye so that you can show them to your stylist.

2. Consult with a stylist: If you're not sure which hairstyle will suit you best, don't be afraid to ask for help. A stylist can give you advice on which styles will work well with your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

3. Be open to change: Don't be afraid to try something new! Sometimes, the best hairstyle is one that you never would have considered before. Be open to trying new styles and trust your stylist's advice.

FAQs

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, it's recommended that you get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Q: How do I know if a hairstyle will suit me?

A: The best way to know if a hairstyle will suit you is to try it out! Consult with a stylist and ask for their opinion. They can give you advice on which styles will work well with your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suited to your hair type.

2. Avoid excessive heat styling, as it can damage your hair.

3. Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

4. Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to find a hairstyle that suits you perfectly. Remember to be open to trying new styles and consult with a stylist if you're not sure which hairstyle will work best for you.
