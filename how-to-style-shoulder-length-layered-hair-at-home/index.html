How To Style Shoulder Length Layered Hair At Home

Written by Christine Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have shoulder length layered hair, you may be wondering how to style it at home. Whether you want to create a sleek look for work or a tousled look for a night out, the possibilities are endless. In this article, we will guide you through how to style shoulder length layered hair at home, step by step. We will also provide you with tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Shoulder Length Layered Hair at Home
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Layered Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Advice for Shoulder Length Layered Hair

Step by Step Guide

1. Wash and Dry Your Hair

The first step to styling shoulder length layered hair is to wash and dry your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. After washing your hair, gently towel dry it or use a blow dryer on a low heat setting. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls.

2. Apply Heat Protectant

Before using any heat styling tools, make sure to apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

3. Straighten Your Hair

If you want to create a sleek look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, making sure to use a comb or brush to guide the hair through the flat iron. For a more natural look, you can leave the ends slightly curled.

4. Curl Your Hair

If you want to create loose curls, use a curling wand or curling iron to curl your hair. Start at the mid-length of your hair and wrap the hair around the barrel, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process until all of your hair is curled.

5. Use Hair Accessories

You can add some flair to your hairstyle by using hair accessories such as clips, headbands, or scrunchies. These accessories can help keep your hair out of your face and add a pop of color to your look.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Layered Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect style for your shoulder length layered hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Experiment with different styling products, such as mousse, hair gel, or hairspray, to achieve different looks.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques.
  • Take care of your hair by using a nourishing hair mask once a week and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my shoulder length layered hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it once or twice a week. If you exercise frequently or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash your hair more often.

Q: Can I color my shoulder length layered hair?

A: Yes, you can color your hair. However, it's important to use a hair dye that is suitable for your hair type and to follow the instructions carefully. You may also want to consult a professional stylist to help you achieve the desired color.

Hair Care Advice for Shoulder Length Layered Hair

Here are some tips to help you take care of your shoulder length layered hair:

  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a month to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.

With these tips and techniques, you can style your shoulder length layered hair at home and achieve the perfect look for any occasion. Remember to take care of your hair by using quality hair products and following a regular hair care routine. Happy styling!
