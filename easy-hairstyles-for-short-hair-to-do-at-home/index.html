Easy Hairstyles For Short Hair To Do At Home

Written by Frank Aug 11, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that your options for styling are limited. However, there are plenty of easy hairstyles for short hair that you can do at home. Whether you're looking for something quick and simple for everyday wear or a more polished look for a special occasion, these hairstyles are sure to impress. In this article, we'll go over some simple techniques for styling short hair, as well as some tips for taking care of your locks.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Hairstyle Tutorials
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair

How to Style Short Hair

The first step to styling short hair is to find the right products for your hair type. If you have fine hair, you may want to use a volumizing mousse or spray to add body. If your hair is curly or wavy, you might benefit from a curl-enhancing cream or gel. Once you have your products, you can start experimenting with different styles.

One of the easiest ways to style short hair is to add some texture. You can do this by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo on your roots and ends. Then, use your fingers to scrunch your hair for a messy, tousled look. You can also try using a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or curls.

Step-by-Step Hairstyle Tutorials

Here are some quick and easy hairstyles for short hair that you can try at home:

1. Half-Up Bun

Step 1: Split your hair down the middle and gather the top half into a ponytail.

Step 2: Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins.

Step 3: Allow the bottom half of your hair to hang loose.

2. Messy Top Knot

Step 1: Gather your hair into a high ponytail.

Step 2: Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with bobby pins.

Step 3: Use your fingers to pull out some pieces of hair around your face for a messy look.

3. Braided Crown

Step 1: Part your hair down the middle and braid each side from the front to the back.

Step 2: Cross the braids over the top of your head, tucking in the ends and securing with bobby pins.

Step 3: Use your fingers to pull out some pieces of hair around your face for a softer look.

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair:

  • Use products that are designed for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different textures and styles to find what works best for you.
  • Invest in a good quality styling tool, such as a curling iron or flat iron.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new!

FAQs

What are the best products for styling short hair?

The best products for styling short hair will depend on your hair type and the look you're going for. For fine hair, a volumizing mousse or spray can add body. For curly or wavy hair, a curl-enhancing cream or gel can help define your curls.

How do I keep my short hair looking healthy?

Keeping your short hair looking healthy requires regular maintenance, including regular trims to keep split ends at bay. You should also use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires regular maintenance to stay healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your locks:

  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

With these easy hairstyles for short hair and some simple hair care tips, you can keep your locks looking healthy and stylish. Whether you're heading to work or getting ready for a night out, these simple styles are sure to impress.
