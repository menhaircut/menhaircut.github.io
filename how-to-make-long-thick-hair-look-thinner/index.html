How To Make Long Thick Hair Look Thinner

Written by Luffy Jul 09, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have long thick hair, you know how gorgeous it can look when it’s styled just right. But sometimes, you might want to create a sleeker, more streamlined look. That’s where our tips for how to make long thick hair look thinner come in. We’ll walk you through the steps to help you achieve the look you want while still keeping your hair healthy and strong.

Description

Long thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it’s beautiful and can be styled in so many different ways, it can also be heavy and difficult to manage. It can also make your head feel hot and heavy, and it can take longer to dry and style than thinner hair. In this post, we’ll show you how to make long thick hair look thinner using a variety of techniques, products, and styling tools.

How to Make Long Thick Hair Look Thinner: Step by Step

Step 1: Get a Haircut

The first step in making long thick hair look thinner is to get a haircut. Long layers can help to thin out your hair and create movement and texture. You can also opt for a blunt cut with a few layers around your face to create a more sleek and streamlined look. Be sure to communicate your hair goals with your stylist so they can give you the cut that’s right for you.

Step 2: Use Products

Using the right products can also help to make long thick hair look thinner. Look for volumizing shampoos and conditioners that won’t weigh your hair down. You can also use texturizing sprays and dry shampoos to add texture and volume to your hair without adding weight.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can also help to make it look thinner. Use a round brush to create volume at the roots, and be sure to blow dry in sections to ensure that each section is fully dry. You can also use a straightening iron to create a sleek and straight look.

Step 4: Use the Right Styling Tools

Using the right styling tools can also make long thick hair look thinner. Look for flat irons with ceramic plates that will glide smoothly through your hair without snagging or pulling. You can also use a boar bristle brush to smooth your hair and create a sleek and polished look.

Tips for Making Long Thick Hair Look Thinner

Here are some additional tips for making long thick hair look thinner:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heavy oils and serums that can weigh your hair down.
  • When blow drying your hair, use a nozzle attachment to direct the airflow and create a sleeker look.
  • Consider getting a keratin treatment or other smoothing treatment to help tame your hair.

FAQ

Can I make my long thick hair look thinner without cutting it?

Yes! Using the right products and styling tools can help to make long thick hair look thinner without cutting it. Look for volumizing products and use a round brush to create volume at the roots.

What haircuts are best for making long thick hair look thinner?

Long layers and blunt cuts with a few face-framing layers can help to make long thick hair look thinner. Be sure to communicate your hair goals with your stylist so they can give you the cut that’s right for you.

Hair Care for Long Thick Hair

Long thick hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Here are some tips for caring for your long thick hair:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

By following these tips for how to make long thick hair look thinner, you can create the sleek and polished look you want while still keeping your hair healthy and strong. With the right haircuts, products, and styling tools, you can achieve any hair goal you have in mind.
