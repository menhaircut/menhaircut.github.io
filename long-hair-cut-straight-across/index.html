Long Hair Cut Straight Across A Timeless Classic

Written by Daniel Jun 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're someone who loves long hair, you know how important it is to keep it looking healthy and stylish. One of the most classic and timeless ways to wear long hair is by getting a straight cut across the ends. This style has been popular for decades and is still a favorite among many women. In this article, we'll take a closer look at how to achieve this look, as well as some tips and tricks for maintaining it.

Description

The long hair cut straight across is just as it sounds – a cut that goes straight across the ends of your hair. This style is known for its simplicity and the fact that it can be worn by just about anyone. It is a low-maintenance style that requires little effort to maintain and can look great with just a few styling products.

How to Cut Long Hair Straight Across

If you're looking to achieve this style, it's important to know how to cut your hair straight across. While you can certainly do this yourself, we recommend going to a professional stylist to get the best results. Here's a step-by-step guide to cutting long hair straight across:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you get an even cut.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Using a comb, divide your hair into four or more sections, depending on how thick your hair is. Make sure the sections are even in size and use hair clips to secure them in place.

Step 3: Cut Each Section Evenly

Starting with the bottom section, use sharp scissors to cut the hair straight across. Make sure you cut each section evenly, taking care not to cut too much off at once.

Step 4: Check for Evenness

After you've cut all the sections, check your hair in the mirror to make sure it's even. If you notice any uneven spots, go back and trim them until your hair is perfectly straight across.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair Cut Straight Across

Once you've achieved the long hair cut straight across, it's important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you keep your hair healthy and stylish:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.
  • Invest in a high-quality hairbrush to prevent tangles and breakage.

FAQ

Is the long hair cut straight across suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this style can be worn by anyone, regardless of their face shape. However, if you have a round face, you may want to add layers to your hair to create more dimension.

Can I achieve this style at home?

While it's certainly possible to cut your hair straight across at home, we recommend going to a professional stylist to get the best results.

How often should I trim my hair?

We recommend trimming your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care, the long hair cut straight across is a fairly low-maintenance style. However, there are a few things you can do to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to prevent it from becoming too dry.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve and maintain the long hair cut straight across with ease. Whether you're going for a sleek and sophisticated look or a more casual vibe, this style is a classic that never goes out of fashion.
