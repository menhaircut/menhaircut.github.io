Will Medium Short Hair Suit Me Find Out With These Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting a medium short haircut but are unsure if it will suit you? This is a common concern for many people, and it's understandable. After all, a haircut can completely transform your look and it's important to choose one that complements your facial features and personal style. In this article, we'll provide you with helpful tips and tricks to determine if medium short hair will suit you. We'll cover everything from face shape to hair texture, so let's get started.

Description

Medium short hair is a versatile and trendy haircut that falls between chin and shoulder length. It's a great option for those who want a low maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish and chic. This haircut works well for all hair types, from straight to curly, and can be easily customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How To Determine If Medium Short Hair Will Suit You

There are several factors to consider when determining if medium short hair will suit you. Here are some things to keep in mind:

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining which haircut will suit you. If you have a round face, a medium short haircut with layers can help elongate your face and create a more balanced look. If you have a square face, a bob or lob with soft layers can help soften your features. If you have an oval or heart-shaped face, you're in luck, as medium short hair suits these face shapes particularly well.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also impact how well a medium short haircut will suit you. If you have fine hair, a layered bob or lob can add volume and movement to your hair, making it look fuller. If you have thick hair, a blunt bob or lob can help tame your hair and make it more manageable. If you have curly hair, a layered cut can help define your curls and create a more polished look.

Personal Style

Ultimately, your personal style should also be considered when choosing a haircut. If you prefer a more classic look, a blunt bob or lob may be the best option for you. If you're more adventurous, a layered cut with bangs can add a touch of edge to your look.

Step By Step Guide To Determine If Medium Short Hair Will Suit You

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you determine if medium short hair will suit you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Then, compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Take note of your hair texture and consider which medium short haircut will work best for your hair type.

Step 3: Look for Inspiration

Search for inspiration photos of medium short haircuts that you like and that suit your face shape and hair texture.

Step 4: Consult with a Hairstylist

Consult with a hairstylist to determine which medium short haircut will suit you best. A hairstylist can provide you with personalized recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

Tips for Maintaining Medium Short Hair

Once you've determined that medium short hair will suit you, it's important to know how to maintain your new haircut. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when styling your hair to prevent damage.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are suited for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Will medium short hair suit me if I have a round face?

A: Yes, a layered medium short haircut can help elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Q: Can I still style my medium short hair in different ways?

A: Absolutely! Medium short hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, from sleek and straight to tousled and wavy.

Q: Will medium short hair suit me if I have thick hair?

A: Yes, a blunt bob or lob can help tame your hair and make it more manageable.

Conclusion

Determining if medium short hair will suit you requires considering your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. By following our tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a medium short haircut that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. Remember to consult with a hairstylist for personalized recommendations and to take good care of your new haircut to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
