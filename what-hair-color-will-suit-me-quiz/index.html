What Hair Color Will Suit Me Quiz Find Your Perfect Shade

Written by Bardi Jul 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair color but don't know what shade to switch to? Taking a hair color quiz can help you determine which color will best suit you. In this article, we'll walk you through how to take a hair color quiz and provide some tips for maintaining your new shade.

How to Take a Hair Color Quiz

There are many hair color quizzes available online, but not all of them are created equal. To find the best quiz for you, search for "what hair color will suit me quiz" and browse through the results. Look for quizzes that ask specific questions about your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. These factors can all affect which hair color will look best on you.

Once you've found a quiz you like, take your time answering each question honestly. Don't be afraid to take the quiz more than once if you're unsure about your answers. Some quizzes may also provide multiple results, so you can compare your options.

Step by Step Guide

1. Search for "what hair color will suit me quiz"
2. Browse through the results and find a quiz that asks specific questions about your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color.
3. Take your time answering each question honestly.
4. Don't be afraid to take the quiz more than once.
5. Compare your results and choose the hair color that best suits you.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Shade

Once you've chosen your new hair color, it's important to take care of it to ensure it looks great for as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining your new shade:

- Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
- Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the color.
- Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
- Get regular touch-ups to keep your color looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Can I take a hair color quiz if I have dyed hair?

A: Yes, you can still take a hair color quiz if you have dyed hair. However, keep in mind that the quiz may not take into account any damage or previous color that may affect the results.

Q: Is it better to go to a professional to dye my hair?

A: While there are many at-home hair dye kits available, it's generally safer to go to a professional. A stylist can help you choose the best color for your skin tone and provide tips for maintaining your new shade.

Q: Can I dye my hair a drastically different color than my natural hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair a drastically different color than your natural hair. However, keep in mind that this may require more upkeep and touch-ups than a more subtle change.

Hair Care

Regardless of which hair color you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to hydrate your hair.
- Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, especially when it's wet.
- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Conclusion

Taking a hair color quiz can be a fun and helpful way to determine which shade will best suit you. Remember to take your time answering each question honestly and to choose a quiz that asks specific questions about your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color. Once you've chosen your new shade, be sure to take care of it to keep it looking great.
