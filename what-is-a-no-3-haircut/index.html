What Is A No 3 Haircut

Written by William Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of always telling your barber what you want, but never quite getting the result you were hoping for? A No 3 haircut might be the answer! In this article, we will discuss what a No 3 haircut is, how to get it, and some tips for maintaining it.

Description

A No 3 haircut is a clipper cut that leaves hair 3/8 of an inch long. It is a popular choice for men who want a short, clean-cut look without going bald. The No 3 clipper guard is the third shortest guard size, with the No 1 being the shortest and the No 8 being the longest.

How to

Getting a No 3 haircut is relatively easy. Simply ask your barber for a No 3 clipper cut, or if you're cutting your own hair at home, attach the No 3 guard to your clippers and start cutting. Make sure to go against the grain of your hair for the best results.

If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, it's always best to go to a professional. They will be able to give you the exact look you're going for, and can also make recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step

Here is a step by step guide on how to get a No 3 haircut:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Attach the No 3 guard to your clippers.
  3. Start at the back of your head and work your way up, using short strokes against the grain of your hair.
  4. Once you've cut the back, move to the sides and then the top of your head.
  5. If you want a shorter cut on top, switch to a shorter guard size or use scissors.
  6. Use a comb to blend any lines or uneven spots.
  7. Finish with a trimmer to clean up any stray hairs around your neck and ears.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining a No 3 haircut:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Consider investing in a good quality pair of clippers for at-home touch-ups.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a side part or messy fringe, to switch up your look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about No 3 haircuts:

Is a No 3 haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a No 3 haircut can work for all hair types, from straight to curly. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in different ways depending on your personal preference.

Can I do a No 3 haircut at home?

Yes, you can do a No 3 haircut at home if you have the right tools and technique. However, if you're unsure, it's always best to go to a professional.

How often do I need to get a No 3 haircut?

It's recommended to get a No 3 haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape. However, this can vary depending on how fast your hair grows and how neat you want to keep your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo, conditioning your hair to add moisture and prevent breakage, and using a heat protectant if you style your hair with heat tools.

It's also important to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals, as these nutrients are essential for healthy hair growth. Drinking plenty of water and getting enough sleep can also help keep your hair looking shiny and strong.

In conclusion, a No 3 haircut is a popular choice for men who want a short, clean-cut look without going bald. Whether you're cutting your own hair at home or going to a professional, following a good hair care routine can help keep your hair looking its best.
