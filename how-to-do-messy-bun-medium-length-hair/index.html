How To Do Messy Bun Medium Length Hair In Relaxed English Language

If you're looking for an easy and effortless hairstyle that can be done in minutes, then a messy bun is the perfect choice for you. It's a great way to style your medium length hair when you're in a hurry or when you just want to keep your hair out of your face. In this article, we'll show you how to do a messy bun on medium length hair in relaxed English language. Let's get started!

How to Do Messy Bun on Medium Length Hair

The messy bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. It's perfect for medium length hair, as it can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your mood and the occasion. Here's how to do a messy bun on medium length hair:

What You'll Need:

  • Comb or Brush
  • Hair Elastic
  • Bobby Pins
  • Hairspray (optional)

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfect messy bun on medium length hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  3. Twist the ponytail loosely and wrap it around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with a hair elastic.
  5. Loosen the bun by pulling it gently apart with your fingers to create a messy look.
  6. Take a few strands of hair from the front of your head and wrap them around the hair elastic to hide it.
  7. Secure the strands of hair with a bobby pin.
  8. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair around the bun.
  9. Finish the look by spraying hairspray (optional).

Tips for Perfect Messy Bun

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy bun on medium length hair:

  • Use a hair elastic that matches your hair color to make it less visible.
  • Don't worry if your bun isn't perfect. Imperfections are what make the messy bun look so effortless.
  • Pull out a few strands of hair around your face for a more relaxed look.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair around the bun.
  • Use hairspray to set the bun in place and prevent flyaways.

FAQs

Can I do a messy bun on short hair?

Yes, you can still do a messy bun on short hair. However, you may need to use more bobby pins to secure the bun in place.

How can I make my messy bun stay in place all day?

Start by using a hair elastic that is tight enough to hold your hair in place. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair around the bun. Finish the look by using hairspray to set the bun in place and prevent flyaways.

Is a messy bun appropriate for a formal occasion?

Yes, a messy bun can be appropriate for a formal occasion. Just make sure to style it in a more polished way and use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your medium length hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup.
  • Condition your hair after every wash to keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to do a messy bun on medium length hair, you can easily create this effortless hairstyle whenever you want. Follow these simple steps, and you'll have a chic and stylish look in no time!
