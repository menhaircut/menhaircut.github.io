How To Cover Temple Hair Loss Tips And Tricks

Written by William Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Temple hair loss, also known as temporal hair loss, is a common hair loss problem that affects many people. It is characterized by thinning hair or hair loss around the temples, which can be a source of embarrassment and low self-esteem. If you're experiencing temple hair loss, don't worry. There are many ways to cover it up and restore your confidence. In this blog post, we'll explore some tips and tricks on how to cover temple hair loss.

How to

1. Hairstyles

One of the easiest ways to cover temple hair loss is by choosing the right hairstyle. You can opt for a side-swept fringe, which will cover the temples, or a layered haircut that will add volume to your hair. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair back, as they can worsen hair loss.

2. Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are a quick and easy way to cover up temple hair loss. They are made of keratin fibers that bind to your existing hair and create the illusion of thicker hair. Simply sprinkle the fibers on your temples and blend them in with your fingers. They are available in different colors to match your hair color.

3. Wigs and Hairpieces

If you have severe temple hair loss, wigs and hairpieces are a great option. They come in different styles and colors, and you can find one that matches your natural hair. Wigs and hairpieces can be a bit expensive, but they are a long-term solution to hair loss.

4. Hair Extensions

Hair extensions are another option to cover up temple hair loss. They can add volume and length to your hair, and you can clip them on your temples to cover up the thinning areas. However, be careful not to use heavy extensions that can pull on your hair and worsen hair loss.

Step by Step

1. Choose the Right Hairstyle

Start by choosing a hairstyle that covers your temples. A side-swept fringe or a layered haircut can work well. Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair back.

2. Use Hair Fibers

Apply hair fibers to your temples by sprinkling them on and blending them in with your fingers. Use a color that matches your hair color.

3. Try Wigs and Hairpieces

If you have severe temple hair loss, try a wig or hairpiece that matches your natural hair. They can be expensive but are a long-term solution.

4. Use Hair Extensions

Add volume and length to your hair by using hair extensions. Clip them on your temples to cover up the thinning areas.

Tips

- Avoid tight hairstyles that pull your hair back.

- Use hair fibers that match your hair color.

- Choose a wig or hairpiece that matches your natural hair color.

- Use light hair extensions that do not pull on your hair.

FAQ

Can temple hair loss be reversed?

Yes, in some cases, temple hair loss can be reversed with hair loss treatments such as Minoxidil or Finasteride.

What causes temple hair loss?

Temple hair loss can be caused by genetics, aging, hormonal changes, or medical conditions such as alopecia.

Are wigs and hairpieces uncomfortable?

No, wigs and hairpieces are designed to be comfortable and breathable, and they can be adjusted to fit your head.

Hair Care

To prevent further temple hair loss, take good care of your hair. Avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling, and use a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, and exercise regularly to improve circulation to your scalp.

With these tips and tricks, you can cover up temple hair loss and restore your confidence. Remember to take good care of your hair and consult a dermatologist if you have severe hair loss.
