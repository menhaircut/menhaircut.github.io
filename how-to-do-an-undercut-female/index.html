How To Do An Undercut Female The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Jun 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy new hairstyle, look no further than the undercut. Popularized in the 1920s and 30s, this hairstyle has made a major comeback in recent years, with women of all ages and backgrounds sporting the look. But how exactly do you achieve an undercut female? In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve this daring and stylish look.

Description

An undercut is a hairstyle where the sides and/or back of the head are shaved or closely cut, while the top is left longer. For women, this can be a bold and daring look that can be adapted to suit any hair texture or length.

How to

To achieve an undercut female, you'll need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Choose your undercut style

There are a few different styles of undercut to choose from, including the classic undercut, the side undercut, and the nape undercut. Each style involves shaving or cutting different sections of hair, so choose the one that suits your face shape and personal style.

Step 2: Gather your tools

You'll need a few tools to achieve an undercut female, including clippers or scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure you have everything you need before you start.

Step 3: Section off your hair

Using your comb, section off the hair you want to keep on top of your head. Clip it out of the way so you can focus on the undercut.

Step 4: Start shaving or cutting

Using your clippers or scissors, start shaving or cutting the hair on the sides and/or back of your head. Be careful to follow the natural shape of your head and use a mirror to check your progress.

Step 5: Refine the look

Once you've shaved or cut the hair to your desired length, use your clippers or scissors to refine the look. You may want to create a fade effect, where the hair gradually gets shorter as it approaches the shaved section.

Step 6: Style your hair

Once you're happy with your undercut, style the hair on top of your head as desired. You can leave it messy and textured, or slick it back for a more polished look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing an undercut female:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually go shorter if you're unsure.
  • Consult a professional stylist if you're not confident doing it yourself.
  • Consider the maintenance involved in an undercut before committing to the look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing an undercut female:

Q: Will an undercut suit my face shape?

A: Yes, there's an undercut style to suit every face shape. Consult with a stylist to find the right one for you.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with an undercut. It may take a bit of creativity to cover up the shaved section, but it's definitely doable.

Q: How often do I need to maintain my undercut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you keep the shaved section. Generally, you'll need to touch it up every few weeks to keep it looking sharp.

Hair Care

To keep your undercut looking its best, make sure you're taking good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using heat protectant when styling, and getting regular trims to keep split ends at bay.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect undercut female in no time. So go ahead, embrace your bold and daring side with this edgy and stylish look.
