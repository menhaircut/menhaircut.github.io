Description

The mens hair style short sides long top has become one of the most popular haircuts for men in recent years. This style features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that can be styled in a variety of ways. This versatility makes it a great choice for any man looking to switch up his look.

How to Achieve the Mens Hair Style Short Sides Long Top

Getting the mens hair style short sides long top is easy, but it does require some effort. You will need to visit a barber or stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. They will be able to help you achieve the perfect look for your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving the mens hair style short sides long top:

  1. Choose a reputable barber or stylist who has experience with this cut.
  2. Decide on the length of the hair on the sides and back of your head.
  3. Decide on the length of the hair on the top of your head.
  4. Ask your barber or stylist to use clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head to the desired length.
  5. Ask your barber or stylist to use scissors to trim the hair on the top of your head to the desired length.
  6. Style your hair on top using a hair product of your choice.

Tips for Maintaining the Mens Hair Style Short Sides Long Top

Maintaining the mens hair style short sides long top requires some effort, but it is worth it to keep your hair looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining this style:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly to keep your hair trimmed and looking neat.
  • Use a high-quality hair product to style your hair on top.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about the mens hair style short sides long top:

Q: Will this style work for all hair types?

A: Yes, this style can work for all hair types, but it may require some adjustments to achieve the desired look.

Q: How often should I get this cut?

A: It is recommended to get this cut every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I style my hair on top differently each day?

A: Yes, the mens hair style short sides long top is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit your mood and outfit.

Hair Care for the Mens Hair Style Short Sides Long Top

Maintaining the mens hair style short sides long top also involves proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you need to use heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using too much hair product, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.

With these tips and a little effort, you can easily achieve and maintain the mens hair style short sides long top. This versatile haircut is perfect for any occasion and can be tailored to suit your personal style.
