How To Style Bangs For Long Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have long hair and you want to add some flair to your look, bangs are a great option. But styling bangs can be tricky, especially if you're new to the game. Don't worry, though, because we're here to help. In this article, we'll show you how to style bangs for long hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Bangs for Long Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Bangs

How to Style Bangs for Long Hair

The first thing you need to do when styling bangs is to decide what type of bangs you want. There are different types of bangs, including blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, and wispy bangs. Once you've decided on the type of bangs you want, you can start styling them.

Here are some general tips to keep in mind when styling bangs for long hair:

  • Blow-dry your bangs first before styling them
  • Use a round brush to create volume
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs
  • Apply some hairspray to hold your bangs in place

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the general tips, here's a step-by-step guide on how to style bangs for long hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a towel to dry off your hair slightly, but don't completely dry it.
  2. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush. Make sure to blow-dry them in the direction you want them to fall.
  3. If you want to add some volume to your bangs, use a round brush to lift them up and blow-dry them from the roots.
  4. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to straighten them. Make sure to be careful not to burn your skin or hair.
  5. If you have side-swept bangs, use a flat iron to create a slight curve in them.
  6. Apply some hairspray to your bangs to hold them in place.
  7. Use a comb to gently comb out any tangles.
  8. Style the rest of your hair as desired. You can leave it down or put it up in a ponytail or bun.

Tips for Styling Bangs

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage
  • Apply some dry shampoo to your bangs if they get oily throughout the day
  • Use a hairspray that is specifically designed for holding bangs in place
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them looking fresh
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling bangs for long hair:

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

What type of bangs should I get?

The type of bangs you get depends on your face shape and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best bangs for you.

Can I style my bangs if they're wet?

No, you should never style your bangs when they're wet. This can cause damage to your hair and make your bangs look uneven.

Hair Care for Bangs

Here are some tips for taking care of your bangs:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days
  • Apply a hair mask to your bangs once a week to keep them healthy
  • Avoid using too much heat on your bangs
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much, as this can make them oily

By following these tips, you can keep your bangs looking stylish and healthy.

In conclusion, styling bangs for long hair can be a bit of a challenge, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the look you want. Remember to take good care of your hair, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Happy styling!
