Guys Long Hairstyles 2021 How To Rock Long Hair In Style

Written by Eveline Jul 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for some inspiration for your long locks this year? Look no further! In this article, we'll go over some of the hottest guys long hairstyles for 2021. From messy man buns to sleek slick backs, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Out Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Long Hair
  • FAQs about Guys Long Hairstyles

How to Grow Out Your Hair

If you're starting from scratch and want to grow out your hair, it's important to be patient. Depending on how fast your hair grows, it can take anywhere from six months to a year to get the length you want. During this time, you may experience some awkward phases where your hair is too long to style like a short haircut but not quite long enough to achieve the look you want.

To help you get through this phase, we recommend keeping your hair trimmed every six to eight weeks. This will help keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends from causing breakage. You can also experiment with different hair products, such as pomades or waxes, to help you style your hair in new and creative ways.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Long Hair

Once you've grown out your hair to the desired length, it's time to start experimenting with different styles. Here's a step-by-step guide to a simple yet stylish long hair look:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  2. Towel-dry your hair and apply a small amount of styling product to your hands.
  3. Work the product through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. For a messy look, tousle your hair with your fingers. For a sleeker look, use a comb to create a sharp part.
  5. Finish with a bit of hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and strong. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your long locks:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting at the tips and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Be gentle when washing your hair, and avoid using hot water, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQs about Guys Long Hairstyles

Q: What are some popular long hairstyles for guys in 2021?

A: Some of the most popular long hairstyles for guys in 2021 include the man bun, the slick back, and the messy surfer look.

Q: How often should I wash my long hair?

A: It's important to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy, but washing too often can strip your hair of its natural oils. We recommend washing your hair every two to three days.

Q: What kind of hair products should I use for long hair?

A: The type of hair products you use will depend on your hair type and the style you're trying to achieve. Pomades and waxes are great for creating a slick, polished look, while sea salt sprays can help you achieve a beachy, tousled look.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep in mind as you rock your new long hair look:

  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair, especially when it's wet.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or treatments that can damage your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock any of the hottest guys long hairstyles for 2021 with confidence and style. Whether you prefer a sleek, polished look or a more relaxed, beachy vibe, there's a long hair look out there for everyone. So go ahead and embrace your inner rockstar – your long locks will thank you!
