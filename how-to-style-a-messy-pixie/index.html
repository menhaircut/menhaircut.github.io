How To Style A Messy Pixie

Written by Robby Jul 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your boring old haircut and want to try something new and exciting? Look no further than the messy pixie cut! This trendy and effortless style is perfect for those who want to switch up their look and add some edge to their hair. In this article, we will go over how to style a messy pixie in easy-to-understand language.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style a Messy Pixie
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips for Maintaining a Messy Pixie
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for a Messy Pixie

How to Style a Messy Pixie

First things first, let's talk about the basics of the messy pixie cut. This style is all about volume and texture, so it's important to have the right cut before you start styling. Make sure your hair is cut short and layered, with longer pieces on top and shorter pieces around the sides and back.

Once you have the right cut, it's time to start styling! Here are the steps to achieve a perfectly messy pixie:

Step-by-Step

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is too clean, it may be too slippery to style, so try washing it the night before.
  2. Apply a texturizing spray or pomade to your hair, focusing on the roots and ends. This will help create volume and definition.
  3. Using your fingers, tousle your hair to create a messy, undone look. Don't worry about making it perfect – the beauty of the messy pixie is in its imperfection.
  4. If you want to add even more texture, use a curling iron or flat iron to create random waves or curls throughout your hair.
  5. Finish off the look with a light hairspray or dry shampoo to hold everything in place.

And voila – you have a perfectly styled messy pixie!

Tips for Maintaining a Messy Pixie

Now that you know how to style a messy pixie, here are some tips for keeping it looking great:

  • Use a good quality texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and definition to your hair.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your pixie cut and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to keep your look fresh and exciting.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about styling a messy pixie:

Q: Can I style a messy pixie with wet hair?
A: It's best to start with dry hair, as wet hair can be too slippery to style properly. Q: How often should I wash my hair with a messy pixie cut?
A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people with a pixie cut wash their hair every 2-3 days. Q: Can I use a straightener to style my messy pixie?
A: Yes, a straightener can be a great tool for adding texture and waves to your hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant spray before styling.

Hair Care for a Messy Pixie

Finally, let's talk about how to care for your hair with a messy pixie cut:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality dry shampoo to help absorb excess oil and keep your hair looking fresh between washes.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a perfectly messy pixie in no time. So go ahead and embrace your inner rebel – your hair will thank you for it!
