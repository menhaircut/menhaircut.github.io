How To Put In A Messy Bun Hair Piece A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying to create the perfect messy bun but always falling short? A messy bun hair piece may be the solution you've been looking for. This hair accessory can help you achieve the messy bun look effortlessly. In this article, we will guide you through the steps on how to put in a messy bun hair piece.

Description

A messy bun hair piece is a hair accessory that can be used to create an effortless messy bun. It is a synthetic hairpiece that is designed to blend in with your natural hair. It comes in different shades to match your hair color, and it can be styled in various ways to achieve different looks.

How to Put in a Messy Bun Hair Piece

Step 1: Gather Your Materials

Before you start, you will need a few things: a messy bun hair piece, hair ties, bobby pins, a comb, and hairspray.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Brush your hair to remove any tangles and make sure it is smooth. If you want to add some texture, you can use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.

Step 3: Create a Ponytail

Pull your hair back into a ponytail and secure it with a hair tie. The placement of the ponytail will depend on where you want your messy bun to sit. You can have it high on your head, in the middle, or low at the nape of your neck.

Step 4: Place the Hair Piece

Take the messy bun hair piece and slide it over your ponytail. Make sure the hairpiece is secure and covers the hair tie. You can adjust the hairpiece to sit higher or lower, depending on the look you want to achieve.

Step 5: Wrap Your Hair

Take the hair from your ponytail and wrap it around the hairpiece. Use bobby pins to secure the hair in place. You can leave some hair out to create a more effortless and messy look.

Step 6: Secure Your Bun

Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair and make sure the bun is secure. You can also use hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

  • Use a hairpiece that matches your hair color to make it look more natural.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with the placement of the hairpiece to achieve different looks.
  • Use bobby pins to secure any loose hair and keep your bun in place.
  • Add some texture to your hair before putting in the hairpiece to create a more effortless look.

FAQ

Can I wear a messy bun hairpiece every day?

Yes, you can wear a messy bun hairpiece every day if you want to. Just make sure you take care of your natural hair and don't put too much tension on it.

How do I clean my messy bun hairpiece?

You can clean your messy bun hairpiece with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Make sure you rinse it thoroughly and let it air dry.

Can I style my messy bun hairpiece?

Yes, you can style your messy bun hairpiece. You can use heat tools like a curling iron or straightener to create different looks.

Hair Care

Make sure you take care of your natural hair even when wearing a hairpiece. Use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat tools too often. Give your hair a break from hairpieces and styles that put tension on it.

Now that you know how to put in a messy bun hairpiece, you can achieve the perfect messy bun effortlessly. Play around with different styles and enjoy the convenience of this hair accessory.
