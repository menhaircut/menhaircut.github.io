Pixie Cuts For Over 60S A Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new haircut, you might be considering a pixie cut. Pixie cuts are short, low-maintenance hairstyles that can be both stylish and practical. In this guide, we'll go over everything you need to know about getting a pixie cut as an over 60.

Description

Pixie cuts are short hairstyles that typically feature short layers and a tapered back. They can be worn with or without bangs and can be styled in a variety of ways. Pixie cuts are a popular choice for women over 60 because they are easy to manage and can make you look youthful and trendy.

How to Get a Pixie Cut

Step 1: Find a reputable stylist

The first step in getting a pixie cut is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting short hair. Look for a stylist who has a portfolio of pixie cuts they've done in the past and who understands the unique challenges of cutting hair for older women.

Step 2: Choose a style

Once you've found a stylist, it's time to choose a style. There are many different types of pixie cuts, so it's important to choose one that complements your face shape and hair texture. Some popular pixie cut styles for over 60s include the classic pixie, the layered pixie, and the textured pixie.

Step 3: Get the cut

Once you've chosen a style, it's time to get the cut. Your stylist will use scissors and/or clippers to cut your hair to the desired length and shape. They may also use texturizing techniques to add volume and movement to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining a Pixie Cut

Once you've gotten your pixie cut, there are a few tips you can follow to keep it looking great:

  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your cut
  • Use a light styling product to add texture and volume
  • Avoid using heavy products or over-styling your hair, as this can weigh it down
  • Consider using a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes

FAQ

Q: Will a pixie cut look good on me if I have thinning hair?

A: Yes! Pixie cuts can actually be a great choice for women with thinning hair, as they can add volume and texture to your hair. Talk to your stylist about which type of pixie cut would work best for your hair texture and face shape.

Q: How often will I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: It's recommended that you get your pixie cut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in different styles if I get a pixie cut?

A: Yes! While pixie cuts are typically short, they can still be styled in a variety of ways. You can add texture and volume with styling products, or use accessories like headbands and clips to change up your look.

Hair Care for Pixie Cuts

When it comes to caring for your pixie cut, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, as these can damage your hair
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and healthy
  • Cover your hair with a scarf or hat when you're outside to protect it from the sun and wind

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a stylish and low-maintenance pixie cut at any age!
