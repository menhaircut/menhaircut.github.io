How To Know Which Haircut Suits You A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Jun 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't flatter your face shape or hair texture? It's time to take matters into your own hands and figure out which haircut suits you best. In this article, we'll provide you with tips on how to determine the best haircut for your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in figuring out which haircut suits you is to determine your face shape. Your face shape will dictate which hairstyles will look best on you. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. Take note of the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face. Use these characteristics to match your face shape to one of the six basic shapes.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start to look for hairstyles that will flatter your face shape. Keep in mind that some hairstyles will look better on certain face shapes than others.

Understanding Your Hair Texture

Another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut is your hair texture. Hair texture refers to the thickness and coarseness of your hair. There are three basic hair textures: fine, medium, and thick.

For fine hair, you'll want to choose a haircut that adds volume and texture. Layers and choppy cuts work well for fine hair. For medium hair, you have more options. You can choose a variety of cuts from short to long. Thick hair benefits from layered cuts and texturizing to help thin it out.

Matching Your Haircut to Your Personal Style

Your personal style is another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. If you have a more classic or conservative style, you may want to choose a more traditional haircut. If you have a more trendy or edgy style, you may want to choose a more modern or unique haircut.

It's important to choose a haircut that not only flatters your face shape and hair texture but also matches your personal style. This will help you feel confident and comfortable with your new haircut.

Haircut Tips for Different Face Shapes

Now that you know your face shape and hair texture, it's time to explore some haircut options. Here are some haircut tips for different face shapes:

Oval Face Shape

An oval face shape is considered the ideal face shape. Many hairstyles work well with this face shape, including long layers, a bob, or a pixie cut.

Round Face Shape

A round face shape benefits from hairstyles that add height and length. Long layers, a side-swept bang, or a long bob can help elongate the face.

Square Face Shape

A square face shape benefits from hairstyles that soften the angles of the face. Soft layers, a side-swept bang, or a medium-length bob can help create a more rounded appearance.

Heart Face Shape

A heart face shape benefits from hairstyles that add width to the forehead and temples. Side-swept bangs, a chin-length bob, or a layered cut can help create balance.

Diamond Face Shape

A diamond face shape benefits from hairstyles that add width to the forehead and chin. A chin-length bob or a layered cut can help create balance.

Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape benefits from hairstyles that add width to the face. A side-swept bang, a medium-length bob, or a layered cut can help create balance.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every six to eight weeks to maintain your hairstyle.

Can I pull off a short haircut?

Short haircuts can work for many face shapes and hair textures. It's important to choose a short haircut that flatters your face shape and hair texture.

Will a haircut damage my hair?

A haircut itself will not damage your hair. However, if you use heat styling tools or chemical treatments, these can cause damage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have your new haircut, it's important to take care of it to maintain its shape and style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specific to your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your new haircut looking fresh and stylish.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut can seem overwhelming, but by determining your face shape, hair texture, and personal style, you can narrow down your options. By following our haircut tips, you can find the perfect haircut for you. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its shape and style.
