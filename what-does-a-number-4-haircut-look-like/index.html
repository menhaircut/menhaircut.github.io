What Does A Number 4 Haircut Look Like

Written by Oliver Nov 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you wondering what a number 4 haircut looks like? Maybe you want to try it out but you're not sure what to expect. Look no further as we break down everything you need to know about this popular hairstyle. From its appearance to the styling process, we've got you covered.

Description

A number 4 haircut is a medium-length hairstyle that leaves about 1/2 inch of hair on the scalp. This haircut is typically achieved using clippers with a number 4 guard attached, hence the name. The hair on top of the head is usually left longer than the sides and back, creating a neat and tidy appearance.

How To

If you're interested in getting a number 4 haircut, you can either go to a professional hairstylist or do it yourself at home. Here's how to achieve the look:

Go to a Professional

If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, it's best to go to a professional hairstylist. They will know exactly how to achieve the number 4 haircut and can even give you some styling tips. Just make sure to bring a picture or explain what you want in detail to ensure you get the desired result.

Do it Yourself

If you're feeling confident, you can do a number 4 haircut yourself at home. Here are the steps to follow:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair
  2. Attach a number 4 guard to your clippers
  3. Starting at the sides, run the clippers up to the top of the head
  4. Repeat on the other side
  5. Move to the back of the head and repeat, making sure to blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides
  6. Trim the hair on top of the head with scissors to the desired length
  7. Style as desired

Tips

If you're doing a number 4 haircut at home, here are some tips to ensure you get the best result:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head
  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if needed
  • Trim the hair on top slowly and carefully to avoid cutting too much off
  • Take breaks if you need to rest your arms or hands
  • Clean your clippers after use

FAQ

Is a number 4 haircut suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a number 4 haircut is suitable for all hair types. However, it may not look the same on everyone due to differences in hair texture and thickness.

Can I style my hair differently after getting a number 4 haircut?

Yes, you can style your hair differently after getting a number 4 haircut. The length on top allows for various styling options such as slicking it back, adding volume, or even spiking it up.

How often should I get a number 4 haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to maintain the length. Typically, a number 4 haircut can last anywhere from 4-6 weeks before needing a touch-up.

Hair Care

To keep your number 4 haircut looking its best, it's important to maintain good hair care habits. This includes washing and conditioning regularly, using a wide-tooth comb to detangle, and using a heat protectant when styling with hot tools.

Now that you know what a number 4 haircut looks like and how to achieve it, you can confidently rock this stylish and versatile hairstyle.
