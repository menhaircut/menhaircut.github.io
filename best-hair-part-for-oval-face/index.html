Best Hair Part For Oval Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle but don't know how to switch things up? One simple change that can make a big impact is changing your hair part. But with an oval face shape, it can be hard to know which part will flatter your features the best. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about finding the best hair part for oval face shapes.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Find Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Part
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Your Face Shape

Description

Before we dive into finding the best hair part for oval face shapes, let's first define what an oval face shape is. An oval face shape is characterized by a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin, with high cheekbones and a gently rounded jawline. This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and balanced, meaning that most hair parts will suit it well.

How To Find Your Face Shape

If you're unsure of your face shape, there are a few ways you can determine it. One method is to stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a washable marker. Then, step back and analyze the shape you've drawn. Another method is to take a selfie and use an app or website that can analyze your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's move on to finding the best hair part for oval face shapes. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with a Center Part

A center part is a great starting point for oval face shapes. This part will highlight your balanced features and can work well with any hair length.

Step 2: Try a Side Part

If you're looking for a change from your center part, try a side part. A deep side part can add volume to one side of your hair and create a flattering asymmetrical look.

Step 3: Experiment with Off-Center Parts

If you want to add a bit of edge to your look, try an off-center part. This can be a subtle shift from your center part or a more dramatic side part.

Step 4: Test Out Zigzag Parts

Zigzag parts are a fun way to add interest to your hairstyle. Try creating a zigzag part with a comb or your fingers for a unique look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Part

Once you've found your perfect hair part, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a fine-tooth comb to create a precise part.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction of your part to set it in place.
  • Use hairspray or hair gel to keep your part in place throughout the day.
  • Touch up your part throughout the day if it starts to shift.

FAQ

What if I have bangs?

If you have bangs, you can still experiment with different parts. Try a center part with your bangs swept to the side, or a side part with your bangs straight down.

What if I have curly hair?

Curly hair can be more difficult to part due to its texture. Try experimenting with a zigzag part or a center part that is slightly off-center.

Can I change my part every day?

Yes! Changing your part is a quick and easy way to switch up your hairstyle. Just be sure to use a fine-tooth comb to create a precise part each time.

Hair Care for Your Face Shape

In addition to finding the best hair part for your oval face shape, it's important to care for your hair properly. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.

Now that you know how to find the best hair part for oval face shapes, it's time to experiment with different styles! Remember, your hair part can make a big impact on your overall look, so don't be afraid to try something new.
