Best Short Haircut For Oblong Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Christine Sep 11, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oblong face, finding the right hairstyle can be a bit challenging. But fret not, we’re here to help! In this guide, we’ll be discussing the best short haircut for oblong face shapes. We’ll also be sharing tips on how to style and care for your hair, so keep reading!

Best Short Haircut for Oblong Face

One of the best short haircuts for oblong faces is the bob. This style adds volume to the sides of your face, making it appear wider and more balanced. You can opt for a chin-length bob, a longer bob with layers, or an asymmetrical bob.

Another great option is the pixie cut. It’s a low-maintenance style that works well for busy individuals. You can choose a classic pixie cut or add some layers to give it more texture and volume.

If you’re feeling adventurous, you can try a short shag haircut. This style is all about texture and movement, which is perfect for oblong faces. It adds volume to the crown of your head, making your face appear shorter.

How to Style Your Hair

Styling your hair is just as important as getting the right cut. Here are some tips on how to style your hair based on the short haircut you’ve chosen:

  • Bob: Blow dry your hair with a round brush to add volume. You can also use a flat iron to create a sleek look.
  • Pixie Cut: Apply pomade or wax to your hair to create texture and hold. You can also use a flat iron to smooth out any frizz.
  • Short Shag: Use a texturizing spray to create movement and volume. You can also use a flat iron to create waves or curls.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Cut

Getting the right cut is crucial for oblong faces. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect short haircut:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist and show them pictures of the haircut you want. They’ll be able to advise you on whether it’s suitable for your face shape.
  2. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to create volume and texture.
  3. Consider adding bangs to shorten the length of your face. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs work well for oblong faces.
  4. Take note of the styling tips your hairstylist gives you. They’ll know how to style your hair to complement your face shape.

Tips for Styling and Maintaining Your Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style and maintain your short haircut:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Invest in a good styling product, such as pomade or texturizing spray, to create the look you want.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQs

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, opt for a shorter bob with layers. This will create the illusion of thicker hair.

Can I still wear a bob if I have a long face?

Absolutely! Bobs work well for long faces too. Just make sure to add volume to the sides of your face to balance it out.

How often should I wash my hair?

It’s best to wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry. If you have oily hair, you can wash it every day.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different kind of care compared to longer hair. Here are some tips to keep your short haircut looking healthy and shiny:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect short haircut for your oblong face shape. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Happy styling!
