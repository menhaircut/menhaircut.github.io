Long Brown Hair With Layers And Side Bangs

Written by Christine Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will give you a fresh look? Long brown hair with layers and side bangs might be the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle is versatile, easy to maintain, and can suit any face shape. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this hairstyle, tips, and hair care.

Description

Long brown hair with layers and side bangs is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for years. The layers add volume and texture to the hair, while the side bangs frame the face and give a youthful look. This hairstyle can be worn straight or wavy and can be styled in different ways. It is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance yet stylish look.

How To

To achieve long brown hair with layers and side bangs, you need to follow these steps:

  1. Start with clean and dry hair.
  2. Section your hair into layers, starting from the bottom and moving upwards.
  3. Take a small section of hair and cut it at an angle.
  4. Repeat step 3 for all the layers.
  5. For the side bangs, section your hair from the crown to the ear.
  6. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the shortest point at the front and gradually getting longer towards the back.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

It is recommended to have a professional stylist cut your hair to achieve the perfect look.

Step By Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to achieve long brown hair with layers and side bangs:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and comb it through to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into layers, starting from the bottom and moving upwards. Use hair clips to secure each layer as you go.

Step 3: Cut Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and cut it at an angle. Repeat step 3 for all the layers. For the side bangs, section your hair from the crown to the ear. Cut the hair at an angle, starting from the shortest point at the front and gradually getting longer towards the back.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired. You can blow-dry your hair straight or use a curling iron to create loose waves. Apply a styling product to your hair to add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling long brown hair with layers and side bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the layers and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long brown hair with layers and side bangs:

Is long brown hair with layers and side bangs suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this hairstyle can suit any face shape. The layers and side bangs can be adjusted to flatter the face shape.

How often should I trim my hair?

It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the layers and prevent split ends.

Can I style my hair without using hot tools?

Yes, you can air-dry your hair and use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long brown hair with layers and side bangs healthy:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Long brown hair with layers and side bangs is a timeless hairstyle that can give you a fresh and youthful look. Follow these tips and hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
