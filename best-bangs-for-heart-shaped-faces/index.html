Best Bangs For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Aprilia Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you know how important it is to find the right haircut and hairstyle to flatter your features. One of the most popular and versatile options is bangs. However, not all bangs are created equal. In this article, we will discuss the best bangs for heart shaped faces, including how to style them and care for them.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. The goal of choosing the right bangs is to balance out the face shape and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones while softening the chin. The best bangs for heart-shaped faces are those that hit right above the eyebrows or just below the cheekbones.

How to

When choosing the best bangs for a heart-shaped face, it's essential to consider the length, thickness, and texture of the hair. The best bangs for heart-shaped faces are generally long and side-swept or blunt and thick. Side-swept bangs that are longer on the sides than in the middle can help to balance out the face shape. Blunt bangs that hit just above the eyebrows can also be flattering for heart-shaped faces.

Step by Step

To achieve the perfect bangs for a heart-shaped face, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best bangs for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose a length and style that flatters your features, such as side-swept or blunt bangs.
  3. Maintain your bangs with regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and healthy.
  4. Style your bangs with a round brush and blow dryer to add volume and shape.
  5. Use a lightweight styling product, such as a hair wax or pomade, to add texture and hold.

Tips

When styling bangs for a heart-shaped face, it's essential to consider the overall balance of the face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Opt for side-swept or blunt bangs that hit just above the eyebrows or below the cheekbones.
  • Avoid short, choppy bangs that accentuate the wideness of the forehead.
  • Use a volumizing product to add body and texture to the bangs.
  • Consider incorporating layers into the rest of the hair to create a more balanced look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best bangs for heart-shaped faces:

Q: Can heart-shaped faces pull off bangs?

A: Yes, heart-shaped faces can pull off bangs. However, it's important to choose the right style and length to balance out the face shape.

Q: What type of bangs should heart-shaped faces avoid?

A: Heart-shaped faces should avoid short, choppy bangs that accentuate the wideness of the forehead.

Q: How often should I trim my bangs?

A: To keep your bangs looking fresh and healthy, it's recommended to trim them every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

To keep your bangs looking their best, it's essential to take good care of them. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, beautiful bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your bangs, as they can cause damage and split ends.
  • Use a lightweight, hydrating conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your bangs, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Consider incorporating a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment into your weekly hair care routine to keep your bangs healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, the best bangs for heart-shaped faces are those that flatter your features and balance out your face shape. Whether you opt for side-swept or blunt bangs, be sure to consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect style for you. With proper care and styling, you can enjoy beautiful, flattering bangs that enhance your natural beauty.
