How Long Does It Take To Grow Slicked Back Hair

Written by Aprilia Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Growing slicked back hair can be a challenging process, especially if you have short hair. However, with patience and the right hair care routine, you can achieve the slicked back hairstyle you desire. In this article, we will discuss how long it takes to grow slicked back hair, how to do it, and some tips and FAQs to help you along the way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Grow Slicked Back Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Growing Slicked Back Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

How to Grow Slicked Back Hair

The first step in growing slicked back hair is to have patience. Depending on your hair length and the style you want to achieve, it can take anywhere from 6 months to a year or more to grow your hair long enough to slick it back.

Another important factor in growing slicked back hair is to have a healthy hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a good conditioner, and avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by growing your hair out to the desired length. This can take anywhere from 6 months to a year or more.

2. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

3. Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair.

4. Once your hair is long enough, comb it straight back using a fine-tooth comb.

5. Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair to keep it in place.

6. Use your fingers to smooth out any bumps or unevenness in your hair.

7. Repeat this process every day until your hair is consistently slicked back.

Tips for Growing Slicked Back Hair

- Be patient: Growing slicked back hair takes time, so don't expect overnight results.

- Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.

- Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair.

- Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

- Experiment with different hair products to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I grow slicked back hair if I have short hair?

A: Yes, but it may take longer to achieve the desired length.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how often you use hair products. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: What kind of hair products should I use?

A: It's best to experiment with different products to find the one that works best for your hair. Look for products that provide hold without making your hair feel greasy or weighed down.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

Once you've achieved your desired slicked back hairstyle, it's important to maintain a healthy hair care routine. This includes regular washing and conditioning, getting regular trims to prevent split ends, and using hair products that are gentle on your hair.

Additionally, it's important to avoid pulling your hair back too tightly or using hair elastics that can cause damage to your hair. Instead, use a hair tie that is gentle on your hair, or simply let your hair down when you're not wearing it slicked back.

Overall, growing slicked back hair takes time and patience, but with the right hair care routine and the right products, you can achieve the sleek, sophisticated look you're after.
