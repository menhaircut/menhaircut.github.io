Best Hair Cut For Oblong Face Female A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Luffy Sep 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want to enhance your features and balance out the length of your face, but not all hairstyles are created equal. In this guide, we'll explore the best hair cut for oblong face female and provide tips on how to care for your hair afterwards.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a straight forehead and a narrow chin. The goal of a flattering haircut is to add volume and width to the sides of the face, while avoiding styles that emphasize the length of the face. The best hair cut for oblong face female will typically have layers, bangs, or waves to create dimension and balance.

How to

When choosing a haircut, consider the following:

  • Length: Stay away from haircuts that are too long or too short. A length that hits just below the collarbone or above the shoulders is ideal.
  • Layers: Layers add texture and movement to the hair, which can visually widen the face. Opt for long layers or choppy layers to create volume.
  • Bangs: Bangs can complement an oblong face shape by shortening the forehead and adding width to the face. Side-swept bangs or curtain bangs are great options.
  • Waves: Soft waves or curls can help soften the angles of an oblong face shape. Use a curling iron or rollers to add texture to your hair.

Step by Step

To achieve the best hair cut for oblong face female, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with a hairstylist who is familiar with oblong face shapes.
  2. Choose a length that hits just below the collarbone or above the shoulders.
  3. Add layers to create volume and texture.
  4. Consider adding bangs, either side-swept or curtain.
  5. Add soft waves or curls for dimension.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling and caring for your hair:

  • Use volumizing products: Products that add volume, such as mousse or root-lifting spray, can help create the illusion of width in the face.
  • Avoid straight, flat hair: Straight, flat hair can accentuate the length of an oblong face shape. Use a curling iron or rollers to add texture.
  • Trim your hair regularly: To keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends, trim your hair every 6-8 weeks.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair cut for oblong face female:

  • Can I wear my hair in a ponytail? Yes, but avoid pulling your hair back tightly as this can emphasize the length of your face. Leave some hair out around your face to soften the look.
  • What if I don't want bangs? Bangs are not a requirement for oblong face shapes, but they can be helpful for balancing out the length of the face. If you prefer not to have bangs, try adding layers or waves instead.
  • What if my hair is naturally straight? Straight hair can work well for oblong face shapes, but be sure to add some texture with layers or waves.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, shiny mane. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo: Choose a shampoo that is free of harsh chemicals and sulfates to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition regularly: Conditioner helps to soften and detangle hair, making it easier to manage.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat can damage your hair, so limit your use of hot tools like curling irons and straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: UV rays can damage your hair, so wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF when spending time outdoors.

By following these tips and choosing the best hair cut for oblong face female, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your hairstyle.
