Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Straight Hair

If you have shoulder length straight hair and are looking for the perfect wedding hairstyle, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will be discussing some of the best wedding hairstyles for shoulder length straight hair that will make you look stunning on your special day.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder length straight hair is a versatile hair type that can be styled in many different ways. Whether you want a classic updo, a trendy half-up half-down style, or a simple but elegant look, there are plenty of options when it comes to wedding hairstyles for shoulder length straight hair.

How To

Before we get into the specific hairstyles, it's important to know how to prepare your hair for styling. First, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray if you plan on using hot tools. Use a comb to detangle your hair, and then section it off into manageable sections.

Step By Step

Classic Updo

This elegant hairstyle is perfect for a formal wedding. To achieve this look, start by pulling your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun. Secure the bun with bobby pins and finish with hairspray.

Half-Up Half-Down

This trendy hairstyle is perfect for a more relaxed wedding. Start by curling your hair with a curling iron. Then, take a section of hair from each side of your head and pull it back, securing it with a bobby pin. Leave the rest of your hair down and tousled.

Simple and Elegant

If you want a more understated look, a simple and elegant hairstyle is the way to go. Start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, take a small section of hair from each side and twist it back, securing it with bobby pins. Leave the rest of your hair down and straight.

Tips

When choosing a wedding hairstyle, it's important to consider the overall style of your wedding. If you're having a formal wedding, a classic updo may be more appropriate. If you're having a more casual or bohemian wedding, a half-up half-down style may be more fitting.

It's also important to consider your dress and accessories when choosing a hairstyle. If you have a high neckline, an updo may be more flattering. If you have statement earrings, a simple hairstyle may be more appropriate.

FAQ

Can I add hair extensions to my shoulder length hair?

Yes, you can add hair extensions to your shoulder length hair to create more volume or length for your wedding hairstyle. Just make sure to choose high-quality extensions that match your natural hair color.

How do I make my wedding hairstyle last all day?

To make your wedding hairstyle last all day, use a strong hold hairspray and secure it with bobby pins. You can also use a hair net or hair pins to keep the style in place.

Hair Care

Leading up to your wedding day, make sure to take good care of your hair. This includes regular trims to keep your ends healthy, and deep conditioning treatments to keep your hair shiny and smooth. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when you do.

On your wedding day, make sure to use high-quality hair products that will keep your hair looking its best. This includes a strong hold hairspray, a heat protectant spray, and a shine serum or spray.

With these wedding hairstyles for shoulder length straight hair, you're sure to look stunning on your special day. Just remember to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one.
