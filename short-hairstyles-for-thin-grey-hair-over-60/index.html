Short Hairstyles For Thin Grey Hair Over 60

Written by Frank Oct 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your thin grey hair? Look no further than these short hairstyles that are perfect for women over 60. From pixie cuts to bobs, there is a style for every hair type and face shape. In this article, we will go over how to choose the perfect short hairstyle for your thin grey hair, step-by-step instructions for styling it, tips for maintaining your hair, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle

Before choosing a short hairstyle, it's important to consider your hair type and face shape. For thin grey hair, it's best to choose a style that adds volume and texture. Pixie cuts and bobs are great options as they create the illusion of fullness. If you have a round face, opt for a layered bob that frames your face. If you have a square face, a pixie cut with side-swept bangs will soften your features.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Styling Your Short Hair

1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to the roots.

3. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots to create volume.

4. Use a flat iron to add texture to your hair.

5. Finish with a texturizing spray or hairspray.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Hair

1. Schedule regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

2. Use a dry shampoo in between washes to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

4. Avoid over-washing your hair as it can cause dryness and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear bangs with thin grey hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs. Opt for side-swept bangs that add texture and volume to your hair.

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look too harsh?

A: Not necessarily. A pixie cut can soften your features if you choose the right one for your face shape.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the health of your thin grey hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

3. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

4. Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis as it can cause damage and breakage.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for thin grey hair over 60 can be flattering and low-maintenance. By choosing the right style for your face shape and hair type, and following proper hair care tips, you can achieve a stylish and healthy look.
