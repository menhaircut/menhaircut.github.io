How To Style Wet Hair So It Dries Curly

Written by Christine Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to style your hair? Want to embrace your natural curls? Styling wet hair to dry curly is a great way to achieve bouncy, defined curls without using heat. Here's how to do it:

Description

Styling wet hair to dry curly is a low-maintenance and healthy way to achieve defined curls. This method involves applying products to wet hair and allowing it to air dry, resulting in bouncy and natural-looking curls.

How to Style Wet Hair So It Dries Curly

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your wet hair to dry curly:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. A sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner will help your hair retain moisture and keep it healthy.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to your wet hair. A leave-in conditioner will help to detangle your hair, reduce frizz, and add moisture. Use a wide-tooth comb to evenly distribute the leave-in conditioner throughout your hair.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your wet hair. This can be a curl cream, gel, mousse, or any other product that works best for your hair type. Apply the product from roots to ends, making sure to evenly distribute it throughout your hair.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

After applying the styling product, scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp to encourage the curls to form. This technique will help to enhance your natural curl pattern and create bouncy curls.

Step 5: Allow Your Hair to Air Dry

Once you've scrunched your hair, allow it to air dry. Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently scrunch excess water out of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when styling your wet hair to dry curly:

  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair while it's drying, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curl pattern.
  • Consider sleeping with a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz and preserve your curls.

FAQ

Can I style my hair wet every day?

Yes, you can style your hair wet every day. In fact, styling your hair wet is a healthy and low-maintenance way to achieve defined curls.

How long does it take for wet hair to dry curly?

The amount of time it takes for wet hair to dry curly depends on your hair type and the products you've used. On average, it can take anywhere from 2-4 hours for hair to air dry.

Can I use a hair dryer to dry my hair?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer to dry your hair, but be sure to use a diffuser attachment to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Alternatively, you can allow your hair to air dry naturally.

Hair Care

Styling your hair wet to dry curly is a healthy way to achieve defined curls without using heat. To keep your hair healthy, be sure to use sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, apply a leave-in conditioner, and avoid using heat tools on a regular basis.
