What Does A Heart Shaped Face Look Like

Written by Luffy Nov 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have ever wondered what a heart shaped face looks like, this article will help you understand the features that define this facial shape. In this article, we will discuss the characteristics of a heart shaped face, how to style your hair to flatter this face shape, and provide tips on how to take care of your hair.

Description

A heart shaped face is defined by a wider forehead and cheekbones that narrow down to a pointed chin. The face has a distinct V-shape that resembles a heart. The jawline is usually slender, and the chin is often pointy. This facial shape is considered one of the most feminine of all face shapes.

How to Determine if You Have a Heart Shaped Face

The easiest way to determine if you have a heart shaped face is to pull your hair back away from your face and stand in front of a mirror. Look for the following characteristics:

  • A wide forehead
  • High cheekbones
  • A narrow jawline and chin

If you have these features, then you most likely have a heart shaped face.

How to Style Hair for a Heart Shaped Face

There are many hairstyles that can flatter a heart shaped face. Here are a few tips to consider:

  • Long, layered hair can help balance the face shape and soften the pointed chin.
  • Side-swept bangs can add width to the forehead and balance out the face shape.
  • A bob that hits just below the chin can also flatter this facial shape.

Remember to avoid hairstyles that add volume to the top of the head as this can make the forehead appear even wider.

Step by Step Guide for Styling Hair for a Heart Shaped Face

Follow these simple steps to style your hair in a way that flatters your heart shaped face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair on the side to add width to the forehead.
  3. Add layers to your hair to soften the pointed chin.
  4. Avoid adding volume to the top of the head.
  5. Consider adding side-swept bangs to balance out the face shape.

Tips for Taking Care of Hair for a Heart Shaped Face

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools to prevent damage.
  • Be gentle when brushing your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

FAQs

1. Can I wear my hair short if I have a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear your hair short if you have a heart shaped face. However, make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape. A bob that hits just below the chin can be a great option.

2. Can I wear bangs if I have a heart shaped face?

Yes, you can wear bangs if you have a heart shaped face. Side-swept bangs can add width to the forehead and balance out the face shape.

3. What hair color looks best on a heart shaped face?

There is no one hair color that looks best on a heart shaped face. Choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and personal style.

Conclusion

Now that you know what a heart shaped face looks like, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your facial shape. Remember to keep your hair healthy and hydrated with a regular hair care routine. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your heart shaped face with confidence!
