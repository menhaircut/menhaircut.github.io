How Can I Make My Straight Hair Curly Permanently

Written by Daniel Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly using heat tools to achieve curly hair? Or maybe you're looking for a more permanent solution to switch up your hair texture. Whatever your reason may be, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll discuss how you can make your straight hair curly permanently.

Table of Contents:

  • How To Make Straight Hair Curly Permanently
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How To Make Straight Hair Curly Permanently

The most effective way to make straight hair curly permanently is by getting a perm. A perm is a chemical treatment that alters the structure of your hair, creating curls that last for several months. However, it's important to note that getting a perm is a drastic change and requires proper care to avoid damage to your hair.

Another option is to get a Brazilian keratin treatment. This treatment not only adds curls to your hair, but it also smooths out frizz and adds shine. However, it's important to note that this treatment is not recommended for those with extremely damaged hair.

Step By Step Guide

Before getting a perm or keratin treatment, it's important to consult with a professional stylist to determine the best treatment for your hair type and to avoid any potential damage. The process of getting a perm or keratin treatment typically involves the following steps:

  1. Your stylist will start by washing your hair to remove any buildup and prepare it for the treatment.
  2. If you're getting a keratin treatment, your stylist will apply the treatment to your hair, section by section. If you're getting a perm, your stylist will wrap your hair around perm rods and apply the perm solution.
  3. Your hair will then be covered with a plastic cap and left to process for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair type and desired results.
  4. Once the processing time is up, your stylist will rinse your hair thoroughly and apply a neutralizer to set the curls or smooth out the keratin treatment.
  5. Your hair will then be washed and styled to reveal your new curls or smooth hair.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Once you have your new curly hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and longevity. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and alter the shape of your curls.
  • Invest in a silk or satin pillowcase to avoid friction and breakage while you sleep.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a perm last?

A perm typically lasts for 3-6 months, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of it.

Can I still straighten my hair after getting a perm?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair after getting a perm, but it's important to wait at least 48 hours to allow the curls to set properly.

Can I color my hair after getting a perm or keratin treatment?

You should wait at least two weeks before coloring your hair after getting a perm or keratin treatment to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires specific care to maintain its shape and health. Here are some additional tips:

  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out your curls.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Avoid touching your curls too much, as this can cause frizz and alter their shape.

Now that you know how to make your straight hair curly permanently, it's time to book an appointment with your stylist and transform your hair! Remember to take proper care of your curls to keep them healthy and beautiful.
