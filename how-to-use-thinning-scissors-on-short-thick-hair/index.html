How To Use Thinning Scissors On Short Thick Hair

Written by Robby Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to styling short thick hair, thinning scissors can be a great tool to achieve a sleek and manageable look. Thinning scissors are specially designed to remove bulk from the hair without sacrificing length, making them a great option for those with thick hair who want to reduce the weight of their hair while keeping the length intact. In this article, we'll go over how to use thinning scissors on short thick hair, along with some tips and tricks to get the best results.

Description

Thinning scissors are a type of scissors that have teeth on one or both blades. When the scissors are used on the hair, the teeth grab and remove some of the hair, creating a softer and more textured look. Thinning scissors are often used by hairstylists to thin out hair that is too thick or to add texture to a hairstyle. They are also popular among people with thick hair who want to reduce the weight of their hair without losing length.

How to Use Thinning Scissors on Short Thick Hair

Thinning scissors can be used on short thick hair to create a more manageable and sleek look. Here's how to use thinning scissors on short thick hair:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before using thinning scissors on your hair, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you see the natural texture and thickness of your hair, making it easier to know where to thin out the hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will help you to work on one section at a time, making it easier to thin out the hair evenly.

Step 3: Hold the Scissors Properly

Hold the thinning scissors with your thumb and middle finger, while resting your index finger on the scissor handle. The teeth of the scissors should be pointing towards the hair.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Starting at the base of the hair, use the thinning scissors to cut small sections of hair. Move the scissors upwards towards the ends of the hair, but do not cut all the way to the ends. This will help to remove some of the bulk from the hair while keeping the length intact.

Step 5: Repeat on All Sections

Repeat this process on all sections of your hair, working from the back of your head to the front. Make sure to cut the hair evenly and avoid cutting too much hair in one section.

Tips for Using Thinning Scissors on Short Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when using thinning scissors on short thick hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging the hair
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections
  • Use a comb to help guide the scissors through the hair
  • Be careful not to over-thin the hair, as this can create a choppy and uneven look

FAQ About Using Thinning Scissors on Short Thick Hair

Can thinning scissors be used on all hair types?

Thinning scissors can be used on all hair types, but they are particularly useful for thick hair. If you have fine or thin hair, it's best to use thinning scissors sparingly to avoid creating a choppy or uneven look.

How often should I use thinning scissors on my hair?

It's best to use thinning scissors on your hair once every few months, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how much you want to thin out your hair. Overuse of thinning scissors can lead to damaged hair, so it's important to use them sparingly.

Hair Care After Using Thinning Scissors

After using thinning scissors on your hair, it's important to take care of your hair to avoid damage and breakage. Here are some tips to help care for your hair after using thinning scissors:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the hair
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly when styling it, as this can lead to breakage

By following these tips and using thinning scissors properly, you can achieve a sleek and manageable look for your short thick hair. Remember to use thinning scissors sparingly and to take care of your hair to avoid damage and breakage.
