What Is A High Taper Haircut

Written by Frank Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A high taper haircut is a popular men's hairstyle that involves gradually cutting the hair shorter towards the nape of the neck. The haircut is characterized by a sharp contrast between the longer hair on top and the shorter hair on the sides and back of the head. The high taper cut is a versatile style that can be worn in a variety of ways, from a classic slicked-back look to a more modern textured style.

How to Get a High Taper Haircut

Getting a high taper haircut is best done by a professional barber or hairstylist. Before getting started, make sure to bring in a photo of the style you're looking for to ensure that your stylist knows exactly what you want. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a high taper haircut:

Step 1: Choose Your Hair Length

Decide how long you want your hair to be on top. The length can vary depending on your personal preference and the style you're going for.

Step 2: Determine the Fade Length

The next step is to determine how short you want the hair to be on the sides and back of your head. A high taper cut generally involves a shorter fade, which means the hair gradually gets shorter towards the nape of the neck.

Step 3: Begin the Cut

Your stylist will begin the cut by trimming the hair on top to the desired length. They'll then use clippers to create the fade, gradually cutting the hair shorter as they move towards the nape of the neck. They may also use scissors to blend the hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides and back.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once the cut is complete, you can style your hair as desired. A high taper cut can be worn in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. Experiment with different products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining Your High Taper Haircut

To keep your high taper haircut looking sharp and stylish, consider these tips:

  • Visit your barber or stylist regularly for touch-ups
  • Use high-quality hair products to keep your hair looking healthy and styled
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you
  • Consider investing in a good quality hair trimmer to maintain your fade at home

FAQ About High Taper Haircuts

What is the difference between a taper and a fade?

A taper involves gradually cutting the hair shorter towards the nape of the neck, while a fade involves a more abrupt transition from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back.

Can I get a high taper haircut if I have curly hair?

Yes, a high taper cut can work well with curly hair. Your stylist can adjust the length and fade to suit your hair type and texture.

How often should I get a touch-up for my high taper haircut?

It's recommended to get a touch-up every 2-4 weeks to keep your haircut looking sharp and well-maintained.

Hair Care Tips for High Taper Haircuts

To keep your high taper haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen

By following these tips and getting regular touch-ups from your stylist, you can maintain a stylish and modern high taper haircut that looks great and suits your personal style.
