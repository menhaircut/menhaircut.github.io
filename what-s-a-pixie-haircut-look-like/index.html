What s A Pixie Haircut Look Like A Relaxed English Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, trendy haircut, the pixie cut might be for you. This short hairstyle has been popular for years and is a great way to show off your facial features. Keep reading to learn more about what a pixie haircut looks like and how to style it.

Description

A pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that typically features hair that is cut close to the scalp on the sides and back and slightly longer on top. This cut is often styled with a side-swept fringe or bangs, and it can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture.

The length of a pixie cut can vary, but it usually falls between a half-inch to three inches in length. This hairstyle is perfect for those with fine or thin hair, as it can add volume and texture to their hair. However, those with thick hair can also pull off this look with the help of some layering and thinning techniques.

How to

If you're interested in getting a pixie cut, it's important to find a stylist who is experienced in this type of haircut. Once you've found a stylist, follow these steps to achieve a perfect pixie cut:

  1. Consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  3. Sit still and let your stylist work their magic!
  4. After your cut, style your pixie cut using a small amount of hair product, such as wax or pomade, to add texture and hold.

Step by Step

If you're interested in styling your pixie cut at home, follow these easy steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair product, such as wax or pomade, to your fingertips.
  3. Rub your fingertips together to distribute the product evenly.
  4. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create texture.
  6. If desired, use a small amount of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking sharp.
  • Use a good quality hair product to add texture and hold.
  • Experiment with different partings and fringe styles to keep your look fresh.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie haircuts:

Can anyone pull off a pixie cut?

Yes! With the help of an experienced stylist, anyone can pull off a pixie cut. However, it's important to consider your face shape and hair texture to determine the best length and style for you.

How do I know if a pixie cut is right for me?

Consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape and hair texture. You can also try on wigs or use virtual makeover tools to see how you would look with a pixie cut before committing to the chop.

How often do I need to get a pixie cut?

It's recommended to get a trim every four to six weeks to keep your pixie cut looking sharp.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Now that you know what a pixie haircut looks like and how to style it, you're ready to rock this trendy and low-maintenance haircut. Happy styling!
