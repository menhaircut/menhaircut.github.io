What Haircuts Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by Eveline Jul 04, 2023 · 5 min read
What haircuts make your face look slimmer?

If you're looking for a way to make your face look slimmer, a new haircut might be just what you need. There are plenty of hairstyles that can help to create the illusion of a slimmer face, so you don't have to resort to drastic measures like plastic surgery or extreme dieting.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a haircut that will make your face look slimmer is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and requires a different approach when it comes to choosing a haircut.

Round Face: If you have a round face, your cheeks are the widest part of your face and your chin is rounded. Haircuts that add height to the crown of your head, like a high ponytail or a layered bob, can help to elongate your face.

Oval Face: If you have an oval face, your forehead is slightly wider than your chin and your face is longer than it is wide. Almost any haircut will flatter an oval face, but a long layered cut or a side-swept bang can help to highlight your cheekbones.

Square Face: If you have a square face, your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. Haircuts that soften your features, like a long bob or a side-swept fringe, can help to make your face look slimmer.

Heart Face: If you have a heart-shaped face, your forehead is wider than your jawline and your chin comes to a point. Haircuts that draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, like a pixie cut or a side-parted bob, can help to balance out your face.

Diamond Face: If you have a diamond-shaped face, your cheekbones are the widest part of your face and your chin and forehead are both narrow. Haircuts that add volume to your crown, like a messy updo or a layered cut, can help to create the illusion of a more balanced face.

Long Face: If you have a long face, your face is longer than it is wide and your chin is slightly pointed. Haircuts that add width to your face, like a blunt bob or a side-swept bang, can help to make your face look shorter and slimmer.

Haircuts that Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to choose a haircut that will make your face look slimmer. Here are some of the most flattering haircuts for each face shape:

Round Face: A long layered cut with side-swept bangs, a high ponytail, or a messy updo.

Oval Face: A long layered cut, a side-swept bang, or a blunt bob.

Square Face: A long bob, a side-swept fringe, or a layered cut.

Heart Face: A pixie cut, a side-parted bob, or a messy updo.

Diamond Face: A messy updo, a layered cut, or a side-swept fringe.

Long Face: A blunt bob, a side-swept bang, or a messy updo.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut that will make your face look slimmer, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness. Some haircuts work better with certain hair types.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you don't have a lot of time to style your hair, choose a low-maintenance cut.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new. A new haircut can be a great way to refresh your look and boost your confidence.

FAQs

Q: Will a shorter haircut make my face look slimmer?

A: Not necessarily. It's more about the shape of the cut than the length. Some shorter cuts, like a pixie cut, can actually make your face look wider.

Q: Can bangs make my face look slimmer?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer profile.

Q: Is it better to have one-length hair or layers?

A: It depends on your face shape and hair type. Layers can help to add volume and movement, while one-length hair can create a sleek, slimming effect.

Hair Care for Slimming Haircuts

Once you've chosen a haircut that will make your face look slimmer, it's important to take good care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make your hair look frizzy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

With the right haircut and some simple hair care tips, you can create the illusion of a slimmer face and feel confident in your appearance.
