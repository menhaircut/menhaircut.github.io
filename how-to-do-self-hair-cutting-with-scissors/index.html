How To Do Self Hair Cutting With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending time and money at the salon for a simple trim? With a few simple steps, you can learn how to do self hair cutting with scissors. In this guide, we will go through the steps to achieve a professional-looking cut at home.

Description

Self hair cutting with scissors is a process of trimming your own hair using scissors. This can be done for regular hair maintenance or for a specific hairstyle. This guide will help you achieve a successful haircut without risking a bad outcome.

How to do Self Hair Cutting with Scissors

Before you begin, make sure you have the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean and dry hair. This makes it easier to see what you are doing and reduces the risk of uneven cuts.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use your comb to part your hair in the middle and then from ear to ear. Clip the top section of your hair up and out of the way.

Step 3: Cut the Bottom Section

Starting with the bottom section, comb your hair straight down and trim the ends using your scissors. Be sure to cut in small increments to avoid taking off too much hair.

Step 4: Cut the Top Section

Release the top section of your hair and comb it straight down. Hold your hair between your fingers and trim the ends using your scissors. Again, cut in small increments to ensure a natural look.

Step 5: Check for Evenness

Check for any uneven areas by running your fingers through your hair. If you find any areas that need trimming, repeat steps 3 and 4.

Step 6: Finish with Bangs

If you have bangs, use your scissors to trim them. Comb your bangs straight down and hold them between your fingers. Trim the ends in small increments until you reach your desired length.

Tips for Self Hair Cutting with Scissors

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting your own hair:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends
  • Start with small cuts and work your way up
  • Trim your hair when it is dry, as wet hair can appear longer
  • Use a mirror to check your progress

FAQs

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair and can cause split ends. It is recommended to use hair scissors for the best results.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended to cut your hair every 6 to 8 weeks to maintain healthy hair.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain a healthy look. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat tools too often
  • Use a hair mask once a week to restore moisture

By following these steps, you can learn how to do self hair cutting with scissors and achieve a professional-looking cut at home. Remember to take your time and start with small cuts to avoid any mistakes. Happy haircutting!
