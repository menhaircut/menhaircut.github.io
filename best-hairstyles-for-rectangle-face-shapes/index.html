Description

Rectangle face shapes are characterized by a long and narrow face with a strong jawline. If you have a rectangle face shape, you may find it challenging to find hairstyles that flatter your features. However, there are plenty of hairstyles that can enhance your face shape and make you look your best.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best hairstyle for your face shape is to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and take a good look at your face in the mirror. Pay attention to the proportions of your face, including the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

If your face is longer than it is wide, with a strong jawline and forehead, you likely have a rectangle face shape.

Best Hairstyles for Rectangle Face Shapes

When choosing a hairstyle for a rectangle face shape, the goal is to soften your strong jawline and add width to your forehead and cheekbones. Here are some of the best hairstyles for rectangle face shapes:

Bangs

Bangs are a great way to add width to your forehead and soften your strong jawline. Wispy, side-swept bangs are particularly flattering for rectangle face shapes.

Layers

Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, which can help balance out your strong jawline. Try adding layers around your face or throughout your hair for a more flattering look.

Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that can work well for rectangle face shapes. Opt for a chin-length or shoulder-length bob with layers to add volume and soften your features.

Wavy or Curly Hair

If you have naturally wavy or curly hair, embrace it! The volume and texture of wavy or curly hair can help balance out your strong jawline and add width to your forehead and cheekbones.

Pixie Cut

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut can be a great option for rectangle face shapes. A short, layered pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair, while also emphasizing your cheekbones.

Tips for Styling Your Hair with a Rectangle Face Shape

When styling your hair with a rectangle face shape, keep these tips in mind:

  • Avoid straight, flat hair that can emphasize your strong jawline.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to your forehead and cheekbones.
  • Experiment with layers and texture to add movement to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they can make your face look even longer.

FAQs About Hairstyles for Rectangle Face Shapes

What hairstyles should I avoid with a rectangle face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as they can emphasize your strong jawline and make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for hairstyles that add volume and width to your forehead and cheekbones.

Can I wear a middle part with a rectangle face shape?

If you have a rectangle face shape, a middle part can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for a side part to add width to your forehead and cheekbones.

What are the best bangs for a rectangle face shape?

Wispy, side-swept bangs are particularly flattering for rectangle face shapes. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs that can make your face look even longer.

Hair Care Tips for Rectangle Face Shapes

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful hair. Here are some tips for caring for your hair with a rectangle face shape:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and make it more difficult to style.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can enhance your rectangle face shape and look your best. Experiment with different styles to find the perfect look for you, and don't be afraid to try something new!
