Short Hairstyles For Round Face With Double Chin Over 50

Written by Bardi Oct 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As women age, they may experience changes in their hair such as thinning, graying, and even hair loss. For women over 50 with round faces and double chins, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be a challenge. However, there are many options that can flatter the face and reduce the appearance of a double chin. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short hairstyles for round face with double chin over 50.

Description

A round face with a double chin can be softened and made more flattering with the right short hairstyle. Short hairstyles can create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention away from the chin area. The key is to find a style that complements your face shape and makes you feel confident.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Look for hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as layered cuts or pixie cuts.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the head, as this can make the face appear wider.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs, as this can help to elongate the face.
  • Avoid blunt cuts, as they can accentuate a double chin.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some of the best short hairstyles for round face with double chin over 50:

The Layered Bob

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Part your hair in the middle.
  • Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair, adding volume to the crown of your head.
  • Using a razor, create layers throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray.

The Pixie Cut

  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Using a pair of scissors or clippers, cut your hair short, leaving the top slightly longer than the sides.
  • Use a small amount of styling product to add texture to the top of your hair.
  • Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face with a double chin:

  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too severe or angular, as they can make the face appear more round.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and styling products to help maintain your hairstyle.
  • Consult with a stylist who has experience working with mature clients.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for round face with double chin over 50:

Q: Will a short hairstyle make my double chin more noticeable?

A: No, not necessarily. The right short hairstyle can actually help to reduce the appearance of a double chin by drawing attention to other areas of the face.

Q: Can I still have bangs with a round face and double chin?

A: Yes, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Side-swept bangs can help to elongate the face and draw attention away from the chin area.

Hair Care

When it comes to hair care for women over 50, it's important to use products that are gentle and nourishing. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for mature hair and contain ingredients such as argan oil, keratin, and biotin to help support healthy hair growth. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help to keep your hair looking its best.

Overall, there are many short hairstyles that can flatter a round face with a double chin over 50. With the right cut and styling, you can look and feel your best at any age.
