Does Blonde Hair Make You Look Darker

Written by Christine Oct 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering going blonde, you might be wondering if the lighter shade will make you look darker or wash you out. In this blog post, we'll explore this topic and give you tips on how to choose the right shade of blonde for your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Shade of Blonde
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Going Blonde
  • Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair
  • FAQs

Description

Blonde hair can make you look darker or lighter depending on your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, a warm blonde shade like honey or caramel will complement your skin and make you look radiant. If you have cool undertones, a cool blonde shade like platinum or ash will bring out the pink or blue undertones in your skin and make you look fresh.

Blonde hair can also make you look darker or lighter depending on how you style it. If you wear cool-toned makeup and clothing, a cool blonde shade will enhance your overall look. If you wear warm-toned makeup and clothing, a warm blonde shade will complement your style and make you look put-together.

How to Choose the Right Shade of Blonde

When choosing a shade of blonde, consider your skin tone and natural hair color. If you have fair skin and light hair, you can go for a lighter blonde shade like platinum. If you have medium skin and medium hair, a honey or caramel shade will look great. If you have dark skin and dark hair, a darker blonde shade like golden blonde will complement your skin tone.

You can also consult with a hair stylist to help you choose the right shade of blonde for your skin tone and natural hair color. They can recommend the best shade for you and help you achieve the look you want.

Warm Blonde Shades

Warm blonde shades are perfect for those with warm undertones in their skin. These shades include:

  • Honey blonde
  • Caramel blonde
  • Golden blonde

Cool Blonde Shades

Cool blonde shades are perfect for those with cool undertones in their skin. These shades include:

  • Platinum blonde
  • Ash blonde
  • Silver blonde

Step-by-Step Guide to Going Blonde

If you're ready to go blonde, follow these steps:

  1. Consult with a hair stylist to choose the right shade of blonde for your skin tone and natural hair color.
  2. Prepare your hair by deep conditioning and trimming split ends.
  3. Lighten your hair using bleach or a color remover.
  4. Tone your hair to remove any brassiness or yellow tones.
  5. Style your new blonde hair and enjoy!

Tips for Maintaining Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires extra care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a purple shampoo to neutralize any yellow tones in your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Will blonde hair make me look darker?

A: It depends on your skin tone and the shade of blonde you choose. Warm blonde shades can complement warm undertones in your skin and make you look radiant, while cool blonde shades can bring out cool undertones in your skin and make you look fresh.

Q: Can I go blonde if I have dark hair?

A: Yes, but you may need to lighten your hair several times to achieve the desired blonde shade. It's best to consult with a hair stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair

Blonde hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Overall, going blonde can be a fun and exciting change. Just remember to choose the right shade for your skin tone and natural hair color, and take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.
