How Do I Know What Texture My Hair Is A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we all know, understanding your hair texture is essential to taking care of it properly. However, figuring out what texture your hair is can often be confusing and overwhelming, especially if you're new to the world of hair care. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps to identifying your hair texture and providing tips on how to care for it.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To Identify Your Hair Texture
  3. Step-By-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Hair Care Based on Texture
  5. FAQs

Description

Before we dive into the details, let's first understand what hair texture is. In simple terms, hair texture refers to the diameter of each individual hair strand. The most common hair textures are fine, medium, and thick. Additionally, hair texture can be further categorized into straight, wavy, curly, and coily.

How To Identify Your Hair Texture

There are two ways you can identify your hair texture - visual inspection and the hair strand test.

Visual Inspection

The easiest and most common way to identify your hair texture is through visual inspection. Look at your hair in natural light and ask yourself the following questions:

  • Is my hair thin, medium, or thick in diameter?
  • Does my hair lay flat or have volume?
  • Is my hair straight, wavy, curly, or coily?

Based on your answers, you can determine your hair texture.

Hair Strand Test

If you want to be more precise in determining your hair texture, you can perform the hair strand test.

  1. Take a single strand of hair from your head and hold it between your fingers.
  2. Run your fingers along the strand from the root to the end.
  3. If you can barely feel the hair strand or can't feel it at all, you have fine hair.
  4. If you can feel the hair strand, but it's not thick or thin, you have medium hair.
  5. If you can feel the hair strand and it's thick, you have thick hair.

Keep in mind that this test doesn't determine your hair's curl pattern.

Step-By-Step Guide

If you're still unsure about your hair texture, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and condition your hair as you normally would.
  2. Pat your hair dry with a towel and avoid using any styling products.
  3. Let your hair air dry completely.
  4. Take a close look at your hair in natural light and determine its texture based on the visual inspection method.

If you're still unsure, perform the hair strand test.

Tips for Hair Care Based on Texture

Once you've identified your hair texture, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some general tips:

  • For fine hair, use lightweight products that won't weigh your hair down. Avoid heavy oils and products that contain silicones.
  • For medium hair, try to strike a balance between lightweight and heavy products.
  • For thick hair, use heavier products that can provide moisture and tame frizz.
  • For straight hair, avoid using heavy products that can make your hair look greasy.
  • For wavy hair, use products that can define your waves and provide moisture.
  • For curly hair, use products that can enhance your curls and provide moisture.
  • For coily hair, use products that can define your curls and provide moisture.

FAQs

Q: Can I have more than one hair texture?

A: Yes, it's common to have more than one hair texture on your head. For example, you can have fine hair in some areas and thick hair in others.

Q: Can my hair texture change over time?

A: Yes, your hair texture can change due to hormonal fluctuations, aging, and other factors.

Q: Can I change my hair texture?

A: No, you can't change your hair texture permanently. However, you can alter your hair's appearance temporarily through styling and chemical treatments.

By following these tips and understanding your hair texture, you can achieve healthy, beautiful hair that looks and feels its best.
