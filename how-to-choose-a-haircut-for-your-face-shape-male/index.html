How To Choose A Haircut For Your Face Shape Male

Written by Petter Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task, especially for men. Your haircut can make or break your look, and it’s important to choose one that complements your face shape. In this article, we’ll discuss the different face shapes and how to choose a haircut that works best for each one.

Table of Contents

  1. Face Shapes
  2. How to Choose a Haircut
  3. Step by Step Guide
  4. Tips for Choosing a Haircut
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care Tips

Face Shapes

The first step in choosing a haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Triangle

Each face shape has its own unique characteristics and requires a specific haircut to enhance its features.

How to Choose a Haircut

Once you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to choose a haircut that will complement it. Here are some tips:

  • Round faces: Choose a haircut that adds height to the top of the head and keeps the sides short. This will elongate your face and create a more angular look.
  • Square faces: Choose a haircut that softens the angles of your face. A layered cut with textured ends will work well.
  • Oval faces: Lucky you! Oval faces can pull off almost any haircut, so feel free to experiment.
  • Heart faces: Choose a haircut that balances out the width of your forehead and the narrowness of your chin. A side-swept fringe or textured crop works well.
  • Diamond faces: Choose a haircut that adds width at the temples and jawline. A textured crop or quiff will work well.
  • Triangle faces: Choose a haircut that adds volume at the top of the head and keeps the sides short. A textured crop or side-swept fringe will work well.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to choosing a haircut for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape.
  2. Research haircuts that work well for your face shape.
  3. Choose a few haircuts that you like.
  4. Consult with your hairstylist to determine which haircut will work best for your hair type and face shape.
  5. Ask your hairstylist for styling tips to maintain your new haircut.

Tips for Choosing a Haircut

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut:

  • Consider your hair type. Some haircuts work better for thick hair, while others work better for thin hair.
  • Think about your lifestyle. Choose a haircut that works with your daily routine.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment. If you don’t like your new haircut, it will grow back!

FAQs

Q: Can I pull off a buzz cut?

A: If you have a round face, a buzz cut may not be the best option. However, if you have a square or oval face, a buzz cut can look great!

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. Generally, it’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Q: What should I do if I don’t like my new haircut?

A: Talk to your hairstylist about what you don’t like and see if they can make any adjustments. If you’re still not happy, you can always wait for your hair to grow out and try a different style.

Hair Care Tips

Once you’ve chosen your new haircut, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

Choosing a haircut that complements your face shape can be a game-changer. Use the tips in this article to find the perfect haircut for you!
