What Haircuts Suit A Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling like your face looks too round for most haircuts? Don't worry, you're not alone. Millions of people around the world have round faces and struggle to find the perfect haircut that suits their face shape. In this article, we'll provide you with a guide on what haircuts suit a round face, along with tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Description

If you have a round face, you may feel like your options for haircuts are limited. However, there are many haircuts that can flatter your face shape and make you feel confident and beautiful. The key is to find a haircut that elongates your face and creates the illusion of a more oval shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for round faces, it's important to determine your face shape. Here's how:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Use a lipstick or eyeliner to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and examine the shape you've drawn.
  4. Determine which of the following face shapes you have: round, oval, square, heart, or diamond.

If you have trouble determining your face shape, you can also consult with a hairstylist who can help you identify your unique features and suggest hairstyles that will suit you best.

Haircuts for Round Faces

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to find a haircut that complements your features. Here are some haircuts that are perfect for round faces:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle that works well on round faces. It elongates the neck and creates the illusion of a longer face. This haircut is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance, edgy look.

The Bob

The bob is a classic haircut that never goes out of style. It's perfect for round faces because it elongates the face and draws attention to the cheekbones. The key is to keep the length just below the chin.

The Lob

The lob, or long bob, is a versatile haircut that works well on round faces. It's longer than the traditional bob but shorter than long hair. The lob can be styled in many different ways, making it perfect for those who like to switch up their look.

The Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that adds texture and volume to the hair. It's perfect for round faces because it adds height at the crown, elongating the face. The shag is a great choice for those who want a low-maintenance, rock-and-roll look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to get the perfect haircut for your round face:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best haircut for your face shape.
  2. Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your appointment.
  3. Discuss any concerns or questions you have with your hairstylist.
  4. Trust your stylist's expertise and let them work their magic.
  5. Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and using quality hair products.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use quality hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear bangs with a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear bangs with a round face. However, it's important to choose a style that flatters your face shape. Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs work well on round faces.

Q: What hair colors work best on round faces?

A: Any hair color can work on a round face, but it's important to choose a shade that complements your skin tone. Warm, golden tones and cool, ashy tones are both great options.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

Here are some hair care tips for round faces:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.
  • Brush your hair gently to prevent breakage.

Now that you know what haircuts suit a round face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and try out a new style. Remember to choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and beautiful, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.
