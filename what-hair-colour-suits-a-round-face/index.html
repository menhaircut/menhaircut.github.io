What Hair Colour Suits A Round Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right hair colour can be tricky, especially if you have a round face. The wrong hair colour can accentuate the roundness of your face, making it appear wider and fuller. In this article, we’ll share some tips on what hair colour suits a round face, how to choose the right shade, and how to take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • What is a Round Face?
  • How to Choose the Right Hair Colour for a Round Face?
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour for a Round Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

What is a Round Face?

A round face is characterized by a circular shape, with the width and length of the face being almost equal. People with round faces tend to have fuller cheeks and soft jawlines. Examples of celebrities with round faces include Selena Gomez, Chrissy Teigen, and Emma Stone.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour for a Round Face?

When choosing a hair colour for a round face, it’s important to consider your skin tone as well. People with warm skin tones should opt for warm hair colours like golden blonde, copper, and honey brown. On the other hand, people with cool skin tones should go for cool hair colours like ash blonde, platinum, and jet black.

In general, it’s best to avoid hair colours that are too dark or too light, as they can make your face appear wider. Instead, opt for hair colours that are close to your natural hair colour, or choose a shade that’s only a few shades lighter or darker.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour for a Round Face

Here are some tips for choosing the right hair colour for a round face:

  • Opt for hair colours that are close to your natural hair colour.
  • Avoid hair colours that are too dark or too light.
  • Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair colour.
  • Choose a hair colour that complements your eye colour.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose the right hair colour that complements your face shape and skin tone.

Q: Can I go blonde if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can go blonde if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a shade of blonde that complements your skin tone.

Q: What hair colours should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: It’s best to avoid hair colours that are too dark or too light, as they can make your face appear wider. Also, avoid hair colours that have too much warmth or coolness, as they can clash with your skin tone.

Hair Care Tips for Coloured Hair

Once you’ve chosen the right hair colour for your round face, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain its colour and health. Here are some hair care tips for coloured hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent colour fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair colour for a round face doesn’t have to be difficult. By considering your skin tone and following some simple tips, you can find a hair colour that complements your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
