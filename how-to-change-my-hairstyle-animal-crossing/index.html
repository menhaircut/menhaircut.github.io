How To Change My Hairstyle In Animal Crossing

Written by Luffy Nov 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle in Animal Crossing? Well, you’re in luck! In this article, we will discuss how to change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing. Whether you’re looking for a new look or just want to switch things up, changing your hairstyle is easy and fun. Let’s get started!

How to Change Your Hairstyle

Changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing is a simple process. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Visit Harriet’s Salon

The first step in changing your hairstyle is to visit Harriet’s Salon. Harriet’s Salon is located in the back of the Able Sisters building. Once you enter the building, head to the back and you’ll see Harriet’s Salon on the left-hand side.

Step 2: Choose Your New Hairstyle

Once you’re inside Harriet’s Salon, you’ll be greeted by Harriet herself. She will ask you if you want to change your hairstyle. Say yes and she will show you a list of all the hairstyles available in the game. Choose the one you like the most.

Step 3: Get Your Haircut

After you’ve chosen your new hairstyle, Harriet will ask you if you want to go through with the haircut. Say yes and she will cut your hair. Once your hair is cut, you’ll see your new hairstyle in all its glory.

Step 4: Pay for Your New Hairstyle

After you’ve gotten your new haircut, Harriet will ask you to pay for it. The cost of the haircut will vary depending on the hairstyle you choose. Make sure you have enough bells to pay for it.

Tips for Choosing Your New Hairstyle

Choosing a new hairstyle can be overwhelming. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect one:

Tip 1: Look at Your Face Shape

Your face shape can play a big role in which hairstyle looks best on you. If you have a round face, for example, you might want to avoid hairstyles that add width to your face.

Tip 2: Consider Your Style

Your new hairstyle should match your personal style. If you’re someone who likes to keep things simple, a short and simple haircut might be the way to go. If you’re more adventurous, a bold and colorful hairstyle might be more your style.

Tip 3: Think About Maintenance

Some hairstyles require more maintenance than others. If you’re someone who likes to keep things low-key, you might want to choose a hairstyle that doesn’t require a lot of upkeep.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hairstyle in Animal Crossing:

Can I Change My Hairstyle Whenever I Want?

Yes, you can change your hairstyle whenever you want. Just visit Harriet’s Salon and follow the steps we outlined above.

Can I Change My Hair Color?

No, you cannot change your hair color in Animal Crossing. Your hair color is determined by your character’s appearance settings.

How Much Does a Haircut Cost?

The cost of a haircut varies depending on the hairstyle you choose. The more elaborate the hairstyle, the more it will cost.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have a new hairstyle, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking great:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly will keep it clean and healthy. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair over time. If you must use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.

Tip 3: Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts will keep your hair looking fresh and healthy. Aim to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Now that you know how to change your hairstyle in Animal Crossing, it’s time to try it out for yourself. Whether you’re looking for a new look or just want to switch things up, changing your hairstyle is a fun and easy way to do it. Just remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking great!
