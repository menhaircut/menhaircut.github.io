How To Cut Your Own Side Swept Bangs Step By Step

Written by Frank Jul 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of always going to the salon just to have your side swept bangs trimmed? Well, you don't have to anymore! Cutting your own side swept bangs may seem daunting, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve salon-worthy results in the comfort of your own home. Follow these step-by-step instructions to learn how to cut your own side swept bangs.

Description

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle that can frame your face and add a touch of elegance to your look. However, maintaining this hairstyle can be costly and time-consuming. By learning how to cut your own side swept bangs, you can save money and have the freedom to style your hair whenever you want.

How to

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before cutting your bangs, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Brush your hair straight and part it in the middle or to the side, depending on your preference. Use hair clips to separate your bangs from the rest of your hair.

Step 2: Measure Your Bangs

Using a comb, separate the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The base of the triangle should be at the middle of your forehead, and the tip of the triangle should be at the tip of your nose. This will give you a guide for how much hair to cut.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Hold your scissors at a slight angle and begin cutting your bangs from the center of your forehead outwards. Cut small sections at a time and be careful not to cut too much hair at once. Check your progress frequently by looking in the mirror and adjusting the length as needed.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Once you've cut your bangs to the desired length, style them by using a round brush and blow dryer to create a side swept effect. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to add more texture and volume to your bangs.

Tips

  • Invest in a good pair of hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time to avoid any mistakes.
  • Don't cut your bangs too short, as they may appear uneven or difficult to style.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own bangs, practice on a wig or a friend's hair first.

FAQ

Can I cut my own bangs if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can cut your own side swept bangs even if you have curly hair. Just make sure to cut your bangs when your hair is dry and styled in its natural state. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls and add volume to your bangs.

What should I do if I make a mistake while cutting my bangs?

If you make a mistake while cutting your bangs, don't panic. Try to even out the length as best as you can and avoid cutting any more hair. You can also style your bangs differently to hide any mistakes.

How often should I trim my side swept bangs?

It's recommended to trim your side swept bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care

To keep your side swept bangs looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type and avoid using too much heat when styling your hair. You can also use a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
