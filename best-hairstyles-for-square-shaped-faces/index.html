Best Hairstyles For Square Shaped Faces

Written by Daniel Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a square-shaped face, you're in luck! You have a versatile face shape that can rock a variety of hairstyles. From short to long, there are endless options to choose from. In this article, we'll explore the best hairstyles for square shaped faces, how to achieve them, and hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square-shaped face has a strong jawline and a wide forehead. The goal of choosing a hairstyle for this face shape is to soften the angles and create balance. You can achieve this by adding layers, volume, and texture to your hair. Avoid blunt cuts and styles that emphasize the squareness of your face.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a square-shaped face, consider your hair texture, length, and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to find the perfect cut that flatters your face shape. Additionally, you can take inspiration from celebrities with square-shaped faces, such as Angelina Jolie and Olivia Wilde, who have rocked a variety of hairstyles over the years.

Step by Step

Here are some of the best hairstyles for square shaped faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers soften the angles of a square-shaped face and add movement and volume to your hair. This style works well with straight or wavy hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs draw attention away from the wide forehead and add a touch of femininity to your look. This style works well with medium to long hair.

3. Bob with Soft Layers

A bob with soft layers is a great option for those who want to go short. The layers add texture and dimension to your hair, while the bob frames your face and highlights your cheekbones.

4. Beachy Waves

Beachy waves add volume and texture to your hair, making it look fuller and softer. This style works well with medium to long hair.

5. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring option for those who want to go short. The short layers and asymmetrical cut soften the angles of a square-shaped face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Avoid blunt cuts and styles that emphasize the squareness of your face.
  • Add layers, volume, and texture to your hair to soften the angles.
  • Use a round brush to create volume and movement.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage and dry it out.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products, such as shampoo, conditioner, and styling products, to keep your locks healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for a square-shaped face?

The best hairstyle for a square-shaped face is one that softens the angles and creates balance. Long layers, side-swept bangs, and beachy waves are great options to consider.

Can I wear a bob with a square-shaped face?

Yes, you can wear a bob with a square-shaped face. Just make sure to add soft layers to the cut to create movement and texture.

What should I avoid when choosing a hairstyle for a square-shaped face?

Avoid blunt cuts and styles that emphasize the squareness of your face. Instead, opt for styles that add layers, volume, and texture to your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your locks healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair every time you wash it.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and flat irons.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your locks.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyle options, you can find a look that flatters your square-shaped face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
