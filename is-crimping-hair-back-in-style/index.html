Description

When it comes to hairstyling, there are always trends that come and go. One of the latest trends is crimping your hair. You may be wondering, is crimping hair back in style? The answer is yes! Crimped hair is back in style in 2023 and is a great way to add texture and volume to your hair.

Crimping hair involves using a crimping iron to create a zigzag pattern in your hair. This style was popular in the 80s and early 90s, but it's making a comeback now. Crimped hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from wearing it down to creating unique updos.

How to Crimp Hair

If you're new to crimping hair, it can seem intimidating at first. However, with the right tools and some practice, you can achieve the perfect crimped look. Here's what you'll need:

  • Crimping iron
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Hairspray

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to crimp your hair:

  1. Begin by washing and drying your hair. Make sure it's completely dry before you start crimping.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the crimping iron.
  3. Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties.
  4. Take one section of hair and clamp the crimping iron down on it. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  5. Continue crimping small sections of hair until your entire head is crimped.
  6. Once you've finished crimping, use hairspray to set the style in place.

Tips for Perfect Crimped Hair

To achieve the perfect crimped look, follow these tips:

  • Use a high-quality crimping iron to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't crimp your hair too close to the roots, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the crimping iron.
  • Don't over-crimp your hair. Less is more when it comes to this style.
  • Use hairspray to set the style in place.

FAQs

Can I crimp my hair if it's already curly?

Yes, you can crimp curly hair. However, keep in mind that crimping will change the texture of your curls.

How long will crimped hair last?

Crimped hair can last for several days if you use hairspray to set the style in place.

Can I crimp my hair if it's thin or fine?

Yes, you can crimp thin or fine hair. However, be sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't over-crimp your hair.

Hair Care for Crimped Hair

Crimped hair requires some extra care to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can cause the crimped pattern to fade.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as a brush can disrupt the crimped pattern.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your crimped hair, as this can damage the texture.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Now that you know crimped hair is back in style, give it a try! With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve the perfect crimped look.
