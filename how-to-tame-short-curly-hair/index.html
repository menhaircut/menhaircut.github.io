How To Tame Short Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short curly hair, you know that it can be both a blessing and a curse. On one hand, it’s easy to maintain and can look effortlessly chic. On the other hand, it can be difficult to tame and style. In this article, we’ll show you how to tame short curly hair in a few simple steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Tame Short Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Taming Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs

How to Tame Short Curly Hair

Before we get started, it’s important to understand that curly hair is different from straight hair. Curly hair tends to be dryer and more prone to frizz, so it needs extra moisture and care. Here’s what you’ll need to tame your short curly hair:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Detangling brush
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Styling gel
  • Hair oil or serum

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Rinse thoroughly and gently pat your hair dry with a towel. Don’t rub your hair vigorously, as this can cause frizz.

Step 2: Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your damp hair, making sure to distribute it evenly from root to tip. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots.

Step 3: Apply Styling Gel

Apply a small amount of styling gel to your hair, focusing on the ends and areas prone to frizz. Use your fingers to scrunch your hair and encourage your natural curls.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your head and move it around to dry your curls evenly. Avoid using a regular hair dryer without a diffuser, as this can cause frizz and damage.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Oil or Serum

Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to your hair to add shine and tame any remaining frizz. Use your fingers to gently scrunch your curls and shape them as desired.

Tips for Taming Short Curly Hair

  • Don’t wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to reduce frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause frizz.
  • If your hair is especially dry, consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

FAQs

Q: Can I brush my curly hair?

A: Yes, but it’s important to use a detangling brush or wide-tooth comb and be gentle to avoid causing damage and breakage.

Q: How can I refresh my curls between washes?

A: Use a water-based curl refresher spray or mix water and leave-in conditioner in a spray bottle and mist your hair.

Q: Can I straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and a flat iron with adjustable temperature settings. Avoid using a flat iron on wet or damp hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

In addition to the steps and tips we’ve already covered, there are a few other things you can do to keep your short curly hair looking its best:

  • Avoid using products with sulfates and silicones, as these can dry out and damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.

By following these steps and tips, you can tame your short curly hair and achieve the gorgeous, bouncy curls you’ve always wanted.
