How Do You Know If A Bob Suits You

Written by Daniel Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering getting a bob but not sure if it will suit you? Well, you’re not alone. The bob is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades, and it continues to be a popular choice today. However, not everyone can pull off this look. In this article, we’ll discuss how to determine if a bob suits you and provide some tips on how to care for your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How To Determine If A Bob Suits You
  • Step-By-Step Guide To Getting A Bob
  • Tips For Maintaining Your Bob
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips For Your Bob

How To Determine If A Bob Suits You

The first thing you need to consider when deciding if a bob suits you is your face shape. Bobs tend to look best on those with oval or heart-shaped faces. If you have a round or square face, a bob may not be the best choice for you.

Another factor to consider is your hair type. Bobs work well on straight or wavy hair, but if you have curly hair, you may want to think twice before getting a bob. This is because bobs can make curly hair look shorter and frizzier.

Finally, think about your lifestyle. If you’re someone who likes to style their hair every day, a bob may be a good choice for you. Bobs are easy to style and require minimal maintenance. However, if you prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle, a bob may not be the best choice for you.

Step-By-Step Guide To Getting A Bob

If you’ve decided that a bob is the right choice for you, here’s a step-by-step guide on how to get one:

  1. Choose the right length. Bobs can be short or long, and the length you choose will depend on your face shape and hair type.
  2. Find a good stylist. Look for a stylist who has experience cutting bobs and who can help you choose the right length and style for your face shape.
  3. Get a consultation. Before getting your bob, schedule a consultation with your stylist to discuss the length and style you want.
  4. Take care of your new hairstyle. Bobs require regular trims to maintain their shape, so make sure to schedule regular appointments with your stylist.

Tips For Maintaining Your Bob

Here are some tips on how to keep your bob looking its best:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner. Bobs require regular washing, so make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Invest in a good styling product. Bobs are easy to style, but you may need a styling product to help keep your hair in place.
  • Get regular trims. Bobs require regular trims to maintain their shape, so make sure to schedule appointments with your stylist every 6-8 weeks.

FAQs

Q: Will a bob make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on your face shape. Bobs tend to look best on those with oval or heart-shaped faces, but if you have a round or square face, a bob may not be the best choice for you.

Q: How do I choose the right length for my bob?

A: The length you choose will depend on your face shape and hair type. It’s best to consult with a stylist to help you choose the right length for you.

Hair Care Tips For Your Bob

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your bob looking its best:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. Bobs are easy to style, but using heat styling tools too often can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun. UV rays can damage your hair, so make sure to wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray when you’re outside.
  • Be gentle when brushing and styling. Bobs require minimal maintenance, but make sure to be gentle when brushing and styling your hair to avoid damaging it.

Now that you know how to determine if a bob suits you, it’s time to make a decision. If you decide to go for it, follow our step-by-step guide and hair care tips to keep your bob looking its best.
