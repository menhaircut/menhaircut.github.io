How To Make A Round Face Look Smaller Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you have a round face? While there's no shame in having this face shape, you might want to make it appear smaller for a variety of reasons. Fortunately, there are many ways you can achieve this look without resorting to drastic measures. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to make a round face look smaller.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Make a Round Face Look Smaller
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Round Faces

How to Make a Round Face Look Smaller

Here are some ways to make a round face look smaller:

  • Contouring: Use a contouring kit to create shadows and depth on your face. Apply a darker shade of foundation or powder to the sides of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.
  • Hairstyle: Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheeks or round your face. A long bob or layered haircut can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.
  • Makeup: Use makeup to enhance your features and draw attention away from your cheeks. Apply a bold lipstick or eye makeup to emphasize your eyes or lips.
  • Clothing: Wear clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape. Avoid baggy or oversized clothing that can make you look bigger. Choose clothing with vertical stripes or patterns to create an elongated effect.
  • Accessories: Wear accessories that draw attention away from your face. Choose statement earrings or a necklace to emphasize your neck and shoulders.

Remember, making your face look smaller is all about creating the illusion of length and depth. With the right techniques and tools, you can achieve this look easily.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make a round face look smaller:

  1. Start with a clean and moisturized face.
  2. Apply a primer to even out your skin tone.
  3. Use a contouring kit to create shadows on your face. Apply a darker shade of foundation or powder to the sides of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline.
  4. Apply a lighter shade of foundation or powder to the center of your face to highlight your features.
  5. Blend the contour and highlighter using a makeup brush or sponge.
  6. Use a brow pencil or powder to define your eyebrows.
  7. Apply a bold lipstick or eye makeup to draw attention away from your cheeks.
  8. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. A long bob or layered haircut can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.
  9. Wear clothes that fit well and flatter your body shape.
  10. Wear accessories that draw attention away from your face.

With these steps, you can achieve a slimmer-looking face in no time.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to make a round face look smaller:

  • Use a matte finish foundation or powder to avoid adding shine to your face.
  • Avoid bangs or short hairstyles that add volume to your cheeks.
  • Use a bronzer to add warmth to your face and create a natural-looking tan.
  • Avoid wearing hats or headbands that add width to your face.
  • Drink plenty of water and eat a healthy diet to keep your skin looking fresh and radiant.

Remember, these tips are just guidelines. Experiment with different techniques and find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Can face exercises make my face look smaller?

A: While some people believe that face exercises can make your face look smaller, there is no scientific evidence to support this claim. Instead, focus on contouring and other makeup techniques to achieve the desired look.

Q: Can a short haircut make my face look rounder?

A: Yes, a short haircut can make your face look rounder if it's not styled correctly. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheeks or jawline. Instead, opt for a long bob or layered haircut that elongates your face.

Hair Care for Round Faces

Choosing the right hairstyle is key to making a round face look smaller. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. A long bob or layered haircut can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheeks or jawline.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair without adding volume to your face.
  • Avoid using hair accessories that add width to your face, such as headbands or wide barrettes.
  • Consider getting bangs to soften your face shape, but avoid blunt or heavy bangs that add volume to your face.

Remember, the right hairstyle can make all the difference in making your face look smaller. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.

Conclusion

Having a round face is nothing to be ashamed of, but if you want to make it appear smaller, there are many ways to achieve this look. From contouring and makeup to hairstyles and clothing, there are many tips and tricks you can use to make your face look slimmer. Experiment with different techniques and find what works best for you. With the right tools and knowledge, you can achieve the look you desire.
