What To Wear In Summer Over 60

Written by William Oct 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Summer is here, and it's time to update your wardrobe. But if you're over 60, you may be wondering what to wear in the hot weather. Don't worry, we've got you covered with some tips and ideas on how to look stylish and feel comfortable in the summer heat. Here's what you need to know.

Description

As we grow older, our bodies change, and so do our fashion needs. What may have looked great on us a few years ago may not be suitable for us now. When it comes to summer fashion, it's essential to choose outfits that are comfortable, flattering, and appropriate for our age. But that doesn't mean you have to sacrifice style for comfort. With a little bit of creativity, you can look fabulous and feel great.

How to

So, what should you wear in the summer over 60? Here are some ideas:

1. Light and breezy fabrics

When the temperature rises, you want to wear clothes that are light and airy. Opt for fabrics like cotton, linen, and rayon that are breathable and comfortable. Avoid heavy fabrics like wool and polyester that can make you feel hot and sticky.

2. Loose-fitting clothes

Tight clothes may look great, but they're not always the most comfortable option in the summer heat. Choose loose-fitting tops, dresses, and pants that allow your skin to breathe. Avoid clothes that are too tight or too loose, as they can make you look frumpy.

3. Bright colors and prints

Summer is the perfect time to experiment with colors and prints. Bright colors like yellow, orange, and pink can add a pop of color to your outfit and make you look more youthful. Floral prints, polka dots, and stripes are also great options.

4. Sandals or comfortable shoes

In the summer, it's all about the footwear. You want shoes that are comfortable, stylish, and easy to wear. Sandals are a great option as they allow your feet to breathe and come in a variety of styles. If you prefer closed-toe shoes, opt for sneakers or loafers.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to put together a summer outfit:

Step 1: Pick a light and breezy fabric

Choose a fabric that is comfortable and breathable, like cotton or linen.

Step 2: Choose a loose-fitting top or dress

Opt for a top or dress that is loose-fitting and allows your skin to breathe. Avoid clothes that are too tight or too loose.

Step 3: Add a pop of color or print

Add a pop of color or print to your outfit to make it more interesting. Choose bright colors or bold prints.

Step 4: Choose comfortable shoes

Choose shoes that are comfortable and easy to wear, like sandals or sneakers.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing what to wear in summer over 60:

1. Don't be afraid to show some skin

Showing a little bit of skin can be flattering and make you feel more confident. But remember to keep it classy and age-appropriate.

2. Accessorize

Add some accessories like a statement necklace or a colorful scarf to your outfit to make it more interesting.

3. Wear sunscreen

Protect your skin from the sun's harmful rays by wearing sunscreen every day.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear shorts in the summer over 60?

A: Yes, you can wear shorts as long as they are comfortable and age-appropriate. Opt for knee-length shorts instead of short shorts.

Q: What colors should I avoid wearing in the summer?

A: Avoid wearing dark colors like black and navy as they can absorb heat and make you feel hotter.

Hair Care

Don't forget about your hair in the summer heat. Keep your hair off your face by wearing a ponytail or a bun. Use a hat or a scarf to protect your hair from the sun.

In conclusion, what you wear in the summer over 60 should be comfortable, flattering, and age-appropriate. Choose light and breezy fabrics, loose-fitting clothes, and add a pop of color or print to your outfit. Don't forget to accessorize and wear sunscreen to protect your skin. And finally, keep your hair off your face and protected from the sun.
