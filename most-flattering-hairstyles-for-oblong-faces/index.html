Most Flattering Hairstyles For Oblong Faces

Written by William Jun 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may have trouble finding the most flattering hairstyles. An oblong face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow jawline and forehead. But don't worry, there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter your face shape. In this article, we'll explore how to choose the most flattering hairstyles for oblong faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Oblong face shapes tend to look best with hairstyles that add width to the face. This can be achieved with layers, curls, and waves. Bangs can also be flattering for oblong faces, as they help to shorten the forehead. However, avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as they can make the face look even longer.

How to

The first step in choosing a flattering hairstyle for an oblong face is to determine your face shape. Look in the mirror and measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. If this measurement is significantly longer than the width of your face, you likely have an oblong face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, consider the length of your hair. Shorter hairstyles tend to be more flattering for oblong faces, as they add volume and width to the face. However, if you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers or waves to create the illusion of width.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by measuring the length and width of your face.

2. Consider the length of your hair and whether you prefer shorter or longer styles.

3. If you prefer shorter hair, consider a bob or pixie cut with layers.

4. If you prefer longer hair, consider adding layers or waves to create the illusion of width.

5. Bangs can also be flattering for oblong faces, but be sure to choose a style that is right for your face shape.

Tips

- When choosing a hairstyle, consider your hair texture and thickness. Thin, straight hair may not hold a curl well, while thick, curly hair may require more maintenance.

- Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

- Don't be afraid to ask your hairstylist for advice on which styles would be most flattering for your face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can oblong faces wear bangs?

A: Yes, bangs can be flattering for oblong faces. However, be sure to choose a style that is right for your face shape. Side-swept or layered bangs can help to shorten the forehead and add width to the face.

Q: Are there any hairstyles that oblong faces should avoid?

A: Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as they can make the face look even longer. Also, avoid hairstyles that are too flat, as they can make the face look narrow and unbalanced.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to use products that are appropriate for your hair type and texture. Regular trims can also help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. If you color your hair, be sure to use products that are designed for color-treated hair to prevent fading and damage.

By following these tips, you can find the most flattering hairstyles for oblong faces and feel confident and beautiful every day.
