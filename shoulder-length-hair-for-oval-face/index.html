Shoulder Length Hair For Oval Face The Perfect Look

Written by Christine Dec 01, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oval face, you are in luck because you can pull off almost any hairstyle. But if you are looking for a timeless, low-maintenance hairstyle that flatters your face shape, shoulder-length hair is a great option. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve the perfect shoulder-length hair for oval face, step by step.

Description

Shoulder length hair is a versatile hairstyle that suits almost everyone, especially those with an oval face. Oval faces have balanced proportions, meaning they are longer than wide, with a slightly rounded chin and forehead. This face shape can pull off any length of hair, but shoulder length hair is particularly flattering as it accentuates the length of the face while framing it nicely.

How to Achieve the Perfect Shoulder Length Hair for Oval Face

Step 1: Consult with your stylist

The first step in achieving the perfect shoulder length hair for oval face is to consult with your stylist. They can help you determine the best length, cut, and style that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle.

Step 2: Choose the right length

Shoulder length hair can vary in length, from just above the shoulders to the collarbone. The right length for you will depend on your face shape and personal style. If you have a longer neck, you can opt for a longer length, while those with a shorter neck may prefer a shorter length.

Step 3: Choose the right cut

The right cut for shoulder length hair for oval face is a layered cut. Layers add volume and texture to the hair, making it look fuller and more dimensional. Your stylist can help you determine the right amount of layering for your hair type and face shape.

Step 4: Choose the right style

There are many ways to style shoulder length hair, from straight and sleek to wavy and tousled. The right style for you will depend on your personal style, hair type, and face shape. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any style, but a middle part or side part with loose waves is particularly flattering.

Tips for Maintaining Shoulder Length Hair for Oval Face

Once you have achieved the perfect shoulder length hair for oval face, it is important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking healthy and vibrant:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and damage it

FAQ

Can I pull off shoulder length hair if I don't have an oval face?

Yes, shoulder length hair can look great on any face shape, but it is particularly flattering on oval faces.

Do I need to use special products for shoulder length hair?

No, you can use the same products you normally use for your hair, but it is important to choose products that are suitable for your hair type.

Is shoulder length hair low maintenance?

Yes, shoulder length hair is relatively low maintenance compared to longer hairstyles, but it still requires regular upkeep to keep it looking healthy and vibrant.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Hair for Oval Face

Proper hair care is important for maintaining shoulder length hair for oval face. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and damage it
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as this can cause breakage

By following these tips, you can keep your shoulder length hair for oval face looking healthy, vibrant, and beautiful.
