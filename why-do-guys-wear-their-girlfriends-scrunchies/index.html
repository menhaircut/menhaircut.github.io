Why Do Guys Wear Their Girlfriends Scrunchies

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever noticed your boyfriend or male friend wearing your scrunchies on their wrist or even in their hair? It may seem strange, but it’s becoming a common trend among guys. In this blog post, we’ll explore why do guys wear their girlfriends scrunchies and the reasons behind this phenomenon.

What is a Scrunchie?

Before we dive into the reasons why guys wear scrunchies, let’s first define what a scrunchie is. A scrunchie is a hair accessory that is used to tie a ponytail or a bun. It’s made of a fabric-covered elastic band and is usually adorned with different designs and patterns.

Why Do Guys Wear Scrunchies?

There are several reasons why guys wear scrunchies. One of the most common reasons is that they want to show off their girlfriend’s or female friend’s affection. When a guy wears his girlfriend’s scrunchie, it’s a sign that he’s taken and proud to be in a relationship.

Another reason why guys wear scrunchies is for fashion. Scrunchies have become a trendy accessory, and guys are jumping on the bandwagon. They’re wearing scrunchies as a way to accessorize their outfits and add a pop of color to their looks.

Some guys also wear scrunchies as a practical solution to keep their hair out of their face. Whether they have long hair or short hair, a scrunchie can be a convenient hair tie that’s easy to use and comfortable to wear.

How to Wear a Scrunchie

If you’re a guy who wants to try wearing a scrunchie, here’s how to wear it:

  1. Choose a scrunchie that matches your outfit or personality.
  2. Decide whether you want to wear it on your wrist or in your hair.
  3. If you want to wear it on your wrist, simply wrap it around your wrist like a bracelet.
  4. If you want to wear it in your hair, gather your hair into a ponytail or bun and wrap the scrunchie around it.

Tips for Wearing a Scrunchie

If you’re new to wearing scrunchies, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a scrunchie that’s comfortable to wear and doesn’t pull your hair.
  • Don’t wear a scrunchie that’s too tight, as it can cause headaches and hair breakage.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as wearing a scrunchie on the side of your head or using multiple scrunchies for a layered look.
  • Have fun with it! Wearing a scrunchie is all about expressing your personality and style.

FAQs About Guys Wearing Scrunchies

Q: Is it weird for guys to wear scrunchies?

A: No, it’s not weird for guys to wear scrunchies. It’s becoming a popular trend, and many guys are embracing it.

Q: Can guys wear any type of scrunchie?

A: Yes, guys can wear any type of scrunchie that they feel comfortable with. There are different designs and patterns to choose from, so it’s all about personal preference.

Q: How do I know if a scrunchie is too tight?

A: If a scrunchie is too tight, you may feel discomfort or pain around your head. You may also notice hair breakage or headaches. It’s important to choose a scrunchie that’s comfortable to wear and doesn’t pull your hair.

Hair Care Tips When Wearing Scrunchies

When wearing scrunchies, it’s important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid wearing scrunchies too tight, as it can cause hair breakage and headaches.
  • Use a detangling spray or conditioner to prevent knots and tangles.
  • Don’t wear scrunchies to bed, as they can cause friction and damage to your hair.
  • Wash your hair regularly and use a nourishing hair mask to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Now that you know why do guys wear their girlfriends scrunchies, you can decide whether you want to try this trend for yourself. Whether you wear a scrunchie for fashion or practicality, remember to have fun with it and express your personality.
