Haircuts For Round Faces And Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of trying out different haircuts for your round face and curly hair but nothing seems to work? Well, worry no more because we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the best haircuts for round faces and curly hair that will flatter your features and enhance your natural curls. So, sit back and relax as we take you through this ultimate guide.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

Round faces and curly hair can be a tricky combination when it comes to choosing the right haircut. You want to enhance your natural curls while also flattering your features. The key is to choose a haircut that adds height to your crown and elongates your face. In this guide, we will show you the best haircuts for round faces and curly hair that will do just that.

How To:

The first step in choosing the right haircut for your round face and curly hair is to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is to measure the length and width of your face and compare the two. If they are roughly the same, then you have a round face. Once you have determined your face shape, it's time to choose the right haircut.

Step By Step:

1. Long Layers:

Long layers work best for round faces and curly hair as they add height to your crown and elongate your face. Ask your stylist to cut long layers that start at your chin and go all the way down. This will create movement and texture in your hair while also enhancing your natural curls.

2. Bob:

A bob is another great haircut for round faces and curly hair. Ask your stylist to cut a chin-length bob that is angled towards the front. This will create a flattering shape that elongates your face while also highlighting your natural curls.

3. Pixie Cut:

If you're feeling bold, a pixie cut is a great option for round faces and curly hair. Ask your stylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back while leaving the top longer. This will create a flattering shape that adds height to your crown and elongates your face.

Tips:

When choosing a haircut for round faces and curly hair, it's important to consider your hair texture and density. If you have thin hair, opt for a haircut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, opt for a haircut that thins out your hair and creates movement.

FAQ:

1. Can I straighten my curly hair?

Yes, you can straighten your curly hair. However, it's important to use heat protectant products and a low heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

2. How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your haircut and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care:

To keep your hair healthy and enhance your natural curls, it's important to use the right hair care products. Opt for sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils. Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and a curl-enhancing product to define your curls.

So, there you have it, the ultimate guide to haircuts for round faces and curly hair. Choose the right haircut for your face shape and hair texture, and use the right hair care products to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
