How To Use Toner To Fix Brassy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Sep 29, 2023 · 5 min read
If you have blonde or highlighted hair, you may have experienced the dreaded brassiness. This happens when your hair turns yellow or orange instead of the cool, icy tones you were aiming for. But don't panic! There's an easy solution: toner. In this post, we'll show you how to use toner to fix brassy hair, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Use Toner to Fix Brassy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Toner
  • FAQ

Description

Toner is a hair product that neutralizes unwanted tones in your hair. It can be used on blonde, gray, and silver hair to remove brassy or yellow tones. Toner is usually applied after bleaching or highlighting, but it can also be used on natural hair that has turned brassy over time.

How to Use Toner to Fix Brassy Hair

Using toner is not difficult, but it does require some preparation and knowledge. Here are the basic steps for using toner to fix brassy hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Choose the Right Toner There are many toners available on the market, so you need to choose the right one for your hair. Look for a toner that is specifically designed for your hair color and the level of brassiness you want to correct. If you're not sure which toner to choose, ask your hairstylist for recommendations. Step 2: Prepare Your Hair Before applying toner, you need to prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo. This will remove any buildup or residue from your hair, allowing the toner to penetrate better. Make sure to rinse your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it. Step 3: Mix the Toner Follow the instructions on the toner package to mix it with the developer. The developer is a liquid that activates the toner and helps it penetrate your hair. Mix the two products in a plastic bowl with a tint brush. Step 4: Apply the Toner Using the tint brush, apply the toner to your hair, starting from the roots and working your way down to the ends. Make sure to saturate all the hair evenly. If you have long hair, it may be easier to apply the toner in sections. Step 5: Wait The timing for toner varies depending on the product you're using and the level of brassiness you want to correct. Follow the instructions on the package and set a timer. Don't leave the toner on for too long, or it may overtone your hair. Step 6: Rinse and Condition Once the timer goes off, rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water until the water runs clear. Don't use shampoo, as it can strip the toner from your hair. Instead, apply a deep conditioner and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it off.

Tips for Using Toner

- Always do a patch test before using toner to make sure you're not allergic to the product. - Use gloves and old clothes when applying toner, as it can stain your skin and clothes. - If you have very brassy hair, you may need to use a toner with a higher volume of developer. - Don't leave the toner on for too long, or it may turn your hair purple or gray. - Use a purple shampoo and conditioner after toning to maintain your cool tones.

FAQ

Q: Can toner damage my hair? A: Toner is a gentle product that doesn't damage hair. However, if you bleach or highlight your hair too often, it can become dry and brittle. Q: How often should I use toner? A: It depends on your hair color, but most people use toner every 4-6 weeks to maintain their cool tones. Q: Can I use toner on natural hair? A: Yes, toner can be used on natural hair that has turned brassy over time.

Hair Care after Toning

After toning your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain your cool tones. Here are some tips for hair care after toning: - Use a purple shampoo and conditioner to prevent brassiness. - Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can damage it and fade the toner. - Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and shiny. - Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage. - Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray. Using toner to fix brassy hair is easy and effective. Just follow our step-by-step guide, and you'll have cool, icy tones in no time. Remember to choose the right toner, prepare your hair, and follow the instructions carefully. With the right care, your hair will look beautiful and healthy.
