The Most Popular Type Of Hair Extension A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you curious about hair extensions but don't know which type to choose? With so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is right for you. In this article, we'll dive into the most popular type of hair extension and give you all the information you need to make an informed decision.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The most popular type of hair extension is the clip-in extension. Clip-in extensions are easy to install and remove, and they're perfect for adding length, volume, or highlights to your hair. They come in various lengths, colors, and styles, making it easy to find the perfect match for your hair.

Clip-in extensions are made from human hair or synthetic fibers. Human hair extensions are more expensive but look more natural and can be styled with heat tools like straighteners and curling irons. Synthetic extensions are more affordable but can't be styled with heat tools and don't look as natural as human hair extensions.

How to

Installing clip-in extensions is easy and can be done at home without the help of a professional. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Section your hair from ear to ear and clip the top section away.
  3. Take a weft of hair extension and clip it onto the roots of your hair in the section you just created.
  4. Release the top section of your hair and repeat steps 2-3 until you've added all the wefts you want.
  5. Once you've installed all the wefts, use a straightener or curling iron to blend your hair and the extensions together.

Step by step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to install clip-in extensions:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry or blow-drying it.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Section your hair from ear to ear and clip the top section away.
  4. Take a weft of hair extension and measure it against the section of your hair where you want to add length or volume. Trim the weft if necessary.
  5. Open the clips on the weft.
  6. Starting at the back of your head, clip the weft onto the roots of your hair in the section you just created. Make sure the weft is secure and the clips are hidden under your hair.
  7. Release the top section of your hair and repeat steps 3-6 until you've added all the wefts you want.
  8. Once you've installed all the wefts, use a straightener or curling iron to blend your hair and the extensions together.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your clip-in extensions:

  • Choose extensions that match your hair color for a natural look.
  • Don't install too many wefts at once, or your hair will look bulky and unnatural.
  • Use heat protectant spray if you're styling your extensions with heat tools.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when you're not wearing them.
  • Don't sleep with your extensions in to avoid tangling and damage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about clip-in extensions:

How long do clip-in extensions last?

Clip-in extensions can last up to a year with proper care.

Can I dye my clip-in extensions?

Human hair clip-in extensions can be dyed, but synthetic extensions cannot.

Can I swim with my clip-in extensions?

It's best to avoid swimming with your clip-in extensions, as chlorine and salt water can damage them.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your clip-in extensions looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Brush your extensions gently with a wide-tooth comb to avoid tangling.
  • Wash your extensions every 6-8 wears with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat tools on your extensions too often.
  • Don't use heavy products like hair spray or gel on your extensions, as they can weigh them down.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when you're not wearing them.

Now that you know all about clip-in extensions, you're ready to choose the perfect set for you. Whether you're looking for length, volume, or highlights, clip-in extensions are a versatile and easy-to-use option that can transform your look in minutes.
