Once you know your skin tone, you can start choosing colors that compliment it. Here are some general guidelines:

  • Warm skin tones look best in colors that have yellow or golden undertones, such as mustard, coral, and olive.

FAQ

What colors should I avoid if I have a cool skin tone?

Colors that have yellow or orange undertones should be avoided, as they can make cool skin tones look sallow or washed out. These colors include mustard, peach, and rust.

What colors should I avoid if I have a warm skin tone?

Colors that have blue or purple undertones should be avoided, as they can make warm skin tones look dull or ashy. These colors include navy, lavender, and silver.
