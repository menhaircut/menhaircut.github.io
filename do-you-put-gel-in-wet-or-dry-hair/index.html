Do You Put Gel In Wet Or Dry Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who loves using hair gel in your everyday styling routine, you may have asked yourself, "do you put gel in wet or dry hair?" The answer to this question can vary depending on the type of gel you're using and the hairstyle you're trying to achieve. In this article, we'll explore the different factors to consider when using hair gel and help you determine whether you should apply it to wet or dry hair.

Description

Hair gel is a styling product that is commonly used to help hold hair in place. It comes in a variety of formulas and strengths, ranging from light hold to strong hold. Some gels are designed to be applied to wet hair, while others work best on dry hair. Understanding the differences between these types of gels and when to use them is key to achieving your desired look.

How to

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

The first step in determining whether to apply gel to wet or dry hair is to consider your hair type. If you have fine or thin hair, applying gel to wet hair can help add volume and texture. On the other hand, if you have thick or curly hair, applying gel to dry hair can help define your curls and minimize frizz.

Step 2: Choose the Right Gel Formula

Another important factor to consider is the formula of the gel you're using. If you're using a lightweight gel that is designed for wet hair, applying it to dry hair can leave your hair feeling stiff and crunchy. Similarly, if you're using a heavy-duty gel that is designed for dry hair, applying it to wet hair may not provide enough hold.

Step 3: Apply the Gel

Once you've determined whether to apply your gel to wet or dry hair, it's time to apply it. If you're applying gel to wet hair, start by towel-drying your hair so that it's damp but not dripping wet. Then, apply a quarter-sized amount of gel to your palms and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. If you're applying gel to dry hair, start by warming a small amount of gel between your palms and then apply it to your hair, focusing on the areas where you want the most hold.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when using gel:

  • Use a small amount of gel to start and add more as needed.
  • Don't apply gel directly to your scalp, as this can cause buildup and flaking.
  • Experiment with different gel formulas and application methods to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Can you apply gel to both wet and dry hair?

Yes, some gels are designed to be used on both wet and dry hair. However, it's important to read the label and follow the instructions to ensure you're using the gel correctly for your hair type.

How much gel should I use?

Start with a small amount of gel and add more as needed. Using too much gel can leave your hair feeling stiff and crunchy.

Can you use gel every day?

Yes, you can use gel every day, but it's important to wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup and flaking.

Hair Care

Using hair gel is a great way to achieve your desired hairstyle, but it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Make sure to wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot styling tools too frequently. Additionally, consider using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or straightening your hair to minimize damage.

In conclusion, the answer to the question "do you put gel in wet or dry hair?" depends on your hair type and the gel formula you're using. By following these tips and experimenting with different application methods, you can achieve your desired look while keeping your hair healthy and strong.
