How To Wear A Hat With Short Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 04, 2023 ¬∑ 4 min read

Wearing a hat can add a stylish touch to any outfit, but for those with short curly hair, it can be a bit tricky. If you're worried about messing up your curls, or if you're not sure how to properly wear a hat with your hair type, don't worry! In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to wear a hat with short curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear a Hat with Short Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Hat with Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

How to Wear a Hat with Short Curly Hair

The key to wearing a hat with short curly hair is to find the right style that works for you. Here are some popular hat styles that work well with short curly hair:

  • Beanie
  • Beret
  • Baseball Cap
  • Fedora
  • Bucket Hat

When choosing a hat, keep in mind the size and shape of your head, as well as your hair type and the style of the hat. A hat that is too small or too large can look awkward, and certain styles may not work well with your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to wear a hat with short curly hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Avoid wearing a hat on wet or damp hair, as this can cause frizz and damage to your curls.
  2. Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to moisturize and define your curls.
  3. Choose a hat that fits comfortably and doesn't squash your curls.
  4. Position the hat on your head, making sure it sits securely and comfortably.
  5. If you're wearing a beanie or beret, pull it down just above your eyebrows. For a baseball cap, position it so that the brim is parallel to your eyebrows.
  6. Style your curls as desired. You can leave them loose or pull them back into a low ponytail or bun.

Remember, the key is to find a hat style that works with your curls and complements your overall look.

Tips for Wearing a Hat with Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for wearing a hat with short curly hair:

  • Avoid wearing tight-fitting hats, as they can squish your curls and cause frizz.
  • If you have a lot of volume, consider wearing a hat with a wider brim, such as a fedora or bucket hat.
  • If you have a shorter haircut, try a beanie or beret for a more casual look.
  • If you're wearing a baseball cap, consider wearing your hair in a low ponytail or bun to avoid flattening your curls.
  • Experiment with different hat styles and hair accessories to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing a hat with short curly hair:

Q: Can I wear a hat on wet or damp hair?

A: It's best to avoid wearing a hat on wet or damp hair, as this can cause frizz and damage to your curls.

Q: How do I prevent my curls from flattening under a hat?

A: Try wearing a hat with a wider brim, such as a fedora or bucket hat, or wear your hair in a low ponytail or bun to avoid flattening your curls.

Q: What is the best hat style for short curly hair?

A: Popular hat styles that work well with short curly hair include beanies, berets, baseball caps, fedoras, and bucket hats. It's important to find a hat style that complements your curls and your overall look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips for caring for your short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to moisturize and define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can cause damage to your curls.
  • Protect your curls from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a hat with your short curly hair and rock any look you choose.
