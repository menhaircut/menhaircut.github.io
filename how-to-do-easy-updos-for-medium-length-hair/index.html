How To Do Easy Updos For Medium Length Hair

Written by Bardi Oct 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same hairstyle every day? Do you want to try something new without spending a lot of time and money? If yes, then keep reading! Updos are the perfect option for those who want to elevate their look without a lot of effort. In this article, we will show you how to do easy updos for medium length hair.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

Updos are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. They are versatile, elegant, and perfect for any occasion. Whether you are going to a wedding, a job interview, or a night out with friends, updos can be the perfect solution to a bad hair day. They are also great for medium length hair because they can help you create volume and texture.

How to

To create an easy updo for medium length hair, you will need some basic tools such as bobby pins, hair ties, and hairspray. You can also use some accessories like headbands, clips, or flowers to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.

Step by Step

Follow these simple steps to create an easy updo for medium length hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Use hairspray to set your hairstyle in place.

Alternatively, you can create a messy bun by pulling some hair out of the hair tie and loosening the bun. This will create a more casual and effortless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your updo look even better:

  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add some volume to your hair before creating the updo.
  • Experiment with different accessories like headbands, clips, or flowers to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • If you have thin or fine hair, you can use extensions to add some volume and length to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with different styles and techniques to find the perfect updo for your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can updos be done on all hair types?

A: Yes, updos can be done on all hair types. However, some hair types may require extra styling products or techniques to achieve the desired look.

Q: How long does it take to create an updo?

A: It depends on the complexity of the updo and your skill level. Simple updos can be done in a few minutes, while more complex updos may take up to an hour.

Hair Care

Updos can be a great way to protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling and environmental factors. However, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can create easy updos for medium length hair that will make you look and feel great. So, what are you waiting for? Give it a try and see how much of a difference an updo can make!
