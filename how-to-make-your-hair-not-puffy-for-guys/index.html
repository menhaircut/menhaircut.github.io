How To Make Your Hair Not Puffy For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with naturally curly or wavy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to deal with puffiness. It can make styling your hair a challenge and leave you feeling less than confident in your appearance. But fear not! There are steps you can take to make your hair less puffy and more manageable. In this article, we'll provide you with some tips and tricks for achieving the perfect, non-puffy look.

How to Make Your Hair Not Puffy for Guys

Step 1: Use the Right Products

The first step in preventing puffy hair is to use the right products. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are specifically designed for curly or wavy hair. These products will help smooth your hair and tame frizz, leaving you with a sleeker, less puffy look.

Step 2: Avoid Over-Shampooing

Over-shampooing your hair can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and prone to puffiness. Instead, try washing your hair every other day or every few days, and use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo that won't dry out your hair.

Step 3: Air Dry Your Hair

Blow-drying your hair can cause it to become puffy, so try air-drying instead. After washing your hair, gently squeeze out any excess water with a towel, and then let your hair dry naturally. If you need to speed up the drying process, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help distribute the heat more evenly.

Step 4: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

When combing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb instead of a brush. This will help prevent tangles and breakage, which can lead to more frizz and puffiness. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots, being careful not to yank or pull on your hair.

Step 5: Apply Product When Your Hair is Wet

When applying styling products to your hair, do so when it's still wet. This will help distribute the product evenly and prevent clumping or buildup. Use a dime-sized amount of product and work it through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.

Tips for Preventing Puffy Hair

Here are some additional tips for preventing puffy hair:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and puffiness.
  • Wear a hat or beanie on rainy or humid days to protect your hair from the elements.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and cause it to become puffy.
  • Consider getting a haircut that's specifically designed for curly or wavy hair, as this can help reduce puffiness and make your hair easier to manage.

FAQs

Q: Can I still use gel or mousse on my hair?

A: Yes! Just be sure to use a small amount and apply it when your hair is wet. Avoid using too much product, as this can cause buildup and make your hair look greasy.

Q: What should I do if my hair starts to get puffy during the day?

A: Keep a small bottle of hair serum or oil with you to help smooth down any frizz or puffiness. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and gently work it through your hair.

Hair Care for Non-Puffy Hair

Now that you know how to prevent puffy hair, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, non-puffy hair:

  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking and feeling its best.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair, as these can damage your hair and make it more prone to puffiness.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can both damage your hair and make it more prone to puffiness.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect, non-puffy look for your curly or wavy hair. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and styling techniques until you find what works best for you.
