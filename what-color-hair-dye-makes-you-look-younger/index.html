What Color Hair Dye Makes You Look Younger

Written by Aprilia Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to freshen up your look and turn back the clock, consider changing your hair color! A new hair color can take years off your appearance and make you look and feel younger. But what color hair dye makes you look younger? In this article, we'll explore the answer to that question and provide some tips and tricks for achieving a youthful, vibrant look.

Description

As we age, our hair naturally loses pigment and becomes more brittle and prone to damage. This can lead to dull, lifeless hair that makes us look older than we feel. But by choosing the right hair color, you can add shine, depth, and dimension to your locks and create a more youthful appearance.

How to Choose the Right Hair Color

When it comes to choosing a hair color that will make you look younger, there are a few things to keep in mind:

Consider Your Skin Tone

The right hair color for you will depend largely on your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, you'll look best in shades of red, copper, and warm blondes. If you have cool undertones, go for ash blondes, cool browns, and black.

Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hair color, consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance you're willing to put in. If you're busy and don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, opt for a low-maintenance color that doesn't require a lot of upkeep.

Don't Go Too Dark

As we age, our skin tends to become lighter and more translucent. If you go too dark with your hair color, it can create a harsh contrast that makes you look older. Stick to lighter shades that will brighten up your complexion.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Color

Follow these steps to find the perfect hair color for a more youthful look:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they look green, you have warm undertones.

Step 2: Choose a Shade

Based on your skin tone, choose a shade that will complement your complexion. If you have warm undertones, go for shades of red, copper, and warm blondes. If you have cool undertones, opt for ash blondes, cool browns, and black.

Step 3: Consider Your Lifestyle

Think about how much maintenance you're willing to put in. If you're busy and don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, choose a low-maintenance color that doesn't require a lot of upkeep.

Step 4: Test It Out

Before committing to a hair color, do a patch test to make sure you're not allergic to any of the ingredients. You can also try a temporary color or highlights to see how you like the new shade before making a permanent change.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've found the perfect hair color, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking fresh and vibrant. Here are some tips for maintaining your new shade:

Use Color-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Regular shampoo and conditioner can strip the color from your hair, so it's important to use products specifically designed for color-treated hair.

Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause your color to fade more quickly. Try to limit your use of these tools and use a heat protectant spray when you do.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can cause your hair color to fade, so be sure to wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you're out in the sun for extended periods of time.

FAQ

What hair color makes you look younger?

Shades of red, copper, warm blondes, ash blondes, cool browns, and black can all make you look younger, depending on your skin tone.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home with a store-bought hair color kit. Just be sure to follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test first.

How often should I touch up my roots?

This will depend on your hair growth rate and the color you choose. Generally, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.
