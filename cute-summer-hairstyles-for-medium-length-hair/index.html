Cute Summer Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Aprilia Jul 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Summer is the perfect time to experiment with new hairstyles, especially if you have medium length hair. There are so many fun and easy styles to try that will keep you looking cute and cool all summer long. In this article, we will show you some of the best cute summer hairstyles for medium length hair!

Description

Medium length hair is a versatile length that allows you to try different hairstyles without having to worry about the length being too short or too long. During the summer, it's important to have hairstyles that will keep you cool and comfortable, while still looking stylish. Cute summer hairstyles for medium length hair can range from braids to buns, and everything in between. These hairstyles are easy to do and will keep you looking cute and put together all summer long.

How To

The first step in creating cute summer hairstyles for medium length hair is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can start by washing your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup or excess oils. Once your hair is clean, you can use a lightweight conditioner to add moisture and prevent frizz.

Next, you can prep your hair by applying a heat protectant spray, especially if you plan on using hot tools to style your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy. You can also apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to add volume and hold to your hair.

Once your hair is prepped, you can start creating your cute summer hairstyle. Depending on the style you choose, you may need to section your hair and use hair ties or clips to keep it in place. You can also use hair accessories, such as headbands or scrunchies, to add extra flair to your hairstyle.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for creating some of the best cute summer hairstyles for medium length hair:

1. Beachy Waves

To create beachy waves, start by dividing your hair into sections. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron or wand, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process for all sections of your hair, alternating the direction of the curls. Once your hair is curled, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural, beachy look.

2. Braided Crown

To create a braided crown, start by parting your hair down the middle. Take a small section of hair from one side of your head and braid it, securing the end with a hair tie. Repeat this process on the other side of your head. Once you have two braids, cross them over your head and secure them with bobby pins. You can also add flowers or other hair accessories to complete the look.

3. Messy Bun

To create a messy bun, start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail. Twist the ponytail around itself until it forms a bun, securing it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy, relaxed look. You can also add a headband or scarf to complete the look.

Tips

Here are some tips for creating the best cute summer hairstyles for medium length hair:

- Use lightweight styling products to prevent your hair from feeling heavy or greasy - Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and scrunchies, to add extra flair to your hairstyle - Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques, and have fun with your hair!

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cute summer hairstyles for medium length hair:

What are some easy summer hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some easy summer hairstyles for medium length hair include beachy waves, braids, messy buns, and half-up half-down styles.

What are some cute hairstyles for medium length hair?

Some cute hairstyles for medium length hair include braided crowns, top knots, low ponytails, and side swept hairstyles.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair during the summer, especially if you plan on spending a lot of time outdoors. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and hydrated:

- Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove product buildup - Apply a leave-in conditioner to add moisture and prevent frizz - Wear a hat or use a hair sunscreen to protect your hair from UV rays - Avoid using hot tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray when you do use them

Now that you know some of the best cute summer hairstyles for medium length hair, it's time to start experimenting and finding the styles that work best for you. Whether you prefer braids, buns, or beachy waves, there's a cute summer hairstyle out there for everyone!
