How To Blow Dry Men s Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be difficult to manage. But with the right tools and techniques, you can tame those curls and create a smooth, stylish look. In this article, we'll show you how to blow dry men's curly hair, step by step. Whether you're getting ready for work or a night out, these tips will help you achieve the perfect hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • How to Prepare Your Hair for Blow Drying
  • How to Blow Dry Men's Curly Hair: Step by Step
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfect Blowout
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Prepare Your Hair for Blow Drying

Before you start blow drying your hair, it's important to prepare your locks properly. Here are the steps you should follow:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, as they will help to hydrate and nourish your locks.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Next, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to prevent damage from the blow dryer and other styling tools. Spray the heat protectant onto your damp hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that are particularly dry.

Step 3: Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots, taking care not to pull or tug on your locks.

How to Blow Dry Men's Curly Hair: Step by Step

Now that your hair is prepared for blow drying, it's time to get started. Follow these steps for the best results:

Step 1: Divide Your Hair into Sections

Divide your hair into sections, starting at the back of your head. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 2: Use a Round Brush

Use a round brush to help smooth your hair as you blow dry. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the brush to pull your hair straight.

Step 3: Use the Right Heat Setting

Use a low or medium heat setting on your blow dryer, as high heat can damage your hair. Hold the dryer about six inches away from your head, and move it around as you dry each section.

Step 4: Finish with a Blast of Cool Air

When you've finished blow drying your hair, switch your dryer to the cool air setting and give your locks a final blast of cool air. This will help to set your style and make it last longer.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Blowout

To get the best results from your blowout, follow these tips:

  • Use a high-quality blow dryer with a diffuser attachment.
  • Invest in a good round brush, ideally one with natural bristles.
  • Don't forget to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Take your time and work in small sections to ensure that each part of your hair is properly dried.
  • Finish with a spritz of hairspray to keep your style in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions about blow drying men's curly hair:

How often should I blow dry my hair?

It's best to limit blow drying to once or twice a week, as frequent heat styling can damage your hair.

Can I blow dry my hair without a round brush?

Yes, you can use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to help smooth your hair as you blow dry.

How long does it take to blow dry curly hair?

It depends on the length and thickness of your hair, but you should allow at least 20-30 minutes for the process.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

To keep your curls looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your locks hydrated.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent breakage and frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a smooth, stylish blowout for your curly hair. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and protect your hair from heat damage. Happy styling!
