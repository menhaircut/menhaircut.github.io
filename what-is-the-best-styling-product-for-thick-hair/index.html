What Is The Best Styling Product For Thick Hair

Written by Frank Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse. It’s great for creating voluminous styles, but it can also be difficult to manage and style. Luckily, there are many styling products on the market that can help tame and style thick hair. In this article, we’ll discuss the best styling product for thick hair, how to use it, and some tips and tricks for maintaining healthy hair.

Table of Contents

1. Description
2. How to Use
3. Step by Step
4. Tips and Tricks
5. Frequently Asked Questions
6. Hair Care Tips

Description

The best styling product for thick hair is a pomade. Pomades are great for thick hair because they provide hold and control without weighing the hair down. They also add shine and can be used to create a variety of styles, from slicked-back looks to messy, textured styles.

When choosing a pomade for thick hair, look for one that is water-based. Water-based pomades are easier to wash out and won’t leave residue in your hair. They also tend to have a lighter feel than oil-based pomades.

How to Use

To use pomade on thick hair, start with clean, damp hair. Rub a small amount of pomade between your palms and work it through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.

If you’re looking for a slicked-back look, comb your hair straight back from your forehead. For a messy, textured look, use your fingers to create a tousled look.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, damp hair.
2. Rub a small amount of pomade between your palms.
3. Work the pomade through your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired.

Tips and Tricks

1. Use a small amount of pomade to avoid weighing your hair down.
2. Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair type.
3. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
4. Avoid using heat-styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
5. Use a leave-in conditioner to help prevent frizz and keep your hair healthy.
6. Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on styling products and techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can pomade be used on all hair types?
A: Yes, pomade can be used on all hair types, but it is especially effective on thick hair.

Q: Will pomade make my hair look greasy?
A: No, as long as you use a water-based pomade and apply it sparingly, your hair should not look greasy.

Q: How often should I use pomade?
A: It depends on your hair type and the style you’re trying to achieve. Experiment with different amounts and frequencies to find what works best for you.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
2. Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
3. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of natural oils.
4. Use a hair serum to add shine and protect your hair from heat styling tools.
5. Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

Overall, pomade is the best styling product for thick hair. It provides hold and control without weighing your hair down, and it can be used to create a variety of styles. Use the tips and tricks in this article to achieve the perfect look for your thick hair, and don’t forget to take care of your hair with regular maintenance and healthy habits.
