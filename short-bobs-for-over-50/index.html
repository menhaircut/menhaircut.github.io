Short Bobs For Over 50 A Guide To Timeless Style

Written by Eveline Nov 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you over 50 and looking for a fresh, modern hairstyle? Consider a short bob! This versatile cut is perfect for women of all ages, and it can be tailored to suit your unique features and personality. In this guide, we’ll show you how to get the perfect short bob for your age, face shape, and hair type.

How to Choose the Right Short Bob for Your Face Shape

Before you book your appointment, it’s important to consider your face shape. A short bob can be flattering for almost any face shape, but you’ll want to choose the right length and shape to accentuate your best features.

Round Face

If you have a round face, a chin-length bob with layers can help elongate your features and create a more flattering shape. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as these can make your face appear wider.

Square Face

A square face can be softened with a short, layered bob that falls just below your chin. This style will help frame your face and bring attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Oval Face

If you have an oval face, you’re in luck – almost any short bob style will flatter your features! Consider a blunt cut that falls just above your shoulders, or a layered bob with side-swept bangs.

Heart-Shaped Face

A heart-shaped face looks great with a short bob that’s layered and angled around your chin. This style will balance out your features and draw attention to your jawline.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Bob

Ready to take the plunge and get a short bob? Follow these steps for a seamless salon experience:

  1. Do your research and choose a salon that specializes in short hairstyles.
  2. Bring inspiration photos to your appointment so your stylist can get a sense of the look you’re going for.
  3. Discuss your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle with your stylist to determine the best cut for you.
  4. Sit back and relax while your stylist works their magic!
  5. Ask your stylist for tips on styling your new cut at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Bob

Once you’ve got your short bob, you’ll want to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips for maintaining your new cut:

  • Visit the salon every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your cut looking sharp.
  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove buildup and keep your hair healthy.
  • Invest in a high-quality styling product, like a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray, to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider switching up your part for a fresh, new look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear a short bob if I have thin hair?

Absolutely! A short bob can actually make thin hair look fuller and more voluminous. Just be sure to ask your stylist for layers and texture to add dimension to your cut.

Is a short bob high maintenance?

Not necessarily. While a short bob does require more frequent trims than longer styles, it can actually be easier to style and maintain on a day-to-day basis.

What’s the best way to style a short bob?

There are endless ways to style a short bob, depending on your hair type and personal preference. Experiment with different styling products, like texturizing spray, volumizing mousse, and smoothing serum, to find what works best for you.

Final Thoughts on Short Bobs for Over 50

A short bob is a timeless, stylish cut that’s perfect for women of all ages, especially those over 50. With the right length, shape, and styling, you can create a look that’s flattering, low-maintenance, and easy to maintain. So why not book your appointment today and try out this classic cut for yourself?
