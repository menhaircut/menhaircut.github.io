Can Anyone Pull Off A Pixie Cut

Written by Daniel Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're considering a pixie cut, you might be wondering if you have the right face shape or hair type to pull it off. The truth is, anyone can rock a pixie cut! It's all about finding the right style that flatters your features and suits your personality. In this article, we'll answer your most pressing questions about pixie cuts and give you tips on how to make the most of your new 'do.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically features layers and a tapered back. It can be styled in a variety of ways, from sleek and polished to tousled and messy. Pixie cuts are popular among women of all ages and hair types, and they're a great way to make a bold statement and showcase your unique style.

How To

If you're ready to take the plunge and get a pixie cut, here's what you need to know:

Step 1: Find the Right Style

There are many different types of pixie cuts, so it's important to find one that flatters your features and suits your personal style. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle when choosing a pixie cut. A professional stylist can help you find the perfect style for you.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before getting a pixie cut, it's important to prepare your hair. Avoid washing your hair for a day or two before your appointment, as freshly washed hair can be harder to style. If you have long hair, consider donating it to a charity that makes wigs for cancer patients.

Step 3: Get Your Pixie Cut

Once you've chosen your pixie cut style and prepared your hair, it's time to get your cut! Make sure you go to a professional stylist who has experience cutting short hair. Be open to their suggestions and ask for advice on how to style your new cut.

Step 4: Style Your Pixie Cut

There are many ways to style a pixie cut, from slicked-back to tousled and messy. Experiment with different products, such as pomade, wax, and hairspray, to find the right look for you. Use a flat iron or curling wand to add texture and dimension to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your pixie cut:

  • Invest in high-quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your pixie cut's shape and style.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands and barrettes, to add interest to your look.
  • Consider getting a bold hair color to complement your new cut.

FAQ

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

A: Not necessarily! A pixie cut can actually make your face appear slimmer and more defined, especially if you choose a style with volume on top and tapered sides.

Q: Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with a pixie cut?

A: Depending on the length of your hair, you may still be able to wear it in a small ponytail or bun. However, most pixie cuts are too short for a traditional ponytail.

Q: How often do I need to get my pixie cut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain your pixie cut's shape and style.

Hair Care

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it's still important to take care of your locks. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling wands, too frequently, as they can damage your hair. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine to your hair.
