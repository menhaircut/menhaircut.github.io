Description

Mens hairstyles high fade long on top is a popular hairstyle that has been trending for years. It is a versatile style that looks great on all hair types, lengths, and textures. This hairstyle features a high fade on the sides and back of the head, with longer hair on top that can be styled in various ways. The high fade ensures that the hair is shorter on the sides, while the long hair on top adds volume and texture to the overall look. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to look trendy, stylish, and confident.

How to

If you are interested in getting a mens high fade long on top hairstyle, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who can create the look you want. Once you have found a hairstylist, you should discuss your desired look and ask for their professional opinion on what will work best for your hair type, face shape, and personal style.

Step by step

The following are the steps involved in getting a mens high fade long on top hairstyle:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into the top and bottom sections.
  3. Clip the bottom section of your hair to keep it out of the way.
  4. Start cutting the sides and back of your hair using a clipper.
  5. Blend the cut hair into the longer hair on top using scissors.
  6. Style the hair on top using a hair product of your choice.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a mens high fade long on top hairstyle:

  • Make sure to maintain your hairstyle by getting regular trims to keep it looking fresh.
  • Use a good quality hair product to style your hair on top.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing a style for the top of your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about mens high fade long on top hairstyles:

Who can wear this hairstyle?
Anyone can wear this hairstyle regardless of their hair type, length, or texture.
How long does it take to style?
The time it takes to style this hairstyle can vary depending on your hair type and the products you use.
Is it easy to maintain?
Yes, this hairstyle is easy to maintain with regular trims and the right hair products.
Can it be worn for formal occasions?
Yes, this hairstyle can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Hair Care

To maintain a mens high fade long on top hairstyle, it is important to take good care of your hair. This includes washing it regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner, using a hair mask or treatment once a week, and protecting it from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools. It is also important to avoid using too much product as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Overall, a mens high fade long on top hairstyle is a great choice for anyone who wants to look stylish and on-trend. With the right hairstylist, products, and care, you can easily maintain this look and enjoy the benefits of a versatile and trendy hairstyle.
