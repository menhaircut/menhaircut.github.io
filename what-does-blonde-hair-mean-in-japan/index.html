Description

Blonde hair is quite uncommon in Japan, with only a small percentage of the population having naturally blonde hair. As such, blonde hair is often associated with foreigners or gaijin, which is a Japanese term for non-Japanese people. However, in recent years, more and more Japanese people have started to dye their hair blonde as a fashion statement.

How to Get Blonde Hair in Japan

If you're looking to get blonde hair in Japan, there are a few options available to you. The easiest and most common method is to visit a hair salon and have your hair professionally dyed. However, this can be quite expensive, with prices ranging from 10,000 to 30,000 yen depending on the salon and the length of your hair.

Alternatively, you can purchase a hair dye kit from a drugstore and dye your hair at home. This is a cheaper option, with prices ranging from 500 to 3,000 yen for a kit. However, it can be quite tricky to get the desired shade of blonde, and you may end up damaging your hair if you're not careful.

Step-by-Step Process

If you decide to dye your hair blonde at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Choose a hair dye kit that's specifically designed for blonde hair.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and make sure you have all the necessary tools, such as gloves and a mixing bowl.
  3. Follow the instructions to mix the dye and apply it to your hair.
  4. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time, usually around 30 minutes.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water until the water runs clear.
  6. Apply the conditioner that comes with the kit to help nourish your hair.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips on Maintaining Blonde Hair in Japan

Blonde hair requires a lot of maintenance, especially in Japan where the climate can be quite harsh on your hair. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your blonde hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for blonde hair to help maintain the color.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip the color and cause damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair and make the color fade faster.

FAQs

Is blonde hair popular in Japan?

No, blonde hair is quite uncommon in Japan, with only a small percentage of the population having naturally blonde hair. However, more and more Japanese people have started to dye their hair blonde as a fashion statement.

Can Japanese people have naturally blonde hair?

Yes, although it's quite rare. Only around 1-2% of the Japanese population has naturally blonde hair.

Hair Care for Blonde Hair in Japan

If you have blonde hair in Japan, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that's specifically designed for blonde hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip the color and cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair and make the color fade faster.
