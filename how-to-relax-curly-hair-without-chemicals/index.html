How To Relax Curly Hair Without Chemicals A Complete Guide

Written by William Jun 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of using chemical relaxers on your curly hair? Do you want a more natural way to relax your curls? Look no further! In this article, we will show you how to relax curly hair without chemicals. From the how-to steps to the hair care tips, we’ve got you covered. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Relax Curly Hair Without Chemicals
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Relaxing Curly Hair Naturally
  • FAQs

How to Relax Curly Hair Without Chemicals

There are several ways to relax curly hair without chemicals. Some people use heat, while others prefer natural ingredients. Here are some of the most popular methods:

  • Blow-drying
  • Stretching using braids or twists
  • Using natural ingredients such as coconut milk, olive oil, and avocado

Blow-drying

Blow-drying is a popular method for relaxing curly hair without chemicals. To do this, you will need a blow dryer, a round brush, and a heat protectant spray. Simply blow-dry your hair straight using the round brush to pull your curls taut. Use the heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.

Stretching Using Braids or Twists

Another method for relaxing curly hair without chemicals is stretching your hair using braids or twists. This method involves braiding or twisting your hair while it is wet and allowing it to air dry. Once your hair is dry, take out the braids or twists to reveal stretched, relaxed curls.

Using Natural Ingredients

Using natural ingredients is another popular method for relaxing curly hair without chemicals. Here are some examples:

  • Coconut milk: Apply coconut milk to your hair and leave it in for an hour. Rinse it out with water and your curls will be more relaxed.
  • Olive oil: Apply olive oil to your hair and leave it in for 30 minutes. Rinse it out with water and your curls will be softer and more manageable.
  • Avocado: Mash up an avocado and apply it to your hair. Leave it in for 30 minutes and rinse it out with water. Your curls will be more defined and less frizzy.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide for relaxing curly hair without chemicals using the blow-drying and stretching methods:

Blow-Drying

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  3. Unclip one section at a time and use a round brush to blow-dry your hair straight.
  4. Repeat with the other sections until your hair is completely dry and straight.

Stretching Using Braids or Twists

  1. Wash your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and braid or twist each section.
  3. Allow your hair to air dry completely.
  4. Take out the braids or twists and fluff your hair to reveal stretched, relaxed curls.

Tips for Relaxing Curly Hair Naturally

Here are some tips to help you relax your curly hair naturally:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to help prevent breakage.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can cause damage to your hair.

FAQs

Can you relax curly hair without chemicals?

Yes, you can relax curly hair without chemicals. Some popular methods include blow-drying, stretching using braids or twists, and using natural ingredients such as coconut milk, olive oil, and avocado.

Is it safe to relax curly hair without chemicals?

Yes, it is safe to relax curly hair without chemicals as long as you use the right methods and take care of your hair properly.

Hair Care Tips for Relaxed Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining relaxed curly hair:

  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.

There you have it – a complete guide to relaxing curly hair without chemicals. We hope you found this article helpful and that you can achieve the relaxed curls you desire!
