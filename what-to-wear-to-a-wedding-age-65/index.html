What To Wear To A Wedding Age 65 Tips And Ideas

Written by Petter Aug 07, 2023 · 3 min read

As we age, attending weddings can be a fun and exciting event, but it can also be a challenge to figure out what to wear. The good news is that there are plenty of options available that are both stylish and age-appropriate. In this article, we’ll share some tips and ideas on what to wear to a wedding age 65, including dress code guidelines, outfit ideas, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Dress Code Guidelines
  • Outfit Ideas
  • Hair Care Tips
  • FAQ

Dress Code Guidelines

Before you start shopping for a wedding outfit, it’s important to know the dress code. Most wedding invitations will include a dress code, which usually falls into one of the following categories:

  • Black Tie: This is the most formal dress code and requires a full-length gown or a formal cocktail dress.
  • Semi-Formal: This dress code is less formal than black tie, but still requires dressy attire. A knee-length dress or dressy separates are appropriate.
  • Cocktail Attire: This dress code is less formal than semi-formal but still dressy. A knee-length dress or dressy separates are appropriate.
  • Casual: This dress code allows for more relaxed attire, such as a sundress or dressy separates.

It’s important to follow the dress code guidelines to ensure that you don’t stand out for the wrong reasons. If you’re unsure about the dress code, don’t hesitate to ask the bride or groom for clarification.

Outfit Ideas

Now that you know the dress code, it’s time to start thinking about outfit ideas. Here are some age-appropriate and stylish options:

  • A long floral dress with a jacket or shawl for a black-tie wedding.
  • A knee-length dress with a matching jacket for a semi-formal wedding.
  • A knee-length sheath dress with a cardigan or wrap for a cocktail attire wedding.
  • A dressy pantsuit with a blouse for a casual wedding.

When it comes to colors, it’s best to avoid white, which is reserved for the bride. Instead, opt for bold prints or bright colors that complement your skin tone.

Hair Care Tips

One of the most important aspects of your wedding day look is your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Get a trim a week or two before the wedding to get rid of split ends and give your hair a healthy shine.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your outfit and the dress code.
  • Consider using hair extensions if you want to add length or volume to your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Finish your style with a hairspray or hair oil to keep it in place and add shine.

FAQ

Can I wear pants to a wedding?

Yes, pants are an appropriate option for a casual or semi-formal wedding. Just make sure to pair them with a dressy blouse and elegant accessories.

What should I avoid wearing to a wedding?

Avoid wearing white, which is reserved for the bride. Also, avoid anything too revealing or overly casual, such as jeans or shorts.

Can I wear black to a wedding?

Yes, black is an appropriate color for a wedding, especially for a formal or semi-formal wedding. Just make sure to add some color and accessories to avoid looking too somber.

With these tips and ideas, you’ll be sure to look stylish and age-appropriate at any wedding you attend. Remember to follow the dress code guidelines, choose an outfit that complements your style and personality, and take care of your hair for a polished and put-together look.
