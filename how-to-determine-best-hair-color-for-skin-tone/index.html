How To Determine The Best Hair Color For Your Skin Tone

Written by Frank Jul 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your old hair color? Do you want to change your look and try something new? Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you are not sure which color suits your skin tone. In this article, we will guide you on how to determine the best hair color for your skin tone.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  • Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones
  • Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones
  • Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in determining the best hair color for your skin tone is to know your skin tone. There are three main categories of skin tones: cool, warm, and neutral.

The easiest way to determine your skin tone is by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If your veins appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you cannot tell whether your veins are blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Another way to determine your skin tone is to look at the undertones in your skin. If you have a cool skin tone, your skin will have pink, red, or blue undertones. If you have a warm skin tone, your skin will have yellow, peach, or golden undertones. If you have a neutral skin tone, your skin will have a mix of cool and warm undertones.

Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, choose hair colors that have cool undertones, such as ash brown, platinum blonde, and black. Avoid warm tones, such as gold and copper, as they can wash you out.

Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, choose hair colors that have warm undertones, such as honey blonde, caramel brown, and auburn. Avoid cool tones, such as ash blonde and black, as they can make you look sallow.

Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you can wear both warm and cool hair colors. Some hair colors that work well for neutral skin tones are light brown, beige blonde, and chestnut brown.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Color

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your natural hair color. If you want a subtle change, choose a shade that is close to your natural hair color.
  • Think about your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, choose a low-maintenance hair color.
  • Consult a hair colorist. A professional can help you choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and suits your style.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair a different color if I have a warm skin tone?

A: Yes, you can. However, it is important to choose a hair color that has warm undertones to complement your skin tone.

Q: Can I dye my hair a different color if I have a cool skin tone?

A: Yes, you can. However, it is important to choose a hair color that has cool undertones to complement your skin tone.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Once you have chosen the right hair color, it is important to take care of your colored hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent your hair color from fading.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent your hair color from fading.

Choosing the right hair color can make a big difference in your overall look. By following these tips, you can determine the best hair color for your skin tone and keep your colored hair healthy and vibrant.
