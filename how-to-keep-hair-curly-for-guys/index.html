How To Keep Hair Curly For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair is a blessing for guys who want to add some extra style to their look. It’s a versatile and attractive hairstyle that can be worn in many different ways. However, keeping hair curly can be a challenge for some men. If you’re struggling to maintain your curls, don’t worry – we’ve got you covered. In this post, we’ll share some tips and tricks on how to keep hair curly for guys.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Keep Hair Curly for Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Keep Hair Curly for Guys

The key to keeping hair curly for guys is moisture. Curly hair is naturally dry and prone to frizz, so it’s important to keep it hydrated to maintain its shape. Here are some tips to help you keep your curls looking their best:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and glycerin. These ingredients will help to hydrate your hair and keep it soft and manageable.

2. Don’t over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leaving it dry and frizzy. Try to limit your hair washing to 2-3 times a week, and use a dry shampoo in between washes to absorb excess oil and refresh your curls.

3. Apply a leave-in conditioner

A leave-in conditioner is a great way to add extra moisture to your curls. Apply it to damp hair after washing, and use a wide-tooth comb to distribute it evenly. This will help to detangle your curls and prevent breakage.

4. Use a diffuser when blow-drying

A diffuser attachment on your blow dryer can help to distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz. Use it on a low heat setting to dry your hair, and scrunch your curls with your hands as you go. This will help to enhance your natural curl pattern and add volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to keep hair curly for guys:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to cleanse your hair and add hydration.

Step 2: Apply a leave-in conditioner

Add extra moisture to your curls by applying a leave-in conditioner to damp hair.

Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer

Dry your hair with a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curl pattern and reduce frizz.

Step 4: Style your curls

Once your hair is dry, style your curls with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb. Add a styling product like a curl cream or gel to define and hold your curls in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep hair curly for guys:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends, which can make your curls look frizzy and uneven.
  • Experiment with different hair products to find what works best for your hair type and curl pattern.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It’s best to limit your hair washing to 2-3 times a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: What’s the best way to style curly hair?

A: The best way to style curly hair is to embrace your natural curl pattern and use products that enhance and define your curls.

Q: Can I use a regular towel to dry my curly hair?

A: No, regular towels can cause frizz and breakage in curly hair. Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently blot your hair dry instead.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it looking its best. Here are some additional tips for hair care for curly hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they’re wet.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add extra moisture and hydration.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your curls.

With these tips and tricks, you can keep your hair curly and stylish all year round.
