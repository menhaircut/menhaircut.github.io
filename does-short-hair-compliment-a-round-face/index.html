Does Short Hair Compliment A Round Face

Written by Daniel Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering if short hair is a good choice for you. Short hair can be a great option for those with round faces, but it's important to choose the right style to flatter your features. In this article, we'll explore the question "does short hair compliment a round face?" and provide tips on choosing the right short hairstyle for your face shape.

Description

A round face is characterized by soft, curved lines and a width that is roughly equal to the length. People with round faces often have full cheeks and a rounded chin. While this face shape is often associated with youthfulness and femininity, it can also be challenging to find hairstyles that flatter the face.

How to

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, there are a few key things to keep in mind:

Consider the Length

Short hair can be a great option for those with round faces, but it's important to choose the right length. A pixie cut, for example, may not be the best choice as it can make the face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for a short bob or lob (long bob) that falls just below the chin.

Focus on Layers

Layered hair can help to add dimension and movement to a round face. Ask your hairstylist to add layers throughout the length of your hair, but avoid adding too much volume at the crown as this can make the face appear rounder.

Avoid Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs can be a tricky choice for those with round faces. While they can look great on some people, they can also make the face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or textured fringe that falls at an angle.

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for a round face:

  1. Consider the length - opt for a bob or lob that falls just below the chin.
  2. Focus on layers - ask your hairstylist to add layers throughout the length of your hair.
  3. Avoid blunt bangs - opt for side-swept bangs or textured fringe that falls at an angle.
  4. Experiment with curls - adding some loose waves or curls can help to add dimension to a round face.
  5. Consider the color - adding highlights or lowlights can help to create the illusion of dimension and depth.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for choosing a short hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the crown.
  • Consider a side part to create the illusion of length.
  • Choose a hairstyle that frames the face.
  • Experiment with different textures, such as beachy waves or sleek and straight styles.

FAQ

What short hairstyles are best for round faces?

Short bobs and lobs that fall just below the chin are a great option for those with round faces. Layered hair can help to add dimension and movement, while side-swept bangs or textured fringe can flatter the face.

Should I avoid short hair if I have a round face?

No, short hair can be a great option for those with round faces. It's simply a matter of choosing the right style to flatter your features.

How can I create the illusion of length with a short hairstyle?

Try a side part or a hairstyle that frames the face to create the illusion of length. Avoid adding volume at the crown, as this can make the face appear even rounder.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for short hair, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape of your hairstyle.
  • Use a lightweight, volumizing mousse to add texture and hold to your hair.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Invest in a high-quality flat iron or curling wand to create different textures and styles.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling.

In conclusion, short hair can be a great option for those with round faces. By choosing the right style and following these tips for hair care, you can rock a short hairstyle that flatters your features and boosts your confidence.
