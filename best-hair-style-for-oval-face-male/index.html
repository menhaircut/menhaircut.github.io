Best Hair Style for Oval Face Male - Tips and Ideas

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! This face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and highlights your best assets. In this article, we'll discuss the best hair style for oval face male and provide you with some tips and ideas to help you achieve your desired look.

  • Best Hairstyles for Oval Face Male

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best hair style for oval face male, it's important to determine your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, diamond, heart, and oblong. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Take a measuring tape and measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Measure the length of your face from the hairline to the chin.
  4. Compare your measurements to the following:
    • Oval: Forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the length of the face is about one and a half times the width.
    • Round: Cheekbones and face length have a similar measurement. They are larger than the forehead and jawline, which also have a similar measurement.
    • Square: All measurements are fairly similar. The jawline has a sharp angle.
    • Diamond: Face length is largest. Then, in descending order: cheekbones, forehead, and smallest is the jawline. The chin is pointed.
    • Heart: Forehead is wider than the cheekbones and jawline. The chin is pointed.
    • Oblong: Face length is largest. Forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are similar in size.

Now that you know your face shape, let's move on to the best hair style for oval face male.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Face Male

As mentioned earlier, oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile and can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, here are some of the best hairstyles for oval face male:

1. Short Buzz Cut

The short buzz cut is a great option for men with oval faces as it highlights the face shape and draws attention to the facial features. It's also a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling.

2. Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It works well for oval face male as it adds volume to the crown and creates a balanced look. You can add some texture to the hair for a more modern look.

3. Pompadour

The pompadour is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that suits oval face male. It features short sides and a voluminous top that is styled upwards and slicked back. This hairstyle works best with thick hair.

4. Quiff

The quiff is a versatile hairstyle that works well for oval face male. It features short sides and a longer top that is styled upwards and back. You can add some texture to the hair for a more casual look or keep it sleek for a more formal look.

5. Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern and stylish hairstyle that suits oval face male. It features short sides and a textured top that is styled upwards and forward. This hairstyle works best with thick and wavy hair.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Choosing the right hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially if you have an oval face. Here are some tips to help you choose the best hair style for oval face male:

1. Consider Your Hair Type

The hairstyle you choose should complement your hair type. If you have thick and wavy hair, opt for a textured crop or quiff. If you have thin and straight hair, go for a side part or pompadour.

2. Consider Your Lifestyle

The hairstyle you choose should also fit your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, opt for a low maintenance hairstyle like a short buzz cut. If you have more time to style your hair, go for a more elaborate hairstyle like a quiff or pompadour.

3. Consult with Your Barber or Stylist

Your barber or stylist can provide you with valuable advice on the best hair style for oval face male. They can also recommend products that work well with your hair type and help you maintain your hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can oval face male pull off any hairstyle?

Yes, oval face male can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, it's important to choose a hairstyle that complements your features and highlights your best assets.

2. What is the best hair style for oval face male with thin hair?

The best hair style for oval face male with thin hair is a side part or pompadour. These hairstyles add volume to the hair and create a balanced look.

3. What is the best hair style for oval face male with thick hair?

The best hair style for oval face male with thick hair is a textured crop or quiff. These hairstyles work well with thick hair and add texture to the hair.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've chosen the best hair style for oval face male, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your hairstyle:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in the way your hair looks and feels. Choose products that work well with your hair type and hairstyle. For example, if you have a textured crop, use a texturizing spray to enhance the texture of your hair.

2. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly is important to keep it clean and healthy. However, don't wash your hair too frequently as it can strip the natural oils from your scalp and hair.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts is important to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair in shape.

Choosing the best hair style for oval face male can be overwhelming, but with the right tips and ideas, you can achieve your desired look. Consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle when choosing a hairstyle and don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain your desired look.
