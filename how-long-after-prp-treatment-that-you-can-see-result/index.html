How Long After Prp Treatment Can You See Results

Written by Daniel Nov 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

PRP or Platelet Rich Plasma therapy is a non-surgical hair restoration treatment that involves drawing a small amount of blood from the patient, processing it to concentrate the platelets, and then injecting it into the scalp. PRP therapy has been gaining popularity due to its effectiveness in treating hair loss and promoting hair growth. However, one question that many people ask is how long after PRP treatment can you see results?

How To Prepare for PRP Treatment

Before getting PRP treatment, there are a few things you should do to prepare for the procedure:

  • Do not take any anti-inflammatory medications such as ibuprofen or aspirin for at least seven days before the treatment.
  • Avoid alcohol consumption for at least 24 hours before the treatment.
  • Make sure to eat a light meal before the treatment to avoid feeling lightheaded.

Step-by-Step PRP Treatment Process

The PRP treatment process involves the following steps:

  1. The patient's blood is drawn from their arm.
  2. The blood is then placed in a centrifuge to separate the platelets from the plasma.
  3. The platelet-rich plasma is then injected into the scalp using a microneedle or injection.
  4. The entire procedure takes around 30 minutes to an hour.

Tips For Getting The Best Results From PRP Treatment

Here are some tips to get the best results from PRP treatment:

  • Follow the post-treatment instructions provided by your doctor.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after the treatment.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain harsh chemicals.
  • Stay hydrated and eat a healthy diet.
  • Be patient as it may take a few months to see results.

Frequently Asked Questions About PRP Treatment

Here are some common questions people ask about PRP treatment:

1. Is PRP treatment painful?

PRP treatment may cause some discomfort during the injection process, but it is generally not painful. Your doctor may use a local anesthetic to numb the scalp before the injection.

2. How long does PRP treatment last?

The effects of PRP treatment can last up to 12 months, but this may vary depending on the individual.

3. Are there any side effects of PRP treatment?

PRP treatment is generally safe, but some people may experience mild swelling, redness, or bruising at the injection site.

Hair Care After PRP Treatment

After PRP treatment, it is important to take care of your hair to get the best results. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair for at least a week after the treatment.
  • Avoid exposing your scalp to direct sunlight for at least a week after the treatment.
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is free of harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid rubbing your scalp vigorously when washing your hair.
  • Do not scratch or pick at the injection site.

So, how long after PRP treatment can you see results? It may take a few months to see noticeable results from PRP treatment. However, some people may see improvement in hair density and thickness after just a few weeks. It is important to follow your doctor's post-treatment instructions and be patient as the results may vary from person to person.
