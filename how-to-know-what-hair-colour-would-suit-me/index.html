How To Know What Hair Colour Would Suit Me

Written by Petter Jul 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and want to try something new and exciting? Changing your hair colour can be a great way to switch up your look and boost your confidence. However, choosing the right colour can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide what will suit you best. In this article, we will explore some tips and tricks to help you determine what hair colour would suit you.

  • How to Determine the Right Hair Colour

How to Determine the Right Hair Colour

Before choosing a new hair colour, it's essential to determine what will suit you best. Here are some factors to consider:

Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a significant role in determining what hair colour would suit you. Cool skin tones look best with cool-toned hair colours like platinum blonde, ash brown, or black. On the other hand, warm skin tones complement warm-toned hair colours like golden blonde, auburn, or caramel brown.

Eye Colour

Your eye colour is another factor to consider when choosing a hair colour. People with blue or green eyes often suit hair colours with cool undertones, while those with brown or hazel eyes look great with warmer tones.

Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour can give you a clue as to what hair colour would suit you best. If you have warm-toned hair, then warm-toned colours like golden blonde or auburn may suit you. Conversely, if you have cool-toned hair, then cooler tones like ash blonde or black may look better on you.

Personality

Your personality also plays a role in determining what hair colour would suit you. If you have a bold and adventurous personality, then you may want to try a bright and vibrant hair colour like purple or pink. Alternatively, if you have a more reserved personality, then a natural-looking hair colour like brown or blonde may be more suitable.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Hair Colour

Now that you know what factors to consider, here is a step-by-step guide to help you determine your perfect hair colour:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, then you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, then you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

If you have blue or green eyes, then you likely have a cool skin tone. If you have brown or hazel eyes, then you likely have a warm skin tone.

Step 3: Determine Your Natural Hair Colour

If you have warm-toned hair, then warm-toned colours like golden blonde or auburn may suit you. If you have cool-toned hair, then cooler tones like ash blonde or black may look better on you.

Step 4: Choose a Hair Colour Based on Your Personality

If you have a bold and adventurous personality, then you may want to try a bright and vibrant hair colour like purple or pink. Alternatively, if you have a more reserved personality, then a natural-looking hair colour like brown or blonde may be more suitable.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

  • Consider your lifestyle. If you have a busy schedule, then a low-maintenance hair colour may be more suitable.
  • Consult with a professional. A hairstylist can provide expert advice on what hair colour would suit you best.
  • Try a temporary hair colour. If you're unsure about a permanent hair colour, then try a temporary colour to see how it looks on you.
  • Consider your wardrobe. Your hair colour should complement your clothing style.

Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Colour

Here are some common questions about hair colour:

What hair colour suits pale skin?

People with pale skin often suit cool-toned hair colours like platinum blonde, ash brown, or black.

What hair colour suits dark skin?

People with dark skin can pull off a wide range of hair colours, including bold and bright colours like red or purple.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home, but it's recommended to seek professional advice if you're unsure about the process.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Colour

Once you've found your perfect hair colour, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-protecting shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent colour fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause colour fading.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and maintenance.

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, but by considering your skin tone, eye colour, natural hair colour, and personality, you can determine what will suit you best. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy and consult with a professional if you're unsure about the process.
