Best Styling Product For Curly Hair 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 · 6 min read

Curly hair can be challenging to manage, but with the right styling product, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls. In this guide, we'll explore the best styling products for curly hair in 2020. From gels to creams, we've got you covered.

Description

Curly hair is unique and requires specialized care. The best styling products for curly hair 2020 are designed to enhance and define curls while providing hydration and protection from damage. These products can range from gels to creams, mousses, and oils.

How to Choose the Best Styling Product for Curly Hair

When choosing the best styling product for curly hair, it is essential to consider your hair type, texture, and curl pattern. Here are some factors to keep in mind:

  • Texture: Is your hair fine, medium, or coarse?
  • Curl pattern: Are your curls loose, wavy, or tightly coiled?
  • Porosity: How easily does your hair absorb moisture?
  • Ingredients: Look for products that are free of sulfates, parabens, and alcohol, as these can dry out and damage curly hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curly Hair

Styling curly hair can be a bit tricky, but with the right technique, you can achieve beautiful, defined curls. Here is a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair. Use a quarter-sized amount for short hair and a golf ball-sized amount for longer hair.
  3. Distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers or a wide-tooth comb.
  4. Scrunch your hair to enhance your curls.
  5. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair on low heat.
  6. Once your hair is dry, scrunch your hair again to break up any stiffness or crunchiness from the styling product.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Maintaining curly hair requires a bit of effort, but it is well worth it for beautiful, healthy-looking curls. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a satin pillowcase or bonnet to prevent frizz and preserve your curls while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your curls.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

Q: Can I use the same styling product for curly hair as I do for straight hair?

A: No, curly hair requires specialized care and styling products. Look for products that are designed specifically for curly hair.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and texture. Some people with curly hair can go a week or more between washes, while others need to wash their hair every few days. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Is it okay to brush my curly hair?

A: It is best to avoid brushing curly hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. If you need to detangle your hair, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers when your hair is wet and has conditioner in it.

Hair Care Routine for Curly Hair

A good hair care routine is essential for maintaining healthy, beautiful-looking curls. Here is a basic hair care routine for curly hair:

  1. Shampoo your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo once or twice a week.
  2. Deep condition your hair once a week.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing it.
  4. Use a styling product to define your curls and enhance their natural shape.
  5. Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can damage your curls.
