How To Get The Short Messy Hair Look Guys A Step By Step Guide

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, cool and effortless hairstyle, then the short messy hair look is perfect for you. It's a versatile style that suits most hair types and face shapes. In this article, we'll show you how to get the short messy hair look guys in just a few simple steps.

How to Get the Short Messy Hair Look Guys

The short messy hair look is all about creating a textured, tousled and undone appearance. It's a style that's achieved by using the right products, tools and techniques. Here's what you need to do:

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair
Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it. Then use a hair dryer to dry your hair completely. Make sure your hair is completely dry before moving to the next step.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product
Apply a styling product such as a clay or pomade to your hair. Use a small amount of product and rub it between your hands to warm it up. Then apply it evenly to your hair, focusing on the roots and the tips.

Step 3: Create Texture
Use your fingers or a comb to create texture in your hair. Scrunch your hair with your fingers to create a messy and tousled appearance. Use a comb to create a more defined texture if you prefer.

Step 4: Style Your Hair
Style your hair to your desired look. Use your fingers to create a messy and tousled appearance or use a comb to create a more defined look. Play around with your hair until you achieve the desired look.

Tips for Maintaining the Short Messy Hair Look

Here are some tips to help you maintain the short messy hair look:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair to avoid damaging it.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and texture of the style.

FAQs

Q: Is the short messy hair look suitable for all hair types?
A: Yes, the short messy hair look can be achieved on all hair types, although it may require different products and techniques depending on your hair type.

Q: Can I achieve the short messy hair look with long hair?
A: No, the short messy hair look is designed for short hair lengths.

Q: How often do I need to wash my hair to maintain the short messy hair look?
A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but generally washing your hair every 2-3 days is sufficient.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to help you maintain healthy hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often as it can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind and other harsh environmental factors.

Now that you know how to get the short messy hair look guys, go ahead and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. Remember to take care of your hair by using good quality products and following a healthy hair care routine.
