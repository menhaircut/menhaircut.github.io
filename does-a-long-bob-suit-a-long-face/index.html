Does A Long Bob Suit A Long Face

Written by Petter Jul 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a long face, you may have wondered if a long bob hairstyle would suit you. The long bob, also known as the lob, is a trendy and versatile hairstyle that has been popular for several years now. But, will it look good on a long face? In this article, we will explore this question and provide you with tips and advice on how to rock a long bob with a long face.

How to Determine if You Have a Long Face

Before we dive into the topic of long bobs, let's first determine if you have a long face. A long face is characterized by a high forehead, narrow chin, and elongated shape. It is also known as an oblong face shape. If you're unsure if you have a long face, take a look at yourself in the mirror or consult with a hairstylist.

What is a Long Bob?

A long bob is a shoulder-length haircut that falls between a bob and a mid-length cut. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The long bob is a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling, making it a popular choice for busy women.

Does a Long Bob Suit a Long Face?

The answer to this question is yes! A long bob can actually complement a long face by adding width and volume to the sides of the face. If you have a long face, consider going for a long bob with layers or textured ends. This will create movement and dimension in your hair, making your face appear wider.

Additionally, a long bob with a side part can also help balance out a long face. Avoid center parts, as they can elongate your face even more.

How to Rock a Long Bob with a Long Face

Now that we know a long bob can suit a long face, let's talk about how to rock this hairstyle. Here are some tips:

  • Opt for a long bob with layers or textured ends
  • Consider a side part instead of a center part
  • Add volume to the sides of your hair with a volumizing product
  • Avoid sleek and straight styles, as they can elongate your face

Tips for Styling Your Long Bob

Styling a long bob is easy and effortless. Here are some tips:

  • Use a volumizing product to add volume to your hair
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves
  • Try a messy bun or ponytail for a casual look
  • Add a hair accessory, such as a headband or scarf, for a pop of color

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a long bob if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still wear a long bob if you have thin hair. Opt for a long bob with layers or textured ends to add volume and dimension to your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my long bob?

A: It's recommended to trim your long bob every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Your Long Bob

To keep your long bob healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

In conclusion, a long bob can definitely suit a long face! With the right cut and styling, you can rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
