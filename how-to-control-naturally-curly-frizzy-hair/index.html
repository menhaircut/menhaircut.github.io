How To Control Naturally Curly Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jun 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to control frizz. Frizz can make your hair look unkempt and unmanageable, but there are ways to tame it. In this article, we will discuss how to control naturally curly frizzy hair and provide you with tips and tricks to keep your hair looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Control Naturally Curly Frizzy Hair
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs

Description

Curly hair is prone to frizz because of its structure. The natural oils produced by your scalp have a harder time traveling down the curly hair strands. As a result, your hair can become dry and brittle, which leads to frizz. Frizz can also be caused by humidity, heat, and harsh hair products.

How to Control Naturally Curly Frizzy Hair

There are several ways to control naturally curly frizzy hair. The first step is to choose the right hair products. Look for products that are designed for curly hair and contain moisturizing ingredients like shea butter or coconut oil. Avoid products that contain alcohol or sulfates, as these can strip your hair of its natural oils and make frizz worse.

Another way to control frizz is to avoid heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. Heat can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz. Instead, embrace your natural curls and use styling products like mousse or gel to enhance them.

Lastly, make sure to protect your hair from humidity. Humidity can cause your hair to absorb moisture from the air, which leads to frizz. To protect your hair, use a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum that contains silicone. Silicone helps to create a barrier around your hair strands and prevents moisture from entering.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to control naturally curly frizzy hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Gently towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too hard.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or anti-frizz serum to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  5. Apply a styling product like mousse or gel to your hair, scrunching it as you go.
  6. Avoid heat styling tools and let your hair air dry.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to help you control naturally curly frizzy hair:

  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause breakage and make frizz worse.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangles.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and reduce frizz.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to deep condition your hair and prevent dryness.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about controlling naturally curly frizzy hair:

  1. How often should I wash my curly hair? Curly hair should be washed every 2-3 days to prevent dryness and breakage.
  2. What is the best way to detangle curly hair? Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet and coated with conditioner.
  3. What should I do if my hair gets frizzy during the day? Apply a small amount of anti-frizz serum or styling product to your hair and scrunch it to revive your curls.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your naturally curly hair healthy:

  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can control naturally curly frizzy hair and embrace your beautiful curls. Remember to choose the right hair products, avoid heat styling tools, and protect your hair from humidity. With a little TLC, your curls will be healthy, shiny, and frizz-free.
