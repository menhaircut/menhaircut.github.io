How To Curl Super Fine Straight Hair

Written by Christine Sep 26, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of having super fine straight hair that won't hold a curl? Do you want to add some volume and texture to your hair? Well, you've come to the right place! In this article, we will be discussing how to curl super fine straight hair. We will cover everything from how to prep your hair for curling, to different curling techniques, and hair care tips to maintain your curls. So, grab your curling iron and let's get started!

  2. Curling Techniques
  3. Products for Curling
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Prepping Your Hair for Curling

Before you begin curling your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help add some texture and volume to your hair, making it easier to curl. Once you've washed your hair, towel dry it gently and apply a heat protectant spray. This is crucial to protect your hair from heat damage caused by curling irons.

Next, blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Once your hair is dry, apply a small amount of mousse or styling cream to your roots to add more volume. Finally, use a comb to part your hair into sections. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Curling Techniques

There are several techniques you can use to curl super fine straight hair. Here are a few:

Classic Curling Iron

The classic curling iron is the most popular way to curl hair. To use this technique, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel of the curling iron and hold for a few seconds. Release the hair and repeat until your entire head is curled. For super fine hair, use a smaller curling iron barrel to create tighter curls.

Wand Curling Iron

The wand curling iron is similar to the classic curling iron, but it doesn't have a clamp. To use this technique, wrap small sections of hair around the wand and hold for a few seconds. Release the hair and repeat until your entire head is curled. This technique is great for creating loose, beachy waves.

Straightener Curling

Straightener curling is a great technique for creating loose, natural-looking curls. To use this technique, clamp a small section of hair with a straightener, then twist the straightener away from your face. Move the straightener down the hair shaft, twisting as you go. Release the hair and repeat until your entire head is curled.

Products for Curling

There are several products that can help you achieve the perfect curl. Here are a few:

Curling Spray

Curling spray is designed to help your curls hold their shape. It also adds volume and texture to your hair. Spray it on your hair before curling to help your curls last longer.

Hair Mousse

Hair mousse is a lightweight product that helps add volume and texture to your hair. Apply it to your roots before blow drying to get the most volume.

Styling Cream

Styling cream is a heavier product that helps tame frizz and add texture to your hair. Apply it to your hair before curling to help your curls hold their shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curls

Once you've curled your hair, you'll want to make sure your curls last as long as possible. Here are a few tips:

Use Hairspray

Hairspray is a great way to help your curls last longer. Spray it on your hair after curling to help hold your curls in place.

Avoid Touching Your Hair

The more you touch your hair, the more likely your curls are to fall out. Try to avoid touching your hair as much as possible to help your curls last longer.

Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are gentle on your hair and can help prevent your curls from getting tangled or flattened while you sleep.

FAQs

Can I curl super fine straight hair?

Yes, you can curl super fine straight hair! It just takes some practice and the right technique.

What size curling iron should I use?

For super fine hair, use a smaller curling iron barrel to create tighter curls. A 1-inch barrel is a good size to start with.

How long will my curls last?

How long your curls last depends on several factors, including your hair type and the products you use. On average, your curls should last 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.

Avoid Heat Damage

Heat damage can cause your hair to become dry and brittle. Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.

Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim for a trim every 8-12 weeks.
