How To Revive My Synthetic Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re a wig wearer, you already know that synthetic wigs are a popular choice because of their affordability and low maintenance. However, with frequent use, synthetic wigs can become dull, tangled, and frizzy, losing their natural shine and luster. In this article, we’ll show you how to revive your synthetic wig and make it look as good as new.

Table of Contents

  • Section 1: How to Revive Your Synthetic Wig
  • Section 2: Tips for Maintaining Your Synthetic Wig
  • Section 3: FAQ About Synthetic Wig Care
  • Section 4: Hair Care Tips for a Healthy Wig

Section 1: How to Revive Your Synthetic Wig

Here are the steps you can follow to revive your synthetic wig:

Step 1: Detangle Your Wig

Before you start washing your wig, make sure to detangle it first. Use a wide-tooth comb or a wig brush to gently remove any knots or tangles. Be careful not to pull too hard or use too much force as it can damage the fibers of the wig.

Step 2: Wash Your Wig

Fill a sink or a basin with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Place your wig in the water and gently swirl it around for a few minutes. Avoid rubbing or scrubbing the wig as it can damage the fibers. Rinse the wig thoroughly with cool water.

Step 3: Condition Your Wig

After washing your wig, apply a small amount of wig conditioner to the hair fibers. Gently work the conditioner through the hair with your fingers. Leave the conditioner on for a few minutes and rinse it off with cool water.

Step 4: Dry Your Wig

Use a towel to gently blot the excess water from your wig. Avoid rubbing or twisting the wig as it can damage the fibers. Place your wig on a wig stand or a wig head and let it air dry. Avoid using a blow dryer or any other heat source as it can damage the fibers.

Section 2: Tips for Maintaining Your Synthetic Wig

Here are some tips to help you maintain your synthetic wig and keep it looking good:

Tip 1: Store Your Wig Properly

When you’re not wearing your wig, store it on a wig stand or a wig head to maintain its shape and prevent it from tangling.

Tip 2: Avoid Heat

Avoid exposing your wig to heat sources such as direct sunlight, hot water, or blow dryers. Excessive heat can damage the fibers and cause them to frizz.

Tip 3: Use a Wig Brush

Use a wig brush or a wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig gently. Avoid using regular hair brushes as they can damage the fibers.

Section 3: FAQ About Synthetic Wig Care

Q: How often should I wash my synthetic wig?

A: It depends on how often you wear your wig and how much product you use on it. As a general rule, you should wash your synthetic wig every 6-8 wears.

Q: Can I use regular shampoo and conditioner on my synthetic wig?

A: No, you should only use wig shampoo and conditioner on your synthetic wig. Regular shampoo and conditioner can damage the fibers and cause them to frizz.

Q: Can I style my synthetic wig with heat tools?

A: No, you should not use heat tools such as curling irons, flat irons, or hot rollers on your synthetic wig. Excessive heat can damage the fibers and cause them to melt.

Section 4: Hair Care Tips for a Healthy Wig

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your wig healthy:

Tip 1: Use a Silk or Satin Pillowcase

Using a silk or satin pillowcase can help prevent tangles and friction that can damage your wig.

Tip 2: Avoid Chlorine

Avoid swimming in chlorinated water as it can damage the fibers of your wig.

Tip 3: Trim Your Wig

Regularly trimming your wig can help prevent split ends and keep it looking fresh.

Reviving your synthetic wig is easy if you follow these simple steps. By maintaining your wig properly, you can extend its lifespan and keep it looking good for longer. If you have any questions or concerns about wig care, consult a wig specialist or a professional stylist.
