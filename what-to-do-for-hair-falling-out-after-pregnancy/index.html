What To Do For Hair Falling Out After Pregnancy Tips And Solutions

Written by Frank Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Experiencing hair loss after pregnancy is not uncommon among women. It is due to the hormonal changes that occur during pregnancy and after childbirth. Hair falling out after pregnancy can be distressing, but there are things you can do to help stop or reduce the hair loss.

How to Deal with Postpartum Hair Loss

The following are some tips and solutions that can help you deal with hair loss after pregnancy:

1. Be Gentle with Your Hair

When taking care of your hair, it is essential to be gentle. Avoid pulling, tugging, or brushing your hair too harshly, as it can cause more hair to fall out.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on wet hair, as it can break the hair. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to detangle your hair.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools, like flat irons and curling irons, can cause damage to your hair, making it more prone to breakage and hair loss. Instead, consider air-drying your hair or using a low heat setting on your styling tools.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help promote hair growth. Ensure that you are getting enough vitamins and minerals, such as iron, zinc, and biotin, which are essential for healthy hair.

5. Take Supplements

You can also take supplements that are specifically designed to promote hair growth. Some of the supplements that can help include biotin, vitamin D, and iron.

6. Get Regular Exercise

Exercising regularly can help improve blood flow to the scalp, which can help promote hair growth.

7. Try Hair Masks

You can also try using hair masks to help strengthen and nourish your hair. Some of the best hair masks for hair falling out after pregnancy include coconut oil, avocado, and egg yolk masks.

FAQs

Can hair falling out after pregnancy be prevented?

Unfortunately, hair loss after pregnancy cannot be prevented entirely, but you can take steps to reduce the amount of hair loss you experience.

How long does hair loss after pregnancy last?

Hair loss after pregnancy can last anywhere from 6 to 12 months. However, some women may experience hair loss for up to two years after childbirth.

Is postpartum hair loss permanent?

No, postpartum hair loss is not permanent. Your hair should grow back within a few months after you give birth.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips that can help prevent hair loss and promote healthy hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Less Frequently

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it more prone to breakage and hair loss. Try washing your hair every other day or every three days.

2. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is free of harsh chemicals, like sulfates and parabens, which can cause damage to your hair.

3. Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help improve blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays from the sun can damage your hair, making it more prone to breakage and hair loss. Consider wearing a hat or using a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

5. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles, like braids and ponytails, as they can cause tension on the hair, leading to breakage and hair loss.

In conclusion, hair falling out after pregnancy is common, but it can be distressing. However, by following the tips and solutions outlined in this article, you can help reduce the hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.
