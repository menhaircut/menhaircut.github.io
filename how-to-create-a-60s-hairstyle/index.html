How To Create A 60S Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to add some retro flair to your hairstyle? Why not try a 60s-inspired look! This era was known for its bold and fun hairstyles, perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to create a 60s hairstyle.

Description

The 60s were a time of change and revolution, especially in fashion and beauty. Women were experimenting with bold and playful hairstyles, from the iconic beehive to the sleek and straight look. These hairstyles were often accessorized with headbands, ribbons, and scarves, making them even more fun and unique.

How to

To create a 60s hairstyle, you'll need a few tools, including a comb, hair ties or bobby pins, hairspray, and possibly a straightener or curling iron. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Straight or Curly

The first step is to decide whether you want to go for a straight or curly look. If you have naturally straight hair, you can use a straightener to smooth out any kinks or frizz. If you have curly hair, you can use a curling iron to create loose waves or ringlets.

Step 2: Add Volume

Once you have your desired texture, it's time to add some volume to your hair. This can be achieved by backcombing or teasing your hair. Start at the roots and work your way down, gently teasing small sections of hair.

Step 3: Create a Bouffant

The bouffant was a popular hairstyle in the 60s, characterized by its voluminous crown and smooth sides. To create this look, gather the top section of your hair and tease it until it's the desired height. Smooth down the sides and secure with bobby pins or a hair tie.

Step 4: Add Accessories

To complete your 60s look, add some fun accessories such as a headband, scarf, or ribbon. These can be tied around your head, worn as a bow, or used to secure your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for creating a 60s hairstyle:

  • Use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Experiment with different textures, such as waves or curls.
  • Accessorize with bold and fun accessories.
  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair for added volume.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about creating a 60s hairstyle:

How long does it take to create a 60s hairstyle?

It depends on the complexity of the hairstyle and your hair texture. Simple styles such as a straight and sleek look can be achieved in a matter of minutes, while more intricate styles such as a beehive may take longer.

Do I need any special tools to create a 60s hairstyle?

You'll need a few basic tools such as a comb, hair ties, and hairspray. Depending on your desired look, you may also need a straightener, curling iron, or teasing comb.

Can I create a 60s hairstyle on short hair?

Yes, you can! While some 60s styles were more suited for longer hair, there are plenty of options for shorter hair such as a sleek bob or a pixie cut with a headband.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair before and after styling. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can create a fun and playful 60s-inspired hairstyle that is sure to turn heads!
