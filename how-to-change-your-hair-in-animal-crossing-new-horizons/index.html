How To Change Your Hair In Animal Crossing New Horizons

Written by Oliver Jun 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Horizons? Do you want to switch up your look? Changing your hair in the game is a simple process that can be done in a few easy steps. In this article, we'll go over how to change your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons, along with tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How To Change Your Hair
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips for Changing Your Hair
  • FAQs

How To Change Your Hair

To change your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons, you need to visit Harriet at the Shampoodle salon. Harriet is a poodle who runs the salon and can help you change your hair, eye color, and makeup.

Requirements

Before you can change your hair, you need to meet the following requirements:

  • Have a Nintendo Switch Online subscription
  • Have upgraded Resident Services to a building
  • Have at least one hair styling coupon

Step By Step Guide

Follow these steps to change your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  1. Go to Resident Services and speak to Isabelle
  2. Select "Redeem Nook Miles"
  3. Choose "Nook Shopping"
  4. Select "Special Goods"
  5. Choose "Hair Salon Reservation"
  6. Purchase a Hair Styling Coupon for 3,000 Bells
  7. Go to Harriet's salon (located on the right side of Resident Services)
  8. Speak to Harriet and select "Makeover"
  9. Choose the hairstyle you want
  10. Confirm your selection and pay with a Hair Styling Coupon

Tips for Changing Your Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when changing your hair in Animal Crossing New Horizons:

  • Make sure to have a clear idea of the hairstyle you want before going to the salon
  • Consider trying on different wigs at Able Sisters to see what style suits you best
  • Save up Hair Styling Coupons so you can switch up your hairstyle whenever you want

FAQs

How many Hair Styling Coupons do I need?

You only need one Hair Styling Coupon per visit to Harriet's salon.

Can I change my hair color?

No, you can only change your hairstyle, eye color, and makeup.

How do I get more Hair Styling Coupons?

You can purchase more Hair Styling Coupons from Nook Shopping for 3,000 Bells each.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've changed your hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Horizons, you'll want to take care of it to keep it looking fresh. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit Harriet regularly to switch up your hairstyle and keep it looking trendy
  • Use a hairbrush to keep your hair looking neat and tidy
  • Consider using hair accessories like hats, headbands, or hair clips to add some flair to your look

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to change up your hairstyle in Animal Crossing New Horizons in no time. Have fun experimenting with different looks and don't be afraid to try something new!
