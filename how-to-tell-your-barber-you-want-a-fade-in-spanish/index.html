How To Tell Your Barber You Want A Fade In Spanish

Written by Daniel Sep 25, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you a non-native Spanish speaker who wants to get a fade at a Spanish-speaking barber shop? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to tell your barber you want a fade in Spanish.

Description

A fade is a popular hairstyle that involves gradually blending the hair from short to long. It's a versatile haircut that looks great on all hair types and can be customized to suit your personal style. If you're a non-native Spanish speaker, it can be challenging to communicate your desired haircut to your barber. However, with a few key phrases, you can confidently ask for a fade in Spanish.

How to Tell Your Barber You Want a Fade in Spanish

Here are the steps to follow when telling your barber you want a fade in Spanish:

Step 1: Greet Your Barber

Begin by greeting your barber in Spanish. You can say "Hola" (hello) or "Buenos dias" (good morning) depending on the time of day.

Step 2: Request a Fade

To ask for a fade, you can say "Quiero un degradado" (I want a fade) or "Quiero un corte de pelo degradado" (I want a faded haircut).

Step 3: Specify the Length

Next, you'll need to specify the length of the fade. You can say "Quiero un degradado bajo" (I want a low fade) or "Quiero un degradado alto" (I want a high fade).

Step 4: Specify the Style

You can also specify the style of the fade. For example, you can say "Quiero un degradado con la línea dura" (I want a hard part fade) or "Quiero un degradado con la línea suave" (I want a soft part fade).

Step 5: Confirm Your Request

Finally, confirm your request by saying "Por favor" (please) and "Gracias" (thank you). For example, you can say "Quiero un degradado bajo con la línea dura, por favor. Gracias."

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when communicating with your Spanish-speaking barber:

  • Be polite and respectful
  • Speak slowly and clearly
  • Use basic vocabulary and avoid complex sentence structures
  • Bring a picture of the desired hairstyle to help your barber understand your request

FAQ

What is a fade?

A fade is a popular hairstyle that involves gradually blending the hair from short to long.

How do I ask for a fade in Spanish?

You can say "Quiero un degradado" (I want a fade) or "Quiero un corte de pelo degradado" (I want a faded haircut).

What should I bring to the barber shop?

Bring a picture of the desired hairstyle to help your barber understand your request.

Hair Care

To maintain your fade, you'll need to visit your barber regularly for touch-ups. You can also use styling products like hair wax or pomade to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know how to tell your barber you want a fade in Spanish, you can confidently visit a Spanish-speaking barber shop and get the haircut you want. Remember to be polite, speak clearly, and bring a picture to help your barber understand your request. Good luck!
