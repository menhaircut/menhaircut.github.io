How To Do A Perm At Home For Guys With Rubber Bands

Written by Petter Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and looking for a way to add some texture and volume to your locks? A perm might be the solution for you! But, going to a salon can be expensive and time-consuming. Don't worry; you can do a perm at home using rubber bands. In this blog post, we will guide you through the process of how to do a perm at home for guys with rubber bands.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of the hair to create curls or waves. The traditional method of perming involves using rods or rollers to set the hair in place while the chemical solution is applied. However, with rubber bands, you can create a similar effect without the need for rods or rollers.

How to

Before you start, you should gather all the necessary equipment. You will need rubber bands, perm solution, neutralizer, gloves, a comb, and a towel. You can buy a perm kit from a beauty supply store or online. It's important to follow the instructions that come with the kit carefully.

Step by Step

1. Shampoo your hair and towel dry it. Do not use any conditioner or styling products.

2. Section your hair into small sections using the comb and secure them with the rubber bands. The tighter you secure the hair, the tighter the curls will be.

3. Apply the perm solution to each section, making sure to saturate the hair completely. Be careful not to get any solution on your skin or clothes.

4. Cover your hair with a plastic cap and let it sit for the recommended time. This can vary depending on the brand, so follow the instructions carefully.

5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove any excess solution. Do not remove the rubber bands yet.

6. Apply the neutralizer to each section of hair, saturating it completely. Let it sit for the recommended time.

7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water to remove any excess neutralizer.

8. Remove the rubber bands carefully, and gently towel dry your hair.

9. Style your hair as desired. You can use a diffuser to enhance the curls or let it air dry for a more natural look.

Tips

- Make sure to read the instructions carefully and follow them closely.

- Wear gloves to protect your hands from the chemicals.

- Don't leave the solution on for too long, as it can damage your hair.

- If you have any doubts, seek professional help.

FAQ

Q: How long does a perm last?

A: A perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it.

Q: Can I use rubber bands for a traditional perm?

A: No, rubber bands are not suitable for a traditional perm as they don't provide the same level of control and support as rods or rollers.

Q: Can I perm dyed hair?

A: Yes, but you should be careful not to damage the hair further. It's best to seek professional help if you're unsure.

Hair Care

After getting a perm, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

- Use a mild shampoo and conditioner designed for permed hair.

- Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair further.

- Don't brush or comb your hair when it's wet, as it's more prone to breakage.

- Avoid harsh chemicals and treatments, such as bleaching or coloring, as they can weaken the hair.

Following these tips will help you maintain healthy, beautiful permed hair!

Now that you know how to do a perm at home for guys with rubber bands, you can try it out for yourself. Just remember to follow the instructions carefully and use caution when working with chemicals. Good luck!
