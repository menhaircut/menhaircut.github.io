How Long Is Number 1 Haircut In Mm

Written by Oliver Sep 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to getting a haircut, there are many different terms and measurements that can be confusing. One of the most common questions that people ask is, "How long is a number 1 haircut in mm?" In this article, we will break down everything you need to know about this popular haircut length.

Description

A number 1 haircut is a very short buzz cut that is approximately 3mm in length. This haircut is often referred to as a "crew cut" or "butch cut" and is popular among men who prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle. The number 1 haircut is also a popular choice for military personnel and athletes.

How to

If you are interested in getting a number 1 haircut, you can either visit a barber or attempt to do it yourself. To achieve this haircut at home, you will need a set of clippers with a number 1 guard attachment. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start with clean, dry hair. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is free of any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Attach the number 1 guard to your clippers

The number 1 guard is typically the shortest guard available and will give you a 3mm length. Attach the guard to your clippers before beginning your haircut.

Step 3: Start at the sides and back

Begin by trimming the sides and back of your head. Use the clippers to move in an upward motion, starting at the nape of your neck and moving toward the crown of your head. Repeat this process until the desired length is achieved.

Step 4: Trim the top

Once you have finished the sides and back, move on to the top of your head. Trim the hair in a forward motion, starting at the hairline and moving toward the crown of your head. Be sure to blend the top with the sides and back for a seamless look.

Tips

When attempting a number 1 haircut at home, it is important to take your time and go slowly. Use a mirror to check your progress and make sure that you are achieving an even cut. If you are unsure about cutting your own hair, it is always best to visit a professional barber.

FAQ

What other hair lengths are available?

There are many different hair lengths available, ranging from a number 0 (approximately 1.5mm) to a number 8 (approximately 25mm). The length you choose will depend on your personal preference and the style you are trying to achieve.

Is a number 1 haircut suitable for all hair types?

A number 1 haircut can work well for most hair types, but may not be suitable for those with very curly or thick hair. If you have curly or thick hair, you may need to use a longer guard attachment to achieve the desired length.

Hair Care

After getting a number 1 haircut, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized, and avoid using harsh styling products that can damage your hair. Regular trims every 4-6 weeks can also help to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

Conclusion

A number 1 haircut is a popular choice for those who prefer a low-maintenance, short hairstyle. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can achieve a number 1 haircut at home or visit a professional barber for a clean and precise cut. Remember to take care of your hair after your cut to keep it looking and feeling healthy.
