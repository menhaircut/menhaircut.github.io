What Hairstyles Are Popular For 2020

Written by Aprilia Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for some new hairstyle inspiration? Look no further than the most popular hairstyles of 2020! From long locks to short pixie cuts, there's a style to suit everyone. In this article, we'll explore the top trending hairstyles of 2020, how to achieve them, and some tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

The hairstyles that are popular for 2020 are all about embracing natural texture and movement. Long layers, soft waves, and messy buns are just a few of the looks that have been seen on the runways and red carpets this year. Short haircuts are also in, with pixie cuts and blunt bobs being especially popular.

How to

If you're looking to achieve one of the popular hairstyles of 2020, the first step is to find a stylist who can help you achieve your desired look. Bring in some photos of the style you're interested in, and talk to your stylist about what would work best for your hair type and face shape.

Once you've got your new cut or style, it's important to know how to maintain it. Depending on the look you're going for, you may need to invest in some styling products, such as mousse, hairspray, or smoothing serum. Your stylist can recommend the best products for your hair type and style.

Step by step

If you're looking to achieve a specific hairstyle, such as a messy bun or beachy waves, there are plenty of tutorials available online to help you. Here's a step-by-step guide for achieving a popular 2020 hairstyle: the messy bun.

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail, leaving out a few face-framing pieces if you like.
  2. Wrap the ponytail around the base to create a bun, securing it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out a few pieces of hair around your face to create a relaxed, messy look.
  4. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Invest in a good quality shampoo and conditioner that's suited to your hair type.
  • Use heat protectant spray when using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling wands.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type and style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about popular hairstyles for 2020:

Q: What's the most popular hairstyle for 2020?

A: There are a few popular styles this year, including long layers, messy buns, and blunt bobs. It really depends on your personal preference and hair type.

Q: Is it better to have short or long hair in 2020?

A: Both short and long hair are in style this year, so it really depends on what you feel most comfortable with and what suits your face shape.

Q: How do I know what hairstyle will suit me?

A: It's best to consult with a stylist, who can recommend a style that will suit your face shape and hair type.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. This means washing and conditioning regularly, using heat protectant spray when using hot tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends. If you're unsure about how to care for your hair, consult with a stylist or do some research online.

With these tips and popular hairstyles of 2020, you'll be sure to find a look that suits you and makes you feel confident and stylish. Happy styling!
