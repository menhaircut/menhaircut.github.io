How To Cut Men s Hair Like A Barber

Written by Robby Oct 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the barbershop? Do you want to learn how to cut men's hair like a barber? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve a professional haircut from the comfort of your own home. We will cover everything from the tools you need to the techniques you should use. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools Needed
  • Preparation
  • Cutting Techniques
  • Finishing Touches
  • Tips for Success
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Cut

Tools Needed

Before you start cutting, you must gather the necessary tools. Here is a list of what you will need:

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Cape or towel
  • Haircutting spray bottle
  • Haircutting shears
  • Electric razor (optional)

Make sure all of your tools are clean and in good working condition. Dull scissors or clippers will make the process more difficult and result in a less polished look.

Preparation

Once you have gathered your tools, it's time to prepare the hair for cutting. Here are the steps you should follow:

  1. Wash the hair and towel dry it until it's slightly damp.
  2. Comb the hair in the direction it naturally falls.
  3. Choose the desired length for the cut.
  4. Begin by trimming the sides and back with clippers using an appropriate guard size.
  5. Blend the sides and back into the top using scissors.
  6. Cut the top to the desired length using scissors or clippers.

It's important to work slowly and methodically to ensure an even cut. If necessary, use a spray bottle to wet the hair and make it easier to cut.

Cutting Techniques

Now that you have prepared the hair, it's time to start cutting. Here are some cutting techniques that will help you achieve a professional-looking haircut:

  • Use the comb as a guide to create a straight line when cutting with scissors.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time to ensure an even cut.
  • Start at the front and work your way to the back of the head.
  • Take breaks to step back and assess the cut.
  • Use the clippers to create a fade or blend the sides and back into the top.
  • Use the electric razor to clean up the hairline and sideburns.

Remember to use caution when working around the ears and temples. Use your fingers as a guide when cutting around these areas to avoid accidentally cutting too much hair.

Finishing Touches

Once you have finished cutting the hair, it's time to add the finishing touches. Here are some steps you can take to achieve a polished look:

  1. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or uneven areas.
  2. Use the clippers or electric razor to create a clean neckline.
  3. Apply hair product, such as gel or pomade, to style the hair as desired.
  4. Clean up any excess hair around the ears and neck with a towel or brush.

Remember to take your time and work slowly to achieve the best results.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help you succeed in cutting men's hair like a barber:

  • Practice makes perfect. Don't expect to achieve a perfect cut on your first try.
  • Invest in high-quality tools. They will make the process easier and result in a more polished look.
  • Watch online tutorials to learn new techniques and tips.
  • Communicate with the person you are cutting hair for to ensure you achieve the desired look.
  • Take your time and work slowly to avoid mistakes.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting men's hair like a barber:

Do I need professional training to cut men's hair?

No, you do not need professional training to cut men's hair. However, it's important to practice and learn proper techniques to achieve the best results.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair shorter or blend the mistake into the rest of the hair. Remember to take your time and work slowly to avoid mistakes in the first place.

What if I don't have all of the necessary tools?

If you don't have all of the necessary tools, you can still achieve a basic haircut. However, investing in high-quality tools will make the process easier and result in a better cut.

Hair Care After the Cut

Once you have finished cutting the hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for hair care after the cut:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Apply a conditioner to keep the hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers or flat irons, too often.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the desired length.

By following these tips, you can ensure that the hair stays healthy and looks good between haircuts.

Conclusion

With the right tools and techniques, anyone can learn how to cut men's hair like a barber. By following this step-by-step guide, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Remember to take your time, work slowly, and practice to achieve the best results.
