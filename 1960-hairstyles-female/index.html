How to Achieve the 1960 Hairstyles Female Look

The 1960s were all about big hair, bold colors, and bold cuts. The most popular hairstyles of the time were the bouffant, beehive, and the pixie cut. These styles were often paired with bold makeup and accessories, such as headbands or scarves.

To achieve the perfect 1960s hairstyle, you'll need to follow a few key steps. First, you'll need to choose the right cut for your face shape and hair type. Then, you'll need to style your hair using the right products and techniques. Finally, you'll need to accessorize your hair with the right accessories to complete the look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to achieve the perfect 1960s hairstyle:

  1. Choose the right cut for your face shape and hair type. The bouffant and beehive styles work best on thick, medium to long hair. The pixie cut is great for those with shorter hair. If you're not sure what style will work best for you, consult with a hairstylist.
  2. Wash and condition your hair with products that are formulated for your hair type. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  3. Dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots. This will help add volume and hold to your hair.
  4. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair, focusing on the roots. Lift your hair up and away from your scalp to create volume.
  5. If you're going for a bouffant or beehive style, tease your hair at the crown using a fine-tooth comb. This will add height and volume to your hair.
  6. If you're going for a pixie cut, use a styling wax or pomade to create texture and definition in your hair. Use your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  7. Finish off your style with a strong-hold hairspray to keep everything in place.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect 1960s Hairstyle

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 1960s hairstyle:

  • Use a rat tail comb to tease your hair for added volume.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, scarves, or hair clips.
  • Experiment with different hair colors, such as blonde, red, or even pink.
  • Pair your hairstyle with bold makeup, such as dramatic eyeliner or bright lipstick.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 1960s hairstyles female:

What is the most popular 1960s hairstyle?

The most popular 1960s hairstyles for women were the bouffant, beehive, and pixie cut.

How do you create a beehive hairstyle?

To create a beehive hairstyle, you'll need to tease your hair at the crown and smooth it over with a brush. Then, pin your hair up and back, leaving the ends out.

What hair products should I use for a 1960s hairstyle?

To achieve a 1960s hairstyle, you'll need to use volumizing shampoo and conditioner, a volumizing mousse or spray, and a strong-hold hairspray.

Hair Care Advice to Keep Your Locks Looking Their Best

Here are some hair care tips to help keep your locks looking their best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect 1960s hairstyles female look. Whether you go for a bouffant, beehive, or pixie cut, you'll be sure to turn heads with your bold and iconic style.
