How To Trim The Back Of A Man s Hair With Clippers

Written by Oliver Jul 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending time and money at the barbershop? Do you want to learn how to trim the back of your hair with clippers? Look no further! In this article, we will give you step-by-step instructions on how to trim the back of a man's hair with clippers.

How to

The first step in trimming the back of your hair with clippers is to make sure you have the right tools. You will need a set of clippers with different length guards, a handheld mirror, and a regular mirror.

Once you have your tools, it's time to start trimming. Follow these steps:

Step 1:

Start by putting the longest guard on your clippers. Turn them on and begin trimming the hair at the nape of your neck. Use the handheld mirror to help you see the back of your head.

Step 2:

As you move up your head, switch to a shorter guard. Use the same technique of trimming the hair at the nape of your neck and working your way up.

Step 3:

Once you have trimmed your hair with the shortest guard, take it off and use the clippers without a guard to trim the hairline around your neck.

Step 4:

Use the handheld mirror and regular mirror to check the back of your head for any missed spots.

Tips

Trimming the back of your hair with clippers can be tricky, but these tips will make it easier:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard
  • Use the handheld mirror to help you see the back of your head
  • Go slow and be careful not to nick your skin
  • Check the back of your head with both mirrors to make sure you didn't miss any spots
  • Practice makes perfect! Don't get discouraged if it doesn't turn out perfectly the first time

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about trimming the back of a man's hair with clippers:

Can I trim my hair by myself?

Yes, you can! It may take some practice, but with the right tools and technique, you can trim the back of your hair with clippers by yourself.

What if I mess up?

If you accidentally trim too much hair or nick your skin, don't worry. Hair grows back and small cuts will heal quickly.

How often should I trim the back of my hair?

This depends on how quickly your hair grows and how short you want it to be. On average, you should trim the back of your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Trimming the back of your hair with clippers is just one part of hair care. Here are some other tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and manageable
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy

With these tips and techniques, you can trim the back of your hair with clippers like a pro. Say goodbye to expensive trips to the barbershop and hello to a fresh, clean haircut anytime you want.
