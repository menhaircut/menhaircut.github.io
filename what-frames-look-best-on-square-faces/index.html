What Frames Look Best On Square Faces

Written by Robby Nov 01, 2023 · 3 min read

In this year 2023, eyewear has become a statement piece in fashion. With a variety of shapes and styles, selecting the perfect frame can be challenging. When it comes to square faces, finding the right frame can enhance your features and make you feel confident. In this article, we’ll discuss how to choose the perfect frames for square faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Right Frames
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Selecting Frames
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Square Faces

Description

Square faces have a strong jawline, wide forehead, and prominent cheekbones. The goal of selecting the perfect frame is to soften the jawline and balance the face. Round, oval, and cat-eye frames are excellent options for square faces. These shapes add curves and soften the angular features of the face. Avoid angular or square frames, as they can make the face look even more boxy.

How to Choose the Right Frames

The key to choosing the right frames is to select a shape that contrasts your face shape. For square faces, round, oval, and cat-eye frames are the best options. Look for frames with curves and soft edges. Avoid angular or square frames, as they can make the face look even more boxy.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine your face shape. Square faces have a strong jawline, wide forehead, and prominent cheekbones.

Step 2: Look for frames with curves and soft edges.

Step 3: Avoid angular or square frames.

Step 4: Try on different frames to find the perfect fit.

Step 5: Consider the color and material of the frames.

Tips for Selecting Frames

Tip 1: Look for frames with a wider width than the widest part of your face.

Tip 2: Choose frames with a bridge that sits high on the nose.

Tip 3: Consider the color and material of the frames. Darker colors and thicker frames can make the face appear smaller.

Tip 4: Avoid frames with sharp angles or corners.

Tip 5: Don’t be afraid to try on different styles and shapes until you find the perfect fit.

FAQs

Q: Can square faces wear aviator frames?

A: Yes, square faces can wear aviator frames. However, make sure to choose frames with softer edges and curves.

Q: Can square faces wear rectangular frames?

A: It is best to avoid rectangular frames, as they can make the face look even more boxy.

Hair Care for Square Faces

Hair can also play a role in enhancing the features of square faces. Soft layers or waves can add curves and balance the jawline. Side-swept bangs can also soften the forehead and create a more oval-shaped face. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face appear more angular.

Choosing the perfect frames for square faces can enhance your features and make you feel confident. Remember to look for frames with curves and soft edges and avoid angular or square frames. Don’t be afraid to try on different styles and shapes until you find the perfect fit. And for hair care, soft layers or waves and side-swept bangs can balance the face and soften the features.
