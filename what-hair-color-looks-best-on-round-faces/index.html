What Hair Color Looks Best On Round Faces

Written by Eveline Nov 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to choosing a hair color, it can be difficult to know what will look best on your face shape. Round faces, in particular, can be tricky to work with. But fear not, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be sharing our top tips on what hair color looks best on round faces.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and fullness in the cheeks. When it comes to hair color, the goal is to create the illusion of length and balance. This can be achieved through strategic placement of highlights and lowlights or opting for a hair color that creates contrast against the skin tone.

How To

When choosing a hair color for a round face, it's important to take into account both the skin tone and eye color. Generally speaking, warm tones like golden blonde, honey blonde, and caramel work well with warm skin tones. Cool tones like ash blonde, platinum blonde, and cool brown work well with cool skin tones. If you're unsure of your skin tone, consult with a professional colorist.

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

Stand in natural light and look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you may have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Take into account your eye color when choosing a hair color. Brown eyes work well with warm tones, while blue and green eyes work well with cool tones.

Step 3: Choose Your Hair Color

Based on your skin tone and eye color, choose a hair color that will complement your features. Highlights and lowlights can also be added to create depth and dimension.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color for a round face:

  • Avoid all-over dark hair colors, as they can make the face appear rounder
  • Opt for asymmetrical cuts and styles to create the illusion of length
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make the face appear shorter
  • Consider face-framing highlights to draw attention to the cheekbones

FAQ

What hair color looks best on fair skin and round face?

For fair skin and a round face, cool tones like ash blonde and cool brown work well. Highlights can also be added to create dimension.

Can a round face pull off a pixie cut?

A pixie cut can be a great option for a round face, as long as it includes layers and texture to create the illusion of length.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your hair color, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils. Regular trims can also help to prevent split ends and keep the hair looking neat and tidy.

Now that you know what hair color looks best on round faces, it's time to book your appointment with a professional colorist and get ready to rock your new look!
