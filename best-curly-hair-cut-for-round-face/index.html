Best Curly Hair Cut For Round Face

Written by Bardi Oct 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse, especially if you have a round face. Round faces are characterized by a round chin and a wide forehead, which can make it difficult to find a hairstyle that flatters your features. However, with the right curly hair cut, you can enhance your natural beauty and show off your gorgeous curls. In this article, we'll discuss the best curly hair cut for round face, as well as some tips for hair care and styling.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

The best curly hair cut for round face is a layered cut that adds volume and movement to your curls. This cut should be shoulder-length or longer, with layers that start at the chin and work their way down. The layers will help to frame your face and create a more angular look, which will balance out the roundness of your face.

Another important factor to consider when choosing a curly hair cut for round face is the shape of your curls. If you have tight, spiral curls, a shorter cut may be more flattering, while looser waves can be enhanced with longer layers. Your stylist can help you determine the best length and layering technique for your unique hair texture and face shape.

How to

To achieve the best curly hair cut for round face, it's important to find a stylist who specializes in curly hair. Curly hair requires a different cutting technique than straight hair, and not all stylists are trained in this technique. Look for a stylist who has experience working with curly hair and can offer advice on the best cut for your face shape.

When you go to your appointment, be sure to bring pictures of curly haircuts that you like. This will help your stylist understand the look you're going for and give them a better idea of how to cut your hair. Don't be afraid to ask questions or offer feedback during the cutting process, as communication is key to achieving your desired result.

Step by step

The best curly hair cut for round face can be achieved in several steps:

  1. Find a stylist who specializes in curly hair
  2. Bring pictures of curly haircuts that you like
  3. Communicate with your stylist throughout the cutting process
  4. Consider adding layers to create volume and movement
  5. Choose a length that flatters your face shape and hair texture

Tips

To maintain your curly hair cut and keep your curls looking their best, follow these tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can create frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Avoid heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and reduce frizz

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best curly hair cut for round face:

Q: Will a curly hair cut make my face look rounder?

A: No, a curly hair cut can actually help to balance out the roundness of your face by creating more angular lines.

Q: How often should I get my curly hair cut?

A: It's recommended to get a curly hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your shape and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I color my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can color your curly hair, but it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and doesn't damage your curls.

Hair Care

In addition to following the tips listed above, it's important to take good care of your curly hair to keep it looking its best. This includes:

  • Avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools
  • Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz
  • Protecting your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Drinking plenty of water and eating a balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth

By following these tips and choosing the best curly hair cut for round face, you can enhance your natural beauty and show off your gorgeous curls.
