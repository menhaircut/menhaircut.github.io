How To Get Emo Hair Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to get an emo hairstyle, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go through the steps to achieve the perfect emo hair boy look. From choosing the right haircut to styling your hair, we've got you covered. So, without further ado, let's get started.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

The first step to achieving the emo hair boy look is to choose the right haircut. There are several options to choose from, but the most popular ones are:

  • The classic emo haircut
  • The shaggy emo haircut
  • The layered emo haircut

Each of these haircuts has its unique features, so choose one that suits your face shape and personal style. If you're unsure, ask your stylist for recommendations.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you have your haircut, it's time to style your hair. The key to achieving the emo hair boy look is to make your hair look messy and tousled. Here's how to do it:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your hair. Be sure to distribute it evenly throughout your hair.
  3. Using your fingers, tousle your hair to create a messy, textured look. You can also use a comb or brush to style your hair if you prefer a neater look.
  4. Finish by applying hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Remember, the key to achieving the emo hair boy look is to make your hair look messy and tousled, so don't worry too much about getting it perfect.

Tips for Maintaining Your Emo Hair Boy Look

Now that you've achieved the perfect emo hair boy look, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers or straighteners, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair every six to eight weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of styling product should I use?

You can use pomade, wax, or clay to style your hair. Experiment with different products to see which one works best for your hair type.

Do I need to have straight hair to achieve the emo hair boy look?

No, you don't need to have straight hair. The emo hair boy look can be achieved with curly or wavy hair as well.

Do I need to dye my hair to achieve the emo hair boy look?

No, you don't need to dye your hair. However, if you want to add some color, you can choose from a variety of shades, such as black, red, or blue.

Hair Care is Key

Getting an emo hair boy look is all about choosing the right haircut and styling your hair correctly. However, it's important to remember that hair care is key to maintaining your look. Follow the tips we've outlined above, and you'll be sure to keep your hair looking healthy and stylish.
