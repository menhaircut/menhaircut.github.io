Best Short Haircuts For Square Faces 2021

Written by Petter Jul 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for square faces in 2021. If you have a square face, you know that finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. A square face shape is characterized by a strong jawline, a broad forehead, and a wide face. The goal is to find a haircut that will soften these features and create a more balanced look.

Description

Square faces are common among women, and it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut to complement the shape. The key is to choose a hairstyle that softens the angular features of the face, like the jawline and forehead. Short haircuts are a great option as they can create an illusion of length and add volume to the hair.

How to

The first step in choosing the best short haircut for your square face is to identify your face shape. To do this, measure your face from your hairline to your chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If these measurements are equal, you have a square face.

Once you have identified your face shape, the next step is to choose a haircut that flatters your features. A short bob, for example, can soften the jawline and add volume to the hair. A pixie cut can also be a great option, as it draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

Step by step

1. Identify your face shape
2. Choose a short haircut
3. Consult with a hairstylist
4. Consider your hair texture
5. Experiment with different styles

Tips

When choosing a short haircut for your square face, keep these tips in mind:

- Choose a haircut that adds height to the crown of your head.
- Avoid blunt cuts that emphasize the squareness of your face.
- Soft layers can create a more feminine look.
- Consider adding bangs to soften your features.
- Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear a bob if I have a square face?
A: Yes, a short bob can be a great option for square faces. Just make sure to add soft layers to create a more balanced look.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a square face?
A: No, short haircuts can actually be a great option for square faces. Just make sure to choose a style that softens your features.

Hair Care

Regardless of your face shape, it is essential to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

- Wash your hair regularly
- Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny
- Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently
- Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools
- Get regular trims to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

By following these tips and choosing the best short haircut for your square face, you can create a look that is both stylish and flattering. So go ahead and experiment with different styles until you find the one that works best for you.
