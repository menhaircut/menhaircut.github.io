Is Curly Hair Better Short The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Jun 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. It’s beautiful, but it can also be challenging to manage. One of the biggest questions people with curly hair have is whether it’s better to keep their hair short or let it grow long. In this guide, we’ll explore the benefits of having short curly hair and provide tips for maintaining it.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short curly hair can be a great choice for many reasons. For one, it’s easier to manage. It takes less time to wash, dry, and style, which is a plus for those with busy schedules. Short hair also tends to be healthier because it’s less prone to breakage and split ends. Plus, it can be a great way to show off your curls.

However, there are some downsides to having short curly hair. It can be more difficult to create different styles and looks. It’s also less versatile than longer hair because you can’t put it up in a ponytail or bun. Finally, some people feel that short hair doesn’t suit their face shape or style.

How to

If you’re considering getting a short haircut for your curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it’s important to find a stylist who is experienced with curly hair. They will be able to give you advice on which styles will work best for your hair type and face shape.

When it comes to styling your hair, there are a few different options. You can use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls and create volume. You can also use a curling iron or wand to create loose, beachy waves. Finally, you can use a straightening iron to create a sleek, polished look.

Step by Step

If you’re new to styling short curly hair, here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. If you don’t have a diffuser, you can scrunch your hair with your hands as it dries.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves. You can also use a straightening iron to create a sleek look.
  5. Finish with hairspray or another styling product to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Will short curly hair make my face look rounder?

A: It depends on the haircut and your face shape. A layered, textured cut can help create the illusion of a slimmer face, while a blunt cut may make your face appear rounder.

Q: Can I still put my short curly hair up in a ponytail?

A: It may be more difficult to put short hair up in a ponytail, but it’s not impossible. You can use bobby pins to secure your hair in place, or try a half-up, half-down style.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your short curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

Overall, short curly hair can be a great choice for those who want an easy-to-manage, stylish look. With the right cut, styling tools, and products, you can show off your curls and look your best.
