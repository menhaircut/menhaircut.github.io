Short Hair Styles For Round Face Over 60

Table of Contents: - Introduction - How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for a Round Face Over 60 - Top Short Hair Styles for Round Face Over 60 - The Pixie Cut - The Bob Cut - The Shag Cut - Tips for Maintaining Short Hair Styles for Round Face Over 60 - Frequently Asked Questions - Conclusion

As we age, our hair changes in texture and thickness, and our face shape may become rounder. However, this doesn't mean we can't rock a short hairstyle! In this article, we'll discuss the best short hair styles for women over 60 with round faces, how to choose the right style, and tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for a Round Face Over 60

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face over 60, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right style:

  • Look for styles that add height and volume to the crown of your head, as this helps elongate your face
  • Avoid styles that are too blunt or straight, as this can make your face appear wider
  • Consider getting layers or bangs to frame your face and create movement
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair

Top Short Hair Styles for Round Face Over 60

Now that you know what to look for, let's discuss some of the best short hair styles for round faces over 60:

The Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for women with round faces. This style is short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top that can be styled with texture or volume. The pixie cut is low maintenance and can be styled with a little product, making it perfect for busy women.

The Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short hairstyle that works well for round faces over 60. This style is typically chin-length and can be layered or straight. The bob cut is versatile and can be styled with waves, curls, or straightened for a polished look.

The Shag Cut

The shag cut is a layered hairstyle that works well for women with round faces over 60. This style has layers throughout the hair, creating texture and movement. The shag cut can be worn straight or with waves for a more relaxed look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair Styles for Round Face Over 60

Maintaining a short hairstyle requires a little more upkeep than longer styles. Here are some tips for keeping your short hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims to keep your style fresh and prevent split ends
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Invest in styling products that work for your hair type and style
  • Talk to your stylist about how to maintain your specific style at home

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I'm not sure which style is right for me?

If you're not sure which short hairstyle is right for you, talk to your stylist. They can help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair texture and lifestyle.

Can I still color my hair with a short hairstyle?

Yes, you can still color your hair with a short hairstyle. Talk to your stylist about which hair color will work best with your skin tone and hair type.

Will a short hairstyle make me look older?

No, a short hairstyle won't necessarily make you look older. In fact, a well-styled short hairstyle can make you look more youthful and vibrant.

Conclusion

Short hair styles for round faces over 60 can be flattering and stylish. With the right cut and maintenance, you can rock a short hairstyle at any age. Remember to choose a style that flatters your face shape and works with your hair texture and lifestyle. Talk to your stylist about which style is right for you and how to maintain it at home.
