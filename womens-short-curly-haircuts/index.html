Women s Short Curly Haircuts A Guide To Styling And Maintenance

Written by Bardi Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short curly haircuts are a popular choice among women who want a low-maintenance yet stylish hairstyle. Whether you have natural curls or you want to add some texture to your hair, there are many ways to style and maintain short curly haircuts. In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on women's short curly haircuts, including how to style and maintain them, tips on how to choose the right haircut for your face shape, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Short Curly Haircuts
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Curly Hair
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Short Curly Haircut for Your Face Shape
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Style Short Curly Haircuts

Short curly haircuts can be styled in many ways, from a messy, tousled look to a sleek and polished finish. Here are some tips on how to style your short curly hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to define your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush or comb on your hair, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz.
  • Apply a curl-defining cream or mousse to your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair, instead of a brush.
  • For a sleek and polished look, straighten your hair with a flat iron or use a blow dryer with a round brush.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Curly Hair

If you want to cut your short curly hair at home, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and condition your hair, then let it air dry until it's about 80% dry.
  2. Comb your hair straight, then divide it into sections using hair clips.
  3. Start at the back of your head and cut small sections of hair, holding each section between your fingers and cutting at an angle.
  4. Move to the sides of your head, then the top, cutting small sections of hair at a time.
  5. Use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.
  6. Use hair scissors to trim any stray hairs or split ends.

Tips for Choosing the Right Short Curly Haircut for Your Face Shape

When choosing a short curly haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a round face, opt for a short curly haircut that adds height to your hair, such as a pixie cut with volume on top.
  • If you have a square face, choose a short curly haircut with soft, rounded edges, such as a curly bob.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, go for a short curly haircut that adds volume to the sides of your face, such as a layered pixie cut.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any short curly haircut, from a curly bob to a pixie cut.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I cut my short curly hair?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your short curly haircut and prevent split ends.

Q: Can I color my short curly hair?

A: Yes, you can color your short curly hair, but be sure to use a hair dye that is specifically formulated for curly hair to prevent damage and frizz.

Q: How do I prevent frizz in my short curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's wet, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer, and apply a frizz-reducing serum to your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Curly Haircuts

To maintain the health and appearance of your short curly haircut, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage to your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage and frizz.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair and cause frizz.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily style and maintain your short curly haircut, and enjoy a chic and effortless hairstyle.
