Which Is The Best Hairstyle For Square Face

Description:

If you have a square face, you're in luck! Square faces are known for their strong jawlines and angular features, which means you can pull off a lot of different hairstyles. However, not all hairstyles are created equal, so it's important to find the one that flatters your face shape the most. In this article, we'll talk about the best hairstyles for square faces, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair.

How to:

The best way to find the perfect hairstyle for your square face is to work with your natural hair texture and length. You want to choose a style that balances out your face shape by softening your features and creating some angles. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Avoid straight, blunt cuts that emphasize your square jawline.
  • Look for styles that add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Consider face-framing layers that soften your features.
  • Try a side part or a center part with a little bit of volume on top.
  • Experiment with different lengths, but avoid cuts that hit right at your jawline.

Step by Step:

Here are some of the best hairstyles for square faces:

Long Layers

Long layers are great for square faces because they add movement and softness to your hair. Ask your stylist for face-framing layers that start at your chin or collarbone. You can wear your hair straight, wavy, or curly with this cut.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to soften your features and draw attention to your eyes. Ask your stylist for bangs that start at your temples and angle down towards your cheekbones. You can wear them straight or tousled.

Messy Bob

A messy bob is a great way to add some texture and movement to your hair. Ask your stylist for a chin-length bob with choppy layers. You can wear it straight, wavy, or tousled.

Tips:

Here are some tips for styling your hair if you have a square face:

  • Avoid middle parts, as they can make your face look even more square.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add some lift to your roots.
  • Avoid flat-ironing your hair too often, as it can make your hair look too straight and emphasize your jawline.
  • Experiment with different partings to see what works best for you.

FAQ:

Q: Can I wear short hair if I have a square face?

A: Yes! Short hair can be a great option for square faces, as long as you choose the right cut. Look for styles that add volume and texture, and avoid cuts that hit right at your jawline.

Q: Should I avoid bangs if I have a square face?

A: Not necessarily. Bangs can be a great way to soften your features and draw attention to your eyes. Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape, like side-swept bangs.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut if I have a square face?

A: It depends on the length and style of your hair. If you have a shorter cut, you may need to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape. If you have longer hair, you can go 8-12 weeks between haircuts.

Hair Care:

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair if you have a square face:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Now that you know which is the best hairstyle for square face, it's time to go out and try something new! Remember to work with your natural texture and length, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. With a little bit of effort, you can find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your square face and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
