How To Make Long Curly Hair Look Short

Written by Oliver Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have long curly hair, you may be looking for ways to switch up your look. While cutting your hair may seem like the obvious solution, there are ways to make your long curly hair look short without sacrificing length. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for achieving a shorter hairstyle with your curly locks.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Make Long Curly Hair Look Short
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

How to Make Long Curly Hair Look Short

There are a few key ways to make your long curly hair look shorter:

  • Use a diffuser to create volume at the roots and shrink the length of your curls
  • Create a faux bob by tucking your hair under and securing with bobby pins
  • Braid your hair and then tuck the ends under to create a shorter-looking style
  • Use a hair tie to create a faux ponytail that sits higher up on your head, giving the illusion of shorter hair

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for creating a faux bob:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Create a deep side part.
  3. Take small sections of hair and curl them with a curling iron or wand.
  4. Once all of your hair is curled, brush through it to create loose waves.
  5. Gather the bottom half of your hair and tuck it under, securing it with bobby pins.
  6. Take small sections of the top half of your hair and tuck them under, also securing with bobby pins.
  7. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Long Curly Hair

When it comes to styling long curly hair, there are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls and prevent frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair without disrupting your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as it can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangling and frizz.

FAQs

Can I make my long curly hair look short without cutting it?

Yes, there are several ways to make long curly hair look shorter without cutting it. You can use a diffuser to shrink the length of your curls, create a faux bob, braid your hair and tuck the ends under, or use a hair tie to create a faux ponytail that sits higher up on your head.

What products should I use to style my long curly hair?

For long curly hair, it's important to use products that will define your curls and prevent frizz. Look for leave-in conditioners, curl creams, and gels that are specifically designed for curly hair.

How often should I wash my long curly hair?

How often you should wash your long curly hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others prefer to wash their hair more frequently. Experiment to find a washing schedule that works best for your hair.

Hair Care for Long Curly Hair

In addition to styling your long curly hair, it's important to take care of it to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for caring for your long curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your long curly hair look shorter without sacrificing length. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the look that works best for you.
