How To Make Short Hair Look Good Guys A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Sep 05, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have short hair, you might think that there’s not much you can do with it. But that’s not true! With a little bit of effort and some styling know-how, you can make short hair look good guys. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about styling short hair, from how to cut it to how to style it.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Short Hair
  2. How to Style Short Hair
  3. Tips for Styling Short Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Cut Short Hair

Before you can style your short hair, you need to make sure it’s cut properly. Here are a few tips for cutting short hair:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use sharp scissors
  • Take your time
  • Use a mirror to check your work

It’s also a good idea to go to a professional stylist if you’re not comfortable cutting your own hair. They can give you a great cut that will be easy to style.

How to Style Short Hair

Now that you have a great cut, it’s time to style your short hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a good quality styling product
  • Apply the product evenly throughout your hair
  • Use your fingers to style your hair
  • Don’t use too much product
  • Experiment with different styles

You might also want to invest in a good quality hairdryer and styling tools, such as a straightener or curling iron. These tools can help you achieve a variety of different styles.

Short Spiky Hair

One popular short hair style for guys is the spiky look. Here’s how to achieve it:

  1. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair
  2. Use your fingers to spike your hair up
  3. Use a hairdryer on low heat to set the style

Short Slicked Back Hair

If you prefer a more polished look, you might want to try slicking your short hair back. Here’s how:

  1. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair
  2. Use a comb to slick your hair back
  3. Use a hairdryer on low heat to set the style

Tips for Styling Short Hair

Here are a few more tips for styling short hair:

  • Don’t be afraid to try new styles
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your style
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it

FAQ

Q: Can I style short hair if it’s thinning?

A: Yes, you can still style short hair if it’s thinning. Just be gentle when styling and avoid using too much product or heat on your hair.

Q: How often should I wash short hair?

A: It’s best to wash short hair every other day, or as needed. Using a good quality shampoo and conditioner can help keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

In addition to styling your short hair, it’s important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your style

With these tips and tricks, you can make short hair look good guys. Whether you prefer a spiky or slicked back style, there’s a look for everyone. Just remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
