How To Wear Hair Up With Round Face Tips Tricks And Style Ideas

Written by Aprilia Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you have a round face and find it challenging to wear your hair up? Don't worry; you're not alone. Many women with round faces struggle to find the right hairstyle that flatters their facial features. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can wear your hair up and look great.

Description

A round face has equal width and length, with soft curves and no angles. The cheeks are typically full, and the chin is round. The goal is to create an illusion of length and angles using your hairstyle. Wearing your hair up is an excellent way to achieve this, but it's essential to choose the right updo that complements your face shape.

How to Wear Hair Up with Round Face

Here are some tips on how to wear hair up with a round face:

1. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help elongate your face. Consider using a volumizing shampoo and blow-drying your hair upside down to add height. You can also use a teasing comb to backcomb your hair gently and create the illusion of a longer face.

2. Avoid Tight Updos

Tight buns or ponytails can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for loose, messy updos that add height and volume. You can also pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face and soften the look.

3. Try Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a longer face. Consider getting long, side-swept bangs that sweep across your forehead and blend into your hair. You can also add layers to your hair to create texture and movement.

4. Experiment with Different Updos

Don't be afraid to experiment with different updos to find the one that flatters your face shape the most. Consider trying a messy bun, a braided updo, or a half-up, half-down hairstyle. You can also add accessories like headbands or hairpins to add some sparkle and interest.

Step by Step: How to Create a Messy Bun

Creating a messy bun is an easy and effortless way to wear your hair up with a round face. Here's how to do it:

Step 1:

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with an elastic band.

Step 2:

Twist your hair around the elastic band to create a bun. You can make it as messy or neat as you like. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Step 3:

Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to soften the look. You can also use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head to add height and volume.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Use a Good Shampoo and Conditioner

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and addresses any specific concerns you may have, such as dryness or oiliness. Avoid using harsh chemicals or sulfates that can damage your hair.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

Using heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before styling, and avoid using high heat settings.

3. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and nutrients can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Include foods like eggs, nuts, and leafy greens in your diet.

FAQs

Q: Can I wear my hair up if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair up if you have a round face. The key is to choose an updo that flatters your face shape and adds height and volume.

Q: What kind of updos should I avoid if I have a round face?

A: Avoid tight updos like sleek buns or ponytails. Instead, opt for loose, messy updos that add volume and texture.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often than if you have dry hair. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

Conclusion

Wearing your hair up with a round face can be a challenge, but it's not impossible. With the right tips and tricks, you can create a flattering updo that elongates your face and adds volume and texture to your hair. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that suits your face shape and personal style the best. Lastly, don't forget to take care of your hair by using the right products and maintaining a healthy diet and lifestyle.
