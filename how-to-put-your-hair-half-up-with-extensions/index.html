How To Put Your Hair Half Up With Extensions A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some volume and length to your hair, extensions are a great option. And if you want to wear your hair half up, extensions can still be a great choice. Here's how to put your hair half up with extensions in just a few easy steps.

Description

Putting your hair half up with extensions can be a bit tricky at first, but with a little practice, you'll be able to do it quickly and easily. This style is perfect for anyone who wants to add some volume and length to their hair, while still keeping it out of their face.

How To

Here are the steps to put your hair half up with extensions:

Step 1: Brush and Prep Your Hair

Brush your hair thoroughly to detangle it and remove any knots or tangles. Then, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to protect it from heat damage.

Step 2: Clip in Your Extensions

Clip in your extensions, starting at the nape of your neck and working your way up. Make sure to blend your extensions with your natural hair for a seamless look.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section off the top half of your hair, using a comb or your fingers. Make sure to leave enough hair down to cover the clips of your extensions.

Step 4: Tie Your Hair Half Up

Use a hair tie or elastic to tie the top half of your hair into a half ponytail. Make sure to leave some hair down to cover the clips of your extensions.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

Tease the top section of your hair to add volume and texture. Use a comb or teasing brush to gently backcomb your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down.

Step 6: Smooth Your Hair

Smooth the top section of your hair with a brush, blending the teased hair with the un-teased hair. Make sure to smooth out any tangles or knots.

Step 7: Secure Your Half Up Style

Secure your half up style with bobby pins or hairpins. Use as many as you need to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you put your hair half up with extensions:

  • Choose extensions that match your hair color and texture for a seamless look.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Tease your hair gently to avoid causing damage or breakage.
  • Secure your half up style with bobby pins or hairpins to keep it in place all day.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about putting your hair half up with extensions:

What type of extensions are best for a half up style?

Clip-in extensions are the best type of extensions for a half up style, as they are easy to clip in and blend with your natural hair.

How do I choose the right color of extensions?

Choose extensions that match your hair color as closely as possible. If you're not sure, you can always ask a stylist for help.

Can I wear my hair half up with extensions every day?

Yes, you can wear your hair half up with extensions every day, as long as you take good care of your hair and extensions.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair and extensions:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and extensions clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair and extensions.
  • Brush your hair and extensions regularly to prevent tangles and knots.
  • Store your extensions properly when you're not wearing them, to prevent them from getting tangled or damaged.

Now that you know how to put your hair half up with extensions, you can try this style for yourself. It's a great way to add some volume and length to your hair, while still keeping it out of your face. Just remember to take good care of your hair and extensions, and you'll have beautiful, healthy hair that looks great every day.
