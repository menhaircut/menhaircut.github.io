How To Start Locs With Natural Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Starting locs with natural hair can be a great way to embrace your natural hair texture and style. However, it can be intimidating for those who have never done it before. In this guide, we will take you through the steps to start locs with natural hair, including tips and hair care advice to ensure your locs look and feel great.

Table of Contents

  • How to Start Locs with Natural Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Starting Locs

How to Start Locs with Natural Hair

Locs are formed by allowing natural hair to tangle and knot together into cylindrical sections. To start locs with natural hair, you will need to stop using any chemical treatments and allow your hair to grow out. Once your hair has grown out enough, you can start the process of forming your locs.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to start locs with natural hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue.
  2. Section your hair into small, even parts using a comb or your fingers.
  3. Twist or braid each section of hair tightly from the root to the end.
  4. Apply a natural oil or locking gel to each twisted section of hair to help it lock and hold its shape.
  5. Repeat this process for each section of hair, taking care to keep the sections even and uniform in size.
  6. Allow your hair to air dry or sit under a hooded dryer until it is completely dry.
  7. Once your hair is dry, you can gently unravel each twist or braid to reveal your new locs.

Tips for Starting Locs

Starting locs with natural hair can take time and patience. Here are some tips to help you along the way:

  • Be patient. Locs take time to form and mature, so don't expect instant results.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp healthy.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase or bonnet to protect your locs while you sleep.
  • Avoid using heavy products or oils that can weigh down your locs and cause buildup.
  • Be gentle when styling and handling your locs to avoid breakage or damage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about starting locs with natural hair:

How long does it take to start locs?

It can take several months to a year or more for locs to fully form and mature.

Can I start locs with short hair?

Yes, you can start locs with short hair, but it may take longer for them to fully form and mature.

Do I need to use a special shampoo for locs?

No, you can use any gentle shampoo to wash your locs. Avoid using any products that contain sulfates or harsh chemicals that can cause buildup.

Hair Care for Locs

Once you have started your locs, it is important to take care of them to ensure they remain healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for caring for your locs:

  • Wash your locs regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heavy products or oils that can cause buildup or attract dirt and debris.
  • Wrap your locs in a scarf or hat when exposed to extreme weather or sunlight.
  • Avoid using excessive heat or styling tools on your locs, as they can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim any loose or frayed ends to keep your locs looking neat and tidy.

In conclusion, starting locs with natural hair can be a great way to embrace your natural hair texture and style. With patience, care, and the right techniques, you can achieve beautiful, healthy locs that will look great for years to come.
