How To Create Volume On Top Of Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 23, 2023 · 3 min read

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Volume on top of hair is an essential component of many hairstyles. Whether you’re going for a sleek updo or a voluminous blowout, having volume on top of your hair can make all the difference in achieving the perfect look. In this article, we’ll go over how to create volume on top of hair, step by step.

How to

Creating volume on top of hair can be done in a few different ways. You can use products such as volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray, or you can use styling techniques such as backcombing or blow-drying. To create volume on top of hair, you’ll need:

  • Volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray
  • A comb
  • A blow dryer
  • A round brush

Step by Step

Step 1: Apply Volumizing Mousse or Root-Lifting Spray

Start by applying your chosen volumizing product to your roots. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the roots where you want to create volume. Use your fingers to work the product in and ensure that it’s evenly distributed.

Step 2: Backcomb Your Hair

Using a comb, gently backcomb the hair at the crown of your head. Take small sections of hair and backcomb each section towards the roots. This will create volume and lift at the crown of your head.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, focusing on the roots. Use the brush to lift and pull the hair upwards as you blow dry, creating volume and height. Continue blow drying until your hair is dry and styled to your liking.

Tips

  • Start with clean, dry hair for best results.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to help create volume from the start.
  • Don’t be afraid to backcomb your hair – it’s a great way to create volume quickly.
  • When blow drying, use the cool setting to set your style and help it last longer.

FAQ

Q: Can volumizing products damage your hair?

A: Volumizing products are generally safe to use, but overuse can lead to dryness and damage. Be sure to use them in moderation and follow the instructions on the product label.

Q: Can you create volume on top of hair without using products?

A: Yes, you can create volume on top of hair using styling techniques such as backcombing or blow-drying. However, using volumizing products can make the process easier and more effective.

Hair Care

Creating volume on top of hair is just one part of a healthy hair care routine. To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of vitamins and minerals.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the techniques outlined in this article, you can create volume on top of hair and achieve the perfect hairstyle every time.
