How To Cut A Super Short Pixie With Clippers

Written by Eveline Sep 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance haircut that still looks stylish and chic, a super short pixie might be just what you need. And with the right tools and techniques, you can even cut it yourself at home with clippers. In this post, we'll walk you through the steps to create a super short pixie with clippers, and provide tips for hair care and maintenance.

How to Cut a Super Short Pixie with Clippers

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a good-quality set of clippers with a range of guards, sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water.

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp. Use a comb to detangle any knots or tangles, and spray your hair with water to make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Choose Your Guard

Depending on how short you want your pixie to be, choose the appropriate guard for your clippers. If you're not sure, start with a longer guard and work your way down until you find the length you like.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Starting at the back of your head, use the clippers to create a straight line across your hairline. Then, work your way up the back of your head, using the same guard to create a uniform length. Use the comb to lift sections of hair, and trim any longer pieces with the scissors.

Step 4: Move to the Sides

Once you've finished the back, move to the sides of your head. Use the same guard to create a uniform length, and use the scissors to trim any longer pieces. Be sure to blend the sides into the back for a seamless look.

Step 5: Finish the Top

Finally, move to the top of your head. Use the scissors to create texture and layers, and blend the top into the sides and back for a cohesive look.

Tips for Cutting a Super Short Pixie with Clippers

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use the scissors to create texture and layers for a more natural look.
  • Don't be afraid to make mistakes â hair grows back!
  • Practice on a mannequin head or a willing friend before attempting to cut your own hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a super short pixie with clippers:

Can I cut a super short pixie with scissors?

While it's possible to cut a pixie with scissors, using clippers can make the process easier and more precise.

How often do I need to cut a super short pixie?

Because a super short pixie requires frequent maintenance, you'll need to trim it every 4-6 weeks to keep it looking fresh.

What products should I use to style my super short pixie?

A pomade or wax can help you achieve a sleek, polished look, while a texturizing spray can add volume and texture.

Hair Care for a Super Short Pixie

To keep your super short pixie looking its best, be sure to:

  • Wash it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot styling tools.
  • Protect it from sun damage and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently cut a super short pixie with clippers and enjoy a chic, low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
