How To Make Your Hair Look Like A Boy With A Beanie

Written by Daniel Nov 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you want to rock a boyish look with a beanie? You're not alone! This style has been popular among both men and women for years. But how do you achieve the perfect hair look to go with it? In this article, we'll show you how to make your hair look like a boy with a beanie in easy steps.

Description

The boy with a beanie look is a casual and effortless style that can be achieved with any hair type. This look is characterized by having hair that is styled to have a slight messy look, with bangs or fringes left to hang over the forehead. The beanie is then worn over the hair, slightly slouching to one side or pulled down to cover the ears.

How To

If you want to achieve the boy with a beanie look, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to achieving this look is to wash your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Rinse thoroughly with water and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as a texturizing spray or a volumizing mousse. This will help give your hair the messy look that is characteristic of the boy with a beanie style.

Step 3: Blow-dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to create volume at the roots. Use your fingers to tousle your hair as you dry it, to achieve the messy look you're going for.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs or Fringes

If you have bangs or fringes, style them to hang over your forehead. You can use a flat iron to straighten them or a curling iron to create loose waves.

Step 5: Put on Your Beanie

Put on your beanie, pulling it down over your ears or slouching it to one side. Make sure it fits snugly on your head, but not too tight that it flattens your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect boy with a beanie look:

  • Choose a beanie that complements your outfit and personal style.
  • If you have long hair, tie it up in a messy bun before putting on your beanie.
  • Use a hairspray to keep your hair in place and prevent flyaways.
  • If you have a fringe, trim it regularly to keep it at the right length.

FAQ

What hair length is best for the boy with a beanie look?

The boy with a beanie look can be achieved with any hair length, from short to long. However, it looks best on hair that is shoulder-length or shorter.

Can I wear a beanie with curly hair?

Yes, you can wear a beanie with curly hair. Just make sure to use a styling product that enhances your curls and gives them definition.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy hair, make sure to follow a good hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.

Now that you know how to make your hair look like a boy with a beanie, go ahead and try it out for yourself! With the right styling products and a bit of practice, you'll be rocking this casual and effortless look in no time.
