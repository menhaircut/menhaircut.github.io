How To Make Easy Hairstyles A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Aug 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring hairstyle? Do you want to switch up your look but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this article, we'll provide you with easy-to-follow steps on how to make easy hairstyles. From braids to buns, we've got you covered. So, grab your hairbrush and let's get started!

Table of Contents

  1. Braids
  2. Buns
  3. Ponytails
  4. Twists
  5. Curls

Braids

Braids are a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. There are many different types of braids, such as the French braid, Dutch braid, and fishtail braid. Here's how to make a simple three-strand braid:

How To:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three equal sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie off the end with a hair tie or ribbon.

Tips: To make a French braid, start at the top of your head and add more hair to each section as you braid. To make a Dutch braid, braid under instead of over.

Buns

Buns are a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still looking stylish. Here's how to make a simple bun:

How To:

  1. Brush your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun shape.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Spray with hairspray to keep in place.

Tips: For a messy bun, pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face. For a sleek bun, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways.

Ponytails

Ponytails are a quick and easy hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. Here's how to make a simple ponytail:

How To:

  1. Brush your hair into a high or low ponytail.
  2. Secure with a hair tie.
  3. Pull out a few strands of hair to frame your face.

Tips: For a more polished look, use a comb to smooth down any bumps or flyaways. For a more casual look, leave your hair slightly messy.

Twists

Twists are a fun and easy way to add some texture to your hair. Here's how to make a simple twist:

How To:

  1. Take a small section of hair and twist it around itself.
  2. Secure the twist with a bobby pin.
  3. Repeat with as many sections as you like.

Tips: For a more intricate look, twist larger sections of hair and secure with more bobby pins. For a more natural look, twist smaller sections of hair and leave the ends loose.

Curls

Curls are a great way to add some volume to your hair. Here's how to make simple curls:

How To:

  1. Section your hair into small sections.
  2. Wrap each section around a curling iron.
  3. Hold for 10-15 seconds.
  4. Release the curl and let it cool.
  5. Repeat with as many sections as you like.

Tips: For a more natural look, curl your hair in different directions. For a more polished look, curl all of your hair in the same direction.

FAQ

Q: How long does it take to make these hairstyles?

A: These hairstyles can take anywhere from 5-20 minutes depending on the complexity and your hair length.

Q: Can I make these hairstyles on short hair?

A: Yes, many of these hairstyles can be adapted for short hair. For example, you can make a small bun or twist with shorter hair.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair by using a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling. Always use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools, and try not to wash your hair every day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

With these easy-to-follow steps, you'll be able to make a variety of hairstyles in no time. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things. Who knows, you may just discover your new go-to hairstyle!
