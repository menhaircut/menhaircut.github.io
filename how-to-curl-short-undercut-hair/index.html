How To Curl Short Undercut Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 11, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking to switch things up? One way to add some texture and volume to your short undercut hair is to curl it. But how exactly do you do that? In this post, we’ll walk you through the process of curling short undercut hair, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Curl Short Undercut Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curling Short Undercut Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curled Short Undercut Hair

How to Curl Short Undercut Hair

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s first discuss the types of curls you can achieve with short undercut hair. The most common types are:

  • Beachy waves
  • Tight curls
  • Loose curls

The type of curl you want to achieve will determine the size of the curling iron you’ll need. For beachy waves, a larger barrel is recommended, while tighter curls require a smaller barrel.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now, let’s go through the steps of curling short undercut hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. You can also use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  2. Section your hair into small sections using clips.
  3. Take one section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold for a few seconds, then release.
  4. Repeat step 3 with the rest of your hair, working in small sections.
  5. Once you’ve curled all of your hair, flip your head upside down and shake out the curls.
  6. Run your fingers through your hair to break up the curls and create a more natural look.
  7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Short Undercut Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when curling short undercut hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Work in small sections to ensure all of your hair is curled evenly.
  • Don’t hold the curling iron on your hair for too long, as this can cause damage.
  • For beachy waves, curl your hair away from your face.
  • For tighter curls, curl your hair towards your face.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling short undercut hair:

Q: Can I curl my hair if it’s wet?

A: No, you should always curl your hair when it’s dry to prevent damage.

Q: How long will the curls last?

A: The curls should last for several hours, but this can depend on your hair type and the products you use.

Q: Can I curl my hair without a curling iron?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener to curl your hair or try using foam rollers for a heatless option.

Hair Care for Curled Short Undercut Hair

To keep your curled short undercut hair looking its best, here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair and maintain its health.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to curl your short undercut hair with ease. Experiment with different types of curls and products to find what works best for you.
