How To Cut Men s Long Curly Hair Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to cut your long curly hair short, you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieving the perfect short haircut for men with curly hair. From prepping your hair to choosing the right tools, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents:

  • Prepping Your Hair
  • Choosing the Right Tools
  • Cutting Your Hair
  • Styling Your New Cut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Men with Curly Hair

Prepping Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to properly prep it. Begin by washing and conditioning your hair with a curl-enhancing product. This will help define your curls and make them easier to cut.

Once your hair is clean and conditioned, let it air dry or use a diffuser attachment on a blow dryer to dry your curls. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Choosing the Right Tools

When it comes to cutting curly hair, it's important to choose the right tools. Here are a few items you'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips

You may also want to consider investing in a pair of thinning shears. These scissors have teeth on one blade that help create a softer look and reduce bulk.

Cutting Your Hair

Now that your hair is prepped and you have the right tools, it's time to start cutting. Follow these steps:

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Begin with the top section. Using your comb, lift a small section of hair and cut it to your desired length. Continue cutting small sections of hair until the entire top section is complete.
  3. Next, move on to the sides. Using your comb, lift a small section of hair and cut it to match the length of the top section. Continue cutting small sections of hair until both sides are complete.
  4. Finally, move on to the back. Using your comb, lift a small section of hair and cut it to match the length of the sides and top. Continue cutting small sections of hair until the entire back section is complete.

Remember to cut your hair slightly longer than your desired length, as curly hair tends to shrink as it dries.

Styling Your New Cut

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair and scrunch it to define your curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on a blow dryer to enhance your curls.

For a more polished look, you can use a pomade or wax to tame any flyaways and add shine.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Curly Hair

Here are a few tips for maintaining your short curly hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair, as this can cause frizz.
  • Invest in a silk pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent frizz.

FAQs

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended that you get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your short curly hair.

Can I cut my own curly hair?

While it's possible to cut your own curly hair, it's recommended that you see a professional stylist for the best results.

What's the best way to dry curly hair?

The best way to dry curly hair is by using a diffuser attachment on a blow dryer. This helps enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Curly Hair

Here are a few additional hair care tips for men with curly hair:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos that contain sulfates, as these can strip your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can cause damage to your curls.

Now that you know how to cut men's long curly hair short, you can achieve the perfect short haircut for your curls. Remember to properly prep your hair, choose the right tools, and follow our step-by-step guide for the best results.
