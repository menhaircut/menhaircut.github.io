Best Hair Color For 60 Year Old A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a 60 year old woman looking to change up your hair color? Choosing the right hair color can make a big difference in how you look and feel. But with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one is best for you. In this article, we will discuss the best hair color for 60 year old women and provide you with tips and advice to help you make the right choice.

How to Choose the Best Hair Color for 60 Year Old Women

When choosing a hair color, there are several factors to consider:

Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a major role in determining the best hair color for you. If you have a warm skin tone, you should opt for warm hair colors like golden blonde, honey blonde, or copper. If you have a cool skin tone, go for cool hair colors like ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy.

Natural Hair Color

Your natural hair color is also important in determining the best hair color for you. If you have dark hair, you may want to stick to darker shades like chocolate brown or black. If you have light hair, you can experiment with lighter shades like platinum blonde or honey blonde.

Lifestyle and Personality

Your lifestyle and personality should also influence your hair color choice. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want to opt for a low-maintenance hair color. If you have a bold personality, you may want to experiment with a bright or unusual hair color.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Best Hair Color for 60 Year Old Women

Follow these steps to choose the best hair color for you:

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they are blue, you have a cool skin tone. If they are green, you have a warm skin tone.

Step 2: Consider your natural hair color

Take a look at your natural hair color and decide whether you want to stick with a similar shade or try something new.

Step 3: Think about your lifestyle and personality

Consider how much time and effort you are willing to put into maintaining your hair color. Also, think about whether you want a subtle or bold change.

Tips for Choosing the Best Hair Color for 60 Year Old Women

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the best hair color for you:

Tip 1: Don't go too drastic

As you get older, it's important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone and facial features. Avoid going too drastic with your hair color, as this can make you look older.

Tip 2: Consider a professional consultation

If you're unsure about which hair color to choose, consider consulting a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose a color that suits you best.

Tip 3: Experiment with temporary hair color

If you're not ready to commit to a permanent hair color, experiment with temporary hair color first. This will allow you to try out different shades without the permanent commitment.

FAQs About Best Hair Color for 60 Year Old Women

Q: Is it okay to dye your hair at 60?

A: Yes, it's perfectly fine to dye your hair at 60. In fact, many women in their 60s choose to dye their hair to cover up gray hair or to try a new look.

Q: What hair color makes you look younger?

A: Hair colors like honey blonde, caramel, and chestnut can make you look younger because they add warmth and dimension to your hair.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of hair color you choose and how fast your hair grows. Generally, permanent hair color should be touched up every 4-6 weeks, while semi-permanent hair color can last up to 8 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for 60 Year Old Women

Regardless of which hair color you choose, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for 60 year old women:

Tip 1: Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can be harsh on your hair, so choose a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tip 2: Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning can help to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Do a deep conditioning treatment at least once a week.

Tip 3: Protect your hair from heat

Using heat styling tools like blow dryers and flat irons can damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair to prevent damage.

Choosing the best hair color for 60 year old women can be a fun and exciting process. With these tips and advice, you can choose a hair color that makes you look and feel your best.
