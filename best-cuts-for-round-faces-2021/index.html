Best Cuts For Round Faces 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the best haircuts for round faces in 2021? Look no further! In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you through the best haircuts for round faces, how to style them, and hair care tips for maintaining your new look. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have soft and curved features, and they’re shorter in length than they are in width. The best haircuts for round faces are those that elongate the face and create the illusion of length. So, if you have a round face, you’ll want to avoid haircuts that add too much volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder.

How to

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it’s important to consider your hair type, texture, and lifestyle. Some haircuts require more maintenance than others, and some may not work for your hair type or texture. It’s also important to consider your personal style and what will make you feel confident and comfortable.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces

The best haircuts for round faces are those that add height to the crown of the head and length to the face. Here are some of the best haircuts for round faces in 2021:

  • Long Layers
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Pixie Cut
  • Bob Cut
  • Asymmetrical Cut
  • Shoulder-Length Cut
  • Blunt Cut

Step by Step

When getting a haircut for a round face, it’s important to communicate with your hairstylist about the look you’re going for. Here are some steps to follow when getting a haircut for a round face:

  1. Research different haircuts and styles that work for round faces.
  2. Communicate your desired look with your hairstylist.
  3. Consider your hair type and texture.
  4. Maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling.

Tips

  • Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features.
  • Consider your hair type and lifestyle when choosing a haircut.
  • Maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Use hair products that work with your hair type and texture.

FAQ

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face?

A: The best haircut for a round face is one that adds height to the crown of the head and length to the face. Some of the best haircuts for round faces include long layers, side-swept bangs, and asymmetrical cuts.

Q: Can I wear a bob cut if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can wear a bob cut if you have a round face. However, it’s important to choose a bob cut that adds length to the face and avoids adding too much volume to the sides.

Q: How do I style my hair after getting a haircut for a round face?

A: To style your hair after getting a haircut for a round face, use a round brush to add volume at the crown of the head, and avoid adding too much volume to the sides. Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

To maintain your new haircut, it’s important to follow a hair care routine that works for your hair type and texture. Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your new look:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to hydrate and protect your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain your haircut.

So, there you have it! The best haircuts for round faces in 2021, how to style them, and hair care tips for maintaining your new look. We hope this guide has been helpful, and happy hairstyling!
