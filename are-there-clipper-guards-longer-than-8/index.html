Are There Clipper Guards Longer Than 8

Written by Luffy Nov 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Clipper guards are an essential tool for those who want to maintain a consistent length of hair when cutting their own hair or someone else's. They come in various sizes, usually ranging from 0.5mm to 25mm. However, many people wonder if there are clipper guards longer than 8. The short answer is yes, there are. In this article, we will explore everything you need to know about clipper guards longer than 8.

Description

Clipper guards are the plastic attachments that fit onto the blades of hair clippers. They allow you to adjust the length of the hair that you cut. The guards are usually color-coded, with each color representing a different length. For example, a #1 guard is usually 3mm long, while an #8 guard is usually 25mm long.

How To

If you are interested in using clipper guards longer than 8, you will need to purchase them separately. Most clipper brands sell longer guards that can be used with their clippers. You can also find third-party guards that are compatible with most clippers.

Once you have the longer guards, you can use them in the same way that you would use any other guard. Simply attach the guard to the clipper blade and adjust the length as desired. Start with a longer length and gradually work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use clipper guards longer than 8:

  1. Purchase clipper guards longer than 8
  2. Attach the guard to the clipper blade
  3. Adjust the guard to the desired length
  4. Start cutting the hair at the longest length
  5. Gradually work your way down to the desired length

Tips

When using clipper guards longer than 8, it is important to keep in mind that longer guards may not be suitable for all hair types. Thick, curly hair may require shorter guards to achieve the desired length. Additionally, it is important to clean the guards regularly to prevent hair and debris from building up.

FAQ

Q: What is the longest clipper guard available?

A: The longest clipper guard available on the market is usually a #12 guard, which is 38mm long.

Q: Can clipper guards be used on all types of clippers?

A: Clipper guards are usually designed to be used with specific brands and models of clippers. However, many third-party guards are designed to be compatible with most clippers.

Q: Can clipper guards be used on wet hair?

A: It is not recommended to use clipper guards on wet hair, as wet hair is more prone to snagging and pulling. It is best to dry the hair thoroughly before using clippers.

Hair Care

Using clipper guards longer than 8 can be a great way to achieve a longer, more even length when cutting hair. However, it is important to take proper care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Regular trims, conditioning treatments, and using high-quality hair products can all help to keep your hair looking its best.

In conclusion, if you are looking for clipper guards longer than 8, they are available. Just make sure to choose the right length for your hair type and take proper care of your hair to keep it looking great.
