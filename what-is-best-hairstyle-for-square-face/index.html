What Is The Best Hairstyle For Square Face

Written by Luffy Nov 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a square face shape, you're in luck because you can pull off a variety of hairstyles! However, with so many options, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect one. In this article, we'll discuss the best hairstyles for square faces, how to style them, and hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face shape has a strong jawline and forehead, with the width of the face equal to its length. The best hairstyles for square faces soften the angles of the face and draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider.

How to

The best hairstyles for square faces are those that create balance and soften the angles of the face. Consider hairstyles with layers or waves to add movement and volume. Side-swept bangs or a center part can also help to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Step by Step

1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine your face shape and discuss the best hairstyle options.
2. Consider adding layers or waves to your hair to create movement and volume.
3. Experiment with side-swept bangs or a center part to find the best look for your face shape.
4. Use styling products to enhance your hairstyle and hold it in place.

Tips

- Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of the face.
- Consider a shoulder-length hairstyle to create balance.
- Use a round brush when blow-drying to add volume and create a soft, bouncy look.
- Try a messy bun or loose braid to add texture and create a relaxed, casual look.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of the face, such as a blunt bob or a slicked-back ponytail.

What is the best hair length for a square face?

A shoulder-length hairstyle is ideal for a square face, as it creates balance and softens the angles of the face.

What are some styling products that are good for square faces?

Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray to add movement and volume to your hair. A hairspray with a flexible hold can also help to keep your hairstyle in place without making it look stiff or unnatural.

Hair Care

Keep your hair healthy and shiny by following a regular hair care routine. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to hydrate your hair and prevent breakage. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray when blow-drying or using hot tools. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Now that you know the best hairstyles for square faces, it's time to experiment and find the perfect look for you. Remember to consult with your hairstylist for personalized recommendations and tips on how to style your hair at home. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you'll be turning heads in no time!
