Men s Hairstyles Medium Length On Top A Guide To The Best Looks

Written by Bardi Aug 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Medium length hair for men has been a popular trend for years, and it's not going anywhere anytime soon. In particular, the style of having medium length on top of the head has become increasingly popular. This article will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best men's hairstyles medium length on top.

Description

Medium length hair on top of the head is a versatile style that can be worn in various ways. It's a perfect style for men who want to keep their hair length in between short and long. The hair on top can be styled in different ways, such as slicked back, messy, textured, or with a side part.

How to

If you want to achieve the perfect men's hairstyle medium length on top, here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Get the right haircut

To achieve the perfect medium length on top, make sure to ask your barber for a medium length cut with layered hair. This will allow you to have a more textured and voluminous look.

Step 2: Use the right products

To style your hair, use the right products that work best for your hair type. For example, if you have thick hair, use a pomade or wax to keep your hair in place. If you have thinner hair, use a light hair spray or mousse to add volume.

Step 3: Style your hair

Once you have the right haircut and products, it's time to style your hair. Depending on the look you want to achieve, you can slick it back, push it to the side, or add some texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your men's hairstyle medium length on top:

- Choose the right haircut that complements your face shape. - Use the right products that work best for your hair type. - Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. - Regularly trim and maintain your hair to keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyles medium length on top:

Q: How long should the hair be on top for a medium length style?

A: Ideally, the hair on top should be about 3-4 inches long to achieve the perfect medium length style.

Q: Can I still have a medium length style if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! In fact, curly hair can add more texture and volume to your medium length style.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to follow these hair care tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. - Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair nourished. - Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often. - Don't forget to trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect men's hairstyle medium length on top that will have you looking and feeling your best.
