How To Know If A Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're thinking about getting a new hairstyle, you might be wondering if it will suit you or not. There are a few things to consider when choosing a new hairstyle, such as your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to know if a hairstyle suits you.

Consider Your Face Shape

The first thing to consider when choosing a new hairstyle is your face shape. Different hairstyles can flatter different face shapes. For example, if you have a round face, a long layered haircut can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, soft waves can help soften your features.

Think About Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider. Certain hairstyles work better with certain hair types. For example, if you have fine hair, a blunt bob can help create the illusion of thicker hair. If you have curly hair, a layered haircut can help define your curls.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also important to consider when choosing a new hairstyle. If you're always on the go and don't have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance haircut like a pixie cut or a bob might be a good choice. If you have a more formal job, a classic updo might be more appropriate.

Try Face-Framing Layers

Face-framing layers are a great way to add dimension and movement to your hair. They can also help accentuate your facial features. If you're not sure about getting a drastic haircut, face-framing layers are a good way to test the waters.

Try Bangs

Bangs are another way to change up your look without a drastic haircut. There are many different types of bangs to choose from, such as blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, and curtain bangs. Bangs can help frame your face and add a touch of personality to your hairstyle.

Consider Length

The length of your hair can make a big difference in how it looks. If you have a long face, a shorter haircut can help balance out your features. If you have a round face, a longer haircut can help elongate your face. Think about the proportions of your face when choosing a length.

Think About Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also affect how a hairstyle looks on you. If you have thick hair, a layered haircut can help lighten the weight and make it more manageable. If you have fine hair, a blunt bob can help create the illusion of thicker hair.

Consider Hair Color

Your hair color can also affect how a hairstyle looks on you. Lighter hair colors can help soften your features, while darker hair colors can help create contrast and make your features stand out. Consider your skin tone when choosing a hair color.

Schedule a Haircut Consultation

If you're still not sure which hairstyle is right for you, schedule a consultation with a hairstylist. They can give you personalized advice based on your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. They can also recommend haircuts that will flatter your features and work with your hair texture.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen a new hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

What face shape looks best with short hair?

Short hair can flatter many different face shapes, but it looks particularly good on oval and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, a longer bob or a pixie cut with longer layers can help elongate your face.

What hair color looks best on fair skin?

For fair skin, lighter hair colors like blonde, light brown, and strawberry blonde can help soften your features. If you have cool undertones, ash blonde or platinum blonde might be a good choice. If you have warm undertones, golden or honey blonde might be more flattering.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. As a general rule, you should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. If you have shorter hair, you might need to get a haircut more often to maintain the shape.

Conclusion

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a fun way to change up your look and express your personality. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and works with your hair texture. Don't be afraid to try something new!
