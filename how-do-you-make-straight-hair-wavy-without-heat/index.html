How To Make Straight Hair Wavy Without Heat

Written by Frank Oct 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of using heat tools to curl your hair? Not only do they damage your hair, but they can also be time-consuming. Luckily, there are ways to achieve wavy hair without using heat. In this article, we'll show you how to make straight hair wavy without heat.

Table of Content

  • How to Make Straight Hair Wavy Without Heat
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips to Make Wavy Hair Last Longer
  • Frequently Asked Questions

Step-by-Step Guide

Here are the steps to make straight hair wavy without heat:

1. Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to create a good base for your waves. Towel dry your hair until it's slightly damp.

2. Apply Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair to give it more body and hold. Scrunch your hair to distribute the mousse evenly.

3. Braid Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections and braid each section. Make sure to braid tightly and secure the ends with a hair tie.

4. Sleep with Braids

Sleep with the braids in your hair overnight. In the morning, remove the hair ties and gently undo the braids.

5. Apply Hairspray

Apply a light hold hairspray to your hair to set the waves in place.

Tips to Make Wavy Hair Last Longer

Here are some tips to make your wavy hair last longer:

1. Use Dry Shampoo

Apply dry shampoo to your roots to absorb excess oil and refresh your hair. This will help your waves last longer.

2. Avoid Touching Your Hair

Try not to touch your hair too much as this can cause your waves to loosen and lose their shape.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

Sleep on a silk pillowcase to prevent friction between your hair and the pillow. This will help your waves last longer and prevent frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make my hair wavy without using any products?

Yes, you can make your hair wavy without using any products by braiding your hair and sleeping with the braids in overnight.

How long will the waves last?

The waves can last anywhere from 1-3 days depending on your hair type and how well you maintain them.

Can I use this method on short hair?

Yes, this method can work on short hair. Just make sure to braid tightly and use a light hold hairspray to set the waves in place.

Hair Care Tips

1. Use Heat Protectant

If you do use heat tools on your hair, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

2. Deep Condition Your Hair

Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and hydrated.

3. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to make straight hair wavy without heat, you can achieve beautiful, beachy waves without damaging your hair. Follow these tips and tricks to keep your waves looking great for days.
