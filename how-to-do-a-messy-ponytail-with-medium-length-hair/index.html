How To Do A Messy Ponytail With Medium Length Hair

Written by Oliver Nov 21, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have medium length hair and want to try a new hairstyle, a messy ponytail is a great option. This laid-back style is perfect for casual occasions, and it's easy to create in just a few simple steps. In this article, we'll show you how to do a messy ponytail with medium length hair, as well as provide some tips and tricks for getting the perfect look.

Description

A messy ponytail is a casual hairstyle that is perfect for everyday wear. It's a great option for those with medium length hair who want to keep their hair out of their face while still looking stylish. The key to creating a messy ponytail is to embrace the imperfections and let your hair have a natural, tousled look.

How to

Here's how to do a messy ponytail with medium length hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots.
  2. Using your fingers, loosely gather your hair towards the back of your head. Don't worry about making it too neat or tidy – the messier, the better!
  3. Secure your hair in a ponytail using a hair tie. Make sure to leave a few strands of hair out around your face to create a more relaxed, effortless look.
  4. Using your fingers, gently pull on the hair at the crown of your head to create some volume and texture.
  5. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin.
  6. Using your fingers, gently pull on the hair in the ponytail to create a more relaxed, tousled look.
  7. Finish off the look by spritzing some hairspray over your hair to hold it in place.

Step by Step

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide on how to do a messy ponytail with medium length hair:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. Use a wide-toothed comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair

Using your fingers, loosely gather your hair towards the back of your head. Don't worry about making it too neat or tidy – the messier, the better! Make sure to leave a few strands of hair out around your face to create a more relaxed, effortless look.

Step 3: Secure Your Hair in a Ponytail

Secure your hair in a ponytail using a hair tie. Make sure to position the ponytail where you want it – high, low, or somewhere in between.

Step 4: Create Volume and Texture

Using your fingers, gently pull on the hair at the crown of your head to create some volume and texture. This will help to give your messy ponytail a more natural look.

Step 5: Hide the Hair Tie

Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie to hide it. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin. This will help to make your messy ponytail look more polished and put-together.

Step 6: Tousle Your Hair

Using your fingers, gently pull on the hair in the ponytail to create a more relaxed, tousled look. Don't be afraid to play around with your hair and create different textures and shapes.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off the look by spritzing some hairspray over your hair to hold it in place. This will help to keep your messy ponytail looking great all day long.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you get the perfect messy ponytail:

  • Don't worry about making it too neat or tidy – the messier, the better!
  • Experiment with different hair ties and bobby pins to find ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Try adding some hair accessories, like a headband or hair clips, to give your messy ponytail a more personalized touch.
  • Use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray before styling to give your hair some extra oomph.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about messy ponytails:

What should I do if my hair is too short for a messy ponytail?

If your hair is too short for a messy ponytail, try a half-up, half-down style instead. You can still create a messy look by leaving some strands of hair out around your face and using texturizing products to add volume and texture.

Can I wear a messy ponytail to a formal event?

A messy ponytail is generally considered a casual hairstyle, but you can dress it up for a formal event by adding some hair accessories or styling it in a more polished way. Just make sure to keep it looking relaxed and effortless – that's the key to a great messy ponytail!

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, make sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner that are suited to your hair type. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage it and make it harder to style. If you're using heat tools, always use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. And don't forget to get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and free of split ends.

So, there you have it – everything you need to know about how to do a messy ponytail with medium length hair. With just a few simple steps, you can create a laid-back, effortless look that's perfect for any occasion. So go ahead and give it a try – you might just fall in love with this easy and stylish hairstyle!
