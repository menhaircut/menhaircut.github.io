How To Cut Men s Medium Curly Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Cutting men's medium curly hair with scissors can be a daunting task, especially if you have never done it before. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great-looking haircut that your clients will love. In this guide, we will walk you through the process of cutting men's medium curly hair with scissors. We'll cover everything from how to prepare the hair to the finishing touches.

How to Cut Men's Medium Curly Hair with Scissors

The first step in cutting men's medium curly hair with scissors is to gather the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a spray bottle filled with water. It's also a good idea to have a mirror and a cape to protect the client's clothes. Once you have everything you need, you can begin the process of cutting the hair.

Step 1: Prepare the Hair

Before you start cutting, you should prepare the hair by washing and conditioning it. This will make the hair easier to work with and will help you achieve a better result. Once the hair is clean, you can towel dry it and comb it out to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Next, you should section the hair to make it easier to cut. You can do this by dividing the hair into four sections: the top, the back, and the sides. Use clips to secure each section so that you can work on one section at a time.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Now that the hair is prepared and sectioned, you can start cutting. Begin by cutting the hair on the sides and back, using the comb to guide the scissors. Be sure to cut the hair evenly and to keep the scissors at a slight angle to create a natural-looking finish.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Once you have finished cutting the sides and back, you can move on to the top. Use the comb to lift the hair up and cut it to the desired length. Again, be sure to keep the scissors at a slight angle to create a natural-looking finish.

Step 5: Finish the Haircut

Once you have finished cutting the hair, you can remove the clips and style the hair as desired. Use the comb and scissors to make any final adjustments, and be sure to trim any stray hairs that you may have missed.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you are still unsure about how to cut men's medium curly hair with scissors, here is a step-by-step guide that you can follow:

  1. Prepare the hair by washing and conditioning it
  2. Section the hair into four sections: the top, the back, and the sides
  3. Cut the hair on the sides and back, using the comb to guide the scissors
  4. Cut the hair on the top, using the comb to lift the hair up and cut it to the desired length
  5. Remove the clips and style the hair as desired

Tips for Cutting Men's Medium Curly Hair with Scissors

Here are some tips that can help you achieve a great-looking haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Work with small sections of hair to ensure evenness
  • Use a spray bottle to moisten the hair if it becomes dry or difficult to work with
  • Keep the scissors at a slight angle to create a natural-looking finish
  • Trim the hair gradually, rather than cutting off too much at once

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut men's medium curly hair with clippers?

Yes, you can cut men's medium curly hair with clippers, but it may be more difficult to achieve a natural-looking finish. Scissors allow for more control and precision, which can be important when working with curly hair.

How often should men with medium curly hair get a haircut?

Men with medium curly hair should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style of the haircut.

What products should men with medium curly hair use?

Men with medium curly hair should use products that moisturize and define their curls, such as curl creams, gels, and mousses. They should also use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out their hair.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Medium Curly Hair

Here are some tips that men with medium curly hair can follow to keep their hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash and condition their hair regularly with sulfate-free products
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or their fingers to detangle their hair
  • Moisturize their hair with a leave-in conditioner or oil
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly and with a heat protectant
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of their hair

By following these tips, men with medium curly hair can keep their hair looking great between haircuts.
