How To Create 40S Wave Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a vintage hairstyle that exudes sophistication and glamour? Look no further than the 40s wave hair! This classic hairstyle has made a comeback in recent years, thanks to its timeless appeal and versatility. Whether you're attending a formal event or just want to add some vintage flair to your everyday look, the 40s wave hair is a great choice.

Description

The 40s wave hair is characterized by its signature S-shaped waves that frame the face and add volume to the hair. This hairstyle was popularized by Hollywood stars such as Veronica Lake and Rita Hayworth, and it has since become a go-to style for vintage enthusiasts and fashionistas alike.

The 40s wave hair can be created on medium to long hair and works best on hair that has some texture or wave. If you have straight hair, you may need to use some styling products and tools to create the waves.

How to Create 40s Wave Hair

Creating 40s wave hair may seem daunting at first, but with the right tools and techniques, anyone can achieve this classic look. Here's what you'll need:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron or wand
  • Clips or bobby pins
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create your own 40s wave hair:

  1. Start by applying a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from the curling iron.
  2. Using a curling iron or wand, curl small sections of hair away from your face. Hold the curl for a few seconds, then release and clip it up to cool.
  3. Repeat step 2 until all of your hair is curled and clipped up.
  4. Once your hair has cooled, remove the clips and gently brush out the curls with a soft-bristled brush or your fingers.
  5. Next, create a deep side part on one side of your head.
  6. Take a small section of hair from the front and twist it away from your face. Pin it in place with a bobby pin.
  7. Take another small section of hair from the front and twist it in the opposite direction. Pin it in place with a bobby pin.
  8. Continue twisting and pinning small sections of hair until you reach the back of your head. The twists should create an S-shape pattern.
  9. Repeat on the other side of your head.
  10. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the waves in place.

Tips for Creating 40s Wave Hair

Here are a few tips to help you perfect your 40s wave hairstyle:

  • Use a curling iron or wand with a barrel size of at least 1 inch to create loose waves.
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the roots, as this can create a vintage "bubble" look.
  • For a more dramatic effect, curl your hair in the same direction all the way around your head.
  • To create more volume, tease the hair at the crown of your head before creating the waves.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that best suits your face shape.

FAQ

What if I have short hair?

While the 40s wave hairstyle is typically created on medium to long hair, you can still achieve a similar look on shorter hair by using a smaller curling iron or wand and creating tighter curls. You can also try using hair extensions to add length and volume.

Can I create 40s wave hair without heat styling?

If you prefer not to use heat styling tools, you can try creating 40s wave hair using braids or rollers. Simply braid your hair in small sections and leave it overnight to create waves, or use foam rollers to create curls.

Hair Care Tips for 40s Wave Hair

To keep your 40s wave hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip away natural oils and cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can create beautiful 40s wave hair that will turn heads and make you feel like a Hollywood star! Whether you're going for a vintage-inspired look or just want to add some glamour to your everyday style, the 40s wave hair is a timeless choice.
