How To Know If You d Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Robby Jul 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're considering getting curtain bangs, you might be wondering if this trendy hairstyle will suit you. Curtain bangs are a versatile style that can flatter most face shapes, but there are a few things to keep in mind before making the cut. In this guide, we'll show you how to know if you'd look good with curtain bangs.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Considering Your Hair Texture
  • Evaluating Your Forehead Size
  • Matching Your Personality
  • Trying It Out Virtually

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in knowing if you'd look good with curtain bangs is to determine your face shape. Curtain bangs work well with oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces. If you have a long or narrow face, you may want to avoid curtain bangs as they can make your face appear even longer.

Here's how to determine your face shape:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a washable marker to trace the outline of your face on the mirror.
  3. Step back and look at the shape you drew.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can move on to considering your hair texture.

Considering Your Hair Texture

Another factor to consider when deciding if you'd look good with curtain bangs is your hair texture. Curtain bangs work well with straight, wavy, and curly hair, but they may require more styling if you have thick or coarse hair. If you have very fine hair, curtain bangs can add volume and texture to your look.

Keep in mind that if you have very curly or kinky hair, you may want to avoid curtain bangs as they can be difficult to style and may not stay in place.

Evaluating Your Forehead Size

One of the main features of curtain bangs is that they frame your face and draw attention to your forehead. If you have a large forehead, curtain bangs can help balance your features and make your forehead appear smaller. However, if you have a very small forehead, curtain bangs may not be the best choice as they can make your forehead appear even smaller.

Matching Your Personality

Curtain bangs are a playful and feminine hairstyle that can complement a variety of styles and personalities. If you have a bubbly and outgoing personality, curtain bangs can help showcase your sense of fun. If you have a more reserved personality, curtain bangs can add a touch of whimsy to your look.

Trying It Out Virtually

If you're still not sure if you'd look good with curtain bangs, you can try them out virtually using a hairstyle app or website. Simply upload a photo of yourself and try on different styles to see how they look.

Tips for Getting Curtain Bangs

If you've decided that you'd look good with curtain bangs, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Find a stylist who has experience cutting curtain bangs.
  • Bring photos of the style you want to your appointment.
  • Ask your stylist to cut your bangs longer than you think you want them, as they will shrink up when they dry.
  • Be prepared to style your bangs every day.

FAQ

Can I wear curtain bangs if I have glasses?

Absolutely! Curtain bangs can actually complement glasses by framing your face and drawing attention to your eyes.

How often do I need to trim my curtain bangs?

It's recommended to trim your curtain bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and stylish.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create a smooth, voluminous look.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your bangs.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.

Now that you know how to determine if you'd look good with curtain bangs, you can confidently make the cut and enjoy this trendy hairstyle!
