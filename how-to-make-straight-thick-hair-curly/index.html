How To Make Straight Thick Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have naturally straight and thick hair, you may be wondering how you can achieve beautiful curls without damaging your hair. The good news is that it's possible to make your straight hair curly with a few simple steps and the right hair tools. In this blog post, we'll show you how to make straight thick hair curly in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

How to Make Straight Thick Hair Curly

The key to making straight thick hair curly is to use the right tools and techniques. Here's how to get started:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Make sure to use a conditioner that's designed to hydrate and soften your hair. This will help to prepare your hair for curling and prevent damage.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before you start using heat tools on your hair, it's important to protect it from damage. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair before you start curling. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and keep it healthy.

Step 3: Choose Your Curling Iron

When it comes to making straight thick hair curly, the right curling iron is essential. Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that matches the size of curls you want to create. For tight curls, choose a small barrel size. For loose waves, choose a larger barrel size.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections, starting from the bottom. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate and make it easier to curl your hair.

Step 5: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the hair. Repeat this process on each section of your hair until you've curled all of your hair.

Step 6: Add Some Texture

Once your hair is curled, you can add some texture by running your fingers through your hair or using a texture spray. This will help to create a more natural and effortless look.

Tips for Making Straight Thick Hair Curly

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before you start curling.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can damage your hair and create an unnatural look.
  • Use a hairspray or styling cream to help hold your curls in place.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQ About Making Straight Thick Hair Curly

Q: Can I make my hair curly without using heat tools?

A: Yes, you can use other methods like braiding your hair or using rollers to create curls without using heat tools. However, these methods may not be as effective as using a curling iron.

Q: How often should I curl my hair?

A: It's best to avoid curling your hair every day, as this can damage your hair. Instead, try to limit curling to once or twice a week.

Q: How can I keep my curls looking fresh?

A: To keep your curls looking fresh, try sleeping on a silk pillowcase to prevent frizz, and use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil.

Hair Care for Straight Thick Hair

Making your straight thick hair curly can be a fun and exciting way to change up your look. However, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and keep it healthy. Here are some tips for caring for your straight thick hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

By following these tips, you can make your straight thick hair curly without damaging your hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve beautiful curls that will make you feel confident and stylish.
