How To Make Square Face Look Round Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Having a square face shape can be challenging when it comes to hairstyling, as it can make your face look boxy and angular. However, with the right techniques and haircuts, you can easily soften your features and make your face look rounder. In this article, we will share some tips and tricks on how to make square face look round.

Table of Content

  • How to Make Square Face Look Round
  • Step by Step Guide

How to Make Square Face Look Round

The key to making a square face look round is to add softness and curves to your features. You can achieve this through the following techniques:

  • Opt for hairstyles that have layers and volume on the top and sides of your head.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too short or blunt, as they will accentuate your square jawline.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs or a middle part to create the illusion of a rounder face.
  • Experiment with different hair textures, such as waves or curls, to add dimension to your hair and soften your features.
  • Use makeup to contour your face and create shadows that will make your face appear rounder.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make square face look round:

  1. Start by determining your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. As mentioned earlier, avoid haircuts that are too short or blunt, and opt for hairstyles that have layers and volume on the top and sides of your head.
  3. Add side-swept bangs or a middle part to your haircut to create the illusion of a rounder face.
  4. Experiment with different hair textures, such as waves or curls. Use a curling iron or a flat iron to create soft waves or curls.
  5. Use makeup to contour your face. Apply a bronzer or a contour powder to the hollows of your cheeks, temples, and jawline to create shadows that will make your face appear rounder.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to make square face look round:

  • Choose earrings and necklaces that have curves or round shapes to soften your features.
  • Wear clothing that has a V-neckline or a scoop neckline to create the illusion of a longer, rounder face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back, as they can accentuate your square jawline.
  • Invest in a good quality hair serum or oil to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear short hair if I have a square face?

A: Yes, you can! Just make sure to choose a haircut that has layers and volume to add softness and curves to your features.

Q: Will bangs make my face look rounder?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a rounder face. Just make sure to avoid blunt bangs, as they can accentuate your square jawline.

Q: How often should I get my hair trimmed?

A: It depends on your hair type and length. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6 to 8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Square Faces

Aside from choosing the right haircut and styling techniques, it's also important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some hair care tips for square faces:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and make it look dull.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily make your square face look round and soft. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles and makeup techniques to find the ones that work best for you. As always, taking care of your hair and keeping it healthy is key to achieving a flattering and beautiful look.
