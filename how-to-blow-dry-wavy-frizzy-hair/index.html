How To Blow Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 · 4 min read
If you have wavy frizzy hair, you know how difficult it can be to style it properly. Blow drying your hair can be a nightmare, especially if you don't know the right technique. In this article, we'll teach you how to blow dry wavy frizzy hair in a way that will leave you with smooth and shiny locks.

Description

Blow drying your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you have wavy frizzy hair. It's easy to get frustrated when your hair refuses to cooperate, but with the right technique, you can achieve a sleek look without damaging your hair. In this article, we'll show you how to blow dry wavy frizzy hair like a pro.

How to Blow Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair

Blow drying wavy frizzy hair can be a challenge, but with the right technique, you can achieve a sleek and shiny look. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin blow drying your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any tangles. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for wavy frizzy hair to keep your locks hydrated and silky smooth.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

To prevent damage to your hair, it's important to apply a heat protectant before blow drying. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair healthy. Apply the heat protectant evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair evenly and avoid any tangles.

Step 4: Start Blow Drying

Using a round brush, begin blow drying your hair in sections. Start with the bottom layers and work your way up. Use the brush to pull your hair taut and direct the heat downwards.

Step 5: Finish with a Cool Blast

Once you've finished blow drying your hair, give it a cool blast of air to set the style. This will help to seal the cuticle and prevent frizz.

Tips

- Use a diffuser attachment to minimize frizz and enhance your natural waves. - Avoid using high heat settings, as this can damage your hair. - Don't forget to condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and healthy.

FAQ

Q: Can I blow dry my hair every day?

A: It's not recommended to blow dry your hair every day, as this can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of heat styling tools and use a heat protectant when you do.

Q: What type of brush should I use when blow drying my hair?

A: A round brush is ideal for blow drying wavy frizzy hair, as it helps to smooth and straighten your locks.

Q: How can I avoid frizz when blow drying my hair?

A: To avoid frizz, make sure your hair is completely dry before you finish blow drying. Use a cool blast of air to set the style and avoid touching your hair too much once it's dry.

Hair Care

To keep your wavy frizzy hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help to prevent split ends and keep your hair healthy. In conclusion, blow drying wavy frizzy hair doesn't have to be a nightmare. With the right technique and a little bit of patience, you can achieve a sleek and shiny look. Remember to use a heat protectant, section your hair, and finish with a cool blast to prevent frizz. Happy styling!
