Curly Hairstyles For Square Faces Over 50 A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a square face and are over 50, you might be wondering what kind of hairstyle would suit you best. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural features and look years younger. In this article, we will guide you through the best curly hairstyles for square faces over 50, including how to achieve them, step by step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and forehead, and the length and width of the face are almost the same. Curly hairstyles can soften the features of a square face and make it look more youthful. As we age, our hair tends to lose volume and elasticity, which is why curly hairstyles are perfect for women over 50.

How To

To achieve curly hairstyles for square faces over 50, you will need to start with clean, dry hair. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves. You can also braid your hair and leave it overnight to achieve natural-looking curls. Use a styling product such as mousse or hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Step By Step

Step 1: Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Dry your hair with a towel and a hairdryer.

Step 3: Apply a styling product such as mousse or hairspray to your hair.

Step 4: Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves.

Step 5: Finish off with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair before curling.
  • Experiment with different curl sizes and directions to find the perfect curly hairstyle for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve curly hairstyles without using heat?

A: Yes, you can braid your hair and leave it overnight to achieve natural-looking curls.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, washing your hair every other day or every two days is recommended.

Q: How can I make my curls last longer?

A: Use a styling product such as mousse or hairspray to hold the curls in place. You can also wrap your hair in a silk scarf before going to bed to prevent frizz.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and beautiful curly hair, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals such as bleach and hair dyes.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect curly hairstyle for your square face over 50. Remember to experiment and have fun with different hairstyles until you find the one that suits you best.
