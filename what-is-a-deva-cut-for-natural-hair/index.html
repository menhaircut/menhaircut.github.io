What Is A Deva Cut For Natural Hair All You Need To Know

Written by Frank Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Deva Cut is a special hair cutting technique developed by Lorraine Massey, co-owner of Devachan Salon in New York City, designed specifically for curly and wavy hair textures. The technique is designed to enhance the natural curl pattern, create shape, and add definition to the hair. In this blog post, we will discuss everything you need to know about Deva Cut for natural hair, including how to do it, its benefits, and how to take care of your hair after the cut.

How to Do a Deva Cut on Natural Hair

The Deva Cut technique involves cutting the hair dry, rather than wet, to achieve the best results. This allows the stylist to see the natural curl pattern and cut the hair accordingly. Before the cut, your hair will be washed and styled using DevaCurl products, which are specially formulated for curly hair. Here are the steps involved in a Deva Cut:

Step 1: Consultation

The stylist will start by discussing your hair goals and preferences. They will examine your hair type, texture, and curl pattern to determine the best cut for your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Condition

Your hair will be washed and conditioned with DevaCurl products to remove any buildup and detangle the hair. The stylist will use their fingers to comb through your hair to avoid damaging or disrupting the curl pattern. After washing, the stylist will apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free during the cut.

Step 3: Cut

The stylist will cut your hair dry, section by section, using special techniques to enhance your natural curl pattern. They may use a combination of scissors and a razor to create shape and definition. The cut will be customized to your hair type and desired style.

Step 4: Style

After the cut, the stylist will style your hair using DevaCurl products, which are specifically designed to enhance your natural curl pattern. They may use a diffuser to dry your hair or encourage you to air dry your hair to avoid heat damage.

Benefits of Deva Cut for Natural Hair

Here are some of the benefits of Deva Cut for natural hair:

  • Enhances the natural curl pattern
  • Creates shape and definition
  • Reduces frizz
  • Customized to your hair type and style
  • Allows for easy maintenance

Tips for Maintaining Your Deva Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your Deva Cut:

  • Use DevaCurl products to wash and style your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce frizz
  • Avoid brushing your hair, use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb instead
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and health of your hair

FAQs About Deva Cut for Natural Hair

1. How often should I get a Deva Cut?

You should get a Deva Cut every 3-4 months to maintain the shape and health of your hair.

2. Can I do a Deva Cut on relaxed hair?

Deva Cut is designed for natural hair textures and may not work as well on relaxed hair. However, you can consult with a stylist to determine if a Deva Cut is right for you.

3. Can I do a Deva Cut on short hair?

Deva Cut can be done on hair of any length, but may not be as effective on very short hair.

Final Thoughts

Deva Cut is a great technique for enhancing the natural curl pattern of your hair and creating shape and definition. It is important to consult with a stylist who is experienced in this technique to achieve the best results. Remember to use DevaCurl products and follow the tips for maintaining your Deva Cut to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
