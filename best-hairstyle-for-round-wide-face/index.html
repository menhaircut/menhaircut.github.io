The Best Hairstyle For Round Wide Face A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Sep 15, 2023 · 3 min read

If you have a round wide face, you might find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that complements your face shape. But don't worry, because we've got you covered. In this article, we will share with you the best hairstyle for round wide face, along with tips, step-by-step guide, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round wide face typically has a wider forehead and cheekbones with a rounded chin. The best hairstyle for this face shape is one that adds length and angles to the face. This means hairstyles that have volume on top and are longer on the sides will make your face look more elongated and less round.

How to

The best hairstyle for round wide face is the long layered haircut. This hairstyle adds volume on top and elongates the face, creating the illusion of a slimmer face shape. To achieve this hairstyle, you can ask your hairdresser to cut your hair in long layers and add some face-framing layers to soften your features.

Step by step

Here are the step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the long layered haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume on top.
  3. Divide your hair into sections and start cutting your hair in long layers, starting from the back and moving towards the front.
  4. Add some face-framing layers to soften your features.
  5. Finish by styling your hair using a curling iron or straightener to add some texture and volume.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your long layered haircut:

  • Use a volumizing spray or mousse to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume on top.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What other hairstyles work well for round wide face?

Aside from the long layered haircut, other hairstyles that work well for round wide face include angled bob, side-swept bangs, and layered pixie cut.

What hair color works best for round wide face?

Any hair color can work well for round wide face, but it's best to avoid hair colors that are too dark or too light, as they can make your face look wider. Opt for hair colors that are in the medium range, such as caramel, honey, or chestnut brown.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise regularly to reduce stress, which can cause hair loss.

With these tips and the long layered haircut, you can achieve a beautiful hairstyle that complements your round wide face. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful.
