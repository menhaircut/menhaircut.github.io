What Swimsuit Flatters Your Body Type A Guide To Finding The Perfect Suit

Written by Eveline Sep 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Summer is just around the corner, and it’s time to start thinking about swimsuits. But with so many styles and options available, finding the perfect suit can be overwhelming. That’s why we’ve put together a guide to help you find the swimsuit that flatters your body type best. Read on for tips, tricks, and recommendations.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine Your Body Type
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Suit
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Suit
  • FAQ: Answering Your Swimsuit Questions
  • Hair Care Tips for the Beach

How to Determine Your Body Type

The first step in finding the perfect swimsuit is to determine your body type. There are four main body types:

  • Hourglass
  • Pear
  • Apple
  • Straight

To figure out which body type you are, stand in front of a mirror and take note of your measurements:

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your bust.
  • Waist: Measure around the smallest part of your waist.
  • Hips: Measure around the widest part of your hips.

Once you have your measurements, use the following formula to determine your body type:

  • Hourglass: Bust and hips are roughly the same size, with a smaller waist.
  • Pear: Hips are wider than bust.
  • Apple: Bust is wider than hips.
  • Straight: Bust, waist, and hips are all about the same size.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Suit

Now that you know your body type, it’s time to find the perfect swimsuit. Follow these steps:

  1. Identify your problem areas. What do you want to hide or accentuate?
  2. Choose a style that flatters your body type. See below for recommendations.
  3. Choose a color or pattern that flatters your skin tone.
  4. Try on multiple sizes to ensure a perfect fit. Don’t be afraid to try different brands or styles.
  5. Accessorize with cover-ups, hats, and sunglasses.

Hourglass

If you have an hourglass figure, you’re in luck – almost any swimsuit will flatter your body type. However, you may want to accentuate your curves with a high-waisted bikini or a one-piece with cutouts.

Pear

If you have a pear-shaped figure, you’ll want to draw attention to your upper body while minimizing your hips. Try a bikini top with ruffles or embellishments, and pair it with a high-waisted bottom. A one-piece swimsuit with a plunging neckline can also be flattering.

Apple

If you have an apple-shaped figure, you’ll want to draw attention away from your midsection while accentuating your legs. Look for swimsuits with ruching or draping around the waist, and choose a bottom with a higher cut. A one-piece swimsuit with a deep V-neckline can also be flattering.

Straight

If you have a straight figure, you’ll want to create the illusion of curves. Look for swimsuits with cutouts or ruffles to add dimension, and choose a bottom with a higher cut to lengthen your legs. A one-piece swimsuit with a belted waist can also be flattering.

Tips for Choosing the Right Suit

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a swimsuit:

  • Choose a suit that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
  • Consider the occasion – a family beach day may call for a more modest suit than a girls’ weekend in Miami.
  • Don’t be afraid to mix and match – a patterned top with a solid bottom can be a fun way to add some personality to your look.
  • Invest in quality – a well-made swimsuit will last longer and fit better than a cheap one.

FAQ: Answering Your Swimsuit Questions

Q: Can I wear a bikini if I’m not confident with my body?

A: Absolutely! There’s no reason you can’t wear a bikini if you don’t have a “perfect” body. The key is to find a style that makes you feel comfortable and confident. Consider a high-waisted bottom or a tankini top if you’re feeling self-conscious.

Q: What if I have a larger bust?

A: Look for swimsuits with thicker straps or underwire for added support. A halter top can also be flattering.

Q: What if I’m petite?

A: Look for swimsuits with less coverage, such as a bandeau bikini or a one-piece with a low back. These styles will elongate your frame.

Hair Care Tips for the Beach

Don’t forget about your hair when you’re at the beach! Here are some tips:

  • Apply a leave-in conditioner before you hit the sand.
  • Wear a hat to protect your hair from the sun.
  • Rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming in saltwater or chlorine.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair – embrace your natural beachy waves!

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be ready to hit the beach in style. Remember – the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in your swimsuit. Happy shopping!
