Is Red Hair In Fashion 2021

Written by Petter Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Red hair has always been a statement color, and it's no surprise that it's still in fashion in 2021. Many celebrities have been spotted with red hair, and it's a popular trend among fashion enthusiasts. In this article, we'll explore the question of whether red hair is still in fashion in 2021 and provide some tips on how to rock the trend.

How to Get Red Hair?

If you're looking to try out the red hair trend, there are a few things you should know beforehand. Firstly, it's important to choose the right shade of red that complements your skin tone. There are many shades of red, ranging from bright red to copper and auburn. Secondly, it's important to consult a professional hairdresser to ensure that the color is applied correctly and safely.

Step by Step Guide to Get Red Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect red hair:

  • Consult a professional hairdresser to help you choose the right shade of red.
  • Prepare your hair by washing it thoroughly and avoiding any products that may interfere with the color process.
  • The colorist will apply the dye to your hair, starting from the roots and working their way down to the tips.
  • After the dye has been applied, it will be left on your hair for a specific amount of time, depending on the shade of red you've chosen.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly and apply a conditioner or treatment to keep it healthy and shiny.

Tips for Maintaining Red Hair

Once you've achieved the perfect shade of red, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color and prevent it from fading. Here are some tips to help you maintain your red hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent the color from fading.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently as this can cause the color to fade faster.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight as this can cause the color to fade.
  • Use a color-depositing conditioner or treatment to refresh the color between salon visits.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently as this can damage your hair and cause the color to fade faster.

FAQ about Red Hair

Q. Is red hair difficult to maintain?

A. Red hair requires a bit of maintenance to keep the color looking vibrant and prevent it from fading. It's important to use the right products and avoid washing your hair too frequently or exposing it to direct sunlight.

Q. Who can rock red hair?

A. Red hair can look great on anyone, as long as the right shade is chosen to complement their skin tone. It's a bold and statement color that can add a pop of color to any look.

Q. Can I dye my hair red at home?

A. While it's possible to dye your hair red at home, it's recommended to consult a professional hairdresser to ensure that the color is applied correctly and safely.

Hair Care with Red Hair

Red hair requires a bit of extra care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for red hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, as this will prevent breakage and damage.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can cause the color to fade faster.
  • Apply a hair mask or treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or products that can damage your hair, such as bleach or hair dyes that contain ammonia.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and keep it looking healthy.

In conclusion, red hair is still in fashion in 2021 and it's a bold and statement color that can add a pop of color to any look. If you're thinking of trying out the trend, it's important to choose the right shade of red and consult a professional hairdresser to ensure that the color is applied correctly and safely. With the right care and maintenance, your red hair can look healthy and vibrant for months to come.
