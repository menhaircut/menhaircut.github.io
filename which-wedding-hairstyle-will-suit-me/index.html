Which Wedding Hairstyle Will Suit Me

Written by Eveline Aug 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're planning for your big day, there are a lot of things to take into consideration. One of the most important aspects is your wedding hairstyle. Your hairstyle can make or break your entire look, so it's essential to choose one that suits your face shape, dress style, and personal preference. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to choose the perfect wedding hairstyle for you.

Description

Choosing a wedding hairstyle can be overwhelming, especially if you don't have any idea of what you want. There are numerous options available, from classic updos to bohemian braids, and modern twists. The key is to find a style that complements your features and makes you feel confident and comfortable.

How to Choose the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle?

Here are some tips on how to choose the perfect wedding hairstyle for you:

Consider Your Dress Style

Your wedding dress style should be the primary consideration when choosing your hairstyle. If you have an intricate dress, you might want to choose a simple hairstyle that doesn't compete with the dress. If your dress has a lot of details at the back, you might want to consider an updo to showcase the dress.

Consider Your Face Shape

Your face shape should also be taken into account when choosing a wedding hairstyle. For example, if you have a round face, you might want to choose a hairstyle that adds height and elongates your face. If you have a heart-shaped face, you might want to consider a soft, romantic hairstyle that frames your face.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another factor to consider when choosing a wedding hairstyle. If you have fine hair, you might want to choose a style that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, you might want to consider an updo that showcases your hair's thickness.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to choose the perfect wedding hairstyle:

Step 1: Research

Start by researching different wedding hairstyles. Look through bridal magazines, Pinterest, and Instagram for inspiration. Save the styles you like and take note of the ones that you don't like.

Step 2: Consider Your Dress Style

Consider your dress style and the neckline. Choose a hairstyle that complements your dress and doesn't compete with it.

Step 3: Consider Your Face Shape

Consider your face shape when choosing a hairstyle. Choose a style that flatters your features and complements your face shape.

Step 4: Consider Your Hair Type

Consider your hair type when choosing a hairstyle. Choose a style that works with your hair type and texture.

Step 5: Book a Trial

Book a hair trial with your hairstylist. This will give you the opportunity to try out different styles and see which one suits you best.

Step 6: Make Final Decision

Once you've tried out different styles and found the one that suits you best, make the final decision.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Wedding Hairstyle

Here are some tips for choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle:

Tip 1: Start Early

Start planning your hairstyle early. This will give you enough time to research and find the perfect style.

Tip 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Consult with your hairstylist. They will be able to guide you on what styles will work best for your hair type and face shape.

Tip 3: Consider Your Comfort

Consider your comfort when choosing a hairstyle. You don't want to choose a hairstyle that will make you feel uncomfortable or self-conscious.

FAQ

What if I Can't Decide on a Hairstyle?

If you're having trouble deciding on a hairstyle, book a trial with your hairstylist. They will be able to give you advice and help you choose the perfect style.

Can I Wear My Hair Down for My Wedding?

Yes, you can wear your hair down for your wedding. Just make sure it complements your dress and face shape.

Can I Wear Hair Accessories with My Wedding Hairstyle?

Yes, you can wear hair accessories with your wedding hairstyle. Just make sure it complements your dress and hairstyle.

Hair Care

On your wedding day, you want your hair to look its best. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair before the big day:

Tip 1: Get Regular Haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and free from split ends.

Tip 2: Use Heat Protectant

Use a heat protectant when styling your hair to prevent damage.

Tip 3: Use Quality Hair Products

Use quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

Tip 4: Avoid Over-styling Your Hair

Avoid over-styling your hair, as this can cause damage and make it harder to style on your wedding day.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect wedding hairstyle can be overwhelming, but with these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find a style that suits you best. Remember to consider your dress style, face shape, and hair type when choosing a hairstyle. Book a hair trial with your hairstylist to try out different styles and make the final decision. Follow these hair care tips in the lead up to your wedding day to ensure your hair looks its best.
