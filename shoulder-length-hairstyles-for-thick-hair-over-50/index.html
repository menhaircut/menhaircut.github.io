Shoulder Length Hairstyles For Thick Hair Over 50

Written by Christine Jul 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 50 and have thick hair, you may be looking for a new hairstyle that's easy to manage and looks great. One option to consider is a shoulder length haircut, which can be flattering and stylish. In this article, we'll discuss some of the best shoulder length hairstyles for thick hair over 50, as well as tips for caring for your hair.

Description

Shoulder length hairstyles are versatile and can be customized to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. They're also practical for older women who want a low-maintenance haircut that doesn't require a lot of styling or upkeep. Thick hair can be a blessing and a curse, as it can be difficult to manage and style. However, with the right haircut and products, you can make the most of your thick locks.

How to

When choosing a shoulder length haircut for thick hair over 50, consider your face shape and hair texture. A layered cut with long face-framing layers can soften a square or angular face, while a blunt cut can add definition to a round face. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, a shoulder length cut can enhance your texture and create a flattering shape.

To style shoulder length hair, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to damp hair and blow dry with a round brush. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls, or leave it straight for a sleek look. You can also experiment with different partings and accessories, such as headbands or barrettes.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

2. Apply a volumizing product to your roots

3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape

4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add waves or curls

5. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, thick hair:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils

- Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair

- Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools

- Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy length

FAQ

Q: Will a shoulder length haircut make my face look older?

A: Not necessarily. A shoulder length haircut can be youthful and flattering, as long as it's tailored to your face shape and hair texture. A professional stylist can help you find the right haircut for your features.

Q: Is it okay to color my hair at 50?

A: Yes, it's perfectly fine to color your hair at any age. Just be sure to choose a color that complements your skin tone and doesn't require too much upkeep.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your hair, it's important to use products that are gentle and nourishing. Look for shampoos and conditioners that contain natural ingredients like argan oil, coconut oil, and shea butter. These ingredients can help to moisturize and strengthen your hair, leaving it soft and shiny.

In addition to using the right products, it's important to protect your hair from heat and environmental damage. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when you're out in the sun, and avoid using hot tools on a daily basis.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock a stylish shoulder length hairstyle well into your 50s and beyond.
