How To Go From Naturally Straight Hair To Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've always had naturally straight hair and want to switch things up, going curly can be a fun and exciting change. However, making this transition isn't always easy, and it can require a bit of work and patience. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps you need to take to go from naturally straight hair to curly, as well as provide some helpful tips and answers to frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Go From Naturally Straight Hair to Curly
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Transitioning to Curly Hair
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Go From Naturally Straight Hair to Curly

There are a few different methods you can use to go from naturally straight hair to curly, including:

  • Using heat tools, such as a curling iron or wand
  • Getting a perm
  • Using styling products, such as mousse or curl cream, to encourage natural curls

Each of these methods has its pros and cons, and the best option for you will depend on your hair type, lifestyle, and desired outcome.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're interested in using styling products to encourage natural curls, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can make it more difficult to encourage curls.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to rub it too vigorously. You want to avoid causing frizz.
  3. Apply a curl-defining product, such as mousse or curl cream, to your hair. Use a wide-toothed comb to distribute the product evenly.
  4. Scrunch your hair with your hands to encourage curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it.
  5. If your hair is still damp, you can let it air dry. Otherwise, use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to finish drying your hair.
  6. Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron or wand to enhance any curls that need a little extra help.

Tips for Transitioning to Curly Hair

Here are some helpful tips to keep in mind as you transition to curly hair:

  • Be patient. It can take time for your hair to adjust to its new texture, so don't expect to see perfect curls right away.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to trim your hair regularly. Curly hair can be prone to split ends, and regular trims can help keep it healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to help preserve your curls overnight.

FAQ

Here are some answers to frequently asked questions about transitioning to curly hair:

Can I transition to curly hair without using heat or chemicals?

Yes! You can use styling products, such as mousse or curl cream, to encourage natural curls without using heat or chemicals.

How do I prevent frizz?

To prevent frizz, avoid rubbing your hair with a towel and use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while scrunching it. You can also use a frizz-fighting serum or oil on your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair as often as you feel is necessary. However, keep in mind that curly hair can be prone to dryness, so you may want to avoid washing it too frequently.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Once you've successfully transitioned to curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or detangler to your hair before combing it.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat tools too frequently, as this can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can successfully transition from naturally straight hair to curly and enjoy all the benefits that come with this fun and playful hair texture.
