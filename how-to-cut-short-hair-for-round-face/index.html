How To Cut Short Hair For Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Oct 04, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round face, you might feel that short hair isn't for you. But, with the right cut and style, short hair can make your face look slimmer and more defined. In this article, we'll be discussing how to cut short hair for round faces, including how to choose the right cut, step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care.

Table of Content

  • How to Choose the Right Cut
  • Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Choose the Right Cut

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, it's important to consider the length, layers, and texture. The goal is to create angles that elongate the face and slim down the cheeks. Here are a few tips on how to choose the right cut:

  • Length: Go for a cut that falls just above or below the chin. This will help to create the illusion of a longer face.
  • Layers: Opt for layers that start at the cheekbones and frame the face. This will create angles and slim down the cheeks.
  • Texture: Wispy, textured styles work well for round faces as they add movement and dimension.

Step-by-Step Instructions

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair short, here's how to do it:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  3. Start with the back section and use a clip to secure the rest of the hair out of the way.
  4. Use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Remember to cut in a straight line.
  5. Move on to the side sections, cutting them slightly shorter than the back.
  6. Finally, move on to the top section and cut it slightly shorter than the sides.
  7. Once you've finished cutting, use a comb to blend the layers together.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when cutting short hair for a round face:

  • Don't go too short: Avoid cuts that are above the chin, as they can make the face look wider.
  • Add volume: Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to the crown of your head.
  • Experiment with bangs: Side-swept bangs or textured bangs can help to balance out a round face.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will a pixie cut work for a round face?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can work for a round face. Opt for a cut with textured layers and volume at the crown of the head.

Q: Should I go for a straight or curly style?

A: Either can work, but avoid styles that add too much volume to the sides of the face.

Hair Care

To keep your short hair looking great, be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

In conclusion, cutting short hair for a round face can be a bit intimidating, but with the right cut and style, it can be incredibly flattering. Follow these tips and tricks, and you'll be rocking a fabulous new look in no time.
