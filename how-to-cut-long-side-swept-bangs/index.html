How To Cut Long Side Swept Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Oct 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering updating your hairstyle with long side swept bangs? This trendy look can add a touch of glamour and sophistication to any hair length or texture. However, before you grab a pair of scissors and start snipping away, it's essential to learn how to cut long side swept bangs correctly.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cut Long Side Swept Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs

How to Cut Long Side Swept Bangs

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let's go over some essential tools you'll need:

  • Sharp hairdressing scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle filled with water

It's also a good idea to have a reference photo of your desired bangs style. This will help you visualize the length and angle of your cut more easily.

Step-by-Step Guide:

Now that you have your tools and reference photo, let's get started:

  1. Begin by dividing your hair into sections. Clip the rest of your hair back, leaving only the front section loose. This will be the section you'll cut your bangs from.
  2. Use your comb to separate the front section into a triangle shape. The point of the triangle should be at the crown of your head.
  3. Wet your bangs with the spray bottle to make them easier to cut. Comb them straight down and hold them between your fingers at the desired length. Remember, you can always trim more later if needed, so it's best to start longer than you want.
  4. Using your hairdressing scissors, cut your bangs in a straight line. Be sure to cut them slightly longer on the sides than in the middle, as this will create the side swept effect.
  5. Release the triangle section and check your work. If your bangs are too long, trim them slightly until you achieve your desired length.
  6. Once you're happy with the length, it's time to create the side swept effect. Comb your bangs to one side and hold them between your fingers at an angle. Use your scissors to cut into the ends at a slight angle, creating a soft, feathery effect.
  7. Repeat the process on the other side, combing your bangs to the opposite direction and cutting into the ends at an angle.
  8. Take a step back and check your work in the mirror. If you notice any uneven spots or longer pieces, trim them as needed.
  9. Once you're satisfied with your bangs, style the rest of your hair as desired.
  10. Enjoy your new, trendy look!

Tips for Perfect Bangs:

Cutting your own bangs can be intimidating, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve salon-worthy results. Here are some tips to help you get perfect side swept bangs:

  • Start with longer bangs than you want to avoid cutting them too short.
  • Cut your bangs dry, as wet hair can shrink and look shorter when it dries.
  • Use hairdressing scissors and avoid using regular household scissors, which can damage your hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time, checking your work as you go.
  • Use a mirror to check your work from different angles.

FAQs:

Here are some commonly asked questions about cutting long side swept bangs:

1. Can I cut my bangs with regular scissors?

No, it's best to use hairdressing scissors to avoid damaging your hair and achieving a clean cut.

2. How can I make my bangs look fuller?

You can blow-dry your bangs with a round brush, or use a volumizing product like mousse or dry shampoo.

3. How often should I trim my bangs?

It's best to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care for Side Swept Bangs:

To keep your side swept bangs looking their best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your bangs regularly to keep them clean and free of oil.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as they can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to create volume and a smooth finish.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can transfer oil from your hands and make them look greasy.

Cutting long side swept bangs can be a fun and exciting way to update your look. With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect style for your face shape and hair texture. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll have beautiful, trendy bangs in no time!
