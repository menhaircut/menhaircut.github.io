Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair Round Face

Written by Christine Sep 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you having trouble finding the perfect hairstyle for your shoulder-length hair and round face? Look no further! In this article, we'll provide you with tips and tricks on how to style your hair to flatter your face shape.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Style Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Shoulder-length hair is perfect for those who want a versatile look that's easy to maintain. However, if you have a round face, it's important to choose a hairstyle that will accentuate your features and minimize the roundness of your face.

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider the length, layers, and volume. Shoulder-length hair with layers can help elongate your face and give the appearance of a slimmer jawline. Adding volume to the crown of your head can also help balance out your face and minimize the roundness.

How to Style Your Hair

There are many ways to style shoulder-length hair for a round face, including:

  • Long Layers
  • Side-Swept Bangs
  • Beach Waves
  • Straight and Sleek
  • Half-Up Half-Down

Long Layers

Long layers are perfect for shoulder-length hair because they help to create movement and texture, which can help elongate your face. This style is also low maintenance and easy to maintain. To create long layers, ask your hairstylist to cut your hair at an angle, starting from your shoulders and ending at your chin.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to create a slimmer appearance of your face. This style is perfect for those who don't want to commit to a full fringe. To create side-swept bangs, part your hair on one side and sweep the bangs to the opposite side of your face.

Beach Waves

Beach waves are a popular hairstyle that can work well for a round face. This style is perfect for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair. To create beach waves, use a curling iron or a wand to curl your hair, then brush through the curls with your fingers to create a natural, tousled look.

Straight and Sleek

Straight and sleek hair can help to elongate your face and give the illusion of a slimmer jawline. This style is perfect for those who want a simple and classic look. To create a straight and sleek look, use a flat iron to straighten your hair and apply a smoothing serum to tame any frizz.

Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down style is perfect for those who want a versatile look that can work for any occasion. This style is perfect for shoulder-length hair because it can help to create volume and texture at the crown of your head. To create a half-up half-down style, simply pull the top half of your hair back and secure it with a clip or elastic.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create a simple and easy hairstyle for shoulder-length hair and a round face:

  1. Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse to your roots.
  2. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume at the crown of your head.
  3. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or a wand to create loose waves.
  4. Brush through your curls with your fingers to create a natural, tousled look.
  5. Spray a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you style your shoulder-length hair for a round face:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to add volume to the crown of your head.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face appear rounder.
  • Add layers to your hair to create movement and texture.
  • Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect one for you.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume at the sides of your face, such as blunt bangs or a bob that ends at your chin. These styles can make your face appear rounder.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

Yes! A ponytail can be a great hairstyle for a round face. To elongate your face, pull your hair back tightly and add volume at the crown of your head.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated to promote healthy hair growth.

Now that you have some ideas for hairstyles for shoulder-length hair and a round face, it's time to experiment and find the perfect style for you. Remember to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style when choosing a hairstyle. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can have a flattering and effortless look that will make you feel confident and beautiful.
