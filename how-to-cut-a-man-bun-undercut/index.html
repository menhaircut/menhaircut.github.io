How To Cut A Man Bun Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that’s both trendy and versatile? The man bun undercut might be the perfect option for you! This hairstyle combines a classic man bun with an undercut, creating a cool and edgy look. But how do you cut a man bun undercut? In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this style.

Description

The man bun undercut is a hairstyle where the hair on top is tied in a bun while the sides and back are shaved or buzzed. This creates a contrast between the long hair on top and the short hair on the sides, making the bun stand out even more. The man bun undercut has become increasingly popular in recent years, especially among men who want a stylish and low-maintenance hairstyle.

How to Cut a Man Bun Undercut

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need clippers, scissors, a comb, and some hair ties. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will make it easier to cut and style. Then, dry your hair with a towel or a hair dryer.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

Decide on the length you want for the top and the sides. Use the clippers to buzz the sides and back, starting with a higher guard and working your way down until you reach the desired length. Leave the hair on top long enough to pull into a bun.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Use the scissors to cut the hair on top to the desired length. You can follow the natural shape of your head or create layers for more texture. Make sure the hair on top is long enough to tie into a bun.

Step 4: Blend the Sides and Top

Blend the hair on the sides and top using the clippers and scissors. This will create a seamless transition between the long hair on top and the short hair on the sides.

Step 5: Style the Bun

Once you’ve cut your hair, it’s time to style the bun. Use a hair tie to gather the hair on top and tie it into a bun. You can place the bun on the crown of your head or towards the back.

Tips

Here are some tips to make sure you get the best results:

  • Start with a longer guard on the clippers and work your way down gradually.
  • Use a comb to help you section the hair and create a clean line between the top and the sides.
  • Don’t cut too much hair at once. It’s better to take small sections and work slowly.
  • Make sure you have good lighting and a mirror to see all angles of your head.
  • Experiment with different lengths and styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the man bun undercut:

Q: Can I still get a man bun undercut if I have thin hair?

A: Yes, you can still get a man bun undercut if you have thin hair. You might want to keep the hair on top shorter and create more layers to add volume.

Q: How often do I need to get a haircut to maintain the man bun undercut?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how often you want to trim the sides. You’ll need to trim the sides every few weeks to keep the undercut looking sharp.

Q: Can I style the bun in different ways?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as a messy bun or a braided bun. You can also add accessories like a headband or a scarf to add more personality to your look.

Hair Care

To keep your man bun undercut looking its best, make sure you take good care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that’s suitable for your hair type, and avoid using too much heat when styling. You can also use hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.

Now that you know how to cut a man bun undercut, it’s time to try it out for yourself! With the right tools and techniques, you can create a stylish and versatile hairstyle that suits your personality and lifestyle.
