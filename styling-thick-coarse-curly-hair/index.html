Styling Thick Coarse Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Sep 03, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have thick coarse curly hair, you know that it can be a challenge to style. But with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a beautiful and manageable hairstyle. In this article, we'll share our best tips and tricks for styling thick coarse curly hair.

Table of Content

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Style
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ

Hair Care

The first step to styling thick coarse curly hair is proper hair care. This means using the right products and techniques to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, and always use a heat protectant spray if you must use them.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and frizz-free.

By following these hair care tips, you'll be able to start with a healthy and hydrated base for styling your thick coarse curly hair.

How to Style

Now that you've got your hair care routine down, it's time to start styling your hair. Here's how:

  • Start by applying a styling product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  • Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling thick coarse curly hair. You don't want to weigh down your curls with too much product or heat styling.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your thick coarse curly hair:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with sulfate-free products.
  2. Apply a curl cream or gel to your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or hair oil to keep your hair in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling thick coarse curly hair:

  • Experiment with different products to find the ones that work best for your hair.
  • Try air-drying your hair instead of using a diffuser attachment for a more natural look.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush, which can cause breakage.
  • Consider getting a haircut that's specifically designed for curly hair to make styling easier.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling thick coarse curly hair:

Q. Can I straighten my thick coarse curly hair?

A. Yes, you can straighten your hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray and avoid doing it too often to prevent damage.

Q. How often should I wash my thick coarse curly hair?

A. It's best to wash your hair once or twice a week to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Q. Can I use a regular shampoo and conditioner on my thick coarse curly hair?

A. It's best to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to style your thick coarse curly hair with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair with a proper hair care routine, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you.
