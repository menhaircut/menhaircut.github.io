How To Cut A Man s Flat Top Haircut Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to give your man a fresh new look? A flat top haircut might be just the style you need. In this step by step guide, we’ll show you how to cut a man’s flat top haircut from start to finish. Here’s what you’ll need:

Tools Required

  • Clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Clipper guards
  • Hair spray or gel

How to Cut a Man's Flat Top Haircut

Step 1: Clean the Hair

Start by wetting the hair and combing it thoroughly to get rid of any tangles. Make sure the hair is clean and dry before you begin cutting.

Step 2: Choose the Length

Decide on the length of the flat top haircut you want to achieve. Use the clipper guards to adjust the clipper's length setting based on your desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you achieve your desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Start by cutting the sides with a clipper, making sure to keep the clippers parallel to the head. Cut upwards, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Use a shorter guard for the lower part of the sides and a longer guard for the top part of the sides.

Step 4: Blend the Sides

Blend the sides by using the clippers without a guard to blend the sides into the top. Use the clipper in a flicking motion to create a smooth blend on both sides.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Cut the top of the hair with scissors. Start at the front and work your way to the back, using the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. The top should be flat and level, with a slight slope towards the front.

Step 6: Blend the Top

Blend the top by using scissors to cut horizontally across the top of the hair. This will help to create a smooth blend between the top and the sides.

Step 7: Style the Hair

Use hair spray or gel to style the hair into the desired shape. The flat top can be styled with a slight spiky texture or a more natural look. Use your fingers to create the desired shape and style.

Tips for Cutting a Man's Flat Top Haircut

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to a shorter one to achieve the desired length.
  • Use scissors to blend the top and the sides for a smoother look.
  • Use hair spray or gel to style the hair into the desired shape.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting a flat top requires precision and attention to detail.

FAQ

What is a flat top haircut?

A flat top haircut is a style where the hair on the top of the head is cut flat and level, giving the appearance of a flat surface.

What tools do I need to cut a flat top haircut?

You will need clippers, scissors, a comb, clipper guards, and hair spray or gel.

How do I choose the length for a flat top haircut?

Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to a shorter one until you achieve the desired length.

Hair Care Tips

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to give your man a fresh new look with a flat top haircut. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
