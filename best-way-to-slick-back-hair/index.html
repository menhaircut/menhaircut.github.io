Best Way To Slick Back Hair A Guide For Men

Written by William Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Slicking back hair is a classic hairstyle that has been around for generations. It’s a timeless look that’s perfect for men who want to exude sophistication, elegance, and power. In this guide, we’ll be discussing the best way to slick back hair, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQ, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Back Hair
  • Step by Step Instructions
  • Tips for Slicking Back Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

How to Slick Back Hair

Before we dive into the steps, let’s first discuss the materials you’ll need. To slick back your hair, you’ll need a comb, hair gel or pomade, and hairspray.

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

It’s important to start with clean hair, as oil and dirt can prevent your hair from staying in place. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to ensure your hair is clean and healthy.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel or Pomade

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the tips. Make sure to evenly distribute the product throughout your hair.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Using a comb, comb your hair back, starting from the hairline and working your way to the back of your head. Make sure to smooth out any bumps or lumps as you go.

Step 4: Set the Hair

Once you’ve combed your hair back, use a hairspray to set it in place. Hold the hairspray about 6 inches away from your head and spray lightly. Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked back hair:

  • Use a high-quality hair gel or pomade that suits your hair type
  • Experiment with different comb styles to find the one that works best for you
  • Don’t use too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause it to lose its shape
  • If you have thinning hair, avoid slicking it back too tightly, as this can make your hairline look more pronounced

FAQs

Q: Can I slick back my hair if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can slick back curly hair. However, you may need to use a stronger hair gel or pomade to ensure your curls stay in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back?

A: It’s important to wash your hair regularly to avoid product buildup. Aim to wash your hair every other day to keep it clean and healthy.

Q: Can I slick back my hair if it’s short?

A: Yes, you can slick back short hair. However, you may need to use less product to avoid weighing your hair down.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it’s important to take good care of it. Here are some tips to help you maintain your slicked back hair:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause it to lose its shape
  • If you have thinning hair, avoid slicking it back too tightly, as this can make your hairline look more pronounced
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage

With these tips and steps, you’re well on your way to achieving the perfect slicked back hair. Remember to experiment and find the style that works best for you.
