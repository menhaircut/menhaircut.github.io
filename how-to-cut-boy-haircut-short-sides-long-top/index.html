How To Cut Boy Haircut Short Sides Long Top In Relaxed English Language

If you want to give your boy a stylish and trendy haircut, then a short sides long top haircut is perfect. This style is easy to maintain and versatile enough to be styled in various ways. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to cut boy haircut short sides long top in relaxed English language. So, let's get started!

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Description

A short sides long top haircut is a popular hairstyle among boys. It features short hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This style is versatile and can be styled in various ways, such as a slick back, messy, or textured. This haircut is suitable for boys of all ages and can be customized to suit their face shape and hair type.

How to

To cut a boy's short sides long top haircut, you will need a few tools, such as a pair of scissors, clippers, and a comb. Here are the basic steps to follow:

Step by Step

  1. Start by washing and drying the hair. This will make it easier to cut and style.
  2. Use the clippers to trim the sides of the hair. Start with a longer guard size and gradually go shorter until you achieve the desired length.
  3. Blend the sides into the top using scissors. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it in a straight line.
  4. Trim the top of the hair to the desired length using scissors. You can leave it longer or shorter, depending on the style you want.
  5. Use the scissors to add texture to the hair by point cutting or using a texturizing technique.
  6. Style the hair using a product of your choice. You can use a pomade, wax, or gel to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut a boy's short sides long top haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard size on the clippers and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair when cutting to ensure a straight line.
  • Consider the boy's face shape and hair type when cutting the hair.
  • Use a texturizing technique to add texture and movement to the hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to achieve the desired look.

FAQ

1. How often should I cut my boy's short sides long top haircut?

It depends on how fast their hair grows and how quickly it loses its shape. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-8 weeks to maintain the style.

2. Can a short sides long top haircut be styled in different ways?

Yes, this style is versatile and can be styled in various ways, such as a slick back, messy, or textured. It's up to the boy and their personal preference.

3. Is a short sides long top haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this style can be customized to suit different face shapes by adjusting the length and texture of the hair.

Hair Care

To maintain a boy's short sides long top haircut, it's important to take care of the hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash the hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and style.

Now that you know how to cut boy haircut short sides long top in relaxed English language, you can give your boy a stylish and trendy haircut. Remember to consider their face shape and hair type when cutting the hair, and experiment with different styling products to achieve the desired look. Happy cutting!
