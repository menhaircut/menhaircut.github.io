How To Get A Diamond Face Shape Naturally Tips And Tricks

Written by William Nov 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for ways to enhance your facial features, you might want to consider getting a diamond face shape. This facial shape is characterized by a narrower forehead, wider cheekbones, and a pointed chin. It's a popular face shape among celebrities like Rihanna, Angelina Jolie, and Scarlett Johansson.

In this post, we'll show you how to get a diamond face shape naturally through a combination of contouring techniques, facial exercises, and hair styling tips. Here's what you need to know:

How to Get a Diamond Face Shape Naturally

Step 1: Identify Your Face Shape

Before you start trying to change your face shape, it's important to know what your current face shape is. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and triangle. You can use a mirror or take a selfie to help you determine your face shape.

Step 2: Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker and lighter shades to create the illusion of different facial shapes. To create a diamond shape, you'll want to focus on your cheekbones and jawline. Use a contouring powder or cream that's a few shades darker than your skin tone to create shadows under your cheekbones and along your jawline.

Step 3: Do Facial Exercises

Facial exercises can help tone and define your facial muscles, which can help create a more defined diamond shape. Some exercises to try include cheek lifts, jawline exercises, and tongue twisters.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Your hairstyle can also help enhance your facial features. For a diamond face shape, you'll want to focus on adding volume to the top of your head to balance out your wider cheekbones. Try a side-swept bang or a layered bob to create the illusion of more volume and height.

Tips for Getting a Diamond Face Shape Naturally

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve a diamond face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheeks
  • Use a highlighter on your cheekbones to make them stand out
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your skin looking its best
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as these can damage your skin and lead to premature aging

FAQs About Getting a Diamond Face Shape Naturally

Can I get a diamond face shape without makeup?

Yes, you can get a diamond face shape naturally through a combination of contouring techniques, facial exercises, and hair styling tips.

How long does it take to get a diamond face shape?

It depends on your starting facial shape and the techniques you use. Consistency is key, so make sure to stick to your contouring, exercise, and hair styling routine for best results.

Can I get a diamond face shape if I have a round face?

Yes, with the right techniques and patience, you can enhance your facial features and create a more defined diamond shape.

Hair Care for Diamond Face Shapes

When it comes to hair care for diamond face shapes, it's important to focus on adding volume to the top of your head. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and try blow-drying your hair upside down to create more volume. Avoid hairstyles that add volume to your cheeks, as this can make your face appear more round or square.

Overall, getting a diamond face shape naturally is a combination of techniques and tips that enhance your facial features. By focusing on contouring, facial exercises, hair styling, and overall health and wellness, you can achieve a more defined and balanced facial shape.
