How To Use A Volumizer Hair Dryer For Perfectly Volumized Hair

Written by Eveline Oct 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a quick and easy way to add volume to your hair, a volumizer hair dryer is the perfect tool for the job. With its unique design, this type of hair dryer is specifically designed to add volume and body to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker. In this article, we'll show you how to use a volumizer hair dryer to achieve the perfect voluminous hairdo.

Description

A volumizer hair dryer is a type of hair dryer that is specifically designed to add volume and body to your hair. It has a unique design that includes a round brush attached to the dryer, allowing you to dry and style your hair at the same time. This type of hair dryer is perfect for those who want to add volume to their hair without having to spend a lot of time styling it.

How to Use

Using a volumizer hair dryer is actually quite simple. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step in using a volumizer hair dryer is to wash your hair. Use your regular shampoo and conditioner, and make sure to remove all excess water from your hair before you start drying it.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant

Before you start using the volumizer hair dryer, it's important to apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage and keep it looking healthy and shiny.

Step 3: Section your hair

Next, you'll want to section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to dry and style your hair with the volumizer hair dryer. Use clips to keep the sections in place.

Step 4: Start drying and styling

Now it's time to start drying and styling your hair with the volumizer hair dryer. Take one section of hair and place the round brush at the root of your hair. Use the hair dryer to blow dry your hair, moving the brush from the root to the tip of your hair. Repeat this process for each section of hair, working your way around your head.

Step 5: Finish with a blast of cool air

Once you've dried and styled your hair with the volumizer hair dryer, finish with a blast of cool air. This will help to set the style and give your hair a shiny, polished finish.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect voluminous hairdo:

- Use a volumizing mousse or spray before using the volumizer hair dryer to enhance the volumizing effect.

- Don't forget to apply a heat protectant to your hair before using the volumizer hair dryer to prevent heat damage.

- Use the low heat setting on the hair dryer to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about using a volumizer hair dryer:

Q: Can I use a volumizer hair dryer on wet hair?

A: Yes, you can use a volumizer hair dryer on wet hair. However, it's important to remove as much excess water from your hair as possible before using the dryer.

Q: Can I use a volumizer hair dryer on all hair types?

A: Yes, you can use a volumizer hair dryer on all hair types. However, if you have very thick or curly hair, you may need to use a straightener or other styling tool to achieve the desired look.

Hair Care

Using a volumizer hair dryer can be a great way to add volume to your hair, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are a few tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair.

- Avoid using too much heat on your hair by using the low heat setting on your hair dryer.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips and using a volumizer hair dryer, you can achieve the perfect voluminous hairdo in no time.
