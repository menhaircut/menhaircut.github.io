Should I Get A Bald Fade

Written by Daniel Jun 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are considering getting a new haircut, you may be wondering if a bald fade is the right choice for you. A bald fade is a popular haircut that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back of the head while leaving the hair longer on top. This creates a fade effect that blends the hair smoothly from shortest to longest. In this article, we will discuss the pros and cons of getting a bald fade and provide you with some tips on how to take care of your hair after the haircut.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get a Bald Fade
  • Step-by-Step Guide

Description

A bald fade is a stylish haircut that is suitable for men of all ages and hair types. It is a versatile haircut that can be worn in different ways depending on your personal style and preference. It is characterized by a gradual tapering of the hair from short to long, which creates a fade effect. The hair on the sides and back of the head is cut very short or shaved off completely, while the hair on top is left longer. This creates a contrast between the short and long hair, which gives the haircut its distinctive look.

How to Get a Bald Fade

Getting a bald fade requires some skill and expertise, so it is important to choose a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with this type of haircut. The first step is to decide on the length and style of your hair. You can choose to have your hair cut short, medium or long, depending on your preference. You should also decide on the type of fade you want, whether it is a low fade or high fade. A low fade is where the hair is tapered down to the skin around the ears and neckline, while a high fade is where the hair is tapered down to the scalp above the temples.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get a bald fade:

  1. Choose a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with this type of haircut.
  2. Decide on the length and style of your hair.
  3. Decide on the type of fade you want.
  4. Wash your hair thoroughly before your appointment.
  5. Sit in the barber's chair and let them know what you want.
  6. The barber will start by cutting your hair short on the sides and back.
  7. They will then use clippers to create a fade effect, gradually tapering the hair from short to long.
  8. The hair on top of your head will be left longer, which you can style as desired.
  9. The barber will finish by trimming any stray hairs and shaping your hairline.
  10. Take care of your hair after the haircut by washing and conditioning it regularly.

Tips for Maintaining Your Bald Fade

Here are some tips on how to maintain your bald fade:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.
  • Experiment with different styles and products to find the one that suits you best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a bald fade:

What is the difference between a low fade and a high fade?

A low fade is where the hair is tapered down to the skin around the ears and neckline, while a high fade is where the hair is tapered down to the scalp above the temples.

Is a bald fade suitable for all hair types?

Yes, a bald fade is suitable for all hair types, but it may look different depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

How often should I get a bald fade?

You should get a bald fade every 2-4 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows and how tidy you want your haircut to look.

Hair Care After a Bald Fade

After getting a bald fade, it is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair after a bald fade:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.
  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat or sunlight, as this can damage it.
  • Visit your barber regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh and tidy.

In conclusion, a bald fade is a stylish and versatile haircut that is suitable for men of all ages and hair types. It is important to choose a skilled barber or hairstylist who has experience with this type of haircut and to take care of your hair after the haircut. By following these tips, you can keep your bald fade looking fresh and stylish for weeks to come.
