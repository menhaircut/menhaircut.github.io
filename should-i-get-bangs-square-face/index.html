Should I Get Bangs For My Square Face

Written by Petter Oct 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but unsure if they will suit your square face shape? This article will guide you through the decision-making process and provide tips on how to style and care for your new bangs.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Bangs can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some personality to your look. However, when it comes to square face shapes, it's important to consider whether bangs will flatter your features or accentuate the angles of your face.

Typically, square face shapes have a strong jawline and forehead, with similar measurements across the face. The goal with bangs is to soften and balance out these features, creating a more oval-shaped appearance.

How To

The first step in determining whether bangs are right for your square face is to consult with a hairstylist. They can assess your face shape and recommend a suitable bang style.

When it comes to square faces, the best bangs are typically side-swept or asymmetrical, as they create a diagonal line that softens the angles of the face. Avoid blunt, straight-across bangs, as these can accentuate the squareness of the face.

Step-by-Step

If you've decided to go for bangs, here's how to style them:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Section off the hair you want to use for your bangs.
  3. Using a fine-tooth comb, comb the hair forward and hold it at the desired length.
  4. Trim the hair in small increments, checking the length after each cut.
  5. Style your bangs using a round brush and a hairdryer, sweeping them to the side or creating a soft, asymmetrical look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling bangs on a square face:

  • Use a lightweight styling product to keep your bangs in place.
  • Avoid heavy, volumizing products that can make your hair appear thicker and wider, further accentuating the squareness of your face.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair, as this can help soften the angles of your face and create a more flattering look overall.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting bangs for a square face:

Will bangs make my face look wider?

If you choose the right bang style, they can actually make your face appear narrower. Asymmetrical or side-swept bangs create a diagonal line that softens the angles of the face, while blunt, straight-across bangs can accentuate the squareness of the face.

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

Depending on the length and style of your bangs, you may need to trim them every few weeks to maintain the desired length and shape.

Hair Care

Regular hair care is essential for keeping your bangs looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly, using a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your bangs too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • If you have oily skin or sweat a lot, use a dry shampoo to keep your bangs looking fresh between washes.

With these tips and guidelines, you can confidently decide whether bangs are right for your square face shape and rock your new hairstyle with ease!
