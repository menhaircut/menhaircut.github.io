How To Cover Bald Edges With Weave A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling with bald edges but still want to rock a flawless hairstyle? Worry not, because there's a solution: weave! In this article, we'll show you how to cover bald edges with weave in easy steps. We'll also give you tips on how to maintain your hair and answer some frequently asked questions about weave. So, let's get started!

How to Cover Bald Edges with Weave: Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start, make sure your hair is clean and moisturized. Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your scalp and hair to keep it hydrated. Comb your hair and detangle it to make sure there are no knots or tangles.

Step 2: Choose Your Weave

Choose the right type of weave for your hair. If you have thin hair, go for a lightweight weave to avoid putting too much tension on your edges. If you have thick hair, you can choose a heavier weave. You can also choose between synthetic and human hair. Human hair is the better option, as it looks more natural and lasts longer.

Step 3: Braid Your Hair

Start by braiding your hair. You can either do a full head weave or a partial weave. For a full head weave, braid your hair in cornrows. For a partial weave, braid only the area where you want to cover your bald edges. Make sure the braids are tight enough to hold the weave in place, but not too tight to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 4: Sew the Weave

Take your weave and sew it onto your braids. Start at the back and work your way to the front. Make sure the weave covers your bald edges. Use hairpins to secure the weave in place as you go along.

Step 5: Cut and Style the Weave

Once you've sewn the weave in place, cut it to your desired length and style it as you wish. You can curl it, straighten it or leave it as it is.

Tips for Maintaining Your Weave

Here are some tips to help you maintain your weave:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and fresh
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging the weave
  • Avoid using too much heat on your weave to prevent it from drying out
  • Use a silk scarf or bonnet to protect your weave while you sleep
  • Avoid swimming or getting your weave wet

FAQs About Weave

Can a weave damage my hair?

A weave can damage your hair if it's done improperly or if it's worn for too long. Make sure you choose the right type of weave for your hair and don't wear it for more than six weeks.

How long does a weave last?

A weave can last up to six weeks if it's maintained properly. However, it's best to remove it after four weeks to avoid damaging your hair.

Can I wash my hair with a weave?

Yes, you can wash your hair with a weave. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner and avoid getting the weave too wet.

Can I style my weave with heat?

Yes, you can style your weave with heat, but it's best to avoid using too much heat to prevent it from drying out or becoming damaged.

Can I swim with a weave?

Avoid swimming or getting your weave wet, as it can damage the weave and your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Hair

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and fresh
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to avoid damaging your hair
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent it from drying out
  • Use a silk scarf or bonnet to protect your hair while you sleep
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote healthy hair growth

Now that you know how to cover your bald edges with weave, you can confidently rock any hairstyle you want. Just remember to take care of your hair and weave to keep it looking flawless. Happy styling!
