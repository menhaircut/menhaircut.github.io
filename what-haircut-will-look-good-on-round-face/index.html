What Haircut Will Look Good On Round Face A Comprehensive Guide In 2023

Written by Petter Sep 29, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle that doesn't flatter your round face? You're not alone! Round faces are characterized by fuller cheeks and a softer, circular shape. The right haircut can help elongate your face, highlight your best features, and create a more balanced look. In this blog post, we'll explore different haircuts that will look good on round faces, how to style them, and how to take care of your hair. Let's get started!

Description

Round faces have a circular shape with fuller cheeks and a softer jawline. The goal of a good haircut is to elongate your face, create more angles, and highlight your best features. A round face often benefits from haircuts that add height and volume to the crown, create angles around the face, and add length to the neck. The right haircut can create a more balanced and flattering look.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Round Face

When choosing a haircut for round faces, consider the following factors:

  • The length of your hair
  • The shape of your face
  • Your hair texture and thickness
  • Your personal style and preferences

Here are some haircuts that look good on round faces:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can help elongate your face and create more movement and texture in your hair. Ask your stylist to add layers that start below the chin and angle downwards towards the ends. This will add length to your face and create more volume and movement in your hair.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create angles around your face and draw attention to your eyes. Ask your stylist to cut your bangs at an angle and sweep them to the side. This will create a more flattering and balanced look.

3. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold and flattering choice for round faces. A short cut can help create more angles and elongate your face. Ask your stylist to add volume at the crown and keep the sides and back short.

4. Bob Cut

A bob cut can be a versatile and easy-to-style option for round faces. Ask your stylist to add layers and angles to create more movement and texture. A longer bob that falls just below the chin can help elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Your Haircut

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your round face, it's important to know how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.

Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and pat it dry with a towel. Avoid rubbing your hair with the towel, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

2. Apply a heat protectant.

Before using any heat styling tools, apply a heat protectant to your hair. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair healthy.

3. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair.

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. This will add volume and movement to your hair.

4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair.

Depending on the look you want to achieve, use a flat iron or curling iron to style your hair. For a sleek and straight look, use a flat iron. For more volume and curls, use a curling iron.

5. Finish with a hairspray or styling product.

To keep your hairstyle in place, finish with a hairspray or styling product. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and texture.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.

FAQ

1. What haircut looks best on a round face?

A haircut that adds height and volume at the crown, creates angles around the face, and adds length to the neck can look good on a round face. Long layers, side-swept bangs, pixie cuts, and bob cuts are some examples of haircuts that flatter round faces.

2. Can I still wear my hair short if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Short haircuts can be a flattering and bold choice for round faces. Just make sure to choose a style that adds height and volume at the crown and creates angles around the face.

Hair Care Routine for Round Face

Here's a hair care routine for round faces:

1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that works well with your hair type and texture. Avoid using harsh products that can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

To keep your hair moisturized and healthy, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week. Apply the treatment to your hair and leave it on for 10-15 minutes before rinsing it off.

3. Protect your hair from heat and sun damage.

Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools, and wear a hat or use a hair protectant spray when you're out in the sun or harsh weather.

4. Get regular trims.

Get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh and healthy.

5. Use styling products that work well with your hair type and texture.

Choose styling products that help you achieve the look you want without damaging your hair. Use a hairspray or styling product to keep your hair in place.

Choosing the right haircut for your round face can be a game-changer. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can create a more balanced and flattering look. Use these tips and tricks to find the perfect haircut for your round face, and enjoy a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
