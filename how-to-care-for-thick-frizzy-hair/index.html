How To Care For Thick Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Thick frizzy hair can be a challenge to manage, but with the right care and products, you can transform your unruly locks into a sleek and shiny mane. In this article, we’ll take a look at some simple steps you can take to care for your thick frizzy hair, including how to prevent frizz, how to style your hair, and the best products to use.

Table of Contents

  • Preventing Frizz
  • Washing Your Hair
  • Conditioning Your Hair
  • Brushing Your Hair
  • Styling Your Hair
  • Product Recommendations
  • FAQ

Preventing Frizz

The key to managing thick frizzy hair is to prevent frizz from occurring in the first place. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to frizz.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair. Hot water can dry out your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Don't rub your hair with a towel after washing it. Instead, gently squeeze out the excess water with a towel or use an old t-shirt to dry your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet. This will minimize breakage and frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis. Heat can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.

Washing Your Hair

When washing your hair, it’s important to use the right shampoo and technique:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo that is designed for your hair type. If you have thick frizzy hair, look for a moisturizing shampoo that will hydrate your hair and prevent frizz.
  • Wet your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  • Apply a small amount of shampoo to your scalp and massage it in with your fingertips.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  • If necessary, repeat the shampooing process.

Conditioning Your Hair

Conditioning your hair is an important step in preventing frizz and keeping your hair healthy and hydrated:

  • Use a conditioner that is designed for your hair type. If you have thick frizzy hair, look for a moisturizing conditioner that will hydrate your hair and prevent frizz.
  • Apply the conditioner to the ends of your hair and work your way up towards your scalp.
  • Leave the conditioner in your hair for 3-5 minutes.
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.

Brushing Your Hair

Brushing your hair can help distribute natural oils and prevent tangling and breakage:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush designed for your hair type. If you have thick frizzy hair, look for a brush with boar bristles.
  • Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up towards your scalp.
  • Be gentle when brushing your hair to prevent breakage and frizz.

Styling Your Hair

Styling your hair can be a challenge when you have thick frizzy hair, but there are some simple techniques you can try:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser when blow-drying.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or styling product that is designed for your hair type. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil or coconut oil, which can help hydrate your hair and prevent frizz.
  • If you want to straighten your hair, use a flat iron with ceramic plates and a temperature control setting. Start at a low temperature and gradually increase it until you achieve the desired result.
  • If you want to curl your hair, use a curling iron with a ceramic barrel and a temperature control setting. Start at a low temperature and gradually increase it until you achieve the desired result.

Product Recommendations

Here are some product recommendations for caring for thick frizzy hair:

  • Hydrate Shea Butter Moisturizing Shampoo
  • Hydrate Shea Butter Moisturizing Conditioner
  • Argan Oil Heat Protectant Spray
  • Argan Oil Hair Serum

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have thick frizzy hair, you may only need to wash it once or twice a week. However, if you exercise frequently or live in a humid climate, you may need to wash it more often.

Q: Is it okay to use hot tools on my hair?

A: Yes, but it’s important to use them sparingly and to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Q: Can I use the same shampoo and conditioner for my hair?

A: Yes, but it’s important to choose products that are designed for your hair type. If you have thick frizzy hair, you may need a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that will hydrate your hair and prevent frizz.

By following these simple tips and tricks, you can care for your thick frizzy hair and achieve a sleek and shiny mane. Remember to choose products that are designed for your hair type and to avoid using heat styling tools on a daily basis.
