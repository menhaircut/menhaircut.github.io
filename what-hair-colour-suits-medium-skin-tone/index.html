What Hair Colour Suits Medium Skin Tone

Written by Bardi Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a medium skin tone, you might be wondering what hair colour would suit you best. Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this blog post, we will discuss the factors that come into play when selecting the perfect hair colour for medium skin tone, along with some useful tips and tricks to keep in mind. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium skin tone is a neutral skin tone that falls between light and dark skin. It is not too light or too dark, making it a versatile skin tone that can complement a wide range of hair colours. When selecting a hair colour for medium skin tone, it is essential to consider the undertones of your skin. Medium skin tones can have warm, cool, or neutral undertones, so choosing a hair colour that complements your undertones is crucial.

How to

When choosing a hair colour that suits medium skin tone, consider the following:

  • Undertones: Determine the undertones of your skin. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm hair colours like golden blonde, caramel, or copper. If you have cool undertones, choose cool hair colours like ash blonde, platinum blonde, or black. If you have neutral undertones, you can choose either warm or cool hair colours.
  • Contrast: Consider the contrast between your skin tone and hair colour. If you want a subtle change, choose a hair colour that is close to your natural hair colour. If you want a more dramatic change, go for a hair colour that contrasts with your skin tone.
  • Eye Colour: Take your eye colour into consideration when choosing a hair colour. If you have warm-toned eyes, opt for warm hair colours. If you have cool-toned eyes, choose cool hair colours.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when selecting a hair colour that suits medium skin tone:

  1. Determine the undertones of your skin.
  2. Consider the contrast between your skin tone and hair colour.
  3. Take your eye colour into consideration.
  4. Choose a hair colour that complements your undertones.
  5. Decide on the level of contrast you want.
  6. Choose a hair colour that complements your eye colour.
  7. Consider the maintenance of the hair colour you choose.
  8. Consult a professional hairstylist for advice.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair colour that suits medium skin tone:

  • Go for warm hair colours if you have warm undertones, and cool hair colours if you have cool undertones.
  • Consider the maintenance of the hair colour you choose. Some hair colours require more maintenance than others.
  • Choose a hair colour that complements your eye colour.
  • Consult a professional hairstylist for advice.

FAQ

Q: What are some warm hair colours that suit medium skin tone?

A: Some warm hair colours that suit medium skin tone include golden blonde, caramel, and copper.

Q: What are some cool hair colours that suit medium skin tone?

A: Some cool hair colours that suit medium skin tone include ash blonde, platinum blonde, and black.

Hair Care

After you choose a hair colour that suits your medium skin tone, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for colour-treated hair.
  • Limit the use of hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, as they can damage coloured hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the hair of its natural oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protecting hair product.
  • Visit a professional hairstylist for regular trims and touch-ups to maintain the health and vibrancy of your coloured hair.

Choosing a hair colour that suits medium skin tone can be a fun and exciting process. By considering your skin undertones, eye colour, and maintenance preferences, you can find the perfect hair colour that complements your natural beauty. Remember to take care of your coloured hair to maintain its health and vibrancy. Consult a professional hairstylist for advice and guidance along the way. Happy hair colouring!
