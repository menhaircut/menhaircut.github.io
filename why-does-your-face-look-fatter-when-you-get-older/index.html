Why Does Your Face Look Fatter When You Get Older

Written by Aprilia Oct 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have ever looked at a photo of yourself from a decade ago and compared it to a current photo, you may have noticed that your face looks fuller and rounder. This is a common concern for many people as they age, but why does your face look fatter when you get older?

Description:

As we age, our skin undergoes a number of changes that can contribute to a fuller-looking face. These changes include a loss of collagen and elastin, which are important for maintaining skin elasticity, as well as a decrease in the amount of fat in the face. Additionally, the bone structure in our face can change over time, leading to a loss of definition in the cheeks and jawline.

How to:

While there is no way to completely reverse the effects of aging on the face, there are a few things you can do to help minimize the appearance of a fuller face. Here are a few tips:

1. Stay hydrated:

Drinking plenty of water can help keep your skin looking plump and full, which can help disguise any loss of fat in the face.

2. Exercise regularly:

Regular exercise can help you maintain a healthy weight, which can help prevent your face from looking too full. Additionally, exercises that target the muscles in the face, such as facial yoga or facial massage, can help improve the overall appearance of the face.

3. Use skin care products:

Using skin care products that contain ingredients like retinol, vitamin C, or hyaluronic acid can help improve the texture and tone of the skin, which can help make the face look more youthful and full.

Step by step:

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you minimize the appearance of a fuller face:

Step 1: Drink plenty of water:

Staying hydrated can help keep your skin looking plump and full, which can help disguise any loss of fat in the face.

Step 2: Exercise regularly:

Step 3: Use skin care products:

Using skin care products that contain ingredients like retinol, vitamin C, or hyaluronic acid can help improve the texture and tone of the skin, which can help make the face look more youthful and full.

Tips:

Here are a few additional tips to help you minimize the appearance of a fuller face:

1. Consider facial fillers:

If you are concerned about the loss of fat in your face, you may want to consider getting facial fillers. These can help restore volume to the face and make it look more youthful.

2. Get enough sleep:

Getting enough sleep can help reduce the appearance of puffiness in the face and make it look more rested and youthful.

FAQ:

Q: Can losing weight help reduce the appearance of a fuller face?

A: Yes, losing weight can help reduce the appearance of a fuller face. However, it is important to maintain a healthy weight and not lose too much weight too quickly, as this can cause the face to look gaunt and sunken.

Q: Are there any foods that can help improve the appearance of the skin?

A: Yes, foods that are high in antioxidants, such as berries, leafy greens, and nuts, can help improve the appearance of the skin and make it look more youthful and full.

Hair Care:

While hair care may not directly impact the appearance of the face, it can still play a role in making the face look fuller or more youthful. Here are a few tips for taking care of your hair:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo:

Using a volumizing shampoo can help give your hair more body and make your face look fuller.

2. Avoid over-styling:

Over-styling your hair can cause damage, which can make it look thinner and less voluminous. Try to avoid using heat styling tools too often, and opt for air-drying or using a diffuser instead.

3. Get regular haircuts:

Getting regular haircuts can help keep your hair looking healthy and full, which can help make your face look more youthful.
