Haircuts For Long Faces And Big Foreheads A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 22, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a long face and a big forehead, you might feel like it’s difficult to find a haircut that flatters your features. But fear not, because there are plenty of hairstyles that can complement your face shape and make you look great. In this article, we’ll explore the best haircuts for long faces and big foreheads, as well as tips for styling and caring for your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to Choose the Right Haircut
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  4. Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  5. FAQ

Description

Long faces and big foreheads can be a challenge when it comes to finding a flattering haircut. The key is to choose a style that balances out your features and draws attention away from your forehead. This might mean adding volume to the sides of your hair, or choosing a style with wispy bangs that soften your face. Ultimately, the goal is to create a look that enhances your natural beauty and makes you feel confident.

How to Choose the Right Haircut

When choosing a haircut for a long face and big forehead, there are a few key things to keep in mind. First, avoid styles that are too long or too short, as these can elongate your face or make your forehead appear larger. Instead, opt for a mid-length cut that falls just below your shoulders. This length will help balance out your features and give you a more proportional look.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you’ll want to choose a style that adds volume and body. This might mean opting for layers or a textured cut that adds movement to your hair. If you have thick hair, on the other hand, you might want to go for a sleek, straight style that minimizes volume and creates a more streamlined look.

Finally, consider your face shape when choosing a haircut. If you have a long face, you’ll want to avoid styles that add height to the top of your head, as these can make your face appear even longer. Instead, opt for styles that add volume to the sides of your hair, such as a layered bob or a shaggy cut with wispy bangs. Similarly, if you have a big forehead, you’ll want to avoid styles with blunt bangs that draw attention to your forehead. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a layered cut that softens your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen the right haircut for your face shape and hair type, it’s important to know how to style it properly. Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suited to your hair type.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the roots to add volume and create movement.
  3. If your hair is prone to frizz, use a smoothing serum or cream to tame flyaways.
  4. Use a curling iron or straightener to add texture and shape to your hair.
  5. If you have bangs, use a small round brush to blow dry them in the direction you want them to fall.
  6. Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold everything in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Once you’ve got your perfect haircut, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Regularly trim your hair to keep the ends looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.

FAQ

What kind of bangs should I get if I have a big forehead?

If you have a big forehead, you’ll want to avoid blunt bangs that draw attention to your forehead. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or a layered cut that softens your face.

What length should I go for if I have a long face?

If you have a long face, you’ll want to avoid styles that are too long or too short, as these can elongate your face or make your forehead appear larger. Instead, opt for a mid-length cut that falls just below your shoulders.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep the ends looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care Tips for Long Faces and Big Foreheads

Finally, here are some general hair care tips for long faces and big foreheads:

  • Choose hair products that are suited to your hair type and use them regularly.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from heat styling tools by using a heat protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to find the perfect haircut for your long face and big forehead, and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
