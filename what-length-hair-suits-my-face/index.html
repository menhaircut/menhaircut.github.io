What Length Hair Suits My Face

Are you wondering what length hair would suit your face? Choosing the right hairstyle can make a huge difference in enhancing your facial features. In this article, we will guide you through how to determine what length hair suits your face shape, and provide tips on how to maintain and care for your hair.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in determining what length hair suits your face is to identify your face shape. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, you can use a measuring tape or ruler to measure the following:

  • Forehead: Measure from the peak of one eyebrow arch to the peak of the opposite arch.
  • Cheekbones: Measure across your cheekbones, starting and ending at the pointiest part below the outer corner of each eye.
  • Jawline: Measure from the tip of your chin to below your ear at the point where your jaw angles upwards.
  • Face length: Measure from the center of your hairline to the tip of your chin.

Once you have these measurements, you can compare them to the following guidelines to determine your face shape:

Oval Face

If your face length is longer than your cheekbones and your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have an oval face shape. Lucky for you, an oval face shape can pull off almost any hairstyle and length, from short bobs to long layers.

Round Face

If your face is as wide as it is long and you have full cheeks, you have a round face shape. To elongate your face, go for longer hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown, such as a long layered cut. Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as this can accentuate the roundness of your face.

Square Face

If you have a strong jawline, square forehead, and wide cheekbones, you have a square face shape. Soften your features with hairstyles that have soft layers and wispy bangs, such as a long layered cut or a shoulder-length bob. Avoid angular cuts that emphasize your jawline.

Heart Face

If your forehead is wider than your jawline and you have a pointy chin, you have a heart face shape. To balance out your features, opt for chin-length hairstyles with layers and side-swept bangs. Avoid short hairstyles that accentuate your forehead.

Diamond Face

If you have high cheekbones and a narrow forehead and jawline, you have a diamond face shape. Draw attention to your cheekbones with hairstyles that add volume at the crown, such as a layered bob or a side-swept pixie cut. Avoid hairstyles that add width to your cheeks.

Oblong Face

If your face is longer than it is wide and you have a long, straight jawline, you have an oblong face shape. Create the illusion of width with hairstyles that add volume to the sides, such as a shoulder-length bob or a layered cut. Avoid hairstyles that elongate your face, such as a long straight cut.

Hair Care Tips

Now that you've determined what length hair suits your face, it's important to maintain and care for your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools such as hair straighteners or curling irons.
  • Avoid washing your hair daily, as this can strip it of its natural oils. Instead, aim to wash it every other day or every third day.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hair elastics with metal clasps, as these can damage your hair. Use fabric-covered elastics instead.

FAQs

What is the best length of hair for a round face?

The best length for a round face is a longer hairstyle that adds height and volume at the crown, such as a long layered cut.

Can short hair suit a heart-shaped face?

Yes, chin-length hairstyles with layers and side-swept bangs can suit a heart-shaped face.

What length of hair should I avoid if I have a square face?

Avoid angular cuts that emphasize your jawline if you have a square face. Opt for soft layers and wispy bangs instead.

Choosing the right length of hair for your face shape can enhance your facial features and make you feel confident and beautiful. With these tips and tricks, you can choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
