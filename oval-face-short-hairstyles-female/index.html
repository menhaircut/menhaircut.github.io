Are you looking for a new hairstyle in 2023? If you have an oval face shape, there are plenty of short hairstyles that will complement your features. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step by step guide on how to style your hair, tips, and hair care advice to make sure your hair stays healthy and shiny. Let's get started!

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile face shape. You can pull off almost any hairstyle with this face shape, but short hairstyles are particularly flattering. Short hairstyles accentuate the features of your face and create a more defined look. There are plenty of options to choose from, such as bobs, pixie cuts, and shaved styles.

How to Style Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it is important to consider your hair type and texture. Short hairstyles work well on thick, straight, and wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you can still rock a short hairstyle, but it may require more maintenance.

1. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that works well on oval face shapes. This hairstyle is versatile and can be styled in various ways, such as a sleek and straight look or a messy and textured look.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a bold and daring hairstyle that is perfect for oval face shapes. This hairstyle accentuates your features, such as your cheekbones and jawline. A pixie cut can be styled in various ways, such as a messy and textured look or a sleek and polished look.

3. Shaved Style

A shaved style is a bold and edgy hairstyle that works well on oval face shapes. This hairstyle accentuates your features and creates a dramatic look. A shaved style can be styled in various ways, such as a side shave or an undercut.

Step by Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.

2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and texture.

3. Apply a styling product, such as a mousse or a hair gel, to create the desired look.

4. Use a hair straightener or a curling iron to add texture and dimension to your hair.

5. Finish the look with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks

- If you have a round face shape, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face.

- If you have a square face shape, avoid hairstyles that have sharp angles or blunt ends.

- If you have a heart-shaped face, avoid hairstyles that add volume at the top of your head.

FAQ

What are the best short hairstyles for oval face shapes?

The best short hairstyles for oval face shapes are bobs, pixie cuts, and shaved styles. These hairstyles accentuate your features and create a more defined look.

Can I still rock a short hairstyle if I have curly hair?

Yes, you can still rock a short hairstyle if you have curly hair. However, it may require more maintenance to keep your curls defined and frizz-free.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and texture. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair once or twice a week.

Hair Care Advice

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, it is important to follow a hair care routine. Here are some tips:

- Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.

- Avoid using hot tools, such as hair straighteners and curling irons, too often.

- Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

- Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock a short hairstyle that complements your oval face shape. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.
