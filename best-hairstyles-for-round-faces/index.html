Best Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Eveline Jun 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyles for round faces that will enhance your features and make you look stunning. Read on to know more.

Description

Round faces have soft curves with no sharp angles, and the width and length are almost equal. The best hairstyles for round faces are those that create the illusion of length and add angles to your face. This means that hairstyles with added height and layers around the crown can make your face appear longer and slimmer.

How to

Before we dive into the best hairstyles for round faces, it's essential to determine your face shape. To do this, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If they are equal, you have a round face. Once you have determined your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that flatters your features.

Short Hair

Short hairstyles can be perfect for round faces, provided they add height and angles. A pixie cut with layers on the crown or a short bob that hits just above the jawline can create the illusion of length. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbones, as they can make your face appear wider.

Medium Hair

For medium-length hair, try a layered bob or a shoulder-length cut with side-swept bangs. Layers around the crown can add height and angles, while side-swept bangs can create the illusion of length. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the jawline, as they can make your face appear wider.

Long Hair

Long hair can be tricky for round faces, but with the right cut, you can look stunning. Try a layered cut with face-framing layers that start at the cheekbones. This will add angles to your face and create the illusion of length. Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbones, as they can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to style your hair for a round face:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots to add height.
  3. Blow-dry your hair, lifting it at the roots to create volume.
  4. Curl your hair away from your face using a curling iron or wand. This will add angles to your face.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs or a deep side part to create the illusion of length.
  • Try highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for round faces:

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbones, as they can make your face appear wider. Also, avoid center parts, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Can I wear my hair up if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair up if you have a round face. Try a high ponytail or a top knot to create the illusion of height.

Do bangs look good on round faces?

Yes, bangs can look good on round faces, provided they are side-swept or asymmetrical. Blunt bangs can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

It's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shine. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools. Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or straightener to minimize damage to your hair. Also, get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, the best hairstyles for round faces are those that add height and angles to your face. Short haircuts with layers, medium-length haircuts with side-swept bangs, and long haircuts with face-framing layers are perfect for round faces. By keeping these tips in mind, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
