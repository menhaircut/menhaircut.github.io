Would I Look Better With Blonde Or Red Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering a hair color change but unsure which shade to choose? It's a common question many people ask themselves. In this article, we will explore the age-old question - would I look better with blonde or red hair? We will look at the pros and cons of both shades, how to choose the right shade, and hair care tips to maintain your new color.

Description

Choosing a new hair color can be overwhelming. With so many shades to choose from, it's challenging to know which one will suit you best. That's why we are here to help. In this article, we will focus on two popular shades - blonde and red. We will discuss the different shades, skin tones that suit each shade, and how to maintain your new color.

How to Choose the Right Shade

The first step in choosing the right shade is to determine your skin tone. Skin tone plays a crucial role in which hair color will suit you best. If you have warm undertones, you will look better with red hair. If you have cool undertones, blonde hair will suit you best. However, there are exceptions to this rule. Some people with warm undertones look great with blonde hair, and vice versa.

Another factor to consider when choosing a new hair color is your natural hair color. If you have dark hair, it's best to choose a shade that is close to your natural hair color. Going too light can be damaging to your hair and may not suit your skin tone. If you have light hair, you have more flexibility in choosing a new shade.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

Step 2: Choose a shade that complements your skin tone

Step 3: Consider your natural hair color

Step 4: Consult with a professional stylist

Tips

1. Do your research before committing to a new hair color.

2. Consider the maintenance required for your new color.

3. Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your new color.

4. Avoid using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage.

FAQ

Q: Will blonde hair make me look younger?

A: Blonde hair can make you look younger, but it depends on the shade and your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, a warmer shade of blonde will suit you better.

Q: Will red hair suit me?

A: Red hair can suit anyone, but it depends on the shade. If you have warm undertones, a warmer shade of red will suit you better.

Hair Care

After choosing your new hair color, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain your new shade. Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading. Avoid using hot tools on your hair to prevent damage. Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, whether you would look better with blonde or red hair depends on your skin tone, natural hair color, and personal preference. Consider all factors before committing to a new shade, and consult with a professional stylist if you're unsure. With proper care, you can rock your new hair color with confidence!
