What Hair Colour Goes With Olive Skin And Brown Eyes

Written by Frank Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of always sporting the same hair colour and want to try something new that complements your olive skin and brown eyes? Look no further because we've got you covered! In this article, we'll be discussing the best hair colours that suit olive skin and brown eyes, how to achieve them, and how to take care of your hair after the colouring process.

Description

With so many hair colours to choose from, it can be overwhelming to decide which one will look best on you. Your skin tone and eye colour play a huge role in determining what shades will complement your features, and in this case, we're focusing on those with olive skin and brown eyes. Olive skin is a warm-toned complexion that has green or yellow undertones, while brown eyes are versatile and can range from light to dark shades. Finding the perfect hair colour that suits both can be a challenge, but we're here to help!

How To

The first step in choosing a hair colour is to determine your skin tone. If you have olive skin, warm-toned colours such as caramel, honey, and golden blonde are your best bet. These colours will bring out the warmth in your skin tone and complement your brown eyes. On the other hand, cool-toned colours such as ash blonde or platinum blonde may clash with your skin tone and make you look washed out.

Next, consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to create dimension and depth. For instance, if you have dark brown eyes, adding caramel highlights to your brown hair will create a beautiful contrast that will make your eyes pop. If you want to go for a bolder look, you can try adding red or burgundy highlights to your hair. These colours will add warmth to your complexion and complement your brown eyes.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

Step 2: Choose warm-toned colours such as caramel, honey, and golden blonde

Step 3: Consider adding highlights or lowlights for dimension and depth

Step 4: Try red or burgundy highlights for a bolder look

Step 5: Consult with a professional hairstylist to achieve the perfect shade

Tips

1. Always use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain the vibrancy of your hair colour.

2. Avoid washing your hair too frequently as this can cause the colour to fade.

3. Protect your hair from the sun's harmful UV rays by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

4. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: While it's possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional hairstylist to ensure that the colour is done correctly and matches your skin tone and eye colour.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair colour?

A: This depends on the type of hair colour you have and how quickly your hair grows. Typically, it's recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After colouring your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and vibrancy. Here are some tips:

1. Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner.

2. Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons too frequently as this can damage your hair.

3. Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

4. Protect your hair from the sun's harmful UV rays by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

5. Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and maintain its health.

With these tips in mind, you're now ready to choose the perfect hair colour that suits your olive skin and brown eyes. Don't be afraid to experiment with different shades and styles until you find the perfect one that makes you feel confident and beautiful!
