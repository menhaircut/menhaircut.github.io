How To Wear A Maxi Dress If You Are Short And Curvy

Written by Eveline Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Maxi dresses are a great outfit choice for those lazy summer days, but for short and curvy women, it can be challenging to find a maxi dress that fits well and flatters your figure. However, with a few tricks and tips, you can look great in a maxi dress regardless of your height and size. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to wear a maxi dress if you are short and curvy.

  1. How to Wear a Maxi Dress

How to Wear a Maxi Dress

The key to wearing a maxi dress when you are short and curvy is to find the right dress that complements your body shape. Look for dresses that have a fitted waistline, flowy fabric, and a shorter length. Avoid dresses that are too long and flowy as they can overwhelm your figure. Instead, go for dresses that are knee-length or slightly above your ankle.

Choose the Right Fabric

The fabric of your maxi dress can make a significant difference in how it looks on you. If you are curvy, look for dresses made with lightweight and breathable fabrics such as cotton, rayon, or linen. These fabrics will flow over your curves without adding bulk to your frame. Avoid dresses made with heavy fabrics like denim or corduroy as they can add unwanted volume to your shape.

Go for a Fitted Waistline

A fitted waistline can help to accentuate your curves and create an hourglass silhouette. Look for dresses that have a defined waistline or add a belt to your dress to cinch in your waist. This will help to create a more flattering shape and prevent the dress from making you look shapeless.

Choose the Right Length

When it comes to maxi dresses, length is crucial. If you are short, look for dresses that are knee-length or slightly above your ankle. This length will help to elongate your legs and create the illusion of height. Avoid dresses that are too long as they can make you look shorter and wider than you actually are.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to wear a maxi dress if you are short and curvy:

Step 1: Choose the Right Dress

Look for a maxi dress that has a fitted waistline, flowy fabric, and a shorter length. Avoid dresses that are too long and flowy as they can overwhelm your figure.

Step 2: Add a Belt

If your dress doesn't have a defined waistline, add a belt to cinch in your waist and create an hourglass shape.

Step 3: Choose the Right Shoes

Wear shoes with a bit of height such as wedges or platform sandals. This will help to elongate your legs and create the illusion of height.

Step 4: Accessorize

Add some accessories to complete your look. A statement necklace or earrings can draw attention to your face and away from your body.

Tips to Follow

Here are some tips to help you wear a maxi dress if you are short and curvy:

  • Choose the right fabric that is lightweight and flowy
  • Look for dresses with a fitted waistline
  • Avoid dresses that are too long
  • Add a belt to cinch in your waist
  • Wear shoes with a bit of height
  • Add accessories to complete your look

FAQs

Q. Can short and curvy women wear a maxi dress?

A. Yes, short and curvy women can wear a maxi dress, but they need to find the right dress that flatters their body shape.

Q. What shoes should I wear with a maxi dress?

A. Wear shoes with a bit of height such as wedges or platform sandals to elongate your legs.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to complement your maxi dress:

  • Wear your hair in a loose, natural style
  • Consider a half-up, half-down hairstyle
  • Add braids or twists to your hair for a bohemian look
  • Accessorize with a hairband or headscarf

Now that you know how to wear a maxi dress if you are short and curvy, it's time to embrace this stylish and comfortable trend. Follow these tips, find the right dress, and accessorize to create a look that flatters your body shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
