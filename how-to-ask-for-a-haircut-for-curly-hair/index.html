How To Ask For A Haircut For Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it's unique and beautiful, it can also be difficult to manage, especially when it comes to getting a haircut. Many hairstylists aren't familiar with how to properly cut curly hair, which can result in a disastrous haircut. In this article, we'll give you a step-by-step guide on how to ask for a haircut for curly hair.

Description

Before we dive into how to ask for a haircut for curly hair, let's talk about why it's important. Curly hair has a different texture and growth pattern than straight hair, which means it needs to be cut differently. If a hairstylist doesn't know how to properly cut curly hair, it can result in an uneven haircut, split ends, and even damage to the hair. By knowing how to ask for a haircut for curly hair, you can ensure that you get the best possible haircut and keep your curls looking healthy and beautiful.

How to Ask for a Haircut for Curly Hair

When you're booking your appointment with the hairstylist, make sure you mention that you have curly hair and that you're looking for someone who knows how to cut curly hair. You can also ask for recommendations from friends or do a quick online search for hairstylists in your area who specialize in cutting curly hair.

When you arrive at the salon, make sure you bring pictures of the type of haircut you're looking for. This will give the hairstylist a better idea of what you're looking for and will help them determine the best way to cut your hair.

During the consultation, make sure you explain to the hairstylist how you normally style your hair, what products you use, and any issues you've had with previous haircuts. This will give the hairstylist a better understanding of your hair and will help them determine the best way to cut it.

When it comes to the actual haircut, make sure the hairstylist is cutting your hair when it's dry. This is because curly hair shrinks up when it's dry, and cutting it when it's wet can result in a much shorter haircut than you intended. The hairstylist should also be cutting your hair curl by curl, rather than straightening it out and cutting it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to ask for a haircut for curly hair:

  1. Mention that you have curly hair when booking your appointment
  2. Bring pictures of the type of haircut you're looking for
  3. During the consultation, explain how you normally style your hair and any issues you've had with previous haircuts
  4. Make sure the hairstylist is cutting your hair when it's dry
  5. Ask the hairstylist to cut your hair curl by curl
  6. Ask the hairstylist to start by cutting a little bit off at a time
  7. Keep checking in with the hairstylist throughout the haircut to make sure you're happy with the length
  8. Ask the hairstylist to shape your hair once it's dry, rather than blow drying it straight
  9. Ask for tips on how to maintain your new haircut at home
  10. Book your next appointment before you leave the salon

Tips for Getting the Perfect Haircut

Here are some additional tips for getting the perfect haircut for your curly hair:

  • Don't be afraid to speak up if you're not happy with the haircut. A good hairstylist will want to make sure you're happy with the end result.
  • Make sure you're using the right products for your hair type. This can make a big difference in how your haircut looks and how your curls behave.
  • Consider getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

FAQ

How much should I tip my hairstylist?

The standard tip for a hairstylist is 15-20% of the total cost of the haircut.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

Curly hair doesn't need to be washed as often as straight hair. You should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Finally, here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to help define your curls
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz

By following these tips and knowing how to ask for a haircut for curly hair, you can ensure that your curls always look their best.
