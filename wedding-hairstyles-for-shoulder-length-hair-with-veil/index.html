Wedding Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair With Veil

Written by Eveline Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a bride-to-be with shoulder length hair and struggling to find the perfect wedding hairstyle for your big day? Look no further! We have rounded up some of the best wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair with veil that will make you look stunning on your special day. Read on to discover our tips and tricks for achieving the perfect bridal look.

Description

When it comes to wedding hairstyles, there are countless options to choose from. However, if you have shoulder length hair and want to wear a veil, you may feel limited in your choices. But fear not! There are plenty of beautiful and elegant hairstyles that will work perfectly with your hair length and veil. From chic updos to loose waves, we've got you covered.

How to

The key to achieving the perfect wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair with veil is to find the right balance between the length of your hair and the length of your veil. You want to make sure that your hairstyle complements your veil and doesn't overpower it. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step by Step

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair the night before your wedding day. This will give your hair enough time to air dry and settle into its natural texture.
  2. On the day of your wedding, start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.
  3. If you want to add some curls or waves to your hair, use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose, natural-looking waves.
  4. If you prefer an updo, gather your hair into a low bun or a sleek ponytail. You can add some texture or volume to your updo by teasing your hair at the crown or adding braids or twists.
  5. Once your hairstyle is complete, it's time to add your veil. Place the veil on top of your hairstyle and secure it with bobby pins or a comb.
  6. Finish off your look with some hairspray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a wedding hairstyle for shoulder length hair with veil:

  • Consider the style of your dress when choosing your hairstyle. A sleek, modern dress may call for a more minimalist hairstyle, while a romantic, bohemian dress may work well with loose waves or braids.
  • Don't be afraid to add some accessories to your hairstyle, such as a delicate hairpin or a flower crown.
  • Practice your hairstyle before your wedding day to make sure it's exactly what you want.
  • Consider hiring a professional hairstylist to help you achieve the perfect look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about wedding hairstyles for shoulder length hair with veil:

What hairstyles work best with shoulder length hair and veil?

Some of the best hairstyles for shoulder length hair with veil include loose waves, braids, low buns, and sleek ponytails.

How can I make my shoulder length hair look longer with a veil?

You can add some length and volume to your hair by using clip-in hair extensions or opting for a voluminous updo.

Can I wear a veil with a half-up, half-down hairstyle?

A half-up, half-down hairstyle can work well with a veil, especially if you add some volume to the top section of your hair and secure the veil underneath.

Hair Care

Before your wedding day, make sure to take good care of your hair to ensure it looks its best. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoiding heat styling tools as much as possible. You can also visit a professional hairstylist for a trim or a haircut to ensure your hair is healthy and free of split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect wedding hairstyle for your shoulder length hair with veil and look stunning on your special day. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories until you find the perfect look that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
