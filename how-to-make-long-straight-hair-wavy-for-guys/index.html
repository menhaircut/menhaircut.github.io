How To Make Long Straight Hair Wavy For Guys

Written by William Aug 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same old straight hair every day? Do you want to switch things up and try a wavy hairstyle? If you have long straight hair, making it wavy can be a bit of a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this post, we'll show you how to make long straight hair wavy for guys.

Description

Wavy hair has been trending for a while now, and it's not just for women. Men with long straight hair can also rock this hairstyle. Wavy hair adds volume, texture, and movement to your hair, making it look more interesting and stylish. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, wavy hair can complement any outfit.

How to Make Long Straight Hair Wavy for Guys

There are several ways to make long straight hair wavy for guys. Here are some of the most popular methods:

Method 1: Braids

One of the easiest ways to make long straight hair wavy is by braiding it. Here's how to do it:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly.
  3. Secure the braids with hair ties or bobby pins.
  4. Leave the braids overnight or for a few hours.
  5. Unbraid your hair and run your fingers through it to loosen the waves.

Tip: For tighter waves, braid your hair when it's damp. For looser waves, braid your hair when it's dry.

Method 2: Curling Iron

If you want more control over the size and shape of your waves, you can use a curling iron. Here's how to do it:

  1. Apply heat protectant spray to your hair.
  2. Divide your hair into sections.
  3. Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron.
  4. Hold the curling iron for a few seconds, then release the hair.
  5. Repeat with the remaining sections of hair.
  6. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.

Tip: Use a lower heat setting to prevent damage to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to making long straight hair wavy:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Decide which method you want to use (braids or curling iron).
  3. If you're using the braids method, divide your hair into sections and braid each section tightly. If you're using the curling iron method, divide your hair into sections.
  4. If you're using the braids method, leave the braids overnight or for a few hours. If you're using the curling iron method, apply heat protectant spray to your hair.
  5. If you're using the curling iron method, take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron for a few seconds, then release the hair. Repeat with the remaining sections of hair.
  6. Unbraid your hair if you used the braids method.
  7. Run your fingers through your hair to loosen the waves.
  8. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you make long straight hair wavy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different methods to find the one that works best for you.
  • Don't overdo it with the heat. Too much heat can damage your hair.
  • Be patient. It may take a few tries to get the waves you want.

FAQ

Q: Can I make my hair wavy without heat?

A: Yes, you can use the braids method to make your hair wavy without heat.

Q: How long will the waves last?

A: The waves can last for a few days, depending on how well you maintain them.

Q: Will making my hair wavy damage it?

A: Using too much heat or leaving braids in for too long can damage your hair. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't leave braids in for too long.

Hair Care

Wavy hair requires a bit of maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as it can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your wavy hair looking healthy and stylish.

Conclusion

Now that you know how to make long straight hair wavy for guys, you can try out this trendy hairstyle for yourself. Whether you prefer the braids method or the curling iron method, there's a way to achieve the wavy look that you want. Just remember to take care of your hair and experiment until you find the method that works best for you.
