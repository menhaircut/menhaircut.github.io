What Haircut Suits My Round Face A Complete Guide For 2023

As we enter a new year, it's important to start it off with a fresh new look. For those with a round face shape, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But fear not, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll be discussing what haircut suits a round face, along with tips, step-by-step guides, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A round face shape is characterized by cheekbones and a rounded chin. The goal of a haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved by adding layers and volume to the crown of the head, as well as keeping the length below the chin. A side-swept bang can also help create the illusion of angles.

How To

When selecting a haircut for a round face, it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style. A short bob or pixie cut can work well for those with straight or fine hair, while a layered cut can add volume and movement to curly or thick hair.

Consider Your Hair Texture

For straight or fine hair, a short bob or pixie cut can help create the illusion of length and angles. Adding layers to the crown of the head can also add volume and movement to the hair.

For curly or thick hair, a layered cut can help create the illusion of length and angles, while also adding volume and movement to the hair. A side-swept bang can also help create the illusion of angles.

Consider Your Personal Style

When selecting a haircut, it's important to consider your personal style. A blunt bob or pixie cut can work well for those who prefer a more edgy look, while a layered cut can create a softer, more romantic look.

Step-by-Step

Here are some step-by-step guides for different haircuts that suit a round face:

Short Bob

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Starting at the nape of the neck, cut the hair in a blunt line.
  3. Continue cutting the hair in a blunt line, working your way up to the crown of the head.
  4. Add layers to the crown of the head, using thinning shears or a razor.
  5. Finish with a side-swept bang.

Layered Cut

  1. Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back.
  2. Starting at the nape of the neck, cut the hair in a blunt line.
  3. Continue cutting the hair in a blunt line, working your way up to the crown of the head.
  4. Add layers to the crown of the head, using thinning shears or a razor.
  5. Finish with a side-swept bang.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting a haircut for a round face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can emphasize the roundness of the face.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs, as they can create the illusion of angles.
  • Consider adding layers to the crown of the head, as they can add volume and movement to the hair.
  • Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What haircuts should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can emphasize the roundness of the face. Instead, opt for layered cuts that add volume and movement to the hair.

What haircuts work well for a round face?

Short bobs, pixie cuts, and layered cuts work well for a round face. Adding layers to the crown of the head can help create the illusion of length and angles, while side-swept bangs can add angles to the face.

Hair Care

Regardless of the haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know what haircut suits a round face, it's time to book that appointment and start the year off with a fresh new look!
