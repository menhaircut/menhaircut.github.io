How To Make Puffy Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Aug 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old straight hair? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your locks? Look no further than puffy curls! This style is perfect for any occasion and can be achieved with just a few simple steps. In this article, we will guide you through the process of making puffy curls from start to finish. So, grab your curling iron and let's get started!

How to Make Puffy Curls

Before we start, gather all the necessary tools and products. You will need a curling iron, heat protectant, hair spray, and a hairbrush or comb. Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant to prevent damage from the curling iron. Comb through your hair to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 2: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel. Hold for a few seconds and release. Continue this process until all of your hair is curled.

Step 3: Brush Out Your Curls

Using a hairbrush or comb, gently brush out your curls. This will create a puffy, voluminous effect.

Step 4: Apply Hairspray

To hold your curls in place, apply a light mist of hairspray all over your hair. This will also add extra volume and texture.

Tips for Making Puffy Curls

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect puffy curls:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel for bigger, more voluminous curls.
  • Don't curl your hair too tightly, as this can make the curls look too small and tight.
  • Brush out your curls gently to avoid causing frizz or breakage.
  • Apply hairspray in a light mist to avoid weighing down your curls.

FAQ

How long will puffy curls last?

Puffy curls can last for several hours to a whole day, depending on the texture and length of your hair. To make your curls last longer, apply hairspray after curling and avoid touching your hair too much.

Can I make puffy curls with a straightening iron?

While it is possible to make curls with a straightening iron, it may not create the same puffy effect as a curling iron. If you want to achieve the perfect puffy curls, we recommend using a curling iron.

Is it okay to curl my hair every day?

Curling your hair every day can cause damage and breakage. We recommend giving your hair a break between styling sessions and using a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Hair Care for Puffy Curls

To keep your puffy curls looking their best, make sure to use a nourishing shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and try to air dry whenever possible. Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect puffy curls in no time. Experiment with different curling techniques and products to find what works best for you. Have fun and embrace your curls!
