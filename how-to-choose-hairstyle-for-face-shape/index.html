How To Choose Hairstyle For Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can make all the difference in your overall appearance. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to figure out what will work best for you. In this guide, we'll break down everything you need to know about how to choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape.

  2. How to Choose Hairstyle for Face Shape
  3. Step by Step Guide

Description

Before we dive into the specifics of how to choose a hairstyle for your face shape, let's first define what we mean by "face shape." Essentially, your face shape refers to the overall shape of your face, including the width and length of your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and chin. There are six main face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can help balance out your features, highlight your best features, and create a more harmonious overall look. Keep reading to learn how to choose a hairstyle that will flatter your face shape.

How to Choose Hairstyle for Face Shape

Here are some general guidelines for choosing a hairstyle based on your face shape:

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, congratulations! This is considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. The key is to avoid hairstyles that add too much height or volume on top, as this can elongate your face shape. Instead, opt for styles that frame your face and highlight your best features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you'll want to choose hairstyles that add height and length to your face. Avoid styles that are too short or too round, as this can accentuate the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for longer hairstyles with layers or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, you'll want to soften your strong jawline with a hairstyle that adds volume and height on top. Avoid straight, blunt cuts and instead opt for layered styles with soft edges. Side-swept bangs can also help soften your features and create a more balanced overall look.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose hairstyles that balance out your wider forehead and narrower chin. Avoid styles that add too much volume on top, as this can make your forehead appear even wider. Instead, opt for styles with volume at the chin level, such as chin-length bobs or layered styles with volume at the bottom.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, you'll want to choose hairstyles that balance out your narrow forehead and chin with your wider cheekbones. Avoid styles that add too much volume on top, as this can elongate your face shape. Instead, opt for styles with volume at the chin level or below, such as chin-length bobs or layered styles with volume at the bottom.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, you'll want to choose hairstyles that add width and volume to your face. Avoid styles that are too long or straight, as this can elongate your face shape even more. Instead, opt for styles with volume at the sides, such as layered styles with volume at the cheekbones or chin-length bobs with soft waves.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for how to choose a hairstyle for your face shape:

  1. Determine your face shape. Use a measuring tape to measure the width and length of your forehead, cheekbones, jawline, and chin to determine your face shape.
  2. Research hairstyles that flatter your face shape. Look for hairstyles that balance out your features and highlight your best features.
  3. Consider your hair texture and thickness. Your hair texture and thickness can affect how certain hairstyles will look on you, so be sure to take this into account.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can help you determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and give you personalized recommendations based on your hair texture and thickness.
  5. Try on different hairstyles. If you're not sure which hairstyle will look best on you, try on different wigs or use virtual hairstyling apps to see how different styles will look on your face shape.

Tips for Choosing the Right Hairstyle

Here are some additional tips for choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape:

  • Consider your lifestyle. Choose a hairstyle that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.
  • Take your personality into account. Choose a hairstyle that reflects your personality and makes you feel confident.
  • Experiment with different styles. Don't be afraid to try out different hairstyles until you find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider face-framing highlights. Face-framing highlights can help accentuate your best features and create a more balanced overall look.

FAQs

What is the most versatile face shape?

The oval face shape is considered the most versatile face shape and can pull off a variety of hairstyles.

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face shape?

If you have a round face shape, you should avoid hairstyles that are too short or too round, as this can accentuate the roundness of your face. Instead, opt for longer hairstyles with layers or side-swept bangs to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

What hairstyles are best for a heart-shaped face?

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to choose hairstyles that balance out your wider forehead and narrower chin. Opt for styles with volume at the chin level, such as chin-length bobs or layered styles with volume at the bottom.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can seem daunting, but with these tips and guidelines, you'll be well on your way to finding the perfect style for you. Remember to take your hair texture and thickness into account, consult with a hairstylist, and experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
