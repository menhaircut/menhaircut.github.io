How To Know If You Suit Short Hair

Written by Christine Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of chopping off your locks and going for a short hairstyle? Short hair can be a game-changer for many people, but it's important to make sure that it suits you before taking the plunge. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks to help you figure out if short hair is the right choice for you.

  2. Consider Your Hair Texture

Determine Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding if short hair will suit you is your face shape. Certain styles will flatter different face shapes, so it's crucial to figure out what yours is before making any decisions.

If you have a heart-shaped face, a pixie cut or short bob can look great. For those with round faces, a longer bob or lob can help elongate the face. Those with square faces can try a textured, layered cut or a short bob with side-swept bangs. And if you have an oval face, you're in luck - pretty much any short hairstyle will suit you!

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider. If you have thick, coarse hair, a blunt bob or pixie cut can work well. For those with fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and movement. If you have curly hair, a short haircut can be a great way to show off your natural texture.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is also important to consider when deciding if short hair is right for you. If you're someone who loves spending hours styling your hair, then a short cut may not be the best choice. On the other hand, if you're someone who is always on the go and doesn't have a lot of time to spend on your hair, a low-maintenance short cut may be perfect.

Take Your Hair Color into Account

Your hair color can also play a role in whether or not short hair suits you. If you have a bold or vibrant hair color, a short cut can help showcase it. However, if you have a more natural hair color, a longer cut may be more flattering.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different kind of hair care than longer hair. You'll need to wash your hair more often to keep it looking fresh and clean. You may also need to use different styling products to achieve the look you want. Talk to your hairstylist about the best products and techniques for caring for your short hair.

Tips for Pulling Off Short Hair

If you've decided that short hair is right for you, here are some tips to help you pull it off:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Invest in good hair care products to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.
  • Accessorize with earrings or a bold lip color to add some extra flair.

FAQs

Q: Will short hair suit me if I have a double chin?

A: Short hair can actually be quite flattering if you have a double chin. A pixie cut or short bob can draw attention away from the chin area and highlight your facial features instead.

Q: Can I still style my short hair in different ways?

A: Absolutely! There are plenty of styling options for short hair, from sleek and straight to tousled and textured. Talk to your hairstylist about different styling techniques and products to help you achieve the look you want.

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look wider?

A: It depends on the cut you choose. Certain styles can actually help elongate the face, while others may make it look wider. Talk to your hairstylist about what will work best for you.

Q: How often do I need to get my short hair cut?

A: It depends on the style you choose and how fast your hair grows. Generally, you'll need to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your short cut.

Now that you know how to determine if short hair will suit you, it's time to take the plunge and try it out for yourself! Remember, hair grows back, so don't be afraid to experiment and have fun with your new look.
