Description

If you have a chubby face, you may be wondering if short hair is a good option for you. The answer is yes! Chubby faces can absolutely rock short hair. However, there are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle that will flatter your face shape.

How to

When considering a short hairstyle for a chubby face, it's important to take into account the shape of your face. Generally, a round face shape is the most common among those with chubby faces. In this case, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that will help elongate your face and create the illusion of a slimmer shape.

Step by step

Here are a few steps to follow when choosing a short hairstyle for a chubby face:

  1. Consider a side part to create asymmetry and help elongate the face.
  2. Opt for a hairstyle with layers or texture to add volume and movement.
  3. Avoid blunt cuts, as they can make the face appear even rounder.
  4. Consider a shorter cut around the back of the neck to create the illusion of a longer neck.
  5. Choose a style that you feel confident and comfortable in!

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for a chubby face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, as this can make the face appear wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to your hair to add dimension and create the illusion of a slimmer face.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you!

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for chubby faces:

Q: Can I still have bangs with a short hairstyle?

A: Yes! Bangs can be a great addition to a short hairstyle for a chubby face. Consider side-swept bangs or choppy bangs to create texture and add dimension.

Q: Should I consider a pixie cut?

A: Pixie cuts can be a great option for those with chubby faces, as they can help elongate the face and create the illusion of a slimmer shape. However, they may not be the best option for everyone. Consider your face shape and hair texture before opting for a pixie cut.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen a short hairstyle for your chubby face, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume and movement to your hair.
  • Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to damp hair before blow drying to add texture and hold.
  • Use a round brush when blow drying to create volume and movement.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to add volume and texture.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and healthy.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can absolutely rock short hair with a chubby face. Remember to consider your face shape and hair texture when choosing a style, and don't be afraid to experiment until you find the perfect look for you!
