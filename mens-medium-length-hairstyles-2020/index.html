Description

Men's medium length hairstyles 2020 are all about versatility and effortless style. This year, we saw an uptick in the popularity of longer hairstyles for men. Medium length hair allows for a variety of stylish looks that can be worn in casual, professional, or formal settings.

The great thing about men's medium length hairstyles is that they work well for all hair types, whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair. From textured crops to slicked-back styles, there's a medium length hairstyle that will suit your face shape and hair texture.

How to Achieve Men's Medium Length Hairstyles 2020

The first step in achieving a men's medium length hairstyle is to grow out your hair to the desired length. This can take anywhere from a few months to a year, depending on how fast your hair grows.

Once your hair is at the desired length, you can work with your stylist to choose a hairstyle that will suit your face shape and hair texture. Consider bringing in photos of medium length hairstyles that you like to show your stylist.

To style your hair, you'll need a good quality styling product such as pomade, wax, or clay. Apply a small amount of product to your hair and distribute it evenly, working from the roots to the tips. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving a popular men's medium length hairstyle:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair.
  3. Distribute the product evenly, working from the roots to the tips.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Men's Medium Length Hairstyles 2020

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling men's medium length hairstyles:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that works well with your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that suits you best.
  • Consider using a hair dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and trends.
  • Remember to trim your hair regularly to keep it looking healthy and well-maintained.

FAQs

What are some popular men's medium length hairstyles for 2020?

Some popular men's medium length hairstyles for 2020 include the textured crop, the slicked-back style, and the messy quiff.

How do I choose a men's medium length hairstyle that suits my face shape?

Consider consulting with a stylist to determine your face shape and which hairstyles will work best for you. Generally, those with oval faces can pull off most styles, while those with square or round faces may want to choose a style that adds height or volume to the top of the head.

Hair Care Tips for Men's Medium Length Hairstyles 2020

To keep your hair looking healthy and well-maintained, consider these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair health.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a stylish and on-trend men's medium length hairstyle for 2020.
