How To Deal With Side Bangs A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Sep 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Side bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add a touch of sophistication and style to any hair type. However, dealing with side bangs can be tricky, especially if you're new to the style. In this post, we'll go over everything you need to know about how to deal with side bangs, including how to style them, care for them, and keep them looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Side Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Dealing with Side Bangs
  • Tips for Maintaining Side Bangs
  • FAQs about Side Bangs
  • Hair Care Tips for Side Bangs

How to Style Side Bangs

Styling side bangs can be a bit tricky, but with a little practice, you'll be able to master the look in no time. Here are some tips to help you style your side bangs:

  • Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to give them volume and bounce.
  • Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs if you prefer a sleeker look.
  • Add some texture to your bangs by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for you.

Remember, practice makes perfect when it comes to styling side bangs. Don't be afraid to try different techniques and products until you find the look that you love.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dealing with Side Bangs

If you're new to side bangs, dealing with them can be overwhelming. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair as usual.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently, being careful not to pull or tug on your bangs.
  3. Use a comb or brush to part your hair where you want your bangs to start.
  4. Trim your bangs to the desired length, being careful not to cut them too short.
  5. Blow-dry your bangs using a round brush to give them volume and bounce.
  6. If you prefer a sleeker look, use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.
  7. Add some texture to your bangs by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.
  8. Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that works best for you.
  9. Touch up your bangs as needed throughout the day to keep them looking their best.

By following these steps, you'll be able to deal with your side bangs like a pro.

Tips for Maintaining Side Bangs

Maintaining side bangs can be a bit challenging, but with the right tips, it's easy to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to help you maintain your side bangs:

  • Use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day to prevent them from becoming greasy or frizzy.
  • Use a small amount of hairspray to keep your bangs in place without weighing them down.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to prevent them from becoming too long or unmanageable.

By following these tips, you'll be able to keep your side bangs looking their best between trims.

FAQs about Side Bangs

Here are some frequently asked questions about side bangs:

1. What face shape is best for side bangs?

Side bangs work best on oval and heart-shaped faces, but they can be adapted to suit other face shapes as well.

2. How do I know what length to cut my side bangs?

The length of your side bangs will depend on your hair type and face shape. It's best to consult with a hairstylist to determine the best length for your bangs.

3. How often should I trim my side bangs?

You should trim your side bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking their best.

4. Can I wear my side bangs straight or do they need to be curled?

You can wear your side bangs straight or curled, depending on your personal preference.

Hair Care Tips for Side Bangs

Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your side bangs looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too often, as this can cause your bangs to become frizzy and unruly.

By following these hair care tips, you'll be able to keep your side bangs looking healthy and beautiful.

Conclusion

Side bangs are a beautiful and versatile hairstyle that can add a touch of sophistication and style to any look. By following the tips and techniques outlined in this post, you'll be able to deal with your side bangs like a pro. Remember to experiment with different styles and products until you find the look that you love. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to rock your side bangs with confidence.
