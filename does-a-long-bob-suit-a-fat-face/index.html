Does A Long Bob Suit A Fat Face Find Out Here

Written by Daniel Aug 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, you may be wondering whether a long bob haircut will suit you. The good news is that a long bob, also known as a lob, can flatter a fat face by giving it a more structured and defined look. In this post, we will explore how to rock a long bob with a chubby face, step by step, and give you some tips on how to take care of your new hairstyle. Let's get started!

Description

A long bob is a versatile haircut that falls somewhere between a shoulder-length cut and a short bob. It's a popular style because it looks good on almost every face shape, including round faces. A long bob can create the illusion of a slimmer face by adding definition and angles to the jawline and cheekbones. It's also low-maintenance and easy to style, making it a great choice for busy women.

How to

If you're considering a long bob, here's how to get the look:

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's important to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you choose the right length and style that will flatter your face shape and features. They can also give you advice on how to maintain your new hairstyle.

Step 2: Choose the right length

A long bob can be tailored to suit your face shape and features. For a round face, a longer length that falls just below the chin can help elongate the face. Adding layers can also create movement and texture, making the face look slimmer. Avoid a blunt cut that can make the face appear wider.

Step 3: Style your hair

A long bob can be styled in many ways, from sleek and straight to messy and textured. Use a flat iron or curling wand to create loose waves or beachy curls. You can also use hair products like mousse or texturizing spray to add volume and texture.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a long bob:

  • Consider face-framing layers to add definition to your face.
  • Avoid a middle part, as it can make the face appear rounder.
  • Opt for side-swept bangs that can help elongate the face.
  • Use hair products that are suited for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

Q: Can a long bob make a round face look slimmer?

A: Yes, a long bob can add definition and angles to the face, making it appear slimmer. Adding layers and side-swept bangs can also help elongate the face.

Q: Is a long bob easy to maintain?

A: Yes, a long bob is low-maintenance and easy to style. It's a great choice for busy women who don't have a lot of time to spend on their hair.

Hair Care

To keep your long bob looking healthy and shiny, here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain your hairstyle.

In conclusion, a long bob can definitely suit a fat face. It's a versatile and low-maintenance hairstyle that can add definition and angles to the face, making it appear slimmer. By following the tips and hair care advice in this post, you can rock a long bob with confidence and style.
