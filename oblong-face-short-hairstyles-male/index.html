Oblong Face Short Hairstyles Male Tips And Ideas

If you have an oblong face shape, you may find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that suits you. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll share some tips and ideas for oblong face short hairstyles male that will help you look your best.

Description

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow appearance, with a forehead, cheekbones, and jawline that are all about the same width. This face shape is also known as rectangular or elongated, and it's more common in men than women.

How To Choose the Right Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for an oblong face shape, the goal is to create the illusion of a shorter and wider face. Here are some tips:

1. Shorter Length

Shorter hairstyles work best for oblong face shapes as they don't elongate the face further. Aim for a length that falls between your ears and your chin. Avoid styles that are too long, as they will make your face look even longer.

2. Fringe

A fringe or bangs can help shorten the appearance of your forehead, making your face appear shorter. Opt for a side-swept fringe that falls diagonally across your forehead. Avoid straight fringes that are cut straight across, as they will emphasize the length of your face.

3. Volume on the Sides

Adding volume to the sides of your hair can balance out the length of your face. Try a style with tapered or undercut sides, leaving more length on top. This will create the illusion of a wider face.

4. Texture

Hair with texture can help break up an oblong face shape. Consider adding layers or waves to your hair, as this will add volume and movement to your style.

Step by Step Guide to Styling Oblong Face Short Hairstyles Male

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling an oblong face short hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash and Dry

Wash your hair with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, then towel-dry your hair until it's damp.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a styling product of your choice to your hair, such as a mousse or gel. This will help hold your style in place.

Step 3: Blow-Dry

Using a blow dryer, dry your hair while using a brush to add volume and texture. Use a comb to create a side part if desired.

Step 4: Style

Style your hair using your fingers or a comb, creating the desired shape and texture. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your oblong face short hairstyle:

1. Regular Trims

Regular trims are important to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Aim for a trim every 4-6 weeks.

2. Avoid Overwashing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

3. Use a Quality Shampoo and Conditioner

Invest in a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. This will help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

4. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

When using heat styling tools, such as a blow dryer or straightener, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I still have long hair if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have an oblong face shape. However, it's best to avoid hairstyles that are too long, as they will elongate your face further. Aim for a length that falls between your shoulders and your chin.

2. Should I avoid hairstyles with a lot of height on top?

Yes, hairstyles with a lot of height on top should be avoided if you have an oblong face shape. This will only make your face look longer. Instead, opt for a style with volume on the sides.

3. Can I still have a fringe if I have an oblong face shape?

Yes, you can still have a fringe if you have an oblong face shape. Opt for a side-swept fringe that falls diagonally across your forehead. Avoid straight fringes that are cut straight across, as they will emphasize the length of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Men

If you want to keep your hair looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.

2. Avoid Overstyling

Overstyling your hair can cause damage and breakage. Use heat styling tools sparingly and avoid using too much product.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can damage your hair and cause it to become dry and brittle. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help keep your hair healthy and strong.

In conclusion, an oblong face shape can be challenging when it comes to finding the right hairstyle. But with the right tips and ideas, you can find a style that suits you and makes you feel confident. Follow our guide to oblong face short hairstyles male and maintain your hair with our hair care tips to keep it looking its best.
