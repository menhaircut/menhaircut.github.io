How To Look Like A Boy With Medium Hair

Written by Eveline Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a girl who wants to switch up your look and try out a more masculine style? Or maybe you're a non-binary individual who wants to present more androgynously. Whatever your reason may be, if you have medium-length hair and want to look like a boy, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look, as well as some tips on how to maintain it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Look Like a Boy with Medium Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining the Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's how to look like a boy with medium hair:

1. Cut Your Hair

The first step is to get a haircut that is more masculine. This could mean getting a short, textured cut or even a buzz cut. It's important to communicate with your hairstylist about the specific style you're going for, so they can give you the best cut possible.

2. Style Your Hair

Once you've got your new cut, you'll need to style your hair to achieve a more masculine look. This could involve using products like gel or pomade to create a slicked-back or messy style. Alternatively, you could leave your hair natural and tousled for a more relaxed and laid-back look.

3. Dress the Part

To complete the look, you'll need to dress in a more masculine style. This could mean wearing clothes like t-shirts, button-downs, jeans, and sneakers. You could also add accessories like hats or watches to enhance the look.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your new look:

1. Keep Your Hair Clean

Make sure to wash your hair regularly to prevent buildup of oils and product. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type and style. This will help you achieve your desired look while also keeping your hair healthy.

3. Get Regular Haircuts

To maintain your new style, you'll need to get regular haircuts. This will help keep your hair looking fresh and prevent split ends and other damage.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to look like a boy with medium hair:

Q: Do I need to cut my hair short to look like a boy?

A: Not necessarily. While shorter hair can help achieve a more masculine look, you can still look like a boy with medium-length hair if you style it correctly.

Q: Can I still wear makeup with this look?

A: Absolutely! You can still wear makeup if you want to, but you may want to opt for a more natural look to complement your masculine appearance.

Hair Care

Here are some general tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

As mentioned earlier, it's important to wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. However, be careful not to over-wash, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools like a straightener or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

What you eat can have a big impact on the health of your hair. Make sure to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve a more masculine look with your medium-length hair. Whether you want to experiment with a new style or embrace your androgynous side, this is a great option to try out. Good luck!
