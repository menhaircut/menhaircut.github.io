How To Keep Grey Hair Silver Tips And Tricks

Written by Robby Oct 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have embraced your natural grey hair or dyed your hair silver, you must be wondering how to maintain the silver hue. Grey hair can turn yellow over time due to various reasons, including environmental factors, hair care products, and natural aging. In this blog post, we will discuss how to keep grey hair silver and maintain its shine and vibrancy.

How to Keep Grey Hair Silver: Step by Step

Here are some steps you can take to keep your grey hair silver:

1. Use a Purple Shampoo

One of the most effective ways to keep grey hair silver is by using a purple shampoo. Purple shampoo neutralizes the yellow tones in grey hair, making it look brighter and more vibrant. Use a purple shampoo once a week or as needed, depending on your hair's condition.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons can damage your hair and cause it to turn yellow. Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible or use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can also cause grey hair to turn yellow. Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.

4. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Grey hair can become dry and brittle, making it more prone to damage and yellowing. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

5. Get Regular Haircuts

Regular haircuts can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Split ends can make your hair look dull and yellow.

Tips for Keeping Grey Hair Silver

Here are some additional tips for keeping your grey hair silver:

1. Use a Clarifying Shampoo

Use a clarifying shampoo once a month to remove buildup from hair care products and environmental factors. Buildup can cause your hair to turn yellow.

2. Avoid Chlorine

Chlorine can cause grey hair to turn green or yellow. Avoid swimming in chlorinated pools or wear a swimming cap to protect your hair.

3. Use a Hair Toner

Use a hair toner after coloring your hair silver to maintain the silver hue. Hair toners are designed to neutralize any unwanted tones in your hair.

FAQs About Keeping Grey Hair Silver

1. Can I use a regular shampoo on grey hair?

Yes, you can use a regular shampoo on grey hair. However, using a purple shampoo can help keep your hair silver and prevent yellowing.

2. How often should I use a purple shampoo?

You should use a purple shampoo once a week or as needed, depending on your hair's condition. Overuse of purple shampoo can cause your hair to turn purple.

3. Can I use a hair toner on natural grey hair?

Yes, you can use a hair toner on natural grey hair to maintain the silver hue. Hair toners are designed to neutralize any unwanted tones in your hair.

Final Thoughts

Keeping your grey hair silver requires some effort and care, but it's worth it to maintain its shine and vibrancy. Use a purple shampoo, avoid heat styling, protect your hair from the sun, use a deep conditioning treatment, and get regular haircuts to keep your grey hair silver.

Remember to use a clarifying shampoo, avoid chlorine, and use a hair toner as needed. By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your grey hair silver and looking its best.
