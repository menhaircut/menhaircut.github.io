How To Trim A Pixie Wig Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Nov 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you want to change up your look with a short haircut, but don't want to commit to chopping off your own locks, a pixie wig is a great option. Pixie wigs have become increasingly popular in recent years, offering a trendy and low-maintenance style. However, one downside to wigs is that they often come with excess hair that needs to be trimmed to fit your face shape and style. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps on how to trim a pixie wig and achieve the perfect look.

Description

A pixie wig is a short, cropped wig that mimics the look of a pixie haircut. It is a popular choice for those who want to try a short hairstyle without the commitment of cutting their own hair. However, wigs often come with excess hair that needs to be trimmed to fit the wearer's face shape and style.

How to Trim a Pixie Wig

Step 1: Wash and dry the wig

Before you start trimming, make sure the wig is clean and dry. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to wash the wig, then let it air dry or blow dry on low heat. This will ensure that the hair is smooth and easy to work with.

Step 2: Put on the wig

Put on the wig and adjust it to fit your head properly. Use clips or bobby pins to secure any loose areas that need to be trimmed.

Step 3: Determine the length

Determine the length you want for your pixie wig. Use a comb or your fingers to section off the hair to be trimmed. Remember, it's always better to start with a longer length and gradually trim more if needed.

Step 4: Trim the hair

Using hair cutting shears, begin trimming the hair in small sections. Take your time and make sure you are cutting straight across. Keep checking the length as you go to ensure you are achieving the desired look.

Step 5: Blend the hair

Once you have trimmed the hair to the desired length, use thinning shears to blend the hair and create a more natural look. This will help to remove any excess bulk and create a more seamless transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step 6: Style the hair

Style the hair as desired using a comb or your fingers. Apply a small amount of hair gel or hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Always start with a longer length and gradually trim more if needed.
  • Be patient and take your time when trimming the hair.
  • Use hair cutting shears and thinning shears for best results.
  • Don't be afraid to seek professional help if you are unsure about how to trim your wig.

FAQ

Can I trim a synthetic wig?

Yes, you can trim a synthetic wig. However, it is important to use the correct tools and techniques to avoid damaging the synthetic fibers.

How often should I trim my wig?

It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how often you wear the wig. As a general rule, you should trim your wig every 6-8 weeks to maintain the desired length and style.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to keep your wig looking and feeling its best. Use a wig shampoo and conditioner to wash the wig, and avoid using hot tools such as curling irons or straighteners. Store the wig on a mannequin head or in a wig box to help maintain its shape.

With these simple steps, you can easily trim your pixie wig and achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look. Remember to take your time, use the right tools, and seek professional help if needed.
