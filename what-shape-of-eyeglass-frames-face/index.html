What Shape Of Eyeglass Frames Face A Guide For Choosing The Perfect Fit

Written by Petter Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you having trouble finding the right eyeglass frames that suit your face shape? It's important to choose frames that complement your facial features and enhance your overall appearance. In this guide, we'll walk you through the different face shapes and the best eyeglass frame styles for each.

Table of Contents

  • Round Face
  • Square Face
  • Heart Face
  • Diamond Face

Oval Face

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck! This face shape is considered the most versatile and can pull off almost any frame style. Look for frames that are wider than the broadest part of your face and feature soft, rounded edges. Rectangular or square frames can also work well to accentuate your features. Avoid frames that are too small or too oversized, as they can overwhelm your face.

Round Face

For those with a round face shape, angular frames are your best bet. Look for styles with sharp, defined edges that contrast with the soft curves of your face. Rectangular or square frames can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. Avoid round or oval frames, as they can make your face look even rounder.

Square Face

If you have a square face shape with strong jawlines and wide cheekbones, choose frames that soften your features. Round or oval frames can help balance out the sharp angles of your face and add a touch of femininity. Cat-eye frames are also a great option, as they accentuate your cheekbones and draw attention to your eyes. Avoid square or rectangular frames, as they can make your face look too boxy.

Heart Face

Heart-shaped faces are characterized by a wide forehead and narrow jawline. Look for frames that balance out your facial proportions and draw attention downward. Cat-eye frames or frames with curved edges can help accentuate your cheekbones and add softness to your features. Avoid frames that are too top-heavy or oversized, as they can make your forehead appear even wider.

Diamond Face

Diamond-shaped faces have narrow foreheads and jawlines, with high cheekbones. Look for frames that highlight your cheekbones and add width to your forehead and jawline. Oval or cat-eye frames can help soften your features and balance out your facial proportions. Avoid frames that are too narrow or angular, as they can make your face look too sharp.

Triangle Face

Triangle-shaped faces have a wider jawline and narrow forehead. Look for frames that add width to your forehead and draw attention upward. Cat-eye or oval frames can help balance out your features and add softness to your face. Avoid frames that are too bottom-heavy or oversized, as they can make your jawline appear even wider.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear any frame style if I have an oval face shape?

A: Yes! Oval faces are the most versatile and can pull off almost any frame style.

Q: What frames should I avoid if I have a round face shape?

A: Avoid round or oval frames, as they can make your face look even rounder.

Q: What frames should I choose if I have a square face shape?

A: Look for frames that soften your features, such as round or oval frames.

Q: Can I wear oversized frames if I have a heart-shaped face?

A: It's best to avoid frames that are too top-heavy or oversized, as they can make your forehead appear even wider.

Hair Care Tips

When choosing eyeglass frames, it's important to consider your hairstyle as well. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • If you have long hair, consider frames that won't get caught in your hair or interfere with your hairstyle.
  • If you have short hair, consider frames that won't overpower your features or make your face look too small.
  • If you have bangs, consider frames that won't cover your eyebrows or clash with your hairline.
  • If you wear glasses every day, make sure to clean them regularly to avoid buildup of oils and debris that can transfer to your hair and cause breakouts or irritation.

By following these tips and choosing the right eyeglass frames for your face shape, you can enhance your appearance and feel confident in your glasses. Happy shopping!
