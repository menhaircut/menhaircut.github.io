Which Hair Colour Will Suit Me Tips Ideas And Care

Written by Bardi Jul 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hair colour but don't know which one to choose? It can be overwhelming to decide on a new hair colour, especially when there are so many options available. Fear not, because in this article, we will help you determine which hair colour will suit you best. We will provide you with tips, ideas and hair care advice to make sure you achieve the perfect hair colour that complements your features and skin tone.

How to Determine Which Hair Colour Will Suit You

Before we dive into the different hair colour options, it's important to know how to determine which hair colour will suit you best. Here are some factors to consider:

Skin Tone

Your skin tone plays a crucial role in determining which hair colour will suit you. If you have warm undertones, then warm hair colours like blonde, brown, and red will complement your skin tone. On the other hand, if you have cool undertones, then cool hair colours like ash blonde, cool brown, and black will suit you better.

Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also influence which hair colour will suit you best. If you have blue or green eyes, then blonde, brown, and red hair colours will look great on you. If you have brown or hazel eyes, then warm colours like caramel and auburn will complement your eyes better.

Natural Hair Colour

Your natural hair colour can also determine which shade of hair colour will suit you best. If you have dark hair, then going for a lighter shade like blonde or caramel can make a huge difference. If you have light hair, then going for a deeper shade like brown or red can add depth and dimension to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Colour

Now that you know the factors to consider, here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right hair colour:

Step 1: Determine your skin tone

Use the vein test to determine whether you have warm or cool undertones. If your veins appear blue or purple, then you have cool undertones. If your veins appear green, then you have warm undertones.

Step 2: Consider your eye colour

Take a look at your eyes and determine whether they are blue, green, brown, or hazel. Use the guide above to determine which hair colours will complement your eyes.

Step 3: Determine your natural hair colour

Take a look at your natural hair colour and determine whether it's light or dark. Use the guide above to determine which shades will suit you best.

Step 4: Research different hair colours

Use social media and hair colour websites to research different hair colours. Look for photos of people with similar skin tones, eye colours, and natural hair colours to see how different hair colours look on them.

Step 5: Consult with a Professional

Consult with a hair colour professional who can provide you with recommendations based on your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour. They can also help you achieve the hair colour you want while keeping your hair healthy and vibrant.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Colour

Once you have chosen a new hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy and health. Here are some tips:

Use a Colour-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for coloured hair can help maintain its vibrancy and prevent fading.

Avoid Hot Styling Tools

Hot styling tools like curling irons and straighteners can damage your hair and cause your colour to fade faster. If you must use hot styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair.

Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause your colour to fade faster. Try to wash your hair every other day, or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can cause your hair colour to fade faster. Wear a hat or use a leave-in conditioner with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's damaged?

A: It's best to avoid dyeing your hair if it's damaged. Dyeing your hair can further damage it and cause breakage. It's best to wait until your hair is healthy before dyeing it.

Q: How often should I touch up my roots?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Generally, it's recommended to touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: It's possible to dye your hair at home, but it's recommended to consult with a professional first. They can provide you with guidance on which products to use and how to achieve the desired colour.

Conclusion

Choosing a new hair colour can be a fun and exciting experience. By considering your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour, you can determine which hair colour will suit you best. Remember to take care of your hair to maintain its vibrancy and health. If you're unsure about which hair colour to choose, consult with a professional who can provide you with recommendations and guidance.
