Description

Surfer hair is a popular hairstyle that has been around for decades. It's a natural, laid-back look that is perfect for those who love to spend time at the beach. Surfer hair is characterized by its messy, tousled appearance, which is achieved through a combination of sun, saltwater, and wind. In this article, we'll show you how to style surfer hair in a relaxed, easy-to-follow guide.

How to Style Surfer Hair

The key to styling surfer hair is to keep it natural and messy. You don't want to spend too much time on your hair, as the whole point of surfer hair is to look like you just came out of the ocean. You can achieve this look by following a few simple steps.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or product buildup from your hair, and leave it clean and fresh.

Step 2: Let Your Hair Air Dry

Once you've washed your hair, let it air dry. Don't use a hairdryer, as this will make your hair look too neat and tidy. Surfer hair should look natural and messy, so let your hair dry naturally.

Step 3: Apply a Styling Product

Once your hair is dry, apply a styling product to give it some texture and hold. You can use a sea salt spray or a hair wax, depending on your hair type and the look you're going for.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use your hands to style your hair, rather than a comb or brush. This will help to create a messy, tousled look that is perfect for surfer hair. Scrunch your hair up with your hands to create volume and texture, and use your fingers to create a few loose waves.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling surfer hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Let your hair air dry naturally.
  3. Apply a styling product, such as sea salt spray or hair wax.
  4. Use your hands to style your hair, creating volume and texture.
  5. Create loose waves with your fingers.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Styling Surfer Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect surfer hair look:

  • Don't use too much product - a little goes a long way with surfer hair.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer, as this will make your hair look too neat and tidy.
  • Use your hands to style your hair, rather than a comb or brush.
  • Create loose waves with your fingers to add texture to your hair.

FAQs

Can anyone get surfer hair?

Surfer hair works best on those with longer hair, as it's easier to create volume and texture. However, anyone can try the surfer hair look, regardless of hair length.

Do I need to go to the beach to achieve surfer hair?

No, you don't need to go to the beach to achieve surfer hair. You can create the look at home using a few simple styling products.

Can I use any styling product for surfer hair?

No, not all styling products are suitable for surfer hair. You'll need to use a product that adds texture and hold, such as sea salt spray or hair wax.

Hair Care for Surfer Hair

To keep your surfer hair looking great, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any dirt or product buildup.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a good quality conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer, as this can damage your hair and make it look frizzy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect surfer hair look. Remember to keep it natural and messy, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for you.
