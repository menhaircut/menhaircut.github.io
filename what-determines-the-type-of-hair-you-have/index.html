What Determines The Type Of Hair You Have

Written by Aprilia Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A person's hair type is determined by various factors, including genetics, ethnicity, and lifestyle habits. Understanding your hair type is essential in determining the best hair care routine that works for you. In this article, we will discuss the different factors that determine the type of hair you have.

What is Hair Type?

Hair type refers to the natural texture and shape of your hair, which is influenced by the shape of the hair follicles. There are different hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily. Each hair type has unique characteristics that require specific hair care treatments.

Factors That Determine Hair Type

Genetics

Genetics play a significant role in determining hair type. The genes you inherit from your parents determine the thickness, texture, and color of your hair. For instance, people with curly hair have inherited a gene that makes their hair follicles produce curly hair.

Ethnicity

The ethnicity of a person also influences their hair type. People from different ethnic backgrounds have different hair types. For instance, people of African descent tend to have coily or kinky hair, while people of Asian descent tend to have straight hair.

Lifestyle Habits

Lifestyle habits can also determine your hair type. Factors such as diet, hair care routine, and environmental factors can affect the health and texture of your hair. For instance, using harsh hair products and exposing your hair to heat can damage your hair, making it brittle and dry.

How to Determine Your Hair Type

The best way to determine your hair type is by examining your hair after washing and conditioning it. You can also consult a hair care professional who can examine your hair and recommend the best hair care routine for your hair type.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Type

To maintain your hair type, you should follow a hair care routine that is tailored to your hair type. This includes using the right hair products, avoiding harsh chemicals, and minimizing heat exposure. Additionally, you should maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle to promote healthy hair growth.

FAQs

Can Your Hair Type Change?

Yes, your hair type can change over time, especially due to hormonal changes, aging, or lifestyle habits. For instance, people who use chemical treatments such as relaxers or dyes may experience changes in their hair type.

How Often Should You Wash Your Hair?

The frequency of washing your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle habits. People with oily hair may need to wash their hair more frequently, while people with dry hair may need to wash their hair less frequently.

Hair Care Tips for Different Hair Types

Straight Hair

People with straight hair should use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that does not weigh down their hair. Additionally, they should avoid using heavy styling products that can make their hair appear greasy.

Wavy Hair

People with wavy hair should use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent their hair from becoming dry and frizzy. Additionally, they should use a diffuser when blow-drying their hair to enhance their natural waves.

Curly Hair

People with curly hair should use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent their hair from becoming dry and brittle. Additionally, they should use a leave-in conditioner and a curl-enhancing gel to define their curls.

Coily Hair

People with coily hair should use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that does not strip their hair of its natural oils. Additionally, they should use a moisturizing hair mask once a week and twist their hair at night to prevent breakage.

Conclusion

Understanding your hair type is essential in determining the best hair care routine that works for you. By considering factors such as genetics, ethnicity, and lifestyle habits, you can determine your hair type and maintain healthy, beautiful hair.
