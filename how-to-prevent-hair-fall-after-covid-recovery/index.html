How To Prevent Hair Fall After Covid Recovery Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you experiencing hair fall after recovering from COVID? You are not alone. Post-COVID hair fall has become a common issue among many people. It can be stressful and affect your confidence. But don't worry, there are ways to prevent hair fall after COVID recovery. In this article, we will discuss how to prevent hair fall after COVID recovery, step by step.

  2. Step by Step Guide

How to Prevent Hair Fall After COVID Recovery

COVID-19 can cause hair loss due to stress, fever, and nutritional deficiencies. Here are some tips to prevent hair fall after COVID recovery:

Eat a Balanced Diet

Include protein-rich foods like eggs, fish, and dairy products in your diet. Also, eat fruits and vegetables that are rich in vitamins and minerals.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and fluids to keep your body hydrated. This will also help in maintaining healthy hair.

Manage Stress

Stress can be a major cause of hair fall. Practice relaxation techniques like yoga and meditation to manage stress.

Take Supplements

Consult your doctor and take supplements like biotin, iron, and vitamin D to prevent hair fall.

Use Gentle Hair Products

Avoid using harsh chemicals and hair products that can damage your hair. Use gentle shampoos and conditioners that are suitable for your hair type.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to prevent hair fall after COVID recovery:

Step 1: Consult a Doctor

Consult a doctor to rule out any underlying medical conditions that may be causing hair fall.

Step 2: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eat a balanced diet that includes protein-rich foods and fruits and vegetables.

Step 3: Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and fluids to keep your body hydrated.

Step 4: Manage Stress

Practice relaxation techniques like yoga and meditation to manage stress.

Step 5: Take Supplements

Consult your doctor and take supplements like biotin, iron, and vitamin D to prevent hair fall.

Step 6: Use Gentle Hair Products

Use gentle shampoos and conditioners that are suitable for your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals and hair products that can damage your hair.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall After COVID Recovery

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall after COVID recovery:

Tip 1: Massage Your Scalp

Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.

Tip 2: Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause hair fall.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like straighteners and curling irons. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.

Tip 4: Get Enough Sleep

Get enough sleep to allow your body to repair and regenerate.

FAQs

Q: Can COVID-19 cause hair fall?

A: Yes, COVID-19 can cause hair fall due to stress, fever, and nutritional deficiencies.

Q: How long does post-COVID hair fall last?

A: Post-COVID hair fall can last for several months. However, it usually resolves on its own.

Hair Care for Preventing Hair Fall

Here are some additional hair care tips to prevent hair fall:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt and oil buildup that can clog hair follicles.

Tip 2: Condition Your Hair

Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and prevent breakage.

Tip 3: Trim Your Hair

Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and prevent breakage.

Tip 4: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Avoid prolonged exposure to the sun, as it can damage your hair.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can prevent hair fall after COVID recovery. Remember to be patient, as it may take some time for your hair to recover. If you experience severe hair fall, consult a doctor or a hair specialist.
