How To Cut Spike Women s Short Hair

Written by Daniel Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your boring short haircut? Do you want to add some edge and personality to your look? Then spiking your hair may be the perfect solution for you. In this article, we will show you how to cut spike women's short hair in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner.

Description

Spiking your hair is a popular hairstyle trend that is perfect for women who want to add some edge and attitude to their look. This style is characterized by short hair that is cut and styled to stand up on its own, creating a spiky effect. It is a great option for those who want to experiment with their hair and try something new.

How to Cut Spike Women's Short Hair - Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you begin cutting your hair, it is essential to prepare it properly. Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any dirt or debris. Then, towel-dry your hair and comb it to remove any tangles.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

The first step in cutting spiky hair is to decide on the length you want. Short hair is essential for this style, so choose a length that falls between one and three inches. Use hair clippers to achieve an even length all over your head.

Step 3: Cut the Hair on Top

Once you have decided on the length, use scissors to cut the hair on top of your head. Cut small sections of hair at a time, holding the hair between your fingers and cutting it to your desired length. Cut the hair shorter towards the back of your head and leave it slightly longer towards the front.

Step 4: Cut the Sides and Back

Use hair clippers to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. The hair on the sides should be shorter than the hair on top, while the hair at the back should be tapered and shorter. Keep the hair at the nape of your neck slightly longer to create a more natural look.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have cut your hair, it is time to style it into spikes. Use a hair gel or wax to create the spikes by rubbing a small amount of product between your palms and running it through your hair. Use your fingers to shape the spikes into your desired style, and you're done!

Tips for Cutting Spiky Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect spiky haircut:

  • Use sharp scissors to avoid split ends and uneven cuts
  • Start cutting your hair from the top and work your way down to the sides and back
  • Use hair clippers with different guards to achieve different lengths
  • Apply hair gel or wax sparingly to avoid a greasy look
  • Use your fingers to create the spikes instead of a comb or brush

FAQs

Q: Is spiky hair suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, spiky hair can work for all face shapes. However, it is essential to choose a length and style that complements your face shape and features.

Q: Do I need to use hair gel or wax to style my spiky hair?

A: Yes, hair gel or wax is essential to create and hold the spikes in place. Without product, your hair may look flat and lifeless.

Q: How often should I cut my spiky hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want to maintain the spiky look. Generally, you should cut your hair every four to six weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Hair Care for Spiky Hair

Spiky hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your spiky hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the natural oils from your hair
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can damage and dry out your hair
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause the spikes to fall out of place

Now that you know how to cut spike women's short hair, it's time to try it out for yourself. Remember to follow these steps carefully and use the right tools and products to achieve the perfect spiky look. With a little practice and patience, you'll be rocking edgy and stylish spiky hair in no time.
