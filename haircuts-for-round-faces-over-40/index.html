Haircuts For Round Faces Over 40 How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by Robby Jun 06, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a round face and are over 40, finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features while still looking age-appropriate. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to choose the perfect haircut for a round face over 40, as well as some popular styles to consider.

How to Choose the Perfect Haircut for a Round Face Over 40

When choosing a haircut for a round face over 40, there are several factors to consider. First, you want a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Second, you want a cut that will complement your face shape and highlight your best features. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider the Length

The length of your hair is an important factor when choosing a haircut for a round face. Longer hair can elongate the face, while shorter hair can make it appear wider. However, this doesn't mean you have to stick to one length. Layered haircuts can add dimension and movement to your hair, while still flattering your face shape.

Look for Volume on Top

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider. Instead, look for styles that add volume on top, such as layered bobs or pixie cuts. This will create the illusion of a longer face shape.

Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in choosing the perfect haircut. If you have fine hair, you may want to avoid heavy layers that can weigh your hair down. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, layers can help to lighten your hair and make it more manageable.

Think About Your Lifestyle

The perfect haircut should also fit with your lifestyle. If you're a busy mom or have a demanding job, you may want a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling. On the other hand, if you enjoy experimenting with different looks, you may want a more versatile cut that can be styled in different ways.

Popular Haircuts for Round Faces Over 40

Now that you know what to look for in a haircut, here are some popular styles to consider:

Long Layers

Long layers can be a great option for round faces over 40, as they can help to elongate the face and create a more flattering shape. This style is also versatile, as you can wear it straight or add some waves or curls for a more textured look.

Textured Pixie Cut

A textured pixie cut can be a great option for those who want a low-maintenance style. The choppy layers add volume and texture to your hair, while the shorter length elongates your face. This style works well with all hair types, but is especially flattering for those with thick hair.

Long Bob with Bangs

A long bob with bangs can be a great option for those who want a classic, elegant look. The length of the bob elongates the face, while the bangs add some texture and dimension. This style works well with all hair types, but is especially flattering for those with fine hair.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces Over 40

Once you've chosen the perfect haircut, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Use a Moisturizing Shampoo and Conditioner

As we age, our hair can become more prone to dryness and damage. To keep your hair looking healthy, use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner that will nourish your hair and leave it feeling soft and silky.

Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Heat styling tools can be damaging to your hair, especially if you use them frequently. To avoid damage, try to limit your use of heat styling tools and let your hair air-dry whenever possible. If you do need to use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are important to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every six to eight weeks to keep your hair in good shape.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a round face over 40?

The best haircut for a round face over 40 is one that elongates the face and highlights your best features. Long layers, a textured pixie cut, and a long bob with bangs are all great options to consider.

How do I style my hair to flatter a round face?

To flatter a round face, try adding volume on top with layers or a volumizing product. Avoid styles that add volume to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear wider.

What hair care products should I use for my hair?

For round faces over 40, it's important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to nourish your hair and prevent dryness. You can also use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage from heat styling tools.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect haircut for a round face over 40 can be a challenge, but with these tips and popular styles to consider, you're sure to find a look that flatters your face and enhances your best features. Remember to take good care of your hair with regular trims and moisturizing products, and you'll have beautiful, healthy hair that looks great at any age.
