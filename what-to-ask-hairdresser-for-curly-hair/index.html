What To Ask Your Hairdresser For Curly Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. When it looks good, it looks really good. But when it's unruly and frizzy, it can be a nightmare to deal with. That's why it's important to find a hairdresser who understands curly hair and knows how to work with it. When you're going to the salon, it's crucial to know what to ask for to get the best results. In this post, we'll give you some tips on what to ask your hairdresser for curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair requires special care and attention. Not all hairdressers are trained in working with curly hair, so it's important to find someone who is. When you're looking for a hairdresser, ask if they have experience working with curly hair, and if they have any photos of curly hairstyles they've done. This will give you a good idea of their skill level.

How to

When you're at the salon, here are some things you should ask your hairdresser for curly hair:

Step by step

  1. Ask for a consultation before your appointment. This will give you a chance to discuss your hair type and any concerns you have with your hairdresser.
  2. Ask for a dry cut. Curly hair is best cut when it's dry, so your hairdresser can see the natural shape of your curls.
  3. Ask for layers. Layers can help give your curls more definition and make them look less heavy.
  4. Ask for a deep conditioning treatment. Curly hair tends to be dry, so a deep conditioning treatment can help restore moisture and shine.
  5. Ask for a diffuser. Using a diffuser on your hairdryer can help enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  6. Ask for styling tips. Your hairdresser should be able to show you how to style your hair at home to achieve the same look they created in the salon.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for working with curly hair:

  • Don't brush your hair when it's dry. This can cause frizz and breakage. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible. Heat can damage your hair and make it more prone to frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much. This can cause frizz and disrupt the natural shape of your curls.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curly hair:

  • How often should I wash my curly hair?
  • What's the best way to dry my curly hair?
  • How can I make my curls last longer?
  • What's the best way to style curly hair?

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care and attention. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling as much as possible.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much.

By following these tips and asking the right questions, you can ensure that you get the best results possible when it comes to your curly hair. Remember, finding a hairdresser who understands curly hair is key to achieving the look you want.
