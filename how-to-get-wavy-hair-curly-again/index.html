How To Get Wavy Hair Curly Again

Written by Christine Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your wavy hair falling flat and losing its curl? Don't worry, you're not alone! Many people struggle with maintaining their curly hair, but with the right techniques and products, you can get your wavy hair curly again. In this article, we'll go over how to achieve curly hair from wavy hair, step-by-step, and some tips and tricks to keep your curls bouncy and beautiful.

Description

Wavy hair can be beautiful, but sometimes you want more volume and definition. Curly hair is a great way to achieve that look, but it takes some effort and patience. You'll need to use the right products and techniques to get your hair to curl, and you'll need to take care of your curls to keep them looking fresh.

How to

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before you begin styling your hair, make sure it's clean and free of any buildup. Use a clarifying shampoo to remove any product residue or oil from your scalp. If you have dry hair, use a moisturizing shampoo to hydrate your strands.

Step 2: Apply a Curl-Enhancing Product

Once you've washed your hair, apply a curl-enhancing product to damp hair. This will help define your curls and keep them bouncy throughout the day. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, or argan oil to moisturize your hair and reduce frizz.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

Instead of using a regular blow dryer, use a diffuser attachment to dry your hair. This will help keep your curls intact and prevent frizz. Make sure to use a low heat setting and don't over-dry your hair. You want your curls to be slightly damp when you finish drying them.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, scrunch it with your hands to help define your curls. Don't use a brush or comb, as this can separate your curls and make them frizzy. Just use your hands to gently scrunch your hair from the ends to the roots.

Step 5: Set Your Curls

To help your curls stay in place, use a setting product like a hair gel or mousse. Apply the product to your hair using your hands, scrunching it in from the ends to the roots. This will help define your curls and keep them bouncy all day.

Tips

- Don't wash your hair every day. Curly hair tends to be drier than straight hair, so you don't want to strip your hair of its natural oils by washing it too often.

- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet. This will help prevent breakage and keep your curls intact.

- Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to help prevent frizz and keep your curls looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: Can I use heat to style my curls?

A: Yes, you can use heat to style your curls, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray first. Also, don't use too much heat or you risk damaging your hair.

Q: How often should I trim my curly hair?

A: It's recommended that you trim your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Q: Can I still straighten my curly hair?

A: Yes, you can still straighten your curly hair, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray and don't use too much heat. Also, be aware that straightening your hair too often can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care

To keep your curls looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized, and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often. Also, make sure to protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside.

By following these tips and techniques, you can get your wavy hair curly again and keep your curls looking beautiful and bouncy. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type. With a little effort and care, you can have gorgeous curls that will turn heads wherever you go!
