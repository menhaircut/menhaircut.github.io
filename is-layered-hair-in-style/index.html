Description

If you're considering getting a new hairstyle, you might be wondering if layered hair is in style. The good news is that layered hair is still very much on-trend in 2023. Layered hair is a versatile look that can work for a variety of hair types, lengths, and textures. Whether you have long, short, curly, or straight hair, there's a layered haircut that can work for you.

Layered haircuts involve cutting the hair at different lengths to create depth and movement. This can help add volume to thin hair and create definition for thick hair. Whether you want a subtle, face-framing layer or a more dramatic, choppy cut, there's a layered hairstyle that can suit your preferences.

How To Get Layered Hair

If you're interested in getting layered hair, your first step should be to find a qualified hairstylist who has experience with layered haircuts. Look for someone who can provide you with examples of their work and who can give you personalized recommendations based on your hair type and face shape.

Before your appointment, you may want to gather some inspiration photos to show your stylist. This can help them get a sense of the look you're going for and can ensure that you're both on the same page.

Step-By-Step Guide in Layering Your Hair

The process of layering hair can vary depending on the desired outcome, but generally, the following steps are involved:

  1. The stylist will section off your hair and determine where to start cutting
  2. They will use scissors to cut the hair at different lengths
  3. They may use a razor or texturizing shears to create more movement and texture
  4. The stylist will then style your hair to show off the layers and ensure that they're even.

Tips When Getting Layered Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting layered hair:

  • Consider your face shape: Different layering techniques can flatter different face shapes. For example, long layers can elongate a round face, while shorter layers can add width to a narrow face.
  • Think about maintenance: Layered haircuts can require more upkeep than a one-length cut, so make sure you're prepared to style your hair regularly.
  • Communicate with your stylist: Be clear about the look you want and be open to their suggestions. They can help you achieve a layered haircut that works for your hair type and lifestyle.

FAQs About Layered Hair

What hair types can have layered hair?

Layered hair can work for a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, curly, and coily hair. Your stylist can determine the best layering technique for your hair texture.

Can layered hair add volume?

Yes! Layering can help add volume and movement to thin or limp hair.

How often should I get my layered hair cut?

This can vary depending on your hair type and how quickly it grows, but generally, you should plan on getting a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and structure of your layered haircut.

Hair Care for Layered Hair

To keep your layered hair looking its best, you'll want to use hair care products that nourish and protect your strands. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it harder to maintain your layers
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your strands healthy and hydrated
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

Overall, layered hair is definitely still in style in 2023. Whether you're looking for a subtle, face-framing layer or a more dramatic choppy cut, there's a layered haircut that can work for your hair type and lifestyle. By following the tips and recommendations above, you can rock a layered hairstyle that's both stylish and easy to maintain.
