What Kind Of Hairstyle Suits A Round Face

Written by Christine Oct 15, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a round face, you may be wondering what kind of hairstyle will flatter your features. While there is no one-size-fits-all answer, there are certain styles that tend to work well for round faces. In this article, we’ll explore some of the best options and offer tips for making the most of your hair.

Description: What is a Round Face?

First, let’s define what we mean by a round face. This face shape is characterized by a wide forehead, full cheeks, and a rounded chin. It is often described as having a circular appearance, with the width and length of the face being roughly equal. Some celebrities with round faces include Chrissy Teigen, Emma Stone, and Selena Gomez.

How to: Choosing the Right Hairstyle for a Round Face

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a round face, the goal is to create the illusion of a more oval or elongated shape. This can be achieved by adding volume at the crown, creating height with layers, or opting for a side part. Here are some of the best styles to consider:

1. Long Layers

Long layers that frame the face can help to create the illusion of length. They can be styled straight or wavy, and can be parted in the middle or on the side. This style works well for those with thick hair, as it helps to add movement and texture.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to draw attention away from the width of the face and create a more balanced look. They can be blended into the rest of the hair or cut bluntly for a more dramatic effect.

3. Bob with Volume

A bob that is chin-length or slightly longer can work well for those with round faces, as long as there is some volume at the crown. This can be achieved with a bit of teasing or by using volumizing products. A side part can also help to create a more elongated shape.

Step by Step: How to Style Hair for a Round Face

Once you’ve chosen a hairstyle that works well for your round face, it’s important to know how to style it properly. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Blow Dry with a Round Brush

When blow-drying your hair, use a round brush to create volume at the crown. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, pulling the brush up and away from your face as you go.

2. Use Volumizing Products

Products like mousse, volumizing spray, and dry shampoo can all help to add body and texture to your hair. Apply them to the roots and mid-lengths, then use your fingers to work them through the hair.

3. Avoid Heavy, Blunt Bangs

While side-swept bangs can be flattering for round faces, heavy, blunt bangs can actually emphasize the width of the face. If you want to try bangs, opt for a wispy, layered style instead.

Tips: Making the Most of Your Hair

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle and styling it properly, there are a few other tips to keep in mind when it comes to hair care for round faces:

1. Consider Face-Framing Highlights

Face-framing highlights can help to draw attention to your best features and create a more balanced look. Talk to your stylist about adding highlights that complement your skin tone and eye color.

2. Experiment with Accessories

Accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves can all add interest and dimension to your hair. Try different styles to see what works best for you.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, don’t be afraid to embrace it! Adding layers can help to enhance your texture and create a more dynamic look.

FAQ: Common Questions About Hairstyles for Round Faces

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but it’s best to avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can emphasize the width of your face. Instead, opt for a loose, messy ponytail or a low bun.

Q: Are there any hairstyles I should avoid if I have a round face?

A: Yes, avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, like big, bouncy curls. Also, avoid heavy, blunt bangs and super-short styles that don’t provide any length or height.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It depends on the length and style of your hair, but most people should aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep their hair looking healthy and fresh.

Hair Care: Taking Care of Your Hair

Finally, it’s important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

1. Use the Right Products

Choose products that are formulated for your hair type and texture. This can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent damage.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Use a heat protectant spray or serum when styling your hair with heat tools like flat irons and curling wands. This can help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking shiny.

3. Get Regular Trims

As mentioned earlier, getting regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Conclusion

There are many different hairstyles that can work well for those with round faces. Whether you opt for long layers, side-swept bangs, or a bob with volume, the key is to create the illusion of a more elongated shape. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.
