Does Relaxed Hair Grow Faster

Written by Eveline Sep 08, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have relaxed hair, you may have heard the myth that relaxed hair grows faster than natural hair. But is there any truth to this claim? In this article, we'll explore whether relaxed hair actually grows faster and provide some tips for healthy hair growth.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Grow Relaxed Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide for Healthy Hair Growth
  • Tips for Promoting Hair Growth
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Relaxed Hair

Description

Relaxed hair refers to hair that has been chemically treated to remove its natural curl pattern. This process is also known as "texturizing" or "perming." The chemicals used in relaxers break down the protein bonds in the hair, allowing it to be straightened.

Many people believe that relaxed hair grows faster than natural hair because it appears longer due to its straightened state. However, this is a myth. Hair growth is determined by genetics, not by whether it is relaxed or natural.

How to Grow Relaxed Hair

While relaxed hair does not grow faster than natural hair, there are things you can do to promote healthy hair growth. Here are some tips:

1. Keep Your Scalp Clean

A healthy scalp is essential for healthy hair growth. Be sure to wash your hair and scalp regularly to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.

2. Moisturize Your Hair

Relaxed hair tends to be dry and prone to breakage. To prevent this, use a moisturizing conditioner after every wash. You can also use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated between washes.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protective styles such as braids, twists, and buns can help to prevent breakage and promote healthy hair growth. Be sure to use gentle hair ties and avoid styles that pull on your hair too tightly.

Step-by-Step Guide for Healthy Hair Growth

Here is a step-by-step guide for promoting healthy hair growth:

Step 1: Cleanse Your Scalp

Wash your hair and scalp with a gentle shampoo to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup.

Step 2: Condition Your Hair

Apply a moisturizing conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing.

Step 3: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to your hair to keep it hydrated.

Step 4: Protect Your Hair

Style your hair in a protective style such as braids or twists to prevent breakage.

Tips for Promoting Hair Growth

Here are some additional tips for promoting healthy hair growth:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals is essential for healthy hair growth. Be sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and protein.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and cause breakage. Try to avoid using them as much as possible.

3. Protect Your Hair at Night

Wear a silk or satin bonnet or scarf to protect your hair while you sleep. This will help to prevent breakage and preserve your hairstyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does relaxed hair grow faster than natural hair?

No, hair growth is determined by genetics, not by whether it is relaxed or natural.

How can I promote healthy hair growth?

To promote healthy hair growth, be sure to keep your scalp clean, moisturize your hair, protect your hair with gentle styles, eat a healthy diet, and avoid heat styling.

Hair Care for Relaxed Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for relaxed hair:

1. Use a Mild Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle and sulfate-free to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Trim Your Ends

Trimming your ends regularly can help to prevent split ends and breakage.

In conclusion, relaxed hair does not grow faster than natural hair. However, by following these tips for healthy hair growth and caring for your relaxed hair, you can promote strong, healthy, and beautiful hair.
