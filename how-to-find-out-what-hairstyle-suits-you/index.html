How To Find Out What Hairstyle Suits You

Written by Oliver Jul 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle? Do you want to try something new but don't know where to start? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll show you how to find out what hairstyle suits you.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

There are several ways to determine what hairstyle suits you. Here are a few methods you can try:

Look at Your Face Shape

Your face shape plays a significant role in determining what hairstyle suits you. Determine if you have a round, oval, square, heart, or diamond-shaped face. Once you know your face shape, research hairstyles that suit your face shape.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type, such as curly, straight, or wavy, also affects what hairstyle suits you. Certain hairstyles work better with specific hair types. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to consider a layered haircut to enhance your curls.

Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in determining what hairstyle suits you. If you have an active lifestyle, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that doesn't require a lot of styling. Alternatively, if you work in a professional setting, you may want a more polished hairstyle.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to determine what hairstyle suits you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. Once you have your measurements, compare them to determine your face shape.

Step 2: Research Hairstyles

Once you know your face shape, research hairstyles that suit your face shape. Look for inspiration on social media platforms like Instagram and Pinterest.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

Consider your hair type when selecting a hairstyle. If you have curly hair, look for hairstyles that enhance your curls. If you have straight hair, consider a sleek, straight hairstyle.

Step 4: Think About Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in determining what hairstyle suits you. If you have an active lifestyle, consider a low-maintenance hairstyle. If you work in a professional setting, look for a polished hairstyle that is suitable for the workplace.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you find a hairstyle that suits you:

  • Consider your skin tone when selecting a hair color.
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairdresser to help them understand what you're looking for.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

What if I don't know my face shape?

There are several online resources that can help you determine your face shape. Alternatively, you can ask your hairdresser for assistance.

What if I don't like the hairstyle I chose?

It's okay to experiment with different hairstyles. If you don't like the hairstyle you chose, you can always change it.

Hair Care

Once you've found a hairstyle that suits you, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its appearance. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, finding a hairstyle that suits you can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By considering your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle, you can find a hairstyle that enhances your appearance.
