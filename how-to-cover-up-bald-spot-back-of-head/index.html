How To Cover Up Bald Spot Back Of Head Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 29, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you struggling with a bald spot at the back of your head? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people experience hair loss or thinning, and it can be a source of insecurity for some. Fortunately, there are several ways to hide or cover up a bald spot. In this article, we'll explore some of the most effective methods.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Cover Up Bald Spot Back of Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Up Bald Spots
  • FAQs

How to Cover Up Bald Spot Back of Head

There are several ways to cover up a bald spot at the back of your head:

Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are tiny fibers that attach to your existing hair to create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair. You can sprinkle the fibers onto your bald spot and then gently pat them down. They come in a range of colors and are easy to apply.

Spray-On Hair

Spray-on hair is a temporary solution that can last up to a week. It's a type of hair dye that you spray onto your scalp to create the appearance of hair. It comes in a range of colors and can be applied quickly and easily.

Hair Pieces

Hair pieces, also known as hair toupees or wigs, are a more permanent solution. They come in a range of styles and colors and can be custom-made to fit your head. Hair pieces require regular maintenance, but they can provide a natural-looking solution to bald spots.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're using hair fibers or spray-on hair, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a product in a color that matches your existing hair.
  2. Clean and dry your hair and scalp.
  3. Apply the product to your bald spot.
  4. Gently pat the product down to create a natural-looking finish.

If you're using a hair piece, follow these steps:

  1. Choose a hair piece that matches your existing hair.
  2. Have the hair piece custom-made to fit your head.
  3. Carefully attach the hair piece to your scalp using clips or adhesive.
  4. Style the hair piece to blend in with your existing hair.

Tips for Covering Up Bald Spots

Here are some additional tips for covering up bald spots:

  • Choose a product that matches your existing hair color.
  • Apply the product sparingly for a natural-looking finish.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.
  • Consider consulting with a hair stylist or dermatologist for additional advice.

FAQs

Q: Are there any permanent solutions for bald spots?

A: Yes, hair transplants can provide a permanent solution for bald spots. However, they can be expensive and require surgery.

Q: Can I use regular hair dye to cover up a bald spot?

A: It's not recommended. Regular hair dye is designed for use on hair, not the scalp, and can irritate your skin.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently cover up your bald spot and feel good about your appearance.
