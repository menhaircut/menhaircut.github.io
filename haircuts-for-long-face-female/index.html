Haircuts For Long Face Female The Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

A long face can be a beautiful feature, but it can also be challenging when it comes to finding the right haircut that accentuates your features. Whether you have wavy, straight, or curly hair, there are plenty of haircuts that can flatter your long face. In this guide, we’ll go over some of the best haircuts for long face females, along with tips on how to care for your hair to keep it looking its best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Long Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut
  • FAQs About Haircuts for Long Face Females
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Face Females

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Long Face

Before you head to the salon, it’s important to understand what type of haircut will flatter your long face. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a cut that adds width to your face. This can be achieved with layers, curls, or waves.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face appear even longer.
  • Consider a side-swept fringe or bangs to balance out your forehead.
  • Opt for styles that add volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of a shorter face.

Best Haircuts for Long Face Females

Now that you know what to look for, here are some of the best haircuts for long face females:

  • Long Layers: Long layers add volume and movement to your hair while also framing your face.
  • Textured Bob: A textured bob that hits just above your shoulders can add width to your face and create the illusion of a shorter face.
  • Soft Waves: Soft waves that start at your cheekbones can help balance out your face shape.
  • Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs that hit just below your eyebrows can help balance out a long forehead.
  • Curly Bob: A curly bob that falls just above your shoulders can add volume and width to your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut

Once you’ve chosen your ideal haircut, it’s time to head to the salon. Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

  1. Bring a photo of your desired haircut to show your stylist.
  2. Discuss your face shape and hair type with your stylist.
  3. Ask your stylist to add layers or volume to create width in your face.
  4. Don’t be afraid to ask questions or voice any concerns you may have.
  5. Once your haircut is finished, ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have your new haircut, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Try different styling techniques, such as braids or updos, to switch up your look.

FAQs About Haircuts for Long Face Females

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a long face?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a long face. Just be sure to add layers to create volume and movement.

Q: Are bangs a good option for long face females?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great option for balancing out a long forehead.

Q: What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

A: Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin, as they can make your face appear even longer.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Females

Finally, here are a few hair care tips to keep your long face looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can cause damage and frizz.
  • Invest in a quality heat protectant spray to use before using heat styling tools.

With these tips and haircuts for long face females, you’ll be able to find the perfect style to flatter your features and keep your hair looking its best.
