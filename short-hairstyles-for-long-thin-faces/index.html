Short Hairstyles For Long Thin Faces A Guide For 2023

If you have a long and thin face, finding the right hairstyle can be a bit of a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your face shape and enhance your features, while also being easy to maintain and style on a daily basis. In this article, we'll be sharing some of the best short hairstyles for long thin faces, along with tips on how to care for and style your hair. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Long Thin Faces

When it comes to choosing a short hairstyle for a long and thin face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First and foremost, you want to choose a style that adds volume and width to your face, as this will help balance out your features. Styles that are too straight and flat can make your face look even longer and thinner.

Some great short hairstyles for long thin faces include:

1. Bob

A classic bob is a great choice for long and thin faces, as it adds volume and shape to your hair. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer style that falls just below your jawline. If you have fine hair, consider adding some layers to give your hair more texture and movement.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is another great option for long and thin faces, as it adds volume and dimension to your hair. This style is particularly flattering if you have delicate features, as it draws attention to your eyes and cheekbones. You can opt for a tousled, messy look or a sleek and polished style, depending on your personal preference.

3. Shag

A shaggy, layered cut is a great choice if you have fine hair, as it adds volume and movement to your locks. This style is particularly flattering if you have a long neck, as it draws attention to your collarbones and shoulders. You can opt for a messy, bedhead look or a more polished style, depending on your mood and the occasion.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Long Thin Faces

Now that you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your long and thin face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling short hair:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. You can use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and moisturize your locks, then towel-dry your hair gently to remove excess moisture.

2. Apply styling products.

Next, apply your favorite styling products to your hair. This could include a volumizing mousse, a texturizing spray, or a smoothing serum, depending on your hair type and the style you're going for. Work the products through your hair evenly, using your fingers to distribute them from roots to ends.

3. Use a blow dryer.

Once you've applied your styling products, use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume, while using the dryer to smooth your hair and set your style in place. You can also use a diffuser attachment to create waves and curls in your hair, if desired.

4. Finish with hairspray.

Finally, finish your style with a light mist of hairspray to hold everything in place. You can also use a texturizing spray or a dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling Short Hair for Long Thin Faces

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when maintaining and styling short hair for long and thin faces:

1. Trim your hair regularly.

To keep your short haircut looking fresh and stylish, it's important to trim your hair regularly. Aim to get a trim every 4-6 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

2. Use heat protectant products.

If you're using heat tools to style your hair, such as a blow dryer or flat iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Experiment with different styles.

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks to find the one that works best for you. You can try out different products, tools, and techniques to create different textures and shapes in your hair.

FAQs About Short Hairstyles for Long Thin Faces

1. Can I still wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a long and thin face?

Yes, you can definitely wear bangs with a short hairstyle for a long and thin face! Just make sure to choose a style that flatters your face shape and doesn't create too much weight or bulk around your forehead.

2. What if I have curly or wavy hair?

If you have curly or wavy hair, you can still rock a short hairstyle for a long and thin face! Just make sure to choose a style that works with your natural texture, and use products and tools to enhance and define your curls or waves.

3. Can I still wear my hair up with a short hairstyle?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short hairstyle! Try experimenting with different updos and half-up styles to find the one that works best for you.

Hair Care Tips for Long Thin Faces

Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind if you have a long and thin face:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Choose a shampoo and conditioner that's gentle and moisturizing, as this will help keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Avoid over-styling your hair.

Try to avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools and products, as this can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage and split ends.

3. Protect your hair from the sun.

If you're spending time outdoors, make sure to protect your hair from the sun's harmful UV rays. You can wear a hat or scarf, or use a hair product with SPF protection.

In conclusion, there are many great short hairstyles for long thin faces that can flatter your features and enhance your natural beauty. By choosing the right style for your face shape, using the right products and tools, and taking good care of your hair, you can create a look that's both stylish and easy to maintain. Happy styling!
