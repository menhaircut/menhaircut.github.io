How To Cut Back Of Hair With Clippers Pixie

Written by Oliver Oct 31, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to maintain, the pixie cut is a great option. However, if you're looking to keep your hair short and sleek, you'll need to know how to trim the back of your hair with clippers. In this article, we'll give you step-by-step instructions on how to cut the back of your hair with clippers pixie style.

Description

The pixie cut is a short hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It's a low-maintenance hairstyle that's easy to style and maintain. The back of the hair is typically shorter than the front and sides, which is why it's important to know how to trim it properly with clippers. In this article, we'll show you how to cut the back of your hair with clippers pixie style.

How to Cut Back of Hair with Clippers Pixie: Step by Step

Step 1: Prepare Your Clippers

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to make sure your clippers are clean and oiled. You'll also need to choose the right guard size for the length you want to cut.

Step 2: Start at the Nape of Your Neck

Begin by cutting the hair at the nape of your neck. Start with the longest guard size and work your way down to a shorter guard size until you reach your desired length. Make sure to use slow, steady movements to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

Step 3: Move Upward

Continue moving upward, section by section, until you've trimmed the entire back of your hair. Check your progress in a mirror to make sure you're cutting evenly.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Once you've trimmed the back of your hair, you'll need to blend it with the rest of your hair. Use the clippers without a guard to trim around the edges and create a seamless transition from the back to the sides and top of your hair.

Step 5: Clean Up

After you've finished trimming your hair, use a comb and scissors to clean up any stray hairs that you may have missed.

Tips for Cutting Back of Hair with Clippers Pixie

  • Start with a longer guard size and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use slow, steady movements to ensure an even cut.
  • Check your progress in a mirror to make sure you're cutting evenly.
  • Blend the back of your hair with the sides and top to create a seamless transition.
  • Use a comb and scissors to clean up any stray hairs you may have missed.

FAQ

How often should I trim the back of my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly it starts to look unkempt. Generally, it's a good idea to trim the back of your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Can I cut the back of my hair myself?

Yes, you can cut the back of your hair yourself with clippers. However, if you're not confident in your abilities, it's always best to go to a professional stylist.

What guard size should I use?

The guard size you use will depend on the length you want to cut. Generally, a #2 or #3 guard will give you a short, pixie-style cut.

Hair Care

After you've cut the back of your hair with clippers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's designed for your hair type, and avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair. Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
