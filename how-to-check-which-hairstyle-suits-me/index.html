How To Check Which Hairstyle Suits Me Tips And Tricks

Written by Petter Jun 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old hairstyle every day? Do you want to try something new and fresh but not sure which hairstyle suits you? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll provide some tips and tricks on how to check which hairstyle suits you best.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Check Which Hairstyle Suits Me
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care

How to Check Which Hairstyle Suits Me

Before you start experimenting with different hairstyles, it's important to understand your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining which hairstyle suits you best. There are six face shapes - round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Once you determine your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that complements it.

Another important factor to consider is your hair texture. If you have fine hair, you might want to opt for a short and layered haircut to add volume. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a longer hairstyle with layers can help to reduce bulk and add movement.

Lastly, your personal style should also be taken into consideration. Do you prefer a classic and polished look or a more edgy and modern vibe? Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and make you feel confident and comfortable.

Step by Step Guide

Now that you have a basic understanding of what to consider before choosing a hairstyle, let's dive into the step-by-step guide:

  1. Identify your face shape by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Research different hairstyles that complement your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist to get their expert opinion and recommendations.
  4. Consider your lifestyle and daily routine - do you have time to style your hair every day?
  5. Try on wigs or use virtual makeover tools to visualize different hairstyles on yourself.
  6. Experiment with small changes, such as adding bangs or layers, before committing to a drastic haircut.
  7. Take photos of your new hairstyle from different angles to see how it looks.
  8. Adjust your hair care routine to maintain your new hairstyle.
  9. Have fun and don't be afraid to try something new!

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to keep in mind when choosing a new hairstyle:

  • Consider your skin tone when choosing hair color - warm tones look best with warm hair colors, while cool tones complement cooler shades.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help - a hairstylist can provide valuable insight and recommendations based on their expertise.
  • Look for inspiration on social media and in magazines - save photos of hairstyles you love to show your hairstylist.
  • Be realistic about your hair type and texture - a hairstyle that looks great on someone else might not work for you.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing a hairstyle:

1. How do I know if a certain hairstyle will look good on me?

The best way to know if a certain hairstyle will look good on you is to try it out! Use virtual makeover tools or try on wigs to visualize different hairstyles on yourself before committing to a haircut.

2. Can I pull off a short haircut if I have a round face?

A short haircut can work for a round face, but it's important to choose a style that adds height and volume at the crown to elongate the face. A pixie cut or a layered bob can be flattering on a round face.

3. How often should I change my hairstyle?

It's entirely up to you! Some people prefer to stick with the same hairstyle for years, while others like to switch it up every few months. As long as you're happy and confident with your hairstyle, that's all that matters.

Hair Care

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to adjust your hair care routine to maintain it. Here are some tips:

  • Use hair products that are specifically formulated for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Consider getting a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Choosing a new hairstyle can be exciting and fun, but it can also be overwhelming. By following these tips and tricks, you can find a hairstyle that suits you and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Happy hairstyling!
