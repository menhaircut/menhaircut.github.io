How To Refresh Curly Hair After Sleeping On It

Written by Bardi Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Waking up with a head full of curls can be both a blessing and a curse. While your curls may look fabulous right after washing and styling, sleeping on them can cause them to lose their definition and become frizzy. However, refreshing your curls in the morning doesn't have to be difficult. In this guide, we'll show you how to refresh curly hair after sleeping on it, step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Step 1: Spritz Your Hair with Water
  • Step 2: Apply a Curl Refresher
  • Step 3: Use a Diffuser to Dry Your Hair
  • Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Curly Hair Care Tips

Step 1: Spritz Your Hair with Water

The first thing you should do when refreshing your curly hair in the morning is to spritz it with water. Fill a spray bottle with water and mist your hair all over. This will help to revive your curls and add some moisture back into your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Refresher

Next, apply a curl refresher to your hair. This can be a leave-in conditioner, a curl cream, or a curl spray. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair and that contain ingredients that will help to define your curls and reduce frizz. Apply the product all over your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser to Dry Your Hair

Using a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer can help to dry your hair without causing frizz. Attach the diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to a low heat setting. Gently cup your curls in the diffuser and hold it close to your hair, moving it around to dry all areas of your head.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your curls. This will help to revive them and give them some bounce. You can also use a wide-tooth comb or a pick to gently separate your curls and add volume.

Tips for Refreshing Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for refreshing your curly hair:

  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use an old t-shirt or a microfiber towel.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or a pick to separate your curls.
  • Try using a hair oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I refresh my curly hair?

It really depends on your hair type and how quickly it becomes frizzy. Some people may need to refresh their curls every day, while others can go a few days without refreshing. Experiment with different routines and products to find what works best for you.

Can I refresh my curly hair without washing it?

Absolutely! You don't need to wash your hair every time you refresh it. Simply spritz your hair with water and apply a curl refresher, then use a diffuser to dry your hair and scrunch your curls.

What should I do if my curls are still frizzy after refreshing them?

If your curls are still frizzy after refreshing them, try applying a bit more curl refresher or hair oil to your hair. You can also try using a hair gel or mousse to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Curly Hair Care Tips

If you have curly hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to add moisture and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as this can weigh down your curls and make them look flat.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

By following these tips and refreshing your curls regularly, you can keep your curly hair looking healthy, defined, and beautiful.
