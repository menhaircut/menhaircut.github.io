How To Slick Back Natural Hair Into A Ponytail

Written by Frank Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish yet effortless hairstyle for your natural hair? Look no further! Slicking back your natural hair into a ponytail is a classic and timeless look that can be dressed up or down for any occasion. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to achieve the perfect slicked back ponytail.

Description

Slicking back natural hair into a ponytail is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn on any occasion. It is a great option for those who prefer a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to maintain. Slicking back natural hair into a ponytail also helps to keep the hair out of your face, making it perfect for work or school.

How to Slick Back Natural Hair into a Ponytail

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before slicking back your natural hair into a ponytail, it is important to start with clean and dry hair. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and let it air dry or use a blow dryer to dry it completely.

Step 2: Apply Gel or Edge Control

Apply a small amount of gel or edge control to the edges of your hairline. Use a brush or comb to smooth the gel or edge control into your hair, making sure it is evenly distributed.

Step 3: Brush Hair Back

Using a brush or comb, brush your hair back towards the crown of your head. Make sure to brush your hair back in a smooth and even motion, avoiding any bumps or lumps.

Step 4: Secure Ponytail

Once your hair is brushed back, secure it into a ponytail using a hair tie. Make sure to wrap the hair tie around your hair tightly, to keep the ponytail secure.

Step 5: Smooth Edges

Using a brush or comb, smooth any flyaways or baby hairs around your edges. You can use a small amount of gel or edge control to help keep the edges smooth.

Tips

Here are some helpful tips to ensure that your slicked back ponytail stays in place:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to make it less noticeable.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tight, as this can cause breakage and damage to your hair.
  • Use a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to help protect your hair from breakage.
  • Experiment with different hair textures, such as braids or twists, to add some variety to your slicked back ponytail.

FAQ

Can I slick back my natural hair if it is short?

Yes, you can still slick back your natural hair even if it is short. Use a small amount of gel or edge control to smooth the hair back, and secure it into a small ponytail or bun.

How often should I wash my hair if I slick it back into a ponytail?

It is recommended to wash your hair at least once a week, or as needed. Avoid using heavy oils or products that can build up on your scalp and hair.

Can I wear my slicked back ponytail to a formal event?

Yes, a slicked back ponytail can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. You can add some decorative hair accessories or pull some strands of hair out to create a softer look.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your natural hair, especially when slicking it back into a ponytail. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment at least once a month to help nourish and strengthen your hair.

Now that you know how to slick back your natural hair into a ponytail, you can achieve a sleek and stylish look in no time. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different styles to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
