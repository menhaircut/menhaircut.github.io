How To Make Oblong Face Look Shorter Tips And Tricks

Written by Luffy Nov 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oblong face shape, you may feel like your face looks too long and narrow. However, there are several simple tricks you can use to make your face appear shorter and more balanced. In this article, we'll explore some expert tips and techniques for making your oblong face look shorter and more proportionate.

Description: What is an Oblong Face Shape?

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long, narrow shape with a high forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow chin. This face shape is often referred to as "rectangular" or "oblong," and it's quite common among both men and women.

How to Make Oblong Face Look Shorter: Step by Step

Here are some simple steps you can follow to make your oblong face appear shorter and more balanced:

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

One of the easiest ways to make your face appear shorter is to choose a flattering haircut. Look for styles that add volume and width to the sides of your face, while minimizing volume at the top. Some great options for oblong faces include:

  • Layered cuts
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Soft waves or curls

Avoid styles that are too long or too straight, as these can make your face look even longer.

Step 2: Contour Your Face

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create the illusion of depth and shadow. By contouring your face, you can make your features look more balanced and proportionate. To contour an oblong face, focus on adding shadow to your forehead and chin, while highlighting your cheekbones. Use a contouring powder or cream that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone, and blend well.

Step 3: Choose the Right Accessories

The right accessories can also help to balance out your face shape. Look for earrings, necklaces, and scarves that add volume and width to the sides of your face. Avoid accessories that draw attention to your forehead or chin, such as chokers or headbands.

Step 4: Consider Facial Hair

If you're a man with an oblong face, consider growing a beard or mustache to add volume to your chin area. Just be sure to keep your facial hair neat and trimmed, and choose a style that flatters your face shape.

Tips for Making Oblong Face Look Shorter

Here are some additional tips and tricks for making your oblong face appear shorter and more balanced:

  • Choose glasses with a wide, rectangular frame to add width to your face.
  • Avoid center parts, which can make your face look longer.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Try a darker hair color to create the illusion of depth.
  • Experiment with bold lip colors to draw attention away from your forehead and chin.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of makeup should I use for an oblong face?

For an oblong face, focus on contouring your forehead and chin while highlighting your cheekbones. Use a contouring powder or cream that's one or two shades darker than your skin tone, and blend well.

What kind of hairstyles should I avoid with an oblong face?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too straight, as these can make your face look even longer. Instead, look for styles that add volume and width to the sides of your face, while minimizing volume at the top.

Hair Care for Oblong Faces

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind if you have an oblong face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy styling products, which can weigh down your hair and make your face look longer.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and full.
  • Consider using a root-lifting spray to add volume to your hair at the roots.

By following these simple tips and tricks, you can make your oblong face appear shorter and more proportionate. Whether you're choosing a new haircut, experimenting with makeup, or trying out new accessories, there are plenty of ways to flatter your face shape and feel confident in your skin.
