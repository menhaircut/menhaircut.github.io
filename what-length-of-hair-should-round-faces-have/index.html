What Length Of Hair Should Round Faces Have

Written by Aprilia Sep 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face? It can be challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, but don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will discuss what length of hair works best for round faces, and provide tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hairstyle
  • Tips for Styling Round Face Hair
  • FAQs

Description

Round faces are characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer face shape. The right haircut can help to elongate your face and make it appear slimmer. The length of your hair can play a significant role in creating this illusion, so it's essential to choose the right length for your face shape.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best length of hair for round faces, let's first determine if you have a round face shape. Here's a simple guide to help you determine your face shape:

  • Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back away from your face.
  • Examine your face shape to determine if it is round, square, oval, heart, or diamond-shaped.
  • If your face is widest at the cheeks and has a rounded chin, you have a round face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding the Perfect Hairstyle

Now that you know you have a round face shape, let's discuss the best length of hair for your face. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect hairstyle:

  1. Consider your hair texture and thickness. Thin, fine hair may not look as good in longer styles, while thick hair can hold up to longer lengths.
  2. Look for hairstyles that elongate the face, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.
  3. Consider a length that falls just below the chin or longer. Longer hair can help to elongate the face, while shorter hair can accentuate the roundness of the face.
  4. Avoid blunt cuts that stop at the chin, as this can make the face appear wider.
  5. Consider adding layers to your hair for movement and dimension.
  6. Try a side part to create the illusion of a longer face shape.
  7. Consider adding volume at the crown of your head to elongate the face.
  8. Play around with different hairstyles to find what works best for you. Don't be afraid to experiment and try new things!

Tips for Styling Round Face Hair

Now that you've found the perfect length for your hair, here are some additional tips for styling round face hair:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height and volume at the crown of your head.
  • Try a side part to elongate your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or blunt cuts that end at the chin.
  • Consider adding layers to your hair for movement and dimension.
  • Try a messy bun or ponytail to create the illusion of length.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best length of hair for round faces:

1. Can I have short hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can have short hair if you have a round face. However, it's essential to choose a style that flatters your face shape. Consider a short bob with layers or a pixie cut with side-swept bangs.

2. Can I have long hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can have long hair if you have a round face. In fact, longer hair can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Consider adding layers or a side-swept fringe to your long hair for added dimension.

3. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or blunt cuts that end at the chin. Instead, opt for hairstyles that elongate your face, such as long layers or a side-swept fringe.

Hair Care Tips for Round Face Hair

Now that you've found the perfect hairstyle for your round face, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for round face hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using heated styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair mask to nourish and hydrate your hair.

In conclusion, the best length of hair for round faces is a length that falls just below the chin or longer. Longer hair can help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Consider adding layers, a side-swept fringe, or volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of length. Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles until you find what works best for you!
