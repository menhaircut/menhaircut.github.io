How To Cut A Mullet Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle, a mullet might be just what you need. This classic '80s haircut has made a comeback in recent years, and many people are now opting to cut their own mullet at home.

Description

A mullet is a hairstyle that features short hair on the front and sides of the head and long hair at the back. It's a versatile cut that can be customized to suit your preferences, whether you want a subtle or dramatic look.

How to Cut a Mullet Yourself

Before you begin, you'll need a few tools: a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here's how to cut a mullet yourself:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

It's best to start with clean, dry hair. Wash and condition your hair as usual, and then let it air dry or use a blow dryer. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section your hair

Use a comb to section your hair into two parts: the top and the bottom. The top section should be about one-third of your hair, and the bottom section should be two-thirds.

Step 3: Cut the top section

Start with the top section of your hair. Comb it straight up, and then use your scissors to cut the length you want. For a subtle mullet, you can cut just a little bit off. For a more dramatic look, you can cut more.

Step 4: Cut the sides

Next, move on to the sides of your hair. Comb the hair down towards your ear, and then cut it to the length you want. Repeat on the other side.

Step 5: Cut the back

Finally, it's time to cut the back of your hair. Comb it straight down, and then cut it to the length you want. For a classic mullet, you can leave it long and shaggy. For a more modern look, you can cut it shorter.

Step 6: Blend the layers

Use your scissors to blend the layers together. You don't want any harsh lines or choppy edges. Instead, aim for a soft, natural look.

Step 7: Check your work

Use a mirror to check your work from all angles. Make any adjustments as needed.

Tips

If you're new to cutting hair, it's best to start with a subtle mullet and work your way up to a more dramatic look. Remember, you can always cut more hair off, but you can't put it back on.

It's also a good idea to have someone else help you, especially with the back of your hair. If you don't have a friend or family member who can assist you, consider using a handheld mirror to see the back of your head.

FAQ

How long does a mullet last?

A mullet can last anywhere from a few weeks to several months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how often you trim it.

Can anyone pull off a mullet?

While a mullet might not be the right look for everyone, it can work for many different hair types and face shapes. It's all about finding the right length and style for you.

Hair Care

Once you've cut your mullet, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. Regular trims can also help keep your mullet looking fresh.

Cutting a mullet yourself can be a fun and rewarding experience. With these tips and step-by-step instructions, you'll be able to achieve the perfect mullet in no time.
