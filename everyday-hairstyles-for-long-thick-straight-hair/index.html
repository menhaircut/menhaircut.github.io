Everyday Hairstyles For Long Thick Straight Hair

Written by Frank Oct 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have long, thick, straight hair, you know that it can be a challenge to find hairstyles that are both manageable and stylish. Fortunately, there are plenty of everyday hairstyles that are perfect for this hair type. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best everyday hairstyles for long, thick, straight hair.

Table of Contents:

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to:

The first step to achieving a great hairstyle is to start with clean, healthy hair. Make sure to wash and condition your hair regularly, and use a heat protectant spray if you plan on using any heat styling tools. Once your hair is clean and dry, you can start styling.

Braids:

Braids are a great option for long, thick, straight hair. They can be simple or intricate, and they can be worn in a variety of ways. Here are a few ideas:

  • A simple side braid
  • A fishtail braid
  • A French braid
  • A Dutch braid

Ponytails:

Ponytails are a classic hairstyle that are perfect for everyday wear. Here are a few ways to switch things up:

  • A low ponytail with a part in the middle
  • A high ponytail with a wrapped section of hair around the elastic
  • A side ponytail with a braid

Half-Up, Half-Down:

A half-up, half-down hairstyle is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your length. Here are a few ideas:

  • A simple half-up, half-down style with a clip or barrette
  • A twisted half-up, half-down style
  • A braided half-up, half-down style

Step by Step:

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating a simple braided hairstyle:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into three sections.
  3. Cross the right section over the middle section.
  4. Cross the left section over the middle section.
  5. Continue crossing the right and left sections over the middle section until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Secure the braid with an elastic.

Tips:

  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as clips, barrettes, and headbands.
  • Try using a curling iron or straightener to add some texture to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and comb to keep your hair healthy and tangle-free.

FAQ:

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle, but most people can get away with washing their hair every other day or every three days.

Q: How can I keep my hair healthy?

A: Eat a healthy diet, avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Hair Care:

To keep your long, thick, straight hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and use a heat protectant spray when you do use heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

By following these tips and trying out some of these everyday hairstyles, you can keep your long, thick, straight hair looking great every day.
