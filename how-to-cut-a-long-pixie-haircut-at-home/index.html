How To Cut A Long Pixie Haircut At Home

Written by Robby Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon and spending a fortune on your hair? Do you want to try something new and edgy? A long pixie haircut might be just the thing you need. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a long pixie haircut at home and achieve a stylish look without breaking the bank.

Description

A long pixie haircut is a short hairstyle that is longer on top and shorter on the sides and back. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in many ways and suits all face shapes. This low-maintenance haircut is perfect for those who want to try something new and trendy.

How to Cut a Long Pixie Haircut at Home

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and clips to section your hair. It's also a good idea to have a mirror, so you can see what you're doing.

Follow these steps to cut a long pixie haircut at home:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Wash your hair and let it air dry or blow-dry it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into four parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use clips to secure each section.

Step 3: Start with the sides

Start with one side of your head and comb your hair straight down. Use your scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Repeat on the other side.

Step 4: Cut the back

Move to the back of your head and comb your hair straight down. Cut your hair to the desired length.

Step 5: Cut the top

Now it's time to cut the top of your head. Comb your hair forward and use your scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. You can also use thinning scissors to add texture.

Step 6: Blend the sides and the top

Use your scissors to blend the sides and the top of your head. Make sure there are no harsh lines.

Step 7: Check your hair

Take a look at your hair in the mirror and make any necessary adjustments. You can always cut more, but you can't put hair back.

Step 8: Style your hair

Style your hair as desired. You can use a hair straightener, curling iron, or hair products to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut a long pixie haircut at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up to longer cuts.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will look longer when it dries.
  • Invest in a good pair of scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a long pixie haircut at home:

  • Can I cut my hair if it's curly?
  • Yes, you can cut curly hair, but make sure you cut it when it's dry.
  • How often should I cut my hair?
  • It depends on how fast your hair grows, but every 6-8 weeks is recommended.
  • What if I make a mistake?
  • If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a salon to have it fixed.

Hair Care

To keep your long pixie haircut looking its best, you'll need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

By following these steps and tips, you can easily cut a long pixie haircut at home and achieve a stylish and trendy look. Remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
