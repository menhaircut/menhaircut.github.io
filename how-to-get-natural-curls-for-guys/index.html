How To Get Natural Curls For Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 18, 2023 · 4 min read

In today's world, men are becoming more and more conscious about their appearance, and one of the latest trends is to have natural curls. While some men are naturally blessed with curls, others need to put in some effort to achieve the desired look. If you're looking for ways to get natural curls for guys, this post is for you. Here's a step-by-step guide on how you can achieve natural curls without damaging your hair.

Description

Natural curls have been a fashion statement for years, and many men today are looking for ways to achieve this style. While some men are born with naturally curly hair, others need to put in some effort to achieve the desired look. In this post, we'll discuss how to get natural curls for guys, including step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

How to Get Natural Curls for Guys

Step 1: Shampoo and Condition Your Hair

The first step to achieving natural curls is to wash your hair with a good shampoo and conditioner. This will help to remove any dirt or oil from your hair and scalp, which can weigh down your curls. Make sure to use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Step 2: Apply a Curl Enhancing Product

Once you've washed your hair, apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair, such as a curl cream or gel. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place throughout the day. Make sure to use a product that is specifically designed for curly hair, as this will provide the best results.

Step 3: Use a Diffuser

After applying the curl-enhancing product, use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help to enhance your natural curls and prevent frizz. To use a diffuser, simply attach it to your hairdryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting, using a circular motion.

Step 4: Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards towards your scalp. This will help to enhance your curls and create more volume. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your curls.

Step 5: Finish with a Hair Spray

Finally, finish your style with a light hold hair spray. This will help to keep your curls in place and prevent them from falling flat throughout the day. Make sure to use a hair spray that is specifically designed for curly hair, as this will provide the best results.

Tips for Getting Natural Curls for Guys

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve natural curls:

  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause your curls to become flat and lose their definition.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair and disrupt your natural curls.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is specifically designed for curly hair, as this can enhance your curls and make them easier to manage.

FAQ

How long does it take to get natural curls for guys?

The time it takes to get natural curls for guys can vary depending on your hair type and texture. Generally, it can take several weeks to several months to achieve natural curls, depending on how much effort you put in.

Can I achieve natural curls without using any products?

While it is possible to achieve natural curls without using any products, it can be more difficult to maintain your curls throughout the day. Using a curl-enhancing product can help to define your curls and keep them in place.

Hair Care for Natural Curls

Here are some tips to help you care for your natural curls:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage while you sleep.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve natural curls for guys without damaging your hair. Remember to be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you'll soon have the curls you've always wanted.
