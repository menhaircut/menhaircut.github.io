How To Sleep With Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair is beautiful, but it can be a challenge to manage, especially when it comes to sleeping. If you have curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to wake up with frizz and tangles. Fortunately, there are some simple steps you can take to keep your curls intact while you sleep. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for how to sleep with curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Sleep with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Sleep with Curly Hair

One of the best ways to preserve your curls overnight is to sleep with your hair in a protective style. This can help prevent frizz, tangles, and breakage. Here are some popular protective styles for curly hair:

  • Silk or satin bonnet
  • Silk or satin pillowcase
  • Pineapple
  • Braids or twists

Each of these styles has its own benefits and drawbacks, so you may want to try a few different options to see what works best for you.

Silk or Satin Bonnet

A silk or satin bonnet is a great option for keeping your curls intact while you sleep. These materials are gentle on hair and can help prevent tangles and breakage. Simply put your hair in a loose ponytail or pineapple and cover it with the bonnet before bed. You can also use a silk scarf if you don’t have a bonnet.

Silk or Satin Pillowcase

If you don’t want to wear a bonnet or scarf, you can also try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase. This will help reduce friction and prevent your hair from getting tangled. However, keep in mind that this method may not work as well for those with very long or thick hair.

Pineapple

A pineapple is a high ponytail that sits on top of your head. This style can help prevent your hair from getting flattened or squished while you sleep. To create a pineapple, gather your hair at the top of your head and secure it with a scrunchie or hair tie. Make sure the ponytail is loose to avoid creating a dent in your curls.

Braids or Twists

Braids or twists can also be a great option for keeping your curls intact overnight. This style can help prevent tangles and frizz, and can also create a nice curl pattern. To create braids or twists, simply section your hair and braid or twist each section before bed.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for how to sleep with curly hair:

  1. Decide on a protective style that works for you (silk or satin bonnet, silk or satin pillowcase, pineapple, braids, or twists).
  2. Cleanse and condition your hair as usual.
  3. Apply your favorite styling products to damp hair.
  4. Gently detangle your hair with a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  5. Create your protective style (bonnet, pillowcase, pineapple, braids, or twists).
  6. Go to bed and get a good night’s sleep!
  7. In the morning, remove your protective style and gently fluff your curls with your fingers.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for how to sleep with curly hair:

  • Use a silk or satin scarf or pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent tangles.
  • Avoid using cotton pillowcases or towels, as these can create friction and cause frizz.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • If you have very long hair, try braiding or twisting your hair in sections to prevent tangles.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair before bed to help lock in moisture.

FAQs

Q: Can I sleep with my hair down?

A: While you can sleep with your hair down, it’s not the best option for preserving your curls. Sleeping with your hair down can cause tangles, frizz, and flattened curls.

Q: Do I need to use a silk or satin pillowcase?

A: No, you don’t need to use a silk or satin pillowcase, but it can be helpful in reducing friction and preventing tangles.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people with curly hair prefer to wash their hair every day, while others only wash once a week. Experiment to find a routine that works for you.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle

By following these tips and tricks, you can help preserve your curls overnight and wake up with beautiful, bouncy curls in the morning.
