What Hair Colours Suit Me A Complete Guide

Written by Luffy Nov 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking about changing your hair colour, you might be wondering which colours will suit you best. With so many different hair colours to choose from, it can be difficult to know where to start. In this article, we'll take a closer look at what hair colours suit different skin tones, eye colours, and natural hair colours. So, whether you're looking for a subtle change or a dramatic transformation, read on to find out what hair colours suit you best.

Description

Choosing the right hair colour can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what will suit you best. There are so many factors to consider, from your skin tone and eye colour to your natural hair colour and personal style. But don't worry, we're here to help. In this article, we'll give you all the information you need to choose the perfect hair colour for you.

How to

The first step in choosing the right hair colour is to consider your skin tone. If you have a warm skin tone, you'll want to choose hair colours that have warm undertones, such as golden blondes, honey browns, and copper reds. If you have a cool skin tone, you'll want to choose hair colours with cool undertones, such as ash blondes, cool browns, and burgundy reds.

The next factor to consider is your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, you'll want to choose hair colours that complement your eye colour, such as platinum blondes or rich browns. If you have brown eyes, you can experiment with a wider range of hair colours, from warm caramels to cool blues.

Another important factor to consider is your natural hair colour. If you have dark hair, you may need to lighten your hair gradually to achieve your desired hair colour. If you have light hair, you may need to add warmth or depth to your hair to achieve a more flattering hair colour.

Step by Step

1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins in your wrist. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones.

2. Determine your eye colour. If you have blue or green eyes, you have cool undertones. If you have brown eyes, you have warm undertones.

3. Consider your natural hair colour. If you have dark hair, you may need to lighten gradually. If you have light hair, you may need to add warmth or depth.

4. Choose a hair colour that complements your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour.

Tips

- If you're unsure about which hair colour to choose, consider consulting a professional hair stylist. They can help you choose a hair colour that will suit your skin tone, eye colour, and natural hair colour.

- If you're looking for a low-maintenance hair colour, consider choosing a hair colour that is close to your natural hair colour.

- If you're looking for a dramatic change, consider choosing a hair colour that is significantly different from your natural hair colour.

FAQ

Q: What hair colours suit warm skin tones?

A: Hair colours with warm undertones, such as golden blondes, honey browns, and copper reds, suit warm skin tones.

Q: What hair colours suit cool skin tones?

A: Hair colours with cool undertones, such as ash blondes, cool browns, and burgundy reds, suit cool skin tones.

Q: What hair colours suit blue or green eyes?

A: Hair colours that complement blue or green eyes, such as platinum blondes or rich browns, suit blue or green eyes.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen your new hair colour, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for coloured hair, and avoid using heat-styling tools too often. If you're going to be spending time in the sun, protect your hair with a hat or a leave-in conditioner with SPF. With the right hair care routine, your new hair colour will look great for weeks to come.
