Which Guard Cuts The Least Amount Of Hair

Written by William Jun 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

When it comes to giving yourself a haircut at home, it's important to have the right tools. One of the most important tools is the guard that you attach to your clippers. Guards come in different sizes, ranging from 0.5mm to 25mm. But which guard cuts the least amount of hair? In this article, we will explore the answer to this question.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The guard that cuts the least amount of hair is the 0.5mm guard. This guard is also known as the "no guard" or "bald" guard. It is the shortest guard available and it is often used for creating a buzz cut or a bald look. It is also used for trimming facial hair or for creating sharp lines around the edges of a haircut.

How to

To use the 0.5mm guard, you will need a pair of clippers that are compatible with this guard. Make sure that your hair is clean and dry before you start. Attach the 0.5mm guard to your clippers and turn them on. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, using short, even strokes. Be careful not to apply too much pressure, as this can cause the clippers to pull your hair.

Step by step

1. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you start.

2. Attach the 0.5mm guard to your clippers.

3. Turn the clippers on.

4. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward.

5. Use short, even strokes.

6. Be careful not to apply too much pressure.

Tips

If you are using the 0.5mm guard for the first time, it's a good idea to start with a longer guard and work your way down gradually. This will help you get used to the feel of the clippers and prevent you from cutting your hair too short.

It's also important to maintain your clippers by cleaning them regularly and oiling the blades. This will ensure that they work properly and prevent them from pulling your hair.

FAQ

Can I use the 0.5mm guard on any type of hair?

The 0.5mm guard is best suited for people with short hair. If you have long hair, it may be difficult to use this guard effectively.

Is it easy to cut your own hair with the 0.5mm guard?

Using the 0.5mm guard requires some skill and practice. It's important to go slowly and be careful not to cut your hair too short.

Can I use the 0.5mm guard for trimming my beard?

Yes, the 0.5mm guard can be used for trimming facial hair.

Hair Care

If you have just cut your hair with the 0.5mm guard, it's important to take care of your scalp. Apply a moisturizing lotion or oil to prevent dryness and itchiness. Avoid exposing your scalp to the sun for prolonged periods of time, as this can cause sunburn.

Overall, the 0.5mm guard is a useful tool for anyone who wants a very short haircut or needs to create sharp lines around the edges of a haircut. With some practice and care, you can use this guard to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home.
