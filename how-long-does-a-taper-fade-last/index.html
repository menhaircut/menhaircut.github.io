How Long Does A Taper Fade Last

Written by Bardi Sep 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering getting a taper fade haircut, but wondering how long it will last? Taper fades are a popular style for men, characterized by a gradual fade from longer hair on top to shorter hair on the sides and back. In this article, we'll discuss how long you can expect a taper fade to last and provide tips for maintaining your new haircut.

How to Get a Taper Fade

If you're not familiar with taper fades, it's important to find a skilled barber who can give you the look you want. When you go to the barber, you'll want to explain what kind of taper fade you're looking for. There are different types, including low, mid, and high fades, and variations like skin fades and bald fades. Be sure to communicate your preferences clearly to your barber.

How Long Does a Taper Fade Last?

On average, a taper fade will last around two to three weeks before it starts to grow out. However, this can vary depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you take care of it. If you're someone who likes to keep their hair short, you may need to get a touch-up every two weeks or so to maintain the look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Taper Fade

If you want your taper fade to last as long as possible, there are a few things you can do. First, make sure you're using the right hair products. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. You may also want to use a styling product like hair wax or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Second, avoid wearing hats or other headwear that can flatten your hair and ruin your taper fade. If you do wear a hat, make sure it's not too tight and take it off as soon as you can.

Finally, schedule regular appointments with your barber to get touch-ups and keep your taper fade looking fresh. They'll be able to trim the sides and back and blend the fade with the longer hair on top.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a taper fade?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you like to keep it. On average, you'll need to get a touch-up every two to three weeks to maintain the look.

Can I get a taper fade if I have curly hair?

Yes! Taper fades work well with all hair types, including curly hair. Just make sure you find a barber who is experienced with cutting curly hair.

How do I know if a taper fade will look good on me?

The best way to know if a taper fade will look good on you is to try it out! If you're not sure, ask your barber for their opinion.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining your taper fade. Make sure you're using high-quality hair products, avoiding tight headwear, and scheduling regular appointments with your barber. With these tips, you can enjoy your taper fade for as long as possible.
