Number 1 Back And Sides 3 On Top A Classic Men s Haircut

Written by Frank Sep 05, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking for a low-maintenance, classic haircut that never goes out of style, the number 1 back and sides 3 on top is a great option. This haircut is also known as a "blended fade" or "taper fade," and it's popular among men of all ages and hair types. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

Description

The number 1 back and sides 3 on top haircut is characterized by short hair on the back and sides of the head, which gradually fades into longer hair on the top. The "number 1" refers to the length of the hair on the back and sides, which is usually around ⅛ inch. The "number 3" refers to the length of the hair on the top, which is usually around ⅜ inch. This creates a clean, tapered look that's easy to maintain.

How to Get It

If you're interested in getting a number 1 back and sides 3 on top haircut, the first step is to find a barber who's experienced with this style. When you're in the chair, ask for a tapered fade with a number 1 guard on the back and sides, and a number 3 guard on the top. Your barber will use clippers and scissors to blend the hair and create a seamless transition between the different lengths.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a number 1 back and sides 3 on top haircut:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Show your barber a picture of the style you want.
  3. Sit still and let your barber work their magic.
  4. Once the haircut is finished, check it in the mirror to make sure you're happy with the length and shape.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a number 1 back and sides 3 on top haircut:

  • Make sure you're comfortable with short hair on the back and sides before committing to this style.
  • Consider your face shape when choosing the length of the hair on top.
  • Invest in a good-quality pair of clippers to maintain the length of the hair on the back and sides.
  • Use a lightweight, matte hair product to style the hair on top.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the number 1 back and sides 3 on top haircut:

Is this a good haircut for men with thinning hair?

Yes, this is a great haircut for men with thinning hair because it creates the illusion of thicker hair on top.

How often do I need to get this haircut?

Generally, you'll need to get this haircut every 2-4 weeks to maintain the length and shape.

Can I style this haircut in different ways?

Yes, you can style this haircut in a variety of ways depending on your personal preference. You can comb it to the side for a classic look, or spike it up for a more modern look.

Hair Care

To keep your number 1 back and sides 3 on top haircut looking sharp, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo every 2-3 days.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair product to the hair on top to style it.
  • Visit your barber regularly to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

Overall, the number 1 back and sides 3 on top haircut is a timeless style that's easy to maintain and looks great on men of all ages. With the right barber, hair care routine, and styling products, you can rock this classic look with confidence.
