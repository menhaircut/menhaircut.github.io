How To Shape Eyebrows For Face Shape

Written by Robby Oct 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying to shape your eyebrows but ending up with a look that doesn't suit your face shape? Worry no more! In this article, we will guide you on how to shape eyebrows for face shape. We'll give you step-by-step instructions and tips to help you achieve the perfect look.

Description

The eyebrows are an essential part of our face. They frame the eyes and enhance our overall look. However, not everyone has the same face shape, and it's crucial to shape your eyebrows according to your face shape to achieve the best look.

How to Shape Eyebrows for Face Shape

Shaping eyebrows for face shape is not as challenging as it sounds. You need to follow a few simple steps to get it right. Here is a step-by-step guide to shaping eyebrows for face shape:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to shaping eyebrows for face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six common face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and long. You can determine your face shape by looking at the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Find Your Eyebrow Shape

Once you determine your face shape, find the eyebrow shape that suits it. Here are some guidelines:

  • Round face: go for a high arch to elongate the face
  • Oval face: a soft arch works best to balance the features
  • Square face: a strong, angled arch can soften the features
  • Heart face: a rounded arch complements the face shape
  • Diamond face: a curved arch can soften the features
  • Long face: a flat brow can balance the features

Step 3: Pluck or Shape Your Eyebrows

Once you know the right eyebrow shape for your face, it's time to pluck or shape them. Here are some tips:

  • Use a spoolie brush to brush your eyebrows upwards
  • Use a white eyeliner pencil to mark your ideal eyebrow shape
  • Use tweezers to pluck any stray hairs outside the marked shape
  • Use scissors to trim any long eyebrow hairs
  • Fill in any gaps with an eyebrow pencil or powder

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you shape your eyebrows for face shape:

  • Don't overpluck your eyebrows as it can make you look older
  • Avoid using a magnifying mirror, as it can distort your view
  • Use a numbing cream if you have a low pain threshold
  • Consider going to a professional for your first eyebrow shaping

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about shaping eyebrows for face shape:

What if I have thin eyebrows?

You can use an eyebrow pencil or powder to fill in any gaps and make your eyebrows appear thicker. You can also consider getting a brow tint to darken the hair.

What if I make a mistake while shaping my eyebrows?

If you accidentally overpluck your eyebrows, don't panic. You can use an eyebrow pencil or powder to fill in any gaps. You can also use a clear eyebrow gel to hold the hairs in place while they grow back.

How often should I shape my eyebrows?

You should shape your eyebrows every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape. However, it may take longer for the hairs to grow back if you overpluck them.

Hair Care

After shaping your eyebrows, it's essential to take care of them. Here are some tips:

  • Apply aloe vera gel to soothe the skin after plucking
  • Use a brow serum to promote hair growth and thickness
  • Avoid rubbing your eyebrows when washing your face
  • Use a spoolie brush to keep your eyebrows in place

Conclusion

Shaping eyebrows for face shape is a simple process that can make a significant difference in your overall look. By following the steps and tips in this article, you can achieve the perfect eyebrow shape for your face. Remember to take care of your eyebrows to maintain their shape and health.
