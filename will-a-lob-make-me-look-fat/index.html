Will A Lob Make Me Look Fat

Description

A lob, or long bob, is a popular haircut that falls just above the shoulders. If you're considering getting a lob, you may be wondering if it will make you look fat. The answer is no, a lob won't necessarily make you look fat. However, the way you style your hair can affect your overall appearance. In this article, we'll discuss how to get a lob, style it, and care for it to ensure it looks great on you.

How to Get a Lob

If you're interested in getting a lob, the first step is to find a good hairstylist. Look for someone who has experience cutting lobs and can help you find the right length and style for your face shape and hair type. Once you've found a stylist, schedule a consultation to discuss your options.

Choosing the Right Length

The length of your lob will depend on your personal preference and face shape. If you have a round face, a longer lob may be more flattering. If you have a square or heart-shaped face, a shorter lob may be more flattering. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right length.

Choosing the Right Style

There are many different styles of lobs to choose from, including blunt lobs, layered lobs, and asymmetrical lobs. Your hairstylist can help you choose the right style for your face shape and hair type.

Step-by-Step Guide

Once you've decided on the length and style of your lob, it's time to get it cut. Here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and blow-dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, bottom, left, and right.
  3. Starting with the bottom section, cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Move on to the left and right sections, cutting them to the same length as the bottom section.
  5. Finally, cut the top section to the desired length.
  6. Style your lob as desired.

Tips for Styling Your Lob

Here are some tips for styling your lob:

  • Use a round brush to add volume.
  • Use a straightening iron to create sleek, straight strands.
  • Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  • Add texture with a texturizing spray.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look.

FAQ

Will a lob make me look fat?

No, a lob won't necessarily make you look fat. However, the way you style your hair can affect your overall appearance. Experiment with different styling techniques to find the most flattering look.

How often should I get my lob trimmed?

You should get your lob trimmed every six to eight weeks to keep it looking its best.

Can I still wear my hair up with a lob?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a lob. Try a half-up, half-down style, or a low ponytail.

Hair Care for Your Lob

To keep your lob looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair every time you wash it.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.
  • Protect your hair from the sun with a hat or scarf.

Conclusion

A lob is a versatile and stylish haircut that can flatter any face shape. By choosing the right length and style, and using the right styling techniques and hair care products, you can ensure that your lob looks great on you. So go ahead and get that lob – it won't make you look fat!
