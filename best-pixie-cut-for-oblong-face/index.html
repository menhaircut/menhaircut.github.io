Description

If you have an oblong face shape, you may be wondering what the best pixie cut is for you. An oblong face shape is characterized by a long, narrow shape with a prominent forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. The goal of a pixie cut for an oblong face is to create balance and proportion. In this article, we will share the best pixie cut for oblong face, how to style it, and hair care tips to maintain your new look.

How to

The best pixie cut for oblong face is the one that adds width to your face and softens your sharp features. You should choose a pixie cut that has volume on top and sides, and that will complement your facial features. The ideal length is around 2 to 3 inches on top and shorter on the sides and back. You can opt for a side-swept pixie, a layered pixie, or a textured pixie.

Step by step

If you have decided to get a pixie cut, here are the steps you should follow:

  1. Choose the right pixie cut for your face shape.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best style for you.
  3. Wash and condition your hair before the haircut.
  4. Sit still and let your stylist do their job.
  5. Style your new pixie cut using a blow dryer, round brush, and styling products.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your pixie cut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim.
  • Use high-quality hair care products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • Accessorize your pixie cut with headbands, clips, and other hair accessories.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts for oblong face:

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look longer?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually make your face look shorter by adding volume on top and sides.

Q: Can I style my pixie cut differently every day?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styling techniques to create different looks.

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with glasses?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can look great with glasses. Just make sure to choose frames that complement your face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut looking great, you should follow a regular hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and curling irons too often.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and cold weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

With the right pixie cut and hair care routine, you can rock this trendy and chic hairstyle with confidence. Good luck!
