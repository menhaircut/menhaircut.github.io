How To Figure Out What Haircut Looks Good On You

Written by Eveline Jul 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of getting haircuts that don't suit you? Do you want to know how to figure out what haircut looks good on you? This article will guide you through the process of finding the perfect haircut for your face shape, hair texture, and personality.

Table of Contents

  • How to Figure Out What Haircut Looks Good on You
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

Step by Step Guide

Here are the steps you should follow to figure out what haircut looks good on you:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six different face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. You can determine your face shape by measuring your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture is another important factor to consider when choosing a haircut. There are three main hair textures: fine, medium, and thick. Each texture requires a different type of haircut to look its best.

Step 3: Decide on a Length

The length of your hair is also crucial in determining the right haircut. Short haircuts work well for some people, while others look better with longer hair. Consider your lifestyle, facial features, and personal style when deciding on a length.

Step 4: Think About Your Personality

Your personality should also be taken into account when choosing a haircut. If you're outgoing and love to stand out, a bold haircut may be the best choice for you. On the other hand, if you're more reserved, a classic and understated haircut may be more your style.

Tips for Choosing the Right Haircut

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your hairdresser
  • Consider your hair's natural texture when choosing a haircut
  • Ask for advice from friends and family
  • Be open to trying something new
  • Consider your hair's maintenance requirements

FAQs

What is the best haircut for round faces?

The best haircut for round faces is one that adds length to the face. This can be achieved with a layered haircut or one that has side-swept bangs.

What is the best haircut for thick hair?

The best haircut for thick hair is one that thins out the hair and adds movement. A layered haircut or one with long, face-framing layers works well for thick hair.

What is the best haircut for fine hair?

The best haircut for fine hair is one that adds volume and texture. A layered bob or a pixie cut with textured layers can make fine hair look fuller.

Hair Care Tips

After you've found the perfect haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to prevent damage to your hair
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to find the perfect haircut for your face shape, hair texture, and personality. Don't be afraid to try something new and experiment with different styles until you find the one that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
