What Hair Color Fits My Skin Tone

Written by Robby Nov 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure what shade will complement your skin tone. There are several factors to consider, such as whether you have warm or cool undertones, and what shades will bring out the best in your complexion. In this article, we'll explore what hair color fits your skin tone, and provide tips on how to choose the perfect shade.

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right hair color is to determine your skin tone. There are three main categories: warm, cool, and neutral. Here are some tips to help you determine which category you fall into:

Vein Test

Look at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If they appear blue or purple, you likely have cool undertones. If they're a mix of both, you may have neutral undertones.

Jewelry Test

Consider which metal jewelry looks best on your skin. If gold looks best, you likely have warm undertones. If silver looks best, you likely have cool undertones. If both look great, you may have neutral undertones.

Eye and Hair Color

People with warm undertones tend to have brown, amber, or hazel eyes, and hair with warm tones like golden blonde or chestnut. People with cool undertones tend to have blue, gray, or green eyes, and hair with cool tones like ash blonde or black. People with neutral undertones may have a mix of warm and cool tones in their hair and eyes.

Once you've determined your skin tone, you can start exploring hair colors that will complement it.

Hair Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have warm undertones, you'll want to choose a hair color that enhances your natural warmth. Here are some shades to consider:

  • Golden blonde
  • Caramel
  • Copper
  • Auburn
  • Chocolate brown

These shades will bring out the warmth in your skin tone and make you look radiant.

Hair Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have cool undertones, you'll want to choose a hair color that complements your coolness. Here are some shades to consider:

  • Platinum blonde
  • Ash blonde
  • Jet black
  • Cool brown
  • Burgundy

These shades will bring out the coolness in your skin tone and make you look fresh and vibrant.

Hair Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have neutral undertones, you're lucky because you can pull off a wide range of hair colors. Here are some shades to consider:

  • Beige blonde
  • Neutral brown
  • Rich red
  • Warm or cool black
  • Any shade of brown or blonde

Experiment with different shades to see what looks best on you.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Hair Color

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color:

  • Consider your lifestyle: If you don't have a lot of time to spend on upkeep, choose a low-maintenance color that won't require frequent touch-ups.
  • Think about your natural hair color: If you want to make a dramatic change, consider going no more than two shades lighter or darker than your natural color.
  • Consult with a stylist: A professional stylist can help you choose a color that will complement your skin tone and bring out your best features.

FAQ

What if I want to go outside of my natural hair color range?

If you want to make a dramatic change, it's important to choose a color that still complements your skin tone. For example, if you have warm undertones and want to go blonde, choose a shade with warm undertones like golden blonde or honey blonde.

What if I don't like the way my hair looks after coloring it?

If you're not happy with the way your hair looks after coloring it, don't panic. You can always go back to your natural color or try a different shade. It's important to work with a professional stylist to avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Colored Hair

Colored hair requires special care to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid hot tools like curling irons and straighteners, which can damage colored hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain healthy-looking hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking beautiful and healthy, no matter what color you choose.
