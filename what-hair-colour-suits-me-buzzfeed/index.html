What Hair Colour Suits Me Buzzfeed A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Aug 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your current hair colour and want to try something new? With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the right shade that suits you. Luckily, Buzzfeed has created a quiz that can help you determine which hair colour suits you best. In this article, we will discuss the quiz and provide additional tips and information to help you make the best decision for your hair.

Description: What is the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Buzzfeed" Quiz?

The "What Hair Colour Suits Me Buzzfeed" quiz is a fun and interactive way to determine which hair colour is best for you. The quiz consists of a series of questions that take into account your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style. Once you answer all the questions, the quiz will generate a hair colour suggestion that is tailored to your individual needs.

How to: Take the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Buzzfeed" Quiz

Taking the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Buzzfeed" quiz is easy and can be done in just a few minutes. All you need to do is visit the Buzzfeed website and search for the quiz. Once you find it, click on the link and answer the questions honestly. Once you have completed the quiz, your hair colour suggestion will be revealed.

Step by Step: Choosing the Right Hair Colour

While the Buzzfeed quiz is a great tool to help you choose a hair colour, there are other factors you should consider before making a final decision.

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

One of the most important factors to consider when choosing a hair colour is your skin tone. There are two main types of skin tones: warm and cool. If you have warm undertones, you should opt for hair colours with golden or reddish hues. If you have cool undertones, you should choose hair colours with ashy or blue-based undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Colour

Your eye colour can also play a role in determining which hair colour suits you best. If you have blue or green eyes, warmer hair colours such as blonde, copper, or red can help make your eyes pop. If you have brown eyes, cooler hair colours such as ash brown or black can complement your eyes.

Step 3: Take Your Personal Style into Account

Your personal style should also be considered when choosing a hair colour. If you prefer a natural look, you may want to opt for a hair colour that is close to your natural shade. If you like to stand out, you may want to try a bold or unconventional colour.

Tips: Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Once you have chosen your hair colour, it is important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your hair colour:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for coloured hair
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors
  • Get regular touch-ups to keep your colour looking fresh

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

Can I dye my hair at home?

While it is possible to dye your hair at home, it is recommended to visit a professional stylist to ensure that the colour is applied correctly and evenly.

Will dyeing my hair damage it?

Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, but using high-quality products and following proper hair care techniques can help minimize the damage.

How often should I touch up my roots?

This will vary depending on the individual and the colour of their hair. It is recommended to touch up roots every 4-6 weeks to keep the colour looking fresh.

Hair Care: Taking Care of Your Hair

Choosing the right hair colour is just the first step in achieving a great look. It is important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are a few tips to help you take care of your hair:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid applying heat to your hair too frequently
  • Eat a healthy diet to promote hair growth and strength
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends

By following these tips, you can help keep your hair looking great and feeling healthy.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can be a fun and exciting process. By using the "What Hair Colour Suits Me Buzzfeed" quiz and considering your skin tone, eye colour, and personal style, you can find a colour that suits you perfectly. Remember to take proper care of your hair to keep it looking its best. With these tips in mind, you are sure to have a great hair day every day!
