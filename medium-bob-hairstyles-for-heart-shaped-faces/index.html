Medium Bob Hairstyles For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Aprilia Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart-shaped face, you know how important it is to find the perfect hairstyle. A medium bob can be a great option for heart-shaped faces, as it can accentuate your cheekbones and balance out your jawline. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about medium bob hairstyles for heart-shaped faces, including how to style them, tips, and hair care.

Description

A medium bob is a versatile hairstyle that falls between the chin and shoulders. It can be styled in various ways, making it a popular choice for many women. If you have a heart-shaped face, a medium bob can be a great option as it can soften your pointy chin and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

How to

To achieve a medium bob hairstyle, you need to follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by getting a haircut from a professional hairstylist. Make sure you ask for a medium bob that flatters your face shape.
  2. Wash your hair and towel dry it. Apply a heat protectant to your hair to prevent damage from heat styling tools.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to give it volume and shape.
  4. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  5. Finish off your hairstyle with a texturizing spray or hairspray to hold it in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a medium bob hairstyle:

Step 1: Choose the right length

The first step is to choose the right length for your medium bob. Make sure it flatters your face shape and highlights your best features.

Step 2: Get a professional haircut

Visit a professional hairstylist to get a medium bob haircut. They will be able to cut your hair to the right length and shape it to flatter your face shape.

Step 3: Wash and towel dry your hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant to prevent heat damage.

Step 4: Blow-dry your hair

Use a round brush to blow-dry your hair. This will give it volume and shape. Make sure you blow-dry your hair in sections.

Step 5: Style your hair with a flat iron or curling iron

Use a flat iron or curling iron to create loose waves or curls. This will give your medium bob hairstyle some texture and movement.

Step 6: Finish off with hairspray

Finish off your hairstyle with a texturizing spray or hairspray to hold it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your medium bob hairstyle:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage.
  • Blow-dry your hair in sections to achieve the best results.
  • Use a round brush to create volume and shape.
  • Experiment with different styling tools to create different looks.
  • Use hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about medium bob hairstyles for heart-shaped faces:

What is the best length for a medium bob?

The best length for a medium bob is between the chin and shoulders. This length can flatter most face shapes.

Can I style my medium bob with a flat iron?

Yes, you can style your medium bob with a flat iron to create straight and sleek hair. You can also use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.

Is a medium bob hairstyle suitable for heart-shaped faces?

Yes, a medium bob hairstyle can be a great option for heart-shaped faces. It can soften your pointy chin and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

Hair Care

To keep your medium bob hairstyle looking great, you need to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant before using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape of your medium bob.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too many styling products as they can weigh down your hair.

By following these tips, you can keep your medium bob hairstyle looking great and healthy.

Conclusion

A medium bob hairstyle can be a great option for heart-shaped faces. It can soften your pointy chin and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. By following the tips and steps in this article, you can achieve a beautiful medium bob hairstyle that flatters your face shape.
