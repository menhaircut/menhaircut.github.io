How To Fix Really Thin Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Sep 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling with thin hair and looking for ways to fix it? Thin hair is a common problem that can be caused by genetics, age, stress, and other factors. But don't worry, there are several things you can do to improve the appearance of your thin hair. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you fix really thin hair.

  • How to Fix Really Thin Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Fix Really Thin Hair

There are several ways to fix really thin hair, including:

1. Use volumizing products

One of the easiest ways to give your thin hair a boost is to use volumizing products. Look for shampoos, conditioners, and styling products that are designed to add volume to your hair. These products work by lifting your hair from the roots and making it appear thicker.

2. Get a haircut

A good haircut can make a big difference in the appearance of your thin hair. Opt for a shorter hairstyle that will make your hair look fuller and thicker. A layered haircut can also help to add volume and movement to your hair.

3. Change your part

Changing your part can instantly give your hair more volume. If you always part your hair on the same side, try switching it up to the other side. You can also try a middle part or a zigzag part to create the illusion of thicker hair.

4. Use hot tools

Hot tools like curling irons, flat irons, and hot rollers can be used to add volume and texture to your hair. Use a volumizing mousse or spray before styling to give your hair extra hold and lift.

5. Consider extensions

If you want to add length and volume to your hair, consider getting extensions. There are several different types of extensions available, including clip-ins, tape-ins, and sew-ins. Consult with a hairstylist to determine which type of extension is right for you.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you fix really thin hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help to add volume and thickness to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a volumizing mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your damp hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to lift your hair and create volume.

Step 3: Blow dry your hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and add texture. Focus on lifting your hair from the roots to create maximum volume.

Step 4: Use hot tools

Use hot tools like curling irons or flat irons to add texture and volume to your hair. Apply a heat protectant spray before using hot tools to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hold hairspray to keep your hair in place and add extra volume.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you fix really thin hair:

1. Don't over-wash your hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it look thinner. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days to keep it healthy and full.

2. Avoid heavy hair products

Avoid using heavy hair products like oils and serums, which can weigh down your hair and make it look thinner. Stick to lightweight products that won't weigh your hair down.

3. Use dry shampoo

Dry shampoo can be a lifesaver for thin hair. It can help to absorb excess oil and add volume to your hair when you don't have time to wash it.

4. Brush your hair gently

Brushing your hair too roughly can cause it to break and become even thinner. Use a wide-tooth comb or a gentle brush to detangle your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can thin hair be thickened?

A: Yes, there are several ways to thicken thin hair, including using volumizing products, getting a haircut, changing your part, using hot tools, and getting extensions.

Q: Can stress cause thinning hair?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss and thinning hair. If you're experiencing hair loss or thinning, it's important to manage your stress levels and seek professional help if needed.

Q: Can diet affect hair thickness?

A: Yes, a healthy diet can help to promote hair growth and thickness. Make sure you're getting enough protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet to support healthy hair growth.

Hair Care for Thin Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for thin hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that won't strip your hair of its natural oils. Look for shampoos that are specifically designed for thin hair.

2. Avoid heat styling

Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons too often, as they can cause damage to your hair and make it look thinner.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Exposure to the sun can cause damage to your hair and make it look thinner. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun.

4. Get regular trims

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair look thinner.

By following these tips and tricks, you can fix really thin hair and achieve a fuller, more voluminous look. Remember, healthy hair starts with proper care and attention!
