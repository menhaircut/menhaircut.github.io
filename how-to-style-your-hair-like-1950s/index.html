How To Style Your Hair Like 1950S A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle, then the 1950s is the era to look to. The hairstyles of this era were all about glamour, femininity, and elegance, and they have remained popular even today. In this article, we will show you how to style your hair like 1950s in a relaxed and easy-to-follow manner. From victory rolls to pin curls, we've got you covered.

How to Style Your Hair Like 1950s

The hairstyles of the 1950s were all about volume, curls, and waves. Women often wore their hair up in elaborate styles, and hair accessories like headbands and ribbons were popular too. Here are some of the key elements of a 1950s hairstyle:

  • Volume at the crown
  • Curls and waves
  • Pinned or rolled sections of hair
  • Headbands or other hair accessories

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know what the key elements of a 1950s hairstyle are, let's get into the step-by-step guide on how to achieve this look.

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you start styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't weigh your hair down.

Step 2: Add volume to your crown

Take a section of hair at the crown of your head and backcomb it gently to add volume. Spray with hairspray to hold the volume in place.

Step 3: Create curls or waves

Using a curling iron or hot rollers, create curls or waves throughout your hair. To make the curls look more natural, alternate the direction in which you curl each section of hair.

Step 4: Pin or roll sections of hair

Take small sections of hair and either pin them back with bobby pins or roll them up and secure them with hair clips. This will give your hair more texture and dimension.

Step 5: Add a hair accessory

Finish off your look by adding a headband, scarf, or ribbon. This will add a touch of glamour to your 1950s hairstyle.

Tips for Styling Your Hair Like 1950s

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect 1950s hairstyle:

  • Use hairspray to hold your curls and waves in place.
  • Don't be afraid to tease your hair a little to add more volume.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to find the one that works best for you.
  • If you have shorter hair, try a pixie cut or a bob with curls.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling your hair like 1950s:

Q: Can I achieve a 1950s hairstyle with short hair?

A: Yes, you can. Short hair was also popular in the 1950s, and you can achieve a 1950s look with a pixie cut or a bob with curls.

Q: Do I need to use hot rollers to create curls?

A: No, you don't. You can also use a curling iron or even overnight braids to create curls.

Q: How can I make my curls last longer?

A: Use hairspray to set your curls in place, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Hair Care for 1950s Hairstyles

Here are some tips on how to take care of your hair when styling it like 1950s:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid weighing your hair down.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do need to use heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to style your hair like 1950s, it's time to give it a try. Whether you're going for a classic pin-up look or a more relaxed retro vibe, there's a 1950s hairstyle that will work for you. So go ahead and channel your inner Marilyn Monroe or Audrey Hepburn and rock that retro look!
