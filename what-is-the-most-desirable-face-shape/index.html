What Is The Most Desirable Face Shape

Description

When it comes to physical appearance, the shape of your face plays a crucial role. It can affect how you look in certain hairstyles, glasses, and even makeup. But what is the most desirable face shape?

The answer to that question isn't so simple. In fact, there are several face shapes that are considered desirable, depending on cultural and societal norms. However, there are some common characteristics that make a face shape attractive.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in determining your face shape is to pull your hair back and take a good look at your face in the mirror. Then, you can use the following guidelines:

  • Oval: The length of your face is about 1.5 times the width, and your forehead is slightly wider than your jawline.
  • Round: The length and width of your face are almost equal, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face.
  • Square: The length and width of your face are almost equal, and your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width.
  • Heart: Your forehead is wider than your jawline, and your face tapers to a point at your chin.
  • Diamond: Your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, and your forehead and jawline are about the same width.
  • Pear: Your jawline is wider than your forehead, and your face tapers to a point at your chin.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Face Shape

Still not sure what your face shape is? Follow these steps:

  1. Pull your hair back and take a photo of your face.
  2. Print out the photo or use a photo editing app to draw lines on it. Connect the following points: the top of your forehead, the widest part of your cheekbones, and the tip of your chin.
  3. Examine the shape that the lines create:
    • If the lines create an oval shape, you have an oval face.
    • If the lines are roughly equal in length and create a circular shape, you have a round face.
    • If the lines create a square or rectangle shape, you have a square face.
    • If the lines are wide at the forehead, narrow at the jawline, and come to a point at the chin, you have a heart-shaped face.
    • If the lines create a diamond shape, you have a diamond face.
    • If the lines are narrow at the forehead, wide at the jawline, and come to a point at the chin, you have a pear-shaped face.

Tips for Accentuating Your Face Shape

Once you've determined your face shape, you can use the following tips to accentuate your features:

  • Oval: Almost any hairstyle looks good on an oval face, but avoid styles with heavy bangs that can shorten the appearance of your face.
  • Round: Opt for hairstyles with height at the crown to elongate your face, and avoid styles that add volume to the sides.
  • Square: Soften your angles with hairstyles that have layers and wispy bangs.
  • Heart: Balance your face with hairstyles that add volume at the chin, such as a bob or long layers.
  • Diamond: Soften your cheekbones with hairstyles that have volume at the crown or sides.
  • Pear: Draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones with hairstyles that have volume at the crown.

FAQs About Face Shapes

Q: Can I change my face shape?

Unfortunately, no. Your face shape is determined by your bone structure, which can't be altered without surgery.

Q: What face shape is most common?

The most common face shape is oval.

Q: Can face shape affect my glasses choice?

Yes, certain glasses styles can look better on certain face shapes. For example, round glasses can complement a square face, while angular glasses can complement a round face.

Hair Care Tips for Your Face Shape

Aside from choosing the right hairstyle, you can take care of your hair to complement your face shape:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to thin hair and balance a heart-shaped face.
  • Apply a shine serum to add dimension to long, oval faces.
  • Avoid heavy, oily products that can weigh down hair and make a round face appear wider.
  • Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add volume to a diamond-shaped face.
  • Avoid blunt bangs that can shorten a round face, and opt for side-swept bangs instead.

So, what is the most desirable face shape? It really depends on who you ask. But regardless of your face shape, you can always accentuate your features and feel confident in your own skin.
