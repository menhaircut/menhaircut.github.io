Why Is Purple Hair Popular Exploring The Trend In 2023

Written by Christine Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're scrolling through Instagram or TikTok in 2023, you've probably noticed a trend: purple hair. From pastel lavender to deep violet, this color is taking over the world of hair dye. In this article, we'll explore why purple hair is so popular and give you some tips for pulling off the look yourself.

Table of Contents

  • What Makes Purple Hair So Popular?
  • How to Dye Your Hair Purple
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Dying Your Hair Purple
  • Tips for Maintaining Purple Hair
  • FAQ About Purple Hair

What Makes Purple Hair So Popular?

There are a few factors that contribute to the popularity of purple hair. First and foremost, it's a unique and eye-catching color. While natural hair colors will always be popular, many people enjoy experimenting with different hair colors to express themselves and stand out from the crowd.

Another reason for the popularity of purple hair is its versatility. From subtle highlights to all-over color, there are a variety of ways to incorporate purple into your hair. Additionally, purple works well on a variety of skin tones, making it accessible to a wide range of people.

Finally, the rise of social media has played a big role in the popularity of purple hair. With influencers and celebrities showing off their own purple locks, it's no wonder that others want to try the trend for themselves.

How to Dye Your Hair Purple

If you're interested in trying out purple hair for yourself, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, it's important to choose the right shade of purple for your skin tone and personal style. If you're unsure which shade to choose, consult with a hairstylist or do some research online.

Next, you'll need to decide whether you want to dye your entire head of hair or just add some highlights. This will depend on your personal preferences and the current state of your hair. If your hair is already colored or damaged, adding highlights may be a better option to avoid further damage.

Step-by-Step Guide to Dying Your Hair Purple

1. Gather your supplies: hair dye, gloves, a mixing bowl, a tint brush, and an old t-shirt or towel.

2. Protect your clothing and work area by laying down the old t-shirt or towel.

3. Put on your gloves to protect your hands from the dye.

4. Mix the hair dye according to the instructions on the package.

5. Apply the dye to your hair using the tint brush, making sure to cover all areas evenly.

6. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time, typically 20-30 minutes.

7. Rinse out the dye with cool water until the water runs clear.

8. Apply a color-safe conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.

9. Rinse out the conditioner with cool water.

10. Style your hair as desired and enjoy your new purple locks!

Tips for Maintaining Purple Hair

Once you've dyed your hair purple, it's important to take good care of it to ensure the color lasts as long as possible. Here are some tips for maintaining purple hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping the color.
  • Avoid hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, which can fade the color.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, which can also fade the color.
  • Consider using a color-depositing conditioner or hair mask to keep the color vibrant.

FAQ About Purple Hair

Here are some common questions about purple hair:

Is purple hair hard to maintain?

Purple hair requires some special care to maintain its vibrancy, but it's not necessarily harder to maintain than other hair colors. With the right products and techniques, you can keep your purple hair looking great for weeks or even months.

Can anyone pull off purple hair?

While purple hair looks great on a variety of skin tones, it may not be the best choice for everyone. If you're unsure whether purple hair is right for you, consult with a hairstylist or try a temporary color before committing to a permanent dye job.

Hair Care for Purple Hair

When you dye your hair any color, it's important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips for caring for your purple hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are designed for colored hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip the color.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons, which can damage your hair and fade the color.
  • Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be rocking purple hair like a pro in no time!
