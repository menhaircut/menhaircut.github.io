Best Hairstyle For Long Face And Big Nose Tips And Tricks

If you have a long face and a big nose, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for long faces and big noses, as well as provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

A long face and big nose can be a challenging combination when it comes to finding the perfect hairstyle. However, with the right hairstyle, you can balance out your features and create a flattering look. The key is to find a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face and draws attention away from your nose. In this article, we will explore the best hairstyles for long faces and big noses and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face and big nose, the goal is to create balance and proportion. This means choosing a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face and draws attention away from your nose. Some hairstyles to consider include:

  • Layered cuts
  • Bangs
  • Soft waves
  • Bob cuts
  • Updos

Layered Cuts

Layered cuts are an excellent option for those with long faces and big noses. Layers can add volume and movement to your hair, creating a more balanced look. If you have straight hair, consider adding long layers to create movement and texture. If you have curly hair, consider adding shorter layers to create definition and bounce.

Bangs

Bangs can be a great way to draw attention away from your nose and create a more balanced look. Consider getting side-swept bangs or curtain bangs that frame your face and add volume to the sides. Avoid getting blunt bangs that can make your nose appear more prominent.

Soft Waves

Soft waves can add volume and movement to your hair, creating a more balanced look. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves that frame your face. Avoid tight curls that can make your face appear longer.

Bob Cuts

Bob cuts can be a great way to add volume to the sides of your face and create a more balanced look. Consider getting a chin-length bob or a longer bob with layers. Avoid getting a bob that is too short, as this can make your face appear longer.

Updos

Updos can be a great way to draw attention away from your nose and create a more balanced look. Consider getting a low bun or a braided updo that adds volume to the sides of your face. Avoid getting a high bun or a sleek updo that can make your face appear longer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create a hairstyle that flatters your long face and big nose:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that adds volume to the sides of your face.
  2. Create soft waves or curls using a curling iron or hot rollers.
  3. Add layers to your hair to create movement and texture.
  4. Add side-swept or curtain bangs to frame your face.
  5. Avoid getting blunt bangs or a bob that is too short.
  6. Create an updo that adds volume to the sides of your face.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for choosing the best hairstyle for a long face and big nose:

  • Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair straight back from your face, as this can make your nose appear more prominent.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.
  • Avoid getting a haircut that is too short, as this can make your face appear longer.
  • Consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with long faces and big noses.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face and big nose?

Avoid hairstyles that pull your hair straight back from your face, as this can make your nose appear more prominent. Also, avoid getting blunt bangs or a bob that is too short.

What hairstyles are best for adding volume to the sides of my face?

Layered cuts, bangs, soft waves, and updos are all great options for adding volume to the sides of your face.

Should I consult with a hairstylist before choosing a hairstyle?

Yes, it's a good idea to consult with a hairstylist who has experience working with long faces and big noses. They can help you choose a hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident.

Hair Care

When caring for your hair, it's important to choose products that are gentle and nourishing. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools too often, as these can damage your hair and make it difficult to style. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and shiny, and avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip it of its natural oils.

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can create a look that flatters your long face and big nose and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Use the tips and tricks in this article to find the perfect hairstyle for you, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and looks until you find the one that works best for you.
