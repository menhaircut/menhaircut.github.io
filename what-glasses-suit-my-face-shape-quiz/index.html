What Glasses Suit My Face Shape Quiz A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're in need of a new pair of glasses, it's important to consider your face shape before making a purchase. Glasses that look great on one person may not necessarily suit another. Fortunately, there are quizzes available online that can help you determine what glasses suit your face shape best. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to take the what glasses suit my face shape quiz, tips on how to choose the perfect pair of glasses, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Take the What Glasses Suit My Face Shape Quiz

Taking the what glasses suit my face shape quiz is easy and can be done in just a few simple steps:

  1. Find a reliable quiz online. There are several quizzes available on the internet that can help you determine your face shape and what glasses would suit you best. Make sure to choose one that is reputable.
  2. Take a photo of yourself. For accurate results, ensure that the photo is taken in good lighting, with your hair pulled back, and your face straight ahead.
  3. Upload your photo. Upload the photo to the quiz website and answer any additional questions that the quiz may have.
  4. Get your results. The quiz will analyze your face shape and suggest glasses that would suit you best.

Tips on Choosing the Perfect Pair of Glasses

Once you know your face shape and what glasses would suit you best, it's time to start shopping. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing your perfect pair of glasses:

  • Consider your personal style. Choose glasses that not only suit your face shape but also fit your personal style.
  • Think about the frame size. Ensure that the frame size is proportionate to your face shape.
  • Pay attention to the color. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair color.
  • Try on several pairs. Don't be afraid to try on several pairs of glasses to find the perfect fit.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about what glasses suit my face shape:

What are the different face shapes?

There are several face shapes, including square, round, oval, heart-shaped, and diamond-shaped.

What glasses suit a round face shape?

Glasses that have a rectangular or square shape are best for round faces. These shapes help to add structure and angles to a round face.

What glasses suit an oval face shape?

Oval faces are considered to be the most versatile face shape, and most glasses will suit this shape. However, glasses with a wider frame or a bold shape can help to accentuate an oval face.

What glasses suit a square face shape?

Glasses with round or oval frames are best for square faces. These shapes help to soften the angles of a square face.

Hair Care

When choosing glasses, it's also important to consider your hair. Here are some tips on how to choose glasses based on your hair:

  • Short hair: Choose glasses that are rectangular or square-shaped to add structure to your face.
  • Long hair: Choose glasses that are wider than your face to balance out the length of your hair.
  • Curly hair: Choose glasses that have a bold frame to complement the volume of your hair.
  • Straight hair: Choose glasses with a round or oval frame to soften the angles of your face.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect pair of glasses can be a daunting task, but by taking the what glasses suit my face shape quiz, keeping in mind our tips, and considering your hair, you'll be sure to find the perfect pair in no time.
