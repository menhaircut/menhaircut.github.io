Curtain Bangs For Round Face Straight Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will flatter your round face and straight hair? Look no further than curtain bangs! This trendy and versatile style has been gaining popularity in recent years and is perfect for those with round faces and straight hair. In this article, we'll share with you everything you need to know about curtain bangs for round face straight hair, including how to style them, tips for maintaining them, and more!

  • How to Get Curtain Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of fringe that are parted in the middle and swept to the sides, creating a "curtain" effect. This style is particularly well-suited for those with round faces and straight hair, as the length and layers help to elongate and slim the face. Curtain bangs can also be customized to suit a variety of face shapes and hair textures, making them a versatile choice for anyone looking to change up their hairstyle.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're interested in getting curtain bangs for your round face and straight hair, here's what you need to know:

1. Find a stylist who specializes in bangs.

Not all hairstylists are created equal when it comes to cutting bangs, so it's important to find someone who has experience and expertise in this area. Look for a stylist who has a portfolio of bangs styles they've created, and be sure to communicate your specific hair type and face shape to ensure the best possible results.

2. Decide on the length and shape of your bangs.

There are many different styles of curtain bangs, from short and choppy to long and wispy. Consider your personal style, hair texture, and face shape when deciding on the length and shape of your bangs. Your stylist can provide guidance and recommendations based on your specific features.

3. Prepare for regular maintenance.

Curtain bangs require regular upkeep to maintain their shape and style. Plan on visiting your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim, and be prepared to style your bangs daily to keep them looking their best.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Curtain Bangs

Once you've got your curtain bangs cut, it's important to know how to style them properly to achieve the desired effect. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling curtain bangs:

1. Start with clean, dry hair.

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before styling your bangs. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair straight and smooth, focusing on your bangs to ensure they are straight and evenly parted.

2. Part your bangs in the middle.

Use a comb to create a straight part down the middle of your bangs, separating them into two equal sections.

3. Use a flat iron to straighten your bangs.

Using a flat iron, straighten your bangs so that they are smooth and sleek. Be sure to hold the iron at a slight angle to create a slight curve at the ends, which will help to frame your face.

4. Apply a small amount of styling product.

Use a small amount of hair styling product, such as a lightweight hair serum or shine spray, to smooth down any flyaways and add shine to your bangs.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a heat protectant.

When using heat styling tools on your bangs, be sure to use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent damage and breakage.

2. Regularly trim your bangs.

Plan on visiting your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your bangs looking their best.

3. Experiment with different styling techniques.

Don't be afraid to try out different styling techniques, such as blow drying your bangs with a round brush or adding a slight curl at the ends with a curling iron. Experimenting with different styles can help you find the look that best suits your face shape and personal style.

FAQs

Q: Are curtain bangs suitable for all hair types?

A: While curtain bangs can be customized to suit a variety of hair types, they are particularly well-suited for those with straight hair. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may need to style your bangs more carefully to ensure they lie flat and smooth.

Q: Will curtain bangs work for my round face?

A: Yes! Curtain bangs are particularly well-suited for those with round faces, as they help to elongate and slim the face.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

To keep your curtain bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

Choose a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Avoid using too much heat on your hair.

Excessive heat can damage your hair and cause breakage, so be sure to use heat styling tools sparingly and always use a heat protectant product.

3. Protect your hair from the elements.

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or scarf when outdoors.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the perfect curtain bangs for your round face and straight hair. Whether you're looking for a subtle change or a dramatic new look, curtain bangs are a versatile and stylish choice that are sure to turn heads!
