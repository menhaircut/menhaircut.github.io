Does Shorter Hair Make Your Face Look Slimmer

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair has been a popular trend for many years, and it's no surprise that many women opt for a shorter hairstyle. One of the many reasons for this is the belief that shorter hair can make your face look slimmer. In this article, we will explore this topic in more detail and answer the question, "does shorter hair make your face look slimmer?"

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Many women believe that shorter hair can make their face look slimmer. This is because shorter hair doesn't weigh down the face, and it can also highlight the cheekbones and jawline. However, this is not always the case. The right haircut depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

It's essential to find a hairstyle that balances your features and enhances your natural beauty. A good hairstylist can help you find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

How to

To determine whether shorter hair will make your face look slimmer, you need to consider your face shape. Different face shapes require different haircuts to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

  • If you have a round face, a layered bob or a pixie cut with volume at the top can create the illusion of a longer face.
  • If you have a square face, a short bob or a long bob with side-swept bangs can soften your features.
  • If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any haircut, but a chin-length bob or a lob can accentuate your cheekbones.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, a side-parted bob or a pixie cut with long bangs can balance your features.

Step by step

Here are some steps you can take to find the right haircut for your face shape:

  1. Identify your face shape by looking in the mirror and measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Research different hairstyles that flatter your face shape.
  3. Consult a hairstylist for their professional opinion.
  4. Consider your hair texture and personal style.
  5. Choose a haircut that balances your features and makes you feel confident and beautiful.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to consider when choosing a shorter hairstyle:

  • Ask your hairstylist to add layers to create volume and movement.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the perfect look for you.

FAQ

Q: Will a shorter haircut make my face look slimmer?

A: It depends on your face shape and the specific haircut. Consult with a hairstylist to find the right hairstyle for you.

Q: Can I still wear longer hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can. A longer haircut can still flatter your face shape if it's layered and has volume at the top.

Q: How often do I need to get my haircut to maintain the shape?

A: It depends on the length of your hair and how fast it grows. Typically, you should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape.

Hair Care

To keep your shorter haircut looking its best, consider the following hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from drying out.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush to detangle your hair without causing breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.

In conclusion, whether shorter hair makes your face look slimmer depends on your face shape and the specific haircut. However, the right hairstyle can enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and beautiful. Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect haircut for you, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
