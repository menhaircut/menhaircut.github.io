Top 10 Men s Haircuts Of All Time A Guide For The Modern Man

Written by Aprilia Aug 20, 2023 · 7 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle and looking for a change? Look no further than this guide to the top 10 men's haircuts of all time. From classic to modern, there is a look for every man out there. So grab your favorite styling product and let's get started.

1. The Crew Cut

The crew cut is a classic hairstyle that has been around for decades. It is a short, low-maintenance cut that is perfect for the modern man. The hair on top is cut short and tapered towards the back and sides. This is a great option for men with thick hair or those who want a clean-cut look.

How to

To achieve the crew cut, you will need to visit a barber or stylist. Ask for a short cut on top that is tapered towards the back and sides. The sides and back should be faded or tapered down to a shorter length. You can style with a small amount of product for a polished look.

Tips

Keep your crew cut looking fresh by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks. Use a matte finish product for a natural look, or a pomade for a more polished finish.

FAQ

Q: Is the crew cut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, the crew cut is a versatile style that can be adapted to suit all face shapes.

Hair Care

Keep your crew cut looking sharp by washing with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a light styling product to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

2. The Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a simple, no-fuss haircut that is perfect for men who want a low-maintenance look. It is a very short cut that is achieved with clippers, and can be customized to suit your individual style.

How to

To achieve the buzz cut, you will need clippers and a guard attachment. Choose the length you want and run the clippers all over your head, making sure to go in all directions to avoid any stray hairs.

Tips

The buzz cut is a great option for men with thinning hair or those who want to hide a receding hairline. Keep your scalp moisturized with a light oil or serum.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a buzz cut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows, but most men get a buzz cut every 2-3 weeks.

Hair Care

Keep your scalp moisturized with a light oil or serum. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid any scalp irritation.

3. The Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It is a versatile cut that can be adapted to suit any occasion, from the office to a night out on the town.

How to

To achieve the side part, start by washing your hair and combing it into your preferred part. Use a blow dryer to dry your hair and create volume. Finish with a light-hold product to keep your hair in place.

Tips

The side part is a great option for men with square or oval face shapes. Keep your hair looking neat by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the side part with curly hair?

A: Yes, the side part can be adapted to suit any hair type. Use a curl-defining product to enhance your natural texture.

Hair Care

Keep your hair looking its best by washing with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying to prevent damage.

4. The Quiff

The quiff is a stylish haircut that has been popularized by celebrities like David Beckham and Brad Pitt. It is a versatile cut that can be adapted to suit any face shape or hair type.

How to

To achieve the quiff, start by washing your hair and blow drying it. Use a round brush to create volume at the crown, then use a light-hold product to keep your hair in place.

Tips

The quiff is a great option for men with round or oval face shapes. Keep your hair looking neat by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Q: How do I maintain the quiff?

A: Use a light-hold product to keep your hair in place throughout the day. You may also want to use a volumizing spray or powder to enhance the volume at the crown.

Hair Care

Keep your hair looking its best by washing with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying to prevent damage.

5. The Pompadour

The pompadour is a classic haircut that has been popular since the 1950s. It is a stylish look that requires some maintenance, but it is worth the effort.

How to

To achieve the pompadour, start by washing your hair and blow drying it. Use a round brush to create volume at the crown, then use a high-hold product to keep your hair in place.

Tips

The pompadour is a great option for men with square or oval face shapes. Keep your hair looking neat by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the pompadour with thinning hair?

A: Yes, the pompadour can be adapted to suit any hair type. Use a volumizing spray or powder to enhance your natural texture.

Hair Care

Keep your hair looking its best by washing with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying to prevent damage.

6. The French Crop

The French crop is a low-maintenance haircut that is perfect for men who want a stylish look without the hassle. It is a short cut that is tapered towards the back and sides, with longer hair on top.

How to

To achieve the French crop, start by washing your hair and combing it forward. Use scissors to cut the hair on top to your desired length, then taper the back and sides using clippers.

Tips

The French crop is a great option for men with square or oval face shapes. Keep your hair looking neat by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve the French crop with curly hair?

A: Yes, the French crop can be adapted to suit any hair type. Use a curl-defining product to enhance your natural texture.

Hair Care

Keep your hair looking its best by washing with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Use a light-hold product to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

7. The Slick Back

The slick back is a classic hairstyle that has been popular for decades. It is a versatile cut that can be adapted to suit any occasion, from the office to a night out on the town.

How to
