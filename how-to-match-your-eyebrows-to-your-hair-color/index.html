How To Match Your Eyebrows To Your Hair Color

Written by Petter Aug 28, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your eyebrows looking mismatched with your hair color? It's important to make sure your eyebrows complement your hair color for a cohesive and polished look. In this article, we'll go over how to match your eyebrows to your hair color, step by step.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Match Your Eyebrows to Your Hair Color
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfectly Matched Eyebrows and Hair Color
  • FAQs

How to Match Your Eyebrows to Your Hair Color

The first step to matching your eyebrows to your hair color is to determine your hair color. Is your hair blonde, brunette, red or black? Once you have determined your hair color, it's important to select an eyebrow color that complements your hair color.

If you have blonde hair, you should opt for a lighter shade of eyebrow pencil or powder. For brunettes, choose a color that is one to two shades darker than your hair color. If you have red hair, choose a soft brown or auburn shade to match. For those with black hair, a dark brown or black eyebrow pencil will work best.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine your hair color.

Step 2: Choose an eyebrow color that complements your hair color.

Step 3: Use a spoolie or eyebrow brush to brush your eyebrows upwards, removing any excess powder or product.

Step 4: Fill in your eyebrows with your chosen eyebrow pencil or powder, using short, light strokes to mimic the appearance of eyebrow hairs.

Step 5: Use a spoolie or eyebrow brush to blend the product, ensuring a natural and cohesive look.

Tips for Perfectly Matched Eyebrows and Hair Color

Tip 1: If you're unsure about which color to choose, opt for a shade that is slightly lighter than your hair color. You can always add more product if needed.

Tip 2: Avoid using a shade that is too dark, as this can make your eyebrows appear harsh and unnatural.

Tip 3: For a more natural look, use eyebrow powder instead of a pencil. Powders allow for more control and a softer finish.

Tip 4: Don't forget to trim any stray eyebrow hairs for a polished look.

FAQs

Q: Can I dye my eyebrows to match my hair color?

A: It's not recommended to dye your eyebrows, as the skin around your eyebrows is more sensitive than your scalp. Instead, use an eyebrow pencil or powder to match your hair color.

Q: Can I use a brow gel to match my hair color?

A: Yes, you can use a brow gel in a color that matches your hair color for a more natural and polished look.

Hair Care Routines for Maintaining Your Look

Now that you know how to match your eyebrows to your hair color, it's important to maintain your look with a proper hair care routine. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and vibrant. Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant when necessary. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve perfectly matched eyebrows and hair color for a polished and cohesive look.
