What s The Best Human Hair Extensions

Written by Robby Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to add length or volume to your hair? Do you want to experiment with different styles without committing to a haircut? Human hair extensions may be the perfect solution for you. But with so many options on the market, how do you know which one to choose? In this article, we will explore what's the best human hair extensions for you and provide tips on how to care for them.

  • How to Choose Human Hair Extensions

Description

Human hair extensions are made from real human hair, giving them a more natural look and feel compared to synthetic hair extensions. They come in a variety of lengths, textures, and colors, making it easy to find a match for your hair. Human hair extensions can be clipped in, sewn in, or glued in, depending on your preference and hair type.

How to Choose Human Hair Extensions

When choosing human hair extensions, there are a few factors to consider:

  • Hair Type: Make sure to choose human hair extensions that match your natural hair texture. If you have straight hair, choose straight extensions. If you have curly hair, choose curly extensions.
  • Length: Decide how much length you want to add to your hair. Human hair extensions come in a variety of lengths, from 12 inches to 24 inches or longer.
  • Color: Choose a color that matches your natural hair color. If you can't find an exact match, consider getting custom extensions made.
  • Quality: Look for human hair extensions that are made from high-quality hair. The higher the quality, the more natural and long-lasting the extensions will be.

Step-by-Step Guide to Installing Human Hair Extensions

Installing human hair extensions can be done at home or at a salon. Here's a step-by-step guide for installing clip-in extensions:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Separate a section of hair at the nape of your neck and clip the rest of your hair out of the way.
  3. Open the clips on the extension and position it underneath the section of hair you just separated.
  4. Clip the extension securely into place.
  5. Repeat this process, working your way up your head in sections, until all the extensions are in place.
  6. Blend your natural hair with the extensions using a brush or comb.
  7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Human Hair Extensions

Proper care and maintenance will help your human hair extensions last longer. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your extensions: Wash your extensions every 6-8 weeks or as needed with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid heat styling: Heat can damage human hair extensions, so use heat styling tools sparingly or not at all.
  • Brush regularly: Brush your extensions daily with a soft-bristled brush to prevent tangling and matting.
  • Store properly: Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use. Avoid storing them in direct sunlight or in humid areas.

FAQs About Human Hair Extensions

Here are some frequently asked questions about human hair extensions:

  • How long do human hair extensions last? With proper care, human hair extensions can last up to a year or longer.
  • Can you swim with human hair extensions? Yes, you can swim with human hair extensions, but it's best to wear a swim cap to protect them from chlorine and salt water.
  • Can you dye human hair extensions? Yes, human hair extensions can be dyed, but it's best to have a professional do it to avoid damaging the extensions.

Hair Care for Human Hair Extensions

Human hair extensions require special care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for caring for your human hair extensions:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for human hair extensions.
  • Avoid using heat: Avoid using heat styling tools on your extensions, or use them sparingly.
  • Brush regularly: Brush your extensions daily with a soft-bristled brush to prevent tangling and matting.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair: Make sure your hair and extensions are completely dry before going to bed to prevent tangling.

Now that you know what's the best human hair extensions for you and how to care for them, you can confidently experiment with new styles and transform your look.
