Short Back And Sides Haircut For Girls A Bold And Edgy Look

Written by Aprilia Oct 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're looking for a bold and edgy haircut, a short back and sides haircut for girls might be just what you need. This versatile haircut can be customized to suit your unique style, whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or something more textured and messy. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about the short back and sides haircut, from how to get it to how to care for it.

What is a Short Back and Sides Haircut for Girls?

A short back and sides haircut for girls typically involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides, while leaving the top longer. This creates a contrast between the short and long sections, which can be styled in a variety of ways. Some common variations of this haircut include an undercut, where the sides and back are shaved or closely cropped, and a fade, where the hair gradually gets shorter from the top to the sides and back.

How to Get a Short Back and Sides Haircut for Girls

If you're interested in getting a short back and sides haircut for girls, the first step is to find a skilled hairstylist who can help you achieve the look you want. You'll want to bring in photos of hairstyles you like, as well as any specific requests you have for the cut. Your hairstylist will work with you to determine the best length and shape for your face shape and hair texture.

Once you've settled on a plan, your hairstylist will begin the cutting process. They may start by sectioning off the top of your hair and clipping it out of the way, then using clippers or scissors to cut the sides and back to your desired length. They may also use a razor or texturizing shears to add texture and movement to the cut. Once the sides and back are complete, they'll move on to the top and blend it seamlessly with the rest of the cut.

Tips for Styling a Short Back and Sides Haircut for Girls

One of the great things about a short back and sides haircut for girls is how versatile it can be. Here are some tips for styling your new cut:

1. Use the Right Products

Depending on your hair texture and the look you want to achieve, you may want to use different styling products. If you want a sleek and polished look, try a styling cream or pomade. If you want a more textured, messy look, try a sea salt spray or texturizing paste.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

With a short back and sides haircut, you have the freedom to experiment with different styles. Try slicking the top back for a classic look, or spiking it up for something more edgy. You can also play with different parting styles, such as a deep side part or a zigzag part.

3. Consider Color

Adding some color to your short back and sides haircut can really make it pop. Consider adding highlights or lowlights to the top section for a dimensional look, or going for an all-over color for a bold statement.

FAQs About Short Back and Sides Haircuts for Girls

1. Will a short back and sides haircut look good on me?

Short back and sides haircuts can work for a variety of face shapes and hair types. However, it's important to work with a skilled hairstylist who can help you choose a cut that flatters your unique features.

2. How often should I get my short back and sides haircut trimmed?

To maintain the shape and style of your short back and sides haircut, you'll want to get it trimmed every 4-6 weeks. Your hairstylist can help you determine the best maintenance schedule for your individual cut.

3. Can I style a short back and sides haircut differently for different occasions?

Absolutely! One of the great things about this haircut is how versatile it can be. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to create different looks for different occasions.

Hair Care Tips for Short Back and Sides Haircuts for Girls

To keep your short back and sides haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips to follow:

1. Wash and Condition Regularly

Keeping your hair clean and moisturized is key to maintaining a healthy, shiny look. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and wash your hair every 2-3 days.

2. Protect Your Hair from Heat Damage

If you use heat styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling wand, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

3. Schedule Regular Trims

To keep your short back and sides haircut looking sharp, schedule regular trims with your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short back and sides haircut for girls with confidence and style.
