How To Know If You Look Good With Dark Hair

Written by Frank Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you thinking of going for a dark hair color but unsure if it will suit you? It can be daunting to make such a drastic change to your appearance, but don't let fear hold you back! With a few tips and tricks, you can easily figure out if you'll look good with dark hair. In this article, we'll guide you through the process so you can confidently make the switch.

  • Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in figuring out if you look good with dark hair is to determine your skin tone. This will help you pick the right shade of dark hair that will complement your complexion. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones, and if they look green, you have warm undertones. If you can't tell, you likely have neutral undertones.

For cool undertones, go for ashy or cool-toned dark hair colors, such as espresso or jet black. For warm undertones, opt for rich, warm-toned dark hair colors, such as chestnut or mahogany. Neutral undertones can go for any dark hair color, but try to avoid shades that are too warm or too cool.

Step 2: Consider Your Eye Color

Your eye color can also play a role in determining if dark hair will suit you. If you have light-colored eyes, such as blue or green, dark hair can create a striking contrast that will make your eyes pop. If you have dark-colored eyes, such as brown or black, dark hair can complement and enhance the depth of your eye color.

Step 3: Analyze Your Current Hair Color

If you currently have light-colored hair, such as blonde or light brown, going for a dark hair color can be a drastic change. However, if you have naturally dark hair or have previously had dark hair, it may be easier to make the switch. Consider how drastic of a change you're comfortable with and if you're willing to commit to the maintenance required for dark hair.

Step 4: Use Technology to Your Advantage

If you're still unsure if dark hair will suit you, try using technology to your advantage. There are various online tools and apps that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and virtually try on different hair colors. This can give you a better idea of how you'll look with dark hair without the commitment.

Tips for Maintaining Dark Hair

Once you've made the switch to dark hair, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for dark hair to prevent fading.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair and cause it to fade.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

FAQs

What if I have a lot of gray hair?

Dark hair can be a great option for covering up gray hair as it provides good coverage and can create a youthful appearance. However, be sure to choose a shade that is not too dark as this can create a harsh contrast with your skin and make you look older.

Can I dye my hair dark at home?

While it is possible to dye your hair dark at home, it's always recommended to go to a professional stylist. They can ensure that the color is applied evenly and can help you choose the right shade for your skin tone and eye color.

Hair Care for Dark Hair

Dark hair requires proper care to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a clarifying shampoo once a week to remove any buildup from styling products.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to too much sunlight as this can cause it to fade.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or peroxide, on your hair as this can cause damage and breakage.
