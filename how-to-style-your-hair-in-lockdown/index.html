How To Style Your Hair In Lockdown Tips Tricks And Ideas

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

The pandemic has changed the way we live our lives, including how we take care of ourselves. With salons and barbershops closed or operating at limited capacity, many of us are left to fend for ourselves when it comes to our hair. But don't worry, you don't have to resort to shaving it all off just yet. In this article, we'll share some tips, tricks, and ideas on how to style your hair in lockdown.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Your Hair in Lockdown
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair in Lockdown
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Style Your Hair in Lockdown

Before we dive into the specifics, it's important to note that everyone's hair is different. What works for one person may not work for another. That being said, here are some general guidelines on how to style your hair in lockdown:

  • Experiment with different styles: This is the perfect time to try out new hairstyles that you may not have had the chance to before.
  • Invest in some styling tools: A good hair dryer, straightener, or curling iron can make a world of difference in achieving the look you want.
  • Use the right products: Whether you're trying to tame frizz, add volume, or define curls, there's a product out there for you. Do some research and find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help: If you're really struggling, ask a trusted friend or family member for their opinion or even for help styling your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style your hair in lockdown:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Decide on the style you want to achieve.
  3. If necessary, use a hair straightener or curling iron to create the base for your style.
  4. Apply any necessary styling products, such as mousse, gel, or hairspray.
  5. Use a hair dryer to set the style in place.
  6. Finish with any final touches, such as a hair accessory or a spritz of shine spray.

Tips for Styling Your Hair in Lockdown

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair in lockdown:

  • Try out different braids: There are so many different types of braids you can experiment with, from the classic French braid to the trendy fishtail braid.
  • Embrace your natural texture: If you have curly hair, embrace it! Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and create a natural, effortless style.
  • Accessorize: A cute headband, scarf, or hair clip can add a pop of color and interest to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to trim your own hair: If you're feeling daring, you can attempt to trim your own hair at home. Just be sure to do your research and use proper techniques and tools.

FAQs

Here are some common questions about styling your hair in lockdown:

Is it possible to cut your own hair at home?

Yes, it is possible to cut your own hair at home, but it can be risky. If you're going to attempt it, be sure to do your research and use proper techniques and tools.

What are some easy hairstyles to try at home?

Some easy hairstyles to try at home include a messy bun, a low ponytail, or a simple braid.

How can I make my hair look less greasy?

There are a few things you can do to make your hair look less greasy, such as using dry shampoo, washing your hair less frequently, and avoiding heavy products.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair during lockdown. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly: While you don't want to over-wash your hair, it's important to keep it clean and free of buildup.
  • Use a good conditioner: A good conditioner can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, making it easier to style.
  • Avoid heat styling: If possible, avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Eat a healthy diet: Eating a diet rich in vitamins and nutrients can help to promote healthy hair growth and overall hair health.

In conclusion, styling your hair in lockdown may seem daunting at first, but with a little experimentation and some good products, you can achieve a great look from the comfort of your own home. Just remember to take care of your hair and have fun with it!
