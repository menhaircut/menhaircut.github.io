How To Make Short Flat Hair Fluffy A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 18, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you have short hair that always looks flat and lifeless? You're not alone! Many people struggle with flat hair, especially when it's short. Fortunately, there are several ways to make short hair look fluffy and voluminous. In this article, we'll show you how to make short flat hair fluffy with easy steps, tips, and hair care tips. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Short Flat Hair Fluffy
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Make Short Flat Hair Fluffy

The first step in making your short hair fluffy is to choose the right hairstyle. Short haircuts that have layers and texture will help create volume and movement in your hair. However, if you have a blunt haircut, you can still create volume with the right styling techniques.

Here are some easy ways to make short flat hair fluffy:

1. Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

One of the easiest ways to add volume to your hair is to blow dry it upside down. This technique allows your hair to dry in the opposite direction, which creates more lift and volume. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying to create even more volume.

2. Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair. These products contain ingredients that help lift the hair at the roots and add texture to the hair shaft.

3. Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an old-school trick that still works wonders for creating volume. Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb your hair at the roots, then smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section. This will create a fluffy, voluminous look that lasts all day.

4. Use Hair Rollers

Hair rollers are another great tool for creating volume in short hair. Use small, velcro rollers on the top section of your hair and leave them in for about 15-20 minutes. Once you remove the rollers, use your fingers to gently tousle your hair and create more volume.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some easy ways to make short flat hair fluffy, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair with a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. These products will help create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Avoid rubbing or pulling your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Step 3: Apply a Volumizing Mousse

Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help create lift and volume.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair in the opposite direction. Use a round brush to lift your hair at the roots while blow drying to create even more volume.

Step 5: Tease Your Hair

Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb your hair at the roots, then smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section. This will create a fluffy, voluminous look that lasts all day.

Step 6: Use Hair Rollers

For added volume, use small, velcro rollers on the top section of your hair and leave them in for about 15-20 minutes. Once you remove the rollers, use your fingers to gently tousle your hair and create more volume.

Tips for Fluffy Short Hair

Here are some additional tips for making short hair look fluffy and voluminous:

  • Use a texturizing spray to add grip and hold to your hair
  • Try a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add volume
  • Use a hair wax to separate and define your layers
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair

FAQs

How often should I wash my short, flat hair?

It's important not to over-wash your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days, or as needed.

What if I have thin or fine hair?

If you have thin or fine hair, it's even more important to choose the right products and styling techniques. Look for lightweight, volumizing products that won't weigh your hair down, and avoid heavy oils or serums.

Hair Care for Fluffy Short Hair

Maintaining fluffy, voluminous hair requires proper hair care. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and vibrant:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair
  • Use a heat protectant spray before blow drying or using hot tools
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain your style and prevent split ends
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair

With these tips and techniques, you can make your short flat hair look fluffy and voluminous. Remember to choose the right products and styling techniques for your hair type, and take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
