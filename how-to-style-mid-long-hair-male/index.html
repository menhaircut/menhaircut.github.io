How To Style Mid Long Hair Male A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Oct 09, 2023 · 5 min read

Mid long hair is a popular hairstyle among men. It’s versatile, easy to maintain, and can be styled in various ways. If you’re looking to style your mid long hair, this guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice. Read on to learn how to style mid long hair male.

  • How to Style Mid Long Hair Male
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Mid Long Hair Male
  • FAQs

How to Style Mid Long Hair Male

Styling mid long hair male is all about finding the right balance between length, texture, and shape. Here are some popular ways to style mid long hair male:

  • Man bun
  • Ponytail
  • Half-up half-down
  • Wavy
  • Slicked back

Each of these styles has its unique look and requires different techniques to achieve. Let’s dive into the step-by-step guide on how to style mid long hair male.

Step-by-Step Guide

Man Bun

The man bun is a popular mid long hairstyle that’s easy to achieve. Here’s how:

  1. Brush your hair back and gather it into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins.
  4. Use a hairspray to keep the bun in place.

For a more relaxed look, leave a few strands of hair loose around your face and ears.

Ponytail

The ponytail is another classic mid long hairstyle that’s perfect for any occasion. Here’s how to achieve it:

  1. Brush your hair back and gather it into a low or high ponytail.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. For a more polished look, wrap a strand of hair around the hair tie and secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Use a hairspray to keep the ponytail in place.

You can also add some texture to your ponytail by using a curling iron or a hair straightener.

Half-up Half-down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a trendy mid long hairstyle that’s perfect for a casual day out. Here’s how to achieve it:

  1. Brush your hair back and gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Secure the ponytail with a hair tie.
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose.
  4. You can add some texture to the bottom half of your hair by using a curling iron or a hair straightener.

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to show off their long hair while keeping it out of their face.

Wavy

The wavy mid long hairstyle is perfect for those who want to add some texture to their hair. Here’s how to achieve it:

  1. Apply a texturizing spray or mousse to your hair.
  2. Use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to dry your hair while scrunching it with your hands.
  3. You can also use a curling iron or a hair straightener to add some waves.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep the waves in place.

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a relaxed and effortless look.

Slicked Back

The slicked-back mid long hairstyle is perfect for a formal occasion. Here’s how to achieve it:

  1. Apply a pomade or a hair gel to your hair.
  2. Use a comb to slick your hair back.
  3. You can also use a hair dryer to achieve a more polished look.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to keep the slicked-back look in place.

This hairstyle is perfect for those who want a sophisticated and elegant look.

Tips for Styling Mid Long Hair Male

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect mid long hairstyle:

  • Use the right hair products for your hair type.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb.
  • Don’t overuse hair products as it can weigh your hair down.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.
  • Visit a hairstylist regularly to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained.

FAQs

Can I style my mid long hair without using any hair products?

Yes, you can style your mid long hair without using any hair products. However, using hair products can help add texture, volume, and hold to your hair.

How often should I visit a hairstylist for trimming?

You should visit a hairstylist every 6 to 8 weeks for trimming to keep your hair healthy and well-maintained.

Hair Care for Mid Long Hair Male

Here are some hair care tips for mid long hair male:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can dry out your hair.
  • Avoid using a hairbrush on wet hair as it can cause breakage.
  • Avoid using hair styling tools on wet hair as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.

By following these hair care tips, you can maintain healthy and well-maintained mid long hair.

Conclusion

Mid long hair is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in various ways. By following this comprehensive guide, you can achieve the perfect mid long hairstyle that suits your style and personality. Don’t be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles and hair products to find what works best for you. Remember to also take care of your hair by following the hair care tips provided in this guide. With the right styling techniques and hair care, you can maintain healthy, shiny, and well-maintained mid long hair.
