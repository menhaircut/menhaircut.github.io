Hairstyles For Thin Grey Hair Over 60 Tips And Ideas

Written by Bardi Nov 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

As we age, our hair gets thinner and weaker, especially for women who have gone through menopause. Grey hair also becomes more noticeable and requires extra care to look its best. But there's no need to worry, as there are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter thin grey hair over 60. In this article, we'll show you how to style your hair to enhance your beauty and confidence.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Thin Grey Hair over 60

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it's important to consider a few factors when choosing the right one for you:

Face Shape

Your face shape plays a crucial role in determining which hairstyle will look best on you. If you have a round face, go for a layered cut that frames your face and elongates it. If you have a square face, opt for soft waves that soften your features. For an oval face, any hairstyle will do.

Hair Texture

Thin hair can be fine or coarse, straight or curly, and each texture requires a different approach. Fine hair needs volume and texture, while coarse hair needs to be tamed and softened. Straight hair benefits from layers and waves to create movement, while curly hair can be shaped into a chic bob or left natural.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle also plays a role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you're an active person who enjoys outdoor activities, opt for a low-maintenance hairstyle that can withstand wind and sweat. If you're a social butterfly who loves to dress up, go for a sophisticated updo or a sleek blowout.

10 Hairstyles for Thin Grey Hair over 60

Now that you know what to consider, let's explore some of the best hairstyles for thin grey hair over 60:

1. Layered Bob

A layered bob is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It adds volume and texture to thin hair and frames the face beautifully. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer one, depending on your preference.

2. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a daring hairstyle that can transform your look and boost your confidence. It's a low-maintenance style that requires minimal styling, making it ideal for busy women over 60.

3. Shaggy Bob

A shaggy bob is a modern take on the classic bob that adds movement and texture to thin hair. It's a versatile hairstyle that can be worn straight or wavy, and it's perfect for women who want a chic and edgy look.

4. Layered Lob

A layered lob is a longer version of the bob that falls just above the shoulders. It's a great option for women who want a low-maintenance style that's easy to style and looks great with or without waves.

5. Short Curly Bob

A short curly bob is a playful and feminine hairstyle that adds volume and texture to thin hair. It's perfect for women with natural curls who want to embrace their texture and show off their grey locks.

6. Messy Updo

A messy updo is a casual and chic hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. It's a great way to add volume and texture to thin hair and create a relaxed and effortless look.

7. Sleek Ponytail

A sleek ponytail is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that's perfect for formal events or a night out. It's a great way to show off your grey hair and add some sophistication to your look.

8. Layered Waves

Layered waves are a great way to add movement and texture to thin hair. They're perfect for women who want a natural and effortless look that's easy to style and maintain.

9. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a flattering hairstyle that softens the face and adds dimension to thin hair. They're perfect for women who want a subtle change without going too short or too bold.

10. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a casual and comfortable hairstyle that's perfect for everyday wear. It's a great way to keep your hair out of your face and add some volume and texture to thin hair.

Hair Care Tips for Thin Grey Hair over 60

Now that you have some hairstyle ideas, let's talk about how to take care of your thin grey hair to keep it healthy and beautiful:

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner

A volumizing shampoo and conditioner can help add volume and texture to thin hair. Look for products that are designed for your hair type and avoid using too much conditioner, as it can weigh down your hair.

2. Avoid heat styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it look brittle and dry. If you must use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant spray and keep the temperature low to prevent damage.

3. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

4. Embrace your natural texture

Embrace your natural texture and work with it instead of against it. If you have curly hair, use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls. If you have straight hair, use a texturizing spray to add some movement and body.

5. Protect your hair from the sun

UV rays can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

FAQ

What is the best hairstyle for thin grey hair over 60?

The best hairstyle for thin grey hair over 60 depends on your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Some popular options include layered bob, pixie cut, shaggy bob, layered lob, short curly bob, messy updo, sleek ponytail, layered waves, side-swept bangs, and messy bun.

How do I make my thin grey hair look thicker?

You can make your thin grey hair look thicker by using volumizing products, getting regular trims, avoiding heat styling, and embracing your natural texture.

How often should I wash my thin grey hair?

You should wash your thin grey hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Avoid washing it too often, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it look dry and brittle.

Conclusion

Hairstyles for thin grey hair over 60 are all about enhancing your natural beauty and confidence. Whether you prefer a short and sassy cut or a long and layered style, there's a hairstyle that will suit your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Follow our hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful, and embrace your grey locks with pride!
