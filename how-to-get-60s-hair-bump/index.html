How to Get 60s Hair Bump

If you're looking to add some vintage flair to your hairstyle, the 60s hair bump is a great option. This retro look can be achieved with a few simple steps and some basic styling tools. In this article, we will guide you on how to get 60s hair bump and provide tips and hair care advice along the way.

How to Get 60s Hair Bump

Here are the steps to follow to get the 60s hair bump:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair to ensure that it's clean and free of any product buildup. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and body to your hair.

Step 2: Apply Mousse

Apply a generous amount of mousse to your hair, focusing on the roots. This will help to add volume and hold to your hair.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, lifting the roots as you go. This will help to create the bump at the crown of your head. Once your hair is dry, use a teasing comb to tease the roots of your hair to add more height and volume.

Step 4: Pin Your Hair

Take a small section of hair from the crown of your head and tease it with a comb. Spray some hairspray onto the teased section and then gently smooth the top layer of hair over the teased section. Pin the section in place with bobby pins.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you have pinned the bump in place, spray your hair with a good-quality hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect 60s hair bump:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add thickness and body to your hair.
  • Apply mousse to your hair before blow drying to add volume and hold.
  • Tease the roots of your hair to add height and volume.
  • Use bobby pins to secure the bump in place.
  • Finish with a good-quality hairspray to hold the style in place.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the 60s hair bump:

  1. Can I achieve the 60s hair bump with short hair?
    Yes, you can achieve the hair bump with short hair by teasing the hair at the crown of your head and pinning it in place.
  2. How long will the 60s hair bump last?
    The hair bump should last for several hours if you use a good-quality hairspray to hold the style in place.
  3. Can I achieve the 60s hair bump without using heat styling tools?
    Yes, you can achieve the hair bump without using heat styling tools by teasing the hair at the crown of your head and pinning it in place.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

Now that you know how to get 60s hair bump, you can rock this retro style with confidence. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
