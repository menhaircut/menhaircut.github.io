How To Cut Your Own Hair With Clippers Undercut A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh new hairstyle, but don't want to spend a fortune at the salon, consider cutting your own hair with clippers. An undercut is a popular style that involves shaving the hair on the sides and back of your head, while leaving the top longer. It's a trendy look that can be achieved at home with the right tools and techniques. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut your own hair with clippers undercut.

How to Cut Your Own Hair with Clippers Undercut

Before we get started, you'll need a few things:

  • Clippers
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • Scissors
  • Clipper guards

Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that your hair is clean before you begin.

Step 2: Choose the Right Clipper Guard

The clipper guard you choose will determine how short your hair will be. If you're not sure what size guard to use, start with a longer one and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Start with the Sides

Begin by using the clippers to shave the hair on the sides of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the clipper guards to control the length. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure you're cutting it evenly.

Step 4: Move to the Back

Once you've finished the sides, move to the back of your head. Use the same technique, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Make sure you're cutting the hair evenly and using the right clipper guard.

Step 5: Trim the Top

Once you've shaved the sides and back, it's time to trim the top. Use the scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. You can choose to leave it longer or shorter, depending on your preference.

Step 6: Blend the Hair

To blend the hair on the sides and back with the top, use the clippers without a guard. This will create a smooth transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step 7: Check Your Work

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use the mirror to check your work. Make any necessary adjustments to ensure that your hair is even and looks the way you want it to.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair with Clippers Undercut

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut:

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to avoid cutting your hair too short.
  • Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure you're cutting it evenly.
  • Make sure the clippers are clean and oiled before you begin.
  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting your own hair takes practice, so don't get discouraged if it doesn't look perfect the first time.

FAQ

Can I Cut My Own Hair with Clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with clippers. It takes some practice, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut at home.

What Clipper Guard Should I Use?

The clipper guard you use will depend on how short you want your hair to be. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Do I Need to Use Scissors?

Yes, you will need to use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head. Clippers are great for shaving the sides and back, but scissors are better for cutting longer hair on top.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair with Clippers Undercut

After cutting your own hair with clippers undercut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great haircut at home with clippers undercut. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to create a trendy new look without spending a fortune at the salon.
