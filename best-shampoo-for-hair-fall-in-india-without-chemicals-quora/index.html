How to Choose the Best Shampoo for Hair Fall in India Without Chemicals Quora

When choosing a shampoo for hair fall, look for one that contains natural ingredients like aloe vera, neem, and tea tree oil. These ingredients are known for their hair-strengthening properties and can help reduce hair fall. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, parabens, and artificial fragrances as they can damage your hair.
