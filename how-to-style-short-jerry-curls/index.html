How To Style Short Jerry Curls A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have short jerry curls, you might be wondering how to style them to achieve the best look. Styling short jerry curls is not as difficult as you might think. With the right products and techniques, you can easily achieve a stylish and defined look. In this article, we will take you through the step-by-step process of how to style short jerry curls.

Description

Short jerry curls are a popular hairstyle for people with natural hair. This hairstyle is achieved by using a chemical process to curl the hair, resulting in a defined and bouncy curl pattern. To maintain the curls, it is important to use the right hair products and styling techniques.

How to Style Short Jerry Curls

Here are the steps to follow when styling short jerry curls:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Before styling your short jerry curls, you need to make sure your hair is clean. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to wash your hair, and follow up with a conditioner to keep your curls moisturized.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Once your hair is clean, apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated. This will help to prevent frizz and keep your curls defined.

Step 3: Apply a Curl Cream

Next, apply a curl cream to your hair. This will help to define your curls and give them bounce. Make sure to evenly distribute the curl cream throughout your hair.

Step 4: Use a Diffuser

To dry your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer. This will help to evenly distribute the heat and prevent your curls from becoming frizzy.

Step 5: Define Your Curls

Once your hair is dry, use a curl defining cream to further define your curls. Apply the cream to your hair in small sections and use your fingers to twist and define each curl.

Step 6: Set Your Style

To set your style, use a hairspray or a styling gel. This will help to keep your curls in place and prevent them from becoming frizzy throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you style your short jerry curls:

  • Use a microfiber towel or a t-shirt to dry your hair. This will help to prevent frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or products that contain silicone. These can weigh down your curls and make them look greasy.
  • Try to avoid using heat tools such as flat irons or curling irons. These can damage your curls and cause breakage.

FAQ

Can I style my short jerry curls without using heat?

Yes, you can style your short jerry curls without using heat. Instead of using a diffuser, you can air dry your hair or use a hooded dryer. You can also use rollers or flexi-rods to achieve a curly style without using heat.

How often should I wash my short jerry curls?

You should aim to wash your short jerry curls every 1-2 weeks. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Hair Care

To keep your short jerry curls looking their best, it is important to take good care of your hair. This means using the right products and techniques to keep your curls moisturized and defined. Here are some tips to help you care for your short jerry curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to wash your hair.
  • Follow up with a conditioner to keep your curls hydrated.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to further moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and using the right products and techniques, you can keep your short jerry curls looking their best.
