How To Change Your Hair Colour In Animal Crossing

Written by Luffy Jul 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Animal Crossing allows players to customize their characters in a variety of ways, including changing their hair colour. If you're looking to switch things up and give your character a new look, here's a step-by-step guide on how to change your hair colour in Animal Crossing.

How to Change Your Hair Colour

Changing your hair colour in Animal Crossing is a simple process that can be done at any time. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Visit Harriet's Salon

To change your hair colour, you'll need to visit Harriet's Salon. The salon can be found on Main Street, and Harriet will be waiting for you inside.

Step 2: Choose Your Hair Colour

Once you're inside Harriet's Salon, talk to her to begin the hair colouring process. She will present you with a menu of hair colours to choose from. Scroll through the options until you find the one you like best.

Step 3: Confirm Your Choice

After you've chosen your hair colour, Harriet will show you a preview of what your character will look like with the new hair colour. If you're happy with the preview, confirm your choice and Harriet will begin the colouring process.

Step 4: Wait for the Colour to Set

After Harriet has finished colouring your hair, you'll need to wait for the colour to set. This should only take a few moments, so be patient.

Step 5: Check Your New Hair Colour

Once the colour has set, check your character's appearance to make sure you're happy with the new hair colour. If you're not satisfied, you can always visit Harriet again to try a different colour.

Tips for Changing Your Hair Colour

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing your hair colour in Animal Crossing:

  • Experiment with different colours to find the one that suits you best.
  • Remember that you can always change your hair colour again if you don't like the first choice.
  • Consider your character's overall appearance when choosing a new hair colour.
  • Don't forget to save your game after changing your hair colour.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about changing your hair colour in Animal Crossing:

Can I change my hair colour more than once?

Yes, you can change your hair colour as many times as you like.

Can I change my hair colour without visiting Harriet's Salon?

No, you must visit Harriet's Salon to change your hair colour.

How much does it cost to change my hair colour?

Changing your hair colour at Harriet's Salon costs 3,000 Bells.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your character's hair after changing its colour. Make sure to brush it regularly and use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep it looking healthy and shiny.

Changing your hair colour in Animal Crossing is a fun way to switch things up and give your character a new look. With these simple steps, you'll be able to change your hair colour in no time. So go ahead and experiment with different colours until you find the one that's just right for you!
