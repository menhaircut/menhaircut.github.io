How To Make Waves Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Oct 07, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to add some texture to your locks? Look no further than the wave hairstyle! This versatile look can be dressed up or down, and it's perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to make waves hairstyle in just a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Waves Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Perfect Waves

How to Make Waves Hairstyle

The wave hairstyle involves creating soft, loose waves in your hair. This can be done with a variety of tools, including curling irons, flat irons, and even braids. Below, we'll show you how to make waves with a curling iron.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you begin curling, you'll need to prep your hair. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed to add volume and texture. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the curling iron.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting at the bottom. Clip the top layers out of the way so that you can work on the bottom sections first.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel, holding it for a few seconds before releasing. Repeat this process with the rest of your hair, working your way up from the bottom sections to the top.

Step 4: Create Loose Waves

Once you've curled all of your hair, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create soft, loose waves. You can also use a wide-toothed comb or a paddle brush to help create the waves.

Step 5: Finish With Hairspray

To keep your waves in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Hold the can about 12 inches away from your hair and spray evenly.

Tips for Perfect Waves

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect wave hairstyle:

  • Use a curling iron with a larger barrel to create loose waves.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse to your roots before blow-drying to add volume.
  • Don't curl all of your hair in the same direction - alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

Can I make waves with a flat iron?

Yes, you can create waves with a flat iron by twisting the hair as you glide the iron down the hair shaft. However, a curling iron may be easier to use if you're new to creating waves.

How do I create beachy waves?

To create beachy waves, start by applying a salt spray to your damp hair. Then, braid your hair and sleep with the braids in overnight. In the morning, remove the braids and use your fingers to loosen the waves.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner designed for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day - limit to once or twice a week.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to create perfect waves in no time. Whether you're going for a casual daytime look or a glamorous evening style, the wave hairstyle is sure to make you feel confident and beautiful.
