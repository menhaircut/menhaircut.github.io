The Best Hair Colour For 70 Year Old Woman A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a 70 year old woman looking to give your hair a new lease on life, a fresh hair colour can be just the thing you need. However, it's important to choose the right hair colour to match your skin tone and hair type. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about hair colour for 70 year old woman, including tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice.

Why Choose a New Hair Colour?

As we age, our hair naturally loses some of its pigment, which can result in a dull, lifeless appearance. A new hair colour can help to brighten up your complexion and make you feel more youthful and vibrant. Additionally, a new hair colour can help to cover up any grey hairs, which can be a source of frustration for many women.

How to Choose the Right Hair Colour

Choosing the right hair colour is essential to achieving the best results. Here are some tips to help you choose the right hair colour:

Consider Your Skin Tone

Your skin tone is an essential factor to consider when choosing a new hair colour. If you have warm undertones, opt for warm colours like golden blonde, copper, or auburn. If you have cool undertones, choose cool colours like ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another crucial factor to consider when choosing a new hair colour. If you have thin hair, choose a lighter hair colour to give your hair more volume. If you have thick hair, opt for a darker hair colour to help define your locks.

Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle is another essential factor to consider when choosing a new hair colour. If you have an active lifestyle, choose a low-maintenance hair colour that won't require frequent touch-ups. If you have a more relaxed lifestyle, opt for a high-maintenance hair colour that will give you a more polished look.

Step-by-Step Guide for Hair Colour for 70 Year Old Woman

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect hair colour:

Step 1: Choose Your Hair Colour

Choose your hair colour based on your skin tone, hair type, and lifestyle.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash your hair thoroughly and dry it with a towel. Make sure your hair is completely dry before applying any hair colour.

Step 3: Apply the Hair Colour

Follow the instructions provided with your hair colour kit to apply the hair colour evenly to your hair. Be sure to follow the recommended processing time.

Step 4: Rinse Your Hair

Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water until the water runs clear.

Step 5: Condition Your Hair

Apply the conditioner provided with your hair colour kit to your hair and leave it on for the recommended time. Rinse your hair thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as desired using a blow dryer or curling iron.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Colour

Here are some tips to help you maintain your new hair colour:

Use Colour-Safe Shampoo and Conditioner

Use a shampoo and conditioner that is specifically designed for colour-treated hair to help maintain the vibrancy of your hair colour.

Avoid Hot Styling Tools

Avoid using hot styling tools like curling irons and straighteners, as they can cause your hair colour to fade more quickly.

Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Try to avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can cause your hair colour to fade more quickly. Instead, opt for dry shampoo or a gentle cleansing conditioner.

Frequently Asked Questions About Hair Colour for 70 Year Old Woman

What is the best hair colour for 70 year old woman?

The best hair colour for 70 year old woman depends on her skin tone, hair type, and lifestyle. Warm colours like golden blonde, copper, or auburn are great for women with warm undertones, while cool colours like ash blonde, platinum, or burgundy are better for women with cool undertones.

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home using a hair colour kit. However, it's important to follow the instructions provided with the kit carefully to achieve the best results.

How often should I touch up my hair colour?

This depends on your hair type and the colour you choose. Some hair colours require more frequent touch-ups than others. Consult with your stylist or read the instructions provided with your hair colour kit for guidance.

Will dyeing my hair damage it?

Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, especially if you choose a drastic hair colour change. However, using a high-quality hair colour and following the instructions carefully can minimize any potential damage.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hair colour can be a game-changer for 70 year old woman. Whether you're looking to cover up grey hairs, brighten up your complexion, or simply try something new, a new hair colour can help you achieve your goals. By following the tips, step-by-step instructions, and hair care advice provided in this guide, you'll be well on your way to achieving the perfect hair colour for 70 year old woman.
