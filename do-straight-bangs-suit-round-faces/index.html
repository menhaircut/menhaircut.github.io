Do Straight Bangs Suit Round Faces

Written by Oliver Jun 29, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may have heard that straight bangs are not for you. However, that is not entirely true. Straight bangs can actually be a great option for those with round faces – it just depends on how you style them. In this article, we will explore whether straight bangs suit round faces and provide some tips on how to make them work for you.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved lines and a lack of angular features. The width and length of the face are approximately equal, with a rounded chin and full cheeks. When it comes to hairstyles, the goal is usually to create the illusion of a more angular face shape. This is where straight bangs come in.

How to

When considering straight bangs for a round face, it's important to think about the length and thickness of the bangs. Ideally, the bangs should be long enough to cover the forehead but not so long that they create a horizontal line across the face. This can make the face appear wider and more round.

Additionally, the thickness of the bangs is important. Thick, heavy bangs can overwhelm a round face and make it look even rounder. Instead, opt for thinner, wispy bangs that frame the face and create a more delicate look.

Step by Step

If you're ready to give straight bangs a try, here are some steps to get you started:

  1. Start by selecting the right length and thickness of bangs for your face shape.
  2. Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you begin.
  3. Section off the hair that will become the bangs. This will usually be a triangle-shaped section at the front of your hairline.
  4. Use a comb to create a straight line across the section of hair you've separated. This will be the line where you cut the bangs.
  5. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut straight across, following the line you created with the comb. It's best to cut less hair than you think you need, as you can always go back and trim more later.
  6. Once you've cut the bangs, use a blow dryer and a round brush to style them. This will help create volume and shape.
  7. Finish with a bit of hairspray to hold the bangs in place.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to keep in mind when styling straight bangs for a round face:

  • Consider adding some layers to the rest of your hair to create more angles and balance out the roundness of your face.
  • If you're not sure about committing to straight bangs, try clip-in bangs first to see how they look and feel.
  • Experiment with different ways of parting your hair to create different looks with your bangs.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about straight bangs and round faces:

Can straight bangs make a round face look even rounder?

It's possible, but it depends on how the bangs are cut and styled. As mentioned earlier, it's important to choose the right length and thickness of bangs to avoid creating a horizontal line across the face.

Are there any other types of bangs that work well for round faces?

Yes! Side-swept bangs can be a great option for those with round faces. They create an asymmetrical look that helps break up the roundness of the face.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep your straight bangs looking their best. This means using a high-quality shampoo and conditioner, avoiding excessive heat styling, and getting regular trims to keep your bangs looking neat and tidy.

Overall, straight bangs can be a great option for those with round faces. With the right length, thickness, and styling techniques, you can create a chic and flattering look that highlights your best features.
