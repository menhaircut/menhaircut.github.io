Can I Have Short Hair With A Fat Face

Written by Robby Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round or chubby face, you may have wondered whether you can pull off short hair. The good news is that the answer is yes! Short hair can look great on anyone, regardless of their face shape. In fact, there are many short hairstyles that can complement a round face and bring out your best features. In this article, we will discuss how to choose the right short haircut for a fat face, and provide some tips on how to care for and style your new look.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut

When it comes to choosing a short haircut for a round face, there are a few things to consider. First, you want to find a style that will balance out the roundness of your face. This can be achieved by adding volume to the top of your head or creating angles with your hair. Second, you want to find a style that will flatter your best features, whether that's your eyes, cheekbones, or jawline.

Step by Step

Here are some steps you can follow to choose the right short haircut for your fat face:

  • Step 1: Determine your face shape. You can do this by measuring the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If these measurements are roughly equal, you have a round face.
  • Step 2: Look for hairstyles that add height to the crown of your head, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers.
  • Step 3: Consider hairstyles with angles, such as an asymmetrical bob or a short cut with side-swept bangs.
  • Step 4: Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your head, such as a blunt bob or a bowl cut.
  • Step 5: Consult with a hairstylist to find the best short haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you care for and style your short hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  • Use a texturizing spray or wax to add definition and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid heavy products that can weigh down your hair, such as oils or serums.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to maintain your short haircut and keep it looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair and fat faces:

  • Q: Can I have bangs with a short haircut and a fat face?
  • A: Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut for a round face.
  • Q: Should I go for a blunt or layered haircut?
  • A: Layered haircuts can be more flattering for a round face, as they add movement and texture to the hair.
  • Q: How often should I get my short haircut trimmed?
  • A: It's recommended to get your haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care

Whether you have a fat face or not, taking care of your hair is important to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is tailored to your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, as this can promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water, as this can keep your hair and scalp moisturized.

In conclusion, having a fat face should not hold you back from rocking a short haircut. By following these tips and choosing the right style for your face shape, you can look and feel confident with your new look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
