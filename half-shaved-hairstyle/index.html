The Half Shaved Hairstyle A Bold And Edgy Look

Description

The half shaved hairstyle is a bold and edgy look that has been popular for a few years now. This hairstyle involves shaving off one side or half of your head, leaving the other side with long hair. It's a versatile haircut that can be styled in different ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy, tousled style.

This hairstyle is often associated with the punk and alternative scene, but it has become more mainstream in recent years. Celebrities like Rihanna, Cassie, and Miley Cyrus have all rocked the half shaved look, inspiring many to try it themselves.

How to

If you're considering getting a half shaved hairstyle, it's important to find a skilled hairstylist who can create the look you want. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Choose your side

Decide which side of your head you want to shave. It's important to consider your face shape and hair texture when making this decision. If you have a round face, for example, shaving the side that's closer to your cheekbone can make your face appear slimmer.

2. Decide on the length

There are different lengths you can choose for the shaved side, from a buzz cut to a longer, faded look. Discuss your options with your hairstylist to find the best length for your hair type and face shape.

3. Prepare your hair

Before your hairstylist starts shaving, they will need to section off the hair that will remain long. They may also need to wash and dry your hair to make it easier to work with.

4. Shave the side

Your hairstylist will use clippers or a razor to shave the chosen side of your head. They may also use scissors to blend the shaved section with the longer hair.

5. Style your hair

Once the shaving is done, your hairstylist can help you style your hair in different ways. You can try a sleek, polished look with gel or pomade, or go for a more tousled, textured style with sea salt spray or wax.

Step by step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting a half shaved hairstyle:

Step 1: Find inspiration

Look for pictures of half shaved hairstyles that you like and bring them to your hairstylist. This will help them understand the look you're going for.

Step 2: Consult with your hairstylist

Talk to your hairstylist about your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine if a half shaved hairstyle is right for you. They can also recommend the best length and style for your hair.

Step 3: Prepare your hair

Wash and dry your hair before your appointment. Your hairstylist may also want to trim your hair before shaving.

Step 4: Section off your hair

Your hairstylist will section off the hair that will remain long and clip it out of the way.

Step 5: Shave the side

Your hairstylist will use clippers or a razor to shave the chosen side of your head. They may also use scissors to blend the shaved section with the longer hair.

Step 6: Style your hair

Your hairstylist can help you style your hair in different ways, from a sleek, polished look to a tousled, textured style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind if you're considering a half shaved hairstyle:

  • Choose a skilled hairstylist who has experience with this haircut.
  • Consider your face shape and hair texture when deciding on the length and side to shave.
  • Be prepared for regular maintenance to keep the shaved side looking fresh.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the look you like.
  • Accessorize with jewelry or bold makeup to enhance the edgy look.

FAQ

Q: Will shaving half of my head damage my hair?
A: Shaving your hair won't damage it, but it can cause some discomfort if you have a sensitive scalp. It's important to take care of your scalp by keeping it clean and moisturized.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a half shaved hairstyle?
A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a half shaved hairstyle. You can try a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun that shows off the shaved side.

Q: Will a half shaved hairstyle suit my face shape?
A: A half shaved hairstyle can suit many different face shapes, but it's important to consult with a hairstylist to find the best look for you.

Hair Care

Here are some tips to take care of your half shaved hairstyle:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep both the shaved and long sections clean.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and irritation on the scalp.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on the shaved side, as it can cause damage and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to the longer section to keep it healthy and hydrated.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep the shaved side looking fresh.

Overall, the half shaved hairstyle is a bold and edgy look that can be customized to suit your personal style. With the right hairstylist and hair care routine, you can rock this hairstyle with confidence.
