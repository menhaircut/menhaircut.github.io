How To Make Your Hair More Flowy Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are a man who desires to have hair that is flowy, soft, and bouncy, you are not alone. Men’s hair tends to be thicker, coarser, and less prone to movement, but there are ways to make your hair more flowy. In this article, we will guide you through the steps to achieve your desired hairstyle. Read on for tips, how-to’s, and hair care advice.

Description

Flowy hair is a hairstyle that is characterized by soft, bouncy, and voluminous locks. It is a popular hairstyle among men and women alike. Flowy hair is achieved by using the right hair products, adopting the right hair care routine, and styling your hair appropriately.

How To Make Your Hair More Flowy Guys: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose the Right Hair Products

The first step to achieving flowy hair is to use the right hair products. Choose a shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for your hair type. If you have thick hair, opt for a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have thin hair, choose a shampoo and conditioner that add volume and thickness. Use a leave-in conditioner to hydrate and nourish your hair.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, towel dry your hair to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair vigorously with a towel as this can cause damage and breakage. Instead, gently pat your hair dry with a towel.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair. Choose a product that is designed to add volume and movement to your hair. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume. Blow dry your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up. Once your hair is dry, use a cool shot to set the style.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair using your fingers or a comb. Create movement by running your fingers through your hair. You can also use a curling iron to add curls and waves to your hair.

Tips for Making Your Hair More Flowy

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using heavy hair products
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often as this can cause damage

Frequently Asked Questions

What is flowy hair?

Flowy hair is a hairstyle that is characterized by soft, bouncy, and voluminous locks. It is achieved by using the right hair products, adopting the right hair care routine, and styling your hair appropriately.

What products can I use to make my hair more flowy?

You can use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, a leave-in conditioner, and a styling product that is designed to add volume and movement to your hair.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair every two to three days. Over-washing your hair can strip it of natural oils, which can make it dry and brittle.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Men’s hair tends to be thicker, coarser, and less prone to movement, but there are ways to make your hair more flowy. To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Choose hair products that are formulated for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often
  • Wash your hair every two to three days
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair as this can cause damage and breakage

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the flowy hair that you desire. Remember to choose the right hair products, adopt a good hair care routine, and style your hair appropriately.
