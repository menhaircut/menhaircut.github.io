Hair Cutting For Long Face Girl A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a long face girl looking for the best hair cutting styles to flatter your features? Look no further! In this article, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive guide to hair cutting for long face girls. We’ll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to the best hairstyles for long face girls, along with tips for maintaining your new haircut.

Table of Contents:

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Best Hairstyles for Long Face Girls
  • Haircut Tips for Long Face Girls
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Face Girls

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing a flattering haircut for a long face girl is to determine your face shape. Long face girls have faces that are longer than they are wide, with high foreheads, prominent chins, and narrow jawlines. To determine if you have a long face, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin and compare it to the width of your face at its widest point.

If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have a long face shape. Other common face shapes include oval, round, square, and heart-shaped faces.

Best Hairstyles for Long Face Girls

When it comes to hair cutting for long face girls, the goal is to create balance and proportion. The best hairstyles for long face girls are those that add width to the face, either through layers, curls, or bangs. Here are some hairstyle ideas to consider:

1. Layers

Layers are a great way to add volume and movement to long face girls’ hair. Opt for long, face-framing layers that start at your cheekbones or collarbone. Avoid layers that are too short or choppy, as they can make your face appear even longer.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to add width to a long face. They can be worn long or short, depending on your preference. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look longer.

3. Soft Waves

Soft waves are a great way to add volume and width to long face girls’ hair. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose waves that start at your cheekbones or collarbone. Avoid tight curls, as they can make your face appear even longer.

4. Bob Haircut

A bob haircut can be a great option for long face girls, especially if it hits just above the shoulders. This length creates width and volume around the face, making it appear less long. Avoid bobs that are too short or too long, as they can make your face look longer.

Haircut Tips for Long Face Girls

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut as a long face girl:

1. Avoid Straight, Blunt Cuts

Straight, blunt cuts can make a long face appear even longer. Opt for styles with layers, curls, or waves instead.

2. Add Volume at the Sides

Adding volume at the sides of your hair can help balance out a long face. Consider a style with layers or curls that start at your cheekbones or collarbone.

3. Consider Bangs

Side-swept or wispy bangs can help break up a long face and add width. Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face look even longer.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Girls

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long face girl haircut looking its best:

1. Use a Volumizing Shampoo

A volumizing shampoo can help add volume and texture to your hair, making it appear fuller and thicker.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

A wide-tooth comb can help prevent breakage and damage when detangling your long hair.

3. Avoid Over-Styling

Over-styling your hair with heat tools can cause damage and breakage. Try to limit your use of flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for a long face girl?

The best haircut for a long face girl is one that adds volume and width to the face, such as layers, curls, or side-swept bangs.

Can long face girls wear short haircuts?

Yes, but it’s important to choose a short haircut that adds volume and width to the face, such as a layered bob or pixie cut.

How often should long face girls get their hair cut?

Long face girls should get their hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain their style and prevent split ends.


In conclusion, choosing the right hair cutting style for long face girls is all about creating balance and proportion. Layers, curls, and side-swept bangs can all help add width and volume to the face, while avoiding blunt cuts and tight curls can help prevent elongating the face. With the right haircut and hair care routine, long face girls can look and feel their best every day.
