What A Handlebar Moustache Says About You

Written by Eveline Sep 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering growing a handlebar moustache? Or do you already have one and wonder what it says about you? In this blog post, we'll explore what a handlebar moustache says about your personality and how to take care of it.

What is a Handlebar Moustache?

A handlebar moustache is a style of moustache that extends past the lips and curls upwards at the ends, resembling the handlebars of a bicycle. It was popular in the late 19th century and has recently made a comeback in modern times.

What Does a Handlebar Moustache Say About You?

A handlebar moustache can say many things about your personality. It can portray you as a confident and stylish person who takes grooming seriously. It can also suggest that you have a rebellious or adventurous streak, as it is not a common style of facial hair.

Some people may see a handlebar moustache as a symbol of masculinity and strength, as it requires patience and dedication to grow and maintain. Others may view it as a sign of elegance and sophistication, as it was once worn by wealthy gentlemen in the past.

How to Grow a Handlebar Moustache

If you're ready to grow a handlebar moustache, here are some steps to get started:

Step 1: Stop Shaving

The first step in growing a handlebar moustache is to stop shaving your upper lip. Let your moustache grow out for a few weeks until it reaches a length that you can start shaping.

Step 2: Trim and Shape

Once your moustache has grown out, you can start shaping it into a handlebar style. Use a pair of small scissors to trim any stray hairs and shape the ends into curls. You can also use a moustache comb to help guide the hairs into place.

Step 3: Use Moustache Wax

To maintain the shape of your handlebar moustache, you'll need to use moustache wax. Apply a small amount of wax to the ends of your moustache and use your fingers to twist and shape them into curls. Repeat this process every day to keep your moustache looking neat and tidy.

Tips for Taking Care of Your Handlebar Moustache

Here are some tips to keep your handlebar moustache looking its best:

  • Trim your moustache regularly to keep it neat and tidy.
  • Use a high-quality moustache wax to maintain the shape of your curls.
  • Don't forget to clean your moustache regularly with a moustache shampoo or soap.
  • Avoid getting food or drinks in your moustache, as it can be difficult to clean.
  • Be patient and don't give up if your moustache takes time to grow and shape.

FAQs

Is a Handlebar Moustache Easy to Maintain?

A handlebar moustache requires regular maintenance to keep it looking neat and tidy. You'll need to trim it regularly and use moustache wax to maintain the shape of your curls.

Can Anyone Grow a Handlebar Moustache?

Most men can grow a handlebar moustache if they have enough facial hair. However, it may take longer for some men to grow a full moustache than others.

What is the Best Moustache Wax to Use?

There are many different types of moustache wax available, but the best one for you will depend on your personal preferences. Look for a wax that is easy to apply and provides a strong hold without feeling sticky or greasy.

Hair Care for Your Handlebar Moustache

Your handlebar moustache is made of hair, so it's important to take care of it just like you would the hair on your head. Use a moustache shampoo or soap to keep it clean and hydrated, and avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools.

With proper care and maintenance, your handlebar moustache can be a stylish and unique addition to your look. Whether you're going for a classic or modern style, embrace your handlebar moustache and wear it with confidence!
