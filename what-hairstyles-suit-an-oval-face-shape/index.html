What Hairstyles Suit An Oval Face Shape

Written by Aprilia Jul 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky because you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, there are certain hairstyles that are more flattering for your face shape. In this article, we will discuss what hairstyles suit an oval face shape and give you some tips to help you find the perfect hairstyle for you.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Oval face shapes have balanced proportions, with a slightly wider forehead than the chin and high cheekbones. This face shape is considered ideal because it can wear almost any hairstyle. However, certain hairstyles can enhance your features and flatter your face shape.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for an oval face shape is to determine your face shape. If you are unsure, you can measure your face from your hairline to your chin and from cheekbone to cheekbone. If these measurements are roughly the same, you have an oval face shape.

Next, you should consider your hair type, texture, and length. Some hairstyles work better with certain hair types and textures. For example, if you have curly hair, you may want to consider a layered haircut to enhance your curls. If you have fine hair, you may want to try a bob or a pixie cut to add volume.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape.

2. Consider your hair type, texture, and length.

3. Look for hairstyles that enhance your features.

4. Experiment with different styles.

5. Consult with a hairstylist.

Tips

When choosing a hairstyle for an oval face shape, you should consider your facial features. If you have a prominent nose or chin, you may want to avoid hairstyles that draw attention to these areas. Instead, opt for hairstyles that enhance your eyes or cheekbones.

Another tip is to consider your lifestyle and personal style. If you are a busy mom, you may want a low-maintenance hairstyle that is easy to style. If you are a fashion-forward person, you may want to experiment with different colors and cuts.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oval face shape?

Avoid hairstyles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or heavy layers around the jawline.

What hairstyles are best for an oval face shape?

Hairstyles that are flattering for an oval face shape include long layers, a bob, a pixie cut, and a side-swept fringe.

Can I wear my hair in a middle part if I have an oval face shape?

Yes, a middle part is a flattering hairstyle for an oval face shape.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, you should follow a hair care routine that includes shampooing and conditioning regularly, using a heat protectant before using hot tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, if you have an oval face shape, you can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, certain hairstyles can enhance your features and flatter your face shape. Consider your hair type, texture, and length, and experiment with different styles to find the perfect hairstyle for you.
