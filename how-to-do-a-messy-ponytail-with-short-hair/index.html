How To Do A Messy Ponytail With Short Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 08, 2023 · 3 min read

Short hair doesn't have to limit your styling options. In fact, a messy ponytail can be a fun and easy way to switch up your look. Follow this step-by-step guide to achieve a stylish and effortless messy ponytail with short hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a Messy Ponytail with Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for a Perfect Messy Ponytail
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Do a Messy Ponytail with Short Hair

The first thing you need to do is gather all the necessary tools and products. Here's what you'll need:

  • Comb or brush
  • Hair elastic
  • Bobby pins
  • Texturizing spray or dry shampoo

Once you have everything you need, you can start with the actual styling process. Here's how:

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Start by brushing your hair to remove any tangles or knots. You can also use a comb if you prefer.

Step 2: Apply texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair. This will make it easier to style and give your ponytail a messy, effortless look.

Step 3: Gather your hair at the crown of your head and secure it with a hair elastic. Make sure to leave some pieces loose to create that messy look.

Step 4: Use bobby pins to secure any loose pieces of hair that are sticking out. You can also pull out some strands to frame your face.

Step 5: Gently tug on the hair at the crown of your head to create some volume and messiness. You can also use your fingers to tousle your hair and create more texture.

Step 6: Finally, apply a bit more texturizing spray or dry shampoo to the ponytail to add some extra texture and hold.

Tips for a Perfect Messy Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect messy ponytail:

  • Use a hair elastic that matches your hair color for a seamless look.
  • Don't worry if your ponytail isn't perfectly centered or symmetrical. The messier, the better!
  • Add a cute scrunchie or hair accessory for some extra flair.
  • Experiment with different texturizing sprays and dry shampoos to find the one that works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Q: Can I do a messy ponytail with really short hair?

A: Yes! Even if your hair is just a few inches long, you can still create a messy ponytail by following the same steps and using plenty of texturizing spray.

Q: Can I wear a messy ponytail to a formal event?

A: Absolutely! A messy ponytail can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. You can add a fancy hair accessory or pull out some strands to frame your face for a more formal look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some general hair care tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips, you can achieve a stylish and effortless messy ponytail with short hair. Have fun experimenting with different texturizing sprays and accessories to make the look your own!
