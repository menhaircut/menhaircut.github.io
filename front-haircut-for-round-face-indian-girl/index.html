Front Haircut For Round Face Indian Girl

Written by Frank Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that suits your round face? As an Indian girl, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape. This guide will help you discover the ideal front haircut for round face Indian girl that will enhance your natural beauty.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have a wider cheekbone and a softer jawline, making it essential to choose a hairstyle that slims down the face. The ideal front haircut for a round face Indian girl is one that adds volume to the crown while slimming down the cheeks. This style draws attention to the eyes and cheekbones, creating a more balanced look.

How to

Before getting a new haircut, it is essential to determine your face shape. To do this, tie your hair back and take a picture of your face straight on. Draw a line from the top of your forehead to the bottom of your chin and another line from the widest part of your cheeks. If the two lines meet to form a circle, you have a round face.

Step by Step

The best front haircut for round face Indian girl is the layered bob. This haircut adds volume to the crown and slims down the cheeks. Here are the steps to achieve this look:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it.
  2. Part your hair down the middle and section it into four parts.
  3. Take the front section and cut it into a blunt bob that hits just below the chin.
  4. Graduate the back section by cutting it shorter than the front.
  5. Add layers to the crown section to add volume.
  6. Use a flat iron to add a slight bend to the ends of the hair.
  7. Apply a texturizing spray to finish the look.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when getting a front haircut for round face Indian girl:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that fall at the cheekbones as they can make your face look wider.
  • Choose layers that start at the chin or below to slim down the face.
  • Avoid center parts as they can make the face appear wider.
  • Go for side-swept bangs to draw attention to the eyes.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for a round face Indian girl?

The best haircut for a round face Indian girl is the layered bob. This haircut adds volume to the crown and slims down the cheeks.

What hairstyles should round face Indian girls avoid?

Round face Indian girls should avoid blunt cuts that fall at the cheekbones as they can make the face look wider. They should also avoid center parts as they can make the face appear wider.

What should I tell my hairstylist if I want a front haircut for a round face?

When getting a front haircut for a round face, tell your hairstylist that you want a layered bob that adds volume to the crown and slims down the cheeks. Ask for side-swept bangs to draw attention to the eyes.

Hair Care

To maintain the front haircut for round face Indian girl, it is essential to follow a hair care routine. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent oil buildup.
  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to the crown.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep the hair healthy.
  • Trim the ends every six to eight weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know the best front haircut for round face Indian girl, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist. With this guide, you'll be able to achieve a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your natural beauty.
