What Is The Most Popular Length Of Hair Extensions

Written by Aprilia Nov 08, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you considering getting hair extensions but not sure what length to choose? The length of your hair extensions can make a big difference in your overall look. In this article, we’ll explore what is the most popular length of hair extensions and give you some tips on how to care for them.

Table of Contents

  • What is the Most Popular Length of Hair Extensions?
  • How to Choose the Right Length of Hair Extensions
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Installing Hair Extensions
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Extensions
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Your Extensions

What is the Most Popular Length of Hair Extensions?

The most popular length of hair extensions is 18 inches. This length falls just below the shoulders and gives a natural, full look. It’s a versatile length that works for both short and long hairstyles. Many women choose 18-inch extensions because they can be styled in a variety of ways, such as straight, wavy, or curly.

However, the length of your hair extensions should ultimately depend on your personal preference and the look you’re trying to achieve. Some women prefer shorter or longer extensions for specific styles or occasions.

Other Popular Lengths of Hair Extensions

Aside from 18 inches, other popular lengths of hair extensions include:

  • 14 inches: This length falls just above the shoulders and is great for adding volume to shorter hair.
  • 20 inches: This length falls just below the chest and is great for longer hairstyles.
  • 22 inches: This length falls at or just above the navel and is great for dramatic, long hairstyles.

How to Choose the Right Length of Hair Extensions

Choosing the right length of hair extensions can be tricky, but there are a few factors to consider:

  • Your natural hair length: If you have short hair, you may want to choose shorter extensions to blend in better. If you have longer hair, you may want to choose longer extensions to add volume and length.
  • Your face shape: Certain lengths of hair extensions can complement your face shape better than others. If you have a round face, for example, longer extensions can help elongate your face.
  • Your lifestyle: Consider how often you’ll be styling your hair with the extensions. Longer extensions may require more maintenance and styling time.
  • Your budget: Longer extensions tend to be more expensive than shorter ones, so consider your budget when choosing the length.

Step-by-Step Guide to Installing Hair Extensions

If you’re new to hair extensions, installing them can seem daunting. However, with the right tools and technique, it can be a simple process. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  2. Section your hair by creating a horizontal part at the back of your head and clipping the top section up.
  3. Starting at the nape of your neck, take a small section of hair and clip the rest out of the way.
  4. Apply a small amount of extension glue to the weft of the extension and press it onto the root of your hair.
  5. Repeat this process, working your way up to the top of your head.
  6. Once all extensions are attached, style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair Extensions

Proper maintenance is key to keeping your hair extensions looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the extensions.
  • Brush your extensions daily with a wide-toothed comb or brush to prevent tangling.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage the extensions.
  • Avoid sleeping with wet hair to prevent matting and tangling.
  • Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when not in use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do hair extensions damage your hair?

If installed and maintained properly, hair extensions should not damage your hair. However, improper installation or maintenance can lead to breakage and damage.

How long do hair extensions last?

The lifespan of hair extensions depends on the quality of the extensions and how well they’re maintained. High-quality extensions can last up to six months or longer with proper care.

Can you swim with hair extensions?

You can swim with hair extensions, but it’s best to wear a swim cap to protect them from chlorine and saltwater. After swimming, rinse your hair and extensions thoroughly with fresh water and apply a leave-in conditioner.

Hair Care for Your Extensions

Caring for your hair extensions is important to keep them looking their best. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid using products with alcohol, as this can dry out the extensions.
  • Avoid using products with silicone, as this can cause buildup on the extensions.
  • Avoid using heavy oils or serums on the extensions, as this can weigh them down.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as these can damage the extensions.

Now that you know what is the most popular length of hair extensions, you can confidently choose the right length for you. Remember to take proper care of your extensions to keep them looking beautiful and healthy.
