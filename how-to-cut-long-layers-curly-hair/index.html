How To Cut Long Layers Curly Hair

Written by William Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. It can be beautiful and voluminous, but it can also be difficult to manage. One way to make curly hair more manageable is to cut it into long layers. Long layers can help to reduce bulk and add movement to curly hair. In this article, we will discuss how to cut long layers curly hair in relaxed English language.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Long Layers Curly Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Long layers are a great way to add shape and movement to curly hair. They can help to reduce bulk and make your curls look more defined. Cutting long layers into curly hair can be tricky, however, and it is important to follow the right steps to achieve the desired look.

How to Cut Long Layers Curly Hair

The first step in cutting long layers into curly hair is to make sure that the hair is clean and dry. It is best to cut curly hair when it is dry, as wet hair can be misleading and may result in uneven layers.

Next, you will need to section the hair. Divide the hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to secure each section.

Starting with the back sections, take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use a pair of sharp hairdressing scissors to cut the hair at an angle. The angle of the cut will depend on the length of the hair and the desired length of the layers. It is best to start with a small angle and gradually increase it until you achieve the desired effect.

Once you have cut the first section, use it as a guide for the rest of the sections. Cut each section at the same angle, being careful to keep the sections even and symmetrical.

Step by Step

To cut long layers into curly hair, follow these steps:

  1. Wash and dry the hair.
  2. Section the hair into four parts.
  3. Start with the back sections and take a small section of hair.
  4. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut at an angle.
  5. Use the first section as a guide for the rest of the sections.
  6. Cut each section at the same angle, being careful to keep the sections even and symmetrical.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut long layers into curly hair:

  • Use sharp hairdressing scissors.
  • Start with a small angle and gradually increase it.
  • Take your time and be patient.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Make sure that each section is even and symmetrical.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting long layers into curly hair:

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It is recommended to cut your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my curly hair when it's wet?

It is best to cut curly hair when it is dry, as wet hair can be misleading and may result in uneven layers.

Can I cut my own curly hair?

It is possible to cut your own curly hair, but it is recommended to have it done by a professional hairdresser to avoid any mistakes.

Hair Care

After cutting long layers into curly hair, it is important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage curly hair.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Cutting long layers into curly hair can be a great way to add shape and movement to your curls. By following the right steps and taking good care of your hair, you can achieve a beautiful and healthy head of curly hair.
