How To Style Short Hair Shaved Sides A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 06, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short hair with shaved sides and are looking for ways to style it, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you through step by step on how to style short hair shaved sides. From the latest trends to easy tips, we have got it all covered. Whether you have long or short hair, these hairstyles will work for you. Let's get started.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Short hair with shaved sides is a popular hairstyle among women and men. It is a bold and edgy look that requires minimal maintenance. The hairstyle features short hair on the top and shaved sides. The length of the hair on the top varies, ranging from a pixie cut to a bob. You can style your hair in different ways, depending on your hair type, length, and personal style.

How To

Styling short hair with shaved sides is easy and fun. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Cleanse your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair. Use a hairdryer to dry your hair completely.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel. This will help to hold your hair in place and add texture.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use a hairbrush or your fingers to style your hair. You can create different hairstyles, such as a messy top knot, a slicked-back look, or a textured bob. Be creative and experiment with different styles.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style short hair with shaved sides:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Cleanse your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Rinse thoroughly and towel-dry your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or gel. This will help to hold your hair in place and add texture.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into two parts: the top and the sides. Use hair clips to secure the top part of your hair.

Step 4: Style the Sides

Use a hair trimmer to shave the sides of your hair. Choose the length that suits your personal style.

Step 5: Style the Top

Use a hairbrush or your fingers to style the top part of your hair. You can create different styles, such as a messy top knot or a textured bob.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a hairspray to hold your hair in place and add shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair with shaved sides:

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits your face shape and personal style.
  • Use a hair trimmer with different length settings to achieve different looks.
  • Apply a hair serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush and styling products.
  • Visit your hairdresser regularly to maintain your hairstyle.

FAQ

Q: Can I style my hair with shaved sides if I have curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. Use a curl-enhancing product to define your curls and add texture to your hair.

Q: How often do I need to shave the sides of my hair?

A: It depends on how fast your hair grows. You may need to shave your hair every 2-4 weeks to maintain the look.

Q: Can I color my hair if I have shaved sides?

A: Yes, you can. Talk to your hairdresser to find the best color for your hair type and personal style.

Hair Care

Short hair with shaved sides requires minimal maintenance. Follow these tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair serum to add shine and moisture to your hair.
  • Visit your hairdresser regularly to trim your hair and maintain your hairstyle.

Now that you know how to style short hair with shaved sides, it's time to try it out. Experiment with different hairstyles and have fun with your new look.
