How To Cover Thinning Hair With Braids

Written by Petter Sep 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling with thinning hair? Do you find yourself constantly trying to hide your scalp? If so, braids can be a great solution. Not only do they provide a stylish look, but they can also cover up any thinning areas. In this article, we will discuss how to cover thinning hair with braids.

Description

Thinning hair can be caused by a variety of factors such as genetics, stress, and aging. It can be frustrating and can lead to a lack of confidence. Braids can help to hide thinning areas and provide a fuller look. They can also be a protective style for your hair.

How to

Step 1: Start with clean and detangled hair. This will make the braiding process easier and smoother.

Step 2: Choose the type of braids that you want to use. Some popular options include box braids, cornrows, and twists.

Step 3: Section your hair into small parts. This will make the braiding process easier and more manageable.

Step 4: Start braiding your hair. If you are not confident in your braiding skills, you can seek the help of a professional stylist.

Step 5: Once your braids are complete, you can style them as desired. You can add accessories such as beads or hair ties.

Step by Step

Step 1: Wash and detangle your hair.

Step 2: Choose the type of braids that you want to use.

Step 3: Section your hair into small parts.

Step 4: Start braiding your hair.

Step 5: Style your braids as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when using braids to cover thinning hair:

  • Choose a style that you are comfortable with.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause further damage.
  • Take breaks from braiding to give your hair a rest.
  • Be gentle when taking out your braids to avoid damaging your hair.

FAQ

Can braids cause thinning hair?

Yes, braids can cause thinning hair if they are done too tightly or if they are left in for too long. It is important to be gentle when braiding and to take breaks to give your hair a rest.

How long can I leave my braids in?

You can leave your braids in for up to 8 weeks, but it is recommended to take them out after 6 weeks to avoid damaging your hair.

How can I prevent thinning hair?

You can prevent thinning hair by maintaining a healthy diet, reducing stress, avoiding tight hairstyles, and using hair care products that are gentle on your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair when using braids as a way to cover thinning areas. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat on your hair.
  • Take breaks from braiding to give your hair a rest.
  • Be gentle when taking out your braids to avoid damaging your hair.

Now that you know how to cover thinning hair with braids, you can confidently rock this stylish look while also hiding any thinning areas. Remember to take care of your hair and to be gentle when braiding to avoid further damage.
