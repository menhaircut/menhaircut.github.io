Why Long Hair Falls Out More Understanding The Causes And Solutions

Written by Christine Jul 07, 2023 · 5 min read

Long hair is often considered a woman’s crowning glory. It adds beauty and femininity to a woman’s appearance. However, long hair also requires more maintenance and care than short hair. One of the most common hair problems that women with long hair face is hair fall. In this article, we will discuss why long hair falls out more and what you can do to prevent it.

  2. How to Prevent Hair Fall in Long Hair

Causes of Hair Fall in Long Hair

Long hair falls out more than short hair because it is subjected to more stress and strain. Here are some of the common causes of hair fall in long hair:

  • Physical stress: Long hair is heavier than short hair, and the weight of the hair can cause physical stress on the hair follicles. This can lead to hair fall.
  • Chemical treatments: Long hair is often exposed to chemical treatments like coloring, perming, and straightening. These treatments can weaken the hair and cause it to fall out.
  • Heat styling: Long hair is also more likely to be exposed to heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. The heat can damage the hair and cause it to fall out.
  • Poor diet: Long hair requires more nutrients to grow strong and healthy. A poor diet can lead to hair fall.
  • Hormonal changes: Hormonal changes like pregnancy, menopause, and thyroid disorders can also lead to hair fall in long hair.

How to Prevent Hair Fall in Long Hair

Preventing hair fall in long hair requires a combination of good hair care practices and a healthy lifestyle. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb: Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush. This will prevent hair breakage and fall.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles: Avoid tight hairstyles like ponytails and braids that can pull on your hair and cause it to fall out.
  • Avoid chemical treatments: Avoid chemical treatments like coloring, perming, and straightening that can weaken your hair and cause it to fall out.
  • Limit heat styling: Limit your use of heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons. Use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet: Eat a diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals that promote hair growth and health.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.
  • Get enough sleep: Get enough sleep to reduce stress and promote hair growth.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hair Fall Prevention

Here is a step-by-step guide to preventing hair fall in long hair:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair when it is damp.
  3. Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel. Instead, gently pat it dry.
  4. Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.
  5. Avoid chemical treatments like coloring, perming, and straightening.
  6. Limit your use of heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons.
  7. Eat a healthy diet rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  8. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  9. Get enough sleep to reduce stress and promote hair growth.
  10. Use hair care products specifically designed for long hair.

Tips for Maintaining Long Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining long hair:

  • Trim your hair regularly: Regular trims can help prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair: Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Avoid using hot water: Hot water can also strip your hair of its natural oils. Use lukewarm water instead.
  • Protect your hair from the sun: Wear a hat or use a hair care product with SPF to protect your hair from the sun’s harmful rays.

FAQs About Hair Fall in Long Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair fall in long hair:

  • Q: How much hair fall is normal?
    A: It is normal to lose 50-100 hairs per day.
  • Q: Does hair fall more in the summer?
    A: Yes, hair fall can increase in the summer due to exposure to the sun and heat.
  • Q: Does stress cause hair fall?
    A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall.
  • Q: Can hair fall be genetic?
    A: Yes, hair fall can be genetic.

Hair Care Products for Long Hair

Here are some hair care products specifically designed for long hair:

  • Shampoo and conditioner: Look for a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for long hair. These products will help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Deep conditioning treatment: Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Heat protectant spray: Use a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Hair oil: Use a hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Hair mask: Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Long hair is beautiful, but it requires more maintenance and care than short hair. By following good hair care practices and a healthy lifestyle, you can prevent hair fall in long hair and keep your hair healthy and strong.
