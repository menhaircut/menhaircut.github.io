Short Spiky Haircut A Trendy And Bold Look

Written by Frank Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old boring hairstyle and looking for a bold transformation? Look no further! The short spiky haircut is the perfect way to switch up your look and make a statement. This edgy hairstyle has been gaining popularity in recent years and is perfect for those who want to add some edge to their appearance. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the short spiky haircut, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Description:

The short spiky haircut is a hairstyle that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, while the top is styled into spikes. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to fit your personal style. This haircut is perfect for those who want to make a bold statement and stand out from the crowd.

How to Get a Short Spiky Haircut:

Getting a short spiky haircut is easy, and it can be done at any salon or barbershop. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to consult with your stylist or barber. They will help you determine if the short spiky haircut is right for you and will give you advice on how to style it.

Step 2: Cutting

Next, the stylist or barber will cut the hair on the sides and back of the head short. The length is up to you, but typically, it's around 1-2 inches long. The top of the head is left longer, around 3-4 inches, to allow for styling into spikes.

Step 3: Styling

Finally, the stylist or barber will use a styling product, such as hair gel or wax, to create the spikes on the top of your head. They will show you how to style it and give you tips on how to maintain it at home.

Tips for Styling a Short Spiky Haircut:

Here are some tips for styling a short spiky haircut:

  • Use a strong-hold styling product to keep the spikes in place.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Use a blow dryer to set the spikes in place.
  • Use your fingers to create a natural-looking spike instead of a uniform shape.
  • Trim the hair regularly to maintain the shape and style.

FAQ:

1. Is the short spiky haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, the short spiky haircut can be customized to suit any face shape. Your stylist or barber will help you determine the best style for your face shape.

2. Can I style my short spiky haircut differently?

Yes, the short spiky haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to find the look that works best for you.

3. How often do I need to get my short spiky haircut trimmed?

You should get your short spiky haircut trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

Hair Care:

Maintaining a short spiky haircut is easy with the right hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

The short spiky haircut is a bold and trendy look that is perfect for those who want to make a statement. With the right styling products and hair care routine, you can maintain this edgy look and turn heads wherever you go. Consult with your stylist or barber to see if this haircut is right for you, and let your personality shine through with a short spiky haircut!
