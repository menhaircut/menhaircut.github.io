Afro natural hair is a type of hair that has not been chemically straightened or altered. It is characterized by its tight coils and thick texture. Afro natural hair can be styled in many ways, but one of the most popular styles is the big afro. A big afro is a style where the hair is grown out and styled into a large, round shape.
