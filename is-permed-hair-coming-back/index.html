Is Permed Hair Coming Back

Written by Bardi Jul 31, 2023 · 4 min read

In recent years, there has been a resurgence of hair trends from the past. The 90s inspired butterfly clips and scrunchies have made a comeback, and now we are seeing a revival of permed hair. Permed hair was a popular style in the 80s and 90s, but it fell out of favor in the early 2000s. However, in 2023, it seems that permed hair is making a comeback. In this article, we will explore whether permed hair is really coming back and how to achieve the look.

Description

Permed hair is created by using chemicals to break down the natural bonds in the hair and then reforming them in a new shape. This process creates curls or waves in the hair that can last for several months. Permed hair was popular in the 80s and 90s, but it fell out of fashion in the early 2000s. However, in recent years, we have seen a resurgence in permed hair styles, particularly among younger generations.

How to Permed Hair

If you are interested in perming your hair, there are a few things you should know. First, you should find a stylist who is experienced in perming hair. Perming chemicals can be damaging to your hair, so it is important to work with someone who knows what they are doing. Second, you should make sure that your hair is in good condition before perming it. Perming chemicals can cause damage to already weakened hair.

Step by Step

The process of perming hair typically involves the following steps:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Section your hair into small sections and apply the perming solution.
  3. Wrap your hair around perm rods and secure them in place.
  4. Apply a neutralizing solution to set the curls.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly and style as desired.

Tips for Permed Hair

If you decide to perm your hair, there are a few tips you should keep in mind to maintain healthy, voluminous curls:

  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 48 hours after getting a perm.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner made for permed hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your permed hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Is perming hair damaging?

Perming hair involves using chemicals that can be damaging to the hair. However, if done properly and with the right aftercare, permed hair can be healthy and voluminous.

Can you perm dyed hair?

Yes, you can perm dyed hair. However, it is important to make sure that your hair is in good condition before perming it. Dyeing your hair can weaken it, so it is important to work with a stylist who knows how to safely perm dyed hair.

Hair Care for Permed Hair

If you decide to perm your hair, it is important to take good care of it to keep it healthy and voluminous. Here are a few tips for caring for permed hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner made for permed hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your permed hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

Conclusion

Permed hair is making a comeback in 2023, and it is easy to see why. Permed hair can be a fun and voluminous way to switch up your hairstyle. However, it is important to take good care of your permed hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
