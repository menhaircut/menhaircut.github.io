How To Do A Good High Ponytail A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jul 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles and want to try something new? A high ponytail is a classic look that is easy to achieve and perfect for any occasion. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to do a good high ponytail, as well as some tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Do a High Ponytail
  • Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail

How to Do a High Ponytail

Follow these simple steps to create a beautiful high ponytail:

Step 1: Start with Clean and Dry Hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before starting. If your hair is too greasy or oily, it will be difficult to style and may not hold the ponytail well. Use a brush or comb to detangle your hair and remove any knots or tangles.

Step 2: Gather Your Hair at the Crown

Use a brush or comb to gather all of your hair at the crown of your head. Hold it firmly with one hand while smoothing down any flyaways with the other hand.

Step 3: Secure Your Hair with a Hair Tie

Use a hair tie to secure your hair in place. Make sure it is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not too tight that it hurts. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Step 4: Smooth Out Your Ponytail

Use a brush or comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your ponytail. You can also use hair gel or hairspray to keep it in place and add some shine.

Step 5: Add Some Volume

If you want to add some volume to your ponytail, you can tease the hair at the crown of your head before securing it. This will create a more dramatic and full look.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish your high ponytail with a light mist of hairspray to keep it in place all day.

Tips for a Perfect High Ponytail

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect high ponytail:

  • Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to hide it better.
  • Tease your hair for more volume and texture.
  • Use a brush or comb to smooth out any bumps or lumps in your ponytail.
  • Apply hair gel or hairspray to tame flyaways and add shine.
  • Loosen the hair tie slightly for a more relaxed look.
  • Experiment with different ponytail heights to find your perfect style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about high ponytails:

Q: Can I do a high ponytail with short hair?

A: Yes, you can still do a high ponytail with short hair. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands and create a more polished look.

Q: How do I prevent my high ponytail from slipping?

A: Use a hair tie that is tight enough to hold your hair in place, but not too tight that it hurts. You can also use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Q: How do I add volume to my high ponytail?

A: Tease the hair at the crown of your head before securing it with a hair tie. This will create a more dramatic and full look.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove dirt, oil, and buildup.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair, as this can cause damage and hair loss.

Now that you know how to do a good high ponytail, you can try it out for yourself and rock this classic hairstyle with confidence.
