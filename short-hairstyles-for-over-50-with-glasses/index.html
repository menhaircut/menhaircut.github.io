Short Hairstyles For Over 50 With Glasses

Written by Petter Jul 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our hair starts to lose its natural shine and volume. If you wear glasses, finding the right short hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right haircut, you can enhance your style and confidence. In this article, we will guide you through the best short hairstyles for over 50 with glasses, including tips and tricks for hair care.

Description

Short hairstyles are a great choice for women over 50 with glasses, as they require low maintenance and can make you look younger. However, the right hairstyle depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. The key is to find a haircut that suits your features, enhances your best assets, and complements your glasses.

How To

If you are looking for a new short hairstyle, here are some tips to follow:

1. Research

Before going to the salon, do some research on short hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses. Look for inspiration on social media platforms, such as Pinterest or Instagram, and save some pictures that you like.

2. Consultation

When you visit your hairstylist, bring your saved photos and discuss your hair type, face shape, and glasses. Your hairstylist can recommend a haircut that suits your features and enhances your style.

3. Maintenance

To maintain your short hairstyle, use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to trim your hair and maintain your style.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions for some popular short hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that suits women of all ages. Here's how to get it:

  • Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair short on the sides and back and leave some length on top.
  • Style your hair with a styling product, such as a wax or gel, and use your fingers to create texture.
  • Wear your glasses with confidence, and you're ready to go!

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is a trendy short hairstyle that can make you look younger. Here's how to get it:

  • Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair chin-length or above and add some layers for volume.
  • Style your hair with a round brush and blow dryer, and finish with a hairspray.
  • Wear your glasses on the bridge of your nose, and you're good to go!

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a short hairstyle for women over 50 with glasses:

  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and enhances your features.
  • Consider your hair texture and choose a hairstyle that is easy to maintain.
  • Choose glasses that complement your hairstyle and face shape.
  • Use hair care products that suit your hair type and avoid using too much heat.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses:

1. Can I wear short hair if I wear glasses?

Yes, you can wear short hair if you wear glasses. The key is to choose a hairstyle that complements your glasses and face shape.

2. What is the best short hairstyle for women over 50 with glasses?

The best short hairstyle for women over 50 with glasses depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Consult with your hairstylist to find the right haircut for you.

3. How can I maintain my short hairstyle?

To maintain your short hairstyle, use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair. Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to trim your hair and maintain your style.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short hairstyle healthy and shiny:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools that can damage your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks to trim your hair and maintain your style.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp healthy.

In conclusion, short hairstyles for women over 50 with glasses can enhance your style and confidence. Choose a haircut that suits your features, enhances your best assets, and complements your glasses. Follow these tips and tricks for hair care, and you'll have a stylish and low-maintenance short hairstyle.
