Toddler Boy Haircuts Long On Top Short On Sides A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Nov 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a parent of a toddler boy, you know how important it is to keep their hair looking good. One of the most popular hairstyles for little boys is the long on top short on sides look. This style is easy to maintain and looks great on most kids. If you're considering this haircut for your little one, read on for our complete guide.

Description

The long on top short on sides haircut is exactly what it sounds like. The top of the hair is left long, while the sides and back are cut short. This creates a sleek and modern look that is perfect for little boys who want to look cool and stylish.

How to Get the Haircut

The long on top short on sides haircut is fairly easy to achieve. Here's how:

Step 1: Choose the Length

Decide how long you want the top of your child's hair to be. This will depend on personal preference, but generally, the top should be longer than the sides.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Using clippers or scissors, cut the sides and back of the hair to the desired length. This can be anywhere from a buzz cut to a short fade.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Cut the top of the hair to the desired length. This can be done with scissors or clippers, depending on the look you're going for.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

Using scissors or clippers, blend the hair on the sides with the hair on top. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Tips for Maintaining the Haircut

Keeping your child's long on top short on sides haircut looking good is easy with these tips:

- Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and shape. - Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep the hair healthy and shiny. - Style the hair with a little bit of gel or pomade for a polished look. - Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the long on top short on sides haircut:

Q: Will this haircut work on all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can work on all hair types, from straight to curly.

Q: How long does the hair on top need to be?

A: This will depend on personal preference, but generally, the hair on top should be at least 2-3 inches long.

Q: Is this haircut easy to maintain?

A: Yes, this is a very easy haircut to maintain. It requires minimal styling and only needs to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for keeping your child's long on top short on sides haircut looking good. Here are some tips:

- Wash the hair regularly with a quality shampoo and conditioner. - Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle the hair. - Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair. - Consider using a leave-in conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.

Overall, the long on top short on sides haircut is a great choice for toddler boys. It's easy to maintain, looks great, and works on all hair types. Follow our guide for a seamless haircut experience!
