Layered Haircuts For Thick Curly Hair With Bangs

Written by Eveline Sep 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to find a hairstyle that works for you. Layered haircuts with bangs are a great option for adding shape and dimension to your curls while keeping them under control. In this article, we'll give you tips on how to get the perfect layered haircut for your thick curly hair with bangs, and how to take care of it.

Description

Layered haircuts for thick curly hair with bangs are designed to add volume and texture to your curls while also keeping them manageable. The layers will help to create movement and shape, while the bangs will frame your face and accentuate your features. This hairstyle is perfect for those with naturally curly hair who want to add some personality and style to their look.

How To

If you're ready to get a layered haircut with bangs for your thick curly hair, here's what you need to do:

Step 1: Find the Right Stylist

When it comes to getting a good haircut, the most important thing is finding the right stylist. Look for someone who is experienced with cutting curly hair and who has a portfolio of layered haircuts with bangs that you like.

Step 2: Choose Your Bangs

There are different types of bangs to choose from, so it's important to pick the style that suits your face shape and hair texture. Some options include blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, and curly bangs.

Step 3: Decide on the Layers

The number and length of the layers will depend on the length and thickness of your hair. Generally, the shorter the layers, the more volume and bounce your curls will have.

Step 4: Get the Cut

Once you've discussed your options with your stylist, it's time to get the cut. Your stylist will start by washing and drying your hair, then start cutting the layers and bangs. Make sure to communicate with your stylist throughout the process to ensure you're happy with the result.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a layered haircut with bangs for your thick curly hair:

- Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to show your stylist - Don't be afraid to ask questions or speak up if you're not happy with something - Use hair products specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls looking healthy and defined - Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and prevent split ends

FAQ

What face shape is best for layered haircuts with bangs?

Layered haircuts with bangs are versatile and can work for many face shapes. However, if you have a round face, you may want to go for longer, side-swept bangs to elongate your face.

Can I still straighten my hair after getting a layered haircut with bangs?

Yes, you can still straighten your hair after getting a layered haircut with bangs. However, keep in mind that straightening your hair too often can damage your curls and make them harder to manage.

Hair Care

To keep your layered haircut with bangs looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils - Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to add moisture and definition to your curls - Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your curls without causing frizz - Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can rock a layered haircut with bangs for your thick curly hair and feel confident and beautiful.
