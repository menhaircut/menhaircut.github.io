Short Natural Haircuts For Black Females With Round Faces 2020

Written by Bardi Aug 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a black female with a round face and looking for a new short haircut? Look no further because we have got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing the top short natural haircuts for black females with round faces in 2020. Keep reading to find the perfect hairstyle that will flatter your round face shape.

Description

Short natural haircuts are perfect for black females with round faces as they help to elongate the face and create a more balanced look. Round faces have wider cheekbones and a rounded jawline, which can sometimes make it difficult to find a flattering haircut. Short haircuts that are layered and have volume on top can help to create the illusion of a longer face shape.

How To

The first step in getting a short natural haircut is to find a stylist who specializes in natural hair. They will be able to assess your face shape and recommend a haircut that will flatter your features. It is important to communicate with your stylist and let them know what you want and what you don't want in a haircut.

Once you have decided on a haircut, make sure to maintain it with regular trims to keep it looking fresh and healthy. You can also experiment with different styling techniques, such as twists, braids, and bantu knots, to create different looks with your short haircut.

Step by Step

Step 1: Find a stylist who specializes in natural hair

Step 2: Discuss your face shape and desired haircut with your stylist

Step 3: Maintain your haircut with regular trims

Step 4: Experiment with different styling techniques

Tips

1. Avoid haircuts that emphasize the width of your face, such as blunt bangs or a chin-length bob.

2. Opt for haircuts that add height and volume to the top of your head, such as a pixie cut or a tapered fro.

3. Experiment with different hair colors and highlights to add dimension to your short haircut.

FAQ

Q: What are the best short natural haircuts for black females with round faces?

A: The best short natural haircuts for black females with round faces are those that add height and volume to the top of the head, such as a pixie cut or a tapered fro.

Q: How often should I get a trim for my short natural haircut?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your short haircut.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your natural hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy natural hair:

1. Wash your hair regularly with a sulfate-free shampoo.

2. Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.

3. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to avoid breakage.

4. Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

5. Wear protective styles, such as braids and twists, to protect your hair from damage.

In conclusion, there are many short natural haircuts for black females with round faces in 2020 that can flatter your features and make you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to communicate with your stylist and maintain your haircut with regular trims and styling techniques.
