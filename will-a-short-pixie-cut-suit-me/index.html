Will A Short Pixie Cut Suit Me

Written by Oliver Aug 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're thinking about getting a short pixie cut, you may be wondering if it will suit you. It's a valid concern, as cutting your hair short can be a big change. In this article, we'll discuss how to determine if a short pixie cut is right for you.

Description

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that typically involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides while leaving it longer on the top. It's a bold and edgy look that can be flattering on many face shapes and hair types.

How To

The best way to determine if a short pixie cut will suit you is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can assess your face shape, hair type, and personal style to recommend a haircut that will flatter you.

You can also try on different virtual hairstyles online or in a salon to get an idea of what a short pixie cut would look like on you.

Step by Step

1. Research different pixie cut styles to find one that you like.
2. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if the cut will flatter your face shape and hair type.
3. Try on virtual hairstyles or physically try on wigs to see how the cut will look on you.
4. Consider the maintenance and styling requirements of a short pixie cut before committing to the style.

Tips

If you have a round face shape, a longer pixie cut may be more flattering than a shorter one.
If you have thin hair, a pixie cut can add volume and texture to your hair.
Pixie cuts require regular maintenance to keep the style looking fresh and polished.

FAQ

Q: Will a short pixie cut suit me if I have curly hair?
A: Yes, a short pixie cut can look great on curly hair. Just make sure to consult with a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair to ensure that the cut flatters your curls.

Q: Will a short pixie cut suit me if I have a square face shape?
A: Yes, a short pixie cut can look great on a square face shape. Just make sure to choose a style that softens the angles of your face.

Hair Care

After getting a short pixie cut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Here are some tips:

- Wash your hair every other day to keep it looking fresh.
- Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to your hair.
- Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for trims and touch-ups.
- Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to style your hair, as brushes can create too much volume.

In conclusion, a short pixie cut can be a great hairstyle choice for many people. By consulting with a professional hairstylist and considering your face shape and hair type, you can determine if a pixie cut will suit you. Remember to also consider the maintenance and styling requirements of the cut before committing to the style.
