Hair Shaved On Sides Long On Top Girl A Trending Hairstyle

Written by Luffy Sep 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you thinking of changing your hairstyle? Do you want to try something new and bold? Then you should consider the hair shaved on sides long on top girl style. This hairstyle has been trending for a few years now and is loved by many people around the world. In this article, we will discuss how to achieve this look, its benefits, and how to take care of it.

Description

The hair shaved on sides long on top girl style is a combination of two different haircuts. The sides of the head are shaved or cut very short, while the top part of the hair is left long. The length of the hair on top can vary from a few inches to shoulder length or longer, depending on personal preference. This hairstyle is suitable for all hair types and can be styled in many different ways.

How to Achieve This Look

The first step is to decide on the length of your hair. You can either keep your hair long on top or go for a shorter length. Next, you will need to visit a professional hairstylist who can help you achieve this look. They will start by shaving or cutting the sides of your hair, leaving the top part untouched. They will then style the top part of your hair in a way that suits your face shape and hair type.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for achieving the hair shaved on sides long on top girl style:

  1. Decide on the length of your hair on top
  2. Visit a professional hairstylist
  3. Get the sides of your hair shaved or cut very short
  4. Style the top part of your hair

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting the hair shaved on sides long on top girl style:

  • Choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type
  • Keep your hair well-maintained by getting regular trims
  • Use hair products such as hairspray, gel, or pomade to style your hair
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the hair shaved on sides long on top girl style:

Q: Is this hairstyle suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this hairstyle can be adapted to suit all hair types.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: It is recommended to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

Q: Can I style my hair in different ways?

A: Yes, you can style your hair in many different ways, such as slicked back, messy, or curly.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair shaved on sides long on top girl style:

  • Wash your hair regularly with shampoo and conditioner
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools such as straighteners or curling irons
  • Avoid using too much product in your hair, as it can weigh it down
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

So, there you have it, everything you need to know about the hair shaved on sides long on top girl style. Go ahead and try this bold and trendy hairstyle and see how it can transform your look!
