Description

Are you over 50 and looking for a new hairstyle that is low maintenance and stylish? Consider a pixie haircut! Pixie haircuts are a great option for women with wavy hair over 50, as they are versatile, easy to style, and can accentuate your facial features.

In this article, we'll share some tips, ideas, and care instructions for pixie haircuts for wavy hair over 50, so you can confidently rock your new hairstyle.

How To

If you're considering a pixie haircut, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Find inspiration: Look for pictures of pixie haircuts that you like and show them to your hairstylist.
  2. Consult with your hairstylist: Your hairstylist can help you determine if a pixie haircut is right for you, and can recommend a style that will flatter your face shape and hair texture.
  3. Prepare for the cut: Wash your hair the day before your appointment and avoid using any styling products.
  4. Get the cut: Your hairstylist will use scissors and/or clippers to create your pixie haircut.
  5. Style your hair: Use a hair dryer and/or styling products to create your desired look.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide to styling your pixie haircut:

  1. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush, lifting the roots for volume.
  3. Use a flat iron to smooth any frizz or waves.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to add texture and hold.
  5. Finish with hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a pixie haircut for wavy hair over 50:

  • Consider your face shape: Your pixie haircut should flatter your face shape and accentuate your best features.
  • Choose the right length: A longer pixie haircut can be more versatile and easier to style, while a shorter pixie haircut can be edgier and more dramatic.
  • Use the right products: Use styling products that are designed for your hair type and texture, and avoid any products that can weigh down your hair or make it look greasy.
  • Maintain your haircut: Regular trims can help keep your pixie haircut looking fresh and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie haircuts for wavy hair over 50:

Q: Will a pixie haircut work for my face shape?

A: Yes, a pixie haircut can work for any face shape. Your hairstylist can help you choose a style that will flatter your unique features.

Q: Can I still style my pixie haircut if my hair is wavy?

A: Yes, wavy hair can be styled in a pixie haircut. The right styling products and techniques can help enhance your natural waves and create a textured, tousled look.

Q: Will a pixie haircut require a lot of maintenance?

A: No, a pixie haircut is typically low maintenance and easy to style. However, regular trims may be necessary to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using styling products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

By following these tips and tricks, you can rock your pixie haircut with confidence and style!
