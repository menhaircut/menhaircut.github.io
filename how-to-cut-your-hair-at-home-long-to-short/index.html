How To Cut Your Hair At Home Long To Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Description

Are you tired of your long locks and looking for a change? Cutting your hair from long to short can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish new look from the comfort of your own home. In this blog post, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut your hair at home from long to short, along with tips and tricks for achieving the perfect cut.

How to

Before you begin cutting your hair, it's important to gather the necessary tools. You will need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. It's also a good idea to have a mirror set up so you can see the back of your head.

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back. Use hair clips to secure each section in place.

Step 2: Determine Your Desired Length

Decide how short you want to cut your hair and mark the length with a comb or your fingers.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Begin cutting your hair at the back, starting at the nape of your neck. Cut small sections of hair at a time, using the comb to guide your scissors. Remember to cut straight across and not at an angle.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Move on to the sides, cutting small sections at a time. Use the length you cut in the back as a guide for the sides.

Step 5: Cut the Top

Finally, move on to the top section of your hair. Use the sides as a guide for the length and cut small sections at a time.

Step 6: Check Your Work

After you have cut all sections of your hair, it's important to check your work. Make sure the length is even and adjust any uneven sections.

Tips

- Start with small cuts and work your way up to your desired length. - Use hair clips to secure sections of hair as you go. - Cut in a well-lit area with a mirror. - If you're unsure about cutting your hair, consider practicing on a wig or mannequin head first. - Don't forget to trim your bangs or fringe, if you have any.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair wet or dry? A: It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as wet hair can appear longer and may result in an uneven cut. Q: Can I cut my hair with regular scissors? A: It's recommended to use hair cutting scissors, as they are sharper and designed specifically for cutting hair. Using regular scissors can result in a jagged or uneven cut.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly to maintain its health and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often and use a heat protectant spray before styling. Regular trims every 6-8 weeks can also help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. Cutting your hair at home from long to short can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish new look without leaving your house. Remember to take your time and be patient, and always check your work as you go. Good luck!
