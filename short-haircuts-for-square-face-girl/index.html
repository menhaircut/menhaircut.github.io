Short Haircuts For Square Face Girl Find The Perfect Style For You

Written by Christine Jul 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a square face girl looking for a new haircut? Look no further. Short haircuts can be a great option for those with square faces, as they can help soften angular features and create a more balanced look. In this article, we’ll cover everything you need to know about short haircuts for square face girls, including how to find the perfect style for your face shape, step by step instructions on how to get the haircut, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

How to Find the Perfect Short Haircut for Square Face Girls

The first step in finding the perfect short haircut for your square face is to determine your face shape. A square face is characterized by a strong jawline, wide forehead, and square chin. To soften these features, you’ll want to look for hairstyles that add volume and texture to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short.

A few styles to consider include the pixie cut, the bob, and the layered bob. The pixie cut is a short, choppy haircut that can add volume to the top of your head and soften your features. The bob is a classic haircut that can be adapted to suit a square face by adding layers and volume to the top. The layered bob is a variation of the bob that adds texture and movement to the hair, making it a great choice for square face girls.

Step by Step Instructions for Getting a Short Haircut for Square Face Girls

Once you’ve chosen a short haircut that suits your face shape, it’s time to head to the salon. Here are some step by step instructions to help you get the perfect haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair and letting it air dry.
  2. Show your stylist a picture of the haircut you want.
  3. Ask your stylist to add layers and volume to the top of your head while keeping the sides and back short.
  4. Once your haircut is finished, style it using a volumizing mousse or spray.
  5. Finish off with a light hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Styling Short Haircuts for Square Face Girls

Now that you’ve got your new short haircut, it’s time to style it. Here are some tips to help you make the most of your new look:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and volume to the top of your head.
  • Blow-dry your hair upside down to add even more volume.
  • Use a small amount of styling wax or pomade to create definition and texture.
  • Experiment with different partings to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQ: Short Haircuts for Square Face Girls

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts for square face girls:

Q: Will a short haircut make my square face look even more angular?

A: No, a short haircut can actually help soften angular features by adding volume and texture to the top of your head.

Q: What if I don’t like my short haircut?

A: If you don’t like your new haircut, don’t worry. Hair grows back, and you can always experiment with different styles until you find one that works for you.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. Experiment with different updos and accessories to find a style that works for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Square Face Girls

Short haircuts require a little extra care to keep them looking their best. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your new look:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Now that you know everything there is to know about short haircuts for square face girls, it’s time to find the perfect style for you. Whether you opt for a pixie cut, a bob, or a layered bob, a short haircut can be a great way to soften angular features and create a more balanced look. So go ahead, take the plunge, and embrace your new short hairstyle!
