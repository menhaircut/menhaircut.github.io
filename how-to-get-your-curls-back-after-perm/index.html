How To Get Your Curls Back After Perm A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you feeling frustrated with your perm? Maybe you didn’t get the results you were hoping for, or perhaps your curls have fallen flat over time. Whatever the reason, you don’t have to settle for lackluster locks. In this article, we’ll show you how to get your curls back after a perm using simple tips and tricks that you can do at home. Read on to learn more!

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Your Curls Back After Perm
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Curls
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Healthy Curls

How to Get Your Curls Back After Perm

The first step in getting your curls back after a perm is to assess the damage. If your curls have fallen flat or disappeared altogether, you may need to give your hair some extra TLC. Here are some tips to help you get your curls back:

1. Use a Curl-Enhancing Shampoo and Conditioner

One of the easiest ways to enhance your curls is to use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for curly hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and jojoba oil, which can help to nourish and define your curls.

2. Try a Protein Treatment

Protein treatments can help to strengthen your hair and restore elasticity, which is essential for bouncy, defined curls. Look for treatments that contain hydrolyzed keratin or collagen, which can penetrate the hair shaft and provide long-lasting benefits.

3. Use a Diffuser

A diffuser is a must-have tool for anyone with curly hair. This attachment for your blow dryer helps to distribute heat evenly and reduce frizz, resulting in defined, bouncy curls. To use a diffuser, simply attach it to your blow dryer and scrunch your hair as you dry it.

4. Apply a Curl-Defining Cream

A curl-defining cream can help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil or shea butter, which can nourish and hydrate your hair while defining your curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Ready to get your curls back? Follow these steps:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, and towel dry it gently.

2. Apply a Protein Treatment

Apply a protein treatment to your hair, following the instructions on the package. Leave it on for the recommended amount of time, then rinse it out thoroughly.

3. Apply a Curl-Defining Cream

Apply a curl-defining cream to your hair, focusing on the ends and any areas that need extra definition. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the product evenly.

4. Diffuse Your Hair

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to low heat. Scrunch your hair as you dry it, using the diffuser attachment to distribute heat evenly.

5. Style as Desired

Once your hair is dry, you can style it as desired. You may want to use a curling iron or hot rollers to add even more definition to your curls.

Tips and Tricks for Maintaining Curls

Once you’ve gotten your curls back, you’ll want to maintain them for as long as possible. Here are some tips to help you keep your curls looking great:

1. Avoid Over-Washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, leading to dryness and frizz. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days, and use a dry shampoo in between washes if needed.

2. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Cotton pillowcases can cause friction and lead to frizz and tangles. Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to reduce friction and keep your curls looking smooth and defined.

3. Use a Satin Hair Cap

If you’re prone to tossing and turning in your sleep, a satin hair cap can help to protect your curls and prevent them from getting flattened or tangled.

FAQs

Q: Can I get my curls back after a perm?

A: Yes, you can! By using the right products and techniques, you can enhance your curls and restore their bounce and definition.

Q: How often should I use a protein treatment?

A: It depends on your hair’s needs. If your curls are damaged or lack elasticity, you may want to use a protein treatment once a week. If your hair is relatively healthy, you can use a treatment every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I use a curling iron on my curly hair?

A: Yes, but be careful not to overdo it. Curling irons can cause damage and breakage if used too often or at high heat. If you do use a curling iron, use a heat protectant spray and set the temperature to medium or low.

Hair Care Tips for Healthy Curls

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to help you maintain healthy, bouncy curls:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and breakage, which can make your curls look dull and lifeless.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Whenever Possible

Heat styling can cause damage and breakage, so it’s best to avoid it whenever possible. If you do use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray and set the temperature to low or medium.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can cause damage to your hair, leading to dryness, breakage, and fading. To protect your hair from the sun, wear a hat or use a UV-protectant spray.

By following these tips and tricks, you can get your curls back after a perm and keep them looking great for months to come. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type and texture. Good luck!
