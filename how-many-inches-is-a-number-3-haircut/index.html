How Many Inches Is A Number 3 Haircut

Written by Bardi Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're new to the world of haircuts, you might be wondering what a "number 3" haircut means. A number 3 haircut is a popular buzz cut style that leaves hair 3/8 of an inch long. In this blog post, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to get it, maintenance tips, and more. So, let's get started!

Description

A number 3 haircut is one of the most popular buzz cut styles for men. It's a short, low-maintenance haircut that can be done at home with clippers. The number 3 refers to the guard size on the clippers, which determines how much hair is left on the head. With a number 3 guard, the hair is cut to 3/8 of an inch, which is approximately 9.5mm.

How to Get a Number 3 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a number 3 haircut, you have a few options. You can either go to a barber or salon and ask for a number 3 buzz cut, or you can do it yourself at home with clippers. If you decide to do it yourself, make sure you have a good pair of clippers with a number 3 guard. You'll also need a comb, scissors, and a mirror.

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Attach the number 3 guard to your clippers.
  3. Starting at the sides, run the clippers up your head, against the grain of your hair. Repeat until all the hair is the same length.
  4. Use scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers missed.
  5. Use a comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as necessary.
  6. Use the clippers to clean up the neckline and sideburns.
  7. Take a shower to rinse off any loose hairs.

Tips for Maintaining a Number 3 Haircut

Maintaining a number 3 haircut is easy and requires minimal effort. Here are a few tips to help you keep your haircut looking fresh:

  • Trim your hair every 2-3 weeks to keep it at the same length.
  • Use a comb to check for any uneven spots and trim as necessary.
  • Clean your clippers after every use to prevent bacteria buildup.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using hair products that can weigh down your hair.

FAQs

What other haircuts are similar to a number 3?

Other buzz cut styles that are similar to a number 3 include a number 2 (1/4 inch) and a number 4 (1/2 inch).

Can I still style my hair with a number 3 haircut?

Yes, you can still style your hair with a number 3 haircut. However, the options are limited. You can try a messy, textured look or slick it back with some hair gel.

Is a number 3 haircut suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, a number 3 haircut is suitable for all face shapes. It's a versatile haircut that can be tailored to suit your individual features.

Hair Care

Even though a number 3 haircut is low maintenance, it's still important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can damage your hair and scalp.

In conclusion, a number 3 haircut is a popular buzz cut style that leaves hair 3/8 of an inch long. It's a low maintenance haircut that can be done at home with clippers or at a salon. To maintain your haircut, trim your hair every 2-3 weeks and use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. With proper care, your number 3 haircut will look great for weeks to come!
