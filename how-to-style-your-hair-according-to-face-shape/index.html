How To Style Your Hair According To Face Shape

When it comes to styling your hair, it's important to consider your face shape. Different hairstyles flatter different face shapes, and choosing the right one can make a big difference in your overall look. In this post, we'll go over how to style your hair according to your face shape, as well as some tips and hair care advice.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we get into specific hairstyles, it's important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose a style that flatters your features and complements your overall look.

There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart-shaped, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a look at the shape of your face and compare it to the descriptions below:

Oval Face

An oval face has balanced proportions and a slightly curved jawline. The forehead is slightly wider than the chin, and the cheekbones are the widest part of the face.

Round Face

A round face has full cheeks and a rounded chin. The width and length of the face are about the same, with no prominent angles.

Square Face

A square face has a strong jawline and a square forehead. The length and width of the face are about the same, and the jawline is the most prominent feature.

Heart-Shaped Face

A heart-shaped face has a wider forehead and a narrow chin. The cheekbones are the widest part of the face.

Diamond Face

A diamond face has high cheekbones and a narrow forehead and chin. The face is widest at the temples.

Long Face

A long face has a long forehead and chin, with the width of the face being relatively the same from top to bottom.

Hairstyles for Oval Faces

If you have an oval face, you're in luck! Oval faces are the most versatile, and can pull off a variety of hairstyles. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Updos

Hairstyles for Round Faces

If you have a round face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and adds some angles. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Asymmetrical cuts
  • Pixie cuts

Hairstyles for Square Faces

If you have a square face, you'll want to soften your features with a hairstyle that adds some curves. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Soft curls
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob with layers

Hairstyles for Heart-Shaped Faces

If you have a heart-shaped face, you'll want to draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Pixie cuts
  • Bob with layers

Hairstyles for Long Faces

If you have a long face, you'll want to choose a hairstyle that adds some width and volume. Some great options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Bob with layers
  • Beachy waves

Tips for Styling Your Hair According to Face Shape

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle based on your face shape:

  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a style.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to find the perfect cut and style for your face shape.
  • Invest in quality hair products to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

FAQs

Can I pull off a style that doesn't flatter my face shape?

While it's possible to wear any style you like, choosing a style that flatters your face shape will help you look your best. If you're not sure what style to choose, consult with a hairstylist for advice.

How often should I get my hair cut?

This will depend on your hair type and the style you choose. Generally, it's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair looking its best, be sure to:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are appropriate for your hair type.

By following these tips and choosing a hairstyle that flatters your face shape, you can achieve a look that's both stylish and flattering. Remember to consult with a hairstylist for advice on finding the perfect style for you.
