Shoulder Length Haircuts For Over 60S A Guide

Written by William Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new haircut? If so, shoulder length haircuts may be the perfect option for you. Not only are they versatile and easy to maintain, but they can also help you look and feel younger. Keep reading for our guide on shoulder length haircuts for over 60s, including tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Description: What are Shoulder Length Haircuts?

Shoulder length haircuts are hairstyles that fall somewhere between the chin and the collarbone. They can be straight, wavy, or curly, and can be styled in a variety of ways. Shoulder length haircuts are popular among women of all ages, but they are particularly flattering on those over 60.

How to: Choosing the Right Shoulder Length Haircut

When choosing a shoulder length haircut, it's important to consider your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

1. Consider your face shape

If you have a round face, choose a haircut that adds height and length to your face. If you have a square face, choose a haircut that softens your features. If you have an oval face, you're lucky – almost any haircut will look great on you!

2. Consider your hair texture

If you have thin hair, choose a haircut that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, choose a haircut that thins out your hair while still maintaining its length. If you have curly hair, choose a haircut that emphasizes your curls.

3. Consider your personal style

Choose a haircut that reflects your personality and personal style. If you're adventurous, try a bold new look. If you're more conservative, choose a classic haircut that never goes out of style.

Step by Step: Getting a Shoulder Length Haircut

If you've decided to get a shoulder length haircut, here's what you can expect:

1. Choose a salon and stylist

Choose a salon and stylist that has experience with shoulder length haircuts. Ask for recommendations from friends or read reviews online.

2. Consult with your stylist

Before your haircut, consult with your stylist about the length, style, and maintenance of your new haircut. Bring pictures or examples of haircuts you like.

3. Get your haircut

Sit back and relax while your stylist gives you a new shoulder length haircut. Enjoy the pampering and the new look!

Tips: Maintaining Your Shoulder Length Haircut

Once you have your new shoulder length haircut, you'll want to maintain it to keep it looking great. Here are some tips:

1. Get regular trims

Get regular trims every 6-8 weeks to maintain the length and shape of your haircut.

2. Use the right products

Use products that are designed for your hair type and style, such as volumizing mousse or curl-defining cream.

3. Style your hair

Experiment with different styling techniques to find the right look for you, such as blow-drying, curling, or straightening.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will a shoulder length haircut make me look younger?

A shoulder length haircut can help you look and feel younger by adding volume and texture to your hair.

2. Can I still wear my hair up with a shoulder length haircut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a shoulder length haircut. Try a ponytail, bun, or half-up, half-down style.

3. What if I don't like my new haircut?

If you don't like your new haircut, talk to your stylist about making changes or adjustments.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Hair Healthy

Finally, it's important to keep your hair healthy to maintain your new shoulder length haircut. Here are some tips:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.

2. Protect your hair from heat damage

Avoid using heat tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, too often. When you do use them, use a heat protectant spray.

3. Eat a healthy diet

Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and advice, you'll be able to choose, get, and maintain the perfect shoulder length haircut for you. Enjoy your new look!
