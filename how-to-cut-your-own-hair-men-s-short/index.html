How To Cut Your Own Hair Men s Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you in need of a haircut but don't want to leave your house? Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a fresh, stylish look. In this article, we will provide a step-by-step guide on how to cut your own hair men's short. We'll also provide tips, FAQs and hair care advice to ensure you get the perfect cut every time.

Tools You'll Need

Before you begin, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Clippers with guards
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Mirror
  • Hair spray or gel (optional)

Preparing for the Cut

Start by taking a shower and washing your hair thoroughly. Dry your hair completely before starting the cut. Next, put on a shirt that you don't mind getting hair on. Position yourself in front of a mirror and make sure you have good lighting.

Starting the Cut

Begin by attaching the desired guard to your clippers. Start with the sides and work your way up to the top of your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and the clippers to trim it to the desired length. Take your time and make sure the hair is even on both sides.

Cutting the Sides

Next, switch to a smaller guard and trim the sides again. Use your scissors to trim any longer hairs that the clippers may have missed. Make sure the hair around your ears is even.

Cutting the Top

For the top of your head, use scissors to cut the hair to the desired length. Take small sections and cut straight across. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure it's even on both sides.

Cutting the Back

The back of your head can be tricky to cut, but with a little practice, you can do it. Use your clippers to trim the hair at the nape of your neck. Then, use your scissors to cut the hair at the crown of your head, making sure it's even on both sides.

Outlining the Hairline

Use your clippers to create a straight hairline around the neck and ears. Start at the center of the neck and work your way outwards. Use the comb to lift the hair and make sure the hairline is even on both sides.

Clean Up

Once you've finished cutting your hair, use a vacuum or broom to clean up the hair on the floor. Take a shower and wash your hair to remove any loose hairs.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

  • Start with a higher guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair.
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head.
  • Always cut less hair than you think you need.
  • Ask a friend or family member for help if you're unsure.

FAQs

Q: How often should I cut my own hair?

A: It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain your desired look.

Q: Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

A: It's best to use hair cutting scissors as they are sharper and designed specifically for cutting hair.

Q: What if I make a mistake?

A: Don't worry, hair grows back! If you make a mistake, try to blend the hair or leave it longer until it grows out.

Hair Care After the Cut

After cutting your own hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain a healthy, stylish look. Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. You can also use hair spray or gel to keep your hair in place. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and trim it regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair men's short, you can save time and money by doing it yourself. With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut from the comfort of your own home. Happy cutting!
