How To Cut Layers Around Face Long Hair Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 24, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have long hair, adding layers around your face can create a beautiful and flattering look. It can also add volume and dimension to your hair. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to cut layers around face long hair in a relaxed and easy-to-understand way.

Description

Cutting layers around the face is a great way to frame your facial features and give your hair a stylish look. However, it is important to note that cutting your hair at home can be risky, especially if you are not a professional hairdresser. Therefore, it is recommended that you seek the help of a professional if you are not experienced in cutting hair.

How to Cut Layers Around Face Long Hair

Before we start, it is important to have the right tools. You will need a sharp pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section your hair. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it dry naturally. This will help you see the natural texture and length of your hair.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using the comb, section your hair into three parts. The top part should start at the crown of your head, and the bottom part should be at the nape of your neck. Clip the middle section away.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Take a small section of your hair from the bottom layer and hold it between your fingers. Using the scissors, cut the hair at an angle, starting from the middle of the hair shaft and working towards the ends. Repeat this process for all the sections of your hair.

Step 4: Cut the Top Layer

Once you have finished cutting the bottom layer, unclip the middle layer and repeat the same process. Finally, cut the top layer of your hair, making sure that the layers around your face are slightly shorter than the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Blend the Layers

After you have finished cutting, use the comb to blend the layers together. This will create a natural and seamless look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect layered look:

  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up.
  • Use a sharp pair of scissors to avoid split ends.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Make sure the layers around your face are shorter than the rest of your hair.
  • Blend the layers together to create a natural look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting layers around face long hair:

Is it easy to cut layers around the face?

It can be challenging to cut layers around the face, especially if you are not experienced in cutting hair. It is recommended that you seek the help of a professional if you are unsure.

How often should I cut my hair?

It is recommended that you cut your hair every 6-8 weeks to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my hair when it's wet?

It is not recommended to cut your hair when it's wet as it can be difficult to see the natural texture and length of your hair.

Hair Care

Cutting your hair can be damaging, so it's important to take care of your hair after you cut it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid pulling and damaging your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair healthy and looking beautiful.

Conclusion

Cutting layers around face long hair can create a beautiful and flattering look. However, it is important to be cautious and seek the help of a professional if you are not experienced in cutting hair. By following these step-by-step instructions and tips, you can achieve the perfect layered look and keep your hair healthy and beautiful.
