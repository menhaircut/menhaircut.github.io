Does Hair Get Wavy As You Age

Written by Aprilia Sep 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our bodies undergo various changes, including changes in our hair. One question that many people ask is, "does hair get wavy as you age?" The answer is yes and no. While some people experience changes in their hair texture as they age, it's not a universal occurrence. In this article, we'll explore the reasons why hair can get wavy as you age, how to embrace these changes, and hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best.

Table of Contents

  • Why Hair Gets Wavy As You Age
  • How to Embrace Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Caring for Wavy Hair
  • Tips for Keeping Wavy Hair Healthy
  • FAQ

Why Hair Gets Wavy As You Age

The texture of your hair is determined by your genetics. However, as you age, your hormone levels change, and this can affect the texture of your hair. For example, women going through menopause may notice that their hair becomes drier and more brittle, which can lead to an increase in wave or curl.

Another reason why hair can get wavy as you age is due to damage from heat styling or chemical treatments. Over time, hair can become weak and prone to breakage, leading to changes in texture.

How to Embrace Wavy Hair

If you've noticed that your hair is getting wavier as you age, it can be a great opportunity to embrace a new look. Wavy hair can be incredibly versatile, and there are many styles that you can try. For example, you can opt for a messy, beachy look by adding some texturizing spray or mousse to your hair. Alternatively, you can create a sleek, polished look by straightening your hair with a flat iron.

It's also important to embrace the natural texture of your hair. Many people spend hours trying to straighten or curl their hair, but by embracing your natural texture, you can save time and effort. Plus, wavy hair can be incredibly flattering, and it's a great way to add some volume and dimension to your locks.

Step-by-Step Guide to Caring for Wavy Hair

If you have wavy hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here's a step-by-step guide to caring for wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are specifically designed for wavy hair, as these will help to enhance your natural texture.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your locks and keep them hydrated.

Step 3: Air Dry Your Hair

Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible, as these can damage your hair and lead to breakage. Instead, let your hair air dry naturally, or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your waves.

Step 4: Use a Curl-Enhancing Product

If you want to enhance your waves, use a curl-enhancing product such as a mousse or texturizing spray. Apply the product to damp hair and scrunch your hair gently to encourage your waves.

Tips for Keeping Wavy Hair Healthy

In addition to following a good hair care routine, there are several things you can do to keep your wavy hair healthy:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid using hot tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals

FAQ

Can hair get wavier with age?

Yes, hair can get wavier with age due to changes in hormone levels and damage from heat styling or chemical treatments.

Can you make straight hair wavy?

Yes, you can make straight hair wavy by using a curling iron or texturizing spray. However, it's important to be gentle with your hair to avoid damage.

Why is my hair getting wavy?

Your hair may be getting wavy due to changes in hormone levels or damage from heat styling or chemical treatments.

How do you keep wavy hair from frizzing?

To keep wavy hair from frizzing, make sure to use a leave-in conditioner and avoid using hot tools too often. You can also use a texturizing spray or mousse to enhance your waves without causing frizz.

What is the best way to style wavy hair?

The best way to style wavy hair is to embrace your natural texture and use products that enhance your waves. You can opt for a messy, beachy look or a sleek, polished style depending on your mood.


Overall, hair can get wavy as you age due to changes in hormone levels and damage from heat styling or chemical treatments. By embracing your natural texture and following a good hair care routine, you can keep your wavy locks looking their best at any age.
