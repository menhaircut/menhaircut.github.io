Mens Hairstyles For Thick Wavy Curly Hair A Guide For 2023

Written by Petter Sep 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Thick, wavy, and curly hair can be a blessing and a curse for men. While it can create a unique and stylish look, it can also be difficult to manage and style. In this article, we’ll explore the best mens hairstyles for thick wavy curly hair, including how to style them, tips for maintenance, and answers to frequently asked questions.

How to Style Mens Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

The key to styling mens hairstyles for thick wavy curly hair is to embrace the natural texture and work with it, rather than against it. A great haircut is the foundation for any good hairstyle, so start by finding a stylist who understands your hair type and can recommend a cut that will work for you.

When it comes to styling, use a lightweight, moisturizing product like a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance your curls and control frizz. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, using your fingers to scrunch and shape your curls into place. Avoid using a brush or comb, as this can cause your curls to separate and lose their shape.

For a more polished look, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair while enhancing your curls. Alternatively, you can also let your hair air dry for a more natural look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Mens Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

1. Start with clean, damp hair

2. Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream evenly throughout your hair

3. Use your fingers to scrunch and shape your curls into place

4. Allow your hair to air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer

5. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently fluff and shape your curls

Tips for Maintaining Mens Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

1. Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and frizzy

2. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it’s wet, to avoid damaging your curls

3. Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase, as these materials are gentler on your hair and can reduce frizz

4. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain a healthy appearance

Frequently Asked Questions about Mens Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

Q: What is the best haircut for thick wavy curly hair?

A: The best haircut for thick wavy curly hair is one that works with your natural texture and face shape. A layered cut can help to reduce bulk and add movement, while a longer cut can enhance your curls and create a more dramatic look.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my thick wavy curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz in thick wavy curly hair, avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can dry out your hair and cause it to become frizzy. Instead, opt for lightweight, moisturizing products like leave-in conditioners and curl creams to enhance your curls and control frizz.

Q: Is it okay to brush or comb my thick wavy curly hair?

A: It’s best to avoid brushing or combing thick wavy curly hair, as this can cause your curls to separate and lose their shape. Instead, use your fingers to gently detangle your hair while it’s wet, or use a wide-tooth comb to separate any knots or tangles.

Hair Care for Mens Hairstyles for Thick Wavy Curly Hair

When it comes to hair care for mens hairstyles for thick wavy curly hair, it’s important to use products that are gentle, moisturizing, and free from harsh chemicals. Look for products that are specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid using products that contain sulfates or alcohol, as these can be drying and damaging to your hair.

In addition to using the right products, it’s also important to maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle, as this can have a significant impact on the health and appearance of your hair. Be sure to eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and nutrients, and drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated.

With the right haircut, styling techniques, and hair care regimen, mens hairstyles for thick wavy curly hair can be both stylish and manageable. Embrace your natural texture and experiment with different styles to find the look that works best for you.
