How To Cut The Top Of A Man s Thinning Hair

Written by William Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As men age, they often experience thinning hair. This can be a frustrating experience, but there are ways to manage it. One way to deal with thinning hair is by cutting it in a way that makes it look fuller. In this article, we'll go over how to cut the top of a man's hair when it's thinning, step by step. We'll also include tips, FAQs, and hair care advice to help you maintain your hair's health.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut the Top of a Man's Thinning Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Step by Step

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and clippers with guards. Here are the steps to cut the top of a man's hair when it's thinning:

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp. This will make it easier to cut.
  2. Use the clippers with the longest guard to trim the sides and back of your head. This will create a contrast between the short sides and the longer top.
  3. Next, use the comb to lift the hair on the top of your head. Cut the hair in small sections, starting at the crown and working your way forward. Use the scissors to cut the hair at an angle, so it's shorter in the back and longer in the front. This will create a layered look that makes your hair appear fuller.
  4. When you're done cutting the top of your hair, use the clippers without a guard to trim around the edges of your hairline and sideburns.
  5. Style your hair as desired.

It's important to take your time when cutting your hair. If you rush, you may end up cutting too much off or creating an uneven cut. If you're not confident in your ability to cut your own hair, consider going to a professional stylist.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting the top of a man's hair:

  • Use a mirror to check your work as you go.
  • Don't cut too much off at once. It's better to cut too little than too much.
  • Keep your scissors sharp for a clean cut.
  • Use hair products to add volume to your hair, such as volumizing shampoo or mousse.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the top of a man's hair when it's thinning:

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Q: Can I use clippers to cut the top of my hair?

A: Yes, you can use clippers to trim the top of your hair, but make sure to use guards to avoid cutting too much off.

Q: How do I know if my hair is thinning?

A: Look for signs such as a receding hairline or a visible scalp when your hair is wet or styled.

Hair Care

In addition to cutting your hair in a way that makes it look fuller, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as flat irons or blow dryers, too often.
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals, such as iron and biotin, to promote hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

By following these tips and cutting your hair in a way that makes it look fuller, you can manage thinning hair and maintain healthy, stylish hair.
