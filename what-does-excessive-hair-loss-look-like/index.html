What Does Excessive Hair Loss Look Like

Written by Oliver Nov 30, 2023 · 3 min read
What Does Excessive Hair Loss Look Like? | Hair Care Article

Experiencing hair loss can be a frustrating and worrying experience, but it's important to know what is considered normal shedding and what is excessive. In this article, we will discuss what excessive hair loss looks like and provide tips on how to manage it.

Description

Excessive hair loss can be identified by an increased amount of shedding or thinning of the hair. It's normal to lose around 50 to 100 hairs per day, but if you notice clumps of hair falling out or a noticeable decrease in the thickness of your hair, it may be a sign of excessive hair loss.

How to Identify Excessive Hair Loss

One way to identify excessive hair loss is to pay attention to the amount of hair you are shedding daily. If you notice more hair than usual on your brush, pillow or in the shower, it may be a sign of excessive hair loss. Another way to identify excessive hair loss is to look for thinning areas on your scalp or a widening part.

Step by Step Guide on Managing Excessive Hair Loss

Step 1: Identify the Cause of Hair Loss

Identifying the cause of your hair loss is the first step in managing it. Common causes of hair loss include genetics, hormonal changes, stress, nutritional deficiencies, and certain medications. Consult with a dermatologist or hair specialist to determine the cause of your hair loss.

Step 2: Change Your Hair Care Routine

If you are experiencing excessive hair loss, it may be time to change your hair care routine. Avoid using harsh chemicals such as relaxers, dyes, and perms, as they can damage your hair and lead to breakage. Instead, use gentle, natural products that are specifically designed for your hair type.

Step 3: Adopt a Healthy Lifestyle

Adopting a healthy lifestyle can also help manage excessive hair loss. Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals, exercise regularly to reduce stress, and get enough sleep to ensure your body is rested and healthy.

Tips on Preventing Excessive Hair Loss

Preventing excessive hair loss is all about taking care of your hair and scalp. Here are a few tips:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Avoid over-brushing or over-styling your hair
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle wet hair
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons

FAQs

Q: Is it normal to lose hair on a daily basis?

A: Yes, it is normal to lose around 50 to 100 hairs per day, but if you notice an increase in hair loss, it may be a sign of excessive shedding or thinning.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you are stressed, your body produces hormones that can lead to hair loss or thinning.

Conclusion

Excessive hair loss can be a frustrating and worrying experience, but it's important to know what is considered normal shedding and what is excessive. By identifying the cause of your hair loss, changing your hair care routine, adopting a healthy lifestyle, and taking preventative measures, you can manage and prevent excessive hair loss.
