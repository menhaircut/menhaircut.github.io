How To Wear A Birdcage Veil With Hair Down A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're planning on wearing a birdcage veil for your wedding day or special occasion, you may be wondering how to style your hair with it. One popular option is wearing a birdcage veil with your hair down. This look is both elegant and timeless, and it's perfect for brides who want to show off their long locks. In this article, we'll show you how to wear a birdcage veil with hair down, including tips and tricks to make it look flawless.

Description

A birdcage veil is a short veil that covers the face and falls to the chin or just below it. It's typically made from netting, tulle, or lace and is attached to a comb or headband. The birdcage veil is a popular choice for brides who want to add a touch of vintage glamour to their wedding day look. It's also a great option for special occasions like proms, parties, or even a night out on the town.

How to Wear a Birdcage Veil with Hair Down

Step 1: Choose the Right Veil

Before you can style your hair with a birdcage veil, you need to choose the right one. Look for a veil that complements your dress and fits your personal style. You can choose from different materials, lengths, colors, and embellishments. Once you've found the perfect veil, you're ready to start styling.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing and blow-drying your hair. You want your hair to be clean, dry, and free of any tangles or knots. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from hot styling tools.

Step 3: Add Volume

If you have thin or flat hair, you may want to add some volume before styling. Use a volumizing mousse or spray on your roots and blow-dry your hair upside down. This will create more lift and body.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Using a curling wand or iron, curl your hair in loose waves. Make sure to curl away from your face and leave the ends out for a more natural look. Once you've curled your entire head, run your fingers through your hair to loosen the curls.

Step 5: Position the Veil

Now it's time to add the veil. Place the comb or headband where you want the veil to sit. You can position it at the top of your head or closer to your forehead. Make sure the veil covers your face but doesn't obstruct your vision.

Step 6: Secure the Veil

Once you've positioned the veil, secure it with bobby pins. You can hide the pins under your hair or use decorative pins to add some sparkle.

Step 7: Finishing Touches

Finally, add some hairspray to hold your style in place. You can also add accessories like a hairpin or flower for a touch of glamour.

Tips for Wearing a Birdcage Veil with Hair Down

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Choose a veil that complements your dress and personal style
  • Prep your hair with a heat protectant spray and volumizing product
  • Curl your hair in loose waves for a natural look
  • Position the veil at the top of your head or closer to your forehead
  • Secure the veil with bobby pins and hide them under your hair
  • Add accessories like a hairpin or flower for a touch of glamour

FAQ

Can I wear a birdcage veil with any hair length?

Yes, you can wear a birdcage veil with short or long hair. However, longer hair is easier to style with a birdcage veil.

What hairstyles work best with a birdcage veil?

Hairstyles that work best with a birdcage veil include loose waves, a low bun, a half-up/half-down style, or a side-swept hairstyle.

Do I need to use a special comb or headband for my birdcage veil?

No, you can use a regular comb or headband to attach your birdcage veil. However, some veils come with a comb or headband attached for convenience.

Hair Care

After your event, make sure to take care of your hair. Wash it with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny. Avoid using hot styling tools too often, and use a heat protectant spray when you do. Schedule regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain your hair's shape.
