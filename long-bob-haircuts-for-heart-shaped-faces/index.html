Long Bob Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Aprilia Jun 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know the struggle of finding the perfect haircut that compliments your features. Luckily, the long bob, or "lob," is a versatile cut that works well with heart shaped faces. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose the right long bob haircut for your heart shaped face, and provide tips for maintaining your new look.

Description

The long bob, or "lob," is a popular haircut that falls between the chin and shoulders. It's a versatile cut that can be styled in many ways, from straight and sleek to wavy and tousled. For heart shaped faces, a long bob that falls just below the chin is ideal, as it helps to balance out the proportions of the face.

How to Choose the Right Long Bob

When choosing a long bob haircut for a heart shaped face, it's important to consider the following factors:

Length

As mentioned above, a long bob that falls just below the chin is ideal for heart shaped faces. This length helps to balance out the proportions of the face and draw attention away from the forehead.

Layers

Layers can also help to balance out a heart shaped face. Long layers that begin at the chin can help to soften the jawline and add movement to the hair.

Side Part

A side part can help to create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. This can be achieved by parting the hair to one side and sweeping it over the forehead.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve a long bob haircut for a heart shaped face:

Step 1: Consultation

Consult with your stylist to determine the best length and layers for your hair type and face shape. Bring in photos of long bobs you like for reference.

Step 2: Wash and Dry

Wash and dry your hair before your appointment. Your stylist may want to cut your hair while it's dry to ensure the length is just right.

Step 3: Cut

Your stylist will cut your hair to the desired length and add layers if needed. They may also create a side part if desired.

Step 4: Style

Your stylist will style your hair using a blow dryer and round brush to create volume and movement. They may also use a flat iron to create sleek, straight hair or a curling iron to create loose waves.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your long bob haircut:

Trim Regularly

To maintain the length and shape of your long bob, it's important to get regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Use Heat Protectant

When styling your hair with a flat iron or curling iron, be sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage.

Experiment with Products

Try different styling products, such as texturizing spray or mousse, to achieve different looks with your long bob.

FAQ

Can I wear my long bob straight and sleek?

Absolutely! A long bob can be styled in many ways, including straight and sleek. Use a flat iron to create a smooth, polished look.

What if I have thin hair?

If you have thin hair, a long bob with layers can help to create the illusion of volume and movement. Talk to your stylist about adding layers to your haircut.

Can I wear my long bob with bangs?

Sure! If you have a heart shaped face, try opting for side-swept bangs to balance out your features.

Hair Care

To keep your long bob looking healthy and shiny, be sure to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated. Avoid using hot tools too often, and use a heat protectant when you do. Lastly, try to avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Conclusion

The long bob is a versatile haircut that works well with heart shaped faces. By choosing the right length, layers, and part, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look. Remember to maintain your haircut with regular trims and proper hair care, and experiment with different styling products to achieve different looks.
