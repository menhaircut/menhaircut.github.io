How Do I Style My Short Pixie Cut

Written by Daniel Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've recently chopped off your hair into a short pixie cut, you may be wondering how to style it. Pixie cuts are stylish and low maintenance, but it can be challenging to know how to make them look different day after day. In this article, we'll provide some tips and tricks for styling your short pixie cut.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Your Short Pixie Cut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Cuts

How to Style Your Short Pixie Cut

The great thing about a short pixie cut is that it is versatile and can be styled in many ways. You can create a sleek, sophisticated look for work, or add some texture and volume for a more casual look. Below are some popular ways to style a short pixie cut:

  • Slick it back with some gel for a sleek and professional look.
  • Add some texture with a texturizing spray for a messy yet chic look.
  • Curl the ends with a curling iron for a playful and flirty look.
  • Add volume to the top with a volumizing mousse for a bold and edgy look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your short pixie cut:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using products that are suitable for your hair type.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently by patting it with a towel. Do not rub your hair vigorously as it can cause damage and breakage.
  3. Apply a styling product of your choice, such as a gel, mousse or texturizing spray, to damp hair.
  4. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  5. If you want to add some curls, use a curling iron on the ends of your hair.
  6. Finish off with a hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short pixie cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep-conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a quality hairbrush to avoid tangles and breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short pixie cuts:

Q: How often should I wash my short pixie cut?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day or every other day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently, such as two or three times a week.

Q: Can I still wear accessories with a short pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can still wear accessories such as headbands, clips, and pins. Just make sure they are suitable for short hair and do not weigh your hair down.

Q: Can I dye my short pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can dye your short pixie cut. However, it's important to choose a color that complements your skin tone and to use products that are gentle on your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Short Pixie Cuts

Here are some hair care tips specifically for short pixie cuts:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons too often.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a hair sunscreen.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair soft and shiny.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate hair growth.

Now that you know how to style your short pixie cut, go ahead and experiment with different looks. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great.
