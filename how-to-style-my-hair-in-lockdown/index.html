How To Style My Hair In Lockdown Tips Steps And Faq

Written by Aprilia Jul 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're one of the many people still stuck at home due to the ongoing pandemic, you might be wondering how to style your hair without access to salons and hairstylists. Don't worry, though - with a few tips and tricks, you can still achieve a great look even while in lockdown. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps to style your hair in lockdown, answer some frequently asked questions, and provide some hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and shiny. Read on!

How to Style Your Hair in Lockdown: Step by Step

Before we dive into the specific styling techniques, it's important to note that healthy hair starts with proper care. Even if you're not going out as much, you should still follow a hair care routine that includes regular washing, conditioning, and moisturizing. This will keep your hair strong, soft, and less prone to damage.

Step 1: Determine Your Hair Type

The first step to styling your hair in lockdown is to determine your hair type. This will help you choose the right products and techniques that work best for you. The most common hair types are:

  • Straight hair
  • Wavy hair
  • Curly hair
  • Kinky/Coily hair

Each hair type has its own unique characteristics and requires different care and styling methods. Once you know your hair type, you can research and experiment with different styles that suit you.

Step 2: Choose Your Style

Once you know your hair type, it's time to choose a style that you want to achieve. There are many options to choose from, such as:

  • Braids
  • Buns
  • Ponytails
  • Curls
  • Sleek and straight
  • Updos

Consider the occasion, your hair type, and your personal style when choosing a look. You can also experiment with different accessories like headbands, clips, and scarves to add some flair to your hairstyle.

Step 3: Gather Your Tools and Products

Before you start styling, make sure you have all the tools and products you need. This might include:

  • Brush or comb
  • Hair ties or clips
  • Hair spray or gel
  • Heat styling tools (if applicable)
  • Moisturizing products like leave-in conditioner or hair oil

Make sure to choose high-quality products that are suitable for your hair type and won't cause damage or breakage.

Step 4: Start Styling

Now that you have everything you need, it's time to start styling! Follow these general tips:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a heat protectant if using heat styling tools
  • Work in sections for better control
  • Don't overdo it - too much styling or product can weigh down your hair and cause damage
  • Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for you

Remember, practice makes perfect! Don't be afraid to try new things and have fun with your hair.

Tips for Styling Your Hair in Lockdown

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect lockdown hairstyle:

  • Use dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes
  • Try a hair mask or deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and breakage
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair - let it air dry when possible
  • Try a new hair color or cut at home with caution

FAQ: Styling Your Hair in Lockdown

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling your hair in lockdown:

Can I dye my hair at home?

Yes, you can dye your hair at home using a box dye or semi-permanent color. However, be aware that it can be tricky to achieve the same results as a professional salon. Follow the instructions carefully and do a patch test first to avoid any allergic reactions or damage.

What if I have a bad hair day?

Don't worry - we all have bad hair days! Try a quick and easy hairstyle like a messy bun or ponytail, or cover it up with a hat or scarf. You can also use a little bit of dry shampoo or hair oil to revive your locks.

How can I grow my hair faster?

There's no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, but some tips include eating a healthy diet, getting enough sleep, reducing stress, and avoiding heat and chemical damage. You can also try using hair growth products like biotin supplements or scalp treatments.

Hair Care Tips for Lockdown

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your locks healthy and happy during lockdown:

  • Wash your hair regularly, but not too often - once or twice a week is usually enough
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's suitable for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat, chemicals, or styling products that can cause damage or breakage
  • Eat a healthy diet and stay hydrated for strong and shiny hair
  • Take breaks from tight hairstyles like braids or ponytails to prevent hair loss or damage

With these tips and techniques, you can style your hair in lockdown like a pro! Whether you're working from home, attending virtual meetings, or just want to switch up your look, there's a hairstyle out there for you. Remember to take care of your hair and have fun experimenting with new styles and products. Good luck!
