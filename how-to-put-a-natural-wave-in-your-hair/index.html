How To Put A Natural Wave In Your Hair

Written by William Jun 11, 2023 · 3 min read
How to Put a Natural Wave in Your Hair | Step-by-Step Guide

If you’re looking for a hairstyle that’s simple, effortless, and beautiful, a natural wave might be just what you need. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a beautiful wave in your hair that looks like you just stepped off the beach. Here’s our step-by-step guide on how to put a natural wave in your hair.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Put a Natural Wave in Your Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Creating a Natural Wave in Your Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Natural Wave

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to create a natural wave in your hair:

1. Start with clean, damp hair.

Wash your hair and towel dry it until it’s damp. You can also use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if you don’t want to wash it.

2. Apply a styling product.

Apply a styling product to your hair to help hold the wave in place. We recommend using a sea salt spray or a curl defining cream.

3. Create twists in your hair.

Take a small section of hair and twist it around your finger. Continue twisting until you reach the ends of your hair. Repeat this process all over your head.

4. Let your hair air dry or use a diffuser.

You can either let your hair air dry or use a diffuser to dry your hair. A diffuser will help enhance the natural wave in your hair.

5. Scrunch your hair.

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair. This will help enhance the natural wave in your hair.

6. Finish with hairspray.

Finish your hairstyle with a light hold hairspray to help hold the wave in place.

Tips for Creating a Natural Wave in Your Hair

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect natural wave:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before you start styling.
  • Avoid using a brush as it can make your hair too smooth and straight.
  • Twist your hair in different directions to create a more natural wave.
  • Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a diffuser on your hair dryer to enhance the natural wave in your hair.

FAQs

How long will the natural wave last?

The natural wave will last for a few days, depending on how well you maintain it. You can refresh the wave by scrunching your hair with a little water and reapplying styling product.

Can I create a natural wave in my hair if it’s naturally straight?

Yes, you can create a natural wave in your hair even if it’s naturally straight. Use a sea salt spray or curl defining cream to help hold the wave in place.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Natural Wave

Here are some hair care tips to help maintain your natural wave:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair instead of a brush.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair with a towel as this can cause frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you can create a beautiful natural wave in your hair that looks effortless and chic. Try it out and see how it works for you!
