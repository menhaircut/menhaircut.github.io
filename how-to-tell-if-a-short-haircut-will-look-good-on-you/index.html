How To Tell If A Short Haircut Will Look Good On You

Written by Eveline Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're thinking about getting a short haircut, you might be wondering if it will look good on you. Short haircuts can be a great way to switch up your look and show off your features, but they're not for everyone. In this article, we'll go over some tips on how to tell if a short haircut will look good on you.

Determine Your Face Shape

The first thing to consider when deciding if a short haircut will look good on you is your face shape. Short haircuts look best on those with oval, heart, and square-shaped faces. If you have a round or long face, a short haircut might not be the best choice for you. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and look in the mirror. You can also use a tape measure to measure the width and length of your face.

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type is another factor to consider when deciding if a short haircut will look good on you. If you have thin or fine hair, a short haircut can add volume and texture. If you have thick or curly hair, a short haircut can help control frizz and make styling easier. However, if you have very curly or kinky hair, a short haircut might not be the best choice as it can be difficult to style and maintain.

Highlight Your Best Features

A short haircut can draw attention to your best features, such as your eyes, cheekbones, or jawline. If you have a feature you want to highlight, talk to your hairstylist about how a short haircut can help bring it out. For example, a pixie cut can accentuate your cheekbones, while a bob can draw attention to your jawline.

Reflect Your Personality

Your hairstyle should reflect your personality and personal style. If you're outgoing and adventurous, a bold and edgy short haircut might be the perfect fit for you. If you're more reserved and classic, a simple and elegant short haircut might be more your style. Consider your wardrobe and overall aesthetic when deciding on a short haircut.

Get a Professional Consultation

If you're still not sure if a short haircut will look good on you, book a consultation with a professional hairstylist. They can offer advice on what styles will work best for your face shape, hair type, and personal style. They can also show you examples of short haircuts that would look good on you.

Think About Maintenance

A short haircut requires more maintenance than longer hairstyles. You'll need to get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut, and you might need to style it more often. Consider how much time and effort you're willing to put into styling and maintaining your short haircut before making the decision to cut your hair.

Consider Your Styling Preferences

Before getting a short haircut, think about your styling preferences. Do you like to wear your hair straight or curly? Do you prefer a messy, textured look or a sleek and polished style? Talk to your hairstylist about how you like to style your hair and they can recommend a short haircut that will work with your styling preferences.

Believe in Yourself

Ultimately, the most important factor when deciding if a short haircut will look good on you is confidence. If you feel confident and comfortable in your new haircut, it will show. Don't be afraid to take a risk and try something new. Hair grows back, and you can always change your style if you're not happy with it.

FAQs

Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

If you have a round face, a short haircut can make your face look even rounder. It's best to stick with longer hairstyles that elongate the face.

Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with a short haircut. You might need to use bobby pins or a hair tie to keep it in place.

What should I do if I don't like my short haircut?

If you don't like your short haircut, don't panic. Talk to your hairstylist about what you don't like and they can recommend ways to fix it. You can also try different styling techniques to change up your look.

Hair Care Tips

Regardless of whether you have a short or long haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools on a daily basis. Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. And most importantly, love your hair and embrace your unique style!
