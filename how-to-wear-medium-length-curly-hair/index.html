How To Wear Medium Length Curly Hair A Guide To Relaxed Styling

If you have medium length curly hair, you might find it challenging to style and maintain. However, with the right techniques and products, you can make your curls look amazing and effortless. In this guide, we'll show you how to wear medium length curly hair in a relaxed and natural way. From how to create defined curls to tips for keeping your hair healthy, we've got you covered.

How to Wear Medium Length Curly Hair

When it comes to styling medium length curly hair, the key is to embrace your natural texture and work with it. Here are some tips for wearing your curls in a relaxed and effortless way:

1. Use the Right Products

Start by using a shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair. Look for products that are sulfate-free and contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter. After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and add moisture.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling wands, as they can damage your hair and cause frizz. Instead, let your hair air dry or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your curls without disrupting their natural shape.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Don't try to fight your curls or make them look like something they're not. Instead, embrace your natural texture and work with it. Use your fingers to gently separate your curls and add volume, or scrunch your hair with a towel to encourage your curls to form.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide for wearing your medium length curly hair in a relaxed and natural way:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Begin by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Rinse your hair thoroughly and gently squeeze out any excess water.

2. Apply Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to work the product through your curls, making sure to distribute it evenly.

3. Use a Diffuser Attachment

Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to low heat and low speed. Use the diffuser to dry your hair, scrunching your curls with your fingers as you go.

4. Define Your Curls

Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate your curls and add volume. You can also use a curling cream or gel to define your curls and add shine.

Tips for Styling Medium Length Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for styling your medium length curly hair:

1. Sleep with a Satin Pillowcase

Sleeping on a satin pillowcase can help reduce frizz and protect your curls from damage. If you don't have a satin pillowcase, wrap your hair in a satin scarf or bonnet before bed.

2. Don't Over-Brush Your Hair

Brushing your hair too much can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls, starting at the ends and working your way up.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, like braids, buns, or half-up hairstyles. You can also try using hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add some extra flair to your look.

FAQs

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use, but most people with curly hair can get away with washing their hair once or twice a week.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid brushing your hair too much, use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream, and sleep on a satin pillowcase.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

To keep your medium length curly hair looking its best, be sure to follow these hair care tips:

1. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to help keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Look for products that contain ingredients like argan oil or avocado oil.

2. Trim Your Ends Regularly

Get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons or curling wands, or use them sparingly. When you do use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

With these tips and techniques, you can wear your medium length curly hair in a relaxed and effortless way. Remember to embrace your natural texture, use the right products, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful.
