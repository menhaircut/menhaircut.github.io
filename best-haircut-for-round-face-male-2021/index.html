Best Haircut For Round Face Male 2021 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you are a man with a round face, then choosing the right haircut can be a daunting task. A round face tends to have a soft appearance with a circular shape, which makes it difficult to find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape. However, with the right haircut and styling, you can enhance your features and make your face appear more angular. In this guide, we will explore the best haircut for round face male 2021, including how to choose the right haircut, tips for styling, and hair care tips.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Round Face?

Before choosing the right haircut, it's essential to determine your face shape. The easiest way to do this is by measuring the length and width of your face. If the measurements are nearly equal, then you have a round face. Once you have determined your face shape, it's important to choose a haircut that balances your features and creates the illusion of a more angular face shape. Here are some tips to consider:

1. Shorter Hairstyles:

Shorter hairstyles tend to work best for round faces as they create the illusion of length and height. Consider a buzz cut, crew cut, or a short crop. These hairstyles can be styled with a side part or textured on top to add volume.

2. Angular Hairstyles:

Angular hairstyles with sharp lines and edges can help create the illusion of a more angular face shape. Consider a side-swept fringe, a quiff, or a faux hawk. These styles can be styled with a matte finish or a high shine for a more polished look.

3. Avoid Round Haircuts:

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a round bowl cut or a mushroom cut. These hairstyles will only make your face appear rounder and less defined.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling the Best Haircut for Round Face Male 2021

Once you have chosen the right haircut, it's essential to know how to style it to enhance your features. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Wash Your Hair:

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. This will remove any dirt and oil buildup and prepare your hair for styling.

2. Apply Styling Product:

Apply a styling product such as a hair gel, wax, or pomade to your hair. The type of product will depend on the hairstyle you have chosen. Apply the product evenly to your hair and work it through with your fingers.

3. Blow Dry Your Hair:

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while styling it with a brush or your fingers. This will help add volume and texture to your hair and create the desired look.

4. Finish with Hairspray:

Finish off your style with a hairspray to hold it in place throughout the day. Choose a hairspray that suits your hair type and provides the desired amount of hold.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have the perfect haircut, it's important to maintain it to ensure it looks great every day. Here are some tips to consider:

1. Regular Trims:

Visit your barber every four to six weeks for a trim to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.

2. Use Quality Hair Products:

Invest in quality hair products that suit your hair type and provide the desired hold and finish.

3. Protect Your Hair:

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or using hot styling tools.

FAQ

1. Can I have long hair with a round face?

Yes, you can have long hair with a round face. However, it's important to choose a haircut that adds volume at the top and avoids adding volume to the sides of your face.

2. Should I avoid bangs with a round face?

Not necessarily. Bangs can work with a round face if they are side-swept or textured to add volume and height.

3. Can I style my hair differently for formal occasions?

Yes, you can style your hair differently for formal occasions such as weddings or job interviews. Consider a sleek back or a side-parted hairstyle for a more polished look.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some hair care tips to keep your hair looking healthy and shiny:

1. Don't Overwash:

Avoid overwashing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils and leave it dry and brittle. Aim to wash your hair every two to three days.

2. Use Conditioner:

Conditioner helps to restore moisture to your hair and keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a conditioner every time you wash your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun:

Exposure to the sun can damage your hair and leave it looking dull and lifeless. Protect your hair by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF protection.

In conclusion, choosing the best haircut for round face male 2021 involves considering your face shape, selecting the right haircut, styling it correctly, and maintaining it with proper hair care. Follow the tips and steps outlined in this guide to achieve the perfect hairstyle that enhances your features and makes you look your best.
