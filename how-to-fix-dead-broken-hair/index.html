How To Fix Dead Broken Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Jun 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Dead broken hair can be frustrating and can affect your self-esteem. However, it is possible to revive your hair and bring it back to life. In this article, we will discuss how to fix dead broken hair, step by step. We will also provide you with helpful tips and tricks to help maintain healthy hair.

How to Fix Dead Broken Hair

Dead broken hair can be caused by various factors such as heat damage, chemical treatments, and lack of proper hair care. Here are some ways to fix dead broken hair:

1. Trim Your Hair

The first thing you need to do is to trim your hair. Cutting off the damaged hair will help to prevent further breakage. You don't have to cut off all your hair, just enough to get rid of the dead ends.

2. Use a Protein Treatment

A protein treatment can help to strengthen your hair and prevent breakage. Look for a protein treatment that contains keratin or collagen. Apply the treatment to your hair according to the instructions on the package.

3. Moisturize Your Hair

Dead broken hair is often dry and brittle. Moisturizing your hair is key to reviving it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner, and apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair after washing it.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage. Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons and curling irons. If you must use them, make sure to use a heat protectant spray before styling your hair.

Tips to Maintain Healthy Hair

Here are some tips to help maintain healthy hair:

1. Avoid Over-washing Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, making it dry and prone to breakage. Aim to wash your hair no more than two to three times a week.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Avoid using a brush on your hair, as it can cause breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up to the roots.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet can help to promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to eat foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as B vitamins, vitamin C, and iron.

FAQs About How to Fix Dead Broken Hair

Q. Can I fix dead broken hair?

A. Yes, you can fix dead broken hair. The key is to trim your hair, use a protein treatment, moisturize your hair, and avoid heat styling.

Q. How long will it take to fix dead broken hair?

A. It can take several months to fix dead broken hair. Be patient and consistent with your hair care routine, and you should start to see results.

Q. Can I prevent dead broken hair?

A. Yes, you can prevent dead broken hair by avoiding heat styling, using a wide-tooth comb, and eating a healthy diet.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair to prevent it from becoming dead and broken. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too many hair products. Also, make sure to protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

Fixing dead broken hair takes time and patience, but with the right hair care routine, you can bring your hair back to life. Follow the steps outlined in this article, and don't forget to maintain healthy hair habits to prevent future damage.
