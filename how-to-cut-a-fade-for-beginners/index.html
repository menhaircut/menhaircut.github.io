How To Cut A Fade For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, then getting a fade might be a good option for you. A fade is a popular men's haircut that involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back, while gradually blending it into longer hair on top. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps on how to cut a fade for beginners.

How to Cut a Fade for Beginners

Before we get started, there are a few things you'll need:

  • Hair clippers
  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape
  • Mirror
  • Spray bottle

Step One: Decide on the Length

The first step in cutting a fade is to decide on the length of the hair. You can choose to keep the hair on top long, medium, or short, depending on your preference. The sides and back should be cut short, but the length can vary depending on the type of fade you want to achieve.

Step Two: Start with the Sides

Begin by using the clippers with a guard to cut the hair on the sides and back. Depending on the length you want, you can start with a higher guard and work your way down. Use a comb to lift the hair and clip it evenly. Make sure to blend the hair as you move down to create a fade effect.

Step Three: Blend the Top

Once you've finished cutting the sides and back, it's time to blend the top. Use scissors to trim the hair on top to the desired length. Then, use the clippers without a guard to create a seamless blend between the top and sides.

Step Four: Refine the Fade

After blending the top, go over the fade with a lower guard to refine the look. This will create a smoother and more natural transition between the different lengths of hair.

Step Five: Clean Up

Once you're happy with the fade, use scissors to trim any stray hairs and clean up the neckline and around the ears. Use a spray bottle to wet the hair and comb it to make sure it's even and tidy.

Tips for Cutting a Fade

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect fade:

  • Take your time and work slowly to make sure you're creating an even fade
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from different angles
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're not sure what you're doing
  • Practice makes perfect, so keep trying until you get it right

FAQs

How long does a fade last?

A fade can last anywhere from a few weeks to a few months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how short you cut it.

Can you cut a fade with long hair on top?

Yes, you can still achieve a fade with longer hair on top. Just make sure to gradually blend the hair on the sides and back into the longer hair on top.

Hair Care for Fades

After cutting a fade, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the look. Here are some tips:

  • Use a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as this can strip it of its natural oils
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized
  • Visit a barber regularly to maintain the shape of your fade

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you'll be able to cut a fade like a pro in no time. Remember to take your time and be patient, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Good luck!
