What Gel Do You Use To Slick Back Hair

Written by Luffy Sep 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a sleek and polished hairstyle that will last all day? Slicked back hair is a classic look that has been popular for decades. But, what gel do you use to slick back hair? In this post, we will discuss the best gels and techniques for achieving a perfect slicked back look.

Description

Slicked back hair is a hairstyle that is combed straight back and held in place with gel or pomade. This style is versatile and can be worn by men and women of all ages. It is a popular choice for formal events, business meetings, and everyday wear. The key to achieving this look is to use the right product and technique.

How to Slick Back Hair

To achieve a slicked back hairstyle, you will need to follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair

Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any product buildup. Towel dry your hair until it is slightly damp.

Step 2: Apply gel or pomade

Take a small amount of gel or pomade and rub it between your hands to warm it up. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, making sure to distribute it from roots to tips.

Step 3: Comb hair straight back

Using a fine-tooth comb, comb your hair straight back from your forehead to the nape of your neck. Smooth out any bumps or tangles as you go.

Step 4: Style as desired

You can leave your hair slicked back for a classic look, or you can add volume to the top for a more modern style. Use a hairdryer and a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.

Tips for Slicking Back Hair

When slicking back your hair, keep these tips in mind:

  • Use a high-quality gel or pomade that is designed for slicked back hairstyles.
  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed. Too much product can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Use a fine-tooth comb to create a smooth, polished look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for your hair type and face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about slicked back hair:

What gel do you use to slick back hair?

The best gel to use for slicked back hair is one that provides strong hold without making your hair look greasy or weighed down. Look for a gel or pomade that is specifically designed for slicked back hairstyles.

Can you slick back thin hair?

Yes, you can slick back thin hair. Use a volumizing gel or pomade to add thickness and hold to your hair.

How long does slicked back hair last?

Slicked back hair can last all day if you use the right product and technique. Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause the gel to break down and lose its hold.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and straighteners.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and well-groomed.

In conclusion, the best gel to use for slicked back hair is one that provides strong hold without making your hair look greasy or weighed down. Use a fine-tooth comb to create a smooth, polished look, and experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you. With these tips and techniques, you can achieve a perfect slicked back hairstyle that will last all day.
