How To Shape Long Hair Around Your Face

Written by Bardi Oct 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair can add volume and beauty to your face, but it can be difficult to shape it properly. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and techniques to help you shape your long hair around your face. We’ll also provide some hair care tips to ensure your long hair stays healthy and vibrant.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Shape Long Hair Around Your Face
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Shaping Long Hair Around Your Face
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

How to Shape Long Hair Around Your Face

Shaping long hair around your face can be a daunting task, but it doesn’t have to be. Here are some things you can do to help shape your hair:

  • Consider your face shape: The first step in shaping your hair around your face is to consider your face shape. Different face shapes require different hairstyles. For example, if you have a round face, you may want to consider longer layers that add volume to the top of your head.
  • Look for inspiration: Look for inspiration pictures online or in magazines. Find pictures of hairstyles that you like and that would work well with your face shape.
  • Consult with a hairstylist: A hairstylist can help you determine the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type. They can also help you maintain your hair’s health and prevent breakage.
  • Use styling products: Styling products can help you shape your hair around your face. Use products like mousse or gel to add volume and hold to your hair. You can also use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you shape your long hair around your face:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume to your hair.
  3. Section off your hair and use a flat iron to add texture and shape to your hair.
  4. Use a styling product like mousse or gel to add hold to your hair.
  5. Part your hair in the desired location.
  6. Use a curling iron to add curls or waves to your hair.
  7. Finish with a hairspray to hold your hairstyle in place.

Tips for Shaping Long Hair Around Your Face

Here are some additional tips to help you shape your long hair around your face:

  • Be patient: Shaping long hair around your face can take time and practice. Don’t get discouraged if it doesn’t turn out perfect the first time.
  • Use the right tools: Use tools like a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and shape to your hair. Make sure to use heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Consider your lifestyle: When choosing a hairstyle, consider your lifestyle. If you are active or work in a professional setting, you may want a more low-maintenance hairstyle.
  • Get regular trims: Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for a round face?

A layered hairstyle with longer layers that add volume to the top of the head can be flattering for a round face.

How often should I wash my long hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or sweat a lot, you may need to wash your hair more often. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils. Try to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair

Long hair requires special care to stay healthy and vibrant. Here are some hair care tips for long hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or brush to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair health.

Shaping long hair around your face can be a fun and rewarding experience. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a beautiful hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant.
