Baldness is caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, medication, and lifestyle factors. Male pattern baldness, for example, is a genetic condition that affects many men as they age. Female pattern baldness, on the other hand, is often caused by hormonal changes, such as menopause.
