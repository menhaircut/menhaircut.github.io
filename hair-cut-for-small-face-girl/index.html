In this blog post, we will discuss various hair cut options that will flatter your small face. We will also share some tips on how to choose the perfect hair cut, steps to achieve it, and how to take care of your hair after the cut. Let's get started!

How to Choose the Perfect Hair Cut

Choosing the right hair cut for a small face is crucial as it can make or break your entire look. Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting a hair cut:

  • Consider your face shape: While small face girls can pull off various hair cut styles, it is important to choose a style that complements your face shape. If you have a round face, go for angular hair cuts, and if you have an oval face, opt for a layered cut.
  • Think about your hair texture: Your hair texture will play a significant role in determining the hair cut that suits you best. If you have thin hair, go for a blunt bob or a pixie cut. If you have thick hair, consider a layered cut.
  • Look for inspiration: Browse through magazines or social media to find hair cut styles that you like. It can give you an idea of what will suit you best.

Hair Cut Options for Small Face Girl

Now that you know how to choose the perfect hair cut, let's take a look at some hair cut options that will flatter your small face:

Blunt Bob

A blunt bob is a classic hair cut that looks great on small face girls. It is a low-maintenance cut that is easy to style. You can opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob depending on your preference.

Pixie Cut

If you are feeling adventurous, consider a pixie cut. It is a short hair cut that looks great on small face girls. It is easy to maintain and style.

Layered Cut

A layered cut is perfect if you have thick hair. It adds dimension and texture to your hair. You can opt for long layers or short layers depending on your preference.

Steps to Achieve the Perfect Hair Cut

Once you have chosen the perfect hair cut, it is time to achieve it. Here are some steps to follow:

  • Find a reputable hair stylist who has experience cutting hair for small face girls.
  • Bring a picture of the hair cut you want to achieve.
  • Communicate with your hair stylist about what you want and don't want.
  • Trust your hair stylist's expertise.
  • After the cut, style your hair as desired.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a hair cut:

  • Get a hair cut that complements your face shape and hair texture.
  • Communicate with your hair stylist about what you want.
  • Trust your hair stylist's expertise.
  • Be open to trying new hair cut styles.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hair cut for small face girls:

1. How often should I get a hair cut?

You should get a hair cut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and maintain the style.

2. Will a short hair cut make my face look smaller?

Yes, a short hair cut can make your face look smaller as it draws attention away from your face.

3. Can I still wear my hair up after getting a hair cut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up after getting a hair cut. It may require some styling, but it is possible.

Hair Care After the Cut

After getting a hair cut, it is important to take care of your hair to maintain the style and keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the style.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine if you go swimming.

Congratulations, you are now armed with all the information you need to get the perfect hair cut for your small face. Remember to communicate with your hair stylist, choose a style that complements your face shape and hair texture, and take care of your hair after the cut. Good luck!
