Best Haircut For Receding Hairline And Round Face A Complete Guide

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you struggling to find the best haircut for your receding hairline and round face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many men face this challenge, but with the right haircut, you can look and feel your best. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for receding hairline and round face, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to maintain it. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Achieve the Best Haircut for Receding Hairline and Round Face
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Men with receding hairline and round face often struggle to find the perfect haircut that complements their features. A round face can make the head look smaller, while a receding hairline can make it look larger. Therefore, it's essential to choose a haircut that balances these features and enhances your look.

How to Achieve the Best Haircut for Receding Hairline and Round Face

The best haircut for receding hairline and round face is one that adds height and volume to the crown while keeping the sides short. This style draws attention away from the receding hairline and accentuates the face's natural features. Some popular haircuts that work well for this face shape include the buzz cut, crew cut, side part, and undercut.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic hairstyle that works well for men with receding hairlines and round faces. It's a low maintenance style that requires little upkeep, making it perfect for busy guys. The buzz cut is achieved by cutting the hair very short all over the head, giving it a uniform appearance. This style is perfect for men who want to embrace their baldness and look confident.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is another popular hairstyle that suits men with receding hairlines and round faces. This style is achieved by cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving it longer on top. The top hair is then styled forward, creating a textured look that adds height and volume to the crown. This style is perfect for men who want a stylish yet low maintenance look.

Side Part

The side part is a classic hairstyle that works well for men with round faces and receding hairlines. This style is achieved by combing the hair to one side and creating a part on the opposite side. The hair is then cut short on the sides and back, while the top is styled with a pomade or gel. This style is perfect for men who want a polished and sophisticated look.

Undercut

The undercut is a trendy hairstyle that suits men with receding hairlines and round faces. This style involves cutting the hair short on the back and sides while leaving it longer on top. The top hair is then styled back or to one side, creating a textured and edgy look. This style is perfect for men who want a modern and stylish look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know which haircuts work best for your face shape let's take a look at how to achieve them.

Buzz Cut

  1. Start by cutting your hair to an even length using clippers.
  2. Use a shorter guard to trim the hair on the sides and back of the head.
  3. Use a longer guard to trim the hair on the top of the head.
  4. Blend the top and sides using scissors or clippers.
  5. Use a razor to shave the hairline and create a clean look.

Crew Cut

  1. Start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head short using clippers.
  2. Leave the hair on top of the head longer and textured.
  3. Use a pomade or gel to style the hair forward, creating height and volume on the crown.
  4. Trim the hair on the sides and back every few weeks to maintain the look.

Side Part

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair, creating volume on the crown.
  3. Create a side part using a comb.
  4. Apply a pomade or gel to the hair on top of the head and style it to one side.
  5. Trim the hair on the sides and back every few weeks to maintain the look.

Undercut

  1. Start by cutting the hair on the sides and back of the head short using clippers.
  2. Leave the hair on top of the head longer and textured.
  3. Use a pomade or gel to style the hair back or to one side, creating a textured and edgy look.
  4. Trim the hair on the sides and back every few weeks to maintain the look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you maintain your new haircut:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and strong.
  • Try different hair styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type.
  • Visit your barber every few weeks to maintain the look.
  • Use a hairbrush or comb to style your hair and create the desired look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQs

Q: Will a buzz cut suit my round face?

A: Yes, a buzz cut is a great option for men with round faces. It creates a uniform look that draws attention away from the face's roundness.

Q: Can I style my hair differently every day?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different hair styling products and techniques to create different looks every day.

Q: How often should I visit my barber?

A: You should visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to maintain the look and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy scalp and strong hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water, as it can damage your hair and scalp.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it and cause breakage.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.

In conclusion, finding the best haircut for your receding hairline and round face is easy when you know what to look for. Choose a style that adds height and volume to the crown while keeping the sides short. Experiment with different hair styling products and techniques to create different looks every day. Take care of your hair by washing it regularly and avoiding too much heat. With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect hairstyle that complements your features and makes you look and feel your best.
