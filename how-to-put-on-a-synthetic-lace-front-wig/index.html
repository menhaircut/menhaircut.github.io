How To Put On A Synthetic Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're new to wearing wigs or just looking for a more natural look, a synthetic lace front wig might be just what you need. Lace front wigs are designed to mimic a natural hairline, making them look more realistic than traditional wigs. However, putting on a lace front wig can be intimidating if you've never done it before. In this article, we'll walk you through the process step-by-step so you can confidently wear your new wig.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Wig
  • How to Prepare Your Hair
  • How to Put on Your Wig
  • Tips for a Natural Look
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Synthetic Wigs

How to Choose the Right Wig

Before you can put on your wig, you need to make sure you've chosen the right one. There are a few things to consider when selecting a synthetic lace front wig:

  • Length: Do you want a short or long wig?
  • Style: Do you want straight, wavy, or curly hair?
  • Color: What color best matches your natural hair?

Once you've selected your wig, make sure it's the right size for your head. Most wigs come in small, medium, and large sizes. Measure your head before purchasing your wig to ensure a proper fit.


How to Prepare Your Hair

Before putting on your wig, you need to prepare your hair. If you have long hair, braid it close to your head or put it in a low ponytail. If you have short hair, use hair clips to keep it in place. Cover your hair with a wig cap to keep it in place and create a smooth surface for the wig to sit on.


How to Put on Your Wig

Now it's time to put on your wig:

  1. Hold the wig by the nape with the lace facing up.
  2. Place the wig on your head, starting at the front hairline.
  3. Adjust the wig so it sits comfortably on your head. Use the ear tabs to position the wig properly.
  4. Secure the wig in place using the combs or clips on the inside of the wig.
  5. Cut the lace along the hairline, being careful not to cut the hair.
  6. Blend the wig with your natural hairline using a brush or your fingers.

Tips for a Natural Look

Here are some tips to help your wig look more natural:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place and create a smooth surface for the wig.
  • Choose a wig that matches your natural hair color and style.
  • Trim the lace to fit your natural hairline.
  • Use a wig adhesive for a more secure fit.
  • Style the wig to match your personal style.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about synthetic lace front wigs:

  • Can I wear a synthetic lace front wig every day? Yes, synthetic wigs can be worn daily, but they will need to be washed more frequently than natural hair.
  • How do I wash my synthetic wig? Fill a sink with cool water and add a small amount of wig shampoo. Gently swish the wig in the water for a few minutes, then rinse with cool water. Hang the wig to dry.
  • Can I style my synthetic wig with heat? No, synthetic wigs cannot be styled with heat. Use a low-heat setting on your hair dryer if you need to dry the wig.

Hair Care for Synthetic Wigs

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your synthetic wig looking its best:

  • Wash your wig every 6-8 wears.
  • Use a wig brush or wide-tooth comb to detangle your wig.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to help maintain its shape.
  • Avoid exposing your wig to heat or direct sunlight.

Putting on a synthetic lace front wig can be a little intimidating at first, but with these step-by-step instructions, you'll be a pro in no time. Remember to choose the right wig for your head size and personal style, prepare your hair properly, and care for your wig to keep it looking its best.
