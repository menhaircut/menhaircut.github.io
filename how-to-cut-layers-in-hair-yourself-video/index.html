How To Cut Layers In Hair Yourself Video

Written by Oliver Aug 24, 2023 · 3 min read
How to Cut Layers in Hair Yourself Video | Step by Step Guide

If you're looking to add some volume and dimension to your hair without going to the salon, cutting your own layers can be a great solution. In this step-by-step guide, we'll show you how to cut layers in hair yourself video.

Description

Before we get started, let's go over some basics. Layers are created by cutting different lengths of hair throughout your head, which creates movement and texture. Layers can be cut in a variety of ways, from long, subtle layers to shorter, choppy layers.

How to Cut Layers in Hair Yourself Video: Step by Step

Step 1: Choose Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair. This will help you get a more accurate idea of how your hair will look once it's cut.

Step 3: Section Off Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections, starting with the top layer. Use hair clips to keep each section separate.

Step 4: Cut Your First Section

Start with the top section of your hair. Hold the hair straight up with your fingers, and cut horizontally across.

Step 5: Continue Cutting Layers

Drop down the next section of hair, and use the previously cut layer as a guide for the length of the new layer. Continue cutting layers until you've cut all of the sections of your hair.

Step 6: Blend Your Layers

Blend your layers by combing your hair straight down, and using your scissors to trim any uneven pieces. This will help your layers look more natural.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind as you cut your own layers:

  • Start with small sections of hair. It's always better to cut too little than too much.
  • Use a light touch when cutting. You can always go back and cut more if you need to.
  • Be patient. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, especially if you're not used to it.

FAQ

Can I cut layers in my own hair?

Yes, you can cut layers in your own hair. It may take some practice, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve great results.

What's the best way to cut layers?

There is no one "best" way to cut layers. The technique you use will depend on the length and texture of your hair, as well as the type of layers you want to create.

How often should I cut my layers?

How often you cut your layers will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain it. On average, it's a good idea to trim your layers every 6-8 weeks to keep them looking fresh.

Hair Care

Now that you've cut your own layers, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh.

With these tips and techniques, you can cut your own layers and achieve salon-quality results in the comfort of your own home.
