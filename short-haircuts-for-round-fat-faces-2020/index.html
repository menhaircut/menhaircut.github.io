Short Haircuts For Round Fat Faces 2020

Written by Aprilia Oct 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a round and fat face, you might think that short haircuts are not for you. However, that's not true. Short haircuts can actually complement your face shape and make you look even more beautiful. In this article, we will discuss the best short haircuts for round fat faces in 2020.

Description

Round and fat faces have a circular shape, and the width and length are almost the same. The goal of any haircut for this face shape is to create an illusion of length and slimness. Short haircuts can help in achieving this goal by adding volume to the crown and reducing volume on the sides.

How To

The first step in choosing a short haircut for a round fat face is to determine the type of hair you have. If you have curly hair, you should go for a haircut that adds volume to the crown and keeps the sides short. If you have straight hair, you can experiment with different hairstyles that suit your face shape.

Once you have determined your hair type, you can choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. The best short haircuts for round fat faces are pixie cuts, bob cuts, and asymmetrical cuts. Pixie cuts are very short haircuts that add volume to the crown and reduce volume on the sides. Bob cuts are shoulder-length haircuts that can be styled in different ways to suit your face shape. Asymmetrical cuts are haircuts that are longer on one side and shorter on the other. They can create an illusion of length and slimness.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your hair type
Step 2: Choose a haircut that flatters your face shape
Step 3: Experiment with different hairstyles that suit your face shape
Step 4: Style your hair in different ways to create a unique look

Tips

Tip 1: Avoid haircuts that add volume to the sides
Tip 2: Opt for haircuts that add volume to the crown
Tip 3: Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best
Tip 4: Use hair products that add volume and texture to your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have a round fat face?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round fat face. However, you should avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides and opt for hairstyles that add volume to the crown. You can also experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best.

Q: What hair products should I use to add volume and texture to my hair?

A: You can use hair products such as volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, and dry shampoo to add volume and texture to your hair. These products can help create an illusion of length and slimness.

Hair Care

Short haircuts require less maintenance than long haircuts, but you should still take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type, and avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curlers too often. You can also use hair masks and oils to nourish your hair and prevent damage.

Short haircuts can be stylish and flattering for round fat faces. With the right haircut and styling, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance. Use the tips and advice in this article to find the perfect short haircut for your face shape and hair type.
