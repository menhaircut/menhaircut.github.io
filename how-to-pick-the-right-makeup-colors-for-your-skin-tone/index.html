How To Pick The Right Makeup Colors For Your Skin Tone

Written by Christine Jul 22, 2023 · 4 min read

As a makeup lover, one of the most important things to consider when choosing your makeup products is your skin tone. The right makeup colors can enhance your natural beauty, while the wrong ones can make you look washed out or even clownish. Here are some tips on how to pick the right makeup colors for your skin tone.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Determine Your Skin Tone
  2. Makeup Colors for Cool Skin Tones
  3. Makeup Colors for Warm Skin Tones
  4. Makeup Colors for Neutral Skin Tones
  5. Tips for Choosing the Right Makeup Colors
  6. FAQs

How to Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step in choosing the right makeup colors is to determine your skin tone. This can be done by looking at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone. If you can’t tell whether your veins appear blue or green, you have a neutral skin tone.

Makeup Colors for Cool Skin Tones

If you have a cool skin tone, you should look for makeup colors that have blue or pink undertones. Some good choices include:

  • Foundation: Choose a shade with a pink or neutral undertone.
  • Blush: Look for shades of pink, rose, and mauve.
  • Lipstick: Choose shades of pink, berry, and red.
  • Eyeshadow: Look for shades of blue, purple, and gray.

Makeup Colors for Warm Skin Tones

If you have a warm skin tone, you should look for makeup colors that have yellow or golden undertones. Some good choices include:

  • Foundation: Choose a shade with a yellow or golden undertone.
  • Blush: Look for shades of peach, coral, and bronze.
  • Lipstick: Choose shades of peach, coral, and orange.
  • Eyeshadow: Look for shades of gold, copper, and bronze.

Makeup Colors for Neutral Skin Tones

If you have a neutral skin tone, you can wear a wide range of makeup colors. Some good choices include:

  • Foundation: Look for a shade that matches your skin tone without any strong undertones.
  • Blush: Look for shades of pink, peach, and brown.
  • Lipstick: Choose shades of pink, nude, and red.
  • Eyeshadow: Look for shades of brown, taupe, and gray.

Tips for Choosing the Right Makeup Colors

  • Consider the occasion: Different makeup colors are appropriate for different occasions. For example, you might choose a bold lipstick for a night out, but a more neutral shade for work.
  • Don’t be afraid to experiment: While there are some general guidelines for choosing makeup colors based on your skin tone, there are no hard and fast rules. If you find a color that you love, try it out!
  • Take lighting into account: The way makeup colors look can vary depending on the lighting. Try applying makeup in natural light to get a better sense of how it looks on your skin.

FAQs

What if I have multiple skin tones?

Some people have multiple skin tones, such as a warm face and cool neck. In this case, you should choose makeup colors that are flattering to the majority of your skin.

What if I’m still not sure what my skin tone is?

If you’re still not sure what your skin tone is, you can ask a makeup artist for help. They will be able to recommend makeup colors that flatter your skin.

Can I wear any makeup colors if I have a darker skin tone?

Yes! People with darker skin tones can wear a wide range of makeup colors. However, some colors may be more flattering than others, depending on your individual skin tone.

How to Take Care of Your Hair and Skin

In addition to choosing the right makeup colors, it’s important to take care of your hair and skin to enhance your natural beauty. Here are some tips:

  • Drink plenty of water to keep your skin hydrated.
  • Use a gentle cleanser to remove makeup and impurities from your skin.
  • Apply a moisturizer to your skin daily to keep it soft and supple.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Get a regular trim to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and choosing the right makeup colors for your skin tone, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance.
