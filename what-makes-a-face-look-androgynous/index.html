What Makes A Face Look Androgynous

Written by Eveline Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Androgyny is a term used to describe individuals who display both masculine and feminine physical characteristics. An androgynous look has become increasingly popular in recent years, with many people opting for a gender-neutral appearance. In this article, we will explore what makes a face look androgynous, including the features and makeup techniques that can help you achieve this look.

What Makes a Face Look Androgynous?

The androgynous look is all about balance. It's about finding the perfect blend of masculine and feminine features to create a gender-neutral appearance. Some of the key features that make a face look androgynous include:

  • High cheekbones: Both men and women tend to have high cheekbones, making them a key feature in achieving an androgynous look.
  • Soft jawline: A softer jawline can make the face look more feminine, while a stronger, more angular jawline can make it look more masculine.
  • Full lips: Full lips are often associated with femininity, but they can also be a key feature in achieving an androgynous look.
  • Straight eyebrows: Straight eyebrows are considered more masculine, while arched eyebrows are considered more feminine. Achieving a straight brow can help create a more androgynous look.
  • Small nose: A smaller nose can help create a more feminine appearance, while a larger nose can create a more masculine appearance.

Makeup Techniques for an Androgynous Look

Makeup can also play a key role in achieving an androgynous look. Here are some makeup techniques to consider:

  • Contouring: Contouring can be used to create a more masculine or feminine appearance, depending on where the contour is applied. For example, applying contour to the jawline can create a more masculine appearance, while applying it to the cheeks can create a more feminine appearance.
  • Foundation: Choosing the right foundation shade is important in creating an androgynous look. A shade that is too light or too dark can create a more feminine or masculine appearance, respectively. Opt for a shade that matches your skin tone.
  • Eyebrows: As mentioned earlier, achieving a straight brow can help create a more androgynous look. Use an eyebrow pencil to fill in any gaps and create a straighter shape.
  • Lipstick: Choosing a neutral lipstick can help create a gender-neutral appearance. Avoid shades that are too bold or too light.

Step-by-Step Guide to Achieving an Androgynous Look

Now that we've covered the features and makeup techniques that can help you achieve an androgynous look, here's a step-by-step guide to get you started:

  1. Start with a clean, moisturized face.
  2. Apply foundation, making sure to choose a shade that matches your skin tone.
  3. Use an eyebrow pencil to fill in any gaps and create a straighter brow shape.
  4. Apply contour to the areas you want to create a more masculine or feminine appearance.
  5. Apply a neutral lipstick to create a gender-neutral appearance.
  6. Finish with a setting spray to make your makeup last all day.

Tips for Maintaining an Androgynous Look

Once you've achieved your desired androgynous look, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

  • Stay hydrated: Drinking plenty of water can help keep your skin looking healthy and glowing.
  • Moisturize: Moisturizing regularly can help keep your skin looking smooth and hydrated.
  • Trim your eyebrows: Keeping your eyebrows trimmed and neat can help maintain a straighter brow shape.
  • Get regular haircuts: Keeping your hair short and styled in a gender-neutral way can help maintain your androgynous look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about achieving an androgynous look:

Can anyone achieve an androgynous look?

Yes, anyone can achieve an androgynous look regardless of their gender identity.

Do I have to wear makeup to achieve an androgynous look?

No, makeup is not necessary to achieve an androgynous look. It is simply a tool that can help you achieve your desired appearance.

Will an androgynous look work for my face shape?

Yes, an androgynous look can work for any face shape. It's all about finding the right balance of masculine and feminine features.

Hair Care for an Androgynous Look

When it comes to achieving an androgynous look, hair is just as important as makeup and features. Here are some tips for maintaining an androgynous hairstyle:

  • Choose a gender-neutral hairstyle: Opt for a hairstyle that is not too masculine or too feminine.
  • Keep your hair short: Short hair is often associated with a more masculine appearance, making it a popular choice for those looking to achieve an androgynous look.
  • Experiment with color: Choosing a bold hair color can help create a more gender-neutral appearance.
  • Maintain your hair: Regular haircuts and conditioning treatments can help keep your hair looking healthy and styled.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve an androgynous look that is unique and gender-neutral. Remember, it's all about finding the right balance of masculine and feminine features to create a look that works for you.
