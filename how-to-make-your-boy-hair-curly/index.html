How To Make Your Boy Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curly hair is a popular trend among boys. It adds a fun and playful element to their hair and makes them stand out from the crowd. If your boy has straight hair and you want to give him a curly look, don't worry, it's not as difficult as you might think. In this article, we'll show you how to make your boy hair curly in a relaxed English language.

How to Make Your Boy Hair Curly

Before we get started, it's important to note that there are different methods of making boys' hair curly. Some methods involve the use of heat, while others require you to leave your hair overnight. In this article, we'll focus on the most popular and easiest way to make your boy hair curly â€“ using hair rollers.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to make your boy hair curly:

  1. Start by washing your boy's hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep his hair hydrated and healthy.
  2. Blow-dry his hair until it's about 80% dry. You don't want to use rollers on wet hair as it can damage the hair.
  3. Apply a small amount of curl-enhancing cream to his hair. This will help to define the curls and hold them in place.
  4. Divide his hair into sections and roll each section around a hair roller. Make sure to roll the hair in the direction you want the curls to go.
  5. Leave the rollers in his hair for at least an hour or overnight if you want more defined curls.
  6. Gently remove the rollers from his hair and use your fingers to separate the curls.
  7. Finish by applying a small amount of hairspray to keep the curls in place.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when making your boy hair curly:

  • Use a curl-enhancing cream that's specifically designed for boys' hair.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh down the hair and prevent the curls from forming.
  • Use smaller rollers for tighter curls and larger rollers for looser curls.
  • Don't brush or comb the curls as it can cause frizz.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making boy hair curly:

Can you make straight hair curly?

Yes, you can make straight hair curly using different methods such as using hair rollers, curling irons, or braiding the hair.

How long does it take to make boy hair curly?

It takes about an hour to make boy hair curly using hair rollers. However, if you want more defined curls, you can leave the rollers in overnight.

How long do the curls last?

The curls can last up to 2-3 days depending on how well you take care of them.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your boy's hair to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some tips:

  • Wash his hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers and curling irons too often as they can damage the hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep his hair hydrated.
  • Trim his hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your boy hair curly, it's time to try it out. Remember to follow the steps and tips to get the best results. Have fun and enjoy the new curly look!
