How To Straighten Thick Wavy Short Hair

Written by Robby Aug 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with unruly, wavy hair? Do you want to achieve a sleek, straight look? If so, this guide is for you! We'll take you through the steps to straighten your thick, wavy, short hair, and give you tips and tricks along the way. Let's get started!

  • Step by Step

Description

Thick, wavy hair can be difficult to manage, especially if you're looking to achieve a straight hairstyle. However, with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, straight look that will last all day.

Before you begin, it's important to choose the right straightening tool for your hair type. For thick, wavy hair, a flat iron with wide plates is the best option. This will help to evenly distribute heat and straighten your hair more efficiently.

How to Straighten Thick Wavy Short Hair

Follow these steps to straighten your thick, wavy short hair:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help hydrate your hair and prepare it for straightening. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from heat damage caused by the straightening tool.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair. Use a round brush to help smooth out your hair as you dry it. This will help to reduce frizz and make it easier to straighten your hair later.

Step 4: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties. This will help you to straighten your hair more efficiently and evenly.

Step 5: Straighten Your Hair

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, use your flat iron to straighten your hair. Clamp the flat iron down on a small section of your hair, and slowly pull it down to the ends of your hair. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair until your entire head is straight.

Step 6: Finish with Serum

Apply a finishing serum to your hair to add shine and reduce frizz. This will help to keep your hair looking sleek and straight throughout the day.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when straightening your thick, wavy short hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before straightening.
  • Don't straighten your hair when it's wet. This can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a flat iron with adjustable heat settings to avoid overheating your hair.
  • Take breaks between sections to avoid over-straightening your hair.
  • Use a finishing spray or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

FAQ

Can I straighten my hair without heat?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without heat. One option is to use a straightening brush, which uses heatless technology to straighten your hair. You can also use rollers or a hair straightening cream to achieve a sleek, straight look.

How often should I straighten my hair?

You should avoid straightening your hair every day, as this can cause damage to your hair. Instead, try to straighten your hair no more than once or twice a week, and use a heat protectant to help reduce damage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to achieving and maintaining healthy, straight hair. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to help hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or dyes on your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a sleek, straight look for your thick, wavy short hair. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best!
