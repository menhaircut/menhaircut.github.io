How To Cut Your Hair Short At Home A Guide For Men

Written by Oliver Aug 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to cut your hair short at home, you're not alone. Many men are taking matters into their own hands and trimming their own hair. But, before you pick up those clippers, it's important to know how to do it properly to avoid any mishaps. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps to achieve a great short haircut at home.

Description:

Cutting your own hair can be a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be a great experience. This guide will show you how to cut your hair short at home, providing you with tips, tricks, and the essential tools you'll need. Whether you're looking to save money or just want to try something new, this guide will help you achieve a great short haircut.

How to Cut Your Hair Short at Home: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting your hair short at home is to gather the necessary tools. You'll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure your clippers are sharp and your scissors are clean and sharp. You may also want to invest in a cape or towel to drape over your shoulders to catch any hair.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, it's important to wash it thoroughly. This will remove any dirt, oil, or product buildup that may interfere with the cutting process. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

Step 3: Determine Your Desired Length

Decide on the length you want your hair to be. It's a good idea to start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter. Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top slightly longer. Use scissors to trim the hair on top to your desired length.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Begin by using the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using the comb to lift the hair as you go. Move the clippers in an upward motion, against the direction of hair growth, to achieve an even cut. Use the scissors to trim the hair on top to your desired length.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Throughout the cutting process, check your progress in the mirror. Make sure both sides are even and that the hair on top is the desired length. Take your time and make small adjustments as needed.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair Short at Home

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great short haircut:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually work your way shorter.
  • Use the clippers to cut the hair on the sides and back of your head, leaving the hair on top slightly longer.
  • Use scissors to trim the hair on top to your desired length.
  • Check your progress in the mirror throughout the cutting process.
  • Take your time and make small adjustments as needed.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair Short at Home

After cutting your hair short at home, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type, and avoid using too much product. Visit a professional stylist every few months for a trim to keep your hair looking its best.
