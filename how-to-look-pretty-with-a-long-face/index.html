How To Look Pretty With A Long Face

Written by William Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on how to look pretty with a long face. If you have a long face, it can be challenging to find the right hairstyle and makeup that enhances your features. However, with the right tips and tricks, you can accentuate your natural beauty and look gorgeous every day. In this article, we will share with you some easy steps and hair care tips to help you look your best.

Table of Contents

  • How to Look Pretty with a Long Face
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Step by Step

Here are some steps to follow to look pretty with a long face:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle and makeup, it's essential to determine your face shape. A long face is typically longer than it is wide, with a prominent forehead and narrow chin. Knowing your face shape will help you choose the right style to flatter your features.

Step 2: Find the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make all the difference in enhancing your features. For a long face, look for hairstyles that add width to your face, such as layered cuts or bangs. Avoid long straight hairstyles that can make your face appear longer.

Step 3: Enhance Your Eyes and Lips

When it comes to makeup, enhance your eyes and lips to draw attention away from your forehead. Play up your eyes with dark eyeliner and mascara, and add a pop of color to your lips with a bold lipstick or gloss.

Step 4: Contour Your Face

Contouring is an excellent way to add definition to your face and create a more balanced look. Use a contouring powder or cream to shade the areas you want to recede, such as your forehead and chin, and highlight the areas you want to bring forward, such as your cheekbones.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you look pretty with a long face:

  • Choose hairstyles that create volume and add width to your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight.
  • Play up your eyes and lips with makeup.
  • Use contouring to create a more balanced look.
  • Accessorize with statement jewelry or scarves to draw attention away from your forehead.

FAQ

What Are the Best Hairstyles for a Long Face?

The best hairstyles for a long face are those that add width and volume to your face, such as layered cuts, textured bobs, and side-swept bangs.

What Makeup Looks Best on a Long Face?

When it comes to makeup, focus on enhancing your eyes and lips to draw attention away from your forehead. Use dark eyeliner and mascara to play up your eyes and add a pop of color to your lips with a bold lipstick or gloss.

How Can I Make My Face Look Less Long?

You can make your face look less long by choosing hairstyles that add width and volume to your face, using contouring to create a more balanced look, and accessorizing with statement jewelry or scarves to draw attention away from your forehead.

Hair Care

Lastly, it's essential to take good care of your hair to look your best. Here are some hair care tips for long faces:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as flat irons and curling irons.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type.

With these tips and tricks, you can look pretty with a long face and feel confident in your own skin. Remember to choose hairstyles and makeup that flatter your features, and take good care of your hair to look your best every day.
