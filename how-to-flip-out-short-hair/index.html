How To Flip Out Short Hair The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 18, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of your short hair looking flat and lifeless? Do you want to add some volume and texture to your locks? If so, then flipping out your short hair may be the perfect solution for you! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to flip out short hair, as well as some tips and tricks to make the process easier. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Flip Out Short Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Flip Out Short Hair

Flipping out short hair involves curling the ends of your hair away from your face, creating a flipped-out effect. Here are some tools you will need:

  • Heat protectant spray
  • Curling iron
  • Clips
  • Hairspray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to flip out your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Divide your hair into sections and clip them up.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron, curling away from your face.
  4. Hold for a few seconds and release.
  5. Repeat with the remaining sections of hair.
  6. Once all sections are curled, flip your head upside down and shake out the curls.
  7. Flip your head back up and use your fingers to separate and shape the curls as desired.
  8. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style.

Tips and Tricks

To achieve the best results when flipping out short hair, here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel size (around 1 inch) to create tight curls.
  • Don't curl too much hair at once to ensure that each section gets properly heated.
  • Hold the curling iron vertically and wrap the hair around it, rather than using the clamp.
  • For more volume, curl your hair in different directions and alternate between flipping the ends in and out.
  • Use clips to keep your hair out of the way while you curl.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about flipping out short hair:

Q: Can I flip out my short hair without using heat?

A: Yes, you can use a styling product like mousse or gel to scrunch your hair and create a flipped-out effect without using heat.

Q: Will flipping out my short hair damage it?

A: Curling your hair with heat can cause damage over time, but using a heat protectant spray and not curling your hair too often can help minimize the damage.

Q: How long will my flipped-out short hair last?

A: The duration of your flipped-out style will depend on your hair type, the products you use, and the weather conditions. Generally, it should last for a day or two.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your short hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair every few months to get rid of split ends.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair, as it can cause breakage.

By following these tips and using the right tools, you can easily flip out your short hair and achieve a bouncy, voluminous look. So, go ahead and try it out for yourself!
