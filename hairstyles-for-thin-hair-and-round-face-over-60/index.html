Hairstyles For Thin Hair And Round Face Over 60

Written by Oliver Jul 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 60 and have thin hair and a round face, finding the right hairstyle can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this post, we'll share some hairstyles that will flatter your face shape and make your hair look fuller and thicker. We'll also give you some tips on how to care for your hair and keep it healthy.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Products for Thin Hair

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a round face with thin hair, the goal is to create the illusion of volume and length. Hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous can make your face look wider, so it's important to find a balance.

Short Hairstyles

Short hairstyles like pixie cuts and bobs can work well for round faces with thin hair. These hairstyles add volume to the top of the head and make the face appear longer. However, be careful not to go too short, as this can make your face look wider.

Medium-Length Hairstyles

Medium-length hairstyles like shags and layered cuts can also work well for round faces with thin hair. These hairstyles create movement and texture, which can make your hair look fuller. Again, be careful not to add too much volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look wider.

Long Hairstyles

Long hairstyles like beachy waves and loose curls can also be flattering for round faces with thin hair. These hairstyles add length to the face and create a soft, feminine look. However, be careful not to add too much volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you've chosen a hairstyle, it's time to learn how to style it. Follow these steps to create a voluminous, flattering look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume to the top of your head.
  4. Use a curling iron or hot rollers to create loose curls or waves.
  5. Finish with a volumizing hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

Healthy hair is the key to any great hairstyle, so it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking its best:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it hydrated and soft.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

FAQs

What are the best hairstyles for a round face with thin hair?

The best hairstyles for a round face with thin hair are short or medium-length cuts with volume at the top of the head. Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the sides of your head, as this can make your face look wider.

How can I make my thin hair look thicker?

You can make your thin hair look thicker by using volumizing products like mousse or spray, blow drying your hair with a round brush, and using hot rollers or a curling iron to create loose curls or waves.

What should I avoid when styling my hair?

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Also, avoid using products that contain sulfates, as they can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care Products for Thin Hair

If you have thin hair, it's important to use hair care products that will add volume and thickness to your hair. Here are some products to consider:

  • Volumizing mousse or spray
  • Root lifting spray
  • Dry shampoo
  • Volumizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Hair thickening serum

With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best at any age. Embrace your natural beauty and experiment with different hairstyles until you find the one that works for you!
