Best Haircuts For Big Round Faces Flattering Styles For 2023

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircuts for big round faces, it's important to determine your face shape. This can help you choose the most flattering style for your features. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back.
  2. Use a measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  3. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are roughly the same width, you have a round face shape.

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start looking for the best haircuts for big round faces.

Best Haircuts for Big Round Faces

When it comes to haircuts for big round faces, the goal is to create the illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved through a variety of styles, including:

Long Layers

Long layers can help to elongate the face and create the illusion of angles. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that start below the chin. You can also add side-swept bangs to further elongate the face.

Asymmetrical Bob

An asymmetrical bob can help to balance out a round face shape. This style features a shorter length on one side and a longer length on the other. This creates the illusion of angles and helps to elongate the face.

Side Part

A side part can help to create the illusion of length and angles. To achieve this look, simply part your hair to one side and add some volume at the crown of your head. This will help to elongate the face and create a more angular look.

Layered Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a shorter style, a layered pixie cut can be a great option for a round face shape. This style features longer layers on top and shorter layers on the sides, which helps to create the illusion of length and angles.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves can help to soften a round face shape and create a more relaxed look. To achieve this look, use a curling iron to create loose waves throughout your hair. You can also add some texture spray to give your hair a more beachy feel.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your big round face, it's important to know how to style it to flatter your features. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Add volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of length.
  • Use a side part to create the illusion of angles.
  • Avoid blunt bangs, as they can make a round face shape appear even rounder.
  • Use a round brush to add volume and create a more angular look.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create depth and dimension.

FAQs About Haircuts for Big Round Faces

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face shape?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face shape. Just be sure to add some volume at the crown of your head to create the illusion of length.

Q: Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face shape?

A: Not necessarily. Short haircuts can be flattering on a round face shape if they're styled correctly. Consider a layered pixie cut or an asymmetrical bob to create the illusion of length and angles.

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a round face shape?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear bangs if you have a round face shape. Just be sure to avoid blunt bangs, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs or wispy bangs to create the illusion of angles.

Hair Care Tips for Round Faces

When it comes to hair care for round faces, it's important to keep your hair healthy and well-nourished. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and choosing the right haircut for your big round face, you can create a flattering and stylish look that highlights your features. Whether you prefer long layers or a short pixie cut, there's a style out there that's perfect for you.
