How Do You Curl Your Hair With A Lange Straightener

Written by William Jul 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of using a curling iron to achieve perfect curls? Have you ever tried using a straightener to curl your hair? If not, you're missing out on a quick and easy way to get flawless curls without damaging your hair. In this article, we will go through step-by-step on how to curl your hair with a Lange straightener.

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

The first thing you need to do is select the correct straightener. We recommend using the Lange Le Vite Straightener as it has a curved edge that allows you to curl your hair easily. You also need to make sure your hair is clean and dry, and you have applied heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Next, section your hair. It's easier to work with small sections rather than large ones. Use hair clips to hold the rest of your hair out of the way while you work on one section at a time.

When you're ready to start curling, take a small section of hair and clamp the straightener at the root of your hair. Twist the straightener away from your face, making sure to keep the hair in the straightener as you go.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to curl your hair with a Lange straightener:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply heat protectant spray.
  3. Section your hair.
  4. Take a small section of hair.
  5. Clamp the straightener at the root of your hair.
  6. Twist the straightener away from your face.
  7. Keep the hair in the straightener as you go.
  8. Hold for a few seconds.
  9. Release the hair from the straightener.
  10. Repeat until all sections are curled.

Tips

Here are some tips to achieve the perfect curls:

  • Use small sections of hair to achieve tighter curls.
  • Use larger sections of hair to achieve looser curls.
  • Twist the straightener away from your face for a natural look.
  • Twist the straightener towards your face for a more dramatic look.
  • Don't hold the hair in the straightener for too long as it can damage your hair.
  • Apply hairspray to set the curls.

FAQ

Can I use any straightener to curl my hair?

No, we recommend using a straightener that has a curved edge as it makes it easier to curl your hair.

How long should I hold the hair in the straightener?

Hold the hair in the straightener for a few seconds. Don't hold it for too long as it can damage your hair.

What can I do if my curls don't hold?

Apply hairspray to set the curls. You can also try using a higher heat setting.

Hair Care

Remember to always use heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to avoid damaging your hair. Don't use the straightener on wet hair as it can cause damage. Make sure to also use a conditioning treatment to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.

Curling your hair with a Lange straightener can give you beautiful, effortless curls in no time. Follow our step-by-step guide and tips to achieve perfect curls every time. Remember to take care of your hair by using heat protectant spray and conditioning treatments. Happy curling!
