How To Get Hair To Grow Back From Tension Alopecia

Written by Christine Aug 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Tension alopecia is a condition caused by prolonged tension and pulling on the hair follicles. This can occur due to tight braids, weaves, or ponytails, and can result in hair loss and bald spots. If you are suffering from tension alopecia, you may be wondering how to get your hair to grow back. In this article, we will discuss some tips and techniques for promoting hair growth and reversing the effects of tension alopecia.

Description

Tension alopecia is a type of hair loss that occurs when the hair follicles are subjected to prolonged tension and pulling. This can happen due to tight hairstyles, such as braids, weaves, or ponytails, and can lead to hair loss and bald spots. If left untreated, tension alopecia can become permanent, so it is important to take steps to promote hair growth and prevent further damage to the follicles.

How to

Step 1: Identify and Avoid Tight Hairstyles

The first step in treating tension alopecia is to identify and avoid any tight hairstyles that may be causing the problem. This may involve removing braids, weaves, or extensions that are pulling on your hair, or simply loosening your ponytail or bun. Be sure to give your hair a break from these styles and allow your scalp to rest for a few days.

Step 2: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can help to increase blood flow to the hair follicles, which can promote hair growth. Try massaging your scalp for a few minutes each day, using circular motions and gentle pressure. You can also use a scalp massager or a soft-bristled brush to help stimulate the follicles.

Step 3: Use Essential Oils

Essential oils, such as lavender, peppermint, and rosemary, can help to promote hair growth and reduce inflammation in the scalp. You can mix a few drops of these oils with a carrier oil, such as coconut or olive oil, and apply the mixture to your scalp. Leave it on for a few hours or overnight before washing it out.

Step 4: Take Supplements

Supplements, such as biotin and vitamin E, can help to promote hair growth and improve the health of your hair follicles. Be sure to speak with your doctor before starting any new supplements, as they may interact with other medications you are taking.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for promoting hair growth and preventing tension alopecia:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as relaxers or dyes
  • Eat a healthy diet, rich in protein and vitamins
  • Get enough sleep and reduce stress levels
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage

FAQ

What causes tension alopecia?

Tension alopecia is caused by prolonged tension and pulling on the hair follicles, often due to tight hairstyles such as braids, weaves, or ponytails.

Is tension alopecia permanent?

If left untreated, tension alopecia can become permanent. However, with proper treatment and care, it is possible to reverse the effects and promote hair growth.

Can essential oils help to promote hair growth?

Yes, essential oils such as lavender, peppermint, and rosemary can help to promote hair growth and reduce inflammation in the scalp.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for promoting hair growth and preventing tension alopecia. Be sure to avoid tight hairstyles and harsh chemicals, and to take steps to protect your hair from heat and sun damage. Eat a healthy diet, get enough sleep, and reduce stress levels to promote overall hair health. With proper care and treatment, you can reverse the effects of tension alopecia and enjoy healthy, beautiful hair once again.
