How Many Mm Is A Number 2 Cut

Written by Frank Jun 20, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you planning to get a number 2 haircut, but not sure how long it will be? Well, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss how many mm is a number 2 cut and everything else you need to know about this popular haircut.

Description

A number 2 cut is a clipper cut that leaves hair 1/4 inch or 6.35 mm in length. It is a popular choice for men who want a short, neat, and low-maintenance haircut. The number 2 clipper guard is usually used in the sides and back, while the top is left longer and styled as per preference.

How To

If you are planning to get a number 2 cut, you can either do it yourself or ask a professional barber to do it for you. If you are doing it yourself, you will need a clipper with a number 2 guard, a mirror, and a comb. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Prepare

Start by preparing the hair. Wash it thoroughly and dry it completely. Comb the hair in the direction it grows.

Step 2: Attach the Guard

Attach the number 2 guard to your clipper. Make sure it is securely attached.

Step 3: Start Clipping

Start clipping from the bottom of the head and move upwards. Use the comb to lift the hair and clip it. Make sure the clipper is flat against the head to ensure an even cut. Continue clipping until you have covered the sides and back of the head.

Step 4: Blend the Top

Blend the top of the head with scissors or clippers without any guard. If you want to keep the length of the top, you can simply trim the ends.

Tips

Here are some tips that can help you achieve the perfect number 2 cut:

  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while clipping.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and clip slowly to ensure an even cut.
  • If you are not sure how to do it, ask a professional barber for help.
  • Maintain the haircut by trimming it every 2-3 weeks.

FAQ

1. Can I use a number 2 guard on long hair?

No, a number 2 guard is designed for short hair only. If you have long hair, you will need to cut it first before using a number 2 guard.

2. How long does a number 2 cut last?

A number 2 cut can last up to 2-3 weeks before it starts to grow out.

3. Can I style my hair after a number 2 cut?

Yes, you can style your hair after a number 2 cut. The top of the hair is usually left longer, which gives you the freedom to style it in different ways.

Hair Care

After getting a number 2 cut, it is essential to take good care of your hair to maintain its look and health. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, such as blow dryers and hot irons.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its length and shape.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as hair dyes and bleach.

Now that you know how many mm is a number 2 cut, you can confidently ask your barber for this popular haircut. It is a classic style that looks good on almost everyone and is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut.
