How To Make Your Hair Naturally Curly Out Of The Shower

Written by Petter Oct 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of using hot tools to curl your hair? Do you want to achieve natural curls without damaging your hair? If yes, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss how to make your hair naturally curly out of the shower. We will provide you with step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips to help you achieve the perfect curls.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

The first step to achieve natural curls is to start with clean hair. Make sure to wash your hair thoroughly and use a conditioner to make your hair smooth and soft. After washing your hair, gently towel-dry it to avoid damaging your hair.

Next, apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair. You can use a curl cream or a styling gel to define your curls. Make sure to apply the product evenly throughout your hair.

Once you have applied the product, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to distribute the product evenly and prevent any tangles.

After you have combed your hair, scrunch your hair gently with your hands to enhance your curls. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve natural curls out of the shower:

  1. Wash your hair thoroughly and use a conditioner to make your hair smooth and soft.
  2. Towel-dry your hair gently to avoid damaging your hair.
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  5. Scrunch your hair gently with your hands to enhance your curls.
  6. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

Tips

  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel to avoid frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it is drying to prevent frizz.
  • Sleep with your hair in a pineapple or a loose bun to maintain your curls overnight.
  • Use a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve natural curls without using any products?

A: It is possible to achieve natural curls without using any products, but using a curl-enhancing product can help to define your curls and make them last longer.

Q: Can I air-dry my hair instead of using a diffuser attachment?

A: Yes, you can air-dry your hair, but using a diffuser attachment can help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.

Hair Care

To maintain healthy and beautiful curls, it is important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips, you can achieve natural curls out of the shower and maintain healthy and beautiful hair.
