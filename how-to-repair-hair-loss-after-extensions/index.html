How To Repair Hair Loss After Extensions

Written by Luffy Oct 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you experiencing hair loss after getting hair extensions? You’re not alone. Many women have experienced hair loss after getting hair extensions. The good news is that hair loss from extensions can be repaired. In this article, we’ll show you how to repair hair loss after extensions and restore your hair’s health.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Repair Hair Loss After Extensions
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Hair extensions are a popular way to add length and volume to hair, but they can cause hair loss if not installed or maintained properly. The weight of the extensions, combined with the constant pulling and tugging, can cause tension alopecia, which is hair loss caused by traction on the hair follicles.

Other factors that can contribute to hair loss after extensions include improper installation, using the wrong type of extensions for your hair type, leaving extensions in for too long, and not taking care of your hair while you have extensions.

How to Repair Hair Loss After Extensions

The first step to repairing hair loss after extensions is to remove the extensions. If the extensions were installed improperly or left in for too long, they may have caused damage to your natural hair. Removing the extensions will allow your hair to breathe and begin the healing process.

Once the extensions are removed, you can begin repairing the damage. One of the best ways to repair hair loss after extensions is to use a hair growth serum. Look for a serum that contains ingredients like biotin, keratin, and castor oil, which can help to promote hair growth and strengthen your hair.

You should also make sure to keep your scalp clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free from sulfates and parabens. Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood flow and stimulate hair growth.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to repair hair loss after extensions:

  1. Remove the extensions.
  2. Use a hair growth serum.
  3. Keep your scalp clean and healthy.
  4. Massage your scalp regularly.
  5. Avoid using heat styling tools.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help repair hair loss after extensions:

  • Be patient. It can take time for your hair to fully recover.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can cause tension on your hair follicles.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and nutrients that promote hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement that contains biotin, vitamin D, and other hair-healthy nutrients.

FAQ

How long does it take for hair to grow back after extensions?

It can take several months for hair to grow back after extensions. The exact amount of time will depend on how much damage was done to your natural hair.

Can hair loss from extensions be permanent?

In most cases, hair loss from extensions is not permanent. With proper care and treatment, your hair should grow back.

Can I get extensions again after experiencing hair loss?

If you’ve experienced hair loss from extensions, it’s important to give your hair time to fully recover before getting extensions again. Make sure to choose a reputable stylist who can properly install and maintain your extensions.

Hair Care

One of the most important things you can do to prevent hair loss after extensions is to take care of your hair. Avoid using heat styling tools, and make sure to use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is free from sulfates and parabens. Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood flow and stimulate hair growth.

If you’re considering getting extensions again, make sure to choose a reputable stylist who can properly install and maintain your extensions. With the right care and treatment, you can repair hair loss after extensions and restore your hair’s health.
