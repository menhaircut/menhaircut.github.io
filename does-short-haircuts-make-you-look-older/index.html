Does Short Haircuts Make You Look Older

Written by Bardi Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short haircuts are popular among women of all ages. However, there is a common misconception that short haircuts make you look older. In this blog post, we will explore whether or not short haircuts make you look older and provide tips on how to style short hair to look youthful and fresh.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Style Short Hair
  • Tips to Keep Your Short Hair Looking Fresh
  • FAQ

How to Style Short Hair to Look Younger

Short haircuts come in various styles, such as pixie cuts, bobs, and lobs. To avoid looking older with a short haircut, it is essential to choose a style that flatters your face shape and features. Consider a soft layered bob or a textured pixie cut to add movement and dimension to your hair.

Another way to style short hair to look younger is to add volume to the crown of your head. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to lift the roots and create a fuller look. You can also add texture with a sea salt spray or a texturizing paste to give your hair a messy, effortless look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Style Short Hair

Here is a step-by-step guide to style short hair to look younger:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots and crown of your head.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and lift to the roots.
  4. If you have a bob or lob, use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  5. If you have a pixie cut, use a texturizing paste or wax to add definition and texture to your hair.
  6. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips to Keep Your Short Hair Looking Fresh

Short hair requires regular maintenance to keep it looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips to keep your short hair looking its best:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes and keep your hair looking clean and fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Invest in high-quality styling tools, such as a good hair dryer, curling iron, and straightener.

FAQ

Does short hair make you look older?

Short hair does not necessarily make you look older. It all depends on the style and how it flatters your face shape and features. Choosing the right short haircut can actually make you look younger and more youthful.

What is the best short haircut to look younger?

The best short haircut to look younger is one that adds volume, texture, and movement to your hair. Consider a soft layered bob, a textured pixie cut, or a wavy lob to give your hair a youthful, fresh look.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different hair care routine than long hair. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

In conclusion, short haircuts do not necessarily make you look older. It all depends on the style and how it flatters your face shape and features. With the right haircut and styling techniques, short hair can actually make you look younger and more youthful.
