How To Style Short Hair With Bangs Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Christine Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for fun and stylish ways to style your short hair with bangs? Look no further because we got you covered. Whether you have straight, curly or wavy hair, we’ve got the perfect styling tips for you. In this article, we will explore how to style short hair with bangs, give you step-by-step instructions, and provide some tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking fabulous.

Table of Contents:

  • How to Style Short Hair with Bangs
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Styling Short Hair with Bangs
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair with Bangs

How to Style Short Hair with Bangs

Short hair with bangs is a great look that can be styled in many different ways. Here are some popular styles to try:

1. Straight and Sleek

This style is perfect for those who prefer a polished and sophisticated look. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and bangs, and finish with a shine serum or spray for added shine.

2. Wavy and Tousled

If you have naturally wavy hair, embrace it and add some texture to your bangs. Use a curling iron to create loose waves, and use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

3. Curly and Defined

If you have curly hair, define your curls with a curl-enhancing product and let your bangs frame your face. This look is perfect for a fun and playful vibe.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to style your short hair with bangs:

1. Prep your hair

Wash and condition your hair, and apply a heat protectant if you plan on using hot tools. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume.

2. Style your bangs

Use a flat iron or curling iron to style your bangs according to the look you want to achieve. Use a hairspray or texturizing spray to hold your bangs in place.

3. Style the rest of your hair

Use a flat iron, curling iron or texturizing spray to style the rest of your hair. Finish with a hairspray or shine spray for added hold and shine.

Tips for Styling Short Hair with Bangs

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling short hair with bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant to prevent heat damage
  • Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and bounce
  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold
  • Use a hairspray or shine spray to finish your look
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best

FAQs

1. Can I style my bangs differently every day?

Yes, of course! Short hair with bangs is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

2. How often should I trim my bangs?

It depends on your hair growth rate and how fast your bangs grow. Most people need to trim their bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care for Short Hair with Bangs

Short hair with bangs requires regular trimming and maintenance to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape and length
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent drying it out

Now that you know how to style short hair with bangs, you can rock this look with confidence. Experiment with different styles, keep your hair healthy and moisturized, and don’t be afraid to try something new.
