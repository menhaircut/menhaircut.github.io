How Can I Tell If A Hairstyle Will Look Good On Me

Written by Luffy Jun 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Choosing a new hairstyle can be exciting, but it can also be nerve-wracking. After all, you don't want to end up with a cut or color that doesn't suit you. So, how can you tell if a hairstyle will look good on you? Here are some tips to help you make the right decision.

1. Consider your face shape

One of the most important things to consider when choosing a hairstyle is your face shape. Different styles will suit different face shapes, so it's important to choose one that complements your features. For example, if you have a round face, a bob or a long, layered cut can help to elongate your face.

2. Look at your hair texture

Your hair texture can also play a big role in determining which hairstyle will suit you best. If you have fine hair, for example, a short, layered cut can help to add volume and texture. On the other hand, if you have thick, curly hair, a long, layered cut can help to tame your curls and make them more manageable.

3. Consider your lifestyle

When choosing a new hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you're someone who's always on the go and doesn't have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance cut like a pixie or a lob might be a good choice. On the other hand, if you have more time to spend on your hair, a more intricate style like a braid or an updo might be a better option.

4. Try on wigs or use virtual try-ons

If you're still not sure which hairstyle will look good on you, there are a few things you can do to try them out before committing to a cut. One option is to try on wigs or use virtual try-ons, which can give you a good idea of what different styles will look like on you.

5. Ask for advice

Finally, don't be afraid to ask for advice from your hairstylist or friends and family members. They can offer valuable insights and help you make the right decision.

Hair Care Tips

Once you've chosen your new hairstyle, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips to help you maintain your new look:

1. Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help to keep it clean and healthy. However, be careful not to over-wash, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and leave it looking dry and brittle.

2. Use the right products

Using the right products can also make a big difference in the health and appearance of your hair. Choose products that are tailored to your hair type and needs, and avoid using too many styling products, which can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

3. Protect your hair from heat

If you use heat styling tools like straighteners or curling irons, be sure to protect your hair with a heat protectant spray or serum. This can help to prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

4. Get regular trims

Regular trims can help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking neat and tidy. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks, depending on how quickly your hair grows.

FAQ

What if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you're not happy with your new hairstyle, don't panic! Hair grows back, and there are always ways to fix a bad cut or color. Talk to your hairstylist about what you don't like and see if they can make any adjustments.

What if I can't decide on a hairstyle?

If you're struggling to decide on a hairstyle, try to narrow down your options by considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. You can also try on wigs or use virtual try-ons to see what different styles will look like on you.

How often should I wash my hair?

How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have fine hair, you may need to wash it every day to keep it looking clean and fresh. If you have thick or curly hair, you may be able to go a few days between washes.

What can I do to make my hair grow faster?

There are a few things you can do to help your hair grow faster, such as eating a healthy diet, taking supplements like biotin or vitamin D, and avoiding heat styling tools and harsh chemicals.

Conclusion

Choosing a new hairstyle can be a fun and exciting experience, but it's important to take the time to consider your options and make the right decision. By considering your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle, trying on wigs or using virtual try-ons, and asking for advice from your hairstylist or friends and family members, you can find the perfect hairstyle to suit you.
