How To Style Your Lace Front Wig A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Nov 04, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of struggling with your lace front wig and not knowing how to style it properly? We’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll show you how to style your lace front wig in a few simple steps. We’ll also provide you with some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Your Lace Front Wig
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips and Tricks
  4. FAQ
  5. Hair Care

How to Style Your Lace Front Wig

Before we dive into the step-by-step guide, let’s talk about the tools you’ll need to style your lace front wig:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Heat protectant spray
  • Flat iron or curling iron
  • Hairspray

Now that you have your tools ready, let’s move on to the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Comb Your Wig

Start by combing your wig with a wide-tooth comb to remove any tangles. Be gentle when combing your wig to avoid damaging the lace.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Next, apply a heat protectant spray to your wig to protect it from heat damage. This is especially important if you plan to use a flat iron or curling iron.

Step 3: Style Your Wig

Now it’s time to style your wig. Use a flat iron or curling iron to create the desired look. If you’re using a flat iron, start at the roots and slowly glide the iron down to the ends. If you’re using a curling iron, wrap small sections of hair around the barrel and hold for a few seconds before releasing.

Step 4: Set Your Style with Hairspray

Once you’ve styled your wig, set the look with hairspray. Hold the can about 10 inches away from your wig and spray lightly to avoid making the hair stiff or crunchy.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect look:

  • Use a wig stand to style your wig. This will make it easier to work with and will prevent the wig from getting tangled.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your wig. This can damage the hair and the lace.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling lace front wigs:

Is it okay to use heat on a lace front wig?

Yes, it’s okay to use heat on a lace front wig as long as you use a heat protectant spray and don’t apply too much heat.

How often should I style my lace front wig?

You can style your lace front wig as often as you like, but it’s important to take good care of it to prevent damage.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your lace front wig:

  • Wash your wig regularly with a wig shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid brushing your wig when it’s wet.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or in a wig box to prevent tangling.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to style your lace front wig like a pro in no time. Remember to take good care of your wig to keep it looking great for longer.
