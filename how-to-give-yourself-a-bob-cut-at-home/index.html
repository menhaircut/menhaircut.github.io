How To Give Yourself A Bob Cut At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and looking for a change? A bob cut is a classic and timeless hairstyle that can make you look chic and stylish. However, going to a salon can be expensive, and you might not always get the cut you want. But don't worry! You can give yourself a bob cut at home with the right tools and techniques. In this article, we will guide you through the process of giving yourself a bob cut at home.

How to

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair ties. It's also a good idea to have a mirror, so you can see the back of your head. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and drying it thoroughly. It's easier to cut your hair when it's clean and dry.

Step 2: Divide Your Hair

Divide your hair into two sections, one at the front and one at the back. Use a hair tie to secure the back section.

Step 3: Cut the Back Section

Start by cutting the back section of your hair. Take small sections of hair and cut them straight across. Don't cut too much hair at once, as it can be difficult to fix if you make a mistake. Cut your hair to the desired length, depending on how short you want your bob to be.

Step 4: Cut the Front Section

Once you have cut the back section, release the front section of your hair. Use the back section as a guide, and cut the front section to the same length. Again, take small sections of hair and cut them straight across.

Step 5: Check Your Hair

Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it's even. If you notice any uneven sections, trim them until your hair is even. Don't worry if it's not perfect, as you can always make adjustments later.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you have cut your hair, style it as desired. You can use a straightener or curling iron to add some texture and shape to your bob. Don't be afraid to experiment to find the perfect look for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when giving yourself a bob cut:

  • Start with small sections of hair and cut slowly. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once you've cut it.
  • If you have curly hair, it's best to straighten it before cutting it, as curly hair can shrink when it dries.
  • If you're not confident cutting your hair, ask a friend or family member to help you.
  • Use hair clips to keep sections of hair out of the way when cutting.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving yourself a bob cut:

Can I give myself a bob cut if I have thick hair?

Yes, you can give yourself a bob cut even if you have thick hair. However, it may take longer to cut your hair, as you'll need to cut smaller sections at a time.

Can I give myself a bob cut if I have short hair?

If you already have short hair, it may be difficult to give yourself a bob cut. It's best to ask a professional stylist for help.

How often should I trim my bob cut?

You should trim your bob cut every six to eight weeks to keep it looking neat and fresh.

Hair Care

Once you've given yourself a bob cut, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can damage it. Use a heat protectant spray if you're using a straightener or curling iron.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.

With these tips and techniques, you can give yourself a bob cut at home and look stylish and chic. So, what are you waiting for? Get your scissors and start cutting!
