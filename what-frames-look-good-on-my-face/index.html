What Frames Look Good On My Face

Written by Daniel Sep 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you wear glasses, you know it's important to choose the right frames. Not only do your frames affect your vision, but they can also impact your overall look. But with so many options out there, how do you know what frames look good on your face? In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to choose the perfect frames for your face shape and style.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in choosing the right frames is determining your face shape. Face shapes typically fall into one of a few categories: round, oval, square, heart, or diamond. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Take a look at the overall shape of your face and the angles of your jawline and forehead.

Round faces have soft, curved lines and are roughly the same width and length. Oval faces are longer than they are wide, with balanced proportions. Square faces have a strong jawline and forehead, with a width and length that are roughly equal. Heart-shaped faces have wider foreheads and narrower chins. Diamond-shaped faces are narrow at the forehead and jawline, with wider cheekbones.

Choosing a Frame Style

Once you've determined your face shape, you can start thinking about frame styles that will complement your features. As a general rule, try to choose frames that are the opposite of your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, look for frames that are more angular, like rectangular or square frames. If you have a square face, look for frames with softer, rounded edges, like oval or round frames.

Another thing to consider is your personal style. Do you prefer bold, statement-making frames, or more understated styles? Do you want something classic and timeless, or trendy and of-the-moment? Think about the colors and patterns you typically wear, and look for frames that complement your favorite outfits.

Considering Frame Colors

When it comes to frame colors, the key is to choose something that complements your skin tone and hair color. If you have warm undertones (like yellow or golden), look for frames in warm colors like brown, gold, or tortoise. If you have cool undertones (like pink or blue), look for frames in cool colors like black, silver, or blue.

Of course, you don't have to stick with neutral colors if you don't want to. If you're feeling bold, try a bright, statement-making frame in a color that makes you happy. Just be sure the color complements your skin tone and doesn't clash with your outfit.

Choosing Frame Materials

Frames come in a variety of materials, from plastic to metal to acetate. When choosing a material, consider your lifestyle and personal style. If you're active and need something durable, look for frames made from titanium or stainless steel. If you want something lightweight and comfortable, try a plastic frame. Acetate frames are also a popular option, as they come in a range of colors and patterns and are hypoallergenic.

Matching Frames to Your Lifestyle

Finally, think about your lifestyle when choosing frames. Are you a student who spends a lot of time reading and studying? Look for frames that are comfortable and won't slip down your nose. Are you an athlete who needs glasses for sports? Look for frames that are durable and won't break easily. Are you a professional who wants to look polished and put-together? Look for frames that are classic and timeless.

FAQs

How do I know if my glasses fit correctly?

Your glasses should sit comfortably on your nose and ears, without slipping down or feeling too tight. The frames should also be centered on your face, with the lenses positioned directly in front of your eyes.

Can I wear glasses with any hairstyle?

Absolutely! Glasses can be styled with any hairstyle, whether you wear your hair up or down. Just be sure to choose frames that complement your face shape and personal style.

Can I wear glasses with makeup?

Yes! Just be sure to choose frames that don't hide your makeup or clash with your eyeshadow or lipstick. You may also want to consider using a magnifying mirror to help you apply your makeup more precisely.

Tips for Styling Your Hair with Glasses

If you wear glasses, you know that they can sometimes get in the way of your hairstyle. Here are some tips for styling your hair with glasses:

  • Opt for hairstyles that keep your hair away from your face, like updos or half-up hairstyles.
  • Try a hairstyle with volume at the crown to balance out your frames.
  • Consider adding bangs to soften your look and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands or barrettes, to add some visual interest to your hairstyle.

By following these tips, you can choose the perfect frames for your face shape and style, and feel confident and stylish every time you put them on.
