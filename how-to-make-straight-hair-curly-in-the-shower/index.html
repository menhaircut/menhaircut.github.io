How To Make Straight Hair Curly In The Shower

Written by Petter Nov 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of having straight hair all the time? Do you want to switch up your look and try out some curls? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we will discuss how to make straight hair curly in the shower. From the how-to to the step-by-step process, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How To

The first step in making your straight hair curly in the shower is to use curl-enhancing products. These products can range from curl creams to gels and mousses. Choose a product that works best for your hair type and apply it to damp hair.

Next, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. The diffuser attachment will help to enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Make sure to dry your hair thoroughly before moving on to the next step.

Step by Step

1. Start by shampooing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your damp hair.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair.

4. Scrunch your hair as you dry it to enhance your curls.

5. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or curling wand to touch up any areas that need more definition.

Tips

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before applying any products.

- Try not to touch your hair too much while it's drying as this can lead to frizz.

- Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your curls hydrated and frizz-free.

FAQ

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: This will depend on your hair type and the products you use. On average, curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days.

Q: Can I make my hair curly without using heat?

A: Yes, you can use alternative methods such as braiding your hair while it's damp or using hair rollers.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your curls.

- Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your curls hydrated.

- Avoid using heat styling tools too often as this can damage your hair.

Now that you know how to make straight hair curly in the shower, it's time to try it out for yourself! Remember to take care of your curls and use the right products to keep them looking their best.
