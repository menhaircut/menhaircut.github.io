Find The Best Haircut For Your Face Shape

Written by Petter Aug 11, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of getting a haircut that just doesn't look right on you? It might be because you're not considering your face shape. Finding the best haircut for your face shape can make all the difference in how you look and feel. In this article, we'll show you how to determine your face shape and give you tips on finding the perfect haircut.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the best haircut for your face shape is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes:

  • Round
  • Oval
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Rectangle

To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and:

  • Measure your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline
  • Check the length of your face
  • Identify any unique features, such as a prominent chin or high cheekbones

Once you've identified your face shape, you can start looking for haircuts that will flatter your features.

Haircuts for Round Faces

If you have a round face, you want to choose a haircut that will elongate your face and add definition to your cheekbones. Some good options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • A-line bobs
  • Asymmetrical cuts

Haircuts for Oval Faces

Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, as almost any haircut can look good on them. However, some popular options include:

  • Long layers
  • Blunt bobs
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Wavy or curly hair

Haircuts for Square Faces

If you have a square face, you want to soften your strong jawline and add some roundness to your face. Some good options include:

  • Soft layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Long bobs
  • Curly or wavy hair

Haircuts for Heart Faces

Heart-shaped faces are wider at the forehead and narrow at the chin, so you want to choose a haircut that balances out your features. Some good options include:

  • Long layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • Chin-length bobs
  • Curly or wavy hair

Tips for Choosing a Haircut

When choosing a haircut for your face shape, keep these tips in mind:

  • Consider your hair type (straight, curly, etc.)
  • Think about your lifestyle (how much time do you have to style your hair?)
  • Choose a haircut that complements your personality
  • Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your stylist

FAQ

What if I have a mix of face shapes?

It's common to have a mix of face shapes, so choose a haircut that flatters your most prominent features. For example, if you have a heart-shaped face with a strong jawline, choose a haircut that softens your jawline.

Can I still get a haircut that's not recommended for my face shape?

Of course! These are just guidelines, and ultimately, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and happy.

How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and the style you want to maintain. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Hair Care Tips

No matter what haircut you choose, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy

By following these tips and choosing a haircut that flatters your face shape, you're sure to look and feel your best!
