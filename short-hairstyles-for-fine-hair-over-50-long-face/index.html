Short Hairstyles For Fine Hair Over 50 Long Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Aprilia Sep 25, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a woman in your 50s with fine hair and a long face? Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features and makes you feel confident? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best short hairstyles for fine hair over 50 long face. From pixie cuts to bobs, we have got you covered!

Description

Fine hair can be challenging to style, especially if you have a long face. But don't worry! With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can achieve a chic and sophisticated look that complements your features. In this article, we will provide you with tips, tricks, and inspiration to help you find the perfect short hairstyle for your fine hair and long face.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect short hairstyle for your fine hair over 50 long face is to determine your face shape. A long face is characterized by a narrow forehead, high cheekbones, and a long jawline. Once you have identified your face shape, you can choose a hairstyle that balances your features and enhances your natural beauty.

The next step is to consider your hair texture and density. Fine hair can benefit from layers and texture, as they create the illusion of fullness and volume. A short haircut with layers can add movement and dimension to your hair, making it look thicker and more voluminous.

Now, let's explore some of the best short hairstyles for fine hair over 50 long face:

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short and stylish haircut that suits women of all ages. It is a great option for fine hair as it adds volume and texture to your hair. A pixie cut also accentuates your features and highlights your face shape. You can add layers and texture to your pixie cut to create a more modern and edgy look.

Bob

A bob is a classic and timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. It is a versatile haircut that can be customized to suit your face shape and hair texture. A bob can add volume and fullness to your fine hair, making it look thicker and more voluminous. You can opt for a blunt bob or a layered bob to add texture and movement to your hair.

Layered Crop

A layered crop is a short and chic haircut that suits women with fine hair and a long face. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling. A layered crop can add volume and texture to your hair, creating the illusion of fullness and thickness. You can also add bangs to your layered crop to frame your face and highlight your features.

Step by Step

If you have decided to go for a short hairstyle for your fine hair over 50 long face, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best short haircut for your face shape and hair texture.
  2. Choose a hairstyle that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to add layers and texture to your hair to create the illusion of fullness and volume.
  4. Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for fine hair, such as volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  5. Style your hair using a lightweight mousse or volumizing spray to add volume and texture.
  6. Use a round brush to create volume and movement in your hair.
  7. Finish your hairstyle with a light-hold hairspray to keep it in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short hairstyle for fine hair over 50 long face:

  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and style.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair and minimize breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hairstyles for fine hair over 50 long face:

Q: Can I wear bangs with a short hairstyle for fine hair over 50 long face?

A: Yes, you can! Bangs can frame your face and highlight your features, adding a youthful and playful touch to your hairstyle. Opt for wispy or side-swept bangs to soften your features and create a more natural look.

Q: Is it possible to add volume and fullness to my fine hair?

A: Yes, it is! With the right haircut and styling techniques, you can create the illusion of fullness and thickness in your fine hair. Ask your hairstylist to add layers and texture to your hair to create volume and movement. Use volumizing products and styling tools to enhance your hair's natural body and bounce.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have fine hair, you may need to wash it more frequently to prevent oil buildup and maintain its cleanliness. However, washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and cause dryness and damage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days and use a gentle shampoo that is designed for fine hair.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is essential to maintaining a beautiful and stylish short hairstyle. Here are some tips for caring for your fine hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is designed for fine hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can damage your hair and cause dryness.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a stylish and flattering short hairstyle for your fine hair over 50 long face. Remember, confidence is key! Embrace your natural beauty and rock your new haircut with pride.
