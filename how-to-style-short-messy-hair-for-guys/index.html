How To Style Short Messy Hair For Guys A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Oct 05, 2023 · 5 min read

Short messy hair is a popular hairstyle among guys, and for a good reason. It's low maintenance, easy to style, and looks effortlessly cool. But achieving the perfect messy look can be a bit tricky, especially if you're new to this style. In this guide, we'll show you how to style short messy hair for guys, step by step.

  • Description
  • How to Style Short Messy Hair for Guys
  • Step by Step Guide

Description

Short messy hair is a hairstyle that looks tousled and effortless, like you just rolled out of bed. It's perfect for guys who want a low maintenance look that still looks cool and trendy. The best part about this hairstyle is that it works for all hair types and lengths. Whether you have straight, curly, or wavy hair, you can rock the short messy look.

How to Style Short Messy Hair for Guys

Styling short messy hair for guys is all about creating texture and volume. You want your hair to look tousled and undone, but still intentional. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect short messy look:

1. Choose the right haircut

The first step to achieving the perfect short messy look is to get the right haircut. You want a cut that's short on the sides and back, but longer on top. This will give you the length you need to create texture and volume. A textured crop, messy quiff, or side-swept fringe are all great options for short messy hair.

2. Use the right products

The right products can make all the difference when it comes to styling short messy hair. You'll want to use a product that provides hold and texture, but still allows your hair to move naturally. A clay or wax-based product is a great choice for short messy hair. You can also use a sea salt spray to add texture and volume.

3. Start with damp hair

When styling short messy hair, it's important to start with damp hair. This will make it easier to apply your products evenly and create the texture you need. Use a towel to dry your hair until it's just slightly damp.

4. Work in sections

To create texture and volume, you'll want to work in sections. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, applying product to small sections of hair at a time. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair, creating texture and volume as you go.

5. Add volume at the roots

To create even more volume, you can add product directly to your roots. This will lift your hair up and give it a more tousled look. Use your fingers to work the product into your roots, then use a blow dryer to dry your hair while lifting it up at the roots.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step by step guide to styling short messy hair for guys:

Step 1: Choose the right haircut

The first step is to choose the right haircut for short messy hair. A textured crop, messy quiff, or side-swept fringe are all great options.

Step 2: Use the right products

Next, choose the right products for your hair type. A clay or wax-based product is a great choice for short messy hair. You can also use a sea salt spray to add texture and volume.

Step 3: Start with damp hair

Begin by towel-drying your hair until it's just slightly damp.

Step 4: Work in sections

Take small sections of hair and apply product to each section, working your way from the back to the front of your head. Use your fingers to create texture and volume as you go.

Step 5: Add volume at the roots

For even more volume, apply product directly to your roots and use a blow dryer to dry your hair while lifting it up at the roots.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you achieve the perfect short messy look:

  • Don't overdo it with the product. A little goes a long way when it comes to short messy hair.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Use a comb or brush to create a side part or to smooth down any flyaways.
  • Don't be afraid to play around with your hair and create different looks. Short messy hair is all about experimentation and having fun.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair type is best for short messy hair?

Short messy hair works for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair.

How often should I wash my short messy hair?

You should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Can I use a hair dryer to style short messy hair?

Yes, a hair dryer can be a useful tool for creating volume and texture in short messy hair.

Hair Care for Short Messy Hair

Proper hair care is important for maintaining healthy, great-looking hair. Here are some tips for taking care of short messy hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain your desired length and shape.

By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect short messy look that's both stylish and low maintenance. Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair type, and don't be afraid to have fun with your hair.
