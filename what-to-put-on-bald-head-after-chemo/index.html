What To Put On Bald Head After Chemo Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 06, 2023 · 5 min read

Going through chemotherapy can be a difficult and challenging time for anyone. One of the side effects of chemotherapy is hair loss, which can leave your scalp feeling exposed and vulnerable. It's important to take care of your scalp during this time to prevent irritation and discomfort. In this article, we'll go over what to put on your bald head after chemo and provide some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

How to Take Care of Your Bald Head After Chemo

The first step in taking care of your bald head after chemo is to keep it clean and moisturized. Your scalp may be more sensitive than usual during this time, so it's important to use gentle products that won't irritate your skin. You can use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp clean and hydrated. It's also a good idea to avoid using hot water, which can dry out your scalp and cause irritation.

You should also protect your scalp from the sun and the cold. Wear a hat or a scarf when you're outside to prevent sunburn or windburn. If you're going to be in direct sunlight for a long period of time, use a sunscreen designed for sensitive skin to protect your scalp from UV rays.

What to Put on Bald Head After Chemo

There are several products you can use to keep your bald head moisturized and comfortable after chemo. Here are some of the most effective options:

1. Moisturizer

A good moisturizer can help soothe your scalp and prevent dryness and irritation. Look for a product that's specifically designed for sensitive skin, and avoid any moisturizers that contain alcohol or fragrances, which can be irritating.

2. Aloe Vera Gel

Aloe vera gel is a natural remedy that can help soothe your scalp and reduce inflammation. You can apply it directly to your scalp or mix it with your moisturizer for added hydration.

3. Petroleum Jelly

Another option is to use petroleum jelly, which can help lock in moisture and prevent dryness. It's important to use a high-quality product that's free of fragrances and other additives.

4. Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a natural moisturizer that can help soothe your scalp and prevent dryness. You can apply it directly to your scalp or mix it with your moisturizer for added hydration.

Step-by-Step Guide to Putting Products on Your Bald Head

Here's a step-by-step guide to putting products on your bald head after chemo:

  1. Start with a clean scalp. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to wash your scalp and remove any dirt or oil.
  2. Dry your scalp gently with a soft towel.
  3. Apply your chosen product to your scalp, making sure to cover your entire head.
  4. Gently massage the product into your scalp using circular motions.
  5. Let the product absorb into your scalp for a few minutes.
  6. If you're using a moisturizer or aloe vera gel, you can leave it on your scalp. If you're using petroleum jelly or coconut oil, you may want to rinse it off after a few minutes to prevent buildup.

Tips for Caring for Your Bald Head After Chemo

Here are some additional tips to help you care for your bald head after chemo:

  • Wear a hat or a scarf to protect your scalp from the sun and the cold.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your scalp, as it can cause dryness and irritation.
  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products on your scalp, as they can cause irritation.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your hair to avoid pulling on your scalp.

FAQs About What to Put on Bald Head After Chemo

What should I do if my scalp is itchy after chemo?

If your scalp is itchy after chemo, try using a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep your scalp clean and hydrated. You can also try using aloe vera gel or a moisturizer to soothe your scalp.

Can I use regular sunscreen on my bald head?

You can use regular sunscreen on your bald head, but it's best to choose a product that's designed for sensitive skin. Look for a sunscreen that's free of fragrances and other additives that can irritate your scalp.

How often should I moisturize my bald head after chemo?

You should moisturize your bald head after chemo as often as necessary to keep your scalp hydrated and comfortable. This may vary depending on your skin type and the climate where you live.

Hair Care After Chemo

After your hair starts growing back, it's important to continue taking care of your scalp and hair. Use gentle products that won't irritate your scalp, and avoid using harsh chemicals or styling products. You can also try using a scalp massage brush or a scalp serum to promote healthy hair growth.

Going through chemotherapy can be a challenging time, but taking care of your scalp and hair can help you feel more comfortable and confident. With the right products and care, you can keep your bald head healthy and hydrated during this time.
