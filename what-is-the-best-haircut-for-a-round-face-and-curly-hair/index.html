What Is The Best Haircut For A Round Face And Curly Hair

Written by Robby Jun 16, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut for your round face and curly hair? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best haircuts for a round face and curly hair, along with tips and tricks for maintaining your curls.

Description

Curly hair can be a challenge to manage, especially when paired with a round face shape. A round face shape is characterized by a wider forehead, rounded cheeks, and a rounded chin. The goal of a haircut for a round face and curly hair is to elongate the face and create a more angular appearance.

How to

When it comes to a round face and curly hair, the best haircut is one that is layered and has volume on top. This will help elongate the face and create a more angular appearance. Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long, as they can make your face appear even rounder.

Step by Step

Step 1: Consult with your hairstylist about the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Opt for a layered haircut that has volume on top.

Step 3: Avoid haircuts that are too short or too long.

Step 4: Use hair products that enhance your natural curls and add volume.

Step 5: Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims every 6-8 weeks.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for maintaining your curly hair and round face:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as it can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight, as they can pull on your hair and cause damage.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have bangs with a round face and curly hair?

A: Yes, you can! Opt for side-swept bangs that are longer in the front and shorter towards the back.

Q: How can I prevent my curls from becoming frizzy?

A: Use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to add moisture to your hair and prevent frizz.

Hair Care

Maintaining your curly hair and round face requires proper hair care. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying out your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, as they can cause damage.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

With the right haircut and hair care routine, you can embrace your curly hair and round face shape. Consult with your hairstylist and experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect look for you.
