If you want to continue using Devacurl products, there are steps you can take to minimize the risk of hair loss. These include:

  1. Use the products as directed on the label.
  2. Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and cause breakage.

  4. Be gentle when detangling your hair, and use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers instead of a brush.
  5. Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to help moisturize and protect your hair.
