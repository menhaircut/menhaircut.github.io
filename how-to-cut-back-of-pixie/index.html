How to Cut Back of Pixie - Step by Step Guide [2023]

Do you have a pixie haircut that needs trimming at the back? Cutting the back of a pixie can be tricky, but with a few tips and techniques, you can do it yourself at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting the back of a pixie haircut step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Cut Back of Pixie
  2. Step by Step Guide
  3. Tips for Cutting Back of Pixie
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care after Cutting Pixie

How to Cut Back of Pixie

To cut the back of a pixie haircut, you will need a few tools like a pair of scissors, a comb, and hair clips. The first step is to wash your hair and dry it completely. Then, section your hair into small sections and clip them individually. Start cutting the hair at the nape of your neck and work your way up to the crown. Always cut less hair than you think you need to avoid any mistakes.

Tools Required

Before you start cutting, gather the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair Clips
  • Spray Bottle

Things to Consider

Here are a few things you should consider before cutting your hair:

  • The length of your hair
  • The texture of your hair
  • The style of your pixie
  • Your skill level

These factors will determine how much hair you need to cut and how precise you need to be.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut the back of your pixie:

  1. Wash and dry your hair completely.
  2. Section your hair into small sections and clip them.
  3. Start cutting the hair at the nape of your neck.
  4. Cut less hair than you think you need to avoid any mistakes.
  5. Use a comb to check the length and make sure it's even.
  6. Move up to the crown and repeat the process.
  7. Cut the hair around your ear using the same technique.
  8. Blend the sides and back of your hair with scissors.
  9. Check the length and make any final adjustments.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Cutting Back of Pixie

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting the back of your pixie:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to the desired length.
  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if it's too dry.
  • Take breaks if you feel tired or overwhelmed.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head while cutting.
  • Keep your scissors sharp for a clean cut.

FAQs

1. How often should I cut the back of my pixie?

You should cut the back of your pixie every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.

2. Can I cut the back of my pixie myself?

Yes, you can cut the back of your pixie yourself, but it's always better to get it done by a professional.

3. What if I make a mistake while cutting the back of my pixie?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the rest of your hair to match the mistake or visit a hairstylist to fix it.

Hair Care after Cutting Pixie

Once you've cut the back of your pixie, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain the style. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner and avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons. Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends, and always wear a hat or scarf if you're out in the sun for an extended period.

Cutting the back of a pixie can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can do it yourself at home. Remember to follow these steps carefully and take your time to avoid any mistakes. Happy cutting!
