How To Do Up Hairstyles For Medium Length Hair

Written by Aprilia Aug 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same hairstyle every day? Do you want to switch up your look with some updos? Medium length hair is perfect for trying out different styles, and with a little practice, you can create beautiful updos that will keep you looking stylish all day long. In this article, we will guide you on how to do up hairstyles for medium length hair.

Description

Medium length hair can be challenging to style, but it is not impossible to create stunning updos. Whether you have a wedding, prom, or any special event coming up, we have got you covered with some easy-to-do up hairstyles for medium length hair.

How to

Before you begin, make sure you have all the necessary tools, including bobby pins, hair ties, a comb, and hairspray. Follow these simple steps to achieve a beautiful hairstyle:

1. Messy Bun

The messy bun is a trendy and effortless hairstyle that looks great on medium length hair. To achieve this look, follow these steps:

  • Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure it with a hairband.
  • Twist the hair and wrap it around the hair tie, securing it with bobby pins.
  • Pull out some strands for a messy look and spritz with hairspray to hold the style.

2. Braided Updo

The braided updo is a classic hairstyle that works well with medium length hair. Here's how to achieve it:

  • Part your hair in the middle and start braiding each side from the front, adding sections of hair as you go.
  • Once you reach the back of your head, secure the braids with bobby pins.
  • Twist the remaining hair into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  • Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

1. Low Bun

The low bun is an elegant and easy-to-do hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. Here's how to create it:

  1. Start by brushing your hair and gathering it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist the hair and wrap it around the hair tie, securing it with bobby pins.
  3. Pull out some strands for a messy look and spray with hairspray to hold the style.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is a versatile and stylish look that's perfect for medium length hair. Follow these steps to achieve this look:

  1. Section off the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie.
  2. Twist the hair and wrap it around the hair tie, securing it with bobby pins.
  3. Let the remaining hair flow down and spritz with hairspray to hold the style.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect updo:

  • Use a volumizing mousse to make your hair easier to style.
  • Don't be afraid to use hairspray to hold the style in place.
  • Experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

What products should I use to create an updo?

You will need bobby pins, hair ties, a comb, and hairspray to create an updo. You may also want to use a volumizing mousse to make your hair easier to style.

Is it easy to create an updo with medium length hair?

Yes, it is easy to create an updo with medium length hair. With a little practice and the right tools, you can create beautiful updos that will keep you looking stylish all day long.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and strong, make sure to follow a proper hair care routine. This includes washing your hair regularly with a mild shampoo, conditioning it, and using a hair mask once a week. Avoid using too much heat on your hair and always use a heat protectant spray when using hot tools.

With these tips and tricks, you can create beautiful updos for your medium length hair. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and find the one that works best for you. With a little practice, you can create stunning hairstyles that will keep you looking stylish and confident all day long.
