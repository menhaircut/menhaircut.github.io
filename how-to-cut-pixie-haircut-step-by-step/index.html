How To Cut Pixie Haircut Step By Step

How to Cut Pixie Haircut Step by Step | Haircutting Tips

Are you thinking about getting a pixie haircut but don't know where to start? Cutting your hair short can be intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a stylish and low-maintenance look. In this article, we'll guide you through the steps of how to cut a pixie haircut step by step.

Table of Contents

  1. Tools You'll Need
  2. Preparing Your Hair
  3. Cutting Your Hair
  4. Styling Your Pixie Haircut
  5. Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair
  6. Frequently Asked Questions
  7. Hair Care Tips for Pixie Haircuts

Tools You'll Need

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clipper with guards
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Spray bottle with water

Preparing Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel drying it. Comb it out and divide it into sections using hair clips. Make sure you have good lighting in the room you'll be cutting your hair in.

Cutting Your Hair

Begin by cutting the back of your hair with the clipper and a guard. Start with a longer guard and work your way down until you reach your desired length. Next, use your scissors to cut the sides and top of your hair. Take small sections and hold them between your fingers. Cut at an angle, following the shape of your head. Blend the sides and back with the top to create a seamless look.

Styling Your Pixie Haircut

Once you've finished cutting your hair, you can style it using pomade or wax. Apply a small amount to your fingertips and work it through your hair, starting at the back and working your way forward. Use your fingers to create texture and volume.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to your desired length to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Take small sections of hair when cutting with scissors to ensure accuracy.
  • Blend the sides and back with the top to create a seamless look.
  • Use a spray bottle with water to dampen your hair if it becomes too dry during cutting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

You can cut your hair either wet or dry, but cutting it wet will give you a more accurate cut.

How often should I cut my pixie haircut?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Most people with pixie haircuts get them trimmed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Haircuts

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and healthy.

By following these steps and tips, you can cut your own pixie haircut and keep it looking stylish and healthy. Remember to take your time, use good tools, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Happy cutting!
