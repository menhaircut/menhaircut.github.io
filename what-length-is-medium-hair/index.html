What Length Is Medium Hair

Written by Oliver Aug 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Medium hair is a popular hair length that falls between short and long hair. It is a versatile hair length that can be styled in various ways, making it a favorite among many women. In this article, we will discuss what length is considered medium hair, how to measure it, and tips on how to care for it.

How to Measure Medium Hair Length

The length of medium hair can vary from person to person depending on their height, face shape, and hair texture. Generally, medium hair falls between the chin and shoulders. To measure your hair length, you can follow these steps:

  1. Comb your hair straight and stand in front of a mirror.
  2. Take a measuring tape and place it at the center of your forehead, just above your eyebrows.
  3. Hold the measuring tape at the nape of your neck, where your hairline ends.
  4. Note down the measurement. If it falls between 10 and 14 inches, then your hair length is considered medium.

Tips for Styling Medium Hair

Medium hair is a versatile length that can be styled in various ways. Here are some tips on how to style medium hair:

  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush for a sleek and polished look.
  • Create loose waves with a curling iron for a romantic and effortless look.
  • Add volume to your hair by teasing the roots and spraying hairspray.
  • Try braiding your hair for a chic and trendy look.
  • Tie your hair in a high ponytail for a casual and sporty look.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ideal medium hair length?

The ideal medium hair length is different for every person, depending on their face shape and hair texture. Generally, medium hair falls between the chin and shoulders, but it can vary by a few inches.

Can I style my medium hair in an updo?

Yes, medium hair is long enough to be styled in an updo. You can try a messy bun, a braided updo, or a sleek chignon for a formal event.

How often should I trim my medium hair?

It is recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair.

How to Take Care of Medium Hair

Here are some tips on how to take care of medium hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to maintain the natural oils in your hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray.
  • Brush your hair gently to prevent breakage and tangles.

Medium hair is a versatile and popular hair length that can be styled in various ways. Whether you prefer a sleek and polished look or a romantic and effortless style, there are endless possibilities with medium hair. By following these tips, you can maintain healthy and beautiful hair at the perfect length.
