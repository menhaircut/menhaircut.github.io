Are Female Undercuts Still In Style 2020

Written by Christine Sep 29, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to hairstyles, there are always new trends that come and go. However, some styles have proven to be timeless, and one of those is the female undercut. This edgy hairstyle has been around for a while and is still popular among women. But the question is, are female undercuts still in style in 2020? In this article, we’ll explore this trend and give you tips on how to rock it.

Description

For those who are not familiar with the female undercut, it’s a hairstyle that involves shaving or cutting the hair on the sides or back of the head while leaving the hair on top longer. It creates a bold and edgy look that’s perfect for those who want to stand out. The female undercut can be styled in many ways, from sleek and polished to messy and textured.

How to

If you’re thinking of getting a female undercut, here’s how to do it:

Step 1: Choose your undercut style

There are different types of female undercuts, and you need to choose the one that suits you best. The most popular styles include the side undercut, back undercut, and nape undercut. You can also opt for a partial undercut, which only shaves a portion of your hair.

Step 2: Find a reputable hairstylist

Getting an undercut is not something you should do at home. You need to find a professional hairstylist who has experience in cutting this style. Look for someone who has good reviews and can show you their portfolio of previous undercuts.

Step 3: Prepare for the cut

Before you get your undercut, make sure to wash your hair and don’t use any hair products. Wear a top that allows your hairstylist to easily access the area they will be cutting.

Step 4: Get the cut

Once you’re ready, your hairstylist will section off the hair they will leave long and shave or cut the hair on the sides or back of your head. Depending on the style you choose, the cut can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

Step 5: Style your undercut

Once you have your undercut, it’s time to style it. You can leave the long hair on top down or tie it up into a bun or ponytail. You can also add texture or volume to your hair using hair products like texturizing spray or mousse.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you rock your female undercut:

- Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best. - Use hair products to add texture, volume, or hold to your hair. - Accessorize your undercut with hair clips or headbands. - Don’t be afraid to embrace the edgy look and show off your personality.

FAQ

Q: Will an undercut suit my face shape?

A: Yes, an undercut can suit any face shape. You just need to choose the right style that complements your features.

Q: Will my hair grow back after an undercut?

A: Yes, your hair will grow back after an undercut. However, it may take some time for it to grow back to its original length, so be prepared for a transition period.

Q: Can I still tie up my hair with an undercut?

A: Yes, you can still tie up your hair with an undercut. You just need to leave the longer hair on top down or tie it up into a bun or ponytail.

Hair Care

After getting an undercut, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking good. Here are some hair care tips:

- Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo. - Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and moisturized. - Avoid using styling products that contain harsh chemicals. - Get regular trims to maintain your undercut.

Conclusion

So, are female undercuts still in style in 2020? The answer is yes! This edgy hairstyle has stood the test of time and is still popular among women. With the right style and care, you can rock an undercut and show off your personality.
