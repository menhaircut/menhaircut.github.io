How To Cut Curly Male Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a man with curly hair, you know how difficult it can be to find a stylist who knows how to work with your locks. That's why many curly-haired men choose to cut their own hair at home. But cutting curly hair can be tricky, especially if you're new to it. In this guide, we'll show you everything you need to know about how to cut curly male hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Curly Male Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Description

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks great when styled properly, it can also be difficult to manage and style. When it comes to cutting curly hair, it's important to remember that each curl is unique, and what works for one person may not work for another. That's why it's important to understand the basics of how to cut curly male hair, so you can get the best results possible.

How to Cut Curly Male Hair

Cutting curly hair isn't as simple as just picking up a pair of scissors and going to town. Here are some things you'll need to keep in mind:

  • Use sharp scissors or shears
  • Work with dry hair
  • Start with a longer length
  • Cut in small sections
  • Don't cut too much at once
  • Check your work often

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basics, here's a step-by-step guide to cutting curly male hair:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair.
  2. Use a comb to separate your hair into sections.
  3. Start cutting one section at a time, using the comb to hold the hair in place as you cut.
  4. Cut your hair in small sections, taking care not to cut too much at once.
  5. Check your work often to make sure you're cutting evenly and not taking off too much length.
  6. Once you've finished cutting, use a mirror to check the back of your head and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for Cutting Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results when cutting curly hair:

  • Use a spray bottle to dampen your hair if it's too dry to work with.
  • Use a mirror to check your work at every angle.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help if you're having trouble.
  • If you're not sure what length you want, start longer and work your way down.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting curly male hair:

Can I cut my curly hair when it's wet?

It's not recommended to cut curly hair when it's wet, as it can shrink up when it dries and you may end up cutting off more than you intended.

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your curls looking their best.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a professional stylist to have it fixed, or wait for it to grow out and start over.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Now that you know how to cut curly male hair, it's important to take care of your curls to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips for curly hair care:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve great results when cutting curly male hair. Remember to be patient and take your time, and don't be afraid to ask for help if you're not sure what you're doing. With a little practice, you'll be a pro at cutting your own curly hair in no time!
