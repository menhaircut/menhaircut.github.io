What Is A No 4 Haircut In Mm

Written by Robby Aug 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to get a new haircut, you may have heard the term "No 4" thrown around. But what does it actually mean? In short, a No 4 haircut in mm refers to the length of the hair that will be left after the cut.

What is a No 4 Haircut?

A No 4 haircut is a type of clipper cut that leaves the hair 1/2 inch long. This measurement is in millimeters, which is why you may see it referred to as a "No 4 haircut in mm."

How to Get a No 4 Haircut

If you're interested in getting a No 4 haircut, the first step is to find a barber or stylist who is familiar with this type of cut. Once you're in the chair, your stylist will use clippers to trim your hair to the desired length.

It's important to note that a No 4 haircut is relatively short, so it may not be the best option for everyone. If you're unsure whether this length would suit you, it's a good idea to talk to your stylist about your options.

Tips for Maintaining a No 4 Haircut

If you do decide to go for a No 4 haircut, there are a few things you can do to keep it looking its best. First and foremost, you'll need to schedule regular trims to keep the length consistent.

You may also want to invest in some quality hair care products, such as a good shampoo and conditioner, to keep your hair healthy and shiny. And if you're looking for a little extra styling help, a pomade or styling cream can help you achieve the look you want.

FAQ

What other haircuts are similar to a No 4?

Other haircuts that leave the hair around 1/2 inch long include a No 3, No 5, and No 6.

Is a No 4 haircut suitable for all hair types?

While a No 4 haircut can work well for many people, it may not be the best choice for those with very fine or thinning hair.

How often should I get a No 4 haircut?

It's generally recommended that you get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the length consistent.

Hair Care

In addition to regular trims and quality hair care products, there are a few other things you can do to keep your hair looking its best. For example, it's important to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when styling with hot tools.

You should also avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers, as these can cause damage and breakage. And finally, make sure you're getting enough nutrients in your diet to support healthy hair growth. Foods high in protein, vitamins A and C, and omega-3 fatty acids are all great choices.

Conclusion

A No 4 haircut in mm refers to a clipper cut that leaves the hair 1/2 inch long. If you're interested in this type of cut, be sure to find a stylist who is familiar with it and talk to them about whether it would be a good choice for your hair type and face shape. And remember to take good care of your hair between trims to keep it looking healthy and shiny!
