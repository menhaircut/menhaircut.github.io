Best Hairstyles For Long Faces 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Aug 23, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a long face and are looking for the perfect hairstyle to flatter your features, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll be discussing the best hairstyles for long faces in 2020. Whether you prefer short, medium, or long hair, we've got you covered. So, sit back, relax, and let's get started!

Description

A long face is characterized by a high forehead, elongated chin, and narrow width. The key to finding the perfect hairstyle for a long face is to choose a style that adds width to the face, while also balancing out the length. In this post, we'll be sharing a variety of hairstyles that do just that.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for a Long Face

Before we dive into the specific hairstyles, it's important to understand how to choose the right one for your face shape. Here are some tips:

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

The first step is to determine whether you have a long face shape. This can be done by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two. If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have a long face shape.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture can also play a role in choosing the right hairstyle. If you have thick hair, you may want to consider a shorter style to avoid adding too much width to your face. If you have thin hair, a longer style can help add volume and balance out your features.

Step 3: Think About Your Lifestyle

When choosing a hairstyle, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you're someone who doesn't have a lot of time to style your hair, a low-maintenance cut may be the best option.

Best Hairstyles for Long Faces 2020

Now that you know how to choose the right hairstyle, let's take a look at some of the best hairstyles for long faces in 2020:

1. Long Bob

The long bob, or "lob," is a popular hairstyle that works well for long faces. This style falls just below the chin and adds volume to the sides of the face, creating a more balanced look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can also help balance out a long face shape. These bangs frame the face and add width to the forehead, while also drawing attention away from the elongated chin.

3. Layered Haircut

A layered haircut is another great option for long faces. This style adds volume and movement to the hair, creating a more balanced look. It's also a versatile style that can be worn straight or curly.

4. Pixie Cut

If you're looking for a shorter style, the pixie cut is a great option for long faces. This style adds volume to the top of the head, creating the illusion of a wider face shape.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your long face, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Regular Trims

To keep your hairstyle looking fresh, regular trims are a must. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking its best.

2. Use Quality Products

Using quality hair products can also help maintain your hairstyle. Look for products that are formulated for your hair type and texture.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protecting your hair from heat damage is also important. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, and avoid using them too often.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a long face?

Hairstyles that add too much length to the face should be avoided. This includes very long hair or styles that are slicked back.

Can I still wear my hair curly if I have a long face?

Absolutely! Curly hair can actually work well for long faces, as it adds volume and width to the sides of the face.

Do I need to have bangs if I have a long face?

No, bangs are not necessary if you have a long face. However, side-swept bangs can help balance out your features.

Hair Care for Long Faces

Lastly, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help keep it healthy and clean. Aim to wash your hair 2-3 times per week.

2. Use a Deep Conditioner

Using a deep conditioner once a week can help keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

UV rays can damage your hair, so it's important to protect it from the sun. Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to keep your hair healthy.

With these tips and hairstyle options, you're sure to find the perfect look for your long face. Remember to choose a style that adds width to your face while also balancing out the length. And don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best!
