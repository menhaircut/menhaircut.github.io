When Did Curly Hair Become Fashionable?

History of Curly Hair

Curly hair has been around for centuries, but it hasn't always been considered fashionable. In fact, in many cultures, straight hair was the ideal. For example, in ancient Greece and Rome, women would use hot irons to straighten their hair.

However, in other cultures, curly hair was seen as a sign of beauty and fertility. In Egypt, for example, both men and women would wear wigs with tight curls. In Sub-Saharan Africa, curly hair was celebrated and often styled in intricate braids and twists.

Curly Hair in Fashion

It wasn't until the 1960s and 70s that curly hair became fashionable in the Western world. The rise of the counterculture movement and the Afro hairstyle popularized by Black activists like Angela Davis and the Black Panther Party helped to shift beauty standards.

During the same time period, rock stars like Mick Jagger and Jimi Hendrix also popularized curly hair among men. Women with naturally curly hair began to embrace their texture, with Farrah Fawcett's feathered curls becoming an iconic look in the 1970s.

In the 1980s and 90s, curly hair fell out of fashion again as straight hair became the norm. However, in recent years, there has been a resurgence of curly hair in pop culture and fashion. Celebrities like Zendaya, Solange Knowles, and Lorde have all embraced their natural curls, inspiring others to do the same.

Tips for Styling Curly Hair

If you have curly hair, there are a few things you can do to make sure your curls look their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as it can cause frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls
  • Avoid using heat tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair

FAQs About Curly Hair

Q: Can you straighten curly hair?

A: Yes, you can straighten curly hair with heat tools like flat irons or chemical treatments like keratin treatments. However, it's important to be gentle with your hair and use heat protectant products to avoid damage.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp is. Some people with curly hair can go several days between washes, while others may need to wash their hair more frequently. Experiment to find what works best for you.

Q: Can I wear my hair curly to a job interview?

A: Yes, you can definitely wear your hair curly to a job interview! Just make sure your hair is clean and styled neatly. If you're worried about looking too casual, you can pull your hair back into a low ponytail or bun.

How to Care for Curly Hair

In addition to the styling tips mentioned earlier, there are a few things you can do to keep your curly hair healthy:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated
  • Avoid using hair products with alcohol, as they can dry out your hair
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz

By following these tips and embracing your natural curls, you can rock the latest fashion trend with confidence!
