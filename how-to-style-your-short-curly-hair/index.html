How To Style Your Short Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it’s undeniably beautiful, it can also be challenging to manage, especially if you have short hair. However, with the right styling techniques and products, you can make your short curly hair look fabulous every day. In this article, we’ll walk you through how to style your short curly hair from start to finish.

Table of Contents

  1. Hair Care
  2. How to Style Your Short Curly Hair
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair
  5. FAQs

Hair Care

Before you start styling your short curly hair, it’s essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to moisturize your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair while it’s wet.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray first.
  • Sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

How to Style Your Short Curly Hair

Now that you’ve taken care of your hair, it’s time to style it. Here are some popular styling options for short curly hair:

  • Wash-and-Go: This is the simplest and most low-maintenance option. Simply wash your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream, and let it air dry.
  • Pineapple Updo: This is a great option if you want to keep your hair out of your face. Gather your hair into a high ponytail on top of your head, leaving the curls at the front loose.
  • Braid-Out: Braid your hair while it’s wet, leave it to dry overnight, and take out the braids in the morning. This will give you defined, bouncy curls.
  • Twist-Out: Twist small sections of your hair while it’s wet, leave it to dry overnight, and take out the twists in the morning. This will give you more elongated, stretched-out curls.
  • Finger Coils: This is a popular option for short natural hair. Define small sections of your hair by twisting them around your finger, and let them air dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide to styling your short curly hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair gently.
  3. Apply a leave-in conditioner or styling cream to your hair, focusing on the ends.
  4. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  5. Choose your styling option (wash-and-go, pineapple updo, braid-out, twist-out, or finger coils).
  6. Follow the steps for your chosen styling option.
  7. Once your hair is dry, use a hair pick or your fingers to fluff and shape your curls.
  8. Apply a lightweight oil or serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Styling Short Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you style your short curly hair:

  • Don’t touch your hair too much while it’s drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh your hair down.
  • Experiment with different products and techniques to find what works best for your hair.
  • Invest in a silk or satin bonnet to protect your hair while you sleep.
  • Don’t be afraid to accessorize your hair with clips, headbands, or scarves.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about styling short curly hair:

1. Can I straighten my short curly hair?

Yes, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray first and avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.

2. How often should I wash my short curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how active you are. Some people can go a week without washing their hair, while others need to wash it every other day. Experiment to find what works best for you.

3. Can I use a diffuser to dry my short curly hair?

Yes, a diffuser can help enhance your curls and reduce frizz. Use it on low heat and low speed settings to avoid damaging your hair.

4. How can I prevent my short curly hair from getting tangled?

Detangle your hair while it’s wet using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers. You can also sleep on a satin pillowcase to prevent tangles.

5. How can I style my short curly hair for a formal event?

You can try a sleek updo, a side part with loose curls, or a curly bob.

With these tips and techniques, you can make your short curly hair look its best every day. Experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for you, and don’t be afraid to have fun and express yourself.
