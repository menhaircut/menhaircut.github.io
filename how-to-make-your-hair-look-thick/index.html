How To Make Your Hair Look Thick Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you have thin or fine hair that just won't seem to volumize? You're not alone. Many people struggle with flat, lifeless hair, but there are ways to make your hair look thick and full. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the look you want.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Your Hair Look Thick
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Thick Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Make Your Hair Look Thick

There are several ways to make your hair look thicker. Some involve styling tricks, while others focus on hair care. Here are some tips to try:

1. Use volumizing products

Volumizing shampoos, conditioners, and styling products can all help to add body to your hair. Look for products that contain ingredients like biotin, collagen, or keratin, which can help to strengthen and thicken hair.

2. Blow dry your hair upside down

When blow drying your hair, flip your head upside down and use a round brush to lift your roots. This will help to create volume and lift at the roots.

3. Tease your hair

Teasing your hair at the crown can help to add volume and height. Use a teasing brush or comb to gently backcomb your hair, then smooth the top layer over the teased area.

4. Try clip-in extensions

Clip-in extensions can be a great way to add instant thickness and length to your hair. Look for high-quality, human hair extensions that match your natural hair color.

5. Get a layered haircut

A layered haircut can help to give the illusion of thicker hair. Ask your stylist to add layers throughout your hair, which will create movement and texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to making your hair look thicker:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and blow dry your hair using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If your hair is naturally oily, use a dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and add texture.

2. Apply a volumizing spray or mousse

Apply a volumizing spray or mousse to your roots and mid-lengths. Use your fingers to work the product through your hair.

3. Blow dry your hair upside down

Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair using a round brush. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends.

4. Tease your hair at the crown

Using a teasing brush or comb, gently backcomb your hair at the crown. Smooth the top layer over the teased area.

5. Finish with hairspray

Spray your hair with a light mist of hairspray to hold the volume and keep your style in place.

Tips for Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain thick, healthy hair:

1. Eat a healthy diet

A balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals can help to promote healthy hair growth.

2. Use heat protectant

When using hot tools like curling irons or straighteners, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Avoid over-washing

Washing your hair too often can strip it of natural oils, which can lead to dryness and breakage. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Can I make my hair look thicker without using products?

Yes, you can create volume using styling techniques like blow drying your hair upside down or teasing your hair at the crown.

2. Will using extensions damage my hair?

Clip-in extensions are a safe and temporary way to add thickness and length to your hair, as long as they are properly applied and removed.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care for Thick Hair

Here are some tips to help you care for your thick hair:

1. Use a wide-tooth comb

A wide-tooth comb can help to prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

2. Deep condition regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help to keep your hair soft, shiny, and healthy.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to shield your hair from the damaging effects of the sun.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair look thick and full. Whether you're styling your hair for a special occasion or just want to add some volume to your everyday look, these techniques can help you achieve the perfect style.
