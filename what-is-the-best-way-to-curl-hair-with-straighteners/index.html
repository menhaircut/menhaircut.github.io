What Is The Best Way To Curl Hair With Straighteners

Written by Aprilia Oct 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of using a curling iron to achieve gorgeous curls? Well, it's time to put your straighteners to good use! Many people are unaware of the fact that straighteners can be used to curl your hair just as easily as they can straighten it. So, what is the best way to curl hair with straighteners? Let's find out!

Description

The process of curling your hair with straighteners is also known as "flat iron curls". It involves using a straightener to create curls that are sleek, shiny and bouncy. This method of curling hair is becoming increasingly popular as it is quicker and easier than using a curling iron.

How to Curl Hair with Straighteners

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Begin by washing your hair and applying a heat protectant spray. This will protect your hair from heat damage.

Step 2: Divide your hair into sections. The number of sections will depend on the thickness and length of your hair. For thick hair, you may need to create more sections.

Step 3: Take a section of hair and clamp the straightener at the base of the section. Make sure the straightener is facing downwards.

Step 4: Twist the straightener 180 degrees away from your face.

Step 5: Slowly glide the straightener down the length of your hair while maintaining tension on the hair.

Step 6: Release the hair from the straightener and let it cool for a few seconds.

Step 7: Repeat steps 3-6 until you have curled all of your hair.

Tips for Curling Hair with Straighteners

Tip 1: Use a high-quality straightener that has temperature control. This will ensure that your hair does not get too hot and is not damaged.

Tip 2: Don't clamp the straightener too tightly on your hair. This will create a crease in your curls.

Tip 3: For a natural look, alternate the direction of the curls. This means curling some sections away from your face and others towards your face.

Tip 4: Use a hairspray to hold your curls in place for longer.

FAQs

Q: Can I curl my hair with any straightener?

A: It is best to use a straightener that has temperature control and ceramic plates. This will ensure that your hair does not get damaged.

Q: How long will my curls last?

A: Your curls will last for a few hours if you do not use any hair products. However, if you use a hairspray, your curls can last for up to 24 hours.

Hair Care

It is important to take good care of your hair before and after curling it with straighteners. Here are some tips:

- Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair.

- Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

- Use a wide-tooth comb to untangle your hair before and after curling it.

- Use a hairspray to hold your curls in place for longer.

- Avoid using straighteners on wet hair as this can cause damage.

Conclusion

Curling your hair with straighteners is an easy and quick way to achieve beautiful curls. By following the steps and tips mentioned above, you can create curls that are sleek, shiny and bouncy. Remember to take good care of your hair before and after curling it to maintain its health and shine.
