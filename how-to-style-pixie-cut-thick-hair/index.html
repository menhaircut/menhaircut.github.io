How To Style Pixie Cut Thick Hair A Comprehensive Guide

If you have thick hair and have recently opted for a pixie cut, you might be wondering how to style it. Pixie cuts are a great way to showcase your style and personality, but they can be challenging to maintain, especially if you have thick hair. In this guide, we'll take you through some easy steps on how to style pixie cut thick hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Style Pixie Cut Thick Hair

Styling pixie cut thick hair can be a little tricky, but with the right techniques, you can make it work. Here are some ways to style pixie cut thick hair.

1. Use a Texturizing Spray

Texturizing spray is a great way to add volume to your hair and give it a messy, effortless look. Spray a little bit of texturizing spray on your hair and use your fingers to tousle it up.

2. Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair is a great way to add volume and texture to your pixie cut. Use a round brush to blow dry your hair and add some curls if you want to change up your look.

3. Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to add some texture and shape to your pixie cut. This technique works best if you have shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top.

4. Go for a Messy Look

If you want a low-maintenance style, go for a messy, tousled look. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and add some texture.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style pixie cut thick hair.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add some volume.

Step 2: Apply Texturizing Spray

Apply texturizing spray to your hair and use your fingers to tousle it up.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a round brush to blow dry your hair and add some curls if you want to change up your look.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

Use a flat iron to add some texture and shape to your pixie cut. This technique works best if you have shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off with a light hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling pixie cut thick hair.

1. Keep Your Hair Healthy

To maintain your pixie cut, it's important to keep your hair healthy. Use a nourishing hair mask once a week and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

2. Use the Right Products

Use products that are designed for thick hair and are gentle on your scalp. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

3. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and techniques. Pixie cuts are versatile and can be styled in many different ways.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Q: Can I use a curling iron on my pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron to add some texture and shape to your pixie cut.

Q: How can I add volume to my hair?

A: Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and try using texturizing spray or blow drying your hair with a round brush.

Hair Care

To keep your pixie cut thick hair healthy and looking great, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep in mind.

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that's formulated for thick hair. Avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

3. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

With these tips and techniques, you can easily style pixie cut thick hair and keep it looking great. Remember to experiment with different styles and products, and most importantly, have fun with your new look!
