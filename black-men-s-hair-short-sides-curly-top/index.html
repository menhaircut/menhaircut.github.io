Black Men s Hair Short Sides Curly Top A Guide To Styling And Maintaining

Written by Eveline Sep 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a Black man searching for a new hairstyle, look no further than the short sides curly top. This haircut has become increasingly popular in recent years, and for good reason! It's versatile, stylish, and easy to maintain. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to achieve and maintain this look, as well as tips and tricks to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Description

The short sides curly top is a hairstyle that features short or faded sides and a curly or wavy top. It's a great option for men with naturally curly or wavy hair, as the top is left to grow out and showcase their unique texture. The length and style of the top can be customized to suit your preferences, making it a versatile option for any occasion.

How to Achieve the Look

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the short sides curly top:

Step 1: Choose Your Length

Decide on the length you want for the top of your hair. This will depend on your hair texture and personal preference. If you have tighter curls, you may want to leave the top longer to show off your natural texture. If your curls are looser, you may prefer a shorter top.

Step 2: Cut the Sides

Next, ask your barber or stylist to cut the sides of your hair short or fade them. This will create a contrast between the top and sides, highlighting your curls or waves. Make sure to communicate the length you want for the sides, as well as any specific details or designs you want included.

Step 3: Style the Top

Once the sides are trimmed, it's time to style the top. Apply a curl-enhancing product to wet hair and scrunch it to encourage your curls or waves. You can also use a diffuser to dry your hair and enhance your natural texture. Play around with different products and techniques to find what works best for you.

Tips for Maintaining the Look

Here are some tips for maintaining your short sides curly top:

Tip 1: Moisturize Your Hair

Curly hair tends to be dry, so it's important to moisturize it regularly. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your curls hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Trim Regularly

To keep your short sides looking fresh, make sure to get regular trims. Aim to visit your barber or stylist every 4-6 weeks.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair at Night

To prevent your curls from becoming tangled or flattened while you sleep, use a satin or silk pillowcase or wear a silk or satin scarf.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the short sides curly top:

Q: Can I achieve this look if my hair is straight?

A: While this look is designed for curly or wavy hair, it's possible to achieve it with straight hair. You'll need to use a curling iron or other styling tools to create the desired texture.

Q: How long does this style typically last?

A: The length of time this style lasts will depend on a variety of factors, including your hair texture, the products you use, and how you maintain it. On average, you can expect it to last 2-4 weeks before needing a trim.

Q: What products should I use to style my hair?

A: Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair. Some popular options include curl-enhancing creams, gels, and mousses.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair at least once a week to remove dirt, oil, and product buildup. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Tip 2: Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage. Try to limit your use of these tools as much as possible to avoid drying out your hair.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Make sure to include plenty of protein, fruits, and vegetables in your diet.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock the short sides curly top with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a casual or formal look, this haircut is sure to turn heads and showcase your unique personality.
