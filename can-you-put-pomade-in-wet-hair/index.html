Can You Put Pomade In Wet Hair

Written by Daniel Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Pomade is a popular hair styling product for men that can give you a sleek, polished look. It's a versatile product that can be used to create a variety of hairstyles, from slicked-back looks to textured, messy styles. But can you put pomade in wet hair? In this article, we'll explore the answer to this question and provide some tips for using pomade effectively.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Put Pomade in Wet Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Using Pomade in Wet Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care with Pomade

Description

Pomade is a hair product that is typically used to style men's hair, but it can also be used by women. It's a thick, oily substance that provides a medium to high hold and a shiny finish. Pomade is usually applied to dry hair to create a sleek, polished look, but it can also be used on wet hair.

How to Put Pomade in Wet Hair

If you want to put pomade in wet hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it's important to choose the right type of pomade. Water-based pomades are the best option for wet hair because they won't clump or leave a greasy residue.

To apply pomade to wet hair, start by towel-drying your hair so that it's damp but not soaking wet. Then, take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up. Apply the pomade evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to put pomade in wet hair:

  1. Towel-dry your hair so that it's damp but not soaking wet.
  2. Take a small amount of pomade and rub it between your palms to warm it up.
  3. Apply the pomade evenly throughout your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends.
  4. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Using Pomade in Wet Hair

Here are some tips to keep in mind when using pomade in wet hair:

  • Use a water-based pomade to avoid clumping or greasy residue.
  • Start with a small amount of pomade and add more as needed.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to evenly distribute the pomade throughout your hair.
  • Don't use too much pomade, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the look that works best for you.

FAQ

Can you put pomade in soaking wet hair?

It's not recommended to put pomade in soaking wet hair because the water will dilute the pomade and make it less effective.

What's the best type of pomade to use in wet hair?

Water-based pomades are the best option for wet hair because they won't clump or leave a greasy residue.

Can you restyle hair after applying pomade to wet hair?

Yes, you can restyle your hair after applying pomade to wet hair. Simply add more pomade and reshape your hair as desired.

Hair Care with Pomade

When using pomade, it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent damage and promote healthy growth. Here are some tips for hair care with pomade:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to remove buildup and keep your scalp healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much pomade, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.

In conclusion, you can put pomade in wet hair, but it's important to choose the right type of pomade and apply it evenly throughout your hair. Use a small amount of pomade and add more as needed, and experiment with different hairstyles to find the look that works best for you.
