How To Protect Hair Extensions While Sleeping Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're one of the many women who love to wear hair extensions, you know that they require a bit of extra care to keep them looking their best. One of the most important things you can do to protect your extensions is to take care of them while you sleep. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks for sleeping with hair extensions so that you can wake up with gorgeous hair every day.

Tip #1: Brush Your Hair Extensions Before Bed

Before you hit the hay, make sure to brush your hair extensions thoroughly. This will help to detangle any knots and prevent matting while you sleep. Use a soft-bristled brush or a wide-tooth comb to gently work through your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up to the roots.

Tip: If you have clip-in hair extensions, it's a good idea to remove them before brushing to avoid pulling on your natural hair.

Tip #2: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Silk pillowcases are a game-changer when it comes to protecting hair extensions while you sleep. Unlike cotton, silk is gentle on hair and won't cause friction or tangles. Plus, it helps to keep hair hydrated by preventing moisture loss.

If you don't have a silk pillowcase, consider investing in one. Not only will it benefit your hair, but it's also great for your skin!

Tip #3: Tie Your Hair Up or Braid It

If you have long hair extensions, it's a good idea to tie them up or braid them before bed. This will help to keep them from getting tangled or knotted while you sleep. Use a soft hair tie or scrunchie to avoid pulling on your hair.

If you have short hair extensions, you can try wrapping them in a silk scarf or bonnet to keep them in place.

Tip #4: Avoid Sleeping with Wet Hair Extensions

Wet hair is more prone to damage and breakage, so it's best to avoid sleeping with wet extensions. Make sure to dry your hair thoroughly before hitting the hay. If you're short on time, you can use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to speed up the process.

Tip #5: Use a Hair Net or Scarf

If you're worried about your extensions getting caught on something while you sleep, you can use a hair net or scarf to protect them. Simply wrap your hair up in the net or scarf before bed, and you're good to go.

FAQs

Q: Can I sleep with my hair extensions down?

A: While it's not recommended to sleep with your hair extensions down, it's not necessarily harmful. Just make sure to brush your hair thoroughly before bed to prevent tangling.

Q: How often should I wash my hair extensions?

A: It depends on how often you wear them and how much product you use. As a general rule, you should wash your extensions every 6-8 weeks. If you wear them daily or use a lot of product, you may need to wash them more often.

Q: Can I use heat tools on my hair extensions?

A: Yes, you can use heat tools on most types of hair extensions. However, it's important to use a heat protectant spray and to avoid using high heat settings, as this can damage the extensions.

Hair Care Tips for Extensions

While sleeping with hair extensions is important, it's also crucial to take care of them during the day. Here are some hair care tips to keep your extensions looking their best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent drying out your extensions.
  • Avoid using excessive heat on your extensions, as this can cause damage.
  • Brush your extensions regularly to prevent tangling and matting.
  • Avoid using oil-based products, as these can weigh down your extensions and cause buildup.
  • When swimming, wear a swim cap or tie your hair up to prevent chlorine or saltwater damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can protect your hair extensions while sleeping and keep them looking gorgeous for longer. Sweet dreams!
