How To Cut Men s Hair At Home With Scissors Only

Written by Robby Jun 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of spending money and time getting your hair cut at a salon or barbershop? With the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut men's hair at home with scissors only. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to do it yourself.

Description

Cutting your own hair might seem intimidating, but with some practice and patience, it can be done. By learning how to cut men's hair at home with scissors only, you can save money and time. This guide will provide you with the necessary steps and tips to achieve a professional-looking haircut.

How To

Before you begin, make sure you have a good pair of scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It's also important to wash and dry your hair before cutting it.

Step 1: Start with the sides

Using your comb, separate the top of your hair from the sides. Start by cutting the sides with your scissors, working your way from the bottom to the top. Use your comb to hold the hair in place and cut it to your desired length. Repeat on the other side.

Step 2: Trim the back

Using the same technique as the sides, cut the back of your hair to your desired length. Make sure to use your mirror to check if the length is even on both sides.

Step 3: Cut the top

Comb the top of your hair and lift it up with your fingers. Use your scissors to cut the hair to your desired length. For a layered look, cut the hair at an angle. For a straight look, cut the hair straight across.

Step 4: Blend the hair

Use your scissors to blend the hair between the sides and the top. This will create a seamless transition and a more natural look.

Step 5: Check for evenness

Use your mirror to check if the length is even on all sides. If you notice any areas that need to be trimmed, use your scissors to make the necessary adjustments.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and make small cuts.
  • Use your mirror to check your progress throughout the cutting process.

FAQ

Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

While regular scissors can be used, it's recommended to use scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair. Hair cutting scissors are sharper and more precise, making it easier to achieve a professional-looking haircut.

How often should I cut my hair?

It's recommended to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the length and style.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it to maintain its health and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using hot styling tools too often. It's also recommended to get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Cutting men's hair at home with scissors only can seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it's a simple and cost-effective way to maintain your hairstyle. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut in the comfort of your own home.
