How To Know If An Undercut Will Suit You

Written by Robby Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a new haircut that's trendy, stylish, and easy to maintain, an undercut might be the right choice for you. This edgy hairstyle has been popular for many years, and it doesn't seem to be going anywhere soon. However, not everyone can pull off an undercut, and it's essential to consider a few factors before making the cut. In this article, we'll discuss how to know if an undercut will suit you, and we'll provide you with some tips to ensure that you end up with a style that looks great on you.

  1. How to
  2. Step by step
  3. Tips

How to

The first step in determining whether an undercut will suit you is to consider your face shape. Not all face shapes are suitable for this hairstyle, so it's important to assess your face shape before making the cut. Generally, undercut hairstyles look great on people with oval, square, and heart-shaped faces. If you have a round face, an undercut might not be the best option for you.

Another factor to consider is your hair type. Undercuts work best on hair that is thick and straight. If you have fine or curly hair, an undercut might not be the best choice for you. However, if you have curly hair, you can still opt for a curly undercut, which is a variation of the hairstyle that works great on curly hair.

Step by step

If you've assessed your face shape and hair type and have determined that an undercut will suit you, the next step is to find a good hairstylist. Undercuts require precision cutting, and it's essential to find a stylist who has experience with this hairstyle. You can ask for recommendations from friends, or you can search online for reviews of local salons and stylists.

Before getting the cut, it's essential to have a consultation with your stylist. During this consultation, you can discuss the type of undercut you want and how it will work with your hair type and face shape. Your stylist can also provide you with some styling tips and product recommendations to help you maintain your new hairstyle.

Tips

If you're still unsure whether an undercut will suit you, you can try a faux undercut first. This style involves shaving the hair on one side of your head and sweeping the remaining hair over the shaved area. This way, you can get a feel for the style without committing to a full undercut.

Another tip is to consider the length of the undercut. If you're not comfortable with a short undercut, you can opt for a longer version, which is also known as a disconnected undercut. This style features longer hair on top and a shorter undercut on the sides and back.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with an undercut?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair up with an undercut. In fact, the undercut can make it easier to style your hair in an updo because it provides a clean and smooth base.

Q: Will an undercut work with a beard?

A: Yes, an undercut can work well with a beard. In fact, many men opt for this hairstyle because it complements their facial hair.

Hair Care

After getting an undercut, it's important to take care of your hair to ensure that it stays healthy and looks great. You should wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often. You can also use styling products like pomade or wax to create different looks with your undercut.

Overall, an undercut can be a great hairstyle choice if you have the right face shape and hair type. By considering these factors and following the tips and advice provided in this article, you can determine whether an undercut will suit you and enjoy a trendy and stylish new look.
