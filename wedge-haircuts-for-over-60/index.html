Wedge Haircuts For Over 60 A Timeless Style

Written by Robby Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and looking for a new haircut, consider the timeless style of a wedge haircut. This classic look has been around for decades and is still popular today. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about wedge haircuts for over 60, including how to get the look, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get the Look
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for a Great Wedge Haircut
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for a Wedge Haircut

How to Get the Look

A wedge haircut is a short, layered cut that angles downward from the back to the front. It's a flattering style for many face shapes and can be customized to fit your unique features. To get the look, you'll need to find a stylist who specializes in wedge haircuts. Look for someone who has experience working with mature clients and can recommend a style that will suit your hair type and face shape.

Before your appointment, it's a good idea to bring in photos of wedge haircuts you like. This will give your stylist a better idea of what you're looking for and can help them make suggestions. You should also be prepared to talk about your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling your hair each day.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's how to get a wedge haircut in five easy steps:

  1. Wash and dry your hair before your appointment.
  2. Discuss your desired style with your stylist.
  3. Sit back and relax while your stylist cuts and styles your hair.
  4. If necessary, have your hair colored or highlighted.
  5. Enjoy your new look!

Tips for a Great Wedge Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a wedge haircut:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to opt for longer layers.
  • Consider your hair type. If you have fine hair, a shorter cut may be best to add volume. If you have thick hair, a longer cut may be more manageable.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with color. Highlights or lowlights can add depth and dimension to your cut.
  • Maintain your cut with regular trims. A wedge haircut typically needs to be trimmed every four to six weeks.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wedge haircuts for over 60:

Q: Will a wedge haircut work for my hair type?

A: Yes, a wedge haircut can work for many hair types, including fine, thick, curly, and straight.

Q: How do I style a wedge haircut?

A: Styling a wedge haircut is easy. Simply blow dry your hair with a round brush and use a bit of styling product to add volume and hold.

Q: How often do I need to get my wedge haircut trimmed?

A: A wedge haircut typically needs to be trimmed every four to six weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Hair Care for a Wedge Haircut

Here are some tips for caring for your wedge haircut:

  • Wash your hair every two to three days to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a wedge haircut with confidence and style. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or just a subtle update, this classic look is sure to turn heads and make you feel fabulous. So why wait? Book your appointment today and get ready to feel like a million bucks!
