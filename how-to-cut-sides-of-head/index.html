How To Cut Sides Of Head A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Nov 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to give yourself a fresh new haircut, learning how to cut the sides of your head is a great place to start. This technique can help you achieve a sleek, modern look without having to visit a salon. In this guide, we'll go over the steps you need to take to cut the sides of your head, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Sides of Head
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting the Sides of Your Head
  • FAQs About Cutting the Sides of Your Head
  • Hair Care After Cutting the Sides of Your Head

How to Cut Sides of Head

The first thing you'll need to do is gather your supplies. You'll need a pair of scissors, a comb, and some hair clippers. Once you have everything you need, you can get started.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut the sides of your head:

  1. Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to work with.
  2. Use the comb to separate the top of your hair from the sides. Clip the top section out of the way.
  3. Using the clippers, choose the guard size you want to use. Start with a larger guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  4. Starting at the bottom of your hairline, use the clippers to cut upward. Use a fluid motion and move the clippers up and out to blend the hair.
  5. Work your way up the sides of your head, using the same technique. Be sure to blend the hair as you go.
  6. Once you've finished cutting the sides of your head with the clippers, use the scissors to trim any longer hairs or uneven spots.
  7. Blend the hair between the top and sides by using the scissors to cut at a diagonal angle.
  8. Check your work in the mirror and make any final adjustments as needed.

Tips for Cutting the Sides of Your Head

Here are some tips to remember when cutting the sides of your head:

  • Go slow and be patient. It's better to take your time and get it right than to rush and make mistakes.
  • Start with a larger guard on your clippers and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Use a comb to keep the hair in place as you cut.
  • Be sure to blend the hair as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

FAQs About Cutting the Sides of Your Head

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting the sides of your head:

  • How often should I cut the sides of my head? It's up to you and your personal style. Some people prefer to get a haircut every few weeks, while others may go longer between cuts.
  • What if I make a mistake? Don't panic! You can always trim a little more hair off if needed. If you're really worried, consider visiting a professional stylist for help.
  • Can I cut the sides of my head with just scissors? While it's possible, using clippers will give you a more even and precise cut.

Hair Care After Cutting the Sides of Your Head

After cutting the sides of your head, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair smooth and moisturized.
  • Trim the sides of your head regularly to maintain your style.

Learning how to cut the sides of your head can be a great way to save money and achieve a fresh new look. By following these steps and tips, you can give yourself a great haircut right at home. Good luck!
