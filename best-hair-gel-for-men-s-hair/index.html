The Best Hair Gel For Men s Hair In 2023 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to styling men's hair, hair gel is a popular choice. However, with so many options on the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one for your hair type and desired style. In this article, we will break down the best hair gels for men's hair in 2023, how to use them, and tips for achieving your desired look.

What is Hair Gel?

Hair gel is a styling product that is used to hold hair in place. It is typically made up of water, alcohol, and polymers that work together to create a firm hold. Hair gel can be used on a variety of hair types and styles, from short to long, curly to straight.

How to Use Hair Gel

Using hair gel is simple, but there are some key steps to follow to achieve the best results:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

It's important to start with clean, dry hair to ensure that the gel can properly adhere to your strands. If your hair is wet, the gel will not be able to hold as well.

Step 2: Apply the Gel

Squeeze a small amount of gel into your palm and rub your hands together to distribute it evenly. Then, apply the gel to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the tips. Be sure to use enough gel to fully coat your hair.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair as desired. If you want a slicked-back look, use a comb to smooth your hair back. If you want a messy, textured look, use your fingers to create definition.

The Best Hair Gels for Men's Hair in 2023

Now that you know how to use hair gel, let's dive into the best options on the market:

1. American Crew Firm Hold Styling Gel

American Crew is a well-known brand in the men's grooming industry, and their Firm Hold Styling Gel is a top pick. It provides a strong hold without leaving hair feeling stiff or crunchy, and it's easy to wash out.

2. Redken Brews Stand Tough Extreme Gel

This high-hold gel from Redken Brews is perfect for men with thicker hair. It provides a strong hold that lasts all day, and it has a matte finish for a natural look.

3. Jack Black Body-Building Hair Gel

If you're looking for a hair gel that also adds volume, Jack Black's Body-Building Hair Gel is a great option. It provides a medium hold and helps to thicken hair for a fuller look.

Tips for Achieving Your Desired Look

When using hair gel, there are some tips and tricks that can help you achieve your desired look:

1. Use a Small Amount

It's important to use the right amount of hair gel. Using too much can lead to a stiff, crunchy look. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to try different styles with your hair gel. Play around with slicked-back looks, messy styles, and everything in between until you find what works best for you.

3. Don't Overwork Your Hair

Once you've styled your hair with gel, try not to touch it too much. Overworking your hair can cause the gel to break down, leading to a less-than-perfect hold.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the Difference Between Hair Gel and Hair Wax?

Hair wax provides a more flexible hold than hair gel. It's better suited for textured, messy styles, while hair gel works best for slicked-back or defined looks.

Can Hair Gel Damage Your Hair?

When used in moderation, hair gel is unlikely to cause any damage to your hair. However, using too much or using it too frequently can lead to dryness and breakage.

Can Hair Gel Cause Dandruff?

Using hair gel can contribute to dandruff, as it can build up on the scalp and cause irritation. To avoid this, be sure to wash your hair regularly and use a clarifying shampoo once a week.

Hair Care for Men: Final Thoughts

Choosing the right hair gel for your hair type and desired style is key to achieving a polished, put-together look. With the right product and some styling tips, you can create a look that's both stylish and functional. Whether you prefer a slicked-back look or a messy, textured style, there's a hair gel out there that's perfect for you.
