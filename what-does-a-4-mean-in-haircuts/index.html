What Does A 4 Mean In Haircuts

Written by Bardi Nov 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're planning on getting a haircut and the barber or stylist mentions a "4", you might be wondering what they're talking about. In the world of haircuts, a "4" is a specific clipper guard size that determines the length of your hair. In this article, we'll dive deeper into what a 4 means in haircuts, how to achieve this length, tips on styling, and hair care for this look.

Description

A clipper guard is a plastic attachment that fits onto a hair clipper and determines how much hair is cut. Guard sizes range from 0.5 to 8, with 0.5 being the shortest and 8 being the longest. A "4" guard is in the middle range and measures at 1/2 inch or 12.5mm. This means that if you use a 4 guard on your hair, the length of your hair will be half an inch all around.

How to Achieve a 4 Haircut

Getting a 4 haircut is relatively simple, but it's important to go to a professional barber or stylist to achieve a clean and even cut. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Before getting your haircut, make sure to wash your hair thoroughly. This will remove any excess oil or dirt and make it easier for the barber or stylist to work with.

Step 2: Communicate with Your Barber/Stylist

Let your barber or stylist know that you want a 4 haircut. They will then use a clipper with a 4 guard attachment and run it over your head to achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Clean Up the Edges

After the bulk of your hair has been cut, your barber or stylist will use scissors to clean up the edges and make sure everything is even.

Tips for Styling a 4 Haircut

Styling a 4 haircut is relatively easy since the length is uniform all around. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a light hold styling product to keep your hair in place without weighing it down.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as a messy fringe or a slicked-back look.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can make your hair look greasy or oily.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve a 4 haircut at home?

A: While it's possible to achieve a 4 haircut at home, it's recommended to go to a professional barber or stylist to ensure a clean and even cut.

Q: Will a 4 haircut work for all hair types?

A: A 4 haircut can work for most hair types, but it's best to consult with your barber or stylist to see if it's the right length for your hair.

Q: How often should I get a 4 haircut?

A: This depends on how quickly your hair grows and your personal preference. On average, it's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care for a 4 Haircut

Since a 4 haircut is relatively short, it's important to take care of your scalp and hair to maintain its health. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to remove any buildup or excess oil.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, as this can damage your hair and scalp.

Conclusion

A 4 haircut is a classic and versatile length that can work for most hair types. Whether you're looking for a low-maintenance style or want to experiment with different hairstyles, a 4 haircut is a great option. Just remember to go to a professional barber or stylist, communicate your desired length, and take care of your hair to maintain its health.
