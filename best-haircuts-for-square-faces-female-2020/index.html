Best Haircuts For Square Faces Female 2020

Written by Bardi Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In 2020, the best haircuts for square faces female were all about creating a balance between the sharp angles of the face and the softness of the hair. Whether you have short or long hair, there is a haircut that will flatter your square face shape. In this article, we will explore the best haircuts for square faces female in 2020 and provide tips on how to care for your hair.

Description

A square face has a strong jawline and a broad forehead. The goal of a haircut for a square face is to soften the features and create a more balanced look. The best haircuts for square faces female in 2020 were all about creating layers, adding texture, and incorporating face-framing layers to soften the jawline.

How to

The first step in finding the best haircut for a square face is to determine the length of your hair. Short haircuts, such as a pixie cut or a bob, can be great options for square faces. Long haircuts, such as long layers or a shag, can also work well. The key is to create movement and texture.

Short Haircuts

Short haircuts can be a great option for square faces as they can help to balance out the strong jawline. A pixie cut can look great on a square face, especially if it is styled with texture and volume. A bob can also work well, especially if it is cut with layers and styled with waves or curls.

Long Haircuts

Long haircuts can also be a great option for square faces, especially if they are styled with layers and texture. A shag haircut can be a great option as it creates movement and texture in the hair. Long layers can also work well, especially if they are styled with waves or curls.

Step by Step

To get the best haircut for your square face, follow these steps:

  1. Determine the length of your hair
  2. Choose a haircut that adds movement and texture
  3. Incorporate face-framing layers to soften the jawline
  4. Style your hair with waves or curls to create softness

Tips

When choosing a haircut for a square face, it is important to consider your hair type and texture. If you have fine hair, a short haircut may not be the best option as it can make your hair look flat. If you have thick hair, a long haircut may not be the best option as it can make your hair look bulky. It is also important to consider your lifestyle and how much time you are willing to spend styling your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs with a square face?

A: Yes, you can wear bangs with a square face. Side-swept bangs can be a great option as they can help to soften the forehead and create balance.

Q: How often should I get my hair cut?

A: It is recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking its best, it is important to take care of it. This includes washing your hair regularly, using a conditioner to keep it hydrated, and using a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools. It is also important to get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, the best haircuts for square faces female in 2020 were all about creating balance and softness. Whether you have short or long hair, there is a haircut that will flatter your square face shape. By following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can find the perfect haircut for your square face and keep your hair looking its best.
