Best Men s Haircuts For Oblong Faces A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 20, 2023 · 4 min read

When it comes to choosing a haircut, it’s important to consider your face shape. If you have an oblong face, you want to choose a style that will help balance out your features. In this article, we will discuss the best men’s haircuts for oblong faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oblong Faces
  • Step by Step Guide to Get the Perfect Haircut
  • Tips for Styling Your Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Oblong Faces

Description

An oblong face is characterized by a long, narrow shape with a high forehead and prominent chin. The goal when choosing a haircut for this face shape is to create balance by adding width to the sides of the face. This can be achieved through certain styles and techniques.

How to Choose the Best Haircut for Oblong Faces

When choosing a haircut for an oblong face, you want to consider styles that add width and volume to the sides of the face. Some popular options include:

  • Short sides and long top
  • Textured crop
  • Side-swept fringe
  • Pompadour
  • Buzz cut
  • Crew cut
  • Quiff

It’s important to also consider your hair type and texture when choosing a style. For example, if you have thick, curly hair, a textured crop or quiff may work better for you than a buzz cut or crew cut.

Step by Step Guide to Get the Perfect Haircut

Once you’ve chosen a style, it’s important to communicate your desired look to your stylist. Here is a step by step guide to getting the perfect haircut:

  1. Bring a photo of the style you want
  2. Communicate your hair type and texture to your stylist
  3. Ask for advice on how to style your hair at home
  4. Book regular appointments for trims and maintenance

Remember, communication is key when it comes to getting the perfect haircut. Don’t be afraid to ask questions or speak up if you’re not happy with the results.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you have your new haircut, it’s important to know how to style it. Here are some tips for styling your hair:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to lay
  • Use a styling product, such as pomade or wax, to add texture and hold
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works for you

Remember, it may take some trial and error to find the perfect style and products for your hair. Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on what products to use.

FAQs

What hairstyles should I avoid with an oblong face?

You should avoid hairstyles that make your face look even longer, such as slicked-back styles or center parts. These styles can accentuate the length of your face and make it appear even more narrow.

Can I still have long hair with an oblong face?

Yes, you can still have long hair with an oblong face. However, it’s important to add layers and volume to the sides of your face to create balance. A long, straight cut may not be the best option for this face shape.

How often should I get a haircut?

It’s recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your style and keep your hair healthy.

Hair Care Tips for Oblong Faces

In addition to choosing the right haircut and styling your hair, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some hair care tips for oblong faces:

  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, too often
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and vibrant, no matter what style you choose.

Conclusion

Choosing the right haircut for an oblong face can enhance your features and create balance. By considering your face shape, hair type, and personal style, you can find the perfect haircut to suit your needs. Remember to communicate with your stylist, experiment with different styles, and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
