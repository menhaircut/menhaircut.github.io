Men s Medium Long Hairstyles 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Aug 21, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're a man with medium long hair, you may be wondering what the latest trends are for 2020. This guide will provide you with everything you need to know about men's medium long hairstyles, including how to style your hair, tips for maintaining your hair, and answers to frequently asked questions.

Description

Men's medium long hairstyles have become increasingly popular in recent years. This style is perfect for men who want to keep their hair longer than a traditional short cut but don't want to commit to a full-length mane. It's versatile, easy to style, and can be worn in a variety of ways.

How To Style Men's Medium Long Hair

Styling men's medium long hair can be easy if you know what you're doing. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step in styling men's medium long hair is to wash it thoroughly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type, and make sure to rinse out all the product.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

Once you've washed your hair, use a towel to dry it. Be gentle and avoid rubbing your hair too vigorously, as this can cause damage.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Next, apply a styling product to your hair. There are a variety of products to choose from, including mousse, gel, and pomade. Choose a product that works well with your hair type and the look you're trying to achieve.

Step 4: Blow Dry Your Hair

Use a blow dryer to dry your hair while using a brush or comb to style it. This will help you achieve the desired shape and volume.

Step 5: Finish With Hairspray

Finally, finish your look with a light mist of hairspray. This will help your hair hold its shape throughout the day.

Tips for Maintaining Men's Medium Long Hair

Keeping men's medium long hair healthy and looking great requires some maintenance. Here are a few tips to help you maintain your hair:

Tip 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

Even if you're growing your hair out, it's important to trim it regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Tip 2: Use a Conditioner

Using a conditioner can help keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage. Look for a conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.

Tip 3: Protect Your Hair From Heat

Heat can cause damage to your hair, so it's important to protect it when using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners. Use a heat protectant spray before using these tools.

FAQ

What Are Some Popular Men's Medium Long Hairstyles for 2020?

Some popular men's medium long hairstyles for 2020 include the shaggy cut, the textured crop, and the messy fringe.

Can I Style Men's Medium Long Hair in Different Ways?

Yes! Men's medium long hair is versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, and curly.

How Often Should I Wash My Men's Medium Long Hair?

It's best to wash men's medium long hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too dry.

Hair Care Products for Men's Medium Long Hair

There are a variety of hair care products that are perfect for men's medium long hair. Here are a few of our favorites:

Product 1: American Crew Fiber

American Crew Fiber is a popular styling product that provides a firm hold and matte finish. It's perfect for creating texture and definition in men's medium long hair.

Product 2: Redken Brews Daily Conditioner

Redken Brews Daily Conditioner is a lightweight conditioner that helps hydrate and detangle men's medium long hair. It's perfect for daily use.

Product 3: TRESemmé Thermal Creations Heat Protectant Spray

TRESemmé Thermal Creations Heat Protectant Spray is a must-have for men who use hot tools on their hair. It helps protect hair from damage caused by heat and provides a light hold.

The Bottom Line

Men's medium long hairstyles are trendy, versatile, and easy to style. By following the tips and steps outlined in this guide, you can achieve the perfect look for 2020 and beyond.
