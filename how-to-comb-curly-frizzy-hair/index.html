How to Comb Curly Frizzy Hair

Step 1: Use the Right Tools

Start by using a wide-tooth comb or a detangling brush specifically designed for curly hair. Avoid using a regular brush, as this can cause more frizz and breakage.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Before combing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your curls. This will make it easier to comb through your hair without causing more frizz.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to comb through. Use clips or hair ties to keep each section in place.

Step 4: Start Combing

Starting at the bottom of each section, gently comb through your hair using the wide-tooth comb or detangling brush. Work your way up, being careful not to tug or pull at your hair.

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair, focusing on the ends and any particularly dry or frizzy areas.
  3. Use a wide-tooth comb or detangling brush to gently comb through your hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Be sure to detangle each section before moving on to the next.

  6. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently fluff your curls and create volume.

Tips for Combating Frizz

In addition to combing your hair properly, there are some other things you can do to combat frizz:

  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as these can dry out your hair and create more frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, which can create friction and cause frizz.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can disrupt the curl pattern and create more frizz.
  • Invest in a satin or silk pillowcase to help prevent frizz while you sleep.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

In addition to combing your hair properly and using the right products, there are some other things you can do to care for your curly hair:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause dryness and frizz.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair and cause more frizz.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.
