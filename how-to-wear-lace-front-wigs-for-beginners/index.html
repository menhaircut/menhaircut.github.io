How To Wear Lace Front Wigs For Beginners A Step By Step Guide

Are you new to wearing lace front wigs? Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll guide you through the process of wearing a lace front wig, from how to put it on to how to take care of it. We'll also answer some frequently asked questions and give you some tips to make the process easier. So, let's get started!

How to Wear a Lace Front Wig

A lace front wig is a type of wig that has a sheer lace panel along the front hairline. The lace front allows for a natural-looking hairline and can be styled in different ways. Here's how to wear a lace front wig:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

To wear a lace front wig, you need to prepare your hair first. If you have long hair, you'll need to braid it or put it in a low bun. If your hair is short, you can use a wig cap to keep it flat against your head.

Step 2: Put on the Wig Cap

Put on a wig cap that matches your skin tone. This will help keep your wig in place and protect your natural hair. Make sure the cap is tight enough to hold your hair in place but not too tight that it causes discomfort.

Step 3: Adjust the Wig

Put the wig on and adjust it so that it sits comfortably on your head. The ear tabs should be aligned with your ears, and the back of the wig should be pulled down to the nape of your neck. Use the adjustable straps to tighten or loosen the wig as needed.

Step 4: Cut the Lace

Using a pair of scissors, cut the lace along the hairline. Be careful not to cut too much, as you don't want to damage the wig. You can also use tweezers to pluck some of the hairs along the hairline to make it look more natural.

Step 5: Style Your Wig

Style your wig as desired, using a wig brush or comb. You can use heat styling tools like curling irons and straighteners, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray first. You can also use hair products like hairspray and mousse to hold your style in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear a lace front wig:

  1. Prepare your hair by braiding it or putting it in a low bun.
  2. Put on a wig cap that matches your skin tone.
  3. Put the wig on and adjust it so that it sits comfortably on your head.
  4. Cut the lace along the hairline using a pair of scissors.
  5. Style your wig using a wig brush or comb.

Tips for Wearing a Lace Front Wig

Here are some tips to make wearing a lace front wig easier:

  • Use a wig cap to keep your natural hair in place and protect it from the wig.
  • Choose a wig that matches your skin tone and hair type.
  • Be gentle when brushing or combing your wig to avoid damaging it.
  • Use heat styling tools and hair products designed for wigs.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape and prevent tangling.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about wearing lace front wigs:

Q: How long can I wear a lace front wig?

A: You can wear a lace front wig for up to 6 weeks at a time, depending on how well you take care of it.

Q: Can I sleep in my lace front wig?

A: It's not recommended to sleep in your lace front wig, as it can cause tangling and damage to the wig.

Q: Can I swim in my lace front wig?

A: It's not recommended to swim in your lace front wig, as chlorine and salt water can damage the wig.

Hair Care Tips for Your Wig

Here are some tips for taking care of your lace front wig:

  • Wash your wig every 2-3 weeks with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water or rubbing the wig when washing it.
  • Pat the wig dry with a towel and let it air dry on a wig stand.
  • Use a wig detangler or leave-in conditioner to keep the hair soft and manageable.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

By following these tips and steps, you'll be able to wear your lace front wig with confidence and style. Remember to take care of your wig to ensure it lasts as long as possible. Good luck!
