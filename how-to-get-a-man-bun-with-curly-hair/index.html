How To Get A Man Bun With Curly Hair A Complete Guide

If you have curly hair, you might think that getting a man bun is impossible. However, with a little bit of patience and the right techniques, you can definitely achieve this trendy look. In this article, we will go through the steps you need to take to get a man bun with curly hair, including some tips and tricks to make the process easier. Let's get started!

How to Get a Man Bun with Curly Hair

Before we dive into the steps, it's important to understand that getting a man bun with curly hair requires a bit more effort than if you had straight hair. You will need to train your curls to stay in place and use the right products to keep them under control. With that being said, here are the basic steps:

1. Grow Your Hair Out

The first step to getting a man bun is to have long hair. Depending on your hair type, this might take a few months or even a year. You will need to be patient and resist the urge to cut your hair when it gets too unruly. The longer your hair is, the easier it will be to create a bun.

2. Determine Your Hair Type

Curly hair comes in many shapes and sizes, from loose waves to tight coils. Before you start styling your hair, you need to determine your hair type. This will help you choose the right products and techniques to achieve the best results.

3. Use the Right Products

To get a man bun with curly hair, you will need to use the right products. This includes a good shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized, as well as a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and prevent frizz. You might also want to use a styling gel or wax to help hold your hair in place.

4. Train Your Curls

If you have curly hair, you know that your curls have a mind of their own. To get them to stay in place, you will need to train them. This means brushing or combing your hair in the direction you want it to go and using a hair tie to secure it in a bun. Over time, your curls will start to adapt to this new shape.

5. Practice

Getting a man bun with curly hair takes practice. You might need to try a few different techniques and products before you find what works best for you. Don't get discouraged if your first attempts don't turn out the way you want them to. Keep practicing and you will get there!

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know the basic steps, let's go through them in more detail:

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

The first step is to wash and condition your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair. This will help keep your hair moisturized and prevent frizz.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner or Curl Cream

After you wash your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls. This will help them stay in place when you create your bun.

Step 3: Brush or Comb Your Hair

Brush or comb your hair in the direction you want it to go. If you want a high bun, brush your hair straight up. If you want a low bun, brush your hair back. Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your curls.

Step 4: Secure Your Hair in a Bun

Use a hair tie to secure your hair in a bun. If you have a lot of hair, you might need to use two hair ties. Make sure the bun is tight enough to stay in place, but not so tight that it pulls on your scalp.

Step 5: Use Styling Products (Optional)

If you want your bun to stay in place all day, you can use a styling gel or wax. Apply a small amount to your hair and use your fingers to shape your bun. This will help hold your hair in place and prevent flyaways.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to help you get the perfect man bun with curly hair:

  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz while you sleep.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry. This can cause frizz and damage your curls.
  • Experiment with different hair ties to find what works best for you. Some people prefer elastic hair ties, while others prefer scrunchies or cloth hair ties.
  • If you have trouble getting your bun to stay in place, try using bobby pins to secure it.

FAQs

Can you get a man bun with short curly hair?

It's possible to get a man bun with short curly hair, but it will be more difficult. You will need to grow your hair out and use styling products to keep it in place.

How do you prevent frizz when creating a man bun with curly hair?

To prevent frizz, use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls. You can also use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent friction while you sleep.

How often should you wash curly hair?

Curly hair is more prone to dryness, so you should only wash it once or twice a week. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair to keep your hair moisturized.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Getting a man bun with curly hair requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your curls healthy:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your curls.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a brush with soft bristles to avoid damaging your curls.
  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to define your curls and prevent frizz.

With these tips and techniques, you can get a great-looking man bun with curly hair. Remember to be patient and experiment with different products and techniques until you find what works best for you. Good luck!
