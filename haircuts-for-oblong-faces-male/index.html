Best Haircuts For Oblong Faces Male

Are you tired of your hair looking dull and lifeless? Do you feel like your hair is just not doing you any favors? If you have an oblong face, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. But don't worry, we've got you covered with this comprehensive guide on the best haircuts for oblong faces male.

Description

An oblong face is defined by having a longer face shape with a narrow width. This face shape tends to have a more prominent forehead and a longer chin. The goal of a haircut for an oblong face is to create balance and proportion by adding width to the sides of the face while keeping the length on top.

How to

When it comes to choosing the right haircut for your oblong face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to avoid haircuts that are too long or too short. A haircut that is too long can make your face look even longer, while a haircut that is too short can make your face look too narrow.

You also want to avoid haircuts with too much volume on top, as this can make your face look even longer. Instead, opt for a haircut that has some volume on the sides to create the illusion of width.

Step by step

Here are the steps to take when choosing the best haircut for your oblong face:

  1. Identify your face shape: Look in the mirror and determine if you have an oblong face shape.
  2. Research different haircuts: Look for haircuts that are designed for oblong face shapes.
  3. Consult with a hairstylist: Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on which haircut would work best for your face shape.
  4. Consider your lifestyle: Choose a haircut that fits your lifestyle and is easy to maintain.
  5. Experiment with different styles: Once you've found the right haircut, try out different styles to see what works best for you.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut for an oblong face:

  • Choose a haircut with some volume on the sides to create the illusion of width.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too long or too short.
  • Consider your lifestyle when choosing a haircut.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for you.
  • Ask your hairstylist for their opinion on which haircut would work best for your face shape.

FAQ

What is the best haircut for an oblong face?

The best haircut for an oblong face is one that adds width to the sides of the face while keeping the length on top. This can be achieved with a layered haircut or a haircut with some volume on the sides.

Can an oblong face pull off a buzz cut?

An oblong face can pull off a buzz cut, but it's important to consider the length of the buzz cut. A buzz cut that is too short can make the face look even longer, while a buzz cut that is slightly longer can create the illusion of width on the sides of the face.

Hair Care

Once you've found the perfect haircut for your oblong face, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and smooth.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a styling product to create volume and texture.

With these tips and tricks, you're sure to find the perfect haircut for your oblong face and keep your hair looking its best.
