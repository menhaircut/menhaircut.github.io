Will A Long Pixie Cut Suit Me

Written by Aprilia Jun 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a new hairstyle and wondering if a long pixie cut would suit you? Pixie cuts have been a popular hairstyle choice for many years and can be a great way to freshen up your look. However, it is important to consider whether a long pixie cut would suit your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. In this article, we will explore the factors to consider when deciding if a long pixie cut is right for you.

Description

A long pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is longer than a traditional pixie cut. It typically features longer layers on top that can be styled in a variety of ways, such as swept back or to the side. The sides and back are usually tapered or cut short, creating a more dramatic look than a bob or lob.

How to

The first step in determining if a long pixie cut will suit you is to consider your face shape. Pixie cuts tend to look best on oval, heart-shaped, or square faces. If you have a round face, a long pixie cut may not be the best choice as it can accentuate the roundness.

Next, consider your hair type. A long pixie cut can work well on straight or wavy hair, but may not be the best choice for curly or thick hair. If you have curly or thick hair, you may want to consider a different short hairstyle that will be easier to manage.

When deciding if a long pixie cut will suit your lifestyle, consider how much time you are willing to spend on styling your hair. A long pixie cut can require more maintenance than a traditional pixie cut as the longer layers may need to be styled daily to maintain the desired look.

Step by Step

If you have decided that a long pixie cut is right for you, here are some steps to take when getting the cut:

  1. Research and find a salon or stylist that has experience with pixie cuts.
  2. Bring pictures of the style you want to show your stylist.
  3. Discuss your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle with your stylist to ensure the cut will suit you.
  4. Maintain the cut by getting regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling your long pixie cut:

  • Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add volume and definition to your layers.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that works best for you.
  • If you are unsure about committing to a long pixie cut, consider trying a shorter pixie cut first to see how you like it.

FAQ

Will a long pixie cut suit me if I have thin hair?

Yes, a long pixie cut can be a great choice for those with thin hair as the longer layers can add volume and texture to the hair.

Can a long pixie cut be styled for a formal event?

Yes, a long pixie cut can be styled in a variety of ways for a formal event, such as adding accessories or styling the longer layers into an updo.

What face shape is best suited for a long pixie cut?

A long pixie cut tends to look best on oval, heart-shaped, or square faces.

Hair Care

Maintaining a long pixie cut requires regular trims to keep the shape looking fresh. Using a texturizing spray or pomade can help add volume and definition to the layers. It is also important to use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools to prevent damage to the hair.

In conclusion, a long pixie cut can be a great way to switch up your hairstyle and add some edge to your look. However, it is important to consider your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle before committing to the cut. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can rock a long pixie cut with confidence.
