How To Cut A Girl s Hair Like A Boy A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking to give a girl's hair a more masculine look, you may be wondering how to cut a girl's hair like a boy. It's a popular trend that's not going away anytime soon. Whether you're a professional stylist or a parent looking to give your child a new look, this guide will walk you through the steps of cutting a girl's hair like a boy.

Description

Cutting a girl's hair like a boy involves creating a shorter, more angular look that is typically associated with boys' hairstyles. This can include a shorter length, layers, and a more textured finish. It's important to keep in mind that every girl's hair is different, and the style you choose will depend on her hair type and texture.

How to Cut a Girl's Hair Like a Boy

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

Before you start cutting, make sure you have all the necessary tools on hand. You'll need a pair of sharp haircutting scissors, a comb, and possibly clippers if you're going for a more extreme look.

Step 2: Decide on the Length

The first step is to decide on the length you want to cut the hair. If you're not sure, start longer and gradually work your way shorter. Remember, it's easier to cut more hair off than it is to add it back on.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Section off the hair into manageable sections using clips. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Starting at the back of the head, use your scissors to cut the hair in a straight line. Work your way around the head, cutting each section to the desired length.

Step 5: Create Texture

To create a more textured look, use your scissors to cut into the hair at an angle. This will create a choppy, layered effect.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Once you've finished cutting, style the hair as desired. This may include using product or blow-drying the hair into place.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting a girl's hair like a boy:

  • Start longer and work your way shorter to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Use a comb to ensure that each section is even.
  • Don't be afraid to use clippers for a more extreme look.
  • Take breaks if necessary to avoid cutting too much off at once.
  • Ask the girl what she wants and make sure she's comfortable with the style.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a girl's hair like a boy:

Can any girl's hair be cut like a boy's?

It depends on the girl's hair type and texture. Talk to a professional stylist if you're not sure.

Do I need to use clippers?

Clippers can be used for a more extreme look, but they aren't necessary.

How short should I go?

The length you choose will depend on the girl's preference and hair type. Start longer and work your way shorter.

Hair Care

Once you've cut the hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that is designed for the girl's hair type, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

Now that you know how to cut a girl's hair like a boy, you can create a new, trendy look that is sure to turn heads. Just remember to take your time, be patient, and have fun!
