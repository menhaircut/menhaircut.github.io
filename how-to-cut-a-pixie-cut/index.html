How To Cut A Pixie Cut Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a dramatic change in your hairstyle, a pixie cut is a great option. This trendy haircut is low-maintenance and versatile, making it perfect for women on-the-go. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut a pixie cut step-by-step. We'll also provide you with tips on how to care for your new haircut and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Cut a Pixie Cut

Cutting a pixie cut requires some skill and practice, so if you're not confident in your abilities, it's best to visit a professional. However, if you're feeling adventurous and want to attempt it yourself, here's how:

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clippers with a guard. You may also need hair clips to section off your hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it. Blow-dry your hair until it's completely dry.

Step 3: Section Off Your Hair

Use hair clips to section off your hair into four parts: the top, sides, and back. Start with the top section first.

Step 4: Cut the Top Section

Starting at the crown of your head, use your comb to lift a section of hair. Hold it between your fingers and cut it to your desired length. Repeat this process until you've cut all of the hair in the top section.

Step 5: Cut the Side Sections

Starting at the front of your head, use your clippers with a guard to cut the hair on the sides of your head. Work your way toward the back, making sure to blend the hair on top with the hair on the sides.

Step 6: Cut the Back Section

Starting at the nape of your neck, use your clippers to cut the hair in the back of your head. Work your way up, making sure to blend the hair on top with the hair in the back.

Step 7: Clean Up the Edges

Use your scissors to clean up any uneven edges and to create a tapered look. Take your time and be careful not to cut too much off.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Use a styling product, such as a wax or pomade, to style your hair. Experiment with different looks to find one that suits you.

Tips for Cutting a Pixie Cut

Here are some tips to help you cut a pixie cut like a pro:

  • Start with a longer length and cut off more as needed. You can always cut more off, but you can't put it back on.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Take breaks as needed to avoid fatigue and mistakes.
  • Consider investing in professional-grade tools for better results.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cuts:

How often do I need to trim my pixie cut?

You should trim your pixie cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Can I color my pixie cut?

Yes, you can color your pixie cut. However, it's best to visit a professional to avoid damaging your hair.

How do I style my pixie cut?

You can style your pixie cut in a variety of ways, such as using a wax or pomade to create a messy, textured look, or using a flat iron to create a sleek, polished look.

Hair Care Tips for Pixie Cuts

Here are some hair care tips to keep your pixie cut looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as curling irons and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or applying a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Regularly trim your hair to maintain its shape and style.

Cutting a pixie cut can be a fun and exciting experience. By following these steps and tips, you can achieve a beautiful and trendy hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
