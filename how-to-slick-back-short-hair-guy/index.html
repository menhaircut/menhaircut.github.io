How To Slick Back Short Hair Guy

If you're looking for a timeless and classic hairstyle, the slicked-back look is a great option. This style works for all hair types, including short hair. In this article, we'll show you how to slick back short hair guys in a relaxed and easy-to-follow way.

Table of Contents

  • How to Slick Back Short Hair Guy
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Slicking Back Short Hair
  • FAQs

How to Slick Back Short Hair Guy

The first step in slicking back short hair guys is to make sure your hair is clean and dry. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and easy to manage.

Next, you'll need to apply a styling product to your hair. We recommend using a pomade or wax for a strong hold. Apply a small amount of product to your palms and rub your hands together.

Now, run your hands through your hair, starting at the front and working your way back. Make sure you distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Once you've applied the product, use a comb to slick your hair back. Start at the front of your hairline and work your way back, making sure to smooth down any flyaways or bumps.

Finally, use a hair dryer to lock in the style. Use a low heat setting and aim the dryer at your hair, moving it back and forth until your hair is completely dry.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to slick back short hair guys:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Apply a styling product to your hair
  3. Comb your hair back
  4. Use a hair dryer to lock in the style

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling it.

Step 2: Apply a Styling Product to Your Hair

Take a small amount of pomade or wax and rub it between your palms. Apply the product to your hair, starting at the front and working your way back.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair Back

Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting at the front of your hairline and working your way back. Smooth down any flyaways or bumps as you go.

Step 4: Use a Hair Dryer to Lock in the Style

Use a hair dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair and lock in the style. Move the dryer back and forth until your hair is completely dry.

Tips for Slicking Back Short Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect slicked-back look:

  • Start with clean, dry hair
  • Use a high-quality styling product
  • Apply the product evenly throughout your hair
  • Use a comb to slick your hair back
  • Smooth down any flyaways or bumps
  • Use a hair dryer to lock in the style

FAQs

How long does the slicked-back hairstyle last?

The slicked-back hairstyle can last all day if you use a strong-hold styling product and lock in the style with a hair dryer.

What kind of styling product should I use?

We recommend using a pomade or wax for a strong hold. Look for products that are specifically designed for short hair.

Can I slick back my hair if it's thin or fine?

Yes, you can still slick back your hair if it's thin or fine. Use a light-hold styling product and be gentle when combing your hair back.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a good quality shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

With these tips and techniques, you'll be able to achieve the perfect slicked-back look for short hair guys. Whether you're going for a classic or modern style, this look is sure to turn heads.
