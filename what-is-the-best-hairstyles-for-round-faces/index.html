Best Hairstyles For Round Faces

Written by Bardi Oct 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of constantly searching for the best hairstyles for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive guide on the best hairstyles for round faces. From short to long hair, we've got you covered.

Description

Round faces have full cheeks and a rounded chin. It's important to choose a hairstyle that compliments your facial features and enhances your natural beauty. The right hairstyle can make your face appear more elongated and balanced.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face, it's important to consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair. The right hairstyle can create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

Short Hair

If you have short hair, consider a pixie cut or a bob with volume on top. These styles create the illusion of height and elongate the face. Avoid short haircuts that are too blunt or flat, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Medium Length Hair

If you have medium length hair, consider a layered haircut with volume on top. This style adds dimension to your hair and creates the illusion of a longer face. Avoid blunt cuts and straight hairstyles, as they can make your face appear wider.

Long Hair

If you have long hair, consider a layered haircut with volume on top. This style adds dimension to your hair and creates the illusion of a longer face. Avoid long straight hair, as it can make your face appear wider.

Step by Step

Step 1: Determine your face shape. If you have a round face, choose a hairstyle that compliments your facial features.

Step 2: Consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair. Choose a hairstyle that creates the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

Step 3: Consult with a hairstylist for recommendations on the best hairstyles for your face shape and hair type.

Tips

Tip 1: Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or flat, as they can make your face appear rounder.

Tip 2: Add volume to the top of your hair to create the illusion of height and elongate the face.

Tip 3: Consider using hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add dimension to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but make sure they are side-swept and not too blunt. Side-swept bangs create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face.

Q: Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A: Yes, but avoid pulling your hair too tightly. A loose ponytail or messy bun can create the illusion of height and elongate the face.

Hair Care

Regardless of your hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type and use heat protectant products when using styling tools. Regular trims can also help maintain healthy hair and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, the best hairstyles for round faces are those that create the illusion of a more oval-shaped face. Consider the length, texture, and volume of your hair when choosing a hairstyle. Consult with a hairstylist for recommendations and take care of your hair for healthy, beautiful locks.
