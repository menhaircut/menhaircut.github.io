Curtain Bangs Make You Look Younger A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of looking older than your actual age? Are you looking for a hairstyle that can make you look younger? Well, look no further than curtain bangs. Curtain bangs have been the latest trend in hairstyles, and many people have been embracing it. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to look younger without having to go through the hassle of getting a full haircut. In this article, we will discuss how curtain bangs make you look younger, how to do them, and hair care tips for maintaining them.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curtain bangs are a type of bangs that are parted down the middle, framing the sides of the face. They are called "curtain" bangs because they resemble curtains that are parted in the middle. This hairstyle was popular in the 70s and has made a comeback in recent years. Curtain bangs are perfect for those who don't want to commit to a full bangs hairstyle but still want to change up their look. The best thing about curtain bangs is that they make you look younger.

How to

To achieve curtain bangs, you will need to visit a hairstylist. They will be able to cut your hair in a way that will give you the perfect curtain bangs. You will need to show them a picture of the type of curtain bangs you want. The hairstylist will then cut your hair in a way that will frame your face perfectly. Once you have your curtain bangs, you can style them in different ways. You can either straighten them or curl them, depending on your preference.

Step by step

The following are the steps to achieve curtain bangs:

  1. Visit a hairstylist
  2. Show them a picture of the type of curtain bangs you want
  3. Have them cut your hair in a way that will frame your face perfectly
  4. Style your curtain bangs by either straightening them or curling them

Tips

The following are tips for styling your curtain bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools
  • Use a round brush to blow dry your curtain bangs
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to your curtain bangs to add shine
  • Use a hairspray to keep your curtain bangs in place

FAQ

The following are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

How long do curtain bangs last?

Curtain bangs can last up to 6 weeks before you need to get them trimmed.

Can you wear curtain bangs with glasses?

Yes, you can wear curtain bangs with glasses. In fact, it will make you look younger.

What face shape is best for curtain bangs?

Curtain bangs are best for those with an oval or heart-shaped face.

Hair Care

The following are tips for maintaining your curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your curtain bangs
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your curtain bangs

Curtain bangs are a great way to change up your look and make you look younger. Follow the tips and steps in this article to achieve perfect curtain bangs. Don't forget to take care of your hair to maintain the health and style of your curtain bangs. With curtain bangs, you'll be turning heads and looking younger in no time.
