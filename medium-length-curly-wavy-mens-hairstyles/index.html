Medium Length Curly Wavy Mens Hairstyles Tips How To And Care

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's low maintenance but still stylish, medium length curly wavy haircuts for men are a great option. Whether you have natural curls or waves or want to add some texture to your hair, this type of haircut can work for you. In this article, we'll cover tips on how to style and care for medium length curly wavy hair, as well as some popular styles you can try.

  • How to Style Medium Length Curly Wavy Hair
  • Popular Medium Length Curly Wavy Hairstyles for Men

How to Style Medium Length Curly Wavy Hair

The key to styling medium length curly wavy hair is to embrace your natural texture and work with it rather than trying to fight it. Here are some styling tips:

  • Start with clean, damp hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls or waves.
  • Apply a curl-defining or wave-enhancing product, such as mousse, gel, or cream, to your hair while it's still damp. Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly.
  • If you want more defined curls, you can use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your hair. If you prefer looser waves, let your hair air dry.
  • Once your hair is dry, you can use a curling iron or wand to touch up any areas that need more definition or volume. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.
  • Finish with a hairspray or pomade to hold your style in place.

Popular Medium Length Curly Wavy Hairstyles for Men

There are many different styles you can try with medium length curly wavy hair. Here are some popular options:

  • The messy textured look: This style involves tousled curls or waves that look effortless and messy. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to create this look.
  • The side part: This classic style works well with medium length curly wavy hair. Use a comb to create a deep side part and apply a pomade or gel to keep your hair in place.
  • The undercut: If you want a more edgy look, try an undercut with your medium length curly wavy hair. Shave the sides and back of your head and leave the top longer. Style with a texturizing product.

Hair Care Tips for Medium Length Curly Wavy Hair

Caring for medium length curly wavy hair requires a few extra steps to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your curls or waves.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb when your hair is damp.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray first.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

FAQs

Q: Can I still style my medium length curly wavy hair if I don't have natural curls or waves?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair to create curls or waves even if you don't have natural texture. Use a curling iron or wand to create the look you want.

Q: How often should I wash my medium length curly wavy hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how oily your scalp gets. Generally, you can wash your hair every 2-3 days, but some people may need to wash more or less frequently.

Q: Can I dye my medium length curly wavy hair?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair, but be sure to use a dye that's safe for curly or wavy hair and follow the instructions carefully. Dyeing your hair can be drying, so be sure to deep condition your hair regularly.

With these tips and styles, you can rock medium length curly wavy hair with confidence. Remember to embrace your natural texture and keep your hair healthy with proper care.
