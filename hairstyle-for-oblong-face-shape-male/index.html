Description

Having an oblong face shape means your face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow forehead and jawline. It can be a challenging face shape to find the right hairstyle for, as you don't want to elongate your face even more. In this article, we'll provide a step-by-step guide to finding the perfect hairstyle for oblong face shape male, along with tips and frequently asked questions.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right hairstyle for oblong face shape male is to determine your face shape. This can be done by following these steps:

  • Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back
  • Use a washable marker or lipstick to trace the outline of your face on the mirror
  • Step back and look at the shape you drew
  • Compare your shape to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, triangle, and oblong
  • If your face is longer than it is wide, with a narrow forehead and jawline, you have an oblong face shape

Step by Step Guide to Hairstyle for Oblong Face Shape Male

Now that you've determined your face shape, let's get into the step-by-step guide to finding the perfect hairstyle for oblong face shape male:

  1. Consider a short haircut, as longer hair can elongate your face even more
  2. Opt for a side part to add width to your forehead
  3. Avoid hairstyles with too much height on top, as this can further elongate your face
  4. Try a textured crop or buzz cut, as these styles can add volume and texture without elongating your face
  5. Consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and dimension
  6. Avoid hairstyles that are too slicked back, as this can make your face look even longer
  7. Experiment with different styles and lengths to find what works best for you

Tips for Hairstyle for Oblong Face Shape Male

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for oblong face shape male:

  • Avoid center parts, as they can further elongate your face
  • Consider adding facial hair to add width to your jawline
  • Don't be afraid to try new things and experiment with different styles
  • Bring pictures of hairstyles you like to your stylist to help communicate your desired look

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for oblong face shape male?

The best hairstyle for oblong face shape male is one that adds width to your forehead and jawline, without elongating your face even more. Short haircuts, side parts, and textured crops are all great options to consider.

What hairstyles should oblong face shape male avoid?

Oblong face shape males should avoid hairstyles that are too long or too slicked back, as these can elongate your face even more. Center parts should also be avoided.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Shape Male

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle for oblong face shape male, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny
