Cute Easy Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair

Written by William Jul 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have medium length curly hair, you may find it challenging to style it on a daily basis. However, there are numerous cute and easy hairstyles you can try to switch up your look. In this article, we will share some of our favorite hairstyles for medium length curly hair.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Medium length curly hair can be styled in various ways, from updos to half up half down hairstyles. These hairstyles are perfect for both casual and formal events, and they are easy to create at home. With a few styling products and tools, you can achieve a cute and effortless look in no time.

How to

The key to styling medium length curly hair is to enhance your natural texture and keep your hair hydrated. You can achieve this by using a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner, as well as a leave-in conditioner or curl cream. Once your hair is prepped, you can start styling it in various ways.

1. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a perfect hairstyle for those days when you don't have time to wash your hair. To create this look, gather your hair into a high ponytail, leaving a few strands out to frame your face. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun, and secure it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands of hair from the bun to make it look messy and effortless.

2. Half Up Half Down

A half up half down hairstyle is a classic look that works well for medium length curly hair. To create this look, gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail, leaving the rest of your hair down. You can add a braid or twist to the ponytail to make it more interesting.

3. Braided Crown

A braided crown is a romantic hairstyle that is perfect for formal events. To create this look, part your hair down the middle and braid each side. Cross the braids over the top of your head, tucking the ends underneath the opposite braid. Secure the braids with bobby pins, and you're ready to go.

Step by step

1. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair from the bun to make it look messy.

2. Half Up Half Down

  1. Gather the top half of your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Leave the rest of your hair down.
  3. Add a braid or twist to the ponytail if desired.

3. Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Braid each side.
  3. Cross the braids over the top of your head.
  4. Tuck the ends underneath the opposite braid.
  5. Secure the braids with bobby pins.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you style your medium length curly hair:

  • Use a curl-enhancing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls defined and frizz-free.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.
  • Use a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural curl pattern.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash it once a week. If you have an active lifestyle, you may need to wash it more often to remove sweat and dirt.

Q: What is the best way to detangle curly hair?

A: Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet and saturated with conditioner. Start at the ends and work your way up to the roots.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair before styling it. Avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day, as this can cause frizz. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction while you sleep.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your curly hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you maintain your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it hydrated.
  • Trim your hair every few months to get rid of split ends.

With these cute and easy hairstyles for medium length curly hair, you'll never run out of ideas for your next hairdo. Try them out and see which ones work best for you!
