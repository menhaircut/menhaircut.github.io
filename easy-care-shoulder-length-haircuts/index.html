Easy Care Shoulder Length Haircuts A Guide To Low Maintenance Hair

Written by Oliver Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending hours styling your hair every morning? Do you want a low maintenance haircut that can still look chic and stylish? Look no further than easy care shoulder length haircuts! In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about this versatile hairstyle, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it.

What is a Shoulder Length Haircut?

A shoulder length haircut is a hairstyle that falls right around the shoulders, typically between chin and collarbone length. This length is considered to be both practical and versatile, as it can be worn up or down, straight or curly, and can be styled in a variety of ways.

Benefits of Shoulder Length Haircuts

There are many benefits to having a shoulder length haircut, including:

  • Low maintenance
  • Versatile styling options
  • Flattering for most face shapes
  • Can be worn up or down
  • Looks great with layers or bangs

How to Get a Shoulder Length Haircut

The first step to getting a shoulder length haircut is to find a stylist who is experienced in cutting this length of hair. Once you've found a stylist, you'll want to discuss the specific length and style you're looking for. Some popular shoulder length haircuts include:

  • The lob (a long bob)
  • The shag
  • The blunt cut
  • The layered cut

Once you've decided on the length and style, your stylist will trim your hair to the desired length and shape. It's important to communicate with your stylist throughout the process to ensure that you're happy with the end result.

How to Style a Shoulder Length Haircut

One of the best things about a shoulder length haircut is its versatility when it comes to styling. Here are a few easy ways to style your new cut:

  • Straight and Sleek: Use a flat iron to straighten your hair and add shine.
  • Natural Waves: Apply a sea salt spray to damp hair and let it air dry for effortless waves.
  • Curls: Use a curling iron or hot rollers to add curls or waves to your hair.
  • Ponytail: Pull your hair back into a low ponytail for a chic and easy look.

Tips for Maintaining a Shoulder Length Haircut

To keep your shoulder length haircut looking its best, here are a few tips:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of the cut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Use a dry shampoo to extend the time between washes and keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQs About Shoulder Length Haircuts

Can shoulder length haircuts be worn by all hair types?

Yes! Shoulder length haircuts can be adapted to suit any hair type, whether it's straight, wavy, curly, or coily.

Is a shoulder length haircut suitable for all face shapes?

While some haircuts may be more flattering for certain face shapes, a shoulder length haircut is generally considered to be a universally flattering length.

How often should I trim my shoulder length haircut?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape of the cut and prevent split ends.

Final Thoughts

A shoulder length haircut is a low maintenance hairstyle that can still look chic and stylish. With the right cut and styling techniques, you can easily achieve a variety of looks with minimal effort. By following the tips and advice in this guide, you'll be well on your way to rocking an easy care shoulder length haircut!
