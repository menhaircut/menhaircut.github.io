How To Get Curly Hair Thinner Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Nov 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know that it can be difficult to manage. One of the biggest challenges is managing the thickness of your hair. Thick curly hair can be hard to style and can feel heavy on your head. In this article, we'll explore some tips and tricks for getting curly hair thinner, so you can achieve the style you want.

How to Get Curly Hair Thinner: Step by Step

Step 1: Get a Haircut

The first step to getting curly hair thinner is to get a haircut. A layered haircut can help to thin out your hair and give it more shape. Be sure to find a hairstylist who has experience with cutting curly hair, as it can be tricky to get the right look.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a big difference in how your curly hair looks and feels. Look for products that are designed to add volume and reduce frizz, as these can help to make your hair appear thinner. Avoid heavy products like oils and creams, as these can weigh down your hair and make it look even thicker.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow drying your hair can help to make it appear thinner, as it can add volume and lift. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help define your curls and prevent frizz. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Step 4: Use a Flat Iron

If you want to take your hair styling to the next level, you can use a flat iron to straighten your hair. This can help to make your hair appear thinner and more manageable. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray and work in small sections to avoid damaging your hair.

Step 5: Get Regular Trims

To maintain your new, thinner look, be sure to get regular trims. This will help to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make your hair appear thicker and more unruly.

Tips for Getting Curly Hair Thinner

Here are some additional tips for getting curly hair thinner:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and tangles
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day, as this can cause damage

FAQ About Getting Curly Hair Thinner

Can I Get Curly Hair Thinner Without Cutting It?

While getting a haircut is the most effective way to thin out curly hair, you can also try using products and styling techniques to achieve a thinner look.

Will Thinning Shears Work on Curly Hair?

Thinning shears can be used on curly hair, but it's important to find a hairstylist who has experience with using them. Thinning shears can be tricky to use on curly hair, as they can cause frizz and unevenness if not used correctly.

How Often Should I Get a Haircut to Keep My Hair Thinner?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hair's shape and prevent split ends. However, the frequency of haircuts may vary depending on your hair type and length.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as this can cause damage
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid towel-drying your hair, as this can cause frizz

Conclusion

Getting curly hair thinner can be a challenge, but with the right products and styling techniques, it's possible to achieve the look you want. Remember to take care of your hair by using gentle products and getting regular trims, and your curls will look and feel their best.
