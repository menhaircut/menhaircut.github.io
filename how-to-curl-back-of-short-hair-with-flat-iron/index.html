How To Curl The Back Of Short Hair With A Flat Iron

Written by Frank Jun 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have short hair, you may think that curling the back of your hair is impossible. But with a few tips and tricks, you can achieve those beautiful curls that you've always wanted. In this article, we will show you how to curl the back of short hair with a flat iron.

Description

Curling the back of short hair can be tricky, especially if you're not used to using a flat iron. But don't worry, with a little practice, you'll be able to curl the back of your hair with ease. In this article, we will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to curl the back of short hair with a flat iron.

How to Curl the Back of Short Hair with a Flat Iron

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you start curling your hair, you need to prepare it first. Wash and dry your hair, and apply a heat protectant spray. This will protect your hair from heat damage and make it easier to curl.

Step 2: Section your hair

Next, section your hair into small sections. This will make it easier to curl the back of your hair. Use clips to hold the sections in place.

Step 3: Heat up your flat iron

Turn on your flat iron and let it heat up. Make sure that it's hot enough to curl your hair, but not too hot that it will damage your hair.

Step 4: Curl your hair

Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the root. Twist the flat iron away from your face and pull it down towards the end of your hair. Hold it for a few seconds and release. Repeat this process for the rest of your hair.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once you've curled all of your hair, finish with a light hairspray. This will help your curls to last longer and keep them in place.

Tips

  • Practice makes perfect, so don't be discouraged if you don't get it right the first time.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Section your hair into small sections to make it easier to curl.
  • Use a light hairspray to finish your curls and keep them in place.

FAQ

Can I use a curling iron instead of a flat iron?

Yes, you can use a curling iron to curl the back of short hair. However, a flat iron may be easier to maneuver and give you more control over your curls.

How long will my curls last?

Your curls should last for a few hours to a day, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy, it's important to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools. You should also deep condition your hair once a week and avoid using too many styling products. If you notice any damage or split ends, it's best to trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy.

With these tips and tricks, you can now curl the back of short hair with a flat iron. Remember to practice and be patient, and soon you'll be able to achieve those beautiful curls that you've always wanted.
