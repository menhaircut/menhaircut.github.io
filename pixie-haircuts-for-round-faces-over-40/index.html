Pixie Haircuts For Round Faces Over 40 A Complete Guide

Written by William Nov 03, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you over 40 and looking for a new haircut to match your round face shape? Look no further than the versatile pixie cut. This short, chic hairstyle has been a popular choice for women of all ages, and it’s perfect for those who want to showcase their facial features while also maintaining a low-maintenance hairstyle.

Description

The pixie cut is a short, cropped hairstyle typically cut close to the scalp, with longer layers on top. This cut can be done in a variety of styles, from classic and sleek to edgy and spiky. The key to finding the perfect pixie cut for your round face is to choose a style that adds height and volume at the crown, which helps elongate your face.

How To

Getting a pixie cut is easy, but it’s important to choose a stylist who has experience cutting short hair. Here are the steps to getting the perfect pixie cut:

Step 1: Choose Your Style

Before you head to the salon, decide on the style of pixie cut you want. Browse photos online or in magazines to find inspiration, or ask your stylist for recommendations based on your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Consultation

During your consultation, your stylist will discuss your hair type, face shape, and desired style. They may also suggest modifications to the style you chose to better suit your features.

Step 3: Cut and Style

Once you and your stylist have decided on a style, they will begin cutting your hair. The process typically involves cutting the sides and back short, with longer layers on top. After the cut is complete, your stylist will style your hair using product and tools to achieve the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a pixie cut for a round face:

  • Choose a style that adds height at the crown.
  • Consider adding texture to the longer layers on top.
  • Use product to add volume and definition.
  • Maintain your cut with regular trims every 4-6 weeks.

FAQ

Q: Will a pixie cut make my face look rounder?

A: No, a pixie cut can actually elongate your face if done properly. The key is to choose a style that adds height at the crown.

Q: Can I still style my hair after getting a pixie cut?

A: Yes, you can still style your hair with product and tools. Your stylist can recommend products that work best for your hair type and desired style.

Hair Care

Maintaining a pixie cut is easy with the right hair care routine. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers and flat irons too often.
  • Use a lightweight styling product to add volume and texture.
  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain your cut.

Overall, a pixie cut is a great choice for women with round faces over 40 who want a low-maintenance, stylish haircut. With the right style and hair care routine, you’ll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.
