How To Hide Extensions Thin Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Oct 12, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Extensions are a great way to add volume and length to thin hair. However, it can be challenging to blend them seamlessly with your natural hair. You don't want your extensions to be visible, and you don't want to look like you're wearing a wig. In this post, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to hide extensions thin hair and make them look natural.

Description

Thin hair can be a real challenge when it comes to hair extensions. The key is to find the right type of extensions that will blend well with your natural hair. You don't want to choose extensions that are too heavy or thick, or they will be visible and uncomfortable to wear. You also want to make sure that the color of the extensions matches your natural hair color.

How to Hide Extensions Thin Hair

Here are some tips and tricks on how to hide extensions thin hair:

1. Choose the Right Type of Extensions

The best type of extensions for thin hair are clip-in extensions. They are easy to install and remove, and they won't damage your natural hair. You can choose the length and volume that you want, and you can clip them in wherever you need them.

2. Use Hair Styling Products

Before you clip in your extensions, apply some hair styling products to your natural hair. Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume to your roots, and use a texturizing spray to add texture to your hair. This will help your extensions blend in better with your natural hair.

3. Tease Your Hair

To create a seamless blend between your natural hair and your extensions, tease your hair at the roots. This will create a bit of texture and volume, which will make it easier to clip in your extensions.

4. Clip in Your Extensions

Clip in your extensions one at a time, starting from the bottom of your head and working your way up. Make sure that the clips are securely attached to your natural hair, and that the extensions are evenly distributed.

5. Style Your Hair

Once your extensions are clipped in, style your hair as you normally would. Use a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or straighten your hair. You can also add some hair accessories, such as a headband or hair clips, to make your hairstyle more interesting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to hide extensions thin hair:

Step 1: Choose the Right Type of Extensions

Choose clip-in extensions that are made specifically for thin hair. Make sure that the color of the extensions matches your natural hair color.

Step 2: Apply Hair Styling Products

Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your natural hair to add volume, and a texturizing spray to add texture. This will help your extensions blend in better with your natural hair.

Step 3: Tease Your Hair

Tease your hair at the roots to create texture and volume. This will make it easier to clip in your extensions.

Step 4: Clip in Your Extensions

Clip in your extensions one at a time, starting at the bottom of your head and working your way up. Make sure that the clips are securely attached to your natural hair, and that the extensions are evenly distributed.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Style your hair as you normally would, using a curling iron or flat iron to create waves or straighten your hair. Add hair accessories, such as a headband or hair clips, to make your hairstyle more interesting.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you hide extensions thin hair:

1. Choose the Right Length and Volume

Choose extensions that are the right length and volume for your natural hair. If your hair is very thin, you don't want to choose extensions that are too long or heavy, or they will be visible and uncomfortable to wear.

2. Blend the Extensions

Blend the extensions with your natural hair by using a comb or brush to blend them together. This will make them look more natural and seamless.

3. Trim the Extensions

If your extensions are too long, you can trim them to the right length. This will make them look more natural and blend better with your natural hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear extensions if I have very thin hair?

A: Yes, you can wear extensions if you have very thin hair. Just make sure that you choose the right type of extensions that will blend well with your natural hair.

Q: How do I know if the color of the extensions matches my natural hair color?

A: You can take a small section of your natural hair to the store and compare it to the color of the extensions. This will help you choose the right color.

Q: How long can I wear clip-in extensions?

A: You can wear clip-in extensions for up to 8 hours a day. Make sure that you remove them before you go to bed, and that you don't wear them for more than 8 hours at a time.

Hair Care

Extensions require special care to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips on how to care for your extensions:

1. Brush Your Extensions

Brush your extensions regularly to prevent tangling and matting. Use a soft-bristled brush or a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your extensions.

2. Wash Your Extensions

Wash your extensions every 2-3 weeks to keep them clean and fresh. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, and let them air dry.

3. Store Your Extensions

Store your extensions in a cool, dry place when you're not wearing them. Use a hair extension hanger or a storage bag to keep them organized and tangle-free.

Conclusion

Extensions are a great way to add volume and length to thin hair. By following these tips and tricks on how to hide extensions thin hair, you can make them look natural and seamless. Remember to choose the right type of extensions, apply hair styling products, tease your hair, clip in your extensions, and style your hair. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to create a stunning hairstyle that looks like your natural hair.
