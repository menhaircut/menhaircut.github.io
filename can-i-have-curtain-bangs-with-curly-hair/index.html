Can I Have Curtain Bangs With Curly Hair

Written by Bardi Jul 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you someone with curly hair and wondering if you can rock the trendy curtain bangs? The answer is a resounding yes! Curtain bangs work well with all hair types, including curly hair. In this blog post, we'll discuss how to get curtain bangs for curly hair, tips to maintain them, and answer some frequently asked questions.

How to Get Curtain Bangs for Curly Hair?

The first step in getting curtain bangs for curly hair is to find a hairstylist who is experienced in cutting curly hair. A good stylist will know how to cut your hair in a way that complements your curls and gives your face a flattering shape.

When getting curtain bangs for curly hair, it's essential to consider the length and thickness of your curls. If you have tighter curls, you may want to go for shorter bangs, while looser curls can handle longer bangs. Make sure to communicate your preferences with your stylist to get the desired look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Curtain Bangs for Curly Hair

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting curtain bangs for curly hair:

  1. Find a hairstylist who has experience in cutting curly hair.
  2. Communicate your preferences and the length of your curls to the stylist.
  3. Section off the hair that will become the bangs and cut it to the desired length.
  4. Trim the edges of the bangs to create a soft, wispy effect.
  5. Style your bangs using a diffuser to enhance your curls.

Tips to Maintain Curtain Bangs for Curly Hair

Maintaining curtain bangs for curly hair requires some effort, but it's worth it. Here are some tips to keep your bangs looking fresh:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and bouncy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Use a hair oil or serum to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to keep them at the desired length.
  • Style your bangs using a diffuser to enhance your curls.

FAQs

Can you have curtain bangs with thick curly hair?

Yes, you can have curtain bangs with thick curly hair. However, it's essential to consider the length and thickness of your curls when getting curtain bangs. A good stylist can help you determine the best length and shape for your face.

How do you style curtain bangs for curly hair?

You can style curtain bangs for curly hair using a diffuser to enhance your curls. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your wet bangs and diffuse them using low heat. You can also use a hair oil or serum to keep your curls moisturized and bouncy.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Caring for curly hair requires a different approach than caring for straight hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy, bouncy curls:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to keep your curls moisturized.
  • Detangle your hair using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, you can definitely have curtain bangs with curly hair. The key is to find a stylist who knows how to cut curly hair and communicate your preferences clearly. With the right care and maintenance, your curtain bangs will look fabulous and complement your curls.
