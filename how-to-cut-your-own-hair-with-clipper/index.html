How to Cut Your Own Hair with Clipper

Cutting your hair with clippers is not rocket science, but it requires some practice and patience to get it right. Here is a brief overview of how to cut your own hair with clippers:

  1. Choose a good quality clipper with various attachments
  2. Decide on the length of hair you want to cut
  3. Wash and dry your hair
  4. Attach the appropriate clipper guard to the clipper
  5. Start cutting your hair, working from the bottom up
  6. Blend the sides and back with the top
  7. Clean up the neckline and sideburns
  8. Take a shower and clean up any stray hairs

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you have an overview of how to cut your own hair with clippers, let's go through the step-by-step process in more detail:

Step 1: Choose a good quality clipper with various attachments

Investing in a good quality clipper with various attachments is essential for cutting your hair at home. Look for a clipper that has different clipper guards, a powerful motor, and is easy to use. Some popular clipper brands include Wahl, Andis, and Oster.

Step 2: Decide on the length of hair you want to cut

Before you start cutting your hair, decide on the length of hair you want to cut. This will help you choose the appropriate clipper guard to attach to the clipper. If you are not sure about the length, start with a longer guard and work your way down.

Step 3: Wash and dry your hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before you start cutting it. Wet hair is harder to cut and can clog up the clipper blades. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 4: Attach the appropriate clipper guard to the clipper

Attach the appropriate clipper guard to the clipper based on the length of hair you want to cut. Clipper guards come in different sizes, and each size corresponds to a specific length of hair. The smaller the number on the guard, the shorter the hair will be.

Step 5: Start cutting your hair, working from the bottom up

Start cutting your hair from the bottom up, using the clipper with the attached guard. Create a guideline by running the clipper through your hair in a straight line. Work in sections, gradually moving up the head, and trimming the hair to the desired length.

Step 6: Blend the sides and back with the top

Blend the sides and back of your hair with the top using a blending or tapering technique. Use a shorter clipper guard to trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck. Use a longer guard to blend the hair on the top of the head with the sides and back.

Step 7: Clean up the neckline and sideburns

Use a trimmer or a razor to clean up the neckline and sideburns. Trim the hair around the ears and the back of the neck to create a clean and polished look.

Step 8: Take a shower and clean up any stray hairs

Take a shower to wash away any loose hairs and clean up any stray hairs with a mirror and a pair of scissors. Make sure you have removed all the hair from your clothes and the bathroom floor.

Tips for Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting your hair with clippers:

  • Start with a longer clipper guard and work your way down to avoid cutting too much hair at once
  • Use a mirror to check the back of your head to ensure you have trimmed it evenly
  • Go slow and be patient, especially if you are a beginner
  • Use a spray bottle to wet your hair if it becomes too dry during the cutting process
  • Clean your clipper blades regularly to keep them working smoothly

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cut my hair with a regular pair of scissors?

While it is possible to cut your hair with a regular pair of scissors, it is not recommended. Hair scissors are much sharper, and they are designed to create precise cuts. Regular scissors can damage your hair and make it look uneven.

How often should I cut my hair with clippers?

The frequency of cutting your hair with clippers depends on your hair's growth rate and the desired length. On average, most people cut their hair every 4-6 weeks.

Can I use clippers to cut long hair?

Clippers are primarily designed to cut short hair. If you have long hair, it is recommended to use scissors to trim it.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Hair with Clippers

After cutting your hair with clippers, it is essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner to keep it clean and soft
  • Avoid using hot styling tools, such as hairdryers and straighteners, too often as they can damage your hair
  • Use a hair serum or oil to keep your hair moisturized and prevent split ends
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength

Now that you know how to cut your own hair with clippers, you can save money and time by doing it yourself. Remember to be patient and take your time, and you will get great results!
