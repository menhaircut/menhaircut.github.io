How To Big Waves Hair A Relaxed English Guide

Written by William Oct 30, 2023 ¬∑ 5 min read

If you're looking for a way to add some volume and texture to your hair, big waves might be just the hairstyle you need. Not only are big waves trendy and stylish, but they can also be achieved with a variety of tools and techniques. In this guide, we'll show you how to big waves hair in relaxed English, with step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice.

  2. Step-by-Step Instructions

How to Big Waves Hair

To big waves hair, you'll need a few key tools and products, including a curling iron, hairspray, and heat protectant. You can also use a straightening iron or a hair wand to achieve big waves. Follow these steps to achieve big, bouncy waves:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Before you start curling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. Apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent heat damage. Section your hair into manageable sections and clip them out of the way.

Step 2: Heat Up Your Curling Iron

Heat up your curling iron to the desired temperature. If you have thin hair, use a lower temperature setting, and if you have thick hair, use a higher temperature.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Take a section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron barrel, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Hold the curl in place for a few seconds, then release. Repeat this process with the rest of your hair, making sure to curl away from your face.

Step 4: Brush Out Your Waves

Once you've curled your entire head, use a soft bristle brush to gently brush out your waves. This will soften the curls and create more volume.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your big waves with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here's a more detailed breakdown of how to big waves hair:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray or serum.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Heat Up Your Curling Iron

Heat up your curling iron to the desired temperature. If you're using a straightening iron or hair wand, heat it up to the appropriate temperature as well.

Step 4: Start Curling

Take a small section of hair and clamp it into the curling iron. Roll the iron up towards your scalp, holding it in place for a few seconds. Release the curl and move on to the next section.

Step 5: Alternate Directions

To create a more natural-looking wave, alternate the direction of your curls. Curl some sections away from your face and others towards your face.

Step 6: Brush Out Your Waves

Once you've curled your entire head, use a soft bristle brush to gently brush out your waves. This will soften the curls and create more volume.

Step 7: Finish with Hairspray

To hold your big waves in place, finish off with a light mist of hairspray.

Tips for Achieving Big Waves

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect big waves:

  • Use a curling iron or hair wand with a larger barrel size to create bigger waves
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the roots, leave a little bit of space for a more natural look
  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add texture and hold to your hair
  • Experiment with different curling techniques, such as twisting the hair before curling or curling in different directions
  • Use a flexible hold hairspray to keep your waves in place without making them stiff or crunchy

FAQs

Can I achieve big waves without heat tools?

Yes, you can achieve big waves without heat tools by braiding your hair while it's damp and allowing it to air dry. Once your hair is dry, release the braids and gently finger comb your hair to create waves.

How long will my big waves last?

Your big waves should last for at least a day, but you can make them last longer by sleeping with your hair in a loose bun or braid.

Can I big waves hair if I have short hair?

Yes, you can big waves hair even if you have short hair. Use a smaller curling wand or iron to create tighter waves that will add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care Advice for Big Waves

To keep your big waves looking their best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray or serum
  • Don't wash your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair, as a regular brush can cause breakage
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain healthy hair

With these tips and techniques, you can easily big waves hair and enjoy a chic and stylish hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
