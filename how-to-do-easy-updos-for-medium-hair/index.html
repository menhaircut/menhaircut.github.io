Easy Updos For Medium Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Jul 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of leaving your hair down all the time? Do you want to spice up your look with an easy updo for your medium hair? Well, you're in luck because we have got you covered with this step-by-step guide on how to do easy updos for medium hair. From braids to buns, we have got it all. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step-by-Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the actual updos, here are a few things you will need:

  • Bobby pins
  • Hair elastics
  • Hairspray
  • Comb

Now, let's talk about the different updos you can try on your medium hair.

Braided Updo

Braids are a great way to add some texture to your hair. Here's how you can do a braided updo:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from one side and braid it. Secure the end with a hair elastic.
  3. Repeat step 2 on the other side.
  4. Take one of the braids and wrap it around the back of your head, securing it with bobby pins as you go.
  5. Repeat step 4 with the other braid.
  6. Use hairspray to set the updo in place.

Messy Bun

A messy bun is a classic updo that is perfect for medium hair. Here's how you can do it:

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base to form a bun. Secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Use your fingers to loosen the bun and create a messy look.
  4. Use hairspray to set the updo in place.

Step-by-Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to do an easy updo for medium hair:

  1. Start by brushing your hair to get rid of any tangles.
  2. Decide on the type of updo you want to do.
  3. Follow the steps for the specific updo (braided updo or messy bun).
  4. Use bobby pins and hair elastics to secure the updo in place.
  5. Use hairspray to set the updo and prevent any flyaways.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when doing easy updos for medium hair:

  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before you begin.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them better.
  • Don't make the updo too tight as it can cause headaches.
  • Experiment with different updos to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

Q. Can I do an updo on oily hair?

A. It's best to do an updo on clean, dry hair to prevent any oiliness from showing.

Q. How long does an updo last?

A. An updo can last anywhere from a few hours to a day depending on the type of updo and how well it is secured.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to prevent any damage. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a heat protectant before using any hot tools.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh down your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.

And there you have it, an easy updo for your medium hair. With a little practice, you'll be able to do it in no time. So, go ahead and try it out!
