Why Do I Look Better With A Fringe A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering why you look better with a fringe? Well, you're not alone! Bangs or fringes have been a popular hairstyle for years now, and it's not hard to see why. Not only do they add a touch of uniqueness to your look, but they also have the power to enhance your features and make you look more youthful. In this guide, we'll go over the reasons why you might look better with a fringe, how to get one, and how to take care of it.

Table of Contents

  • Reasons why a fringe might suit you
  • How to get a fringe
  • Tips for styling your fringe
  • Hair care tips for maintaining your fringe
  • FAQs about fringes

Reasons why a fringe might suit you

There are several reasons why a fringe might suit you:

1. Enhances your facial features

A fringe can draw attention to your eyes, eyebrows, cheekbones, and jawline. If you have a round face, a fringe can make it appear narrower. If you have a long face, a fringe can make it appear shorter.

2. Hides imperfections

Do you have a high forehead or a small scar? A fringe can hide these imperfections and make you feel more confident.

3. Makes you look youthful

A fringe can add a touch of youthfulness to your look. It can also make you look more approachable and friendly.

How to get a fringe

If you're thinking of getting a fringe, here's how to do it:

1. Choose the right type of fringe

There are several types of fringes to choose from, including:

  • Straight fringe
  • Side swept fringe
  • Curtain fringe
  • Micro fringe

Choose the one that suits your face shape and hair type the best.

2. Get it done by a professional

It's best to get your fringe done by a professional hairstylist. They'll be able to advise you on the best type of fringe for your face shape and hair type.

3. Maintain it regularly

Once you have your fringe, you'll need to maintain it regularly. This means trimming it every few weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Tips for styling your fringe

Here are some tips for styling your fringe:

1. Use a round brush

Use a round brush to blow dry your fringe. This will give it a smooth and polished look.

2. Use a styling product

Use a styling product to keep your fringe in place. This can be a hairspray, wax, or pomade.

3. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. You can wear your fringe straight, parted in the middle, or swept to the side.

Hair care tips for maintaining your fringe

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your fringe:

1. Wash it regularly

Wash your fringe regularly to keep it clean and prevent it from becoming greasy.

2. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

3. Avoid over-washing

Don't over-wash your hair, as this can dry it out and make it prone to breakage.

FAQs about fringes

1. Will a fringe suit me?

It depends on your face shape and hair type. Speak to a professional hairstylist to get advice on the best type of fringe for you.

2. How often should I trim my fringe?

You should trim your fringe every few weeks to keep it looking neat and tidy.

3. Can I style my fringe with heat tools?

Yes, you can style your fringe with heat tools, but make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

In conclusion, a fringe can enhance your features, hide imperfections, and make you look more youthful. If you're thinking of getting a fringe, speak to a professional hairstylist and maintain it regularly to keep it looking its best. With these tips, you'll be rocking a fabulous fringe in no time!
