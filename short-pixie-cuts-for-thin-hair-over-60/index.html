Short Pixie Cuts For Thin Hair Over 60 A Guide To Looking Stylish And Chic

Written by William Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're over 60 and have thin hair, you may be wondering what hairstyles will flatter your hair type and age. Luckily, there are plenty of options, and one of the most popular is the short pixie cut. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about short pixie cuts for thin hair over 60, including how to get the look, styling tips, and hair care advice. Let's get started!

Description: What is a Short Pixie Cut?

A short pixie cut is a hairstyle that's typically cut short on the sides and back, with longer layers on top. The style can be customized to suit your face shape, hair texture, and personal preferences, and can be worn with or without bangs. Pixie cuts are popular because they're low-maintenance, versatile, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

How to: Getting a Short Pixie Cut

If you're interested in getting a short pixie cut, the first step is to find a stylist who specializes in the style. Look for someone who has experience with cutting short hair and can recommend a style that will flatter your face shape and hair type. When you go in for your appointment, be prepared to discuss your preferences and bring photos of styles you like.

The stylist will start by sectioning your hair and cutting it short on the sides and back. They may use clippers or scissors, depending on the look you're going for. The top layers will be left longer, and the stylist may use texturizing techniques to add volume and movement. Once the cut is complete, they'll style it and show you how to maintain the look at home.

Step-by-Step: Getting a Short Pixie Cut

  1. Research styles and find a stylist who specializes in short hair.
  2. Discuss your preferences and bring photos to your appointment.
  3. The stylist will section your hair and cut it short on the sides and back.
  4. The top layers will be left longer and texturized for volume and movement.
  5. The stylist will style your hair and show you how to maintain the look at home.

Tips: Styling Short Pixie Cuts for Thin Hair Over 60

Styling a short pixie cut is easy and can be done in just a few minutes. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your new style:

  • Use a volumizing mousse or spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create lift and movement.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, like headbands, clips, and barrettes.
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your cut.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions About Short Pixie Cuts for Thin Hair Over 60

Q: Will a short pixie cut flatter my face shape?

A: Yes! A short pixie cut can flatter any face shape, as long as it's customized to suit your unique features. Talk to your stylist about which style will work best for you.

Q: How often do I need to get my hair cut?

A: It depends on how quickly your hair grows and how well you maintain the style. Generally, you'll need to get a trim every 4-6 weeks to keep the shape and style of your cut.

Q: Can I wear a short pixie cut to formal events?

A: Absolutely! A short pixie cut can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Consider adding a sparkly hair accessory or wearing a bold lipstick to amp up the glam factor.

Hair Care: Maintaining Your Short Pixie Cut

Keeping your short pixie cut looking fresh and stylish is easy with a few simple hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and bounce.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Use a dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture between washes.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your cut.

Conclusion

Short pixie cuts are a great option for women over 60 with thin hair. They're low-maintenance, stylish, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal preferences. Whether you're looking for a chic and edgy style or a classic and elegant look, a short pixie cut can help you look and feel your best. Use the tips and advice in this article to help you get the most out of your new style!
