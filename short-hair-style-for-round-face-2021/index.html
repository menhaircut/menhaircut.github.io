Short Hair Style For Round Face 2021 A Guide To The Best Look

Written by Oliver Jul 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle and looking to try something new? If you have a round face, finding the perfect short hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, as we have compiled a list of the best short hair styles for round faces in 2021 that will give you the perfect look. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle.

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with equal length and width. The goal of a good hairstyle for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer, slimmer face. Short hair styles can be a great way to achieve this look as they draw attention to your facial features and away from the roundness of your face.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect short hair style for a round face is to determine your face shape. This can be done by measuring the length and width of your face and comparing the two. Once you have identified your face shape, it's time to choose a style that complements your features.

Some of the best short hair styles for round faces include the pixie cut, bob, and lob. These styles are all versatile and can be customized to suit your unique features. For example, a layered bob can add volume to your hair while a side-swept pixie cut can create the illusion of length.

Step-by-Step

1. Determine your face shape

2. Choose a short hair style that complements your features

3. Consult with a hairstylist to customize your style

4. Use styling products to achieve your desired look

Tips

When choosing a short hair style for a round face, there are a few tips to keep in mind. First, avoid styles that are too short, as they can make your face appear even rounder. Second, consider adding layers to your hair to create movement and texture. Finally, don't be afraid to experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you.

FAQ

1. Can I still have bangs with a short hair style for a round face?

Yes, bangs can be a great addition to a short hair style for a round face. Side-swept bangs can create the illusion of length and help balance out the roundness of your face.

2. How often should I get my hair cut?

It is recommended to get your hair cut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your short hair style and keep it looking fresh.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintaining the health and appearance of your short hair style. Be sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for your hair type and avoid over-styling with heat tools. Regular trims and deep conditioning treatments can also help keep your hair looking its best.

In conclusion, finding the perfect short hair style for a round face can be a challenge, but with the right guidance, it is possible to achieve the perfect look. By following our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice, you can have the confidence to rock your new short hair style in 2021.
