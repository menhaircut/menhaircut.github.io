How Short Is A 4 Guard

Are you wondering how short is a 4 guard? If you're new to hair cutting or have never heard of a "4 guard," you may be confused about what it means. In this article, we'll explain what a 4 guard is and how short it can be.

Description

A 4 guard is a clipper attachment that is used to cut hair to a specific length. The number 4 refers to the length of the guard, which is 1/2 inch or 12.7 millimeters. The clipper guard is attached to the clipper blade and helps to control the length of hair that is cut. It's important to note that the length of hair cut with a 4 guard may vary depending on the type of hair and the desired style.

How To

To use a 4 guard, you'll need a set of hair clippers and the 4 guard attachment. First, make sure the hair is clean and dry. Next, attach the 4 guard to the clipper blade by snapping it on. Turn on the clippers and run them through the hair, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure it's all evenly cut. Keep in mind that the length of hair cut with a 4 guard may vary depending on the type of hair and the desired style.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions for using a 4 guard:

  1. Clean and dry the hair.
  2. Attach the 4 guard to the clipper blade.
  3. Turn on the clippers.
  4. Start at the bottom of the hair and work your way up.
  5. Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure it's all evenly cut.

Tips

Here are some tips for using a 4 guard:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter length.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and make sure it's even.
  • Go slowly to ensure that you're cutting the hair evenly.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.

After using a 4 guard, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
