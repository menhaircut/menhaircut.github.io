Hairstyles For Thick Coarse Wavy Hair Over 50

If you are over 50 and have thick, coarse, wavy hair, you may be wondering what hairstyles are best suited for your hair type. Don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we will be discussing hairstyles that will make you look and feel your best. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Thick coarse wavy hair can be challenging to manage, but it's also a blessing in disguise. With the right haircut, you can showcase your hair's natural texture and thickness while also looking stylish and chic. Short hairstyles work well for women over 50 with thick coarse wavy hair, as they help to frame the face and create a more youthful appearance. You can also opt for long hairstyles that feature layers or bangs to add movement and texture to your hair.

How to

The first step in finding the perfect hairstyle for your hair type is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They can help you determine what haircut will work best for your face shape and hair texture. Be sure to bring in pictures of hairstyles that you like and explain what you want to achieve with your hair. This will help your hairstylist create a look that is tailored to your needs.

Step by step

Here are a few hairstyles that are perfect for women over 50 with thick coarse wavy hair:

Short Pixie Cut

A short pixie cut is perfect for women who want a low-maintenance hairstyle that still looks stylish. This cut features short layers that add texture and movement to the hair. It's also a great way to show off your natural waves.

Bob with Bangs

A bob with bangs is a timeless hairstyle that looks great on women of all ages. This cut features a chin-length bob with side-swept bangs that frame the face. The layers in the bob add movement and texture to the hair, while the bangs help to soften the overall look.

Long Layers

If you prefer long hair, opt for long layers that add movement and texture to your hair. This cut works well for women with thick coarse wavy hair, as it helps to reduce bulk and weight in the hair. You can also add side-swept bangs to this cut to frame the face.

Tips

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Invest in a good quality hair serum or oil to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.

FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions about hairstyles for thick coarse wavy hair over 50:

  • Q: Can I wear my hair straight if it's thick, coarse, and wavy?
  • A: Yes, you can! Just be sure to use a good quality flat iron and heat protectant spray to prevent damage.
  • Q: Are bangs a good idea for women over 50?
  • A: Yes, bangs can be a great way to soften the face and add a youthful touch to your hairstyle.
  • Q: How often should I trim my hair?
  • A: It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and promote healthy hair growth.

Hair Care

Here are a few tips to keep your hair looking and feeling healthy:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to hydrate and nourish your hair.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you are sure to find a look that suits your hair type and makes you feel confident and beautiful. Remember to consult with a professional hairstylist for personalized advice and recommendations.
