What Haircut Suits A Chubby Face

Written by Eveline Nov 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of looking in the mirror and feeling self-conscious about your chubby face? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with finding the right haircut to suit their face shape. In this article, we'll go over the best haircuts for a chubby face, how to achieve them, and some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Before we get into the best haircuts for a chubby face, it's important to understand what face shape you have. A chubby face typically has a rounder shape with fuller cheeks and a wider jawline. The goal of a haircut for a chubby face is to create the illusion of a slimmer face by elongating the features and drawing attention away from the cheeks.

How To

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for your chubby face is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to assess your face shape and recommend the best style for you. It's important to bring in pictures of hairstyles that you like, but be open to their suggestions as well.

Step By Step

Once you've found the right haircut for your chubby face, it's important to maintain it properly. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume at the crown of your head.
  3. If you want to use a straightener or curling iron, make sure to use a heat protectant spray.
  4. Use a light hairspray or hair wax to keep your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a chubby face:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin, as they can make your face look wider.
  • Opt for layers to add texture and dimension to your hair.
  • Consider getting bangs to draw attention away from your cheeks.
  • Try a side part to create the illusion of a slimmer face.

FAQ

Q: Can I still wear my hair long if I have a chubby face?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair long if you have a chubby face. Just make sure to add layers and texture to create a more slimming effect.

Q: Are there any haircuts I should avoid?

A: Yes, you should avoid haircuts that add width to your face, such as blunt cuts that end at the chin or heavy bangs that cover your forehead.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy, beautiful head of hair. Here are some tips to follow:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know what haircut suits a chubby face, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get the perfect style for you. Remember to maintain your haircut properly and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
