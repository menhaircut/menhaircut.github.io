How To Cut Men s Hair Clippers And Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Are you tired of going to the barbershop every month for a haircut? With the right tools and techniques, you can easily cut your own hair at home. In this guide, we will show you how to cut men's hair using clippers and scissors, so you can save time and money while still looking sharp.

How to Cut Men's Hair Clippers and Scissors

Cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors is not as complicated as it seems. However, it does require some practice and patience. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • You will need a pair of clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.
  • Choose the right blade guard size for the clipper, depending on how long you want your hair to be.
  • Start with clean, dry hair.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut men's hair with clippers and scissors:

  1. Start by choosing the right blade guard size for your clippers. The higher the number, the longer the hair will be. For a shorter cut, use a lower number.
  2. Using the clippers, start at the nape of the neck and work your way up towards the crown. Use an upward motion to create an even cut.
  3. Move to the sides and blend the hair from the top to the sides, using the same upward motion.
  4. Use scissors to trim the top of the hair to your desired length. Use a comb to lift the hair up and cut it straight across.
  5. Blend the sides into the top by using scissors to trim the hair that hangs over the clipper cut.
  6. Trim the hair around the ears and neckline using scissors or clippers without a guard.
  7. Check for any uneven spots and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips for a Great Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve a great haircut:

  • Invest in high-quality clippers and scissors for a clean cut.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair up and trim it evenly.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once, as this can lead to mistakes.
  • Check your work in the mirror frequently to ensure an even cut.
  • Ask a friend or family member to help you with hard-to-reach areas, such as the back of your head.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors:

Can I cut my own hair with just clippers?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with just clippers. However, scissors are necessary for blending and trimming the top of the hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. On average, most men need a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

What should I do if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always trim the hair shorter or blend the mistake into the rest of the hair. If you're unsure, it's better to err on the side of caution and cut less hair.

Hair Care After the Cut

After you've finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and clean.
  • Use a styling product, such as gel or pomade, to create your desired look.
  • Visit a professional barber every few months for a trim and to maintain the shape of your cut.

Cutting men's hair with clippers and scissors may seem intimidating at first, but with practice and patience, you can achieve a great haircut at home. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be on your way to a fresh, stylish haircut in no time.
