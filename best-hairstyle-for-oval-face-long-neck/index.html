Best Hairstyle For Oval Face Long Neck A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 25, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the best hairstyle for your oval face and long neck? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through the best hairstyles that would suit your face shape and neck length. From short to long, curly to straight, we have got all the styles covered. So, let's get started.

Description

An oval face shape is considered the most versatile and balanced of all the face shapes. If you have an oval face shape, then you have the freedom to experiment with different hairstyles. However, if you also have a long neck, then you need to be careful while choosing a hairstyle. A wrong hairstyle can make your neck look even longer, which can be unflattering. Therefore, it is essential to choose a hairstyle that complements both your face shape and neck length.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for Oval Face Long Neck

Choosing the right hairstyle can be challenging, but it's not impossible. Here are some tips that can help you choose the best hairstyle for your oval face and long neck:

1. Consider the Length

The length of your hair plays a crucial role in determining the right hairstyle. If you have a long neck, then it's best to avoid hairstyles that are too short. Short hairstyles can make your neck look longer, which can be unflattering. Instead, opt for hairstyles that are shoulder-length or longer. These hairstyles will balance your face and neck length.

2. Consider the Texture

The texture of your hair can also impact your hairstyle. If you have thick hair, then it's best to avoid hairstyles that add volume to your hair. These hairstyles can make your neck look shorter, which can be unflattering. Instead, opt for hairstyles that are sleek and straight. These hairstyles will elongate your neck and balance your face shape.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also impact your hairstyle. If you have a busy lifestyle, then it's best to opt for low-maintenance hairstyles. These hairstyles will save you time and effort. However, if you have the time and patience to maintain your hair, then you can experiment with different hairstyles.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Face Long Neck

1. Long Layers

Long layers are a great option for oval face shapes with long necks. These layers will add movement and texture to your hair, without adding volume. You can style your hair in loose waves or pin-straight. This hairstyle is versatile and can be worn on any occasion.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that suits almost every face shape. If you have an oval face with a long neck, then a bob can be a great option. Opt for a shoulder-length bob that grazes your collarbone. This hairstyle will balance your face and neck length.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

If you want to add some dimension to your hairstyle, then side-swept bangs can be a great option. These bangs will frame your face and create a soft look. You can style your hair in loose waves or pin-straight. This hairstyle is perfect for any occasion.

4. High Ponytail

A high ponytail is a simple yet elegant hairstyle that can elongate your neck. This hairstyle is perfect for formal occasions or a night out. You can add some volume to your hair to create a more dramatic look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips that can help you choose the best hairstyle:

1. Consult with a Hairstylist

If you are unsure about which hairstyle to choose, then it's best to consult with a hairstylist. A professional hairstylist can guide you through the best hairstyles that would suit your face shape and neck length.

2. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles. Trying out different styles can help you find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape and neck length.

FAQ

1. Can Short Hairstyles Suit Oval Face with Long Neck?

Short hairstyles can be tricky for oval face shapes with long necks. Short hairstyles can make your neck look even longer, which can be unflattering. Therefore, it's best to opt for hairstyles that are shoulder-length or longer.

2. Can Curly Hair Suit Oval Face with Long Neck?

Yes, curly hair can suit oval face shapes with long necks. However, it's best to avoid hairstyles that add volume to your hair. Instead, opt for hairstyles that elongate your neck and balance your face shape.

Hair Care

Choosing the right hairstyle is essential, but taking care of your hair is equally important. Here are some tips that can help you take care of your hair:

1. Use the Right Products

Using the right products can make a significant difference in your hair's health. Choose products that suit your hair type and texture. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help you get rid of split ends and maintain healthy hair. It's recommended to trim your hair every six to eight weeks.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Excessive heat can damage your hair. Therefore, it's essential to protect your hair from heat. Use heat protectant products before using any heat styling tools.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hairstyle for oval face and long neck can be challenging, but it's not impossible. By considering your face shape, neck length, and lifestyle, you can find the perfect hairstyle that suits you. Experiment with different styles and consult with a hairstylist if you are unsure. Taking care of your hair is equally important, so make sure to use the right products and trim your hair regularly. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best.
