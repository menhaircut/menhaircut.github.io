How To Slick Natural Hair Without Gel

Written by Bardi Oct 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a way to style your natural hair without using gel, you're not alone. Gel can be harsh on hair, and some people just prefer a more natural look. Fortunately, there are many ways to slick your hair without gel. In this article, we'll explore some of the best techniques for achieving a sleek, smooth look that works for all hair types.

Description

Slicking your hair back without gel can be a bit more challenging, but the results are worth it. You'll achieve a more natural look that doesn't involve harsh chemicals. Plus, many of these techniques are easy to do and require minimal styling products.

How to Slick Natural Hair Without Gel

1. Use a Natural Oil

One of the easiest ways to slick your hair back without gel is to use a natural oil. Some great options include coconut oil, olive oil, and jojoba oil. Simply apply a small amount to your hair and brush it back. The oil will help smooth your hair and keep it in place.

2. Try a Pomade

If you're looking for a product that will help keep your hair in place without gel, consider using a pomade. Pomades are often made with natural ingredients and provide a strong hold. They can be applied to wet or dry hair and help keep your style in place all day.

3. Use a Styling Cream

If you're looking for a more natural option, consider using a styling cream. These products are often made with natural ingredients and provide a medium hold. They can be applied to wet or dry hair and help keep your style in place without making your hair stiff or crunchy.

4. Use a Hair Spray

If you're looking for a product that will help keep your hair in place all day, consider using a hairspray. Hairsprays are often made with natural ingredients and can provide a strong hold. They can be applied to wet or dry hair and help keep your style in place without making your hair stiff or crunchy.

Step by Step

To slick your hair back without gel, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Begin by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will help remove any dirt or oil from your hair and make it easier to style.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply your product of choice to your hair. This can be a natural oil, pomade, styling cream, or hairspray.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a brush or comb, brush your hair back away from your face. This will help create a smooth, sleek look.

Step 4: Secure Your Style

Use bobby pins or hair clips to secure your style in place. This will help keep your hair from falling out of place throughout the day.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the best results when slicking your hair back without gel:

  • Start with a small amount of product and add more as needed.
  • Use a brush or comb to create a smooth, sleek look.
  • Secure your style with bobby pins or hair clips to keep it in place all day.

FAQ

What is the best product to use to slick natural hair without gel?

The best product to use will depend on your hair type and the level of hold you're looking for. Some great options include natural oils, pomades, styling creams, and hairsprays.

Can you slick natural hair without using any product?

Yes, you can slick natural hair without using any product. However, this can be more challenging and may not hold up as well throughout the day.

Hair Care

When slicking your hair back without gel, it's important to take care of your hair. This means washing it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, avoiding harsh chemicals and heat styling tools, and using natural products whenever possible.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a sleek, smooth look without using gel. Whether you're looking for a natural look or just want to avoid harsh chemicals, there are plenty of options available. So go ahead and experiment until you find the perfect product for your hair type and style!
