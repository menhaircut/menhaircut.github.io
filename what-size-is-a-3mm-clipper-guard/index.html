What Size Is A 3Mm Clipper Guard

Written by Bardi Aug 26, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you wondering what size is a 3mm clipper guard? Well, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about 3mm clipper guards, including how to use them, the benefits of using them, and some tips for taking care of your hair.

Description of 3mm Clipper Guard

A 3mm clipper guard is a cutting attachment that is used with hair clippers. It is one of the most popular clipper guards used in hair cutting because it provides a close cut without being too short. The guard is made of plastic or metal and fits over the blades of the clippers to control the length of the hair that is cut.

How to Use a 3mm Clipper Guard

Using a 3mm clipper guard is simple. First, attach the guard to the clippers by sliding it over the blades. Then, turn on the clippers and start cutting the hair. The guard will ensure that the hair is cut to a length of 3mm, which is equivalent to approximately 1/8 of an inch.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to use a 3mm clipper guard:

  1. Attach the guard to the clippers by sliding it over the blades.
  2. Turn on the clippers and start cutting the hair.
  3. Move the clippers in a smooth, even motion over the hair.
  4. Check the length of the hair periodically to ensure it is being cut to the desired length.
  5. Trim the hair around the ears and neckline with scissors.

Tips for Using a 3mm Clipper Guard

Here are some tips for using a 3mm clipper guard:

  • Use a comb to guide the clippers through the hair.
  • Clean the clippers and the guard after each use.
  • Oil the blades of the clippers regularly to keep them working smoothly.
  • Start cutting the hair at the back of the head and work your way to the front.
  • Use a mirror to check the length of the hair while cutting.

FAQ About 3mm Clipper Guards

Here are some frequently asked questions about 3mm clipper guards:

What is the length of a 3mm clipper guard?

A 3mm clipper guard cuts hair to a length of 3mm, which is equivalent to approximately 1/8 of an inch.

Can a 3mm clipper guard be used for all hair types?

Yes, a 3mm clipper guard can be used for all hair types, but it is best suited for short hair.

What are the benefits of using a 3mm clipper guard?

The benefits of using a 3mm clipper guard include a close cut without being too short, ease of use, and versatility.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind when using a 3mm clipper guard:

  • Wash your hair before cutting it to remove any dirt or product buildup.
  • Use a conditioner to make your hair easier to cut.
  • Trim your hair when it is dry to get a more accurate cut.
  • Avoid using the clippers on wet hair as it can damage the blades.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or oil to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

Now that you know what size is a 3mm clipper guard, you can confidently use it to get the perfect haircut. Remember to follow the tips and take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
