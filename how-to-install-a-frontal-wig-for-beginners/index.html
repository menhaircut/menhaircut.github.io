How to Install a Frontal Wig for Beginners

If you are new to wearing wigs, installing a frontal wig may seem intimidating. However, with the right tools and techniques, anyone can install a frontal wig at home. In this article, we will guide you through the process of installing a frontal wig step by step. We will also provide tips on hair care to keep your wig looking great.

Description

A frontal wig is a type of wig that has a lace frontal at the front of the wig. The lace frontal covers the entire hairline, from ear to ear, and gives the appearance of a natural hairline. Frontal wigs are popular because they allow for versatile styling, including pulling the hair into a ponytail or parting the hair in different ways. Frontal wigs can be made from human hair or synthetic hair.

How to Install a Frontal Wig for Beginners

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you install your frontal wig, you need to prepare your hair. If you have long hair, braid your hair into cornrows or put it in a low bun. If you have short hair, use a wig cap to keep your hair flat against your head. This will create a smooth surface for the wig to sit on.

Step 2: Prepare your wig

Before you install your wig, you need to prepare it. Cut off any excess lace from the wig, leaving about 1/4 inch of lace. Use a pair of scissors to carefully cut along the hairline of the lace frontal, being careful not to cut any of the hairs.

Step 3: Apply adhesive

Apply a small amount of adhesive along the hairline of the lace frontal. You can use a wig glue or tape. Be sure to follow the instructions on the adhesive carefully. Allow the adhesive to dry for a few seconds before moving on to the next step.

Step 4: Place the wig

Place the wig on your head, starting from the front and working your way back. Adjust the wig so that it sits comfortably on your head. Use a comb to blend your natural hair with the wig hair.

Step 5: Style your wig

Once your wig is in place, you can style it as desired. You can part the hair in different ways, pull it into a ponytail, or style it with heat tools. Be sure to use heat protectant spray if you are using heat tools.

Tips

  • Use a wig cap to keep your hair flat against your head.
  • Be sure to cut the lace carefully to avoid cutting any of the hairs.
  • Use a small amount of adhesive to avoid any irritation or damage to your skin.
  • Be gentle when styling your wig to avoid damaging the hair.

FAQ

How long can I wear a frontal wig?

You can wear a frontal wig for up to six weeks at a time. However, it is important to take breaks between wearing wigs to allow your scalp to breathe.

How do I wash my frontal wig?

You can wash your frontal wig using a wig shampoo and conditioner. Be sure to follow the instructions on the products carefully. Allow the wig to air dry on a wig stand.

Hair Care

To keep your frontal wig looking great, it is important to take good care of it. Use a wig brush to detangle the hair, and avoid using heat tools too often. Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape. Wash your wig regularly to keep it clean and fresh.

With these tips and techniques, you can install a frontal wig like a pro. Whether you are looking for a new style or simply want to switch up your look, a frontal wig can be a great option. With proper care, your frontal wig can last for months, giving you beautiful hair every day.
