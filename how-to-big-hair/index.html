How to Big Hair: A Step-by-Step Guide

Are you tired of your flat, lifeless hair? Do you want to add some volume and bounce to your locks? If so, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll show you how to big hair in a few simple steps. From proper hair care to styling tips, we’ve got you covered. So, let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Hair Care
  • How To: Big Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ

Hair Care

Before we get into the styling process, it’s important to take care of your hair. Healthy hair is the foundation for any great hairstyle, so make sure to follow these tips:

1. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.

Dry hair can lead to breakage and frizz, which is not ideal for big hair. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

2. Avoid heat styling.

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons can damage your hair and make it look flat. Try to avoid using them as much as possible or use a heat protectant spray before styling.

3. Get regular trims.

Split ends can make your hair look dull and lifeless. Get regular trims every six to eight weeks to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

How To: Big Hair

Now that you’ve taken care of your hair, it’s time to style it. Here’s how to big hair:

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Blow dry your hair upside down.

Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair for a few minutes. This will help add volume to your roots.

2. Use a volumizing product.

Apply a volumizing product like mousse or root-lifting spray to your roots. This will help add even more volume and hold to your hair.

3. Tease your hair.

Use a teasing brush or comb to tease your hair at the roots. Start at the crown of your head and work your way down, teasing small sections of your hair at a time.

4. Smooth the top layer.

Smooth the top layer of your hair with a brush or comb to hide the teased sections and create a polished look.

5. Use hairspray.

Spray your hair with a strong-hold hairspray to keep your big hair in place.

Tips

Here are a few additional tips to help you achieve big hair:

1. Use hot rollers.

Hot rollers are a great way to add volume and curl to your hair. Roll your hair in large sections and leave the rollers in for 10-15 minutes before removing them.

2. Don’t brush your hair too much.

Brushing your hair too much can flatten it and make it look limp. Try to avoid brushing your hair too much and use your fingers instead to create volume.

3. Experiment with different hairstyles.

There are so many different ways to style big hair. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve big hair with short hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve big hair with short hair. Use a volumizing product and tease your hair at the roots to add volume.

Q: What if my hair is naturally straight and flat?

A: If your hair is naturally straight and flat, you may need to use more product and tease your hair more to achieve big hair.

Q: How can I prevent my big hair from falling flat?

A: Use a strong-hold hairspray and avoid touching your hair too much to prevent it from falling flat.

Conclusion

And there you have it – a step-by-step guide on how to big hair. Remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you. With a little practice, you’ll be rocking big hair in no time!
