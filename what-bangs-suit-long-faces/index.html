What Bangs Suit Long Faces A Guide To Flattering Fringes

Written by William Jun 23, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a long face, you may have struggled to find the perfect fringe or bangs that flatter your features. But fear not, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll explore what bangs suit long faces, as well as provide tips on how to style and care for your new 'do.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Determine Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Bangs for Long Faces
  • Tips for Styling Bangs on Long Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Bangs on Long Faces

Description

Bangs are a great way to change up your look and add some personality to your hairstyle. But for those with long faces, finding the right bangs can be a challenge. The wrong style can make your face appear even longer, so it’s important to choose wisely.

In general, the best bangs for long faces are those that add width and volume to the forehead area. This helps to balance out the length of the face and create a more harmonious look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best bangs for long faces, it’s important to determine your face shape. This will help you choose the right style for your features.

To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and pull your hair back. Look at the shape of your face and consider the following:

  • Forehead: Is it wide or narrow?
  • Cheekbones: Are they high or low?
  • Jawline: Is it round or square?

Based on these characteristics, you can determine your face shape. The most common face shapes include:

  • Oval
  • Round
  • Square
  • Heart
  • Diamond
  • Long (also known as oblong)

Step-by-Step Guide to Choosing Bangs for Long Faces

Now that you’ve determined your face shape, let’s take a look at the best bangs for long faces:

1. Blunt Bangs

Blunt bangs are a great option for long faces because they add width to the forehead area. This helps to balance out the length of the face and create a more proportional look. If you have a square jawline, consider adding some texture to your blunt bangs to soften the overall look.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are another great option for long faces. These bangs are longer on one side and shorter on the other, which helps to create a diagonal line across the face. This diagonal line helps to break up the length of the face and create a more balanced look.

3. Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs are a trendy option that work well on long faces. These bangs are parted down the middle and swept to the sides, framing the face. The middle part helps to create the illusion of width in the forehead area, while the side sweep helps to balance out the length of the face.

4. Wispy Bangs

Wispy bangs are a softer option that work well on long faces. These bangs are cut in a feathered, layered style, which helps to add texture and movement to the hair. This texture helps to break up the length of the face and create a more harmonious look.

Tips for Styling Bangs on Long Faces

Once you’ve chosen the perfect bangs for your long face, it’s important to style them properly. Here are some tips:

  • Blow-dry your bangs with a round brush to add volume and create a soft, rounded shape.
  • Use a small amount of hair wax or pomade to add texture and definition to your bangs.
  • Experiment with different parting options to find the most flattering look for your face shape.

FAQs

Can I still wear bangs if I have a long face?

Absolutely! The key is to choose the right style that flatters your features. Blunt bangs, side-swept bangs, curtain bangs, and wispy bangs are all great options for long faces.

How often do I need to trim my bangs?

It’s a good idea to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and polished.

Can I wear bangs if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! Just make sure to choose a style that works well with your natural curl pattern. Side-swept bangs and curtain bangs are great options for curly hair.

Hair Care Tips for Bangs on Long Faces

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your bangs looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent your bangs from becoming greasy.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products on your bangs, as this can weigh them down and make them look greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo between washes to keep your bangs looking fresh.
  • Avoid touching your bangs throughout the day, as this can transfer oils from your hands to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to choose the perfect bangs for your long face and keep them looking great day after day.
