How To Clip Your Hair At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Jul 07, 2023 · 4 min read

Clipping your hair at home can save you time and money, especially during the pandemic when salons are closed or operating at limited capacity. However, it can be intimidating if you’ve never done it before. Don’t worry, though, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll give you a step-by-step guide on how to clip your hair at home. We’ll also give you some tips, answer frequently asked questions, and provide some hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Clip Your Hair at Home
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Clip Your Hair at Home

Before we get started, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to have a towel to cover your shoulders and catch any hair that falls.

Now, let’s get into the steps:

1. Wash and dry your hair

It’s best to start with clean, dry hair. This will help you see the length and texture of your hair more clearly.

2. Decide on the length

Think about what length you want your hair to be. You may want to start with a longer guard on your clippers and work your way down if you’re not sure.

3. Choose a guard

Attach the guard to your clippers. The higher the number on the guard, the longer the hair will be. For example, a #4 guard will leave your hair 1/2 inch long.

4. Start at the bottom

Begin at the nape of your neck and work your way up. Use the clippers in a sweeping motion, going against the direction of hair growth. Be careful around your ears and hairline.

5. Blend the sides and top

Once you’ve finished the back, use the same guard on the sides and top of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, blending the hair as you go.

6. Use scissors for the top

If you want to leave the top of your hair longer, use scissors to trim it. Start by combing your hair straight up and cutting small sections at a time.

7. Check for any missed spots

Use a mirror to check for any spots you may have missed. Touch up as needed.

8. Clean up

Use a brush or towel to remove any loose hair from your shoulders and neck. Rinse your clippers and scissors and let them dry.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a summary of the steps:

  1. Wash and dry your hair
  2. Decide on the length
  3. Choose a guard
  4. Start at the bottom
  5. Blend the sides and top
  6. Use scissors for the top
  7. Check for any missed spots
  8. Clean up

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you’re not sure
  • Go slow and steady
  • Be careful around your ears and hairline
  • Use scissors for the top if you want to leave it longer
  • Check for any missed spots with a mirror

FAQ

Can I clip my hair wet?

No, it’s best to clip your hair dry. Wet hair can be more difficult to cut evenly.

What if I make a mistake?

Don’t worry, hair grows back! If you make a mistake, see if you can blend it in or wait for it to grow out.

How often should I clip my hair?

This depends on how fast your hair grows and how long you want it to be. On average, people clip their hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Now that you’ve clipped your hair at home, it’s important to take care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often
  • Eat a balanced diet with plenty of vitamins and minerals
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can confidently clip your hair at home. Good luck!
