Do Guys Like Shorter Hair

Written by Petter Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Short hair has been a popular trend for quite some time now, and if you're considering chopping off your locks, you may be wondering if guys like shorter hair. The truth is, it really depends on the guy and their personal preferences. However, there are certain styles and lengths that tend to be more universally attractive to men. In this article, we'll explore the question "do guys like shorter hair" and provide tips on how to choose a style that will suit you.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair Short
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair
  • FAQs About Short Hair and Attractiveness
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

How to Choose the Right Short Hair Style

Choosing the right short hair style will depend on a variety of factors, including your face shape, hair texture, and personal style. Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Consider your face shape: If you have a round face, go for a style that adds height and volume on top. If you have an oval face, you can pull off almost any short style. If you have a square face, try a layered cut that softens your features.
  • Think about your hair texture: If you have thick, curly hair, you may want to go for a shorter style that's easy to manage. If you have fine hair, a layered bob can add volume and depth.
  • Look for inspiration: Browse online or in magazines for short hair styles that speak to your personal style.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Your Hair Short

If you've decided to take the plunge and cut your hair short, here's a step-by-step guide to help you through the process:

  1. Start by washing and towel-drying your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts using clips.
  3. Starting at the back, use scissors to cut your hair to the desired length. Work your way up to the crown of your head.
  4. Move to the sides, cutting your hair to the desired length and blending it with the back.
  5. Finish by cutting the front, making sure to keep the length longer than the back and sides.
  6. Style your hair as desired using a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair

Once you've cut your hair short, it's important to take care of it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are a few tips:

  • Get regular trims: Short hair tends to grow out quickly, so be sure to get regular trims to maintain the shape and length of your cut.
  • Use the right products: Use products that are designed for your hair type and style, such as volumizing mousse, texturizing spray, or smoothing serum.
  • Experiment with different styles: Short hair can be versatile, so don't be afraid to play around with different styles, such as braids, top knots, or half-up/half-down styles.

FAQs About Short Hair and Attractiveness

Here are a few common questions people have about short hair and attractiveness:

Q: Do guys like short hair?

A: It really depends on the guy and their personal preferences. Some guys prefer long hair, while others find short hair to be more attractive.

Q: Does short hair make you look older?

A: Short hair can actually make you look younger and more modern, especially if you choose a style that flatters your face shape and features.

Q: Is short hair harder to maintain than long hair?

A: Short hair can be easier to maintain than long hair, since it requires less styling and product. However, you may need to get more frequent haircuts to maintain the shape and length of your cut.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Here are a few additional hair care tips for short hair:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from getting too oily.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good quality brush or comb to detangle your hair gently.

So, do guys like shorter hair? The answer is that it really depends on the guy and their personal preferences. However, if you're considering chopping off your locks, these tips and tricks will help you choose a style that flatters your face shape and features, as well as maintain your short hair for a modern and fashionable look.
