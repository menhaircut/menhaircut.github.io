Mens Haircuts Short Sides Curly Top The Perfect Style For 2023

If you're looking for a versatile and stylish haircut, then the mens haircuts short sides curly top is perfect for you. This style has been gaining popularity in recent years, and it's easy to see why. With short sides and a curly top, this hairstyle is both trendy and practical. In this article, we will discuss how to get this look, tips for maintaining it, and answer some frequently asked questions about this hairstyle.

How to Get Mens Haircuts Short Sides Curly Top

The mens haircuts short sides curly top style is a popular trend that can be achieved with the right tools and techniques. Before you get started, make sure you have the following:

  • Scissors
  • Clipper
  • Comb
  • Hair gel or pomade

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to get the mens haircuts short sides curly top:

  1. Start by trimming the sides of your hair with a clipper. The sides should be short, but not too short. A number two or three guard is ideal.
  2. Use scissors to cut the top of your hair. Leave enough length to create curls. A length of around 2-3 inches is perfect.
  3. Use a comb to separate the top of your hair from the sides. Apply hair gel or pomade to the top of your hair to define the curls and hold the style in place.
  4. Finally, use your fingers to shape and style your curls. You can also use a diffuser on your hair dryer to add volume and definition to your curls.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your mens haircuts short sides curly top:

  • Regularly trim the sides of your hair to keep the style looking fresh.
  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls looking healthy.
  • Apply hair gel or pomade to the top of your hair every day to keep the curls defined and in place.
  • Avoid using a brush on your curls, as this can cause them to frizz and lose their shape.

FAQs About Mens Haircuts Short Sides Curly Top

What face shape does this hairstyle suit?

The mens haircuts short sides curly top style looks great on a variety of face shapes, including round, square, and oval.

Can this hairstyle be worn by men with thinning hair?

Yes, men with thinning hair can still rock this style. Just make sure to keep the sides short to create the illusion of thicker hair.

How often should I trim my hair?

To maintain this style, you should trim your hair every four to six weeks.

Hair Care for Mens Haircuts Short Sides Curly Top

Here are some hair care tips for mens haircuts short sides curly top:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls looking healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as this can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

In conclusion, the mens haircuts short sides curly top is a stylish and versatile haircut that can be achieved with the right tools and techniques. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can rock this style with confidence and ease.
