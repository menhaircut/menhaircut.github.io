Short in Back Long in Front Curly Haircut

If you're looking for a trendy and stylish haircut, then the short in back long in front curly haircut is the perfect option for you. This haircut features a shorter length at the back and longer length in the front, with curly hair adding a touch of sophistication and elegance. In this guide, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this haircut, including how to do it, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The short in back long in front curly haircut is a modern and fashionable hairstyle that adds volume and texture to curly hair. It's a versatile haircut that works well for both men and women, and it can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. The shorter length at the back creates a clean and polished look, while the longer length in the front adds movement and dimension to the hair. This haircut can be styled in different ways, from messy and casual to sleek and sophisticated.

How to

If you want to try the short in back long in front curly haircut, you should first find a professional stylist who specializes in curly haircuts. They can help you determine the best length and shape for your face and hair type. To achieve this haircut, the stylist will typically cut the back of your hair short, gradually increasing the length towards the front. They will also add layers to create volume and movement, and shape the hair to frame your face.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to achieving the short in back long in front curly haircut:

  1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for curly hair.
  2. Towel dry your hair, but don't rub it vigorously as this can cause frizz.
  3. Apply a styling product to your damp hair to enhance your curls and add texture.
  4. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to maintain your curls.
  5. Section your hair into layers, starting from the back and working your way towards the front.
  6. Cut the back of your hair short, leaving the front longer.
  7. Add layers to create volume and movement.
  8. Shape the hair to frame your face.
  9. Style your hair as desired using a curling iron, flat iron, or other heat styling tools.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your short in back long in front curly haircut:

  • Use products that are specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-enhancing creams, mousses, and gels.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.
  • Invest in a high-quality diffuser attachment for your blow dryer, as this can help maintain your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that works best for your hair type and lifestyle.

FAQ

What face shapes does the short in back long in front curly haircut work best for?

This haircut works well for oval, heart, and square face shapes.

Can I still wear my hair up with this haircut?

Yes, you can still wear your hair up with this haircut. Try a half-up, half-down style or a messy bun to show off the length in the front.

How often should I trim my hair?

You should trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

To keep your short in back long in front curly haircut looking its best, you should follow a regular hair care routine. This should include washing and conditioning your hair with products that are suitable for curly hair, using a diffuser attachment when blow-drying your hair, and avoiding over-styling with heat tools. You should also trim your hair regularly to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. By following these tips, you can keep your haircut looking stylish and fashionable.
