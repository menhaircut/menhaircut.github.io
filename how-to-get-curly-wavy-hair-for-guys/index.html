How To Get Curly Wavy Hair For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Christine Oct 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to change up your style? Curly wavy hair can give you a whole new look and add some texture to your hair. In this article, we will discuss how to get curly wavy hair for guys. We will cover everything from how to style your hair to hair care tips to maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Wavy Hair for Guys
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Wavy Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Wavy Hair

How to Get Curly Wavy Hair for Guys

There are several ways to get curly wavy hair for guys. Here are some of the most popular methods:

1. Use a Curling Iron

A curling iron can create curls and waves in straight hair. Choose a curling iron with a barrel size that matches the size of the curls you want. Wrap your hair around the barrel, hold it for a few seconds, and release. Repeat until you have the desired amount of curls.

2. Use a Perm

A perm is a chemical treatment that can make your hair curly or wavy. However, it is a permanent treatment and can damage your hair if not done correctly. It is best to go to a professional hairstylist for a perm.

3. Braid Your Hair

Braiding your hair when it is wet can create waves in your hair when it dries. The tighter the braids, the tighter the waves. Looser braids will create more natural-looking waves.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get curly wavy hair for guys:

1. Wash Your Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. This will help define your curls and reduce frizz.

2. Apply Curling Cream

Apply a curling cream or mousse to your damp hair. This will add texture to your hair and help define your curls.

3. Use a Diffuser

Use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to dry your hair. This will help create more defined curls and reduce frizz.

4. Use a Curling Iron

If you want more defined curls, use a curling iron to touch up any areas that need it.

5. Finish with Hairspray

Finish your look with a hairspray that will hold your curls in place.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Wavy Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly wavy hair:

1. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip your hair of its natural oils and dry it out. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days.

2. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and reduce frizz.

3. Use a Silk Pillowcase

A silk pillowcase can reduce friction on your hair while you sleep, reducing frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Can I get curly wavy hair without using heat?

Yes, you can braid your hair when it is wet and let it dry to create waves. You can also use a sea salt spray to add texture to your hair.

How often should I wash my curly wavy hair?

You should wash your hair every other day or every few days to prevent your hair from drying out.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Wavy Hair

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining your curly wavy hair:

1. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to moisturize your hair and prevent breakage.

2. Don't Use Hot Water

Hot water can dry out your hair and cause frizz. Use lukewarm water instead.

3. Use a Leave-In Conditioner

Use a leave-in conditioner to add moisture to your hair throughout the day.

With these tips and tricks, you can get curly wavy hair for guys and maintain your new look. Remember to take care of your hair to prevent breakage and keep your curls looking their best.
