Ways To Wear Short Curly Hair Up

Written by Eveline Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short curly hair, you may think that your styling options are limited. However, there are plenty of ways to wear short curly hair up that are easy, fun, and stylish. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks for creating beautiful updos that will make the most of your natural curls.

Description

Short curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. On the one hand, it’s full of volume and texture, and it can be incredibly fun to experiment with different styles. On the other hand, it can be difficult to manage, and it can be hard to find styles that work for your hair type. If you’re looking for ways to wear short curly hair up, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll share some of our favorite styles that are perfect for curly hair.

How To

Before we dive into the different ways to wear short curly hair up, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, it’s important to start with clean, well-conditioned hair. This will help your curls look their best and make it easier to style your hair. Second, you’ll want to use the right products for your hair type. Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as creams, gels, and mousses. Finally, be gentle with your hair. Curly hair can be delicate, and rough handling can cause frizz and breakage.

Step-by-Step

1. Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products designed for curly hair. Towel-dry your hair and apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls.

2. Apply a curl-enhancing cream or mousse to your hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths.

3. Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. This will help enhance your natural curls and minimize frizz.

4. Once your hair is dry, use your fingers to gently separate your curls and create volume.

5. Decide on a style that works for you. Some popular options include a messy bun, a half-up style, or a braided updo.

6. Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your hair in place. Be sure to tuck any loose ends under the rest of your hair to create a polished look.

7. Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips

1. Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type. Some people find that creams work better than gels, while others prefer mousses.

2. Don’t be afraid to play with different styles. Short curly hair can be incredibly versatile, and there are plenty of options to choose from.

3. If you’re struggling with frizz, try using a silk pillowcase at night. This can help minimize frizz and keep your curls looking their best.

4. Be gentle with your hair. Curly hair can be delicate, so avoid rough handling or using harsh brushes or combs.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my short curly hair up without using heat?

A: Yes! There are plenty of heat-free styling options for short curly hair. Try using bobby pins or hair ties to create a messy bun or a braided updo.

Q: What products should I use for my short curly hair?

A: Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as creams, gels, and mousses. You may also want to try using a leave-in conditioner to help detangle your curls and keep them looking their best.

Hair Care

When it comes to caring for your short curly hair, there are a few things you should keep in mind. First, be gentle with your hair. Curly hair can be delicate, so avoid rough handling or using harsh brushes or combs. Second, use the right products for your hair type. Look for products that are designed for curly hair, such as creams, gels, and mousses. Finally, be sure to protect your hair from heat damage. If you do use heat styling tools, be sure to use a heat protectant spray first.
