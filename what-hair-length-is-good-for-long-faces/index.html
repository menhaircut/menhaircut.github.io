What Hair Length Is Good For Long Faces

Written by Bardi Jul 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of trying out different hairstyles that just don't seem to work with your long face? As someone with a long face, it can be challenging to find a hairstyle that complements your facial structure. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll share with you what hair length is good for long faces, as well as some tips and hair care advice to help you rock your new look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into what hair length is good for long faces, it's essential to determine your face shape. Knowing your face shape is crucial as it will help you find a hairstyle that flatters your unique features. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Take a photo of yourself facing the camera straight on.
  2. Trace the outline of your face onto the photo using a pen or pencil.
  3. Compare the shape of your face to the following face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, and diamond.

If your face is longer than it is wide, you have a long face shape. Your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, creating an elongated appearance. Now that you know your face shape let's move on to the fun stuff – finding the perfect hair length for your long face.

Step-by-Step Guide: Finding the Perfect Hair Length for Your Long Face

When it comes to finding the perfect hair length for long faces, there are a few key things to keep in mind. The goal is to create balance and add width to your face, so you'll want to look for hairstyles that do just that. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you find the perfect hair length:

Step 1: Consider Your Hair Type

Before you choose a hairstyle, consider your hair type. The length that works best for you will depend on whether you have fine, thick, curly, or straight hair. For example, if you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a longer length to help weigh it down and prevent it from looking too voluminous.

Step 2: Opt for Shoulder-Length Hair

Shoulder-length hair is an excellent option for long faces as it adds width to the face. This length will also help to balance out your facial features. Consider adding layers to your hair to create texture and volume.

Step 3: Try a Bob Cut

If you prefer shorter hair, a bob cut can be a great option for long faces. Choose a bob that falls just above or below the chin to help create the illusion of width. You can also add layers or bangs to create more volume and texture.

Step 4: Avoid Long, Straight Hair

Long, straight hair can make a long face appear even longer. If you have long hair, consider adding layers to create volume and movement. You can also try adding waves or curls to your hair to create more width and volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Long Face Hairstyle

Now that you've found the perfect hair length for your long face, you'll want to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new hairstyle:

  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in quality hair care products that are specifically designed for your hair type.
  • Consider using a heat protectant spray before using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to keep your look fresh and exciting.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for long faces?

The best haircut for long faces is one that adds width and balances out your facial features. Shoulder-length hair and bob cuts are both great options for long faces.

Does short or long hair look better on a long face?

Short to medium-length hair looks best on long faces. Long hair can make a long face appear even longer, while shorter hair adds width and balances out the face.

Can bangs help balance out a long face?

Yes, bangs can help balance out a long face. Consider getting side-swept bangs or choppy, textured bangs to create more width and volume.

Hair Care Tips for Long Face Hairstyles

To keep your long face hairstyle looking its best, it's essential to take care of your hair. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or applying a UV protectant spray.

Now that you know what hair length is good for long faces, it's time to head to the salon and try out a new look. Remember to consider your hair type and face shape when choosing a hairstyle, and don't forget to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
