Shoulder Length Haircuts For Long Faces A Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you someone with a long face who is looking to switch up their hairstyle? Look no further than shoulder length haircuts! This versatile length can work for a variety of face shapes, but particularly suits those with longer faces. In this article, we'll discuss how to choose and style the perfect shoulder length haircut for your long face.

How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Haircut

When choosing a shoulder length haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. Those with longer faces should aim for a cut that adds width and volume to the sides of the face, while still maintaining length. Avoid cuts that are too long or too short, as these can elongate the face even further.

A popular shoulder length haircut for long faces is the long bob, or "lob." This cut typically falls just above or below the shoulders and features layers and texture to add volume and movement to the hair. Another great option is the shoulder-length shag, which incorporates choppy layers and fringe to add texture and volume to the sides of the face.

Step by Step: Getting a Shoulder Length Haircut

Once you've decided on the perfect shoulder length haircut for your long face, it's time to book a salon appointment. Here's what to expect:

  1. Consult with your stylist about the best length and style for your face shape.
  2. Wash and condition your hair before your appointment.
  3. Sit back and relax while your stylist cuts and styles your hair.
  4. Ask your stylist for tips on how to maintain and style your new haircut at home.

Tips for Styling Shoulder Length Haircuts for Long Faces

When styling your new shoulder length haircut, keep these tips in mind:

  • Add volume and texture to the sides of your face with a curling iron or texturizing spray.
  • Avoid center parts, as they can elongate the face even further. Instead, opt for a side part to add width to the face.
  • Consider adding bangs to balance out the length of your face.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories, such as headbands or hair clips, to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ: Shoulder Length Haircuts for Long Faces

Q: Will a shoulder length haircut make my face look even longer?

A: Not necessarily! When done correctly, a shoulder length haircut can actually add width and balance to a long face.

Q: How often should I get my shoulder length haircut trimmed?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and health of your haircut.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a shoulder length haircut?

A: Absolutely! Shoulder length hair can be styled in a variety of updos and half-up styles.

Hair Care for Shoulder Length Haircuts

To keep your shoulder length haircut looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can damage your hair. Instead, opt for heatless hairstyles or use a heat protectant spray before styling.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Conclusion

Shoulder length haircuts are a great option for those with long faces. By choosing the right cut and styling it with volume and texture, you can balance out the proportions of your face and create a chic, versatile look. Follow our tips and hair care advice to keep your shoulder length haircut looking its best!
