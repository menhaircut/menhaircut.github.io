How To Blow Dry Hair With Normal Dryer

Written by Oliver Jun 19, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to achieve a salon-quality blowout at home, you may think you need to invest in a fancy hair dryer. However, you can achieve great results using a normal dryer. Here's how:

Description

Blow drying your hair can be a bit intimidating, especially if you're not sure how to use the tools or what products to use. But with a little practice and the right technique, you can achieve a salon-worthy blowout right at home. Using a normal hair dryer is an affordable and easy way to achieve the look you want.

How to Blow Dry Hair with Normal Dryer

Follow these simple steps to blow dry your hair with a normal dryer:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Towel-Dried Hair

Before you begin blow drying your hair, make sure it's clean and towel-dried. Gently blot your hair with a towel to remove any excess moisture, but don't rub or twist your hair, as this can cause damage.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

To protect your hair from heat damage, apply a heat protectant spray to your hair before you begin blow drying. This will help to prevent your hair from becoming dry and brittle, and will also help to reduce frizz.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to blow dry your hair evenly and will also help to prevent tangles.

Step 4: Start Blow Drying

Start blow drying your hair from the roots to the tips, using a round brush to add volume and shape. Use your other hand to hold the dryer, and direct the airflow down the hair shaft. Be sure to keep the dryer moving, and don't focus the heat on one area for too long, as this can cause damage.

Step 5: Finish with Cool Air

Once you've finished blow drying your hair, switch to the cool setting on your dryer and use it to set your style. Cool air helps to seal the hair cuticle, which can help to reduce frizz and add shine.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve a great blowout:

  • Use a round brush to add volume and shape to your hair
  • Don't focus the heat on one area for too long, as this can cause damage
  • Switch to the cool setting on your dryer to set your style and reduce frizz
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent heat damage
  • Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to blow dry

FAQ

Can I blow dry my hair every day?

Blow drying your hair every day can cause damage and make your hair dry and brittle. Try to limit your blow drying to a few times a week, and use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

What kind of brush should I use when blow drying my hair?

A round brush is best for blow drying your hair, as it can add volume and shape to your hair. Look for a brush with natural bristles, as these are gentler on your hair.

Hair Care

Blow drying your hair can be a great way to achieve a sleek, polished look. However, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage. Be sure to use a heat protectant spray, and limit your blow drying to a few times a week. You can also use a deep conditioning treatment to help nourish your hair and keep it looking healthy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve a great blowout with a normal hair dryer right at home.
