How to Wear a Midi Dress If You Are Short

If you are short, you may feel like midi dresses overwhelm your frame. However, with the right styling, you can rock a midi dress like a pro.

Choose the Right Length

The ideal midi dress length for short women is just above or below the knee. This length will elongate your legs and make you appear taller. Avoid midi dresses that fall mid-calf or lower as they can make you look shorter.

Opt for Vertical Patterns

Vertical patterns, such as pinstripes or vertical stripes, create an illusion of height. They draw the eye up and down and create the appearance of a longer silhouette.

Wear High Heels

High heels are a short woman's best friend. They add height and create the illusion of longer legs. When wearing a midi dress, opt for nude or neutral heels as they will create a seamless look and not cut off your legs.

Accessorize with a Belt

To accentuate your waistline and create a flattering silhouette, add a belt to your midi dress. A belt will break up the dress and create the appearance of longer legs. Opt for a thin belt to avoid overwhelming your frame.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to style your midi dress if you are short:

  1. Choose a midi dress that falls just above or below the knee.
  2. Opt for vertical patterns or solid colors.
  3. Wear high heels to add height.
  4. Accessorize with a thin belt to accentuate your waistline.
  5. Complete the look with simple jewelry and a clutch or crossbody bag.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips to help you wear a midi dress if you are short:

  • Choose a fitted midi dress to avoid overwhelming your frame.
  • Opt for a V-neckline to create the appearance of a longer neck.
  • Wear a cropped denim jacket or cardigan to create a balanced look.
  • Choose a midi dress with a side slit to create the appearance of longer legs.

FAQs

Can short women wear midi dresses?

Yes, short women can wear midi dresses. It's all about choosing the right length and styling it in a flattering way.

How do you wear a midi dress if you are short?

To wear a midi dress if you are short, opt for a dress that falls just above or below the knee, wear high heels, choose vertical patterns, and accessorize with a belt.

What shoes should you wear with a midi dress?

High heels are the best shoes to wear with a midi dress. Opt for nude or neutral heels to create a seamless look.

Hair Care Tips

When wearing a midi dress, your hair can be the finishing touch to your look. Here are some hair care tips to consider:

  • Wear your hair in a high ponytail or bun to create the illusion of height.
  • Opt for a side part to create the appearance of a longer face.
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements the neckline of your midi dress.

By following these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a midi dress if you are short. Remember to choose the right length, opt for vertical patterns, wear high heels, and accessorize with a belt. With these styling techniques, you'll be turning heads in your midi dress in no time!
