How To Wear Big Hair Clips A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jul 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyles and looking to switch things up? One trend that has been gaining popularity is the use of big hair clips. These statement accessories can add a touch of glamour to any outfit and can be worn in a variety of ways. In this article, we’ll show you how to wear big hair clips and provide some tips and tricks for making the most of this trend.

How to wear big hair clips

Before we get into the different ways you can wear big hair clips, it’s important to know how to properly secure them in your hair. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Before you start styling, make sure your hair is clean and dry. You can add some texture or volume by using a texturizing spray or dry shampoo.

Step 2: Choose your clip

There are many different types of big hair clips available, from simple barrettes to embellished designs. Choose one that complements your outfit and personal style.

Step 3: Section your hair

Decide where you want to place your hair clip and section off a small portion of hair at that spot.

Step 4: Secure the clip

Take the hair clip and slide it into the section of hair, making sure it’s secure. You can add a bobby pin or two for extra hold.

Step 5: Repeat

If you want to add more clips, repeat the process in different sections of your hair. Be sure to balance out the placement of the clips so your hairstyle looks symmetrical.

Tips for wearing big hair clips

Now that you know how to wear big hair clips, here are some tips for making the most of this trend:

Choose the right size

When it comes to big hair clips, bigger isn’t always better. If you have fine hair, a giant clip may overwhelm your locks. On the other hand, if you have thick hair, a small clip may get lost in the volume. Experiment with different sizes to find what works best for you.

Coordinate with your outfit

Big hair clips can be a statement accessory, so make sure they complement your outfit. If you’re wearing a bold print or bright color, choose a clip that is more subdued. If you’re wearing a simple outfit, a sparkly or embellished clip can add some pizzazz.

Play with placement

You don’t have to stick to one spot when wearing big hair clips. Try placing them at different angles or heights for a more dynamic look. You can also stack them on top of each other for a more dramatic effect.

Don’t overdo it

While big hair clips can be a fun trend to try, it’s easy to go overboard. Stick to one or two clips at most to avoid looking too flashy.

FAQ

Are big hair clips suitable for all hair types?

Yes, big hair clips can work for all hair types, but you may need to adjust the size and placement based on your hair’s texture and volume.

Can big hair clips be worn for formal occasions?

Yes, big hair clips can be a great addition to a formal hairstyle. Choose a clip that is more elegant and subdued, such as a pearl-encrusted design.

Hair Care

When wearing big hair clips, it’s important to take care of your hair to ensure it looks healthy and voluminous. Here are some tips:

Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body to your hair.

Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and make it harder to style.

Use a texturizing spray or dry shampoo to add texture and grip to your hair before adding the clip.

Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look stiff.

By following these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to rock the big hair clip trend with confidence. Whether you’re wearing a simple barrette or an embellished design, these accessories can add a touch of glamour to any hairstyle.
