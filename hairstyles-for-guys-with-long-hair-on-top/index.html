Hairstyles For Guys With Long Hair On Top A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Long hair on top is a popular hairstyle choice for men. It can provide a unique and stylish look that sets you apart from the crowd. However, maintaining long hair can be challenging without the right hairstyle. In this article, we will explore different hairstyles for guys with long hair on top, how to achieve them, tips for maintenance, and more.

Table of Contents

  • How to Achieve Long Hair on Top
  • Hairstyles for Guys with Long Hair on Top
  • Hair Care Tips for Long Hair on Top
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Achieve Long Hair on Top

Before we dive into hairstyles, let's discuss how to achieve long hair on top. Growing your hair out takes time and patience. You will need to avoid getting regular haircuts and let your hair grow naturally. Depending on how fast your hair grows, it may take several months to a year to achieve the desired length.

During this time, it is essential to keep your hair healthy by eating a balanced diet, using quality hair products, and avoiding heat styling tools. Regularly washing and conditioning your hair can also help keep it healthy and prevent damage.

Hairstyles for Guys with Long Hair on Top

Once you've achieved long hair on top, it's time to choose a hairstyle that suits your personality and style. Here are some popular hairstyles for guys with long hair on top:

The Man Bun

The man bun is a classic hairstyle for guys with long hair on top. To achieve the look, gather your hair into a ponytail at the crown of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure the bun with a hair tie or bobby pins. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual or formal look.

The Top Knot

The top knot is similar to the man bun, but the hair is gathered at the top of the head instead of the crown. To achieve the look, gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it into a knot. Secure the knot with a hair tie or bobby pins. This hairstyle is perfect for a trendy and edgy look.

The Messy Fringe

The messy fringe is a great option for guys with long hair on top who want a more relaxed look. To achieve the look, let your hair fall naturally over your forehead and use a styling product, such as a pomade or wax, to create a messy texture. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual look.

The Slicked-Back Look

The slicked-back look is a classic and sophisticated hairstyle for guys with long hair on top. To achieve the look, use a styling product, such as gel or pomade, to slick your hair back from your forehead. This hairstyle is perfect for a formal or professional look.

Hair Care Tips for Long Hair on Top

Maintaining long hair on top requires proper hair care. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I still style my hair if it's thin?

Yes, you can still style your hair if it's thin. However, certain hairstyles, such as the man bun or top knot, may not be suitable as they require more hair volume. Opt for hairstyles that provide more texture and volume, such as the messy fringe.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash your hair more frequently, such as every other day. If you have dry hair, you may only need to wash your hair once or twice a week. If you exercise often, you may need to wash your hair more frequently to remove sweat and dirt.

What styling product should I use?

It depends on the hairstyle you want to achieve and your hair type. For example, if you want a messy texture, you can use a pomade or wax. If you want a slicked-back look, you can use a gel or pomade. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Long hair on top can provide a unique and stylish look for guys. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can maintain healthy and beautiful hair. Experiment with different hairstyles and products to find what works best for you. Good luck!
