Best Updo For Heart Shaped Face Perfect Hairstyles For Flattering Your Face Shape

Written by Eveline Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the best updo for heart shaped face? Look no further! Heart shaped faces have a unique structure that requires specific hairstyles to flatter your features. In this article, we'll explore the best updo styles for heart shaped faces, how to create them, and tips for maintaining them.

Description

Heart shaped faces are characterized by a wider forehead, prominent cheekbones, and a narrow, pointed chin. The best updo for heart shaped face should draw attention away from the chin and balance out the face's proportions. Choosing the right hairstyle can enhance your best features and make you feel confident and beautiful.

How to

The best updo for heart shaped face is a hairstyle that lifts the hair off the face and adds volume to the crown. Here's how to create the perfect updo:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls throughout the hair.
  3. Gather the hair at the crown of the head and secure with bobby pins or an elastic band.
  4. For a more polished look, smooth the hair with a brush and add hairspray to hold the style in place.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to creating the best updo for heart shaped face:

  1. Begin with clean, dry hair. Brush out any tangles and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  2. Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls throughout the hair. This will add volume and texture to the hair.
  3. Gather the hair at the crown of the head and secure with bobby pins or an elastic band. Make sure to leave some strands of hair loose around the face to soften the look.
  4. Smooth the hair with a brush and add hairspray to hold the style in place.
  5. For a more polished look, you can add a hair accessory such as a headband, hair clip, or ribbon.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining the best updo for heart shaped face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to the hair.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray, as this can weigh down the hair and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to add variety to your updo.
  • If you have shorter hair, try a half-up, half-down style or a side-swept updo.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear my hair down with a heart shaped face?

A: Yes, you can wear your hair down with a heart shaped face, but make sure to add volume to the crown and avoid a center part that can draw attention to the chin.

Q: What other hairstyles are good for heart shaped faces?

A: Other hairstyles that flatter heart shaped faces include side-swept bangs, layered cuts, and long bobs.

Q: Can I wear a ponytail with a heart shaped face?

A: Yes, you can wear a ponytail with a heart shaped face, but make sure to add volume to the crown and avoid a high ponytail that can elongate the face.

Hair Care

Keeping your hair healthy and nourished is essential for maintaining any hairstyle. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as curling irons and straighteners too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and tricks, you can create the best updo for heart shaped face and feel confident and beautiful. Experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect look for you!
