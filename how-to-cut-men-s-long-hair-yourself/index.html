How To Cut Men s Long Hair Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Nov 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

In this day and age, going to the salon for a haircut is not always a feasible option. Whether it's because of a tight schedule or financial constraints, many men may find themselves in need of a haircut but unable to visit a professional. That's where this guide comes in handy. Cutting men's long hair yourself may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done successfully. Read on for a step-by-step guide on how to cut men's long hair yourself.

Description

Cutting men's long hair yourself can seem like a daunting task, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done successfully. This guide will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to cut men's long hair yourself, as well as tips for achieving a professional-looking cut.

How to Cut Men's Long Hair Yourself

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step in cutting men's long hair yourself is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and hair ties. It is also helpful to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair.

Step 2: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before you begin cutting, make sure the hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style. Shampoo and condition the hair as you normally would, and then towel dry it until it is only slightly damp.

Step 3: Section the Hair

Using your comb, section the hair into four equal parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Secure each section with a hair tie so that it is out of the way while you work on the other sections.

Step 4: Start with the Sides

Begin cutting the sides of the hair. Using your clippers, start at the bottom of the hairline and work your way up, using a guard that is appropriate for the length of hair you want to achieve. Use your comb to guide the clippers, and be sure to blend the hair as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 5: Move on to the Back

Once you have finished the sides, move on to the back of the hair. Use the same technique as before, starting at the bottom and working your way up. Be sure to blend the hair as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 6: Cut the Top

Now it's time to cut the top of the hair. Using your scissors, start at the front and work your way back, cutting small sections of hair at a time. Be sure to cut straight across, and blend the hair as you go to avoid any harsh lines.

Step 7: Check for Evenness

Once you have finished cutting the hair, it's important to check for evenness. Use your comb to comb through the hair, and look for any uneven sections that may need to be trimmed.

Step 8: Style the Hair

Now that the hair is cut, it's time to style it. Use your comb and/or your fingers to style the hair as desired. You can use a hair product, such as gel or pomade, to help hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Take your time and be patient. Cutting hair can be time-consuming, but rushing through it can lead to mistakes.
  • Start with a longer guard on your clippers, and work your way down to a shorter guard if necessary. It's easier to take more hair off than it is to put it back on.
  • If you're unsure about cutting the hair, start with a small section and see how it looks before moving on to the rest of the hair.
  • If you're having trouble blending the hair, try using a comb with a longer tooth spacing to help create a smoother transition between lengths.

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair if it's very long?

Cutting long hair can be more challenging than cutting shorter hair, but it is still possible to do it yourself. Just be sure to take your time and use the right tools and techniques.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. Depending on the severity of the mistake, you may be able to fix it by trimming the hair in the affected area. If the mistake is more significant, you may need to visit a professional to have it fixed.

Do I need to use hair product after cutting my hair?

Using hair product after cutting your hair is optional, but it can help hold the style in place and give it a finished look.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it's important to take good care of it to keep it looking healthy and vibrant. This includes washing and conditioning your hair regularly, using a heat protectant when using styling tools, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Cutting men's long hair yourself may seem intimidating, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done successfully. Follow this step-by-step guide and you'll be able to achieve a professional-looking cut from the comfort of your own home.
