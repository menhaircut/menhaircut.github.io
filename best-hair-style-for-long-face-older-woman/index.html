Best Hairstyles For Long Face Older Women

Written by William Jul 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our face shape may change, and some women may find themselves with a longer face shape. Choosing the right hairstyle for a long face can make a big difference in how you look and feel. In this article, we will be discussing the best hairstyles for long face older women.

Description

A long face shape is one that is longer than it is wide. This face shape can be beautiful, but it can also be challenging to find the right hairstyle that complements your features. The goal with any hairstyle is to create balance and proportion, so the face appears more oval.

How to Choose the Best Hairstyle for a Long Face

When choosing a hairstyle for a long face, the goal is to create the illusion of width. This can be achieved by adding volume and texture to the sides of the face. Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle:

1. Avoid Straight and Flat Hair

Straight and flat hair can elongate the face even more. Instead, opt for hairstyles with volume and texture to create width and balance.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help break up the length of the face and add volume to the sides. They can be worn with any length of hair and are a great way to create balance.

3. Layers

Layers can add volume and texture to the hair, which can help create the illusion of width. They can be added to any length of hair, and the placement of the layers will depend on the individual's face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Hair for a Long Face

Now that you know what to look for in a hairstyle, here is a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

1. Start with Clean Hair

Wash and condition your hair using products that are appropriate for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body.

2. Blow-Dry Your Hair

Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture. Focus on the sides of your hair to create width and balance.

3. Add Layers

If you have long hair, consider adding layers to create volume and texture. Work with your hairstylist to determine the best placement of the layers for your face shape.

4. Style Your Bangs

If you have side-swept bangs, style them by blow-drying them to the side using a round brush. If you don't have bangs, consider adding them to break up the length of your face.

5. Finish with Product

Finish your hairstyle with a product that will give your hair hold and shine. Use a lightweight product that won't weigh your hair down or make it look greasy.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Here are some tips for maintaining your hairstyle:

1. Get Regular Trims

Regular trims will keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. They will also help maintain the shape of your hairstyle.

2. Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Elements

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and cold weather by wearing a hat or scarf.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the Best Hairstyle for a Long Face?

The best hairstyle for a long face is one that adds volume and texture to the sides of the face. This can be achieved with layers, side-swept bangs, and hairstyles with volume and texture.

2. Can I Wear Short Hair with a Long Face?

Yes, you can wear short hair with a long face. The key is to add volume and texture to the sides of the face, which can be achieved with layers and side-swept bangs.

3. What is the Best Product to Use for Hold and Shine?

The best product to use for hold and shine will depend on your hair type and the style you are trying to achieve. A lightweight pomade or wax can provide hold and shine without weighing your hair down.

Hair Care for Long Face Older Women

When it comes to hair care for long face older women, the same rules apply as for any age. Use products that are appropriate for your hair type, get regular trims, and protect your hair from the elements. If you have concerns about hair thinning, consider using products that promote hair growth.

Overall, the best hairstyles for long face older women are those that add volume and texture to the sides of the face. With the right hairstyle and proper hair care, you can look and feel your best at any age.
