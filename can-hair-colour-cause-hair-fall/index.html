Can Hair Colour Cause Hair Fall? - Hair Care Tips and FAQ

Description

Many people wonder whether hair colour can cause hair fall. The answer is not straightforward as there are various factors that can contribute to hair fall. However, it is true that hair colouring can lead to hair fall in some cases, especially if certain precautions are not taken.


How to

If you want to colour your hair and avoid hair fall, there are some things you can do:

  • Choose a hair colour that is gentle and does not contain harsh chemicals.
  • Avoid frequent hair colouring as it can weaken the hair and make it more prone to breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors that can damage it.

Step by step

Here are the steps you can follow to colour your hair and reduce the risk of hair fall:

  1. Choose a hair colour that is gentle and does not contain harsh chemicals.
  2. Do a patch test to make sure you are not allergic to the hair colour.
  3. Deep condition your hair a week before colouring to prepare it for the process.
  4. Apply the hair colour according to the instructions and avoid overlapping the colour on previously coloured hair.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly and apply a deep conditioner to restore moisture.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall when colouring your hair:

  • Avoid using hot styling tools on coloured hair as it can make it more prone to breakage.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and avoid rubbing it vigorously with a towel.
  • Consider getting a professional hair colouring treatment if you are not confident in doing it yourself.

FAQ

Can hair colour cause hair fall?

Yes, hair colouring can lead to hair fall in some cases, especially if certain precautions are not taken.


What are some gentle hair colours I can use?

Henna, vegetable-based dyes, and semi-permanent dyes are gentler options for hair colouring.


How often should I colour my hair?

Avoid frequent hair colouring as it can weaken the hair and make it more prone to breakage. Ideally, wait at least 6-8 weeks between colouring sessions.


Hair Care

Regardless of whether you colour your hair or not, taking care of your hair is essential to prevent hair fall. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools frequently and protect your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.
  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Get regular hair trims to prevent split ends.
