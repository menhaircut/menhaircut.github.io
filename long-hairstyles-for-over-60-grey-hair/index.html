Long Hairstyles For Over 60 Grey Hair A Guide To Rocking Your Grey Locks

Written by William Sep 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Welcome to our guide on long hairstyles for over 60 grey hair. As we age, our hair goes through a lot of changes, and one of the most noticeable is the appearance of grey hair. However, this doesn't mean that we have to cut our hair short or settle for boring hairstyles. In fact, long hairstyles can be incredibly flattering for grey hair and can help you look and feel your best. In this guide, we'll share some tips, tricks, and inspiration for rocking long hair in your 60s and beyond.

Why Choose Long Hairstyles for Over 60 Grey Hair?

Long hairstyles have a lot of benefits for women over 60. For starters, they can help to elongate the face and create a more youthful appearance. They're also incredibly versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways to suit different occasions and moods. Plus, long hair can be incredibly low-maintenance, especially if you opt for a simple, wash-and-go style.

How to Choose the Right Long Hairstyle for Your Grey Hair

When choosing a long hairstyle for your grey hair, there are a few things to consider:

Face Shape

Your face shape will play a big role in determining which long hairstyle will look best on you. As a general rule, those with round faces should opt for longer hairstyles that help to elongate the face, while those with square faces should go for softer, layered styles.

Hair Texture

Your hair texture will also play a role in determining which long hairstyle will work best for you. For example, those with fine, straight hair may find that a blunt cut works best, while those with thick, curly hair may prefer a layered style that helps to control the volume.

Lifestyle

Finally, your lifestyle should also be taken into consideration when choosing a long hairstyle. If you're someone who likes to keep things simple and low-maintenance, a wash-and-go style may be best. However, if you enjoy experimenting with different hairstyles and have more time to devote to styling your hair, a more intricate style may be a better fit.

Step-by-Step Guide to Creating Long Hairstyles for Over 60 Grey Hair

Once you've chosen the right long hairstyle for your grey hair, it's time to create the look. Here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will help to keep your grey hair looking bright and healthy.

Step 2: Apply Styling Products

Apply a small amount of styling product to your damp hair, such as a leave-in conditioner or styling cream. This will help to tame frizz and add shine.

Step 3: Blow Dry

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and create a smooth, polished look. If you have curly hair, you may want to use a diffuser attachment to help define your curls.

Step 4: Style

Once your hair is dry, it's time to style it. Depending on the look you're going for, you may want to curl it, straighten it, or leave it as is. You can also experiment with different partings to create different looks.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a light mist of hairspray to help hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Long Grey Hair

Now that you have your long grey hair looking fabulous, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

Use a Purple Shampoo

Grey hair can sometimes take on a yellow or brassy tint, especially if you live in an area with hard water. To combat this, use a purple shampoo once a week to help neutralize any unwanted tones.

Condition Regularly

Grey hair can be more prone to dryness and breakage, so it's important to condition it regularly. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair soft and healthy.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

If you're using heat-styling tools on your hair, such as curling irons or straighteners, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear long hair in my 60s and beyond?

A: Absolutely! Long hair can be incredibly flattering for women over 60, especially when styled in a way that suits your face shape and hair texture.

Q: Will long hair make me look older?

A: Not necessarily. Long hair can actually help to create a more youthful appearance, especially when styled in a way that's modern and on-trend.

Q: How often should I wash my long grey hair?

A: This will depend on your hair type and lifestyle, but as a general rule, you should aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

The Bottom Line

Long hairstyles for over 60 grey hair can be incredibly flattering and versatile. By choosing the right style for your face shape and hair texture, and taking care of your hair with regular conditioning and styling, you can rock your grey locks with confidence and style.
