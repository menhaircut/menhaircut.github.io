Male Haircut Short Sides Medium Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Aug 17, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a stylish yet low-maintenance haircut that will keep you looking sharp? Look no further than the male haircut short sides medium top. This versatile style has been trending for years and shows no signs of slowing down. In this guide, we will provide you with everything you need to know about this trendy haircut, including how to get it, tips for styling, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

The male haircut short sides medium top, also known as the “undercut,” is a modern and stylish haircut that features shaved or closely cropped sides with a longer top. This style offers a lot of versatility and can be customized to suit your individual preferences. Whether you want a slicked-back look or a messy textured style, this haircut can be adapted to fit your aesthetic.

How to

The best way to achieve the male haircut short sides medium top is to visit a professional stylist or barber. However, if you are feeling brave and want to try it at home, here are the basic steps:

Step 1: Gather your tools

You will need a pair of hair clippers, scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 2: Start with the sides

Use the clippers to shave or trim the sides and back of your head. Start with a longer guard and gradually work your way down until you achieve the desired length.

Step 3: Trim the top

Use scissors to trim the hair on top of your head to the length you desire. You can leave it longer for a more textured look or go shorter for a slicked-back style.

Step 4: Blend the sides and top

Use the comb to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 5: Style your hair

Apply your preferred styling product to your hair and style it as desired. You can use a comb for a slicked-back look or your fingers for a messier, textured style.

Step by Step

If you are still unsure about how to achieve the male haircut short sides medium top, here is a more detailed step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Consult with your stylist

Before your stylist starts cutting your hair, discuss your preferences and the look you want to achieve. They can help you choose the right length and style for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Wash your hair

Wash your hair thoroughly and towel-dry it before your haircut. Your hair should be clean and free of any product buildup.

Step 3: Section your hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to cut each section separately and ensure an even cut.

Step 4: Cut the sides and back

Using hair clippers, your stylist will cut the sides and back of your head to your desired length. They will likely start with a longer guard and gradually work their way down to a shorter length.

Step 5: Cut the top

Using scissors, your stylist will trim the hair on top of your head to the desired length. They may use a razor to add texture and dimension to your hair.

Step 6: Blend the sides and top

Your stylist will use a comb to blend the hair on the sides and back with the hair on top. This will create a seamless transition between the two lengths.

Step 7: Style your hair

Your stylist will apply styling product to your hair and style it as desired. They can show you different techniques for achieving different looks.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style your male haircut short sides medium top:

  • Invest in a good quality styling product that works for your hair type.
  • Use a comb or brush to distribute the product evenly through your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that suits you best.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for touch-ups and maintenance.
  • Consider adding a fade or taper to your haircut for a more dramatic look.

FAQ

Q: Is the male haircut short sides medium top suitable for all hair types?

A: Yes, this haircut can be adapted to suit all hair types, from straight to curly and everything in between.

Q: Can I style my hair differently every day?

A: Yes, the male haircut short sides medium top is very versatile and can be styled in many different ways, depending on your mood and preferences.

Q: Will this haircut require a lot of maintenance?

A: No, this is a relatively low-maintenance haircut that only requires regular trims and styling with the right product.

Hair Care

To keep your male haircut short sides medium top looking its best, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as hair dryers and straighteners.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental damage by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.
  • Trim your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and style.
  • Visit your stylist for regular color touch-ups if you have dyed hair.

Now that you have all the information you need about the male haircut short sides medium top, why not give it a try? This stylish and versatile haircut is sure to turn heads and keep you looking sharp and on-trend.
