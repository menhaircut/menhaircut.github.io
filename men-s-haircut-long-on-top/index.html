Men's Haircut Long on Top - A Relaxing Trend in 2023

Description

Long on top haircut for men has been gaining popularity in recent years. In 2023, this trend continues to be a relaxing style for men who want to look good without putting too much effort into grooming. This hairstyle features long hair on the top of the head, usually styled with a side part or slicked back. The sides and back can be shaved, tapered, or fade. This haircut suits various face shapes and hair types, so you can customize it to your liking.

How to Get a Men's Haircut Long on Top

The first step to getting a men's haircut long on top is to find a reliable barber or hair stylist who can do this style. Look for someone who has experience with this haircut and can give you the right recommendations based on your hair type and face shape. You can bring a photo of the style you want to show your barber as a reference.

Step by Step Guide

Here are the basic steps to get a men's haircut long on top:

  1. Wash your hair and let it dry naturally.
  2. Sit on the barber's chair and discuss with them the style you want.
  3. The barber will start by cutting the sides and back to your desired length. They may use clippers or scissors depending on the style you want.
  4. Next, they will cut the top section of your hair. The length will depend on your preference, but it's usually longer than the sides and back.
  5. The barber will style your hair using a comb, brush, and hair product. You can choose to have a side part, slick back or messy look.
  6. After the haircut, the barber will clean up your neck and sideburns with a razor or trimmer.

Tips to Rock Your Long on Top Haircut

Here are some tips to make the most of your men's haircut long on top:

  • Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the style you want. For example, if you have thick hair and want a slick back look, use a pomade or gel. If you have fine hair and want a messy look, use a matte paste or wax.
  • Don't wash your hair too often, as it can strip away natural oils and make your hair dry and frizzy. Aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days or as needed.
  • If you have a receding hairline, avoid having a sharp side part as it can highlight the balding areas. Instead, go for a more natural look with a messy or textured top.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut. Visit your barber every 4-6 weeks to keep your hair looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's haircut long on top:

Is a long on top haircut suitable for all face shapes?
Yes, a long on top haircut can be customized to suit various face shapes. Consult with your barber to find the right style for you.
Can I style my long on top haircut in different ways?
Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as a messy look, quiff, or comb-over, depending on your preference.
How do I take care of my long on top haircut?
Use a hair product that suits your hair type and the style you want. Don't wash your hair too often and get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.

Hair Care for Men's Haircut Long on Top

To keep your hair healthy and looking good, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and the style you want.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to nourish your hair from the inside out.
  • Stay hydrated and get enough sleep to promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips, you can rock your men's haircut long on top in 2023 and beyond.
