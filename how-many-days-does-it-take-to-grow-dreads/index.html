How Many Days Does It Take To Grow Dreads A Comprehensive Guide

Written by William Jul 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Dreadlocks or dreads are a popular hairstyle that has been around for centuries. It's a low maintenance hairstyle that requires minimal styling, making it a perfect choice for people with busy lifestyles. But one question that is commonly asked by people who want to get dreadlocks or those who have just started growing them is, "how many days does it take to grow dreads?" In this article, we'll answer this question and provide you with a comprehensive guide on growing and maintaining dreadlocks.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To Grow Dreads
  • Step By Step Guide
  • Tips For Growing Dreads
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care For Dreads

Description

Dreadlocks are formed by letting your hair grow and then matting them together. The process involves separating your hair into sections and then twisting and knotting each section until it forms a dreadlock. The time it takes to grow dreads depends on various factors such as the length and thickness of your hair, your hair type, and the method you use to create your locks.

How To Grow Dreads

There are various ways to grow dreads, and the method you choose will depend on your hair type and texture. Some popular methods include:

Freeform Method

The freeform method involves letting your hair grow naturally without any manipulation or styling. This method is suitable for people with kinky or curly hair types as their hair naturally tangles and forms locks.

Twist and Rip Method

The twist and rip method involves separating your hair into sections and then twisting and ripping each section until it forms a dreadlock. This method is suitable for people with straight or wavy hair types.

Backcombing Method

The backcombing method involves teasing your hair with a comb until it forms knots. This method is suitable for people with straight or wavy hair types and is a popular method for getting instant dreadlocks.

Step By Step Guide

To grow dreads, follow these simple steps:

  1. Decide on the method you want to use to create your dreads.
  2. Wash your hair thoroughly and let it dry completely.
  3. Separate your hair into sections using a comb or your fingers.
  4. Start twisting and knotting each section until it forms a dreadlock.
  5. Repeat the process until you've twisted and knotted all your hair.
  6. Let your hair grow naturally, and avoid combing or brushing it.
  7. Regularly wash your hair with a residue-free shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  8. Patience is key – it can take several months to a year for your dreads to fully mature.

Tips For Growing Dreads

Here are some tips to help you grow healthy and beautiful dreadlocks:

  • Avoid using wax or other chemical products as they can cause buildup and damage your hair.
  • Regularly wash your hair with a residue-free shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using a hairdryer or other heat styling tools as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or elastics that can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

FAQs

How long does it take to grow dreads?

The time it takes to grow dreads depends on various factors such as the length and thickness of your hair, your hair type, and the method you use to create your locks. On average, it can take anywhere from six months to a year for your dreads to fully mature.

Do I need to cut my hair to get dreads?

No, you don't need to cut your hair to get dreads. You can grow your hair out and then use a method such as the twist and rip or backcombing method to create your locks.

Can I wash my hair with dreads?

Yes, you can wash your hair with dreads. It's important to regularly wash your hair with a residue-free shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.

Hair Care For Dreads

Dreadlocks require minimal styling, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips for caring for your dreadlocks:

  • Regularly wash your hair with a residue-free shampoo to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a crochet hook to tighten loose hair and prevent frizz.
  • Avoid using wax or other chemical products as they can cause buildup and damage your hair.
  • Maintain a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to promote hair growth.
  • Avoid using tight hair ties or elastics that can cause breakage and damage to your hair.

In conclusion, growing dreadlocks takes time and patience, but the results are well worth it. Follow these tips and methods to grow healthy and beautiful dreadlocks that will turn heads wherever you go.
