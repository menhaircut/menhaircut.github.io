How To Look Good With Oval Face Tips And Tricks

Written by Daniel Oct 18, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have an oval face shape, then you are one lucky person! Oval faces have balanced proportions, which means that they can pull off a wide range of hairstyles and makeup looks. However, like any other face shape, you need to know how to accentuate your best features and minimize any flaws. In this article, we will share with you some tips and tricks on how to look good with oval face.

How to Determine if You Have an Oval Face

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, you need to make sure that you actually have an oval face. Here are some characteristics of an oval face shape:

  • Forehead is slightly wider than the chin
  • Face length is about one and a half times the width
  • Cheekbones are the widest part of the face
  • Jawline is rounded

If your face matches these characteristics, then congratulations! You have an oval face.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Look Good with Oval Face

Now that you know you have an oval face, it's time to learn how to accentuate your best features. Follow these steps to look your best:

Step 1: Determine Your Best Features

The first step is to determine your best features. Is it your eyes, lips, cheekbones, or jawline? Once you know what your best feature is, you can focus on accentuating it.

Step 2: Choose the Right Hairstyle

Oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, but some styles work better than others. Generally, you want to avoid hairstyles that add too much height on top of your head or too much volume on the sides. Instead, go for hairstyles that frame your face and show off your best features.

Step 3: Experiment with Makeup

Makeup is a great way to accentuate your best features and create a flawless look. For oval faces, you can experiment with different makeup styles, such as smoky eyes, bold lips, or contoured cheekbones. Just make sure to balance out your features and not go overboard.

Step 4: Dress for Your Body Shape

Your body shape also plays a role in how you look with an oval face. Choose clothes that flatter your figure and create a balanced look. Avoid clothes that are too baggy or too tight, as they can make you look disproportionate.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to look good with oval face:

  • Use a bronzer to contour your cheeks and jawline
  • Avoid hairstyles that cover your forehead
  • Wear earrings that complement your face shape
  • Experiment with different hair parts to create different looks
  • Use a highlighter to accentuate your cheekbones
  • Don't be afraid to try bold colors

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to look good with oval face:

What is the best hairstyle for oval face?

Oval faces can pull off almost any hairstyle, but some styles work better than others. Generally, you want to avoid hairstyles that add too much height on top of your head or too much volume on the sides. Instead, go for hairstyles that frame your face and show off your best features.

How can I contour my oval face?

Use a bronzer to contour your cheeks and jawline. Apply the bronzer in a "3" shape along the sides of your face, starting at your temples and sweeping down to your jawline.

Hair Care for Oval Face

Taking care of your hair is essential to looking good with an oval face. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

By following these tips and tricks, you can look your best with an oval face. Remember, the key is to accentuate your best features and create a balanced look.
