What Style Matches Me A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Look

Written by Bardi Oct 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of feeling like your hair is always a mess? Do you struggle to find a style that suits your face shape and personality? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle to find the perfect hairstyle, but the good news is that with a little guidance, you can discover the look that's right for you. In this guide, we'll explore how to determine what style matches you and provide tips for maintaining your new look.

How to Determine What Style Matches You

The first step in finding your perfect hairstyle is to determine your face shape. This will help you identify which styles will complement your features and which may not work as well. There are six basic face shapes: oval, round, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, look in the mirror and measure the width and length of your face.

If your face is roughly equal in length and width, you likely have an oval face shape. An oval face is versatile and can rock many different styles. If your face is widest at the cheekbones and narrows at the forehead and jawline, you likely have a heart-shaped face. Styles that add volume to the crown or have a side-swept bang work well with this shape. If your face is widest at the jawline and forehead, you likely have a square face. Soft, layered styles work well with this shape.

If your face is longer than it is wide, you likely have an oblong face shape. Long, layered styles work well with this shape. If your face is widest at the temples and narrows at the jawline and forehead, you likely have a diamond face shape. Styles that add width at the forehead and jawline work well with this shape. Finally, if your face is roughly equal in width and length but has a rounded jawline, you likely have a round face shape. Styles that add height and length work well with this shape.

Step-by-Step Guide to Finding Your Perfect Style

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to find your perfect hairstyle. Follow these steps to discover the look that's right for you:

  1. Consider your lifestyle. Do you want a style that's low maintenance or one that requires more styling?
  2. Think about your personality. Do you want a style that's edgy or classic?
  3. Research styles that work well with your face shape.
  4. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best cut and color for you.

Remember, finding the perfect style takes time and experimentation. Don't be afraid to try something new!

Tips for Maintaining Your New Look

Once you've found your perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for keeping your hair looking its best:

  • Invest in quality hair care products.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine.

By following these tips, you can keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about finding the perfect hairstyle:

Can I pull off a short hairstyle?

Short hairstyles can be flattering on many face shapes, but it's important to consider your hair texture and personal style before making the chop. Consult with a hairstylist to determine if a short style is right for you.

What should I do if I don't like my new hairstyle?

If you're not happy with your new hairstyle, don't panic. Talk to your hairstylist and explain what you don't like. They may be able to make adjustments or suggest a different style that will better suit you.

How often should I wash my hair?

How often you should wash your hair depends on your hair type and personal preference. Generally, it's recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.

Hair Care Tips

Finally, here are some general hair care tips to keep your locks looking their best:

  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair, as it can cause breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hair ties with metal clasps, as they can damage hair.

By following these tips and finding the perfect hairstyle for your face shape and personality, you can achieve beautiful, healthy hair that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
