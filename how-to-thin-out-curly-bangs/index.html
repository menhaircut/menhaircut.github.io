How To Thin Out Curly Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair, you know how challenging it can be to manage it, especially when it comes to styling your bangs. While having bangs can add a touch of elegance to your look, they can quickly become overwhelming if they are too thick or curly. Thinning out your curly bangs is the perfect solution to achieve a more manageable and polished look. In this article, we'll show you how to thin out curly bangs in a few simple steps.

How to Thin Out Curly Bangs

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp hair cutting scissors, a fine-toothed comb, and a mirror.

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and allowing it to air dry or blow-drying it. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you begin to cut your bangs.

Step 2: Section Your Bangs

Use the fine-toothed comb to section off your bangs. Pull the rest of your hair back and secure it with a hair tie or clips.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Take a small section of your bangs and twist it tightly. Use the scissors to cut the twisted section of hair, starting from the ends and working your way up. This technique will help you achieve a more textured and natural look.

Step 4: Repeat on the Rest of Your Bangs

Continue twisting and cutting small sections of your bangs until you achieve the desired thickness. Make sure to cut each section at different lengths to create a more natural look.

Step 5: Check Your Work

After you have finished cutting your bangs, use the mirror to check your work. Make sure your bangs are even and that you have achieved the desired thickness. If needed, make any additional cuts until you are satisfied with the result.

Tips for Thinning Out Curly Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results:

  • Start by cutting small sections of your bangs. You can always cut more if needed.
  • Don't rush the process. Take your time and be patient.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Twist your hair tightly before cutting to achieve a more natural look.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once. It's better to cut less and make additional cuts if needed.

FAQs

Can I Thin Out My Curly Bangs at Home?

Yes, you can! Thinning out your curly bangs at home is easy, as long as you have the right tools and follow the steps we outlined above.

How Often Should I Thin Out My Curly Bangs?

The frequency of thinning out your curly bangs will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how thick you want your bangs to be. As a general rule, it's recommended to thin out your bangs every 6-8 weeks.

Can I Thin Out My Curly Bangs If I Have Short Hair?

Yes, you can! Thinning out your curly bangs is possible, regardless of your hair length. Just make sure to follow the same steps and use the right tools.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your curls gently.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it moisturized.

Thinning out your curly bangs can be a game-changer when it comes to managing your curly hair. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a polished and natural look that will make you feel confident and beautiful!
