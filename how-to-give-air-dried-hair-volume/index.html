How To Give Air Dried Hair Volume Tips And Tricks

Written by Frank Aug 19, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re someone who loves air drying your hair, you know how difficult it can be to achieve volume without heat styling. But there are ways to give your air dried hair some extra oomph! In this article, we’ll give you some easy tips and tricks on how to give air dried hair volume.

Table of Contents

  • How to Give Air Dried Hair Volume
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Give Air Dried Hair Volume

If you’re wondering how to give air dried hair volume, the first step is to start with a good shampoo and conditioner. Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture to your hair.

Next, apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair while it’s still damp. This will help to add lift and body to your hair as it dries.

Once you’ve applied the product, use your fingers to gently tousle your hair. This will help to distribute the product evenly and create some texture in your hair.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to give air dried hair volume:

  1. Start with a good shampoo and conditioner designed to add volume to your hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair while it’s still damp.
  3. Use your fingers to gently tousle your hair to distribute the product evenly.
  4. Flip your head upside down and blow dry your hair on low heat, using your fingers to lift your roots.
  5. Once your hair is mostly dry, flip your head back up and continue air drying.
  6. If your hair is still damp, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to add extra volume.
  7. When your hair is completely dry, use a texturizing spray to add some extra body and texture.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks for giving air dried hair volume:

  • Try using a dry shampoo or texturizing spray on your roots to add lift and volume.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look flat.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing, as this will help to prevent breakage and damage.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or messy buns, to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about giving air dried hair volume:

Can I add volume to my hair without using heat?

Absolutely! By using the right products and techniques, you can give your air dried hair plenty of volume and body without using heat.

What kind of products should I use to add volume to my hair?

Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture to your hair, such as volumizing mousses, sprays, and texturizing powders.

How can I prevent my hair from looking flat?

Avoid using heavy styling products, and try using a dry shampoo or texturizing spray on your roots to add lift and volume. You can also experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids or messy buns, to add texture and body to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can give your air dried hair plenty of volume and body without using heat. Experiment with different products and hairstyles to find what works best for you, and don’t be afraid to try something new!
