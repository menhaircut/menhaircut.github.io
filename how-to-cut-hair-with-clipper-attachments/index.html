How To Cut Hair With Clipper Attachments A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 14, 2023 · 4 min read

Cutting hair with clipper attachments can be a great way to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. By using different clipper sizes, you can create a variety of hair lengths and styles. In this guide, we will go through the steps of how to cut hair with clipper attachments, as well as some tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Hair with Clipper Attachments
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After Cutting

How to Cut Hair with Clipper Attachments

Cutting hair with clipper attachments is relatively easy and can be done at home with just a few tools. Here are the things you will need:

  • Hair clippers with various attachments
  • Hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Clip or hair tie
  • Clean towel

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut hair with clipper attachments:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Wet hair can cause the clipper to slip, which can result in uneven cuts.
  2. Choose the right clipper attachment for the desired length. Attach the clipper comb securely to the clipper.
  3. Begin cutting at the nape of the neck and work your way up. Use the clipper to cut the hair to the desired length. Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly.
  4. Move to the sides and cut the hair in the same manner, starting at the bottom and working your way up.
  5. Cut the hair on top of the head using the same technique, making sure to blend the sides and back with the top layer of hair.
  6. Use hair cutting scissors to trim any longer hairs or to create layers. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle.
  7. Check for any uneven spots and make adjustments as needed.
  8. Use a clean towel to wipe away any loose hairs from the neck and shoulders.

Tips for Cutting Hair with Clippers

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting hair with clipper attachments:

  • Start with a longer clipper attachment and work your way down to a shorter length, as it is easier to cut more hair than to add it back.
  • Use a comb to lift the hair and cut it evenly. This will prevent uneven cuts and make it easier to blend the different hair lengths.
  • Take your time and be patient. Rushing can result in uneven cuts and mistakes.
  • Use hair cutting scissors to trim any longer hairs or to create layers. This will give the hair a more polished look.
  • Always clean and oil your clippers after use to ensure they continue to work properly and last longer.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting hair with clipper attachments:

  • Can I use clipper attachments on wet hair?
    It is best to cut hair when it is dry, as wet hair can cause the clipper to slip and result in uneven cuts.
  • What is the best clipper attachment to use for a buzz cut?
    A #1 or #2 clipper attachment is typically used for a buzz cut.
  • How often should I clean my clippers?
    It is recommended to clean and oil your clippers after each use to ensure they continue to work properly and last longer.

Hair Care After Cutting

After cutting hair with clipper attachments, it is important to take care of the hair to maintain the style and prevent damage. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition the hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized.
  • Trim the hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain the style and prevent split ends.

Cutting hair with clipper attachments can be a great way to achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. With the right tools and technique, you can create a variety of hair lengths and styles. By following these steps and tips, you can master the art of cutting hair with clipper attachments.
