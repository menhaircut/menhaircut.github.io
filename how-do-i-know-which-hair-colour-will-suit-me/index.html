How Do I Know Which Hair Colour Will Suit Me

Written by Aprilia Aug 02, 2023 · 3 min read

Choosing a new hair color can be a daunting task. With so many different shades and tones to choose from, it's hard to know which one will suit you best. In this article, we'll guide you through the process of finding your perfect hair color.

Table of Contents:

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description:

Choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone, eye color, and natural hair color can be tricky, but it's not impossible. It's important to remember that not all hair colors suit everyone, and that's okay. You just need to find the one that suits you best.

How To:

The first step in finding your perfect hair color is to determine your skin tone. This can be done by looking at the veins on the underside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If they appear blue-green, you have a neutral undertone.

Once you have determined your skin tone, you can start exploring different hair colors. If you have a cool undertone, look for hair colors with cool tones, such as ash brown or platinum blonde. If you have a warm undertone, look for hair colors with warm tones, such as golden brown or copper red. If you have a neutral undertone, you can experiment with both cool and warm tones.

Step By Step:

  1. Determine your skin tone by looking at the veins on the underside of your wrist.
  2. Look for hair colors that complement your skin tone.
  3. Consider your natural hair color and eye color when choosing a hair color.
  4. Consult with a professional hair stylist if you're unsure.

Tips:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much maintenance your new hair color will require.
  • Start with a subtle change, such as highlights or lowlights, before making a drastic change.
  • Bring pictures of hair colors you like to your hair stylist.

FAQ:

Q: Will a certain hair color make me look younger?

A: While there is no one hair color that will make you look younger, choosing a hair color that complements your skin tone and eye color can help brighten your complexion and give you a more youthful appearance.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home?

A: While it is possible to dye your hair at home, we recommend consulting with a professional hair stylist to ensure that you achieve the desired result and avoid damaging your hair.

Hair Care:

After dying your hair, it's important to take good care of it to maintain its color and health. Use a shampoo and conditioner specifically formulated for color-treated hair, and avoid washing your hair too frequently. Use heat protectant products when using styling tools, and avoid excessive heat styling.

Now that you know how to find your perfect hair color, it's time to book an appointment with your hair stylist and start experimenting!
