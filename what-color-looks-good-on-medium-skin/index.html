What Color Looks Good On Medium Skin

Written by Oliver Aug 05, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old colors and want to switch things up? Choosing the right colors can make all the difference in how you look and feel. If you have medium skin, you might be wondering what colors will complement your skin tone the best. In this article, we will go over some tips and tricks to help you find the perfect color that looks good on medium skin.

Description

Medium skin tones are versatile and can range from olive to caramel. The key is to find colors that will enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident. The right color can make you look radiant and glowing, while the wrong color can make you look dull and washed out.

How to Choose the Right Color

When choosing a color, you want to consider a few factors, such as your undertones, hair color, and the occasion. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect color:

1. Determine Your Undertones

Undertones are the subtle hues underneath your skin. There are three types of undertones: cool, warm, and neutral. If you have medium skin, your undertones are likely warm or neutral. To determine your undertones, look at the veins on your wrist. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue-green, you have neutral undertones.

2. Consider Your Hair Color

Your hair color can also play a role in the colors that look good on you. For example, if you have warm undertones and blonde hair, colors like peach, coral, and turquoise can complement your skin tone. If you have cool undertones and dark hair, colors like emerald green, royal blue, and burgundy can enhance your complexion.

3. Think About the Occasion

The occasion can also dictate the colors that you wear. For example, if you are attending a formal event, you might want to wear colors like navy, burgundy, or emerald green. If you are going to a beach party, you might want to wear bright colors like yellow, orange, or pink.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to help you choose the perfect color for your medium skin tone:

Step 1: Determine Your Undertones

Look at the veins on your wrist to determine your undertones. If they appear blue, you have cool undertones. If they appear green, you have warm undertones. If they appear blue-green, you have neutral undertones.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Color

Think about your hair color and choose colors that will complement it. If you have warm undertones and blonde hair, colors like peach, coral, and turquoise can complement your skin tone. If you have cool undertones and dark hair, colors like emerald green, royal blue, and burgundy can enhance your complexion.

Step 3: Think About the Occasion

Consider the occasion and choose colors that will be appropriate. If you are attending a formal event, you might want to wear colors like navy, burgundy, or emerald green. If you are going to a beach party, you might want to wear bright colors like yellow, orange, or pink.

Step 4: Experiment with Different Colors

Don't be afraid to experiment with different colors to find the perfect match. Try on different shades of the same color to see what works best for you. You might be surprised by what looks good on you!

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the perfect color:

1. Stick to Solid Colors

Solid colors work best on medium skin tones. Avoid patterns and prints that can be overwhelming.

2. Avoid Pastels

Pastel colors can make medium skin tones look washed out. Stick to bold, bright colors instead.

3. Accessorize

Accessories can also help enhance your skin tone. Gold jewelry can complement warm undertones, while silver jewelry can complement cool undertones.

FAQ

What colors should I avoid if I have medium skin?

You should avoid colors that clash with your undertones. For example, if you have warm undertones, you should avoid colors with blue undertones, like baby blue or lavender. If you have cool undertones, you should avoid colors with yellow undertones, like mustard or olive green.

Can I wear black if I have medium skin?

Yes, you can wear black if you have medium skin. Black is a universal color that looks good on all skin tones.

What color lipstick looks good on medium skin?

Medium skin tones can wear a range of lipstick colors. For a natural look, try a nude or pink shade. For a bold look, try a red or berry shade.

Hair Care

Choosing the right color is important, but so is taking care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

1. Use Heat Protectant

If you use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Deep Condition Regularly

Deep conditioning treatments can help nourish and hydrate your hair. Try to deep condition once a week.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun can damage your hair, just like it can damage your skin. Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray to protect your hair from the sun.

Choosing the right color can be a game-changer. With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect color that looks good on medium skin. Remember to have fun and experiment with different colors to find what works best for you!
