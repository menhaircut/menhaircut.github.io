How To Make Your Face Shape Oval A Step By Step Guide

Do you feel like your face shape is too round or square? Do you wish you had a more oval-shaped face? Well, you're in luck! With a few simple tricks, you can make your face shape appear more oval. In this article, we'll show you how to do just that.

Description

An oval face shape is considered to be the most balanced and symmetrical face shape. It's characterized by a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. If you have a round or square face shape, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and accessories to create the illusion of an oval face shape.

How to Make Your Face Shape Oval

The key to making your face shape appear more oval is to create the illusion of more length in your face. Here are some tips to help you achieve an oval-shaped face:

Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create the illusion of shadows and depth on your face. By contouring your face, you can create the illusion of a more defined jawline and a narrower forehead. Use a contouring powder or cream that's two shades darker than your skin tone and apply it along your hairline, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline.

Hairstyles

Your hairstyle can also play a big role in creating the illusion of an oval face shape. Try a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head and length to the sides of your face. A long bob or a layered haircut with side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of an oval-shaped face.

Accessories

Accessories can also help to elongate your face shape. Try wearing earrings that are long and slim, as they can draw the eye downwards and create the illusion of more length in your face. Avoid wearing round or circular earrings, as these can make your face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to make your face shape appear more oval:

  1. Start by applying foundation and concealer to even out your skin tone and hide any blemishes.
  2. Use a contouring powder or cream that's two shades darker than your skin tone and apply it along your hairline, under your cheekbones, and along your jawline.
  3. Blend the contouring powder or cream well, so that there are no harsh lines or edges.
  4. Apply a blush to the apples of your cheeks to add a natural flush to your face.
  5. Use a highlighter to add some shine and dimension to your face. Apply it to the high points of your face, such as your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose.
  6. Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head and length to the sides of your face. A long bob or a layered haircut with side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of an oval-shaped face.
  7. Wear earrings that are long and slim, as they can draw the eye downwards and create the illusion of more length in your face.
  8. Avoid wearing round or circular earrings, as these can make your face look wider.
  9. Remember to blend your makeup well and choose hairstyles and accessories that elongate your face shape.
  10. Practice makes perfect! Keep experimenting with different makeup, hairstyles, and accessories until you find the perfect combination that works for you.

Tips to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips to keep in mind when trying to create the illusion of an oval face shape:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds height to the top of your head and length to the sides of your face.
  • Wear earrings that are long and slim, as they can draw the eye downwards and create the illusion of more length in your face.
  • Avoid wearing round or circular earrings, as these can make your face look wider.
  • Contour your face using a powder or cream that's two shades darker than your skin tone.
  • Blend your makeup well to avoid any harsh lines or edges.

FAQs

What is an oval face shape?

An oval face shape is characterized by a slightly wider forehead, high cheekbones, and a narrow chin. It's considered to be the most balanced and symmetrical face shape.

Can I change my face shape?

No, you can't change your face shape. However, you can use makeup, hairstyles, and accessories to create the illusion of a different face shape.

What kind of earrings should I wear to elongate my face shape?

Long, slim earrings can help to draw the eye downwards and create the illusion of more length in your face. Avoid wearing round or circular earrings, as these can make your face look wider.

Hair Care for Oval-Shaped Faces

If you have an oval-shaped face, you're lucky! Almost any hairstyle will suit your face shape. However, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • A long bob or a layered haircut with side-swept bangs can help to create the illusion of an oval-shaped face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to the top of your head, as these can make your face look longer.
  • Keep your hair away from your face to show off your facial features.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that works best for you.

By following these tips, you can create the illusion of an oval face shape and feel confident in your own skin!
