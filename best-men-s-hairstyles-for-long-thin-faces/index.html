Best Men s Hairstyles For Long Thin Faces In 2023

Written by Robby Aug 07, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you have a long thin face, it can be challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that suits you. Fortunately, there are plenty of options that will flatter your face shape and make you look stylish and confident. In this article, we'll explore some of the best men's hairstyles for long thin faces in 2023, along with tips and tricks to help you maintain your new look.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle
  • Frequently Asked Questions

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle, your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider. For men with long thin faces, the key is to go for styles that add width and volume to your face. Here are some of the best options:

1. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great option for men with long thin faces. This style adds volume to the sides of your face, making it appear wider. To achieve this look, simply sweep your hair to one side and use a bit of product to hold it in place.

2. Messy Top with Short Sides

A messy top with short sides is another great hairstyle for long thin faces. This style adds volume to the top of your head, while the short sides create a contrast that makes your face appear wider. To achieve this look, ask your barber to keep the sides short while leaving the top longer. Then, use a bit of product to create a messy, textured look.

3. Classic Pompadour

The classic pompadour is a timeless hairstyle that works well for men with long thin faces. This style adds volume and height to the top of your head, making your face appear wider. To achieve this look, ask your barber to leave the hair on top longer while keeping the sides shorter. Then, style your hair back and up using a comb and some product.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Once you've chosen the right hairstyle for your face shape, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair to remove any dirt and oil. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are appropriate for your hair type.

2. Towel-Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to gently dry your hair, leaving it slightly damp.

3. Apply Product

Apply a small amount of product to your hair, depending on the style you're going for. Use a comb or your fingers to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

4. Style Your Hair

Using a comb or your fingers, style your hair into the desired shape. For a messy look, use your fingers to create texture and volume. For a more polished look, use a comb to smooth down any flyaways.

5. Finish with Hairspray or Gel

Finish off your hairstyle with a bit of hairspray or gel to hold it in place. Use a light mist of hairspray for a natural look, or a stronger hold gel for a more structured look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Once you've found the perfect hairstyle, it's important to maintain it properly to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips to help you do just that:

1. Get Regular Haircuts

Visit your barber regularly to keep your hair looking neat and tidy. A trim every 4-6 weeks is usually sufficient to maintain your style.

2. Use the Right Products

Choose hair products that are appropriate for your hair type and the style you're going for. For example, if you have fine hair, use a lightweight product that won't weigh it down.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat tools like hairdryers and straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair. If you do use them, use a heat protectant spray to minimize the damage.

4. Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. You might be surprised at how different styles can completely change your look.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best hairstyle for long thin faces?

The best hairstyle for long thin faces is one that adds volume and width to your face. Some good options include side-swept bangs, a messy top with short sides, and a classic pompadour.

How do I know if I have a long thin face?

If your face is longer than it is wide, and your cheekbones and jawline are slim, you likely have a long thin face.

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it less frequently, every 2-3 days.

Hair Care Tips for Men

Maintaining healthy hair is important for both the look and feel of your hair. Here are some hair care tips for men:

1. Use a Gentle Shampoo

Choose a shampoo that is gentle on your hair and scalp, and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates.

2. Condition Regularly

Use a conditioner after shampooing to keep your hair moisturized and healthy. Leave the conditioner in for a few minutes before rinsing it out.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product with SPF to protect your hair and scalp from the harmful effects of the sun.

4. Eat a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

With these tips and hairstyle ideas, you're well on your way to looking your best with a long thin face. Remember to experiment with different styles, and don't be afraid to ask your barber for advice. With a bit of effort, you can achieve a stylish and confident look that suits your face shape perfectly.
