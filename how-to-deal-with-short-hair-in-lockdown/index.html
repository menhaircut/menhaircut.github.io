How To Deal With Short Hair In Lockdown

Written by Luffy Oct 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to manage your short hair during the lockdown? You are not alone. Many people are turning to DIY haircuts and styling at home, but it can be challenging when you have short hair. In this post, we will give you some tips and tricks on how to deal with short hair in lockdown.

  • How to Deal with Short Hair in Lockdown
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • FAQ

Step-by-Step Guide

Before we dive into the tips and tricks, let's start with some basics. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to deal with short hair in lockdown:

Step 1: Wash your hair regularly

Washing your hair regularly is essential to keep it clean and healthy. Use a mild shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage to your hair. Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle.

Step 2: Invest in the right tools

Invest in the right tools to manage your short hair. A good pair of scissors, a comb, and a hairbrush are essential. You can also consider buying a hair trimmer if you want to maintain your hair at home.

Step 3: Experiment with different hairstyles

Short hair can be versatile, and you can experiment with different hairstyles. Look online for inspiration and try out different styles that suit your face shape and hair type. You can also use hair accessories to add some variety to your hairstyle.

Step 4: Keep it simple

Short hair is low maintenance, but it still requires some care. Keep your hair routine simple and avoid using too many products or styling tools. Stick to the basics and focus on keeping your hair healthy and clean.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks on how to deal with short hair in lockdown:

Tip 1: Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly is essential to maintain its shape and prevent split ends. You can do it yourself or ask someone in your household to help you. Use a pair of sharp scissors and cut your hair in small sections, working your way around your head.

Tip 2: Use dry shampoo

Dry shampoo is a lifesaver when you don't have time to wash your hair. It absorbs excess oil and adds volume to your hair. Spray it on your roots and massage it into your scalp for a fresh and clean look.

Tip 3: Embrace the natural texture

Short hair can have a lot of texture, and you can embrace it by using the right products. Use a texturizing spray or pomade to add some definition to your hair. Work the product into your hair with your fingers for a natural and effortless look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to deal with short hair in lockdown:

Q: How often should I wash my short hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it every day. If you have dry hair, you can wash it every other day or every three days.

Q: Can I cut my short hair at home?

A: Yes, you can. However, it's essential to use the right tools and follow some basic guidelines. Start by cutting a little at a time and work your way around your head. You can also watch online tutorials for guidance.

Hair Care

Finally, taking care of your hair is essential to keep it healthy and shiny. Here are some tips on how to care for your short hair:

Tip 1: Use a deep conditioning treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent dryness. Apply the treatment to your hair, leave it on for 5-10 minutes, and rinse it off with warm water.

Tip 2: Protect your hair from heat

Avoid using hot styling tools on your hair as it can damage your hair. If you need to use styling tools, use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage.

Tip 3: Eat a healthy diet

Eating a healthy diet is essential for healthy hair. Include foods that are rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals in your diet. Drink plenty of water to keep your hair hydrated.

In conclusion, managing short hair during the lockdown can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can keep your hair looking great. Follow our tips and tricks, experiment with different hairstyles, and take care of your hair to keep it healthy and shiny.
