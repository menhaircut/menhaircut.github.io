Best Short Haircut For Round Fat Face A Guide For 2023

Written by Robby Aug 17, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have a round and fat face, you may feel that it limits your options when it comes to hairstyles. But fear not, because there are plenty of short haircuts that can flatter your face shape and make you look amazing. In this guide, we’ll take a look at the best short haircut for round fat face, as well as tips for styling and hair care.

What is a Round Fat Face?

A round fat face is a face shape where the width and length are almost equal, and the cheeks are full. This face shape can make you look younger, but it can also be a challenge when it comes to finding the right hairstyle.

Best Short Haircut for Round Fat Face

The best short haircut for round fat face is the pixie cut. This haircut is short and layered, which can help to create the illusion of length and slim down the face. It’s also a low-maintenance haircut that can be styled in different ways.

Another great option is the bob haircut. A bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front can help to elongate the face and create a more streamlined look. Just make sure to avoid a blunt cut, as this can make your face look even rounder.

How to Style Your Short Haircut

Styling your short haircut will depend on the type of haircut you have. For a pixie cut, you can add volume by blow-drying your hair with a round brush. You can also add texture by using a texturizing spray or wax.

For a bob, you can create waves by using a curling iron or adding texture with a sea salt spray. You can also add volume by blow-drying your hair with a round brush.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Short Haircut

1. Wash and condition your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.

2. Towel-dry your hair, then apply a heat protectant.

3. Blow-dry your hair with a round brush, creating volume at the roots.

4. Use a texturizing spray or wax to add texture to your hair.

5. Finish with a hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

1. Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

2. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

3. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

4. Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look even rounder?

A: It depends on the type of haircut you get. A pixie cut or layered bob can help to elongate the face and create a more streamlined look.

Q: Can I still style my short haircut if I have a round fat face?

A: Absolutely! With the right styling products and techniques, you can create a variety of looks that flatter your face shape.

Hair Care for Short Haircuts

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy.

2. Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

3. Use a heat protectant when styling with hot tools.

4. Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

With these tips and the best short haircut for round fat face, you’ll be able to rock your new hairstyle with confidence. Remember to experiment with different styling products and techniques to find what works best for you. Happy styling!
