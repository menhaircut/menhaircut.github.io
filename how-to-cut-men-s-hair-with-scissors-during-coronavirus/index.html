How To Cut Men s Hair With Scissors During Coronavirus

Table of Content:

Description

During the COVID-19 pandemic, many barber shops have closed down or reduced their hours, making it difficult for men to maintain their haircuts. However, cutting men's hair with scissors at home is a viable solution. This article will guide you through the process of cutting men's hair with scissors during coronavirus.

How to

Before starting, it is important to have the right tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. It is also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to dampen the hair.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing the hair and towel-drying it.

2. Use the comb to separate the hair into sections.

3. Starting from the back of the head, use the scissors to trim the hair. Start by cutting small sections of hair and gradually work your way up.

4. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it at an angle for a layered look.

5. Continue trimming the hair until you are satisfied with the length.

6. Pay extra attention to the sides and the back of the head, as these areas can be difficult to cut.

7. Use the comb to blend the hair and make sure there are no uneven sections.

8. Use the scissors to trim any stray hairs or split ends.

9. Check the hair in the mirror and make any final adjustments.

10. Wash the hair again and style it as desired.

Tips

- Start with a longer length and gradually cut more hair to avoid cutting too much at once.

- Use a mirror to check the hair from all angles.

- Don't rush the process and take breaks if needed.

- If you're unsure about a certain area, leave it and come back to it later.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my own hair with scissors?

A: Yes, it is possible to cut your own hair with scissors, but it may be more difficult to achieve a certain style.

Q: Can I use regular household scissors to cut hair?

A: It is not recommended to use regular household scissors, as they may not be sharp enough and could result in uneven cuts.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: It depends on the individual's hair growth rate and desired length. Generally, it is recommended to cut hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting the hair, it is important to take care of it properly. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and avoid using heated styling tools too frequently. Trimming the hair regularly can also help maintain its health and prevent split ends.

By following these steps, cutting men's hair with scissors during coronavirus can be a simple and easy process. It may take some practice to achieve the desired style, but with patience and the right tools, it can be done at home.
