Men s Haircut Long Curly On Top A Guide For 2023

Written by Luffy Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you in search of a new hairstyle that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the men's haircut long curly on top! With its unique and versatile style, it's no wonder why this trend is taking over in 2023. In this article, we will provide you with the ultimate guide to achieving the perfect long curly hairstyle, including step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs, and hair care advice.

Description

The men's haircut long curly on top is a style that features long, curly hair on the top of the head, with shorter sides and back. This style is perfect for those with naturally curly hair or those who are willing to put in the effort to style their hair into curls. The length of the hair on top can vary, depending on personal preference, but typically ranges from 4-8 inches. This style can be worn in a variety of ways, including slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

How to

If you're ready to try out the men's haircut long curly on top, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Find a good stylist

Before you begin, it's essential to find a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair. Look for someone who has a portfolio of work that includes curly hairstyles.

Step 2: Grow out your hair

In order to achieve the long curly style, you will need to grow out your hair on top. This may take several months, depending on the length and texture of your hair.

Step 3: Wash and condition your hair

Prior to cutting your hair, make sure to wash and condition it thoroughly. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 4: Cut your hair

Once your hair is clean and dry, it's time to cut it. Your stylist will typically start by cutting the sides and back short and leaving the hair on top longer. They will then shape your hair into the desired style, taking into account your face shape and personal preferences.

Step 5: Style your hair

Once your hair is cut, it's time to style it. Apply a curl-enhancing product to your hair and use your fingers to scrunch the curls into place. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls further.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your men's haircut long curly on top:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls healthy and hydrated.
  • Invest in a good curl-enhancing product, such as a curl cream or mousse, to help define your curls.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and prevent split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the men's haircut long curly on top:

Can I still wear this style if I don't have naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can! You will just need to use a curling iron or other styling tools to create the curls.

Will this style work for all face shapes?

While this style can work for most face shapes, it's best suited for those with oval, round, or square-shaped faces.

How often should I wash my hair?

This will depend on your hair type and personal preference. Those with curly hair may only need to wash their hair once or twice a week to avoid drying out their curls.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to achieving the perfect men's haircut long curly on top. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet that includes plenty of protein and vitamins, as this can help promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals, such as bleach or hair dye, as these can damage your hair and cause it to become brittle.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet, as this is when it's most vulnerable to breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a men's haircut long curly on top like a pro. So what are you waiting for? Book an appointment with your stylist and get ready to turn heads!
