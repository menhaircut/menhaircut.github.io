Short Hairstyles For Fine Wavy Hair Over 50

Written by Bardi Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are over 50 and have fine wavy hair, you may be looking for a hairstyle that will flatter your hair type and age. Short hairstyles are a great option for those who want a low-maintenance, yet stylish look. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short hairstyles for fine wavy hair over 50.

Description

As we age, our hair becomes thinner and finer, which is why it is important to choose a hairstyle that will enhance our natural beauty. Fine wavy hair can be difficult to manage, but short hairstyles can make it easier to style and maintain. Short hairstyles also have the added benefit of making us look younger and more refreshed.

How to

Before choosing a short hairstyle, it is important to consider your face shape and hair texture. A good hairstylist can help you choose a style that will flatter your features and hair type. Here are some popular short hairstyles for fine wavy hair over 50:

1. Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that works well for those with fine wavy hair. It is easy to maintain and can be styled in many different ways. This hairstyle is especially flattering for those with a heart-shaped face.

2. Bob Cut

The bob cut is another popular short hairstyle for fine wavy hair over 50. It can be worn straight or wavy and can be styled in many different ways. The bob cut is especially flattering for those with a round or square face.

3. Shaggy Cut

The shaggy cut is a great option for those who want a more edgy and modern look. It is a layered hairstyle that works well for those with fine wavy hair. This hairstyle is especially flattering for those with an oval face.

Step by Step

Here are some steps for styling your short hair:

1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.

Using a volumizing shampoo and conditioner will give your hair more body and texture.

2. Towel dry your hair.

Gently towel dry your hair to remove excess water.

3. Apply a styling product.

Apply a styling product, such as mousse or gel, to your hair to help hold the style.

4. Blow dry your hair.

Blow dry your hair using a diffuser to enhance your natural waves.

5. Style your hair.

Style your hair using a comb or your fingers to create the desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your short hairstyle:

1. Trim your hair regularly.

Regular trims will help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

2. Use a heat protectant.

Using a heat protectant will help prevent damage to your hair from styling tools.

3. Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

A deep conditioning treatment will help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQ

What is the best short hairstyle for fine wavy hair over 50?

There are many short hairstyles that work well for fine wavy hair over 50, including the pixie cut, bob cut, and shaggy cut.

How do I style my short hair?

You can style your short hair using a comb or your fingers to create the desired look.

How do I maintain my short hairstyle?

You can maintain your short hairstyle by trimming your hair regularly, using a heat protectant, and using a deep conditioning treatment once a week.

Hair Care

To keep your fine wavy hair healthy, it is important to use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner. You should also avoid using styling tools that use high heat, as this can damage your hair. Using a deep conditioning treatment once a week can also help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Overall, short hairstyles are a great option for those with fine wavy hair over 50. They are easy to maintain and can be styled in many different ways. By following these tips and taking care of your hair, you can achieve a stylish and youthful look.
