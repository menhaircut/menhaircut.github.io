How Fast Do Dreads Grow

Written by William Aug 31, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you thinking of getting dreadlocks, but curious about how fast they'll grow? Well, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll answer the question, "how fast do dreads grow?" and provide you with all the information you need to know about growing and maintaining your dreadlocks.

Description

Dreadlocks, or "locs," are a popular hairstyle that involves matting and twisting sections of hair into rope-like strands. Many people choose to get dreadlocks because they're low-maintenance and can last for years with proper care. However, one of the most common questions people have about dreadlocks is how fast they'll grow.

How To Grow Dreadlocks

Growing dreadlocks is a process that requires patience and dedication. Here's a step-by-step guide to growing your own dreadlocks:

Step 1: Prepare your hair

Before you can start growing your dreadlocks, you need to prepare your hair. This involves washing your hair thoroughly and avoiding any products that contain conditioner. Conditioner can make your hair too slippery, which can make it difficult for your hair to mat and lock.

Step 2: Section your hair

Once your hair is clean and free of any products, you'll need to section your hair into small, equal-sized sections. You can use a comb or your fingers to section your hair, depending on your preference.

Step 3: Twist and knot your hair

Next, you'll need to twist and knot each section of hair. This involves taking a small section of hair, twisting it tightly, and then knotting it at the base. You'll need to repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 4: Maintain your dreadlocks

Once your hair is locked, you'll need to maintain your dreadlocks to ensure they stay healthy and continue to grow. This involves washing your hair regularly, using a residue-free shampoo, and avoiding any products that contain wax or heavy oils.

Tips for Growing Dreadlocks Faster

If you're looking to speed up the process of growing your dreadlocks, here are a few tips you can try:

  • Avoid using a hair dryer, as the heat can cause your hair to dry out and break.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to promote blood flow and stimulate hair growth.
  • Use a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent your hair from drying out and breaking while you sleep.
  • Eat a healthy diet that's rich in vitamins and nutrients to promote hair growth.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about growing dreadlocks:

How fast do dreadlocks grow?

Dreadlocks can grow anywhere from ¼ inch to 1 inch per month, depending on your hair type and how well you take care of your hair.

Can you make your dreadlocks grow faster?

While there's no guaranteed way to make your dreadlocks grow faster, you can try some of the tips we mentioned earlier to help speed up the process.

Can you trim your dreadlocks?

Yes, you can trim your dreadlocks to help them maintain their shape and length.

Hair Care for Dreadlocks

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy, growing dreadlocks. Here are some tips for taking care of your dreadlocks:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a residue-free shampoo.
  • Avoid using any products that contain wax or heavy oils.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and prevent breakage.

Now that you know how fast dreadlocks grow, you can decide if this hairstyle is right for you. Remember, growing dreadlocks is a process that takes time and patience, but with proper care, your dreadlocks can last for years to come.
