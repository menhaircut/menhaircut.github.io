Can You Wear Two Braids In The Army

Written by Petter Oct 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are thinking of joining the army and you love wearing your hair in two braids, you might be wondering if this is allowed. The army has strict grooming standards that all soldiers must follow, so it is important to know what the rules are before you enlist. In this article, we will answer the question "can you wear two braids in the army?" and provide you with all the information you need to know.

Description

The army has specific rules regarding hairstyles, and soldiers must follow these rules at all times. These rules are designed to maintain a professional appearance and to ensure that hairstyles do not interfere with the safe and effective performance of military duties. Soldiers are expected to maintain their hair in a neat and conservative manner, and extreme hairstyles or hair colors are not allowed.

How to Wear Two Braids in the Army

If you want to wear two braids in the army, you need to make sure that your hair is neatly and securely styled. The braids should be tight and close to the scalp, with no loose strands or flyaways. You should also make sure that your hair is not too long, as this can interfere with the proper wearing of military headgear.

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Before you begin braiding your hair, make sure that it is clean and dry. This will help the braids stay in place and prevent them from becoming frizzy or tangled. You can wash your hair the night before or the morning of, but make sure that it is completely dry before you start braiding.

Step 2: Divide your hair in two sections

Using a comb or your fingers, divide your hair down the middle into two equal sections. Make sure that the parts are straight and even, as this will help your braids look neat and tidy.

Step 3: Start braiding

Take one section of hair and divide it into three smaller sections. Cross the right section over the middle section, then cross the left section over the middle section. Continue braiding in this way until you reach the end of your hair, then secure the braid with a hair tie or elastic band. Repeat on the other side.

Tips for Wearing Two Braids in the Army

Here are some tips to help you wear two braids in the army:

  • Make sure that your hair is neatly and securely styled, with no loose strands or flyaways
  • Use hair ties or elastic bands that match your hair color
  • Avoid using excessive amounts of hair products, as this can make your hair look greasy or sticky
  • Make sure that your braids are not too long, as this can interfere with the proper wearing of military headgear

FAQ

Is it allowed to wear braids in the army?

Yes, soldiers are allowed to wear braids in the army as long as they are neatly and securely styled.

How should I style my braids for the army?

Your braids should be tight and close to the scalp, with no loose strands or flyaways. Make sure that your hair is not too long, as this can interfere with the proper wearing of military headgear.

Hair Care for Two Braids in the Army

If you want to wear two braids in the army, it is important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable
  • Avoid using excessive amounts of hair products, as this can make your hair look greasy or sticky
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, as these can damage your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends

Conclusion

In conclusion, soldiers are allowed to wear two braids in the army as long as they are neatly and securely styled. It is important to follow the army's grooming standards to maintain a professional appearance and to ensure that hairstyles do not interfere with the safe and effective performance of military duties. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you can wear two braids in the army with confidence and style.
