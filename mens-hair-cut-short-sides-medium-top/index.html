Mens Hair Cut Short Sides Medium Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Robby Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a trendy yet timeless hairstyle that suits almost everyone, the mens hair cut short sides medium top is the perfect choice. This versatile haircut features short or faded sides and a longer, textured top that can be styled in various ways. In this post, we'll give you a complete guide on how to get this haircut, style it, and take care of it, so keep reading.

Description

The mens hair cut short sides medium top, also known as the undercut or fade haircut, has been one of the most popular hairstyles for men in recent years. This haircut features a gradual or sharp transition from short or shaved sides to a longer, textured top that can be styled in many ways, such as slicked back, messy, or side-parted. The length of the top can vary from medium to long, depending on your preference and face shape.

How to Get the Haircut

If you want to get the mens hair cut short sides medium top, you should go to a professional barber or hairstylist who has experience in this type of haircut. Here's how the haircut process usually goes:

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to discuss your haircut goals and preferences with your barber or hairstylist. They will assess your hair type, face shape, and lifestyle to determine the best style and length for your hair.

Step 2: Clipper Work

The second step is to use clippers to create a short or faded look on the sides and back of your head. The barber will use different clipper guards and techniques to achieve the desired length and texture.

Step 3: Scissor Work

The third step is to use scissors to cut and texture the hair on the top of your head. The barber will create layers, texture, and movement by cutting the hair at different angles and lengths.

Step 4: Styling

The fourth step is to style the hair on the top using a blow dryer, brush, and styling products, such as pomade or wax. The barber will show you how to style your hair and recommend the right products for your hair type and style.

Styling Tips

Here are some styling tips for the mens hair cut short sides medium top:

  • Use a blow dryer to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of styling product to your fingertips and work it through your hair for a natural look.
  • Experiment with different styles, such as slicked back, messy, or side-parted, to find the one that suits you best.
  • Don't overuse styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ

1. Is the mens hair cut short sides medium top suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, this haircut can be adapted to suit almost all face shapes, depending on the length and shape of the top.

2. How often should I get this haircut?

You should get this haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of the top and keep the sides looking clean and sharp.

3. Can I style this haircut for formal occasions?

Yes, you can style this haircut for formal occasions by slicking back the top and using a stronger hold styling product.

Hair Care Tips

To keep your mens hair cut short sides medium top looking fresh and healthy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner every 2-3 days to avoid stripping away natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Trim the ends of your hair regularly to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to excessive heat or sunlight, as it can cause dryness and frizziness.

By following these tips, you can enjoy a stylish and low-maintenance mens hair cut short sides medium top that complements your features and lifestyle. If you have any questions or concerns, don't hesitate to ask your barber or hairstylist for advice.
