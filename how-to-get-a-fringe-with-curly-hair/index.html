How To Get A Fringe With Curly Hair

Written by William Oct 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this article, we will discuss how to get a fringe with curly hair. A fringe is a great way to change up your look and add some style to your curly hair. Whether you have natural curls or relaxed curls, there are several ways to achieve a fringe that suits your face shape and hair type.

Description

A fringe, also known as bangs, is a hair style that involves cutting the hair at the front of the head so that it falls over the forehead. A fringe can be cut straight across, angled, or side-swept, and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types.

How to

The following steps will guide you through how to get a fringe with curly hair:

Step 1: Choose the right fringe for your face shape

Before getting a fringe, it's important to choose a style that suits your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a side-swept fringe can help to elongate your face. If you have a square face, a soft, wispy fringe can help to soften your features.

Step 2: Cut the fringe

If you're getting a fringe cut by a professional, make sure to communicate the style you want and bring photos for reference. If you're cutting the fringe yourself, start by sectioning off the hair at the front of your head. Use sharp hair cutting scissors to cut the hair to the desired length and shape.

Step 3: Style the fringe

Once the fringe is cut, you can style it in different ways depending on your hair type and preference. If you have natural curls, you can use a curl enhancing product to define the curls and create a soft, bouncy fringe. If you have relaxed curls, you can use a flat iron to straighten the fringe or use a curling iron to create loose waves.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the best fringe with curly hair:

  • Choose a fringe style that suits your face shape and hair type
  • Communicate with your stylist to ensure you get the desired result
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to avoid damaging the hair
  • Style the fringe using products that are suitable for curly hair

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting a fringe with curly hair:

Can I get a fringe if I have tight curls?

Yes, you can still get a fringe if you have tight curls. It's important to choose a style that suits your face shape and hair type, and to use products that help to define and control the curls.

How often do I need to trim my fringe?

It's recommended to trim your fringe every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and length.

Hair Care

Curly hair requires special care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of natural oils
  • Avoid brushing or combing the hair when it's dry to prevent frizz
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle the hair when it's wet
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate the hair

By following these tips and techniques, you can get a fringe with curly hair that complements your face shape and adds some style to your look.
