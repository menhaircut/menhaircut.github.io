How To Loosen Curls For Guys Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're a guy with curly hair, you may find yourself wanting to switch things up from time to time. Whether you're looking for a new style or just want to loosen up your curls a bit, there are several methods you can try. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for how to loosen curls for guys.

Table of Contents

  • How to Loosen Curls for Guys
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Loosening Curls
  • FAQs

How to Loosen Curls for Guys

Before we dive into the specific methods, it's important to note that everyone's hair is different. What works for one person may not work for another, so it may take some trial and error to find the right method for you. With that said, here are some ways to loosen curls:

1. Blow-Drying

One of the easiest ways to loosen curls is to blow-dry your hair. Start by washing your hair as usual and then apply a heat protectant spray. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer and dry your hair on a low heat setting. While you're drying your hair, use your fingers to gently pull and stretch your curls. This will help to loosen them up and give you a more relaxed look.

2. Flat Ironing

If you're looking for a more dramatic change, you can try flat ironing your hair. This method will completely straighten your curls, so keep that in mind before you try it. To flat iron your hair, start by washing and blow-drying it as usual. Apply a heat protectant spray and then use a flat iron on a low heat setting. Work in small sections and be sure to use a comb to smooth out any tangles before you flat iron.

3. Chemical Relaxer

If you're looking for a more permanent solution, you can try a chemical relaxer. This method involves applying a chemical solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that make your hair curly. It's important to note that this method can be damaging to your hair, so be sure to consult with a professional before trying it.

Step-by-Step Guide

Now that you know some of the methods for loosening curls, here's a step-by-step guide for blow-drying your hair:

  1. Wash your hair as usual and apply a heat protectant spray.
  2. Attach a diffuser to your blow dryer and set it to a low heat setting.
  3. Start blow-drying your hair, using your fingers to gently pull and stretch your curls.
  4. Continue blow-drying until your hair is completely dry.
  5. Use a styling product, such as a pomade or hair wax, to help hold your new style in place.

Tips for Loosening Curls

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when trying to loosen your curls:

  • Don't overdo it. Loosening your curls too much can lead to hair damage and breakage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from blow-drying or flat ironing.
  • Be gentle when pulling and stretching your curls. Too much force can cause breakage.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find one that works well with your hair type.
  • Consult with a professional before trying a chemical relaxer.

FAQs

Q: Will loosening my curls damage my hair?

A: It depends on the method you use and how often you do it. Blow-drying and flat ironing can cause damage if done too frequently or if not done properly. Chemical relaxers can also be damaging if not done by a professional.

Q: Can I still wear my hair curly after loosening it?

A: Yes, you can still wear your hair curly after loosening it. The extent to which your curls are loosened will depend on the method you use.

Hair Care for Loosened Curls

Once you've loosened your curls, it's important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can easily loosen your curls and switch up your style. Remember to be gentle with your hair and consult with a professional if you're unsure about any of the methods.
