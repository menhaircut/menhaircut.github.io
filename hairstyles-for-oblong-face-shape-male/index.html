Having an oblong face shape can be a blessing or a curse, depending on how you style your hair. If you're a male with an oblong face shape, you might find it challenging to choose the right hairstyle that flatters your facial features. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share some tips and tricks on how to choose the best hairstyles for oblong face shape male.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles, let's first determine your face shape. Here's how to do it:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your hair pulled back.
  2. Take a measurement of your face with a tape measure or ruler.
  3. Measure across the widest part of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  4. Take note of the measurements and compare them.
  5. If your jawline is narrower than your forehead and cheekbones, you have an oblong face shape.

If you're still unsure, you can consult with a hairstylist or use a virtual face shape detector app.

Hairstyles for Oblong Face Shape Male

Now that you've determined your face shape, it's time to choose the best hairstyle that complements your features. Here are some of the best hairstyles for oblong face shape male:

  1. Short and textured - Short haircuts with textured layers on top can add volume to your hair and balance out your face shape. You can style it with a messy or spiky look.
  2. Buzz cut - If you prefer a low-maintenance haircut, a buzz cut can work well for an oblong face shape. It can also make your forehead appear smaller.
  3. Crew cut - A crew cut is a classic and versatile hairstyle that suits most face shapes, including oblong. It's short on the sides and longer on top, which can add width to your forehead.
  4. Side-swept bangs - If you have longer hair, you can try side-swept bangs that cover your forehead partially. It can soften your face shape and make it appear more oval.
  5. Pompadour - A pompadour is a stylish and sophisticated hairstyle that works well for oblong face shape male. It's short on the sides and longer on top with a voluminous quiff that adds height and width to your face.

Remember to choose a style that suits your personal taste and lifestyle. You can also consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

Hair Care Tips for Men with Oblong Face Shape

Aside from choosing the right hairstyle, taking care of your hair can also enhance your facial features. Here are some hair care tips for men with oblong face shape:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.
  • Use a hair styling product that suits your hair type and holds your hairstyle in place.
  • Get regular haircuts to maintain the shape and style of your hair.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long, flat, or slicked back, as they can elongate your face shape.
  • Consider using hair accessories, such as a hat or headband, to add dimension and texture to your hair.

Remember, healthy hair is the foundation of a great hairstyle.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oblong face shape male:

  • What hairstyles should I avoid if I have an oblong face shape?
    Avoid hairstyles that are too long, flat, or slicked back, as they can elongate your face shape. Also, avoid center parts or fringes that cover your forehead completely.
  • Can I wear a beard if I have an oblong face shape?
    Yes, you can wear a beard if you have an oblong face shape. A well-groomed beard can add width and balance to your face. Choose a beard style that suits your facial hair growth and complements your hairstyle.
  • Should I always follow the rules for my face shape?
    No, you don't have to follow the rules for your face shape all the time. It's important to experiment and try different hairstyles that suit your personal taste and lifestyle. However, following the guidelines can help you choose the best hairstyle that flatters your facial features.

That's it for our guide on hairstyles for oblong face shape male. We hope this article has helped you choose the best hairstyle that suits your facial features and style. Remember, confidence is the key to pulling off any hairstyle. Rock your new haircut with pride!
