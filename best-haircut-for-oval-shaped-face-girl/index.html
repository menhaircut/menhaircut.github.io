Are you an oval shaped faced girl looking for the perfect haircut? Look no further! We've got you covered with this guide on the best haircut for oval shaped face girl. In this article, we'll cover everything from how to determine your face shape to the best hairstyles for oval shaped faces. Let's get started!

Determining Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the best haircut for oval shaped face girl, let's first determine if you do, in fact, have an oval shaped face. An oval face is characterized by balanced features, with a forehead that is slightly wider than the chin and a rounded jawline. To confirm that you have an oval shaped face, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline. If all three measurements are relatively the same, you likely have an oval shaped face.

Best Hairstyles for Oval Shaped Faces

Now that you've confirmed that you have an oval shaped face, it's time to choose a haircut that will flatter your features. Oval shaped faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of styles, but some of the best haircuts for oval shaped faces include:

  • Long layers
  • Shoulder length bob
  • Pixie cut
  • Side swept bangs
  • Center part

Long layers are great for adding volume and movement to your hair, while a shoulder length bob can frame your face and accentuate your cheekbones. A pixie cut can also look great on an oval shaped face, especially when paired with side swept bangs. A center part can help elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.

How to Style Your Hair

Once you've chosen your perfect haircut, it's important to know how to style it to achieve the best look. Here are some tips for styling your hair based on the best haircuts for oval shaped faces:

  • Long layers: Use a curling iron to create loose waves for a beachy look.
  • Shoulder length bob: Straighten your hair for a sleek, sophisticated look.
  • Pixie cut: Use a texturizing spray to add volume and dimension to your hair.
  • Side swept bangs: Blow dry your bangs with a round brush for a smooth, polished look.
  • Center part: Use a straightener to create sleek, straight hair.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you know the best haircut for oval shaped face girl and how to style it, it's important to maintain your haircut to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair to prevent stripping it of natural oils.

Frequently Asked Questions

What other hairstyles can I try with an oval shaped face?

While the haircuts we've listed are some of the best for oval shaped faces, there are plenty of other hairstyles that can look great. You can try a messy bun, a high ponytail, or even braids.

Can I still wear bangs with an oval shaped face?

Yes! Oval shaped faces can pull off a variety of bang styles, including blunt bangs, side swept bangs, and even wispy bangs.

What hair color looks best on an oval shaped face?

Since oval shaped faces are versatile, you can experiment with a variety of hair colors. However, highlights or balayage can help frame your face and add dimension to your hair.

Hair Care for Oval Shaped Faces

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking great. Here are some tips for hair care for oval shaped faces:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid over-brushing your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis to prevent heat damage.
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protectant spray when spending time in the sun to prevent sun damage.

Now that you know the best haircut for oval shaped face girl, it's time to schedule your next haircut appointment and try out a new style! Whether you choose long layers or a pixie cut, you're sure to look great with your oval shaped face.
