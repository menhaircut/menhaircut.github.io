How To Make Your Face Look Rounder With Makeup

Written by Petter Nov 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a more angular face shape, you might want to add some softness and roundness to your features. Thankfully, there are plenty of makeup tricks that can help you achieve the look you want. In this article, we'll go over some tips and tricks for making your face look rounder with makeup.

Description

Makeup can be a powerful tool when it comes to shaping your face. By strategically applying products, you can create the illusion of a rounder, softer face shape. This is especially useful for those with a more angular or square-shaped face. By following some simple techniques, you can transform your face into a more rounded and youthful appearance.

How to

Step 1: Moisturize

Before applying any makeup, it's important to prep your skin by moisturizing. This will help create a smooth canvas for your makeup and prevent it from looking cakey or patchy. Use a lightweight, hydrating moisturizer that won't clog your pores.

Step 2: Apply Foundation

Next, apply a foundation that matches your skin tone. Use a brush or sponge to blend the foundation evenly over your face. Choose a foundation that has a dewy finish to add some luminosity to your skin.

Step 3: Contour

Contouring is an excellent technique for creating a rounder face shape. Use a contour powder or cream that is a shade or two darker than your skin tone. Apply the contour product to the hollows of your cheeks, along your hairline, and under your jawline. Blend the product well for a natural-looking finish.

Step 4: Highlight

Highlighting is another key step in creating a rounder face shape. Use a highlighter that is a shade or two lighter than your skin tone. Apply the highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones, the bridge of your nose, and your cupid's bow. This will create the illusion of roundness and add some dimension to your face.

Step 5: Apply Blush

Blush is an essential step in creating a rounder face shape. Choose a blush that is a soft, rosy pink or peach tone. Apply the blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend it upwards towards your temples. This will give your cheeks a natural-looking flush and add to the roundness of your face.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for making your face look rounder with makeup:

  • Use a light hand when applying makeup to avoid a heavy, cakey look.
  • Choose makeup products that have a dewy finish to add some luminosity to your skin.
  • Blend your makeup well to avoid any harsh lines or streaks.
  • Experiment with different shades of blush to find the perfect color for your skin tone.

FAQ

1. Can I make my face look rounder without makeup?

Yes, there are other ways to make your face look rounder without makeup. You can try hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, wear earrings that draw attention to your cheekbones, or even try facial exercises that help tone and sculpt your facial muscles.

2. How do I choose the right contour shade?

When choosing a contour shade, look for a product that is a shade or two darker than your skin tone. You don't want the contour to look too harsh or unnatural. Start with a lighter shade and build up the intensity gradually until you achieve the desired effect.

Hair Care

Choosing the right hairstyle can also help create a rounder face shape. Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layered cuts or soft waves. Avoid hairstyles that are too severe or angular, as they can emphasize the squareness of your face. Be sure to use hair products that add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create a rounder, softer face shape with makeup. Remember to experiment with different products and techniques to find the ones that work best for you. With a little practice, you'll be able to create the perfect round face shape in no time.
