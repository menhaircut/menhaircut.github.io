How To Style Soft Dread Hairstyle A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 10, 2023 · 3 min read

Soft dread hairstyle is a popular hairstyle that has been in trend for years. It is a low maintenance hairstyle that is perfect for those who want a stylish look without spending too much time on their hair. In this article, we will be discussing how to style soft dread hairstyle in easy steps. We will also be providing tips and hair care advice to keep your soft dread hairstyle looking fresh and healthy. So, let's get started.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Style Soft Dread Hairstyle
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling Soft Dread Hairstyle
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Soft Dread Hairstyle

How to Style Soft Dread Hairstyle

Before we dive into the styling process, it is important to note that soft dread hairstyle requires hair extensions. These extensions are made of synthetic hair and come in various lengths and colors. You can purchase them at any hair supply store or online. Once you have your hair extensions, you can begin the styling process.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. This will keep your hair extensions looking fresh and clean.

2. Once your hair is clean, blow-dry it on a low heat setting. This will help to straighten your hair and make it easier to work with.

3. Next, section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to attach the hair extensions.

4. Take a small section of your hair and apply a small amount of hair gel to it. This will help to hold the hair extension in place.

5. Take a hair extension and attach it to your hair using a crochet hook. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends of your hair.

6. Repeat this process until you have attached all of the hair extensions to your hair.

7. Once all the extensions are in place, use a hairbrush to blend your natural hair with the extensions. This will create a seamless look.

8. Finally, use a hair tie to tie your hair up into a ponytail or bun. You can also leave it down if you prefer.

Tips for Styling Soft Dread Hairstyle

- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.

- Apply a small amount of hair oil to your extensions to keep them looking shiny and healthy.

- Try different hairstyles such as braids or twists to keep your soft dread hairstyle looking fresh and new.

FAQs

Q: How long does soft dread hairstyle last?

A: Soft dread hairstyle can last up to 2-3 months with proper care.

Q: Can I swim with soft dread hairstyle?

A: Yes, you can swim with soft dread hairstyle. However, it is important to rinse your hair with fresh water after swimming in saltwater or chlorine.

Hair Care for Soft Dread Hairstyle

- Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair and extensions clean.

- Avoid using heat styling tools such as flat irons or curling irons as they can damage your hair extensions.

- Protect your hair and extensions with a silk or satin scarf while sleeping.

- Use a hair oil or serum to keep your hair and extensions moisturized.

By following these steps and tips, you can easily style your soft dread hairstyle and keep it looking fresh and healthy. Experiment with different hairstyles to keep your look versatile and fun.
