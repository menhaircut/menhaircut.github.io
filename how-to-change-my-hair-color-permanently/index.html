How To Change Your Hair Color Permanently A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jun 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of your current hair color or simply want to try something new, you may be wondering how to change your hair color permanently. While there are a variety of ways to change your hair color, not all methods are permanent. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to change your hair color permanently and provide tips for maintaining your new look.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Change Your Hair Color Permanently
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care Tips

How to Change Your Hair Color Permanently

Changing your hair color permanently typically involves the use of hair dye or bleach. Permanent hair dye contains chemicals that penetrate the hair shaft and bond with the hair's natural pigments, resulting in a lasting color change. Bleach, on the other hand, removes the existing pigments from your hair and allows new color to be deposited.

It's important to note that any permanent hair color change will require some maintenance to keep the color looking fresh and vibrant. This may include touch-ups every few weeks, using color-safe shampoo and conditioner, and avoiding excessive heat styling.

Step-by-Step Guide

Before you begin the process of changing your hair color permanently, it's important to choose the right shade and type of dye for your hair. If you're unsure which shade to choose, consider consulting a professional stylist or doing a strand test before applying the dye.

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Put on gloves to protect your hands from the dye.
  3. Mix the hair dye according to the package instructions.
  4. Apply the dye to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  5. Leave the dye on for the recommended amount of time, typically 30-45 minutes.
  6. Rinse the dye out of your hair with cool water until the water runs clear.
  7. Apply a color-safe conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.
  8. Rinse your hair again with cool water.
  9. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Your New Hair Color

Once you've changed your hair color permanently, there are several steps you can take to keep it looking great:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner to prevent fading.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling, as this can cause the color to fade more quickly.
  • Avoid chlorine and salt water, as these can also cause the color to fade.
  • Consider getting touch-ups every few weeks to keep the color looking fresh.

FAQs

How long does permanent hair dye last?

Permanent hair dye typically lasts for several weeks to several months, depending on how often you wash your hair and how well you care for it.

Can I change my hair color permanently at home?

Yes, you can change your hair color permanently at home using a hair dye kit. However, if you're unsure which shade to choose or how to apply the dye, it's a good idea to consult a professional stylist.

Can I bleach my hair to change the color permanently?

Yes, bleaching your hair can remove the existing pigments and allow new color to be deposited. However, bleaching can be damaging to your hair and should be done carefully.

Hair Care Tips

Changing your hair color permanently can be a great way to refresh your look, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and vibrant. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners every day.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.

With these tips and our step-by-step guide, you can change your hair color permanently and enjoy a fresh new look. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it looking great!
