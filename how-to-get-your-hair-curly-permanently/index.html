How To Get Your Hair Curly Permanently A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 11, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of using curling irons, rollers, and other heat tools to get those beautiful curls? Do you want to have curly hair permanently? In this article, we will show you how to get your hair curly permanently using various methods. We'll also provide you with tips on how to maintain your curls and keep your hair healthy. Let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Method 1: Perming
  • Method 2: Texturizing
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

Method 1: Perming

Perming is a process of chemically altering your hair to create curls. It involves applying a solution to your hair that breaks down the bonds that hold the hair straight. The hair is then wrapped around rods to create curls. Here's how to get your hair curly permanently using perming:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

It's important to consult with a professional stylist before getting a perm. They will evaluate your hair type and condition and recommend the best type of perm for you. They will also advise you on how to prepare your hair for the process.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before getting a perm, you need to prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it with a protein-rich shampoo and conditioner. This will help strengthen your hair and prepare it for the chemical process.

Step 3: Apply the Perm Solution

The stylist will apply the perm solution to your hair, making sure to saturate every strand. They will then wrap your hair around rods of different sizes, depending on the type of curls you want to achieve.

Step 4: Rinse and Neutralize

After the perm solution has been applied, your hair will be rinsed thoroughly to remove any excess solution. Then, a neutralizing solution will be applied to stop the chemical process and restore the pH balance of your hair.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is neutralized, the stylist will remove the rods and style your hair as desired. You can use a diffuser to enhance your curls and create volume.

Method 2: Texturizing

Texturizing is a process of cutting or removing some of the weight from your hair to create curls. This method is less damaging than perming and is suitable for people with fine or thin hair. Here's how to get your hair curly permanently using texturizing:

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

As with perming, it's essential to consult with a professional stylist before getting a texturizing treatment. They will evaluate your hair type and condition and recommend the best type of texturizing for you.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Before getting a texturizing treatment, you need to prepare your hair by washing and conditioning it with a protein-rich shampoo and conditioner. This will help strengthen your hair and prepare it for the process.

Step 3: Cut or Remove Weight from Your Hair

The stylist will cut or remove weight from your hair to create curls. They may use thinning shears or a razor to create texture and movement in your hair.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is texturized, the stylist will style your hair as desired. You can use a diffuser to enhance your curls and create volume.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and frizz-free.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.
  • Sleep on a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQs

Can I perm my hair if it has been previously colored?

Yes, you can perm your hair if it has been previously colored. However, it's essential to consult with a professional stylist to ensure that your hair is strong enough to withstand the chemical process.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last anywhere from three to six months, depending on how fast your hair grows and how well you maintain it.

Can I texturize my hair if it's already curly?

Yes, you can texturize your hair if it's already curly. However, it's essential to consult with a professional stylist to ensure that the texturizing treatment doesn't damage your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some tips for taking care of your curly hair:

  • Wash your hair no more than twice a week to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Avoid using a towel to dry your hair as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a microfiber towel or an old t-shirt to gently squeeze out excess water.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry as it can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it's wet.

Now that you know how to get your hair curly permanently, you can choose the method that's right for you. Remember to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and beautiful. Happy styling!
