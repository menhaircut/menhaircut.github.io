How To Do An Undercut On A Woman

Written by Aprilia Aug 19, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to try a bold and edgy hairstyle, an undercut might be just what you need. This popular hairstyle involves shaving the hair on the underside of your head, creating a cool and unique look. While undercuts have traditionally been associated with men's hairstyles, many women are now embracing this trend. In this article, we'll show you how to do an undercut on a woman, step-by-step.

Description

An undercut is a hairstyle that involves shaving the hair on the underside of your head, leaving the hair on the top and sides intact. This creates a bold and edgy look that's perfect for those who want to stand out from the crowd. Undercuts have been popular with men for many years, but recently, more and more women have been embracing this trend.

How to do an undercut on a woman

Before you start, it's important to decide where you want your undercut to be. You can choose to shave just one side of your head, or you can go for a full undercut, shaving the hair on both sides and the back of your head. Once you've decided where you want your undercut to be, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and dry your hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. This will make it easier to shave and ensure that you don't have any tangles or knots that could get in the way.

Step 2: Section off the hair you want to shave

Using a comb, section off the hair that you want to shave. This will help you avoid accidentally shaving too much hair and ensure that your undercut looks neat and tidy.

Step 3: Use clippers to shave the hair

Using clippers, shave the hair on the underside of your head. Start at the bottom and work your way up, using a higher guard as you go. Be sure to go slowly and carefully, so you don't accidentally shave too much hair.

Step 4: Blend the hair

Once you've finished shaving, use a comb to blend the hair on the top and sides of your head with the shaved hair. This will create a seamless look and ensure that your undercut looks natural.

Step 5: Style your hair

Finally, style your hair as desired. You can leave it down, tie it up in a ponytail, or create a cool and edgy updo. Whatever you choose, your undercut is sure to turn heads!

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect undercut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter cut.
  • Be sure to go slowly and carefully, so you don't accidentally shave too much hair.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Ask a friend for help if you're not confident doing it yourself.

FAQ

Can I do an undercut on curly hair?

Yes, you can do an undercut on curly hair. However, it's important to choose a stylist who is experienced with curly hair, as the texture can make it more difficult to achieve the desired look.

How long does an undercut take to grow out?

It can take several months to grow out an undercut, depending on how much hair was shaved. However, you can still style your hair in different ways as it grows out, so don't be afraid to experiment.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair after getting an undercut. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Condition your hair regularly to keep it soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and steps, you'll be able to achieve the perfect undercut on a woman. Remember to take your time and be patient, and you'll end up with a cool and edgy hairstyle that's sure to turn heads.
