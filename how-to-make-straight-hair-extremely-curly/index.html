How To Make Straight Hair Extremely Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Oct 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have straight hair and are looking to achieve curly locks, you’re in the right place. With a few simple steps, you can transform your straight hair into bouncy, voluminous curls that will turn heads. In this post, we’ll go through everything you need to know about how to make straight hair extremely curly, including tips, tricks, and hair care advice.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make Straight Hair Extremely Curly
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Making Straight Hair Extremely Curly
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

How to Make Straight Hair Extremely Curly

There are several ways to make straight hair extremely curly, including using a curling iron, hot rollers, or a perm. But for the purposes of this post, we’ll focus on how to use a curling iron to achieve curly hair.

Materials:

  • Curling iron
  • Hair clips
  • Hairspray
  • Heat protectant spray

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to make straight hair extremely curly:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair. This will help to define your curls and keep them in place.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant Spray

Before using any hot tools on your hair, it’s important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair, making sure to distribute it evenly.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections, using hair clips to keep each section in place. This will make it easier to curl your hair and ensure that every strand gets curled.

Step 4: Curl Your Hair

Starting with the bottom section of your hair, take a small section and wrap it around the curling iron. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds, then release the curl. Repeat this process for each section of your hair, working your way up to the top.

Step 5: Set Your Curls

Once you’ve curled all of your hair, use your fingers to gently separate the curls and create more volume. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold your curls in place.

Tips for Making Straight Hair Extremely Curly

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when making straight hair extremely curly:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel for tighter curls.
  • Apply a volumizing mousse to your hair before blow-drying to create more volume.
  • Don’t use too much hairspray, as this can make your hair look stiff and unnatural.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to find the one that works best for your hair.

FAQs

Can you make straight hair curly permanently?

Yes, you can make straight hair curly permanently with a perm. However, this is a chemical process that can damage your hair if not done properly.

How long do curly hairstyles last?

Curly hairstyles can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on how well you set your curls and how well you maintain them.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

Curly hair requires a different hair care routine than straight hair. Here are some tips to keep your curly hair looking its best:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and prevent breakage.
  • Sleep with your hair in a loose bun or braid to prevent tangles and breakage.

With these tips and tricks, you can make straight hair extremely curly and achieve the bouncy, voluminous curls you’ve always wanted. Just remember to take care of your hair and experiment with different techniques to find the ones that work best for you.
