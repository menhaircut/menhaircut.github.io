How To Cut Your Own Hair At The Back A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money on haircuts? Do you want to try cutting your own hair at the back? It may seem daunting, but with the right tools and techniques, it’s possible to give yourself a great haircut. In this blog post, we’ll show you how to cut your own hair at the back in relaxed English language.

How to Cut Your Own Hair at the Back

Cutting your own hair at the back requires a few essential tools. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Scissors
  • A handheld mirror
  • Clippers (optional)

Once you have your tools ready, you can start cutting your hair at the back by following these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before cutting your hair, make sure it’s clean and dry. This will help you see the natural shape of your hair and make it easier to cut.

Step 2: Use the Handheld Mirror

Stand in front of a large mirror and use the handheld mirror to see the back of your head. This will help you see the areas that need trimming.

Step 3: Start Trimming

Using the scissors, start trimming the back of your hair. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Use small, light snips to avoid cutting too much hair at once. Keep checking the mirror to make sure you’re cutting evenly.

Step 4: Use Clippers (Optional)

If you have clippers, you can use them to trim the hair on the sides and back of your head. Use a guard to control the length of the hair.

Step 5: Check Your Work

Once you’ve finished trimming your hair, use the handheld mirror to check the back of your head. Make any final adjustments as needed.

Tips for Cutting Your Own Hair at the Back

Here are some additional tips to help you cut your own hair at the back:

  • Start with small snips and work your way up to avoid cutting too much hair at once.
  • Use the handheld mirror to see the back of your head as you cut.
  • If you have long hair, consider tying it up in a ponytail to make it easier to cut.
  • Be patient and take your time. Cutting your own hair can be tricky, but with practice, you’ll get better.

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair at the back without a handheld mirror?

It’s possible to cut your own hair at the back without a handheld mirror, but it can be more difficult. You may need to use a larger mirror or ask someone to help you.

How often should I cut my own hair at the back?

How often you cut your own hair at the back depends on how fast your hair grows and how you want it to look. For most people, cutting your hair every 6-8 weeks is a good rule of thumb.

Hair Care After Cutting Your Own Hair at the Back

After cutting your own hair at the back, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking great. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners too often.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

Cutting your own hair at the back can save you time and money, but it can also be a bit intimidating. By following these tips and techniques, you can give yourself a great haircut and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
