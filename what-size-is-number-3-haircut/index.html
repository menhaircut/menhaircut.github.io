What Size is Number 3 Haircut? A Complete Guide in 2023

If you're wondering what size is number 3 haircut, you've come to the right place. Whether you're getting your hair cut at a barbershop or doing it yourself at home, understanding the different clipper guard sizes is crucial. In this post, we'll be discussing the number 3 haircut size, how to achieve it, and some tips and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How To Achieve A Number 3 Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Achieving the Perfect Number 3 Haircut
  • FAQ

Description

The number 3 haircut is a popular choice for those who want a shorter cut without going too extreme. Also known as a "crew cut," this style is achieved using a clipper guard with a length of 3/8 of an inch, or 9.5mm. This length allows for a short, even cut, while still leaving enough hair to style or comb over if desired.

How To Achieve A Number 3 Haircut

Before attempting to cut your hair, it's important to have the right tools. You'll need a pair of clippers with a number 3 guard attachment, a comb, and a pair of scissors for any touch-ups. If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, it's always best to visit a professional barber who can help achieve the look you want.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with dry, clean hair.

2. Attach the number 3 guard to your clippers.

3. Begin at the sides of your head, using the clippers to trim about 1/2 inch up from the hairline.

4. Move to the back of your head, and work your way up to the crown, using the same technique as the sides.

5. Finally, trim the top of your head, using the comb to guide the clippers and ensure an even cut.

6. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or touch up the edges if needed.

7. Finish with styling products or leave as is.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Number 3 Haircut

- Always start with clean, dry hair for the best results.

- Use a mirror or have someone help you to ensure even cutting.

- Don't be afraid to ask your barber for advice or recommendations.

- Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair type.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve a number 3 haircut with scissors?

A: While it's possible to achieve a similar length with scissors, clippers are recommended for a more even cut.

Q: Will a number 3 haircut suit my face shape?

A: This style can suit a variety of face shapes, but it's always best to consult with a professional barber for personalized advice.

Q: How often should I get a number 3 haircut?

A: This depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you prefer to maintain the length. On average, a number 3 haircut can last up to 4-6 weeks before needing a touch-up.

Hair Care Tips After Getting A Number 3 Haircut

After getting a number 3 haircut, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its healthy appearance. Here are some tips:

- Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep hair clean and hydrated.

- Avoid using too much heat when styling, as this can damage hair.

- Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep hair soft and shiny.

Now that you know what size is number 3 haircut and how to achieve it, you'll be able to rock this classic style with confidence. Whether you're going for a clean-cut look or a more casual style, the number 3 haircut is a versatile choice that can suit a variety of preferences.
