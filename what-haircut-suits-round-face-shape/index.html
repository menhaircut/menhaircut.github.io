What Haircut Suits Round Face Shape

Written by Bardi Jul 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a round-faced beauty struggling to find the perfect haircut? Look no further! We’ve put together a comprehensive guide on what haircut suits round face shape. From short to long, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Round face shape is characterized by a soft, circular shape with no prominent angles. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width, creating a symmetrical appearance. If you have a round face shape, you want to avoid haircuts that add width to the sides of your face, as this can make your face appear even rounder. Instead, you want to focus on haircuts that elongate your face and create the illusion of angles.

How to

Before you start picking out a haircut, it’s important to determine your face shape. To do this, pull your hair back and examine your face in the mirror. If your face is widest at the cheeks and has a rounded chin, you have a round face shape. Once you’ve determined your face shape, it’s time to start exploring haircuts that flatter your features.

Short Haircuts

If you’re looking for a short haircut that flatters your round face shape, consider a pixie cut or a bob. These haircuts draw attention to your cheekbones and create the illusion of height. If you’re feeling daring, you can also try a shaved head or an undercut. These haircuts add edginess and dimension to your look.

Medium Haircuts

If you prefer medium-length hair, consider a layered lob or a shoulder-length cut with face-framing layers. These haircuts create movement and dimension, drawing attention away from the roundness of your face. You can also try a side-swept fringe or a middle part to create the illusion of length.

Long Haircuts

Long hair can be tricky for round face shapes, but it’s not impossible! Consider a long, layered cut with face-framing layers. This haircut creates balance and dimension, drawing attention to your eyes and cheekbones. You can also try a deep side part or boho-style waves to elongate your face.

Step by Step

1. Determine your face shape by pulling your hair back and examining your face in the mirror.

2. Choose a haircut that flatters your round face shape.

3. Consult with your stylist to determine the best length and style for your hair.

4. Maintain your haircut with regular trims to keep it looking fresh and healthy.

Tips

- Avoid haircuts that add width to the sides of your face.

- Consider face-framing layers to create dimension and balance.

- Experiment with different parts to create the illusion of length.

- Don’t be afraid to try something new! Hair grows back.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear bangs with a round face shape?

A: Yes! Bangs can be a great way to create the illusion of length and add dimension to your haircut. Consider side-swept bangs or a wispy fringe.

Q: Should I avoid long haircuts if I have a round face shape?

A: Not necessarily! Long hair can be flattering on round face shapes if it’s styled correctly. Consider a long, layered cut with face-framing layers to create balance and dimension.

Q: How often should I get my haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and style. Generally, it’s recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

Hair Care

It’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that’s formulated for your hair type. Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dull. If you’re going to use hot tools, use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Finally, don’t forget to get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and healthy.
