Choppy Long Hair With Short Layers On Top The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a hairstyle that's both trendy and versatile, then choppy long hair with short layers on top may be just what you need. This look is perfect for those who want to add some texture and movement to their hair without sacrificing their length. In this post, we'll cover everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to get it, styling tips, and hair care.

Description

Choppy long hair with short layers on top is a modern interpretation of the classic layered haircut. The key feature of this style is the short layers on top, which add volume and texture while the length provides a soft and feminine touch. The choppy layers also make this hairstyle perfect for those with naturally wavy or curly hair, as they can help enhance the natural texture.

How to Get Choppy Long Hair with Short Layers on Top

The best way to get this hairstyle is to go to a professional hairstylist. They will be able to assess your hair type and face shape to create a customized look that suits you. However, if you're feeling brave and want to try it at home, here's how:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair thoroughly before starting. This will ensure that the layers are cut evenly and that you can see the final result clearly.

Step 2: Part your hair

Decide where you want your part to be and separate your hair into two sections. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 3: Cut the short layers

Starting from the top section, take small sections of hair and cut them to the desired length. Use scissors to create choppy layers that vary in length. Be careful not to cut too much at once, as you can always go back and cut more later.

Step 4: Cut the longer layers

Once you've finished the short layers, move on to the bottom section of your hair. Cut the longer layers so that they blend in with the short layers on top. Again, use scissors to create choppy layers that vary in length.

Step 5: Style your hair

Now that you've cut your hair, it's time to style it. You can use a curling iron or a straightener to create waves or curls, or you can leave it straight for a more natural look. Use a texturizing spray to add some volume and movement to your hair.

Tips for Styling Choppy Long Hair with Short Layers on Top

Here are some tips to help you style your hair:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using a curling iron or straightener to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Apply a small amount of hair oil to the ends of your hair to keep them from looking dry and frizzy.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair.

FAQ

Q: Will choppy long hair with short layers on top work for all hair types?

A: This hairstyle works best for those with naturally wavy or curly hair, but it can also work for those with straight hair. Your hairstylist will be able to customize the cut to suit your hair type.

Q: How often do I need to get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape and texture of the haircut.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

Choppy long hair with short layers on top is a chic and stylish hairstyle that's perfect for those who want to add some texture and movement to their hair. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve a look that's both modern and feminine. So, go ahead and try it out!
