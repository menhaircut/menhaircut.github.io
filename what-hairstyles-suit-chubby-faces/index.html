What Hairstyles Suit Chubby Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 05, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have a chubby face, you might be wondering what hairstyles would suit you best. The good news is that there are plenty of options that can help you look your best. In this guide, we’ll explore the different hairstyles that are perfect for people with chubby faces. Whether you have short hair, long hair, or something in between, we’ve got you covered.

Description

Having a chubby face doesn’t mean you can’t look stylish and chic. With the right hairstyle, you can enhance your features and complement your face shape. The key is to find a style that frames your face and draws attention to your best features while minimizing any problem areas. From bobs to long layers, there are plenty of options to choose from.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a chubby face, it’s important to consider the length, volume, and texture of your hair. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect style:

1. Embrace Layers

Layers can help add movement and dimension to your hair. They can also help create the illusion of length and slim down your face. Opt for long layers that start at your chin or shorter.

2. Try a Bob

Bobs are a great option for people with chubby faces. They can help frame your face and create a more defined jawline. Opt for a chin-length bob or a longer bob that grazes your collarbone.

3. Go for Bangs

Bangs can help balance out a chubby face and draw attention to your eyes. Opt for side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs that graze your eyebrows.

4. Add Volume

Adding volume to your hair can help create the illusion of a slimmer face. Use a volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray to add volume at the roots. You can also try teasing your hair at the crown for added volume.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect hairstyle for your chubby face:

1. Consider Your Face Shape

Before choosing a hairstyle, consider your face shape. If you have a round face, opt for a style that adds height and length. If you have a square face, opt for a style that softens your features. If you have an oval face, you can pretty much pull off any style.

2. Choose a Length

Decide on the length of your hair. Short hair can help create the illusion of length and slim down your face. Long hair can help balance out a round face.

3. Add Layers

Add layers to create movement and dimension. Opt for long layers that start at your chin or shorter.

4. Consider Bangs

Consider adding bangs to balance out your face. Opt for side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs.

5. Add Volume

Finally, add volume to your hair. Use a volumizing mousse or root-lifting spray to add volume at the roots. You can also try teasing your hair at the crown for added volume.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you find the perfect hairstyle for your chubby face:

1. Avoid Too Much Volume

While volume can be flattering, too much volume can make your face look even rounder. Opt for subtle volume instead.

2. Avoid Short Cuts

Avoid very short cuts that can make your face look wider. Opt for a length that falls just below your chin or longer.

3. Experiment with Color

Experiment with color to add dimension and depth to your hair. Highlights and lowlights can be especially flattering.

4. Consult with Your Stylist

Consult with your stylist to find the perfect hairstyle for your chubby face. They can offer personalized recommendations based on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

FAQ

What kind of bangs are best for a chubby face?

Side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs are best for a chubby face. They can help balance out your face and draw attention to your eyes.

What length of hair is best for a chubby face?

The length of your hair will depend on your personal preferences and face shape. Generally, hair that falls just below your chin or longer is more flattering for a chubby face.

Can I pull off a pixie cut with a chubby face?

Pixie cuts can be tricky to pull off with a chubby face. If you’re set on a pixie cut, opt for a longer, shaggier style that adds volume and dimension to your hair.

Hair Care

Once you’ve found the perfect hairstyle for your chubby face, it’s important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny. Here are some tips:

1. Use Quality Hair Products

Use quality hair products that are formulated for your hair type. Look for products that are free of sulfates, parabens, and other harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid heat styling your hair too often, as this can damage your hair and make it look dry and brittle. If you must use heat styling tools, use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

3. Get Regular Trims

Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim for a trim every 6-8 weeks.

4. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF. Sun damage can cause your hair to look dry and brittle.

Conclusion

If you have a chubby face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. But with the right approach, you can find a style that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty. Whether you opt for long layers, a bob, or bangs, there’s a style out there that’s perfect for you. Follow our tips and advice, consult with your stylist, and take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and shiny.
