How To Do Twist In Black Men s Hair

Written by Oliver Jun 18, 2023 Â· 3 min read

In this article, we will discuss how to do twist in black men's hair. Twists are a popular hairstyle for black men that can be achieved by twisting two sections of hair together. This style is low maintenance and can last for several weeks. Here is a step-by-step guide on how to do twists in black men's hair.

How to

Step 1: Wash and Condition Your Hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair to remove any dirt and oil. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated, which will make it easier to twist. Rinse your hair thoroughly and towel dry it.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to make it easier to manage. A good conditioner will also help to detangle your hair, which is important when creating twists. Use a wide-tooth comb to distribute the conditioner evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into small sections using a comb. The size of the sections will depend on the thickness of your hair and the size of the twists you want to create. Use clips to hold each section in place.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair

Take two sections of hair and twist them together in a clockwise direction. Hold the hair tightly as you twist it to prevent it from unraveling. Continue twisting until you reach the end of the hair. Repeat this process for each section of hair.

Step 5: Secure Your Twists

Once you have twisted all of your hair, use small rubber bands or hair clips to secure the ends of the twists. This will keep them from unraveling.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing twists in black men's hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to make your hair easier to manage.
  • Divide your hair into small sections to make it easier to twist.
  • Hold the hair tightly as you twist it to prevent it from unraveling.
  • Use small rubber bands or hair clips to secure the ends of the twists.

FAQ

How long do twists last?

Twists can last for several weeks with proper care. Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, and sleep with a satin or silk scarf or pillowcase to prevent frizz.

Can you wash your hair with twists?

Yes, you can wash your hair with twists, but be gentle when washing and avoid scrubbing your scalp too hard. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.

How do you take out twists?

To take out twists, simply unravel them in the opposite direction that you twisted them. To prevent tangling, use a detangling spray or conditioner as you unravel each twist.

Hair Care

To keep your twists looking their best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner or oil.
  • Avoid using heavy products that can weigh down your hair.
  • Protect your hair from heat and sun damage by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, which can lead to breakage.

With these tips, you can create and maintain beautiful twists in black men's hair. This low-maintenance style is perfect for busy men who want to look stylish without spending a lot of time on their hair.
