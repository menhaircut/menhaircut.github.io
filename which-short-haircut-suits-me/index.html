Which Short Haircut Suits Me

Written by Aprilia Sep 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and looking for a change? Short haircuts can be a refreshing change and can make you look chic and stylish. But with so many short haircuts to choose from, it can be daunting to choose the perfect one. Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we'll guide you through which short haircut suits you the best.

Description

Short haircuts are a fun and trendy way to change up your look. They can make you look younger, more confident, and can accentuate your best features. But choosing the right short haircut can be challenging, especially if you're not sure what style would suit your face shape, hair texture, and personality.

How-to

The first step in choosing the right short haircut is to determine your face shape. There are four basic face shapes: round, oval, square, and heart-shaped. Once you've determined your face shape, you can choose a short haircut that flatters your features.

For example, if you have a round face, a short bob or pixie cut with side-swept bangs can make your face look slimmer. If you have an oval face, you can experiment with different short haircuts, as most styles will suit your face shape. If you have a square face, a short haircut with layers and soft edges can soften your features. And if you have a heart-shaped face, a short haircut with volume on top and tapered sides can balance your face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to help you choose the right short haircut:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Research short haircuts that suit your face shape
  3. Consult with your hairstylist
  4. Consider your hair texture and personality

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect short haircut:

  • Consider your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend on styling your hair
  • Don't be afraid to experiment with different short haircuts
  • Choose a short haircut that accentuates your best features
  • Choose a short haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture
  • Maintain your short haircut with regular trims and styling products

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about short haircuts:

Q: Will a short haircut suit me?

A: Short haircuts can suit anyone, regardless of age, gender, or hair type. It's all about choosing the right haircut that flatters your features and complements your personal style.

Q: How do I know if a short haircut will suit my face shape?

A: Determine your face shape and research short haircuts that suit your face shape. Consult with your hairstylist, and try on different hairstyles using virtual makeover tools or by trying on wigs.

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Short haircuts can make you look younger and more modern, especially if you choose a style that flatters your features and complements your personal style.

Hair Care

Maintaining a short haircut requires regular trims and styling products. Here are some tips to keep your short haircut looking great:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to maintain your haircut's shape and style
  • Use a lightweight styling product, such as a mousse or pomade, to add volume and texture to your hair
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using styling tools
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny

Now that you know which short haircut suits you the best, it's time to book an appointment with your hairstylist and get ready to rock your new look.
