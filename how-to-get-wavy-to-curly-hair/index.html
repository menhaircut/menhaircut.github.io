How To Get Wavy To Curly Hair A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Aug 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your straight hair and want to achieve those gorgeous curls or waves? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will provide you with a complete guide on how to get wavy to curly hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly or wavy hair is a popular hair trend that many women desire. However, not everyone is blessed with natural curls or waves. Fortunately, there are many ways to achieve curly or wavy hair, including using heat styling tools, applying hair products, or even getting a perm.

How to

The first step in getting wavy to curly hair is to determine what type of curls or waves you want. Do you want loose beachy waves or tight ringlets? This will help you decide which method is best for you.

Heat Styling Tools

If you want to achieve curls or waves quickly, heat styling tools are your best bet. You can use a curling iron, wand, or even a straightener to achieve your desired look. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage to your hair.

Hair Products

Another way to achieve wavy to curly hair is by using hair products. There are many different types of products available, including mousses, gels, and creams. Apply the product to your damp hair and scrunch your hair to encourage waves or curls.

Perm

If you want a more permanent solution, you can get a perm. A perm involves using chemicals to alter the structure of your hair. This will create permanent curls or waves. However, be aware that perms can be damaging to your hair and require regular maintenance.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to achieve wavy to curly hair using a curling iron:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Section your hair.
  4. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around the curling iron.
  5. Hold for a few seconds and release.
  6. Repeat on all sections of hair.
  7. Use your fingers to separate the curls and create a more natural look.
  8. Finish with hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to encourage natural waves.
  • Don't overuse heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Invest in high-quality hair products to achieve the best results.

FAQ

Q: Can I achieve wavy to curly hair without using heat styling tools?

A: Yes, you can use hair products or get a perm to achieve wavy to curly hair without using heat styling tools.

Q: How often should I get a perm?

A: It depends on your hair type and how quickly your hair grows. Generally, perms last around 3-6 months before needing a touch-up.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to maintain healthy and beautiful curls or waves. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid brushing your curls or waves to prevent frizz.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to get wavy to curly hair, it's time to try it out for yourself. Whether you choose to use heat styling tools, hair products, or get a perm, you can achieve those beautiful curls or waves you've always wanted. Just remember to take good care of your hair to maintain healthy and gorgeous locks.
