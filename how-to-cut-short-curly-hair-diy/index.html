How To Cut Short Curly Hair Diy A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Aug 28, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short curly hair and want to save some money, cutting your hair at home is a great option. However, cutting curly hair can be tricky, so it's important to have the right tools and techniques. In this article, we will guide you through the process of cutting short curly hair DIY style. From preparation to finishing touches, we have got you covered.

Table of Contents

  • Preparation
  • How to Cut Short Curly Hair DIY
  • Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care After Cutting Short Curly Hair

Preparation

Before you start cutting your hair, you need to prepare the tools and the space. Here's what you need:

  • A pair of sharp scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • A spray bottle filled with water
  • A towel to protect your clothes
  • A clean, well-lit space

Once you have gathered all the tools, you need to wash and dry your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start cutting.

How to Cut Short Curly Hair DIY

Now that you have all the tools and have prepared your hair, it's time to start the cutting process. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips or hair ties. Start with the bottom layer and work your way up. This will help you to see each section clearly and ensure that you cut evenly.

Step 2: Cut the Ends

Take a small section of hair and twist it tightly. Use your fingers to hold the twisted section of hair and cut the ends with the scissors. Make sure you cut only a small amount at a time, and avoid cutting too much.

Step 3: Cut the Sides

Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it tightly. Hold the twisted section of hair with your fingers and cut the ends with the scissors. Repeat this process on the other side.

Step 4: Check the Length

After you have cut all the sections of your hair, check the length by running your fingers through your hair. Make sure the length is even on both sides.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once you have finished cutting your hair, style it as desired. You can use a curling iron or hair gel to define your curls.

Tips for Cutting Short Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when cutting your short curly hair DIY style:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut
  • Take your time and cut small sections at a time
  • Twist the hair tightly before cutting to ensure even length
  • Check the length frequently to avoid cutting too much
  • Style your hair after cutting to see the final result

FAQ

Can I cut my short curly hair with regular scissors?

No, regular scissors are not designed for cutting hair. They can cause split ends and damage to your hair. It's important to use sharp hair cutting scissors.

How often should I cut my short curly hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain the length. Generally, it's recommended to cut your hair every 6-8 weeks.

Can I cut my short curly hair when it's wet?

It's not recommended to cut curly hair when it's wet, as the length can shrink when it dries. It's best to cut curly hair when it's dry.

Hair Care After Cutting Short Curly Hair

After cutting your short curly hair, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and shape. Here are some tips:

  • Moisturize your hair regularly with a leave-in conditioner
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain the shape

Cutting short curly hair DIY style can be challenging, but with the right tools and techniques, you can achieve great results. Remember to take your time and cut small sections at a time. If you're unsure, it's always best to consult a professional hair stylist.
