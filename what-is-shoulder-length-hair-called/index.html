What Is Shoulder Length Hair Called In Relaxed English Language

If you're trying to describe your hair to a hairdresser or a friend, you may be wondering what shoulder length hair is called in relaxed English language. This length of hair falls right below the shoulders and can be styled in many different ways. In this blog post, we'll discuss what shoulder length hair is called and provide tips for maintaining and styling this length of hair.

What is Shoulder Length Hair Called?

Shoulder length hair is called just that â shoulder length hair. There are no fancy or technical terms for this length of hair in relaxed English language. However, there are different variations of shoulder length hair that you may want to be aware of.

Collarbone Length Hair

Collarbone length hair is just slightly longer than shoulder length hair, falling right at the collarbone. This length of hair is great for those who want a little more length but still want to keep their hair relatively short.

Mid-Length Hair

Mid-length hair falls somewhere between shoulder length hair and collarbone length hair. This length of hair is perfect for those who want a little more length but don't want to commit to long hair.

How to Style Shoulder Length Hair

Shoulder length hair is versatile and can be styled in many different ways. Here are some tips for styling this length of hair:

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a popular style for shoulder length hair. To achieve this look, use a curling iron to create loose waves in your hair. Finish with a texturizing spray for a tousled, carefree look.

Straight and Sleek

If you prefer a more polished look, straighten your shoulder length hair with a flat iron. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and finish with a shine serum for a sleek finish.

Half-Up, Half-Down

A half-up, half-down style is a great way to keep your hair out of your face while still showing off your length. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip.

FAQ About Shoulder Length Hair

Is shoulder length hair easy to maintain?

Yes, shoulder length hair is relatively easy to maintain. However, regular trims are necessary to keep the ends healthy and prevent split ends.

What face shapes look best with shoulder length hair?

Shoulder length hair is versatile and can look great on many different face shapes. However, it is particularly flattering on those with heart-shaped or oval faces.

Can shoulder length hair be styled for formal events?

Absolutely! Shoulder length hair can be styled in many different ways for formal events, including updos, half-up styles, and sleek blowouts.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Hair

Here are some tips for keeping your shoulder length hair healthy and looking great:

Use a Heat Protectant Spray

When using heat tools on your hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage and breakage.

Get Regular Trims

Regular trims are necessary to keep the ends of your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Once a week, use a deep conditioning treatment on your hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.

Conclusion

Shoulder length hair is a versatile and popular length for women. While there are no technical terms for this length of hair in relaxed English language, it can be styled in many different ways to suit your personal style. By following these tips and taking good care of your hair, you can keep your shoulder length hair healthy and looking great.
