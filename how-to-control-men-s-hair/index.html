How To Control Men s Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Men's hair can be difficult to manage, especially when it seems to have a mind of its own. Fortunately, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve the look you want. In this guide, we will share our best tips and tricks on how to control men's hair.

How to

Step 1: Choose the Right Haircut

The first step in controlling your hair is to choose a haircut that suits your hair type and face shape. If you have thick, curly hair, a short, textured cut can help keep your hair under control. If you have fine, straight hair, a longer cut with layers can add volume and movement. Talk to your barber or stylist about which cut is best for you.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Using the right products is key to controlling your hair. Start with a shampoo and conditioner that are tailored to your hair type. If you have dry hair, look for products that contain moisturizing ingredients like coconut oil or shea butter. If you have oily hair, look for products that contain salicylic acid or tea tree oil.

After washing, apply a styling product like a pomade, wax, or gel to your hair. Pomades are great for adding shine and control, while waxes are best for creating texture and definition. Gels are the strongest hold product and work well for slicked-back styles. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

To style your hair, start by blow-drying it in the direction you want it to go. Use a brush to smooth out any kinks or waves. Then, apply your chosen styling product and work it through your hair with your fingers or a comb. If you want more hold, use a hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for controlling men's hair:

  • Don't wash your hair every day. This can strip it of its natural oils and make it harder to manage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair when it's wet.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting a hair treatment like a keratin treatment or hair mask to help strengthen and smooth your hair.

FAQ

How often should men wash their hair?

It depends on your hair type and lifestyle. If you have oily hair or work out frequently, you may need to wash your hair every day. If you have dry hair or don't sweat much, you can get away with washing it every few days.

What styling product is best for controlling men's hair?

It depends on the look you're going for and your hair type. Pomades are great for a sleek, polished look, while waxes are best for creating texture and definition. Gels are the strongest hold product and work well for slicked-back styles. Experiment with different products to find what works best for you.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for keeping it healthy and manageable. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Limit your use of hot tools like hair dryers and flat irons.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep split ends at bay.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture and shine.

With these tips and tricks, you can take control of your hair and achieve the look you want. Remember to be patient and experiment with different techniques and products until you find what works best for you.
