How To Air Dry Naturally Curly Hair Without Frizz

Written by William Oct 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizzy curls when air drying your hair? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with naturally curly hair struggle with frizz when air drying. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful, frizz-free curls without using heat. In this article, we'll show you how to air dry naturally curly hair without frizz.

  • How to Air Dry Naturally Curly Hair Without Frizz
  • Step-by-Step Guide

  • FAQs About Air Drying Curly Hair

How to Air Dry Naturally Curly Hair Without Frizz

The key to air drying curly hair without frizz is to keep your curls defined and hydrated. This means using the right products and techniques to enhance your natural curl pattern. Here are some steps to follow:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and frizz. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair while it's still wet.

Step 2: Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to add moisture and prevent frizz. Make sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends.

Step 3: Use a Curl Enhancing Product

Apply a curl enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel, to your hair. This will help define your curls and keep them in place as they dry.

Step 4: Plop Your Hair

Plopping is a technique that involves wrapping your hair in a t-shirt or microfiber towel to help enhance your natural curl pattern. Here's how to do it:

  1. Flip your head upside down and place a t-shirt or microfiber towel on a flat surface.
  2. Lower your head onto the center of the t-shirt or towel, so that your hair is hanging over your face.
  3. Wrap the sides of the t-shirt or towel around your head, making sure to tuck in any loose ends.
  4. Leave your hair wrapped for 20-30 minutes.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Air Dry

After plopping, let your hair air dry completely. Avoid touching your hair while it's drying, as this can cause frizz. Once your hair is dry, gently scrunch your curls to release any crunchiness from the curl enhancing product.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to air drying your curly hair without frizz:

  1. Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair.
  3. Apply a curl enhancing product, such as a curl cream or gel.
  4. Plop your hair for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Let your hair air dry completely.
  6. Gently scrunch your curls to release any crunchiness from the curl enhancing product.

Tips for Air Drying Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for air drying curly hair without frizz:

  • Avoid touching your hair while it's drying, as this can cause frizz.
  • Use a microfiber towel or t-shirt to dry your hair instead of a regular towel, as it's gentler on your curls.
  • Avoid brushing your hair while it's wet, as this can cause breakage and frizz.
  • Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to dry your roots if you're in a hurry.

FAQs About Air Drying Curly Hair

Can I air dry my curly hair overnight?

Yes, you can air dry your curly hair overnight. Just make sure to protect your hair with a silk scarf or bonnet while you sleep to prevent frizz.

Can I use a hair dryer to speed up the air drying process?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment to speed up the air drying process. Just make sure to use a low heat setting and avoid touching your hair with the dryer nozzle to prevent frizz.

How often should I wash my curly hair?

It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Some people with curly hair can go several days without washing, while others may need to wash every other day. Experiment to find what works best for your hair.

Hair Care Products for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care products that can help enhance your natural curl pattern and prevent frizz:

  • Sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner
  • Leave-in conditioner
  • Curl enhancing cream or gel
  • Microfiber towel or t-shirt
  • Silk scarf or bonnet
  • Diffuser attachment for hair dryer

By following these tips and using the right products, you can air dry your curly hair without frizz. Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair type and curl pattern. With a little patience and practice, you'll have beautiful, frizz-free curls in no time!
