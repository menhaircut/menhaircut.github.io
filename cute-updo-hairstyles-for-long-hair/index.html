Cute Updo Hairstyles For Long Hair

Written by Petter Sep 19, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long hair offers endless possibilities when it comes to styling. Whether you're attending a wedding, a prom, or just want to switch up your everyday look, updos are a great option. In this post, we'll introduce you to some cute updo hairstyles for long hair. Here's what we'll cover:

Table of Contents

  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to

Before we dive into the specific hairstyles, let's go over some general tips for creating updos:

  • Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is too greasy or damp, it will be harder to work with.
  • Use hair products to help your hair hold its shape. Hairspray, mousse, and texturizing spray are all good options.
  • Invest in high-quality hair accessories. Bobby pins, hair ties, and clips can all make or break your updo.
  • Don't be afraid to experiment! Updos are all about creativity, so feel free to try new things and see what works best for you.

Step by Step

Now, let's take a look at some cute updo hairstyles for long hair:

1. Braided Updo

To create this style:

  1. Part your hair down the middle and braid each section.
  2. Twist the braids into a bun at the back of your head and secure with pins.
  3. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold.

2. Messy Bun

This style is perfect for a casual day out. Here's how to create it:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with pins.
  3. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy look.

3. Half-Up Half-Down

This style is great for those who want to show off their long locks. Here's how to do it:

  1. Section off the top half of your hair and tie it into a ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail into a bun and secure with pins.
  3. Leave the bottom half of your hair loose.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when creating updos:

  • If you have thin hair, try teasing it before creating your updo to add volume.
  • If you have curly hair, consider straightening it before creating your updo to make it easier to work with.
  • If you're attending a formal event, consider adding accessories like flowers or jewels to your updo.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about updos for long hair:

What if my hair is too short for an updo?

If your hair is too short for an updo, consider adding extensions to create more length.

How can I make my updo last all day?

Use plenty of hairspray and bobby pins to help your updo hold its shape. You can also try using a texturizing spray to add grip.

Hair Care

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair in order to create beautiful updos. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking fresh and prevent split ends.

With these cute updo hairstyles for long hair, you'll never run out of inspiration for your next event or everyday look. Remember to experiment, use high-quality accessories, and take good care of your hair for the best results!
