Male Short Spiky Haircuts A Guide For 2023

Written by Daniel Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle to rock in 2023? Short spiky haircuts are a popular choice for men who want to look stylish and edgy. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about male short spiky haircuts, including how to get the look, tips for styling and hair care, and answers to common questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Get a Short Spiky Haircut
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Spiky Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Short Spiky Haircut

How to Get a Short Spiky Haircut

The first step in getting a short spiky haircut is to find a barber or hairstylist who specializes in this type of cut. They will be able to guide you on the best style for your face shape and hair type.

When you're at the salon, show your hairstylist a photo of the style you're interested in. This will help them understand what you're looking for and give them a visual reference to work from.

Some popular short spiky haircut styles for men include the textured spiky crop, the messy spiky fringe, and the high and tight spiky fade.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Spiky Hair

Once you have your short spiky haircut, it's important to know how to style it properly. Here's a step-by-step guide to achieve the perfect spiky look:

  1. Start with clean, damp hair.
  2. Apply a small amount of styling product (such as hair wax or pomade) to your palms and rub them together to warm up the product.
  3. Use your hands to work the product through your hair, focusing on the roots and tips.
  4. Use a comb or your fingers to create spikes by lifting sections of hair and pointing them upwards.
  5. Continue styling until you achieve your desired look.
  6. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your spikes in place.

Remember, less is more when it comes to styling product. Start with a small amount and add more as needed.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Spiky Haircut

To keep your short spiky haircut looking sharp, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as this can weigh your hair down and make it look greasy.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find the look that suits you best.

Frequently Asked Questions About Short Spiky Haircuts

Here are some common questions about short spiky haircuts:

Q: Is a short spiky haircut suitable for all hair types?

A: Short spiky haircuts can work for most hair types, but they tend to look best on thick, straight hair. If you have curly or wavy hair, you may need to use more styling product to achieve the spiky look.

Q: How often should I wash my short spiky hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils. If you have particularly oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Q: Can I style my short spiky hair without using product?

A: It's possible to style your short spiky hair without using product, but it may not hold its shape as well. Using a small amount of styling product can help your spikes stay in place all day.

Hair Care for Short Spiky Hair

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and stylish short spiky haircut. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools (such as hair straighteners or curling irons) too frequently, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair hydrated and shiny.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a swim cap.

With the right care and styling techniques, your short spiky haircut can be a versatile and edgy look that's perfect for 2023.
