Which Haircut Suits For Round Face Female With Long Hair

Written by Petter Nov 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect haircut that suits your round face and long hair? If yes, then you are in the right place. With so many hairstyles to choose from, it can be confusing to find the right one for your face shape. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this blog post, we will discuss which haircut suits for round face female with long hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Description

Round faces have a circular shape with full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal of a haircut for a round face is to make the face appear slimmer and elongated. Long hair is a great option for round faces as it can help to create the illusion of a more oval face shape. However, not all long hairstyles will look good on round faces. The key is to choose a haircut that will flatter your face shape and enhance your best features.

How to

The first step in choosing the right haircut for a round face with long hair is to determine your face shape. To do this, take a tape measure and measure the widest point of your face, which is usually across the cheeks. Then, measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin. If the two measurements are almost equal, you have a round face shape.

Consider your Hair Texture

When choosing a haircut for a round face, it's important to consider your hair texture. If you have fine hair, a layered cut can add volume and movement to your hair. If you have thick hair, a blunt cut can help to make your hair look more manageable.

Choose the Right Length

Long hair is a great option for round faces, but the length of your hair is also important. Hair that is too long can make your face appear wider, while hair that is too short can make your face appear rounder. The ideal length for long hair on a round face is between the collarbone and the chest.

Step by Step

Here are the steps to follow when choosing a haircut for a round face with long hair:

  1. Determine your face shape
  2. Consider your hair texture
  3. Choose the right length
  4. Choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape
  5. Consult with a professional stylist

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for a round face with long hair:

  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at the chin or cheekbone, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Layers can help to create the illusion of a more oval face shape.
  • Consider adding side-swept bangs to your haircut, as they can help to slim down your face.
  • Avoid center-parted hairstyles, as they can make your face appear wider.

FAQs

Q. Can I wear my hair straight if I have a round face?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair straight if you have a round face. However, it's important to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features.

Q. Should I avoid short haircuts if I have a round face?

A. Short haircuts can look great on round faces, but it's important to choose the right one. A pixie cut or a bob with layers can be flattering on a round face.

Q. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A. Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail if you have a round face. However, avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as it can make your face appear wider.

Hair Care

Once you've chosen the right haircut for your round face with long hair, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type and texture.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as it can cause damage.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Now that you know which haircut suits for round face female with long hair, you can choose the perfect hairstyle that flatters your face shape and enhances your best features. Remember to choose a hairstyle that you feel confident and comfortable in. Happy hairstyling!
