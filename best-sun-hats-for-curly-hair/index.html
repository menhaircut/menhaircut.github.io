The Best Sun Hats For Curly Hair Protecting Your Curls From The Sun

Written by Luffy Sep 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

As summer approaches, it's important to think about protecting your hair from the sun's harmful rays. For those with curly hair, this can be especially challenging. Not only do you have to worry about protecting your hair from damage, but you also have to worry about maintaining your curl pattern. That's why we've put together this guide to the best sun hats for curly hair.

How to Choose the Right Sun Hat for Your Curly Hair

When it comes to choosing a sun hat for your curly hair, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to look for a hat that provides ample shade and coverage. This will help protect your hair and scalp from the sun's harmful rays. Additionally, you want to look for a hat that won't flatten or mess up your curls. Here are a few tips for choosing the right sun hat for your curly hair:

Tip #1: Look for a hat with a wide brim.

A wide-brimmed hat will provide more shade and coverage than a hat with a smaller brim. This will help protect your hair and scalp from the sun's harmful rays. Additionally, a wide-brimmed hat will help keep your curls looking great by preventing them from getting flattened or messed up.

Tip #2: Choose a hat made from a breathable material.

When it comes to sun hats for curly hair, you want to choose a hat made from a breathable material. This will help keep your head cool and prevent your hair from getting too hot and frizzy. Some great materials to look for include cotton, straw, and raffia.

Tip #3: Consider a hat with a UPF rating.

UPF stands for Ultraviolet Protection Factor. This rating system measures the amount of UV radiation that can penetrate a fabric and reach your skin. The higher the UPF rating, the better the protection. When looking for a sun hat for your curly hair, consider one with a UPF rating of 50 or higher.

The Best Sun Hats for Curly Hair

Now that you know what to look for in a sun hat for your curly hair, let's take a look at some of the best options on the market:

1. Wide-Brimmed Straw Hat

A wide-brimmed straw hat is a classic choice for protecting your curly hair from the sun. It provides ample shade and coverage, while also allowing your hair to breathe. Look for a hat with a loose weave to prevent your curls from getting flattened or messed up.

2. Cotton Bucket Hat

A cotton bucket hat is a great option for those with curly hair. It's made from a breathable material that won't cause your hair to overheat or get frizzy. Additionally, the bucket shape provides ample shade and coverage, while also allowing your curls to maintain their shape.

3. UPF Sun Hat

If you're looking for maximum sun protection, consider a sun hat with a UPF rating. This will provide the best protection from the sun's harmful rays, while also keeping your curls looking great. Look for a hat with a wide brim and a breathable material for best results.

Hair Care Tips for Sun Protection

In addition to wearing a sun hat, there are a few other hair care tips you can follow to protect your curls from the sun:

Tip #1: Use a leave-in conditioner with UV protection.

A leave-in conditioner with UV protection will help protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays. Apply it before heading out into the sun for best results.

Tip #2: Avoid heat styling.

Heat styling can cause damage to your hair, especially when it's exposed to the sun. If possible, avoid using hot tools during the summer months.

Tip #3: Wear a hat or scarf.

In addition to wearing a sun hat, you can also wear a scarf or bandana to protect your hair from the sun. Simply wrap it around your head and tie it in place.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can wearing a sun hat prevent hair damage?

A: Yes, wearing a sun hat can help prevent hair damage by protecting your hair and scalp from the sun's harmful rays.

Q: Will wearing a sun hat cause my curls to flatten?

A: It's possible for a sun hat to cause your curls to flatten, but it depends on the style of hat you choose. Look for a hat with a wide brim and a loose weave to prevent your curls from getting flattened.

Q: How often should I apply leave-in conditioner with UV protection?

A: It's a good idea to apply leave-in conditioner with UV protection every time you wash your hair or before heading out into the sun.

Conclusion

Protecting your curly hair from the sun doesn't have to be a challenge. By choosing the right sun hat and following a few simple hair care tips, you can keep your curls looking great all summer long. So, go ahead and enjoy the sun, knowing that your hair is protected!
