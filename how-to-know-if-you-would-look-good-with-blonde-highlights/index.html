How To Know If You Would Look Good With Blonde Highlights A Guide

Blonde highlights can be a great way to add dimension and texture to your hair. However, not everyone can pull off this look. If you're thinking about getting blonde highlights, but you're not sure if it's the right choice for you, keep reading. In this article, we'll give you some tips on how to know if you would look good with blonde highlights.

  • How to Know If You Would Look Good with Blonde Highlights

How to Know If You Would Look Good with Blonde Highlights

If you're considering getting blonde highlights, there are a few things you should keep in mind. Here's how to know if you would look good with this hair color:

1. Consider Your Skin Tone

If you have a warm skin tone, you may look better with honey or caramel blonde highlights. If you have a cool skin tone, ash blonde highlights may be a better choice. If you're not sure what your skin tone is, you can do a simple test. Look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue or purple, you have a cool skin tone. If they appear green, you have a warm skin tone.

2. Think about Your Natural Hair Color

If you have dark hair, you may need to bleach your hair before getting blonde highlights. This can be damaging to your hair, so it's important to consider the health of your hair before making this decision. If you have light hair, you may be able to get away with just adding some blonde highlights without bleaching your hair.

3. Consider Your Lifestyle

If you have a busy lifestyle, you may not have the time or energy to maintain blonde highlights. This hair color requires regular touch-ups to keep it looking fresh. If you're not willing to commit to this, you may want to consider a different hair color.

4. Look at Pictures

If you're still not sure if blonde highlights would look good on you, look at pictures of people with similar skin tones and hair colors. This can give you an idea of what the final result may look like.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you've decided that you want to get blonde highlights, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve this look:

1. Choose Your Shade

As we mentioned earlier, there are different shades of blonde that you can choose from. Consider your skin tone and natural hair color when choosing your shade.

2. Find a Professional Stylist

Unless you have experience coloring your own hair, it's best to leave this task to a professional stylist. Look for someone who has experience with blonde highlights.

3. Schedule a Consultation

Before your appointment, schedule a consultation with your stylist. This will give you the opportunity to discuss your desired look and ask any questions you may have.

4. Prepare Your Hair

Before your appointment, wash your hair and avoid using any styling products. This will make it easier for your stylist to work with your hair.

5. Sit Back and Relax

Your stylist will apply the bleach or dye to your hair, and then wrap it in foil to process. This can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the desired result.

6. Rinse and Style

Once the bleach or dye has processed, your stylist will rinse your hair and style it. They may also recommend products to help maintain your new look.

Tips for Getting Blonde Highlights

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your blonde highlights:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to help maintain your color.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible, as they can damage your hair and fade your color.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protective spray.

FAQ

Q: Will blonde highlights work on naturally curly hair?

A: Yes, blonde highlights can look great on curly hair. However, it's important to find a stylist who has experience working with curly hair.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my blonde highlights?

A: This depends on your hair growth and how noticeable the roots are. You may need to touch up your highlights every 6-8 weeks.

Q: Can I get blonde highlights if I have gray hair?

A: Yes, but you may need to use a toner to prevent your hair from looking yellow.

Hair Care Tips for Blonde Highlights

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your blonde highlights looking their best:

  • Avoid washing your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a purple shampoo to help counteract any brassiness in your hair.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this can dry out your hair and scalp.
  • Consider using a hair mask once a week to help nourish your hair.

Now that you know how to know if you would look good with blonde highlights, you can make an informed decision about whether or not to try this hair color. Remember to choose a shade that complements your skin tone and natural hair color, and to take good care of your hair to keep it looking its best.
