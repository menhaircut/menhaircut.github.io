What Is The Best Thickening Shampoo Uk In 2023

If you're looking for a way to add volume and thickness to your hair, a good thickening shampoo can work wonders. But with so many options on the market, how do you know which one to choose? In this article, we'll explore the best thickening shampoos available in the UK in 2023, and provide you with tips on how to choose the right one for your hair type and needs.

Why Use a Thickening Shampoo?

Thickening shampoos are designed to add body and volume to your hair. They work by coating the hair strands with ingredients like proteins and polymers that make the hair appear thicker and fuller. Thickening shampoos can be especially helpful for those with fine or thin hair, as they can make hair look and feel more voluminous.

How to Choose the Right Thickening Shampoo

When choosing a thickening shampoo, there are a few things to consider:

Hair Type

Thickening shampoos may work differently depending on your hair type. If you have fine or thin hair, look for a shampoo that is specifically formulated for your hair type.

Ingredients

Look for shampoos that contain ingredients like biotin, keratin, and amino acids, which can help strengthen and thicken hair. Avoid shampoos that contain sulfates, as these can strip the hair of its natural oils and cause dryness.

Brand Reputation

Choose a brand with a good reputation for producing high-quality hair care products. Look for reviews and recommendations from other customers to help you make an informed decision.

Top Thickening Shampoos in the UK

Here are some of the best thickening shampoos available in the UK in 2023:

1. Nioxin Cleanser Shampoo

Nioxin is a well-known brand that specializes in hair care products for thinning hair. Their Cleanser Shampoo is designed to remove sebum and other buildup from the scalp, while also adding volume and thickness to the hair. It contains ingredients like biotin, niacin, and peppermint oil, which help to stimulate the scalp and promote healthy hair growth.

2. Redken High Rise Volume Shampoo

Redken's High Rise Volume Shampoo is formulated to add body and lift to fine or flat hair. It contains a blend of filloxane, silicone polymers, and proteins, which work together to thicken and strengthen hair. It also has a pleasant scent and leaves hair feeling clean and refreshed.

3. L'Oreal Paris Elvive Fibrology Thickening Shampoo

L'Oreal Paris Elvive Fibrology Thickening Shampoo is a budget-friendly option that delivers impressive results. It contains filloxane, which penetrates the hair shaft to plump up each strand and create a fuller, thicker look. It also has a pleasant scent and is gentle enough to use every day.

Tips for Using Thickening Shampoo

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your thickening shampoo:

1. Use a Small Amount

Thickening shampoos are often more concentrated than regular shampoos, so you don't need to use as much. Use a nickel-sized amount for short hair, and a quarter-sized amount for longer hair.

2. Massage into Scalp

Massage the shampoo into your scalp for at least 30 seconds to help stimulate blood flow and promote healthy hair growth.

3. Rinse Thoroughly

Rinse your hair thoroughly to remove all traces of shampoo. Residue can weigh down the hair and make it look limp and flat.

FAQs

Q: Can thickening shampoo make hair grow faster?

A: While thickening shampoos can help to make hair appear thicker and fuller, they do not actually make hair grow faster. To promote healthy hair growth, focus on eating a balanced diet, staying hydrated, and getting plenty of rest.

Q: Can thickening shampoo cause hair loss?

A: No, thickening shampoo does not cause hair loss. In fact, many thickening shampoos contain ingredients that can help to strengthen hair and reduce breakage.

Hair Care for Thicker, Fuller Hair

Using a thickening shampoo is just one step in achieving thicker, fuller hair. Here are some other tips to help you get the most out of your hair care routine:

1. Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

After washing your hair, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle and distribute the conditioner throughout your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

2. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools like flat irons and curling wands can damage the hair and make it look limp and lifeless. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using heat-free styling methods like braids or buns.

3. Trim Regularly

Regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Aim to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking its best.

Conclusion

Choosing the best thickening shampoo for your hair type and needs can be a bit overwhelming, but with a little research and experimentation, you'll be able to find the perfect product to help you achieve thicker, fuller hair. Remember to look for shampoos that contain nourishing ingredients like biotin and keratin, and to use them in conjunction with a healthy hair care routine for best results.
