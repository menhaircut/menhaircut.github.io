Will My Perm Get Curlier After I Wash It - Tips and FAQ

Have you recently gotten a perm and wondering if your hair will get curlier after washing it? You're not alone! Many people have this question, and it's essential to understand how perms work and how to care for them properly. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about how to care for your perm and whether it will get curlier after washing it.

Table of Contents

  • How to Care for Your Perm
  • Will My Perm Get Curlier After I Wash It?
  • Tips for Caring for Your Perm
  • FAQ

How to Care for Your Perm

If you want your perm to last longer and look better, it's essential to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to help you care for your perm:

  1. Wait for at least 48 hours before washing your hair after a perm.
  2. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for permed hair.
  3. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  4. Avoid blow-drying your hair, as it can damage the curls.
  5. Avoid using styling products that contain alcohol, as they can dry out your hair.

By following these tips, you can help your perm last longer and look better.

Will My Perm Get Curlier After I Wash It?

The answer to this question is no. Your perm will not get curlier after washing it. The curls you see after your perm are the curls you'll have until your hair grows out or until you get another perm. Washing your hair will not change the structure of the curls.

However, it's important to note that if you don't take care of your perm properly, the curls can become looser over time. This can happen if you use harsh shampoos or conditioners, blow-dry your hair too often, or use styling products that contain alcohol. So, it's essential to follow the tips we mentioned earlier to keep your curls looking their best.

Tips for Caring for Your Perm

Here are some additional tips for caring for your perm:

  1. Avoid brushing your hair too often, as it can loosen the curls.
  2. Avoid using hair elastics that can pull on your curls.
  3. Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair gently.
  4. Avoid exposing your hair to direct sunlight for long periods, as it can damage the curls.

By following these tips, you can help your perm last longer and look better.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about perms:

  1. How long does a perm last? A perm can last for several months, depending on how well you care for it.
  2. Can I swim with a perm? Yes, you can swim with a perm, but it's important to wear a swim cap to protect your curls from chlorine and saltwater.
  3. Can I color my hair after getting a perm? It's best to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair.

Hair Care

Whether you have a perm or not, it's important to take care of your hair properly. Here are some tips for healthy hair:

  1. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Avoid using hot water when washing your hair.
  3. Avoid using styling products that contain alcohol.
  4. Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  5. Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.

By following these tips, you can help keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

Conclusion

In conclusion, your perm will not get curlier after washing it. However, it's important to take care of your perm properly to keep the curls looking their best. By following the tips we mentioned in this article, you can help your perm last longer and look better. If you have any other questions about perms or hair care, feel free to consult with your stylist.
