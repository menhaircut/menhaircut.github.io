Hairstyles For Oval Face And Big Nose Tips Tricks And Ideas

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your oval face and big nose? Look no further because we have got you covered. In this article, we will guide you through various hairstyles that complement your facial features and bring out your natural beauty. Let's dive in!

Description

Oval face shape is considered to be the most versatile of all. It means that you can pull off almost any hairstyle with ease. However, if you have a big nose, it can be a bit challenging to find the right hairstyle that balances out your facial features. The key is to choose hairstyles that minimize the attention on your nose and draw attention to other features of your face.

How to Choose Hairstyles for Oval Face and Big Nose

When choosing a hairstyle, it is important to keep in mind the shape of your face and the size of your nose. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add volume to the crown area of your head. This will help balance out the size of your nose.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek and straight as they can make your nose appear larger.
  • Choose hairstyles that frame your face such as layers or bangs. This will draw attention away from your nose.
  • Consider hairstyles that have a side part rather than a center part. This will help balance out the size of your nose.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and see what works for you. Don't be afraid to try something new.

Step-by-Step Guide to Hairstyles for Oval Face and Big Nose

Here are some hairstyle ideas that you can try:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a short hairstyle that is perfect for oval face shapes. It is low maintenance and can be styled in various ways. To balance out your big nose, opt for a pixie cut that has volume on the crown area of your head. You can also add some side-swept bangs to frame your face.

2. Bob

A bob is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for oval face shapes. It is versatile and can be styled in many ways. To balance out your big nose, opt for a bob that has layers and volume on the crown area of your head. You can also add some side-swept bangs to frame your face.

3. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are a great way to frame your face and draw attention away from your nose. They work best with mid-length to long hair. To balance out your big nose, opt for side-swept bangs that are longer on one side and shorter on the other. This will create an illusion of a slimmer nose.

4. Half-Up Half-Down

A half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for oval face shapes. It is easy to do and looks great for any occasion. To balance out your big nose, opt for a half-up half-down hairstyle that has volume on the crown area of your head. You can also add some side-swept bangs to frame your face.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some additional tips and tricks to help you choose the perfect hairstyle:

  • Experiment with different hair colors. Darker shades can make your nose appear smaller.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or pulled back as they can make your nose appear larger.
  • Accessorize your hair with headbands, scarves, or hats to draw attention away from your nose.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their expert opinion on what hairstyle would work best for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some frequently asked questions about hairstyles for oval face and big nose:

1. Can I wear a center part with an oval face and big nose?

A center part can make your nose appear longer. It is best to opt for a side part instead.

2. What hair length is best for oval face and big nose?

Any hair length can work for oval face and big nose. It is more about the style than the length.

3. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail with an oval face and big nose?

A high ponytail can make your nose appear larger. It is best to opt for a low ponytail or a messy bun.

Hair Care for Oval Face and Big Nose

It is important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and beautiful. Here are some tips to help you care for your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair serum with SPF.

Now that you have a better understanding of hairstyles for oval face and big nose, it's time to experiment and find the perfect hairstyle that works for you. Remember that confidence is key, and any hairstyle can look great if you wear it with confidence. Happy styling!
