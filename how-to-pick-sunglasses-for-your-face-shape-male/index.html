How To Pick Sunglasses For Your Face Shape Male

If you're a male looking for the perfect sunglasses to complement your face shape, you've come to the right place. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect pair. But don't worry, we've got you covered with this guide on how to pick sunglasses for your face shape male.

How to pick sunglasses for your face shape male

The key to finding the perfect sunglasses is to choose a pair that complements your face shape. Here are some tips to help you pick sunglasses for your face shape:

1. Oval face shape

If you have an oval face shape, you're in luck because you can pretty much wear any style of sunglasses. Aviators, wayfarers, round and rectangular frames all look great on an oval face shape.

2. Round face shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to choose sunglasses that will help elongate your face. Look for rectangular or square frames, as well as wayfarers. Avoid round frames, as they will only make your face look rounder.

3. Square face shape

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften your features a bit. Look for round or oval frames, as well as aviators. Avoid square frames, as they will only make your face look more angular.

4. Heart face shape

If you have a heart face shape, you want to balance out your forehead and jawline. Look for frames that are wider at the top and narrow at the bottom, such as aviators or wayfarers. Avoid frames that are wider at the bottom, as they will only draw attention to your chin.

Step by step guide

Here's a step by step guide to help you pick the perfect sunglasses:

1. Determine your face shape

Before you can pick the perfect sunglasses, you need to determine your face shape. Take a look at yourself in the mirror and determine whether your face is oval, round, square, or heart-shaped.

2. Choose a frame style that complements your face shape

Once you've determined your face shape, choose a frame style that complements it. Refer to the tips above to help you choose the perfect frame style.

3. Consider the size of the frames

The size of the frames is also important. Make sure the frames are proportional to your face. If you have a smaller face, choose smaller frames, and if you have a larger face, choose larger frames.

4. Consider the color of the frames

The color of the frames is also important. Choose a color that complements your skin tone and hair color. If you're unsure, black or tortoise shell frames are always a safe bet.

Tips for picking the perfect sunglasses

Here are some additional tips to help you pick the perfect sunglasses:

  • Try on multiple pairs before making a decision.
  • Consider polarized lenses for better sun protection.
  • Invest in a quality pair of sunglasses that will last you for years to come.
  • Don't be afraid to try something new and different.

FAQs

What if I have a combination of face shapes?

If you have a combination of face shapes, choose a frame style that complements the dominant shape.

What if I have a small or large head?

If you have a small head, choose smaller frames, and if you have a large head, choose larger frames.

How to take care of your sunglasses

To ensure your sunglasses last you for years to come, here are some tips on how to take care of them:

  • Store your sunglasses in a case when not in use.
  • Clean your sunglasses with a microfiber cloth to avoid scratches.
  • Avoid leaving your sunglasses in a hot car or in direct sunlight.
  • Don't wear your sunglasses on top of your head as it can stretch out the frames.

Now that you know how to pick sunglasses for your face shape male, go out and find the perfect pair to complement your style!
