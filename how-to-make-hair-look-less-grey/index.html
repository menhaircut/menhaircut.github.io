How To Make Hair Look Less Grey Tips And Tricks

Written by Oliver Jun 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Grey hair can be a natural part of the aging process, but that doesn’t mean you have to live with it. There are several ways to make your hair look less grey, whether you want to embrace it or conceal it. In this article, we’ll share some tips and tricks to help you achieve the look you want.

How to

1. Use Hair Dye

The most obvious way to make your hair look less grey is to dye it. You can visit a salon or do it yourself at home. There are many different types of hair dye available, including permanent, semi-permanent, and temporary options. Choose a shade that works well with your skin tone and natural hair color.

2. Add Highlights

Another way to make your hair look less grey is to add highlights. This can help to blend the grey strands with your natural hair color, creating a more subtle look. You can choose to have highlights added at a salon or do it yourself using a home highlighting kit.

3. Use Hair Mascara

If you’re looking for a temporary solution, hair mascara can be a great option. This is a type of temporary hair dye that you can apply to your hair to cover up grey strands. It’s easy to use and can be washed out with shampoo.

4. Try Hair Powder

Hair powder is another temporary solution that can help to cover up grey roots. This is a type of powder that you apply to your scalp and hair to create the illusion of fuller, thicker hair. It can be a great way to conceal grey roots between hair dye appointments.

Step by Step

1. Choose Your Hair Dye

If you decide to use hair dye to make your hair look less grey, the first step is to choose the right product. Consider your natural hair color, skin tone, and the level of maintenance you’re willing to commit to.

2. Prepare Your Hair

Before you apply hair dye, it’s important to prepare your hair. This involves washing and drying it thoroughly. You may also need to apply a protective barrier cream or petroleum jelly to your hairline and ears to prevent staining.

3. Apply Hair Dye

Follow the instructions on your hair dye product to apply it to your hair. Be sure to apply it evenly and thoroughly, and leave it on for the recommended amount of time. Rinse it out thoroughly and condition your hair afterwards.

4. Maintain Your Hair

Once you’ve dyed your hair, it’s important to maintain it to keep it looking its best. Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner, and avoid excessive heat styling or exposure to the sun.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair look less grey:

  • Use a toning shampoo to neutralize any brassy tones in your hair
  • Consider getting a professional haircut or style to complement your new hair color
  • Experiment with different hair accessories to add some variety to your look

FAQ

Q: Can I dye my hair if it’s already been dyed?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair if it’s already been dyed. However, you may need to take extra precautions to avoid damaging your hair or altering the color too drastically.

Q: Will dyeing my hair damage it?

A: Dyeing your hair can cause some damage, especially if you use harsh chemicals or bleach. However, using a gentle hair dye and following proper hair care practices can help to minimize damage and maintain healthy, vibrant hair.

Q: Can I use hair dye on my beard or mustache?

A: Yes, you can use hair dye on your beard or mustache. However, be sure to choose a product that’s specifically designed for facial hair, as these areas can be more sensitive than the scalp.

Hair Care

Regardless of whether you choose to dye your hair or embrace your natural grey, it’s important to take good care of your hair. This includes washing and conditioning it regularly, using heat protectant products when styling, and avoiding harsh chemicals or treatments that can cause damage.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your hair look less grey and feel more confident in your appearance. Whether you opt for a bold new hair color or a subtle, natural look, the choice is yours.
