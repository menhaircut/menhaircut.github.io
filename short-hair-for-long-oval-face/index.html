Short Hair For Long Oval Face Flattering Hairstyles For 2023

Written by Robby Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your long, oval face getting lost in your hair? Do you want to try something new and refreshing that will flatter your features? Look no further than short haircuts! In this article, we will go over everything you need to know about short hair for long oval faces, including how to choose the right cut, styling tips, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Choose the Right Cut
  2. Styling Tips
  3. Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair
  4. Hair Care for Short Hair
  5. FAQ

How to Choose the Right Cut

When it comes to short haircuts for long oval faces, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to choose a cut that will balance out the length of your face. This means avoiding styles that are too long, as they will only elongate your face even more. Instead, opt for cuts that are chin-length or above.

Another thing to consider is the shape of your face. Oval faces are versatile and can pull off a variety of styles, but you want to choose a cut that will highlight your best features. If you have high cheekbones, for example, you may want to go for a pixie cut that will draw attention to them.

Some great short haircuts for long oval faces include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob
  • Lob (long bob)
  • Asymmetrical cut
  • Side-swept bangs

Styling Tips

Once you've chosen your cut, it's time to start styling! Short haircuts are easy to maintain and style, but there are a few things to keep in mind to make sure your hair always looks its best.

First, invest in some good styling products. A lightweight mousse or gel can help add volume and texture to your hair, while a hairspray will keep your style in place all day long.

Second, don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques. You can try blow-drying your hair with a round brush for added volume, or using a straightener to create sleek, straight locks. Play around with different looks until you find the one that works best for you.

Step-by-Step Guide to Cutting Short Hair

If you're ready to take the plunge and cut your hair short, here's a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Section your hair into four parts: top, bottom, and two sides.
  3. Starting at the back of your head, use clippers to cut your hair to the desired length.
  4. Move to the sides and top of your head, using scissors to trim any longer pieces.
  5. Blend the layers together for a seamless look.
  6. Finish by using a razor to add texture and movement to your hair.

Hair Care for Short Hair

Short haircuts are low-maintenance, but that doesn't mean you can neglect your hair care routine. Here are a few tips to keep your short hair looking healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid drying it out.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.

FAQ

Here are some common questions about short haircuts for long oval faces:

Q: Will a short haircut make my face look too long?

A: No! As long as you choose the right cut, a short haircut can actually balance out the length of your face.

Q: Can I still wear my hair up with a short haircut?

A: Yes! Short haircuts can be styled in a variety of ways, including updos and half-up styles.

Q: Will a short haircut make me look older?

A: Not necessarily! Short haircuts can be youthful and fun, depending on the cut and styling.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a short haircut that flatters your long oval face and showcases your best features. Don't be afraid to try something new and experiment with different styles until you find the perfect one for you!
