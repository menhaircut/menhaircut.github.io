Best Hair For Rectangle Face A Comprehensive Guide

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that don't flatter your rectangle-shaped face? Don't worry; we're here to help you out. In this article, we'll be discussing the best hair for rectangle face shape and some tips on how to style it. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  2. How to Choose the Best Hair for Rectangle Face
  3. Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair

Description

A rectangle face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a square jawline. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of the same width. The right hairstyle can help balance out the proportions of your face and enhance your best features.

How to Choose the Best Hair for Rectangle Face

When choosing the best hair for rectangle face, you want to create an illusion of a shorter face. You can achieve this by adding volume on the sides and keeping the length above or below the chin. Here are some hairstyles that work well for rectangle face shapes:

Short Bob

A short bob that ends above the chin is perfect for a rectangle face shape. It adds volume on the sides and creates an illusion of a wider face. You can also add bangs to soften the forehead and balance out the proportions of your face.

Layered Hairstyles

Layered hairstyles work well for rectangle face shapes as they create movement and volume. You can opt for a long layered haircut that ends below the chin or a short layered bob. Both styles add volume on the sides and create an illusion of a wider face.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs are perfect for rectangle face shapes as they soften the forehead and create an illusion of a shorter face. You can combine side-swept bangs with any of the hairstyles mentioned above for a chic and trendy look.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Style Your Hair

Now that you've chosen the perfect hairstyle for your rectangle face shape, here's a step-by-step guide on how to style it:

Short Bob

  1. Wash your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to add volume to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume on the sides.
  3. Style your hair using a flat iron or curling iron to add texture.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Layered Hairstyles

  1. Wash your hair and apply a volumizing mousse to add volume to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to create volume on the sides.
  3. Use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture to your hair.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Wash your hair and blow-dry it using a round brush.
  2. Part your hair to the side and sweep the bangs to the side.
  3. Style your hair using a flat iron or curling iron to add texture.
  4. Finish off with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips and Tricks to Keep in Mind

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Avoid center parts as they emphasize the length of your face.
  • Don't add too much volume on top as it will make your face appear longer.
  • Keep your hair length above or below the chin to balance out the proportions of your face.
  • Opt for soft and wispy layers as sharp and angular cuts emphasize the squareness of your jawline.

Frequently Asked Questions

What hair color works best for rectangle face shapes?

Warm and natural hair colors such as caramel, honey, and golden blonde work best for rectangle face shapes. Avoid cool-toned colors such as ash blonde and platinum as they can make your face appear longer.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail?

Yes, you can wear your hair in a ponytail. A low ponytail that is not too tight works well for rectangle face shapes as it adds volume on the sides.

Should I avoid short haircuts?

No, you don't have to avoid short haircuts. Short haircuts that end above the chin work well for rectangle face shapes as they add volume on the sides and create an illusion of a wider face.

Hair Care Tips for Rectangle Face

Here are some hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends.

Now that you know the best hair for rectangle face shape and how to style it, it's time to try out some new hairstyles. Remember to keep these tips and tricks in mind and take good care of your hair. Happy hairstyling!
