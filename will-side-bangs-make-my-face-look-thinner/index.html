Will Side Bangs Make My Face Look Thinner

Written by Robby Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that will slim down your face? Side bangs may just be the solution you need. In this article, we'll explore whether or not side bangs will make your face look thinner, and provide tips for choosing and maintaining this hairstyle.

Description

Side bangs are a popular hairstyle where the hair is cut shorter on one side of the face, usually sweeping across the forehead. This style can be worn with a variety of hair lengths and textures, and can be customized to suit your face shape and personal style.

How To

Getting side bangs is easy, but it's important to choose the right length and shape to flatter your face. Here's how to get side bangs:

  1. Choose the length and shape of your bangs. Side bangs can be short, medium or long, and can be cut straight across or at an angle.
  2. Consult with a hairstylist. They can help you determine the best length and shape for your face shape and hair type.
  3. Get your bangs cut. Once you've chosen the length and shape of your bangs, your hairstylist will cut them according to your specifications.
  4. Style your bangs. Depending on your hair type and personal style, you can style your side bangs with a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron.

Step by Step

If you're unsure about whether or not side bangs will make your face look thinner, follow these steps to find out:

  1. Take a photo of yourself with your hair pulled back off your face.
  2. Print out the photo and draw a line down the center of your face.
  3. Draw in side bangs on one side of your face using a pencil or marker.
  4. Compare the two sides of your face. Do you notice a difference in the appearance of your face when you add side bangs?

Tips

Here are some tips for choosing and maintaining side bangs:

  • Choose the right length and shape for your face. Side bangs that are too short or too long can be unflattering.
  • Maintain your bangs with regular trims. If you let your bangs get too long, they can start to look messy and unflattering.
  • Style your bangs according to your hair type. If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten your bangs with a flat iron to keep them looking sleek.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about side bangs:

Will side bangs make my face look thinner?

Yes, side bangs can make your face look thinner by drawing attention away from the width of your face and towards your eyes.

What face shapes are best suited for side bangs?

Side bangs can be flattering on a variety of face shapes, but work particularly well on round or square faces.

How do I style my side bangs?

You can style your side bangs with a blow dryer, straightener, or curling iron, depending on your hair type and personal style.

Hair Care

In order to keep your side bangs looking their best, it's important to take good care of your hair. This includes:

  • Washing your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoiding excessive heat styling, which can damage your hair.
  • Using a heat protectant spray when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Getting regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.

Overall, side bangs can be a great way to slim down your face and add some interest to your hairstyle. By choosing the right length and shape for your face, and maintaining your bangs with regular trims and styling, you can enjoy this flattering hairstyle for years to come.
