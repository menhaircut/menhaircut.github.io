Best Haircuts For Round Face Over 60 A Guide To Flattering Styles

Written by Oliver Jun 13, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a new haircut that flatters your round face? Look no further! In this article, we'll guide you through the best haircuts for round faces over 60. We'll cover everything from how to choose the right style for your face shape to tips for maintaining healthy hair. So, let's get started!

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Round Face

When it comes to choosing a haircut for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, you want to create the illusion of length and height to balance out the roundness of your face. This can be achieved through layers and volume at the crown of your head. Second, you want to avoid styles that add width to your face, such as blunt bangs or one-length bobs. Instead, opt for side-swept bangs and layered cuts that frame your face.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Flattering Haircut

1. Start by finding inspiration photos of haircuts that you like. Look for styles that flatter round faces and are age-appropriate.

2. Book a consultation with a hairstylist who has experience working with mature clients and round face shapes.

3. During your consultation, discuss your hair texture, lifestyle, and personal preferences with your stylist. They can recommend a style that will work for you.

4. When you're happy with the proposed style, schedule your haircut appointment.

5. After your haircut, ask your stylist for tips on how to style and maintain your new look.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

2. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. If you must use them, apply a heat protectant spray first.

3. Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your haircut.

4. Consider using a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

FAQs

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face over 60?
A: The best haircut for a round face over 60 is a layered cut with volume at the crown and side-swept bangs.

Q: Can I still have long hair over 60 with a round face?
A: Yes, you can still have long hair over 60 with a round face. Just make sure to add layers and volume to create the illusion of length and height.

Q: Can I dye my hair if it's thinning?
A: Yes, you can dye your hair if it's thinning. However, it's important to use a gentle, ammonia-free dye to avoid further damage to your hair.

Hair Care: Dos and Don'ts

Do:
- Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair
- Wash your hair every 2-3 days
- Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage

Don't:
- Use hot water to wash your hair
- Brush your hair when it's wet
- Use harsh chemicals on your hair

Conclusion

Choosing a haircut for a round face over 60 doesn't have to be difficult. By following our guide, you'll be able to find a style that flatters your face shape and makes you feel confident. Remember to choose a style that adds height and length to your face, and to maintain healthy hair with regular trims and a good hair care routine. Happy styling!
