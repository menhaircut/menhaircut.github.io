How To Get Curly Hair With A Relaxer A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're tired of your straight hair and want to try out some curls, getting a relaxer might be a great option. Relaxers are chemicals that can straighten hair, but they can also be used to create curls. In this blog post, we'll show you how to get curly hair with a relaxer, step by step. We'll also provide some tips, answer frequently asked questions, and give you some advice on how to take care of your newly curly locks.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Get Curly Hair with a Relaxer

Getting curly hair with a relaxer is a process that involves several steps. Here are the basic steps:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Choose a relaxer that is specifically designed for curly hair. Look for a product that says "curly perm" or "curly relaxer" on the label.
  2. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or residue. This will help the relaxer to penetrate your hair more effectively.
  3. Apply a protective base to your scalp to prevent the relaxer from burning your skin. You can use petroleum jelly, a thick conditioner, or a special scalp protector that comes with the relaxer kit.
  4. Divide your hair into small sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to apply the relaxer evenly.
  5. Apply the relaxer to each section of hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends. Be sure to follow the instructions on the relaxer kit carefully, as the timing can vary depending on the product.
  6. Rinse the relaxer out of your hair thoroughly with warm water. Do not use hot water, as this can damage your hair.
  7. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to your hair to stop the chemical process of the relaxer. Leave the shampoo on for the recommended amount of time before rinsing it out.
  8. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair to moisturize and nourish your curls. Leave the conditioner on for several minutes before rinsing it out.
  9. Dry your hair gently with a towel, being careful not to rub it vigorously. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to enhance your curls.
  10. Style your curls as desired using a curling iron, rollers, or other styling tools.

Tips for Getting Curly Hair with a Relaxer

Here are some additional tips to help you get the best results from your curly hair relaxer:

  • Choose a relaxer that is gentle and moisturizing, especially if you have dry or damaged hair.
  • Do a patch test before applying the relaxer to your entire head to make sure you're not allergic to the product.
  • Be patient and follow the instructions carefully. Rushing the process or leaving the relaxer on for too long can damage your hair.
  • Avoid washing your hair for at least 24 hours after applying the relaxer to give your curls time to set.
  • Avoid using harsh hair products or styling tools that can damage your curls, such as straightening irons or brushes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people ask about getting curly hair with a relaxer:

  • Can I get curly hair with a relaxer if my hair is already straight? Yes, relaxers can be used to create curls even if your hair is naturally straight.
  • How long will my curls last after using a relaxer? The duration of your curls will depend on your hair type, the type of relaxer you use, and how well you take care of your hair. Typically, relaxed curls can last for several weeks to a few months.
  • Can I color my hair after using a relaxer? It's generally not recommended to color your hair immediately after using a relaxer, as this can damage your hair. It's best to wait a few days or weeks before coloring your hair.
  • Can I swim or go to the beach with relaxed curls? Yes, but be sure to protect your hair from the sun, salt water, and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

Hair Care Tips for Curly Hair

If you want to maintain your curly hair after using a relaxer, here are some additional tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your curls moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid brushing or combing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your curls when they're wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair to enhance your curls and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as this can damage your curls. If you must use a curling iron or flat iron, be sure to use a heat protectant spray.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your curls looking neat and healthy.

By following these tips and using a relaxer to create your curls, you can achieve a gorgeous, bouncy, and voluminous head of hair. Enjoy your new look!
