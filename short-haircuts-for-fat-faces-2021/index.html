Short Haircuts For Fat Faces 2021 A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right cut and styling, you can accentuate your features and create a flattering look. In this guide, we'll show you some of the best short haircuts for fat faces in 2021, along with tips on how to care for your hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Your Fat Face

When choosing a short haircut for a round or chubby face, you want to create the illusion of length and slimness. Here are some tips:

  • Choose a cut that adds height to the crown of your head, such as a pixie cut or a short bob with layers.
  • Avoid blunt cuts that end at your chin or cheekbones, which can make your face look wider.
  • Consider adding bangs to your hairstyle, which can help to elongate your face.
  • Opt for a side part rather than a center part, which can also help to create the illusion of length.

Short Haircuts for Fat Faces 2021:

Here are some of the best short haircuts for fat faces in 2021:

Pixie Cut:

A pixie cut is a short, cropped haircut that works well for round faces. It adds height to the top of your head and creates the illusion of length. You can also add texture and layers to the cut to create more volume.

Short Bob:

A short bob with layers is another great option for a round face. The layers add movement and volume to the hair, while the short length keeps the focus on your face. You can also add bangs to the cut to create more length.

Asymmetrical Bob:

An asymmetrical bob is a modern and edgy haircut that works well for round faces. The longer length on one side of the hair creates the illusion of length and slimness. You can also add layers to the cut to create more volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Haircuts for Fat Faces

Here's how to style your short haircut for a round face:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair upside down to add more height and volume to the crown of your head.
  3. Use a round brush to add more volume and movement to your hair.
  4. Apply a small amount of styling product, such as a texturizing spray or mousse, to add more texture and hold to your hair.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to set your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Haircuts for Fat Faces

Here are some tips for maintaining your short haircut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your haircut looking fresh and to prevent split ends.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools to prevent damage to your hair.

FAQs

What short haircut is best for a round face?

A pixie cut, short bob with layers, or asymmetrical bob are all great short haircuts for round faces.

Should I get bangs with my short haircut?

Bangs can help to elongate your face, so they can be a great addition to a short haircut for a round face. However, make sure to choose a style that works well with your face shape.

How do I style my short haircut?

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, and then blow-dry your hair upside down to add more volume to the crown of your head. Use a round brush to add more movement and volume to your hair, and finish with a styling product and hairspray to set your style in place.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircuts for Fat Faces

Here are some hair care tips for short haircuts:

  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent over-drying.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and avoid using a brush on wet hair to prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using too many styling products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

With the right short haircut and styling, you can flatter your round face and create a confident and stylish look. Use these tips and hair care advice to keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
