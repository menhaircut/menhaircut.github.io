Layered Haircuts For Over 60 A Guide To Looking And Feeling Fabulous

Written by Eveline Jul 30, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you over 60 and looking for a haircut that will make you look and feel fabulous? Look no further than layered haircuts! This versatile style can work for any hair type and face shape, and it's perfect for women who want to add volume and movement to their hair. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about layered haircuts for over 60, from how to get the look to tips for maintaining your new 'do.

How to Get Layered Haircuts for Over 60

The first step in getting a layered haircut is to find a stylist who specializes in this type of cut. Look for someone who has experience working with older women and understands the unique challenges that come with aging hair. Once you've found your stylist, it's time to discuss your options. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

Face Shape

The best layered haircuts for over 60 will complement your face shape. If you have a round face, for example, you may want to opt for longer layers that frame your face. If you have a square face, shorter layers can help soften your features. Your stylist can help you find the right length and style for your face shape.

Hair Type

Layered haircuts can work for any hair type, but the length and style will vary depending on whether you have thin, thick, curly, or straight hair. If you have thin hair, longer layers can help add volume and movement. If you have thick hair, shorter layers can help reduce bulk. Your stylist can help you find the right length and style for your hair type.

Lifestyle

When choosing a layered haircut, it's important to consider your lifestyle. If you're active and on the go, you may want a low-maintenance style that doesn't require a lot of styling. If you're more of a glamour girl, you may want a more high-maintenance style that requires regular styling and upkeep. Your stylist can help you find a style that fits your lifestyle.

Step by Step: How to Style Layered Haircuts for Over 60

Once you've gotten your layered haircut, it's time to style it! Here's a step-by-step guide to styling layered haircuts for over 60:

Step 1: Wash and Condition

Start by washing and conditioning your hair with products that are designed for your hair type. If you have thin hair, look for volumizing products. If you have thick hair, look for products that will help tame frizz and add shine.

Step 2: Towel Dry

Gently towel dry your hair, being careful not to rub it too hard. You want to avoid damaging your hair or causing frizz.

Step 3: Apply Styling Products

Apply any styling products you like, such as mousse, gel, or styling cream. These products can help add volume, texture, and hold to your hair.

Step 4: Blow Dry

Using a blow dryer and a round brush, dry your hair in sections. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends. This will help add volume and lift to your hair.

Step 5: Style

Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or flat iron to add texture and movement to your hair. You can also use a hair dryer and a diffuser attachment to create loose waves. Finish with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Layered Haircuts for Over 60

Now that you have your layered haircut, it's important to maintain it so it looks and feels its best. Here are a few tips for maintaining layered haircuts for over 60:

Regular Trims

To keep your layers looking fresh and healthy, it's important to get regular trims. Your stylist can recommend how often you should come in for a trim based on your hair type and style.

Use Quality Hair Products

Invest in high-quality hair products that are designed for your hair type and style. This will help keep your hair looking its best and prevent damage.

Avoid Over-Styling

Try to avoid over-styling your hair with heat tools, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you do use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray to help minimize damage.

Protect Your Hair from the Elements

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and other elements by wearing a hat or scarf. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy.

FAQ: Layered Haircuts for Over 60

Q: Will layered haircuts work for all hair types?

A: Yes, layered haircuts can work for any hair type, but the length and style will vary depending on your hair type.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It's important to get regular trims to maintain your layered haircut. Your stylist can recommend how often you should come in for a trim.

Q: Can I style my layered haircut at home?

A: Yes, you can style your layered haircut at home with the right products and tools. Your stylist can recommend products and techniques that will work best for your hair type and style.

Q: Will layered haircuts make my hair look thinner?

A: No, layered haircuts can actually help add volume and movement to your hair, making it look fuller and thicker.

Hair Care: Keeping Your Layered Haircut Healthy and Happy

Finally, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and happy. Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly with products that are designed for your hair type. This will help keep your hair clean and healthy.

Avoid Heat Damage

Avoid using heat tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage. If you do use heat tools, use a heat protectant spray to help minimize damage.

Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help keep your hair healthy and strong. Be sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to keep your hair and scalp hydrated. This will help prevent dryness and damage.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock your new layered haircut with confidence and style. Whether you're going for a casual, low-maintenance look or a more glamorous style, layered haircuts for over 60 can work for any woman who wants to look and feel fabulous. So why wait? Make an appointment with your stylist today and get ready to turn heads!
