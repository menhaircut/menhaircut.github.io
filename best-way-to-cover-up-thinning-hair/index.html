Best Way To Cover Up Thinning Hair In 2023

Written by Eveline Jun 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling with thinning hair and looking for ways to cover it up? You are not alone. Thinning hair can be a source of frustration and embarrassment for many people. Fortunately, there are several ways to address this issue and achieve a fuller look. In this article, we will discuss the best way to cover up thinning hair and provide tips on how to maintain healthy hair.

How to Cover Up Thinning Hair

There are several options for covering up thinning hair, including hair extensions, wigs, and hair fibers. The best option for you will depend on your budget, lifestyle, and personal preference. Here are some of the most popular ways to cover up thinning hair:

Hair Extensions

Hair extensions are a great way to add volume and length to your hair. They come in a variety of styles, lengths, and colors, so you can choose the best match for your hair. Hair extensions can be temporary or permanent and can be clipped, glued, or sewn onto your natural hair. They are a popular choice for people who want to add volume and length to their hair quickly and easily.

Wigs

Wigs are another option for covering up thinning hair. They come in a variety of styles, colors, and lengths, and can be made from synthetic or natural hair. Wigs can be a great option if you want to completely change your hairstyle or if you have a medical condition that causes hair loss. They can be expensive, but they are a long-lasting solution for covering up thinning hair.

Hair Fibers

Hair fibers are a quick and easy way to cover up thinning hair. They are made from natural fibers that attach to your existing hair to create a fuller, thicker look. Hair fibers are available in a variety of colors and can be applied using a special spray or applicator. They are a great option for people who want a temporary solution for covering up thinning hair.

Step by Step Guide to Cover Up Thinning Hair

Here are the steps to follow to cover up thinning hair:

Step 1: Choose the Best Option for You

Consider your budget, lifestyle, and personal preference when choosing the best option for covering up thinning hair.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Wash and dry your hair before applying any hair extensions, wigs, or hair fibers. This will help them adhere better to your hair.

Step 3: Apply Your Chosen Method

Follow the instructions for applying your chosen method. Hair extensions may need to be clipped, glued, or sewn onto your natural hair. Wigs may need to be adjusted to fit your head properly. Hair fibers may need to be applied using a special spray or applicator.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is covered, style it as desired. You can use a curling iron, straightener, or other styling tools to create the look you want.

Step 5: Maintain Your Hair

To maintain healthy hair, be sure to wash and condition it regularly. Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools too often.

Tips for Covering Up Thinning Hair

Here are some tips for covering up thinning hair:

Tip 1: Choose a Color Close to Your Natural Hair

Choosing a color that matches your natural hair will make your hair look more natural and blend in better.

Tip 2: Use Hair Fibers to Fill in Bald Spots

Hair fibers can be used to fill in bald spots and create a fuller look.

Tip 3: Invest in a High-Quality Wig

A high-quality wig will look more natural and last longer than a cheaper wig.

FAQs

Q: Can You Wear a Wig Every Day?

A: Yes, you can wear a wig every day, but it is important to take care of it properly to maintain its quality and longevity.

Q: How Long Do Hair Extensions Last?

A: Hair extensions can last anywhere from a few weeks to several months, depending on the type of extension and how well they are taken care of.

Q: Can You Style Hair Fibers?

A: Yes, you can style hair fibers using styling tools like a curling iron or straightener.

Hair Care for Thinning Hair

In addition to covering up thinning hair, it is important to take care of your hair to prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

Tip 1: Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach and relaxers on your hair, as they can damage your hair and cause it to break.

Tip 2: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp can stimulate hair growth and improve circulation to your hair follicles.

Tip 3: Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth. Be sure to eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein.

In conclusion, there are several ways to cover up thinning hair, including hair extensions, wigs, and hair fibers. The best option for you will depend on your budget, lifestyle, and personal preference. Be sure to take care of your hair to maintain healthy hair growth.
