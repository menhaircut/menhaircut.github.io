How To Make Your Hair Look Short With Ribbon

Written by Eveline Jul 14, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of your long hair and want to switch things up? Look no further than adding a ribbon to create the illusion of short hair. This simple hair hack can transform your look in just a few easy steps. Follow our guide to learn how to make your hair look short with ribbon.

How to

Before starting, gather the following materials:

  • Ribbon (any color or material will work)
  • Hair elastic
  • Bobby pins
  • Hairspray (optional)

Now, follow these simple steps:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Gather the top section of your hair and tie it into a ponytail with the hair elastic.
  3. Tie the ribbon around the base of the ponytail, making sure to leave enough ribbon on either side to create the illusion of short hair.
  4. Secure the ribbon in place with bobby pins.
  5. Take the remaining hair and tuck it underneath the ribbon to create the appearance of a shorter hair length.
  6. Secure the tucked hair with bobby pins.
  7. If desired, spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make sure your hair looks its best:

  • Experiment with different ribbon colors and materials to find what looks best on you.
  • To make the illusion of short hair even more convincing, you can use a hair straightener to straighten the tucked hair.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to ensure they blend in seamlessly.
  • If you have thick hair, consider using two hair elastics to secure the top section of your hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your hair look short with ribbon:

  • Can I use a different type of hair accessory instead of a ribbon?
  • Yes, you can use a headband, scarf, or any other type of hair accessory that you prefer.
  • Will this hairstyle work on all hair types?
  • Yes, this hairstyle can be done on all hair types, but it may look slightly different depending on your hair texture and length.
  • How long does this hairstyle take to do?
  • This hairstyle should only take a few minutes to do once you get the hang of it.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair even when using hair accessories. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools on your hair.
  • Don't use hair elastics that are too tight as they can cause hair breakage.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to make your hair look short with ribbon, give it a try and see how it can transform your look. This simple hair hack is perfect for those who want to switch up their hairstyle without committing to a drastic haircut. Have fun experimenting with different ribbon colors and materials to find what works best for you!
