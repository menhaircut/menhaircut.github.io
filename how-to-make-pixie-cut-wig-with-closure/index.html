How to Make Pixie Cut Wig with Closure

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

If you're looking for a stylish and low maintenance hairstyle, a pixie cut is a great option. However, not everyone is ready to commit to such a short haircut. That's where a pixie cut wig with closure comes in. This type of wig offers a natural-looking hairline and the convenience of a wig. In this article, we'll go over how to make a pixie cut wig with closure.

How to

Before we get started, here's what you'll need:

  • Pixie cut wig cap
  • Pixie cut wig closure
  • Wefts of hair extensions
  • Scissors
  • Thread and needle
  • Wig head
  • Wig clips
  • Wig stand

Step by Step

Now that you have all the necessary materials, let's start making the wig:

  1. Place the wig cap on the wig head and secure it with wig clips.
  2. Take the wefts of hair extensions and start sewing them onto the wig cap. Start from the back and work your way up to the front.
  3. Once you've sewn all the wefts onto the wig cap, place the wig closure on the top of the wig cap and sew it in place.
  4. Cut the excess lace off the closure and blend it with the rest of the wig.
  5. Try the wig on and make any necessary adjustments.

Tips

  • When sewing the wefts onto the wig cap, make sure to sew them close together to avoid gaps in the wig.
  • You can use a hair dryer or flat iron to style your wig, but be sure to use a low heat setting.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand to maintain its shape and prevent tangles.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about pixie cut wigs with closure:

How long does a pixie cut wig with closure last?
With proper care, a wig can last up to a year.
Can I wear my wig in the shower?
No, it's best to remove your wig before showering or swimming.
How do I wash my wig?
You can wash your wig with a mild shampoo and conditioner. Let it air dry on a wig stand.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut wig with closure:

  • Brush your wig gently with a wig brush or wide-tooth comb.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your wig.
  • Store your wig in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
