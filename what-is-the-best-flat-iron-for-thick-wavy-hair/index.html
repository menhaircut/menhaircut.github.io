What Is The Best Flat Iron For Thick Wavy Hair

Written by Daniel Aug 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have thick wavy hair, you know how frustrating it can be to style it. Fortunately, a good flat iron can make all the difference. But with so many options on the market, it can be hard to know which one is right for you. In this article, we'll discuss the best flat iron for thick wavy hair and provide tips on how to use it effectively.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Thick Wavy Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Flat Iron on Thick Wavy Hair

How to Choose the Best Flat Iron for Thick Wavy Hair

When shopping for a flat iron, there are several factors to consider:

Plate Material

The most common plate materials are ceramic, titanium, and tourmaline. Ceramic plates are gentle on hair and distribute heat evenly. Titanium plates heat up quickly and are great for thick hair. Tourmaline plates emit negative ions that smooth hair and reduce frizz.

Plate Width

The width of the plates will depend on your hair length and texture. For thick wavy hair, a wider plate is best. This will allow you to straighten larger sections of hair at once, reducing the amount of time it takes to style.

Temperature Control

Look for a flat iron with adjustable temperature control. For thick hair, you'll need a higher temperature to achieve the best results. However, be careful not to use too much heat, as this can damage hair over time.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using a Flat Iron on Thick Wavy Hair

Once you've found the right flat iron for your hair, follow these steps for best results:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start with clean, dry hair. Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the flat iron.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using clips. Start at the bottom and work your way up.

Step 3: Straighten Your Hair

Starting at the roots, slowly glide the flat iron down each section of hair. Use a comb to help guide the hair through the plates. Repeat until all sections are straightened.

Step 4: Finish with Serum

Apply a small amount of hair serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Achieving the Best Results with Your Flat Iron

Follow these tips to get the most out of your flat iron:

Tip 1: Use the Right Products

Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the flat iron. Finish with a serum to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tip 2: Don't Use Too Much Heat

While you'll need a higher temperature for thick hair, be careful not to use too much heat. This can damage hair over time.

Tip 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections to make it easier to straighten.

Tip 4: Glide Slowly

Take your time when gliding the flat iron down each section of hair. This will ensure that each section is straightened evenly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best flat iron for thick wavy hair?

The best flat iron for thick wavy hair will have ceramic or titanium plates, adjustable temperature control, and a wide plate width.

Can I use a flat iron on wet hair?

No, you should never use a flat iron on wet hair. This can cause damage and breakage.

How often can I use a flat iron on my hair?

You should limit your use of a flat iron to once or twice a week to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Wavy Hair

Follow these tips to keep your thick wavy hair healthy:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash

Washing your hair too often can strip it of natural oils. Try washing every other day instead.

Tip 3: Use a Wide-Tooth Comb

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair after washing. This will prevent breakage.

Tip 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair over time.

By following these tips and using the right flat iron, you can achieve beautiful, straight hair that lasts all day.
