How To Cut Own Hair Medium Length Step By Step Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of spending money at the salon or barber shop to get your hair trimmed? Why not try cutting your own hair at home? With a little practice and patience, you can achieve a great medium length haircut without leaving your house. This step by step guide will show you how to cut your own hair medium length in a relaxed, easy to understand language.

How to Cut Own Hair Medium Length

Before we start, here are some things you will need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • Comb
  • Hair clips
  • Mirror

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut your own hair medium length:

  1. Wash and dry your hair thoroughly.
  2. Comb your hair to remove any tangles.
  3. Section your hair into four parts: two in the front and two in the back. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.
  4. Start with one of the front sections. Take a small piece of hair between your fingers and hold it straight up. Cut the ends with your hair cutting scissors. Cut small pieces of hair at a time.
  5. Repeat step 4 with the rest of the hair in the front section.
  6. Move on to the back sections. Take a small piece of hair from the back and hold it straight up. Cut the ends with your hair cutting scissors. Cut small pieces of hair at a time.
  7. Repeat step 6 with the rest of the hair in the back sections.
  8. Blend the front and back sections by cutting small pieces of hair where they meet. Use your comb to blend the sections together.
  9. Check your hair in the mirror to make sure it is even. Make any necessary adjustments.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind while cutting your own hair medium length:

  • Start with small cuts. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut.
  • Use your comb to hold the hair in place while you cut. This will help you achieve a straighter cut.
  • Don't rush. Take your time and make sure you are cutting evenly.
  • If you have curly hair, cut it while it is dry to avoid any surprises when it dries.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting your own hair medium length:

Can I cut my hair with regular scissors?

No, regular scissors are not sharp enough to cut hair. You need hair cutting scissors that are specifically designed for cutting hair.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how quickly you want to maintain your style. On average, people get their hair cut every 6-8 weeks.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go to a salon or barber shop to fix it. Alternatively, you can try to fix it yourself by taking small pieces of hair and blending them into the longer pieces.

Hair Care

After cutting your own hair medium length, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. Here are some tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you must use them, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using too many hair products, as they can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Now that you know how to cut your own hair medium length, give it a try and see how it turns out. With a little practice, you can achieve a great haircut without leaving your house.
