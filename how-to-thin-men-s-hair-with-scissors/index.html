How To Thin Men s Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Dec 02, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Thinning hair is a common problem among men. While some may opt for professional hair thinning services or invest in an electric hair thinning tool, others prefer to do it themselves using scissors. In this guide, we'll walk you through how to thin men's hair with scissors in a few simple steps.

How to Thin Men's Hair with Scissors

Before you start, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a good pair of hair cutting scissors, a comb, and a mirror.

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner. This will make it easier to cut and style.

Step 2: Comb Your Hair

Once your hair is clean, use a comb to remove any tangles or knots.

Step 3: Identify the Areas to Thin

Decide which areas of your hair you want to thin out. This could be the sides, the top, or the back.

Step 4: Divide Your Hair

Separate the hair you want to thin out from the rest of your hair. Use hair clips to hold the rest of your hair in place.

Step 5: Start Cutting

Take a small section of hair and hold it between your fingers. Use the scissors to cut vertically into the hair, starting from the tips and working your way up. Don't cut too much at once, as this could lead to uneven results.

Step 6: Check Your Progress

After each cut, use your comb to check your progress. Make sure you're thinning the hair evenly and not cutting too much.

Step 7: Repeat

Continue cutting and checking until you've thinned out all the sections of hair you want to thin.

Step 8: Blend

Use your scissors to blend the thinned sections of hair with the rest of your hair. This will create a more natural look.

Step 9: Style

Once you're happy with the thinning, style your hair as usual.

Tips for Thinning Men's Hair with Scissors

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Don't cut too much at once. It's better to go slowly and check your progress often.
  • Use sharp scissors. Dull scissors can lead to uneven cuts.
  • Consider thinning your hair gradually over time. This will give you more control and prevent you from cutting too much at once.

FAQ

What should I do if I make a mistake while thinning my hair?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always visit a professional stylist to fix the problem. Alternatively, you can try to blend the mistake with the rest of your hair or wait for it to grow out.

How often should I thin my hair?

This depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. As a general rule, you should aim to thin your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

Thinning your hair can make it more prone to damage, so it's important to take good care of it. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often.

With these tips and tricks, you should be able to thin your hair with scissors like a pro. Good luck!
