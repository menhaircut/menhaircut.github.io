How To Cut A Military Haircut At Home Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of visiting the barber every time you need a haircut? Why don’t you try cutting your hair at home? If you’re looking for a low-maintenance, versatile haircut, a military haircut might be the perfect choice for you. The best part is, it’s easy to do it yourself! In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of cutting a military haircut at home.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

A military haircut is a timeless, classic look that has been popular for years. It’s a short, low-maintenance haircut that is perfect for men who don’t want to spend a lot of time styling their hair. The military haircut is also known as a buzz cut or crew cut, and it’s a popular choice for men who are in the military or law enforcement.

How To

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools. You’ll need a pair of clippers with guards, a comb, and a mirror. You may also want to have a handheld mirror so you can see the back of your head.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut a military haircut at home:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Use a comb to make sure your hair is tangle-free.
  2. Choose the guard size you want to use. The guard size will determine how short your hair will be. If you’re not sure which guard size to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down.
  3. Start at the front of your head and work your way back. Use the clippers to cut your hair against the grain. Make sure you keep the clippers level and flat against your head. Use the comb to lift the hair and guide the clippers.
  4. Repeat the process on the sides and back of your head. Be careful not to cut too high up on the sides or back.
  5. Once you’ve finished cutting your hair, use the clippers without a guard to clean up the edges around your ears and neck.
  6. Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure you didn’t miss any spots.
  7. Shower and shampoo to get rid of any stray hairs.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting a military haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down if you want a shorter haircut.
  • Go slowly and be patient. It’s better to take your time and get it right than to rush and make a mistake.
  • If you’re not sure what guard size to use, ask someone for help or look up pictures of different guard sizes online.
  • Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head and make sure you didn’t miss any spots.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about cutting a military haircut at home:

Is it easy to cut a military haircut at home?

Yes, it’s relatively easy to cut a military haircut at home if you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

What tools do I need to cut a military haircut at home?

You’ll need clippers with guards, a comb, and a mirror.

What guard size should I use?

The guard size you use will depend on how short you want your hair to be. If you’re not sure which guard size to use, start with a longer guard and work your way down.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for maintaining a military haircut:

  • Shampoo your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Use a moisturizing conditioner to keep your hair soft and prevent dryness.
  • Apply a hair oil or serum to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy.
  • Visit a barber every few weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Now that you know how to cut a military haircut at home, it’s time to give it a try! Remember to take your time and be patient, and don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it. With a little practice, you’ll be able to master the art of cutting your own hair.
