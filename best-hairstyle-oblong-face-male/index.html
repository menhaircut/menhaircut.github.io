Understanding Oblong Face Shape

An oblong face shape is characterized by a long and narrow face with a high forehead and an elongated chin. It is also known as a rectangular face shape. If you have an oblong face shape, it is important to choose a hairstyle that adds width to your face and creates an illusion of a more balanced face shape.

Best Hairstyle for Oblong Face Male

The best hairstyle for oblong face male is one that adds width to the face and creates an illusion of a more oval face shape. Some of the best hairstyles for oblong face male include:

  • Side parting with short sides and a long top
  • Short layered cut with a side-swept fringe
  • Crew cut with a textured top
  • Pompadour with a high fade
  • Buzz cut with a full beard

These hairstyles work well for oblong face male as they add volume and width to the sides of the face, balancing out the length of the face.

How to Achieve the Best Hairstyle for Oblong Face Male

To achieve the best hairstyle for oblong face male, follow these simple steps:

  1. Choose the right haircut based on your face shape and hair type
  2. Consult with a hairstylist to determine the best style for you
  3. Maintain your hair regularly by washing and conditioning it
  4. Style your hair using the right products and tools

By following these steps, you can achieve the best hairstyle for oblong face male and look your best.

Tips for Oblong Face Male

Here are some tips that can help you rock your oblong face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that make your face look longer
  • Choose hairstyles that add volume and width to the sides of your face
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too tight or slicked back
  • Use hair products that add volume and texture to your hair

By following these tips, you can enhance your oblong face shape and look your best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can oblong face males have long hair?

A: Yes, they can have long hair but it is recommended to add layers and volume to the hair to balance out the length of the face.

Q: Can oblong face males have a fringe?

A: Yes, they can have a side-swept fringe that adds volume to the sides of the face.

Q: Can oblong face males have a buzz cut?

A: Yes, they can have a buzz cut but it is recommended to have a full beard to add width to the face.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Male

It is important to take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and shiny look. Here are some hair care tips for oblong face male:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot water and harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Use hair products that are suitable for your hair type
  • Trim your hair regularly to avoid split ends and maintain a healthy look
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use heat protectant products before styling your hair

By following these hair care tips, you can maintain healthy and shiny hair that complements your oblong face shape.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for oblong face male is one that adds volume and width to the sides of the face, creating an illusion of a more oval face shape. By choosing the right haircut, following the right hair care routine, and using the right products and tools, you can achieve the best hairstyle for oblong face male and look your best.
