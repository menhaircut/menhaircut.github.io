What Is Curtain Bangs

Written by Oliver Nov 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Curtain bangs have become a popular hairstyle trend in recent years. They are a type of fringe that is parted in the middle, framing the face on either side. Curtain bangs got their name from the way they resemble a set of curtains that are parted in the middle.

Description

Curtain bangs are versatile and can be customized to suit different face shapes and hair types. They are a low-maintenance hairstyle and can be styled in various ways, making them a popular choice for many people.

Curtain bangs can be cut at different lengths and thicknesses, depending on the desired look. They are typically longer than regular bangs, with the shortest pieces reaching the eyebrows and the longest pieces reaching the cheekbones.

How to Get Curtain Bangs

If you're considering getting curtain bangs, it's important to consult with a hairstylist who has experience cutting this style. They can advise you on the best length and thickness for your hair type and face shape.

When getting curtain bangs, it's important to keep in mind that they require regular trims to maintain their shape. You may also need to adjust your styling routine to accommodate your new bangs.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style curtain bangs:

  1. Wash your hair and dry it thoroughly.
  2. Part your hair in the middle, creating two sections.
  3. Brush your bangs forward, then separate them into two sections.
  4. Use a round brush to blow-dry your bangs, curling them inward towards your face.
  5. Once your bangs are dry, use a flat iron to smooth out any kinks or frizz.
  6. Apply a small amount of hair oil or serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.

Tips for Styling Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for styling curtain bangs:

  • Use a round brush when blow-drying your bangs to add volume and create a soft, rounded shape.
  • Apply a small amount of dry shampoo to your roots to absorb any excess oil and add texture.
  • If your bangs are too long, clip them back with bobby pins while they grow out.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that best suits your face shape.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curtain bangs:

Q: Are curtain bangs suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can be customized to suit different face shapes. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best length and thickness for your face shape.

Q: Can I style my curtain bangs without blow-drying them?

A: Yes, you can air-dry your bangs and style them with a flat iron or curling iron to create the desired shape.

Hair Care for Curtain Bangs

Here are some tips for caring for your curtain bangs:

  • Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup on your bangs.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your bangs.
  • Trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.
  • Protect your bangs from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before blow-drying or styling.

With these tips and tricks, you can enjoy your new curtain bangs and rock this trendy hairstyle with confidence!
