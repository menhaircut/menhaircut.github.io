How To Cut Men s Hair Long On Top Short Sides

Written by Christine Jul 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting men's hair long on top and short on the sides is a popular hairstyle that has been trending for a few years now. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways, making it perfect for men who want to switch up their look. In this article, we will go through the steps on how to cut men's hair long on top short sides, as well as provide tips and answer some of the most frequently asked questions about this style.

Description

The long on top short sides hairstyle is characterized by having longer hair on the top of the head, while the sides and back are cut short. This style is also known as the undercut or disconnected undercut. It is a popular hairstyle among men of all ages and is suitable for different hair types and face shapes.

How to Cut Men's Hair Long on Top Short Sides

Before cutting men's hair long on top short sides, you will need the following tools:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Hair spray

Now, let's go through the steps on how to cut men's hair long on top short sides:

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Before cutting the hair, make sure it is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and style. Use a shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair by dividing it into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Use clips to secure the hair that you are not cutting.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Using clippers, start cutting the sides and back of the head. Use a shorter guard for a closer cut, or a longer guard for a more gradual fade. Start at the bottom and work your way up. Make sure to blend the hair between the sides and the top.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Using scissors, cut the hair on the top of the head. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Use the comb to lift the hair and cut it to the desired length. Make sure to blend the hair between the top and the sides.

Step 5: Style the Hair

Apply some hair spray to keep the hair in place. Style the hair as desired. You can comb it to the side, slick it back, or create a messy look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting men's hair long on top short sides:

  • Start with a longer length and gradually cut more if needed.
  • Use a mirror to check the back of the head while cutting.
  • Make sure to blend the hair between the top and the sides for a seamless look.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best.

FAQ

1. Is long on top short sides suitable for all face shapes?

Yes, long on top short sides is suitable for all face shapes. However, the length and style of the top can be adjusted to complement the face shape.

2. How often should I get a haircut for long on top short sides?

You should get a haircut every 3 to 4 weeks to maintain the length and style of the hair.

3. Can I style long on top short sides with curly hair?

Yes, you can style long on top short sides with curly hair. However, you may need to use some hair products to keep the curls in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, make sure to wash it regularly, use conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on it. You can also use hair products such as hair oil or hair mask to keep it nourished and hydrated.

Cutting men's hair long on top short sides is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that can be achieved with the right tools and technique. Follow the steps and tips in this article to get the perfect long on top short sides haircut.
