Can Your Hair Turn Curly With Age

Written by William Nov 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As we age, our bodies undergo many changes, including changes to our hair. One question that some people may have is whether their hair can turn curly with age. In this article, we will explore this topic in-depth and provide you with all the information you need to know.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Curly hair is caused by the shape of the hair follicle. If the follicle is oval-shaped, the hair will be curly, and if it is round, the hair will be straight. As we age, the shape of our hair follicles can change, which can result in a change in the texture of our hair.

Some people may find that their hair becomes curlier with age, while others may find that their hair becomes straighter. This can depend on a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, and environmental factors.

How to

If you are interested in changing the texture of your hair, there are some things you can do to encourage your hair to become curlier. One option is to get a perm, which is a chemical treatment that can help to curl your hair. Another option is to use styling products that are designed to enhance curls, such as mousse, gel, or curling cream.

However, it is important to note that these treatments and products can be damaging to your hair if not used properly. Be sure to follow the instructions carefully and consider consulting a professional stylist for guidance.

Step by Step

If you are considering getting a perm to make your hair curlier, here are the steps involved:

  1. Consult with a professional stylist to determine whether a perm is right for you.
  2. Wash your hair thoroughly and do not apply any styling products.
  3. Apply the perm solution to your hair, following the instructions carefully.
  4. Cover your hair with a plastic cap and allow the solution to set for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse your hair thoroughly with water, then apply a neutralizing solution to stop the perm process.
  6. Style your hair as desired using styling products designed for curly hair.

Tips

If you are looking to enhance your curls without getting a perm, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like flat irons and curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting a haircut that is designed to enhance curly hair, such as layers or a curly bob.

FAQ

Can stress cause curly hair?

While stress can cause a variety of physical symptoms, it is unlikely to cause your hair to become curly.

Can pregnancy cause curly hair?

Hormonal changes during pregnancy can cause changes to your hair, including making it curlier. However, this is not a guaranteed outcome and can vary from person to person.

Can medication cause curly hair?

Some medications can cause changes to your hair texture as a side effect. Be sure to consult with your doctor if you notice any changes to your hair while taking medication.

Hair Care

Whether your hair is naturally curly or you are looking to enhance your curls, it is important to take good care of your hair. This includes using gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding heat styling tools, and protecting your hair from the sun and other environmental factors.

Additionally, be sure to schedule regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends. With the right care and attention, you can enjoy beautiful, healthy curls at any age.
