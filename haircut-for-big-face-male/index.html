Haircut For Big Face Male How To Find The Perfect Style

Written by Frank Aug 04, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a guy with a big face? Are you struggling to find the perfect haircut that suits your face shape? Well, worry no more! In this article, we will guide you on how to choose the best haircut for big face male. From how to determine your face shape to the best hairstyles for big faces, we got you covered! Read on to find out more.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the perfect haircut for big face male is to determine your face shape. There are four basic face shapes: round, square, oval, and heart-shaped. To determine your face shape, stand in front of a mirror and trace the outline of your face with a lipstick or eyeliner. Step back and analyze the shape you drew. Is it round, square, oval, or heart-shaped?

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length and slimness. The best haircuts for big face male with round faces are those that add height to the crown and have short sides. Examples of haircuts for round faces include the high and tight, the messy fringe, and the textured side part.

Square Face Shape

Guys with square faces should choose haircuts that soften the angles of their face. You should avoid haircuts that have sharp lines and angles. The best haircuts for big face male with square faces are those that have layers and texture. Examples of haircuts for square faces include the messy crop, the undercut, and the textured quiff.

Oval Face Shape

If you have an oval face shape, you are lucky! You can pull off almost any haircut. However, you want to avoid haircuts that add height to the crown. The best haircuts for big face male with oval faces are those that have a natural flow and blend with your hairline. Examples of haircuts for oval faces include the side-swept fringe, the classic taper, and the slicked-back undercut.

Heart-Shaped Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to create the illusion of width at the forehead and balance out the narrow chin. The best haircuts for big face male with heart-shaped faces are those that have volume at the top and are tapered at the sides. Examples of haircuts for heart-shaped faces include the messy fringe, the side-swept quiff, and the textured crop.

Step by Step Guide to Getting the Perfect Haircut for Big Face Male

Now that you know your face shape, it's time to head to the barbershop! Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect haircut for big face male:

Step 1: Research and Find Inspiration

Before heading to the barbershop, do some research and find inspiration for your haircut. Look for pictures of haircuts that suit your face shape and hair type. Save these pictures on your phone and show them to your barber.

Step 2: Communicate with Your Barber

When you get to the barbershop, communicate with your barber. Show them the pictures you saved and explain what you like about each haircut. Ask for their opinion and advice on what will suit you best.

Step 3: Consider Your Hair Type

When choosing a haircut, consider your hair type. If you have thick hair, you want a haircut that will thin it out. If you have thin hair, you want a haircut that will add volume. Your barber can help you choose a haircut that works with your hair type.

Step 4: Follow the Barber's Instructions

Once you and your barber have decided on the perfect haircut, sit back and let them work their magic. Follow their instructions and don't be afraid to ask questions if you're unsure about something.

Tips for Maintaining Your Haircut

Now that you have the perfect haircut for your big face, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips for maintaining your haircut:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Properly

Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Massage the shampoo into your scalp and rinse thoroughly. Apply conditioner to the ends of your hair and rinse thoroughly.

Tip 2: Use the Right Products

Use hair products that suit your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For example, if you have thick hair and want a messy look, use a texturizing paste. If you have thin hair and want to add volume, use a volumizing mousse.

Tip 3: Visit the Barber Regularly

Visit the barber regularly for touch-ups and trims. How often you need to visit the barber depends on the length of your hair and how fast it grows.

FAQ

What if I don't know my face shape?

If you're unsure about your face shape, ask your barber for help. They can help you determine your face shape and choose a haircut that suits you.

Can I still get a haircut that's not recommended for my face shape?

Yes, you can! At the end of the day, it's your hair and you should get a haircut that makes you happy. However, keep in mind that haircuts that are recommended for your face shape tend to look more flattering.

Hair Care for Big Face Male

Lastly, taking care of your hair is important for maintaining a healthy and stylish look. Here are some hair care tips for big face male:

Tip 1: Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools like hair dryers and straighteners too often. Excessive heat can damage your hair and make it look dull.

Tip 2: Eat a Balanced Diet

Eating a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals is important for healthy hair growth. Include foods like eggs, nuts, and leafy greens in your diet.

Tip 3: Get Enough Sleep

Getting enough sleep is important for overall health, including hair health. Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night.

There you have it, a complete guide on how to find the perfect haircut for big face male. Remember to determine your face shape, communicate with your barber, and maintain your haircut with proper hair care. With these tips, you'll have a stylish and flattering haircut that suits your big face in no time!
