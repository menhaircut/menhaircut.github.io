How To Do An Easy Fade At Home A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Sep 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to switch up your hairstyle, a fade might be the perfect choice. Fades are versatile and can be tailored to suit your style, whether you prefer a low, medium, or high fade. However, getting a fade at the barbershop can be expensive, so why not learn how to do an easy fade at home? In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to achieve a perfect fade.

Description

A fade is a popular hairstyle that involves blending the hair from short to long. The hair gradually fades from one length to another, creating a seamless transition. Fades can be done on any length of hair and can be customized to suit your preferences.

How to

Before starting, gather all the necessary tools, including clippers, guards, a comb, and scissors. It's also important to wash your hair and towel dry it before starting the fade.

Step 1: Choose the Right Guard

The first step is to choose the right guard for the clippers. The guard number determines the length of hair that will be left. For a low fade, start with a number two guard, for a medium fade, use a number four guard, and for a high fade, use a number six or eight guard.

Step 2: Start with the Sides

Start by using the clippers on the sides of your head. Begin at the bottom and work your way up to the top, using an upward motion. Use the same guard number throughout the process to ensure a consistent length.

Step 3: Blend the Top

Next, use the scissors to blend the top of the hair with the sides. Starting from the back, cut the hair in a diagonal direction, using the scissors to blend the hair from the sides to the top. Continue until the hair is evenly blended.

Step 4: Taper the Neckline

Taper the neckline by using the clippers without a guard. Start at the bottom of the neckline and work your way up, using a gentle upward motion. Blend the hair to create a natural-looking neckline.

Step 5: Clean Up

Finally, clean up any stray hairs and touch up any areas that need it. Use a comb to ensure the hair is even and trim any areas that are too long.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you achieve the perfect fade:

  • Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard for a more gradual fade.
  • Use the mirror to check your progress and make adjustments as needed.
  • Take your time and don't rush the process.
  • Practice makes perfect, so keep trying until you get it right.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing an easy fade at home:

Can I use any type of clippers?

While it's best to use clippers specifically designed for haircuts, you can use any clippers as long as they have the right guards.

What if I make a mistake?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always start over or ask a friend or family member to help you fix it.

How often should I get a fade?

This depends on your personal preference and how fast your hair grows. Some people get a fade every two weeks, while others wait four to six weeks.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain a healthy-looking fade. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the fade.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep the hair moisturized.

With these tips and steps, you can easily achieve an easy fade at home. Whether you're looking for a low, medium, or high fade, you can customize it to suit your style. With practice, you'll become a pro at giving yourself a fade.
