How to Make Your Hair Wavy When It's Naturally Curly

Making curly hair wavy is easier than you might think. All you need is a few hair products and some basic styling tools. Here are the steps to achieve wavy hair:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

The first step to making your hair wavy is to wash it. Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and healthy. Avoid using products that contain sulfates, which can dry out curly hair.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

Before you start styling your hair, apply a heat protectant to prevent damage from hot styling tools. Use a product that is specifically designed for curly hair to keep your curls intact.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to style each section and ensure that all of your hair gets evenly styled.

Step 4: Use a Curling Wand

Use a curling wand to create loose waves. Wrap each section of hair around the wand, hold it for a few seconds, and then release. Repeat this process with each section of hair, alternating the direction of the curls for a more natural look.

Step 5: Finish with Hair Spray

Finish your wavy hairstyle with a mist of hair spray to hold the waves in place. Use a flexible hold hairspray to avoid making your hair too stiff.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you're more of a visual learner, here's a step-by-step guide to help you make your hair wavy:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  3. Section your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  4. Use a curling wand to create loose waves.
  5. Alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.
  6. Finish with a flexible hold hairspray.

Tips for Making Your Hair Wavy

Here are some additional tips to help you make your hair wavy:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Don't use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair.
  • Try using a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your natural curls before styling.
  • Experiment with different curling wand sizes to find the one that works best for your hair.
  • Don't touch your hair too much after styling, as this can make the waves fall out.

FAQs

Here are some commonly asked questions about making hair wavy:

Can you make curly hair wavy?

Yes, you can make curly hair wavy by using a curling wand and wrapping small sections of hair around it.

What is the best way to make hair wavy?

The best way to make hair wavy is to use a curling wand and alternate the direction of the curls for a more natural look.

How long do wavy curls last?

Wavy curls can last anywhere from a few hours to a few days, depending on your hair type and the products you use.

Hair Care Tips for Wavy Hair

Now that you've achieved the wavy hairstyle of your dreams, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and hydrated. Here are some hair care tips for wavy hair:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hair elastics that can cause breakage.

That's it for our guide on how to make your hair wavy when it's naturally curly. We hope you found this article helpful and that you're now ready to switch up your hairstyle and try something new. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and hydrated, and don't be afraid to experiment with different styling techniques to find the one that works best for you!
