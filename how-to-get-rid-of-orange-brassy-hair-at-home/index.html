How To Get Rid Of Orange Brassy Hair At Home

Written by William Jun 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of looking at your orange brassy hair? It can be frustrating when you try to dye your hair a new color, and it turns out orange instead of the desired shade. But don't worry, in this blog post, we will be discussing how to get rid of orange brassy hair at home. We will cover everything from the causes of brassy hair to the steps you can take to fix it. So, let's get started!

  2. How to Get Rid of Orange Brassy Hair at Home

Causes of Orange Brassy Hair

Before we get into how to fix brassy hair, let's understand what causes it. There are several reasons why your hair may turn orange or brassy:

1. Bleaching

When you bleach your hair, the pigment is removed, and the natural color of your hair is exposed. Bleaching can cause hair to become orange or brassy, especially if you have dark hair.

2. Sun Exposure

Exposure to the sun can also cause hair to turn brassy. The UV rays can damage the hair and cause it to become dry and brittle, which can lead to brassiness.

3. Hard Water

Hard water can also contribute to brassy hair. The minerals in hard water can build up on the hair, causing it to become discolored.

How to Get Rid of Orange Brassy Hair at Home

Now that we understand what causes brassy hair, let's discuss how to fix it. The good news is that you can get rid of orange brassy hair at home, and you don't need to spend a lot of money on salon treatments.

Step-by-Step Guide to Removing Brassy Hair

Step 1: Choose a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is a great way to counteract the orange tones in your hair. The purple color neutralizes the orange, and your hair will look more toned. Use a purple shampoo every time you wash your hair.

Step 2: Use a Toner

A toner is a hair product that is used to remove brassiness and add shine to your hair. You can use a toner at home by mixing it with developer and applying it to your hair. Follow the instructions on the toner package.

Step 3: Use a Hair Mask

A hair mask can help to nourish your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

Tips to Prevent Brassy Hair

Prevention is always better than cure. Here are some tips to prevent your hair from becoming brassy:

1. Use a Quality Hair Dye

Make sure to use a high-quality hair dye that is designed for your hair type. Cheap hair dyes can cause damage to your hair and make it more prone to brassiness.

2. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wear a hat or use a hair product that contains SPF to protect your hair from the sun's damaging rays.

3. Use a Water Softener

If you live in an area with hard water, consider using a water softener to reduce the mineral buildup on your hair.

FAQs

1. How often should I use a toner?

You should use a toner once a month to maintain your hair's color and prevent brassiness.

2. Can I use a purple shampoo on colored hair?

Yes, you can use a purple shampoo on colored hair. It will not damage your hair, and it will help to maintain the color.

3. Can I leave a hair mask on overnight?

Yes, you can leave a hair mask on overnight. It will help to nourish your hair and make it more manageable.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and shiny:

1. Trim Your Hair Regularly

Trimming your hair regularly will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

2. Use a Heat Protectant

Always use a heat protectant before using any heat styling tools on your hair.

3. Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils and make it dry and brittle. Try to wash your hair every other day, or every three days if possible.

By following these tips, you can get rid of orange brassy hair at home and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny. Remember, prevention is always better than cure, so take care of your hair and avoid the causes of brassy hair.
