How To Stop Hair Falling Out After Bleaching A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Jul 23, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you worried about your hair falling out after bleaching? Bleaching your hair can be a fun way to change your look, but it can also damage your hair if not done properly. In this article, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to stop hair falling out after bleaching.

Table of Contents:

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description:

When you bleach your hair, it can cause damage to your hair and scalp. This damage can result in hair loss, breakage, and thinning. Hair falling out after bleaching is a common problem, but there are ways to prevent it from happening. The key is to take care of your hair properly after bleaching and to avoid further damage.

How To:

If you want to stop hair falling out after bleaching, here are some tips to follow:

1. Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment:

After you bleach your hair, it’s important to use a deep conditioning treatment to help repair any damage. Look for a treatment that contains ingredients like keratin, biotin, and vitamins to help strengthen and nourish your hair.

2. Avoid Heat Styling:

Heat styling can further damage your hair, so it’s important to avoid using hot tools like flat irons, curling irons, and blow dryers. Instead, let your hair air dry and use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.

3. Use a Gentle Shampoo:

Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for color-treated hair. These shampoos are formulated to be gentle on your hair and scalp while still cleaning your hair effectively.

4. Avoid Chemical Treatments:

Avoid chemical treatments like perms, relaxers, and hair dyes for at least a few weeks after bleaching. These treatments can further damage your hair and scalp, making hair falling out after bleaching more likely.

Step By Step:

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to stop hair falling out after bleaching:

Step 1:

Use a deep conditioning treatment on your hair immediately after bleaching.

Step 2:

Avoid heat styling and let your hair air dry.

Step 3:

Use a gentle shampoo that is designed for color-treated hair.

Step 4:

Avoid chemical treatments like perms, relaxers, and hair dyes for at least a few weeks after bleaching.

Tips:

Here are some additional tips to help prevent hair falling out after bleaching:

1. Use a Hair Mask:

Use a hair mask once a week to help nourish your hair and prevent further damage.

2. Trim Your Hair Regularly:

Trimming your hair regularly can help get rid of split ends and prevent further breakage.

3. Eat a Healthy Diet:

Eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote healthy hair growth.

FAQ:

Q: How long should I wait before washing my hair after bleaching?

A: Wait at least 24-48 hours before washing your hair after bleaching to allow your hair to recover.

Q: Can I use hair oil after bleaching?

A: Yes, you can use hair oil after bleaching to help nourish and protect your hair.

Q: Will my hair fall out if I bleach it too often?

A: Yes, if you bleach your hair too often, it can cause hair loss and damage.

Hair Care:

Taking care of your hair properly after bleaching is key to preventing hair falling out. Use a deep conditioning treatment, avoid heat styling, use a gentle shampoo, avoid chemical treatments, and follow the other tips we’ve provided to keep your hair healthy and strong.

By following these tips and taking care of your hair properly, you can prevent hair falling out after bleaching and keep your hair looking great.
