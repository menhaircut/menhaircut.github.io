How To Get Rid Of Extreme Hair Fall

Written by Aprilia Oct 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are experiencing extreme hair fall, it can be a distressing and frustrating experience. Hair loss can be caused by a variety of factors such as stress, genetics, hormonal changes, nutritional deficiencies, and more. However, the good news is that there are several ways to combat hair fall and promote hair growth. In this article, we will discuss some effective ways to get rid of extreme hair fall.

Description

Hair fall is a common problem that affects both men and women. It can be caused by various factors such as hormonal changes, nutritional deficiencies, stress, pollution, genetics, and more. Extreme hair fall can lead to thinning of hair, baldness, and loss of confidence. Therefore, it is essential to take appropriate measures to control hair fall and promote hair growth.

How to

Here are some ways to get rid of extreme hair fall:

1. Maintain a Healthy Diet

A healthy diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to include foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, zinc, biotin, vitamin A, vitamin C, and omega-3 fatty acids. Some examples of such foods are eggs, fish, nuts, spinach, carrots, and citrus fruits.

2. Reduce Stress

Stress can have a significant impact on hair fall. Therefore, it is essential to manage stress levels by practicing relaxation techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises.

3. Use Mild Shampoos

Harsh shampoos can damage hair and lead to hair fall. Therefore, it is essential to use mild shampoos that are gentle on the scalp and hair.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Excessive heat styling can damage hair and lead to hair fall. Therefore, it is essential to avoid using heating tools such as blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons.

5. Massage Scalp Regularly

Massaging the scalp regularly can improve blood circulation and promote hair growth. Use a natural oil such as coconut oil or olive oil and massage gently for a few minutes.

6. Get Enough Sleep

Adequate sleep is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure to get at least 7-8 hours of sleep every night.

Step by Step

Here are the step-by-step instructions to get rid of extreme hair fall:

Step 1: Maintain a Healthy Diet

Eat a diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals such as iron, zinc, biotin, vitamin A, vitamin C, and omega-3 fatty acids. Include foods such as eggs, fish, nuts, spinach, carrots, and citrus fruits in your diet.

Step 2: Reduce Stress

Practice relaxation techniques such as meditation, yoga, or deep breathing exercises to manage stress levels.

Step 3: Use Mild Shampoos

Use mild shampoos that are gentle on the scalp and hair. Avoid harsh shampoos that can damage hair and lead to hair fall.

Step 4: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heating tools such as blow dryers, straighteners, and curling irons that can damage hair and lead to hair fall.

Step 5: Massage Scalp Regularly

Massage the scalp regularly using a natural oil such as coconut oil or olive oil. Massage gently for a few minutes to improve blood circulation and promote hair growth.

Step 6: Get Enough Sleep

Get at least 7-8 hours of sleep every night to promote healthy hair growth.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to get rid of extreme hair fall:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on hair and lead to hair fall.
  • Protect hair from the sun and pollution by wearing a scarf or hat.
  • Take supplements such as biotin or iron after consulting with a doctor.
  • Consult a dermatologist if you are experiencing severe hair fall.

FAQ

Q1. Can extreme hair fall be reversed?

A1. Yes, extreme hair fall can be reversed by taking appropriate measures such as maintaining a healthy diet, reducing stress levels, and avoiding harsh hair treatments.

Q2. How long does it take to see results?

A2. Results may vary depending on the individual and the cause of hair fall. It may take several weeks or months to see noticeable results.

Q3. Can genetics cause hair fall?

A3. Yes, genetics can be a significant factor in hair fall. If you have a family history of hair loss, you may be prone to hair fall.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential for healthy hair growth. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use a conditioner to keep hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash hair as it can damage hair.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle hair gently.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair as it can lead to hair fall.
  • Trim hair regularly to prevent split ends.

By following these tips and methods, you can effectively get rid of extreme hair fall and promote healthy hair growth.
