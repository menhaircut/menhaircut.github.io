Face Framing Haircut A Guide To Enhance Your Facial Features

Written by Aprilia Dec 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

A face framing haircut is a trendy hairstyle that enhances your facial features by framing your face with layers of hair. This haircut is perfect for people who want to add dimension to their hair without sacrificing their hair length. In this article, we will guide you on how to get a face framing haircut, tips, FAQs, and hair care tips to keep your look fresh and stylish.

How to Get a Face Framing Haircut

Getting a face framing haircut is easy and can be done at any salon or with a trusted hairstylist. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Choose the Right Style

Before going to the salon, decide on the style you want. You can choose from long or short layers, blunt or choppy cuts, and wispy or tapered ends. You can also show your hairstylist pictures of the style you want to achieve.

Step 2: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Once you have decided on the style, consult with your hairstylist. They will recommend the best style that suits your face shape, hair texture, and preference. They will also advise you on the maintenance and frequency of the haircut.

Step 3: Wash and Prepare Your Hair

Before the haircut, make sure you wash and dry your hair. This will make it easier for your hairstylist to cut your hair. Avoid using any hair products, as this may affect the haircut's outcome.

Step 4: Get the Haircut

Once your hair is prepared, your hairstylist will commence the haircut. They will use scissors or a razor to cut layers of hair to frame your face. They will also incorporate your desired style into the haircut.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After the haircut, your hairstylist will style your hair according to your preference. They may use styling products, such as mousse, hairspray, or wax, to enhance your hair's texture and volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Face Framing Haircut

A face framing haircut requires regular maintenance to keep your hair looking fresh and stylish. Here are some tips:

TIP 1: Trim Your Hair Regularly

To maintain the shape of your haircut, trim your hair every six to eight weeks. This will prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

TIP 2: Use Hair Products

Use hair products, such as leave-in conditioner, to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Avoid using too much product, as this may weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

TIP 3: Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using any styling tools, such as a flat iron or curling iron. Also, avoid using hot water when washing your hair, as this may damage your hair.

FAQs About Face Framing Haircut

Q: Is a face framing haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, a face framing haircut can suit all face shapes. It will depend on the hairstylist to recommend the best style that suits your face shape.

Q: Can I style my hair differently after getting a face framing haircut?

A: Yes, you can style your hair differently after getting a face framing haircut. The layers of hair will allow you to experiment with different hairstyles, such as updos or half up and half down hairstyles.

Q: Can I color my hair after getting a face framing haircut?

A: Yes, you can color your hair after getting a face framing haircut. However, it is best to wait for a few days before coloring your hair to allow the new haircut to set in.

Hair Care Tips for Face Framing Haircut

TIP 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and texture.

TIP 2: Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair. This will prevent breakage and damage to your hair.

TIP 3: Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun's harmful rays by wearing a hat or using a hair product that contains SPF.

TIP 4: Use a Silk Pillowcase

Use a silk pillowcase to prevent hair breakage and tangles while you sleep. Silk is gentle on the hair and reduces friction.

A face framing haircut is a stylish and trendy way to enhance your facial features. With the right style, maintenance, and hair care tips, you can keep your hair looking fresh and healthy. Consult with your hairstylist for the best style that suits your face shape and hair texture.
