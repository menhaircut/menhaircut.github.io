How To Get Thick Wavy Hair Guys A Guide To Perfectly Textured Locks

Written by William Aug 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a guy who's been dreaming of having thick, wavy hair, now is the time to make that dream a reality. Whether you're starting from scratch or just need a bit of a boost, there are plenty of ways to achieve the perfect texture and volume for your locks. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about how to get thick wavy hair guys, including tips, tricks, and plenty of hair care advice to help keep your new 'do looking its best.

Description

Thick, wavy hair is a desirable look for many guys, but achieving it can be a challenge. Fortunately, with some simple styling techniques and a bit of patience, you can create the perfect texture and volume for your locks. Whether you're starting with thin, straight hair or thick, curly locks, there are plenty of ways to get the wavy, textured look you're after.

How to Get Thick Wavy Hair Guys: Step by Step

Step 1: Start with the Right Cut

The first step to achieving thick, wavy hair is to start with the right cut. If you're working with thin or fine hair, a shorter cut may be best, as it can help create the illusion of thicker locks. For guys with thicker hair, longer styles can help enhance natural waves and create more volume.

Step 2: Use the Right Products

Once you've got the right cut, the next step is to use the right products to enhance your natural texture. Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture, such as sea salt sprays, texturizing powders, and volumizing mousses.

Step 3: Embrace Heat Styling

Heat styling can be a great way to enhance natural waves and create volume. Use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves, or blow dry your hair with a diffuser attachment to enhance natural texture.

Step 4: Experiment with Different Styles

One of the best things about thick, wavy hair is that it's versatile and can be styled in a variety of ways. Experiment with different styles, such as messy, tousled looks or sleek, polished waves, to find the one that works best for you.

Tips for Getting Thick Wavy Hair Guys

Want to take your thick, wavy hair to the next level? Here are a few tips to help you make the most of your locks:

Tip 1: Use a Deep Conditioning Treatment

Deep conditioning treatments can help keep your hair healthy and hydrated, which can in turn enhance natural waves and create more volume.

Tip 2: Don't Overwash Your Hair

Washing your hair too often can strip it of natural oils, which can make it more difficult to create texture and volume. Aim to wash your hair every other day or every few days, depending on your hair type.

Tip 3: Use a Boar Bristle Brush

A boar bristle brush can help distribute natural oils throughout your hair, which can help create more volume and enhance natural waves.

Tip 4: Avoid Heavy Products

Heavy products like gels and pomades can weigh down your hair and make it more difficult to create texture and volume. Stick to lightweight products like sea salt sprays and mousses.

FAQ

Q: Can I still get thick, wavy hair if I have thin or fine hair?

A: Yes! With the right cut and styling products, you can create the illusion of thicker, more voluminous hair.

Q: How often should I use heat styling tools on my hair?

A: Heat styling can be damaging to your hair, so it's best to limit use to a few times a week at most.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from becoming frizzy?

A: To prevent frizz, avoid overwashing your hair, use a deep conditioning treatment, and avoid brushing your hair when it's wet.

Hair Care Tips for Guys with Thick Wavy Hair

Want to keep your thick, wavy hair looking its best? Here are a few hair care tips to keep in mind:

Tip 1: Use a Shampoo and Conditioner Designed for Your Hair Type

Look for shampoos and conditioners that are designed for thick, wavy hair to help keep your locks healthy and hydrated.

Tip 2: Use a Heat Protectant Spray

Before using heat styling tools, be sure to apply a heat protectant spray to help minimize damage to your locks.

Tip 3: Get Regular Trims

Regular trims can help keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends, which can make it more difficult to create texture and volume.

Tip 4: Avoid Overusing Products

Using too many styling products can weigh down your hair and make it more difficult to create texture and volume. Use products sparingly and only when necessary.

With these tips and techniques, you'll be well on your way to achieving the thick, wavy hair you've always wanted. Remember to be patient and experiment with different styles and products to find what works best for your hair type and personal style.
