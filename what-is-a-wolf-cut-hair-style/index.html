What Is A Wolf Cut Hair Style

Written by Robby Sep 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've been scrolling through social media lately, you may have come across a new hair trend called the Wolf Cut. This hairstyle has been taking the internet by storm, with many people wanting to try it out for themselves. In this blog post, we'll be discussing what exactly a Wolf Cut is, how to achieve it, and some tips and tricks to make sure your hair looks its best.

Description

The Wolf Cut is a layered hairstyle that features shaggy, choppy layers that are longer on top and shorter on the bottom. This hairstyle is meant to mimic the natural layers of a wolf's fur, hence the name. The hair is typically cut with a razor to create a more textured look and then styled with a bit of product to create a messy, undone look.

How to Achieve the Wolf Cut

The Wolf Cut is a bit more complicated than your average haircut, so it's important to find a stylist who is well-versed in this particular style. When you go to your appointment, be sure to bring photos of the look you're trying to achieve so your stylist can get a better idea of what you're looking for. Once the cut is complete, you can style your hair at home using a bit of texturizing spray or mousse to create that messy, undone look.

Step by Step

1. Start with clean, dry hair

2. Section your hair into four parts: top, sides, and back

3. Start at the back of your hair and work your way up, using a razor to create choppy, textured layers

4. Move to the sides and then the top, creating longer layers on top and shorter layers on the bottom

5. Once the cut is complete, use a bit of texturizing spray or mousse to create a messy, undone look

Tips for Maintaining the Wolf Cut

The key to maintaining the Wolf Cut is to embrace the messy, undone look. Use a texturizing spray or mousse to create volume and texture, and avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage. It's also important to get regular trims to maintain the shape of the cut and keep your hair healthy.

FAQ

What hair types work best with the Wolf Cut?

The Wolf Cut works best on hair that is medium to long in length and has a bit of natural texture or wave. However, it can be customized to work with any hair type.

Can I achieve the Wolf Cut at home?

We recommend going to a stylist to achieve the Wolf Cut, as it can be a bit more complicated than your average haircut. However, if you're feeling confident, you can try it at home using a razor and some patience.

Hair Care for the Wolf Cut

When it comes to caring for your Wolf Cut, it's important to avoid using too much heat on your hair to prevent damage. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools, and try to air dry your hair as much as possible. It's also important to get regular trims to maintain the shape of the cut and keep your hair healthy.

In conclusion, the Wolf Cut is a trendy new hairstyle that has been taking the internet by storm. This layered, textured cut is meant to mimic the natural layers of a wolf's fur and is perfect for those looking to achieve a messy, undone look. Be sure to find a stylist who is well-versed in this particular style and embrace the messy, undone look to keep your hair looking its best.
