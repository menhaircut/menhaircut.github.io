Men s Hairstyle For Round Face Shape

Written by Petter Oct 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your round face shape? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best men's hairstyles that flatter round face shapes. Read on to discover expert tips and step-by-step guides to get the perfect haircut for your face shape.

Description

Round face shape is characterized by a circular shape with a rounded jawline and cheekbones. The challenge for men with round face shapes is to find a hairstyle that adds length to their face and creates a more defined jawline. The right hairstyle can work wonders to balance out the proportions of your face and enhance your features.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape, the key is to create height and volume on top of the head while keeping the sides and back short. This will elongate your face shape and give the illusion of a more defined jawline. The following are some of the best men's hairstyles for round face shapes:

1. Pompadour

The pompadour is a classic hairstyle that works well for round face shapes. This hairstyle features short sides and a long, voluminous top that is styled upwards and back. The height on top of the head elongates the face and creates a more balanced look.

2. Side Part

The side part is a timeless hairstyle that looks great on round face shapes. This hairstyle features a deep side part with a slicked-back top and short sides. The side part creates a diagonal line that gives the illusion of a longer face shape.

3. Faux Hawk

The faux hawk is a modern and edgy hairstyle that works well for round face shapes. This hairstyle features short sides and a long, spiky top that is styled upwards. The height on top of the head creates a vertical line that elongates the face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

To get the perfect haircut for your round face shape, follow these simple steps:

1. Consult with a Professional

Start by consulting with a professional hairstylist who can assess your face shape and recommend a suitable hairstyle. They can also advise you on the best hair products and styling techniques to maintain your new hairstyle.

2. Choose a Hairstyle

Select a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and suits your personal style. Bring photos of hairstyles you like to show your hairstylist, and discuss any concerns or preferences you have.

3. Maintain Your Hairstyle

Maintain your new hairstyle by using high-quality hair products and following a regular grooming routine. Visit your hairstylist regularly for touch-ups and trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh and stylish.

Tips

Here are some expert tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face shape:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the head, as this can make your face shape appear wider.
  • Experiment with different hair lengths and styles to find the perfect look for you.
  • Consider adding texture to your hair to create more dimension and movement.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hairstyles for round face shapes:

1. Can men with round face shapes wear short hair?

Yes, men with round face shapes can wear short hair, but it's important to keep the sides and back short while adding height and volume on top.

2. What are the best hair products for men's hairstyles?

The best hair products for men's hairstyles depend on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. Consult with your hairstylist for recommendations on the best products for your hair.

3. How often should I get a haircut?

It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep your hair looking healthy and well-groomed.

Hair Care

Healthy hair is the foundation of any great hairstyle. Follow these hair care tips to keep your hair looking and feeling its best:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and flat irons too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals that promote healthy hair growth.

With these expert tips and step-by-step guides, you can find the perfect men's hairstyle for your round face shape. Experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that works best for you and your personal style.
