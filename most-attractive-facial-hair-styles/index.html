The Most Attractive Facial Hair Styles For Men In 2023

Written by William Nov 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to change up your facial hair style this year? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the most attractive facial hair styles for men in 2023. From the classic styles to the trendy ones, we’ve got you covered.

Table of Content

  • Classic Styles
  • Trendy Styles
  • How to Achieve the Look
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Facial Hair
  • FAQs
  • Facial Hair Care

Classic Styles

Classic facial hair styles never go out of fashion. They are timeless and can suit any face shape and hair type. These styles include:

The Mustache

The mustache has been around for centuries and is still a popular facial hair style today. It can be worn alone or paired with a beard. This style can be trimmed to suit your face shape and hair type. It is easy to maintain and can be styled in different ways.

The Goatee

The goatee is a classic style that consists of a small beard under the chin. It can be paired with a mustache or worn alone. This style is perfect for men with a square or round face shape.

The Full Beard

The full beard is a classic style that never goes out of fashion. It is perfect for men with a strong jawline and can be trimmed to suit your face shape and hair type. This style requires regular maintenance to keep it looking neat and tidy.

Trendy Styles

Trendy facial hair styles are perfect for men who want to stand out from the crowd. These styles include:

The Stubble

The stubble is a popular style that is easy to maintain. It consists of short hair on the face and can be trimmed to suit your face shape and hair type. This style is perfect for men with a round or oval face shape.

The Soul Patch

The soul patch is a trendy style that consists of a small patch of hair under the lower lip. It can be worn alone or paired with a mustache. This style is perfect for men with a slim face shape.

The Anchor Beard

The anchor beard is a trendy style that consists of a beard that is shaped like an anchor. It is perfect for men with a triangular or heart-shaped face. This style requires regular maintenance to keep it looking neat and tidy.

How to Achieve the Look

To achieve these facial hair styles, you will need to follow these steps:

  • Decide on the style you want to achieve
  • Grow out your facial hair to the desired length
  • Trim and shape your facial hair using clippers or scissors
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and nourished

Tips for Maintaining Your Facial Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining your facial hair:

  • Regularly trim and shape your facial hair
  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and nourished
  • Cleanse your facial hair regularly to prevent buildup
  • Brush your facial hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about facial hair:

How long does it take to grow facial hair?

It can take anywhere from a few days to a few months to grow facial hair, depending on your genetics and age.

Do I need to use a special shampoo for my facial hair?

Yes, you should use a shampoo that is specifically designed for facial hair to keep it clean and healthy.

Can I style my facial hair with a hair dryer?

Yes, you can use a hair dryer to style your facial hair. Use a comb or brush to shape your facial hair while using the hair dryer.

Facial Hair Care

Facial hair requires regular care to keep it looking healthy and nourished. Here are some tips for facial hair care:

  • Use a beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and nourished
  • Cleanse your facial hair regularly to prevent buildup
  • Regularly trim and shape your facial hair
  • Brush your facial hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy
  • Use a shampoo that is specifically designed for facial hair

Now that you have a better understanding of the most attractive facial hair styles for men in 2023, it’s time to choose a style that suits you. Remember to maintain your facial hair regularly to keep it looking neat and tidy. Happy styling!
