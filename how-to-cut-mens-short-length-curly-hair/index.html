How To Cut Men s Short Length Curly Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair can be a bit tricky to cut, especially when it comes to short lengths. But fear not! With the right technique and tools, you can achieve a great haircut for your curly-haired clients. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps of cutting men's short length curly hair in a relaxed English language. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • How to Cut Men's Short Length Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Men's Short Length Curly Hair

How to Cut Men's Short Length Curly Hair

Cutting men's short length curly hair requires a bit of technique and skill. Here are some tips to get you started:

  • Use a pair of sharp, high-quality scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start by sectioning the hair and cutting it in small sections to ensure an even cut.
  • Take into consideration the curl pattern and growth direction of the hair.
  • Don't overcut the hair, as curly hair tends to shrink up after it's cut.
  • Remember that curly hair needs more moisture and hydration than straight hair, so don't be afraid to leave a bit of length to maintain the hair's natural texture.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to cut men's short length curly hair:

  1. Start by washing the hair and towel-drying it to remove excess water.
  2. Section the hair into four quadrants using clips.
  3. Starting from the nape of the neck, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down.
  4. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers and cut the desired length using your scissors.
  5. Move on to the next section of hair, working your way up the head.
  6. Once you've finished one quadrant, move on to the next until all four quadrants have been cut.
  7. Blend the hair using scissors or clippers, depending on the desired look.
  8. Check for any uneven areas or stray hairs and make any necessary adjustments.
  9. Style the hair using a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance the natural texture.

Tips for Cutting Men's Short Length Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting men's short length curly hair:

  • Don't cut the hair too short, as it will shrink up and appear even shorter once it dries.
  • Avoid using thinning shears on curly hair, as they can create an uneven texture.
  • Take into consideration the client's face shape and hairline when cutting the hair.
  • Don't be afraid to leave a bit of length to maintain the hair's natural curl pattern.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying the hair to enhance the curls and reduce frizz.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some commonly asked questions about cutting men's short length curly hair:

Can you cut curly hair when it's dry?

Yes, you can cut curly hair when it's dry. In fact, cutting curly hair when it's dry allows you to see the hair's natural texture and curl pattern, making it easier to achieve an even cut.

How often should men with curly hair get a haircut?

Men with curly hair should get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to maintain a healthy length and shape.

What's the best way to style short length curly hair?

The best way to style short length curly hair is to use a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to enhance the natural texture. You can also use a diffuser when blow-drying the hair to enhance the curls and reduce frizz.

Hair Care for Men's Short Length Curly Hair

Maintaining healthy, hydrated hair is key to achieving a great haircut for men's short length curly hair. Here are some tips for hair care:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage the hair and cause frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to damp hair to enhance the natural texture and reduce frizz.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals like bleach or relaxers, as they can damage the hair and cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to maintain a healthy length and shape.

Following these tips and techniques will help you achieve a great haircut for men's short length curly hair. Remember to take into consideration the hair's natural texture and curl pattern, and don't be afraid to leave a bit of length to maintain the hair's natural bounce and movement. Happy cutting!
