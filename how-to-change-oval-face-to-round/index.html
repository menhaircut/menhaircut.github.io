If you have an oval-shaped face, you may be wondering how to transform it into a more rounded shape. While there is no magic solution to completely change the shape of your face, there are several techniques you can use to create the illusion of a rounder face. How to Change Oval Face to Round

There are several ways to create the illusion of a rounder face shape:

  • Hairstyles: Certain hairstyles can help to create the illusion of a rounder face shape. For example, a bob with rounded ends or a side-swept fringe can help to soften the angles of an oval face.
  • Makeup: Using makeup to contour and highlight your face can also help to create the illusion of a rounder face shape. Apply a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to the temples and cheekbones to create shadows, and highlight the center of your forehead, nose, and chin to draw attention to the center of your face.
  • Jewelry: Wearing earrings that are round or geometric can help to create the illusion of a rounder face shape. Avoid long, dangling earrings that can elongate the face.

By using a combination of these techniques, you can create the illusion of a rounder face shape.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to change oval face to round:

  1. Choose a hairstyle that will help to create the illusion of a rounder face shape. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for your face shape.
  2. Apply a primer to your face to create a smooth base for your makeup.
  3. Apply foundation to your face, blending it well to create an even skin tone.
  4. Use concealer to cover any blemishes or dark circles under your eyes.
  5. Apply a contouring powder or cream to the temples and cheekbones to create shadows.
  6. Apply a highlighter to the center of your forehead, nose, and chin to draw attention to the center of your face.
  7. Apply a blush to the apples of your cheeks to add color to your face.
  8. Choose earrings that are round or geometric to create the illusion of a rounder face shape.
  9. Avoid wearing sunglasses that are too angular or have sharp edges.
  10. Finally, smile! A smile can help to soften the angles of your face and create a more rounded appearance.

Tips for Round Face Shapes

If you have a round face shape, here are some additional tips:

  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short or too voluminous, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Wear necklaces that are long and slim to elongate your neckline.
  • Choose sunglasses that have round or oval frames to complement your face shape.
  • Wear hats that have a wide brim to balance the proportions of your face.

FAQs: Hair Care for Round Faces

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have a round face?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preference. However, it is generally recommended to wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent it from becoming too greasy or dry.

Q: What types of haircuts are best for round faces?

A: Hairstyles that create volume at the crown of the head and have layers around the face can help to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. Consult with a hairstylist to find the best style for your face shape.

Q: Should I avoid wearing my hair in a high ponytail if I have a round face?

A: A high ponytail can actually help to elongate your face and create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. However, make sure to avoid wearing a tight ponytail that can create a bulge at the top of your head.

By following these tips and techniques, you can create the illusion of a rounder face shape and feel confident in your appearance!
