How To Fake Front Bangs A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 05, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Have you ever wanted to try out front bangs but didn't want to commit to a haircut? Well, we have good news for you! You can easily fake front bangs with just a few simple steps. In this article, we'll show you how to do it and give you some tips on how to make it look natural.

How to Fake Front Bangs

Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

Start by brushing your hair back and away from your face. Make sure your hair is tangle-free and easy to work with.

Step 2: Take a Section of Hair

Next, take a section of hair from the front of your head. This section should be about the width of your forehead and go back about 3-4 inches.

Step 3: Twist and Pin

Twist this section of hair and pull it across your forehead, as if you were creating a bang. Use bobby pins to secure it in place. Make sure the pins are hidden and the twist looks natural.

Step 4: Adjust and Style

Adjust the twist and make sure it looks even on both sides. You can also use a straightener or curling iron to style it and make it blend in with the rest of your hair.

Tips for Faking Front Bangs

Here are some tips to make your fake front bangs look more natural:

Tip 1: Use Bobby Pins That Match Your Hair Color

Using bobby pins that match your hair color will make them less noticeable and help your fake bangs blend in with the rest of your hair.

Tip 2: Add Volume to Your Hair

Adding volume to your hair will make your fake bangs look more natural. You can use a volumizing spray or mousse to achieve this.

Tip 3: Use Hairspray

Using hairspray will help keep your fake bangs in place and make them last longer. You can also use a light hold hairspray to keep them looking natural.

FAQ

Can I use hair extensions to fake front bangs?

Yes, you can use hair extensions to create fake front bangs. Simply clip them in and style them to look like bangs.

How long will my fake front bangs last?

Your fake front bangs should last all day if you use enough bobby pins and hairspray to keep them in place.

Will my fake front bangs look natural?

If you follow the steps and tips we've provided, your fake front bangs should look natural and blend in with the rest of your hair.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair when you're faking front bangs. Here are some tips:

Tip 1: Use a Heat Protectant

If you're using a straightener or curling iron to style your fake bangs, make sure to use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

Tip 2: Don't Leave Bobby Pins in Too Long

Leaving bobby pins in your hair for too long can cause breakage and damage. Make sure to remove them at the end of the day.

Tip 3: Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly will help keep it healthy and prevent buildup from hairspray and other styling products.

Now that you know how to fake front bangs, it's time to give it a try! This is a great way to switch up your look without committing to a haircut. Just remember to follow our tips and take care of your hair.
