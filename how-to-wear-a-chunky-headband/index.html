How To Wear A Chunky Headband A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Dec 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Chunky headbands are a stylish accessory that can add a pop of color and texture to any outfit. But if you're not sure how to wear them, you're not alone. In this guide, we'll show you how to wear a chunky headband and share some tips on how to style them.

Description

A chunky headband is a wide, thick band made from a variety of materials like wool, cotton or silk. It can be plain, patterned or embellished with beads or jewels. Chunky headbands come in a range of colors and can be worn all year round. They are perfect for keeping your hair out of your face during a workout or adding a stylish touch to your outfit.

How to Wear a Chunky Headband

Step 1: Choose the Right Headband

The first step to wearing a chunky headband is to choose the right one. Consider the color, material, and embellishments. Pick one that complements your outfit and fits comfortably on your head.

Step 2: Prep Your Hair

Before putting on the headband, prep your hair. You can wear the headband with your hair down or up in a ponytail or bun. If you're wearing your hair down, blow-dry it straight or curl it for added texture.

Step 3: Put on the Headband

To put on the headband, place it on your head and pull it down towards your forehead. Make sure it sits comfortably on your head and doesn't slip off. You can adjust the position of the headband to either cover your ears or sit higher up on your head.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you've put on the headband, style your hair around it. You can leave your hair loose or tuck it behind your ears for a sleek look. If you're wearing a ponytail or bun, make sure the headband sits above it and doesn't push it down.

Tips

  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the perfect look.
  • Choose a headband that complements your skin tone and outfit.
  • If you have short hair, choose a thinner headband that won't overwhelm your hair.
  • If you have long hair, try wearing the headband with a messy bun or braid for a boho look.
  • Don't be afraid to mix and match headbands with different outfits.

FAQ

Can I wear a chunky headband with glasses?

Yes, you can wear a chunky headband with glasses. Just make sure the headband doesn't push your glasses down or cause them to slip off.

Can I wear a chunky headband to work?

Yes, you can wear a chunky headband to work. Choose one that's plain or has a subtle pattern, and pair it with a professional outfit.

How do I care for my chunky headband?

Care for your chunky headband according to the material it's made from. Wool headbands can be hand-washed in cold water, while cotton and silk headbands can be machine-washed on a gentle cycle. Hang them to dry and store them in a dry, cool place.

Hair Care

When wearing a chunky headband, it's important to take care of your hair. Make sure it's clean and dry before putting on the headband. Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with hot tools and avoid using hair ties that can cause breakage. Finally, give your hair a break from headbands and other accessories every once in a while to prevent damage.
