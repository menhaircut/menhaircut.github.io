What To Do When You Have Falling Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are experiencing hair fall, then you are not alone. Hair fall is a common problem that affects people of all ages and genders. It can be caused by a variety of factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, stress, poor diet, and certain medical conditions. However, the good news is that there are many things you can do to prevent hair fall and promote hair growth. In this blog post, we will discuss how to deal with falling hair, step by step.

  1. How To Deal With Falling Hair
  2. Step By Step Guide

How To Deal With Falling Hair

The first step in dealing with falling hair is to understand what is causing it. While there are many causes of hair fall, some of the most common include:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Stress
  • Poor diet
  • Medical conditions

Once you have identified the cause of your hair fall, you can take steps to prevent it. Here are some things you can do:

1. Improve Your Diet

A healthy diet is essential for healthy hair growth. Make sure you are eating foods that are rich in vitamins and minerals, such as:

  • Vitamin A
  • Vitamin C
  • Vitamin D
  • Vitamin E
  • Iron
  • Zinc

You can also take supplements to ensure you are getting enough of these nutrients.

2. Manage Stress

Stress can cause hair fall, so it's important to manage it. You can do this by practicing relaxation techniques, such as meditation or yoga, or by engaging in physical activity.

3. Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Avoid using harsh hair care products, such as those that contain sulfates or alcohol. Instead, use gentle, natural products that are designed for your hair type.

4. Avoid Heat Styling

Heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause it to fall out. Try to avoid using them as much as possible, or use them on a low heat setting.

Step By Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to deal with falling hair:

Step 1: Identify the Cause

Determine what is causing your hair fall. This will help you take the appropriate steps to prevent it.

Step 2: Improve Your Diet

Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.

Step 3: Manage Stress

Practice relaxation techniques or engage in physical activity to manage stress.

Step 4: Use Gentle Hair Care Products

Avoid using harsh hair care products and use gentle, natural products instead.

Step 5: Avoid Heat Styling

Avoid using heat styling tools or use them on a low heat setting.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some additional hair care tips to help prevent hair fall:

  • Massage your scalp regularly to improve blood circulation
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine

FAQs

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair fall. It's important to manage stress to prevent hair fall.

Q: Can a poor diet cause hair fall?

A: Yes, a poor diet can cause hair fall. Make sure you are eating a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals.

Q: Can heat styling tools cause hair fall?

A: Yes, heat styling tools can damage your hair and cause it to fall out. Avoid using them as much as possible, or use them on a low heat setting.

In conclusion, hair fall is a common problem that can be caused by a variety of factors. However, by following the steps outlined in this blog post, you can prevent hair fall and promote healthy hair growth. Remember to eat a healthy diet, manage stress, use gentle hair care products, avoid heat styling, and follow the additional hair care tips provided. If you continue to experience hair fall despite taking these steps, consult a dermatologist for further advice.
