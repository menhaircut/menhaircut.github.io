How To Make Your Face Look Shorter Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Oct 14, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you have a long face and want to make it look shorter? You're not alone! Many people feel self-conscious about their face shape and want to find ways to make it appear more balanced. Fortunately, there are several simple tricks you can use to create the illusion of a shorter face. In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best tips and tricks for making your face look shorter.

Description

Having a long face can make certain hairstyles and makeup looks difficult to pull off. However, with the right techniques, you can create a more proportional and balanced appearance. Whether you have a naturally long face or simply want to create a new look, these tips and tricks can help you achieve your desired result.

How-To

There are several ways to make your face look shorter, including using makeup, hairstyles, and facial exercises. Here are some tips to get you started:

Makeup Tips

One of the easiest ways to create the illusion of a shorter face is by using makeup. Here are some techniques to try:

  1. Contour: Use a contouring powder or cream to shade the areas of your face that you want to appear shorter. Apply the contouring product to the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline.
  2. Highlight: Apply a highlighter to the center of your forehead, down the bridge of your nose, and on your chin. This will make your face appear shorter by drawing attention to the center of your face.
  3. Blush: Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend it upwards towards your temples. This will help to balance out the length of your face.

Hairstyle Tips

Your hairstyle can also play a big role in how your face appears. Here are some tips to try:

  1. Shorter Haircuts: Shorter haircuts can help to create the illusion of a shorter face. Consider a bob or pixie cut to help balance out your features.
  2. Layers: Layers can help to add volume and texture to your hair, which can make your face appear shorter.
  3. Side-Swept Bangs: Side-swept bangs can help to break up the length of your face and create a more balanced appearance.

Facial Exercise Tips

Facial exercises can help to strengthen your facial muscles and create a more defined jawline. Here are some exercises to try:

  1. Jaw Clenches: Clench your jaw for several seconds and release. Repeat this exercise several times a day to help define your jawline.
  2. Chin Lifts: Tilt your head back and lift your chin towards the ceiling. Hold for several seconds and release. Repeat several times a day to help tighten the muscles in your neck and chin.
  3. Cheek Puffs: Puff out your cheeks and hold for several seconds. Release and repeat several times a day to help define your cheekbones.

Step-by-Step

To make your face look shorter, follow these step-by-step instructions:

  1. Apply contouring powder or cream to the sides of your forehead, temples, and jawline.
  2. Apply highlighter to the center of your forehead, down the bridge of your nose, and on your chin.
  3. Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks and blend it upwards towards your temples.
  4. Consider a shorter haircut, layers, or side-swept bangs to help balance out your features.
  5. Try facial exercises like jaw clenches, chin lifts, and cheek puffs to help define your facial muscles.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you make your face look shorter:

  • Consider wearing statement earrings or bold necklaces to draw attention away from the length of your face.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too long or straight, as these can make your face appear even longer.
  • Experiment with different makeup looks and hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about making your face look shorter:

What is the best haircut for a long face?

The best haircut for a long face is one that adds volume and texture to the sides of your face. Consider a bob or pixie cut with layers to help balance out your features.

Can facial exercises really help to define your jawline?

Yes, facial exercises can help to strengthen your facial muscles and create a more defined jawline. However, it's important to remember that results may vary and it can take several weeks or months to see noticeable changes.

What makeup products should I use to make my face look shorter?

To make your face look shorter, you should use contouring powder or cream to shade the areas of your face that you want to appear shorter. You should also use highlighter to draw attention to the center of your face and blush to balance out the length of your face.

Hair Care

When choosing a hairstyle to make your face look shorter, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. Talk to your hairstylist about what cuts and styles will work best for you. Additionally, make sure to use hair care products that are designed for your hair type to keep your hair healthy and looking its best.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your face look shorter and feel more confident in your appearance. Whether you prefer to use makeup, hairstyles, or facial exercises, there are plenty of options to help you achieve your desired look.
