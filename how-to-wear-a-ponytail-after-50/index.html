How To Wear A Ponytail After 50 Tips And Tricks

Written by Eveline Aug 16, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you over 50 and wondering if you can still rock a ponytail? The answer is yes! A ponytail is a timeless and easy hairstyle that can be worn by women of all ages. In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks on how to wear a ponytail after 50.

Table of Content

  • How to Wear a Ponytail After 50
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing a Ponytail After 50
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Wear a Ponytail After 50

Wearing a ponytail after 50 is easy and can be done in many different ways. Here are some popular ways to style a ponytail:

  • Sleek and low ponytail
  • High and messy ponytail
  • Side ponytail
  • Braided ponytail
  • Ponytail with a scarf or headband

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to wear a sleek and low ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Apply a small amount of hair serum to add shine and smoothness to your hair.
  3. Gather your hair at the nape of your neck and secure it with a hair tie.
  4. Use a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways.
  5. Wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it and secure it with a bobby pin.

For a high and messy ponytail, follow these steps:

  1. Flip your head upside down and gather your hair at the crown of your head.
  2. Secure your hair with a hair tie.
  3. Use your fingers to loosen the hair on top of your head to create a messy look.
  4. Pull out a few face-framing pieces of hair to soften the look.

Tips for Wearing a Ponytail After 50

Here are some tips for wearing a ponytail after 50:

  • Choose a style that flatters your face shape and hair texture.
  • Experiment with different hair ties and accessories to add interest to your ponytail.
  • Avoid pulling your hair too tightly, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Consider using a volumizing product to add fullness to your ponytail.
  • Accessorize with a scarf or headband to add color and texture to your hairstyle.

FAQs

Can I wear a ponytail if I have thinning hair?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail if you have thinning hair. Consider using a volumizing product to add fullness and texture to your hair.

How can I make my ponytail look more polished?

To make your ponytail look more polished, use a brush to smooth out any bumps or flyaways. You can also wrap a small section of hair around the hair tie to hide it and secure it with a bobby pin.

What kind of hair tie should I use?

You can use any type of hair tie that is comfortable for you. However, avoid using elastic bands that can cause damage and breakage to your hair.

Hair Care Tips

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Get enough sleep and exercise to reduce stress, which can cause hair loss.

Now that you know how to wear a ponytail after 50, try out some of these styles and rock your new look with confidence!
