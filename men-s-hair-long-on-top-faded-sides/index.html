Men s Hair Long On Top Faded Sides The Trending Hairstyle In 2023

Written by Luffy Aug 06, 2023 · 3 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and modern hairstyle, then men's hair long on top faded sides is the perfect choice for you. This hairstyle has been trending for a few years now, and it's not going away anytime soon. In this blog post, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle, including how to achieve it, tips, and hair care.

Description

Men's hair long on top faded sides is a hairstyle that features long hair on the top of the head, while the sides are cut short and faded. This hairstyle is versatile, and you can style it in different ways, such as slicking it back, combing it over, or leaving it messy. This hairstyle is perfect for men who want to keep their hair long while maintaining a clean and neat appearance.

How to Achieve Men's Hair Long on Top Faded Sides

To achieve this hairstyle, you need to go to a professional hairstylist who knows how to do a fade haircut. The hairstylist will cut the hair on the sides short and gradually fade it into the longer hair on top. The length of the hair on top depends on your preference, but it's usually around 3 to 6 inches long. To style the hair on top, you can use a hair wax or pomade to create the desired look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve men's hair long on top faded sides:

  1. Choose a professional hairstylist who has experience in doing fade haircuts.
  2. Decide on the length of the hair on top.
  3. Ask the hairstylist to cut the sides short and gradually fade it into the longer hair on top.
  4. Style the hair on top using a hair wax or pomade.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain and style men's hair long on top faded sides:

  • Use a hair wax or pomade to style the hair on top.
  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Blow-dry your hair on a low heat setting to avoid damaging it.
  • Trim the hair on top regularly to avoid split ends.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about men's hair long on top faded sides:

Q: Is men's hair long on top faded sides suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is suitable for all face shapes.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair every other day to avoid drying it out.

Q: Can I style men's hair long on top faded sides in different ways?

A: Yes, this hairstyle is versatile, and you can style it in different ways, such as slicking it back, combing it over, or leaving it messy.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and shiny, you need to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips for men's hair long on top faded sides:

  • Wash your hair regularly using a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot water to wash your hair as it can damage the hair follicles.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish and moisturize your hair.
  • Trim the hair on top every six to eight weeks to avoid split ends and promote hair growth.

Men's hair long on top faded sides is a stylish and trendy hairstyle that's perfect for men who want to keep their hair long while maintaining a neat and clean appearance. With the right haircut and styling products, you can achieve this look and keep it looking great for a long time.
