What Round Brush Is Best For Thick Hair

Written by Luffy Nov 19, 2023 · 5 min read

If you have thick hair, you know how challenging it can be to style it. Thick hair requires more time and effort when it comes to styling, and using the right tools can make all the difference. One tool that is essential for styling thick hair is a round brush. But with so many different types of round brushes available, it can be hard to know which one is the best for your hair type. In this article, we will discuss the best round brush for thick hair and provide you with some tips on how to use it.

What is a Round Brush?

A round brush is a hair styling tool that is shaped like a cylinder with bristles around the outside. It is used to create volume, curl, and smoothness in the hair. Round brushes come in different sizes and materials, such as boar bristle, ceramic, and titanium. The size and material of the brush you choose will depend on your hair type and the style you want to achieve.

How to Choose the Best Round Brush for Thick Hair

When it comes to choosing the best round brush for thick hair, there are a few things to consider:

Size

The size of the round brush you choose will depend on the length of your hair. For thick hair, it's best to choose a larger size brush, such as a 2-inch brush, to help smooth and straighten your hair.

Material

The material of the brush can also make a difference in how well it works on thick hair. Boar bristle brushes are great for thick hair because they help distribute natural oils throughout the hair, which can help prevent breakage and damage. Ceramic and titanium brushes are also good options because they heat up quickly and evenly, which can speed up styling time.

Bristles

The bristles on a round brush can also make a difference in how well it works on thick hair. Look for brushes with sturdy, flexible bristles that can easily grip and smooth your hair.

How to Use a Round Brush on Thick Hair

Using a round brush on thick hair can be tricky, but with a few tips, you can achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. Make sure your hair is completely dry before you start styling.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Section your hair into manageable sections using clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 3: Start at the Roots

Start at the roots of your hair and work your way down to the ends. Use the brush to lift your hair at the roots and then pull it through to the ends.

Step 4: Use a Blow Dryer

Use a blow dryer on a low heat setting to dry your hair as you style it. This will help set the style and add volume to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with a Serum

Finish your style with a serum or oil to add shine and reduce frizz.

Tips for Using a Round Brush on Thick Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you get the most out of your round brush:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before styling to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Choose a brush with a comfortable handle to make styling easier.
  • Don't use too much product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it harder to style.
  • Invest in a high-quality brush that will last for years.

Frequently Asked Questions

What size round brush is best for thick hair?

For thick hair, it's best to use a larger size brush, such as a 2-inch brush.

What material is best for a round brush for thick hair?

Boar bristle brushes are great for thick hair because they help distribute natural oils throughout the hair, which can help prevent breakage and damage. Ceramic and titanium brushes are also good options because they heat up quickly and evenly, which can speed up styling time.

Can I use a round brush on wet hair?

It's best to use a round brush on dry hair, as wet hair is more prone to breakage and damage.

Hair Care Tips for Thick Hair

Here are some additional hair care tips to keep your thick hair healthy and beautiful:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Using the right round brush can make all the difference when it comes to styling thick hair. By choosing the right size, material, and bristles, you can achieve a salon-worthy blowout at home. With a few tips and tricks, you can easily incorporate a round brush into your hair care routine and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful.
