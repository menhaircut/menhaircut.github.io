How To Cut A Bob At Home 2020 A Step By Step Guide

Cutting your own hair can be an intimidating task, especially if you're going for a new style like a bob. But with the right tools and techniques, you can create a chic and trendy bob in the comfort of your own home. In this guide, we'll take you through the steps of how to cut a bob at home in 2020. How to Cut a Bob at Home

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure you have the right tools on hand. You'll need:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Clippers or trimmers (optional)
  • Comb
  • Hair ties
  • Hair clips

Once you have your tools ready, you can start by:

1. Washing and Drying Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and letting it dry completely. This will give you a better idea of how your hair naturally falls and will make it easier to cut.

2. Sectioning Your Hair

Using your comb, divide your hair into four sections: two in the front and two in the back. Secure each section with a hair tie or clip.

3. Cutting the Back of Your Hair

Start with the back sections of your hair. Take one section at a time and comb it straight down. Hold the ends of your hair between your fingers and cut straight across. Repeat this process for each back section.

4. Cutting the Front of Your Hair

Move on to the front sections of your hair. Comb each section forward and hold it between your fingers. Cut at an angle, starting from the front and angling towards the back. This will create a layered effect.

5. Finishing Touches

Once you've cut all four sections of your hair, comb it straight down and make any necessary adjustments. Use your clippers or trimmers to clean up the ends and create a more polished look.

Tips for Cutting a Bob at Home

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when cutting a bob at home:

  • Start with small cuts and work your way up. It's always easier to cut more hair than to try to fix a mistake.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress as you go.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as this can cause it to look shorter than you intended.
  • If you're not sure about a certain cut, it's better to leave it and come back to it later.
  • Consider using a hair cutting guide or tutorial to help you get the look you want.

FAQs

Can I cut a bob with curly hair?

Yes, you can cut a bob with curly hair. However, keep in mind that curly hair tends to shrink up when it dries, so you may want to cut it slightly longer than you want it to be.

How often should I cut my bob?

It's recommended to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the style and keep it looking fresh.

What if I make a mistake while cutting my hair?

If you make a mistake while cutting your hair, don't panic. You can always try to fix it or go to a professional stylist to have it corrected.

Hair Care After Cutting a Bob

Once you've cut your bob, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking healthy and stylish. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid drying out your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Consider using a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and prevent split ends.

Now that you know how to cut a bob at home in 2020, you can try out this trendy and chic style for yourself. Just remember to take your time, use the right tools, and follow our step-by-step guide to get the best results.
