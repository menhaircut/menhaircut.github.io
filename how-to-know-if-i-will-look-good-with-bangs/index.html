How To Know If I Will Look Good With Bangs

Written by Daniel Aug 13, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering getting bangs but not sure if they will suit you? Bangs are a popular hairstyle that can add some style and flair to your look, but not everyone is confident that they can pull them off. In this blog post, we will share with you some tips on how to know if you will look good with bangs.

Table of Contents

  • Face Shape
  • Hair Type
  • Forehead Size
  • Lifestyle
  • Try Before You Commit

Face Shape

Your face shape is one of the most important factors to consider when deciding whether or not to get bangs. The right bangs can complement your features and enhance your natural beauty. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a round face, go for side-swept bangs or long, wispy bangs that will make your face appear longer.
  • If you have a square face, try soft, rounded bangs that will balance out your strong jawline.
  • If you have a heart-shaped face, opt for wispy, textured bangs that will soften your forehead.
  • If you have an oval face, lucky you! You can pull off just about any type of bangs.

Hair Type

Your hair type is another important factor to consider when deciding on bangs. Here are some tips:

  • If you have fine hair, go for light, wispy bangs that won't weigh down your hair.
  • If you have thick hair, try blunt, heavy bangs that will add some structure to your hair.
  • If you have curly or wavy hair, consider long, side-swept bangs that won't require too much maintenance.
  • If you have straight hair, you can pull off just about any type of bangs.

Forehead Size

Your forehead size can also affect how bangs will look on you. Here are some tips:

  • If you have a small forehead, try long, side-swept bangs that will make your forehead appear larger.
  • If you have a large forehead, go for blunt, heavy bangs that will cover up some of your forehead.

Lifestyle

Your lifestyle can also play a role in whether or not bangs will work for you. Here are some things to consider:

  • If you exercise frequently, consider getting shorter bangs that won't get in the way.
  • If you live in a humid climate, avoid heavy, blunt bangs that will frizz up in the humidity.
  • If you have a busy schedule, go for low-maintenance bangs that won't take too much time to style.

Try Before You Commit

Before you get bangs, try them out first to make sure they will suit you. You can do this by:

  • Using a virtual hairstyle app to see how you will look with different types of bangs.
  • Wearing a wig with bangs to see how they will look on you.
  • Pinning your hair back to create the illusion of bangs.

Tips for Choosing the Right Bangs

Here are some additional tips to help you choose the right bangs:

  • Consider your personal style and how bangs will fit in with it.
  • Consult with a hairstylist to get their opinion on what type of bangs will suit you.
  • Start with light, wispy bangs before committing to heavier, blunt bangs.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting bangs:

  • Will bangs make me look younger? Bangs can give you a more youthful appearance, but it depends on the type of bangs and how they are styled.
  • How often do I need to trim my bangs? Bangs should be trimmed every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.
  • Can I still wear my hair in a ponytail with bangs? Yes, you can still wear your hair in a ponytail with bangs, but you may need to pin them back or use a headband to keep them out of your face.

Hair Care for Bangs

Here are some tips for keeping your bangs looking their best:

  • Wash your bangs every 2-3 days to prevent them from looking greasy.
  • Blow dry your bangs using a round brush to give them some volume.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too much to prevent them from becoming oily.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your bangs between washes.

Now that you know how to know if you will look good with bangs, go ahead and give them a try! With the right type of bangs and some proper hair care, you can achieve a stylish and flattering new look.
