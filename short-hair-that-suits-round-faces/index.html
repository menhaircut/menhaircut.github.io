Short Hair That Suits Round Faces The Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for the perfect short hairstyle that complements your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will be discussing the different short hairstyles that are perfect for round faces, how to style them, and tips on how to take care of your hair. So let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Choose Short Hair That Suits Round Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces
  • Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Round Faces
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Short Hair That Suits Round Faces

Description

Round faces are characterized by their full cheeks and soft jawline. The right short hairstyle can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look. When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, it's essential to consider the length, texture, and shape of the cut. A short hairstyle that suits a round face should create the illusion of length and add height to the crown of the head.

How to Choose Short Hair That Suits Round Faces

When choosing a short hairstyle for a round face, consider the following:

  • Length – Short hair that falls just below the chin or at the jawline can help to elongate your face.
  • Texture – A layered cut can create the illusion of length and add volume to the crown of the head.
  • Shape – A short hairstyle with an angled cut or asymmetrical style can help to create a more balanced look.

Some short hairstyles that are perfect for round faces include:

  • Pixie cut
  • Bob cut
  • Layered cut
  • Asymmetrical cut
  • Side-swept bangs

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Here are some steps to follow when styling your short hair:

  1. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush, lifting the hair at the crown to add volume.
  4. If you have a pixie cut, apply a texturizing product to add definition to your hair.
  5. If you have a bob cut or layered cut, use a flat iron to add texture to your hair.
  6. Finish off your look with a hairspray to hold your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Round Faces

Here are some tips on how to maintain your short hair:

  • Visit your hairstylist every six to eight weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a dry shampoo to refresh your hair in between washes.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Incorporate a hair mask into your hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about short hair that suits round faces:

Q: Can short hair make my face look rounder?

A: Yes, if the wrong style is chosen. However, choosing the right short hairstyle can help to elongate your face and create a more balanced look.

Q: Can I still have bangs with short hair?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great addition to a short hairstyle for a round face.

Q: Can I style my short hair differently for different occasions?

A: Yes, you can experiment with different styles, such as a slicked-back look or adding accessories like hair clips or headbands.

Hair Care for Short Hair That Suits Round Faces

Here are some tips on how to take care of your short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Incorporate a hair mask into your hair care routine to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly to keep your haircut looking fresh.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to find the perfect short hairstyle that suits your round face and keep your hair looking healthy and beautiful. So go ahead and experiment with different styles to find the one that suits you best!
