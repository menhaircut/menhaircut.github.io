Medium Hairstyles For Round Faces 2021 The Ultimate Guide

Written by William Jul 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect hairstyle for your round face? Look no further! In this article, we will provide you with the latest trends and tips for medium hairstyles for round faces 2021. Whether you're looking for a sleek bob or a bouncy layered cut, we've got you covered. Let's dive in!

Description

Round faces have a balanced shape with soft features, which can make it challenging to find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. The key is to create the illusion of length and angles by adding volume and texture to your hair. Medium hairstyles are an excellent choice for round faces as they provide versatility and balance without overwhelming your features.

How to

When selecting a medium hairstyle for your round face, consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personality. Consult with your hairstylist to find the best cut and style that complements your features and enhances your natural beauty. Here are some popular medium hairstyles for round faces in 2021:

1. Layered Bob

The layered bob is a timeless classic that adds movement and dimension to your hair. It frames your face and elongates your neck, creating the illusion of a slimmer face. Ask your stylist to add layers that start at your cheekbones and gradually get longer towards the ends. You can style it straight, wavy, or curly, depending on your mood and occasion.

2. Shaggy Lob

The shaggy lob is a modern take on the traditional lob that features choppy layers and texture. It's perfect for round faces as it adds definition and edginess to your look. You can wear it messy or sleek, depending on your preference. Use a texturizing spray or mousse to enhance the layers and create a tousled effect.

3. Side Swept Bangs

The side-swept bangs are a flattering option for round faces as they create a diagonal line that breaks up the symmetry of your face. They also highlight your eyes and cheekbones, drawing attention away from your jawline. Ask your hairstylist to cut your bangs at an angle that complements your face shape and hair texture. You can style them with a round brush or a flat iron to add volume and curve.

Step by Step

Here's how to style a medium hairstyle for round faces in 2021:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and condition your hair with products that suit your hair type and texture. Blow-dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and shape.

2. Apply heat protectant

Apply a heat protectant spray or cream to your hair to prevent damage from hot tools. Use a flat iron or curling wand to style your hair, depending on the look you want to achieve.

3. Add texture and volume

Use a texturizing spray, pomade, or dry shampoo to add texture and volume to your hair. Scrunch your hair with your fingers or a diffuser to create a natural-looking wave or curl.

4. Finish with hairspray

Set your style with a medium hold hairspray that provides hold and shine. Avoid using too much product as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your medium hairstyle for round faces:

  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain the shape of your cut.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair and prevent breakage.
  • Avoid using hot tools on wet hair as it can damage your hair and create frizz.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles and accessories to keep your look fresh and versatile.

FAQ

What haircut is best for a round face?

A layered bob, shaggy lob, or side-swept bangs are some of the best haircuts for a round face as they add texture, definition, and angles to your hair.

Does long hair make a round face look fat?

Long hair can make a round face look wider and shorter, especially if it's all one length. However, you can still wear long hair if you add layers and texture that frame your face and create movement.

What hairstyles should I avoid with a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add bulk and width to your hair, such as blunt bangs, one-length cuts, and tight curls. Instead, choose hairstyles that add length and angles to your face.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on high heat as it can damage your hair and cause split ends.
  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from UV rays and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

With these tips and inspiration, you'll be able to find the perfect medium hairstyle for your round face in 2021. Remember to consult with your hairstylist, experiment with different styles, and take care of your hair to maintain a healthy and beautiful look.
