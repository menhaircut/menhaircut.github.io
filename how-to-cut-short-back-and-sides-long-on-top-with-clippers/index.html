How To Cut Short Back And Sides Long On Top With Clippers

Description

Cutting short back and sides long on top with clippers is a trendy men's hairstyle that has been popular for years. This hairstyle is versatile and easy to maintain, making it a great option for those who prefer low-maintenance haircuts. With the right tools and technique, you can easily achieve this look at home without spending a lot of money at the salon.

How to

To cut short back and sides long on top with clippers, you will need a pair of clippers, guards of different lengths, a comb, and scissors. It is also helpful to have a handheld mirror to see the back of your head.

Step by Step

1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it is slightly damp. This will make it easier to cut.

2. Attach the longest guard to your clippers and start at the top of your head. Run the clippers through your hair in the direction of growth, moving from front to back.

3. Move to the sides of your head and switch to a shorter guard. Run the clippers up the sides of your head in a smooth, even motion.

4. Use the comb to blend the longer hair on top with the shorter hair on the sides. Hold the comb at an angle and run the clippers over it to create a gradual fade.

5. Repeat this process with progressively shorter guards until you have achieved the desired length on the sides.

6. Use the scissors to trim any longer strands on top that need to be blended in with the sides.

Tips

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to avoid cutting too much hair at once.

- Use a handheld mirror to check the back of your head for any uneven areas.

- Take breaks and step back to assess your progress. It's easy to get carried away and cut too much hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I cut my hair wet or dry?

A: It is best to cut your hair when it is slightly damp. This will make it easier to cut and ensure that you don't cut too much hair at once.

Q: How often should I cut my hair?

A: This will vary depending on how quickly your hair grows and how long you want to maintain the style. On average, you should plan to cut your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair, it is important to maintain it with regular washing and conditioning. Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using excessive heat styling tools and always use a heat protectant. Trim your hair regularly to maintain the style and prevent split ends.

Cutting short back and sides long on top with clippers is a simple and stylish haircut that you can easily achieve at home. With the right tools and technique, you can create a look that is both trendy and low-maintenance. Follow these steps and tips to achieve the perfect cut every time.
