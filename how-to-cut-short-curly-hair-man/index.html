How To Cut Short Curly Hair Man Tips Step By Step Guide And Hair Care

Written by Petter Oct 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short haircuts for curly hair are becoming more popular among men who want to rock a low-maintenance, effortless look. If you're looking for a stylish yet manageable haircut, you're in the right place. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut short curly hair man with ease. We'll cover everything from the tools you'll need, the step-by-step process, and hair care tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great.

Tools You'll Need

Before we dive into the process, let's talk about the essential tools you'll need to cut your hair at home. Here's what you'll need:

  • Clippers or scissors
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Water spray bottle
  • Haircutting shears
  • Clipper guards
  • Barber's brush

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Preparation

Start by preparing the area where you'll be cutting your hair. Cover the floor with a towel or cape to catch the hair. Make sure your tools are clean and sanitized. Wash your hair and dry it well before cutting.

Step 2: Determine Your Hair Length

Decide how short you want your hair to be. Use clipper guards or shears to achieve your desired length.

Step 3: Cut the Sides and Back

Start by cutting the sides and back of your hair. Use clippers or scissors to trim the hair evenly. Start with a higher guard number and work your way down to achieve the desired length. Use shears to blend the hair on the sides and back to create a natural look.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Use shears to cut the top of your hair. Start at the front and work your way to the back. Use your fingers to separate the hair and cut it to the desired length. Make sure the hair is evenly cut and blended with the sides and back.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

After cutting your hair, use a barber's brush to remove any loose hair. Style your hair using your preferred hair product. If you have trouble styling your hair, ask your stylist for recommendations.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your short curly hair:

  • Start with a higher guard number and work your way down to achieve the desired length.
  • Use shears to blend the hair on the sides and back to create a natural look.
  • Take your time and cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Use a water spray bottle to dampen your hair if it becomes too dry.
  • Don't cut your hair too short, as it may look uneven and unflattering.

FAQs

How often should I cut my short curly hair?

You should cut your hair every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape and style.

Can I cut my curly hair when it's wet?

It's best to cut curly hair when it's dry. Wet hair can stretch and appear longer than it is, making it difficult to achieve the desired length.

Can I cut my hair at home?

Yes, you can cut your hair at home if you have the right tools and follow the proper steps. However, if you're not confident in your skills, it's best to visit a professional stylist.

Hair Care

To keep your short curly hair looking great, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

Cutting short curly hair man is easy if you have the right tools and follow the proper steps. With our step-by-step guide, tips, and hair care advice, you'll be able to achieve a stylish and low-maintenance haircut that looks great on you. Happy cutting!
