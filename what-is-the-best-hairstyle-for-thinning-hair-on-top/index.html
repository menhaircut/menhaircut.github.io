What Is The Best Hairstyle For Thinning Hair On Top

Written by Aprilia Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Thinning hair on top is a common hair problem that affects both men and women. It can be caused by various factors such as genetics, age, stress, and certain medical conditions. If you are experiencing thinning hair on top, you might be wondering what the best hairstyle is to conceal it. In this article, we will discuss the best hairstyle for thinning hair on top, along with tips and tricks to care for your hair.

Description

Thinning hair on top can be a frustrating and embarrassing issue to deal with. It can affect your confidence and self-esteem, especially if you are used to having thick and full hair. While there is no cure for thinning hair, there are several hairstyles that can help to conceal it and make your hair look thicker and fuller.

How to

The best hairstyle for thinning hair on top is a short and layered cut. This type of haircut adds volume and texture to your hair, making it look thicker and fuller. You can also add some highlights or lowlights to create the illusion of depth and dimension. If you have a receding hairline, you can opt for a buzz cut or a shaved head. This look is trendy and stylish, and it can help to draw attention away from your thinning hair.

Step by Step

Step 1: Find a good hairstylist who specializes in cutting thinning hair. They can recommend the best haircut for your hair type and face shape.

Step 2: Choose a short and layered cut that adds volume and texture to your hair.

Step 3: Add some highlights or lowlights to create the illusion of depth and dimension.

Step 4: Experiment with different hair products such as volumizing shampoo, mousse, and hairspray to add more volume and hold to your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to care for your thinning hair:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals and heat styling tools that can damage your hair.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that promote hair growth.
  • Take supplements such as biotin and vitamin D that can help to strengthen your hair and prevent hair loss.
  • Massage your scalp regularly to stimulate blood flow and promote hair growth.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as ponytails and braids that can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

FAQ

Q: Can I still have long hair if I have thinning hair on top?

A: Yes, you can still have long hair if you have thinning hair on top. However, you should avoid having too much weight at the bottom of your hair, as it can create a flat and limp look. Opt for layers and texture to add volume and movement to your hair.

Q: Will coloring my hair make it look thicker?

A: Yes, coloring your hair can make it look thicker and more voluminous. Adding highlights and lowlights can create the illusion of depth and dimension, making your hair appear fuller.

Hair Care

Caring for your thinning hair is essential to promote hair growth and prevent further hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo that is free of sulfates and parabens.
  • Avoid hot showers and use lukewarm water to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or a soft brush to detangle your hair gently.
  • Avoid rubbing your hair vigorously with a towel. Instead, pat it dry with a soft towel or a microfiber cloth.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to harsh weather conditions such as sun, wind, and cold temperatures.

In conclusion, the best hairstyle for thinning hair on top is a short and layered cut that adds volume and texture to your hair. You can also experiment with highlights and lowlights to create the illusion of depth and dimension. Caring for your thinning hair is crucial to promote hair growth and prevent further hair loss. Follow the tips and tricks in this article to achieve thicker and fuller-looking hair.
