Hairstyles For Round Faces Over 60 A Guide

Written by Bardi Nov 12, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, our hair changes along with the rest of our body. For those with round faces over 60, finding a flattering hairstyle can be a challenge. But fear not, as there are plenty of options that can help enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and stylish. In this guide, we’ll explore various hairstyles and provide tips on how to achieve them.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

Before we dive into the hairstyles, it’s important to determine your face shape. To do this, look in the mirror and note which parts of your face are the widest. If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all roughly the same width, you likely have a round face shape. Other common face shapes include oval, square, heart, and diamond.

Hairstyles for Round Faces Over 60

Short Bob

A short bob can be a great option for those with round faces over 60. It helps to elongate the face and draw attention upwards. To achieve this style, ask your stylist for a bob that falls just below the chin. You can also add layers for more volume and texture.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is another option for those who want a low-maintenance style. It can help to create the illusion of a longer face and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for a short cut with tapered sides and layers on top.

Layered Shoulder-Length Cut

A layered shoulder-length cut is a versatile option that can work for a variety of face shapes. It helps to add volume and movement, and can be styled in various ways. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for long layers that frame your face.

Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can help to soften the look of a round face and draw attention to your eyes. To achieve this look, ask your stylist for bangs that start at the top of your forehead and sweep to one side.

Tips for Styling Your Hair

Once you’ve chosen a hairstyle, there are a few tips to keep in mind when styling your hair:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add texture and body to your hair.
  • Blow-dry your hair with a round brush to add volume and create a sleek look.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too sleek or flat, as they can make your face appear wider.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add dimension and depth to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have a round face?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have a round face. However, it’s important to add layers and texture to avoid a flat, wide look.

Q: Are bangs a good option for those with round faces over 60?

A: Yes, side-swept bangs can be a great option for those with round faces over 60. They help to soften the look of the face and draw attention to the eyes.

Hair Care Tips

As we age, our hair can become thinner and more brittle. To keep your hair healthy and strong, consider the following tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Consider taking a hair supplement, such as biotin, to support healthy hair growth.

Conclusion

There are plenty of hairstyles that can flatter those with round faces over 60. Whether you prefer a short cut or a longer style, there are options that can help enhance your natural beauty and make you feel confident and stylish. Remember to consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a style, and always take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong.
