How To Know If You Want Short Hair

Written by Eveline Jun 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of your long locks and are considering a new hairstyle? Short hair can be a great change, but it's not for everyone. Before taking the plunge and chopping off your hair, there are a few things to consider. In this article, we'll discuss how to know if you want short hair and the steps you can take to make the transition easier.

Table of Contents

  • How to Know If You Want Short Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

How to Know If You Want Short Hair

Short hair can be a refreshing change, but it's important to make sure it's the right choice for you. Here are a few things to consider:

Face Shape

Short hair looks great on all face shapes, but certain styles may be more flattering on you than others. If you have a round face, a shorter layered cut can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a bob with soft layers can help soften your features. If you have an oval face, lucky you! You can pretty much pull off any short hairstyle.

Hair Texture

Short hair requires more maintenance, so if you have thick and curly hair, you may need to spend more time styling it. If you have fine hair, a shorter cut can help give the illusion of thicker hair. If you have straight hair, a short bob can be a great option.

Lifestyle

Consider your lifestyle before getting a short cut. Do you have time to style your hair every morning? If not, a low-maintenance style may be a better option. Do you work in a professional environment? Make sure your new cut is appropriate for your workplace.

Step by Step

If you've decided that short hair is the right choice for you, here are some steps you can take to make the transition easier:

Research

Look at pictures of short hairstyles to get an idea of what you like. Bring these pictures to your stylist to help them understand your vision.

Consultation

Before getting your hair cut, schedule a consultation with your stylist. They can help you determine what style will work best for your face shape and hair texture.

Maintenance

Short hair requires more maintenance than long hair. Make sure you have the time and resources to maintain your new style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when deciding if short hair is right for you:

  • Consider your face shape and hair texture
  • Think about your lifestyle and how much time you have to style your hair
  • Research short hairstyles before getting a cut
  • Schedule a consultation with your stylist before making the change
  • Remember that short hair requires more maintenance than long hair

FAQ

Will short hair look good on me?

Short hair can look great on anyone, regardless of face shape or hair texture. It's all about finding the right style for you.

Can I still style my short hair?

Absolutely! There are plenty of styling options for short hair, including braids, updos, and adding accessories.

How often will I need to get my hair cut?

It depends on the style you choose and how fast your hair grows. A pixie cut may need to be trimmed every 4-6 weeks, while a bob can last 8-12 weeks.

Hair Care

Short hair requires different hair care than long hair. Here are some tips to keep your short locks looking their best:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to give your hair lift and texture
  • Invest in a good hair styling product, like a pomade or wax, to help hold your style in place
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to keep your style looking fresh

Short hair can be a great change, but it's important to make sure it's the right choice for you. Consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle before getting a cut. Do your research, schedule a consultation with your stylist, and remember that short hair requires more maintenance than long hair. Follow these tips and you'll have a stylish and low-maintenance new do in no time!
