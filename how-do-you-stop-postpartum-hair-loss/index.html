How Do You Stop Postpartum Hair Loss A Complete Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 21, 2023 · 3 min read

Postpartum hair loss is a common occurrence among new mothers. It happens due to hormonal changes in the body after giving birth. While it is a natural process, it can be frustrating for many women. If you are experiencing postpartum hair loss, don’t worry; there are ways to stop it. In this article, we will discuss everything you need to know about stopping postpartum hair loss.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Stop Postpartum Hair Loss
  • Step by Step Guide

Description

Postpartum hair loss is a common condition that affects many new mothers. It usually starts three months after giving birth and can last up to a year. The condition is caused by hormonal changes in the body, which can cause hair to fall out. While postpartum hair loss is normal, it can be distressing for many women. Fortunately, there are ways to stop postpartum hair loss.

How to Stop Postpartum Hair Loss

The first step in stopping postpartum hair loss is to understand the cause. Postpartum hair loss is caused by hormonal changes in the body. After giving birth, estrogen levels drop, which can cause hair to fall out. To stop postpartum hair loss, you need to address the hormonal imbalance in your body. Here are a few ways to do it:

Diet

Eating a healthy diet can help balance hormones in the body. Make sure you eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein. Avoid processed foods and sugary drinks.

Vitamins

Taking vitamins can also help balance hormones. Vitamin D and Biotin are especially helpful for hair growth. Consult with your doctor before taking any supplements.

Hair Products

Using the right hair products can also help stop postpartum hair loss. Look for products that are gentle on your hair and scalp. Avoid products with harsh chemicals and sulfates.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to stop postpartum hair loss:

  1. Eat a healthy diet
  2. Take vitamins
  3. Use gentle hair products
  4. Avoid tight hairstyles
  5. Don’t use heat styling tools
  6. Massage your scalp
  7. Try essential oils
  8. Get enough sleep
  9. Manage stress
  10. Consult with your doctor

Tips to Prevent Postpartum Hair Loss

Here are some tips to help prevent postpartum hair loss:

  • Eat a healthy diet
  • Take vitamins
  • Use gentle hair products
  • Avoid tight hairstyles
  • Don’t use heat styling tools
  • Massage your scalp
  • Try essential oils
  • Get enough sleep
  • Manage stress

FAQs

Q: How long does postpartum hair loss last?

A: Postpartum hair loss usually starts three months after giving birth and can last up to a year.

Q: Can postpartum hair loss be prevented?

A: While postpartum hair loss cannot be prevented, there are ways to reduce its severity.

Q: Is postpartum hair loss normal?

A: Yes, postpartum hair loss is a normal condition that affects many new mothers.

Hair Care for Postpartum Hair Loss

Here are some hair care tips for postpartum hair loss:

  • Use a gentle shampoo
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and sulfates
  • Don’t use heat styling tools
  • Avoid tight hairstyles
  • Massage your scalp
  • Try essential oils

Postpartum hair loss can be frustrating, but it is a normal condition. By following the tips and advice in this article, you can reduce the severity of postpartum hair loss and promote healthy hair growth.
