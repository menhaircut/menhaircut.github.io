How To Wear Short Layered Hair Up A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Sep 04, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle with your short layered hair? Why not take it up a notch and wear it up? Wearing your hair up can give you a polished and sophisticated look, and it’s perfect for both formal and casual occasions. In this article, we’ll show you how to wear short layered hair up in a few easy steps.

Table of Contents

  • How to Wear Short Layered Hair Up
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Wearing Short Layered Hair Up
  • FAQ

How to Wear Short Layered Hair Up

Wearing short layered hair up can be a challenge, but with the right technique, anyone can do it. Here are some ways to wear your short layered hair up:

Ponytail

A simple ponytail is a classic way to wear short layered hair up. Gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair elastic. You can keep it sleek or add some texture by teasing the crown or adding braids.

Bun

A bun is a chic and elegant way to wear short layered hair up. Gather your hair at the back of your head and twist it into a bun. Secure it with bobby pins or a hair elastic. You can make it messy or slick, depending on the occasion.

Braids

Braids are a fun and creative way to wear short layered hair up. You can braid your hair into a crown, a French braid, or a fishtail braid. Add some texture by teasing the crown or pulling out some strands for a messy look.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to wear short layered hair up:

Step 1: Prep your hair

Start by washing and conditioning your hair. Apply a styling product to add texture and volume to your hair. Blow-dry your hair and use a flat iron to straighten it if necessary.

Step 2: Gather your hair

Gather your hair at the back of your head and secure it with a hair elastic. Make sure it’s tight enough to hold your hair in place but not too tight that it hurts.

Step 3: Create your style

Depending on the style you want, you can twist your hair into a bun, braid it, or leave it as a simple ponytail. Use bobby pins or a hair elastic to secure your style in place.

Step 4: Finish with hairspray

Finish your look with a light mist of hairspray to hold your style in place. Avoid using too much hairspray as it can weigh down your hair and make it look stiff.

Tips for Wearing Short Layered Hair Up

Here are some tips to keep in mind when wearing short layered hair up:

  • Use bobby pins or a hair elastic to secure your style in place.
  • Add texture to your hair by teasing the crown or adding braids.
  • Use hair accessories like headbands or hair clips to add some flair to your look.
  • Avoid using too much hairspray as it can weigh down your hair and make it look stiff.

FAQ

Can I wear my short layered hair up for a formal occasion?

Yes, you can wear your short layered hair up for a formal occasion. A bun or a braided updo can give you a polished and sophisticated look.

Can I wear my short layered hair up for a casual occasion?

Yes, you can wear your short layered hair up for a casual occasion. A messy bun or a simple ponytail can give you a relaxed and effortless look.

Can I wear my short layered hair up if I have a short neck?

Yes, you can wear your short layered hair up even if you have a short neck. Just make sure to keep your hair loose and avoid tight updos that can make your neck look shorter.

Hair Care for Short Layered Hair

Here are some tips to keep your short layered hair healthy and shiny:

  • Wash your hair every other day to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage it. If you need to use a heat styling tool, use a heat protectant spray.
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote hair growth and health.

Now that you know how to wear short layered hair up, you can experiment with different styles and find the perfect one for you. Whether you’re going to a formal event or just running errands, wearing your hair up can give you a polished and sophisticated look.
