How To Be A Barber In Texas The Ultimate Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

Becoming a barber in Texas is an exciting and rewarding career choice. Not only do you get to work with hair and create amazing styles, but you also get to interact with people and build a loyal clientele. In this guide, we’ll go over everything you need to know about how to be a barber in Texas, including requirements, steps, tips, FAQs, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • Requirements
  • Steps
  • Tips
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care

Requirements

Before you start your journey to become a licensed barber in Texas, you must meet certain requirements:

  • Be at least 17 years old
  • Have a high school diploma or GED
  • Complete a 1500-hour barbering program at a licensed school or complete a 300-hour barbering program and have at least 1 year of experience as a licensed barber in another state
  • Pass the Texas Barbering written and practical exams

Once you meet these requirements, you can begin the process of becoming a licensed barber in Texas.

Steps

Here are the steps you need to follow to become a licensed barber in Texas:

Step 1: Find a Licensed Barbering School

Look for a licensed barbering school in Texas that offers a 1500-hour barbering program. You can find a list of licensed schools on the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) website. Make sure the school you choose is accredited and has a good reputation.

Step 2: Enroll in the Barbering Program

Once you’ve found a licensed barbering school, enroll in the 1500-hour barbering program. This program will teach you everything you need to know about hair cutting, styling, shaving, and other essential skills. You’ll also learn about sanitation, safety, and customer service.

Step 3: Complete the Barbering Program

Complete the 1500-hour barbering program. Make sure you attend all classes and meet all the requirements of the program. You’ll also need to complete a certain number of haircuts and shaves under the supervision of a licensed instructor.

Step 4: Pass the Written and Practical Exams

After completing the barbering program, you’ll need to pass the Texas Barbering written and practical exams. These exams test your knowledge and skills in hair cutting, styling, shaving, sanitation, safety, and customer service. You’ll need to pass both exams to get your license.

Step 5: Apply for Your License

Once you’ve passed the exams, you can apply for your Texas Barbering license. You’ll need to submit an application, pay a fee, and provide proof of your education and exam scores. Once your application is approved, you’ll receive your license in the mail.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you succeed as a licensed barber in Texas:

  • Always keep your tools clean and sanitized to prevent the spread of germs and infections.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest hair cutting and styling trends to keep your clients happy and satisfied.
  • Build a loyal clientele by providing excellent customer service and creating a welcoming atmosphere in your shop.
  • Join professional organizations and attend industry events to network with other barbers and stay informed about the latest trends and techniques.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to be a barber in Texas:

What is the average salary of a licensed barber in Texas?

The average salary of a licensed barber in Texas is around $34,000 per year, but this can vary depending on factors such as location, experience, and clientele.

How long does it take to become a licensed barber in Texas?

It typically takes about 1-2 years to become a licensed barber in Texas, depending on whether you choose a 1500-hour or 300-hour program.

What are the benefits of becoming a licensed barber in Texas?

Some benefits of becoming a licensed barber in Texas include being able to work in a creative and rewarding field, building a loyal clientele, and having the potential to earn a good salary.

Hair Care

As a licensed barber in Texas, it’s important to know how to take care of your clients’ hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use high-quality hair products that are suited to your clients’ hair type and needs.
  • Encourage your clients to maintain a healthy diet and lifestyle, as this can improve the health of their hair.
  • Teach your clients how to style and care for their hair at home, so they can maintain their look between appointments.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest hair care products and techniques, so you can provide the best possible service to your clients.

By following these tips and staying committed to your craft, you can become a successful and respected licensed barber in Texas.
