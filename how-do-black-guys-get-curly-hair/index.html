How Do Black Guys Get Curly Hair A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair has always been a coveted feature, but for black guys, it can be a bit challenging to achieve. In this article, we will discuss how black guys can get curly hair and maintain it. Here’s what you need to know:

Table of Contents

  • How to Get Curly Hair as a Black Guy
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Curly Hair
  • Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair

How to Get Curly Hair as a Black Guy

Black guys can get curly hair using a variety of methods, including:

  • Using a texturizer or relaxer
  • Getting a perm
  • Using a curling iron or wand
  • Twisting or braiding the hair

Each method has its pros and cons, and it’s important to choose the one that works best for your hair type and lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting Curly Hair

If you decide to use a texturizer or relaxer to get curly hair, here are the steps to follow:

  1. Shampoo your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply the texturizer or relaxer to your hair, following the instructions on the package.
  3. Leave the product on for the recommended amount of time.
  4. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  5. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.
  6. Rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  7. Towel dry your hair and style it as desired.

If you decide to get a perm, here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Apply the perm solution to your hair, following the instructions on the package.
  3. Wrap your hair around perm rods.
  4. Apply the neutralizer to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes.
  5. Remove the perm rods and rinse your hair thoroughly with warm water.
  6. Towel dry your hair and style it as desired.

If you decide to use a curling iron or wand, here are the steps to follow:

  1. Apply a heat protectant to your hair.
  2. Section your hair and start curling it with the iron or wand.
  3. Hold each curl for a few seconds and release it.
  4. Repeat until all of your hair is curled.
  5. Apply a styling product to your hair to hold the curls.

If you decide to twist or braid your hair, here are the steps to follow:

  1. Wash your hair and towel dry it.
  2. Section your hair and twist or braid each section.
  3. Leave the twists or braids in for a few hours or overnight.
  4. Untwist or unbraid your hair and style it as desired.

Tips for Maintaining Curly Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to prevent frizz.
  • Sleep with a satin or silk pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curly hair as a black guy:

  • Can I get curly hair without using chemicals? Yes, you can twist or braid your hair to create curls without using chemicals.
  • How long does a texturizer or relaxer last? A texturizer or relaxer can last for 6 to 8 weeks.
  • Can I use a texturizer or relaxer on colored hair? It’s not recommended to use a texturizer or relaxer on colored hair, as it can cause damage.
  • How often should I wash my curly hair? It’s best to wash your curly hair once or twice a week to prevent it from drying out.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips specifically for curly hair:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid using a brush, as it can cause frizz.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment to your hair once a week.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Avoid using hair products that contain alcohol, as it can dry out your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, black guys can achieve and maintain curly hair that looks healthy and vibrant.
