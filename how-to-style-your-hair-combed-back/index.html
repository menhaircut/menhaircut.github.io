How To Style Your Hair Combed Back A Relaxed Guide

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle but don't know where to start? Look no further than the classic combed-back style. This versatile look can be dressed up or down and is perfect for any occasion. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the combed-back hairstyle and provide tips for maintaining it. Let's get started!

  • How to Style Your Hair Combed Back
  • Step-by-Step Guide

How to Style Your Hair Combed Back

The combed-back hairstyle is achieved by brushing your hair back away from your face. This style can be worn with short or long hair and looks great on both men and women. Here are the steps:

Step 1: Start with Clean Hair

It's important to start with clean hair to achieve the best results. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and nourish your hair.

Step 2: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your fingertips and work it through your hair. This will help to hold your hair in place and add shine.

Step 3: Brush Your Hair Back

Using a hairbrush or comb, brush your hair back away from your face. Start at the front and work your way to the back, ensuring that all of your hair is brushed back smoothly.

Step 4: Style as Desired

Once your hair is combed back, you can style it as desired. You can leave it slicked back for a classic look, or add some volume and texture for a more modern twist.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a more detailed step-by-step guide for achieving the combed-back hairstyle:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. This will remove any dirt and oil from your hair and prepare it for styling.

Step 2: Dry Your Hair

Use a towel to dry your hair, but avoid rubbing it vigorously as this can cause damage. Instead, gently pat your hair dry until it's damp.

Step 3: Apply Styling Product

Apply a small amount of styling product, such as pomade or wax, to your fingertips and work it through your hair. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

Step 4: Comb Your Hair Back

Using a hairbrush or comb, comb your hair back away from your face. Start at the front and work your way to the back, ensuring that your hair is smooth and free of tangles.

Step 5: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is combed back, you can style it as desired. You can leave it slicked back for a classic look, or add some volume and texture for a more modern twist. Use your fingers to add texture and shape to your hair.

Tips for Maintaining the Style

Here are some tips for maintaining your combed-back hairstyle:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and nourished.
  • Avoid overusing styling products, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking its best.

FAQs

Q: Can I achieve the combed-back hairstyle with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve the combed-back hairstyle with curly hair. Use a styling product that's designed for curly hair to help define your curls and hold them in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair?

A: It's best to wash your hair every other day to prevent stripping it of its natural oils. If you have particularly oily hair, you may need to wash it more frequently.

Hair Care for Combed-Back Hair

To keep your hair healthy and looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and nourish your hair.
  • Avoid overusing styling products, as this can make your hair look greasy and weighed down.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims and touch-ups to keep your hair looking its best.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as straighteners and curling irons, too frequently as this can cause damage to your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to achieve the perfect combed-back hairstyle and keep it looking great. Give it a try and see how it works for you!
