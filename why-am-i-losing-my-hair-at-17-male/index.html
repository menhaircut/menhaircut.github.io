Why Am I Losing My Hair At 17 Male

Written by Robby Jun 09, 2023 · 3 min read

Description | How to | Step by step | Tips | FAQ | Hair Care

Description

Losing hair at 17 as a male can be alarming and concerning. Hair loss or male pattern baldness is a common condition that affects a lot of men. The condition is usually associated with aging, but it can happen to younger men as well. Male pattern baldness is characterized by gradual hair loss, usually starting at the temples and crown of the head. It is a genetic condition that is inherited from your parents, and there is no known cure for it. However, there are ways to slow down the process and maintain the hair that you have.

How to

If you are losing hair at 17 as a male, there are a few things that you can do to slow down the process and maintain the hair that you have:

Step by step

  1. Get a proper diagnosis: Before you start any treatment, it is important to get a proper diagnosis from a dermatologist or hair specialist. They can determine the cause of your hair loss and recommend the best treatment plan for you.
  2. Take care of your scalp: A healthy scalp is essential for healthy hair growth. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid harsh chemicals and heat styling tools.
  3. Eat a healthy diet: A diet rich in vitamins and minerals is important for healthy hair growth. Eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, and lean protein.
  4. Exercise regularly: Regular exercise can improve blood flow to the scalp and promote healthy hair growth.
  5. Consider hair loss treatments: There are several hair loss treatments available, including medications, hair transplants, and scalp micropigmentation. Talk to your doctor about which treatment may be best for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help slow down hair loss:

  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair, such as braids, ponytails, and cornrows.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can contribute to hair loss.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes.
  • Avoid stress, as it can contribute to hair loss. Try relaxation techniques such as yoga and meditation.

FAQ

Q: Is it normal to lose hair at 17?

A: While it is not common, it is possible to experience hair loss at a young age. Male pattern baldness is usually hereditary and can start as early as the teenage years.

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss. When you are stressed, your body produces hormones that can affect hair growth.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot water on your hair, as it can damage the hair follicles.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach and hair dyes.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage the hair.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.

Remember, losing hair at 17 as a male can be concerning, but there are ways to slow down the process and maintain healthy hair. Talk to your doctor about the best treatment plan for you.
