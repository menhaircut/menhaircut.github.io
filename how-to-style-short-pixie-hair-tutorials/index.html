How To Style Short Pixie Hair Tutorials A Beginner s Guide

If you've recently cut your hair into a short pixie style, you may be wondering how to style it. With the right tools and techniques, you can create a variety of looks that range from sophisticated to playful. In this article, we'll provide step-by-step instructions, tips, and hair care advice to help you style your short pixie hair.

How to Style Short Pixie Hair Tutorials

There are many ways to style short pixie hair, including:

  • Using a flat iron to create sleek, straight hair
  • Adding texture with a curling iron or wand
  • Tousling hair with your fingers to create a messy, undone look
  • Accessorizing with headbands, clips, or scarves
  • Creating a half-up, half-down style

Whatever look you're going for, it's important to start with clean, dry hair that's been brushed through to remove any tangles.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your short pixie hair:

  1. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to prevent damage from styling tools.
  2. Using a flat iron, straighten your hair in small sections, starting at the back and working your way to the front. For a sleek look, run the flat iron over each section twice.
  3. If you want to add texture, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls. Wrap small sections of hair around the barrel, hold for a few seconds, and release.
  4. To create a messy, undone look, tousle your hair with your fingers, scrunching and twisting sections as desired.
  5. If you're adding accessories, place them in your hair and secure with bobby pins or clips.
  6. For a half-up, half-down style, use a comb to section off the top half of your hair, leaving the bottom half loose. Secure the top half with a clip or elastic band.
  7. Spritz your hair with a light-hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Styling Short Pixie Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short pixie hair:

  • Invest in quality styling tools, such as a flat iron, curling iron, or wand.
  • Experiment with different products, such as texturizing spray or sea salt spray, to achieve the look you want.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles or accessories.
  • If you're struggling to style your hair, consider visiting a professional stylist for advice or a trim.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my short pixie hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and personal preference, but most people find that washing their hair every 2-3 days is sufficient. Over-washing can strip your hair of natural oils and cause it to become dry and brittle.

Q: How do I keep my short pixie hair looking fresh?

A: Regular trims and touch-ups can help keep your hair looking neat and tidy. You can also use dry shampoo to absorb excess oil and freshen up your hair between washes.

Q: Can I still style my short pixie hair if I have fine or thin hair?

A: Absolutely! There are many styles and techniques that work well with fine or thin hair, such as adding volume with a round brush or creating waves with a curling iron.

Hair Care for Short Pixie Hair

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your short pixie hair looking healthy and vibrant. Here are some tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.
  • Regularly trim your hair to prevent split ends and promote healthy growth.

With these tips and techniques, you can confidently style your short pixie hair and enjoy a variety of different looks. Whether you prefer a sleek and sophisticated style or a playful and messy look, there's a style that's perfect for you.
