How To Cut Korean Fringe A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking for a new hairstyle that's trendy and easy to maintain? Consider cutting Korean fringe! Korean fringe, also known as "see-through" bangs, is a popular hairstyle that frames the face and creates a soft, feminine look. In this guide, we'll show you how to cut Korean fringe at home.

How to Cut Korean Fringe

Before we begin, gather the following tools:

  • Sharp scissors
  • Comb
  • Clips
  • Spray bottle with water

Step 1: Section Your Hair

Start by sectioning your hair. Use your comb to draw a diagonal line from the outer corner of your eyebrow to the middle of your scalp. Clip the top section of your hair away from your face.

Step 2: Wet Your Bangs

Spray your bangs with water. Wetting your hair will make it easier to cut and prevent split ends.

Step 3: Cut Your Bangs

Hold a small section of your bangs between your fingers. Cut your hair in a straight line, starting from the center of your forehead and working towards the outer corners of your eyebrows. Be sure to cut your hair at an angle to create a soft, layered look.

Repeat this process with the rest of your bangs, making sure to cut each section at the same angle.

Step 4: Check Your Bangs

After cutting your bangs, check to make sure they're even. If you notice any uneven areas, trim them with your scissors.

Step 5: Style Your Bangs

Use a hair dryer and a round brush to style your bangs. Blow dry your hair in a downward motion and use the brush to shape your bangs.

Tips for Cutting Korean Fringe

  • Start with a small section of hair and cut slowly.
  • Don't cut your hair too short. You can always trim more later.
  • If you're unsure about cutting your own hair, ask a friend or visit a salon.
  • Use sharp scissors to prevent split ends.

FAQ

Can I cut Korean fringe with curly hair?

Yes, you can cut Korean fringe with curly hair. However, it's important to remember that curly hair tends to shrink when it dries. Cut your hair when it's dry to prevent cutting too much.

How often should I trim my Korean fringe?

You should trim your Korean fringe every 4-6 weeks to maintain its shape.

Hair Care for Korean Fringe

To keep your Korean fringe looking its best, use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot tools on your hair, such as a straightener or curling iron, as they can damage your hair. Instead, use a hair dryer and a round brush to style your hair.

Now that you know how to cut Korean fringe, give it a try! With a little practice, you'll be able to create a trendy, low-maintenance hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion.
