Hairstyles For Full Faces Over 50 Look Beautiful And Confident

Written by Frank Nov 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for hairstyles that flatter your full face and make you look younger and stylish? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we will share some tips and ideas on how to choose the best hairstyles for full faces over 50. From short pixie cuts to long layers, we've got something for everyone. Let's get started!

Description

As we age, our faces tend to lose volume and become fuller. This can make it challenging to find the right hairstyle that complements our face shape and adds dimension to our features. However, with the right hairstyle, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident and gorgeous.

How to Choose the Right Hairstyle for Full Faces Over 50

When it comes to choosing the best hairstyle for full faces over 50, there are a few things to keep in mind. Here are some tips to help you make the right choice:

1. Look for Hairstyles with Layers

Layers can add depth and texture to your hair, making it appear fuller and more voluminous. If you have a full face, layers can also help to create an illusion of a slimmer face.

2. Consider Shorter Hairstyles

Shorter hairstyles like pixie cuts or bobs can be a great option for full faces over 50. These hairstyles can help to balance out your face shape and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

3. Avoid Heavy Bangs

While bangs can be a great way to change up your look, heavy bangs can make your face appear even fuller. Instead, opt for wispy or side-swept bangs that can frame your face and add dimension without adding too much volume.

Step-by-Step Guide: How to Achieve the Perfect Hairstyle for Full Faces Over 50

Now that you have an idea of what to look for in a hairstyle, here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look:

Step 1: Consult with Your Hairstylist

Before making any drastic changes to your hair, it's essential to consult with your hairstylist. They can help you choose the best hairstyle for your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Choose the Right Length

Depending on your face shape and personal style, you can choose a short, medium, or long hairstyle. Just make sure that the length complements your face shape and adds dimension to your features.

Step 3: Add Layers

Adding layers can help to create movement and volume in your hair. Your hairstylist can add layers that frame your face and draw attention to your best features.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once you have the perfect haircut, it's time to style your hair. You can use a blow dryer, curling iron, or straightener to create your desired look.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hairstyle

Now that you have your perfect hairstyle, here are some tips to help you maintain it:

1. Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products can help to keep your hair healthy and shiny. Make sure to choose products that are designed for your hair type and avoid using too many styling products that can weigh down your hair.

2. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims can help to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends. Make sure to schedule regular appointments with your hairstylist to maintain your hairstyle.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Using heat styling tools can damage your hair over time. Make sure to use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools to protect your hair from damage.

FAQs

Q: Can I still wear long hair if I have a full face?

A: Yes, you can still wear long hair if you have a full face. Just make sure to add layers to create movement and avoid hairstyles that add too much volume to your hair.

Q: What are the best hairstyles for thin hair?

A: The best hairstyles for thin hair are those that add volume and texture to your hair. Short pixie cuts, layered bobs, and long layers are great options for thin hair.

Q: How can I make my hair look thicker?

A: You can make your hair look thicker by adding layers, using volumizing products, and avoiding heavy styling products that can weigh down your hair.

Hair Care Tips for Full Faces Over 50

Here are some hair care tips to help you keep your hair healthy and beautiful:

1. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly can help to keep your hair clean and healthy. Make sure to use a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for your hair type.

2. Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help to nourish and hydrate your hair, making it look and feel healthier.

3. Avoid Heat Styling

Avoiding heat styling can help to prevent damage to your hair. Instead, opt for air-drying your hair or using heatless styling methods like braids or twists.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hairstyle for full faces over 50 can be challenging, but with the right tips and ideas, you can find a hairstyle that flatters your face shape and makes you look and feel confident and beautiful. Whether you prefer short hairstyles or long layers, there's a hairstyle out there that's perfect for you. Remember to consult with your hairstylist and maintain your hair regularly to keep it healthy and beautiful.
