Black Women s Haircuts Long On Top Short Back And Sides A Guide

Written by Robby Jun 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a bold and stylish haircut, then the long on top short back and sides is a perfect option for black women. This haircut features a longer top section and shorter sides and back. It's a versatile style that can be tailored to suit your individual preferences and face shape. Here's everything you need to know about black women's haircuts long on top short back and sides.

Description

The long on top short back and sides haircut is a popular style that has been around for decades. It's a versatile haircut that can be adapted to suit different face shapes and hair types. The top section of the hair is left longer, while the sides and back are cut short. This creates a stylish and edgy look that is perfect for black women who want to make a statement.

How to

If you're thinking about getting a black women's haircut long on top short back and sides, then here's a step-by-step guide to help you achieve the perfect look.

Step 1: Consultation

The first step is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to advise you on the best haircut to suit your face shape and hair type.

Step 2: Preparation

Before the haircut, your hair will be washed and dried. This will help the hairstylist to see your hair's natural texture and shape.

Step 3: Cutting the Top Section

The hairstylist will start by cutting the top section of your hair. They will trim it to the desired length, which could be anywhere from a few inches to shoulder length.

Step 4: Cutting the Sides and Back

Next, the hairstylist will cut the sides and back of your hair. They will use clippers to create a short, tapered look that blends seamlessly into the longer top section.

Step 5: Styling

Finally, the hairstylist will style your hair to finish off the look. They may use a styling product to add volume and texture to the top section of your hair.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you maintain your black women's haircut long on top short back and sides.

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot styling tools.
  • Visit your hairstylist regularly for trims to keep your haircut looking fresh.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about black women's haircuts long on top short back and sides.

Q: Will this haircut suit my face shape?

A: Yes, this haircut can be adapted to suit different face shapes. Your hairstylist will be able to advise you on the best haircut for your face shape.

Q: Can I style my hair in different ways?

A: Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be styled in different ways. You can use a styling product to add volume and texture, or you can wear it slicked back for a more polished look.

Q: How often should I get a trim?

A: It's recommended to get a trim every 6-8 weeks to keep your haircut looking fresh.

Hair Care

Here are some hair care tips to keep your black women's haircut long on top short back and sides looking its best.

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With these tips and tricks, you can maintain your black women's haircut long on top short back and sides and rock it with confidence. Whether you prefer a bold and edgy look or a more polished style, this haircut is perfect for black women who want to make a statement.
