What Length Is A Barber s Number 2

Written by Petter Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you've ever been to a barber or hair salon, you may have heard the term "number 2" being thrown around. But what exactly does it mean and what length is a barber's number 2? In this article, we'll break it down for you in simple terms.

Description

A barber's number 2 refers to the length of the hair clipper guard that is used to cut the hair. The number 2 guard is typically 1/4 inch long or 6mm. This means that when the hair is cut with a number 2 guard, it will leave the hair at around 1/4 inch or 6mm in length.

How to Use a Number 2 Guard

If you're looking to cut your hair at home or want to give your barber specific instructions, here's how to use a number 2 guard:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clipper

Make sure you have a clipper that is compatible with the number 2 guard. Most clippers come with a set of guards, but if you're unsure, check the manual or ask your barber for advice.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will make it easier to cut and ensure a more even finish.

Step 3: Attach the Guard

Attach the number 2 guard to your clipper. Make sure it clicks into place securely and is positioned the right way up.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Using the clipper, start cutting your hair against the direction of growth. Use long, smooth strokes and try to keep the clipper at a consistent angle. Start at the bottom and work your way up, taking care around the ears and neckline.

Step 5: Check the Length

Once you've finished cutting, check the length of your hair. If it's too long, you can go over it again with the clipper. If it's too short, you may need to wait for it to grow out or use a longer guard next time.

Tips for Using a Number 2 Guard

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your number 2 guard:

  • Always start with a clean and dry head of hair
  • Use long, smooth strokes for a more even finish
  • Take care around the ears and neckline
  • Check the length regularly to avoid cutting too much off
  • Experiment with different guards to find the perfect length for you

FAQ

What other lengths can I get with hair clipper guards?

Hair clipper guards come in a range of lengths, from very short (number 0) to very long (number 8 or higher). Each guard corresponds to a specific length, so you can experiment and find the perfect length for your hair.

Can I use a number 2 guard on any type of hair?

Yes, the number 2 guard can be used on all hair types, from straight to curly. However, if you have very thick or coarse hair, you may need to go over it a few times to get an even finish.

Can I cut my own hair with a number 2 guard?

Yes, you can cut your own hair with a number 2 guard, but it can be tricky to get an even finish at the back. It's best to have someone else help you or go to a professional barber if you're unsure.

Hair Care

After cutting your hair with a number 2 guard, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo
  • Condition your hair to keep it soft and smooth
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it looking neat

So there you have it, everything you need to know about what length is a barber's number 2. Whether you're cutting your hair at home or giving your barber specific instructions, the number 2 guard is a great choice for a neat, tidy look.
