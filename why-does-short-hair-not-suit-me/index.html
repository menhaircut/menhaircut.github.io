Why Does Short Hair Not Suit Me Understanding The Factors

Written by Oliver Jul 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short hair is a trendy and convenient hairstyle that many women opt for. It looks chic, sophisticated, and requires less maintenance. However, not everyone can pull off a short haircut. If you have ever tried a short hairstyle and felt that it didn't suit you, then you are not alone. In this blog post, we will discuss the reasons why short hair may not suit you and what you can do about it.

Table of Contents

  • Face Shape
  • Hair Texture
  • Personal Style
  • Tips for Choosing the Right Short Haircut
  • FAQs

Face Shape

One of the primary factors that determine whether short hair will suit you is your face shape. Some short hairstyles look great on oval or heart-shaped faces, while others may not look as flattering on round or square faces. For instance, a short pixie cut would look stunning on someone with an oval-shaped face, but it may not suit someone with a round face as it can make their face look wider.

If you are unsure about your face shape, you can consult with a hairstylist or use online face shape analysis tools to determine your face shape. Once you know your face shape, you can choose a short hairstyle that complements your features.

Hair Texture

Another factor that plays a crucial role in determining whether short hair will suit you is your hair texture. Short haircuts work best on hair that is naturally straight or has a slight wave to it. If you have curly or kinky hair, a short hairstyle may not suit you as it can be challenging to manage and style.

If you still want to try a short hairstyle, you can opt for a layered cut that works well with curly hair. This will help add volume and definition to your curls, making them look more manageable.

Personal Style

Your personal style also plays a significant role in determining whether short hair will suit you. If you prefer a more casual and laid-back look, a short hairstyle may not be the best choice for you. On the other hand, if you love experimenting with your hair and enjoy trying out new styles, then a short haircut may be perfect for you.

It is essential to choose a short hairstyle that reflects your personality and complements your personal style. If you are unsure about what kind of short hairstyle would suit you, you can always consult with a hairstylist or look for inspiration online.

Tips for Choosing the Right Short Haircut

If you still want to try a short hairstyle, here are some tips to help you choose the right one:

  • Consider your face shape and hair texture
  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your personal style
  • Consult with a hairstylist before making any drastic changes
  • Look for inspiration online and bring photos to your hairstylist

FAQs

Can anyone pull off a short hairstyle?

No, not everyone can pull off a short hairstyle. It depends on your face shape, hair texture, and personal style.

What kind of short hairstyle would suit someone with a round face?

A layered bob or a long pixie cut with side-swept bangs would look great on someone with a round face.

How can I make my short hairstyle last longer?

Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner, avoid using too much heat, and get regular trims to keep your hairstyle looking fresh.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires less maintenance than long hair, but it still needs proper care to look its best. Here are some hair care tips for short hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your hair

In conclusion, short hair may not suit everyone, but with the right hairstyle and proper care, you can rock a short haircut that complements your features and reflects your personality.
