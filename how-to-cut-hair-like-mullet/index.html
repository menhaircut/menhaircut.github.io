How To Cut Hair Like Mullet A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to try out a new hairstyle and want to channel your inner '80s rocker, then cutting your hair like a mullet may be the perfect option for you. This iconic hairstyle features short hair in the front and long hair in the back, creating a unique and edgy look. In this guide, we'll go through the steps of how to cut hair like mullet, as well as some tips and tricks to make sure your hair looks perfect.

How to Cut Hair Like Mullet: Step-by-Step

Before you begin, gather all the necessary tools, including scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It's also helpful to have a reference photo of the mullet style you want to achieve.

Step 1: Wet Your Hair

Start by wetting your hair so it's easier to work with. Comb through your hair to get rid of any tangles.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using a comb, section your hair into three parts: the top, the sides, and the back. Clip each section to keep them separate.

Step 3: Cut the Top

Starting with the top section, cut your hair to your desired length. Keep in mind that the front should be shorter than the back.

Step 4: Cut the Sides

Next, move on to the side sections. You want to cut these sections shorter than the top, but longer than the back. Use your reference photo to guide you.

Step 5: Cut the Back

Finally, move on to the back section. This is where the length of the mullet comes from, so be sure to cut it to your desired length. You can make it as long or as short as you'd like.

Step 6: Blend the Layers

Once you've cut all the sections, use scissors to blend the layers together. This will create a seamless transition from the shorter hair in the front to the longer hair in the back.

Step 7: Refine the Cut

Take a step back and look at your hair in the mirror. If you see any areas that need to be refined, use your scissors to make any necessary adjustments.

Step 8: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is cut like a mullet, it's time to style it. You can use a hair dryer, a styling product, or both to create your desired look.

Tips for Cutting Hair Like Mullet

Here are some tips to keep in mind when cutting your hair like a mullet:

  • Start with longer hair, as it's easier to cut it shorter than it is to make it longer
  • Be patient and take your time
  • Use a reference photo to guide you
  • Keep your scissors sharp
  • Ask a friend for help if you need it

FAQ

1. Is cutting hair like mullet difficult?

It can be challenging to achieve the perfect mullet look, but with patience and practice, anyone can do it.

2. How long does it take to cut hair like mullet?

The length of time it takes to cut hair like a mullet varies depending on your hair's length and thickness. Generally, it can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour.

3. Can I cut my hair like a mullet if I have curly hair?

Absolutely! The mullet hairstyle looks great on all hair types, including curly hair.

Hair Care after Cutting Hair Like Mullet

After cutting your hair like a mullet, it's essential to take good care of it to keep it looking its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash and condition your hair regularly
  • Use a hairbrush to detangle your hair gently
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your mullet

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to master the art of cutting hair like a mullet and rock this iconic hairstyle with confidence.
