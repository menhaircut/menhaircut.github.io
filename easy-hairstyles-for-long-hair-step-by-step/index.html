Easy Hairstyles For Long Hair Step By Step

Written by Aprilia Nov 01, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Long hair can be both a blessing and a curse, especially when it comes to styling it. On the one hand, you have plenty of length to work with, but on the other hand, it can be difficult to come up with hairstyles that are both easy and flattering. Fortunately, there are plenty of simple hairstyles for long hair that you can create in just a few steps. In this article, we'll take you through some of the easiest and best hairstyles for long hair step by step.

Table of Contents

  • Braided Ponytail
  • Messy Bun
  • Half-Up, Half-Down
  • Twisted Bun

Braided Ponytail

The braided ponytail is a stylish and easy hairstyle for long hair that you can wear for any occasion. Here's how to create it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair and tie it into a ponytail.
  3. Divide the ponytail into three sections.
  4. Braid the sections together.
  5. Tie the end of the braid with a hair tie.

You can also add some extra flair to your braided ponytail by wrapping a piece of hair around the hair tie and securing it with a bobby pin.

Messy Bun

The messy bun is a classic hairstyle that is perfect for those days when you don't have a lot of time to spend on your hair. Here's how to create it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair and tie it into a high ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  5. Pull out a few strands of hair to create a messy, undone look.

The messy bun is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions. You can also add some accessories, such as a headband or hairpin, to dress it up.

Half-Up, Half-Down

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is a simple and elegant way to showcase your long hair. Here's how to create it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it with a hair tie.
  3. Fluff up the hair on the top of your head to create volume.
  4. You can also curl the bottom half of your hair to add extra texture.

The half-up, half-down hairstyle is perfect for any occasion and can be customized to suit your personal style.

Twisted Bun

The twisted bun is a chic and sophisticated hairstyle that is perfect for formal events. Here's how to create it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair and tie it into a low ponytail.
  3. Split the ponytail into two sections.
  4. Twist each section around itself to create two separate buns.
  5. Secure the buns with bobby pins.

The twisted bun is a timeless hairstyle that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid is a bohemian-inspired hairstyle that is perfect for summer. Here's how to create it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Divide your hair into two sections.
  3. Take a small piece of hair from the outside of one section and cross it over to the inside of the other section.
  4. Repeat this process on the other side.
  5. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair.
  6. Tie the end of the braid with a hair tie.

The fishtail braid is a fun and easy hairstyle that can be worn for any occasion. You can also add some flowers or other accessories to give it a bohemian vibe.

Top Knot

The top knot is a stylish and easy hairstyle that is perfect for those days when you want to keep your hair out of your face. Here's how to create it:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair and tie it into a high ponytail.
  3. Twist the ponytail around itself to create a bun.
  4. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

The top knot is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions. You can also add some accessories, such as a headband or hairpin, to dress it up.

Hair Care Tips

In order to keep your long hair healthy and shiny, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, too often.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as this can cause breakage.
  • Use a hair mask or deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized.

FAQ

Q: Can I create these hairstyles on short hair?

A: Some of these hairstyles may be possible on shorter hair, but they are designed for long hair. You may need to modify the steps to suit your hair length.

Q: How long does it take to create these hairstyles?

A: Most of these hairstyles can be created in just a few minutes, making them perfect for busy mornings or when you're short on time.

Conclusion

Long hair can be both versatile and challenging to style, but with these easy hairstyles for long hair step by step, you'll be able to create a variety of looks in no time. Whether you're getting ready for a formal event or just want to switch up your everyday look, these hairstyles are sure to impress.
