How to Style Long Straight Hair Guys

Long straight hair can be challenging to style, but it's not impossible. Here are some of the best hairstyles for men with long straight hair:

Slicked Back Hair

The slicked-back hairstyle is a classic look that works well for men with long straight hair. To achieve this look, use a comb to slick your hair back, starting from the front and working your way to the back. Apply some hair gel or pomade to hold your hair in place and give it a shiny finish.

Side Parting

The side parting is a timeless hairstyle that never goes out of style. To get this look, create a deep side parting on one side of your head and comb your hair to the opposite side. Apply some hair wax or pomade to keep your hair in place.

Man Bun

The man bun is a trendy hairstyle that has been in fashion for a while now. To achieve this look, gather your hair at the back of your head and tie it into a bun. You can also add some braids or twists to your hair to make it look more stylish.

Undercut

The undercut hairstyle has been in fashion for a while now and is a popular choice for men with long straight hair. To get this look, shave the sides and back of your head and leave the top long. Comb your hair back or to one side for a sleek and stylish look.

Step by Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to style long straight hair guys:

  1. Wash and condition your hair using a good quality shampoo and conditioner. This will keep your hair clean and healthy.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage caused by heat styling tools.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and texture to your hair.
  4. Choose your preferred hairstyle and apply hair gel, wax, or pomade to your hair to hold it in place.
  5. Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into your desired look.
  6. Finish off with a hair spray to keep your hair in place throughout the day.

Tips for Styling Long Straight Hair Guys

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling long straight hair guys:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Apply hair gel, wax, or pomade to damp hair for better hold and control.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair before styling.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.

FAQs

What is the best hairstyle for long straight hair guys?

The best hairstyle for long straight hair guys depends on your personal preference and face shape. Some of the popular hairstyles for men with long straight hair include slicked-back hair, side parting, man bun, and undercut.

How often should I wash my long straight hair?

You should wash your long straight hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy. Over-washing can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it look dull and lifeless.

Hair Care Tips for Long Straight Hair Guys

Here are some hair care tips to keep your long straight hair healthy and shiny:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and healthy.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair as it can damage your hair and make it look dull and lifeless.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using any heat styling tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

That's it for our guide on how to style long straight hair guys. We hope you found this article helpful and informative. Remember to experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you best. And don't forget to take care of your hair by using good quality hair care products and following a healthy hair care routine. Good luck!
