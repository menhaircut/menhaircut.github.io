What Hair Parting Suits A Round Face

Written by Oliver Nov 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly trying to find the right hair parting that suits your round face? Well, look no further because we have got you covered! In this blog post, we will guide you on how to choose the perfect hair parting for a round face, including tips and tricks to help you achieve a stunning look.

Description

A round face is characterized by a soft, circular shape with a width and length that are almost equal. The goal when choosing a hair parting for a round face is to create the illusion of a longer face shape. A good hair parting can help elongate the face, making it look slimmer and more defined.

How to Choose the Right Hair Parting for a Round Face?

The key to finding the perfect hair parting for a round face is to opt for a side parting. A side parting will create an asymmetrical look that will help elongate the face, drawing attention away from its circular shape.

You can also try a deep side parting, which will create more volume on one side of the face, making it look longer. This parting style will also help create a more defined jawline.

Step by Step Guide to Choosing the Right Hair Parting for a Round Face

Step 1: Determine the shape of your face.

Step 2: Decide on the type of hair parting that you prefer.

Step 3: Experiment with different hair partings till you find the one that suits your face shape the most.

Step 4: Style your hair to create more volume on one side of your face.

Tips for Styling Hair for a Round Face

1. Avoid center parting as it will emphasize the round shape of the face.

2. Opt for a side parting, preferably a deep side parting to create more volume on one side of the face.

3. If you have long hair, try adding layers to create more texture and volume.

4. Consider getting a fringe that falls to one side of the face to create an asymmetrical look.

5. Use styling products to add volume and texture to your hair.

FAQs

1. Can I still have a center parting if I have a round face?

Absolutely! However, it is not the most flattering hair parting for a round face. If you still prefer a center parting, try adding volume to your hair to create an illusion of a longer face shape.

2. What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as bobs with rounded edges or hairstyles that fall at chin length. These styles will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

3. Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

Absolutely! Just make sure to create a deep side parting to create an illusion of a longer face shape. You can also add volume to your ponytail by backcombing the hair at the crown of your head.

Hair Care Tips for a Round Face

1. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.

2. Use a round brush when blow drying your hair to create more volume and texture.

3. Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

In conclusion, choosing the right hair parting for a round face can be a daunting task, but with the right tips and tricks, you can achieve a stunning look that will highlight your best features. Remember, a side parting is the way to go when it comes to elongating a round face shape.
