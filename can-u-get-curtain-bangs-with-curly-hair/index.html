Can You Get Curtain Bangs With Curly Hair

Written by Christine Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have curly hair and are looking to switch up your hairstyle, you may be wondering if curtain bangs are a good option for you. Curtain bangs have been a popular trend for a few years now, but can they work with curly hair? In this blog post, we will explore the answer to this question and provide you with tips on how to achieve the look.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for Curly Hair with Curtain Bangs

Description

Curtain bangs are a popular style that involves longer bangs that are parted in the middle, framing the face on either side. This style is often associated with straight hair, but it can also work with curly hair. The key is to find a stylist who understands how to cut curly hair and can work with the natural texture to create a flattering look.

How to Get Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair

If you are interested in getting curtain bangs with curly hair, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair. This is important because curly hair requires special attention and techniques to ensure that the cut is even and flatters your natural texture.

When you meet with your stylist, be sure to bring pictures of the style you are interested in, as well as any concerns you may have. Your stylist can work with you to create a customized look that suits your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide to getting curtain bangs with curly hair:

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced with cutting curly hair.
  2. Bring pictures of the style you are interested in and discuss any concerns you may have.
  3. Your stylist will cut your hair while it is dry to ensure that the length and shape are even.
  4. Your stylist will use a razor or scissors to create the curtain effect, cutting the hair in a V-shape at the center part.
  5. Your stylist will then style your hair to ensure that the bangs frame your face in a flattering way.

Tips for Maintaining Curtain Bangs with Curly Hair

Once you have your curtain bangs, it is important to take care of them to ensure that they look their best. Here are some tips for maintaining curtain bangs with curly hair:

  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to enhance your natural texture.
  • Avoid using a brush or comb on your bangs, as this can disrupt the natural curl pattern.
  • Apply a styling cream or gel to your bangs to help define the curls and keep them in place.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for maintenance trims to keep the length and shape of your curtain bangs.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about getting curtain bangs with curly hair:

  • Can you get curtain bangs with any type of curly hair? Yes, curtain bangs can work with any type of curly hair, from loose waves to tight coils.
  • Will curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance? Like any hairstyle, curtain bangs will require some maintenance to keep them looking their best. However, with the right products and styling techniques, you can make the most of your natural texture and minimize the amount of time you spend on your hair.
  • What face shapes are best suited for curtain bangs? Curtain bangs can work with a variety of face shapes, but they are particularly flattering on oval, heart-shaped, and square faces.

Hair Care for Curly Hair with Curtain Bangs

When you have curly hair with curtain bangs, it is important to use hair care products that are designed for your hair type. Look for products that are moisturizing and nourishing, and avoid products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates, which can strip your hair of its natural oils.

Additionally, be sure to protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray when blow-drying or using hot tools. Finally, consider sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase, as these materials are gentle on your hair and can help prevent frizz and breakage.

In conclusion, yes, you can get curtain bangs with curly hair! With the right stylist and care, this style can be a great way to switch up your look and enhance your natural texture. Follow these tips and enjoy your new hairstyle!
