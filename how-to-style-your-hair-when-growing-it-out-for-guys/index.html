How To Style Your Hair When Growing It Out For Guys

Written by Oliver Oct 28, 2023 · 3 min read

Growing out your hair can be a frustrating process, especially if you’re used to having a shorter style. However, with the right styling techniques, you can make the transition much smoother. In this article, we’ll go over some tips and tricks for styling your hair while it’s in that in-between phase.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips
  • FAQ

How to Style Your Hair When Growing it Out for Guys

The key to styling your hair when growing it out is to embrace the natural texture and work with it. For example, if you have wavy hair, let those waves shine through. Don’t try to force your hair into a straight style that it’s not suited for.

Another important factor is to keep your hair healthy. Regular trims and conditioning treatments are essential for preventing split ends and breakage.

Here are some specific styling techniques to try:

1. The Messy Look

The messy look can work well when growing out your hair. Use a texturizing product like sea salt spray or pomade to add some volume and definition to your hair. You can then use your fingers to create a tousled, bedhead look.

2. The Half-Up Look

The half-up look is a great option for when your hair is long enough to tie back, but not quite long enough for a full ponytail. Simply gather the top half of your hair and tie it back with a hair tie or clip. This can help keep your hair out of your face while still showcasing your length.

3. The Side Part

If you have medium-length hair, try parting it to the side for a classic, sophisticated look. Use a comb to create a clean part, then add some product to keep your hair in place.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide for achieving the messy look:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Apply a texturizing product like sea salt spray or pomade.
  3. Use your fingers to tousle your hair and create volume.
  4. If desired, use a hair dryer on low heat to add more volume.
  5. Finish with a light hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask your stylist for advice on how to style your hair during the growing-out process.
  • Be patient – growing out your hair takes time!

FAQ

Q: How often should I trim my hair while growing it out?

A: It’s recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.

Q: Can I still style my hair if it’s thinning?

A: Yes! There are many styling techniques and products that can help create the illusion of thicker hair.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy while growing it out, be sure to:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo
  • Condition regularly
  • Avoid heat styling tools
  • Protect your hair from the sun
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet

With these tips in mind, you can confidently grow out your hair and experiment with new styles along the way.
