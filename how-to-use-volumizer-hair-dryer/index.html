How To Use A Volumizer Hair Dryer For Perfect Volume

Written by Eveline Dec 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of flat and lifeless hair? If so, using a volumizer hair dryer is the perfect solution for you. In this article, we will explain how to use a volumizer hair dryer and share some tips for achieving the perfect volume.

Description

A volumizer hair dryer is a hair tool that combines a hairbrush and a hair dryer in one. It is designed to add volume and body to your hair while drying it at the same time. The bristles on the hairbrush are specially designed to lift the hair from the root and create a voluminous look.

How to Use

Step 1: Wash your hair

The first step in using a volumizer hair dryer is to wash your hair. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add extra volume to your hair.

Step 2: Towel dry your hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it to remove excess water. Do not rub your hair too hard with the towel as it can cause damage and frizz.

Step 3: Apply a volumizing product

Apply a volumizing product, such as a mousse or a root-lifting spray, to your hair. This will help to add extra volume and hold to your hair.

Step 4: Section your hair

Section your hair into small sections using clips. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that you don't miss any sections.

Step 5: Start styling

Start styling your hair with the volumizer hair dryer. Hold the hair dryer close to your roots and use the bristles to lift your hair from the root. Use a rolling motion to create volume and body in your hair.

Step 6: Move down the hair

Once you have styled the roots, move down to the mid-lengths and ends of your hair. Use the bristles to create a smooth and polished look.

Step 7: Finish with a blast of cold air

Finish styling your hair with a blast of cold air. This will help to set the style and add extra shine to your hair.

Tips

  • Use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from heat damage.
  • Use the low heat setting on your hair dryer to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Use a round brush to add extra volume and body to your hair.
  • Don't forget to clean the bristles of your volumizer hair dryer regularly to avoid product buildup.

FAQ

Can I use a volumizer hair dryer on wet hair?

No, it is not recommended to use a volumizer hair dryer on wet hair. You should always towel dry your hair before using a hair dryer.

How often should I use a volumizer hair dryer?

You can use a volumizer hair dryer as often as you like. However, it is recommended to give your hair a break from heat styling every once in a while to avoid damage.

Can I use a volumizer hair dryer on all hair types?

Yes, a volumizer hair dryer can be used on all hair types. However, if you have very fine or thin hair, you should be careful not to use too much heat as it can cause damage.

Hair Care

Using a volumizer hair dryer is a great way to add volume and body to your hair. However, it is important to take care of your hair to avoid damage. Use a heat protectant spray, avoid using too much heat, and give your hair a break from heat styling every once in a while.
