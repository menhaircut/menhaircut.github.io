How To Dry Wavy Frizzy Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Bardi Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of dealing with frizz and waves in your hair? Drying wavy frizzy hair can be a daunting task, but with the right techniques and products, you can achieve a smooth and sleek finish. In this post, we'll be sharing some tips and tricks on how to dry wavy frizzy hair. So, let's get started!

How to

To start, you'll need to have some basic tools on hand. These include a hair dryer with a diffuser attachment, a wide-tooth comb, and a heat protectant spray. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to dry wavy frizzy hair:

Step 1: Wash your hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Avoid using hot water as it can strip your hair of its natural oils, making it appear more frizzy. Instead, use lukewarm water and gently massage your scalp to remove dirt and buildup.

Step 2: Apply a heat protectant spray

Before blow-drying your hair, it's important to protect it from heat damage. Apply a heat protectant spray evenly throughout your hair to minimize damage and breakage. This will also help your hair to dry faster and more evenly.

Step 3: Use a wide-tooth comb

After applying the heat protectant spray, use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. This will help to prevent breakage and minimize frizz. Start at the ends of your hair and work your way up towards the roots.

Step 4: Dry your hair

Attach the diffuser attachment to your hair dryer and set it to a low heat setting. Start by drying your hair at the roots, moving the diffuser around your scalp in circular motions. This will help to add volume and lift to your hair. Then, work your way down towards the ends of your hair, scrunching it gently with your hands as you go.

Step 5: Finish with a cold blast

Once you've finished blow-drying your hair, use the cool setting on your hair dryer to give your hair a final blast of cold air. This will help to set your style and minimize frizz.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the best results when drying wavy frizzy hair:

  • Use a microfiber towel to dry your hair instead of a regular towel. This will help to minimize frizz and breakage.
  • Avoid touching your hair too much while it's drying, as this can cause frizz and disrupt your natural wave pattern.
  • Try using a leave-in conditioner or hair oil after blow-drying your hair to add shine and moisture.

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair?

It depends on your hair type and personal preference. Some people prefer to wash their hair every day, while others only wash it once or twice a week. If you have wavy frizzy hair, it's best to avoid washing it too often as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it appear more frizzy.

Can I air-dry my hair instead of blow-drying it?

Yes, you can air-dry your hair if you prefer. However, this may take longer and may not give you the same level of control over your hair as blow-drying it with a diffuser attachment.

What should I do if my hair becomes too frizzy after blow-drying it?

If your hair becomes too frizzy after blow-drying it, try applying a small amount of hair oil or serum to the ends of your hair. This will help to smooth down any flyaways and add shine to your hair.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining healthy and beautiful hair. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that's appropriate for your hair type.
  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet to promote hair growth and strength.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or using a hair scarf.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain the health of your hair.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a smooth and sleek finish when drying wavy frizzy hair. Remember to take care of your hair by using the right products and techniques, and you'll be rewarded with healthy and beautiful locks.
