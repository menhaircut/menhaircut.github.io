How To Make Fine Natural Hair Look Thicker

Written by Aprilia Oct 21, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you have fine natural hair that lacks volume and body? If so, you're not alone. Many women struggle with fine hair that seems to fall flat no matter what they do. But don't despair - there are ways to make your hair look thicker and fuller. In this article, we'll show you how.

Description

Having fine natural hair can be a blessing and a curse. On the one hand, it's typically easier to manage than thick, unruly hair. On the other hand, it can be hard to achieve the voluminous, bouncy hair that many women desire. The key to making fine hair look thicker is to add texture and body.

How to Make Fine Natural Hair Look Thicker

Step 1: Choose the Right Products

The first step in making fine hair look thicker is to use the right products. Look for products that are designed to add volume and texture, such as volumizing shampoo, mousse, and texturizing spray. Avoid heavy products that can weigh your hair down, such as serums and oils.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair Upside Down

Blow drying your hair upside down is a quick and easy way to add volume and body. Simply flip your head over and blow dry your hair in the opposite direction of the way you want it to lay. Use a round brush to lift the roots and create volume.

Step 3: Use Velcro Rollers

Velcro rollers are a great way to add volume and curl to fine hair. Simply roll your hair onto the rollers and blow dry with a diffuser. Once your hair is dry, remove the rollers and use your fingers to loosen the curls for a natural, voluminous look.

Step 4: Tease Your Hair

Teasing your hair is an old-school trick that still works wonders for adding volume and body. Use a fine-tooth comb to backcomb the roots of your hair, then smooth the top layer over the teased section. This will create a lifted, voluminous look.

Tips

  • Use a volumizing dry shampoo in between washes to refresh your hair and add texture.
  • Avoid brushing your hair too much, as this can cause it to become flat and limp.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, such as braids and updos, to add volume and body to your hair.

FAQ

Q: Can I use a straightener on fine hair?

A: Yes, you can use a straightener on fine hair, but be sure to use a heat protectant spray to avoid damaging your hair.

Q: Will coloring my hair make it look thicker?

A: Coloring your hair can add the appearance of thickness, as the dye coats the hair shaft and creates the illusion of volume. However, it's important to note that excessive coloring can damage your hair and make it look thinner in the long run.

Hair Care

Caring for fine hair is important in order to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for fine hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that are formulated for fine hair.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

By following these tips and tricks, you can make your fine natural hair look thicker and more voluminous. With a little bit of effort and the right products, you can achieve the hair of your dreams!
