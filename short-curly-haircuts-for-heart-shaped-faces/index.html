Short Curly Haircuts For Heart Shaped Faces

Written by Robby Jun 08, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you have a heart shaped face, you know that finding the perfect haircut can be a challenge. You want a style that will flatter your features and highlight your best assets, but you also want something that is easy to maintain and style. Short curly haircuts can be a great option for heart shaped faces, as they can add volume and texture to your hair while also complementing your face shape. In this article, we will discuss some of the best short curly haircuts for heart shaped faces, as well as some tips for caring for and styling your curly hair.

Description

A heart shaped face is characterized by a wider forehead and cheekbones that taper down to a narrower chin. The goal of a good haircut for this face shape is to balance out the width of the forehead and create more volume around the chin area. Short curly haircuts can achieve this by adding volume and texture to your hair, which can help balance out your features and draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones.

How to

When choosing a short curly haircut for a heart shaped face, it's important to consider the length of your hair and the type of curls you have. Some of the best short haircuts for heart shaped faces include the pixie cut, the bob, and the shag. These styles can be customized to suit your individual needs and preferences, but they all share the common goal of adding volume and texture to your hair.

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, cropped haircut that can be a great option for heart shaped faces with curly hair. This style can be customized to suit your individual needs, but it typically involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back while leaving longer bangs or layers on top. This can help balance out the width of your forehead and create more volume around your chin.

Bob

The bob is a classic haircut that can work well for heart shaped faces with curly hair. This style typically involves cutting the hair to chin length or shorter, with layers and texture added to create volume and movement. A bob can help balance out the width of your forehead and draw attention to your cheekbones and eyes.

Shag

The shag is a layered haircut that can be a great option for heart shaped faces with curly hair. This style typically involves cutting the hair into layers of varying lengths, which can help add volume and texture to your hair. A shag can help balance out the width of your forehead and create more volume around your chin, while still allowing you to maintain some length and texture in your hair.

Step by Step

When getting a short curly haircut for a heart shaped face, it's important to communicate your needs and preferences to your stylist. Here are some steps you can follow to achieve the perfect short curly haircut:

  1. Research different short curly haircuts and find some styles that you like.
  2. Bring pictures of these styles to your stylist and discuss which one would be best for your face shape and hair type.
  3. Ask your stylist to add layers and texture to your hair to create volume and movement.
  4. Consider getting highlights or lowlights to add dimension to your hair.
  5. Ask your stylist for tips on how to care for and style your curly hair at home.

Tips

Here are some tips for caring for and styling your short curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it is wet.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair while it is still wet to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

FAQ

What is the best short curly haircut for a heart shaped face?

Some of the best short curly haircuts for heart shaped faces include the pixie cut, the bob, and the shag. These styles can be customized to suit your individual needs and preferences, but they all share the common goal of adding volume and texture to your hair.

How can I style my short curly hair?

You can style your short curly hair by using a curl-enhancing product and scrunching your hair with your hands. You can also use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help define your curls and add volume. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

How can I care for my short curly hair?

You can care for your short curly hair by using a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner, avoiding brushing your hair when it is dry, and applying a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair while it is still wet. You should also avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.

Hair Care

Caring for your short curly hair is important to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for your curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl-enhancing product to your hair while it is still wet to help define your curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and to prevent split ends.

In conclusion, short curly haircuts can be a great option for heart shaped faces, as they can add volume and texture to your hair while also complementing your face shape. When choosing a short curly haircut, it's important to consider the length of your hair and the type of curls you have. Follow these tips for caring for and styling your short curly hair, and you'll have a gorgeous and flattering hairstyle that you love.
