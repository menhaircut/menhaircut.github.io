How To Keep Wig On Head Tips And Tricks

If you are wearing a wig for the first time, you may find it difficult to keep it in place. Whether you are wearing a wig for fashion or medical reasons, it is important to ensure that it stays securely on your head. In this article, we will share tips and tricks on how to keep wig on head.

Table of Contents

  • How to Keep Wig on Head
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips and Tricks
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Wig Wearers

How to Keep Wig on Head

The first step to keeping your wig on your head is to ensure that it fits properly. You can do this by measuring your head and selecting a wig that matches your size. Once you have the right size, you can follow these steps to keep your wig in place:

1. Use Wig Clips

Wig clips are small combs that can be attached to your wig. They can help to keep your wig securely in place. To use wig clips, simply attach them to the wefts of your wig and then snap them onto your natural hair.

2. Use Wig Tape

Wig tape is a double-sided adhesive that can help to keep your wig from slipping. To use wig tape, simply apply it to your scalp and then press your wig firmly onto the tape.

3. Use a Wig Grip

A wig grip is a band that can be worn under your wig. It can help to keep your wig in place and prevent it from slipping. To use a wig grip, simply place it on your head and then put your wig on top.

Step by Step Guide

Here is a step by step guide on how to keep wig on head:

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you put on your wig, make sure that your hair is neatly braided or pinned up. This will help to keep your natural hair from getting tangled with your wig.

Step 2: Put on Your Wig Cap

A wig cap can help to keep your hair in place and prevent it from getting tangled with your wig. Simply put the wig cap over your hair and adjust it so that it fits snugly on your head.

Step 3: Put on Your Wig

Once you have your wig cap on, you can put on your wig. Make sure that your wig is centered on your head and then use wig clips or wig tape to secure it in place.

Step 4: Style Your Wig

Now that your wig is securely in place, you can style it as desired. Use a comb or brush to smooth out any tangles or bumps and then use styling products to create your desired look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks for keeping your wig in place:

  • Use wig clips, wig tape, or a wig grip to secure your wig.
  • Wear a wig cap to keep your hair in place.
  • Avoid using heavy styling products that can weigh down your wig.
  • Avoid wearing your wig in windy or rainy weather.
  • Store your wig on a wig stand or in a wig box to help maintain its shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long can I wear my wig for?

A: You can wear your wig for as long as you like, but it is recommended that you take it off at night to allow your scalp to breathe.

Q: Can I wash my wig?

A: Yes, you can wash your wig. However, it is important to use a wig shampoo and conditioner and to avoid using hot water or a hair dryer.

Q: Can I swim in my wig?

A: It is not recommended to swim in your wig, as chlorine and salt water can damage the fibers.

Hair Care for Wig Wearers

If you are wearing a wig, it is important to take care of your natural hair as well. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair while wearing a wig:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy.
  • Avoid using chemical treatments or harsh styling products that can damage your hair.
  • Take breaks from wearing your wig to allow your scalp to breathe.

By following these tips and tricks, you can keep your wig securely in place and maintain healthy hair underneath. Whether you are wearing a wig for fashion or medical reasons, it is important to feel confident and comfortable in your wig.
