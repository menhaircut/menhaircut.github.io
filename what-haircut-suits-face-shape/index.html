What Haircut Suits Your Face Shape A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 13, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Having a haircut that suits your face shape is essential. It can enhance your features, make your face look more symmetrical, and give you a confidence boost. However, with so many options available, it can be challenging to know which haircut is right for you. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of determining your face shape and finding the perfect haircut for you.

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in finding the right haircut for your face shape is to determine what your face shape is. There are six main face shapes: round, square, oval, heart, diamond, and long. To determine your face shape, follow these steps:

  1. Using a tape measure or ruler, measure the width of your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline.
  2. Measure the length of your face from your hairline to your chin.
  3. Look at the overall shape of your face and determine which of the six shapes it most closely resembles.

Once you have determined your face shape, you can start exploring different haircuts that will flatter your features.

Haircuts for Round Face Shapes

If you have a round face shape, you want to create the illusion of length and slimness. Here are some haircuts that can help:

  • A long, layered haircut that falls below the chin
  • Side-swept bangs that draw attention to the eyes
  • A bob with longer layers in the front
  • Asymmetrical cuts that are longer on one side
  • Shorter haircuts with volume on top

Haircuts for Square Face Shapes

If you have a square face shape, you want to soften the angles and create more length. Here are some haircuts that can help:

  • Long, layered haircuts that fall below the collarbone
  • Soft, side-swept bangs
  • A layered bob that falls just below the chin
  • Loose waves or curls that soften the jawline
  • A pixie cut with longer layers on top

Haircuts for Oval Face Shapes

Oval face shapes are considered the most versatile because they can pull off almost any haircut. Here are some haircuts that are especially flattering for oval faces:

  • A classic bob
  • Long, straight hair with layers
  • Side-swept bangs
  • A pixie cut with long layers on top
  • A long, layered shag

Haircuts for Heart Face Shapes

If you have a heart-shaped face, you want to balance out the width of your forehead with the width of your chin. Here are some haircuts that can help:

  • A long, layered haircut with side-swept bangs
  • A chin-length bob with layers
  • A pixie cut with wispy bangs
  • Loose waves that frame the face
  • A deep side part with volume at the crown

Haircuts for Long Face Shapes

If you have a long face shape, you want to create the illusion of width and shorten the overall length of your face. Here are some haircuts that can help:

  • A chin-length bob with layers
  • Soft, side-swept bangs
  • A layered shag
  • A long bob that falls just below the collarbone
  • Loose waves that add volume to the sides of the face

Haircut Tips for Any Face Shape

No matter what your face shape is, there are some general haircut tips that can help you look your best:

  • Avoid haircuts that emphasize your problem areas
  • Choose a haircut that flatters your best features
  • Consider your hair texture and thickness when choosing a haircut
  • Bring pictures of haircuts you like to your stylist
  • Be open to suggestions from your stylist

FAQ

Q: Can I pull off a certain haircut even if it doesn't suit my face shape?

A: Of course! These guidelines are just suggestions. If you love a particular haircut and feel confident wearing it, go for it!

Q: How often should I get a haircut?

A: It depends on your hair type and how fast it grows. On average, most people get a haircut every 6-8 weeks.

Q: How do I maintain my new haircut?

A: Follow a good hair care routine that includes shampooing and conditioning regularly, using heat protectant products, and getting regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care Tips for Your New Haircut

Now that you have your perfect haircut, it's important to take care of it. Here are some hair care tips to keep your new haircut looking great:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is appropriate for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons and curling irons too often
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape of your haircut
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as it can strip it of its natural oils

In conclusion, finding the right haircut for your face shape can make a huge difference in how you look and feel. Use this guide to determine your face shape and explore different haircuts that will flatter your features. Remember, these are just guidelines, and ultimately, you should choose a haircut that makes you feel confident and beautiful.
