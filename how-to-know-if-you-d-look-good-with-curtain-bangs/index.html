How To Know If You d Look Good With Curtain Bangs

Written by Bardi Nov 03, 2023 Â· 6 min read

If you're thinking about getting a new hairstyle, curtain bangs are a great option. They're trendy, versatile, and can suit different face shapes and hair types. But before you book an appointment with your hairstylist, you might be wondering if curtain bangs will look good on you. In this article, we'll give you some tips and tricks on how to know if you'd look good with curtain bangs.

Consider Your Face Shape

One of the most important factors to consider when deciding if curtain bangs will suit you is your face shape. Curtain bangs, as the name suggests, frame your face like a curtain. They should accentuate your best features and balance out any asymmetry. Here are some general tips on which face shapes can pull off curtain bangs:

  • Round face: Curtain bangs can elongate your face and make it appear slimmer.
  • Oval face: You're in luck! Oval faces can rock almost any type of bangs, including curtain bangs.
  • Square face: Curtain bangs can soften your strong jawline and add some femininity to your look.
  • Heart-shaped face: Curtain bangs can balance out a wider forehead and draw attention to your eyes.
  • Diamond face: Curtain bangs can widen your forehead and narrow your cheekbones, creating a more balanced look.

Of course, these are just general guidelines. Your individual features and proportions will also play a role in how curtain bangs will look on you. That's why it's important to take into account other factors, such as your hair type and length, before making a decision.

Think About Your Hair Type

Curtain bangs can work with different hair types, but some may require more maintenance than others. Here are some things to consider:

  • Straight hair: Curtain bangs can look sleek and polished on straight hair, but they may require frequent trimming to maintain their shape.
  • Wavy hair: Curtain bangs can add some texture and movement to wavy hair, but they may require styling with a curling iron or flat iron to look their best.
  • Curly hair: Curtain bangs can complement curly hair and give it a playful vibe. However, they may require more upkeep and styling to avoid frizz.
  • Thick hair: Curtain bangs can add some dimension and volume to thick hair, but they may require thinning out to prevent them from looking too heavy.
  • Fine hair: Curtain bangs can create the illusion of thicker hair, but they may require some teasing or backcombing to add volume.

Ultimately, your hairstylist can give you the best advice on how curtain bangs will work with your hair type and texture.

Check Your Hair Length

Curtain bangs can work with different hair lengths, but they may look better with certain styles. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Short hair: Curtain bangs can look cute and edgy on short hair, but they may require more styling to avoid looking messy.
  • Medium hair: Curtain bangs can complement medium-length hair and add some dimension to your layers.
  • Long hair: Curtain bangs can look romantic and bohemian on long hair, but they may require more maintenance to prevent them from blending in with the rest of your hair.

Again, your hairstylist can help you decide if curtain bangs will work with your current hair length and if you need to make any adjustments.

Assess Your Personal Style

Curtain bangs can suit different personal styles, but they may not be for everyone. Here are some things to consider:

  • Classic style: If you prefer timeless and elegant looks, curtain bangs can add some sophistication to your hairstyle.
  • Bohemian style: If you're into free-spirited and natural looks, curtain bangs can enhance your earthy vibe.
  • Modern style: If you like to stay on top of the latest trends, curtain bangs can give you a fashion-forward edge.
  • Minimalist style: If you prefer simple and understated looks, curtain bangs can add some interest without being too flashy.

Of course, these are just examples. Your personal style may not fit neatly into one category, and that's okay! The most important thing is to choose a hairstyle that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

Use a Virtual Try-On Tool

If you're still unsure if curtain bangs will look good on you, you can use a virtual try-on tool to see how they will look without committing to a haircut. Many hairstyling apps and websites offer this feature, allowing you to upload a photo of yourself and try different hairstyles and colors. Keep in mind that the virtual results may not be 100% accurate, but they can give you a rough idea of how curtain bangs will suit you.

Ask Your Hairstylist

Your hairstylist is the best person to ask if curtain bangs will look good on you. They have the expertise and experience to assess your face shape, hair type, and personal style, and suggest the best haircut for you. Plus, they can give you some tips on how to style and maintain your curtain bangs once you get them. Don't be afraid to ask questions and share your concerns with your hairstylist!

Curtain Bangs Care Tips

Once you've decided to get curtain bangs, you'll want to take good care of them to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

  • Trim them regularly: Curtain bangs can grow out quickly and lose their shape, so it's important to get them trimmed every 4-6 weeks.
  • Wash and condition them gently: Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Blow-dry them with a round brush: Use a round brush to style your curtain bangs and create some volume and movement.
  • Style them with a heat protectant: Use a heat protectant spray or serum to protect your hair from damage when using hot tools.
  • Accessorize them: Use hair clips, headbands, or scarves to add some interest and variety to your curtain bangs.

FAQs About Curtain Bangs

Q: Will curtain bangs suit me if I have a small forehead?

A: Yes, curtain bangs can actually make your forehead appear larger and more balanced with the rest of your face.

Q: Can I wear my curtain bangs parted in the middle or the side?

A: Yes, curtain bangs are versatile and can be worn in different styles. Experiment with different partings and see what works best for you.

Q: How do I prevent my curtain bangs from getting greasy?

A: Use a dry shampoo or baby powder to absorb excess oil, and avoid touching your bangs with your hands.

Q: Can I still wear a ponytail or a bun with curtain bangs?

A: Yes, you can incorporate your curtain bangs into different hairstyles, such as a high ponytail or a messy bun. You may need to use some bobby pins or hair ties to keep them in place.

Conclusion

Curtain bangs can be a fun and flattering hairstyle, but they may not suit everyone. By considering your face shape, hair type, length, and personal style, you can make an informed decision on whether or not to get curtain bangs.
