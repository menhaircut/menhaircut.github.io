Short Hairstyles To Suit Fat Faces Flattering Cuts Tips

If you have a round or chubby face, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. However, with the right cut and styling, you can accentuate your best features and downplay any areas you may be self-conscious about. In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best short hairstyles to suit fat faces, as well as tips and tricks to make the most out of your hair.

How to Choose the Right Short Hairstyle for Your Face Shape

The key to choosing the perfect short hairstyle for a fat face is to look for cuts that elongate your features and balance out your proportions. Here are a few things to keep in mind when choosing your next haircut:

Consider Your Face Shape

While all fat faces are different, they generally fall into one of three categories: round, oval, or heart-shaped. Depending on your face shape, certain hairstyles may be more flattering than others. For example:

  • Round faces: Avoid blunt bobs and chin-length cuts, which can make your face look even rounder. Instead, opt for asymmetrical cuts, long layers, or pixie cuts with lots of volume at the crown.
  • Oval faces: Lucky you! Oval faces tend to be the most versatile, so you can experiment with a variety of short haircuts.
  • Heart-shaped faces: Look for styles that add volume to the top of your head and taper down towards your chin, like a layered bob or pixie cut.

Choose the Right Length

When it comes to short haircuts for fat faces, there's no one-size-fits-all answer. However, as a general rule of thumb, your haircut should be no shorter than chin-length. Anything shorter than that can make your face appear wider.

Think About Texture

Adding texture to your hair can help create the illusion of length and make your face appear slimmer. Consider adding layers, curls, or waves to your short hairstyle to add volume and dimension.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Fat Faces

Now that you've chosen the perfect short hairstyle for your face shape, it's time to learn how to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to getting the most out of your short haircut:

Step 1: Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Before styling your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.

Step 2: Apply a Heat Protectant

If you plan on using heat tools to style your hair, be sure to apply a heat protectant first. This will help prevent damage and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Step 3: Use a Round Brush to Add Volume

If you have a short bob or pixie cut, you can use a round brush to add volume to your hair while blow-drying. Start at the roots and work your way down, using the brush to lift and shape your hair as you go.

Step 4: Add Texture

To add texture to your hair, use a curling iron or flat iron to create loose waves or curls. Alternatively, you can use a texturizing spray or pomade to add definition and volume to your hair.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

To set your style in place, finish with a light mist of hairspray. Be sure to hold the can at least 6-8 inches away from your head to avoid weighing down your hair.

Tips for Keeping Your Short Hair Looking Great

Now that you know how to style your short hair, here are a few tips to keep it looking great:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Invest in high-quality styling tools, like a good blow dryer or flat iron.
  • Experiment with different products to find what works best for your hair type and texture.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to add depth and dimension to your hair.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and switch up your look from time to time!

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions about Short Hairstyles for Fat Faces

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a fat face?

A: Yes! Pixie cuts can be a great option for fat faces, as long as they are styled correctly. Look for pixie cuts with lots of volume at the crown to elongate your face.

Q: What is the best haircut for a round face?

A: As mentioned earlier, asymmetrical cuts, long layers, and pixie cuts with volume at the crown can be great options for round faces. However, the best haircut for you will depend on your individual face shape and features.

Q: Can I wear short bangs with a fat face?

A: While short bangs can be a cute and trendy look, they may not be the most flattering option for fat faces. Consider longer, side-swept bangs instead.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair

Short hair requires a different kind of care than long hair. Here are a few tips to keep your short hair healthy and looking great:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and texture to your hair.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can strip it of its natural oils.
  • Invest in a good dry shampoo to refresh your hair between washes.
  • Use a heat protectant before using any heat tools on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and tricks, you can find the perfect short hairstyle to suit your fat face and keep your hair looking great. Remember, the key is to experiment and find what works best for you!
