Mid Length Hairstyles For Oval Shaped Faces A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Oct 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have an oval-shaped face, you are blessed with a versatile facial structure that can pull off almost any hairstyle. However, finding the perfect mid-length hairstyle that complements your features can be challenging. In this article, we will be discussing the best mid-length hairstyles for oval-shaped faces, how to style them, and hair care tips to maintain healthy hair.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Mid Length Hairstyle for Oval Shaped Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Hairstyles for Oval Shaped Faces

Description

Mid length hairstyles are perfect for oval-shaped faces as they accentuate the cheekbones and jawline while softening the forehead. The mid length hairstyle falls between the chin and shoulder length, making it a versatile and low-maintenance option. There are several mid length hairstyles that can complement an oval-shaped face, including layered cuts, beachy waves, and sleek bobs.

How to Choose the Right Mid Length Hairstyle for Oval Shaped Faces

When choosing the right mid length hairstyle for your oval-shaped face, consider your hair texture, face shape, and personal style. Oval-shaped faces can pull off almost any mid-length hairstyle, but some styles can complement your features better than others. Here are some mid length hairstyles to consider for oval-shaped faces:

Layered Cuts

Layered cuts are perfect for oval-shaped faces as they add volume and movement to the hair. The layers frame the face and accentuate the cheekbones, giving the face a more defined look. Layered cuts can be styled in several ways, including messy waves, sleek straight styles, or a half-up half-down look.

Beachy Waves

Beachy waves are a great option for oval-shaped faces as they soften the forehead and add volume to the hair. To achieve beachy waves, use a curling iron or hair straightener to create loose waves. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and hold the waves in place.

Sleek Bobs

Sleek bobs are a chic and sophisticated option for oval-shaped faces. The bob should fall just above the chin and be styled straight for a sleek and polished look. To add volume and texture, use a volumizing mousse or texturizing spray.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Mid Length Hairstyles for Oval Shaped Faces

Styling mid length hairstyles for oval-shaped faces is easy and low-maintenance. Follow these steps to achieve the perfect mid length hairstyle:

  1. Wash and condition your hair with a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume and movement.
  4. Style your hair using a curling iron, hair straightener, or texturizing spray to achieve your desired look.
  5. Finish with a hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Mid Length Hair

Maintaining healthy mid length hair is essential to achieving the perfect mid length hairstyle. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping the natural oils from your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, including bleach and hair dyes.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools every day to prevent heat damage.

FAQs

What is the best mid length hairstyle for oval-shaped faces?

The best mid length hairstyle for oval-shaped faces is a layered cut. The layers accentuate the cheekbones and jawline, giving the face a more defined look.

Can oval-shaped faces pull off a bob?

Yes, oval-shaped faces can pull off a bob. A sleek bob that falls just above the chin is a chic and sophisticated option for oval-shaped faces.

How often should I wash my mid length hair?

You should wash your mid length hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping the natural oils from your hair.

Should I use heat protectant spray when styling my mid length hair?

Yes, you should always use heat protectant spray when styling your mid length hair to prevent heat damage.

Overall, mid length hairstyles are a great option for oval-shaped faces. Whether you choose a layered cut, beachy waves, or a sleek bob, mid length hairstyles are versatile, low-maintenance, and can complement your features in the best way possible.
