How To Relax Curly Hair Without Damage A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver Jun 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Curly hair can be a blessing and a curse. While it’s unique and beautiful, it can also be difficult to manage and style. Many people with curly hair turn to relaxers to make their hair more manageable, but these treatments can cause damage and breakage. If you’re looking for ways to relax your curly hair without causing damage, you’ve come to the right place. In this guide, we’ll share tips, step-by-step instructions, and answers to frequently asked questions about how to relax curly hair without damage.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Relax Curly Hair Without Damage
  2. Step-by-Step Instructions
  3. Tips for Relaxing Curly Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care After Relaxing Curly Hair

How to Relax Curly Hair Without Damage

The first step to relaxing curly hair without damage is to choose the right product. There are many different types of relaxers on the market, but not all are created equal. Look for a relaxer that is specifically formulated for curly hair, and choose a mild formula to minimize damage. Some popular options include:

  • Organic Root Stimulator Olive Oil Relaxer
  • Mizani Butter Blend Relaxer
  • ORS Curls Unleashed No-Limits Texture Cream
  • SoftSheen-Carson Optimum Care Defy Breakage No-Lye Relaxer

Once you’ve chosen a relaxer, it’s important to follow the instructions carefully. Don’t leave the relaxer on your hair for longer than recommended, as this can cause damage and breakage. Rinse the relaxer out thoroughly, and neutralize your hair with a neutralizing shampoo to prevent any residual chemicals from causing damage.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Here’s a step-by-step guide to relaxing curly hair without damage:

  1. Choose a mild relaxer specifically formulated for curly hair.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and follow them closely.
  3. Apply the relaxer to your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.
  4. Leave the relaxer on your hair for the recommended amount of time.
  5. Rinse the relaxer out thoroughly with warm water.
  6. Apply a neutralizing shampoo to your hair, and massage it in gently.
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  8. Apply a deep conditioner to your hair, and leave it on for 10-15 minutes.
  9. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water.
  10. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Relaxing Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips for relaxing curly hair without causing damage:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as these can cause damage and breakage.
  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid pulling your hair back tightly, as this can cause breakage and damage.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, and be gentle to avoid breakage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about relaxing curly hair without damage:

  • Can I relax my hair without using chemicals? Yes, there are some natural ways to relax curly hair, such as using aloe vera or coconut milk. However, these methods may not be as effective as chemical relaxers.
  • How often should I relax my hair? It’s best to wait at least 6-8 weeks between relaxer treatments to avoid damage and breakage.
  • Can I color my hair after relaxing it? You should wait at least 2 weeks after relaxing your hair before coloring it to avoid damage.

Hair Care After Relaxing Curly Hair

After relaxing your curly hair, it’s important to take good care of it to prevent damage and breakage. Here are some tips for caring for relaxed hair:

  • Deep condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, or use them sparingly and always use a heat protectant.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and wind by wearing a hat or scarf.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or permanent hair color.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

Relaxing curly hair without causing damage is possible with the right products and techniques. Follow these tips and instructions to achieve smooth, manageable hair without sacrificing its health and beauty.
