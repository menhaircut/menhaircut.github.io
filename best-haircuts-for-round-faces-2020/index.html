Best Haircuts For Round Faces 2020 A Complete Guide

Written by Daniel Nov 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Round faces are characterized by soft, curved features with no prominent angles. If you have a round face, you may find it challenging to choose a haircut that flatters your face shape. In this article, we will explore the best haircuts for round faces in 2020. We will cover everything from the best styles to how to care for your hair. So, let's get started!

Description

Round faces tend to be shorter and wider than other face shapes. They have soft features with no prominent angles or corners. The forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are all about the same width. The goal when choosing a haircut for a round face is to create the illusion of length and angles. This can be achieved with the right cut and styling techniques.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for a Round Face

When choosing a haircut for a round face, you want to choose a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of angles. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut for your face shape:

1. Look for Haircuts with Length

Long hair can elongate a round face and make it appear slimmer. If you prefer short hair, opt for a style with layers and volume on top to create the illusion of length.

2. Avoid Haircuts that Add Width to Your Face

Haircuts with blunt bangs or one-length cuts that end at the chin can make a round face appear wider. Instead, choose a style with layers and angles to create the illusion of length and angles.

3. Choose a Style with Volume on Top

Adding volume on top of your head can create the illusion of height and angles. This can be achieved with a layered cut or using styling products to create volume.

Best Haircuts for Round Faces in 2020

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut for a round face, let's take a look at the best haircuts for round faces in 2020:

1. Long Layers

Long layers can elongate a round face and make it appear slimmer. This style works best with straight or wavy hair. You can add volume on top by using a volumizing mousse or spray.

2. Side-Swept Bangs

Side-swept bangs can create the illusion of angles and make a round face appear slimmer. This style works best with long hair and can be achieved by sweeping the hair to one side and cutting at an angle.

3. Pixie Cut with Side-Swept Bangs

A pixie cut with side-swept bangs can create the illusion of length and angles. This style works best with straight hair and can be achieved by cutting the hair short on the sides and leaving length on top.

4. Layered Bob

A layered bob can create the illusion of length and angles. This style works best with straight or wavy hair and can be achieved by cutting the hair shorter in the back and longer in the front.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Hair

Now that you have chosen the right haircut for your face shape, let's take a look at how to style your hair:

1. Start with Clean, Dry Hair

Wash your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that is suited for your hair type. Towel dry your hair and use a blow dryer to dry it completely.

2. Apply Styling Products

Apply a styling product that is suited for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing mousse or spray. If you have thick hair, use a smoothing serum.

3. Use a Round Brush to Create Volume

Use a round brush to create volume on top of your head. Start at the roots and pull the brush through to the ends while blow drying your hair.

4. Finish with Hairspray

Finish with a light hold hairspray to keep your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Hair

Maintaining your haircut is just as important as choosing the right one. Here are some tips for maintaining your hair:

1. Get Regular Trims

Getting regular trims will help keep your hair in shape and prevent split ends.

2. Use Heat Protectant Products

Using heat protectant products will help prevent damage from heat styling tools.

3. Protect Your Hair from the Sun

Wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray can help protect your hair from the sun.

FAQs

1. Can I wear a bob if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear a bob if you have a round face. Opt for a layered bob that is shorter in the back and longer in the front to create the illusion of length and angles.

2. Can I wear my hair up if I have a round face?

Yes, you can wear your hair up if you have a round face. Opt for a high ponytail or a top knot to elongate your face.

3. What is the best haircut for fine hair and a round face?

The best haircut for fine hair and a round face is a layered bob with volume on top. This will create the illusion of length and angles.

Hair Care for Round Faces

When caring for your hair, it's important to use products that are suited for your hair type. If you have fine hair, use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. If you have thick hair, use a smoothing shampoo and conditioner. It's also important to protect your hair from heat styling tools and the sun.

Choosing the right haircut for a round face can be challenging, but with the right style and styling techniques, you can create the illusion of length and angles. Remember to choose a style with length, avoid styles that add width, and add volume on top to create the illusion of height. With these tips and tricks, you can have a fabulous haircut that flatters your face shape!
