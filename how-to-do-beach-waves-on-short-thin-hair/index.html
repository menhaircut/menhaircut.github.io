How To Do Beach Waves On Short Thin Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Dec 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short and thin hair, achieving beach waves may seem like a daunting task. But don't worry, with the right technique and products, you can achieve effortless beachy waves that will leave you feeling confident and beautiful. In this article, we'll show you how to do beach waves on short thin hair in a simple and easy-to-follow way.

Table of Contents

  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Perfect Beach Waves

How to Do Beach Waves on Short Thin Hair

Beach waves are a great look for summer, but they can be hard to achieve on short and thin hair. Luckily, there are a few tricks you can use to get those perfect beachy waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel
  • Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray
  • Twist your hair in small sections
  • Use your fingers to loosen the curls

With these tips in mind, let's move on to the step-by-step guide.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to achieve beach waves on short thin hair:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from heat damage.
  2. Section your hair. Divide your hair into small sections using hair clips.
  3. Take a small section of hair and wrap it around a curling iron with a small barrel. Hold the curling iron in place for a few seconds.
  4. Release the curling iron and gently tug on the curl to loosen it.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for the rest of your hair.
  6. Once all of your hair is curled, apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray to add volume and texture to your hair.
  7. Use your fingers to gently tousle your hair and loosen the curls.
  8. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your beach waves in place.

With these steps, you'll have gorgeous beachy waves in no time!

Tips for Perfect Beach Waves

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect beach waves:

  • Use a curling iron with a small barrel (around 1 inch) to create small, tight curls.
  • Apply a texturizing spray or sea salt spray before curling to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Twist your hair in different directions to create a more natural-looking wave.
  • Don't curl your hair all the way to the ends – leave the last inch or two straight for a more relaxed look.
  • Use your fingers to gently tousle your hair and break up the curls for a more effortless look.

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about beach waves on short thin hair:

Can I achieve beach waves without a curling iron?

Yes, you can achieve beachy waves using a flat iron or even braiding your hair overnight. However, using a curling iron will give you the most control over the size and shape of your curls.

What if my hair is too short to curl?

If your hair is too short to curl, you can still achieve a beachy look by using a texturizing spray or sea salt spray and tousling your hair with your fingers.

How do I make my beach waves last all day?

Use a light-hold hairspray to keep your beach waves in place, and avoid touching your hair too much throughout the day.

Hair Care for Beach Waves

To keep your beach waves looking their best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils and make it more prone to damage.
  • Use a nourishing hair mask once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, and always use a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.

By following these tips and using the right techniques and products, you can achieve beautiful beach waves on short thin hair. So go ahead and rock that summer look with confidence!
