How To Make A Round Face Look Slim

Written by Christine Aug 26, 2023 · 4 min read

Round faces are adorable, but sometimes you may want to achieve a slimmer look. In this article, we will discuss how to make a round face look slim. We will provide you with tips, step-by-step guides, FAQs, and hair care tips.

Table of Contents

  • How to Make a Round Face Look Slim
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for a Slimmer Face
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care for a Slimmer Face

How to Make a Round Face Look Slim

There are several ways to make a round face look slim. Some of the most effective methods are:

  • Contouring
  • Choosing the right hairstyle
  • Face yoga
  • Losing weight

These methods work differently for everyone, so it’s important to try them out and see which one works best for you.

Step by Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to make a round face look slim:

Contouring

Contouring is a makeup technique that involves using darker shades to create shadows and lighter shades to highlight areas of the face. To contour your face to look slimmer, follow these steps:

  1. Apply foundation and concealer as usual
  2. Use a contouring powder or cream that is one or two shades darker than your skin tone
  3. Apply the contouring powder or cream to the areas of your face that you want to look slimmer, such as your cheekbones, temples, and jawline
  4. Use a blending brush or sponge to blend the contouring powder or cream into your skin
  5. Apply a highlighter to the areas of your face that you want to stand out, such as the tops of your cheekbones and the bridge of your nose

Choosing the Right Hairstyle

The right hairstyle can make a big difference in how your face looks. Here are some tips for choosing a hairstyle that will make your face look slimmer:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as bobs with curls or waves
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the top of your head, such as a high ponytail or a topknot
  • Consider getting a side-swept fringe to create the illusion of a slimmer face

Face Yoga

Face yoga is a natural way to tone and slim your face. Here’s how to do it:

  1. Sit upright with your shoulders relaxed
  2. Take a deep breath in through your nose
  3. Pucker your lips as if you’re about to kiss someone
  4. Hold the kiss pose for five seconds
  5. Relax your lips and exhale slowly through your mouth
  6. Repeat the exercise five times

Losing Weight

Losing weight can help you achieve a slimmer face, but it’s important to do it in a healthy way. Here are some tips for losing weight safely:

  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Get enough sleep
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Avoid fad diets

Tips for a Slimmer Face

Here are some additional tips for achieving a slimmer face:

  • Drink less alcohol
  • Quit smoking
  • Avoid salty and processed foods
  • Chew your food slowly
  • Practice good posture

FAQs

Can I make my face look slimmer without makeup?

Yes, you can make your face look slimmer without makeup by following a healthy diet, exercising regularly, and practicing good posture.

How long will it take to see results from face yoga?

It varies from person to person, but you should start to see results within a few weeks of practicing face yoga regularly.

Hair Care for a Slimmer Face

Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair that complements your slimmer face:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add height to your hair
  • Avoid heavy styling products that weigh your hair down
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy
  • Consider getting highlights to add dimension to your hair

In conclusion, there are many ways to make a round face look slim, from contouring to face yoga to choosing the right hairstyle. By following these tips and tricks, you can achieve the slimmer face you desire.
