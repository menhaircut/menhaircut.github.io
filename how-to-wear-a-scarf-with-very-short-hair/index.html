How To Wear A Scarf With Very Short Hair

Written by William Aug 25, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Scarves are an excellent accessory that can add color, texture and style to any outfit. However, if you have very short hair, you may be wondering how to wear a scarf without it looking awkward or bulky. Don't worry, with a few simple tips and tricks, you can rock a scarf with your short hair like a pro.

Description

This blog post will provide you with step-by-step instructions on how to wear a scarf with very short hair. We'll also give you some tips on how to choose the right type of scarf and how to care for your hair while wearing one.

How to Wear a Scarf with Very Short Hair

1. Choose the right scarf

When choosing a scarf to wear with very short hair, you want to pick one that is lightweight and not too bulky. A silk scarf or a thin cotton scarf would be ideal.

2. Style your hair

Before putting on your scarf, you want to style your hair in a way that compliments the scarf. You could slick your hair back or add some volume at the crown.

3. Fold the scarf

Take the scarf and fold it in half diagonally to create a triangle shape.

4. Place the scarf on your head

Place the scarf at the nape of your neck with the point of the triangle facing forward.

5. Tie the scarf

Tie the scarf at the top of your head and tuck any excess fabric underneath the knot.

Tips

- Experiment with different scarf styles to find what works best for you

- Try adding a hair accessory, such as a barrette or hair clip, to enhance the look

- Don't tie the scarf too tightly, as this can cause discomfort and damage to your hair

FAQ

Q: Can I wear a scarf with very short hair if I have a fringe or bangs?

A: Yes, you can. Just make sure to tuck the fringe or bangs underneath the scarf to avoid them sticking out awkwardly.

Q: Can I wear a scarf with very short hair if I have curly hair?

A: Absolutely! A scarf can look great with curly hair. Just make sure to choose a scarf that won't flatten your curls too much.

Hair Care

When wearing a scarf with very short hair, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips to keep your hair healthy and looking great:

- Don't tie the scarf too tightly, as this can cause tension and breakage.

- Avoid wearing a scarf for long periods of time, as this can cause your hair to become matted and tangled.

- Moisturize your hair regularly to keep it healthy and hydrated.

With these tips and tricks, you can confidently wear a scarf with your very short hair. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and colors to find what looks best on you.
