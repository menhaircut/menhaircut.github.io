Hair Style Short Sides Long Top A Trendy Haircut For Men

If you're looking for a stylish haircut that's versatile and easy to maintain, the short sides long top hairstyle may be just what you need. This popular hairstyle has been gaining popularity among men in recent years and is a great way to add some edge to your look. In this article, we'll discuss everything you need to know about this hairstyle.

Description

The short sides long top hairstyle is characterized by shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This haircut can be achieved with a variety of hair types, including straight, wavy, and curly hair. The length of the hair on the top can vary from a few inches to several inches, depending on your preference.

How to

To achieve the short sides long top hairstyle, you'll need to visit a barber or stylist. The stylist will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides to the desired length. The length of the hair on the top will be left longer and can be styled in a variety of ways, such as slicked back, messy, or with a side part.

Step by Step

1. Visit a barber or stylist
2. Ask for a short sides long top haircut
3. The stylist will use clippers to cut the hair on the sides
4. The length of the hair on the top will be left longer
5. Style the hair on the top as desired

Hair Care

To keep your hair looking healthy and shiny, it's important to take care of it. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and moisturized. Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair. Instead, let your hair air dry whenever possible. Finally, consider using a hair mask or oil treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
