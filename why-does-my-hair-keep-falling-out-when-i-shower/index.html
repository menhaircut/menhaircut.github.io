Why Does My Hair Keep Falling Out When I Shower

Written by Bardi Jul 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will explore the reasons behind hair fall during shower and how to tackle this hair problem. Hair fall can be a frustrating experience, especially when it happens during a shower. It can be a sign of an underlying health condition or a result of improper hair care. Therefore, it is important to identify the root cause and take necessary measures to prevent hair fall.

Description

Hair fall during shower is a common problem faced by many individuals. It occurs when the hair strands weaken and fall out while shampooing or conditioning the hair. This can be due to various factors such as genetics, hormonal changes, stress, and poor hair care habits. Hair fall can lead to thinning of hair and baldness if not addressed in a timely manner. Therefore, it is important to identify the underlying cause and take steps to prevent hair fall.

How to Identify the Cause of Hair Fall?

There are various factors that can contribute to hair fall during shower. Some of the common causes are:

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Dietary deficiencies
  • Stress
  • Medical conditions
  • Environmental factors

If you are experiencing hair fall during shower, it is important to identify the underlying cause. You can consult a dermatologist or a hair specialist to diagnose the cause of hair fall. They may conduct a physical examination and recommend blood tests to identify any underlying health conditions.

Step by Step Solutions to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some steps you can take to prevent hair fall during shower:

Step 1: Choose the Right Shampoo and Conditioner

Using the right shampoo and conditioner can help prevent hair fall during shower. Choose a product that is suitable for your hair type and free from harsh chemicals. Avoid using hot water to rinse your hair, as it can damage the hair strands and lead to hair fall.

Step 2: Massage Your Scalp

Massaging your scalp during shower can help improve blood circulation and strengthen the hair roots. Use your fingertips to gently massage your scalp in circular motions. This can also help remove any dirt or buildup on the scalp, which can contribute to hair fall.

Step 3: Use a Hair Mask

Using a hair mask once a week can help nourish and strengthen your hair. You can make a hair mask at home using natural ingredients such as yogurt, honey, and olive oil. Apply the hair mask to your hair and leave it on for 30 minutes before rinsing it off with lukewarm water.

Step 4: Eat a Balanced Diet

A balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals can help promote hair growth and prevent hair fall. Include foods such as eggs, nuts, spinach, and salmon in your diet to nourish your hair from within.

Tips to Prevent Hair Fall

Here are some additional tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as ponytails and braids
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is wet
  • Protect your hair from environmental factors such as pollution and UV rays

FAQ

Q: Is hair fall during shower normal?

A: Some hair fall during shower is normal. However, if you are experiencing excessive hair fall, it may be a sign of an underlying health condition or improper hair care habits.

Q: How can I prevent hair fall during shower?

A: You can prevent hair fall during shower by using the right shampoo and conditioner, massaging your scalp, using a hair mask, eating a balanced diet, and following proper hair care habits.

Q: Can stress cause hair fall?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair fall. It can disrupt the hair growth cycle and lead to hair fall. Therefore, it is important to manage stress through relaxation techniques such as yoga and meditation.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential to maintain healthy and strong hair. Follow these hair care tips to prevent hair fall:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a mild shampoo
  • Use a conditioner after shampooing your hair
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that pull on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Eat a balanced diet rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Protect your hair from environmental factors such as pollution and UV rays

In conclusion, hair fall during shower can be a frustrating experience. However, by identifying the underlying cause and following proper hair care habits, you can prevent hair fall and maintain healthy and strong hair.
