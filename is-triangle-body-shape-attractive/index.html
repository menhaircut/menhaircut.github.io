Description

Triangle body shape, also known as pear shape, is characterized by a smaller upper body and wider hips, thighs, and buttocks. This body shape is common among women, and some consider it to be less attractive than other shapes. However, with the right clothing and styling, a triangle body shape can be just as attractive as any other shape.

How to Determine Your Body Shape

Determining your body shape is the first step in dressing for your body type. Here are some steps to follow:

  1. Stand in front of a mirror with your feet together and arms at your sides.
  2. Look at your shoulders and hips. Are they roughly the same width or is one wider than the other?
  3. Look at your waist. Is it well-defined or do you have more of a straight up-and-down shape?
  4. Look at your thighs and buttocks. Are they wider than your shoulders?

If your hips and thighs are wider than your shoulders and your waist is well-defined, you have a triangle body shape.

Step by Step Guide to Dressing for Triangle Body Shape

Here are some tips for dressing a triangle body shape:

  1. Highlight your upper body with V-neck tops, off-the-shoulder styles, and boat necklines.
  2. Wear A-line skirts and dresses to balance out wider hips and thighs.
  3. Choose pants and jeans with a wider leg to balance out wider hips.
  4. Wear darker colors on your lower body to create a slimming effect.
  5. Accessorize with statement jewelry, scarves, and hats to draw attention to your upper body.

Tips for Dressing Triangle Body Shape

Here are some additional tips for dressing a triangle body shape:

  • Avoid clingy fabrics on your lower body that emphasize wider hips and thighs.
  • Avoid tops with puffy sleeves or ruffles that add volume to your upper body.
  • Wear high-waisted pants and skirts to elongate your legs.
  • Avoid belts or waist-cinching styles that draw attention to your wider hips.
  • Invest in a good bra that lifts and supports your bust.

FAQ about Triangle Body Shape

1. Can I wear skinny jeans with a triangle body shape?

Yes, you can wear skinny jeans with a triangle body shape. Just make sure to balance out your wider hips with a looser top or jacket.

2. Can I wear a crop top with a triangle body shape?

Yes, you can wear a crop top with a triangle body shape. Just make sure to pair it with high-waisted pants or a skirt to balance out wider hips.

3. Can I wear a maxi dress with a triangle body shape?

Yes, you can wear a maxi dress with a triangle body shape. Look for styles that cinch at the waist and flow away from the hips to create an hourglass shape.

Hair Care Tips for Triangle Body Shape

Your hairstyle can also play a role in balancing out a triangle body shape. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Choose hairstyles that add volume to the top of your head, such as a layered bob or a voluminous updo.
  • Avoid hairstyles that emphasize width around the jawline, such as a blunt bob or a center part.
  • Accessorize with headbands or hair clips to draw attention to your upper body.

Overall, a triangle body shape can be just as attractive as any other shape with the right clothing, styling, and hair care. Embrace your unique shape and have fun experimenting with different styles!
