Tzuyu's Beauty Secrets

If you're wondering how Tzuyu manages to look so gorgeous all the time, you're not alone. Here are some of her beauty secrets:

1. Skincare is Key

Tzuyu is known for her flawless skin, and it's no wonder why. She takes her skincare routine very seriously and makes sure to cleanse, tone, and moisturize every day. She also uses sheet masks and facial oils to keep her skin hydrated and glowing.

2. Healthy Diet

Tzuyu understands the importance of a healthy diet when it comes to maintaining her beauty. She eats lots of fruits and vegetables and avoids processed foods and sugary drinks.

3. Regular Exercise

Tzuyu stays in shape by exercising regularly. She enjoys yoga, Pilates, and dance, which helps her maintain her toned figure.

Other Members' Beauty Secrets

Of course, Tzuyu isn't the only member of Twice with beauty secrets. Here are some tips from other members:

1. Nayeon

Nayeon keeps her skin looking great by using a gentle cleanser and lots of moisturizer. She also makes sure to wear sunscreen every day to protect her skin from sun damage.

2. Jeongyeon

Jeongyeon maintains her youthful appearance by drinking lots of water and getting plenty of sleep. She also avoids smoking and drinking, which can age the skin prematurely.

3. Momo

Momo keeps her figure toned and trim by dancing every day. She also eats a balanced diet and avoids fast food and processed snacks.

Hair Care Tips from Twice

In addition to their skincare and beauty routines, the members of Twice also have some great hair care tips to share. Here are some of their secrets:

1. Sana

Sana keeps her hair looking shiny and healthy by using a deep conditioning treatment once a week. She also avoids using heat styling tools too often, which can damage the hair.

2. Dahyun

Dahyun keeps her hair looking voluminous by using a root-lifting spray. She also avoids washing her hair every day, which can strip it of its natural oils.

3. Chaeyoung

Chaeyoung keeps her hair looking vibrant by using a color-protecting shampoo and conditioner. She also avoids using hot water when washing her hair, as it can cause the color to fade.
