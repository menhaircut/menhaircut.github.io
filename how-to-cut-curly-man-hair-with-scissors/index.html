How To Cut Curly Man Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting curly hair can be a challenge, but with the right technique and tools, it can be done easily. In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut curly man hair with scissors. We will also give you tips on how to care for your curly hair after the cut. So, let's get started!

Description

Curly hair can be tricky to cut, and it requires a different approach than straight hair. Cutting curly hair with scissors requires a certain level of skill and experience. It is essential to understand the texture and pattern of the hair to achieve the desired result. You also need to have the right tools, including a pair of sharp scissors and a comb.

How to Cut Curly Man Hair with Scissors: A Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

The first step in cutting curly hair is to wash and dry it. This ensures that the hair is clean and free from any tangles or knots. Once the hair is dry, comb it out to remove any remaining tangles.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Divide the hair into sections using clips or hair ties. This makes it easier to work through the hair and ensures that you don't miss any spots.

Step 3: Start Cutting

Start cutting the hair at the bottom of the section. Hold the hair between your fingers and cut it at an angle. This creates layers in the hair, which helps to define the curls. Continue cutting the hair in small sections, working your way up to the top of the section.

Step 4: Check the Length

Check the length of the hair as you go along to ensure that it is even. Use your fingers to measure the length of the hair and cut any sections that are longer than the rest.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Blend the hair by cutting into the ends of the hair. This helps to create a more natural look and ensures that the hair is not too bulky.

Step 6: Repeat the Process

Repeat the process on the rest of the sections until all the hair is cut. Make sure that you cut all sections evenly to achieve a uniform look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut curly hair with scissors:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start by cutting less hair than you think you need to. You can always go back and cut more if necessary.
  • Don't cut the hair too short, as this can cause the curls to lose their shape.
  • Use a comb to help guide the scissors and ensure that the hair is evenly cut.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting curly hair can be tiring, so take a break if you feel tired or your hands start to hurt.

FAQ

How often should I cut my curly hair?

It is recommended that you cut your curly hair every 6-8 weeks to maintain its shape and prevent split ends.

Can I cut my curly hair while it is wet?

It is best to cut curly hair when it is dry, as wet hair can appear longer and may cause you to cut too much off.

Should I use thinning shears on my curly hair?

It is not recommended to use thinning shears on curly hair, as this can cause the curls to become frizzy.

Hair Care

After cutting your curly hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its shape and keep it healthy. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping the hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it is dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help define the curls.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, as this can damage the hair and cause it to lose its shape.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep the hair moisturized.

By following these tips, you can keep your curly hair looking great after your cut.

Conclusion

Cutting curly man hair with scissors requires a certain level of skill and experience. By following the steps and tips provided in this article, you can achieve a great haircut that maintains the natural shape of your curls. Remember to take care of your hair after the cut to keep it healthy and looking great.
