Diy Face Frame Haircut At Home A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your same old hairstyle and want to switch it up without going to the salon? A face frame haircut is a perfect way to change your look without losing too much length. It provides a flattering and youthful look, highlighting your best features. In this step-by-step guide, we will show you how to achieve a face frame haircut at home.

What is a Face Frame Haircut?

A face frame haircut is a layering technique that creates layers around the face, framing it beautifully. It enhances your facial features, creating a soft and flattering look. This technique involves snipping sections of hair that frame the face, creating an illusion of a slimmer and more angular face.

How to Achieve a Face Frame Haircut at Home?

Achieving a face frame haircut at home is easier than you think. All you need is a pair of good quality scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Here is a step-by-step guide to help you get started:

Step 1: Clean and Dry Your Hair

Before you start cutting, make sure your hair is clean and dry. This will help you get a more accurate cut. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to clean your hair, and let it dry naturally or blow-dry it.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Use a comb to section your hair. Start by parting your hair down the middle, creating two sections. Then, divide each section into two, creating a total of four sections, two on each side.

Step 3: Begin Cutting

Start by taking a small section of hair from the front and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your fingers, and start cutting it at an angle. Cut diagonally, starting from the chin and working your way upwards, towards the ear.

Repeat this process on the other side, making sure both sides match. Remember to cut slowly and carefully, taking small sections of hair at a time.

Step 4: Blend the Layers

Once you have cut both sides, it's time to blend the layers. Take a comb and comb through your hair, making sure the layers are even on both sides. Use the scissors to trim any uneven ends.

Step 5: Finish the Look

Once you have achieved the desired look, it's time to finish the look. Use a hairbrush to style your hair, and apply a light styling product, such as a serum or mousse, to add volume and shine.

Tips for Achieving the Perfect Face Frame Haircut

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect face frame haircut at home:

  • Take your time and cut slowly.
  • Use good quality scissors to achieve a clean cut.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before starting.
  • Cut small sections of hair at a time.
  • Comb through your hair to ensure even layers.
  • Use a styling product to add volume and shine.

FAQs

Can I achieve a face frame haircut on any hair type?

Yes, a face frame haircut can be achieved on any hair type, whether it's straight, curly, or wavy.

How often should I get a face frame haircut?

A face frame haircut grows out nicely, so it's a low-maintenance haircut. You can get it trimmed every 2-4 months, depending on the length and your hair growth rate.

Can I achieve a face frame haircut on my own?

Yes, you can achieve a face frame haircut on your own, as long as you have the right tools and follow the steps carefully.

Hair Care Tips for Maintaining Your Face Frame Haircut

To keep your face frame haircut looking its best, here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and length of your haircut.
  • Use a hair serum or oil to add moisture to your hair and prevent split ends.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather by wearing a hat or using a protective hair spray.

Achieving a face frame haircut at home is a great way to switch up your look without going to the salon. With the right tools and techniques, you can achieve a beautiful and flattering haircut that highlights your best features. Follow these steps and tips, and you'll be on your way to achieving the perfect face frame haircut.
