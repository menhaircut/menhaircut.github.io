How To Do Twist In Black Men s Hair Short A Step By Step Guide

Written by Frank Dec 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Looking for a new hairstyle to switch things up? Twists are a versatile option that can be worn in a variety of ways. If you have short hair and you’re looking to try twists, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we’ll be breaking down everything you need to know about how to do twist in black men's hair short.

Description

Twists are a popular hairstyle for black men because they’re low-maintenance and they look great. They’re also versatile, so you can wear them in a variety of styles. If you have short hair, you might think that twists aren’t an option for you, but that’s not the case. With the right technique and a little bit of practice, you can do twists in short hair.

How To

Before we dive into the step-by-step process of doing twists in short hair, let’s talk about the tools you’ll need. You’ll need a comb, a brush, some hair gel or pomade, and some hair clips. Once you have these tools, you’re ready to get started.

Step 1: Start With Clean Hair

The first step to doing twists in short hair is to make sure your hair is clean. Shampoo and condition your hair as normal, and then towel dry it until it’s damp.

Step 2: Apply Hair Gel

Apply a small amount of hair gel or pomade to your hair. This will help to hold the twists in place and give your hair some texture.

Step 3: Part Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair into sections. The size of the sections will depend on how thick you want your twists to be. Use hair clips to keep the sections separate.

Step 4: Twist Your Hair

Take one section of hair and divide it into two sections. Twist the two sections around each other until you reach the end of the section. Repeat this process with each section of hair.

Step 5: Let Your Hair Dry

Once you’ve twisted all of your hair, let it dry. You can either let it air dry or use a hair dryer on a low setting.

Step 6: Unravel Your Twists

Once your hair is dry, unravel your twists. Use your fingers to separate the twists and create the desired style.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when doing twists in short hair:

  • Use a hair gel or pomade to help hold the twists in place
  • Part your hair into sections to make the twisting process easier
  • Twist your hair tightly to create defined twists
  • Let your hair dry completely before unraveling the twists

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about doing twists in short hair:

Can you do twists in really short hair?

Yes, you can do twists in really short hair. Just make sure to use a hair gel or pomade to help hold the twists in place.

How long do twists last?

Twists can last anywhere from a few days to a few weeks, depending on how well you take care of them.

How do you maintain twists?

To maintain twists, make sure to keep your hair moisturized and avoid using products that contain alcohol, as this can dry out your hair.

Hair Care

To keep your twists looking their best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo
  • Deep condition your hair once a week
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using heat on your hair

Conclusion

Twists are a great hairstyle for black men with short hair. With a little bit of practice, you can do twists at home and switch up your style. Just remember to use the right tools and take care of your hair to keep your twists looking their best.
