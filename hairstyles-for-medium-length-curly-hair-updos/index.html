Hairstyles For Medium Length Curly Hair Updos

Written by Oliver Oct 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Curly hair can be both a blessing and a curse. While it looks beautiful and voluminous, it can also be difficult to manage and style. If you have medium length curly hair, you may be looking for some updo hairstyles that are easy to create and will keep your curls in place. In this article, we'll take a look at some popular hairstyles for medium length curly hair updos, and provide you with tips and tricks to make them look their best.

Description

Medium length curly hair is hair that falls somewhere between your shoulders and your collarbone. It can be a challenge to style, but the right updo can create a stunning look that will turn heads. Whether you're attending a wedding, a formal event, or just want to change up your everyday look, there are plenty of hairstyles for medium length curly hair updos to choose from.

How to

Creating an updo for medium length curly hair can be done in a few simple steps:

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. If your hair is prone to frizz, you may want to use a smoothing serum or cream to tame flyaways.
  2. Brush your hair to remove any tangles, but don't over-brush, as this can cause your curls to lose their shape.
  3. Decide on the type of updo you want. Some popular options include a messy bun, a braided crown, or a half-up, half-down style.
  4. Begin styling your hair, starting at the back and working your way forward. Use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your hair in place.
  5. Finish with a hairspray or styling product to hold your updo in place.

Step by step

Let's take a closer look at how to create two popular updos for medium length curly hair:

Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.
  2. Twist the ponytail around itself until it forms a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins or a hair tie.
  4. Pull out a few strands of hair around your face to create a messy, effortless look.
  5. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your bun in place.

Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle and create two even sections.
  2. Take a small section of hair from the front of one side and begin braiding it, adding in more hair as you move towards the back of your head.
  3. Once you've reached the back of your head, secure the braid with a bobby pin or hair tie.
  4. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Tuck the ends of the braids behind your ears and secure them with bobby pins.
  6. Spray your hair with a light hold hairspray to keep your braids in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your medium length curly hair updos:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb or your fingers to comb through your curls, rather than a brush.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or oil to your hair to keep it hydrated and healthy.
  • Experiment with different updo styles until you find one that works for you.
  • Don't be afraid to add accessories, such as flowers or hairpins, to your updo to give it a more formal look.

FAQ

Here are some common questions people have about hairstyles for medium length curly hair updos:

What should I do if my curls are frizzy?

If your curls are prone to frizz, try using a smoothing serum or cream before styling your hair. You can also use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to help define your curls and reduce frizz.

Can I wear an updo if I have short curly hair?

While some updos may be more difficult to create with short curly hair, there are still plenty of options to choose from. Consider a messy bun or a braided halo to add some volume and texture to your hair.

How can I make my updo last all day?

Using a strong hold hairspray or styling product can help keep your updo in place all day. You can also use bobby pins or hair ties to secure your hair more tightly.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for keeping your medium length curly hair healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid using harsh shampoos or styling products that contain alcohol, as these can dry out your hair and cause damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools, such as curling irons or straighteners, too often, as these can damage your hair.
  • Consider getting regular trims to keep your ends healthy and prevent split ends.

With these tips and hairstyles for medium length curly hair updos, you'll be able to create a stunning look that will make you feel confident and beautiful. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles and accessories to find the perfect updo for you.
