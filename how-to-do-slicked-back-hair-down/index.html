How to Do Slicked Back Hair Down

The slicked back hair down style is a classic look that never goes out of fashion. Itâs perfect for both casual and formal occasions, and itâs easy to style. Hereâs how to do it:

Step-by-Step Guide:

Step 1: Start with clean, damp hair. Use a comb to part your hair on one side.

Step 2: Apply a small amount of styling product to your hair, such as pomade or gel. Rub the product between your hands to warm it up and distribute it evenly throughout your hair.

Step 3: Use a comb to slick your hair back, starting at the hairline and working your way back to the crown of your head. Keep the sides and back of your hair close to your head for a sleek look.

Step 4: Use a hair dryer on a low setting to dry your hair and set the style. Use a comb to shape your hair as you dry it.

Step 5: Finish with a small amount of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for a Great Look:

Here are some tips for achieving a great slicked back hair down look:

  • Choose the right styling product for your hair type. Pomade works well for thick or curly hair, while gel is better for fine or straight hair.
  • Experiment with different parting styles to find the one that suits you best.
  • Use a comb to create a smooth, polished look. Avoid using your fingers, as this can create a messy, unpolished look.
  • Donât use too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.

FAQ:

Q: Can I do slicked back hair down with long hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, you may need to use more product to keep your hair in place.

Q: How often should I wash my hair if I have slicked back hair down?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Generally, you should wash your hair every other day or every three days to prevent product buildup.

Q: Can I do slicked back hair down with curly hair?

A: Yes, you can. However, you may need to use a stronger styling product to keep your curls in place.

Hair Care for Slicked Back Hair Down:

Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy and looking great with slicked back hair down:

  • Use a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair soft and manageable.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons or curling irons, as these can damage your hair.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and to prevent split ends.
