Is Red Hair Back in Fashion? Everything You Need to Know

If you're considering changing up your hair color, you may be wondering if red hair is back in fashion. The good news is that red hair is definitely making a comeback in 2023, and it's a great time to try out this bold and beautiful shade. In this article, we'll explore everything you need to know about red hair, including how to achieve the perfect shade, tips for maintaining your color, and more.

Why is Red Hair Back in Fashion?

Red hair has always been an attention-grabbing and unique hair color, but it's recently gained popularity because of the rise of celebrities like Emma Stone, Julianne Moore, and Bella Thorne. Red hair is a bold and confident choice that can make you stand out in a crowd. It's also a versatile color that can suit a variety of skin tones and styles.

How to Achieve the Perfect Red Hair Color

If you're ready to try out red hair, there are a few things to keep in mind when choosing your shade. The first step is to determine your skin tone. If you have warm undertones, you'll want to go for a more orange-red shade, whereas cool undertones look best with a blue-red shade.

Next, consider the intensity of the color. If you're a natural redhead, you may want to go for a more subtle shade that enhances your natural color. If you're looking for a dramatic change, a bold and vibrant shade may be the way to go.

When it comes to dyeing your hair, it's always best to visit a professional stylist. They can help you choose the perfect shade and ensure that your hair is properly prepped and cared for during the dyeing process.

Tips for Maintaining Your Red Hair Color

Once you've achieved your perfect shade of red, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain the color. Here are some tips:

Use Color-Safe Products

Make sure to use products that are specifically designed for color-treated hair. These products will help to protect your color and prevent fading.

Avoid Hot Water

Hot water can cause your color to fade faster, so try to use lukewarm water when washing your hair.

Avoid Over-Washing

Washing your hair too often can also cause your color to fade faster. Try to wash your hair every other day or every few days to maintain the color.

Protect Your Hair from the Sun

The sun's UV rays can also cause your color to fade faster, so make sure to wear a hat or use a UV-protecting spray when spending time outdoors.

FAQ About Red Hair

Is red hair high maintenance?

Red hair can be high maintenance, as it requires regular touch-ups and special care to maintain the color. However, with the right products and routine, it can be manageable.

What skin tones look best with red hair?

Red hair can look great on a variety of skin tones, but warm undertones look best with an orange-red shade, while cool undertones look best with a blue-red shade.

Can I dye my hair red at home?

While it's possible to dye your hair red at home, it's always best to visit a professional stylist to ensure that the color is applied correctly and your hair is properly cared for.

Final Thoughts

Red hair is definitely back in fashion in 2023, and it's a great time to try out this bold and beautiful hair color. Whether you're going for a dramatic change or a subtle enhancement, there's a shade of red that will suit you perfectly. Just remember to take care of your hair to maintain the color and ensure that it stays looking its best.
