Best Men s Haircut For Oblong Face A Comprehensive Guide

Having an oblong face shape can make choosing the right haircut a bit tricky. You want to find a style that will flatter your face shape and highlight your best features. In this article, we will go over the best men's haircut for oblong face shapes and provide tips on how to maintain it.

Description

An oblong face shape is longer than it is wide, with a narrow chin and forehead. The goal of a good haircut for oblong face shapes is to add width to the face, making it appear more balanced.

How to Choose the Best Men's Haircut for Oblong Face

When it comes to choosing the best men's haircut for oblong face shapes, there are a few things to consider:

  • Length: Shorter hairstyles tend to work well for oblong face shapes because they add width to the face.
  • Layers: Layers can help add volume and texture to the hair, which can make the face appear wider.
  • Bangs: Bangs can help shorten the forehead and balance out the face shape.
  • Sides: Shorter sides can help create the illusion of a wider face.

Step by Step Guide on Getting the Best Men's Haircut for Oblong Face

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to get the best men's haircut for oblong face shapes:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist: Your hairstylist can help you choose the best haircut for your face shape and hair type.
  2. Choose a style: Consider shorter hairstyles with layers, bangs, and shorter sides.
  3. Communicate with your hairstylist: Be clear with your hairstylist about the style you want and any concerns you may have.
  4. Maintain your haircut: Regular trims will help keep your haircut looking great.

Tips on Maintaining Your Best Men's Haircut for Oblong Face

Here are some tips on how to maintain your best men's haircut for oblong face shapes:

  • Regular trims: Keep your haircut looking fresh by getting regular trims.
  • Use the right products: Use styling products that will add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Consider hair accessories: Hats and headbands can help add volume to your hair and create the illusion of a wider face.

FAQs

What is the best haircut for oblong face shapes?

The best haircut for oblong face shapes is a shorter style with layers, bangs, and shorter sides.

Can I still have longer hair with an oblong face shape?

Yes, you can still have longer hair with an oblong face shape. Just be sure to add layers and volume to create the illusion of a wider face.

What styling products should I use?

Use styling products that will add volume and texture to your hair, such as mousse or texturizing spray.

Hair Care for Oblong Face Shapes

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a great haircut for oblong face shapes. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly.
  • Use a conditioner to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot tools too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a protective spray.

Choosing the best men's haircut for oblong face shapes can be challenging, but with the right style and care, you can look your best. Consider consulting with your hairstylist and following these tips to maintain a great haircut.
