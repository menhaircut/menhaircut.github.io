How To Curl Short Hair With A 1 1 2 Inch Curling Iron

Written by Daniel Nov 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to add some volume and texture to your short hair? Look no further than a 1 1/2 inch curling iron. With the right technique and products, you can achieve beautiful curls that will last all day. In this article, we’ll go over everything you need to know to curl short hair with a 1 1/2 inch curling iron.

Table of Contents

  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Curling Short Hair

How to Curl Short Hair with a 1 1/2 Inch Curling Iron

Before we get started, let's talk about why a 1 1/2 inch curling iron is the perfect tool for short hair. This size barrel creates loose, bouncy waves that add volume and texture to short hair. Plus, it’s easier to use than smaller curling irons because you don’t need to wrap your hair as tightly around the barrel.

Here are the steps to curl short hair with a 1 1/2 inch curling iron:

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start with clean, dry hair. Apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from the hot curling iron.
  2. Section your hair into 1-2 inch sections.
  3. Take one section of hair and clamp the curling iron near the roots.
  4. Wrap the section of hair around the barrel, leaving the ends out. Hold for 5-10 seconds.
  5. Release the curl and let it cool in your hand before moving on to the next section.
  6. Repeat on all sections of hair, curling away from your face.
  7. Once all sections are curled, use your fingers to loosen the curls and create a more natural look.
  8. Finish with a hairspray to hold the curls in place.

Tips for Curling Short Hair

Curling short hair can be tricky, but with these tips, you’ll be a pro in no time:

  • Use a 1 1/2 inch curling iron for loose, bouncy waves.
  • Clamp the curling iron near the roots for maximum volume.
  • Leave the ends of your hair out of the curling iron for a more natural look.
  • Curl away from your face for a flattering look.
  • Loosen the curls with your fingers for a more natural, tousled look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about curling short hair with a 1 1/2 inch curling iron:

Q: Can I use a smaller curling iron for short hair?
A: Yes, you can use a smaller curling iron, but it will create tighter curls. If you want loose, bouncy waves, a 1 1/2 inch curling iron is the way to go. Q: How do I prevent my curls from falling flat?
A: Use a hairspray to hold the curls in place. You can also apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your hair before curling to add extra hold. Q: How often should I curl my hair?
A: It’s best to limit heat styling to once or twice a week to prevent damage to your hair. On non-curling days, you can use dry shampoo or texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair.

Hair Care for Curly Hair

To keep your curls looking their best, it’s important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and frizz.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner or curl cream to your hair before styling to add moisture and definition.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can cause frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair.
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent frizz and breakage.

By following these tips and techniques, you can achieve beautiful, bouncy curls with a 1 1/2 inch curling iron. With a little practice, you’ll be able to curl your short hair like a pro!
