Which Hairstyle Will Suit For Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Written by Oliver Jul 23, 2023 Â· 3 min read

As we all know, hairstyles can make or break your look. But when it comes to round faces, finding the perfect hairstyle can be a challenge. If you have a round face shape, you want to choose a hairstyle that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. In this article, we will give you some tips and tricks on which hairstyle will suit for round face.

Description

A round face is characterized by equal length and width. The cheekbones are typically the widest part of the face, and the jawline is rounded. The goal when choosing a hairstyle for a round face is to add length and create angles to balance out the roundness.

How to

When it comes to choosing a hairstyle for a round face, there are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as a high ponytail or top knot.
  • Long, layered haircuts can be very flattering for round faces as they create the illusion of length.
  • A side-swept fringe can also help to elongate the face.
  • Hairstyles that are too short or too blunt can make the face appear even rounder.

Step by Step

Here are some step-by-step instructions on how to achieve some of the best hairstyles for round faces:

Long Layers

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Part your hair in the center.
  3. Using a curling iron, create loose waves throughout your hair.
  4. Apply a texturizing spray to add volume.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Side-Swept Bangs

  1. Start with clean, dry hair.
  2. Using a round brush, blow dry your hair straight.
  3. Part your hair to the side.
  4. Section off your bangs and blow dry them to the side.
  5. Finish with a light hold hairspray.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle for a round face:

  • Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as a straight bob or blunt fringe.
  • Opt for hairstyles that add height to the crown of the head, such as a messy bun or half-up, half-down hairstyle.
  • Consider adding highlights or lowlights to create dimension and add length to the face.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of the face, such as a straight bob or blunt fringe. These styles can make the face appear even rounder.

What hairstyles are best for a round face?

The best hairstyles for a round face are those that add height to the crown of the head, create angles, and elongate the face. Examples include long layers, side-swept bangs, and high ponytails.

Should I add highlights or lowlights to my hair?

Adding highlights or lowlights can create dimension and add length to the face. Consult with your stylist to determine the best color placement for your face shape.

Hair Care

In addition to choosing the right hairstyle, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it looking its best. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that is formulated for your hair type.
  • Avoid using hot tools on a daily basis as they can cause damage to your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Avoid over-washing your hair as it can strip it of its natural oils.

Now that you know which hairstyle will suit for round face, you can confidently choose a hairstyle that flatters your face shape. Remember to consider your hair type, lifestyle, and personal preferences when choosing a hairstyle.
