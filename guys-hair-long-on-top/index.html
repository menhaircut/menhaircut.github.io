Guys Hair Long On Top A Relaxed Guide

Written by William Oct 08, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're looking for a new hairstyle that's both trendy and easy to maintain, guys hair long on top may be the perfect choice for you. This versatile cut allows you to experiment with different styles and looks, making it a popular choice among men of all ages. In this guide, we'll explore everything you need to know about guys hair long on top, from how to get the look to tips for maintaining your new style.

Description

Guys hair long on top is a hairstyle that features longer hair on the top of the head, with shorter hair on the sides and back. This cut allows you to style your hair in a variety of ways, from slicked back to messy and textured. It's a popular choice among men who want a modern, on-trend look that's easy to maintain.

How to

If you're interested in getting guys hair long on top, the first step is to find a stylist who is experienced in this type of cut. They'll be able to help you determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape. Once you have your haircut, you can experiment with different styling products and techniques to create a look that's all your own.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting guys hair long on top:

  1. Find a stylist who is experienced in this type of cut.
  2. Determine the best length and style for your hair type and face shape.
  3. Get your hair cut and styled.
  4. Experiment with different styling products and techniques to create your desired look.

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your guys hair long on top:

  • Use a high-quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Invest in a good styling product that works for your hair type and desired look.
  • Visit your stylist regularly for trims and touch-ups.
  • Experiment with different styles and looks to keep your hairstyle fresh and on-trend.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about guys hair long on top:

Q: Is guys hair long on top difficult to maintain?

A: Not at all! With the right products and styling techniques, this cut is easy to maintain and style on a daily basis.

Q: What face shapes work best with guys hair long on top?

A: This cut works well with a variety of face shapes, but it tends to look best on men with oval or square-shaped faces.

Q: Can I still get guys hair long on top if I have thinning hair?

A: Absolutely! Your stylist can work with you to create a cut that works with your hair type and helps to give the appearance of thicker, fuller hair.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for keeping your guys hair long on top healthy and looking great:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a high-quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and dryness.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.

With these tips and tricks, you can rock the guys hair long on top look with confidence and style!
