How To Know If Curtain Bangs Look Good On You

Written by Daniel Aug 01, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you thinking of getting curtain bangs but not sure if it would suit your face shape or hair type? Well, you have come to the right place. In this article, we will guide you on how to know if curtain bangs look good on you.

Table of Content

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Type
  • Try Face Framing Strands
  • Use Virtual Try-On Tools
  • Seek Professional Opinion
  • Maintenance Tips for Curtain Bangs

Determining Your Face Shape

The first step in knowing if curtain bangs look good on you is to determine your face shape. Some face shapes that suit curtain bangs are oval, heart, and square. If you have a round or oblong face shape, it may not be the best choice for you. However, there are exceptions to every rule, and if you want to try out curtain bangs, go for it!

How to Determine Your Face Shape:

  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, you have a round face shape.
  • If your forehead is wider than your jawline, you have a heart-shaped face.
  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, but your face is longer than it is wide, you have an oblong face shape.
  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, and your face is as long as it is wide, you have a square face shape.
  • If your forehead, cheekbones, and jawline are of similar width, and your face tapers to a point at the chin, you have a diamond-shaped face.
  • If your forehead and jawline are narrow, and your cheekbones are the widest part of your face, you have a pear-shaped face.

Consider Your Hair Type

Curtain bangs work well with most hair types, but if you have very curly or frizzy hair, it may not be the best choice for you. Curtain bangs tend to look better on straight or wavy hair. If you have curly hair, you may need to straighten your bangs regularly to maintain the look.

Try Face Framing Strands

If you are not sure if curtain bangs will suit you, try face framing strands first. These are shorter layers of hair that frame your face but are not as dramatic as curtain bangs. Face framing strands can help you get used to having shorter hair around your face and help you make a decision about getting curtain bangs.

Use Virtual Try-On Tools

If you are still unsure about getting curtain bangs, try using virtual try-on tools. These are tools that allow you to upload a photo of yourself and try on different hairstyles, including curtain bangs. This will give you a better idea of how it will look on you.

Seek Professional Opinion

If you are still not sure if curtain bangs will suit you, seek professional opinion. Your hairstylist can help you determine if curtain bangs will suit your face shape and hair type. They can also give you advice on how to style and maintain your curtain bangs.

Maintenance Tips for Curtain Bangs

Once you have decided to get curtain bangs, here are some maintenance tips to keep them looking good:

  • Trim your bangs regularly to avoid split ends.
  • Blow dry your bangs with a round brush to create the curtain effect.
  • Use a dry shampoo to avoid washing your bangs too often.
  • Avoid touching your bangs too often as this can make them greasy.

FAQs

How long should curtain bangs be?

Curtain bangs should be long enough to reach your cheekbones or jawline. They should be shorter in the middle and longer on the sides.

Can curtain bangs be styled differently?

Yes, curtain bangs can be styled differently. They can be parted in the middle or on the side. They can also be swept to the side or tucked behind the ears.

Do curtain bangs require a lot of maintenance?

Yes, curtain bangs require regular maintenance. They need to be trimmed regularly to avoid split ends, and they need to be styled with a round brush to create the curtain effect.

Now that you know how to determine if curtain bangs look good on you, go ahead and give them a try. They are a great way to update your hairstyle and add a touch of femininity to your look.
