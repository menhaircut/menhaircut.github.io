How To Thin Guys Hair With Scissors A Step By Step Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 15, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you're a guy with thick hair, you know how frustrating it can be to style it. Fortunately, there's a solution: thinning your hair with scissors. Thinning can help you achieve a more manageable style, while keeping your hair looking healthy and full. In this article, we'll show you how to thin guys hair with scissors in a few easy steps.

How to Thin Guys Hair with Scissors

Here's what you'll need to get started:

  • A pair of hairdressing scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you start thinning your hair, you'll want to wash and dry it. This will help you see the natural texture of your hair, and make it easier to work with.

Step 2: Section Your Hair

Using your comb, section your hair into small parts. The smaller the sections, the more control you'll have over the thinning process.

Step 3: Hold the Scissors Correctly

Hold your scissors with your index and middle fingers through the handles, and your thumb on top of the scissors. This will give you the most control over the blades.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Start at the tips of your hair, and work your way up to the roots. Use the scissors to cut small, diagonal lines into the hair, being careful not to cut too much at once.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've thinned out all of the sections, use your comb to blend the hair together. This will make your hair look more natural, and prevent any obvious lines from forming.

Tips for Thinning Guys Hair with Scissors

Thinning your hair with scissors is an art form, so here are a few tips to help you get the best results:

  • Start with small sections of hair, and work your way up to larger sections
  • Cut at an angle to create a natural-looking finish
  • Don't cut too much at once, as it's difficult to fix mistakes
  • Be patient, and take your time

FAQ About Thinning Guys Hair with Scissors

Is it better to thin hair with scissors or clippers?

It depends on your hair type and the look you're going for. Scissors are better for creating a natural, textured finish, while clippers are better for a more uniform look.

Can you thin your hair too much?

Yes, you can thin your hair too much. Thinning your hair too much can make it look stringy and unhealthy.

How often should I thin my hair?

It depends on your hair type and how quickly it grows. Generally, you should thin your hair every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care Tips for Thinning Guys Hair

Thinning your hair can make it more manageable, but it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that's right for your hair type
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners too often
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized

Thinning your hair with scissors can be a great way to achieve a more manageable style. With a little practice, you can become an expert at thinning your own hair. Just remember to take your time, and be patient.
