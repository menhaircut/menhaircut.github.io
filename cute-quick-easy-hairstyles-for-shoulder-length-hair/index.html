Cute Quick Easy Hairstyles For Shoulder Length Hair

Written by Luffy Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In search of a new hairstyle that is easy to do, cute, and perfect for shoulder length hair? Look no further! This article will provide you with a variety of cute quick and easy hairstyles that are perfect for shoulder length hair. Whether you're running late or just want to switch up your look, these hairstyles are sure to impress.

Description

Shoulder length hair can sometimes be tricky to style, but there are plenty of cute and easy options available. From braids to buns, there are a variety of hairstyles that can be done quickly and easily. These hairstyles are perfect for any occasion, whether you're heading to work or out for a night on the town.

How To

Most of these hairstyles can be achieved in just a few simple steps. All you need is a few hair ties, bobby pins, and a little bit of patience. Follow the steps below for each hairstyle:

1. Half-Up Top Knot

Start by pulling the top half of your hair up into a high ponytail. Then, twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base, securing it with bobby pins. This will create a cute and trendy top knot.

2. Messy Bun

Gather your hair into a high ponytail and twist it loosely. Then, wrap the twisted hair around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins. Pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

3. Braided Crown

Start by parting your hair down the middle. Then, braid both sides of your hair, starting at the top of your head. Once both braids are complete, wrap them around the top of your head, securing them with bobby pins.

4. Low Ponytail

Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Secure it with a hair tie and pull out a few strands to create a relaxed look.

Step By Step

Follow these step-by-step instructions to achieve these cute and easy hairstyles:

1. Half-Up Top Knot

  1. Pull the top half of your hair up into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base.
  3. Secure it with bobby pins.

2. Messy Bun

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail loosely and wrap it around the base.
  3. Secure it with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

3. Braided Crown

  1. Part your hair down the middle.
  2. Braid both sides of your hair, starting at the top of your head.
  3. Wrap the braids around the top of your head.
  4. Secure them with bobby pins.

4. Low Ponytail

  1. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Pull out a few strands to create a relaxed look.

Tips

These hairstyles can be made even cuter with the addition of hair accessories. Try adding a headband, ribbon, or cute hair clip to make your hairstyle stand out even more.

FAQ

Q: Will these hairstyles work for all hair types?

A: Yes! These hairstyles are perfect for all hair types, including straight, curly, and wavy hair.

Q: How long will these hairstyles take to do?

A: These hairstyles can be done in just a few minutes, making them perfect for busy mornings or when you're short on time.

Q: Do I need any special tools to achieve these hairstyles?

A: No, all you need is a few hair ties and bobby pins to achieve these cute and easy hairstyles.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Make sure to use a good shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using heat styling tools too often. If you do use heat styling tools, make sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage. Regular trims can also help keep your hair looking healthy and free of split ends.

There you have it! These cute, quick, and easy hairstyles are perfect for shoulder length hair. Whether you're running late or just want to switch up your look, these hairstyles are sure to impress. Try them out and see which one works best for you!
