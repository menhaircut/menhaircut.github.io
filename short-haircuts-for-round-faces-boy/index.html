Short Haircuts For Round Faces Boy How To Choose The Perfect Style

Written by Petter Aug 27, 2023 Â· 5 min read

If you're a boy with a round face, you might find it challenging to choose a haircut that complements your face shape. However, with the right haircut, you can accentuate your features and create a stylish look. In this article, we will explore some of the best short haircuts for round faces boy and provide tips on how to choose the perfect style for you.

How to Choose the Right Haircut for Your Round Face

Before we dive into the best short haircuts for round faces boy, it's essential to understand how to choose the right style based on your face shape. A round face is characterized by a circular shape with equal width and length, and prominent cheekbones. Here are some tips to keep in mind when selecting a haircut:

Consider Your Hair Type

Your hair type will play a significant role in determining the ideal haircut for your round face. If you have thick hair, you might want to opt for a short and layered style that adds texture and volume. If you have fine hair, a buzz cut or a classic crew cut can make your hair look thicker and fuller.

Think about Your Personal Style

Your personal style should also influence your choice of haircut. If you prefer a more classic and timeless look, a short back and sides haircut can be a great option. If you're more into edgy and modern styles, you might want to go for a spiky or messy haircut that adds some texture and movement.

Pay Attention to Proportions

When choosing a haircut, it's essential to pay attention to proportions. You want to create a balanced look that doesn't make your face look too round or too long. A general rule of thumb is to choose a haircut that adds height to your crown and keeps the hair on the sides and back short.

Short Haircuts for Round Faces Boy: The Best Styles

Now that you know how to choose the right haircut for your round face, let's explore some of the best short haircuts for round faces boy:

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is a classic and timeless style that can work well for boys with round faces. It's a low-maintenance haircut that keeps the hair short all around the head, making your face appear more elongated. This style is perfect for boys who want a simple and straightforward look that requires minimal styling.

Crew Cut

The crew cut is another classic style that adds some texture and volume to your hair. It's a short and tapered cut that leaves more hair on top of your head, creating a balanced look. You can style the hair on top in different ways, such as slicking it back or spiking it up, depending on your preference.

Side Part

The side part is a versatile style that can work well for boys with round faces. It involves creating a deep part on one side of your head and combing the hair on top to the other side. This style adds some height to your crown and creates a sleek and polished look. You can leave the hair on the sides short or taper it gradually.

Textured Crop

The textured crop is a modern and edgy style that adds some movement and volume to your hair. It involves cutting the hair short on the sides and back and leaving more hair on top. The hair on top is then styled in a messy and textured way, creating a casual and effortless look. This style is perfect for boys who want to experiment with different textures and styles.

Tips for Maintaining Your Short Haircut

Once you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your round face, it's essential to maintain it properly to keep it looking great. Here are some tips to help you take care of your short hair:

Wash Your Hair Regularly

Washing your hair regularly is essential to keep it clean and healthy. Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type and follow a regular washing routine.

Trim Your Hair Every Few Weeks

Short haircuts require frequent trimming to keep them looking sharp and neat. Visit your hairstylist every few weeks to get a trim and maintain the shape of your haircut.

Style Your Hair with the Right Products

To style your hair and keep it in place, use the right products that suit your hair type and style. For example, if you have fine hair, use a lightweight styling product that adds volume and texture. If you have thick hair, use a stronger product that keeps your hair in place.

FAQ

What is the best short haircut for a round face boy?

The best short haircut for a round face boy depends on factors such as hair type, personal style, and proportions. Some of the best styles include the buzz cut, crew cut, side part, and textured crop.

How do I choose the right haircut for my face shape?

To choose the right haircut for your face shape, consider factors such as your hair type, personal style, and proportions. Pay attention to the length and volume of your hair, and choose a haircut that creates a balanced and flattering look.

Conclusion

Choosing the perfect short haircut for round faces boy can be a challenge, but with the right tips and styles, you can create a look that suits you. Consider factors such as your hair type, personal style, and proportions, and choose a style that adds texture and volume to your hair. With the right maintenance and styling, you can keep your short haircut looking great and stylish.
