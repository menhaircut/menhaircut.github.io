The long pixie cut is a great option for those who want to keep some length on top. It is a versatile hairstyle that can be styled in many different ways.

2. Short Pixie Cut

If you want to go for a more dramatic look, a short pixie cut is the way to go. It is a low-maintenance hairstyle that will make you stand out.

3. Layered Pixie Cut

A layered pixie cut adds texture and movement to the hair. It is a great option for those with thin hair.

5. Inverted Bob

How to Choose the Perfect Short Hairstyle for Your Round Face

Choosing the right short hairstyle for your round face can be a daunting task. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect hairstyle:

1. Consider Your Hair Texture

Your hair texture will play a big role in the type of short hairstyle that will work best for you. If you have thin hair, you may want to opt for a layered cut that adds volume and movement. If you have thick hair, you may want to opt for a blunt cut that will help to thin out your hair.

2. Look for Hairstyles That Add Height

Hairstyles that add height to the top of the head will elongate your face and make it appear less round. Pixie cuts and layered bobs are great options for adding height to your hair.

3. Avoid Hairstyles That Add Width

Hairstyles that add width to your face will only make your face appear more round. Avoid hairstyles that are too blunt or too straight.

FAQ

1. Will short hair make my face look rounder?

No, short hair can actually make your face appear less round if you choose the right hairstyle. Hairstyles that add height to the top of the head will elongate your face and make it appear less round.

2. Can I still have long hair if I have a round face?

Yes, you can still have long hair if you have a round face. Just make sure to choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair.

3. How often should I get my hair cut?

It is recommended to get your hair cut every 6-8 weeks to maintain your hairstyle and keep it looking fresh.
