Mens Short Hair Fade Sides The Ultimate Guide For 2023

Written by Eveline Aug 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a stylish and trendy haircut that will make you stand out from the crowd? Look no further than the mens short hair fade sides! This popular hairstyle is perfect for men who want a clean and polished look, with a touch of edginess. In this comprehensive guide, we will give you everything you need to know about the mens short hair fade sides, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to take care of it. Let's dive in!

Description

The mens short hair fade sides is a haircut that features short hair on the top, with the sides and back gradually fading into a shorter length. This creates a clean and sharp look that is perfect for men who want to look polished and professional. The fade can be done in a variety of ways, from a low fade to a high fade, and can be customized to suit your individual style.

How To

Step 1: Choose Your Fade

The first step in getting the mens short hair fade sides is to choose the type of fade you want. There are several options to choose from, including:

  • Low fade
  • Mid fade
  • High fade
  • Bald fade

Each type of fade will create a different look, so choose the one that best suits your style and preferences.

Step 2: Cut Your Hair

Once you have chosen your fade, it's time to cut your hair. Start by cutting the top to your desired length, then use clippers to gradually fade the sides and back. Be sure to blend the hair evenly for a smooth and polished look.

Step 3: Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is cut, it's time to style it. Use a styling product of your choice to create the look you want. For a sleek and polished look, use a pomade or wax to smooth down the hair on top. For a more textured look, use a styling cream or clay to add volume and definition.

Tips

  • Visit a professional barber or stylist to ensure a clean and even cut.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find the one that works best for your hair type and style.
  • Maintain your haircut by getting regular trims and touch-ups.
  • Consider adding a beard or mustache to complement your mens short hair fade sides.

FAQ

1. Is the mens short hair fade sides suitable for all hair types?

Yes, the mens short hair fade sides can be done on all hair types, from straight to curly. However, the final look may vary depending on your hair type and texture.

2. How often should I get a touch-up for my mens short hair fade sides?

It's recommended to get a touch-up every 3-4 weeks to maintain the clean and polished look of the fade.

3. Can I style my mens short hair fade sides in different ways?

Yes, the mens short hair fade sides is a versatile haircut that can be styled in a variety of ways, from a slicked-back look to a messy and textured hairstyle.

Hair Care

To keep your mens short hair fade sides looking its best, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools such as blow dryers or straighteners, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid harsh chemicals and dyes that can damage your hair and scalp.

By following these hair care tips, you can keep your mens short hair fade sides looking sharp and stylish.

Conclusion

The mens short hair fade sides is a popular and stylish haircut that is perfect for men who want a clean and polished look. By following the steps and tips outlined in this guide, you can get the perfect mens short hair fade sides and keep it looking its best. Whether you're heading to the office or hitting the town, this versatile haircut is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and stylish.
