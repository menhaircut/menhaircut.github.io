What Haircut Suits Me Best A Guide To Finding The Perfect Hairstyle

Written by Robby Aug 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of your current hairstyle? Do you want to try something new but don't know where to start? Choosing the right haircut can be difficult, but with our guide, you'll find the perfect hairstyle that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. In this article, we'll show you how to determine what haircut suits you best and provide tips on how to maintain and style your new look.

How to Determine Your Face Shape

The first step to finding the right haircut is to determine your face shape. There are six basic face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a close look in the mirror. Look for the widest part of your face, the shape of your jawline, and the length of your face. Once you determine your face shape, you can choose a haircut that complements your features.

Round Face Shape

If you have a round face shape, the best haircut for you is one that adds height and volume to your hair. A layered haircut with long, face-framing layers is perfect for this face shape. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make your face appear wider.

Oval Face Shape

Those with an oval face shape are lucky, as they can pull off almost any haircut. However, a long bob or shoulder-length haircut with soft layers is perfect for enhancing your features. Avoid short, blunt cuts that can make your face appear wider.

Square Face Shape

If you have a square face shape, a haircut with soft, rounded layers can soften your features. A long, layered haircut that falls below your shoulders is perfect for this face shape. Avoid blunt bobs and straight bangs, as they can make your face appear boxy.

Heart Face Shape

If you have a heart-shaped face, a haircut with soft, side-swept bangs can balance out your features. A long bob or shoulder-length haircut with layers is perfect for this face shape. Avoid heavy, blunt cuts that can make your forehead appear too wide.

Diamond Face Shape

If you have a diamond face shape, a short, layered haircut can accentuate your cheekbones. A pixie cut or a bob with choppy layers is perfect for this face shape. Avoid long, straight hairstyles that can make your face appear longer.

Oblong Face Shape

If you have an oblong face shape, a layered haircut with soft waves or curls can add width to your face. A shoulder-length haircut with layers is perfect for this face shape. Avoid straight, blunt cuts that can make your face appear longer.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a New Haircut

Now that you know what haircut suits you best, it's time to visit your hairstylist. Follow these steps to get the perfect haircut:

  1. Bring photos of your desired haircut to your hairstylist.
  2. Explain your lifestyle and how much time you're willing to spend styling your hair.
  3. Ask your hairstylist for their professional opinion on what haircut suits you best.
  4. Discuss the length and layers of your desired haircut.
  5. Ask your hairstylist for tips on how to maintain and style your new haircut.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling Your New Haircut

Once you have your new haircut, it's important to maintain and style it properly. Follow these tips to keep your new hairstyle looking fresh:

  • Use a shampoo and conditioner that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Visit your hairstylist every 6-8 weeks for a trim.
  • Experiment with different styling products to find what works best for your new haircut.

FAQs

What haircut suits a round face?

A layered haircut with long, face-framing layers is perfect for a round face shape. Avoid blunt cuts and heavy bangs, as they can make your face appear wider.

What haircut suits an oval face?

An oval face shape can pull off almost any haircut. However, a long bob or shoulder-length haircut with soft layers is perfect for enhancing your features. Avoid short, blunt cuts that can make your face appear wider.

What haircut suits a square face?

A haircut with soft, rounded layers can soften the features of a square face shape. A long, layered haircut that falls below your shoulders is perfect for this face shape. Avoid blunt bobs and straight bangs, as they can make your face appear boxy.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important to maintain healthy hair. Follow these tips to keep your hair looking beautiful:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping it of its natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day.
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protectant spray.

With our guide, you can find the perfect haircut that suits your face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Follow our tips on how to maintain and style your new look, and you'll have beautiful, healthy hair in no time!
