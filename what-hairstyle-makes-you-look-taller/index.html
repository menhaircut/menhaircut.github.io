What Hairstyle Makes You Look Taller

Written by Oliver Oct 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to appear taller? Did you know that your hairstyle can make a big difference in how tall you look? In this article, we will look at the different hairstyles that can make you look taller.

Table of Content

  1. Description
  2. How to
  3. Step by Step
  4. Tips

Description

Many people are not aware that their hairstyle can affect their height. The right hairstyle can make you look taller while the wrong one can make you appear shorter. The key is to choose a hairstyle that elongates your face and neck. This article will provide you with some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle to make you look taller.

How to

Here are some tips on how to choose the right hairstyle to make you look taller:

1. Choose a hairstyle that adds height

Hairstyles that add height to your head can make you look taller. Some examples include high ponytails, top knots, and high buns. These hairstyles create the illusion of height by adding volume to the top of your head.

2. Avoid hairstyles that add width

Hairstyles that add width to your head can make you look shorter. Some examples include blunt bangs, wide curls, and heavy layers. These hairstyles can make your face and neck look wider, which will make you appear shorter.

3. Choose a hairstyle that elongates your face

Hairstyles that elongate your face can make you look taller. Some examples include long layers, straight hair, and side-swept bangs. These hairstyles create the illusion of a longer face, which will make you appear taller.

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to create a hairstyle that makes you look taller:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling it. This will ensure that your hairstyle will hold its shape and look its best.

2. Create volume at the crown

Use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head. This will create volume and add height to your hair.

3. Pull your hair up

Pull your hair up into a high ponytail or bun. This will create the illusion of height by adding volume to the top of your head.

4. Add finishing touches

Add finishing touches to your hairstyle, such as hairspray or bobby pins, to hold your hair in place and create a polished look.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when choosing a hairstyle to make you look taller:

  • Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape.
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too heavy or bulky.
  • Consider getting a haircut that adds layers or texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Q: Can short hair make you look taller?

Short hair can make you look taller if it is styled in a way that adds height to your head. However, long hair tends to elongate the face and neck, which can make you appear taller.

Q: Can bangs make you look shorter?

Bangs can make you look shorter if they are cut straight across your forehead. However, side-swept bangs can elongate your face and make you appear taller.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is important for achieving a hairstyle that makes you look taller. Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Avoid using too much heat when styling your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and shiny.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking its best.

Now that you know what hairstyle makes you look taller, it's time to experiment with different hairstyles and find the one that works best for you. Remember to choose a hairstyle that adds height to your head and elongates your face to create the illusion of height. With the right hairstyle, you can look taller and feel more confident in your appearance.
