What Is The Thing Barbers Put On You

Written by William Sep 22, 2023 · 4 min read

When you go to a barber, they usually put something on you before they start cutting your hair. But what is that thing barbers put on you? In this article, we’ll explain what it is, how it’s used, and some tips on how to care for your hair after a haircut.

Table of Contents

  1. What is it?
  2. How to Put it On
  3. Step-by-Step Guide
  4. Tips for After the Haircut
  5. FAQs
  6. Hair Care

What is it?

The thing that barbers put on you is called a cape or a hairdressing gown. It’s a piece of fabric that drapes over your clothes and covers your body to protect it from hair clippings, shampoo, and other hair products.

The cape is usually made from nylon or polyester and is designed to be lightweight and easy to clean. Some capes have snaps or velcro closures to fasten them around your neck, while others have ties that can be knotted in the back.

How to Put it On

Putting on a cape is a simple process. Once you’re seated in the barber’s chair, the barber will drape the cape over your shoulders, making sure that it covers your entire back and chest. They may then fasten the cape around your neck or tie it in the back.

If the cape has any closures, make sure to follow the barber’s instructions for fastening it securely. You don’t want any hair or products to sneak in underneath the cape and ruin your clothes.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to put on a hairdressing gown:

  1. Sit down in the barber’s chair.
  2. Hold your arms out to the side.
  3. The barber will drape the cape over your shoulders, making sure it covers your entire back and chest.
  4. If the cape has closures, fasten them securely around your neck, following the barber’s instructions.

Tips for After the Haircut

After the haircut, the barber will remove the cape and brush off any loose hair or products that may have gotten on your skin or clothes. Here are a few tips for caring for your hair after a haircut:

  • Shower or wash your hair to remove any loose hair or products that may be lingering on your scalp.
  • Apply a small amount of conditioner to your hair to keep it moisturized and healthy.
  • Style your hair as desired, using a comb or brush to create the look you want.

FAQs

Q: Do barbers put on capes for every haircut?

A: Yes, most barbers will put on a cape for every haircut to protect your clothes and skin from hair clippings and products.

Q: Can I bring my own cape to the barber?

A: Some barbers may allow you to bring your own cape if you prefer, but it’s best to check with them first before bringing any outside equipment or materials.

Q: Can I wear a cape if I’m getting a shave or beard trim?

A: Yes, you can wear a cape for any type of hair service, including shaves and beard trims.

Hair Care

Caring for your hair after a haircut is important to keep it looking and feeling healthy. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like flat irons or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking neat and healthy.

Now that you know what the thing barbers put on you is, you can rest easy knowing that your clothes and skin are protected during your haircut. Remember to follow the barber’s instructions for fastening the cape, and take care of your hair after the cut to keep it looking healthy and stylish.
