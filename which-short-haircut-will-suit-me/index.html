Which Short Haircut Will Suit Me The Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Sep 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to switch up your hairstyle and go for a shorter cut? Choosing the right short haircut can be a daunting task, but fear not! In this guide, we'll explore different short haircuts and how to determine which one will suit you best.

Table of Contents

  • Determining Your Face Shape
  • Consider Your Hair Type
  • Different Short Haircuts
  • FAQs
  • Short Haircare Tips

Determining Your Face Shape

When choosing a short haircut, it's important to consider your face shape. Different face shapes will suit different cuts. Here are the most common face shapes:

  • Round
  • Square
  • Oval
  • Heart-shaped
  • Diamond-shaped

To determine your face shape, pull your hair back and take a photo of your face straight on. Then, use a ruler or measuring tape to measure the width of your forehead, cheeks, and jawline. Compare the measurements to determine your face shape.

Consider Your Hair Type

Another important factor to consider when choosing a short haircut is your hair type. Different hair types will work better with different cuts. Here are the most common hair types:

  • Straight
  • Curly
  • Wavy
  • Thick
  • Thin

If you have curly hair, for example, you may want to avoid a super short cut as it can be difficult to manage. If you have thin hair, a layered cut can add volume and texture.

Different Short Haircuts

Now that you've determined your face shape and hair type, let's explore some different short haircuts that may suit you:

Pixie Cut

The pixie cut is a short, cropped cut that is perfect for those with an oval or heart-shaped face. It's also great for those with straight or wavy hair. The pixie cut can be a bold statement, so make sure you're ready for a big change.

Bob Cut

The bob cut is a classic short haircut that works well for those with a round or square face. It's also great for those with straight or wavy hair. The bob can be customized to your liking, whether you want a blunt cut or a layered cut.

Buzz Cut

The buzz cut is an ultra-short cut that is perfect for those with a diamond-shaped face. It's also great for those with thick or curly hair. The buzz cut can be a bold and edgy look that is easy to maintain.

FAQs

How do I know if a short haircut will suit me?

Consider your face shape and hair type when choosing a short haircut. Look for inspiration photos of people with similar features to you and consult with a hairstylist for their professional opinion.

What short haircut is best for thick hair?

A layered bob or a pixie cut can work well for those with thick hair. The layers can add movement and texture, while a pixie cut can help to remove some of the weight from your hair.

What short haircut is best for thin hair?

A layered bob can work well for those with thin hair. The layers can add volume and texture, giving the illusion of thicker hair.

Short Haircare Tips

Once you've chosen your short haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Here are some tips for short haircare:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils
  • Avoid over-washing your hair, as this can dry out your scalp and hair
  • Invest in a high-quality hair oil or serum to add shine and moisture to your hair
  • Regularly trim your hair to keep it looking fresh and healthy

Now that you have a better understanding of which short haircut will suit you, it's time to book that salon appointment and embrace your new look!
