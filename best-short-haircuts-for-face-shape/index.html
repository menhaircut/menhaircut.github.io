Best Short Haircuts For Face Shape A Guide To Flattering Hairstyles

Written by Bardi Sep 13, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Choosing a short haircut can be intimidating, but the right style can transform your look and enhance your features. The key is to find a haircut that complements your face shape. In this guide, we'll explore the best short haircuts for various face shapes, from round to square to heart-shaped. We'll also provide tips on how to maintain your new hairstyle and answer common questions about short haircuts.

Best Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Round faces have soft, curved lines and are widest at the cheeks. To balance out the fullness of a round face, opt for a short haircut with volume on top and tapered sides. A pixie cut with longer layers on top and shorter layers on the sides can elongate the face, while a short bob with side-swept bangs can create the illusion of a slimmer face.

Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short haircut that works well for round faces. The key is to add volume to the top of the head, while keeping the sides tapered. This draws attention to the top of the head and elongates the face. A side-swept bang can also help to create a slimming effect. This style is low-maintenance and perfect for those who want a bold, edgy look.

Short Bob

A short bob with side-swept bangs is another great option for round faces. The length of the bob should be chin-length or above, with layers that add volume on top. The side-swept bangs can create a diagonal line across the face, which helps to lengthen it. This style is versatile and can be dressed up or down.

Best Short Haircuts for Oval Faces

Oval faces are considered the most versatile face shape, with balanced proportions and a slightly curved jawline. Almost any short haircut can work for an oval face, but a layered bob or a sleek pixie cut can highlight the facial features.

Layered Bob

A layered bob with a side part can accentuate an oval face. The layers add texture and movement, while the side part creates a diagonal line that lengthens the face. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut that can be styled in different ways.

Sleek Pixie Cut

A sleek pixie cut with a side part can also work well for oval faces. This style is chic and sophisticated, with a minimalist feel. The side part can create a diagonal line that elongates the face, while the short length draws attention to the facial features.

Best Short Haircuts for Square Faces

Square faces have strong, angular features with a prominent jawline. Short haircuts that soften the jawline and add volume on top can balance out the face shape. A bob with layers and a side-swept fringe can create a softer look, while a short shag with choppy layers can add texture and movement.

Layered Bob with Side-Swept Fringe

A layered bob with a side-swept fringe can soften the angles of a square face. The layers add volume on top, while the fringe creates a diagonal line that lengthens the face. This style is feminine and versatile, and can be dressed up or down.

Short Shag with Choppy Layers

A short shag with choppy layers can add texture and movement to a square face. The layers soften the jawline, while the choppy texture creates a playful, edgy look. This style is perfect for those who want a low-maintenance haircut with a lot of personality.

Best Short Haircuts for Heart-Shaped Faces

Heart-shaped faces have a wider forehead and a narrower chin. Short haircuts that create volume at the chin and balance out the forehead can complement this face shape. A layered bob with a side-swept fringe can add volume and create a diagonal line that lengthens the face, while a pixie cut with volume on top can also work well.

Layered Bob with Side-Swept Fringe

A layered bob with a side-swept fringe can work well for heart-shaped faces. The layers add volume at the chin, while the fringe creates a diagonal line that lengthens the face. This style is feminine and versatile, and can be styled in different ways.

Pixie Cut with Volume on Top

A pixie cut with volume on top can also work well for heart-shaped faces. The volume at the top of the head balances out the wider forehead, while the tapered sides draw attention to the chin. This style is bold and confident, and perfect for those who want to make a statement.

Hair Maintenance Tips for Short Haircuts

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Visit your stylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to maintain the shape of your haircut.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are formulated for your hair type.
  • Use a heat protectant when using hot tools, like a curling iron or straightener.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for your hair.

FAQs about Short Haircuts

1. Will a short haircut look good on me?

Short haircuts can look good on anyone, as long as you choose a style that complements your face shape and hair type. Consult with your stylist to find a short haircut that works for you.

2. How do I choose the right short haircut for my face shape?

Choose a short haircut that balances out your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, opt for a haircut with volume on top and tapered sides. Consult with your stylist for personalized recommendations.

3. Will a short haircut be easy to maintain?

Short haircuts can be low-maintenance, but they do require regular trims to maintain their shape. Invest in quality hair products and experiment with different styling techniques to find what works for you.

4. Can I still style my short haircut in different ways?

Yes! Short haircuts can be styled in different ways, depending on the length and texture of your hair. Experiment with different techniques, like braiding or curling, to create different looks.

5. How can I make my short haircut last longer?

Regular trims and quality hair products can help to prolong the life of your short haircut. Avoid using hot tools too frequently, and experiment with different styling techniques to avoid damaging your hair.

With the right short haircut, you can enhance your features and transform your look. Use this guide to find a short haircut that complements your face shape, and experiment with different styling techniques to make it your own.
