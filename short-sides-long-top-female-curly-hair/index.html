Short Sides Long Top Female Curly Hair The Ultimate Guide

If you're looking for a bold and edgy hairstyle that's perfect for curly hair, then short sides long top female curly hair might just be the perfect fit for you! This trendy hairstyle is a great way to showcase your curls while also showing off your fearless attitude. In this article, we'll take you through everything you need to know about short sides long top female curly hair, from how to get the look to how to take care of your curls.

Description

Short sides long top female curly hair is a trendy and edgy haircut that features shorter hair on the sides and longer hair on the top. This hairstyle is perfect for women with curly hair who want to show off their curls while also keeping their hair out of their face. Short sides long top female curly hair is also a great way to add some volume to your hair and make a bold statement with your look.

How to Get the Look

Getting the short sides long top female curly hair look is easier than you might think! Here are the steps you'll need to follow: 1. Choose the Right Length: Start by deciding how short you want your sides to be. Generally, a short buzz cut or fade on the sides will work best for this look. 2. Decide on the Length on Top: Next, decide how long you want your hair to be on top. This will depend on your personal preference and the natural length of your hair. 3. Get the Cut: Once you've decided on the length of your hair, it's time to get the cut! Take your inspiration photo to your stylist and let them know what you want. 4. Style Your Hair: To style your short sides long top female curly hair, simply use a curl-defining product on the top of your hair and scrunch it up. You can also use a diffuser to enhance your curls and add volume.

Step by Step Guide

If you're more of a visual learner, here's a step-by-step guide to getting short sides long top female curly hair: 1. Start with clean, dry hair. 2. Use a curl-defining product on the top of your hair. 3. Scrunch the product into your curls to enhance them. 4. Use a diffuser to dry your hair and add volume. 5. Use a trimmer or razor to buzz the sides of your hair. 6. Blend the sides into the top using scissors or clippers. 7. Style your hair as desired.

Tips for Styling Short Sides Long Top Female Curly Hair

Here are some tips to help you style your short sides long top female curly hair: - Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and add definition. - Use a diffuser to dry your hair and add volume. - Use a trimmer or razor to buzz the sides of your hair. - Blend the sides into the top using scissors or clippers. - Experiment with different styling techniques to find what works best for you.

FAQs

Q: Is short sides long top female curly hair difficult to maintain?
A: No, short sides long top female curly hair is actually quite easy to maintain. Simply use a curl-defining product and a diffuser to enhance your curls and keep them looking great. Q: Will short sides long top female curly hair work for all face shapes?
A: Short sides long top female curly hair can work for many face shapes, but it's best to consult with your stylist to find the best look for you.

Hair Care Tips for Short Sides Long Top Female Curly Hair

Here are some hair care tips to keep your short sides long top female curly hair looking its best: - Use a curl-defining product to enhance your curls and add definition. - Use a diffuser to dry your hair and add volume. - Avoid using heat styling tools, as they can damage your curls. - Trim your ends regularly to prevent split ends. - Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated. In conclusion, short sides long top female curly hair is a bold and edgy hairstyle that's perfect for women with curly hair. With the right cut and styling products, you can achieve this look easily and maintain it with minimal effort. Follow the tips and tricks in this guide and you'll be rocking short sides long top female curly hair in no time!
