How To Blow Dry Hair With Dryer Brush A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Blow drying your hair with a dryer brush is a great way to achieve a salon-worthy look without having to leave your home. With the right technique and tools, you can achieve a smooth, voluminous, and frizz-free hairstyle in no time. In this article, we will show you how to blow dry your hair with a dryer brush, and provide you with some tips and tricks to make the process easier.

Description

Blow drying your hair with a dryer brush involves using a round brush attachment on your hair dryer to dry and style your hair at the same time. This method is great for adding volume and creating a smooth, polished finish. The key to achieving the best results is to use the right products and tools, and to follow the proper technique.

How to Blow Dry Hair with Dryer Brush

Step 1: Prepare Your Hair

Before you start blow drying your hair, you need to make sure that it is clean and dry. If your hair is wet, it will take longer to dry and may become damaged. Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo, and then towel dry it to remove any excess water.

Step 2: Apply Heat Protectant

Next, apply a heat protectant spray or serum to your hair to prevent heat damage. This will also help to keep your hair looking shiny and healthy. Apply the product evenly throughout your hair, focusing on the ends where the hair is most vulnerable to damage.

Step 3: Section Your Hair

Divide your hair into sections using hair clips or hair ties. This will make it easier to work with and ensure that you dry all of your hair evenly. Start by dividing your hair into two sections, one on top and one underneath.

Step 4: Start Blow Drying

Attach the round brush attachment to your hair dryer and turn it on to a low or medium heat setting. Start by blow drying the underneath section of your hair first, using the brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume. Work your way up to the top section, using the same technique.

Step 5: Finish with Cool Air

When you have finished blow drying your hair, switch the hair dryer to the cool air setting and use it to blast your hair for a few seconds. This will help to set the style and make your hair look shiny.

Tips for Blow Drying Hair with Dryer Brush

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when blow drying your hair with a dryer brush:

  • Use a high-quality hair dryer that has a round brush attachment.
  • Choose a brush that is the right size for your hair length and texture.
  • Blow dry your hair in sections to ensure that it dries evenly.
  • Use a heat protectant spray or serum to prevent heat damage.
  • Blow dry your hair on a low or medium heat setting to avoid over-drying.
  • Use the cool air setting to set your style and make your hair look shiny.

FAQs

Can I blow dry my hair every day?

Blow drying your hair every day can cause damage, so it is best to limit it to two or three times a week. If you must blow dry your hair every day, use a heat protectant spray or serum, and avoid using high heat settings.

What is the best type of brush to use for blow drying?

The best type of brush to use for blow drying depends on your hair type and the style you want to achieve. For fine hair, use a round brush with natural bristles. For thick hair, use a ceramic or metal brush with nylon bristles.

Hair Care Tips

In addition to blow drying your hair with a dryer brush, there are other things you can do to keep your hair healthy and looking its best:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to keep your hair healthy from the inside out.

By following these tips and using the right technique and tools, you can achieve a beautiful blowout that lasts for days. Remember to take care of your hair by using gentle products and avoiding excess heat, and you will have healthy, shiny hair that looks great every day.
