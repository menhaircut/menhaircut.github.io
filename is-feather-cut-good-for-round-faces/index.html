Is Feather Cut Good For Round Faces

Written by Robby Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering if a feather cut would suit your round-shaped face? You've come to the right place. In this blog post, we'll explore whether a feather cut is good for round faces. We'll also provide you with some tips and tricks on how to style your feather cut and take care of your hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A feather cut is a popular hairstyle that involves cutting the hair in layers to create a feathered effect. The layers are cut in such a way that they blend seamlessly with each other, creating a soft and voluminous look. Feather cuts are ideal for those who want to add volume and texture to their hair.

When it comes to round faces, a feather cut can be a great option. The feathered layers can help to create an illusion of length, making the face appear less round. However, there are certain factors to consider before getting a feather cut, such as the length of your hair and the shape of your face.

How to

If you're considering getting a feather cut for your round face, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.
  • Consider getting long layers that start at your jawline or below to create the illusion of length.
  • Avoid getting too many short layers around your face, as this can make your face appear wider.
  • Opt for soft, feathered layers that blend seamlessly with each other.
  • Style your hair with volumizing products to enhance the feathered effect.

Step by Step

If you're wondering how to style your feather cut, here's a step-by-step guide:

  1. Wash your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Towel dry your hair and apply a volumizing mousse or spray to the roots.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  4. Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron or wand to create loose waves or curls.
  5. Finish off your look with a texturizing spray to enhance the feathered effect.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you maintain your feather cut:

  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your feathered layers.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and nourished.
  • Consider using a dry shampoo between washes to refresh your hair and add volume.

FAQ

Q: Will a feather cut make my face look too thin?

A: No, a feather cut can actually help to create the illusion of more volume and fullness in your hair.

Q: Can I get a feather cut if I have short hair?

A: Yes, but it's important to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape.

Q: Will a feather cut work for all hair types?

A: Yes, a feather cut can work for all hair types, but the level of volume and texture will vary depending on your hair's natural texture.

Hair Care

To keep your feather cut looking its best, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can damage your feathered layers.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid pulling or tugging on your hair, as this can cause breakage.
  • Get regular trims to keep your layers looking fresh and healthy.

Overall, a feather cut can be a great option for those with round faces. Just be sure to consult with your hairstylist to determine the best length and style for your face shape. With the right care and styling techniques, you can rock a feather cut that flatters your features and enhances your natural beauty.
