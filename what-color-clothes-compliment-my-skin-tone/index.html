What Color Clothes Compliment My Skin Tone

Written by Daniel Sep 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Choosing the right colors of clothes that compliment your skin tone is essential to look your best. However, with so many colors to choose from, it can be overwhelming to know which ones to pick. In this article, we’ll guide you through the process of finding the perfect color clothes that suit your skin tone. Check out the table of contents below to navigate through the article:

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Your skin tone falls under three categories: cool, warm, or neutral. To determine which category you fall under, check the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear bluish, then you have a cool undertone, whereas if they appear greenish, then you have a warm undertone. If you can’t distinguish between the two, then you have neutral undertones. Once you know your skin tone, you can choose clothes that complement it.

How to

Here’s a quick guide on which colors to choose based on your skin tone:

Cool Undertones

If you have cool undertones, you should go for colors with blue or purple undertones. These colors include:

  • Navy blue
  • Lavender
  • Burgundy
  • Emerald green
  • Fuchsia
  • Royal blue

Warm Undertones

If you have warm undertones, you should opt for colors with yellow or orange undertones. These colors include:

  • Peach
  • Golden yellow
  • Coral
  • Rust
  • Olive green
  • Terracotta

Neutral Undertones

If you have neutral undertones, you can wear almost any color. However, it’s best to stick to neutral colors such as black, white, and beige.

Step by step

Follow these steps to find the perfect color clothes that complement your skin tone:

  1. Determine your skin tone by checking the veins on your wrist.
  2. Choose colors that match your undertone (cool, warm, or neutral).
  3. Experiment with different shades of the same color to find the perfect match.
  4. Don't be afraid to mix and match colors to create a unique look.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you choose the best colors for your skin tone:

  • Wear colors that make you feel confident and comfortable.
  • Choose colors that complement your eye color and hair color.
  • Avoid wearing colors that wash out your complexion.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a fashion-savvy friend or stylist.

FAQ

Q: Can I wear black if I have a warm undertone?

A: Yes, you can wear black regardless of your skin tone. However, it’s best to pair it with a color that complements your undertone.

Q: Can I wear white if I have a cool undertone?

A: Yes, you can wear white regardless of your skin tone. However, it’s best to opt for a shade of white that complements your undertone.

Hair Care

The color of your hair can also affect which colors of clothes look best on you. Here are some tips to help you choose the right colors based on your hair color:

Blonde Hair

If you have blonde hair, you should opt for colors that are soft and feminine, such as pastels and light shades of blue and pink. Avoid wearing colors that are too bold or bright.

Brown Hair

If you have brown hair, you can wear almost any color. However, it’s best to stick to warm colors such as orange, yellow, and red.

Black Hair

If you have black hair, you should opt for colors that create contrast, such as white, bright red, and emerald green. Avoid wearing colors that blend in too much with your hair color.

Red Hair

If you have red hair, you should opt for colors that complement your hair color, such as green, brown, and beige. Avoid wearing colors that clash with your hair color.

Choosing the right colors of clothes that complement your skin tone and hair color can make a huge difference in your overall appearance. Now that you know which colors to choose, go ahead and experiment with different shades to find the perfect match.
