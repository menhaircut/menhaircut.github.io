Short Haircut to Suit Round Face - Tips, How to and FAQ

If you have a round face, it can be challenging to find the perfect haircut that flatters your face shape. However, with the right short haircut, you can accentuate your best features and create a balanced look. In this article, we will provide you with tips, how-to's, and frequently asked questions about short haircuts to suit a round face.

Table of Contents

  • How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Face?
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for Round Face
  • Tips for Maintaining a Short Haircut for Round Face
  • FAQs about Short Haircuts for Round Face
  • Hair Care Tips for Short Haircut to Suit Round Face

How to Choose the Right Short Haircut for Round Face?

When choosing a short haircut for a round face, you want to create angles and length to balance out the roundness of your face. Here are some tips:

  • Go for a pixie cut with layers that frame your face and add height to the crown.
  • Choose an asymmetrical bob with longer layers on one side to elongate your face.
  • Opt for a short shag with choppy layers to add texture and movement.
  • Avoid blunt bangs and one-length cuts that make your face look wider.

Step-by-Step Guide to Getting a Short Haircut for Round Face

Now that you know what kind of short haircut to choose, here is a step-by-step guide to getting the perfect cut:

  1. Consult with your hairstylist and bring pictures of the short haircut you want.
  2. Wash and dry your hair before the appointment.
  3. Section your hair and cut the length according to the style you chose.
  4. Add layers and texture to create angles and movement.
  5. Style your hair with a round brush for volume or a flat iron for a sleek look.
  6. Use hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips for Maintaining a Short Haircut for Round Face

Short haircuts require regular maintenance to keep the style looking fresh. Here are some tips:

  • Get regular trims every 4-6 weeks to keep the shape of the cut.
  • Use a dry shampoo to avoid over-washing your hair and to add volume.
  • Invest in quality hair products that are suitable for your hair type.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques to switch up your look.

FAQs about Short Haircuts for Round Face

1. Can I still have bangs with a short haircut for a round face?

Yes, you can have bangs with a short haircut for a round face. However, they should be swept to the side or have longer layers to add length to your face.

2. Will a short haircut make my face look rounder?

No, a short haircut can actually elongate your face if it has angles and layers that add height and movement.

3. Can I style my short haircut differently for special occasions?

Yes, you can experiment with different styling techniques such as curling or braiding to switch up your look for special occasions.

Hair Care Tips for Short Haircut to Suit Round Face

Keeping your hair healthy and shiny is important to maintain the look of your short haircut. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools every day to prevent heat damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a hair protectant spray.
