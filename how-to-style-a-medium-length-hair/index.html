How To Style A Medium Length Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Medium length hair is a versatile length that can be styled in many ways. Whether you have straight, wavy or curly hair, there are a number of styles that you can try. In this article, we will provide some tips, tricks and hair care advice on how to style a medium length hair.

Table of Contents:

  1. How to Style a Medium Length Hair
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Styling a Medium Length Hair
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

How to Style a Medium Length Hair

There are many ways to style medium length hair. Here are some popular styles:

1. Lob

A lob is a long bob that falls just above the shoulders. It is perfect for those who want to keep their hair at a medium length but still want to experiment with different styles. You can wear it straight, wavy or curly.

2. Beach Waves

Beach waves are perfect for those who want a relaxed, effortless look. To achieve this style, use a curling iron or a flat iron to create loose waves. You can also use a sea salt spray to enhance the texture.

3. Half Up, Half Down

The half up, half down style is perfect for those who want to keep their hair out of their face but still want to show off their length. Simply gather the top half of your hair and secure it with a hair tie or clip.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to style a medium length hair:

1. Start with clean, dry hair

Wash and dry your hair before styling. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you can apply some curl cream or mousse to enhance your natural texture.

2. Choose your style

Decide on the style that you want to achieve. You can choose from the styles mentioned above or experiment with your own.

3. Use the right tools

Use the right tools for your chosen style. For example, use a curling iron for beach waves or a flat iron for a sleek look.

4. Section your hair

Section your hair to make styling easier. Use hair clips or ties to keep each section separate.

5. Style each section

Style each section of your hair according to your chosen style. Remember to use heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.

6. Finish with hairspray

Finish your style with hairspray to keep it in place. Choose a hairspray that is suitable for your hair type.

Tips for Styling a Medium Length Hair

Here are some tips for styling a medium length hair:

1. Use heat protectant spray

Always use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools. This will protect your hair from damage and help it to stay healthy.

2. Experiment with different styles

Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles. Try out different looks and find what works best for you.

3. Use the right products

Use products that are suitable for your hair type. For example, use a volumizing shampoo if you have fine hair or a smoothing serum if you have frizzy hair.

4. Get regular haircuts

Get regular haircuts to keep your hair healthy and maintain its shape. A trim every 6-8 weeks is recommended.

FAQs

1. Can I style my medium length hair if it is thin?

Yes, you can style your medium length hair even if it is thin. Try using volumizing products and teasing your hair to create more volume.

2. Can I style my medium length hair if it is curly?

Yes, you can style your medium length hair even if it is curly. Experiment with different styles such as beach waves or a curly updo.

3. How can I make my medium length hair look thicker?

You can make your medium length hair look thicker by using volumizing products and blow drying your hair upside down.

Hair Care for Medium Length Hair

Here are some hair care tips for medium length hair:

1. Use a gentle shampoo

Use a gentle shampoo that is suitable for your hair type. Avoid using harsh chemicals that can damage your hair.

2. Condition regularly

Condition your hair regularly to keep it moisturized and healthy. Use a conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.

3. Protect your hair from the sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV protection spray.

4. Avoid using too much heat

Avoid using too much heat on your hair. Use heat styling tools sparingly and always use a heat protectant spray.

With these tips, tricks and hair care advice, you can style your medium length hair in a variety of ways. Experiment with different styles and find what works best for you.
