How To Straighten Hair With Volume A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

In this article, we will be discussing how to straighten hair with volume. If you have naturally curly or wavy hair, you may find that straightening your hair can leave it looking flat and lifeless. However, with the right techniques and products, you can achieve sleek, straight hair with plenty of volume. In this guide, we will take you through the steps you need to follow in order to straighten your hair while also adding volume.

Description

Straightening hair with volume can seem like a daunting task, but it's really not as difficult as you might think. The key is to use the right products and techniques to achieve the look you want. If you're looking to straighten your hair without losing volume, this guide is for you.

How to Straighten Hair with Volume

Step 1: Prep your hair

The first step in straightening your hair with volume is to prep your hair. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add volume to your hair before you even begin styling it. Once you have washed your hair, towel dry it gently and apply a heat protectant spray to your hair. This will help to protect your hair from damage caused by the heat of your straightening iron.

Step 2: Blow dry your hair

Next, blow dry your hair using a round brush to add volume. Start by blow drying your hair upside down to add volume at the roots. Once your hair is about 80% dry, flip your head back up and continue blow drying your hair section by section. Use the round brush to lift your hair at the roots and create volume.

Step 3: Section your hair

Once your hair is fully dry, section it into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to straighten your hair without losing volume. Start at the back of your head and work your way forward, straightening each section of hair as you go.

Step 4: Straighten your hair

Using a straightening iron, begin straightening your hair section by section. Start at the roots and work your way down to the ends, using the iron to smooth out any kinks or waves. Be sure to keep the iron moving quickly to avoid leaving it on any one section of hair for too long.

Step 5: Finish with a volumizing spray

Once you have straightened all of your hair, finish with a volumizing spray. This will help to add even more volume to your hair and keep it looking sleek and smooth all day long.

Tips

- Use a high-quality straightening iron to ensure that your hair is straightened quickly and effectively.

- Be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage caused by the heat of your straightening iron.

- Use a round brush while blow drying your hair to add volume at the roots.

- Section your hair into small sections to make it easier to straighten your hair without losing volume.

- Finish with a volumizing spray to add even more volume to your hair.

FAQ

Can I straighten my hair without losing volume?

Yes, you can straighten your hair without losing volume. By using the right products and techniques, you can achieve sleek, straight hair with plenty of volume.

What kind of products should I use to straighten my hair with volume?

You should use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner, a heat protectant spray, and a volumizing spray to achieve straight hair with volume.

How often should I straighten my hair?

You should avoid straightening your hair too often, as this can cause damage to your hair. Try to limit straightening to once or twice a week, and be sure to use a heat protectant spray to protect your hair from damage.
