How To Cut Hair Using Clippers With Guards

Written by Daniel Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive haircuts at the salon or barbershop? With the right tools and technique, you can learn how to cut hair using clippers with guards. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps and provide tips for a successful haircut. Let's get started!

Description

Clippers with guards are a popular tool for cutting hair at home. Guards are attachments that fit over the blades of the clippers to create a specific length for cutting hair. They come in different sizes, from 1/8 inch to 1 inch, and are labeled with numbers. The smaller the number, the shorter the cut.

How to Cut Hair Using Clippers with Guards

Before you begin, make sure you have the right tools: clippers with guards, scissors, a comb, and a cape or towel to drape over your client's shoulders. Here are the steps to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Guard Size

Start by deciding on the guard size you want to use. If you're not sure, start with a larger guard and work your way down to a shorter length. Remember, you can always trim more hair off, but you can't put it back on.

Step 2: Comb and Section the Hair

Comb your client's hair and section it into manageable pieces. For longer hair, use hair clips to hold the sections in place.

Step 3: Start at the Bottom

Start at the bottom of the hair and work your way up. Place the guard on the clippers and turn them on. Hold the clippers at a 90-degree angle to the head and move them in an upward motion. Use a light touch and let the clippers do the work.

Step 4: Blend the Hair

As you move up the head, blend the hair by using different guard sizes or removing the guard altogether. Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or create a more precise cut.

Step 5: Clean up the Neckline

Use the clippers without a guard to clean up the neckline and around the ears. Be careful not to cut into the hairline or create a jagged edge.

Step 6: Check for Evenness

Check your work for evenness and make any necessary adjustments. It's better to take your time and get it right than rush through the haircut.

Tips for a Successful Haircut

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you cut hair using clippers with guards:

  • Use a light touch and let the clippers do the work
  • Start with a larger guard and work your way down to a shorter length
  • Blend the hair by using different guard sizes or removing the guard altogether
  • Use scissors to trim any stray hairs or create a more precise cut
  • Check your work for evenness and make any necessary adjustments

FAQ

Can I cut my own hair using clippers with guards?

Yes, you can cut your own hair using clippers with guards. However, it may be easier to have someone else do it for you.

What guard size should I use for a buzz cut?

For a buzz cut, use a 1/8 inch or 1/4 inch guard size.

Can I use clippers with guards on wet hair?

It's best to use clippers with guards on dry hair. Wet hair can cause the clippers to jam or pull the hair, resulting in an uneven cut.

Hair Care

After your haircut, it's important to take care of your hair. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized. Avoid using hot tools like hair dryers or straighteners too often, as they can damage your hair. And don't forget to schedule regular haircuts to keep your hair looking its best.

Conclusion

Cutting hair using clippers with guards takes practice, but with the right tools and technique, you can achieve a professional-looking haircut at home. Follow these steps and tips for a successful haircut, and don't forget to take care of your hair afterwards. Happy cutting!
