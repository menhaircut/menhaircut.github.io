Natural Ways To Prevent Hair Loss During Chemotherapy

Written by Robby Jul 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Chemotherapy is a common cancer treatment that involves the use of strong drugs to kill cancer cells. Though effective, chemotherapy can cause hair loss, which can be a distressing side effect. While hair loss is not an indication of chemotherapy failure, it can affect a person's self-esteem and confidence.

If you're undergoing chemotherapy, you can take several natural steps to prevent or minimize hair loss. Here are some natural ways to prevent hair loss during chemotherapy:

How to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemotherapy Naturally

1. Scalp Massage

Scalp massage can stimulate hair follicles and improve blood flow to the scalp, which can help prevent hair loss during chemotherapy. You can use your fingertips to massage your scalp gently for a few minutes every day. You can also use a soft-bristled brush to massage your scalp.

2. Natural Oils

Natural oils such as coconut oil, olive oil, and castor oil can help prevent hair loss during chemotherapy. These oils are rich in nutrients that nourish hair follicles and promote hair growth. You can apply the oil to your scalp and hair and leave it on for a few hours before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

3. Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a natural remedy that can soothe the scalp and prevent hair loss during chemotherapy. You can apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

4. Onion Juice

Onion juice is rich in sulfur, which can improve blood circulation to the scalp and promote hair growth. You can extract juice from an onion and apply it to your scalp for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo. You can also mix onion juice with coconut oil and apply it to your scalp.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to prevent hair loss during chemotherapy naturally:

Step 1: Massage your scalp

Use your fingertips or a soft-bristled brush to massage your scalp gently for a few minutes every day.

Step 2: Apply natural oils

Apply natural oils such as coconut oil, olive oil, or castor oil to your scalp and hair and leave it on for a few hours before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step 3: Apply aloe vera gel

Apply fresh aloe vera gel to your scalp and leave it on for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo.

Step 4: Apply onion juice

Extract juice from an onion and apply it to your scalp for 30 minutes before washing it off with a mild shampoo. You can also mix onion juice with coconut oil and apply it to your scalp.

Tips to Prevent Hair Loss During Chemotherapy Naturally

Here are some tips to prevent hair loss during chemotherapy naturally:

1. Eat a Healthy Diet

Eating a healthy and balanced diet can provide your body with the necessary nutrients to promote hair growth.

2. Avoid Heat Styling Tools

Avoid using heat styling tools such as straighteners, curling irons, and hairdryers, as they can damage your hair and make it prone to breakage.

3. Use a Mild Shampoo

Use a mild shampoo that is free from harsh chemicals such as sulfates and parabens that can damage your hair.

4. Wear a Wig or Scarf

If you're uncomfortable with your hair loss, you can wear a wig or scarf to cover your head.

FAQs

Q. Does Chemotherapy Always Cause Hair Loss?

Not all chemotherapy drugs cause hair loss. However, some drugs can cause hair loss, and the extent of hair loss may vary from person to person.

Q. Can I Dye My Hair During Chemotherapy?

It is not recommended to dye your hair during chemotherapy, as the chemicals in hair dye can damage your hair and irritate your scalp.

Q. When Will My Hair Grow Back After Chemotherapy?

Your hair will start growing back a few weeks after your last chemotherapy treatment. However, it may take several months for your hair to grow back fully.

Hair Care During Chemotherapy

During chemotherapy, it's essential to take care of your hair to prevent further damage. Here are some tips to take care of your hair during chemotherapy:

1. Use a Wide-Toothed Comb

Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, as it is gentler on your hair and causes less breakage.

2. Avoid Tight Hairstyles

Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, ponytails, and buns, as they can pull on your hair and cause breakage.

3. Protect Your Hair From the Sun

Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or scarf when you're outside, as chemotherapy can make your scalp more sensitive to the sun.

4. Be Gentle With Your Hair

Be gentle with your hair when washing and drying it, as chemotherapy can make your hair more fragile.

Preventing hair loss during chemotherapy naturally is possible with these tips and tricks. Remember to take good care of your hair during chemotherapy to prevent further damage and promote healthy hair growth.
