Rock Your Look With A Spiky Hair Do

Written by Christine Nov 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you are looking for a cool and edgy hairstyle, you should try out the spiky hairdo. This hairstyle is perfect for those who want to experiment with their hair and stand out from the crowd. In this article, we will show you how to achieve the perfect spiky hairdo, step by step, along with some tips, FAQs, and hair care tips.

How to Achieve a Spiky Hair Do

A spiky hairdo is a modern and stylish hairstyle that can be achieved with a few simple steps. The first step is to choose the right haircut. You should opt for a short haircut that is easy to style and maintain. Once you have the right haircut, you need to use the right products to create the spiky look.

Products You Need for Spiky Hair Do

The products you need for a spiky hairdo include:

  • Hair gel or wax
  • Hairspray
  • Blow dryer
  • Comb or brush

Step by Step Guide to Spiky Hair Do

Follow these steps to achieve a perfect spiky hairdo:

  1. Wash your hair with a mild shampoo and conditioner.
  2. Dry your hair with a towel and leave it slightly damp.
  3. Apply a small amount of hair gel or wax to your hair and work it through evenly with your fingers.
  4. Use a comb or brush to style your hair upwards in a spiky style.
  5. Use a blow dryer on low heat to set the style in place.
  6. Apply a small amount of hairspray to your hair to keep it in place.
  7. Use your fingers to adjust the spikes and create the desired look.

Congratulations! You have now achieved the perfect spiky hairdo.

Tips for Maintaining and Styling Spiky Hair

Here are some tips for maintaining and styling your spiky hairdo:

  • Use a good quality hair gel or wax.
  • Don't use too much product as it can weigh your hair down.
  • Choose a haircut that suits your face shape and hair texture.
  • Try different styles and experiment with different lengths and angles.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage your hair.

FAQs About Spiky Hair Do

How long does a spiky hairdo last?

A spiky hairdo can last for several hours to a full day, depending on the products you use and the style you choose.

Can a spiky hairdo work for all hair types?

Yes, a spiky hairdo can work for all hair types, but the results may vary depending on the texture and thickness of your hair.

Can I achieve a spiky hairdo without using hair gel or wax?

You can achieve a spiky hairdo using hairspray or mousse, but the results may not be as long-lasting as using hair gel or wax.

Hair Care Tips for Spiky Hair Do

Here are some hair care tips for maintaining healthy and stylish spiky hair:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner that is suitable for your hair type.
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently as it can strip away natural oils.
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Avoid using hot tools, such as blow dryers and flat irons, too often as they can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to maintain the shape and style of your spiky hairdo.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve a perfect spiky hairdo that will make you stand out from the crowd. Experiment with different styles and products to find the perfect look that suits your personality and style.
