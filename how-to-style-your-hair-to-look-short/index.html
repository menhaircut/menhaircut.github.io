How To Style Your Hair To Look Short

Written by Luffy Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle without committing to a short haircut, there are a few tricks you can use to make your hair appear shorter. In this article, we'll show you how to style your hair to look short and share some tips for maintaining your new 'do.

How To

The key to achieving a short hairstyle without actually cutting your hair is to create the illusion of shorter hair. Here are some techniques you can use to achieve this look:

1. Use a Texturizing Spray

Texturizing sprays are a great way to add volume and texture to your hair, which can make it appear shorter. Spritz some texturizing spray onto your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

2. Opt for a Bob

If you're looking for a more dramatic change, consider getting a bob haircut. Bobs are short hairstyles that typically fall between the chin and the shoulders. They're a great way to add some edge to your look and make your hair appear shorter.

3. Use Hair Pins

If you have long hair, you can use hair pins to create the illusion of a shorter style. Pin your hair up at the nape of your neck and let the shorter sections of your hair fall around your face.

4. Try a Faux Hawk

A faux hawk is a hairstyle that's similar to a mohawk, but it's less extreme. To create a faux hawk, use a comb to tease the hair at the crown of your head and smooth the sides down with some gel or hairspray.

Step by Step

If you're not sure how to achieve a shorter hairstyle, here's a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with shampoo and conditioner to remove any product buildup.

Step 2: Towel Dry Your Hair

After washing your hair, gently towel dry it until it's damp but not dripping wet.

Step 3: Apply Texturizing Spray

Spray some texturizing spray onto your roots and work it through your hair with your fingers to create volume and texture.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Use a comb or your fingers to style your hair into the desired shape. You can create a messy, tousled look by scrunching your hair with your hands, or you can use a brush to create a sleeker style.

Step 5: Use Hair Pins

If you have long hair, you can use hair pins to create the illusion of shorter hair. Pin your hair up at the nape of your neck and let the shorter sections of your hair fall around your face.

Step 6: Finish with Hairspray

Finish off your look with a spritz of hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for styling your hair to look short:

  • Experiment with different hair products to find the ones that work best for your hair type.
  • Don't be afraid to try new hairstyles and techniques.
  • Invest in a good quality hairbrush or comb to help you achieve the perfect style.
  • Consider getting a professional haircut to help you achieve the look you want.

FAQ

Can I style my hair to look short if I have long hair?

Yes, you can use hair pins or braids to create the illusion of shorter hair.

How long will it take to style my hair to look short?

The amount of time it takes to style your hair will depend on the length and thickness of your hair, as well as the style you're trying to achieve.

Do I need to use heat styling tools to style my hair to look short?

No, you can achieve a short hairstyle without using heat styling tools. Texturizing sprays and hair pins are great alternatives.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for maintaining your new 'do:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and straighteners on a daily basis.
  • Get regular haircuts to keep your hair looking neat and tidy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair soft and shiny.

By following these tips, you can achieve a short hairstyle without actually cutting your hair. Whether you're looking for a dramatic change or just want to mix things up, these techniques can help you achieve the perfect look.
