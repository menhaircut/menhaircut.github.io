How To Cut Hair Upside Down A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Nov 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Cutting hair upside down is a technique that many hairstylists use to create volume and texture in the hair. It's a great way to add movement to long hair, and can also be used to create layers and shape in shorter haircuts. In this article, we'll show you how to cut hair upside down, step-by-step, so you can try it out for yourself.

How to Cut Hair Upside Down

Cutting hair upside down involves flipping the hair over the head and cutting it from underneath. This technique allows you to create layers and texture in the hair, as well as add volume and movement.

Before you start cutting, make sure you have the right tools. You'll need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips to section the hair. It's also a good idea to have a spray bottle filled with water to keep the hair damp while you're cutting.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Wash and Dry the Hair

Start by washing and drying the hair. It's important to work with clean, dry hair so you can see the natural texture and movement. If the hair is too wet, it will be heavy and harder to cut.

Step 2: Section the Hair

Section the hair into four parts: two on the top and two on the bottom. Use hair clips to hold each section in place.

Step 3: Flip the Hair

Flip the bottom sections of hair over your head so they're resting on the top of your head. Use hair clips to hold the hair in place.

Step 4: Cut the Hair

Starting with the bottom sections, take small sections of hair and cut them using your scissors. Work your way up the head, taking small sections of hair and cutting them to the desired length. Make sure to cut the hair evenly on both sides of the head.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Once you've cut all the sections of hair, use your scissors to blend the hair together. This will create a seamless, natural look.

Step 6: Style the Hair

Style the hair as desired. You can blow dry the hair for extra volume, or let it air dry for a more natural look.

Tips for Cutting Hair Upside Down

Here are some tips to help you get the best results when cutting hair upside down:

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Start with small sections of hair and work your way up to larger sections.
  • Keep the hair damp while cutting to make it easier to work with.
  • Use a comb to guide the scissors and ensure an even cut.
  • Blend the hair together after cutting to create a natural look.

FAQs

Can you cut your own hair upside down?

Yes, you can cut your own hair upside down. However, it can be tricky to do on your own, so it's best to have someone help you.

What kind of hairstyles work well with cutting hair upside down?

Cutting hair upside down works well for adding volume and movement to long hair, as well as creating layers and texture in shorter haircuts.

How often should you cut your hair upside down?

It depends on your hair type and desired style. Generally, it's recommended to get a haircut every 6-8 weeks to keep your hair looking healthy and styled.

Hair Care After Cutting Hair Upside Down

After cutting your hair upside down, it's important to take care of it to keep it looking healthy and styled. Here are some tips:

  • Use a quality shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair clean and hydrated.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep split ends at bay.
  • Use hair masks and treatments to keep your hair healthy and nourished.

Cutting hair upside down is a great technique for adding volume, movement, and texture to your hair. With the right tools and some practice, you can achieve a salon-quality cut at home. Just remember to take care of your hair afterwards to keep it looking healthy and styled.
