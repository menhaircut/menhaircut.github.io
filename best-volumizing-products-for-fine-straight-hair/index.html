Best Volumizing Products For Fine Straight Hair

Written by Luffy Nov 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have fine straight hair, you know how difficult it can be to achieve voluminous locks. Fortunately, there are a variety of products on the market that can help give your hair the lift it needs. In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the best volumizing products for fine straight hair.

Shampoo

The right shampoo can make a big difference in the volume of your hair. Look for shampoos that are specifically designed for fine hair, and that contain ingredients like keratin and biotin that help to strengthen and plump up individual strands. Some popular options include:

  • OGX Thick & Full Biotin & Collagen Shampoo
  • L'Oreal Paris EverStrong Thickening Shampoo
  • Kerastase Resistance Bain Volumifique Shampoo

Conditioner

Conditioner is an important part of any hair care routine, but when it comes to volumizing fine straight hair, you’ll want to choose a conditioner that doesn’t weigh your hair down. Look for lightweight, volumizing conditioners that contain ingredients like wheat protein, which helps to add body and fullness to hair. Some great options include:

  • Paul Mitchell Lemon Sage Thickening Conditioner
  • TRESemme Beauty-Full Volume Reverse System Conditioner
  • Redken High Rise Volume Lifting Conditioner

Mousse

Mousse is a great styling product for adding volume to fine straight hair. Look for mousses that are lightweight and contain volumizing ingredients like hydrolyzed wheat protein or panthenol. Some popular options include:

  • Kenra Volume Mousse
  • Paul Mitchell Extra-Body Sculpting Foam
  • Aveda Phomollient Styling Foam

Root Lift Spray

A root lift spray is a great product for adding volume to the roots of your hair. Look for sprays that contain ingredients like hydrolyzed wheat protein or keratin, which help to strengthen and plump up individual strands. Some popular options include:

  • Big Sexy Hair Root Pump Plus
  • TIGI Bed Head Superstar Queen For A Day Thickening Spray
  • Redken Guts 10 Volume Spray Foam

Dry Shampoo

Dry shampoo is a great product for adding volume to fine straight hair, especially on days when you don’t have time to wash your hair. Look for dry shampoos that are specifically designed for adding volume, and that contain ingredients like rice starch or silica, which help to absorb oil and add texture. Some popular options include:

  • Batiste Volumizing Dry Shampoo
  • Living Proof Perfect Hair Day Dry Shampoo
  • Amika Perk Up Dry Shampoo

Hair Spray

Hair spray is a great product for adding volume and hold to your finished style. Look for hairsprays that are lightweight and provide a flexible hold, so that your hair doesn’t feel stiff or weighed down. Some popular options include:

  • Kenra Volume Spray 25
  • Bumble and Bumble Thickening Hairspray
  • Oribe Maximista Thickening Spray

Tips for Volumizing Fine Straight Hair

Here are some additional tips for adding volume to fine straight hair:

  • Blow dry your hair upside down to add lift at the roots
  • Use a round brush when blow drying to add volume and bounce
  • Avoid heavy oils and serums, which can weigh your hair down
  • Experiment with different hairstyles, like loose waves or a messy bun, to add volume and texture

FAQs

What causes hair to be fine and straight?

Hair texture is determined by the shape of the hair follicle. In general, straight hair has a round follicle shape, while curly or wavy hair has an oval or elliptical shape. Fine hair refers to the thickness of individual strands, which can be influenced by genetics, age, and other factors.

Can you add volume to fine straight hair?

Yes, there are a variety of products and styling techniques that can help add volume to fine straight hair.

How can I prevent my hair from falling flat throughout the day?

One of the best ways to prevent flat hair is to use products that are specifically designed for adding volume, and to avoid heavy oils and serums that can weigh your hair down. You can also try teasing your roots or using a dry shampoo to add texture throughout the day.

Hair Care Routine for Fine Straight Hair

Here are some additional tips for caring for fine straight hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid over-drying and stripping natural oils
  • Use a wide-toothed comb or brush to avoid pulling and breaking fragile strands
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair and make it more prone to breakage
  • Use a heat protectant spray if you do use hot tools
  • Consider getting regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage

In conclusion, there are a variety of products and techniques that can help add volume to fine straight hair. By using the right products and following a good hair care routine, you can achieve the voluminous locks you’ve always wanted.
