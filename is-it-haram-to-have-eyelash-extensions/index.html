Is It Haram To Have Eyelash Extensions

Written by Petter Nov 12, 2023 · 4 min read

If you are a Muslim woman who loves to wear makeup and enhance her beauty, you may have wondered whether it is haram to have eyelash extensions. In this article, we will explore the topic in detail and answer some frequently asked questions. We will also provide you with some tips on how to take care of your eyelash extensions if you decide to get them.

Description

Eyelash extensions are a popular beauty treatment that involves attaching synthetic or natural hair fibers to your natural lashes. The extensions can be customized to suit your desired length and thickness, giving you a fuller and more dramatic look. The treatment can take up to two hours, and the extensions can last for up to six weeks with proper care.

How to

If you decide to get eyelash extensions, you should first find a reputable salon that uses high-quality materials and follows proper hygiene protocols. During the treatment, you will lie down with your eyes closed, and the technician will use a special adhesive to attach the extensions to your natural lashes. It is important to avoid getting the extensions wet for the first 24-48 hours after the treatment to allow the adhesive to fully bond.

Step by Step

Here are the steps involved in getting eyelash extensions:

  1. Find a reputable salon that uses high-quality materials and follows proper hygiene protocols.
  2. Lie down with your eyes closed.
  3. The technician will use a special adhesive to attach the extensions to your natural lashes.
  4. Avoid getting the extensions wet for the first 24-48 hours after the treatment to allow the adhesive to fully bond.

Tips

Here are some tips on how to take care of your eyelash extensions:

  • Avoid rubbing or pulling on your lashes.
  • Avoid using oil-based eye makeup or makeup removers.
  • Use a spoolie brush to gently comb your lashes daily.
  • Avoid sleeping on your face or with your face pressed into a pillow.
  • Get touch-ups every 2-3 weeks to maintain the fullness of your extensions.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about eyelash extensions:

Is it haram to have eyelash extensions?

There is no clear consensus on whether eyelash extensions are haram or halal in Islam. Some scholars argue that they are permissible as long as they do not harm the natural lashes or cover the eyes excessively. Others argue that they are not allowed because they involve altering the natural state of the body.

Can I wear makeup with eyelash extensions?

Yes, you can wear makeup with eyelash extensions, but it is important to avoid using oil-based products that can weaken the adhesive. You should also avoid using mascara on the extensions and instead use a water-based eyeliner or eyeshadow.

How long do eyelash extensions last?

Eyelash extensions can last for up to six weeks with proper care, but you may need touch-ups every 2-3 weeks to maintain the fullness of your lashes.

Hair Care

If you decide to get eyelash extensions, it is important to take care of your natural lashes as well. Here are some tips on how to keep your lashes healthy:

  • Avoid rubbing or pulling on your lashes.
  • Use a gentle eye makeup remover.
  • Apply castor oil or coconut oil to your lashes to promote growth.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or heated styling tools on your lashes.

In conclusion, whether or not it is haram to have eyelash extensions is a matter of interpretation and personal choice. If you decide to get them, make sure to find a reputable salon and take proper care of your lashes. And if you choose not to get them, remember that natural beauty is always in style.
