Best Haircut For Thinning Hair On Crown

Written by Bardi Nov 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have noticed that your hair is thinning on the crown of your head, you are not alone. Thinning hair is a common problem that affects both men and women. Fortunately, there are haircuts that can help to disguise thinning hair and make it look fuller. In this article, we will discuss the best haircut for thinning hair on crown, as well as some tips for hair care to promote healthy hair growth.

Table of Contents

  • How to Determine the Best Haircut for Your Thinning Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide for the Best Haircut for Thinning Hair on Crown
  • Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair
  • FAQs

How to Determine the Best Haircut for Your Thinning Hair

The first step in determining the best haircut for your thinning hair on crown is to consult with a professional stylist. They will be able to assess the extent of your thinning and recommend a suitable haircut that will help to disguise it. Some general tips to keep in mind when choosing a haircut for thinning hair on crown are:

  • Choose a hairstyle that adds volume and texture to your hair
  • Avoid hairstyles that are too short, as they can highlight the thinning areas
  • Consider getting layers, as they can create the illusion of fuller hair
  • Avoid slicked-back hairstyles, as they can accentuate the thinning areas

Step-by-Step Guide for the Best Haircut for Thinning Hair on Crown

The best haircut for thinning hair on crown is a layered cut with a side part. Here is a step-by-step guide to achieving this haircut:

  1. Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add texture and volume to your hair.
  2. Blow-dry your hair, using a round brush to add volume at the roots.
  3. Part your hair to one side, and use a comb to create a deep part.
  4. Ask your stylist to cut your hair in long layers, starting from the crown of your head.
  5. Ask your stylist to feather the ends of your hair to create texture and movement.
  6. Style your hair with a volumizing mousse or spray, focusing on the roots.
  7. Use a curling iron to add loose waves to the ends of your hair.
  8. Finish with a light-hold hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips for Maintaining Healthy Hair

In addition to choosing the right haircut, there are several things you can do to maintain healthy hair growth:

  • Eat a balanced diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, such as bleach or relaxers
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair and cause damage
  • Use a wide-toothed comb to detangle your hair, starting from the ends and working your way up
  • Avoid using heat styling tools on your hair too often, as they can cause damage and breakage

FAQs

Q: Can haircuts really help to disguise thinning hair on crown?

A: Yes, certain haircuts can help to create the illusion of fuller hair and disguise thinning areas.

Q: Is it possible to promote hair growth on the crown of my head?

A: Yes, by maintaining a healthy diet and hair care routine, you can promote healthy hair growth on the crown of your head.

Q: Can stress cause thinning hair on crown?

A: Yes, stress can contribute to hair loss and thinning on the crown of your head.

By following these tips and getting the right haircut, you can disguise thinning hair on crown and promote healthy hair growth. Consult with a professional stylist to determine the best haircut for your specific hair type and thinning pattern.
