What Does 1 2 3 4 Mean In Haircuts

Written by Daniel Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who frequently visits a barbershop or salon, then you might have heard the terms "1 2 3 4" being thrown around. These numbers are often used as a reference to the length of hair that needs to be trimmed or cut. If you're someone who's new to haircuts, then you might be wondering what does 1 2 3 4 mean in haircuts? In this blog post, we'll explore everything you need to know about these numbers, so you can communicate effectively with your hairdresser or barber.

Description

Barbers and hairdressers have their own way of communicating with clients, and one of the most common ways is through numbers. The numbers 1, 2, 3, and 4 are clipper guard sizes that determine the length of hair that will be cut. Each number represents a specific length, and it's essential to know the difference between these guard sizes to get the haircut you desire.

How to

If you want to achieve a particular haircut, it's essential to know the correct length you want. Here's what each number means:

Number 1

Number 1 means that the hair will be cut at 1/8 inch or 3.2 mm. This length is ideal for those who want a buzz cut or a crew cut.

Number 2

Number 2 means that the hair will be cut at 1/4 inch or 6.4 mm. This length is suitable for those who want a slightly longer buzz cut or a short crop.

Number 3

Number 3 means that the hair will be cut at 3/8 inch or 9.5 mm. This length is ideal for those who want a textured crop, a side part, or a quiff.

Number 4

Number 4 means that the hair will be cut at 1/2 inch or 12.7 mm. This length is suitable for those who want a longer textured crop or a side part.

Step by Step

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to use clipper guards:

  1. Attach the clipper guard to the clippers.
  2. Start at the nape of the neck and move the clippers upward towards the crown of the head.
  3. Move the clippers from side to side, ensuring that you're cutting the hair evenly.
  4. Repeat the process until you've achieved the desired length.
  5. Blend the hair with scissors or clippers to create a natural-looking finish.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a haircut:

  • Communicate clearly with your hairdresser or barber about the length you want.
  • Bring a photo to show the hairdresser or barber what you want.
  • Make sure your hair is clean and dry before getting a haircut.
  • Don't be afraid to ask questions if you're unsure about something.
  • Choose a hairstyle that suits your face shape and hair type.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about clipper guard sizes:

What is the difference between a clipper guard and a clipper blade?

A clipper guard is a plastic attachment that goes on the clipper blade, while the clipper blade is the metal part of the clipper that cuts the hair.

What happens if I use the wrong clipper guard size?

If you use the wrong clipper guard size, you might end up with a haircut that's too short or too long. It's essential to communicate clearly with your hairdresser or barber to avoid any mistakes.

Can I use clipper guards on wet hair?

No, it's not recommended to use clipper guards on wet hair as they can cause the hair to stick to the clipper blade, resulting in an uneven cut.

Hair Care

After getting a haircut, it's essential to take care of your hair to maintain its health and style. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools like hairdryers, straighteners, and curling irons too often.
  • Use a hair mask or treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Get regular trims to maintain the shape and style of your haircut.
  • Avoid using too much product on your hair as it can weigh it down and make it look greasy.

Now that you know what does 1 2 3 4 mean in haircuts, you can confidently communicate with your hairdresser or barber and get the haircut you want. Remember to take care of your hair after getting a haircut to maintain its health and style.
