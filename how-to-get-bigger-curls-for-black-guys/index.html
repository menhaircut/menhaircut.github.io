How To Get Bigger Curls For Black Guys A Step By Step Guide

Written by Bardi Sep 08, 2023 ¬∑ 3 min read

If you're a black guy with relaxed hair, you may be wondering how to achieve bigger, more defined curls. Fortunately, there are several techniques and products that can help you achieve the look you want. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to get bigger curls for black guys.

Table of Contents

  1. How to Get Bigger Curls for Black Guys
  2. Step-by-Step Guide
  3. Tips for Getting Bigger Curls
  4. FAQs
  5. Hair Care for Bigger Curls

How to Get Bigger Curls for Black Guys

The key to getting bigger curls is to use products that will enhance your hair's natural texture and shape. Here are some steps to follow:

1. Use a Moisturizing Shampoo

Start by using a moisturizing shampoo that will help to hydrate your hair and prevent it from becoming dry and brittle. Look for a shampoo that contains natural oils and ingredients like coconut oil, jojoba oil, and shea butter.

2. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

After washing your hair, apply a leave-in conditioner to help detangle and moisturize your hair. Look for a conditioner that contains natural ingredients like aloe vera, glycerin, and honey.

3. Use a Curl-Enhancing Cream

Next, apply a curl-enhancing cream to your hair. This will help to define your curls and add volume. Look for a cream that contains natural ingredients like shea butter, jojoba oil, and argan oil.

4. Style Your Hair

Use your fingers or a wide-tooth comb to style your hair. If you want bigger curls, use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to help enhance your curls. You can also use a curling iron or wand to create bigger curls.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide to getting bigger curls:

  1. Wash your hair with a moisturizing shampoo
  2. Apply a leave-in conditioner
  3. Apply a curl-enhancing cream
  4. Style your hair with your fingers or a wide-tooth comb
  5. Use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer to enhance your curls
  6. Alternatively, use a curling iron or wand to create bigger curls

Tips for Getting Bigger Curls

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve bigger curls:

  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and breakage
  • Try not to touch your hair too much once it's styled, as this can cause your curls to become frizzy and lose definition
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction and prevent your curls from becoming tangled
  • Trim your hair regularly to keep it healthy and prevent split ends

FAQs

What products should I use for bigger curls?

You should use a moisturizing shampoo, leave-in conditioner, and curl-enhancing cream.

What tools should I use to style my hair?

You can use your fingers, a wide-tooth comb, a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer, or a curling iron or wand.

How often should I wash my hair?

You should wash your hair as often as needed to keep it clean and healthy. For most people, this is 1-2 times per week.

Hair Care for Bigger Curls

To keep your hair healthy and maintain your bigger curls, it's important to follow a good hair care routine. Here are some tips:

  • Use a moisturizing shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to reduce friction
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals or relaxers on your hair

By following these tips and using the right products, you can achieve bigger, more defined curls that will make you look and feel great.
