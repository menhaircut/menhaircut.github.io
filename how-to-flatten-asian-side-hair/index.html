How To Flatten Asian Side Hair

Written by Oliver Oct 27, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Do you have Asian side hair that just won't stay flat? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people with this hair type struggle to keep their hair tamed and smooth. But with the right techniques and products, you can finally achieve the sleek, straight look you've been dreaming of. In this article, we'll share our best tips for flattening Asian side hair, so you can feel confident and stylish every day.

Table of Contents

  • How to Flatten Asian Side Hair
  • Step by Step
  • Tips to Keep Your Hair Flat
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care for Asian Side Hair

How to Flatten Asian Side Hair

The first step to flattening Asian side hair is to start with clean, dry hair. This will help the products you use to work more effectively. Here's what you'll need:

  • Flat iron
  • Hair clips
  • Hair serum or oil
  • Hairspray

Once you have all your tools, follow these steps:

Step by Step

  1. Section off your hair into manageable sections using hair clips.
  2. Apply a hair serum or oil to each section to help protect your hair from heat damage.
  3. Starting at the roots, use your flat iron to straighten each section of hair.
  4. Repeat this process until all sections of hair are straightened.
  5. Spray your hair with hairspray to help it stay flat throughout the day.

Tips to Keep Your Hair Flat

Now that you know how to flatten Asian side hair, here are some tips to help you keep your hair looking sleek and straight all day:

  • Avoid touching your hair too much, as this can cause frizz and undo all your hard work.
  • Avoid using heavy hair products that can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent your hair from getting tangled and frizzy while you sleep.
  • Avoid exposing your hair to humidity, as this can cause it to frizz and lose its shape.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions people have about flattening Asian side hair:

Can I use a hair straightener to flatten my hair?

Yes, a hair straightener is a great tool for flattening Asian side hair. Just be sure to use a heat protectant serum or oil to prevent damage.

How often should I flat iron my hair?

You should avoid flat ironing your hair every day, as this can cause damage over time. Instead, try to limit it to once or twice a week.

What can I do to prevent my hair from getting frizzy?

Using a hair serum or oil can help prevent frizz, as can avoiding touching your hair too much and using a silk or satin pillowcase.

Hair Care for Asian Side Hair

Finally, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips for hair care for Asian side hair:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner that won't strip your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools like curling irons and hair dryers too often, as this can cause damage.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to help nourish and strengthen your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy.

By following these tips and techniques, you can finally achieve the sleek, smooth look you've been dreaming of for your Asian side hair. With a little patience and practice, you'll be a pro at flattening your hair in no time!
