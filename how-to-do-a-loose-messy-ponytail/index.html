How To Do A Loose Messy Ponytail The Ultimate Guide

Written by Bardi Jul 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you tired of the same old ponytail look? Do you want to add a bit of flair to your hairstyle? Then you're in the right place! In this guide, we'll show you how to create a loose messy ponytail that is sure to turn heads.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to
  • Step by step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A loose messy ponytail is a hairstyle that is perfect for any occasion. Whether you're going to work, school, or a night out, this hairstyle is easy to create and looks great on everyone. It's a casual yet elegant look that can be dressed up or down depending on your outfit.

How to

To create a loose messy ponytail, you will need the following:

  • Brush
  • Hair Elastic
  • Bobby Pins

Step by step

Follow these simple steps to create a loose messy ponytail:

  1. Brush your hair to remove any tangles.
  2. Gather your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  3. Secure the ponytail with a hair elastic.
  4. Gently pull on the sides of the ponytail to loosen it up.
  5. Use your fingers to create a messy look by pulling out a few strands of hair from the sides.
  6. Take a small section of hair from underneath the ponytail and wrap it around the hair elastic to hide it.
  7. Use bobby pins to secure the wrapped hair in place.
  8. Pull out a few more strands of hair around your face to frame it.
  9. Spray with hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to make your loose messy ponytail look even better:

  • Use a volume spray or mousse before creating the ponytail to give your hair more texture.
  • Tease your hair at the crown of your head before creating the ponytail to add more volume.
  • Use clear hair elastics to create a seamless look.
  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them better.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the loose messy ponytail:

  • Can I create a loose messy ponytail on short hair? Yes, you can! Just make sure to leave some hair out around your face to create a more relaxed look.
  • Can I wear a loose messy ponytail to a formal event? Absolutely! Just make sure to use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair in place.
  • How long will this hairstyle last? This hairstyle can last all day if you use enough hairspray to hold it in place.

Hair Care

To keep your hair healthy and looking great, make sure to:

  • Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair.
  • Get regular trims to keep your hair looking healthy.

Follow these tips and you'll have a beautiful, healthy head of hair to create your loose messy ponytail with.

In conclusion, a loose messy ponytail is a simple yet stylish hairstyle that anyone can create. With a little bit of practice, you'll be able to create this look in no time. So go ahead and give it a try today!
