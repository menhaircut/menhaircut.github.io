What Helps With Brassy Hair Tips Tricks And Hair Care

Written by Aprilia Oct 16, 2023 · 4 min read

If you’re someone who has dyed their hair blonde or have naturally blonde hair, chances are you’ve experienced brassiness. Brassy hair can be frustrating and can make your hair look dull and lifeless. Luckily, there are several ways to fix and prevent brassy hair. In this article, we’ll discuss what helps with brassy hair, including tips, tricks, and hair care.

Table of Contents

  • What is Brassy Hair?
  • Why Does Hair Get Brassy?
  • How to Fix Brassy Hair
  • Tips for Preventing Brassy Hair
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Final Thoughts

What is Brassy Hair?

Brassy hair is when blonde hair turns yellow or orange. This can happen due to a variety of reasons, including exposure to the sun, chlorine, hard water, or using the wrong hair products. When hair turns brassy, it can look dull, lifeless, and unattractive.

Why Does Hair Get Brassy?

Several factors can cause hair to turn brassy, including:

  • Exposure to the sun
  • Chlorine in swimming pools
  • Hard water
  • Using the wrong hair products
  • Not using enough toner during the coloring process

How to Fix Brassy Hair

If you’re dealing with brassy hair, don’t panic. There are several ways to fix it:

Use a Purple Shampoo

Purple shampoo is an excellent product for combating brassy hair. It works by neutralizing the yellow and orange tones in your hair. Use a purple shampoo once or twice a week to keep your hair looking fresh and bright.

Try a Toner

A toner can also help fix brassy hair. Toners work by depositing color onto your hair to neutralize any unwanted tones. Talk to your hairstylist about which toner is best for you.

Use a Hair Mask

A hair mask can also help fix brassy hair. Look for a hair mask that is specifically designed for blonde hair. These masks can help nourish your hair while also neutralizing any unwanted tones.

Tips for Preventing Brassy Hair

Preventing brassy hair is all about taking care of your hair and using the right products. Here are some tips:

Avoid Exposure to the Sun

The sun can be incredibly damaging to your hair, and it can also cause your hair to turn brassy. If you’re going to be spending time outside, wear a hat to protect your hair from the sun’s rays.

Avoid Chlorine

Chlorine can also cause your hair to turn brassy. If you’re going to be swimming in a pool, wear a swim cap to protect your hair from the chlorine.

Use the Right Hair Products

Using the right hair products is essential for preventing brassy hair. Look for products that are specifically designed for blonde hair. These products will help keep your hair looking fresh and bright.

Get Regular Haircuts

Getting regular haircuts is also essential for preventing brassy hair. When your hair is healthy, it’s less likely to turn brassy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Fix Brassy Hair at Home?

Yes, you can fix brassy hair at home. Try using a purple shampoo, toner, or hair mask.

How Often Should I Use Purple Shampoo?

You should use purple shampoo once or twice a week.

Can I Prevent Brassy Hair?

Yes, you can prevent brassy hair by avoiding exposure to the sun, chlorine, and using the right hair products.

Final Thoughts

Brassy hair can be frustrating, but it’s not the end of the world. By using the right products and taking care of your hair, you can fix and prevent brassy hair. If you’re unsure about which products to use, talk to your hairstylist. With a little bit of effort, you can keep your blonde hair looking fresh and bright.
