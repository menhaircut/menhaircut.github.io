How To Cut A Fringe Yourself Youtube

Written by Christine Aug 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking to change up your hairstyle, one easy way to do so is by cutting a fringe. However, getting it done at a salon can be expensive and time-consuming. Fortunately, there is a solution: cutting a fringe yourself with the help of YouTube! In this article, we'll guide you through the process step by step, providing tips and answering frequently asked questions.

Description

A fringe, also known as bangs, is a hairstyle where the hair is cut short and hangs over the forehead. It can be a great way to change up your look without a drastic haircut. Cutting a fringe yourself with the help of YouTube is a cost-effective and convenient way to achieve this look.

How to Cut a Fringe Yourself YouTube: Step by Step

Step 1: Gather your tools

Before you get started, you'll need a few tools. These include:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A fine-tooth comb
  • A mirror (preferably a handheld one and a larger one to see the back of your head)
  • A spray bottle filled with water

Step 2: Decide on the type of fringe you want

There are several different types of fringes, including blunt, side-swept, and wispy. Look up pictures online to decide which type of fringe you want to cut and how long you want it to be.

Step 3: Section your hair

Using the fine-tooth comb, section off the hair you want to cut into a triangle shape. The base of the triangle should be at the top of your head, and the point should be where you want the shortest part of your fringe to be.

Step 4: Cut your hair

Starting at the center of your triangle section, use your scissors to cut your hair in a straight line. Take small sections and make sure to cut slowly and carefully. You can always cut more hair, but you can't put it back once it's cut!

Step 5: Check your work

After you've finished cutting, use your mirror to check your work. If you need to make any adjustments, do so carefully and slowly.

Step 6: Style your fringe

Using your fine-tooth comb, style your fringe how you want it to look. You can also use your spray bottle to dampen your hair if needed.

Tips

  • Start with a longer length than you think you want, as you can always cut more off later.
  • Use sharp hair cutting scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Don't cut your hair when it's wet, as it will look shorter when it dries.
  • Be patient and take your time.

FAQ

Can I cut a fringe myself?

Yes, you can! With a little patience and the right tools, cutting a fringe yourself can be easy and cost-effective.

What type of fringe should I get?

There are several different types of fringes, including blunt, side-swept, and wispy. Look up pictures online to decide which type of fringe you want to cut and how long you want it to be.

Do I need sharp scissors?

Yes, using sharp hair cutting scissors is important to ensure a clean cut.

Should I cut my hair when it's wet or dry?

It's best to cut your hair when it's dry, as it will look shorter when it dries.

Hair Care

After cutting your fringe, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain its health and appearance. This includes regular washing and conditioning, using heat protectant products when styling, and getting regular trims to prevent split ends.

Cutting a fringe yourself with the help of YouTube can be a fun and cost-effective way to change up your look. Just remember to be patient and take your time, and you'll have a great new hairstyle in no time!
