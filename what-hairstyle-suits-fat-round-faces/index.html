What Hairstyle Suits Fat Round Faces A Complete Guide

Written by William Aug 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have a round face, you may find it challenging to find the perfect hairstyle that flatters your features. But don't worry, there are plenty of hairstyles that can make your face look slimmer and more defined. In this article, we'll show you what hairstyle suits fat round faces and provide tips on how to style your hair to accentuate your best features.

Description

Having a round face means that your face's length is the same as its width, with soft and rounded features. Round faces tend to have fuller cheeks, wider foreheads, and rounded chins. If you have a round face, you want to choose a hairstyle that adds height to your face and creates the illusion of length.

How to

When choosing a hairstyle, it's essential to consider your face shape, hair texture, and lifestyle. Here are some tips on how to choose the best hairstyle for your round face:

1. Opt for a layered haircut

A layered haircut adds volume and movement to your hair, which can help make your face look slimmer. Ask your hairstylist to give you long layers that start at your cheekbones and work their way down to the ends of your hair.

2. Side-swept bangs

Side-swept bangs can help create the illusion of length on your face. They also draw attention to your eyes and cheekbones, which are typically the most flattering features on a round face.

3. Long hairstyles

Long hairstyles that fall below your shoulders can help elongate your face. Straight or wavy hair can create a slimming effect, while curls can add volume and movement.

Step by Step

Now that you know what hairstyle suits fat round faces let's talk about how to style your hair to accentuate your best features. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair for a round face:

1. Blow-dry your hair

Start by blow-drying your hair using a round brush. Use the brush to lift the roots of your hair and create volume at the crown of your head.

2. Style your bangs

If you have side-swept bangs, use a flat iron to style them. Sweep them to one side of your face and tuck them behind your ear.

3. Add curls

If you have long hair, add loose curls using a curling iron or a flat iron. Curl your hair away from your face to create a slimming effect.

4. Finish with hairspray

Finish your hairstyle with a light hairspray to keep your hair in place.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when styling your hair for a round face:

1. Avoid blunt cuts

Blunt cuts can make your face look wider and rounder. Instead, opt for a layered haircut that adds volume and movement to your hair.

2. Avoid center parts

A center part can make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for a side part, which can create the illusion of length on your face.

3. Use hair accessories

Hair accessories like headbands and hair clips can help add height to your hair, which can make your face look slimmer.

FAQ

What hairstyles should I avoid if I have a round face?

Avoid hairstyles that add volume to the sides of your face, such as a bob with blunt ends or a short, curly hairstyle.

Can I wear my hair in a ponytail if I have a round face?

A high ponytail can create the illusion of height on your face, making it look slimmer. However, avoid a low ponytail that rests at the nape of your neck, as it can make your face look wider.

How often should I get a haircut?

It's best to get a trim every six to eight weeks to maintain the shape of your haircut.

Hair Care

Maintaining healthy hair is essential to achieving the perfect hairstyle. Here are some tips on how to care for your hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, leaving it dry and brittle. Use a sulfate-free shampoo to keep your hair healthy and shiny.

2. Use a deep conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and nourished.

3. Protect your hair from heat damage

Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools like curling irons and flat irons to prevent heat damage.

In conclusion, choosing the right hairstyle for your face shape can make all the difference in how you look and feel. If you have a round face, remember to opt for a layered haircut, side-swept bangs, and long hairstyles that elongate your face. With the right hairstyle and hair care routine, you can look and feel your best every day.
