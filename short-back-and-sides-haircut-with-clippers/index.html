The Short Back And Sides Haircut With Clippers A Guide For The Perfect Look

Written by Petter Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a classic and timeless hairstyle, the short back and sides haircut with clippers is the way to go. This hairstyle has been popular for decades and continues to be a favorite among men of all ages. Not only is it easy to maintain and style, but it's also versatile and can be worn in many different ways. In this article, we'll show you how to achieve the perfect short back and sides haircut with clippers.

Description

The short back and sides haircut with clippers is a style where the hair on the top is left longer while the sides and back are cut short using clippers. This haircut is also known as the classic crew cut or the military cut. It is a low-maintenance haircut that requires minimal upkeep and is perfect for those who lead a busy lifestyle.

How to

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to achieve the perfect short back and sides haircut with clippers:

Step 1: Choose the Right Clippers

Choosing the right clippers is crucial for achieving the perfect haircut. You'll need a high-quality clipper that's powerful enough to cut through thick hair. Look for clippers that come with different guards for different lengths.

Step 2: Wash Your Hair

Before you start cutting your hair, make sure it's clean and dry. Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, then let it air dry or use a towel to dry it.

Step 3: Trim the Sides and Back

Start by attaching the appropriate guard to your clippers and trim the sides and back of your hair. Use the clipper in an upward motion to create a clean and even cut.

Step 4: Cut the Top

Once you've trimmed the sides and back, it's time to cut the top. Use a pair of scissors to trim the hair on the top to your desired length. You can also use the clippers with a longer guard to trim the top.

Step 5: Blend the Hair

Use the clippers without a guard to blend the hair between the sides and back and the top. Make sure to blend the hair evenly to create a seamless look.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Once you've cut your hair, you can style it as desired. You can use a hair gel or pomade to create a slicked-back look, or you can leave it natural for a more casual look.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when getting a short back and sides haircut with clippers:

- Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter one to avoid cutting too much hair at once. - Use a mirror to check the back of your head to ensure an even cut. - If you're unsure about cutting your hair, visit a professional hairstylist.

FAQ

Is the short back and sides haircut with clippers suitable for all hair types?

Yes, this haircut is suitable for all hair types, including thick, curly, and straight hair.

How often should I get a short back and sides haircut with clippers?

This haircut is low maintenance and can last up to four weeks before needing a touch-up.

Can I style my short back and sides haircut with clippers in different ways?

Yes, this haircut is versatile and can be styled in many different ways, including slicked-back, textured, and messy.

Hair Care

To keep your short back and sides haircut with clippers looking great, follow these hair care tips:

- Shampoo and condition your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. - Use a hair gel or pomade to style your hair. - Visit a professional hairstylist every four weeks for a touch-up.

With these tips and techniques, you can achieve the perfect short back and sides haircut with clippers. It's a classic and timeless hairstyle that's perfect for any occasion. So, grab your clippers and start cutting!
