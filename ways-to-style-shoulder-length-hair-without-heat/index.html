Ways To Style Shoulder Length Hair Without Heat

If you have shoulder length hair and want to style it without using heat, you've come to the right place! In this article, we'll be sharing ten easy and heat-free ways to style your shoulder length hair.

1. Braids

Braids are a great way to style shoulder length hair without heat. There are so many different types of braids to choose from, such as French braids, Dutch braids, and fishtail braids. One of our favorite ways to style shoulder length hair with braids is by doing a half-up half-down braid.

How to:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from the front of your head on one side and braid it all the way down to the end.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Take the two braids and tie them together at the back of your head with a hair tie.
  5. To finish the look, loosen up the braids a bit to give them a more relaxed look.

Tips: Use a texturizing spray to give your hair more grip when braiding.

2. Top Knot

A top knot is a simple and easy way to style your shoulder length hair without heat. This look is perfect for those days when you're in a rush or just want to keep your hair out of your face.

How to:

  1. Start by gathering all of your hair at the top of your head.
  2. Twist your hair into a bun and secure it with a hair tie.
  3. Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands.

Tips: Use a scrunchie instead of a hair tie to avoid creating any creases in your hair.

3. Ponytail

A ponytail is a classic hairstyle that never goes out of style. It's also a great way to keep your shoulder length hair out of your face while still looking stylish.

How to:

  1. Start by brushing your hair back and gathering it at the nape of your neck.
  2. Secure your hair with a hair tie.
  3. To create a more polished look, take a small section of hair from the bottom of your ponytail and wrap it around the hair tie.
  4. Secure the end of the section with a bobby pin.

Tips: Use a hair tie that matches your hair color to create a seamless look.

4. Twists

Twists are a simple and easy way to style your shoulder length hair without heat. This look is perfect for those days when you want to switch up your hairstyle but don't have a lot of time.

How to:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and twist it all the way down to the end.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Take the two twists and tie them together at the back of your head with a hair tie.

Tips: Use a leave-in conditioner to help smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

5. Hats

Hats are a great way to style your shoulder length hair without heat. Not only do they add a stylish touch to any outfit, but they also protect your hair from the sun and wind.

How to:

Simply put on your favorite hat and style your hair however you like!

Tips: Use a dry shampoo to help absorb any excess oil and give your hair more volume.

6. Headbands

Headbands are a simple and easy way to style your shoulder length hair without heat. They come in a variety of styles and colors, so you can easily find one that matches your personal style.

How to:

Simply put on your favorite headband and style your hair however you like!

Tips: Use a hair serum to help smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

7. Scrunchies

Scrunchies are a stylish and easy way to style your shoulder length hair without heat. They come in a variety of colors and patterns, so you can easily find one that matches your personal style.

How to:

  1. Start by gathering your hair into a ponytail.
  2. Wrap your scrunchie around your hair once or twice, depending on how tight you want your ponytail to be.

Tips: Use a scrunchie made of silk or satin to avoid creating any creases in your hair.

8. Hair Clips

Hair clips are a simple and easy way to style your shoulder length hair without heat. They come in a variety of styles and colors, so you can easily find one that matches your personal style.

How to:

Simply clip your hair back with your favorite hair clip!

Tips: Use a hair clip with teeth to help keep your hair in place.

9. Accessories

Accessories, such as scarves and hair ties, are a great way to style your shoulder length hair without heat. They come in a variety of styles and colors, so you can easily find one that matches your personal style.

How to:

Simply tie your scarf or hair tie around your hair and style it however you like!

Tips: Use a scarf made of silk or satin to avoid creating any creases in your hair.

10. Updos

Updos are a chic and elegant way to style your shoulder length hair without heat. They can be dressed up or down, making them the perfect hairstyle for any occasion.

How to:

  1. Start by gathering your hair into a ponytail at the nape of your neck.
  2. Twist your ponytail into a bun and secure it with bobby pins.
  3. Use a comb to smooth out any flyaways.

Tips: Use a hair donut to help create a fuller-looking bun.

FAQ

How can I make my hair look less greasy without using heat?

Use a dry shampoo to absorb any excess oil and give your hair more volume. You can also try using a leave-in conditioner to help smooth out any frizz or flyaways.

How can I make my hair look more voluminous without using heat?

Use a texturizing spray or mousse to give your hair more grip and volume. You can also try backcombing your hair at the roots to create more lift.

Hair Care

It's important to take care of your hair, especially when you're not using heat to style it. Here are a few tips to help keep your hair healthy and strong:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Use a hair mask once a week to help nourish your hair.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's wet, as it can cause breakage.
