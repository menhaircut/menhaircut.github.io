Do Curls Suit Me

Written by Christine Aug 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering getting curls but not sure if they will suit you? We understand that making changes to your hair can be daunting, especially if you're not sure if it will look good. In this article, we will be discussing how to determine if curls suit you and what you can do to make them work for you.

Description

Curly hair can be a great way to add volume, texture, and dimension to your hair. However, not everyone can pull off curls. Depending on your face shape, hair type, and personal style, curls may or may not suit you.

How to Determine if Curls Suit You

The first step in determining if curls suit you is to consider your face shape. Generally, curls look best on faces with soft, round features. If you have a more angular face, straight hair may be more flattering.

Next, consider your hair type. If you have naturally curly hair, adding more curls can enhance your natural texture. However, if you have straight hair, you may need to use styling tools to create curls. This can be time-consuming and may not be worth the effort if the curls don't suit you.

Finally, think about your personal style. If you prefer a more polished, put-together look, curls may not be the best choice for you. On the other hand, if you like a more relaxed, bohemian vibe, curls can be a great way to add some texture to your hair.

Step by Step Guide

Step 1: Determine Your Face Shape

Take a look in the mirror and determine what shape your face is. If you have a round or oval face, curls will likely suit you. If you have a more angular face, straight hair may be more flattering.

Step 2: Consider Your Hair Type

If you have naturally curly hair, adding more curls can enhance your natural texture. If you have straight hair, you may need to use styling tools to create curls. Make sure you're comfortable with the time and effort it takes to create and maintain curls.

Step 3: Think About Your Personal Style

If you prefer a more polished, put-together look, curls may not be the best choice for you. On the other hand, if you like a more relaxed, bohemian vibe, curls can be a great way to add some texture to your hair.

Tips

If you're not sure if curls suit you, start with a small change. For example, you could try curling just the ends of your hair to see how it looks.

Invest in a good quality curling iron or wand. This will make it easier to create and maintain curls.

Use a heat protectant spray before curling your hair to prevent damage.

FAQ

Q: I have straight hair, can I still get curls?

A: Yes, you can use styling tools like a curling iron or wand to create curls in straight hair.

Q: Will curls make my hair look shorter?

A: Yes, curls can make your hair appear shorter because they add volume and texture.

Q: How do I maintain my curls?

A: Use a curl-enhancing product and avoid brushing your curls to maintain their shape.

Hair Care

If you decide to get curls, it's important to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best. Here are some tips for caring for curly hair:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent dryness and damage.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated.
  • Avoid brushing your curls, as this can cause frizz and breakage.

In conclusion, determining if curls suit you depends on your face shape, hair type, and personal style. If you decide to get curls, make sure to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and looking its best.
