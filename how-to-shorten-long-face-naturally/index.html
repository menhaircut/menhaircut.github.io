How To Shorten Long Face Naturally In 2023

Do you feel like your face is too long? Are you looking for natural ways to shorten your face without going under the knife? You're in luck! In this article, we'll discuss how to shorten long face naturally in 2023. We'll cover everything from facial exercises to hairstyles to help you achieve the look you desire. Let's get started!

How to Shorten Long Face Naturally

There are several natural ways to shorten your face, including facial exercises, makeup, and hairstyles. Facial exercises can help to tone and tighten the muscles in your face, giving you a more youthful appearance. Makeup can also be used to create the illusion of a shorter face. And finally, choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference in how long your face appears.

Facial Exercises

Facial exercises are a great way to tone and tighten the muscles in your face. Here are a few exercises that can help to shorten your face:

  • Chin Lifts: Look up at the ceiling and pucker your lips as if you're trying to kiss the ceiling. Hold for 5 seconds and repeat 10 times.
  • Cheek Puffs: Puff out your cheeks as much as possible, hold for 5 seconds, and then release. Repeat 10 times.
  • Jaw Clenches: Clench your jaw as tight as possible, hold for 5 seconds, and then release. Repeat 10 times.

Makeup

Makeup can also be used to create the illusion of a shorter face. Here are a few tips:

  • Contouring: Use a darker shade of foundation or bronzer to contour the sides of your face, making it appear shorter.
  • Highlighting: Use a lighter shade of foundation or highlighter to highlight the center of your face, making it appear more prominent.
  • Blush: Apply blush to the apples of your cheeks, making them appear more prominent and shortening the length of your face.

Hairstyles

Choosing the right hairstyle can make a big difference in how long your face appears. Here are a few tips:

  • Bangs: Opt for bangs that hit above your eyebrows to shorten the appearance of your forehead.
  • Layers: Layers can add volume to your hair and create the illusion of a shorter face.
  • Bobs: A chin-length bob can shorten the appearance of your face.

Step-by-Step Guide

Here's a step-by-step guide on how to shorten long face naturally:

  1. Start by doing facial exercises to tone and tighten the muscles in your face.
  2. Use makeup to contour and highlight your face to create the illusion of a shorter face.
  3. Choose a hairstyle that complements your face shape and shortens the appearance of your face.

Tips to Shorten Long Face Naturally

Here are a few additional tips to help you shorten your face naturally:

  • Stay hydrated to keep your skin looking plump and youthful.
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption, as they can accelerate the aging process.
  • Eat a healthy, balanced diet to nourish your skin from the inside out.

FAQs

Can facial exercises really shorten my face?

Facial exercises can help to tone and tighten the muscles in your face, which can give you a more youthful appearance. While they may not necessarily shorten your face, they can make it appear more lifted and toned.

How can I choose the right hairstyle for my face shape?

The right hairstyle can make a big difference in how long your face appears. Consult with a hairstylist who can help you choose a style that complements your face shape.

How long does it take to see results from facial exercises?

Results from facial exercises may vary, but you may start to see some improvement in the tone and texture of your skin within a few weeks. Consistency is key, so be sure to stick with your routine.

Hair Care for Shortened Face

Now that you've learned how to shorten long face naturally, it's important to take care of your hair to maintain your new look. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to keep your hair healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too frequently, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair to prevent breakage.

And there you have it! With these tips and tricks, you can easily shorten your face naturally without resorting to surgery. Remember to be patient and consistent with your routine, and you'll be on your way to a shorter, more youthful-looking face in no time.
