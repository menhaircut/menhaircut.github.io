How To Change Texture Of Your Hair A Simple Guide

Written by Luffy Jun 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of having the same old hair texture every day? Do you want to change things up and try something new? Well, you're in luck! In this article, we'll show you how to change the texture of your hair in just a few simple steps. From straight to curly or vice versa, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Change Texture of Your Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Changing Hair Texture
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Changing the texture of your hair can be a daunting task, especially if you're not sure where to start. However, it's not as difficult as you might think. With a few simple steps, you can transform your hair from straight to curly or curly to straight. The key is to use the right tools and products, and to take good care of your hair before and after the process.

How to Change Texture of Your Hair

The first step in changing the texture of your hair is to decide what type of texture you want. Do you want to go from straight to curly or curly to straight? Once you've made your decision, you can start gathering the tools and products you'll need.

For straight hair, you'll need a flat iron, heat protectant spray and a comb. For curly hair, you'll need a curling iron, curling cream and a diffuser attachment for your blow dryer.

Step-by-Step Guide

Changing Straight Hair to Curly

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp.
  2. Apply a heat protectant spray to your hair to protect it from the heat of the flat iron.
  3. Take a small section of hair and clamp the flat iron at the root.
  4. Twist the flat iron 180 degrees away from your face and glide it down the length of your hair.
  5. Repeat this process on all sections of your hair.
  6. Once you've straightened all of your hair, use your fingers to separate the strands and create loose waves.

Changing Curly Hair to Straight

  1. Start by washing your hair and towel-drying it until it's damp.
  2. Apply a curling cream to your hair to help loosen the curls.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a diffuser attachment to help maintain the natural curl pattern.
  4. Once your hair is completely dry, use a flat iron to straighten small sections of your hair.
  5. Repeat this process until you've straightened all of your hair.
  6. Finish by applying a smoothing serum to your hair to help prevent frizz.

Tips for Changing Hair Texture

Here are a few tips to keep in mind when changing the texture of your hair:

  • Use high-quality tools and products to protect your hair from damage.
  • Take good care of your hair before and after the process by using a deep conditioning treatment and avoiding heat styling as much as possible.
  • Be patient and take your time. Changing the texture of your hair can be a time-consuming process, but the results are worth it.

FAQ

Can I change my hair texture permanently?

Yes, you can change your hair texture permanently using chemical treatments like a perm or relaxer. However, these treatments can be damaging to your hair and should only be done by a professional stylist.

How often should I change the texture of my hair?

It's up to you! You can change your hair texture as often as you like, but it's important to take good care of your hair to prevent damage.

Hair Care Tips

Whether you're changing the texture of your hair or not, it's important to take good care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are a few tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, and always use a heat protectant spray.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair hydrated and healthy.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles like braids and ponytails that can pull on your hair and cause damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet and drink plenty of water to nourish your hair from the inside out.

And there you have it! Changing the texture of your hair is easy with the right tools and products. Whether you're going for a sleek straight look or soft, bouncy curls, you can achieve the look you want with just a few simple steps. So go ahead and try something new – your hair will thank you for it!
