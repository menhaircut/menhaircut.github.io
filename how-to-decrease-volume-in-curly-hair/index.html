How To Decrease Volume In Curly Hair Tips And Tricks

Written by Aprilia Jun 24, 2023 · 4 min read

Caring for curly hair can be a challenge, especially when it comes to managing volume. Whether you have naturally curly hair or you’ve recently had a curly perm, you may find that your hair has a tendency to puff up and become frizzy. Fortunately, there are ways to decrease volume in curly hair without sacrificing your curls or damaging your locks. Here are some tips and tricks to help you tame your curls and achieve a sleek, polished look.

How to Decrease Volume in Curly Hair: The Basics

If you want to decrease volume in your curly hair, the first step is to invest in the right hair care products. Look for shampoos and conditioners that are specifically designed for curly hair, as these will help to hydrate your locks and prevent frizz. You should also invest in a good quality hair serum or oil, which will help to smooth your hair and reduce volume. When you’re styling your hair, use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to help distribute heat evenly and avoid creating more volume.

Step-by-Step Guide to Decreasing Volume in Curly Hair

Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you decrease volume in your curly hair:

Step 1: Start by washing your hair with a shampoo and conditioner that are designed for curly hair. This will help to hydrate your locks and prepare them for styling.

Step 2: Apply a hair serum or oil to your damp hair, focusing on the ends and mid-lengths. This will help to smooth your hair and reduce volume.

Step 3: Use a diffuser attachment on your hair dryer to dry your hair. Hold the diffuser close to your scalp and use a low heat setting to avoid creating more volume.

Step 4: Once your hair is dry, use a curling iron to define your curls. Choose a curling iron that is the same size as your curls, and wrap small sections of hair around the barrel.

Step 5: Apply a finishing spray or hairspray to set your curls and reduce volume. Use a light touch, as too much product can weigh down your hair and create more volume.

Tips for Decreasing Volume in Curly Hair

Here are some additional tips to help you decrease volume in your curly hair:

  • Don’t shampoo your hair too often, as this can strip your hair of natural oils and cause it to become dry and frizzy.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it’s dry, as this can create more volume and frizz. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when it’s wet.
  • Try using a hair mask once a week to hydrate your locks and reduce volume. Look for masks that contain ingredients like coconut oil, shea butter, or argan oil.
  • If you’re prone to frizz, try sleeping on a silk or satin pillowcase. These fabrics are gentler on your hair and won’t create as much friction as cotton.

FAQs About Decreasing Volume in Curly Hair

Here are some frequently asked questions about decreasing volume in curly hair:

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?
A: It depends on your hair type and how much oil your scalp produces. Generally, it’s best to wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.

Q: Can I use a flat iron to decrease volume in my curly hair?
A: Yes, but be careful not to use too much heat or you could damage your hair. Use a heat protectant spray and a low heat setting, and only flat iron small sections of hair at a time.

Q: How can I prevent my hair from becoming frizzy?
A: Try using a hair serum or oil to smooth your hair and reduce frizz. You can also sleep on a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction and tangles.

The Importance of Hair Care

Caring for your curly hair is essential if you want to achieve a sleek, polished look. By investing in the right hair care products and following the tips and tricks outlined in this article, you can decrease volume in your curly hair and enjoy a more manageable style. Remember to be patient and gentle with your curls, and always protect your hair from heat and environmental damage.
