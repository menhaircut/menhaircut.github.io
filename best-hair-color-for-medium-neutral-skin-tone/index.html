Best Hair Color For Medium Neutral Skin Tone How To Choose The Perfect Shade

Written by Eveline Aug 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Choosing the right hair color can be a daunting task, especially if you have a medium neutral skin tone. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which shade will look best on you. In this article, we will guide you through the process of selecting the best hair color for medium neutral skin tone.

Description

Medium neutral skin tone is characterized by a balance of warm and cool undertones, making it versatile and easy to work with. It is neither too dark nor too light and falls in the middle of the skin tone spectrum. It is important to choose a hair color that complements your skin tone to enhance your natural beauty.

How to Choose the Perfect Shade

The first step in choosing the perfect hair color for medium neutral skin tone is to determine your skin undertones. You can do this by looking at the veins on your wrist. If your veins appear blue or purple, you have cool undertones. If your veins appear green, you have warm undertones. If your veins appear blue-green or teal, you have neutral undertones.

Once you have established your undertones, you can choose a hair color that will complement your skin tone. For medium neutral skin tone, the best hair colors are warm and cool shades that are not too light or too dark. Some of the best hair colors for medium neutral skin tone include:

Warm Shades

  • Golden blonde
  • Caramel
  • Copper
  • Red

Cool Shades

  • Ash blonde
  • Platinum
  • Ash brown
  • Jet black

Step by Step Guide

Follow these steps to choose the perfect hair color for medium neutral skin tone:

  1. Determine your skin undertones.
  2. Choose warm or cool shades that are not too light or too dark.
  3. Consider your personal style and preferences.
  4. Consult with a professional colorist if you are unsure.

Tips

Here are some tips to keep in mind when choosing a hair color for medium neutral skin tone:

  • Avoid extreme shades that are too light or too dark.
  • Consider your natural hair color when choosing a shade.
  • Choose a shade that complements your eye color.
  • Consider your lifestyle and maintenance requirements.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about choosing the best hair color for medium neutral skin tone:

Q: Can I dye my hair a different color if I have medium neutral skin tone?

A: Yes, you can dye your hair any color you like as long as it complements your skin tone.

Q: Should I choose warm or cool shades for medium neutral skin tone?

A: Both warm and cool shades can look great on medium neutral skin tone. It depends on your personal style and preferences.

Q: How often should I touch up my hair color?

A: It depends on the type of dye and your hair growth rate. Generally, touch-ups are needed every 4-6 weeks.

Hair Care

After you have dyed your hair, it is important to take care of it to maintain its color and health. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent color fading.
  • Avoid heat styling tools as much as possible.
  • Apply a hair mask once a week to keep your hair moisturized.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends.

Choosing the best hair color for medium neutral skin tone can be a fun and exciting process. With the right shade, you can enhance your natural beauty and feel confident in your appearance. Follow our guide and tips to choose the perfect hair color for you.
