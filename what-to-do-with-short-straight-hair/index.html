What To Do With Short Straight Hair A Guide For Easy Hairstyles

Written by Aprilia Sep 07, 2023 · 4 min read

If you have short straight hair, you may feel limited in your styling options. However, there are actually many hairstyles that can work well with this hair type. In this guide, we’ll cover how to style short straight hair, as well as tips for hair care and frequently asked questions.

Table of Contents

  • How to Style Short Straight Hair
  • Step-by-Step Instructions for Easy Hairstyles
  • Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance
  • FAQs About Short Straight Hair

How to Style Short Straight Hair

When it comes to styling short straight hair, the key is to create volume and texture. This can be achieved through a variety of techniques, such as blow drying, using a curling iron, or adding products like mousse or texturizing spray.

Before styling your hair, it’s important to start with clean, dry hair. This will help your styling products to hold better and prevent any damage from heat styling tools.

Next, you can try any of the following techniques:

  • Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and smoothness.
  • Use a curling iron to create loose waves or curls.
  • Add texturizing spray or mousse to create a messy, tousled look.

Experiment with different techniques to find what works best for your hair type and personal style.

Step-by-Step Instructions for Easy Hairstyles

Here are some easy hairstyles that you can try with short straight hair:

1. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut is a classic short hairstyle that can be tailored to your face shape and personal style. To style a pixie cut, follow these steps:

  1. Apply a small amount of texturizing spray or mousse to your hair.
  2. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  3. Use a small amount of pomade or wax to define your hair and create texture.

2. Bob Cut

A bob cut is a versatile hairstyle that can be worn straight, wavy, or curly. To style a bob cut, follow these steps:

  1. Blow dry your hair with a round brush to create volume and smoothness.
  2. Use a flat iron to straighten your hair or create loose waves.
  3. Add a small amount of serum or oil to tame any flyaways and add shine.

3. Messy Bun

A messy bun is a quick and easy hairstyle that can be dressed up or down. To style a messy bun, follow these steps:

  1. Gather your hair into a high ponytail and secure with an elastic band.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the base to create a bun shape.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins and pull out a few strands to create a messy look.

Tips for Hair Care and Maintenance

To keep your short straight hair looking healthy and shiny, here are some tips to follow:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as this can damage your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and maintain shape.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

FAQs About Short Straight Hair

Q: Can I curl short straight hair?

A: Yes, you can use a curling iron or hot rollers to create curls or waves in your short straight hair.

Q: How often should I wash my short straight hair?

A: This depends on your hair type and personal preference. If you have oily hair, you may need to wash it more often than someone with dry hair. As a general rule, aim to wash your hair every 2-3 days.

Q: Will using hair products make my hair greasy?

A: It’s possible that using too much product can make your hair greasy. Start with a small amount and gradually add more as needed.

With these tips and easy hairstyles, you can make the most of your short straight hair and create a variety of looks for any occasion.
