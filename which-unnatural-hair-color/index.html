Which Unnatural Hair Color Is Right For You

Written by Robby Oct 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Welcome to our guide on choosing the perfect unnatural hair color for your style! Whether you’re looking to make a bold statement or just want to switch things up, there’s a wide range of colors to choose from. In this article, we’ll cover the most popular choices, how to achieve them, and provide tips for maintaining your new look. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

Blue Hair

Blue hair is a popular choice for those looking to make a statement. Whether you go for a bright, electric blue or a more muted navy, this color is sure to turn heads. To achieve the look, you’ll need to bleach your hair first if it’s not already light blonde. Then, you can apply a semi-permanent blue hair dye. Keep in mind that blue hair can fade quickly, so make sure to use color-safe shampoo and avoid washing your hair too often.

Key Points:

  • Bleach hair first if not already light blonde
  • Apply semi-permanent blue hair dye
  • Use color-safe shampoo and avoid washing too often

Hair Care Tips

Now that you have your new hair color, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips to keep your hair looking vibrant and healthy:

  • Use color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too often
  • Avoid using hot tools like blow dryers and straighteners
  • Use a heat protectant if you must use hot tools
  • Deep condition your hair once a week

FAQ

Q: How long will my new hair color last?

A: It depends on the color and how often you wash your hair. Generally, semi-permanent dyes will last 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair an unnatural color without bleaching it first?

A: It’s possible, but the color won’t be as vibrant and may not show up at all on darker hair.

Q: Can I dye my hair an unnatural color at home?

A: Yes, but it’s important to follow the instructions on the hair dye carefully and be prepared to make a mess.

Conclusion

Unnatural hair colors are a fun and exciting way to express your individuality. Whether you go for blue, pink, purple, green, or silver, there’s a color that’s perfect for you. With the right products and care, you can keep your hair looking vibrant and healthy for weeks to come. Have fun and experiment with different colors to find your perfect match!
