How To Do Updos For Short Hair Yourself

Written by Oliver Oct 07, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Updos are a great way to elevate your look for a special occasion or even for everyday wear. However, if you have short hair, it may seem impossible to create an updo. But fear not! With the right techniques and products, you can create beautiful updos for short hair yourself. In this post, we will guide you through the process of creating updos for short hair, step by step.

Description

Short hair can be a challenge when it comes to styling. However, with the right techniques and tools, you can create beautiful and chic updos. Whether you have a pixie cut or a bob, there are updos that you can create on your own that will take your look to the next level.

How to

The first step in creating an updo for short hair is to gather all the necessary tools and products. You will need bobby pins, hair elastics, hairspray, and a texturizing spray. Once you have all your tools and products ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Start with clean, dry hair

It is important to start with clean, dry hair when creating an updo. This will ensure that your hair stays in place and does not slip out of the updo. Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair before you start styling.

Step 2: Section your hair

Section your hair into two parts, the top, and the bottom. Use a hair elastic to tie the top section into a ponytail, leaving the bottom section loose.

Step 3: Create a twist

Take the bottom section of your hair and twist it upwards, towards the ponytail. Secure the twist to the ponytail with bobby pins, making sure to hide the pins as much as possible.

Step 4: Add volume to the top section

Tease the top section of your hair with a comb to add volume. Smooth the hair back and secure it to the twist with bobby pins.

Step 5: Finish with hairspray

Once you have secured your updo, finish it off with a generous amount of hairspray. This will ensure that your updo stays in place for the entire day.

Tips

  • Use a texturizing spray to add volume and texture to your hair before styling.
  • Hide bobby pins as much as possible by inserting them vertically into your hair and twisting them to secure.
  • Experiment with different updo styles to find what works best for your hair length and texture.

FAQ

Q: Can I create an updo for short hair without using bobby pins?

A: While bobby pins are the easiest and most common way to secure an updo, you can also use hair elastics, hair clips, or even a headband to create an updo for short hair.

Q: How do I make sure my updo stays in place all day?

A: Use a generous amount of hairspray to finish off your updo. You can also use a texturizing spray before styling to add grip and hold to your hair.

Hair Care

It is important to take care of your hair before and after styling. Use a heat protectant spray before using any hot tools on your hair, and use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and strong.

Creating updos for short hair may seem daunting at first, but with the right techniques and products, it can be a fun and easy process. Follow these steps and tips to create beautiful updos for short hair yourself!
