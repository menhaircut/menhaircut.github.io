How To Have Greasy Looking Hair A Guide To Achieving The Perfectly Oily Look

Written by Bardi Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly washing your hair and trying to get rid of that greasy feeling? Well, it's time to embrace the oil and learn how to have greasy looking hair. Not only is this look on-trend, but it can also save you time and money on hair products. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide on achieving the perfect greasy hair look.

How to Have Greasy Looking Hair

Having greasy looking hair may seem like a strange trend, but it's actually become quite popular in recent years. Greasy hair can add texture and volume to your locks, making them look fuller and thicker. Plus, it's a low-maintenance hairstyle that can save you time and money.

Step by Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to achieve the perfectly oily hair look:

1. Don't wash your hair

The first step to having greasy looking hair is to stop washing it. Washing your hair too often can strip it of its natural oils, which can lead to dryness and damage. Instead, try washing your hair once a week or every other week.

2. Use dry shampoo

When your hair starts to feel oily, use dry shampoo to absorb the excess oil. Spray the dry shampoo onto your roots and massage it into your scalp. Then, brush your hair to distribute the product evenly.

3. Add texture

To add texture and volume to your hair, use a texturizing spray or sea salt spray. Spray the product onto your hair and scrunch it with your fingers to create a messy, tousled look.

4. Embrace the shine

Greasy hair is naturally shiny, so embrace the shine and let your locks glisten. Avoid using products that mattify your hair, like hair powder or matte pomades.

Tips

Here are some additional tips for achieving the perfect greasy hair look:

1. Use a boar bristle brush

Boar bristle brushes are great for distributing your hair's natural oils, which can help create the greasy look you're going for. Use the brush to gently comb your hair, starting at the roots and working your way down to the ends.

2. Avoid touching your hair

The more you touch your hair, the more oil you'll produce. So, avoid touching your hair throughout the day to keep your locks looking greasy.

3. Try different hairstyles

Greasy hair can be styled in a variety of ways, from messy buns to braids. Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that works best for you.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about how to have greasy looking hair:

1. Is it healthy to have greasy hair?

While it's not necessarily unhealthy to have greasy hair, it's important to maintain good scalp hygiene. Make sure to regularly brush your hair and clean your scalp to prevent buildup of oil and dirt.

2. Can I achieve the greasy hair look on short hair?

Yes, the greasy hair look can be achieved on hair of any length. Just follow the same steps outlined in this article.

Hair Care

While having greasy looking hair may seem low-maintenance, it's still important to take care of your locks. Here are some tips for maintaining healthy hair:

1. Use a sulfate-free shampoo

When you do wash your hair, use a sulfate-free shampoo to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

2. Condition regularly

Use a conditioner to keep your hair hydrated and prevent breakage.

3. Protect your hair from heat

Use heat protectant products before styling your hair with hot tools, like curling irons or straighteners.

With these tips and tricks, you can achieve the perfect greasy hair look while still maintaining healthy locks. So, go ahead and embrace the oil!
