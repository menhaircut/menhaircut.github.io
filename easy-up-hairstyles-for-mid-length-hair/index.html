Easy Up Hairstyles For Mid Length Hair How To Create Them

Written by Robby Oct 07, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing your mid-length hair down all the time? Well, it’s time to try out some easy up hairstyles for mid-length hair that are not only effortless but also chic and stylish. Whether you’re going to work, a party, or just running errands, these hairstyles will add a touch of glamour to your look. Here are some simple and easy up hairstyles for mid-length hair that you can try out.

Description

Mid-length hair is versatile and suits most face shapes. It’s the perfect length for creating different hairstyles that are easy to manage and maintain. Up hairstyles for mid-length hair are perfect for any occasion, and they add a touch of elegance and sophistication to your look. These hairstyles are easy to create and require minimal effort, making them perfect for busy women.

How to

Creating easy up hairstyles for mid-length hair is simple and straightforward. All you need are some bobby pins, hair ties, and a comb. Here’s how to create some of the most popular up hairstyles for mid-length hair:

1. Messy Bun

To create a messy bun, tie your hair into a loose ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the ponytail, securing it with bobby pins. Pull out some strands of hair from the bun to create a messy look. This hairstyle is perfect for a casual day out or a lazy Sunday brunch.

2. Half-Up Half-Down

To create a half-up half-down hairstyle, take a section of hair from the crown of your head and tie it into a ponytail. Leave the rest of your hair down. You can add some loose curls to the bottom of your hair for a more voluminous look. This hairstyle is perfect for a date night or a girls’ night out.

3. French Twist

To create a French twist, tie your hair into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck. Twist the ponytail and roll it upwards, tucking in the ends of your hair as you go. Secure the twist with bobby pins, and pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look. This hairstyle is perfect for a formal event or a wedding.

Step by Step

Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to create an easy up hairstyle for mid-length hair:

1. High Ponytail

Brush your hair and tie it into a high ponytail at the crown of your head.

Use a hair tie to secure the ponytail in place.

Tease the top section of your ponytail to add volume.

Wrap a strand of hair around the hair tie to hide it and secure it with a bobby pin.

Use bobby pins to secure any loose strands of hair.

2. Braided Bun

Brush your hair and tie it into a low ponytail at the nape of your neck.

Braid the ponytail and secure the ends with a hair tie.

Wrap the braid around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.

Secure the bun with bobby pins, and pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

3. Side Bun

Brush your hair to one side and tie it into a low side ponytail.

Twist the ponytail and wrap it around the base of the hair tie to create a bun.

Secure the bun with bobby pins, and pull out some strands of hair to create a messy look.

Use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you create the perfect up hairstyle for mid-length hair:

  • Use bobby pins that match your hair color to hide them better.
  • Use hairspray to keep your hairstyle in place.
  • Add some hair accessories, such as a headband or a hair clip, to add some extra flair to your hairstyle.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about easy up hairstyles for mid-length hair:

1. What are the best up hairstyles for mid-length hair?

The best up hairstyles for mid-length hair are messy buns, half-up half-down hairstyles, French twists, and braided buns.

2. Can I create up hairstyles for mid-length hair with thin hair?

Yes, you can create up hairstyles for mid-length hair with thin hair. Use some volumizing products to add some volume to your hair before creating the hairstyle.

3. Can I create up hairstyles for mid-length hair with curly hair?

Yes, you can create up hairstyles for mid-length hair with curly hair. Use some hair gel or mousse to tame your curls before creating the hairstyle.

Hair Care

It’s essential to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Use a good quality shampoo and conditioner that suits your hair type. Avoid using hot tools on your hair too often, as they can damage your hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair gently, and avoid brushing your hair when it’s wet to prevent breakage. Get regular haircuts to get rid of split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

These easy up hairstyles for mid-length hair are perfect for any occasion, and they’re effortless to create. Try out different hairstyles to find the one that suits you the best. Don’t forget to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Happy styling!
