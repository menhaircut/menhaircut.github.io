Do Guards Fit All Clippers

Written by Frank Nov 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you wondering whether guards fit all clippers? If you are new to hair cutting or have never used clippers before, the question may seem perplexing. Clippers are a must-have tool for barbers, stylists, and even individuals who love to groom their hair or beard at home. Guards come as attachments to the clippers and are essential for achieving precise and consistent haircuts. In this article, we will answer the question "do guards fit all clippers?" and give you some tips on selecting the right guard for your clippers.

What are guards for clippers?

Guards are plastic attachments that fit onto the blades of hair clippers. They come in different sizes and shapes and are designed to help the user achieve a specific hair length. Guards are essential for achieving a consistent haircut, especially if you want to maintain a specific length of hair. The guard determines how much hair is cut, and it prevents the blades from cutting the hair too short.

Do guards fit all clippers?

The answer is no. Not all guards fit all clippers. Guards come in different sizes, and each size is designed to fit a specific brand or model of hair clippers. Using the wrong guard can result in an uneven haircut or even damage the blades of the clippers. Therefore, it's important to choose the right guard for your clippers.

How to choose the right guard for your clippers?

Choosing the right guard for your clippers is essential for achieving the desired haircut. Here are some tips to help you choose the right guard:

1. Check the brand and model of your clippers

Before buying a guard, check the brand and model of your clippers. Most guards are designed to fit specific brands or models, and using the wrong guard can damage the blades or give an uneven haircut.

2. Consider the length of hair you want to cut

Guards come in different sizes, and each size is designed to cut a specific length of hair. Consider the length of hair you want to cut and choose the guard size accordingly. For example, if you want to cut your hair to a length of 1/8 inch, choose a #1 guard.

3. Buy guards from the same brand as your clippers

Guards from the same brand as your clippers are more likely to fit than guards from a different brand. Therefore, if you have Wahl clippers, buy guards from the same brand.

How to attach guards to clippers?

Attaching guards to clippers is easy. Here are the steps:

1. Turn off the clippers

Before attaching the guard, turn off the clippers to prevent any accidents.

2. Remove the blade

Remove the blade from the clippers by pressing the release button or lever.

3. Attach the guard

Slide the guard onto the blade of the clippers. Make sure it clicks into place.

4. Reattach the blade

Reattach the blade by sliding it back onto the clippers until it clicks into place.

5. Turn on the clippers

Turn on the clippers and start cutting your hair.

FAQ

Can I use guards from a different brand on my clippers?

No, it's not recommended to use guards from a different brand on your clippers. Guards are designed to fit specific brands or models of clippers, and using the wrong guard can result in an uneven haircut or damage to the blades.

Can I use guards on my beard trimmer?

Most beard trimmers come with guards that are designed to fit the specific model. Using guards from a different brand or model can result in an uneven trim or damage to the blades.

Hair Care Tips

Using guards is essential for achieving a consistent and even haircut. However, it's also important to take care of your hair to prevent damage and promote healthy growth. Here are some tips:

1. Use a conditioner

Using a conditioner after shampooing can help keep your hair soft and healthy. It also prevents tangles and makes it easier to comb or style your hair.

2. Avoid using hot tools frequently

Using hot tools like blow dryers, flat irons, or curling irons frequently can damage your hair. Try to limit their use or use them at a lower temperature.

3. Trim your hair regularly

Trimming your hair regularly can help prevent split ends and promote healthy growth. It also helps maintain the shape of your haircut.

In conclusion, guards do not fit all clippers. It's important to choose the right guard for your clippers to achieve a consistent and even haircut. Consider the brand and model of your clippers, the length of hair you want to cut, and buy guards from the same brand as your clippers. Attaching guards to clippers is easy, but make sure to turn off the clippers before attaching the guard. Take care of your hair by using a conditioner, avoiding hot tools frequently, and trimming your hair regularly.
