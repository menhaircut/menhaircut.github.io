Does Long Hair Elongate The Face

Written by Bardi Sep 17, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long hair has always been associated with femininity, beauty, and elegance. But, if you have a round or square face, you might wonder if long hair elongates the face or makes it look even rounder. In this blog post, we will explore whether or not long hair elongates the face, how to style long hair to flatter your face shape, and some tips on how to take care of long hair.

Face Shape

Before we dive into whether long hair elongates the face, let's talk about face shapes. There are six main face shapes: round, oval, square, heart, diamond, and oblong. Knowing your face shape is crucial when choosing a hairstyle that will flatter your features.

If you have a round or square face shape, you might feel that long hair elongates your face. However, if you have an oval or oblong face shape, long hair can actually balance out your features and make your face look more proportionate.

How to Style Long Hair for Your Face Shape

If you have a round or square face shape and want to elongate your face, there are a few hairstyles that you can try:

  • Add layers to your hair to create movement and texture. This will draw attention away from the roundness of your face.
  • Try a side part instead of a middle part. This will create asymmetry and make your face look longer.
  • Opt for hairstyles that are longer in the front and shorter in the back, like an A-line bob or a long layered cut.

If you have an oval or oblong face shape and want to balance out your features, here are some hairstyles that you can try:

  • Keep your hair all one length to create the illusion of width.
  • Try a center part to create symmetry.
  • Opt for hairstyles with volume at the crown of your head to balance out your face.

Tips for Taking Care of Long Hair

Long hair requires more care than short hair. Here are some tips for taking care of your long locks:

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners and curling irons too often, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day, as this can strip your hair of its natural oils.

FAQ

Does long hair make your face look longer?

If you have an oval or oblong face shape, long hair can balance out your features and make your face look more proportionate. However, if you have a round or square face shape, you might feel that long hair elongates your face.

What hairstyles elongate the face?

To elongate a round or square face, try adding layers, opting for a side part, or choosing hairstyles that are longer in the front and shorter in the back. To balance out an oval or oblong face, keep your hair all one length, try a center part, or add volume at the crown of your head.

How do I take care of long hair?

Long hair requires more care than short hair. Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair, avoid using hot tools too often, use a deep conditioning treatment once a week, and avoid washing your hair every day.

Overall, whether or not long hair elongates the face depends on your face shape. By choosing a hairstyle that flatters your features and taking good care of your long locks, you can rock long hair with confidence!
