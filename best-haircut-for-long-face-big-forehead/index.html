Best Haircut For Long Face Big Forehead

Written by Robby Jul 12, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you have a long face with a big forehead, finding the right haircut can be a challenge. You want a hairstyle that will flatter your features and make you look your best. In this article, we will explore the best haircut for long face big forehead, as well as some tips and tricks for maintaining your new style.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

A long face with a big forehead can be a beautiful feature if you know how to style it right. The best haircut for this face shape is one that adds volume to the sides and top of the head, while keeping the length to a minimum. This will help to balance out your facial features and draw attention away from your forehead.

How To

The first step in finding the best haircut for your long face and big forehead is to consult with a professional hairstylist. They will be able to assess your face shape and recommend the best style for you. You should also consider your hair type and lifestyle when choosing a haircut.

Step By Step

Here are the steps to getting the best haircut for long face big forehead:

  1. Research different hairstyles that flatter your face shape and forehead size.
  2. Consult with a professional hairstylist to determine the best haircut for you.
  3. Show your stylist pictures of the hairstyle you want.
  4. Ask your stylist for tips on how to maintain your new haircut.
  5. Enjoy your new look!

Tips

Here are some tips for maintaining your new haircut:

  • Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add volume to your hair.
  • Blow dry your hair upside down to create volume at the roots.
  • Use a round brush to curl the ends of your hair under.
  • Avoid heavy bangs that cover your forehead.
  • Consider using hair accessories like headbands or clips to add interest to your hairstyle.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best haircut for long face big forehead:

What haircut should I avoid if I have a long face and big forehead?

Avoid hairstyles that are too long or too short. Also, avoid heavy bangs that cover your forehead.

What is the best haircut for a long face with a big forehead?

The best haircut is one that adds volume to the sides and top of the head, while keeping the length to a minimum.

Hair Care

Here are some tips for taking care of your hair:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy.
  • Avoid using too much heat styling tools, as they can damage your hair.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

In conclusion, finding the best haircut for long face big forehead is all about balance. You want a hairstyle that flatters your features and draws attention away from your forehead. With the right cut and some tips for maintaining your new style, you can look and feel your best.
