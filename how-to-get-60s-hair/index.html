How To Get 60S Hair A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 · 4 min read

The 60s were a time of radical change in fashion, music, and culture. One of the defining looks of the era was the big, bold, and beautiful hair. If you want to channel your inner Twiggy or Brigitte Bardot, then you need to know how to get 60s hair. In this guide, we’ll show you how to achieve the iconic look in a few simple steps.

Description

60s hair was all about volume, texture, and drama. It was a time when women embraced their natural curls and waves, and experimented with bold colors and styles. From the iconic beehive to the tousled bob, there was a hairstyle to suit every personality and occasion.

How to Get 60s Hair

If you want to get 60s hair, then you need to start with the right tools and products. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • Round brush
  • Hairspray
  • Teasing comb
  • Curling iron
  • Hair clips
  • Volumizing mousse

Once you have all your tools and products ready, follow these steps:

Step 1: Prep Your Hair

Start by washing your hair and applying a volumizing mousse. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair. Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots. Once your hair is dry, part it in the center.

Step 2: Tease Your Hair

Using a teasing comb, backcomb the crown of your head to create volume and height. Take small sections of hair and tease the roots by combing downwards towards your scalp. Use hairspray to hold the tease in place.

Step 3: Create Curls

Using a curling iron, create loose waves throughout your hair. Start at the mid-lengths and wrap small sections of hair around the iron, leaving the ends out. Hold for a few seconds and then release. Repeat this process throughout your hair until you have loose, tousled waves.

Step 4: Pin Your Hair

Take small sections of hair and twist them upwards towards the crown of your head. Use hair clips to secure the twists in place. This will create extra volume and texture at the crown of your head.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Once you’ve pinned your hair, use hairspray to hold everything in place. Spray a generous amount of hairspray all over your hair, focusing on the crown and ends. This will help to keep the volume and texture in place all day long.

Tips for Getting the Perfect 60s Hair

Getting 60s hair takes practice and patience. Here are some tips to help you get the perfect look:

  • Use a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots.
  • Backcomb the crown of your head to create extra height and drama.
  • Experiment with different curling techniques to create loose, tousled waves.
  • Use hair clips to create extra volume and texture at the crown of your head.
  • Finish with hairspray to hold everything in place.

FAQ

What kind of hair works best for 60s hair?

Any hair type can work for 60s hair, whether it’s straight, wavy, or curly. The key is to create volume and texture using the right tools and products.

How long does it take to get 60s hair?

Getting 60s hair can take anywhere from 30 minutes to an hour, depending on your hair length and thickness.

What products should I use for 60s hair?

Use a volumizing mousse, hairspray, and a teasing comb to create volume and texture. You can also use a curling iron to create loose, tousled waves.

Hair Care Tips

Getting 60s hair can be tough on your hair, so it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips for keeping your hair healthy:

  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools.
  • Avoid washing your hair every day to prevent dryness.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish your hair.
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends and breakage.

Now that you know how to get 60s hair, it’s time to put your skills to the test. Experiment with different styles and techniques to find the perfect look for you. Whether you’re going for a bold beehive or a tousled bob, remember to have fun and embrace your inner 60s icon.
