Why Am I Losing Hair At 18 Female

Written by Bardi Jul 10, 2023 Â· 3 min read

If you are an 18-year-old female and have noticed that you are losing hair, you may be wondering why this is happening. Hair loss can be a distressing experience, especially when it happens at such a young age. In this post, we will explore the various reasons why you may be losing hair at 18 and what you can do about it.

Description

Hair loss is a common problem that affects both men and women. However, it is more prevalent in men than women. Women may experience hair loss due to various reasons such as hormonal changes, genetics, stress, and medical conditions such as thyroid disorders, polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS), and autoimmune disorders.

How to

If you are experiencing hair loss, it is essential to identify the underlying cause. Here are some steps you can take:

Step 1: Consult a Doctor or Dermatologist

If you are losing hair, the first step is to consult a doctor or dermatologist. They will carry out a physical examination and ask you about your medical history to identify any underlying medical conditions that may be causing hair loss.

Step 2: Take Care of Your Hair

You can take simple steps to take care of your hair and prevent further hair loss:

  • Use a mild shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot styling tools such as hair straighteners, curling irons, and hair dryers
  • Avoid tight hairstyles such as braids, buns, and ponytails that can pull on your hair
  • Eat a healthy diet that is rich in vitamins and minerals that are essential for hair growth
  • Reduce stress through relaxation techniques such as meditation, yoga, and deep breathing exercises

Tips

Here are some tips to prevent hair loss:

  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair such as hair dyes and bleaches
  • Avoid smoking and excessive alcohol consumption
  • Get enough sleep
  • Avoid crash diets and maintain a healthy weight
  • Avoid over-styling your hair

FAQ

Q: Can stress cause hair loss?

A: Yes, stress can cause hair loss. When you are stressed, your body produces a hormone called cortisol, which can affect the hair growth cycle.

Q: Can hair loss be reversed?

A: Yes, hair loss can be reversed, depending on the underlying cause. For example, if the hair loss is due to a medical condition such as thyroid disorders or PCOS, treating the underlying condition can help reverse hair loss.

Hair Care

Taking care of your hair is essential to prevent hair loss. Here are some tips:

  • Use a wide-toothed comb to avoid pulling on your hair
  • Avoid using hot water when washing your hair
  • Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair
  • Use a hair mask once a week to nourish your hair

Conclusion

Losing hair at 18 can be a distressing experience, but there are steps you can take to prevent further hair loss and promote hair growth. By taking care of your hair, identifying the underlying cause of hair loss, and seeking medical treatment, you can restore your hair's health and beauty.
