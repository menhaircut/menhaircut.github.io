How To Style Thick Curly Hair Female Tips And Tricks

Are you struggling to find the perfect hairstyle for your thick curly hair? You're not alone. Many women with curly hair find it challenging to manage and style their locks. But don't worry, we've got you covered! In this article, we'll provide you with tips, tricks, and step-by-step instructions on how to style your thick curly hair female. From updos to braids, we'll cover it all. So, let's get started!

Hair Care

Before we dive into styling, it's essential to take care of your hair. Thick curly hair requires specific care to keep it healthy and manageable. Here are some hair care tips to keep in mind:

  • Wash your hair with sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping natural oils from your hair.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it moisturized.
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair when wet to avoid breakage.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and harsh weather conditions by wearing a hat or scarf.

How to Style Thick Curly Hair Female

Now that you know how to take care of your hair let's move on to styling. Here are some popular hairstyles for thick curly hair:

The Half-Up Half-Down

The half-up half-down hairstyle is perfect for any occasion. It's effortless to do and looks great on thick curly hair. Here's how:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from each side and pull it back to the crown of your head.
  3. Tie the sections together with a hair elastic.
  4. Fluff up the ponytail to create volume.

Braids

Braids are a great way to keep your hair neat and stylish. Here are a few types of braids you can try:

Crown Braid

The crown braid is a beautiful and elegant hairstyle that looks great on thick curly hair. Here's how:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a section of hair from one side and braid it from the front of your head to the back.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Take the two braids and cross them over the top of your head, securing them with bobby pins.

Fishtail Braid

The fishtail braid is a fun and trendy hairstyle that looks great on thick curly hair. Here's how:

  1. Start by parting your hair down the middle.
  2. Take a small section of hair from one side and cross it over to the other side.
  3. Repeat on the other side.
  4. Continue crossing small sections of hair over until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Tie the braid with a hair elastic.

Updos

Updos are perfect for formal occasions or when you want to keep your hair out of your face. Here are a few updos you can try:

Bun

The bun is a classic and chic hairstyle that looks great on thick curly hair. Here's how:

  1. Start by putting your hair in a high ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the base to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.

Messy Updo

The messy updo is a relaxed and effortless hairstyle that looks great on thick curly hair. Here's how:

  1. Start by putting your hair in a low ponytail.
  2. Twist the ponytail around the base to create a messy bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull out a few curls around your face to create a messy look.

Tips and Tricks

Here are some tips and tricks to keep in mind when styling your thick curly hair:

  • Use hair products specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Don't overwash your hair as it can dry out your curls.
  • Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair to avoid frizz.
  • Try sleeping with your hair in a pineapple to preserve your curls overnight.
  • Experiment with different hairstyles to find what works best for you.

FAQ

Q: How often should I wash my thick curly hair?

A: It's recommended to wash your hair once or twice a week to avoid drying out your curls.

Q: What hair products should I use for my thick curly hair?

A: Look for products specifically designed for curly hair, such as curl-defining creams, leave-in conditioners, and oils.

Q: How can I prevent frizz in my curly hair?

A: Use a diffuser when blow-drying your hair, avoid touching your hair too much, and use a silk or satin pillowcase to prevent friction.

Q: Can I straighten my thick curly hair?

A: You can, but it's recommended to avoid using heat styling tools as much as possible to prevent damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Styling your thick curly hair doesn't have to be a challenge. With the right hair care routine, products, and hairstyles, you can achieve beautiful and manageable curls. Try out different styles and see what works best for you. Remember, confidence is key!
