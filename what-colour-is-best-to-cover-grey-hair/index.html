What Colour Is Best To Cover Grey Hair

Written by Daniel Jul 15, 2023 · 4 min read

As we age, grey hair is inevitable. While some people embrace their natural grey hair, others prefer to cover it up with hair dye. But what colour is best to cover grey hair? In this article, we’ll discuss the best hair colour options for covering grey hair and provide some tips and tricks for maintaining your new hair colour.

Table of Contents

  • How to Cover Grey Hair
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Covering Grey Hair
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care Tips

How to Cover Grey Hair

There are a few different options for covering grey hair. The most popular options include:

  • Semi-permanent hair dye
  • Permanent hair dye
  • Henna hair dye

The best option for you will depend on a few factors, including your natural hair colour, the amount of grey hair you have, and how long you want your new hair colour to last. Semi-permanent hair dye is a great option for those who want a temporary change, while permanent hair dye is ideal for those who want a long-lasting solution.

Step-by-Step Guide

If you’ve decided to cover your grey hair with hair dye, follow these steps:

  1. Choose the right hair dye for your hair type and desired colour
  2. Do a patch test to make sure you’re not allergic to the hair dye
  3. Wash your hair with a clarifying shampoo to remove any build-up
  4. Towel dry your hair
  5. Section your hair into four parts and use a hair dye brush to apply the hair dye to each section, starting at the roots
  6. Leave the hair dye on for the recommended amount of time
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water
  8. Apply a conditioner to your hair and leave it on for a few minutes before rinsing it out

Tips for Covering Grey Hair

Here are some tips to help you achieve the best results when covering your grey hair:

  • Choose a hair dye that is specifically formulated for grey hair coverage
  • Don’t go too dark, as it can look unnatural and draw attention to your grey hair
  • Consider getting highlights or lowlights to break up the grey hair and add dimension
  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner to maintain your new hair colour
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently, as this can strip the colour

FAQs

Here are some frequently asked questions about covering grey hair:

Q: Can I use box dye to cover my grey hair?

A: Yes, you can use box dye to cover your grey hair. However, it’s important to choose a dye that is specifically formulated for grey hair coverage and to follow the instructions carefully.

Q: How often do I need to touch up my roots?

A: This will depend on how quickly your hair grows and how noticeable your roots are. Generally, you should touch up your roots every 4-6 weeks.

Q: Can I dye my hair at home or should I go to a salon?

A: You can dye your hair at home, but if you’re unsure or want a more dramatic change, it’s best to go to a salon and have a professional do it for you.

Hair Care Tips

After you’ve covered your grey hair with hair dye, it’s important to take care of your hair to maintain your new colour. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a colour-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid washing your hair too frequently
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated
  • Avoid using hot tools too frequently, as this can damage your hair and cause the colour to fade
  • Protect your hair from the sun by wearing a hat or using a UV-protectant spray

Now that you know what colour is best to cover grey hair, you can confidently choose a hair dye that will give you the results you want. Just remember to follow the instructions carefully and take care of your hair to maintain your new colour.
