How Do I Trim My Own Long Straight Hair

Written by Aprilia Nov 01, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Long straight hair is a classic look that never goes out of style. However, maintaining it can be a challenge, especially when it comes to trimming. Going to a salon can be expensive, and sometimes you just don't have the time. Knowing how to trim your own long straight hair is a valuable skill that can save you money and time. In this article, we'll go through a step-by-step guide on how to trim your own long straight hair.

Description

Trimming your own hair can be scary, but with the right tools and techniques, it can be done easily. Long straight hair is the easiest hair type to trim because it falls in a straight line, making it easier to cut. The key to trimming long straight hair is to take your time and cut slowly. It's better to cut off less than to cut off too much.

How to Trim Your Own Long Straight Hair

Before we begin, here are some things you'll need:

  • Sharp hair cutting scissors
  • A comb
  • A mirror
  • A hair tie or clip

Once you have everything you need, follow these steps:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing and drying your hair. It's important to trim your hair while it's dry because wet hair is longer and can result in uneven cutting.

Step 2: Part Your Hair

Using your comb, part your hair down the middle and brush it out. You can also part your hair on the side if that's your preferred style.

Step 3: Tie Your Hair

Tie the hair on one side of your head with a hair tie or clip. This will allow you to focus on one side of your head at a time.

Step 4: Start Trimming

Starting with one side of your head, take a small section of hair and comb it straight down. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers, and use your scissors to cut off any split ends or damaged hair. Cut slowly and carefully, taking off small sections at a time.

Repeat this process on the other side of your head until you've trimmed all of your hair.

Tips

  • Always cut your hair in a well-lit area.
  • Use sharp scissors to avoid damaging your hair.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Take breaks if you need to.

FAQ

Can I trim my own hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can. However, cutting curly hair can be more challenging than cutting straight hair. It's best to seek the advice of a professional stylist if you're unsure.

How often should I trim my hair?

It's recommended to trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and damage.

Hair Care

Trimming your hair is just one part of hair care. To keep your long straight hair looking healthy and beautiful, be sure to:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo.
  • Condition your hair after every wash.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often.
  • Use a heat protectant spray when using heat styling tools.
  • Avoid tight hairstyles that can pull on your hair.

By following these tips and techniques, you can trim your own long straight hair with confidence and ease.
