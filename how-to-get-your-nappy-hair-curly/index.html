How To Get Your Nappy Hair Curly A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Jul 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you have nappy hair, you may think that achieving curly hair is impossible. But with the right techniques and products, you can transform your hair into beautiful curls. In this blog post, we'll show you how to get your nappy hair curly in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language. From how to prep your hair to how to style it, we've got you covered. So, let's get started!

How to Prep Your Hair

Before you start styling your hair, it's important to prep it properly. Here are some tips:

1. Cleanse Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a sulfate-free shampoo. Sulfates can strip your hair of its natural oils, which can cause dryness and breakage. Follow up with a conditioner to add moisture back into your hair.

2. Detangle Your Hair

Use a wide-tooth comb to detangle your hair. Start from the ends and work your way up to the roots. Be gentle to avoid causing breakage.

3. Apply a Leave-In Conditioner

Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair to add moisture and prevent frizz. Be sure to distribute the product evenly throughout your hair.

How to Style Your Hair

Now that your hair is prepped, it's time to style it. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Curl-Enhancing Product

Choose a curl-enhancing product that works well for your hair type. Apply the product to your hair in sections, starting from the bottom and working your way up.

2. Section Your Hair

Section your hair into small sections using hair clips. This will make it easier to style your hair and ensure that every strand is coated with product.

3. Use a Diffuser

Attach a diffuser to your hair dryer and set it to low heat. Use the diffuser to dry your hair, starting at the roots and working your way to the ends. This will help to define your curls and prevent frizz.

4. Scrunch Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use your hands to scrunch your hair upwards. This will help to define your curls and give them more volume.

Tips for Maintaining Your Curly Hair

Now that you've achieved curly hair, it's important to maintain it. Here are some tips:

1. Sleep on a Silk Pillowcase

Sleeping on a silk pillowcase can help to prevent frizz and breakage. The smooth surface of the silk pillowcase will also help to preserve your curls.

2. Refresh Your Curls

If your curls start to look flat, refresh them by applying a curl-enhancing product and scrunching your hair upwards.

3. Protect Your Hair from Heat

Avoid using heat styling tools too often, as they can cause damage to your hair. If you do use heat, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to minimize damage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I achieve curly hair with nappy hair?

A: Yes, you can achieve curly hair with nappy hair. It just requires the right techniques and products.

Q: What products should I use to achieve curly hair?

A: Choose a curl-enhancing product that works well for your hair type. Look for products that contain ingredients like shea butter, coconut oil, and argan oil.

Q: How often should I wash my curly hair?

A: It depends on your hair type and how much product you use. Generally, you should wash your hair every 2-3 days to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.

Hair Care for Nappy Hair

It's important to take care of your nappy hair to keep it healthy and strong. Here are some tips:

1. Moisturize Your Hair

Nappy hair tends to be dry, so it's important to moisturize it regularly. Use a leave-in conditioner or hair oil to add moisture back into your hair.

2. Avoid Harsh Chemicals

Avoid using harsh chemicals on your hair, as they can cause damage and breakage. Instead, use natural products that are gentle on your hair.

3. Protect Your Hair

Protect your hair from the sun, wind, and harsh weather conditions. Wear a hat or scarf to cover your hair and prevent damage.

With these tips, you can achieve beautiful, curly hair with nappy hair. Remember to take care of your hair to keep it healthy and strong. Happy styling!
