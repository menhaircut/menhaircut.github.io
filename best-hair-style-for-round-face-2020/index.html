Best Hair Style For Round Face 2020 A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Nov 24, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of trying different hairstyles that just don't seem to work for your round face? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this guide, we will discuss the best hair style for round face 2020 and give you tips on how to achieve the perfect look.

Description

A round face is characterized by full cheeks and a rounded chin. The goal is to create a hairstyle that will elongate the face and make it appear slimmer. A good hair style for round face 2020 should draw attention to the top of the head and add volume to the crown area.

How to

When choosing the best hair style for round face 2020, it's important to consider your hair type and texture. Fine hair may require a different style than thick hair. Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect look:

1. Embrace Layers

Layered haircuts are great for round faces because they add volume and movement to the hair. Layers that start at the jawline or cheekbone can help elongate the face. Avoid layers that start at the chin as they can make the face appear wider.

2. Side Swept Bangs

Side swept bangs can help balance out the width of the face. They also draw attention to the eyes and cheekbones.

3. Long Bob

A long bob, also known as a lob, is a great hair style for round face 2020. It's a versatile cut that can be styled straight or wavy. The length should fall just above the shoulders to elongate the face.

4. Pixie Cut

A pixie cut can be a bold choice for a round face, but it can also be very flattering. The key is to add volume to the crown area and keep the sides short. This will elongate the face and create a more balanced look.

5. High Ponytail

A high ponytail can create the illusion of a longer face. To achieve this look, tease the crown area of the hair and pull it back into a ponytail. Leave some hair out around the face to soften the look.

Step by Step

Here's how to achieve the perfect hair style for round face 2020:

1. Choose Your Cut

Decide on a cut that will elongate your face and add volume to the crown area. Consult with your stylist if you're not sure what will work best for you.

2. Style Your Hair

Use a round brush and blow dryer to add volume to the crown area. Create loose waves with a curling iron or straighten your hair with a flat iron.

3. Add Accessories

Accessories can help draw attention away from the roundness of your face. Try wearing a headband, scarf, or statement earrings.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help you achieve the perfect hair style for round face 2020:

1. Avoid Center Parts

Center parts can make a round face appear wider. Stick to side parts instead.

2. Experiment with Color

Adding highlights or lowlights can create dimension and make the face appear slimmer.

3. Embrace Your Natural Texture

Working with your natural texture can create a unique and flattering look.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the best hair style for round face 2020:

What is the best haircut for a round face?

A layered haircut that starts at the jawline or cheekbone is a great option for a round face.

What hairstyles should round faces avoid?

Round faces should avoid hairstyles that add width to the face, such as blunt bangs or bob cuts that end at the chin.

Can I wear a center part with a round face?

A center part can make a round face appear wider. Stick to side parts instead.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining a healthy and beautiful head of hair. Here are some tips:

1. Use a Heat Protectant

When using heat styling tools, always use a heat protectant to prevent damage to your hair.

2. Wash Your Hair Regularly

Wash your hair every 2-3 days to keep it clean and healthy.

3. Use a Deep Conditioner

Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

Conclusion

Choosing the best hair style for round face 2020 is all about creating balance and elongating the face. Embrace layers, side swept bangs, and a long bob to achieve the perfect look. Avoid center parts and blunt cuts that end at the chin. With these tips and tricks, you'll be sure to turn heads with your new hairstyle.
