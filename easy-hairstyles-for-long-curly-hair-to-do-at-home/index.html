Easy Hairstyles For Long Curly Hair To Do At Home

Written by Aprilia Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Long curly hair can be gorgeous, but it can also be a hassle to style. If you're looking for easy hairstyles for long curly hair to do at home, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll go over some simple styles that you can do yourself without having to go to the salon. Whether you're looking for a casual look or something more formal, we've got you covered.

Table of Contents

  1. Description
  2. How To
  3. Step By Step
  4. Tips
  5. FAQ
  6. Hair Care

Description

Long curly hair requires a bit of extra care and attention, but it's also incredibly versatile. With the right styling techniques, you can create a variety of looks that range from casual to formal. In this article, we'll provide step-by-step instructions for a few easy hairstyles that you can do at home. Whether you're heading to work or getting ready for a night out, these styles will help you look your best.

How To

The first step in creating easy hairstyles for long curly hair is to make sure that your hair is clean and well-conditioned. Use a shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair, and avoid using products that contain harsh chemicals or sulfates. Once your hair is clean, you can begin styling it.

Tools You'll Need

Here are the tools you'll need to create easy hairstyles for long curly hair:

  • Wide-tooth comb
  • Bobby pins
  • Elastic bands
  • Hair spray

Step By Step

1. Half-Up Half-Down

This is a simple and classic style that works well for both casual and formal occasions.

  1. Start by separating the top half of your hair from the bottom half.
  2. Pull the top section up and secure it with an elastic band.
  3. Take a small section of hair from the ponytail and wrap it around the elastic band to hide it.
  4. Pin the section of hair in place with bobby pins.
  5. Use a wide-tooth comb to gently tease the bottom half of your hair to create volume.
  6. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

2. Side Braid

This is a great style for when you want to keep your hair out of your face.

  1. Start by parting your hair to one side.
  2. Gather all of your hair to the side opposite your part.
  3. Begin braiding your hair, starting at the nape of your neck.
  4. Continue braiding until you reach the end of your hair.
  5. Secure the end of the braid with an elastic band.
  6. Loosen the braid by gently pulling on the sections to create a more relaxed look.
  7. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

3. Messy Bun

This is a fun and easy style that works well for casual occasions.

  1. Start by pulling your hair back into a ponytail.
  2. Twist your hair around the base of the ponytail to create a bun.
  3. Secure the bun with bobby pins.
  4. Pull a few strands of hair out around your face to create a messy, relaxed look.
  5. Finish with a light mist of hairspray to hold the style in place.

Tips

  • Use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair, starting at the ends and working your way up.
  • Apply a leave-in conditioner to your hair before styling to keep it moisturized and protected.
  • If you're having trouble getting your hair to stay in place, try using bobby pins that match the color of your hair to hide them.
  • Experiment with different styles to find what works best for your hair type and face shape.

FAQ

Q: Can I create these styles if my hair is really curly?

A: Yes! These styles can work for all types of curly hair, from loose waves to tight coils. Just make sure to adjust the style to suit your hair type and texture.

Q: How can I keep my curls looking defined and frizz-free?

A: Use a curl-defining cream or mousse to help enhance your natural curls. Avoid using too much heat on your hair, as this can cause damage and lead to frizz.

Hair Care

To keep your long curly hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that are specifically designed for curly hair.
  • Avoid using heat on your hair as much as possible, as this can cause damage and breakage.
  • Apply a deep conditioning treatment once a week to help keep your hair moisturized and healthy.
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends and breakage.
  • Avoid brushing your hair when it's dry, as this can cause frizz and breakage. Instead, use a wide-tooth comb to gently detangle your hair when it's wet.

With these easy hairstyles for long curly hair to do at home and hair care tips, you'll be able to keep your curls looking their best. Experiment with different styles and have fun with your hair!
