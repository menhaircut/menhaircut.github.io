What Colours Suit Me Test A Guide To Finding Your Perfect Colours

Written by Frank Dec 02, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're someone who struggles with finding the right colours to wear, you're not alone. It can be overwhelming to try and figure out what colours complement your skin tone, hair, and eyes. That's where the "what colours suit me test" comes in. In this article, we'll go over everything you need to know about taking the test, including how to, step-by-step instructions, tips, FAQs and even hair care tips to make sure you look your best. Let's get started!

Description

The "what colours suit me test" is a simple way to determine which colours look best on you based on your skin tone, hair and eye colour. By knowing which colours complement your natural features, you can choose clothing and makeup that make you look your best. It's a great tool to use when shopping for new clothes or trying out new makeup looks.

How to Take the Test

To take the "what colours suit me test," you'll need to find a well-lit area and gather some items in different colours. Here's how to do it:

Step 1: Determine Your Skin Tone

The first step is to determine your skin tone. There are three main types: cool, warm and neutral. To figure out which one you have, look at the veins on the inside of your wrist. If they appear blue, you have a cool undertone. If they appear green, you have a warm undertone. If you can't tell, you likely have a neutral undertone.

Step 2: Gather Items in Different Colours

Next, gather items in different colours. You'll want to have a range of colours, including warm and cool tones. For example, you could gather a red shirt, a blue shirt, a green shirt and a yellow shirt.

Step 3: Try on the Items

Try on each item and look at yourself in the mirror. Take note of how each colour makes your skin tone, hair and eyes look. Do some colours make you look washed out? Do others make your eyes pop?

Step 4: Determine Which Colours Suit You Best

Based on how each colour looks on you, determine which colours suit you best. You'll want to choose colours that complement your natural features and make you look your best.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when taking the "what colours suit me test:"

  • Look for colours that complement your eye colour
  • Consider your hair colour when choosing colours to wear
  • Don't be afraid to try new colours
  • Consider the occasion when choosing colours to wear
  • Take into account your personal style and preferences

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about the "what colours suit me test:"

What if I have multiple skin tones?

If you have multiple skin tones, you'll want to choose colours that complement both tones. For example, if you have a warm undertone on your face and a cool undertone on your neck, try to find colours that work well with both tones.

Can I take the test on my own?

Yes, you can take the test on your own. Just make sure you have good lighting and a range of colours to choose from.

Do I have to wear the colours?

No, you don't have to wear the colours. You can simply hold them up to your face and see how they make you look.

Hair Care

Now that you know which colours suit you best, it's important to take care of your hair to make sure it looks its best. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy
  • Use a deep conditioner once a week to keep your hair moisturized
  • Avoid using too much heat on your hair
  • Get regular trims to prevent split ends
  • Use a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair smooth and tangle-free

With these tips, you'll be sure to look your best in your favourite colours. Remember, the "what colours suit me test" is just a guide – ultimately, the colours you wear should make you feel confident and comfortable.
