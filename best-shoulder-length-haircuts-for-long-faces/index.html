Best Shoulder Length Haircuts For Long Faces

Written by William Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a new haircut that flatters your long face shape? If so, shoulder length haircuts can be a great option for you. Not only do they frame your face beautifully, but they also offer versatility and low maintenance. In this article, we will share with you the best shoulder length haircuts for long faces, along with tips on how to style and care for your hair.

  • How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Haircut for Long Faces
  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Shoulder Length Haircut

How to Choose the Right Shoulder Length Haircut for Long Faces

The key to choosing the right shoulder length haircut for your long face is to find a style that balances out your facial features. Here are some tips to help you choose the right haircut:

  • Look for haircuts that add volume to the sides of your face, such as layers and waves.
  • Avoid haircuts that are too blunt or straight, as they can elongate your face even more.
  • Consider getting bangs to help shorten the appearance of your face.
  • Consult with your hairstylist to find a haircut that complements your face shape and hair texture.

Some of the best shoulder length haircuts for long faces include:

Long Bob

The long bob, or "lob," is a popular shoulder length haircut that flatters long faces. It hits just above the shoulders and can be styled with layers and waves to add volume to the sides of your face.

Layered Shoulder Length Haircut

A layered shoulder length haircut can also be a great option for long faces. Layers can add movement and texture to your hair, while also helping to balance out your facial features.

Side-Swept Bangs

If you have a long forehead, side-swept bangs can be a great way to shorten the appearance of your face. They can be paired with any of the above haircuts for a flattering look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Your Shoulder Length Haircut

Once you've chosen the right shoulder length haircut for your long face, it's time to style it. Here's a step-by-step guide to styling your hair:

  1. Start with clean, towel-dried hair.
  2. Apply a volumizing mousse or spray to your roots to add lift and volume.
  3. Blow dry your hair using a round brush, pulling the hair away from your face to add volume to the sides.
  4. If you have bangs, use a flat iron to style them to the side.
  5. Use a curling iron or wand to add loose waves to your hair.
  6. Finish with a light hairspray to hold your style in place.

Tips for Maintaining Your Shoulder Length Haircut

To keep your shoulder length haircut looking its best, follow these tips:

  • Get regular trims to keep your hair healthy and prevent split ends.
  • Use a heat protectant spray before using hot tools on your hair.
  • Wash your hair every other day to prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair moisturized and healthy.

FAQs about Shoulder Length Haircuts for Long Faces

Can I still wear my hair straight with a shoulder length haircut for a long face?

Yes, you can still wear your hair straight with a shoulder length haircut for a long face. Just be sure to add some volume to the sides of your face to balance out your features.

What type of bangs are best for a long face?

Side-swept bangs are typically the best type of bangs for a long face. They can help shorten the appearance of your face while also framing your features.

How often should I wash my hair with a shoulder length haircut?

It's best to wash your hair every other day with a shoulder length haircut. This will help prevent it from becoming too oily or dry.

Hair Care Tips for Shoulder Length Haircuts

To keep your shoulder length haircut looking healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to prevent stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair every day to prevent damage.
  • Eat a healthy diet rich in vitamins and minerals to promote healthy hair growth.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and chlorine by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner.

With these tips and the right shoulder length haircut, you can flatter your long face shape and feel confident in your hairstyle.
