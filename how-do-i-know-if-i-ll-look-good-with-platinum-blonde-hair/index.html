How Do I Know If I ll Look Good With Platinum Blonde Hair

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you contemplating a drastic hair color change and wondering whether platinum blonde is the way to go? If so, you're not alone! Platinum blonde hair is a bold and daring color choice that can completely transform your look. However, before taking the plunge, it's important to consider whether the shade will suit your skin tone, facial features, and personal style. In this article, we'll look at some of the key factors to consider when deciding if platinum blonde hair is right for you.

Description

Platinum blonde hair is a light, almost white shade of blonde that is achieved by bleaching the hair to remove its natural pigments. It's a high-maintenance color that requires regular touch-ups to maintain its brightness and prevent brassiness.

How to

So, how can you tell if platinum blonde hair will suit you? Here are some things to consider:

Skin Tone

Platinum blonde hair tends to suit those with cool or neutral skin tones, as the icy hue can clash with warm undertones. Look at the veins on your wrist - if they appear blue or purple, you likely have cool undertones. If they appear green, you likely have warm undertones. If you can't decide, you may have a neutral undertone that can work with either warm or cool shades.

Facial Features

Platinum blonde hair can be a striking contrast against darker eyebrows, eyelashes, and facial hair. If you have naturally light features, this may not be an issue, but if you have a lot of contrast, you may want to consider whether you like the look. Additionally, platinum blonde hair can wash out very fair skin tones, so if you have a very pale complexion, you may want to opt for a warmer blonde shade instead.

Personal Style

Finally, think about your personal style and whether platinum blonde hair would complement your wardrobe and makeup choices. If you're a fan of bold, statement looks, platinum blonde hair could be the perfect accessory. However, if you prefer a more understated style, a subtler blonde shade may be more suitable.

Step by Step

Still unsure whether platinum blonde hair is right for you? Here's a step-by-step guide to help you make the decision:

  1. Determine your skin tone using the vein test.
  2. Consider your facial features and whether the contrast with platinum blonde hair would suit you.
  3. Think about your personal style and whether platinum blonde hair would complement your wardrobe and makeup choices.
  4. Try a temporary hair color or wig to see how you feel about the shade before committing to a permanent change.
  5. Consult with a professional hairstylist to get their opinion and advice on the best shade of blonde for you.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind when considering platinum blonde hair:

  • Choose a high-quality bleach and toner to avoid damage to your hair.
  • Invest in a good purple shampoo to keep your blonde hair looking bright and prevent brassiness.
  • Consider getting a haircut or style change to complement your new hair color.
  • Be prepared for the maintenance that comes with platinum blonde hair, including regular touch-ups and deep conditioning treatments.

FAQ

Still have questions about platinum blonde hair? Here are some frequently asked questions:

Is platinum blonde hair suitable for all skin tones?

Platinum blonde hair tends to suit those with cool or neutral skin tones, but it can be a striking contrast against darker skin tones as well.

Can I achieve platinum blonde hair at home?

We recommend consulting with a professional hairstylist to achieve the best and safest results. Bleaching hair can be a tricky and potentially damaging process if not done correctly.

How often do I need to touch up my platinum blonde hair?

Platinum blonde hair requires regular touch-ups every 4-6 weeks to maintain its brightness and prevent roots from showing.

Hair Care

To keep your platinum blonde hair looking its best, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some hair care tips:

  • Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of its natural oils.
  • Avoid excessive heat styling and use a heat protectant spray when you do.
  • Deep condition your hair once a week to keep it healthy and moisturized.
  • Avoid chlorine and saltwater, which can cause your hair to become brittle and discolored.

By following these tips, you can enjoy your new platinum blonde hair while keeping it healthy and vibrant.

In conclusion, deciding whether platinum blonde hair is right for you requires careful consideration of your skin tone, facial features, and personal style. By following the steps and tips outlined in this article, you can make an informed decision and enjoy your new look with confidence.
