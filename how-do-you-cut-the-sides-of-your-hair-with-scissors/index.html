How Do You Cut The Sides Of Your Hair With Scissors

Written by Bardi Jul 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the salon or barber every few weeks to trim the sides of your hair? Do you want to learn how to do it yourself with scissors at home? Then you've come to the right place! In this article, we will provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to cut the sides of your hair with scissors.

Description

Cutting the sides of your hair with scissors is a great way to save money and time. It's also a useful skill to have in case of emergencies, such as when you can't get an appointment with your stylist or barber. The process involves using a pair of sharp scissors to trim the hair around the sides of your head, leaving it looking neat and tidy.

How to Cut the Sides of Your Hair with Scissors

Step 1: Gather Your Tools

The first step is to gather all the necessary tools. You will need a pair of sharp scissors, a comb, and a mirror. Make sure that the scissors are clean and sharp so that they can cut through the hair easily. You can also use hair clips to section off the hair you want to cut.

Step 2: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Before you begin cutting your hair, it's important to wash and dry it. This will make it easier to comb and cut. Make sure that your hair is completely dry before you start cutting it.

Step 3: Comb Your Hair

Use a comb to part your hair down the middle, from the top of your head to the nape of your neck. This will help you to see where your hair is longer or shorter on either side, and where you need to trim it.

Step 4: Start Cutting

Hold a section of your hair between your fingers, and use the scissors to trim it to the desired length. You can start with longer lengths and gradually work your way up to shorter ones. Make sure that you cut the hair evenly on both sides of your head, and that you don't cut too much at once.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

After you have finished cutting one section of your hair, use a mirror to check your progress. Make sure that the sides of your hair are even and that you haven't missed any spots. If you need to make any adjustments, do so before moving on to the next section.

Step 6: Repeat the Process

Repeat the process on the other side of your head, cutting the hair to the same length as the first side. Make sure that you are cutting the hair evenly on both sides, and that you are trimming it to the desired length.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you cut the sides of your hair with scissors:

  • Start with longer lengths and work your way up to shorter ones.
  • Don't cut too much hair at once.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress and make any necessary adjustments.
  • Take your time and be patient.

FAQ

1. How often should I cut the sides of my hair?

It depends on how fast your hair grows and how short you want to keep it. Generally, it's recommended to trim the sides of your hair every 2-4 weeks.

2. Can I use regular scissors to cut my hair?

No, it's not recommended to use regular scissors to cut your hair. You should use hair-cutting scissors that are sharp and designed for cutting hair.

3. Can I cut the sides of my hair if it's curly?

Yes, you can cut the sides of your hair if it's curly. However, you may need to be more careful and take your time to ensure that you are cutting it evenly.

Hair Care

After you have finished cutting the sides of your hair with scissors, it's important to take care of your hair. You should wash it regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner, and avoid using too much heat on it. You can also use hair oil or serum to keep it soft and shiny.

Now that you know how to cut the sides of your hair with scissors, you can save money and time by doing it yourself at home. Just remember to be patient, take your time, and follow the steps carefully. Happy cutting!
