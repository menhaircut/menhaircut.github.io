Short Clean Sides Haircut The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Jun 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a stylish and low-maintenance haircut, look no further than the short clean sides haircut. This versatile style is perfect for men of all ages and hair types, and it's easy to maintain with regular trims and a few simple hair care tips. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the short clean sides haircut, including how to get it, how to style it, and how to care for it. Let's get started!

Description

The short clean sides haircut is a classic style that features short hair on the sides and back of the head, with slightly longer hair on top. This style can be worn with a variety of textures, from straight to wavy to curly, and it's easy to customize with different lengths and fades. The key to this style is keeping the sides and back short and clean, while leaving enough length on top to create volume and texture.

How to Get a Short Clean Sides Haircut

To get a short clean sides haircut, you'll need to visit a professional barber or hairstylist who is experienced with men's haircuts. Here's what to expect during your appointment:

Step 1: Consultation

Your barber or hairstylist will start by consulting with you about your hair type, face shape, and personal style. They may ask you questions about your daily hair care routine, your preferred styling products, and any specific concerns you have about your hair.

Step 2: Cutting

Once you've agreed on a style, your barber or hairstylist will begin cutting your hair. They'll typically start by cutting the sides and back with clippers, using a shorter guard to create a clean, tapered look. They may also use scissors to blend the hair at the top of the head.

Step 3: Styling

After your haircut is complete, your barber or hairstylist may apply a styling product to your hair, such as a pomade or wax, to create texture and hold. They may also use a blow dryer to add volume and shape to your hair.

Tips for Styling a Short Clean Sides Haircut

Once you've got your short clean sides haircut, it's important to know how to style it to keep it looking sharp. Here are a few tips:

  • Use a high-quality styling product that works well with your hair type and texture.
  • Experiment with different products to find the one that gives you the best hold and texture.
  • Blow dry your hair in the direction you want it to go to create volume and shape.
  • Use a comb or your fingers to create texture and separation.
  • Don't be afraid to try new styles and techniques to keep your haircut looking fresh.

FAQ

Q: How often should I get a short clean sides haircut?

A: It's recommended to get a haircut every 4-6 weeks to maintain the shape and cleanliness of the style.

Q: Can I style a short clean sides haircut with a beard?

A: Absolutely! A short clean sides haircut pairs well with a neat, trimmed beard or goatee.

Q: Is a short clean sides haircut suitable for all face shapes?

A: Yes, this style can be tailored to suit any face shape or hair type.

Hair Care Tips for a Short Clean Sides Haircut

To keep your short clean sides haircut looking sharp and healthy, follow these hair care tips:

  • Wash your hair regularly with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using hot tools like straighteners or curling irons, as they can damage your hair.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using too much styling product, as it can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Protect your hair from the sun and environmental pollutants by wearing a hat or using a leave-in conditioner with SPF.

With these tips and tricks, you'll be able to rock a stylish and low-maintenance short clean sides haircut with confidence. Whether you're heading to the office or hitting the town, this classic style is sure to turn heads and make you feel your best.
