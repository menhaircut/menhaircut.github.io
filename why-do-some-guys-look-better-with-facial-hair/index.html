Why Do Some Guys Look Better With Facial Hair

Written by Petter Sep 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Facial hair has become a popular trend in recent years, and it’s not just limited to the hipster crowd. Many men are now sporting facial hair, and it’s not just for the rugged look. Some simply look better with it. In this article, we’ll explore why this is the case and provide some tips on how to care for your facial hair.

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step By Step
  • Tips
  • FAQ
  • Hair Care

Description

Facial hair can make a man look more mature, confident, and attractive. It can also enhance certain facial features such as the jawline and cheekbones. Some men have a naturally strong jawline, and a beard can help accentuate this feature. Other men may have a rounder face, and a beard can help give the appearance of a more defined jawline.

Facial hair can also help cover up blemishes or scars on the face. It can also help distract from a receding hairline or bald spot. In some cases, a beard can even make a man look younger. This is because it can help hide wrinkles and other signs of aging.

How To

If you’re interested in growing facial hair, there are a few things you need to keep in mind. First, it’s important to have realistic expectations. Not all men can grow a full beard or mustache. Some men may only be able to grow patchy or thin facial hair.

Second, it’s important to choose a style that complements your face shape. For example, if you have a round face, a beard that is shorter on the sides and longer on the chin can help elongate your face. If you have a square face, a shorter beard that is more rounded on the bottom can help soften your features.

Third, it’s important to keep your facial hair well-groomed. This means trimming it regularly and using beard oil or balm to keep it soft and hydrated. You may also want to invest in a good beard comb or brush to help shape your beard and keep it looking neat.

Step By Step

If you’re ready to grow facial hair, here are some steps to follow:

  1. Stop shaving and allow your facial hair to grow for at least four weeks.
  2. Choose a style that complements your face shape.
  3. Trim regularly to keep your facial hair neat and well-groomed.
  4. Use beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and hydrated.
  5. Invest in a good beard comb or brush to help shape your beard.

Tips

Here are some additional tips to keep in mind:

  • Be patient. It can take several weeks or even months for your facial hair to grow in fully.
  • Experiment with different styles to find the one that works best for you.
  • Consider consulting with a barber or stylist for advice on grooming and styling your facial hair.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about facial hair:

Q: Does facial hair make a man look more attractive?

A: It depends on the man and the style of facial hair. Some men look better with facial hair, while others do not. It’s a matter of personal preference.

Q: Can facial hair help cover up acne or scars?

A: Yes, facial hair can help cover up blemishes and scars on the face.

Q: Will shaving make facial hair grow back thicker?

A: No, this is a myth. Shaving does not affect the thickness or growth rate of facial hair.

Hair Care

Just like the hair on your head, your facial hair needs to be cared for properly. Here are some tips:

  • Wash your facial hair regularly with a mild shampoo.
  • Use beard oil or balm to keep your facial hair soft and hydrated.
  • Trim your facial hair regularly to keep it neat and well-groomed.
  • Invest in a good beard comb or brush to help shape your beard.

By following these tips, you can ensure that your facial hair looks its best and complements your overall appearance.
