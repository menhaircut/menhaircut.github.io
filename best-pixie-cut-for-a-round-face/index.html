Best Pixie Cut For A Round Face The Ultimate Guide

Are you thinking about getting a pixie cut but worried that it might not suit your round face shape? Fear not! In this ultimate guide, we'll show you the best pixie cut for a round face, how to get it, and how to take care of it. Let's dive in!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Get the Best Pixie Cut for a Round Face
  • Step by Step Guide
  • Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut
  • FAQs

Description

A pixie cut is a short, chic hairstyle that's perfect for those who want a low-maintenance look. However, if you have a round face shape, you might be hesitant to try this style. The good news is that there are many different types of pixie cuts that can work with a round face shape. By choosing the right cut and styling it in the right way, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look.

How to Get the Best Pixie Cut for a Round Face

The first step to getting the best pixie cut for a round face is to choose the right length. A short pixie cut that's cropped close to the head can make a round face shape appear even rounder. Instead, opt for a pixie cut that's longer on top and shorter on the sides to create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face. You can also add layers to your pixie cut to create volume and movement.

Another important factor to consider when getting a pixie cut for a round face is the shape of the cut. A rounded cut can accentuate the roundness of your face, so it's best to choose a cut with angular lines to create a more structured look. A side-swept fringe can also help to balance out the proportions of your face.

Best Pixie Cut Styles for a Round Face

Here are some of the best pixie cut styles for a round face:

  • Long pixie cut with side-swept fringe
  • Asymmetrical pixie cut with side parting
  • Textured pixie cut with choppy layers
  • Pixie cut with longer layers on top
  • Pixie cut with a deep side parting

Step by Step Guide

Here's how to get the best pixie cut for a round face:

  1. Choose a length and style that flatters your face shape.
  2. Show your hairstylist a picture of the style you want.
  3. Ask your hairstylist to add layers to create volume and movement.
  4. Ask your hairstylist to cut your hair at an angle to create a more structured look.
  5. Ask your hairstylist to create a side-swept fringe to balance out your face shape.
  6. Ask your hairstylist to show you how to style your pixie cut at home.

Tips for Maintaining Your Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for maintaining your pixie cut:

  • Visit your hairstylist every 4-6 weeks for a trim to keep your cut looking fresh.
  • Use a styling product, such as a texturizing spray or pomade, to add volume and texture to your hair.
  • Experiment with different styling techniques, such as using a round brush to create volume or using a flat iron to create a sleek look.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before using hot styling tools.

FAQs

Will a pixie cut make my round face look even rounder?

A short, cropped pixie cut can make a round face shape appear even rounder. However, by choosing a longer pixie cut with layers and angular lines, you can create the illusion of a longer, more oval-shaped face.

What are the best pixie cuts for a round face?

The best pixie cuts for a round face are those that are longer on top and shorter on the sides, with layers and angular lines. Side-swept fringes can also help to balance out the proportions of your face.

Hair Care for Your Pixie Cut

Here are some tips for taking care of your pixie cut:

  • Wash your hair every 2-3 days with a gentle shampoo and conditioner.
  • Avoid using too much product, as this can weigh down your hair and make it look greasy.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to keep your hair healthy and hydrated.
  • Avoid using hot styling tools every day, as this can damage your hair. Instead, try air-drying your hair or using a diffuser on a low heat setting.

With the right pixie cut and styling techniques, you can achieve a flattering and stylish look that suits your round face shape. Follow these tips and tricks to maintain your pixie cut and keep it looking fresh and fabulous!
