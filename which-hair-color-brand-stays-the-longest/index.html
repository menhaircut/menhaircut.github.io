Which Hair Color Brand Stays The Longest

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're someone who loves to experiment with hair color, you'll know how important it is to find a brand that stays put for a long time. There's nothing worse than spending hours coloring your hair, only to have it fade away in a few washes. In this blog post, we'll be discussing which hair color brand stays the longest, and providing you with tips to help make your color last.

Table of Content

  • Description
  • How To
  • Step by Step
  • Tips

Description

After researching and testing various hair color brands, we've found that the brand that stays the longest is Schwarzkopf. This brand offers a range of colors and shades that are both vibrant and long-lasting. Schwarzkopf's hair color products are infused with keratin, which helps to strengthen and protect the hair while coloring it.

Benefits of Schwarzkopf Hair Color

  • Long-lasting color
  • Infused with keratin for hair health
  • Range of vibrant shades
  • Easy to apply and use

How to

When using Schwarzkopf hair color, it's important to follow the instructions on the packaging carefully. Here are some general tips to help make your color last:

Preparation

  • Wash your hair one day before coloring to remove any product buildup or oils
  • Do a patch test before applying the color to your hair to ensure you're not allergic to any of the ingredients

Application

  • Wear gloves to protect your hands from the color
  • Apply the color to dry hair, starting from the roots and working your way to the ends
  • Leave the color on for the recommended time
  • Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water to help seal the color

Step by Step

Here is a step-by-step guide on how to use Schwarzkopf hair color:

  1. Put on the gloves provided in the box
  2. Empty the color cream into the developer lotion bottle
  3. Shake the bottle well to mix the ingredients
  4. Apply the mixture to dry hair
  5. Start at the roots and work your way to the ends
  6. Leave the color on for the recommended time
  7. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water
  8. Apply the conditioner provided in the box and leave it on for 2-3 minutes
  9. Rinse your hair thoroughly with cool water

Tips

Here are some additional tips to help make your hair color last longer:

  • Avoid washing your hair for the first 48 hours after coloring
  • Use a color-safe shampoo and conditioner
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as much as possible
  • Avoid exposing your hair to chlorine or saltwater
  • Wear a hat or use a UV protection spray when spending time in the sun

FAQ

Will Schwarzkopf hair color damage my hair?

When used as directed, Schwarzkopf hair color is safe for all hair types. The keratin in the formula helps to strengthen and protect the hair while coloring it.

How often should I color my hair?

It's best to wait at least 4-6 weeks between colorings to give your hair time to recover and prevent damage.

Can I color my hair if I have a perm?

It's best to wait at least 2 weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair to avoid damaging it.

Hair Care

To maintain the health and vibrancy of your hair color, it's important to take good care of your hair. Here are some tips:

  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week
  • Avoid using hot tools on your hair as much as possible
  • Trim your hair regularly to prevent split ends
  • Avoid brushing wet hair to prevent breakage

Using the right hair color brand can make all the difference in how long your color lasts. Schwarzkopf's long-lasting and vibrant colors, infused with keratin, are a great choice for anyone looking to experiment with their hair color. By following the tips and guidelines provided, you can help make your color last and keep your hair healthy and vibrant.
