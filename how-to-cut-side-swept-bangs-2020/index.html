How To Cut Side Swept Bangs 2020 A Step By Step Guide

Written by Daniel Sep 21, 2023 · 3 min read

Do you want to switch up your hairstyle and add some bangs to your look? Side swept bangs are a popular choice for many because they are versatile and easy to maintain. In this blog post, we will guide you on how to cut side swept bangs 2020 style. Let’s get started!

Table of Contents

  • Description
  • How to Cut Side Swept Bangs 2020
  • Step-by-Step Guide
  • Tips for Cutting Side Swept Bangs
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Hair Care Tips

Description

Side swept bangs are a popular hairstyle where the hair is cut diagonally across the forehead, creating a sweeping effect. This style can be worn with any hair length and texture, making it a versatile option for many. Cutting side swept bangs can be easily done at home with some practice and patience.

How to Cut Side Swept Bangs 2020

Before starting, make sure you have the proper tools such as sharp scissors, a comb, and hair clips. It is recommended to cut your hair while it is dry, as wet hair tends to appear longer and may result in cutting too much.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Start by separating the bangs from the rest of your hair.
  2. Comb your hair straight down in front of your face.
  3. Hold the hair between your index and middle fingers where you want to cut.
  4. Cut the hair diagonally, starting from the middle and working your way outwards.
  5. Cut a little at a time, as it is easier to cut more later than to fix a mistake.
  6. Check the length of your bangs by pulling them to the side.
  7. Repeat on the other side, making sure both sides are even.
  8. Style your bangs as desired.

Tips for Cutting Side Swept Bangs

  • Use sharp scissors to ensure a clean cut.
  • Cut your hair when it is dry to avoid cutting too much.
  • Start by cutting a little at a time, as it is easier to cut more later than to fix a mistake.
  • Check the length of your bangs frequently by pulling them to the side.
  • If you are unsure, it is always better to cut less than to cut too much.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How often should I trim my side swept bangs?

A: It is recommended to trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to maintain their shape.

Q: Can I cut my bangs myself?

A: Yes, you can cut your bangs at home with some practice and patience. However, if you are unsure, it is always best to visit a professional hairstylist.

Q: Can I style my side swept bangs differently?

A: Yes, you can style your side swept bangs in different ways such as straight, curled, or pinned back.

Hair Care Tips

  • Use a heat protectant when styling your hair with heat tools.
  • Wash your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner to prevent damage.
  • Avoid using too much product on your bangs, as it can weigh them down.
  • Trim your bangs regularly to maintain their shape.

With these simple steps and tips, you can easily cut your own side swept bangs at home. Remember to take your time and cut a little at a time to avoid mistakes. Keep your hair healthy and styled with proper hair care practices. Enjoy your new look!
