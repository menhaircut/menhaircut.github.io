How To Tie Up Short Hair With A Scarf A Step By Step Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old hairstyle? Do you want to add a pop of color to your hair? If so, why not try tying up your short hair with a scarf? Not only is it a fun and stylish way to switch up your look, but it also helps keep your hair out of your face on those hot summer days. In this post, we’ll walk you through how to tie up short hair with a scarf, including tips and tricks for making your scarf stay put all day long.

How to Tie Up Short Hair with a Scarf

Before we get started on the step-by-step guide, here are a few things you’ll need:

  • A square scarf
  • A hairbrush or comb
  • Bobby pins or hair ties (optional)

Step 1: Brush Your Hair

The first step to tying up your short hair with a scarf is to brush it thoroughly. This will ensure that your hair is smooth and free from any tangles, making it easier to work with.

Step 2: Fold the Scarf

Next, take your square scarf and fold it in half diagonally to create a triangle shape.

Step 3: Place the Scarf on Your Head

Place the scarf on top of your head with the longest side of the triangle facing downwards. Make sure that the scarf is centered on your head and that the two ends are equal in length.

Step 4: Tie the Scarf at the Back

Take the two ends of the scarf and tie them together at the back of your head. This will create a knot that holds the scarf in place.

Step 5: Tuck in the Loose Ends

If you have any loose ends of the scarf hanging down, tuck them in under the knot at the back of your head. This will create a neat and tidy look.

Step 6: Style Your Hair

Now that your scarf is securely in place, you can style your hair however you like. You can leave it down, tie it up in a ponytail, or create a bun. If you have any shorter pieces of hair that won’t stay put, you can use bobby pins or hair ties to keep them in place.

Tips for Tying Up Short Hair with a Scarf

Here are a few tips and tricks to help you make the most out of your scarf hairstyle:

  • Choose a scarf that complements your outfit and adds a pop of color to your hair.
  • If you have thicker hair, you may need to use bobby pins or hair ties to keep your hair in place.
  • Experiment with different scarf styles, such as a turban or headband.
  • If you’re going to be outside in the wind, use a hairspray or gel to keep your hair from flying around.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about tying up short hair with a scarf:

Can I tie up my hair with any scarf?

Yes, you can use any scarf to tie up your hair. However, a square scarf works best for this style.

Can I tie up my hair with a scarf if I have long hair?

Yes, you can tie up your long hair with a scarf. However, you may need to fold the scarf in a different way to make it work.

How long will the scarf stay in place?

The scarf should stay in place all day as long as you tie it tightly and use bobby pins or hair ties if necessary.

Hair Care

Remember to take care of your hair when tying it up with a scarf. Avoid tying your hair too tightly to prevent hair breakage. Also, make sure to wash your hair regularly and use a good conditioner to keep it healthy and shiny.

Now that you know how to tie up short hair with a scarf, why not give it a try? It’s a fun and easy way to switch up your look and add some color to your hair. Just remember to experiment with different styles and have fun with it!
