Can You Get A Perm If You Have Curly Hair

Written by Frank Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Curly hair is a beautiful and unique feature that many people desire to have. However, for those who have naturally curly hair, it can be difficult to manage and style. One solution that some people consider is getting a perm. But can you get a perm if you have curly hair? In this article, we will answer this question and provide tips on how to care for permed curly hair.

Description

A perm is a chemical treatment that changes the structure of your hair to make it curly or wavy. It involves applying a chemical solution to your hair, which breaks down the bonds that hold the hair in its natural shape. After the solution is applied, your hair is wrapped around rods and a neutralizer is applied to set the curls. The result is permanent curls or waves that can last for several months.

How to

Step 1: Consult with a Professional

Before getting a perm, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist who is experienced in working with curly hair. They will be able to assess your hair type and determine if a perm is suitable for you. They will also be able to recommend the best type of perm solution and rod size to achieve your desired look.

Step 2: Prepare Your Hair

Prior to getting a perm, it is important to prepare your hair by washing it with a clarifying shampoo to remove any buildup or products. Do not use any conditioner or styling products prior to the appointment as it can interfere with the perm solution.

Step 3: Get the Perm

During the perm appointment, your hair will be sectioned and wrapped around rods. The perm solution will be applied and left on for a specific amount of time, depending on your hair type and desired result. Once the solution is rinsed out, a neutralizer will be applied to set the curls.

Step 4: Care for Your Permed Hair

After getting a perm, it is important to follow a proper hair care routine to maintain the health of your hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for permed hair. Avoid using hot tools such as flat irons and curling irons, as they can damage the hair and alter the curls. Instead, air dry your hair or use a diffuser attachment on your blow dryer.

Tips

  • Wait at least 48 hours after getting a perm before washing your hair
  • Avoid tying your hair up in a tight ponytail or bun for at least a week after getting a perm
  • Use a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle your hair to prevent breakage
  • Avoid swimming in chlorinated water as it can cause the curls to loosen
  • Consider getting regular trims to maintain the health of your permed hair

FAQ

Can you get a perm if you have naturally curly hair?

Yes, you can get a perm if you have naturally curly hair. However, it is important to consult with a professional hairstylist to determine if a perm is suitable for you.

How long does a perm last?

A perm can last for several months, depending on the type of perm solution and the care you provide to your hair.

Can you color your hair after getting a perm?

It is generally recommended to wait at least two weeks after getting a perm before coloring your hair. This allows your hair to recover from the chemical treatment and prevent damage.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is essential for maintaining the health of permed curly hair. Use a sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner specifically designed for permed hair to prevent damage and maintain the curls. Avoid using hot tools and tie your hair up loosely to prevent breakage. Consider getting regular trims to maintain the health of your hair and prevent split ends.

In conclusion, getting a perm if you have curly hair is possible. It is important to consult with a professional hairstylist, prepare your hair properly, and follow a proper hair care routine to maintain the health of your permed curly hair.
