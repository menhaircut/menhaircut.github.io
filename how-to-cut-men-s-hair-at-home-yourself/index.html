How To Cut Men s Hair At Home Yourself A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Jun 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're tired of paying for expensive haircuts or just want to try your hand at cutting your own hair, then you've come to the right place. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know about how to cut men's hair at home yourself. From the tools you'll need to the best techniques to use, we've got you covered. So grab your scissors and let's get started!

Table of Contents

  • Tools You'll Need
  • Preparing for the Cut
  • Cutting Techniques
  • Tips for Success
  • FAQs
  • Hair Care After the Cut

Tools You'll Need

Before you get started, you'll need to gather a few essential tools. Here's what you'll need:

  • Scissors
  • Clippers
  • Comb
  • Haircutting cape or towel
  • Spray bottle filled with water

Make sure your scissors are sharp and your clippers are charged before you begin.

Preparing for the Cut

Once you have your tools ready, it's time to prepare for the cut. Here's what to do:

  1. Wash and dry your hair.
  2. Put on your haircutting cape or drape a towel over your shoulders to catch any hair clippings.
  3. Decide on the length and style you want, and find a picture or video tutorial to follow if needed.
  4. Use your comb to separate your hair into sections.

Now you're ready to start cutting!

Cutting Techniques

There are a few different cutting techniques you can use depending on the style you're going for. Here are some of the most common techniques:

Clipper Cut

For a shorter, buzzed style, use your clippers to cut your hair to the desired length. Start with a longer guard and work your way down to a shorter guard as needed. Use a mirror to help you see the back of your head.

Scissor Cut

For a longer, layered style, use your scissors to cut your hair section by section. Start at the back and work your way forward, cutting small amounts at a time. Use your comb to help guide your scissors.

Texturizing

To add texture and depth to your hair, use your scissors to cut into the ends of your hair. This will create a choppy, layered look.

Tips for Success

Here are some tips to help you achieve a successful at-home haircut:

  • Start with a longer guard or cut more hair than you think you need to. You can always go shorter, but you can't put hair back once it's cut.
  • Use a mirror to check your progress from all angles.
  • Take breaks if you need to. Cutting your own hair can be tiring and time-consuming.
  • Don't be too hard on yourself if it's not perfect. Remember, hair grows back!

FAQs

Can I cut my own hair without clippers?

Yes, you can use just scissors to cut your hair. However, it may be more difficult to achieve a short, buzzed style without clippers.

How often should I cut my hair?

It depends on your hair type and style, but most men should get a haircut every 4-6 weeks.

What if I mess up?

If you make a mistake, don't panic. You can always go shorter or try to fix it with some creative styling. Worst case scenario, you can always wear a hat until it grows out!

Hair Care After the Cut

Once you're finished cutting your hair, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are some tips:

  • Shampoo and condition your hair regularly.
  • Use a styling product if desired.
  • Protect your hair from heat damage by using a heat protectant spray before styling with hot tools.
  • Trim any split ends regularly to keep your hair looking healthy.

With these tips and techniques, you should be able to successfully cut your own hair at home. Good luck and happy cutting!
