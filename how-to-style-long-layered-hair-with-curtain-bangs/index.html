How To Style Long Layered Hair With Curtain Bangs

Written by Eveline Sep 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're looking for a fresh and trendy hairstyle, long layered hair with curtain bangs might be the perfect choice for you. This style is versatile and easy to maintain, making it perfect for women who want a low-maintenance look. In this article, we'll show you how to style long layered hair with curtain bangs, step by step.

Description

Long layered hair with curtain bangs is a hairstyle that features long layers and bangs that are swept to the side, framing the face. This style is perfect for women with longer hair who want to add some dimension and movement to their locks. The layers add volume and texture, while the curtain bangs frame the face and add a touch of femininity.

How to

Follow these simple steps to style your long layered hair with curtain bangs:

Step 1: Wash and Dry Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Towel-dry your hair and apply a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.

Step 2: Blow Dry Your Hair

Blow dry your hair using a round brush to create volume and smooth out any kinks. Use a nozzle attachment to direct the air flow and prevent frizz.

Step 3: Curl Your Hair

Use a curling wand or iron to curl your hair in loose waves. Start at the mid-lengths and work your way down to the ends, leaving the roots straight.

Step 4: Style Your Bangs

Use a straightening iron to smooth out your curtain bangs and create a sleek finish. Sweep them to the side and tuck them behind your ear for a polished look.

Step 5: Finish with Hairspray

Spray some hairspray to hold your style in place and add some shine.

Tips

Here are some tips to help you achieve the perfect long layered hair with curtain bangs:

  • Use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from styling tools.
  • Use a round brush to create volume while blow drying your hair.
  • Curl your hair in loose waves for a natural, effortless look.
  • Sweep your curtain bangs to the side and tuck them behind your ear for a polished finish.
  • Use hairspray to hold your style in place and add some shine.

FAQ

Here are some frequently asked questions about long layered hair with curtain bangs:

Can I still wear a ponytail with curtain bangs?

Yes, you can still wear a ponytail with curtain bangs. Simply sweep your bangs to the side and pin them in place with bobby pins.

How often should I trim my bangs?

You should trim your bangs every 4-6 weeks to keep them looking fresh and prevent them from getting too long.

Can I style my curtain bangs differently?

Yes, you can style your curtain bangs in different ways. You can sweep them to the side, part them in the middle, or even wear them straight down.

Hair Care

To keep your long layered hair with curtain bangs looking healthy and beautiful, follow these hair care tips:

  • Use a gentle shampoo and conditioner to avoid stripping your hair of natural oils.
  • Use a deep conditioning treatment once a week to nourish and hydrate your hair.
  • Avoid using heat styling tools too often to prevent damage to your hair.
  • Trim your hair every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends and maintain its shape.

With these tips and tricks, you can style your long layered hair with curtain bangs like a pro. Whether you're heading to the office or out for a night on the town, this hairstyle is sure to turn heads and make you feel confident and beautiful.
