How Long Does It Take To Grow Freeform Dreads

Written by Bardi Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're thinking about growing freeform dreads, you may have a lot of questions about the process. One of the most common questions is how long it takes to grow freeform dreads. The answer to this question can vary depending on a few different factors, but in general, it takes around 6-12 months to grow freeform dreads.

Description

Freeform dreads are a popular hairstyle choice for many people because they are low-maintenance and can look great on just about anyone. Unlike traditional dreadlocks, which are often created using a specific technique, freeform dreads are allowed to form naturally over time.

How To

If you're interested in growing freeform dreads, there are a few steps you can take to help the process along:

Step 1: Stop Cutting Your Hair

In order for your hair to start forming into dreadlocks, you need to stop cutting it. This means letting your hair grow out naturally without getting regular haircuts. The longer your hair is, the easier it will be for it to form into dreads.

Step 2: Stop Using Hair Products

Hair products like shampoo, conditioner, and styling products can interfere with the natural process of dread formation. To help your hair form dreadlocks, you should stop using these products altogether. Instead, you can wash your hair with a simple mixture of baking soda and water.

Step 3: Separate Your Hair

As your hair starts to form into dreads, you may notice that some of the strands are clumping together. To prevent your dreads from becoming too large and difficult to manage, you should separate your hair regularly. This can be done by gently pulling apart any strands that start to stick together.

Tips

Here are a few tips to help you grow healthy, strong freeform dreads:

Tip 1: Be Patient

Growing freeform dreads takes time, so it's important to be patient. It can take several months for your hair to start forming into dreadlocks, and even longer for them to fully mature.

Tip 2: Eat A Healthy Diet

What you eat can have a big impact on the health of your hair. To help your hair grow strong and healthy, make sure you're eating a well-balanced diet that includes plenty of protein, vitamins, and minerals.

Tip 3: Stay Hydrated

Drinking plenty of water is important for the health of your hair as well as your overall health. Make sure you're drinking at least 8 glasses of water per day to keep your hair hydrated.

FAQ

How often should I wash my hair while growing freeform dreads?

You should wash your hair whenever it feels dirty or oily. However, you should avoid using traditional shampoo and instead opt for a baking soda and water mixture.

Can I still style my hair while growing freeform dreads?

You can still style your hair while growing freeform dreads, but you should avoid using any products that can interfere with the natural dread formation process.

Do I need to visit a stylist to grow freeform dreads?

No, you don't need to visit a stylist to grow freeform dreads. They are a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be grown naturally.

Hair Care

Proper hair care is important for the health and maintenance of your freeform dreads. Here are a few tips to keep your dreads looking great:

Tip 1: Wash Your Hair Regularly

You should wash your hair regularly to keep it clean and healthy. Use a baking soda and water mixture instead of traditional shampoo.

Tip 2: Separate Your Hair

Regularly separating your hair can help prevent your dreads from becoming too large and difficult to manage.

Tip 3: Use Aloe Vera Gel

Aloe vera gel can help moisturize your hair and scalp, which can help prevent dryness and breakage.

In conclusion, growing freeform dreads can be a rewarding and low-maintenance hairstyle choice. While it can take several months for your hair to start forming into dreadlocks, with patience and proper hair care, you can grow healthy, strong dreads that look great.
