Does Round Face Look Good With Short Hair

Written by Robby Aug 12, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Many women with round faces often wonder if they can pull off short hair. The answer is yes, absolutely! Short hair can look amazing on a round face, as long as you choose the right cut and style that flatters your features. In this article, we will explore how to choose the best short haircuts for round faces and provide tips for maintaining your new look.

  • Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

  • Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on Round Faces

How to Choose the Best Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Before you head to the salon, it's important to know what type of short haircut will work best for your round face. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Consider Your Face Shape

When it comes to short haircuts for round faces, you want to choose a style that will elongate your face and create the illusion of a more oval shape. Look for cuts that have height at the crown or top of the head, as this will add length to your face. Avoid styles that are too blunt or chin-length, as these will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

Opt for Layers

Layers are a great way to add movement and texture to short hair and can also help to slim down a round face. Ask your stylist to add layers to your cut, focusing on the crown and top of the head. This will help to create volume and height, which will elongate your face.

Choose the Right Bangs

Bangs can be a great addition to a short haircut for a round face, but it's important to choose the right type of bangs. Avoid blunt or straight across bangs, as these will only make your face look rounder. Instead, opt for side-swept or angled bangs, which will help to create a more angular look.

Step-by-Step Guide to Styling Short Hair for Round Faces

Now that you've chosen the perfect short haircut for your round face, it's time to learn how to style it. Here is a step-by-step guide:

Step 1: Wash Your Hair

Start by washing your hair with a volumizing shampoo and conditioner. This will help to add body and fullness to your hair, which is important for a short haircut.

Step 2: Apply Product

Apply a styling product to your hair, such as mousse or a volumizing spray. This will help to add texture and hold to your hair, making it easier to style.

Step 3: Blow Dry Your Hair

Using a round brush, blow dry your hair in sections, starting at the crown and working your way down. This will help to add volume and height to your hair, which will elongate your face.

Step 4: Style Your Hair

Once your hair is dry, use a flat iron or curling iron to add texture and movement to your hair. You can also use a texturizing spray to create a messy, tousled look.

Tips for Maintaining Short Hair for Round Faces

Short haircuts for round faces require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. Here are some tips:

Get Regular Trims

To keep your short haircut looking fresh and stylish, it's important to get regular trims every 4-6 weeks. This will help to prevent split ends and keep your hair looking healthy and shiny.

Use the Right Products

When it comes to styling short hair, it's important to use the right products. Look for products that add volume and texture, such as mousse, texturizing spray, or dry shampoo.

Experiment with Different Styles

Short haircuts for round faces can be versatile and fun. Don't be afraid to experiment with different styles, such as adding braids or accessories to your hair.

Frequently Asked Questions About Short Haircuts for Round Faces

Q: Can I wear a pixie cut with a round face?

A: Yes, a pixie cut can look great on a round face, as long as it's styled in a way that elongates your face. Opt for a pixie cut with lots of texture and height at the crown.

Q: What are the best bangs for a round face?

A: Side-swept or angled bangs are the best choice for a round face, as they help to create a more angular look. Avoid blunt or straight across bangs, which will only emphasize the roundness of your face.

Hair Care Tips for Short Hair on Round Faces

Use a Volumizing Shampoo and Conditioner

When it comes to short haircuts for round faces, volume is key. Use a volumizing shampoo and conditioner to add body and fullness to your hair.

Avoid Overwashing Your Hair

Short hair can be prone to dryness, so it's important to avoid overwashing your hair. Aim to wash your hair every other day, or use a dry shampoo in between washes.

Protect Your Hair from Heat

When styling your short hair, be sure to use a heat protectant spray to prevent damage from hot tools. Also, avoid using high heat settings on your styling tools, as this can cause damage to your hair.

Conclusion

Short haircuts can look amazing on round faces, as long as you choose the right cut and style that flatters your features. Remember to consider your face shape, opt for layers, and choose the right bangs. With the right styling products and regular maintenance, you can rock a short haircut with confidence!
